# World Bank Project Paper — Digital Cabo Verde Project (Additional Financing)

IDA Project Paper (**2 May 2025**) on a proposed **Additional Credit of SDR 15.1 million (≈US$20.0 million)** to the Republic of Cabo Verde for the **[[digital-cabo-verde-project|Digital Cabo Verde Project]]** (parent project **P171099**). The Additional Financing aims to strengthen Cabo Verde's digital-competitiveness foundations and improve the provision and use of digital public services, structured around three components:

1. **Enabling legal and regulatory environment;**
2. **Digital competitiveness;**
3. **Digital public services and marketplace.**

Confirms the World Bank financing behind the [[nosi|NOSi]]-led digitalisation drive and the "60% services online by 2026 / 80% by 2030" targets.
