## Description

Partech Africa's recently launched Africa Fund led a $3M Series A round in TradeDepot, a Nigeria-based SaaS platform connecting FMCG consumer goods brands to informal retailers across Africa. This was Partech Africa's first portfolio investment. The round supports expansion of TradeDepot's footprint in Nigeria and development in other African markets.
