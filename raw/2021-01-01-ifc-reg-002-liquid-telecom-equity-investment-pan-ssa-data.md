## Description

IFC made a $90 million equity investment in Liquid Telecom Jersey Ltd, the holding entity for Liquid's data centre operations across Sub-Saharan Africa. Liquid Intelligent Technologies is the largest independent data centre operator in SSA, with facilities in South Africa, Kenya, Zimbabwe, Zambia, and elsewhere. The investment accelerates expansion of cloud-hosting, colocation, and internet exchange point services. Co-investors in the Cassava Technologies platform include BII (British International Investment), US DFC, FinnFund, Google, Public Investment Corporation (South Africa), and others.
