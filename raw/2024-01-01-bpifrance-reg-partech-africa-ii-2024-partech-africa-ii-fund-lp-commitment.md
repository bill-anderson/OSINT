## Description

Bpifrance LP commitment to Partech Africa II, the largest venture capital fund dedicated to technology startups in Africa (hard cap €280M). The fund invests initial tickets of $1M–$15M from Seed to Series C across African tech and digital companies covering fintech, healthtech, edtech, mobility, logistics and other digital sectors in Sub-Saharan Africa and pan-African markets. This is the first deployment under Bpifrance's €350M Africa co-investment partnership with Mubadala (signed June 2021). Bpifrance and Mubadala announced their joint commitment on 6 March 2024. Fund invests across 27+ African countries.
