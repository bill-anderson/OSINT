DataReportal's Digital 2026 report for Malawi (published end of 2025; data points reflect the latest available as of October 2025). Current baseline headline figures:

- 14.1 million cellular mobile connections in late 2025 (63.0 percent of the population; up 1.1 million / +8.6% from end-2024). GSMA Intelligence: 92.5% of connections are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- 4.02 million internet users at end of 2025; internet penetration 18.0 percent.
- 2.40 million social media user identities in October 2025 (10.7 percent of the population).

Population (UN data): 22.4 million in October 2025 (+562k / +2.6% over the year). 19.0% urban, 81.0% rural. 51.2% female, 48.8% male. Median age 18.1. Note: DataReportal changed its publication cycle (Digital 2026 published end of 2025), so year-on-year comparisons against Digital 2025 figures should not be computed directly.
