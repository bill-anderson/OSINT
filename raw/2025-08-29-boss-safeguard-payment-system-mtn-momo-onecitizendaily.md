# BoSS vows to safeguard robust payment system

*One Citizen Daily, 29 August 2025*

The Bank of South Sudan vowed to remain committed to building and safeguarding a robust national payment system that is secure, inclusive, efficient and built on consumer trust. The statement came during the launch of MTN MoMo Fintech's "Mata fekir" nationwide campaign in Juba.

Central Bank Governor Dr. Addis Ababa Othow commended MTN for the launch, saying the bold and strategic campaign would play a central role in advancing South Sudan's transition toward a modern digital economy. "The Bank of South Sudan remains firmly committed to building and safeguarding a robust national payment system, one that is secure, inclusive, efficient and built on consumer trust," he stated.

Othow said the journey toward a cashless and digital-driven economy cannot be undertaken by government alone but requires strong partnership with an innovative private sector, and recognized MTN's investment in developing the South Sudan digital payment ecosystem. He challenged MTN MoMo to remain at the forefront of innovation by expanding its agent network.
