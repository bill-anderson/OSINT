# Digital Governance Analysis: Seychelles, 2024 Edition

Companion source page for the artefact `2024-01-01 Digital Governance Analysis - Seychelles 2024 Edition.pdf`, held in `raw/`.

Prepared by the **Economic Forum for Sustainable Development** and stated on its cover as
*"Report Created For: The Secretary General of The Commonwealth of Nations"*. It is an index
digest rather than original research: its contents are chapters on active mobile-broadband
subscriptions, the E-Government Development Index, innovation capacity, the Online Service
Index, the Telecommunication Infrastructure Index, the Human Capital Index and the Global
Cybersecurity Index — i.e. it restates other bodies' indices for Seychelles.

**Reference study — cite, do not absorb.** Its dated figures are not to be promoted onto
concept or place pages as current state; cite the study and leave its numbers in it. Fresher
country-level data arrives via clippings. That guard matters doubly here: the figures are
themselves second-hand restatements of ITU/UN indices, so promoting them would launder an
index reading through an intermediary.
