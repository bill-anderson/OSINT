# Starlink enfin disponible officiellement au Niger

On **Thursday 13 March 2025**, the Minister of Communication, Posts and the Digital Economy, **Sidi Mohamed Raliou**, officially announced that **Starlink internet services are available in Niger from 13 March 2025** — residential and business kits accessible online and via approved sellers. Starlink (SpaceX LEO satellite broadband) promises reliable high-speed connectivity even in remote areas; ~80% of Niger's population is rural and internet access has been marked by frequent outages, very slow speeds and high prices.

The Minister stressed regulatory limits: services are "strictly for the personal or professional use of the sole client holding the Starlink kit," who is solely responsible for its use and any sharing. The launch follows a **partnership agreement signed 29 October 2024 between the Nigerien state and SpaceX** authorising nationwide deployment — framed within the junta's digital strategy to reduce the digital divide and stimulate the economy.
