Raxio Group launched **CIV1** in Abidjan (September 2024) — its 5th data centre and third launch of 2024 — a **Tier III carrier-neutral** facility at **VITIB** (Village of Innovation and Technology), first announced November 2022. Part of Côte d'Ivoire's growing Grand-Bassam data-centre cluster and **[[raxio-group]]**'s pan-African footprint.
