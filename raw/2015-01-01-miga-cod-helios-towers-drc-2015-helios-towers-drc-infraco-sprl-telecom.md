## Description

MIGA political risk guarantee for Helios Towers DRC Infraco SPRL (HTD), an independent telecom tower company operating in DRC. Covers upgrade, reactivation and expansion of HTD's tower portfolio, providing passive infrastructure (space on towers, ground space, power, maintenance) to multiple telecom operators under long-term lease agreements. This FY2015 issuance represents additional coverage for the tower network expansion.
