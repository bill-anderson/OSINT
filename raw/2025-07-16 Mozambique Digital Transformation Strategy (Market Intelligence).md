U.S. ITA market-intelligence note on Mozambique's Digital Transformation Strategy (16 July 2025). The 2025 creation of the Ministry of Communications and Digital Transformation unified ICT-focused public institutions. The government is drafting a Digital Transformation Strategy covering connectivity, e-government and social services.

Connectivity: in December 2024 the government launched the "Internet for All" project, aiming to expand internet access from under 25% to 100% of the population by 2030; the National Communications Institute (INCM) plans tenders for rural connectivity infrastructure. E-Government: digital signatures, integration of all e-government systems and unified data management, an e-Gov payment gateway (Ministry of Finance), an Electronic Procurement System (e-GP) launched April 2025, and a Citizen Portal (digital ID, civil registration, tax number). Social services: INCM's "Internet nas Escolas" to connect 13,000 public schools by 2030 and the Ministry of Health's "Health Units Digitalization Project" (December 2024). Demand exists for cybersecurity, cloud, software, rural connectivity, telemedicine, affordable e-devices and customer-service AI tools.
