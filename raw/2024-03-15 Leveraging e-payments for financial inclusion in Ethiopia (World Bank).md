# Leveraging e-payments for financial inclusion in Ethiopia (World Bank)

Ethiopia's safety-net programmes — among Africa's largest, reaching **9m+** people — are shifting to **electronic cash transfers** for efficiency, transparency and as an on-ramp to formal finance. The rural **PSNP** (from 2005) moved from physical cash; the urban **UPSNJP** used e-payments from 2016. Adults with a bank account rose from **22% (2014) to ~45% (2020)** (NBE); Findex put inclusion at 46% (2022). The revised financial-inclusion strategy targeted **70% account ownership by 2025**.
