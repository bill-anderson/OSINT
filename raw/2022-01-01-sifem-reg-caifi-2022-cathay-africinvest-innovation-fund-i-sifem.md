## Description

SIFEM committed EUR 7 million to Cathay AfricInvest Innovation Fund I (CAIF I), a €110 million pan-African venture capital fund jointly managed by AfricInvest and Cathay Innovation. The fund invests in innovative African tech companies across software, financial services, communications, healthcare and education, supporting digital entrepreneurship and financial innovation across SSA.
