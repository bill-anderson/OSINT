## Description

SIFEM committed USD 15 million to Helios Investors Fund V, a pan-African growth equity fund targeting mid-market companies benefiting from Africa's demographic expansion and urbanisation. The fund focuses on digital infrastructure, financial services, and tech-enabled business services, with a 50% gender equality commitment for portfolio companies.
