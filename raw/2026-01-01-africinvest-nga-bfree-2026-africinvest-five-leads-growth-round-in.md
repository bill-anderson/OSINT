## Description

AfricInvest's Financial Inclusion Vehicle (FIVE) leads a growth round in BFREE, a Lagos-based pan-African technology-enabled platform focused on acquisition and servicing of non-performing unsecured retail and SME loan portfolios. BFREE uses proprietary data infrastructure and AI-powered credit risk management to help financial institutions manage distressed credit. Managing over 10 million borrower accounts across 35+ transactions at time of investment. Co-investors include Algebra Ventures (first Nigeria investment), Capria Ventures, VestedWorld, Axian CVC, Angaza Capital, 4Di Capital, and DotExe Ventures.
