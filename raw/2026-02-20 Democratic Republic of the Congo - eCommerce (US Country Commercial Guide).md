The U.S. ITA Country Commercial Guide's DRC e-commerce chapter (February 2026) notes e-commerce is **still nascent but expanding**: only around **20%** of Congolese have mobile-broadband subscriptions and internet penetration is **under 10%**, but **mobile-money use is rising quickly**. It references the **Digital Code**, mobile-money interoperability, the central bank (**BCC**), and payment entrants **Visa Pay** and **M-Pesa** — a reference snapshot of the DRC's digital-commerce and payments landscape.
