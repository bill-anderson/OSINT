# Findex 2025 and Ethiopia's digital financial leap — momentum without maturity

Global **Findex 2025** shows Ethiopian financial-account ownership rose only slightly, **46% (2021) → 49% (2024)**, despite **digital accounts surging from 94.7m (Jun 2022) to 222.1m (Mar 2025)** — inclusion remains uneven, with literacy, smartphone access and distance still excluding millions. Global ownership is 79% (75% in LMICs). **Caveat:** the Ethiopia survey (1,001 adults, Oct–Nov 2024) **excluded the Amhara, Tigray, Harari and Somali regions** — ~30% of the population — a notable representativeness gap.
