Central Bank of Libya (**[[central-bank-of-libya|CBL]]**) statistics (covering 2025 up to 15 February 2026, via the GNU Hakomitna platform) show unprecedented growth in electronic payments:

- **Mobile banking:** 4.29 million subscribers; **200.7 million transactions worth 313.6 billion Libyan dinars**.
- **POS terminals:** 165,313 (from 150,205 in 2024); 288.6 million transactions worth 37.8bn LYD.
- **Bank cards:** 5.5 million activated; ATM withdrawals ~23.9 million transactions / 12.6bn LYD.
- **E-wallets:** 186,817 accounts, circulation >92 million LYD.
- **Instant-transfer platform LYPAY ONEPAY:** 18.3 million transactions worth 108.7bn LYD.

Economists say the rise in digital transactions should reduce the parallel economy, strengthen financial transparency and improve banking-system efficiency.
