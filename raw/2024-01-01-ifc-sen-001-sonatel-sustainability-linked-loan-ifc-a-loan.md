## Description

IFC provided a senior unsecured A-loan of up to €37 million (local currency equivalent) to Sonatel SA, Senegal's leading telecoms operator with operations across Senegal, Mali, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, and Sierra Leone. IFC also mobilised B1 and B2 loans from co-lenders. The total package — including BII (€25M) and Proparco (€30M) — amounts to approximately €87 million in a sustainability-linked structure linking pricing to telecoms infrastructure expansion KPIs. Proceeds support modernisation and expansion of mobile and broadband networks, improving affordable digital access across West Africa.
