# South Africa's Digital Infrastructure Investment Study 2025

A DBSA-commissioned study assessing South Africa's digital communications infrastructure against the ITU Universal & Meaningful Connectivity framework, benchmarked internationally. It covers first/middle/last-mile (fibre, mobile, FWA, data centres), government-facility connectivity, DPI/e-government and affordability, and models the investment required to reach universal and meaningful access under "Beyond the Gap" scenarios. From this it derives policy goals on last-mile reach, device affordability, household subsidy and digital literacy. This is the study behind the national broadband map ([[sadis-2025]]); see also the source `2026-07-10 SA finally has a broadband map - and it reveals where the gaps are`.
