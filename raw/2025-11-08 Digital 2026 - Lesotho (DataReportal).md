DataReportal's Digital 2026 report for Lesotho (published end of 2025; data reflect late 2025):

- Population: 2.37 million (Oct 2025, +1.1% YoY); 31.5% urban, 68.5% rural; median age 21.8.
- Mobile connections: 2.20 million (92.7% of population, +5.2% YoY; 94.0% broadband).
- Internet users: 1.14 million, penetration 48.0% at end of 2025.
- Social media user identities: 653,000 (27.5% of population).

Source: GSMA Intelligence and Kepios. (Baseline connectivity figures for the Lesotho hub.)
