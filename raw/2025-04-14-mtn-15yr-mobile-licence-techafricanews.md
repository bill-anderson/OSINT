# MTN Secures 15-Year License to Deepen Digital Footprint in South Sudan

*TechAfrica News, 14 April 2025*

MTN has announced the successful acquisition of a new 15-year operating license in South Sudan, described as a major step in aligning its operations with the country's evolving legal and regulatory landscape. MTN South Sudan said the license introduces crucial obligations that will benefit the people of South Sudan.

The company said it is energized by the opportunity to enhance service quality, expand its network coverage to reach more communities, and drive innovation in the digital space. The long-term commitment allows it to plan and invest strategically in the infrastructure and technologies that will power South Sudan's future connectivity.

MTN extended gratitude to the Government of South Sudan for its support and trust, framing the license as a reaffirmation of its dedication to being a key enabler of economic and social development for the next 15 years.
