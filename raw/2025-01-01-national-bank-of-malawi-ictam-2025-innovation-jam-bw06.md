# National Bank of Malawi — ICTAM 2025 Innovation Jam, 2025

National Bank of Malawi donated MWK 75.5 million to the ICT Association of Malawi's 2025 Innovation Jam and signed a three-year MoU to support young innovators.

## Description

"There are expectations that Malawian young innovators will turn their ideas into thriving solutions ... courtesy of a K75.5 million boost for the 2025 Innovation Jam. As part of its six-year partnership with the ICT Association of Malawi (ICTAM), National Bank of Malawi (NBM) plc has made the donation to support the Innovation Jam." NBM also "signed a three-year memorandum of understanding (MoU) with ICTAM to ensure consistent mentorship, incubation, and technical support."
