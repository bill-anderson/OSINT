NITDA on 20 May 2025 commissioned a National Cybersecurity Centre at Bayero University, Kano (BUK), intended as a national hub for digital defense, innovation and talent development. DG Kashifu Inuwa Abdullahi (represented by Dr Jide Ajayi) described it as "a national asset" and a strategic intervention to enhance cybersecurity education, research and cross-sector collaboration, aligning with the Renewed Hope Agenda. It will support research, hands-on training and act as a resource hub for regional and national partners. BUK Vice-Chancellor Professor Sagir Adamu Abbas called it a transformative investment in higher education.
