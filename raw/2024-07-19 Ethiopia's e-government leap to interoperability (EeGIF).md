# Ethiopia's e-government leap to interoperability (EeGIF)

The **Ministry of Innovation and Technology (MiNT)** is leading interoperability of e-government applications under the National E-Government Strategy and Enterprise Architecture 2024–2029, implemented with consultant **DT Global**. The **Interoperable Digital Government Initiative** brings ministerial and regional offices under one unified domain — inspired by **Government Interoperability Frameworks (GIF)** in Brazil, the UK and Germany — assessing existing directives/laws to embed digital-by-default principles.

**Artefact:** the Ethiopian eGovernment Interoperability Framework (EeGIF) — Governance and Compliance — [[2024-07-19 Ethiopian eGovernment Interoperability Framework (EeGIF) - Governance and Compliance.pdf]] (MiNT / AAU School of Information Science).
