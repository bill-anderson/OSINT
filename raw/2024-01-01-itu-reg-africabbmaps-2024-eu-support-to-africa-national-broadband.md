## Description

EU-funded ITU-implemented project to establish or strengthen national broadband mapping systems in 11 Sub-Saharan African countries. Project maps broadband infrastructure and service coverage identifies connectivity gaps and supports evidence-based policy. Funded by EU Sub-Saharan Africa Regional Multi-Annual Indicative Programme 2021-2027 (Global Gateway). ITU BDT is implementing agency over 48 months. Countries: Benin Botswana Burundi Cote d'Ivoire Ethiopia Kenya Malawi Nigeria Uganda Zambia Zimbabwe.
