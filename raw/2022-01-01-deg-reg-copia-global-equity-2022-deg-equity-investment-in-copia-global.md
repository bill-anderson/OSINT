## Description

DEG provides USD 7.5 million in equity capital to Copia Global Inc., a pan-African e-commerce company operating in Kenya and Uganda. Copia enables low- and middle-income consumers in East Africa to access a wide range of products through a network of agents using a digital platform. Over the past year, Copia expanded to include integrated FinTech services such as operating loans for Copia Agents, managed through a digital channel. Proceeds grow core business, fund pan-African expansion, and develop integrated financial services. Total round approximately USD 50 million. Co-investors include DFC, Goodwell Investments, Lightrock, and Zebu Investment Partners.
