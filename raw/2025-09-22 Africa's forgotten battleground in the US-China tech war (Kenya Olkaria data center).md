Semafor exclusive (22 September 2025). The $1 billion geothermal-powered data center announced in May 2024 by G42 and Microsoft in Kenya's **Olkaria** region is **languishing** months before its expected first-phase deadline (~May 2026): developers have yet to break ground, with problems from geopolitics to financial snags. Fifteen months after the deal, G42, Microsoft and the Kenyan government are still working to identify the business rationale and a sustainable financial model.

The piece frames Kenya as a battleground in the US-China tech contest. **Huawei** has built ~70% of Africa's 4G networks and is deeply embedded in cloud, data center and power-grid modernization. **G42** severed ties with Huawei in late 2023 to secure access to US chips, after which Microsoft invested $1.5bn in G42; the project was intended as a blueprint for US tech firms to loosen Beijing's grip on African digital infrastructure.
