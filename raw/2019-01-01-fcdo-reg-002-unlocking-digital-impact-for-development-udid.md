## Description

FCDO programme implemented by the Digital Impact Alliance (DIAL) to improve digital solutions for development challenges. Supports open-source digital public goods, digital transformation strategies in partner countries, and dissemination of Principles for Digital Development. Africa (including Sub-Saharan Africa) is a primary focus region for DIAL's work on digital public goods, data interoperability, and DPI ecosystem strengthening. The programme promotes standards and governance frameworks for digital development globally, with significant Africa-facing implementation.
