# Google launches first African cloud region in Johannesburg (GCP)

**[[google|Google]]** officially launched its **first African cloud region in Johannesburg, South Africa** — a milestone in hyperscaler competition on the continent, positioned against **[[amazon-web-services|AWS]]**'s Cape Town region and Microsoft Azure. Enables lower-latency, in-region cloud/AI services and data residency for South African and regional customers. *(Filed under [[ZAF]] — this clip arrived in an Ethiopia batch but concerns the South Africa cloud region, not Ethiopia.)*
