## Description

Accelerates digital economy development in Burkina Faso through improved broadband access, digital government services, digital financial services, and digital entrepreneurship support.

## Development history

- **2024-03-11** — reported as the funding behind the government-branded PACTDIGITAL; components named as broadband for displacement-affected areas, digital public services, public-administration and youth skills, and large-scale WURI unique-ID enrolment. [2024-03-11 Burkina Faso's digital transformation gets 150M World Bank support.md]
- **2025-11-11** — government (Sidwaya / MTDPCE) framed the World Bank project as PACTDIGITAL, the financial backbone of its "12 chantiers" digital agenda; project value FCFA96.7bn with World Bank support, adopted June 2024 by the Assemblée législative de Transition. [2025-11-11-transformation-digitale-burkina-douze-chantiers]
- **2025-12-28** — ISR06425 (sequence 4) archived; the operation, branded PACTDIGITAL nationally, remained active on broadband, digital public services and digital skills components. [2025-12-28 World Bank - Burkina Faso Digital Acceleration Project (PACTDIGITAL) Implementation Status Report.md]
- **2026-03-23** — total project envelope stated as over CFA96 billion (IDA loan plus Burkinabè government contribution); launched 19 September 2024 for five years to 2030. [techafricanews.com]
