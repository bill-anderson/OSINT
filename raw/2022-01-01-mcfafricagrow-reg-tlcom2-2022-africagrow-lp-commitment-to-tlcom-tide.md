## Description

AfricaGrow (through Allianz and DEG Impact joint vehicle) commits to TLcom Capital's TIDE Africa Fund II, Africa's largest early-stage VC fund at $154M final close. Fund targets seed and Series A across East and West Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt, South Africa). Digital focus: fintech, agritech, healthtech, logistics. First close $70M in Jan 2022, final close April 2024.
