# Trident Digital Tech — RDC-PASS national digital ID PPP, DR Congo, 2025

DR Congo is building RDC-PASS, a national digital identity platform valued by the Ministry of Planning at US$97.1 million, under a 20-year public-private partnership signed in June 2025 with Singapore-based Trident Digital Tech, which holds exclusive e-KYC rights.

## Description

"The Ministry of Planning estimates the cost of developing RDC-PASS at $97.1 million. The government launched the project through a public-private partnership signed in June 2025 with Trident Digital Tech DRC Africa SAS, a subsidiary of Singapore-based Trident Digital Tech Holdings. The agreement grants the company exclusive rights to provide electronic know-your-customer (e-KYC) services for 20 years." RDC-PASS supports biometric SIM registration, unified e-government access via a single digital identifier, automated e-KYC for financial institutions, and issuance of secure digital identities. It forms part of a broader government plan to invest $1 billion in digital development between 2026 and 2030. RDC-PASS was officially launched in Kinshasa under President Félix Tshisekedi on 13 June 2026.
