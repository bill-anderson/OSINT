## Description

Commonwealth Digital Identity Initiative improving access to a digitally-enabled identity for every woman and girl in the Commonwealth by 2030. Partners with World Bank, GSMA and Caribou Digital to work with developing countries. Total value AUD 6.0 million over 2 years starting 2017-18. Commonwealth SSA members include Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, Rwanda, Sierra Leone, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, Zimbabwe, South Africa, Mozambique, Malawi and others. Activity supports foundational digital ID infrastructure and civil registration systems.
