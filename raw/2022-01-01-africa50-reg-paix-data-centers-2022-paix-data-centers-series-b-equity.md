## Description

Africa50 made a growth equity investment totalling over USD 30 million in PAIX Data Centres, a pan-African carrier-neutral colocation data centre platform with operating assets in Accra (Ghana) and Nairobi (Kenya), and pipeline data centres in Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, and Rwanda. The USD 20 million first tranche (Series B) was announced February 2022; a follow-on tranche brought the cumulative commitment above USD 30 million by July 2024. Africa50 is PAIX's first institutional investor. Funds used for data centre capacity expansion and entry into new African markets.
