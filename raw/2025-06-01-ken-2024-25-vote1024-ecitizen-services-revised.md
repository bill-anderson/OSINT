## Description

While ICT-vote infrastructure was being cut, Supplementary II quadrupled the e-Citizen line
(+KSh 2.0bn) - the platform monetising government services (KSh 50 convenience fee) got fiscal
priority in the year of revenue crisis. Fully absorbed.

## Notes

Against the money, the output missed badly: **3,458 of the 11,000-service onboarding target**
reached by 30 June 2025 (BIRR Table 4.105). The spend-vs-onboarding gap is the finding.
