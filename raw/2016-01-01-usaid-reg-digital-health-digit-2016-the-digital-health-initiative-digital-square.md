## Description

Digital Square is a USAID-funded initiative implemented by PATH that supports the adoption and scale of digital health tools across countries. Activities span 24+ SSA countries including Ethiopia, Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, Uganda, Mozambique, Malawi, Ghana, Senegal, Rwanda, and others. Supports health information systems, DHIS2, data interoperability, and digital health enabling environments.
