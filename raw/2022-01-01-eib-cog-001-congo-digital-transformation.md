## Description

Implementation of digital transformation programme of the public administration in Congo. Includes: creation of Personal Data Protection Commission; national cybersecurity strategy; CERT establishment; IT interoperability framework and data exchange platform; digital systems for tertiary education and health; digital skills capacity building; IT certification framework. Aligned with Congo Digital 2025 strategy. Total EIB loan EUR 36.3M (split across two tranches).
