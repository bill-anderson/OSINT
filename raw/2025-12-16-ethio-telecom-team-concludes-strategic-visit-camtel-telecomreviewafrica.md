# Ethio telecom Team Concludes Strategic Visit to CAMTEL

*Telecom Review Africa — December 16, 2025*

Ethio telecom has concluded a high-level strategic technical tour in Cameroon as part of its recently signed partnership with CAMTEL. The delegation, led by Ethio telecom CEO, Frehiwot Tamru, and joined by CAMTEL General Manager, Judith Yah Sunday, conducted an extensive working visit across CAMTEL's key technical facilities.

The mission marked the immediate transition from the partnership agreement to tangible joint action, underscoring the commitment of both operators to accelerate cooperation and strengthen operational alignment.

During the visit, the leadership teams toured CAMTEL's Network Operations Center, the Blue Store Warda digital customer experience outlet, and the Zamengoe Data Center. CAMTEL experts provided in-depth briefings on the company's infrastructure, operational processes, and ongoing technological innovations.

Following the tour, executives from both organizations held a wrap-up meeting to outline the next steps in the partnership's implementation, with a focus on technical collaboration, knowledge sharing, and customer-centric digital solutions.

Frehiwot Tamru highlighted the broader vision behind the alliance, noting that the cooperation extends beyond commercial objectives and reflects a shared commitment to advancing a digital ecosystem for Cameroon.

The visit concludes a week of intensive engagement between the two operators as they begin operationalizing their joint roadmap in support of digital transformation across the region.
