# Data Protection Laws of Senegal (reference summary)

Secondary reference summary describing Senegal's **[[senegal-data-protection-law|Law No. 2008-12]]** on the
protection of personal data (2008 ICT reforms; **in force 2014**), which established the
**[[cdp-senegal|Commission de Protection des Données Personnelles (CDP)]]** — noted for an accessible,
informative website and regular activity reports. Summarises data-subject rights (information, access,
objection, rectification/erasure, protection from solely automated adverse decisions), the definition of
sensitive data, and lawful-processing principles (fairness, purpose limitation, adequacy, accuracy, retention
limitation). Reference backing for the instrument entity; cite, don't absorb.
