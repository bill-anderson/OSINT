## Description

D4GW-BFA (Digital for Girls and Women) project aims to give women and men equal opportunities to participate in and contribute to a digital economy. Implemented by Enabel in Burkina Faso (component of a multi-country programme also covering Belgium and Uganda). Budget EUR 2.25M, 2021-2025. Promotes inclusive, rights-based digital transformation including digital skills, digital rights awareness, and gender-sensitive digital policies.
