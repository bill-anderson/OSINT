# EIB — AXIAN Telecom 4G/5G (Madagascar, Tanzania), 2025

The European Investment Bank (EIB Global) announced a US$100 million financing package to AXIAN Telecom to expand 4G and introduce 5G mobile broadband across Madagascar (US$40m) and Tanzania (US$60m), under the EU Global Gateway with a European Commission guarantee.

## Description

"The European Investment Bank (EIB Global) announced a financing package of US$100 million to AXIAN Telecom to support the expansion of its mobile broadband network infrastructure across Madagascar and Tanzania. … US$60 million of the financing will benefit Tanzania and US$40 million will go to Madagascar. … This new Global Gateway investment, backed by a budgetary guarantee of the European Commission, will enhance access to high-speed communications, accelerate inclusive digitalisation…"
