# Malawi Launches Chichewa AI Initiative to Drive Digital Inclusion and Innovation

The Malawi government has launched an initiative to develop **artificial intelligence models in Chichewa**, implemented through the **Malawi Low Resource Language Data Trust Initiative** — developing core AI frameworks and assembling licensed Chichewa-language content to expand training data for AI systems. The project is supported by the **World Bank and the Gates Foundation**, positioning Malawi among countries working to improve AI capabilities for underrepresented languages.

Speaking at the **AI for Malawi National Language Data Trust workshop** in Lilongwe, Minister of Defence Dr Feston Kaupa (representing ICT Minister Dr Shadric Namalomba) emphasized investing in AI for national development, noting local-language AI can expand access to digital technologies. The Chichewa AI initiative aims to create language resources for future applications — digital assistants, translation tools, educational platforms and other AI-powered services tailored to local needs.
