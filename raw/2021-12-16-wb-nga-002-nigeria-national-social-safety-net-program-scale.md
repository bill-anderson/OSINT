## Description

Component 3 of the $800M National Social Safety Net Program Scale-Up finances digital delivery systems for cash transfers: digital payment infrastructure, biometric-linked beneficiary accounts, electronic payment mechanisms. All transfers are digitally delivered to beneficiary-owned accounts backed by biometric ID. $53M of $800M total.
