## Description

Strengthens African AI ecosystems to develop and apply AI responsibly. Investments across: (1) AI policy and governance research supporting Africa-based institutes on risks and governance challenges for national AI policies; (2) AI talent and skills including interdisciplinary post-graduate AI research hubs at African universities; (3) Machine-learning ready datasets improving access for AI models and applications; (4) AI innovation supporting networks of African innovators developing high-impact solutions; (5) Scaling AI impact to catalyse financing for the most promising innovations. All activities have strong focus on responsible AI, gender and inclusion.
