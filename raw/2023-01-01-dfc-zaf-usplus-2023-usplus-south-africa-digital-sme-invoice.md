## Description

DFC direct loan of $8 million to UsPlus Limited (South Africa), a fintech company providing flexible working capital solutions to small and medium enterprises primarily via digital invoice and purchase order financing. UsPlus uses a digital platform to streamline SME access to working capital in South Africa, addressing the financing gap for underserved businesses.
