**VENEEM** is a Burkinabè digital civil-registration platform for the systematic declaration of civil-status events — capturing births and other events at their point of occurrence (health facilities and elsewhere). Per Commissaire principal Ousmane Diallo Nacanabo (Director of the unique identifier and securing of civil-status documents), it runs on both computers and smartphones and supports notifications and level-by-level validation under the Code des personnes et de la famille.

It underpins the **unique identifier (identifiant unique)** and secure civil-status documents — the CRVS foundation beneath Burkina Faso's broader ID stack (WURI enrolment and the CIB-AES card).
