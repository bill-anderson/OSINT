Botswana launched the **Digital Delta Data Centre (DDDC)** in Gaborone — its **first national data centre**, developed and operated by state-owned **Botswana Fibre Networks (BoFiNet)**. It is the country's only **Tier III** certified, carrier-neutral national data centre, fully government-owned, built by Chinese firm Zhong Gan (per DatacenterDynamics) and positioned as a national digital hub to enhance Botswana's ICT capabilities and data sovereignty.
