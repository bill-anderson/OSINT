# The Gambia National Digital Economy Master Plan 2024-2034

The Gambia's ten-year **National Digital Economy Master Plan (2024-2034)**, issued by the Ministry of
Communications and Digital Economy — the overarching strategy tying together the country's connectivity,
digital-government, digital-ID, payments and digital-skills agendas ([[gov.policy]]). The umbrella
instrument behind the batch's Gambian threads (BANTABA payments, digital ID, e-invoicing, data
protection). Reference instrument.
