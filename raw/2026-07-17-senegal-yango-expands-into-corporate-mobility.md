# Senegal: Yango Expands Into Corporate Mobility

Yango has launched its business mobility platform, Yango Rides for Business, in Senegal. The centralized service allows companies to manage employee travel, set spending limits and automate billing. It also provides real-time ride tracking and, according to Yango, can help businesses reduce transport costs by nearly 30% while improving oversight of employee journeys.
