# Djibouti develops national AI strategy with UN support

Djibouti is developing its **first National Artificial Intelligence Strategy**, led by the **Ministry of National Education, Innovation and Digital Development (MENIDjib)** with **UNESCO** and **UN-ESCWA** support. Work began with a **RAM (Readiness Assessment Methodology) diagnostic phase**, facilitated by UNESCO, assessing the country's AI landscape and readiness.

The strategy aims to ensure AI adoption is **ethical, inclusive and aligned with national priorities**, positioning AI as a driver of innovation, equity and economic opportunity while safeguarding **digital sovereignty**. It sits under **[[vision-djibouti-2035]]** and its "Smart Nation" goal.
