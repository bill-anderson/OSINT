## Description

Mubadala Investment Company led an equity and debt financing round for Moove, a Nigeria-founded mobility fintech providing vehicle financing to drivers of ride-hailing platforms including Uber. The total package was USD 76M comprising USD 28M in equity (new and existing investors led by Mubadala), USD 10M in venture debt managed by BlackRock, and USD 38M in previously undisclosed prior-period funds. Mubadala's specific own equity contribution within the USD 28M tranche is not separately disclosed; estimated at ~USD 14M (50% of tranche as lead investor). This was Mubadala's first investment in an African-founded startup. Moove operates in Nigeria, South Africa, Ghana and other markets. Mubadala later participated in Moove's Series B (March 2024, USD 100M led by Uber), which is not separately recorded as Mubadala's incremental commitment in the follow-on is also undisclosed.
