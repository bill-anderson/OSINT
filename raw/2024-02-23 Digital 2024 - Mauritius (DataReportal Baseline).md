DataReportal's Digital 2024: Mauritius report — the annual connectivity baseline. Early-2024 headline figures:

- 982.5 thousand internet users; internet penetration 75.5% (Jan 2024); 318.8 thousand (24.5%) offline.
- 820.9 thousand social media user identities (Jan 2024) = 63.1% of population; +75 thousand (+10.1%) year on year.
- 2.10 million cellular mobile connections in early 2024 = 161.0% of population.
- Population 1.30 million (Jan 2024); median age 37.6; 41.0% urban, 59.0% rural.
- Connection speeds (Ookla, start 2024): median mobile 39.04 Mbps (+77.7% YoY); median fixed 49.75 Mbps (+87.4% YoY).

These are dated early-2024 reference values, not current state.
