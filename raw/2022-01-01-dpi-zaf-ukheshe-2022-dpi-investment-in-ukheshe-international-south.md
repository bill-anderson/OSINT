## Description

Development Partners International (DPI), via African Development Partners III (ADP III), co-invests with Fireball Capital in Ukheshe International, a South African digital fintech enablement platform. Ukheshe provides white-label digital financial services infrastructure to banks, telecoms and fintechs including digital wallets, payment APIs, virtual cards, and remittance solutions. Ukheshe subsequently acquired Masterpass (South Africa) in 2022 and EFT Corporation in 2024, expanding its digital payments rails footprint across southern Africa.
