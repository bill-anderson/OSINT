Indigenous firm 21st Century Technologies signed a strategic partnership with China Mobile International to expand sovereign cloud platforms in Nigeria (signed in Lagos), strengthening local data hosting and accelerating deployment of sovereign AI solutions. It comes as policymakers push for greater data sovereignty and local content retention. Chairman/CEO Wale Ajisebutu said Nigeria generates enormous volumes of digital content and data, much still hosted outside the country, and hopes the deal drives the next phase of cloud adoption.
