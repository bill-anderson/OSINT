## Description

Finnfund provided a USD 12 million senior loan to BCS Group to finance its DRC expansion, building fibre-optic backbone and broadband network infrastructure in East, Central and Southern Africa. BCS provides wholesale broadband and internet services to ISPs, telecoms, corporates and governments, improving internet reliability, accessibility and affordability.
