# E-tax adoption and tax compliance in Ethiopia (ICTD Working Paper 180)

ICTD Working Paper examining, from tax administrative records, the **adoption trend of e-filing** and its correlation with compliance among **large and medium taxpayers** in Ethiopia — measuring timeliness of VAT/CIT filing and amounts declared, and exploring adoption challenges via focus groups. Reference/research on the GovTech-for-revenue theme (complements the Fayda–tax integration analysis).
