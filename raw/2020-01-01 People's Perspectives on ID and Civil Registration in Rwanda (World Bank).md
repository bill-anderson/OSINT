# People's Perspectives on ID and Civil Registration in Rwanda (World Bank, 2020)

Companion source page for the binary artefact
`2020-01-01-Peoples-Perspectives-on-ID-and-CR-in-Rwanda-WorldBank.pdf`
(cited as *World Bank. 2020. People's Perspectives on the National ID, Birth
Registration, and Birth Certificates in Rwanda*, Washington DC).

Qualitative **research report** on citizens' attitudes to, and barriers around, the
national ID card, birth registration and birth certificates — including initial
registration barriers, replacement/updating friction, and data-protection attitudes.

**Reference study (as of 2020).** Cite, don't absorb — its dated figures are **not
promoted** into synthesis pages; used as background for [[nida-rwanda]] and
[[rwanda--dpi-id]]. Note the report's user-side finding on ID **replacement/updating**
friction foreshadows the later Auditor-General flaw on multiple NINs.
