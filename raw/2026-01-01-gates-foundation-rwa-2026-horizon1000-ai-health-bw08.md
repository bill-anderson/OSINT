# Gates Foundation and OpenAI — AI-for-health systems (Horizon1000), Rwanda, 2026

The Gates Foundation and OpenAI announced a $50 million pilot (Horizon1000) to help African governments integrate AI into national health systems, beginning in Rwanda and scaling to 1,000 primary-care clinics by 2028.

## Description

"The pilot initiative, known as Horizon1000, will combine $50 million in funding, technology, and technical support to help countries apply AI tools that strengthen primary healthcare delivery." "Implementation will begin in Rwanda, with plans to scale the programme to 1,000 primary healthcare clinics and surrounding communities across multiple countries by 2028." The programme targets Sub-Saharan Africa's estimated health-workforce shortage of 5.6 million.
