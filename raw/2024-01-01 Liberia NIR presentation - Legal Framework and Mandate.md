# Liberia NIR — Legal Framework & Mandate (presentation)

An NIR presentation setting out its legal framework and mandate: created by the **2011 NIR Act**;
data released only to the applicant or by court order; enroll every citizen/resident; ensure existence
(capture fingerprints), uniqueness (de-duplication), and traceability; issue the unique **NIN** and a
biometric ID card; and seamlessly integrate with government and non-government systems ([[dpi.id]]).
Establishing/reference material for the [[national-identification-registry-liberia|NIR]]. See [[LBR]].
