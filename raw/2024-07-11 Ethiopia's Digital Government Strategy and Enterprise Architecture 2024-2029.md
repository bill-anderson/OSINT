# Ethiopia's Digital Government Strategy and Enterprise Architecture 2024–2029

Innovation and Technology Minister **Belete Molla** presented the **Digital Government Strategy and Government Enterprise Architecture 2024–2029** at a final-review workshop (Addis, 11 Jul 2024) — a blueprint building on the ongoing strategy, incorporating international best practices and digital-transformation principles, developed with continuous stakeholder involvement ahead of government approval. Underpins Ethiopia's e-government interoperability drive (see [[2024-07-19 Ethiopia's e-government leap to interoperability (EeGIF)]]).
