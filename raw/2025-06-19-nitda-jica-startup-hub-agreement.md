NITDA formalized an agreement to implement a Startup Hub Project with a consortium (Oriental Consultants Global, Intem Consulting, Yachiyo Engineering) engaged by JICA. Follows an MoU signed April 10, 2025. The $51.2m project has two components: a $11.2m Startup Hub (implemented by NITDA) and a $40m Project for the Development of a Supporting Environment for Social Startups (executed by the Nigeria Sovereign Investment Authority, NSIA). DG Kashifu Inuwa Abdullahi cited the iHatch initiative (117 direct jobs, 370+ indirect) and linked the project to Tinubu's Renewed Hope Agenda.
