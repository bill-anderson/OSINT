At a ceremony at its Tripoli headquarters, the Libyan General Union of Chambers of Commerce launched the **Unified Digital System for Chambers of Commerce** to develop electronic services. Under the supervision of the Ministry of Economy and Trade, the system unifies chambers' work by issuing standardized **digital certificates of origin** and chamber registration certificates, providing trade statistics through a dedicated portal, and offering a verification system for customs and regulatory bodies.

The Ministry framed it as a step in economic digital transformation consolidating "digital governance." The event included handover of supervisory and technical components to the Ministry of Economy and Trade and the Customs Authority.
