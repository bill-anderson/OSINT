## Description

IFC provided a $20 million senior loan to West Indian Ocean Cable Company Ltd. (WIOCC), a carrier-of-carriers offering wholesale connectivity to over 20 countries across Africa. WIOCC operates terrestrial and submarine fibre optic networks and data centres across Sub-Saharan Africa, supporting ICT infrastructure for internet service providers, mobile operators, and enterprises. The 2018 investment supports WIOCC's expansion of fibre networks and data centre capacity across the continent.
