# Djibouti begins one-stop G2B digital platform for businesses

On **Monday 15 December 2025**, Djibouti's **Ministry of Information and Communication Technologies** launched the design of an integrated **government-to-business (G2B) digital platform** to modernise relations between the public administration and companies, particularly **MSMEs**. Three components: a **dedicated startup portal**; a **G2B digital one-stop shop** streamlining business–administration interactions; and expansion of the **existing one-stop service** to integrate more procedures and **electronic payment systems**.

ICT Minister **Mariam Hamadou Ali** framed the G2B platform as a cornerstone of Djibouti's digital transformation, easing business creation for young entrepreneurs. The initiative falls under **[[vision-djibouti-2035]]** and follows the **[[djibouti-digital-code]]**; Djibouti has also deepened cooperation with the ITU and the EU on its digital-transformation agenda.
