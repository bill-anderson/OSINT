## Description

BII committed $4.4M to Too Much Wifi (TMWF) in December 2024, a wireless broadband connectivity provider offering uncapped, low-cost, high-speed internet services in South Africa, targeting underserved communities and townships. TMWF provides affordable broadband connectivity to households and small businesses in areas typically excluded from fibre rollout. This investment directly supports digital inclusion and connectivity access.
