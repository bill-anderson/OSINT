## Description

AWS invested ZAR 15.6 billion (approx. USD 951 million) in its South Africa cloud infrastructure between 2018 and 2022, covering launch and operation of the AWS Africa (Cape Town) Region (af-south-1), opened April 2020. Investment includes proprietary equipment and software, in-country construction and operations. First AWS cloud region on African continent; three Availability Zones in Cape Town.
