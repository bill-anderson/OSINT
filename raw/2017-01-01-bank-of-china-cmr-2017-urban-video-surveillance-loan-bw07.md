# Bank of China — Cameroon urban video-surveillance loan (Phase I), 2017

Phase I of Cameroon's urban video-surveillance project was financed by a FCFA 45.9 billion loan from Bank of China, following a decree of 19 June 2017.

## Description

"La première phase avait été rendue possible par un financement de 45,9 milliards FCFA accordé par Bank of China, à la suite d'un décret d'habilitation signé le 19 juin 2017." Phase I installed 1,500 surveillance cameras in several Cameroonian cities, complemented by 2,000 portable transceivers for police.
