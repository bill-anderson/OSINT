## Description

DEG provides EUR 4 million in equity capital to M-BIRR, the operator of the mobile money platform in Ethiopia. The cashless money transfer and payment service (M-BIRR) provides an alternative to a bank branch by enabling payment by mobile phone (mobile money). Capital earmarked for business expansion and increasing outreach in Ethiopia. First DEG FinTech financing in Africa. Co-investors include the European Investment Bank (EIB) and Finnish development finance company Finnfund (shareholder since 2012). Over one million customers registered on the platform at year-end 2017.
