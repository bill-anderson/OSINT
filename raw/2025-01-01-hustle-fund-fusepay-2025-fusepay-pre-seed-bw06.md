"The rollout follows a $350,000 pre-seed round in August backed by Hustle Fund, Everywhere Ventures, First Check Ventures, Startup Istanbul, and angel investor Ryan Nesbitt." Fusepay offers virtual business accounts, instant transfers, post-dated digital payments to replace cheques, and automated reconciliation.
