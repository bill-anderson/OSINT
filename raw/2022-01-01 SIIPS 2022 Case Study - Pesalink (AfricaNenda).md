# SIIPS 2022 Case Study — Pesalink (AfricaNenda)

AfricaNenda's **SIIPS (State of Instant and Inclusive Payment Systems in Africa) 2022 case study on
Pesalink** — Kenya's bank-led instant payment network (operated by IPSL, owned by the Kenya Bankers
Association) ([[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]]). The reference case behind Pesalink's role as Kenya's de
facto instant-payment switch and its 2026 PAPSS cross-border integration. Reference document.
