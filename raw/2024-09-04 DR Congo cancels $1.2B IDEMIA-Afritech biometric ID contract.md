The DRC cancelled a **$1.2 billion** contract to establish a national biometric ID system — the project, to be handled by **[[idemia]]** with intermediary **Afritech**, was called off on **12 August 2024**, per **[[onip-drc]]** (National Identification Office) Director-General Richard Ilunga. The cancellation followed a government–consortium meeting and came shortly after a **Bloomberg / Lighthouse Reports** investigation alleging financial mismanagement and cost irregularities (overbilling). This left the DRC's foundational-ID programme to be rebuilt — via Trident's DRCPass (digital ID/e-KYC) and a separate search for a card producer.
