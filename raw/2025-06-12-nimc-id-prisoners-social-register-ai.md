NIMC said 74% of all prison inmates (59,786 inmates) now have a NIN, via collaboration with the Nigerian Correctional Service (June 9 statement). NIMC also announced imminent "ward-level" enrolment nationwide. On the social register: Minister of Humanitarian Affairs Nentawe Yilwatda said the government deployed an AI system to identify poor citizens in urban slums, expanding the social register from 13 million to 19.7 million people — using satellite imagery to locate urban slums, then base-station and telecoms data to identify phone numbers, with AI verifying access to financial services. At end-2024 the government said 25 million Nigerians had received cash payments enabled by digital ID; in May 2025, 2.3 million identities were revalidated through biometric verification.
