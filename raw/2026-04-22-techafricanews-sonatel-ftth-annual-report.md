# Sonatel Expands Fiber and Mobile Data Base as FTTH Subscribers Top 640,000

Tech Africa News - 22 April 2026, by Jennifer Onyeagoro

Sonatel Group's 2025 annual report highlights strong financial performance and accelerated digital transformation across West Africa. Consolidated revenue reached 1,923.1 billion CFA francs (up 8.3%) with net profit of 413.6 billion CFA francs (up 5.2%). Mobile data customers rose 8% to 22.5 million; fixed broadband grew 26.1% to 1.16 million subscribers; fibre-to-the-home exceeded 640,000 active customers.

Orange Money now serves 13 million active users (up 2.8%), and the "Max It" super-app integrates financial and digital services. In 2025 more than 27,000 young people were trained through Orange Digital Centers, and the company operated over 1,000 solar-powered sites in Senegal. Sonatel is preparing 5G rollout across its markets and positioning as a key digital partner for the Dakar 2026 Youth Olympic Games.
