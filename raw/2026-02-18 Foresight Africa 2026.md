## Key facts (attributed to the report)

- Africa: internet penetration **38%** (vs 68% global); **<1%** of global data-centre capacity and **<1%**
  of AI research output; ~12m enter the job market yearly vs ~3m formal jobs. Data governance reframed as
  **industrial policy** resting on DPI. SA AI Institute launched (Jul 2025). Kenya 91% mobile-money
  penetration; GenAI "could unlock $100bn" (McKinsey). Named instruments: Gabon digitalization ordinance,
  Rwanda 2025 data policy, Ghana 2024 digital-economy policy, Nigeria DPA 2023.
