Côte d'Ivoire's Ministry of Digital Transition and Digitalisation will receive **CFA83.2 billion (~$146 million)** in **2026** — **up ~37%** on 2025 (CFA60.78bn), which itself rose from CFA52.6bn in 2024. Parliament adopted the budget unanimously on **21 November 2025** (Minister Ibrahim Kalil Konaté), to strengthen digital performance, expand connectivity (fibre) nationwide, and support cybersecurity and AI — a steadily rising digital-budget trajectory.
