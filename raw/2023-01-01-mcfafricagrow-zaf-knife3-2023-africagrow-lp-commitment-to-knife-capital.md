## Description

AfricaGrow commits to Knife Capital's $50M Series B expansion fund (Knife Fund III) targeting high-growth African technology companies at Series A/B stage. Focus on ICT, fintech, e-commerce, SaaS, healthtech. South Africa-based with pan-African portfolio.
