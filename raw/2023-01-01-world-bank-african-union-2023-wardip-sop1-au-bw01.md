# World Bank — WARDIP SOP1, African Union, 2023

The World Bank committed an SDR 4.6m (US$6.0m) IDA grant to the African Union under WARDIP SOP1 for continental coordination of the digital single market.

## Description

The African Union's allocation under the Digital Transformation for Africa / Western Africa Regional Digital Integration Program (DTfA/WARDIP) Series of Projects 1 (PAD4756, Operation ID P176932, 7 November 2023): "SDR 4.6 MILLION (US$6.0 MILLION EQUIVALENT) TO THE AFRICAN UNION." The AU hosts a Continental Project Coordination Committee for the single-digital-market agenda.
