## Description

KOICA grant ($4.5M, 2022-2026) implemented with Rwanda Information Society Authority (RISA). Deployed 1,000 Digital Ambassadors and 30 supervisors nationwide to train 1,567,034 citizens in basic digital skills and access to e-government services. Established 5 Service Access Points.
