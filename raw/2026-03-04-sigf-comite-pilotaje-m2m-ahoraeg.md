Second meeting of the Steering Committee (Comité de Pilotaje) of the Sistema Integral de Gestión de Funcionarios (SIGF) project — the digitalisation of civil-servant management — held 3 March 2026 at the seat of the Presidency in Malabo II, with representatives of the Moroccan firm M2M Group. The committee analysed the most effective mechanisms for progressive, digitalised nationwide implementation.

M2M Group's technical presentation: the SIGF will incorporate a centralised digital registry of civil servants, biometric attendance control, automated payroll management, digital administrative history, movement control and real-time updating of personnel data — enabling a single, secure, reliable database for strategic decision-making. Framed as a strategic instrument to strengthen transparency, administrative efficiency, HR management and internal control, reduce administrative errors and duplication, and improve budget planning and rigour in public spending. The collaboration was cast as reinforcing bilateral cooperation between Equatorial Guinea and Morocco. Emphasis was placed on inter-ministerial coordination and training of the technical/administrative personnel who will operate the system.
