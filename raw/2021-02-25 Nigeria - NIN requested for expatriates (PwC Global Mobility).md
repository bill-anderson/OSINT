# Nigeria — NIN requested for expatriates (PwC, Feb 2021)

PwC advisory note on the December 2020 Federal Government directive that all SIM phone numbers
be linked to a valid NIN, and its extension to expatriates/legal residents under the NIMC Act
2007, with NIS database harmonisation. Companion source page for the PDF artefact — a **2021
historical baseline** on the NIN–SIM linkage origin; do not present as current state.
See [[nimc]], [[dpi.id]].
