## Description

IFU partnered with Symbiotics to provide USD 22.5 million in debt financing to 15-20 financial institutions in Sub-Saharan Africa through a multi-currency impact bond. The bond finances loans to businesses and households via digital-enabled microfinance institutions and SME banks, supporting financial inclusion and economic recovery post-COVID.
