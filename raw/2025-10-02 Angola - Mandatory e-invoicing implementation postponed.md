Angola's General Tax Administration (AGT) postponed the mandatory e-invoicing requirement to **1 October 2025** (from September) due to technical challenges. Between 1 October and 31 December 2025, taxpayers may keep issuing traditional invoices without penalty while AGT certifies e-invoicing software.

From **1 January 2026**, e-invoicing becomes mandatory for large taxpayers, government suppliers, and transactions exceeding AOA 25 million; it extends to all VAT-registered taxpayers by September 2026. The regime is governed by **Presidential Decree No. 71/25**, with technical standards in Executive Decree No. 2683/25. Key requirements: AGT-certified software, real-time transmission of invoice data, and SAF-T format compatibility.
