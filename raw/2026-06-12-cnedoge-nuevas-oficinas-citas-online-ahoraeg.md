On 12 June 2026 CNEDOGE inaugurated two new provincial offices in Mongomo and Ciudad de la Paz, part of an institutional-expansion strategy to bring official-document issuance closer to the population and reduce travel to central offices. As part of this modernisation, CNEDOGE enabled an **online appointment (cita previa) system through its official website www.cnedoge.com**, allowing citizens to schedule applications in advance, optimise waiting times and improve office flow. The article frames the new offices and digital services as reflecting the institution's commitment to administrative transformation and the government's push to modernise public-service delivery.
