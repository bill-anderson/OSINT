More than **75,000 food-voucher beneficiaries** in Botswana completed digital purchases at local stores using a biometrics-enabled smart-card system from **SmartSwitch Botswana** (a **Lesaka Technologies** subsidiary), built on Botswana's **Universal Electronic Payment System (UEPS)** infrastructure — biometric-linked government social-benefit payments advancing financial inclusion.
