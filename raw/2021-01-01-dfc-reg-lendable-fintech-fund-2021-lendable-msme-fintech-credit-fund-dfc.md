## Description

DFC direct loan of up to $20 million to Lendable Fintech Credit Fund (Luxembourg RAIF), providing senior-secured debt facilities to early and mid-stage fintech companies operating in Africa (Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Senegal) and Asia. Lendable's data-driven approach enables on-lending to digital financial service providers serving underbanked MSMEs. DFC is an anchor investor. Total fund $110M. Promotes financial inclusion through digital lending platforms.
