## Description

"The Democratic Republic of the Congo has partnered with China's Genew Technologies to build a fiber-optic route along the Congo River worth US$1.5 billion. The two entities signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) for the fiber deployment project. The project covers about 2,100km of infrastructure, including 1,700km of fiber-optic cable along the Congo River from Muanda to Kisangani and nearly 400km of terrestrial connections." Genew CEO Wu Minhua said the total cost is estimated at $1.5 billion, with more than $400 million required for the first phase.
