The DRC is stepping up efforts to attract foreign investment into its digital sector. On **5 February 2026**, Digital Economy Minister **Augustin Kibassa Maliba** met a delegation from UAE-based **The Founding** (data-centre project proposals), proposing a working group to review projects and define technical specifications; he also met Tunisian firm **Dirasset International** and **Bayayi Group**. The DRC plans to invest **$1.5 billion** in digital transformation over 2026-2030, on top of international-partner funding — the FDI/data-centre-pipeline dimension of the [[drc-national-digital-plan-pnn2|PNN2]].
