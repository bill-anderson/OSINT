## Description

Mastercard finalized a definitive agreement with MTN Group on February 5, 2024 to acquire a minority stake of up to $200 million in MTN Group Fintech, valuing the business at $5.2 billion. MTN Group Fintech encompasses MTN Mobile Money (MoMo), with 60 million active monthly wallets. The partnership spans 13 African markets and includes: launching a virtual/physical Mastercard companion card for MoMo wallets, expanding merchant acceptance via SME-in-a-Box, cross-border remittances, and cybersecurity solutions across Mastercard's network.
