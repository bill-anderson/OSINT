DataReportal's Digital 2025 profile for Mauritania (data early 2025). Headline figures: 6.14 million cellular mobile connections (equivalent to 117% of the total population; +564k / +10.1% year-on-year; 86.4% "broadband"). Internet users: 1.96 million at the start of 2025, an internet penetration rate of 37.4% (+55k / +2.9% year-on-year); ~3.28 million people offline. Social media: 1.57 million user identities in January 2025 (29.9% of population).

Population: 5.24 million in January 2025 (+146k / +2.9%); 58.8% urban, 41.2% rural; median age 17.4.
