## Description

Vodacom Group (6.2% stake) participated in the Global Partnership for Ethiopia (GPE) consortium that was awarded an operating telecommunications licence in Ethiopia by the Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA) in May/July 2021. The full consortium paid $850 million for the 15-year licence. Other consortium members: Safaricom PLC (55.7%, lead), Sumitomo Corporation (27.2%), CDC Group/BII (10.9%). Vodacom's commitment is limited to its pro-rata 6.2% share of the $850M licence fee = ~$52.7M. Vodacom entered definitive funding agreements with consortium partners on 10 September 2021. Safaricom Ethiopia launched commercial 4G services in Addis Ababa in October 2022, with M-PESA mobile money following in August 2023. Vodacom also holds indirect exposure through its 34.9% stake in Safaricom PLC.
