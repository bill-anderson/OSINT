DataReportal's Digital 2025: Guinea report — essential headlines for early 2025:

- Mobile connections: 14.2 million cellular mobile connections in early 2025, equivalent to 95.1% of the total population (up 133k / +0.9% YoY). GSMA Intelligence indicates 80.8% are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- Internet users: 3.96 million internet users in January 2025, an internet penetration of 26.5% (up 92k / +2.4% YoY). This implies 11.0 million people (73.5%) remained offline.
- Social media: 3.05 million social media user identities in January 2025, equating to 20.4% of the total population.
- Population: 14.9 million in January 2025 (+346k / +2.4% YoY); 38.7% urban, 61.3% rural; median age 18.3.

Source: DataReportal (Kepios / We Are Social / Meltwater), GSMA Intelligence data.
