## Description

KOICA grant (2017-2024, $7.3M) to Rwanda Education Board under MINEDUC. Established 69 ICT smart classrooms (Centres of Excellence) in secondary schools, trained ~30,000 teachers in ICT integration. Developed ICT competency curriculum and 129 master trainers.
