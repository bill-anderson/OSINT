## Description

Norfund is a co-founding equity shareholder in WeLight, established 2018 by Axian Group, Sagemcom, and Norfund. WeLight provides solar mini-grids with digital metering to rural communities in Madagascar (primary) and expanding to Mali. 2023 secured EUR 40M additional funding (EIB EUR 9.5M + EDFI ElectriFI + Triodos EUR 19M; Norfund shareholder loan EUR ~3M). Axian Group integration links to telecom infrastructure. Digital scope: digital smart metering, IoT energy monitoring, mobile payment for energy.
