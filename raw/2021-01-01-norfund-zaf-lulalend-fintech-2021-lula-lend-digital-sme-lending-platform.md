## Description

Norfund local currency loan (NOK 110.9 million equivalent, approx USD 10 million per Norfund 2024 statement) to Lula Lend (Lula), a fully digital SME lending fintech in South Africa. Lula uses data-driven credit scoring and digital platforms to provide unsecured working capital loans to SMEs. Digital scope: fully digital lending platform, data-driven underwriting, API-based business banking.
