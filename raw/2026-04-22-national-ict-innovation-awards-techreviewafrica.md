# Malawi showcases digital transformation progress at National ICT Innovation Awards

Malawi underscored its digital-transformation commitment during the **National ICT Innovation Awards** held in Lilongwe, with officials highlighting momentum in technology-driven development. The event, officially opened by ICT Minister Dr Shadric Namalomba, brought together policymakers, industry stakeholders and innovators.

Secretary for ICT Harold Msusa said the awards reflect a shift in Malawi's digital strategy "from policy ambition to practical implementation," and serve as a platform for nurturing young innovators and local talent. Senior officials including Cabinet Ministers Alfred Gangata, Joel Chigona and Mary Navicha attended, signalling political commitment to leveraging ICT for inclusive growth and job creation.
