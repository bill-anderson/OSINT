## Description

Supports Ethiopia to develop and implement a digital ID system covering legal identity, functional identity for service delivery, and a framework for digital identification management. Aims to provide inclusive, trusted, and verifiable digital identities to over 107 million Ethiopians.

## Development history

- **2023-10-25** — appraisal-stage Environmental and Social Review Summary issued, framing E&S risk for the Fayda rollout including refugees and IDPs. [2023-10-25 WB Ethiopia Digital ID for Inclusion and Services Project - appraisal ESRS.md]
- **2025-02** — over 12 million people registered for Fayda under the US$350m project (which targets 90 million, including refugees and IDPs). [World Bank]
- **2025-10-29** — World Bank Implementation Status & Results Report issued; project confirmed as the financing behind the Fayda national ID rollout. [2025-10-29 WB Ethiopia Digital ID for Inclusion and Services Project - implementation status report.md]
- **2026** — US$54m "Super Agent" procurement launched under the project (per a World Bank March 2026 evaluation-mission recommendation) to facilitate large-scale Fayda registration in underserved communities; project by then reported 39m+ enrollments, 100+ integrated institutions, 78m+ authentications and 9m+ cards delivered. [https://www.biometricupdate.com/202605/ethiopia-moves-to-expand-fayda-enrollment-with-54m-super-agent-procurement]
