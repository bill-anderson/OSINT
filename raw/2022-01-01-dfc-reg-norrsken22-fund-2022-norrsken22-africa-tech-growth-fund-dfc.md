## Description

DFC equity investment of up to $15 million in Norrsken22 Africa Tech Growth Fund, a women-led pan-African growth-stage technology fund investing in fintech, edtech, health tech, and market-enabling solutions across sub-Saharan Africa. Fund achieved final close at $205M. Portfolio companies include TymeBank (digital challenger bank), Smile Identity (identity verification), Autochek (auto financing), Sabi (B2B commerce platform), Shara (merchant financing). DFC is an anchor impact investor.
