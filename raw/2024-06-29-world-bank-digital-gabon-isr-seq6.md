**Digital Gabon Project (P175987)** — Implementation Status & Results Report, Seq No 6, archived 29 June 2024 (ISR01162). Western and Central Africa | Gabon | IBRD/IDA | Investment Project Financing | FY 2022. Implementing agency: **CN-TIPPEE** (National Commission for Public Infrastructure Works and Promotion of Small-Scale Enterprises).

- **Development Objective:** "To enhance adoption of select digitalized public services and increase the number of persons with a legal identity credential that facilitates their access to services." Unchanged since Board approval.
- **Ratings:** Progress towards PDO and Overall Implementation Progress both **Moderately Satisfactory**; Overall Risk **Substantial**. Political & Governance risk raised to Substantial vs Moderate at approval.
- **Status:** As of this report the project was **not yet effective** — "Project implementation will start as soon as the project is declared effective." A Level-2 restructuring had been approved 5 December 2023.
- **Key dates:** Approved 30 July 2021; original closing 30 June 2026.

This report captures the pre-effectiveness delay: approved mid-2021 but not yet launched by mid-2024, consistent with the ~3-year gap between the World Bank commitment and disbursement.
