## Description

KOICA grant ($9.4M, 2015-2021) to establish the ISEP technical college at Diamniadio, Senegal with ICT and automotive engineering departments. Provides vocational ICT education aligned with industry needs. Includes ICT lab equipment, faculty training, and curriculum development.
