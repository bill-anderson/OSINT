## Description

MIGA issued guarantees of $97.2 million to Senegalese telecommunications company SONATEL S.A. covering its 50% equity stake in mobile operator Orange Sierra Leone Ltd (OSL). The acquisition facilitates modernization and expansion of OSL's network in Sierra Leone through deployment of additional telecom towers, improvement in network services, quality and reliability. Supported by IDA18 Private Sector Window (PSW) MIGA Guarantee Facility. Risks covered: expropriation and war & civil disturbance for up to 15 years.
