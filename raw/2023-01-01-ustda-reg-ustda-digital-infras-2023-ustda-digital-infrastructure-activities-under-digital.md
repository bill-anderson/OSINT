## Description

Aggregate of USTDA's 12 digital infrastructure development activities across Africa launched under the DTA Initiative since December 2022. Activities include fiber network feasibility studies, broadband expansion, data center development, cloud training and smart connectivity projects in Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Malawi, Sierra Leone, DRC and other SSA countries. Potential to mobilize over $1 billion in project financing.
