## Description

ADC announced in November 2021 a $200M+ investment in Kenya to develop two further data centre facilities in Nairobi beyond the existing NBO1 (7.5MW, Sameer Industrial Park, Mombasa Road). NBO2, with up to 20MW of IT load, entered development; land was secured for a third facility (NBO3). ADC broke ground on NBO2 at the Sameer site in January 2023, with completion planned in the first half of 2024. The Nairobi expansion is part of the broader $500M pan-African programme and received support from the DFC's $300M master facility. ADC's Nairobi presence makes it the largest data centre operator in Kenya.
