# Cabo Verde MoF — ToR, National Governance Framework for Digital Transformation

Terms of Reference issued by the **Ministry of Finance / Special Projects Management Unit (UGPE)** under the **[[digital-cabo-verde-project|Digital Cabo Verde Project – Additional Financing]]** (IDA-financed; parent **P171099**). Scope: a **National Governance Framework for Digital Transformation and Institutional Coordination** to modernise public administration, coordinated by the Prime Minister's Office through a **Government Digital Service Team (GDST)**. The Additional Financing components: (1) enabling legal/regulatory environment; (2) digital competitiveness; (3) digital public services and marketplace.
