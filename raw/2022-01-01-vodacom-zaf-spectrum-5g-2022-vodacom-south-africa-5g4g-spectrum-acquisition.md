## Description

Vodacom South Africa acquired 110 MHz of high-demand radio frequency spectrum in the ICASA multi-band spectrum auction concluded 17 March 2022 — the largest spectrum purchase in the auction. Portfolio: 2x10MHz in the 700MHz band, 80MHz in the 2600MHz band, and 10MHz in the 3500MHz band, for a total of R5.38 billion. The spectrum enables acceleration of 4G coverage and 5G network rollout across South Africa and supports Vodacom's network performance and digital inclusion goals. Spectrum licence effective 1 July 2022 for a 20-year period. R3.2 billion was paid in H1 FY2023 (before July 2022); remaining R2.2 billion paid October 2023 (800MHz band cleared of interference).
