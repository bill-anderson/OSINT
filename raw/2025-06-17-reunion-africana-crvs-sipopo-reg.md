Equatorial Guinea hosted (at the Sipopo conference complex, Malabo) the **Annual Joint Meeting of the Group of Experts of Registrars General and the Specialised Technical Group on Civil Registration and Vital Statistics (CRVS)** — a continental (African Union / UNECA) gathering. Theme: "APAI-CRVS Strategic Plan 2023–2027: Path towards achieving SDG 16.9" (legal identity for all, including birth registration). Organised by UNECA with the regional core group; participants include national civil-registry directors, statistics institutes, technical experts and development partners across Africa. A coordination meeting (16 June, Ministry of Finance, chaired by Secretary of State for Planning María Ebiaca Moete) reviewed logistics. Hosting was framed as recognition of GNQ's commitment to modernising vital-data systems.
