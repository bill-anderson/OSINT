# Ethio Telecom pursues Shandong Hi-Speed hyperscale data-centre partnership

**[[ethio-telecom|Ethio Telecom]]** (CEO Frehiwot Tamiru) is in strategic talks with China's **Shandong Hi-Speed Group** on building a **hyperscale data centre**, with Shandong open to both construction and **financing**. Framed as advancing Ethiopia's digital infrastructure and economy under the Lead Growth Strategy / Digital Ethiopia. **Talks only — no signed deal** at time of reporting.
