Congo's **Council of Ministers approved (3 July 2024)** a draft bill creating a **National Personal Data Protection Commission** (Commission Nationale de Protection des Données à Caractère Personnel), presented by Posts, Telecoms and Digital Economy Minister **Léon-Juste Ibombo** — to strengthen existing legislation, build confidence in digital services and ensure rigorous control of personal-data use.

This July-2024 draft-law approval is the origin of the commission whose members were later **installed in January 2026**. See [[national-personal-data-protection-commission-congo]].
