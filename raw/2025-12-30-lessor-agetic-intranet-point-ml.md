State daily; capture is not endorsement. At the 22nd ordinary session of AGETIC's board (chaired by Minister of Communication, Digital Economy and Administration Modernisation Alhamdou Ag Ilyene; DG Mohamed Assalia Boncana). AGETIC is progressively interconnecting all public and para-public services into a private, secured network (the government Intranet), framed as building "digital sovereignty," reinforcing the state information system and transparency. The minister called for the Intranet to be consolidated, better secured and extended to new administrative regions amid rising cyberattacks, and for accelerating a single-window portal of digitalised public services (13 services already integrated). A draft decree is in the government approval circuit to make use of the government messaging system mandatory. On the .ml country-code domain: AGETIC managed 13,441 domain names and supported 30 registrars; officials urged public/private/civil-society actors to create and use .ml domains for Mali's cyberspace visibility. 2026 activity/budget adopted in budget-programme mode under the 2025-2026 government action plan; budget down ~0.67% vs 2025. DG noted numerous cyberattack attempts.
