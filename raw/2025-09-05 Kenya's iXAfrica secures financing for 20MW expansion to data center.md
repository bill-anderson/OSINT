Kenya's **[[ixafrica-data-centres]]** has secured financing for a **20MW expansion** to its NBOX1 data center in Nairobi. South African lender **Rand Merchant Bank (RMB**, a division of FirstRand) will provide a multi-tranche funding package to add 20MW; NBOX1's intended total capacity is 22.5MW.

The 17,300 sqm data center on Mombasa Road uses adiabatic cooling and offers 6,621 sqm of colocation; the campus, in progress since 2021, is backed by a $50m investment from Helios and sits on land formerly part of a Schneider Electric complex. iXAfrica has also agreed to buy 11 acres from Tilisi Developments for a second Nairobi campus. RMB has previously financed WIOCC's Open Access Data Centres and Africa Data Centres.
