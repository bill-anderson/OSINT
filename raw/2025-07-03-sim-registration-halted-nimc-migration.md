Nigeria's telecom operators temporarily suspended SIM-related services (new registrations, SIM swaps, NIN registration, mobile number portability) after NIMC's migration to a new identity verification platform, confirmed by the Association of Licensed Telecommunications Operators of Nigeria (ALTON). NIMC is moving to a platform it built and manages; BlueSalt Financial Services will migrate telecom operators to the new NIMC platform. NIMC has registered over 120 million Nigerians and targets 100 million more enrollees by year-end for its 95% national coverage target. NIMC said on July 2 that "recent technical maintenance" was complete and services restored, including NIN enrolment, data modification and authentication.
