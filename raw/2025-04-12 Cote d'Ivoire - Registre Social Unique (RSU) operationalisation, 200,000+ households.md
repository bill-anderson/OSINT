Côte d'Ivoire is operationalising its **Registre Social Unique (RSU)** — a single social registry centralising socio-economic data on poor and vulnerable households to share with social programmes for targeting. As of April 2025, **more than 200,000 households** were in the process of registration, managed through the SIG-RSU information system — the social-registry layer under the country's social-protection system ([[rsu-cote-divoire]]).
