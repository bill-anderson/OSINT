World Bank restructuring paper (Report No. RES47803) for the Madagascar Financial Inclusion Project (P161491), a $45 million IDA investment operation approved 28 February 2018 to promote financial inclusion of individuals and MSMEs. The paper documents a COVID-era restructuring; it references the May-July 2021 mid-term review as the latest activity and a prior Level-2 restructuring approved 14 January 2021, so it postdates July 2021. No explicit approval date is printed in the disclosed document, so the year (2021) is used as a proxy. Reference document; cite for the project's scope and restructuring rationale rather than extracting dated figures.
