## Description

Government of India Line of Credit to Nigeria for establishment of a National Rural Broadband Network (NRBN). The LoC was approved by India's Department of Economic Affairs (DEA) on 18 June 2019 and MEA go-ahead granted 25 June 2019. The project aims to extend broadband connectivity to rural and underserved communities across Nigeria through deployment of ICT infrastructure. NRBN implementation is managed by the Federal Ministry of Communications.
