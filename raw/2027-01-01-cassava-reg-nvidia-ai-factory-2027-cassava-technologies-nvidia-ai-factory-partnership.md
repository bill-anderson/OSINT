## Description

In March–April 2025, Cassava Technologies announced Africa's first AI factory in partnership with NVIDIA. Cassava committed up to USD 720 million to deploy 12,000 NVIDIA GPUs across five regional AI data centre hubs over 3–4 years: South Africa (first phase, 3,000 GPUs live June–November 2025), Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt, and Morocco. The South Africa hub went live in November 2025. In October 2025, NVIDIA made an equity investment in Cassava (amount undisclosed) as part of a USD 150M+ fresh equity capital raise for Liquid Intelligent Technologies; STANLIB also agreed a minority stake in ADC. The NVIDIA investment supports GPU-as-a-Service commercial deployment. Cassava is an NVIDIA Cloud Partner (NCP). The initiative aims to enable sovereign AI capabilities, local model training, and AI skills development for 10 million Africans. Primary SSA countries: ZAF, NGA, KEN. Egypt and Morocco are non-SSA but SSA portion is primary focus.
