Visa launched **Visa Pay** in the DRC (**4 September 2025**) — an interoperable, mobile-first solution enabling banked and unbanked consumers to pay, get paid and move money across participating banks and mobile networks. It offers easy enrolment, risk/fraud prevention, and near-real-time account-to-account transfers in **two currencies (CDF and USD)**, plus cash-in/cash-out at bank branches and online payments via virtual cards — extending the DRC's interoperable digital-payments layer.
