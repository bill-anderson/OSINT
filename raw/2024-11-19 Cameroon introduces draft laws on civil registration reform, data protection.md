Cameroon brought **two bills** vital to its digital transformation to parliament (late 2024): one introducing a **new civil-registration regime** — novelties including a **Unique Personal Identification Number (UPIN)**, electronic declaration of civil-status acts, a national database and digital birth/marriage/death certificates — and one on **personal data protection**. Examined by the National Assembly and Senate for presidential enactment. The civil-registration bill became **[[cameroon-civil-registration-law-2024]]** (Law 2024/016); the data-protection bill is Cameroon's move toward a dedicated DP regime.
