## Description

IFC made an equity investment of approximately $10.66 million in IHS Kenya Ltd, a subsidiary of IHS Towers — Africa's largest independent tower company. The investment supports IHS Kenya's independent telecom tower infrastructure business, enabling mobile network operators to provide 2G/3G/4G coverage across Kenya. IHS Towers is NYSE-listed and operates over 40,000 towers across Africa. The Kenya entity develops and manages passive telecom infrastructure including towers, shelters, and power systems.
