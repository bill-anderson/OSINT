## Description

USD 5 million (EUR 4.3 million) equity commitment to TLcom Capital's TIDE Africa Fund, a venture capital fund dedicated to technology-enabled start-ups in Sub-Saharan Africa. Fund invests EUR 500K–EUR 10M per company at growth stage. Focus: mobile phone and digital applications that provide access to essential services (food security, energy, health, education, financial services). Total fund first close EUR 40M. PROPARCO's commitment enabled the fund's first closing.
