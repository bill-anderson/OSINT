## Description

IFC provided a sustainability-linked debt package to WIOCC Group comprising a $10 million USD loan and a ZAR 200 million (~$10.9 million) loan. Proparco co-financed $20 million and RMB provided an additional $10 million. Total package approximately $50 million. The financing expands WIOCC's core and edge data centres in DRC, Nigeria, and South Africa and grows its fibre networks. Sustainability-linked structure ties pricing to WIOCC's commitment to improve data centre energy efficiency and obtain EDGE green building certification, linking digital infrastructure investment with sustainability performance.
