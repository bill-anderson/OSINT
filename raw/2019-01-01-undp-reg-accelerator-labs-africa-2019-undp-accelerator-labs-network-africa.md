## Description

Network of 33 UNDP Accelerator Labs across Sub-Saharan Africa (Angola, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cabo Verde, Cameroon, Congo, Côte d'Ivoire, DRC, Eritrea, Eswatini, Ethiopia, Gambia, Ghana, Guinea, Kenya, Lesotho, Malawi, Mali, Mozambique, Namibia, Niger, Nigeria, Rwanda, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Somalia, South Africa, South Sudan, Sudan, Tanzania, Togo, Uganda, Zambia, Zimbabwe). Each lab focuses on surfacing, testing and scaling locally-sourced innovation solutions to development challenges. Digital prototyping and technology scouting are core activities. Funded by UNDP core, Germany (GIZ/BMZ), Qatar Fund for Development, Italy.
