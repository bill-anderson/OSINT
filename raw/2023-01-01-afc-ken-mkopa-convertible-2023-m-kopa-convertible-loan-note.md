## Description

AFC provided a USD 7.5 million convertible loan note to M-KOPA Holdings, a Kenyan digital asset finance and fintech platform, in partnership with Lightrock. M-KOPA operates a pay-as-you-go mobile and solar energy finance platform that has connected 1.7 million first-time mobile internet users across Africa, primarily in Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria and Ghana. The convertible note supports M-KOPA's expansion of its mobile device financing and digital financial services to underbanked consumers in Eastern Africa. M-KOPA uses digital credit models enabling consumers to purchase smartphones and other assets through micro-payments.
