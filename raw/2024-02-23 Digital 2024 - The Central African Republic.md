DataReportal's essential headlines for digital adoption and use in the Central African Republic in early 2024:

- There were 616.6 thousand internet users in the Central African Republic at the start of 2024, when internet penetration stood at 10.6 percent.
- The Central African Republic was home to 159.5 thousand social media users in January 2024, equating to 2.7 percent of the total population.
- A total of 1.86 million cellular mobile connections were active in early 2024, equivalent to 32.0 percent of the total population.

Population stood at 5.83 million in January 2024 (+3.0% year on year); 43.9 percent lived in urban centres. Median age is 14.9. Internet users increased by 18 thousand (+3.0 percent) between January 2023 and January 2024; 5.21 million people (89.4 percent) remained offline at the beginning of the year. (Report by We Are Social / Meltwater; analysis by Kepios.)
