## Description

USD 40 million sustainability-linked long-term loan to Raxio Data Centres to fund the first phase development of seven colocation data centres across SSA. Countries: Uganda, Ethiopia, Mozambique, DRC, Côte d'Ivoire, Angola, Tanzania. Provides up to 11 MW of additional neutral open-access data processing and storage capacity. ESG incentives linked to renewable power and energy efficiency. Total facility USD 110M (Proparco USD 40M + EAIF USD 70M).
