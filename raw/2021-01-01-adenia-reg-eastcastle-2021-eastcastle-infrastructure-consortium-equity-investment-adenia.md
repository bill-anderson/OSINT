## Description

Adenia Partners, together with African Infrastructure Investment Managers (AIIM) and IFC, invested a total of USD 130 million in Eastcastle Infrastructure Limited, a newly established telecom tower developer in Africa. Eastcastle was founded in 2020 by former Eaton Towers CFO and COO. The consortium holds 95.8% of Eastcastle (AIIM 37.9%, Adenia 37.9%, IFC 20%, founders 4.2%). Funds support a build-to-suit strategy to develop new telecom towers for 3G/4G/5G deployment in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Nigeria, and Côte d'Ivoire. Adenia's committed share is approximately $49.3 million (37.9% of $130M total).
