The Gabon Ministry of the Interior and Security announced an online platform for citizens to verify their registration on the electoral roll and obtain information on their polling centre, at www.consultation.listelectorale.ga.

Procedure: go to the site, enter your **NIP (Numéro d'Identification Personnel)** and name, and click "Rechercher" to instantly access registration and polling-centre information. The electoral-roll lookup is **keyed on the national NIP, linking the register to the CNIE / national digital-ID system**. Published by ANINF/SSI.
