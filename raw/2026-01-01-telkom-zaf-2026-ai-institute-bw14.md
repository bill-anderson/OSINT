## Description

"Telkom pledges USD 6.1 million to establish the Telkom AI Institute, a platform designed to equip South Africans with AI and digital skills, foster innovation, and accelerate inclusive participation in the digital economy." The pledge was one of several announced as ITU's Partner2Connect Digital Coalition surpassed US$100 billion in commitments at the WSIS Forum 2026 in Geneva.
