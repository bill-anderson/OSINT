Opposition members of Cameroon's National Assembly criticised the new **[[augentic]]/[[dgsn-cameroon]]** national ID system (June 2025) as **more costly and more complex** than the previous one — raised in response to the Speaker's call for mass voter registration ahead of the **October 2025 presidential election**, since a valid CNI is required to register and vote. Thousands of cards had been issued, but **long waits for biometric-capture appointments** (blamed on high applicant volumes) drew concern about cost and timeliness — the access critique on the new ID at a politically sensitive moment.
