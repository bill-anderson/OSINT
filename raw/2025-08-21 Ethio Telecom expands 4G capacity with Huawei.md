# Ethio Telecom expands 4G capacity with Huawei

**[[ethio-telecom|Ethio Telecom]]** expanded its 4G network with **[[huawei|Huawei]]** (GigaAAU FDD tri-band Massive MIMO), reporting **+70% 4G data volume** and ~68% average speed gains. In FY2024/25 it extended LTE to 512 more cities (total **936**) on a **$1bn** investment. Coverage gaps persist: per ITU, **56% of Ethiopia's ~132.1m** lacked 4G coverage in 2024 (GSMA: 41% of subscribers on 4G by 2023; projecting 69% of Ethiopian subscriptions 4G by 2030).
