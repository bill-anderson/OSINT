## Description

Norfund NOK 177.2 million (approximately USD 16.8 million) participation in Kenyan Shilling-denominated USD 130 million sustainable receivables securitisation for Sun King Kenya (Greenlight Planet). Syndicated by Standard Bank Group and Citi. Leverages Sun King Kenya customers' solar product payments to raise growth capital. Structured entirely in Kenyan Shillings to mitigate FX risk. Digital scope: PAYG solar home systems, IoT digital metering, mobile money payment collection.
