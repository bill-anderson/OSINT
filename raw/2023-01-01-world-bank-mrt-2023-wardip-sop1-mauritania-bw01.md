# World Bank — WARDIP SOP1, Mauritania, 2023

The World Bank committed an SDR 45.7m (US$60m) IDA credit to Mauritania under WARDIP SOP1 for broadband access and West African digital-market integration.

## Description

Mauritania's national allocation under the Digital Transformation for Africa / Western Africa Regional Digital Integration Program (DTfA/WARDIP) Series of Projects 1 (PAD4756, Operation ID P176932, 7 November 2023): "SDR 45.7 MILLION (US$60.0 MILLION EQUIVALENT) TO THE ISLAMIC REPUBLIC OF MAURITANIA" as an IDA credit. Implementing agency: Ministère de la Transition Numérique, de l'Innovation et de la Modernisation de l'Administration.
