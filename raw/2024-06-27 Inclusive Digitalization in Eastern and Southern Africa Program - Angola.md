The **IDEA program** (Inclusive Digitalization in Eastern and Southern Africa) increases internet access and inclusive use of digitally enabled services — $2.48 billion in IDA/IBRD funding across 15 countries and Regional Economic Communities over eight years, with **COMESA** spearheading regional coordination. Phase 1 covers **Angola, DR Congo and Malawi**.

In Angola, the **Angola Digital Acceleration Project (PADA)** — a **$300 million** IBRD loan implemented by the **Instituto de Modernização Administrativa (IMA)** — aims to accelerate digital inclusion and is expected to mobilise ~$80 million in private investment. Adão de Almeida, Minister of State and Chief of Staff of the Presidency: "One of our next challenges... is to ensure interoperability between the databases and technological systems across different ministerial departments and public services."
