## Description

AWS announced at the AWS Summit Johannesburg (September 2024) plans to invest an additional ZAR 30.4 billion (approximately USD 1.7 billion) in South Africa cloud and AI infrastructure by 2029, building on ZAR 15.6B already invested 2018-2022. Investment covers construction, operation and maintenance of cloud infrastructure supporting AWS customers. Also includes renewable energy solar plant in Northern Cape Province.
