## Description

MTN South Africa acquired 100 MHz of high-demand radio frequency spectrum across three bands in the ICASA multi-band spectrum auction concluded 17 March 2022: 2x10MHz in the 800MHz band (R2.9bn / $195.5M), 4x10MHz in the 2600MHz band (R1.1bn / $72.8M), and 4x10MHz in the 3500MHz band (R1.1bn / $75.1M). Total cost: R5.2 billion. This spectrum enables 4G capacity expansion and 5G network rollout across South Africa. R3.3bn was paid to ICASA in April 2022, with the remaining R1.9bn paid in H2 2023 once the 800MHz band was cleared of interference. The auction was South Africa's first spectrum auction in over a decade.
