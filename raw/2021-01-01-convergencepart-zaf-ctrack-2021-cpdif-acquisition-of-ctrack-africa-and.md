## Description

Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund (CPDIF) acquired 100% of Ctrack's operations in Africa and the Middle East from Nasdaq-listed Inseego Corp in a transaction valued at approximately ZAR 529 million (~US$36.2 million). This was CPDIF's maiden investment. Ctrack is a leading telematics software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform providing fleet management analytics, insurance risk solutions, and asset tracking solutions across sub-Saharan Africa. Ctrack's IoT and big data analytics platform leverages cloud-based data infrastructure, AI-driven analytics, and IoT connectivity. Headquartered in South Africa. In December 2024, Ctrack additionally acquired Inseego's international telematics business for $52 million.
