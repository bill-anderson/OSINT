## Description

FinDev Canada commits USD 20 million in convertible notes to M-KOPA as part of a USD 250 million+ growth financing round, supporting the fintech platform's expansion into new SSA markets and product lines including smartphones, digital financial services, and consumer credit delivered via mobile pay-as-you-go systems.
