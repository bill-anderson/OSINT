## Description

EUR 999,045 equity investment in ANKA, an Ivorian all-in-one SaaS platform for African businesses enabling e-commerce, payments, and global shipping. ANKA supports 20,000+ merchants (80% women) across 47 African countries. Part of Proparco's Bridge by Digital Africa initiative. Extension of capital increase (December 2021 round). Proparco is a new shareholder supporting ANKA's growth.
