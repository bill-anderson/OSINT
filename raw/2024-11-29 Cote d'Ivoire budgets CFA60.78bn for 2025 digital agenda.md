Côte d'Ivoire's Ministry of Digital Transition and Digitalization had its **2025 budget** approved (25 November 2024) at **CFA60.78 billion (~$96.8 million)** — to enhance connectivity, ensure equitable access to technology, promote digital skills, and support youth and women in the digital sector. (Trajectory: CFA52.6bn in 2024 → CFA60.78bn in 2025 → CFA83.2bn in 2026.)
