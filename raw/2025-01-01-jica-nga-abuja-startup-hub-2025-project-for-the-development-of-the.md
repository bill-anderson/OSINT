## Description

JICA grant aid for construction of a start-up hub in Abuja with digital crafting equipment and makerspace. Includes construction of hub facility, procurement and installation of equipment (digital manufacturing, prototyping), and consulting services. Aims to expand opportunities for manufacturing-related businesses and promote startup collaboration in Nigeria's ICT ecosystem. Grant signed April 10, 2025; implementation April 2025 to December 2030.
