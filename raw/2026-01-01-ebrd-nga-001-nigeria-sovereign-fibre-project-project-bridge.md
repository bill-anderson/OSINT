## Description

Provision of a sovereign loan of up to USD 100 million to the Federal Republic of Nigeria to finance its participation in a special purpose vehicle (SPV) for the nationwide rollout of approximately 90,000 km of fibre-optic broadband infrastructure. The project supports Nigeria's National Broadband Plan, targeting connectivity for 50% of 33 million unserved Nigerians and raising fixed broadband penetration. SPV will operate as open-access wholesale provider. Co-financed in parallel by World Bank (USD 500m) and AfDB (USD 200m). Supported by EUR 22m EU grant for technical designs and capacity building for 2,000 technicians.
