## Description

Google CEO Sundar Pichai announced at Google for Africa (October 6, 2021) a $1 billion investment over five years to support Africa's digital transformation, covering: (1) affordable connectivity including Equiano subsea cable (landed in Nigeria, Namibia, Togo, South Africa, Sao Tome and Principe); (2) building products for African users; (3) investing in entrepreneurs via Africa Investment Fund $50M and Black Founders Fund $3M; (4) supporting nonprofits $40M and SME loans $10M. Sub-components recorded separately where individually quantified.
