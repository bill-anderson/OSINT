DataReportal's Digital 2026 DRC report (data to October 2025): **64.7 million cellular mobile connections** at end-2025 — equivalent to **56.9% of the population** (up 4.7 million / **+7.8%** year on year) — of which GSMA Intelligence indicates **79.6% are "broadband"** (3G/4G/5G). A standing connectivity/inclusion baseline for the DRC: mobile-heavy, with low fixed and overall internet penetration.
