# Cybastion — Angola Telecom digital-sovereignty investment, 2025

U.S. technology firm Cybastion signed a US$170 million investment with Angola Telecom (June 2025) to strengthen Angola's digital infrastructure and cybersecurity, with a US$25 million first phase to establish a national cybersecurity agency and training.

## Description

"U.S.-based technology firm Cybastion has announced a landmark $170 million investment in partnership with Angola's incumbent telecommunications operator, Angola Telecom E.P., aimed at strengthening the country's digital infrastructure and cybersecurity capacity." The partnership forms part of Cybastion's "Digital Fast Track" initiative.

"As part of the initial implementation, $25 million will be allocated to establish a national cybersecurity agency and launch cybersecurity training programs in collaboration with the Cisco Networking Academy. The investment will also support critical digital infrastructure, including the deployment of undersea cables between Luanda and the port city of Lobito." The deal builds on a memorandum of understanding signed in March 2025 at Mobile World Congress in Barcelona.
