## Description

Phase 2 of Women Count programme. Improves production and use of gender-disaggregated data to track SDGs, women's economic empowerment, and gender-based violence. Modernises data systems in Senegal, Chad, and Mauritania; engages statistics institutes, ministries, women's organisations and media. Financed through UN Women.
