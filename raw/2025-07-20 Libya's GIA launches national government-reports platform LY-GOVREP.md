Libya's **[[gia-libya|General Information Authority (GIA)]]** began the initial operation of the national platform for Libyan government reports, **LY-GOVREP**, with the Cabinet's Inspection and Follow-Up Department. The launch (announced 20 July 2025; first technical meeting 14 July) began with 60 governmental entities under the Cabinet.

The platform organises official government reports within a unified, reliable digital platform to support **transparency**, empower oversight bodies and improve decision-making; the technical team discussed data entry, institutional publication controls, and linking government entities to enhance the oversight and evaluation role of the Inspection and Follow-Up Department. It is part of the GIA's wider push, alongside its Open Data Platform and the Seventh National Human Development Report programme with UNDP.
