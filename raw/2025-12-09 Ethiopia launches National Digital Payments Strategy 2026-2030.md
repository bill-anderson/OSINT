# Ethiopia launches National Digital Payments Strategy 2026–2030 and instant payments

Ethiopia launched its **[[ethiopia-ndps|National Digital Payment Strategy 2026–2030]]** and an **Instant Payment System** at the 2nd Ethiopian Digital Payment Conference (Addis, 9 Dec 2025; Deputy PM Temesgen Tiruneh, NBE Governor Eyob Tekalign). The strategy charts interoperability, trust and innovation, and — notably — proposes enabling **low-value outbound cross-border transfers** via cards, wallets and digital banking for the first time. Ethiopia now processes **18.5tn+ birr** annually in digital transactions, enabled by the **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** ID; one-stop centres (e-Gov, MESOB) and Safaricom Ethiopia's entry were cited, alongside remaining urban-rural and gender gaps.
