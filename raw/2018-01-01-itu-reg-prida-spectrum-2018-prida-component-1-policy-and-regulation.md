## Description

ITU-managed spectrum component of the AU-EU-ITU Policy and Regulation Initiative for Digital Africa (PRIDA). Aims to harmonize spectrum utilization across Africa to facilitate wireless broadband deployment. Activities: spectrum allocation policy based on international best practices; spectrum licensing and pricing frameworks; cross-border frequency coordination agreements; IoT awareness. EU-funded via Pan-African Programme. ITU contributes EUR 500K own funds. Covers all 54 African countries.
