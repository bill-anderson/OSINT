**ADMITTED NARROWLY — a scope call, and the second instance of the class ISSUE-004 covers.**

This is a pure energy-access study with **effectively zero data or digital-governance payload**, and that
is not an impression but a tested finding: across the full extracted text the word **"digital" appears
zero times**; "mobile" appears twice, both as "mobile phone charger" in an appliance-load list; "phone"
appears eleven times, every one as an appliance wattage benchmark used to define MTF Tier 1. There is no
mobile money, no digital payments, no PAYG solar financing, no connectivity content. Its subject is grid
and off-grid access tiers, solar lanterns and home systems, cooking fuels, and a gender analysis.
