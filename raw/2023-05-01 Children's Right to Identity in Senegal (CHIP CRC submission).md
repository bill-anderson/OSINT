# Children's Right to Identity in Senegal (CHIP submission to UN CRC)

Submission prepared for the **95th Pre-Sessional Working Group (1–5 May 2023)** of the UN Committee on the
Rights of the Child (State Periodic Report CRC/C/SEN/6-7). CRVS/birth-registration reference on Senegal:
**22.3% of under-fives unregistered (2019)**; modernisation via the **[[nekkal-programme|Nékkal programme]]**;
2021 awareness efforts; ~18.9% of elementary pupils and 8% of preschoolers lacked civil-status documents
(2021, with regional highs — Sédhiou 50.9%, Kaffrine 44%). Also documents late-registration fees (reduced to
CFA500), refugee identity (UNHCR: ~16,205 refugees), and identity-falsification risks (talibé children,
child marriage, trafficking). Foundational identity/CRVS baseline beneath digital ID and DPI.
