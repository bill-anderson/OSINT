## Description

DFC direct loan of $5 million to QuantumID Technologies SASU, a Cambridge, Massachusetts-based company expanding its SmartKargo e-commerce, logistics and cargo tracking platform in Senegal. The platform uses RFID and digital tracking technology to enable last-mile e-commerce and cargo services across Senegal and throughout Africa. Investment supports expansion of proprietary technology, management systems, and last-mile services for e-commerce.
