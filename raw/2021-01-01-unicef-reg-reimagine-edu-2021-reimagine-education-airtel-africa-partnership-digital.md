## Description

UNICEF's Reimagine Education initiative (launched 2020) calls for public and private sector investment in digital learning as an essential service for every child. Airtel Africa, the first African private sector partner, committed USD 57 million over 5 years (2021-2027) to UNICEF's Reimagine Education initiative to connect schools across 13 African countries (Chad, Congo, DRC, Gabon, Kenya, Madagascar, Malawi, Niger, Nigeria, Rwanda, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia) to the internet and provide free access to learning platforms. By end 2023, 1,500+ schools connected, 17,000 teachers trained, reaching nearly 1 million learners across 13 countries. By March 2026, 3,296 schools cumulative. UNICEF provides technical leadership, digital content (Learning Passport), and zero-rated platform access. Airtel provides network infrastructure and financial contribution.
