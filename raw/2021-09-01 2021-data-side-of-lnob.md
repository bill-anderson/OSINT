# The data side of leaving no one behind

A Development Initiatives report arguing that national data infrastructure must be built bottom-up from the administrative systems that register people and record services — clinics, schools, birth registration — rather than top-down from household surveys. It is the foundational statement of the thesis that recurs across the 2026 Data Landscapers essays.
