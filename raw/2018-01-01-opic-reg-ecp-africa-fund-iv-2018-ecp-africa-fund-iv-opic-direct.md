## Description

OPIC direct investment of $100 million in ECP Africa Fund IV A LLC, a private equity fund managed by Emerging Capital Partners (ECP) investing in high-growth companies serving African consumers across multiple sectors. The fund includes IHS Holding Ltd (Nigeria, telecom tower infrastructure) among its portfolio companies. OPIC commitment in 2018 as part of the Connect Africa initiative to support technology and infrastructure across Sub-Saharan Africa.
