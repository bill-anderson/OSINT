## Description

Supports Western Africa's digital integration through Series of Projects (SOP2) approach. Increases broadband access, financing, and usage in participating countries and enables environment for cross-border digital services in Western Africa. Successor to SOP1 (P176932). Covers broadband infrastructure, digital economy governance, and digital public services across West African ECOWAS member states.
