# Registre Social Unifié (RSU) — Guinea

Ministry presentation on Guinea's **Registre Social Unifié (RSU)** — the unified social registry designed as the single entry point for all social-protection programmes ([[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]). Establishing/reference document for the RSU as a standing government data asset. Key points:

- **Origins:** national targeting-tools harmonisation workshop, Kindia, 10-14 July 2017; a Guinea RSU prototype as the single gateway for social-protection programmes.
- **Legal basis:** Decree **D/2019/271/PRG/SGG of 01 October 2019** creating the RSU, defining its mission and functioning.
- **Rollout stages:** politico-administrative and community sensitisation across regions/prefectures; targeting, data collection, and an analysis-report pipeline feeding a robust database.

Complements the FDSI (social-development financing) frame; a social-protection counterpart to the civil-registry/ID stack ([[oneci-guinea]]) and a natural consumer of a future unique identifier.
