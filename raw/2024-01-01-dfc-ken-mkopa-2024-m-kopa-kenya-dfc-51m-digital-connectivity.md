## Description

DFC sustainability-linked loan of $51 million to M-KOPA Kenya Limited, a Nairobi-based asset financing company providing pay-as-you-go smartphone and solar home system financing to underbanked and underserved customers. The loan finances smartphone receivables, solar home system receivables, and cash loans to expand digital connectivity and financial inclusion among low-income Kenyans. DFC commitment announced May 2024 during Kenyan President Ruto's State Visit to the United States. Part of a $202M five-year facility arranged by Standard Bank.
