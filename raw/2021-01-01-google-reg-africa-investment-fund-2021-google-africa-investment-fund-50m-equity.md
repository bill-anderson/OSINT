## Description

Google established the $50 million Africa Investment Fund at Google for Africa 2021 to make equity investments in early- and growth-stage African technology startups. The fund provides equity capital plus access to Google technology, employees, and networks. Initial portfolio (as of 2022): SafeBoda (Uganda, mobility super-app), Carry1st (South Africa, mobile gaming), Lori Systems (Kenya, logistics). Individual ticket sizes are not publicly disclosed.
