# World Bank — Liberia GREAT digital-ID and digital-government, 2025

The World Bank's ID Credit Program funded Liberia's GREAT project with US$30 million (plus US$13m for digital infrastructure) to enrol 2 million+ Liberians for a national digital ID and build a digital-government ecosystem to 2030.

## Description

"Liberia's Ministry of Finance and Development Planning launched the Governance Reform Accountability Transformation (GREAT) project on 20 March 2025 in Monrovia. Funded by the World Bank's ID Credit Program with $30 million (plus an additional $13m for digital infrastructure), GREAT seeks to enroll at least 2 million Liberians for the national digital ID through the NIR." Running to November 2030, it also builds a digital-government ecosystem beginning with nine institutions.
