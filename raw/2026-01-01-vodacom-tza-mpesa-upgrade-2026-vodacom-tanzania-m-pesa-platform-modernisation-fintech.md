## Description

Vodacom Tanzania PLC completed a $28 million investment to migrate its M-Pesa platform from the legacy G2 system to a next-generation 'Fintech 2.0' platform, announced April 2026. The 15-month project, completed April 2026, modernises Tanzania's leading mobile money platform (30 million customers, ~41% market share, 600,000+ merchants). Key improvements: 1,000+ transactions/second capacity; system updates without service interruption (previously required nightly downtime); enhanced security; faster product deployment. The upgrade is part of a wider $100M modernisation programme announced Sep 2025 (Vodacom Tanzania 25th anniversary). The new platform also supports Tanzania becoming the first African country to enable M-Pesa contactless (tap-to-pay) payments.
