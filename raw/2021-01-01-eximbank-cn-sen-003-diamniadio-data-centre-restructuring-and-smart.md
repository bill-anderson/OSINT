## Description

CHEXIM loan for Phase 2 of the Smart Sénégal digital infrastructure program. Covers restructuring/expansion of the Diamniadio National Data Center (inaugurated June 2021), which is a Tier 3 data center built by Huawei under Chinese financing. Aims to centralize government data and digital platforms previously hosted on foreign servers, supporting Senegal's digital sovereignty strategy. Total Smart Sénégal program cost ~$150M USD.
