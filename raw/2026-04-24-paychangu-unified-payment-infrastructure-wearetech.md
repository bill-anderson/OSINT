# Malawian Fintech PayChangu Targets SME Growth with Unified Payment Infrastructure

**PayChangu**, a Malawi-based fintech (founded 2021 by Morgan Tembo, headquartered in Lilongwe), operates as a **payment aggregator** connecting mobile money, cards and bank transfers through a single integration, targeting SMEs with low-cost, developer-friendly tools (APIs, plugins, payment links). It addresses fragmentation in Africa's digital payments market and supports e-commerce expansion.

PayChangu enables businesses to accept payments via mobile money, bank cards and instant bank transfers via one integration, with payment-link generation, invoicing tools and analytics dashboards, plus APIs and plugins compatible with Shopify and WooCommerce. The company positions SMEs at the centre of its strategy with competitive transaction fees and low-code integration, aiming to connect more African businesses to the global digital economy. A profile of one of Malawi's leading homegrown fintechs.
