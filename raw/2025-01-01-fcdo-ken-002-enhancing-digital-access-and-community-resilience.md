## Description

Promotes digital inclusion and strengthens community resilience in eight Arid and Semi-Arid Lands (ASAL) counties in Kenya, including two refugee-hosting counties (Garissa and Turkana). Equips beneficiaries with digital literacy skills and access to digital financial services. Two parallel activities: -403 covers refugee-hosting counties (Garissa, Turkana, £1M); -404 covers wider eight ASAL counties (Wajir, Mandera, Garissa, Tana River, Samburu, Turkana, Marsabit, Isiolo, £1.3M). Both started August-October 2025, completing March 2026.
