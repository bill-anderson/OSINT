Isiolo, 31 July 2025 — Isiolo County officially joined the **[[ardhisasa]]** platform (Blocks 1-10 live). Lands Secretary Sarah Maina urged county land officials and landowners to register for easier, faster and more transparent land transactions, emphasising national-county data sharing.

Ardhisasa is a **homegrown digital land-administration system** offering searches, transfers, subdivisions and more entirely online, built on the **National Land Information Management System (NLIMS)**. The previous week, Mombasa Island Blocks XVI and XVII were onboarded; Marsabit and Baringo (Koibatek) are next. The Ministry continues rolling out Ardhisasa across counties to streamline land governance and address historical land injustices.
