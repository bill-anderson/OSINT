# Lancement officiel des activités de distribution des kits Starlink par NINETEC : « Garantir une connexion internet fiable, accessible partout et à moindre coût »

*(Le Sahel / ONEP is the Niger state daily. Capture is not endorsement.)*

On **Wednesday 30 April 2025**, the Minister of Communication and New Information Technologies, **Ali Salatou Adji**, launched distribution of Starlink kits in Niger through the **NINE-Starlink** campaign of **NINETEC (Nigérienne du Net et des Technologies)**, an approved Starlink distributor partnering with SpaceX. Present: ARCEP DG **Colonel-major Idrissa Chaibou**, NINETEC DG **Ousseini Garba Bechir**, and the economic adviser of the US Embassy in Niger.

The Minister praised NINETEC as a "100% Nigerien" initiative — "technological excellence can be born, grow and shine from our own soil" — pairing local engagement with a world satellite-connectivity leader for the national digital economy, aiming to connect health centres and rural communes. The ARCEP DG reminded NINETEC to comply strictly with **décret N°2020-331 of 8 May 2020** (identification of buyers/users of mobile-telephony and public internet services): each kit must be registered and activated in the final client's name, with Starlink use restricted to personal/professional use — no connection-sharing, sub-letting or resale.
