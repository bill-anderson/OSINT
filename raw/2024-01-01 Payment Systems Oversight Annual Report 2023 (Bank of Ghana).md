# Payment Systems Oversight Annual Report 2023 (Bank of Ghana)

BoG Payment Systems Department annual report; reference statistical resource. Figures are **as of end-2023** — cite, do not promote as current state. Key FY2023 data:
- **Mobile money:** 6.81bn transactions (2023) vs 5.07bn (2022), +34.3%; total value **GH¢1.92 trillion** (2023) vs GH¢1.07tn (2022), +79%. Active agents **609,000** (from 505,122, +20.5%). MMI (interoperability) value and volume both +24%; wallet-to-wallet = 83% of MMI value.
- **GhIPSS Instant Pay (GIP):** 115.4m transactions / **GH¢120.10bn** (2023), +50.8% volume / +104.6% value.
- **Mobile banking:** 7,287,579 registered customers (+51.5%); value GH¢80.40bn. Internet banking 1,317,003 customers (+16.6%).
- **GIS (RTGS):** value GH¢3,443.98bn (+49%). Cheques cleared −4.3% volume; ATMs 2,287 (+1.4%), POS 12,491 (−6.9%).
- Ghana's **first PAPSS transaction March 2023** (Ghana→Nigeria, local currency). **e-Cedi** (CBDC) hackathon with EMTECH concluded 14 Dec 2023.
