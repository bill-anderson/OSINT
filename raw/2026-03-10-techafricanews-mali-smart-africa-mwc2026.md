TechAfrica News. On the sidelines of MWC 2026 in Barcelona, Mali's Minister of Communication, Digital Economy and Administration Modernisation Alhamdou Ag Ilyene met Smart Africa Alliance DG Lacina Kone (3 March 2026). Ilyene reaffirmed Mali's commitment to accelerating digital transformation via a strengthened Smart Africa partnership; talks focused on human-capital development (youth education, national digital-professions skills), reinforcing fibre-optic interconnections with neighbouring countries, and the possibility of Mali hosting the next Transform Africa Summit (TAS). Signals Mali balancing AES-centric digital sovereignty with continental (Smart Africa) cooperation.
