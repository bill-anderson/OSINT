## Key facts

- Three building blocks: data standardization, adaptable data governance, sustainable technical
  infrastructure — each underpinned by human capacity and ownership.
- **aLIVE** = a 4-year, $5m Gates-funded DG program with Ethiopia's Ministry of Agriculture; harmonised
  five livestock databases against the ICAR-ADE standard (endorsed 25 Jan 2024) toward a central
  Livestock Information System. Ethiopia has **no whole-of-government data-sharing policy**; it enacted
  the **Personal Data Proclamation 1321/2024** instead (no cross-ministerial exchange mandate).
