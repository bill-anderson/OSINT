[Liquid C2](https://htxt.co.za/?s=Liquid+C2), a business of Cassava Technologies, has today opened its first Partner Experience Centre powered by Google Cloud.

The Partner Experience Centre is a multifaceted facility that aims to foster AI talent and solutions, giving local businesses a pathway to the world, and the world a pathway into the local market.

Liquid C2 partners will be able to secure official Google Cloud accreditation and Certification through technical training provided by both Google and Liquid C2. Beyond that, the facility will also serve as a collaborative hub, where partners can work with specialist engineers to create solutions that solve their specific problems. These solutions can then be brought to market through Liquid C2’s distribution network, opening up commercial opportunities for partners.

The centre will also give partners access to enterprise grade technologies such as Gemini Enterprise, and the “Gemini Playspace” for rapid AI experimentation.

Senior vice president and chief executive officer of Cassava Technologies, Ziaad Suleman, said that Africa’s digital transformation will be shaped by ecosystems that combine global innovation with local expertise and infrastructure.

“The Partner Experience Centre powered by Google Cloud creates a practical environment where organisations can explore, test, and scale solutions that deliver real business value. By combining our infrastructure, expertise, and continental reach with Google Cloud’s advanced technologies, we are helping to democratise access to AI and cloud capabilities for enterprises across Africa,” he noted in a statement.

Liquid C2 added that the Partner Experience Centre also features industry-specific platforms for financial services, healthcare, and retail. These platforms aim to showcase how AI can reduce operational risk, improve efficiency and more. This is important as, given how new AI is, businesses may need guidance regarding practical AI applications.

Importantly, Liquid C2 outlines the three things that guided the partnership with Google Cloud and the establishment of the facility:

- **Accelerated Partner Enablement**: The centre acts as a dedicated Proof-of-Concept designed to dismantle historical market barriers. It aims to provide localised training, hands-on technology interaction, and business support, leveraging Liquid C2’s capabilities to offer local currency billing and credit to manage financial complexity for resellers.
- **AI and Technology Innovation**: A primary focus is empowering partners to build and deploy advanced AI solutions. The facility features a dedicated “Gemini Playspace & AI Solutions” area to certify technical staff, alongside integrated “Solutions Pods” where partners can demonstrate complete technology stacks to win complex enterprise bids.
- **Economic Growth and Job Creation**: The partnership is a direct investment in Africa’s tech workforce. By strategically broadening the partner network, the initiative will foster deep, localised expertise and act as a catalyst for new economic opportunities, creating a significant ripple effect of job creation for certified engineers and other tech professionals across the continent.

“The Partner Experience Centre is a testament to our belief in the power of a strong partner ecosystem. By combining our advanced AI capabilities, including our Gemini models, with Liquid C2’s localised expertise, we are not just building a facility; we are building a hub for innovation that will empower businesses, create jobs, and deliver the benefits of digital transformation to every corner of the continent,” shared Tara Brady, president of Google Cloud EMEA.

With AI becoming firmly entrenched in business processes, it’s great to see Liquid C2 and Google Cloud collaborating to improve access to AI skills and assisting partners who aim to compete on the world stage, actually reach it.
