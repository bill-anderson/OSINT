# Econet Powers Lesotho's Digital Leap with Major Network Upgrade

*Tech Africa News, 20 June 2025.*

Econet Telecom Lesotho (ETL) is expanding connectivity across all ten districts through a major network upgrade. At its heart is the rapid expansion of 5G infrastructure: the company has **grown active 5G sites from 54 to 149**, powered by the 700MHz spectrum band prized for broad coverage and deep signal penetration.

Rolling out in phases since February 2025, the overhaul began in Maseru and involves replacing legacy systems with high-efficiency radio units and reinforcing the transport network. Enhanced 5G coverage is already reaching Leribe, Mafeteng, Quthing and Butha-Buthe. ETL is also integrating AI systems to monitor and adjust network performance in real time, preventing disruptions.
