Vendor announcement — Semlex (primary source, flagged for verification). The city of Safim, in the Biombo region, now has a new production centre for biometric national identity cards, inaugurated on Friday 30 January 2026 by Guinea-Bissau's Minister of Justice Carlos Pinto Pereira, as part of Ministry of Justice reforms to decentralize civil-identification services and improve access to secure official documents.

The Director General of Civil Identification, Helder Romano Vieira, encouraged local communities to use the new service. The Safim centre has a production capacity of 100 identity cards per day and is the third operational centre in the Biombo region (after Quinhamel and Plack 2); new centres are planned in Sao Domingos and Bolama (Bijagos). To date, Guinea-Bissau has **18 biometric ID card production centres** across the country, including in Bissau.
