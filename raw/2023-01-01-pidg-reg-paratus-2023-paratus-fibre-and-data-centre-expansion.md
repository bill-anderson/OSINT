## Description

EAIF provided USD 18 million (part of a USD 31 million total facility with Ninety One) to Paratus Group, a pan-African ISP and fibre provider. The financing supports expansion of Paratus's fibre and data centre business in Angola, Mozambique, Namibia, Republic of Congo, South Africa and Zambia, including three new fibre routes and Angola's first Tier IV data centre.
