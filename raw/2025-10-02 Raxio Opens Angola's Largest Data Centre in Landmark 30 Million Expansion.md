Raxio Group opened the biggest and most advanced data centre in Angola. Raxio Angola (AG1) delivers up to 3 MW of IT power and hosts over 800 server racks — more than doubling any existing facility in the country — and is the only commercial data centre in Angola built to **Tier III** standards. The $30 million development spans 19,000 m² (Raxio's biggest campus), its sixth on the continent, with plans to more than double capacity to over 7 MW within five years.

"Raxio Angola represents a leap forward for our country's digital economy," said Mário Oliveira, Minister of Telecommunications, Information Technologies and Social Communication, who championed the project and officiated at the opening. Located in Cacuaco, Luanda Province, the facility is intentionally close to the region's main undersea cable landing stations and will host local internet traffic to improve speed and reliability.
