## Description

Africa Data Centres announced in May 2023 construction of its first Ghana data centre at the Ghana Trade Fair Redevelopment Project in Accra's Central Business District. The facility is designed for initial 10MW (expandable to 30MW), built in partnership with Onix Data Centres (partnership confirmed June 2024). The investment is part of ADC's DFC-backed $300M facility; in October 2023 ADC and DFC signed a statement reaffirming their partnership, with DFC signalling intent to mobilise approximately $50M (15% of $300M) of the existing DFC facility for Ghana. Construction was expected to commence in 2024, with the first phase operational within 12–18 months. US President Biden highlighted the Ghana facility at the 2023 G7 Summit in Hiroshima.
