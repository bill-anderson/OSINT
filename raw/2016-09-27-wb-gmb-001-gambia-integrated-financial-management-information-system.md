## Description

Improves public resource management through expansion of the IFMIS system and strengthens government capacity to effectively use the system on a sustainable basis in The Gambia. Supports IFMIS rollout to additional MDAs, interfaces with revenue and treasury systems, and system training. Additional financing to original IFMIS project.
