On 15 February 2026, Libya's **[[gia-libya|General Information Authority (GIA)]]** participated in a workshop organised by the **[[gaci-libya|General Authority for Communications and Informatics (GACI)]]** in Tripoli, "The Future Vision for Data and Cloud Computing Services," supporting the national communications strategy to develop digital infrastructure.

Representing the GIA, Engineer Ayoub Al-Shweikh (Director of the National Information System Department) and Engineer Hussam Al-Rabti presented **"Government Data Governance Policies,"** highlighting the treatment of data as a strategic resource for improving citizens' lives through evidence-based decisions. Context: the GIA had recently launched indicators covering **91 datasets** through its Open Data Platform and was preparing the Seventh National Human Development Report with UNDP.
