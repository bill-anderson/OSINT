# CRVS Systems Snapshot — Ethiopia (Centre of Excellence for CRVS)

Snapshot of Ethiopia's **civil-registration and vital-statistics (CRVS) system** — legal basis, institutional arrangements, registration coverage of births/deaths, and links to identity management. Historical baseline for the CRVS foundations underpinning the later **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** foundational-ID drive.
