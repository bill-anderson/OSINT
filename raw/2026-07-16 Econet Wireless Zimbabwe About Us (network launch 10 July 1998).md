Econet Wireless Zimbabwe's own **About Us** page records the company's founding timeline as a primary
company statement:

- **Network launch: 10 July 1998** — "we launched Zimbabwe's first private network on 10 July 1998
  following a five-year protracted legal battle fighting for a license."
- **Five-year legal battle, 1993–1998** — the page's timeline section is headed **"1993 – 1998,"**
  spanning the licence fight against the state PTC monopoly to the launch.
- **ZSE listing within three months of launch** — "in exactly three months of launching the network,
  Econet Wireless Zimbabwe successfully listed on the Zimbabwe Stock Exchange (ZSE) … and the IPO was
  oversubscribed" (i.e. ~October 1998).

The page gives no incorporation or licence date distinct from the 1998 launch; the **1993** start-of-
venture and **~1997** licence-secured dates come from other records (Stanford GSB; NYU Law; African
Leadership Magazine) — see [[strive-masiyiwa]].
