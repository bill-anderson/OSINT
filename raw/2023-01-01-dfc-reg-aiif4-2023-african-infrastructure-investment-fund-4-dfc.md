## Description

DFC equity investment of up to $50 million in African Infrastructure Investment Fund 4 (AIIF4), a closed-end infrastructure fund managed by AIIM (African Infrastructure Investment Managers) with a target of $748M. Fund invests in digital infrastructure, energy transition, and mobility/logistics across Africa. Digital component includes telecom towers (Eastcastle Infrastructure, DRC and Nigeria, wireless telecom services). Fund achieved hard cap $748M with $954M including co-investments. DFC investment supports expansion of telecommunications and digital infrastructure across Sub-Saharan Africa.
