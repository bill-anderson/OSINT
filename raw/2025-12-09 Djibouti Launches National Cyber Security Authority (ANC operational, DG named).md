Djibouti's Ministère Délégué chargé de l'Économie Numérique et de l'Innovation (MDENI), under Minister
**Mariam Hamadou Ali**, held a working meeting (reported 9 Dec 2025) marking the **official launch of the
National Cyber Security Authority (ANC)** — recently approved by the President — with the **newly appointed
Director-General Abdallah Ibrahim Yonis** in attendance. The Ministry handed over technical equipment
(incident-response computers, firewalls, high-capacity servers, monitoring screens, professional licences) and
issued 20 Coursera training licences. Framed within Djibouti's **Smart Nation Roadmap / Vision 2035** and its
accession to the Hanoi (UN) cybercrime convention; the Minister cast cybersecurity as central to digital
sovereignty.
