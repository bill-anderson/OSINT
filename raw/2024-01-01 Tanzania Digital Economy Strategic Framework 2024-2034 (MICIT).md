# Tanzania Digital Economy Strategic Framework 2024-2034

Companion source page for the artefact `2024-01-01 Tanzania Digital Economy Strategic Framework 2024-2034 (MICIT).pdf`, held in `raw/`.

Ten-year national digital economy strategy, foreword by President Samia Suluhu Hassan as Chairperson of the Tanzania National Business Council. Anchored to the National ICT Policy, the National Five-Year Development Plan 2021/22-2025/26, SDG Agenda 2030 and AU Agenda 2063. Imprint reads (c) MICIT 2024 with no month printed. A separate document in the same batch states the President launched it on 29 July 2024 - external corroboration, not printed here.
