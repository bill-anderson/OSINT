# IFC — Senegal agricultural payments digitization advisory, 2024

The IFC approved a US$500,000 advisory engagement in 2024 to help Senegal's Ministry of Agriculture design and implement a plan to digitize agricultural subsidy payments.

## Description

"Senegal's government is partnering with the International Finance Corporation (IFC), the World Bank's private sector arm, to digitize agricultural subsidies… The project, approved July 29 with a budget of $500,000, will leverage IFC's technical assistance to design and implement a digitization plan, working alongside the ministry and its partners." The initiative, led by the Ministry of Agriculture, aims to improve transparency and efficiency in distributing aid for peanuts, maize, cowpeas and rice.
