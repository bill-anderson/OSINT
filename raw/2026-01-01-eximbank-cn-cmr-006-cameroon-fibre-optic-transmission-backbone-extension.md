## Notes

Presidential Decree No. 2026/084 signed March 6 2026 authorizes MINEPAT to sign loan with China Eximbank for RMB 1,350,963,161.10 = ~$195M (at ~7.18 2026 mid rate) = 108.4 billion CFA. Phase IV extends national fibre from ~12,000 km to rural/semi-urban areas. Also includes national data centre (cat-add-code 250200) and regional fibre to Chad, CAR, Nigeria. Contractor expected to be Huawei (follows pattern of Phases I-III). Amount-quality pledge: decree authorizes but loan not yet formally confirmed signed as of March 2026. Status pipeline.
