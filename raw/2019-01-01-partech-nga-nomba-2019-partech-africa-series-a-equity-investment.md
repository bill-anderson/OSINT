## Description

Partech Africa led a $5M Series A funding round in Kudi (later renamed Nomba), a Nigerian payment service provider enabling digital payments for small businesses and agents. Kudi provides mobile-based financial services and agency banking across Nigeria. Co-investors include Y Combinator CEO Michael Seibel, Khosla Ventures, and Y Combinator.
