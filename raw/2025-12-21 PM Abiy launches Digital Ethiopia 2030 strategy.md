# PM Abiy launches the Digital Ethiopia 2030 strategy

PM **Abiy Ahmed** officially launched the **[[digital-ethiopia-2030|Digital Ethiopia 2030]]** strategy (Addis, 21 Dec 2025), built on lessons of the previous five years and **three pillars: expanding access, ensuring equal opportunities, and building trust** between institutions and people. INSA DG **Tigist Hamid** said Digital Ethiopia 2025 laid cybersecurity foundations — the country built its own cloud infrastructure and a digital forensic lab — and under DE2030 cybersecurity is a strategic priority (resilient key infrastructure, a disaster-response centre, private-sector collaboration). See **[[insa-ethiopia]]**.
