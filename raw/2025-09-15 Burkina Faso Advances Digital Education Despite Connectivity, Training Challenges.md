Burkina Faso's **PACTDIGITAL** project (Ministry of Digital Economy) outlined (10 September 2025) its support to secondary and technical/vocational education: updating telecom/ICT curricula to job-market demands, launching an online learning platform, strengthening local educational-multimedia-content skills, and equipping scientific high schools in Ouagadougou and Bobo-Dioulasso. A project to develop **95 educational mobile applications** was announced in February 2024. The digital-skills strand of PACTDIGITAL, pursued despite connectivity and training constraints.
