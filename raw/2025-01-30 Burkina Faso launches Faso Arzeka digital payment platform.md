**Faso Arzêka** is a Burkinabè government digital-payment platform under the Ministry of Economy and Finance, via the Directorate General of Treasury and Public Accounting (DGTCP), centralising government and financial services to simplify transactions, improve state revenue collection and promote financial inclusion (especially rural).

It offers a mobile app (iOS/Android), a web platform, and a **USSD *700#** channel for users without internet. Payments go via **Arzêka Money**, mobile money, bank cards and bank transfers, for services including **eTimbre** (electronic tax stamps), motor-vehicle tax, residence tax and customs duties — categorised by responsible ministry.
