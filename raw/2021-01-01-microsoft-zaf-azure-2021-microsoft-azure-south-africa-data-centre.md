## Description

Microsoft opened the first enterprise-grade cloud data centre regions on the African continent in Johannesburg (South Africa North) and Cape Town (South Africa West) in March 2019. Cumulative investment through 2024 was ZAR 20.4 billion (approximately USD 1.1 billion), as retrospectively disclosed by Microsoft president Brad Smith in March 2025. Provides Azure, Office 365, Dynamics 365 and Power Platform services from South Africa.
