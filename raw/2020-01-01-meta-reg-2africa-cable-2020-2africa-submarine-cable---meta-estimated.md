## Description

Meta is the lead and dominant partner in the 2Africa consortium (announced May 2020 with Orange, China Mobile International, MTN/Bayobab, STC, Telecom Egypt, Vodafone, WIOCC). 2Africa is 45,000+ km, 33 countries, up to 180 Tbps. Meta executives are quoted (Engineering at Meta, The Dial Oct 2025) as bearing 'the largest share of expenses'; total consortium cost referred to as 'probably around $1 billion'. Meta share estimated at ~40% of $1B total = $400M. SSA landing countries include CIV, SEN, CMR, COD, STP, NAM, ZAF, TZA, MOZ, KEN, DJI, GHA. Core infrastructure completed November 2025.
