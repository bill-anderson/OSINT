The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) is collaborating with the CBN and the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC) to track and freeze illicit digital wallets linked to money laundering, crypto scams and financial crimes (Nigerians lost over N174bn to fraudulent schemes). SEC DG Dr Emomotimi Agama (represented by Mrs Efe Ebelo) disclosed this at the 2025 SEC Journalists' Academy ("The ISA 2025 and the Future of Nigeria's Capital Market"). "The SEC is working closely with the CBN and the EFCC to freeze illicit digital wallets and recover criminal proceeds." Nigeria ranks among the world's top crypto adopters (over one-third of population engaged), driving both innovation and fraud risk.
