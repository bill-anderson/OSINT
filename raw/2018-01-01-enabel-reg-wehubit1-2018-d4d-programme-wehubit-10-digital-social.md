## Description

Wehubit 1.0 (D4D Programme) implemented by Enabel and BIO on behalf of Belgian development cooperation. Supports scaling of digital social innovations (fintech, agritech, e-health, edtech, digital infrastructure) across 14 official Belgian partner countries in SSA. EUR 9.345M Enabel-managed budget; additional EUR 14M committed by BIO for private sector digitisation projects. Programme provided grants of EUR 50K–350K to 27+ digital social innovations across 13 Enabel partner countries.
