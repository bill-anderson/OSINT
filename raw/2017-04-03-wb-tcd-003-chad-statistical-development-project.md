## Description

Strengthens the capacity of the national statistics institute to collect, process, and disseminate data with improved quality, frequency, and timeliness in Chad. Supports national statistical system development including household surveys, administrative data, price statistics, and dissemination systems. Includes digital statistical infrastructure and data management capacity building.
