DataReportal's essential headlines for the adoption and use of connected devices and services in Eswatini in early 2025:

- A total of 1.67 million cellular mobile connections were active in Eswatini in early 2025, equivalent to 134 percent of the total population (some may only include voice/SMS). Mobile connections increased by 123 thousand (+8.0 percent) between the start of 2024 and beginning of 2025. GSMA Intelligence data suggests 94.1% of mobile connections can be considered "broadband" (3G, 4G, or 5G).
- There were 720 thousand individuals using the internet in Eswatini at the start of 2025, when online penetration stood at 57.6 percent. The number of internet users increased by 7,506 (+1.1 percent) between January 2024 and January 2025. 530 thousand people (42.4 percent) remained offline.
- Eswatini was home to 464 thousand social media user identities in January 2025, equating to 37.1 percent of the total population.

Population context: The population of Eswatini stood at 1.25 million in January 2025, up 13 thousand (+1.1 percent) year-on-year. 25.1 percent lived in urban centres, 74.9 percent in rural areas. 50.9 percent female, 49.1 percent male. Median age was 22.5.

Data sources cited: GSMA Intelligence (mobile), Kepios analysis (internet/social media). Supported by Meltwater and We Are Social.
