## Description

USD 12M 10-year senior loan to TowerCo of Africa Uganda (previously Ubuntu Towers Uganda) for installation of 506 solar-powered telecom towers in rural Uganda. Part of USD 40M syndication with EIB (USD 16M) and OeEB (USD 12M). Towers provide 4G/5G broadband coverage, mobile money access, and digital financial services to rural communities currently without mobile network coverage.
