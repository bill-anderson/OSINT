# World Bank — Morocco Digital Transformation Acceleration Program, 2026

The World Bank approved and signed US$250 million in financing (June 2026, five years) for Morocco's Digital Transformation Acceleration Program — accelerating rollout and adoption of user-centred digital public services and supporting private-sector digitization.

## Description

"The meeting followed the approval and signing of the country's Digital Transformation Acceleration Program in June, establishing a common implementation framework based on measurable results and the use of national systems. Backed by $250 million (MAD 2.34 billion) in World Bank financing over five years, the program aims to accelerate the rollout and adoption of user-centered digital public services while supporting the digitization of the private sector to enhance competitiveness and create jobs." The minister "said her department's immediate priority is to ensure the program becomes operational before September 23 this year in coordination with the Ministry of Economy and Finance, by meeting all conditions required for its entry into force."
