## Description

BII committed $15M to the TLCom TIDE Africa Fund I in December 2019. TLcom is an Africa-focused venture fund investing in early-stage businesses that leverage technology to solve Africa's biggest challenges. The fund targets fintech, edtech, agritech, and digital health companies across SSA. Portfolio companies include Andela, Okra, Pula (crop insurance), Omnibiz, uLesson (edtech Nigeria), and others. BII is a significant anchor LP.
