# Algeria (African Startup & Young Innovators Financing Fund) — pan-African startup financing, 2025

Algeria committed US$1 billion to finance African startups through the African Startup and Young Innovators Financing Fund, announced 21 October 2025 by Foreign Trade Minister Kamel Rezig at UNCTAD16 (Geneva).

## Description

"Algeria committed USD 1 billion to finance African startups — announced 21 October 2025 by Kamel Rezig, Minister of Foreign Trade and Export Promotion, at the African Ministerial Meeting on the sidelines of UNCTAD16 (Geneva). The money flows through the African Startup and Young Innovators Financing Fund, launched by President Tebboune in September 2025, targeting technology, education, healthcare and humanitarian projects with strong social/economic impact."
