# Airtel Nigeria — Eko Atlantic 38MW hyperscale data centre, 2025

Airtel Nigeria announced a more than US$120 million investment to build a carrier-neutral 38MW hyperscale data centre in Eko Atlantic City, Lagos, part of its Nxtra network, for cloud and AI workloads.

## Description

"Airtel Nigeria plans to build a carrier-neutral 38MW hyperscale data centre in Eko Atlantic City in Lagos to meet surging demand for cloud and AI services. Part of Airtel's broader Nxtra network, the facility will handle next-generation compute workloads including GPU-powered infrastructure for AI. It will host over 3,000 racks across six floors..."

"Airtel Nigeria CEO Dinesh Balsingh told a media briefing in Lagos that the data centre will cost over US$120 million and was scheduled for completion in Q1 2026."
