## Description

MCF programme to leapfrog African countries into best-in-class EdTech solutions. Supports 40+ EdTech ventures and learning institutions across Africa. Works with EdTech hubs and entrepreneurs to integrate digital solutions into curriculum. Covers Rwanda, Nigeria, Senegal/WAEMU and broader SSA. Includes the EdTech Fellowship program providing up to $50,000 per company.
