This report contains data, insights, and trends on how people in Zambia use digital devices, online platforms, and connected services in 2025.

## The "state of digital" in Zambia in 2025

A total of 19.9 million cellular mobile connections were active in Zambia in early 2025, with this figure equivalent to 92.1 percent of the total population. There were 7.13 million individuals using the internet in Zambia at the start of 2025, when online penetration stood at 33.0 percent. Zambia was home to 3.70 million social media user identities in January 2025, equating to 17.1 percent of the total population.

## Population

The population of Zambia stood at 21.6 million in January 2025, having increased by 595 thousand (+2.8 percent) between early 2024 and the start of 2025. In early 2025, 47.2 percent of Zambia's population lived in urban centres, whereas 52.8 percent lived in rural areas. 50.5 percent of the population was female, 49.5 percent male. The median age was 17.9.

## Mobile connections

Data from GSMA Intelligence shows there were 19.9 million cellular mobile connections in Zambia at the beginning of 2025, equivalent to 92.1 percent of the total population. The number of mobile connections decreased by 224 thousand (-1.1 percent) between the start of 2024 and the beginning of 2025. GSMA Intelligence's data suggests that 90.8% of mobile connections in Zambia can now be considered "broadband" (3G, 4G, or 5G).

## Internet use

The latest available data indicated there were 7.13 million internet users in Zambia in January 2025, an internet penetration rate of 33.0 percent. The number of internet users increased by 196 thousand (+2.8 percent) between January 2024 and January 2025, while the internet adoption rate remained unchanged. These figures suggest that 14.5 million people (67.0 percent of the population) remained "offline" at the start of the year.

## Internet connection speeds

Figures published by Ookla indicate a median fixed internet download speed of 16.50 Mbps at the beginning of 2025—down 10.13 Mbps (-38.0 percent) in the twelve months to January 2025.

## Social media

There were 3.70 million active social media user identities in Zambia in January 2025, equivalent to 17.1 percent of Zambia's total population.
