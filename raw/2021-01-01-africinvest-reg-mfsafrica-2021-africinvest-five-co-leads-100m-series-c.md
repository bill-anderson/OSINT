## Description

AfricInvest's Financial Inclusion Vehicle (FIVE) co-leads a USD 100 million Series C financing round (USD 70M equity + USD 30M debt) for MFS Africa, Africa's largest digital payments network and interoperability hub. Round co-led with existing investors Goodwell Investments and LUN Partners, alongside CommerzVentures, Allan Gray Ventures and Endeavour. MFS Africa connects mobile network operators across 40+ African markets through a single API, enabling mobile money interoperability and cross-border digital payments. AfricInvest also participated in the USD 100M Series C extension in 2022.
