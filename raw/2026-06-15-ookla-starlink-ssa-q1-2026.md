Starlink leads terrestrial ISPs on throughput in Africa but lags on latency, according to Speedtest Intelligence® data.

While satellite coverage is often cited as a solution for the rural connectivity gap, the technology is primarily used as an alternative to high-speed terrestrial broadband service.
