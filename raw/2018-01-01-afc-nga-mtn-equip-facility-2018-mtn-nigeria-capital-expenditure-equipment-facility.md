## Description

AFC served as mandated lead arranger and contributed funding of up to USD 80 million as part of a USD 100 million three-year term loan facility arranged in conjunction with Rand Merchant Bank (RMB). The funding was designated to support MTN Nigeria Communications' capital expenditure, primarily for essential network equipment in Nigeria. MTN Nigeria is the country's largest mobile network operator. The facility supports expansion of voice and data network coverage across Nigeria.
