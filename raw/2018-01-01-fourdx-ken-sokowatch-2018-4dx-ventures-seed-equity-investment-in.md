## Description

4DX Ventures led a $2M seed round in Sokowatch (later rebranded to Wasoko), a Kenya-based B2B e-commerce platform connecting African informal retailers to suppliers and enabling last-mile FMCG distribution. The investment supports Sokowatch's expansion across East Africa. Co-investors include Village Global, Lynett Capital, Golden Palm Investments, and Outlierz Ventures.
