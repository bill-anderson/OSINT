## Description

IFC provided a senior secured loan package of up to $25 million to Atlas Tower Kenya (ATK), an independent wireless infrastructure company, to build up to 450 independent telecom towers in Kenya. IFC's own account contribution is $14 million, with $11 million mobilised from institutional investors through IFC's Managed Co-Lending Portfolio Program (MCPP). The towers expand coverage of mobile and internet services across underserved areas, benefiting Safaricom, Airtel Kenya, and Telkom Kenya. IATI records $13.64M reflecting the IFC A-loan (slight variation from $14M disclosure).
