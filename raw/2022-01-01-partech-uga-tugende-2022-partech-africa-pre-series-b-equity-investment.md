## Description

Partech Africa led a $10M pre-Series B equity round in Tugende, a Uganda-based technology-enabled MSME asset finance company operating in Uganda and Kenya. The round included participation from two new investors. Tugende uses technology to provide productive asset loans (motorcycles, smartphones) to low-income entrepreneurs and MSMEs in East Africa.
