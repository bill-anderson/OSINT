## Description

IFC is providing a ZAR-denominated senior term loan of approximately $58.17 million (ZAR equivalent) to Liquid Intelligent Technologies South Africa. This is part of a broader approximately $220 million financing package supporting the continued expansion of Liquid's fibre, broadband, and data centre infrastructure in South Africa. The ZAR denomination reduces currency risk for the company, which generates revenues primarily in local currency.
