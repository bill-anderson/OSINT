At a Luanda ceremony marking the first stone for a national cloud platform managed by INFOSI, Angola's Minister of Telecommunications, Information Technologies and Social Communication, Mário Oliveira, said the project will accelerate public-sector digitalisation and economic modernisation — a shift from pilot schemes to scalable infrastructure.

In parallel skills development, in partnership with **Huawei** the government has trained **5,000 ICT technicians** and aims to equip **10,000 by 2027**. Angola is also pursuing **AngoSat-II** and a forthcoming Earth-observation satellite to extend the digital agenda into remote sensing for agriculture, resource management and national security.
