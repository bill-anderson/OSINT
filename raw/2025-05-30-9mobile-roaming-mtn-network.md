9mobile received NCC regulatory approval to launch national roaming on MTN's network from June 2025, nearly five years after initial discussions. 9mobile's market share had dwindled to 1.72% (April 2025), down from 6.6% in 2020. Subscribers gain access to MTN infrastructure for calls, texts and data where 9mobile coverage is weak. In exchange, MTN gains utilisation rights to 9mobile's underused spectrum in the 900MHz, 1800MHz and 2100MHz bands. MTN serves over 84 million subscribers. The move supports the NCC's push for infrastructure sharing and spectrum efficiency.
