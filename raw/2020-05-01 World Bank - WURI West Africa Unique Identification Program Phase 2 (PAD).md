**Binary artefact:** [[2020-05-01 World Bank - WURI West Africa Unique Identification Program Phase 2 (PAD).pdf]] (Project Appraisal Document PAD3556, 112 pages).

World Bank appraisal (≈May 2020) of **WURI Phase 2** — **EUR 165.7m (US$182m)** in credits plus **SDR 66.5m (US$91m)** in grants to **Togo, Benin, Burkina Faso and Niger**, under the Multiphase Programmatic Approach the Board approved on 5 June 2018 (US$317.1m). The regional foundational-ID program ([[wuri-programme]]) for unique identification supporting regional integration and inclusion — the instrument behind Burkina Faso's and Benin's WURI enrolment.
