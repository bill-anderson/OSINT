## Description

DFC equity investment of up to $10 million in Janngo Capital Startup Fund SLP, Africa's largest gender-equal tech VC fund, investing in early-stage innovative tech startups primarily in Francophone West Africa (Côte d'Ivoire, Senegal). At least 50% of portfolio companies are women-led or co-led. Fund targets health, education, food, financial services, mobility, and logistics tech startups. Final close at €78M (€$85M). Fund leverages technology to improve access to essential goods and services.
