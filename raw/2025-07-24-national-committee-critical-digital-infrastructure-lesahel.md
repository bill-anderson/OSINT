# Installation d'un comité national chargé de l'opérationnalisation et du déploiement des infrastructures numériques critiques

*(Le Sahel / ONEP is the Niger state daily. Capture is not endorsement.)*

On **23 July 2025**, the Minister of Communication and New Information Technologies, **Adji Ali Salatou**, installed a **national committee to monitor the implementation, operationalisation and deployment of critical digital infrastructure**. The committee will define governance schemes, ensure system interoperability, pilot deployment phases, set up performance dashboards, ensure transparent stakeholder communication and produce quarterly reports.

The Minister enumerated the **critical digital infrastructures** to be deployed:
- **National Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)** — trust foundation for official electronic exchanges;
- **National Cybersecurity Centre (CNAC)**, comprising a **Security Operations Centre (SOC)** and **Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT)** — incident surveillance and response;
- the **national Internet Exchange Point (IXP)** — latency and bandwidth-cost reduction;
- **government reference data centres (Tier III or higher)** — availability and sovereignty of state data;
- a **national digital forensics laboratory (Forensic Lab)** — for judicial/security needs;
- the **national fibre-optic backbone and strategic metropolitan loops**.

These are framed as "essential to our digital sovereignty and national security" and a lever for digital public services (e-government, health, education, finance).
