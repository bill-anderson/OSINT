## Description

BII committed $15M to Novastar Ventures Africa Fund II in October 2018. Novastar is a Nairobi-based venture capital firm specializing in early-stage tech-enabled consumer businesses in East and West Africa. Fund II expanded Novastar's geographic mandate to West Africa and invested in companies including Turaco (insurtech), Pula (digital crop insurance), and others leveraging technology for impact in underserved markets. BII is a significant anchor LP.
