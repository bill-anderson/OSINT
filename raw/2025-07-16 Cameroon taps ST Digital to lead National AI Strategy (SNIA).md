The Cameroonian government plans to work with local firm **[[st-digital]]** to roll out its **National Artificial Intelligence Strategy (SNIA)** and boost digital sovereignty, following a **14 July 2025** visit by Posts and Telecommunications Minister Minette Libom Li Likeng to ST Digital's **Douala datacentre** — a Tier 3 facility with a **sovereign cloud** and AI-specific equipment. The minister called ST Digital "a strategic partner in the development of Cameroon's digital economy" — sovereign-cloud and AI infrastructure as the base for the SNIA.
