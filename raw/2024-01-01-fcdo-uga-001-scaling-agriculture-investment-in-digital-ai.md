## Description

Supports and scales digital agriculture innovations with the most potential for transformative impact, while generating evidence. Catalyses investment at scale in agricultural innovations expanding productivity, incomes and climate resilience. Drives more sustainable and better-integrated agri-food systems delivering safe and nutritious food. Builds a stronger enabling environment including better information on investment opportunities and risks. Increases deployment and adoption of technologies — including digital and AI — for greatest impact on livelihoods and resilience.
