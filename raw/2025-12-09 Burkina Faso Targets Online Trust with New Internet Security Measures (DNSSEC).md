Burkina Faso's regulator **ARCEP** (Electronic Communications and Postal Regulatory Authority) launched **DNSSEC** for the national **".bf"** internet domain on **5 December 2025** in Ouagadougou, adding a cryptographic signature to DNS records to guarantee authenticity and integrity for ".bf" services. The project drew technical support from **ICANN** via its Coalition for Digital Africa "DNSSEC Roadshow" — a trust-infrastructure step alongside Burkina's wider digital-sovereignty agenda.
