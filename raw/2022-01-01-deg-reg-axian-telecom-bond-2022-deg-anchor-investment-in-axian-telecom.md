## Description

DEG invests USD 23 million as anchor investor in AXIAN Telecom's debut USD 420 million bond (7.375% senior notes due 2027). AXIAN Telecom is a pan-African telecommunications group operating in Tanzania (including Tigo Tanzania acquisition), Madagascar, Togo, Senegal, and Uganda. The bond proceeds are used to install approximately 1,250 new mobile-phone antennas, improve digital infrastructure and liquidity for future investments. Four DFI anchor investors: CDC Group (USD 30M), DEG (USD 23M), Emerging Africa Infrastructure Fund (EAIF), and IFC (USD 50M).
