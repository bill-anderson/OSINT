# BEAC National Director visits CAMTEL Data Center

*Date: 2025-12-17 — By: Robert Annor*

CAMTEL recently hosted the National Director of the Bank of Central African States (BEAC), Mr. Pierre Emmanuel Nkoa Ayissi, and his delegation during an official visit to the company's Data Center, underscoring growing institutional interest in Cameroon's national digital infrastructure.

The delegation was received by CAMTEL's General Manager, Mrs. Judith Yah Sunday epse Achidi, alongside members of the company's management and technical teams. During the visit, guests were taken through the core infrastructure of the Data Center, where CAMTEL engineers presented the facility's security systems, energy installations, operational resilience, and service offerings.

The presentations highlighted the reliability, scalability, and maturity of Cameroon's national data infrastructure, with particular emphasis on compliance, business continuity, and the capacity to support mission-critical digital services.

At the conclusion of the visit, Mr. Nkoa Ayissi expressed satisfaction with the quality of the infrastructure and commended CAMTEL for its technical capabilities. He reaffirmed his confidence that Cameroon possesses the skills and facilities required to support large-scale digital projects essential to national development.

The visit follows a series of recent workshops organized by CAMTEL focusing on data centers and sovereign cloud solutions. During those engagements, several senior BEAC officials expressed strong interest in gaining deeper insight into the country's digital infrastructure, leading to the official tour of the Data Center.
