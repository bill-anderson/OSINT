# Botswana: P486 million for ICT development projects in the Ministry of Finance's NDP 12 Public Investment Programme (sought from Parliament)

Within the P8.83 billion the Ministry of Finance is seeking parliamentary approval for under the Twelfth National Development Plan (NDP 12) Public Investment Programme, P486 million is allocated to ICT development projects including a national payment switch, an upgraded accounting and budgeting system, and a fintech regulatory sandbox.
