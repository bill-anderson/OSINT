## Description

SIFEM committed USD 10 million to the Lendable MSME Fintech Credit Fund, a USD 110 million fintech debt fund providing senior-secured debt to early and mid-stage fintech companies across Africa and Asia. The fund supports digital financial inclusion by enabling fintech lenders providing consumer and MSME credit, payments, and remittances to underbanked populations in Africa.
