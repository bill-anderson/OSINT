# Houwiyeti — national (education) certificates extraction service (ami.mr)

*Source: Agence Mauritanienne d'Information — Mauritanian state news agency (government voice; capture is not endorsement).*

Education and Education-System-Reform Minister Houda Babah, with Digital Transformation Minister Ahmed Salem Bede, oversaw on Thursday the launch of a new digital service allowing citizens to extract and confirm their **national (education) certificates** via the **Houwiyati** platform, part of the digital-transformation drive to secure fast, secure access to official documents.

The service lets holders of national certificates view an electronic copy and extract a confirmed copy, provided the certificate was issued after 2013 or was previously linked to the national number (NNI) by the ministry's competent service. The initiative aims to reduce paper procedures and ease authenticity verification for individuals and administrative authorities alike. The ministry called on citizens who have not yet linked their certificates to their national number to do so.
