## Description

MTN Ghana (Scancom PLC) invested GHS 1.9 billion in core network capital expenditure during the 2022 financial year, representing a capex intensity of 17.4% of service revenue. Investments covered: (1) 400 2G sites, 400 3G sites, and 1,142 4G sites deployed, reaching 99.3% 4G population coverage; (2) 820 4G site upgrades; (3) IT systems modernisation; (4) MoMo platform capacity improvements. Revenue from Mobile Money grew 11.6% to GHS 1.9bn. MoMo active users reached ~11 million by end 2022. CapEx reflects the 'Ambition 2025' TechCo transformation strategy with five pillars: FinTech, super-app (Ayoba), Enterprise services, Network-as-a-Service, and API marketplace (Chenosis).
