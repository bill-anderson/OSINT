# Airtel Seychelles Launches 5G Service With SBC Partnership | 6 July 2026
The Seychelles Times

Airtel Seychelles switched on its commercial 5G service, pairing the new network with a content partnership with the Seychelles Broadcasting Corporation (SBC), at a press event at Airtel House on Île Perseverance. The rollout positions Airtel as the second mobile operator in Seychelles to offer 5G, following Cable & Wireless Seychelles (5G on Mahé in 2020 via Huawei).

The package includes 5G mobile plans and a 5G Home Wi-Fi service, with speeds up to 500 Mbps on compatible handsets and home routers, plus complimentary SBC television content and the option to subscribe to DStv over the same connection. MD Eddy Kapuku framed the move as "the first to make 5G truly work for the Seychellois pocket and the Seychellois home." SBC CEO Jude Valmont joined on stage.

The infrastructure builds on a 2021 turnkey agreement between Airtel Africa and Ericsson to modernise the Seychelles network and relocate it to a new data centre, giving Airtel a virtualised core and hybrid network cloud (reported by ITWeb Africa). No launch coverage map or outer-island rollout dates were published.
