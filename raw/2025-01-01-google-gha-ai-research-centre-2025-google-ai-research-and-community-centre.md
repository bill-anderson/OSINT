## Description

Google's AI research centre in Accra, Ghana (first announced 2018) received cumulative $37 million in investment as disclosed in July 2025. Also opened Africa's first AI Community Center in Accra. The centre focuses on AI research relevant to Africa: building language models, mapping infrastructure using AI, African-language tools, and food system AI applications. Includes catalytic capital for 100+ African AI-driven startups in agriculture, health, and education.
