Burundi migrated its flagship **Cash for Jobs** social-protection programme (locally **Merankabandi**) to the **CORE-MIS** solution powered by **openIMIS**. Since **May 2025**, CORE-MIS has managed cash transfers and income-generating-activity financing for **1.25 million beneficiaries** across five provinces, after migrating over **1 million** beneficiary records from the legacy system.

Merankabandi — a World Bank-financed Government of Burundi programme, national since **2022** — supports more than **200,000** vulnerable households and has added emergency cash transfers for disaster-affected households and benefits for **360,000** refugees and host-community members. Burundi's multiple payment providers are now integrated for automated payments and reconciliation, alongside KoboToolbox. Under the National Social Protection Strategy the government is developing a **Unified Social Registry** to coordinate access across health, education, labour and agriculture — for which openIMIS's social-registry features offer a starting point. (CORE-MIS was built by the World Bank during COVID-19 and integrated into openIMIS in 2023, backed by the WB, Germany's BMZ and Switzerland's SDC.)
