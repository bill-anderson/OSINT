## Description

Swedfund invested USD 32 million in Apis Growth Markets Fund III, a private equity fund by Apis Partners targeting companies at the intersection of finance and technology serving low- and middle-income consumers across Africa, South and Southeast Asia. The fund focuses on digitally enabled business models providing financial services, contributing to enhanced digitalisation and innovation, improved access and affordability of financial services, and job creation. African countries targeted include Egypt, Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa.
