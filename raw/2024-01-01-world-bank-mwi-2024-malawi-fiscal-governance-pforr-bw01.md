## Description

World Bank Program Appraisal Document (26 April 2024) for a proposed IDA grant of SDR 60.3 million (US$80 million equivalent) to Malawi for a Fiscal Governance Program-for-Results. The operation supports public financial management reform — revenue mobilisation, budget execution and procurement modernisation — underpinning the digitalisation of government fiscal systems (including the eGP/eGM procurement digitalisation).
