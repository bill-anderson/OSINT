## Description

FinDev Canada provides USD 15 million loan (including USD 7 million from 2X Canada blended finance facility) to Lendable MSME Fintech Credit Fund II, a 7-year global debt fund with USD 250 million target size. The fund deploys asset-backed capital to fintech platforms serving MSMEs and low-income consumers in Latin America, Africa, and Southeast Asia using data integration technology for credit risk monitoring and lending to digitally enabled, underserved segments.
