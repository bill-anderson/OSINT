## Description

EDCF concessional loan ($65M, 2021/signed Oct 2022) to digitise Tanzania's land information system including alphanumeric parcel data, certificates of occupancy, and customary land rights. Implements digital cadastral maps for MLHHSD in Dar es Salaam and zonal offices.
