The AfDB approved (**1 October 2024**) **€9.51 million (~$10.4m)** for the **Project to Support Digitization of the Comorian Economy** ([[project-digitization-comorian-economy]]; total est. €22.53m / $25m) — improving governance, quality, affordability and accessibility of public services.

It funds the **construction and operationalisation of a new data centre** (plus the existing secondary DC) and an incubator, with a **PPP** private operator for operations/maintenance/expansion; a **digital-administration platform**, digital public **payment system**, and an **interoperability and data-exchange platform**; and the creation of a **National Authority for the Protection of Personal Data and Access to Information**. Comoros had spent ~10 years on a six-pillar digital-services strategy with limited progress.
