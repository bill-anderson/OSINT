A World Bank mission led by Daniel Nogueira-Budny (Senior Digital Development Specialist, TTL), with Josemar Adriano (identity management), Deepti Dutt (Digital Public Infrastructure) and Gustavo Murillo Velasquez (Digital consultant), was conducted 8–12 September 2025. Mission objectives: (i) track overall progress including the submarine cable to Príncipe; (ii) advance Digital Identity implementation, jointly led by INIC and DGRN; (iii) plan the mass population registration on the future Digital Identity platform (planned for January/February 2026), proposed jointly with the update of the single social registry (Cadastro Social Único); (iv) advance digitalisation of public services, the government data-exchange interoperability platform, and the citizen portal/mobile app; and (v) launch materialisation of the CSIRT (Computer Security Incident Response Team).

**Project data.** Approved 15 June 2022; financing IDA grant + GIF: $21.8m; 5-year implementation period. PDO: improve equity and sustainability of telecom services between the islands of São Tomé and Príncipe and strengthen data governance, data systems and statistical capacity. Total disbursement as of 11 September 2025: US$7.64m (36%). Overall implementation progress rated Moderately Satisfactory.

**Legal/regulatory (Component 1).** AGER is finalising revision of the telecom legal/regulatory framework. The Regulatory Entities Law (guaranteeing AGER's independence) and the Electronic Communications Law (modernising the sector) have been approved by the Council of Ministers and submitted to the National Assembly; the AGER Statute has also been approved. The mission also flagged as urgent the review, public consultation, finalisation and approval of the new identity law (the "aggregated identification document Law"), warning that absence of its approval could constrain data collection.

**Digital Government: Digital Identity, digitalisation of public services, interoperability platform and single citizen Portal/App.** The mission coincided with the start of work by the System Integrator (Ooru), the consultancy contracted to design and implement — with INIC and DGRN — a new foundational identity (bilhete de identidade) that will be a digital ID. Live demonstrations showed functional prototypes of the Verifiable Credentials Infrastructure (VCI), the Interoperability and data-exchange platform ("DEX"), the citizen Portal and Mobile App, the electronic Signature service and digital forms — all based on open standards and/or with source-code availability to Government. Roadmap: (until) January 2026 — Digital ID implementation and launch of citizen Portal and App; March 2026 — integration of the interoperability layer with the VCI; May 2026 — availability of the electronic Signature solution.

**Data hosting and Data Centre assistance.** The consultancy contracted for technical assistance to the government data centre had assessed the existing centre and presented viable options: (i) requalification of the current data-centre facilities, valued at ~€0.5m; (ii) construction of a new TIER III data centre from scratch, estimated at ~€4.6m; and (iii) a hybrid option still under study (cloud with physical redundancy, or the inverse), to be presented mid-October. The consultancy's principal recommendation was migration of GoSTP systems and data, medium-term, either to a new data centre or to the cloud (in an account controlled and maintained by INIC), given high risks identified at the current sea-level data centre. The System Integrator recommended cloud hosting of the digital-ID system/data and other digital-government solutions, citing financial advantages and scarce human/financial resources.

**Business-registration use case.** The mission presented findings of the legal and ICT diagnostic of the Guiché Único de Empresas (GUE, business one-stop shop), toward turning the GUE into a modern, data-oriented business registry; DGRN and INIC raised the need for offline access given electricity and internet limitations.

Next in-person mission proposed for March 2026, with a dedicated digital-ID registration mission before then.
