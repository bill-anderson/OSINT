# European Union (CEF Digital) — ViaTunisia Bizerte–Marseille submarine cable, 2023

The European Commission's Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) Digital programme funds €9.6 million of the €32 million ViaTunisia project — a direct Bizerte–Marseille submarine cable — coordinated by Orange Wholesale.

## Description

"The ViaTunisia project has a total budget of €32 million of which €9.6 million funded under the Connecting Europe Facility (CEF) Digital programme." The project deploys "a new, direct submarine route between France and Tunisia" (Marseille–Bizerte, 1,050 km, 20 Tb per fibre pair), running 1 March 2023 – 31 August 2025, "undertaken by Orange Wholesale, as coordinator", following the open-access cable model.
