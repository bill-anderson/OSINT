An analyst op-ed (Dr Dimakatso Polokelo) argues **digital public infrastructure is a strategic imperative** for Botswana and African economies — open, interoperable systems (foundational **digital identity**, **interoperable payments**, **data-exchange layers** and **digital authentication protocols**) as the structural backbone for digital services across financial inclusion, e-governance, agriculture, education, health and intra-African trade. Cited as analysis of Botswana's DPI agenda, not primary evidence.
