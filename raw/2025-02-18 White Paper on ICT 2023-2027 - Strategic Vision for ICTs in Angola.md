On **5 December 2024**, Angola approved the **White Paper on ICT 2023-2027** (*Livro Branco das Tecnologias de Informação e Comunicação*, "LBTIC 2023-2027"), styled "the path to digital acceleration and transformation in Angola." It repeals the 2019-2022 White Paper (approved by Presidential Order 129/19).

The LBTIC 2023-2027 defines the ICT sector's political and strategic measures, nested within the Long-Term Strategy (**ELP Angola 2050**), the **National Development Plan 2023-2027**, the AU's **Agenda 2063**, the ITU **Connect 2030** agenda and **SADC Digital 2027**. The Ministry of Telecommunications, Information Technology and Social Communication (**MINTTICS**) ran a public consultation in March 2024. It targets five objectives by 2027 — beginning with faster, more reliable and more affordable communications, and strategic investment in ICT infrastructure and services.
