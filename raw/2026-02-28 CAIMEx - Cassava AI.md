# CAIMEx

## Multi‑Model LLM Exchange for Telcos

Public and private sectors across Africa want to adopt generative AI to transform customer experience, network operations, and enterprise productivity – but face integration complexity, vendor lock‑in risk, and the high cost of integrating proprietary LLM platforms.

![CAIMEx](https://www.cassava.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CAIMEx.jpg)

## AI Multi‑Model Exchange ·  
Unified API · Regional Infrastructure

CAIMEx addresses these challenges by providing a single gateway to access, govern, and scale multiple AI models from one unified API – all running on regional infrastructure designed for low‑latency performance and in‑region data handling where required.

Instead of relying everything on one model, CAIMEx lets telcos run the right AI for each use case – customer care, network operations, revenue operations, or internal productivity – and switch models as needs evolve, without re‑engineering integrations or sacrificing control.

![](https://www.cassava.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/CAIMEx-Diagram-2048x711.jpg)

## The four pillars of CAIMEx

One API. Many models. Fully‑managed LLMOps – aligned to your data, risk, and performance requirements.

### Multi‑Model LLM Exchange

CAIMEx aggregates leading global large language models behind a single exchange, eliminating the need to integrate each model and provider separately.

### Unified Access – Single‑API for All Models

CAIMEx provides one API surface across all participating models, so your applications and AI agents don't need to change when you switch or add models.

### Multi‑Model Agility – Choose and Switch as Your Purpose Changes

CAIMEx lets you route different workloads to different models based on use case, cost, performance, data sensitivity, or geography – and change those decisions dynamically without re‑architecting.

### Fully‑Managed – End‑to‑End LLM Ops Lifecycle

CAIMEx handles the infrastructure, routing, observability, safety controls, and cost monitoring that would otherwise require dedicated engineering teams and custom "glue code."

CAIMEx runs on Cassava Intelligence’s GPU‑accelerated infrastructure and AI factories across Africa and the Middle East, bringing AI compute close to your subscribers and supporting in‑region data handling aligned with local regulatory requirements.

### Ultra low-latency

Run AI where your customers are – reducing round‑trip delays and improving the responsiveness of customer‑facing and network automation use cases.

### Data localisation support

Keep AI workloads on regional platforms to address data residency, sovereignty, and compliance objectives without the complexity of building your own in‑country infrastructure.

### Telco‑grade resilience

Regional redundancy, high availability, and carrier‑class uptime designed for production‑scale deployments, not pilot projects.

## Carrier‑grade platform,  
not point solutions

CAIMEx acts as a **shared, carrier****‑****grade LLM platform** in your network and IT architecture – unified APIs, regional hosting, and managed operations instead of fragmented, project‑by‑project stacks that create technical debt and security risk.

Telcos reduce the integration surface area across your organisation, improve your security posture with centralised access controls, and maintain tighter control over performance and cost – while still giving product and innovation teams the flexibility to experiment with multiple models and providers.

| **Pillar** | **What CAIMEx provides** | **Business value for your telco** |
|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Multi‑Model LLM Exchange | One exchange for multiple global and regional LLMs | Avoid vendor lock‑in; optimise model choice by use case and cost |
| Unified Access | Single, OpenAI‑compatible API across all models | 70–90% faster time‑to‑market for AI use cases; fewer integration points |
| Multi‑Model Agility | Policy‑driven routing, evaluation, and switching | Optimise for quality, latency, cost, and compliance without re‑engineering |
| Fully‑Managed LLM Ops | Managed routing, observability, safety, and cost controls | Reduce operational burden; focus teams on revenue‑generating use cases |
| Regional Infrastructure | AI hosted on African/ME infrastructure with ultra-low latency | Better customer experience; support for data sovereignty and compliance |
