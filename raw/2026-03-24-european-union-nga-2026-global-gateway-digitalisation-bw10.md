# European Union — Nigeria digitalisation (Global Gateway), 2026

The European Union announced an additional EUR 131 million for digitalisation in Nigeria — part of a EUR 290 million Global Gateway package — to roll out 90,000 km of fibre (with the EBRD), build interoperable digital public infrastructure and data-exchange systems, and support digital skills training.

## Description

"The European Union (EU), on Monday, announced a new investment package of EUR 290 million to be immediately mobilised in seven new operations in digitalisation, health and agricultural value chains... in the framework of the EU Global Gateway strategy." "The package of investments includes an additional EUR 131 million in the digitalisation sector, which will support the upgrade of digital connectivity and enhance digital services and skills." "Together with the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, the EU will contribute to the roll-out of 90,000 km of fiber optic cable across the country." "The EU will help develop secure and interoperable digital public infrastructure and data exchange systems for digital services to citizens and in the framework of Nigeria's 3 million Technical Talent programme."
