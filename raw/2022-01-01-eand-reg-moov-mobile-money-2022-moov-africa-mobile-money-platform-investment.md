## Description

Maroc Telecom has invested in mobile money services through its Moov Africa brand across SSA. Mobile Money revenues grew 14.4% in 2024. The IFC EUR 375 million loan (2023) to Moov Africa subsidiaries in 8 SSA countries includes funding for mobile money and digital financial services alongside network capex. Countries: Benin, Burkina Faso, Chad, Côte d'Ivoire, Gabon, Mali, Niger, Togo.
