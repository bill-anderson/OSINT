# Innovations in Electoral Technology: 2015–2025 (INEC, Jul 2025)

Official INEC handbook documenting a decade of electoral-technology adoption in Nigeria
(biometric voter register, BVAS, IReV, e-Reporter apps, etc.). Companion source page for the
PDF artefact. Primary institutional publication — a reference on Nigeria's election-tech stack
and the personal-data register INEC holds. See [[inec]], [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.govtech]].
