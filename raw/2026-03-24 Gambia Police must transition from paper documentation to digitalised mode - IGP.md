The Inspector General of Police, Seedy Mukhtar Touray, said the Gambia Police Force needs to transition from paper-based documentation to a **digitalised mode**, which he described as faster and cheaper. Speaking at his quarterly media briefing at Police Headquarters in Banjul, he noted that digitalisation of police workflow and records management will bring more effectiveness and efficiency. He also highlighted the need for a purpose-built training academy and persistent challenges with mobility for police operations.
