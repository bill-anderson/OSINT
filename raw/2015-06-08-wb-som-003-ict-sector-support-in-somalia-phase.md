## Description

Supports Somalia's ICT sector by contributing to establishment of an enabling policy, regulatory, and institutional environment for telecom and digital infrastructure. Phase II of a trust-funded series providing technical assistance to strengthen ICT regulation, universal access, and digital connectivity in Somalia.
