# World Bank (PATNUC) — ANTIC national CIRT cybersecurity systems, Cameroon, 2026

Cybersecurity systems valued at over FCFA 735 million, acquired under the World Bank-funded PATNUC project, were handed over to Cameroon's ANTIC national CIRT on 20 January 2026.

## Description

"The new equipment was acquired under the World Bank-funded Project for the Acceleration of Digital Transformation of Cameroon (PATNUC) and was officially handed over to ANTIC on January 20, 2026, at a ceremony in Yaoundé". "The upgraded technology includes servers, storage consoles, investigation platforms, workstations, and vulnerability scanning and penetration testing tools." "Valued at over FCFA 735 million, the systems are expected to enhance CIRT's operational capacity." PATNUC is led by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications (MINPOSTEL) with World Bank support.
