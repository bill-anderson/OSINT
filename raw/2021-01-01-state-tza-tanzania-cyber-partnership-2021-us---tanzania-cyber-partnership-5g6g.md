## Description

The Tanzania ICT partnership aligns with USG goals to strengthen international partnerships in improving digital connectivity, promoting stable and secure cyberspace, and upholding digital democracy and freedoms. Implemented by DAI, the program supports 5G/6G policy frameworks and cybersecurity capacity building for Tanzania.
