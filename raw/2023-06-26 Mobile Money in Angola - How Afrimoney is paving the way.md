Africell Angola (market entry 2022) launched its mobile money service **Afrimoney** in April 2023. Angola is characterised by a high share of unbanked individuals (~50%) and a large informal sector; while banks are well established, digital financial services had yet to make a substantial impact on inclusion. Afrimoney aims to bridge formal and informal economies.

Per Simon Andersson-Manjang, Acting Group Co-Head of Afrimoney: after entering a market previously dominated by a single state-owned player, Africell captured ~25% market share within eight months, and launched Afrimoney after 12 months on the ground. Interview by GSMA Mobile for Humanitarian Innovation.
