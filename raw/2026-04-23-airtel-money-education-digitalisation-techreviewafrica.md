# Airtel Money supports digitalisation of education sector in Republic of Congo

Date: 2026-04-23. By: Kanto Kai Okanta.

*Sweep note — PR-wire: Tech Review Africa serves no date to search; the body carries `Date: 2026-04-23` and a named byline. The item reads as Airtel Congo / YABISOSOFT promotional copy. The **primary worth sourcing at ingest** is the E-SCHOOL ACADEMY (4th edition) organiser announcement or Airtel Congo B's own release.*

More than 200 school principals in Brazzaville convened at the Lycée de la Révolution, for the fourth edition of the E-SCHOOL ACADEMY, an initiative aimed at advancing digital transformation in the education sector.

The session, organised by YABISOSOFT, showcased innovative tools designed to modernise school administration, including a digital school management platform that integrates fee payment through Airtel Money.

The solution enables schools to streamline operations while providing parents and guardians with a simple, secure, and accessible method for paying school fees digitally.

Through its partnership with YABISOSOFT, Airtel Congo B is supporting efforts to enhance efficiency and transparency in the education system, while promoting financial inclusion across the Republic of the Congo.

The initiative underscores a broader commitment to leveraging digital innovation to improve service delivery and drive sustainable development within the country's education sector.
