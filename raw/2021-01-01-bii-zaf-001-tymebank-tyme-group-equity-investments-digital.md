## Description

Series of equity investments in TymeBank (Tyme Group Holdings PTE), South Africa's leading digital bank focused on the underbanked. TymeBank integrates physical and digital distribution via kiosks at Pick n Pay and Boxer supermarkets. BII rounds: $18.5M equity (Nov 2021, directly in TymeBank Ltd — subsequently restructured/exited as separate entity), $18.8M equity in Tyme Group Holdings (Sep 2022), $5M equity follow-on (Nov 2022), $2.5M equity (Apr 2023), $1.1M equity (Jul 2024). TymeBank also operates GoTyme in Philippines.
