## Description

Equity LP commitment to TLcom Capital's TIDE Africa Fund II, a USD 154 million venture capital fund dedicated to technology-enabled start-ups in Sub-Saharan Africa. Fund II targets early-stage companies leveraging digital technologies across fintech, agritech, edtech, healthtech. Proparco participated as LP investor alongside BII, IFC, BIO, DEG, Allianz.
