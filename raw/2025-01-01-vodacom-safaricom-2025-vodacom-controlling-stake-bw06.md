# Vodacom Group — Safaricom controlling stake, 2025

Vodacom Group announced a US$2.1 billion deal to raise its Safaricom stake from 35% to 55%, buying 15% from the Kenyan government and 5% from Vodafone.

## Description

"Pan-African telecoms operator Vodacom Group has announced a $2.1 billion deal that will see it increase its stake in Kenya's Safaricom to 55%. Under the terms of the deal, Vodacom has entered into an agreement with the Government of Kenya to acquire 15% of Safaricom PLC and an additional 5% from Vodafone at 34 Kenyan shillings (US$0.26) per share, valuing the deal at $2.1 billion." The transaction requires regulatory and governmental approvals in Kenya, Ethiopia and South Africa; Kenya retains a 20% stake and board representation.
