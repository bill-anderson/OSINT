## Description

Maroc Telecom's Malian subsidiary Sotelma (operating under Moov Africa Malitel brand) paid XOF 160 billion ($256 million) to renew its telecoms licence in Mali in 2024. This was the single largest component of the MAD 3.549 billion in licence/frequency costs incurred by Moov Africa subsidiaries in 2024. Note: In October 2024, the Mali government subsequently acquired majority control of Sotelma, reportedly taking a 51%+ stake.
