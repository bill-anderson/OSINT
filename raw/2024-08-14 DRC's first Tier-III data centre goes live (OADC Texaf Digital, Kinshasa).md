The DRC's **first Tier-III certified**, open-access, carrier-neutral data centre went live in Kinshasa — **"OADC Texaf Digital,"** a joint venture between **Open Access Data Centres (OADC)**, a WIOCC company, and **TEXAF**. The **2MW**-capable facility is the country's first live Uptime Institute Tier-III certified DC, with ISO 27001 certification targeted for Q3 2024. (One of two Tier-III Kinshasa data centres launched August 2024; see also Raxio DRC1.) See [[oadc-texaf-digital]].
