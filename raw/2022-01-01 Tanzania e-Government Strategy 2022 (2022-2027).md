# Tanzania e-Government Strategy 2022 (covering 2022-2027)

Companion source page for the artefact `2022-01-01 Tanzania e-Government Strategy 2022 (2022-2027).pdf`, held in `raw/`.

TANZANIA's - not to be confused with the Uganda e-GIF and Digital Government Strategy in the same batch. The SECOND national e-Government Strategy, to be implemented 2022-2027, replacing the e-Government Strategy 2013 (which ran 2013-2018 and expired). Seven strategic pillars. Predecessor achievements cited: the e-Government Act 2019 and its 2020 Regulations; the e-Government Agency became a fully-fledged e-Government Authority in 2019. Named problems: non-interoperable service delivery systems, unharmonised business processes, limited GovNet bandwidth, inadequate data centre resources, rising cyber-attacks, ICT skills gaps. Built on a stakeholder survey of 379 respondents across 38 institutions.
