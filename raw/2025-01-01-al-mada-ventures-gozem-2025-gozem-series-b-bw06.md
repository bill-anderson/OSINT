# Al Mada Ventures / MSC — Gozem Series B, 2025

Pan-African super app Gozem raised a US$30 million Series B (equity and debt) from investors including SAS Shipping Agencies Services (MSC Group) and Al Mada Ventures, funding its growth and Congo-Brazzaville launch.

## Description

"Gozem is well-resourced to fund its growth, strengthen its vehicle fleet, and activate new financial and logistics services, thanks to a $30 million Series B funding round, combining equity and debt, orchestrated by investors including SAS Shipping Agencies Services Sàrl (a subsidiary of the MSC Group) and Al Mada Ventures." The round funds a super-app model spanning mobility, delivery, mobile payment and fintech services.
