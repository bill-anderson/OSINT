The SADC **Transactions Cleared on an Immediate Basis (TCIB)** cross-border payments scheme, operated by **PayInc**, activated payments into Botswana through **First Capital Bank (FCB) Botswana** — extending real-time SADC cross-border and remittance rails to Botswana, tying the country into the region's instant-payments layer.
