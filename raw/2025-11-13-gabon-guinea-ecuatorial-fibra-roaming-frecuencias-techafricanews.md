Gabon and Equatorial Guinea signed **three landmark bilateral digital agreements on 10 November 2025 in Mongomo**, concluded between the two countries' digital-economy ministers. The accords cover: (1) **interconnection of fibre-optic networks** to improve internet connectivity and stability; (2) **free roaming**, letting citizens of both countries communicate at no extra cost when travelling across borders; and (3) **coordination of radio-electric frequencies** to eliminate cross-border interference.

The signing followed technical collaboration among Gabon's ARCEP, SPIN, ANINF and operators Moov Africa Gabon Telecom and Airtel Gabon, with their Equatoguinean counterparts. Framed by Presidents Brice Clotaire Oligui Nguema (Gabon) and Obiang Nguema Mbasogo (GNQ) as advancing digital sovereignty and regional integration across Central Africa.
