# YCell — Gamcel majority-stake privatisation, 2025

The Gambian government is set to sell 80% of state operator Gamcel to YCell for D6.7 billion, to revive and modernise its network infrastructure.

## Description

"The Minister of Communication and Digital Economy Lamin Jabbi disclosed to lawmakers ... that government is set to sell 80 percent of its shares in Gamcel to a company called YCell for D6.7 billion. ... Y Cell emerged as the most responsive bidder with an investment proposal of D6.7 billion. ... a directive was issued for his Ministry to engage Y Cell for final negotiations with the green light on contract signing to be subject to further cabinet approval." "The first direct payment we expect for the 80 percent of the shares may go close to or above D800 million and the remaining D6 billion will be invested into the infrastructure."
