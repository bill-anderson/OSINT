# Absa (syndicated) — Teraco JB7 data centre, 2024

Teraco secured a ZAR 8 billion syndicated loan through Absa and other financial institutions to finance its 40 MW JB7 hyperscale data centre and renewable-energy programme.

## Description

Teraco has started building JB7, a 40 MW hyperscale data centre (71,000 m², eight data halls) at its Isando Campus in Ekurhuleni, "funded by a new R8-billion syndicated loan… secured through Absa and other financial institutions to finance JB7 and other construction projects." Part of the loan augments Teraco's renewable-energy programme, including a 120 MW utility-scale solar PV plant wheeling power across the Eskom network.
