## Description

Google's non-dilutive Black Founders Fund Africa provided equity-free cash grants to Black-led African startups. Cohort 1 (2021): $3 million to 50 startups ($50K-$100K each). Cohort 2 (2022): $4 million to 60 startups. Subsequent cohorts add further tranches. Total $7+ million disbursed to 110 African startups by end of 2023, as reported by Google in December 2023. Each recipient also receives $200K in Google Cloud Credits.
