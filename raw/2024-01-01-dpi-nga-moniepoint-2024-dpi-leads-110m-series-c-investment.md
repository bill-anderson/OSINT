## Description

Development Partners International (DPI), via its African Development Partners III (ADP III) fund, leads a USD 110 million Series C round in Moniepoint Group, Nigeria's leading business banking, payments and credit platform. Other investors include Google's Africa Investment Fund, Verod Capital Management, and Lightrock. Round makes Moniepoint a unicorn at a USD 1 billion+ valuation. Moniepoint processed 5.2 billion transactions in 2023 and provides payment terminals, digital banking, and credit services to Nigerian SMEs and individuals.
