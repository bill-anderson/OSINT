# CEIEC — Angola civil & criminal identification platform, 2017

CEIEC was awarded a US$243m contract to supply and support Angola's Integrated Civil and Criminal Identification Management Platform, financed on a Chinese commercial credit line.

## Description

A presidential *despacho* signed by José Eduardo dos Santos selected CEIEC for a contract of "fornecimento de bens, instalações e suporte técnico, no valor de 243 milhões de dólares (228 milhões de euros)" to implement the *Plataforma de Gestão Integrada da Identificação Civil e Criminal*, covering birth/death registration, ID-card issuance and the criminal register. The *despacho*, dated January, authorised the Ministry of Finance to frame the contract within "uma linha de crédito comercial a negociar junto de uma instituição financeira da China."
