## Description

ITU coordination and technical contribution to the Giga initiative a joint ITU-UNICEF school connectivity programme. ITU project budget covers global coordination structure mapping methodology technical standards and policy engagement. SSA countries in Phase 1: Benin Kenya Niger Rwanda Sierra Leone Togo Zimbabwe. ITU role: country engagement regulatory support and co-management of school connectivity mapping tool. UNICEF leads financial structuring and the Giga bond (recorded separately under UNICEF). Co-donors: FCDO Switzerland Spain.
