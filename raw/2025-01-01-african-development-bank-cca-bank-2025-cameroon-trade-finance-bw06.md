# AfDB — CCA-Bank Cameroon trade finance facility, 2025

The AfDB approved a US$29 million trade finance facility for Cameroon's CCA-Bank, with a portion earmarked to import telecom equipment.

## Description

"The African Development Bank Group (AfDB) approved a $29 million trade finance facility for Cameroon's Crédit Communautaire d'Afrique-Bank (CCA-Bank) to boost the economy, with a portion of the funds earmarked for the country's telecom industry ... approved during a board meeting on December 1 in Abidjan ... used as a transaction guarantee ... The investment particularly earmarks a portion for the purchase of crucial telecom equipment, addressing an essential demand for infrastructure upgrades." It is the AfDB's first direct financing support to CCA-Bank.

## Notes

The US$29m is a general trade finance facility (transaction guarantee) supporting equipment imports across the industrial, agro-industrial and telecommunications sectors — only an unspecified portion is for telecom equipment, so the full amount is not exclusively a digital-transformation commitment. Subject `infra.connect`/`tech.industry` reflects the source's telecom framing. Board-approved 1 Dec 2025 in Abidjan.
