# AXIAN Telecom — Partner2Connect 4G pledge (Madagascar, Comoros, Tanzania), 2025

AXIAN Telecom pledged US$82 million under the ITU Partner2Connect coalition to expand 4G across Madagascar, Comoros and Tanzania, targeting 97% population coverage and 6 million newly connected people by 2030.

## Description

"Axian Telecom commits $82M to expand 4G in Madagascar, Comoros, and Tanzania, aiming for 97% coverage and connecting 6 million people. … This is a part of Axian Telecom's Partner2Connect Pledge.
