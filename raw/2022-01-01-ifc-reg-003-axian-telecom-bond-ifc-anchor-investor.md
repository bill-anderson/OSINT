## Description

IFC acted as anchor investor in Axian Telecom's debut bond issuance, committing $60 million from its own account. Axian Telecom is a pan-African telecommunications and digital infrastructure company operating in Madagascar, Tanzania, Togo, Senegal, Comoros, and Réunion. The bond — total size approximately €430 million — finances network expansion, spectrum acquisition, and mobile money infrastructure across Axian's SSA markets. IFC's anchor participation was intended to attract market investors at a time of financial market volatility. BII and other DFIs co-participated.
