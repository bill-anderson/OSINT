# Egypt financial inclusion reaches 77.6% (CBE, end-2025)

The **[[central-bank-of-egypt|CBE]]** reported **financial inclusion at 77.6%** of Egyptians aged 15+ by end-2025 (up from 76.3% in June 2025) — **54.7 million of 70.5 million**, counting bank accounts, Egypt Post accounts, mobile wallets and prepaid cards. **Women 71.4%**, **youth (15–35) 56.8%**. Between 2016 and 2025 the overall rate grew 181% (women +316%). The CBE is developing a **Second Financial Inclusion Strategy 2026–2030** (with CAPMAS, World Bank, IFC). Mobile-wallet transaction value rose 80% YoY in Q2 2025; total value +72% to EGP 943bn. Part of the transition to a cashless society under **Vision 2030**; the CBE recently launched SoftPOS contactless payments.
