# Hormuud Telecom plans to build more 'green' data centres

> ## Excerpt
> Somali operator Hormuud Telecom plans to build more green data centres for the
> AI economy, per CEO Ahmed Mohamud Yusuf (via Bloomberg). Hormuud operates eleven
> data centres with a combined 10 MWh of capacity.

---

Somali operator Hormuud Telecom plans to build more "green" data centres to support the growing demands of the local and global AI economy, Bloomberg reported, citing CEO Ahmed Mohamud Yusuf. He said the company operates in a complex environment with security concerns and infrastructure gaps that can make progress slower than it would like, but that Somalia is brimming with opportunity.

Hormuud has eleven data centres with a combined 10 megawatt-hours of capacity, and as much as 95 percent [renewable/green — figure truncated in source].
