# Enhancing healthcare performance in Ethiopia using DHIS2

Ethiopia's **[[dhis2|DHIS2]]**-based national HMIS is **one of the largest in the world** — data from **30,000+ health facilities** for a population of 120m+. The **[[ethiopia-moh|Ministry of Health]]** began full national DHIS2 implementation in 2017, trained 7,000+ workers, and reached all 30,000+ public facilities by 2024; a 2024 PLOS One study found DHIS2 significantly improved data quality and accessibility. At the **2025 Global DPI Summit (Cape Town)**, MoH reported **1 billion+ records** in the national DHIS2 system.
