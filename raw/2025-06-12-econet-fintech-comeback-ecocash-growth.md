Note (ISSUE-009): this concerns Econet Wireless Zimbabwe (the opco) and its EcoCash fintech arm; keep distinct from the wider Econet Group.

Econet Wireless Zimbabwe is reaping the rewards of re-integrating its fintech operations. For the year ending February, the group posted gains in both digital and voice services: a 36% surge in data usage and a 23% increase in voice traffic, credited to consistent network investment.

The fintech rebound drew most attention. The decision to bring back EcoCash and related fintech subsidiaries (originally spun off in 2018) proved a "masterstroke": EcoCash transaction volumes grew 21%, while transaction value rose 210%, driven by increased activity in customer wallets and user funding. "This strategic transformation has yielded the intended benefits," Econet said, adding its focus remains on "further consolidation and optimisation." The company has begun expanding its payment partner network to sustain momentum. The move to reunify tech and financial-services platforms signals a bold approach to resilience in a challenging economic environment.
