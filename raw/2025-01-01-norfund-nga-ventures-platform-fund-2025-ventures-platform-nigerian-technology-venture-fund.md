## Description

Norfund USD 6 million fund investment in Ventures Platform, a Nigeria-focused venture capital fund investing in tech startups that use digital tools to make services more accessible to ordinary people in West Africa. Investments span fintech, e-commerce, logistics, and enterprise SaaS. Portfolio includes OmniRetail (also directly invested by Norfund).
