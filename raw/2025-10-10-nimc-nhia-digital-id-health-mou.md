NIMC signed an MoU with the National Health Insurance Authority (NHIA) to integrate digital identity systems into national health infrastructure. The partnership aims to create a unified framework linking citizens' national identity data with health insurance records, to streamline verification, enhance transparency and improve management of health services — eliminating redundancies and improving access to public services.
