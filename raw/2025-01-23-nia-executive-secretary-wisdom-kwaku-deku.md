# Wisdom Kwaku Deku - National Identification Authority

Digital Address (Head Office): GA – 237 – 1033

Phone: 0302 999 306

**Mr. Wisdom Kwaku Deku**

**Executive Secretary**

Wisdom Kwaku Deku is an accomplished IT leader with a wealth of experience in cybersecurity, systems
analysis, and IT strategy spanning over 15 years. Having served as a Head of Systems Administration at
the National Identification Authority, he played a vital role in spearheading the implementation of
National Identity System (NIS) guaranteeing the integrity and dependability of the national
identification infrastructure.

Dedicated to harnessing technology for process optimization, increased operational efficiency, and
robust security measures, Mr. Deku is driven by a commitment to aligning IT strategies with
organizational objectives to drive growth, innovation, and substantial societal impact.

He holds an MSc in Cyber Security from the University of Liverpool, UK, and boasts certifications such
as Certified Ethical Hacker from ECCouncil, UK, Graduate Certificate in IT Management from the Robert
Gordon University, UK, Oracle Database Administration from IPMC, International Diploma in Computer
Studies from NCC, UK, and Networking and Systems Administration from IPMC, among various other
prestigious credentials in IT and Cybersecurity.

With his vast experience in managing national IT infrastructures and a deep understanding of the
National Identification Authority's operations, Mr. Deku is uniquely positioned to lead the
organization as its Executive Secretary, ensuring it achieves its mission of delivering secure and
efficient identification services for the nation.
