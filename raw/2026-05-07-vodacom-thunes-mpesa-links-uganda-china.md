Vodacom Tanzania and cross-border payments company Thunes have partnered to allow the telco's clients to pay merchants in Uganda and China directly from their mobile phones, enabling real-time payments and reinforcing mobile money as an alternative to traditional international banking. Using Thunes' direct global network, Vodacom users can send payments to merchants in Uganda using MTN MoMo, and to Chinese merchants via Alipay, through the M-Pesa USSD interface or Super App.

Thunes cited growing demand from Tanzanian traders engaged in commerce with Ugandan and Chinese markets. Bilateral trade between Tanzania and Uganda reached approximately $2.23 billion in 2024, up 64% year-on-year. The integration simplifies payment workflows while maintaining security and traceability, targeting consumers and MSMEs.
