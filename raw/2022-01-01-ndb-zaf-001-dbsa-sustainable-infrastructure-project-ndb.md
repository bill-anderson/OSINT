## Description

NDB non-sovereign loan of $100M to the Development Bank of Southern Africa (DBSA) for on-lending to private sector sub-borrowers for sustainable infrastructure projects in South Africa. Covers multiple sectors: renewable energy, social infrastructure (affordable housing, student accommodation, healthcare), and digital infrastructure. Digital component includes extended broadband networks and efficient telecommunications infrastructure across South Africa. NDB finances up to 80% of each sub-project cost. Agreement signed October 13, 2023.
