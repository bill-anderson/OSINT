## Description

FMO debt facility to M-KOPA Kenya via AEF-I and MASSIF funds (USD 5.5M each). M-KOPA is a connected asset financing platform offering solar home systems, smartphones, and digital financial services on pay-as-you-go basis to underbanked customers in Kenya, Uganda, and Nigeria.
