## Description

DEG acquires a EUR 15 million stake in Partech Africa Fund II (PAF II), the largest African VC fund focusing on technology companies including HealthTech, FinTech, and RetailTech. The fund concentrates investments in fast-growing technology companies across Africa, including Nigeria, Egypt, South Africa, Kenya, and Senegal. DEG supports access to long-term financing for young African technology companies. The investment adds Partech as the first African fund in DEG's global tech fund portfolio.
