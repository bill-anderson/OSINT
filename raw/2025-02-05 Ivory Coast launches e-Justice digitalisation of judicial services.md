Côte d'Ivoire launched the **e-Justice platform** (**30 January 2025**) to simplify access to judicial services — initially allowing citizens of Yopougon and Dabou to submit **nationality-certificate applications online** (tied to **[[oneci]]** identity verification via CEV/VERIF). An early e-government/justice digitalisation step, preceding the June 2025 decree formally digitising nationality certificates.
