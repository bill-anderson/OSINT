BANGE (Banco Nacional de Guinea Ecuatorial) launched **BANGE MOBILE GE**, a mobile banking app (free on iOS/Android) for balance checks, transfers, payments, withdrawals and other operations. Directors: Expedito Rafael (Commercial Director) and José Valentín Abeso Eyama (Head of Electronic & Digital Banking). During the trial phase users can transfer up to 2 million FCFA/month, withdraw cash without a card, and pay utility bills. Framed as advancing financial inclusion (access for the unbanked). The app is designed to **integrate with regional payment systems, enabling CEMAC operations via the G-MAT Pay gateway**, and to be compatible with Visa prepaid cards.
