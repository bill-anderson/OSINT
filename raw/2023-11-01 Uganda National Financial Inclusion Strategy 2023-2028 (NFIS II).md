# Uganda National Financial Inclusion Strategy 2023-2028 (NFIS II)

Companion source page for the artefact `2023-11-01 Uganda National Financial Inclusion Strategy 2023-2028 (NFIS II).pdf`, held in `raw/`.

UGANDA's, despite arriving amid Tanzanian files - the cover is explicit. Uganda's SECOND National Financial Inclusion Strategy, succeeding NFIS I. Developed with FSD Uganda support; foreword signed by the Deputy Governor, Bank of Uganda. Priority groups widened over NFIS I to add refugees and persons with disabilities alongside women, youth, rural dwellers and MSMEs. Governed by the Inter-Institutional Committee on Financial Inclusion; aligned to Vision 2040, NDP III and the Parish Development Model.
