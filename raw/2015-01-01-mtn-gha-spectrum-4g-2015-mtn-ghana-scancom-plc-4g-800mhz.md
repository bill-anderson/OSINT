## Description

MTN Ghana (operating as Scancom PLC) was the sole winner of Ghana's National Communications Authority (NCA) 800MHz spectrum auction in December 2015, acquiring 2x10MHz of 800MHz LTE spectrum for $67.5 million. This was Ghana's first 4G spectrum auction and MTN paid the minimum reserve price, as the three other potential bidders withdrew. The licence enabled MTN Ghana to become Ghana's first 4G operator, launching commercial 4G LTE services in early 2016. MTN deployed additional $18M in site infrastructure for the launch. As of 2021, MTN Ghana had 3.4 million active 4G subscribers and a 82% 4G market share.
