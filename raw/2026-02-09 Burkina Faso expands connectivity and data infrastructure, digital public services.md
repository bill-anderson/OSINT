Burkina Faso's Minister of Digital Transition, Dr Aminata Zerbo Sabané, reported the ministry hit a **91% performance target for 2025**: connectivity (telephone and internet) extended to **370 additional localities**; **two new modular data centres** built (for digital sovereignty); **69 new digital platforms** developed for public services; and a partnership with the national postal service to build **20 'citizen houses'** over five years — physical assisted-access points to help citizens use digital government services and bridge the divide.
