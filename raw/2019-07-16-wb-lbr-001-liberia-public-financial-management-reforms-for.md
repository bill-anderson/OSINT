## Description

Improves domestic revenue mobilization systems and strengthens financial control and accountability in public finances of Liberia. Supports IFMIS expansion and upgrade, revenue administration systems, and PFM institutional capacity building. Includes customs management information system enhancements and integrated tax administration system components.
