## Description

MIGA political risk guarantee of $13.5 million to Escotel Mauritius covering its investment in Escotel (SL) Limited, which installs and operates solar-hybrid power systems at telecom tower sites (TNS) in Sierra Leone. Escotel installs solar PV panels replacing diesel generators at ~600 telecom network sites owned/operated by Orange Sierra Leone. Reduces diesel fuel use by 75% and avoids 58,000 tCO2e over the guarantee period. Risks: Transfer Restriction, Expropriation, War and Civil Disturbance for up to 10 years.
