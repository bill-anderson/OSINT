Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 23 June 2026. Ethiopia's Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS) integrated the Fayda national digital ID with the passport application system - passport applications will henceforth be processed on biometric data held by the National Identity Authority (NIDP), per ICS Director General Selamawit Dawit. Part of the government's "utility-first" strategy to drive Fayda adoption by linking it to a growing set of public/private services; also aims to cut passport-issuance delays and move applications fully online.
