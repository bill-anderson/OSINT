## Description

EUR 8 million equity investment in Africa Mobile Network (AMN), a pan-SSA telecom company deploying towers in rural areas. Financing supports deployment of 5,000 new towers by 2022 and expansion to 10,000 towers across 19+ African countries by 2025, bridging the digital divide in rural SSA. Total consortium USD 40 million (Proparco, CDC, DEG, Metier PE).
