*Current state on the delivery date: Q1 2026, superseding the end-2025 target of 2025-08-11. Worth noting the rhetorical tell — this piece describes the rebased schedule as 'on schedule' without acknowledging the earlier commitment.*

The Centenary Group Board of Directors visited the Tier 3 Data Centre project to assess progress and reaffirm commitment to timely delivery, aiming to keep the project on schedule for its official launch in the first quarter of 2026. The Board expressed satisfaction with progress made, noting the project is on schedule and aligned with Centenary Group's vision of building sustainable, future-ready infrastructure.

As Uganda's first Green Tier 3 Data Centre — designed with energy efficiency and environmental sustainability at its core — it will serve as a cornerstone for secure digital transformation across public and private sectors, extending its impact beyond Uganda to the wider East African region. By hosting critical government systems alongside enterprise and financial services, the data centre will strengthen trust, enable innovation and guarantee reliability at scale.

"This project is a foundation for Uganda's digital future. As a green data centre, it symbolizes our commitment to sustainable progress and responsible stewardship," said Prof. John Ddumba Ssentamu, Centenary Group's Chairman Board of Directors.

Mr. Kimanthi Mutua, Centenary Group Director, noted the project represents the belief that Africa's digital transformation must be built on secure, sustainable and future-proof infrastructure. "What we are building here will serve generations," he said.

Dr. Grace Ssekakubo, Centenary Technology Services' Chief Executive, said: "Our customers, partners and communities deserve world-class digital infrastructure. What we saw today confirms that this facility will deliver exactly that — security, reliability and sustainability."

Peter Kahiigi, Chief Technology Officer at Centenary Technology Services, observed: "This data centre embodies resilience, innovation and environmental consciousness. We are building a platform that will enable digital services at scale while reducing the carbon footprint."
