## Description

Norrsken22 makes a follow-on investment in Sabi's $38M Series B round, valuing the Nigerian B2B e-commerce startup at $300M. Sabi provides digital commerce infrastructure to Africa's informal economy, enabling merchants and distributors to access inventory, credit and logistics via mobile platforms. Norrsken22 is a returning investor from Sabi's earlier $20M 2022 venture round. Co-investors include CommerzVentures, Fluent Ventures, Proof VC, CRE Venture Capital and Janngo Capital.
