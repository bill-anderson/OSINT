## Description

Finnfund invested EUR 2 million in Fibertime Group, a South African company providing affordable high-speed fibre internet to underserved township communities. The investment supports Fibertime's goal to connect up to 1.8 million low-income households in South African townships through its fibre and VulaCoin subsidiaries.
