# Eritrea's population digital identity — challenges ahead

Analytical profile of **Eritrea's digital-identity prospects**, one of the least digitally-developed states in the region. Flags the structural challenges to any foundational digital-ID rollout: very low connectivity and mobile penetration, limited digital infrastructure, a state-controlled telecom monopoly (**EriTel**), and weak civil-registration foundations. Reference/analytical baseline — Eritrea has no operational foundational digital ID; the piece sets out what a future system would have to overcome.
