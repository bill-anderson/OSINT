## Description

Swedfund invested USD 10 million in equity in Moniepoint, described as Africa's fastest growing fintech company, to accelerate access to banking and digital payment solutions for Nigeria's underbanked MSMEs. Moniepoint has over four million business users and provides tailored financial products and services delivered via an extensive local distribution network. The investment is alongside DPI, IFC and Proparco.
