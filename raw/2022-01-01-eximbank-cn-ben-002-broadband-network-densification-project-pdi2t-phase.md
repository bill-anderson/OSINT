## Description

Concessional loan of RMB 265 million (~$39M) from China to Benin for Phase 2 of the Telecommunications and ICT Infrastructure Development Project (PDI2T). Loan agreement signed September 14, 2021. Aims to densify Benin's national high-speed broadband network, extending the 2,000 km fiber optic backbone deployed in Phase 1 (2016-2021). Extends fiber to additional municipalities and improves network quality.
