Minister Dr Bosun Tijani announced FG plans to deploy $2 billion fibre optic infrastructure nationwide (Project Bridge) from Q4 2025, covering 90,000 kilometres. The digital sector attracted $191 million FDI in Q1 2024 (up from $22 million Q1 2023). The 3MTT programme (launched October 2023 to train 3 million Nigerians) has trained 117,000 with 35,000 more in training. Nigeria ranks among the world's top 60 for AI readiness and is developing a homegrown LLM (AI Collective Platform supported by Google, Microsoft, Pierre Omidyar). 7,000 telecom towers would be installed aiming for 98% nationwide coverage (FEC approved). 12 states have adopted zero-rated Right-of-Way policies. Nigeria Startup House in San Francisco targets $5 billion in startup funding.
