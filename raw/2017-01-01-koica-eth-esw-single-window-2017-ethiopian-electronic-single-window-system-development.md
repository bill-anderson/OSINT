## Description

KOICA grant ($7.49M, 2017-2023) to develop Ethiopia's electronic single window customs platform. Implemented by Ethiopian Customs Commission (ECC) and Ministry of Revenue. Connects 40+ government agencies for paperless import/export processing. Awarded WHO certificate of merit. Reduced clearance time target from 44 to 13 days.
