Cameroon's **National Civil Status Registration Office ([[bunec]]**) partnered Luxembourg-based **[[liber-technologies]]** (Oct 2025) to accelerate CRVS digital transformation — implementing the new civil-registration law (**[[cameroon-civil-registration-law-2024]]**, Law 2024/016), which authorises **digital birth, marriage and death certificates** and a **Unique Personal Identification Number** for every citizen.

BUNEC aims for a **fully digital CRVS system by 2029**, integrated with the new biometric national ID and biometric voter kits for consistent data management. Liber (created 2017) also provides digital-government services elsewhere in Africa, including Chad (e-visa, digital land registration).
