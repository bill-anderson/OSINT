A civil-society policy brief on the **digitalisation of health services in Kenya** (26 March 2025), by
[[cipesa]], the Danish Institute for Human Rights and KNCHR — the reference
frame for the SHA/SHIF transition, biometric health identification, HMIS rollout and Health Information
Exchange ([[dpi.mis]], [[gov.policy]]). Complements the Ministry of Health's 2025 biometric-health-ID
and digital-superhighway agenda. Reference document.
