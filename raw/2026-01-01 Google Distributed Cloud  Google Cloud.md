## Extend Google Cloud's infrastructure and AI on-premises

GDC is a fully managed software and hardware solution for data centers and edge locations to address regulatory, local data processing, survivability, and low-latency needs.

Contact us[](https://cloud.google.com/contact/)

### Leading AI capabilities

### Enable inference at the edge

### Enable inference at the edge

On-premises computing is not new, but extending AI-optimized cloud infrastructure from the cloud to on-premises deployments is. With Google Distributed Cloud, you can extend the latest in AI models and AI-optimized infrastructure without compromising on data residency, latency, or connectivity.

### Run modern apps on-premises

### Run modern apps on-premises

Build modern apps everywhere in a uniform developer environment from cloud to edge. Google Distributed Cloud enables your team to build, deploy, and scale with a Kubernetes-based developer workflow and leverage an active ecosystem of partners.

### Scale anywhere with cloud-native agility

##### 

Full isolation with no connectivity to the public internet

Safeguard sensitive data and adhere to strict regulations with Google Distributed Cloud's air-gapped option. The solution does not require connectivity to Google Cloud or the public internet to manage infrastructure, services, APIs, or tooling, and is built to remain disconnected in perpetuity. Our product's open architecture creates familiarity across both public and air-gapped private clouds.

#### What is a Google Cloud Ready partner and how does my company become one?

The Google Cloud Ready - Distributed Cloud validation recognizes partner solutions that have met a core set of functional and interoperability requirements. Through the process, partners closely collaborate with Google to provide new integrations to best support customer use cases. Learn more in the [Google Cloud Ready - Distributed Cloud Partner Validation Guide](https://cloud.google.com/distributed-cloud/docs/cloudready).

#### What is a Managed Google Distributed Cloud provider and how does my company become one?

The Managed GDC Providers (MGP) is a strategic partnership initiative by [Google Cloud Partner Advantage program](https://cloud.google.com/partners) designed to accelerate Google Distributed Cloud adoption by collaborating with specialized partners who are skilled in deploying, operating, and managing services. These MGPs form a comprehensive ecosystem, providing end-to-end Google Distributed Cloud solutions, including top-tier support, robust data security, and more. By offering Google Distributed Cloud as a managed service, MGPs empower businesses to scale efficiently while maintaining high service quality.
