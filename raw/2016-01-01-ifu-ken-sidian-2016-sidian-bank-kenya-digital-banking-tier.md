## Description

IFU provided USD 12 million Tier 2 capital injection to Sidian Bank (formerly K-Rep Bank) to support its growth to Tier 2 status and expand digital banking services for unbanked and underbanked Kenyans. Sidian Bank focuses on digital banking transformation to serve the base of the pyramid through mobile and digital channels.
