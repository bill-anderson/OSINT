Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 21 May 2026. Ethiopia's Digital ID for Inclusion and Services Project is procuring "Super Agents" ($54M) to facilitate large-scale Fayda registration and reach underserved communities, per a World Bank March evaluation-mission recommendation. In its second year the project has surpassed 39 million Fayda enrollments, integrated 100+ public/private institutions and recorded 78 million+ authentications; 9 million+ cards delivered; total financing $350 million. A joint task force supported ~190,000 refugee registrations, 170,000 refugee ID issuances and 200,000+ host-community registrations. Pushing toward the 90 million target.
