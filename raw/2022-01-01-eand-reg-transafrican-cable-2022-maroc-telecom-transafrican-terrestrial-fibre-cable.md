## Description

Maroc Telecom invested in a 5,700 km transafrican terrestrial cable connecting Morocco to Niger via Mauritania, Mali, and Burkina Faso, as part of MAD 2 billion+ invested in transafrican connectivity infrastructure. This cable enables the landlocked Moov Africa subsidiaries in Mauritania, Mali, Burkina Faso, and Niger to connect to international bandwidth via the Maroc Telecom network. Investment disclosed as part of Maroc Telecom's 2024 reporting.
