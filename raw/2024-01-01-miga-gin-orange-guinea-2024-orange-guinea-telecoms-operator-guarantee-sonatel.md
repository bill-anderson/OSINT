## Description

MIGA guarantee of EUR 366.33 million (~$399.7M USD at Dec 2023 rates) issued December 18, 2023, to Sonatel SA (Senegal) covering its equity investments and retained earnings in Orange Guinee SA (telecom subsidiary in Guinea) and Orange Finances Mobiles Guinee SA (mobile money subsidiary). Covers risks of Expropriation, War and Civil Disturbance, and Transfer Restrictions & Currency Inconvertibility for 6 years. Supported by IDA PSW shared first-loss facility. This row covers the Orange Guinee SA (telecoms) component (PID 14395, $373.2M USD).
