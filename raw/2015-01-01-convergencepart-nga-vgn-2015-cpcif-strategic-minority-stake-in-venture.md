## Description

Convergence Partners Communications Infrastructure Fund (CPCIF) invested $20 million for a strategic minority stake in Venture Garden Nigeria (VGN), a diversified fintech holding company with significant operations in Nigeria. VGN provides innovative, data-driven, end-to-end technology platforms addressing reconciliation and payment processing inefficiencies across multiple industry verticals including financial services, education, aviation, power, downstream oil and gas, and cybersecurity. Investment was made at CPCIF final close (capital over $200 million). Venture Garden Group later incubated companies including Flutterwave, Helium Health, AppZone, and GreenHouse Capital.
