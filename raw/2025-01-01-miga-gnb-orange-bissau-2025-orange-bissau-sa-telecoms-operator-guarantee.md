## Description

MIGA guarantee to Sonatel SA covering its equity investment in Orange Bissau S.A., the telecoms operator in Guinea-Bissau. Part of the combined Orange Guinea and Guinea Bissau program (EUR 433.7M total across PIDs 14395, 15192, 14394). This tranche (PID 14394) covers $25.4M for telecom operations in Guinea-Bissau including mobile network operations. Risks: Expropriation, War and Civil Disturbance, Transfer Restriction. Approval FY2025.
