## Description

Joint project between UNICEF Sierra Leone and the Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) to connect 523 schools to the internet. The IsDB approved a USD 5 million loan in September 2021 (Sierra Leone Parliament ratified October 2022). Under the co-financing arrangement, IsDB covers connectivity for 403 schools and UNICEF covers 120 schools. Building on the Giga Phase 1 pilot that connected 42 schools. Project aims to scale to 11,000 schools ultimately. Sierra Leone connected 39 hard-to-reach schools by 2023 (Giga Annual Results 2023). Internet costs reduced from USD 12,000 to USD 1,500 per school per year (87.5% reduction). Part of Giga's global initiative (UNICEF-ITU).
