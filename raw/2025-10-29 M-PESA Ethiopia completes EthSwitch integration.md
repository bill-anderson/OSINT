# M-PESA Ethiopia completes EthSwitch integration

**M-PESA Ethiopia** ([[safaricom-ethiopia]]) completed integration with the national switch **[[ethswitch]]**, enabling full interoperability between mobile money and banks: users send from M-PESA wallets to any of **15 major banks** connected to EthSwitch, or pay merchants via bank-issued **EthQR**; bank customers can pay M-PESA merchants or top up M-PESA wallets from mobile banking. Runs on EthSwitch's national infrastructure with real-time settlement — the integration that drove M-PESA Ethiopia's subsequent user surge.
