# StarLink Halts Internet Service to Zimbabwe Pending Licensing

By Godfrey Marawanyika and Ray Ndlovu — April 12, 2024 at 3:32 PM UTC

Elon Musk's StarLink has halted internet services to Zimbabwe until it receives licensing approval from the nation's telecommunications regulator, a top official said.

The Posts and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe said it asked StarLink to disconnect all kits that were illegally operating in the southern African nation, according to Gift Machengete, the director-general at the authority.
