Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 16 July 2026. Ethiopia's national digital ID (Fayda) is described as a critical tool for a new refugee-inclusion framework, "Makatet" (Amharic for inclusion), launched last month. Makatet moves Ethiopia from an aid-dependent encampment model toward a state-led development approach focused on long-term inclusion and self-reliance for displaced persons. Per a Migration Policy Institute (MPI) paper, NIDP is issuing Fayda to refugees to enable access to public and private services. Ethiopia hosts ~1.1 million refugees/asylum seekers from 27 countries plus 2m+ IDPs; Fayda issuance is a key Makatet priority. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
