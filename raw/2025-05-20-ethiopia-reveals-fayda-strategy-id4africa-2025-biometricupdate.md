Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 20 May 2025. On day one of ID4Africa 2025 (Addis Ababa), a panel moderated by NIDP Exec Director Yodahe Zemichael explored the MOSIP-based Fayda program, then with 15 million+ registered, reiterating the commitment to deliver Fayda to all Ethiopians by 2027. Speakers: Dr Belete Molla (Ministry of Innovation and Technology), Tigist Hamid Mohammed (INSA), Yonas Alemayehu (Addis Ababa City CRRSA) - citing political will, workable regulatory/governance frameworks and financing as success factors.
