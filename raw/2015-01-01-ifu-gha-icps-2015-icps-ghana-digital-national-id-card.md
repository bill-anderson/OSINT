## Description

IFU additional investment of USD 5 million to support ICPS (Intelligent Card Production Systems) in Accra, Ghana, to digitalize Ghana's public sector through national ID card production. ICPS is producing biometric national identity cards (Ghana Card) and digital registration for vehicles for the National Identification Authority (NIA). The facility is the largest ISO 9001-certified card production plant in Sub-Saharan Africa.
