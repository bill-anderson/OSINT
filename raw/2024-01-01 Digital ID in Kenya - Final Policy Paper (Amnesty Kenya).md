# Digital ID in Kenya — Final Policy Paper (Amnesty Kenya)

Amnesty International Kenya's policy paper on **digital identity in Kenya** — a rights-focused analysis
weighing the Digital ID / Unique Personal Identifier (**Maisha Namba**) programme against a
multi-layered, cost-benefit and exclusion lens ([[dpi.id]], [[gov.protect]]). Cited by the Human Rights
Research Center case study as the reference for choosing between the Digital ID and UPI approaches.
Reference/advocacy document.
