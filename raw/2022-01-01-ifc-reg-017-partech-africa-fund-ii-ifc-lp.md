## Description

IFC committed up to €25 million (~$26.3M) as a limited partner in Partech Africa Fund II (PAF II), an early-stage venture capital fund investing in Seed to Series D technology businesses across Africa. Partech Africa II reached final close above €300 million in 2024. Co-LPs include BII ($15.6M), KfW, EIB, DEG, FMO, Proparco, Mubadala, and Bpifrance. The fund targets fintech, logistics, digital health, e-commerce, and digital infrastructure companies. Portfolio includes Wave (mobile money), Maad Africa, Revio Pay, Foondamate, and others across 27+ African countries.
