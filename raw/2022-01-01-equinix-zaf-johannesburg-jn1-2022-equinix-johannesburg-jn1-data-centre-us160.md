## Description

Equinix announced in December 2022 its entry into South Africa with a US$160 million data centre investment in Johannesburg, named JN1. The facility, expected to open mid-2024, represents Equinix's second African country entry following the MainOne acquisition. JN1 provides carrier-neutral colocation and interconnection services in Johannesburg, a major digital hub for southern Africa and gateway to hyperscaler cloud on-ramps. TechCabal (June 2025) reports Equinix has opened its first data centre in Johannesburg.
