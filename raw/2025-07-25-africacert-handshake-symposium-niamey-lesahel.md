# 13è édition du symposium AfricaCERT Handshake 2025 : Le Niger au cœur de la stratégie numérique africaine

*(Le Sahel / ONEP is the Niger state daily. Capture is not endorsement.)*

Niger hosted the **13th AfricaCERT Handshake symposium on 24 July 2025** at the Palais des Congrès, Niamey ("Ensemble pour une Afrique numérique et sécurisée"), opened by **Prime Minister and Minister of Economy and Finance Ali Mahaman Lamine Zeine**, with CNSP members and delegations from Burkina Faso and Mali.

The PM stressed digital sovereignty for AES states — "La souveraineté exige que nous construisions nous-mêmes nos infrastructures et que nous formions nos compétences locales. Le Niger ne subit pas la révolution numérique, il la façonne." He listed structural reforms underway: accession to international conventions, **elaboration of a National Cybersecurity Strategy (Stratégie Nationale de Cybersécurité)**, putting public services online, electricity-network development, digitalisation of the economy (digital banks, online insurance, expenditure/revenue chains), and **construction of a national Datacenter**.

AfricaCERT CEO **Jean Robert Hountomey** described three days of workshops, crisis-management scenarios and attack simulations to strengthen collective operational resilience and update the legal/normative cybersecurity arsenal. ANSI Director-General **Anne-Rachelle Marcel Inne** cited Niger's three strategy pillars — infrastructure, human competence, regional alliance — and called to "transform our handshakes into a regional cyber-defence alliance."
