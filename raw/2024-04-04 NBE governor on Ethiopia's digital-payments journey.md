# NBE governor on Ethiopia's digital-payments journey

By NBE Governor **Mamo Mihretu**. Ethiopia launched its first **National Digital Payments Strategy** in 2021 (with the Better Than Cash Alliance), targeting a less-cash economy against infrastructure, literacy, regulatory and gender-gap challenges. **[[telebirr]]** (Ethio Telecom, 2021) reached 42m+ users in two years; the **[[national-bank-of-ethiopia|NBE]]** began licensing non-bank **PIIs/PSOs** — now 11+ (Telebirr, Safaricom M-Pesa, **[[ethswitch]]**, Premier Switch, Kacha, Arif Pay, Chapa). Mobile-money accounts grew from ~15m (2021) to 90m+ (Dec 2023); digital payments rose from 27.2% of transactions (2022) to 130% (2023). Reference/analysis by the governor.
