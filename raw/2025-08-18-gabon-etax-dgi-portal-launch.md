Official announcement (Gouvernement.ga, 18 August 2025) of the new online tax declaration and payment portal (**e-tax**) established by the Direction Générale des Impôts (DGI) of the Republic of Gabon.

As part of the strategy to dematerialize fiscal operations and improve service quality for taxpayers, the DGI has put in place a new internet portal for online tax declaration and payment: e-tax. The DGI states its teams are available to help taxpayers adopt the new secure, fast and simple digital tool. Portal: https://e-tax.dgi.ga/login-econtribuabledgiga . (Companion to the "Digitax Gabon" revenue-digitalization program.)
