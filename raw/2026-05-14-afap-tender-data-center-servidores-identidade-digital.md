Procurement notice issued by AFAP (Agência Fiduciária de Administração e Projetos), the fiduciary agency executing the World Bank-financed "STP Digital" project (P177158): supply of servers for the national data center and disaster-recovery infrastructure for the country's digital-identity system.

Confirms procurement is under way (as of May 2026) for the server/data-centre backbone of the MOSIP-based digital-ID rollout, aligning with the World Bank aide-mémoire (Sept 2025) which recommended either a new TIER III data centre or cloud migration for the digital-ID and digital-government systems. A deadline-extension notice for the same tender was published by Téla Nón on 2026-06-26 (logged as same-tender in the drop log).
