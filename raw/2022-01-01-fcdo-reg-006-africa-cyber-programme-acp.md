## Description

FCDO programme investing £3 million over 16 months (October 2022 to March 2024) to improve cybersecurity capability and reduce cyber harm in Kenya, Nigeria and South Africa. Funded from the Conflict, Stability and Security Fund (CSSF). Builds on the DAP Pillar 2 Trust and Resilience cybersecurity investment. Delivered by KPMG, implementing 6 projects including: supporting government data protection capability in Kenya; strengthening Nigeria's law enforcement digital forensic capability; improving South Africa police ability to prosecute cyber criminals. Contributes to UK vision of thriving, open digital societies powered by trusted technologies.
