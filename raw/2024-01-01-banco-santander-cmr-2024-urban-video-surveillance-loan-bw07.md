# Banco Santander — Cameroon urban video-surveillance loan (2nd tranche / Phase II), 2024

A second loan of FCFA 32.8 billion for Phase II of Cameroon's urban video-surveillance project was authorised in March 2024, via Banco Santander in Hong Kong.
