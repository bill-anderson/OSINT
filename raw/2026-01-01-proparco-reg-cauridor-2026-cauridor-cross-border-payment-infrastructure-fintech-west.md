## Description

USD 2 million equity investment in Cauridor, a fintech company specialised in cross-border payment infrastructure for African corridors. Founded by Guinean entrepreneurs, Cauridor builds payment infrastructure for remittances and cross-border transactions. Investment is part of Cauridor's Series A round (target close Q4 2026). Total funding reaches USD 13 million. Announced at Africa Forward Summit in Nairobi.
