# Convalt Energy — Lesotho AI data centre & power MoU, 2026

Lesotho signed a US$6.2 billion MoU with US-based Convalt Energy to build the country's first AI data centre in Mokhotlong district, alongside 1.2 GW of hydropower, 4.6 GW of solar and up to 4 GWh of battery storage.

## Description

"Earlier in June the government signed a US$6.2 billion MoU with US-based Convalt Energy to construct the AI data centre along with 1.2 GW of hydropower, 4.6 GW of solar generation capacity and up to 4 GWh of battery energy storage (BESS)." "Construction is expected to begin in 2029; once complete, the centre would be among the largest in the region, linking regional fibre infrastructure." The US Embassy said the project gives Lesotho "a central role in powering the AI future" with American exports expected to exceed $2 billion.
