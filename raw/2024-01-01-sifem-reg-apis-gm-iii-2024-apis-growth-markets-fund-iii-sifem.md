## Description

SIFEM committed USD 20 million to Apis Growth Markets Fund III, a USD 500 million closed-end financial services fund targeting growth-stage opportunities in Africa, South Asia and Southeast Asia. The fund invests in transformative tech-enabled financial services including cashless payments, embedded finance and digital lending, enabling underserved populations to participate in the formal economy.
