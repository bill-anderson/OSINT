## Description

BII debt investment in Wave Mobile Money S.A., an interoperable mobile money platform operating across Francophone West Africa (Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Gambia, Mali, Niger, Uganda). Wave became Francophone Africa's first unicorn ($1.7B valuation) in 2021 after a $200M Series A. IATI records $31.7M commitment by BII in June 2025 as part of a €117M ($137M) debt round led by Rand Merchant Bank, with Finnfund and Norfund as co-investors. BII also made an initial investment in 2022 (not separately published in IATI at time of data collection).
