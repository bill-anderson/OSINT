Maliweb (news portal; verify original outlet). On 10 Feb 2026, during the Week of Justice, Mali launched two online platforms for issuing the criminal record (casier judiciaire) and nationality certificate, presided by Justice Minister Mamoudou Kassogue and Digital Economy Minister Alhamdou Ag Ilyene. Citizens apply via ecasierjudiciaire.gouv.ml and enationalite.gouv.ml using their NINA and civil-status details; documents are returned by email, with no physical travel, accessible to the diaspora and remote areas (minister cited access "from Tinzawatene"). Judges/prosecutors get personal professional workspaces to process files remotely. AGETIC built the portals and an "E-verif" authenticity-verification tool (each generated document carries a unique authentic code) to combat forged documents in job applications and public tenders.
