# European Union — Kenya Cyber Resilience (KCR) Project, 2026

The European Union is funding the €3 million (US$3.56 million), 36-month Kenya Cyber Resilience (KCR) Project, launched in Nairobi in January 2026 to strengthen cyber-security governance and protect critical information infrastructure.

## Description

"The $3.56 million (€3 million), 36-month initiative, officially launched in Nairobi yesterday" is "designed to respond to these risks by strengthening cyber security governance, institutional coordination and public awareness, while safeguarding critical digital infrastructure." "Funded by the European Union and implemented by Expertise France, alongside EstDev, the project also supports the protection of Critical Information Infrastructure and lays the groundwork for a future National Cybersecurity Agency."
