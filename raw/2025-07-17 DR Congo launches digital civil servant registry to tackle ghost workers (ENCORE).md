In early 2025 the DRC launched its **first clean, digital directory of civil servants** (central government and Kinshasa province) — before which the exact number of civil servants was unknown and no consolidated cross-ministry database existed (a ghost-worker / wage-bill problem). The launch ceremony was chaired by Prime Minister **Judith Suminwa** and Civil Service Minister **Jean-Pierre Lihau**.

The reform is supported by the World Bank-funded **[[encore-drc]]** project (Enhancing Collection of Revenue and Expenditure Management), implemented with the Ministry of Civil Service and the public-finance reform committee **COREF** — a registry/HRMIS use case aimed at payroll integrity.
