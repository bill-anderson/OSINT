# Egypt's Interior Ministry launches MOIEG-PASS biometric verification app

Egypt's **Ministry of Interior** launched **[[moieg-pass|MOIEG-PASS]]**, a mobile app using **biometric (facial-recognition) verification** for citizens accessing government digital services — described as the country's **first unified national platform of its kind** for securing personal data and online transactions (Android pilot on Google Play). Users authenticate by **scanning their national ID card** and completing facial recognition, registering with mobile number and national-ID details; features include identity verification, tracking official requests, and service notifications. The facial-recognition technology is described as developed by Egyptian engineers to international standards.

Framed as part of modernising the ministry's digital infrastructure and accelerating Egypt's digital transformation, including AI to streamline e-services. (Distinct from the CBE's **[[haweya]]** financial-ID app — MOIEG-PASS is the Interior Ministry's government-services identity gateway.)
