## Description

Swedfund invested USD 10 million in TIDE Africa Fund II, a venture capital fund by TLcom Capital dedicated to financing scalable and innovative digital business models in Africa. The fund supports tech entrepreneurs building digital solutions from local needs in Africa, contributing to increased digitalisation, digital inclusion and economic growth. Portfolio companies operate in Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt and South Africa.
