The FG plans to build a $10 billion tech innovation hub in Katsina State (one of ten states selected to host new hubs), Minister Bosun Tijani said, with construction expected before end of next year. The hub supports the 3-million tech-talent programme. Katsina Governor Dikko Umaru Radda cited the state's Right of Way policy, State Digital Law, Digital Economic Zone and ICT directorate. The government aims to raise ICT's share of GDP to 22% by 2027. To support this, the World Bank approved (September 30) $500 million for Nigeria under the BRIDGE Project — a $1.6 billion initiative also backed by AfDB, EIB and IsDB — to deploy about 90,000km of climate-resilient, renewable-powered fibre built around seven national rings.
