## Description

Orange's network of Orange Digital Centres (ODCs) in SSA provides free digital skills training, startup incubation (Orange Fab accelerator), and maker spaces. SSA countries covered include Senegal (Dakar - 2nd ODC 2019, ODC Champions), Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon, Madagascar, Botswana, DRC, Mali, Burkina Faso, Guinea, Sierra Leone. In 2023, ODC Summer Challenge ran across 11 countries, ODC Champions across 15 countries. Orange Cameroun invested XOF 1.5 billion in its ODCs as of 2025.
