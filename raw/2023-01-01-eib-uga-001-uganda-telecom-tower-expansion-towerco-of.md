## Description

Construction of 659 new mobile sites (506 ultimately signed) in Uganda. At least 50% of towers in rural areas without mobile service coverage. Provides mobile coverage in areas without mobile broadband and increases capacity in saturated areas. Towers powered primarily by renewable energy. Enables 4G and 5G data services and mobile money solutions.
