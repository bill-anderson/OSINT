On **6 August 2024** in Kinshasa, the think tank **Law & Technologies** presented a study on the DRC's personal-data-protection legal framework, stressing the need to **operationalise the Data Protection Authority** required by **Article 262 of the Digital Code** (Loi 20/017). The study calls on the government to draft and sign the decree defining the authority's organisation and functioning.

The DRC thus has a data-protection authority **on paper but not yet operational** — a pointed enforcement gap as the Trident/DRCPass **20-year exclusive e-KYC** concession over citizens' identity data proceeds. See [[drc-digital-code]].
