Nigerian startup Mathesis Analytics has secured funding from First Ally Capital to expand its AI capabilities. Its technology helps banks assess the creditworthiness of people without bank accounts by analyzing their payment patterns across mobile apps. The system has already supported eight million loans in Nigeria, helping broaden access to credit.
