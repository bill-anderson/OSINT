# Senegal ID System Breach – 139TB Hack Claim

Secondary aggregator writeup (vendor/content-marketing, bylined; cites named primaries) compiling a **dated
timeline** of the **[[daf-senegal|DAF]]** breach — cited here for the timeline and the volume discrepancy, not
as sole authority:

- **19 Jan 2026:** leaked [[iris-corporation]]-linked email says 2 DAF servers compromised; card-
  personalisation data stolen. **20–22 Jan:** containment steps; Dakar technical visit planned.
- **5 Feb 2026:** DAF announces temporary suspension of ID-card production, opens investigation, says data
  intact (communiqué signed by Commissioner Ibrahima Dieng). Green Blood Group publishes dark-web "proof,"
  claims **139TB**.
- **6 Feb 2026:** [[senegal-numerique|SENUM SA]] reported compromised (SeneWeb) — possible coordination.
- **9 Feb 2026:** The Record — Senegal confirms breach; police say restoration underway, data integrity intact.
- **12 Feb 2026:** Dark Reading — Green Blood Group cited **139TB** on its leak site but **139GB** elsewhere;
  true volume unverified.

**Unresolved:** official DAF communications dispute any confirmed leak; the stolen volume (**139TB vs 139GB**),
initial access vector, and restoration timeline remain unverified in public reporting.
