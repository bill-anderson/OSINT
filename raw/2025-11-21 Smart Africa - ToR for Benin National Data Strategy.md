**Binary artefact:** [[2025-11-21 Smart Africa - ToR for Benin National Data Strategy.pdf]] (17 pages).

Smart Africa Secretariat Terms of Reference / RFP (ref 141/SAS-GIZ-Data Governance/RFP/11/2025; published **21 November 2025**, closing 17 December 2025) to recruit a consultancy firm or consortium to develop **Benin's National Data Strategy**, under the **[[smart-africa]]**–**GIZ** Data Governance workstream. Signals Benin formalising a national **data-governance strategy** (data as a governed national asset) with continental and German backing — a policy layer above its ID/e-government stack. See [[gov.policy]], [[gov.standards]].
