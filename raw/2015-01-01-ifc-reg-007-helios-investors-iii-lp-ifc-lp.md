## Description

IFC committed $25 million as a limited partner in Helios Investors III L.P., a $1 billion private equity fund managed by Helios Investment Partners focused on Sub-Saharan Africa. The fund targets investments across multiple sectors with a significant allocation to digital infrastructure and technology-enabled businesses, including mobile money, fintech, and ICT. Helios III invested in companies such as Interswitch (Nigerian fintech), Tipco (Nigeria), and other technology-enabled businesses across SSA.
