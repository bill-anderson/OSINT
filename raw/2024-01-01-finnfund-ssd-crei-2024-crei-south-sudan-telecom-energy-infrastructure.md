## Description

Finnfund USD 5 million mezzanine loan to CREI to finance installation, operation and maintenance of 413 solar-hybrid energy systems for MTN South Sudan telecom sites. The project expands mobile network coverage in South Sudan, one of the world's least connected countries, benefiting over 2 million people with reliable mobile and internet access.
