Internet Exchange Point of Nigeria (IXPN) announced it hit a historic peak traffic of 1 Terabit per second (1Tbps), described as a testament to the growing strength of Nigeria's digital ecosystem and increasing reliance on local interconnection. IXPN said this means more efficient data exchange, reduced latency, lower connectivity costs, and a more robust internet experience for millions across Nigeria, thanking its community of ISPs, content providers, CDNs and stakeholders.
