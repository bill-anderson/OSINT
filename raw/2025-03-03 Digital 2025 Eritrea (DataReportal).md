# Digital 2025 — Eritrea (DataReportal)

Connectivity baseline (early 2025): **793,000** cellular mobile connections (**22.2%** of population; +4.5% YoY; 48.0% broadband). Among the lowest penetration rates in Africa — context for Eritrea's minimal digital-services base.
