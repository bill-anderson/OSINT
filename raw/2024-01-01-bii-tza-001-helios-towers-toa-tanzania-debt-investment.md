## Description

BII committed $20M to TowerCo of Africa Tanzania (TOA Tanzania), a provider of passive telecom infrastructure, in May 2024. The financing will fund construction of ~200 new telecom sites across Tanzania, increasing coverage in rural areas and bridging the digital divide. Helios Towers is Africa's leading independent telecoms infrastructure company, operating towers on over 14,000 sites and serving 144+ million mobile subscribers across Africa. The investment directly enables mobile internet and data services for underserved rural populations.
