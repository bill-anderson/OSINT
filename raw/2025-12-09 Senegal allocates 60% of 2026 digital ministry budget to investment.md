# Senegal allocates 60% of 2026 digital ministry budget to investment

Senegal's **2026 Digital Ministry budget** was set at **CFA81.06bn (US$143.93m)**, with ~**60% to
investment** (CFA58.2bn to digital/telecom projects). Priorities: universal coverage, **free connectivity for
1 million people**, a **sovereign cloud**, **biometric digital identity**, administrative interoperability, a
citizen one-stop-shop and "zero-paper" services. The **[[new-deal-technologique]]** targets cumulative
digital investment of **CFA1,105bn by 2026**; a 4 Dec plenary cited the Startup Act, Tech Park, Digital
Factory and a **CFA200bn startup fund**, targeting 15% of GDP from digital by 2034, 500 startups, 100,000
trained graduates, 150,000 jobs. Senegal ranked **135th of 193 on the UN EGDI (score 0.5162, 2024)** —
telecom infra 0.7329, online services 0.4779, human capital 0.3380.
