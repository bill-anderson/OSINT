# Egypt's CBE launches SoftPOS contactless payment-acceptance service

The **[[central-bank-of-egypt|CBE]]** launched a **SoftPOS** service letting merchants use smartphones/tablets as electronic points of sale — an app accepts contactless card payments, with PINs entered on the device via internationally accredited secure protocols. Governor **Hassan Abdalla** said it supports financial inclusion and the transition to a **less-cash society under Vision 2030**, eliminating the cost of traditional POS hardware and integrating small enterprises into the electronic-payment ecosystem. The official rollout follows a pilot capped at EGP 600 per transaction.
