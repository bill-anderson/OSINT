The U.S. ITA Country Commercial Guide (December 2025) describes Botswana's rapidly expanding digital economy, with the government prioritising digital infrastructure and its openness to **satellite-based providers** fostering competition, lower data costs and remote-area connectivity. It references the **SmartBots** digital-transformation strategy, pending **Digital Services** and **Cybersecurity** bills, the **BOTSAT-1** satellite, and **BOCRA** as sector regulator — a snapshot of Botswana's digital-economy policy landscape as of late 2025.
