## Digital Investment Facility

**Who we are**

The Digital Investment Facility (DIF) facilitates investment in green and secure digital infrastructure in Africa. Co-funded by the European Commission, Germany, and Finland, and implemented by GIZ and HAUS, DIF is committed to empowering Africa's digital future.

**Mission**

Our mission is to support promising data centres and IXP projects from early design to contract closing by enhancing their bankability and facilitating access to finance.

**Vision**

Our vision is to bridge the digital divide and drive sustainable growth across the continent.

**How can we support your project?**

The Digital Investment Facility (DIF) transforms promising greenfield and brownfield data center and internet exchange point (IXP) projects in Africa into viable investment opportunities. If your project meets our eligibility criteria, we provide tailored technical assistance and capacity-building support.

DIF guides your project from early-stage design to contract closing, ensuring it meets financiers' expectations. With DIF’s support, your project gains access to leading multilateral financing institutions, enhancing its bankability and sustainability. Partner with us to drive digital growth and innovation in Africa.

## What DIF offers:

Expert technical advisory support

Tailored advisory for early-stage companies on project feasibility, master plans, pre-feasibility, and technical principles within regulatory frameworks.

Financial advisory support

Assessments to identify gaps and challenges hindering bankability, with in-depth reviews of capital and operating expenditures.

Pre-feasibility study support

Expert guidance on pre-feasibility studies, enhancing investment appeal, technical feasibility, and regulatory compliance, including sustainability evaluations.

Project promotion strategy support

Personalized assistance to business promoters, refining proposals to meet financiers' expectations, and establishing connections to relevant financiers.

Contract closing support

Assistance in negotiating optimal financing terms with financiers during contract closing.

Flexible pool of expert’s support

Additionally, DIF offers a versatile team to assist with additional project aspects upon request, subject to eligibility.

### Apply for personalized project support

To receive tailored advisory support for your project, fill out this Expression of Interest form. Your project must meet the following criteria:

1. Minimum investment size of EUR 5 million.
2. Be a digital infrastructure project (e.g. data center or IXP).
3. Generate revenue.
4. Be located in Sub-Saharan Africa.
5. Contribute to climate action and adhere to high environmental, social, and governance standards.
6. Have private sector ownership with a prospect of non-financial benefit (socio-economic additionality).

[Apply now](http://dif-project.com/)

## Powering Africa's digital future: market briefs

Recent market briefs, provided by the Digital Investment Facility, reveal a continent bursting with opportunity, where the vibrant regions of Southern, East, and West and Central Africa are unlocking new frontiers for data centre investment. Amid this growth, a game-changing trend is emerging: the integration of **green energy sourcing mechanisms**, promising not only to fuel digital expansion but to do so sustainably, setting the stage for Africa’s future as a global digital leader.

Explore energy supply, skills gaps, investment trends, and data centre business models in Africa, its regions and states in the market briefs.
