On 22 July 2025, a technical team led by PS for ICT and the Digital Economy **John Tanui** presented the **National Police Service Information Management System (NPSIMS)** to Inspector General of Police Douglas Kanja at NPS Headquarters, Jogoo House.

NPSIMS is an online system designed to consolidate existing fragmented police systems into a unified digital platform, enhance service delivery through citizen-centered access (mobile apps, USSD, web, call centres and social media), and promote transparency and accountability through role-based access, audit trails and a **digital chain of custody**. The IG underscored the importance of digitizing all police services, noting automation is key to the NPS Strategic Plan (2023-2027) and to transforming police stations into centres of excellence.
