## Description

MASSIF senior loan facility to Cooperative Bank of Oromia (COOP), Ethiopia. COOP launched the sector's first digital lending app and alternative banking channels including mobile money and agency networks. The bank provides MSMEs and agriculture sector with digital financial services.
