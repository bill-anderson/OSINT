## Description

4DX Ventures co-invested with Flutterwave in a $2.4M seed round in CinetPay, a Francophone Africa digital payments platform enabling merchants to accept mobile money and other digital payment methods across 9 West and Central African markets (Côte d'Ivoire, Senegal, Mali, Burkina Faso, Togo, Benin, Cameroon, Congo, Guinea). This is 4DX Ventures' first fintech investment in the Francophone region and Flutterwave's first direct institutional investment.
