# Société Générale — Angola Earth Observation Satellite System financing, 2025

Angola's Finance Minister signed €225.3 million in financing agreements with Société Générale in January 2025 to design, manufacture and launch an Earth Observation Satellite System, built in partnership with Airbus.

## Description

"The agreements, signed with French financial institution Société Générale, allocate €35,268,507 and €189,988,694—totaling €225,257,201—for the design, manufacturing, and launch of an Earth Observation Satellite System. This ambitious project, carried out in partnership with European aerospace leader AIRBUS, includes the construction, launch, and in-orbit installation of the satellite." Signed during the Angola-France Economic Forum concluding the President's state visit.

## Development history

- **2026-03-16** — Government of Angola (GGPEN) officially launched construction and deployment of the satellite, named **ANGEO-1**, at an estimated €225m (~US$259m at 2026 conversion), built by Airbus Defence and Space under the National Space Program; the satellite is to provide >1,000 high-resolution images/day. [https://www.wearetech.africa/en/fils-uk/news/tech/angola-launches-259-million-earth-observation-satellite-project ; Angop 2026-03-16, `raw/2026-03-16-arranque-do-projecto-angeo-1-airbus.md`]
- **2025-03-05** *(added by reconcile, 2026-07-23)* — The project appears in **MINTTICS' Plano Anual de Contratação 2025** as a procurement line, under the **"AngoSat-3" / "Construção e Lançamento de um Satélite Doméstico"** name the Angolan government also used for this satellite before the ANGEO-1 branding (the government portal filed the 2024 financing announcement under the tag *angosat-3*). The **first** PAC (21-Jan-2025) priced it at Kz 298,7 biliões — part of a ministry total of Kz 616,7 biliões that exceeded the whole OGE 2025, a documented scale error the ministry left uncorrected for weeks; the **corrected** PAC (dated 27-Feb-2025, published 05-Mar-2025) restates the line at **Kz 712,7 milhões** and a separate *"Sistema de Observação da Terra"* line at **Kz 77,7 milhões**. These are annual procurement estimates, not the project's financing, which is this record. [https://pti.ao/minttics-actualiza-pac-2025-despesas-caem-para-menos-de-230-mil-milhoes-de-kwanzas/ ; `raw/2025-03-25-ago-minttics-pac-2025-actualizacao.md`, `raw/2025-02-21-nj-minttics-ainda-nao-corrigiu-pac-2025-zeros-a-mais.md`]
- **2024-09-09** *(added by reconcile, 2026-07-23)* — The financing was authorised by **Despacho Presidencial n.º 206/24**: an export-credit agreement of **€189,988,694** (Kz 194,288,518,122.61 as stated), covering 85% of the commercial contract and 100% of the SERV insurance premium under **Bpifrance AE** cover, plus **€35,268,507** (Kz 36,066,703,850.42) for the 15% down payment and the risk-mitigation commission — the Finance Minister delegated to sign. [https://minttics.gov.ao/ao/noticias/governo-angolano-autoriza-a-construcao-do-satelite-de-observacao-da-terra-angeo-1/ ; https://governo.gov.ao/noticias/1845/tecnologia/angosat-3/angola-prepara-construcao-de-novo-satelite-de-observacao-da-terra]
