# One year on — Ethiopia's data protection law faces a slow path to enforcement

A year after the **[[ethiopia-pdpp|PDPP]]** (published July 2024), implementation lags: the **secondary directives** needed to operationalise it remain unissued, public awareness of data-subject rights is low, and enforcement by the **[[ethiopian-communications-authority|ECA]]** has yet to gather momentum — a gap between law-on-paper and effect. A live-flag for Ethiopia's fast-moving Fayda/banking data-harmonisation drive, which is proceeding ahead of the data-protection regime's operationalisation.
