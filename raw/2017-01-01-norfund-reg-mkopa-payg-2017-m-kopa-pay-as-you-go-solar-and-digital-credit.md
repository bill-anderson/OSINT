## Description

Norfund USD 13 million debt commitment as part of USD 80 million syndicated facility (Stanbic Bank lead; CDC USD 20M, FMO USD 13M, Norfund USD 13M). M-KOPA provides pay-as-you-go solar home systems and digital mobile money credit to off-grid households in Kenya and Uganda. Financing backed by customer receivables paid over mobile money payment plans. Digital scope: IoT smart metering, mobile money payment rails, digital credit scoring.
