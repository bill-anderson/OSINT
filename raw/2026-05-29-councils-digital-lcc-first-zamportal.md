THE Ministry of Local Government and Rural Development says it has launched Lusaka City Council (LCC) on the ZamPortal, a 24-hour government digital operational platform that allows citizens to access council services online. According to a statement issued by Principal Public Relations Officer Liseli Kanyanga, the initiative is aimed at eliminating physical queues and reducing bureaucratic delays. Kanyanga said with LCC now leading the rollout among the 116 local authorities, the remaining pilot councils which include Ndola, Kitwe, Solwezi, Chingola, Kalulushi, Kalumbila and Chilanga, are expected to go live on the ZamPortal by June 30, 2026.
