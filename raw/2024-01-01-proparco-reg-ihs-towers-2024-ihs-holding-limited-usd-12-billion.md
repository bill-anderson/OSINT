## Description

USD 20 million participation as anchor investor (with IFC and EAIF) in IHS Holding Limited's oversubscribed USD 1.2 billion dual-tranche senior notes issuance. IHS Towers is one of the world's largest independent tower companies with major presence in Nigeria, Zambia, Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, South Africa, Rwanda. Bond catalysed private investment (10x+ mobilisation). Supports digital connectivity across Africa.
