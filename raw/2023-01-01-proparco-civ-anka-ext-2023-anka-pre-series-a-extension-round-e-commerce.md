## Description

Equity participation in the USD 5 million pre-Series A extension round of ANKA, led by IFC with Proparco and Bpifrance. ANKA is an all-in-one SaaS platform for e-commerce, payments, and global shipping targeting African businesses in 47 countries. Round brings total funding to USD 13.5 million.
