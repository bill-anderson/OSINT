## Description

USD 60 million long-term loan to WIOCC, a pan-African telecommunications infrastructure company, to support its expansion in Eastern and Southern Africa. The financing supports WIOCC's fibre backbone, submarine cable, and broadband connectivity infrastructure across multiple SSA countries. Proparco acted as mandated lead arranger. Co-financed with EAIF/PIDG (USD 40 million).
