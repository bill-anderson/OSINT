# Social protection — the RNU rises from 542,000 to one million households

Senegal's **[[rnu-senegal|Registre National Unique (RNU)]]** — the main mechanism for targeting vulnerable
households — **grew from 542,000 to one million households in 2024**, per Loly Diouf Guèye of the
**[[dgpsn-senegal|DGPSN]]** (regional workshop, Ziguinchor, with UNICEF). The registry now integrates both
poor households and those vulnerable to climate/economic shocks. **64,000 people with disabilities** receive
the Family Security Grant (Bourse de sécurité familiale), one of DGPSN's main safety nets; autonomisation
initiatives helped households save over CFA1bn via community savings/credit. Ziguinchor is among the most
vulnerable regions (after Dakar, Thiès, Kaolack). The presented study was under an EU/ILO project on social
protection and public financial management.
