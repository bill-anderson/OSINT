## Description

BII invested $180M equity in Liquid Telecommunications Holding Limited in December 2018 to accelerate expansion of its fibre optic cable network across Sub-Saharan Africa (Cape-Cairo route and Central/West Africa). Liquid is the largest independent pan-African fibre network operator with over 100,000km of cable serving Kenya, Rwanda, DRC, South Africa, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria, and others. Investment supports affordable broadband access for businesses and consumers. BII remains a shareholder in Cassava Technologies (parent company post-restructuring).
