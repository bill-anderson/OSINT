AfriLabs, the pan-African network of technology and innovation hubs, added **18 new hubs**, bringing the total to **496 hubs across 261 cities in 53 African nations**.

Notable additions span the continent: SU LaunchLab (Stellenbosch, AfriLabs' first in the city), ScaleUp Namibia, OceanHub Africa and the Graça Machel Trust's Women Creating Wealth (Southern Africa); DigiArtLivingLab, Connect'Innov and The Dot (Tunisia); PTS Investments and Aria Ventures (Egypt); Mswitch (Abuja); Africa Tech Space (Nakuru), Owl Innovation Labs (Nairobi), RLabs Tanzania (Iringa) and AfriFORTIFIED HUB (Kisumu) in East Africa; and Ovation, KIVUTECH, Revival Innovation Hub and DIAMBILAY in Central Africa (DRC/Cameroon). AfriLabs director of community Mayssa Mrabet welcomed the new members' "unique perspectives and contributions."
