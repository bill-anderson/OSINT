## Description

In December 2024, Cassava Technologies raised USD 90 million in equity as part of a broader USD 310 million financing package. The equity was led by the US International Development Finance Corporation (DFC, USD 50M), Google LLC (USD 20M approx.), and Finnfund (undisclosed). Alongside this, Liquid Intelligent Technologies (Cassava subsidiary) refinanced its South African Rand term loan (~USD 220M equivalent) with Standard Bank, Rand Merchant Bank, Nedbank, and IFC. Cassava simultaneously completed a legal reorganisation to create an integrated digital solutions platform spanning broadband, data centres, cloud, cybersecurity, AI compute, and payments across 30+ countries. Cassava operates fibre networks, Africa Data Centres (ADC) data centres, Liquid C2 cloud/cybersecurity, and GPU-as-a-Service.
