Burkina Faso's Ministry of Economy and Finance launched **SYC@D** (Système Intégré de Gestion du Cadastre et des Domaines) on **21 March 2025** (under PM Ouédraogo), accessible at **sif.bf**, to modernise land, cadastral and domain procedures. It lets the tax and land administration securely and quickly process and issue **land-occupation titles**, and lets users apply for plot allocation, obtain a cadastral extract, and receive provisional and definitive transfer orders online — the land-registry pillar of Burkina's govtech stack.
