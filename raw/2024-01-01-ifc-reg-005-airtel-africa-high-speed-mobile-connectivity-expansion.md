## Description

IFC committed $165 million from its own account plus $35 million from the Managed Co-Lending Portfolio Program (MCPP) — a total of $200 million — to Airtel Africa to support expansion of high-speed mobile connectivity in DRC, Kenya, and Rwanda. Announced September 2024, the financing supports network upgrades including 4G and 5G-ready infrastructure and aims to improve access to high-speed internet for underserved populations across three key markets. This is IFC's second major Airtel Africa financing round, following the $194M package in 2022.
