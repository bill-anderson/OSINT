Angola's data-protection and cybersecurity legal stack (per the CMS expert guide):

- **Law 22/11** of 17 June — Data Protection Law
- **Law 23/11** of 20 June — Electronic Communications and Information Society Services
- **Law 7/17** of 16 February — Network and IT Systems Protection
- **Presidential Decree 108/16** — General Electronic Communications Regulation
- **Presidential Decree 202/11** — Regulation on Information Technologies and Services
- **Resolution 33/19** of 9 July — ratifying the **African Union Convention on Cybersecurity and Data Protection** (Malabo)
- **Law 2/20** (Video surveillance) + **Presidential Decree 308/21** (its regulation)
- **Law 11/20** — Mobile Identification/Location and Electronic Surveillance
- **Law 38/20** — Angolan Criminal Code
- **Presidential Decree 275/20** — Private Credit Information Centres

Confirms Angola's ratification of the **Malabo Convention** (Resolution 33/19) and a maturing surveillance/cyber legal base alongside the 2011 data-protection core.
