Inua Jamii beneficiaries in Ngong (Kajiado North) praised the government for enabling access to funds via **mobile payments (M-Pesa + eCitizen)**, saving on transport to banks. PS for Social Protection Joseph Motari said the President directed that the elderly be paid through mobile phones via M-Pesa and eCitizen.

The State Department disbursed **KES 3.52 billion to 1,760,074 beneficiaries** for the January 2025 cycle (KES 2,000 each). The government is transitioning **[[inua-jamii]]** payments from bank accounts to **[[safaricom]]** M-Pesa agents via eCitizen (pilot began November 2024 in Murang'a and Marsabit). Beneficiaries or caregivers withdraw the full amount without charges at an M-Pesa agent with their national ID by dialling *222#; Safaricom made an exception to allow line registration for beneficiaries/caregivers over 60.
