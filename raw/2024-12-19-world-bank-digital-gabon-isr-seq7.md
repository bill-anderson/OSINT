**Digital Gabon Project (P175987)** — Implementation Status & Results Report, Seq No 7, archived 19 December 2024 (ISR02741). Implementing agency: **CN-TIPPEE**.

- **Effectiveness:** "The project became effective in July, 2024, after a significant delay. Project implementation needs to be accelerated." Loan **IBRD-92830**, effectiveness date **30 July 2024**.
- **Financing (as of Dec 2024):** Original $68.50m; **disbursed 1.92%** ($1.3m), $67.2m undisbursed. Approved 30 July 2021; mid-term review planned 30 October 2025; original closing 30 June 2026.
- **Ratings:** PDO and Implementation Progress both **Moderately Satisfactory**; Overall Risk **Substantial**.
- **Results targets (closing Jun 2026, all at baseline 0 as of Dec 2024):**
  - 100,000 successful digital transactions by individuals for public-service delivery (50,000 of them by female beneficiaries).
  - 5 government entities actively and securely exchanging data on the government interoperability platform.
  - Population 16+ with a NIP (percentage) — target set, actual at baseline.

The near-zero disbursement 3.5 years after approval, and the "implementation needs to be accelerated" flag, are the primary-source evidence behind Gabon's later (2026) push to speed up execution of eGabon / Gabon Digital and raise the disbursement rate.
