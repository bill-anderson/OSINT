## Description

Two debt investments by BII in Axian Telecom, a pan-African telecoms and digital infrastructure company operating in Madagascar, Tanzania, Togo, Senegal, Comoros, and Réunion. Investment 1 ($25M, Feb 2022): Bond investment to add 3 million new subscribers in Tanzania and expand coverage in other SSA markets. Investment 2 ($30M, Feb 2024): Facility to construct 200 new telecom sites across Tanzania (mainland and Zanzibar), increasing rural connectivity for up to 600,000 people. Axian also operates mobile money services (Mvola in Madagascar, T-PESA in Tanzania).
