# Liquid Intelligent Technologies expands services in Botswana

Liquid Intelligent Technologies (LIT), a business of Cassava Technologies, has expanded its digital solutions portfolio in Botswana by introducing cloud and cyber security services aimed at businesses and enterprises of all sizes.

The move coincides with the company's celebration of a decade of operations in the country.

At the centre of the new offering is Secure360, an integrated cyber security framework designed to help organisations transition from reactive responses to cyber threats towards proactive protection, intelligence, and assurance. The solution combines local service delivery with continental-scale infrastructure and global technology partnerships to provide enterprise-grade cloud and digital security capabilities.
