## Description

DFC equity investment of up to $25 million in Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund (CPDIF), a specialist Africa-focused ICT private equity fund. The fund invests in optical fibre installations, wireless towers, data centres, cloud, IoT, and AI infrastructure across sub-Saharan Africa. First close July 2021 at $120M; final close $296M. Portfolio companies include CSquared (Kenya broadband). DFC share is up to $25M out of $296M fund. Target countries: DRC, Ethiopia, Côte d'Ivoire, Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa.
