## Description

Swedfund committed SEK 63 million to Apis Growth Fund I, a private equity fund managed by Apis Partners targeting financial services and fintech companies at the intersection of finance and technology in Africa and South Asia. The fund focuses on mobile payment services, savings, credit and insurance for underserved populations. Africa portfolio companies include Direct Pay Online (pan-Africa online payment platform) and MicroCred (pan-Africa mSME lender). First close August 2015; fund reached USD 287 million final close in 2017.
