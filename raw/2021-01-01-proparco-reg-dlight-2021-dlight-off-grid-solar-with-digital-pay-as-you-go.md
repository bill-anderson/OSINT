## Description

USD 10 million equity investment in d.light, a leading off-grid solar kit provider operating a digital pay-as-you-go (PAYG) model in SSA. d.light uses mobile money and digital payment infrastructure to extend solar energy access to unelectrified households. The PAYG model is built on digital financial services. Operations in Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, Uganda.
