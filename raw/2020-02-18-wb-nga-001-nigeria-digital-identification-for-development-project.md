## Description

Provides digital identification for over 150 million Nigerians to expand financial inclusion and improve access to public services. Supports national ID enrollment, harmonization of existing ID systems, and development of digital authentication infrastructure.

## Development history

- **2024-12-01** — restructured (Report RES00579): enrolment target raised to 180m NINs and closing extended to 30 June 2026. [2024-12-01 Nigeria ID4D Project Restructuring Paper (World Bank, P167183).md]
- **2026-06-05** — NIN enrolments passed 130 million (the largest in Africa); the 2024 restructuring set a 180m-by-31-December-2026 target; the project is funded by the World Bank with AFD and EIB, total US$430m. [biometricupdate.com]
