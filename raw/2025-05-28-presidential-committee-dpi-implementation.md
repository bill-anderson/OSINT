The federal government inaugurated the Presidential Committee on the Implementation of Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI), tasked with robust, coordinated and legally compliant DPI implementation. SGF Sen. George Akume (represented by Minister Dr Bosun Tijani) charged the committee to coordinate integration of the three core DPI pillars — digital identity, financial payments and data exchange. The committee is chaired by President Tinubu, with the SGF (secretariat), Head of Civil Service, Attorney General, and Ministers of Finance, Communications/Digital Economy, Interior, and Budget, plus the DGs of NIMC and NITDA. NIMC DG Abisoye Coker-Odusote pledged support; NITDA DG Kashifu Inuwa Abdullahi stressed unifying government databases for seamless data exchange.
