# Hormuud's EVC Plus earns GSMA re-certification

Mogadishu (HOL), Wednesday August 20, 2025

Hormuud Telecom, Somalia's largest telecommunications operator, has been re-certified by the GSMA Mobile Money Certification programme — placing its EVC Plus service among only ten mobile money platforms worldwide to meet international standards for safety and transparency. The certification is based on independent assessments covering nearly 300 criteria, including fraud prevention, consumer protection, anti-money laundering safeguards and operational resilience. Hormuud first achieved the status in 2022.

EVC Plus joins global leaders such as Safaricom's M-PESA, Vodacom M-PESA and MTN MoMo. According to a World Bank report, Somalis conduct about 155 million mobile money transactions each month, valued at $2.7 billion; nearly three-quarters of adults aged 16+ use mobile money regularly. EVC Plus alone serves more than 4.5 million users daily and is relied upon by over 145 NGOs to distribute humanitarian cash aid nationwide.

"Being re-certified by the GSMA is a major milestone for us," said Mohamed Farah, CEO of Hormuud Telecom. The milestone comes as Hormuud pursues wider smartphone adoption and accelerates its nationwide 5G rollout, aiming to lay the foundation for a digitally inclusive, cashless economy.
