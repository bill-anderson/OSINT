## Description

DEG provides a long-term loan of USD 25 million to IPT Powertech Group SAL (IPT), a Lebanon-based energy system integrator providing decentralised energy solutions for mobile network operators in Africa and the Middle East. IPT operates more than 30,000 towers in 11 countries in Africa and the Middle East. The loan enables investment in energy solutions to ensure telecom towers remain operational, supporting mobile connectivity across Sub-Saharan Africa including Nigeria, Ghana, and other markets. Co-investors include Proparco (USD 20M), Finnfund (USD 10M), and European Investment Bank (USD 60M).
