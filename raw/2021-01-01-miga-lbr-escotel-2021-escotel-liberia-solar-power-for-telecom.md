## Description

MIGA political risk guarantee of $12.06 million to Escotel Mauritius covering its investment in Escotel Liberia Inc., which installs and operates solar-hybrid power systems at ~612 telecom tower sites in Liberia owned/operated by Orange Liberia. Avoids 158,000 tCO2e over the guarantee period. Risks: Transfer Restriction, Expropriation, War and Civil Disturbance for up to 10 years.
