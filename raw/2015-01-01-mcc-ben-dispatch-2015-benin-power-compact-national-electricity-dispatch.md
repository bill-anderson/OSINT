## Description

MCC's $391M Benin Power Compact (signed September 2015, entered force June 2017, closed June 2023) included the National Electricity Dispatch Activity ($31.4M), which financed construction of a national electricity distribution dispatch and control center in Akassato, Abomey-Calavi. The center was inaugurated on 22 June 2023. This is the first national dispatch center in Benin, providing centralised supervisory monitoring and remote control of 41 substations across the national distribution grid. Functions include real-time grid supervision, remote switching, fault detection and dispatch — SCADA-type operational technology. The center connects to the national medium-voltage network to reduce outages, improve reliability, and enable smarter grid management. This was part of the Electricity Distribution Project, alongside the Cotonou Grid Strengthening Activity ($145.3M) and Regional Grid Strengthening Activity ($80.6M).
