## Description

Improves effectiveness of public spending in selected sectors and strengthens the statistics system for evidence-based decision making in Cameroon. Supports national statistical system strengthening including data collection, data quality, and dissemination systems. Includes development of statistical information management infrastructure and statistical capacity building at national and regional levels.
