Cameroon's **[[dgsn-cameroon]]** launched a **rapid biometric ID enrolment campaign in the Far North region** (inaugural ceremony in Maroua, **2 August 2025**) to extend legal identification to underserved and displaced populations (internally displaced persons; UNHCR context). It builds on the 15-year build-operate-transfer contract with **[[augentic]]** (spring 2024) covering biometric enrolment, card production and secure issuance — the inclusion/displacement dimension of the new CNI rollout.
