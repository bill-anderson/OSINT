TechAfrica News, 10 July 2026. Ethiopia took another step toward nationwide interoperable government payments as public institutions, regulators and payment-infrastructure providers convened to operationalise a unified implementation framework aligned with the National Digital Payments Strategy. The Ministry of Finance, the National Bank of Ethiopia (NBE) and national payment switch EthSwitch brought together technical teams to advance a unified framework - marking Ethiopia's transition from digital-payments policy to execution, supporting modernised public services, financial inclusion and interoperable DPI via EthioPay's national payment ecosystem.
