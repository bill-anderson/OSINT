## Description

Visa Inc. acquired a significant minority equity stake (~20%) in Interswitch Group, Nigeria's leading digital payments company, for approximately $200 million. The investment valued Interswitch at $1 billion, making it Nigeria's first fintech unicorn. As part of the strategic partnership, Visa and Interswitch committed to advancing the digital payments ecosystem across Africa.
