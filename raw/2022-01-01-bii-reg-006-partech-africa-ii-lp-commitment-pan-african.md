## Description

BII committed $15.6M to Partech Africa Fund II, a venture capital fund investing in tech-enabled businesses across Africa. Partech Africa II had a €280M hard cap and final close in 2024. LP co-investors include KfW, EIB, DEG, IFC, FMO, Proparco, Mubadala, and Bpifrance. The fund invests from Seed to Series C in African tech startups including fintech, logistics, e-commerce, digital health, and digital infrastructure across 27+ countries. Portfolio includes Wave, Maad Africa, Revio Pay, Foondamate, and others.
