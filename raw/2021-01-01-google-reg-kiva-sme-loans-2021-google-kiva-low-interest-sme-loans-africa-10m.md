## Description

As part of the $1 billion Africa commitment (October 2021), Google pledged $10 million in partnership with Kiva to provide low-interest loans to small and medium-sized businesses in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa. The programme enables SMEs to access affordable credit to grow their digital businesses.
