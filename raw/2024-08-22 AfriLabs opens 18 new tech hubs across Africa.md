**AfriLabs** — the pan-African network of technology and innovation hubs — admitted **18 new member hubs** in Q2 2024, strengthening its presence across all African regions, including its **first hub in Stellenbosch** (South Africa) and several in Tunisia (DigiArtLivingLab, Connect'Innov, The Dot).
