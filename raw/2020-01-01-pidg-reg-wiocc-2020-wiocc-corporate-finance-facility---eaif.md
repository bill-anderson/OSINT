## Description

EAIF loaned USD 40 million (10-year term) as part of a USD 100 million debt package raised by the West Indian Ocean Cable Company (WIOCC). Proparco provided the remaining USD 60 million. WIOCC is a pan-African internet infrastructure provider operating fibre, cable systems, and data centres across Eastern and Southern Africa. Capital spending of at least USD 379 million planned across Eastern and Southern Africa.
