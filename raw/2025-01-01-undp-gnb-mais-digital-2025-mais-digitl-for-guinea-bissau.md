## Description

National digital transformation programme for Guinea-Bissau addressing limited institutional capacities, inadequate digital infrastructure, significant gender disparities in digital literacy, and underdeveloped regulatory frameworks. Implementation of National Digital Transformation Strategy (ENTD.GW) developed with UNU-EGOV and UNDP. 2025-2030.
