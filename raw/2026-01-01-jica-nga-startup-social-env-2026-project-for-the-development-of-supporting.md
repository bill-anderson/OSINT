## Description

JICA grant providing catalytic capital through Nigeria Sovereign Investment Authority (NSIA) to a government-established startup investment fund. JICA provides $14M grant, NSIA contributes $20M to form $50M Impact Innovation Fund. Supports startups in agriculture, healthcare, education, energy, and water. Includes advisory support, business plan consultation, investor awareness, and capacity building for fund establishment. First implementation of JICA's Private Capital Mobilization Scheme globally. Signed February 20, 2026; implementation 159 months.
