# Kenya Advances Digital Economy with Fiber, Wi-Fi, and Youth Programs

Under the Ministry of Information, Communications, and the Digital Economy, Kirinyaga County has witnessed substantial investments in digital infrastructure. A total of 102.4 km of fiber-optic cable has been installed, connecting all five sub-counties to the One Government Network (OGN), thereby improving access to government services and public digital platforms.

Connectivity has expanded, with nine public Wi-Fi hotspots now operational in key locations, including Kutus Market and Kagumo Health Centre. Surveys for an additional 80 sites are underway as part of Phase II of the Digital Superhighway rollout.
