## Description

"Egypt's telecom regulator has awarded a new data center license to Hassan Allam Digital Infrastructure and Data Center Solutions for a project backed by an initial investment of $400 million." "The National Telecom Regulatory Authority (NTRA) granted a license on Monday, May 15, to Hassan Allam Digital Infrastructure and Data Center Solutions to build and operate a data center in the country. The company is developing the project in partnership with technology firm A15 and plans to invest an initial $400 million during the first phase." The facility will provide data hosting, cloud computing and AI-ready infrastructure for government agencies, financial institutions and businesses; it is the tenth data-centre licence issued in Egypt in two years.
