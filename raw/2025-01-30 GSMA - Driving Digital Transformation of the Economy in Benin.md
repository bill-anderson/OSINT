GSMA launched (30 January 2025, Cotonou) the report *Driving Digital Transformation of the Economy in Benin*, estimating that accelerated digitalisation could generate **XOF 1.2 trillion in GDP by 2028**, create **over 300,000 jobs**, and boost tax revenue by XOF 150 billion — lifting total GDP by up to **8% by 2028**. The mobile sector contributed **XOF 960 billion to GDP in 2023**.

Projected sector gains by 2028: agriculture +XOF 197bn (82,000 jobs), manufacturing +XOF 134bn (77,000 jobs), transport +XOF 74bn (25,000 jobs) and trade +XOF 39bn (18,000 jobs). The report ties its recommendations (including on policy reforms and mobile-money taxation) to Benin's National Development Plan — and its figures are the evidence base the Benin government cites for its digital budget and 2031 vision.
