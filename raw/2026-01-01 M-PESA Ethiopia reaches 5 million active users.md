# M-PESA Ethiopia reaches 5 million active users

**M-PESA Ethiopia** ([[safaricom-ethiopia]]) reached **5 million 90-day-active customers** (activity doubled over three months). Since its Aug-2023 launch it has positioned as a low-cost, interoperable wallet: **50,000+ merchants** (fees capped at ETB 5), micro-loan service **Errif Be M-PESA** (1.6m users), nationwide **School Pay**, water-utility payments in several cities, integration with **18 banks**, and an open-API strategy drawing **2,500 developers** / 900+ apps. (Consistent with the ~5.2m figure reported in Q3 FY26 results.)
