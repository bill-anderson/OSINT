## Description

Two debt tranches by BII to M-KOPA: $18M concessional/senior debt (Jun 2017) to fund receivables growth for the PAYG solar and digital lending portfolio, and a $1.99M follow-on debt facility (Apr 2021). As at 2021, both tranches exited (status: Closed/Exited). M-KOPA uses BII debt to pre-finance pay-as-you-go device portfolios across Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria, and Ghana.
