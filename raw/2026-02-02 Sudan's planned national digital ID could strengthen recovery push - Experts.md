# Sudan's planned national digital ID could strengthen recovery push: Experts

Biometric Update reporting on the positive expert reception of Sudan's plans for **SudaPass**, a
unified national digital identity system (announced Dec 2025) framed as central to post-conflict
recovery after nearly three years of SAF–RSF war.

Key points:
- SudaPass positioned not just as a technical registry but a **trust-building tool** for recovery —
  rebuilding state capacity, enabling social protection / education / healthcare delivery, boosting
  economic participation and citizen confidence.
- Ministry of Digital Transformation and Communication says launch is **imminent**; SudaPass will be
  **free**, activated via **passport details and face biometrics**.
- Experts (Yahya & Ibrahim, via a WEF blog) stress trust, inclusivity, transparency, safeguards and
  partnerships **without external control**; warn against exclusion/surveillance failures — **Kenya**
  cited as a cautionary example.
- Government recently launched the **Sudanese Cybersecurity Authority** as part of the drive; Minister
  Ahmed Dirdiri Ghandour outlined pillars at the 29th Arab Telecommunications and Information Ministers
  Council.
