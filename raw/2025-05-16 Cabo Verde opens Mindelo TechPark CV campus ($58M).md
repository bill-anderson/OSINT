# Cabo Verde opens Mindelo TechPark CV campus ($58M)

Cabo Verde officially opened the **Mindelo Technology Park** on São Vicente — the second of two **[[techpark-cv|TechPark CV]]** campuses (the first in the capital, Praia). Backed by **€45.5m from the [[african-development-bank|AfDB]]**, the **€51.85m ($58M)** initiative is a cornerstone of the country's digital-diversification strategy.

At a ceremony on **6 May 2025**, Prime Minister **José Ulisses Correia e Silva** set a goal of raising the digital economy's share of GDP from **7% to 25%**. So far **10 companies from four countries** operate in Mindelo, employing **125 young professionals**, with 80% of the building's 25 office spaces occupied — tenants spanning AI, digital health, fintech and cybersecurity. The campus houses a **data centre**, incubation hub and training centre. AfDB President **Akinwumi Adesina** called it a "turning point."

Mindelo is a designated **Special Digital Economic Zone** (tax exemptions); Cabo Verde's **Remote Working Program** (digital-nomad visa; minimum monthly income €1,500) allows stays up to 12 months. TechPark CV President Carlos Monteiro framed the ambition as a pan-African platform for cross-border startups.
