## Description

Improves Angola's statistical capacity, efficiency in data production, and use of data for public program monitoring. Supports national statistical system modernization, data collection methodology development, statistical dissemination systems, and capacity building for the National Statistics Institute (INE). Covers household surveys, administrative data systems, and statistical data platforms.
