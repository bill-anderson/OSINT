The **Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority (BOCRA)** issued a public notice (March 2024) requiring **mandatory SIM-card registration for all users**, under its mandate via the **Communications Regulatory Authority (CRA) Act 2012**, and directing licensed operators to ensure compliance — the subscriber-identification (SIM-ID) layer of Botswana's identity and communications regulation.
