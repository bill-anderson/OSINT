## Description

UNDP Inclusive Digital Transformation for Malawi (IDT4M) project. Supports Malawi's commitment to SDG 16.9 (legal identity for all), digital public infrastructure, e-government services, and ICT regulatory reform. Implementation 2024-2028. Digital ID, interoperability, and digital public services are key components.
