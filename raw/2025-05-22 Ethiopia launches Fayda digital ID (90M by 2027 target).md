# Ethiopia launches Fayda digital ID (90M-by-2027 target)

Ethiopia officially launched its **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** foundational digital ID (developed with **[[tech5]]** and **[[visa]]**) at the **ID4Africa 2025** conference in Addis Ababa (PM **Abiy Ahmed** addressed the event), targeting **90 million adults by 2027**. As of May 2025, **15m+** had enrolled. NIDP Executive Director **Yodahe Zemichael** later (4 Jun 2025) laid out the roadmap: the eligible population is "everyone, including children" (90m of 120m), segmented into pastoralists, farmers, civil servants, taxpayers, 40m+ bank-account holders and 80m+ SIM subscribers; 16.4m enrolled by early June. Central to the **[[digital-ethiopia-2030]]** strategy.
