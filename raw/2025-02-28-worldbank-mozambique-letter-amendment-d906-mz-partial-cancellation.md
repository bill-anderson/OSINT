> **Note an inconsistency in the Bank's own drafting**, reproduced verbatim below: the operative
> paragraph announces cancellation of "the amounts allocated to **Category (1)**" and then itemises
> "(a) an amount equal to SDR 3,223,331 from **Category (2)**". The attached revised withdrawal table
> and the companion restructuring paper (RES00782) both make clear the cancellation is from
> **Category (2), Emergency Expenditures under Part 5** — Category 2 falls from SDR 35,612,697 to
> SDR 32,389,366 while Category 1 is unchanged at SDR 69,387,303. The "Category (1)" reference is a
> drafting error in the letter.

                Re: REPUBLIC OF MOZAMBIQUE: Digital Governance and Economy Project

                                     Partial Cancellation

                 We refer to the Financing Agreement (“Financing Agreement”) between the Republic of
        Mozambique and the International Development Association (“World Bank”), effective as of
        December 14, 2021, and as amended to date, for the Digital Governance and Economy Project,
        pursuant to which the World Bank has extended the above captioned Grant (“Project”). We also refer
        to your letter Oficio No. 01/GAB-VICE/MEF/619/2024 dated December 30, 2024, received by the
        World Bank on February 10, 2025, requesting cancellation of an amount to the above-captioned
        Project.

              In view of the foregoing, and in accordance with the provisions of Section 6.01 of the
        General Conditions for Credits and Grants applicable to the Grant Agreement, the World Bank
        hereby cancels, as of February 10, 2025, the amounts allocated to Category (1) as set forth in
        Section III.A of Schedule 2 to the Grant Agreement as follows:

              (a) an amount equal to SDR 3,223,331 from Category (2)

              The withdrawal table set forth in Section III.A of Schedule 2 to the Grant Agreement has
        been revised accordingly and the revised withdrawal table is attached to this letter.
