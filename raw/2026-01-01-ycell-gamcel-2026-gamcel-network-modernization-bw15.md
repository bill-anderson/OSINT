# YCELL — GAMCEL network modernisation, 2026

YCELL committed GMD 6.1 billion to modernise GAMCEL's network under a Share Investment and Network Modernization Agreement, alongside acquiring an 80% equity stake in the Gambian state mobile operator.

## Description

"Under the agreement, YCELL will acquire an 80% equity stake in GAMCEL and bring both financial and technical expertise to modernise the company's network infrastructure and transform its business operations." "As part of the agreement, YCELL has committed to invest GMD 6.1 billion in a comprehensive infrastructure and network modernisation programme aimed at delivering world-class telecommunications services across The Gambia." The programme includes "nationwide deployment of 4G/LTE and 5G radio access networks, core network upgrades, and the implementation of next-generation OSS/BSS, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and convergent billing systems," a new GAMCEL head office, hybrid power at tower sites, and revenue-assurance/fraud-management systems. The agreement was signed the day before the 2026-07-10 report (signing ~2026-07-09).
