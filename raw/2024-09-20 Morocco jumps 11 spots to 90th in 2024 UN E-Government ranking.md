Morocco ranked **90th of 193 countries** in the 2024 UN E-Government Development Index (EGDI), up 11 places from 101st in 2022. Conducted by UN DESA, the index gave Morocco a score of **0.6841** (online service index 0.5618; telecommunications infrastructure index 0.8827; human capital and participation index 0.6078).

The report notes Morocco's steady improvement since 2008 (140th), reaching 82nd in 2014 before dips and recovery. Regionally Morocco ranked **fourth in Africa**, behind South Africa (40th globally), Mauritius (76th) and Tunisia (87th); Egypt placed 95th, Algeria 116th, **Libya 125th**, and Senegal 135th. Denmark topped the global index.
