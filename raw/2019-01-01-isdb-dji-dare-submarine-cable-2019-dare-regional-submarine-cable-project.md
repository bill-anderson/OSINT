## Description

The project objective is to contribute to socioeconomic development of Djibouti by providing efficient and reliable Information and Communication Technology Services. Finances the Djibouti Africa Regional Express-1 (DARE-1) submarine cable, a 3,000 km undersea cable connecting Djibouti to Tanzania and other East African countries, making Djibouti a regional ICT hub. Target: internet access for 60 million regional subscribers.
