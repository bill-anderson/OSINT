Starlink paused new orders for residential internet kits in some of Nigeria's busiest cities after reaching network capacity. Parts of Lagos (Victoria Island, Ikoyi, Lagos Island, Surulere) and Abuja are marked "Sold Out"; new subscriptions only via a waitlist with a deposit. In November 2024, Starlink had suspended nationwide orders for nearly eight months citing limited bandwidth and pending NCC approval over price adjustments; orders resumed late June 2025 after upgrades and regulatory clearance. Starlink's monthly fee rose from ~₦38,000 ($25.33) to ₦45,000 ($30) and then ~₦56,000 ($37) in 2025.
