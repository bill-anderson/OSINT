OpenCRVS v2.0 represents the biggest evolution of the platform since its original launch.  
  
Designed in response to feedback from governments and implementation partners around the world, OpenCRVS v2.0 introduces a new generation of configurable civil registration technology that enables countries to tailor workflows around their own legislation, strengthen interoperability across government systems, and deliver secure, scalable digital public services.  
  
In this webinar you'll discover:  
  
• Why OpenCRVS v2.0 was rebuilt from the ground up  
• Configurable workflows and jurisdictions  
• Enhanced offline registration capabilities  
• Verifiable Credentials for digital certificates  
• Modern architecture built for national scale  
• Independent security testing results  
• A country perspective from the Philippines Statistical Authority  
• Reflections from the World Bank Group  
• What's next for the OpenCRVS implementation community  
  
Whether you're a government official, civil registration authority, implementation partner or part of the Digital Public Infrastructure ecosystem, this webinar provides an overview of the latest innovations shaping the future of civil registration.
