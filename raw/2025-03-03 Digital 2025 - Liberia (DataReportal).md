DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Liberia (published 3 March 2025), early-2025 headlines:

- Population: 5.67 million (Jan 2025, +2.1% YoY); 54.3% urban, 45.7% rural; median age 18.8.
- Mobile connections: 5.11 million, equivalent to 90.1% of the population (+6.6% YoY); 87.2% "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- Internet users: 1.84 million, penetration 32.4% (+6.5% YoY); ~3.8 million people remained offline.
- Social media user identities: 941,000 (16.6% of population).

Source: GSMA Intelligence and Kepios. (Baseline connectivity figures for the Liberia hub.)
