Nigeria and Finland signed an MoU on digitalisation and innovation (Abuja, March 23), prioritising cybersecurity cooperation amid a surge in cyberattacks on Nigerian institutions. Signed by Minister Dr Bosun Tijani and Finland's under-secretary of state for international trade Jarno Syrjälä. The MoU covers digital governance, technology infrastructure and cybersecurity. It builds on engagements in Helsinki in February centred on Nigeria's Data Exchange Platform and Finnish participation in Project BRIDGE, involving Finnvera and Finnfund.
