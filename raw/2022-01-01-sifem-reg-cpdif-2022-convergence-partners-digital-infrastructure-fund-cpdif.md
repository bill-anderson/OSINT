## Description

SIFEM committed USD 15 million to the Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund (CPDIF), a USD 296 million fund investing in digital infrastructure and digital services companies across Sub-Saharan Africa. The fund bridges the digital divide in SSA by investing in fibre networks, data centres, cloud infrastructure and telecom platforms.
