# Airtel digitises Malawian farmer payments

**Airtel Money Malawi** and the **Agricultural Development and Marketing Corporation (Admarc)** launched a digital payment platform for the country's agricultural value chain, enabling farmers to receive payments directly into their mobile wallets and letting customers pay electronically for agricultural commodities and services. The partnership integrates Airtel Money into Admarc's operations, reducing reliance on cash.

Speaking at the launch in Blantyre, Airtel Money Malawi head of partnership acquisition Khumbo Phiri said the initiative advances Malawi's digital transformation by integrating fintech into agricultural commerce, expanding digital financial services particularly in rural communities. Admarc CEO Ben Botolo described it as a milestone in modernising the state-owned corporation's payment systems, noting direct mobile-wallet payments improve convenience for farmers and strengthen transparency through electronic transaction records.
