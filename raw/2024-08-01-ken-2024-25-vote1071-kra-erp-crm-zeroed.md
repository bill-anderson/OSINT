## Description

The single largest digital line cut by the post-withdrawal supplementary: KRA's revenue-system
modernisation (ERP and CRM under BETA) lost its entire KSh 3.44bn capital allocation - in the
same fiscal crisis that made tax-administration technology the government's stated alternative to
new tax measures (Parliament PBO, Budget Watch 2024-25).

## Notes

Adjacent KRA lines zeroed in the same schedule: secure border control points (40m), alternate
data recovery centre (17m). The irony is the record: the revenue crisis cut the revenue-system
build.
