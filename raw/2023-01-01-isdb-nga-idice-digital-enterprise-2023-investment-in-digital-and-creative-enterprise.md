## Description

Boosts innovation, creates jobs, and achieves economic transformation by fostering growth in the technology and creative ecosystem in Nigeria. Supports (i) 30 socially oriented startups, (ii) 13,000 MSMEs with Shariah-compliant financing, and (iii) 26,000 direct and indirect jobs. IsDB is one of four co-financiers (AfDB, AFD, IsDB, BOI) in the USD 618M total programme.
