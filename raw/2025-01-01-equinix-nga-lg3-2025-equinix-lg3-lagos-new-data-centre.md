## Description

Equinix announced in November 2025 plans to build LG3, its first newly-constructed data centre in West Africa, at a cost of US$22 million. Set to open in Q1 2026, LG3 is the first phase of a US$100 million investment plan to transform Africa's digital landscape over the next two years. LG3 is located in Lagos, Nigeria and will deliver new colocation capacity supporting Nigeria's strategic position as an international hub for subsea cable connections. The $100 million plan includes expansions at existing sites in Nigeria, Ghana, and Côte d'Ivoire, as well as a new facility in Port Harcourt (PR1) serving as a 2Africa cable landing station.
