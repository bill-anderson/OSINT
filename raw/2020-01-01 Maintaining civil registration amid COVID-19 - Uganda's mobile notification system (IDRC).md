Payload: Uganda's 14-day national lockdown from 31 March 2020 (extended to end-June) did not list civil registration as an essential service, so all district registration offices closed; Jan-Aug 2020 NIRA recorded ~83,200 birth events electronically, 21% of the same period in 2019; MVRS was in use in 112 of 135 districts and 222 of 5,230 birth-handling health facilities; birth-certificate holding 21% (2006), 31% (2011), 32% (2016).
