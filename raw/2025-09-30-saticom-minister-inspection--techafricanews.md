# Algeria's Telecom Minister Inspects SATICOM, Highlights Digital Innovation

September 30, 2025
1 min read
Author: Jennifer Onyeagoro

This inspection visit aligns with Algeria's broader strategy to strengthen national industry, encourage innovation, and localize digital solutions.

Algeria's Minister of Post and Wire and Wireless Transport, Syed Ali Zarouqi, paid an inspection visit on Monday, September 29, 2025, to the headquarters of the Algerian Company for Information and Communications Technology (SATICOM), a subsidiary of Algeria Telecommunications Corporation. The visit is part of a series of field engagements with key sector stakeholders.

During the visit, SATICOM officials presented an overview of the company's services and technical solutions developed for institutions and public administration. The Minister also toured the company's Data Center and IP Multimedia Subsystem Center, which support the infrastructure of Algeria Telecommunications.

The Minister underscored the importance of integrating and developing digital solutions anchored in research, development, and innovation, including open innovation approaches. He highlighted the need to expand SATICOM's activities to meet the evolving demands of the national market, while ensuring Algeria's telecommunications infrastructure is prepared for the upcoming launch of 5G services by mobile operators.

This inspection visit aligns with Algeria's broader strategy to strengthen national industry, encourage innovation, and localize digital solutions. By prioritizing these initiatives, the Ministry aims to reinforce the country's digital sovereignty and ensure long-term resilience in its communications sector.
