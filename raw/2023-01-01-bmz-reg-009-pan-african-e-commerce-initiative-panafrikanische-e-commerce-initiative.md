## Description

Context:   Electronic commerce, also known as e-commerce, is increasingly becoming an important procurement and sales channel in emerging African economies. However, key prerequisites for African companies to participate in cross-border e-commerce are not in place. These include secure and affordable electronic payment systems, consumer and business confidence in e-commerce, digital skills of micro, small and medium-sized enterprises (MSMEs) and a supportive regulatory framework.
