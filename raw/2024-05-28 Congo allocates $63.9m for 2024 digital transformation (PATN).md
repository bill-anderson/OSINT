Congo's **Digital Transformation Acceleration Project (PATN)** — launched January 2023, backed by **$100 million** from the World Bank (WB name: **Congo Digital Acceleration Project**) — set a **FY2024 budget of CFA38.7 billion ($63.9m)**, approved by its Steering Committee on 28 May 2024, to deliver **57 projects** bringing high-speed internet to public administrations, universities, secondary schools and rural areas.

It aligns with the 2022-2026 National Development Plan and the **"Congo Digital 2025"** strategy. Per the regulator **[[arpce-congo]]**, Congo had **3.4 million internet subscribers in 2023 (59.7% penetration)** and 5.9 million mobile subscribers. See [[patn-congo]].
