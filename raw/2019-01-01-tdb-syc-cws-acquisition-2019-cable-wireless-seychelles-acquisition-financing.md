## Description

TDB served as Mandated Lead Arranger for a USD 70 million syndicated acquisition financing for CWS Investment Ltd (a group of Seychellois entrepreneurs) to acquire Cable & Wireless Seychelles (CWS) from Liberty Latin America. TDB provided USD 50 million of its own balance sheet, with co-lending from Absa Group Limited and ABC Banking Corporation of Mauritius. CWS is Seychelles' leading ICT/telecoms operator, providing fixed-line, mobile, broadband, and FTTH services, placing CWS into 100% local ownership for the first time. The deal was TDB's largest transaction in Seychelles to date.
