# Digital Identity in Rwanda (CIS–RIA case study)

Companion source page for the binary artefact
`2021-10-31-Digital-Identity-in-Rwanda-CIS-RIA.pdf`.

A Rwanda **case study** by **Dr Elvis M. Binda (University of Rwanda)**, part of a
**ten-country exploration of socio-digital ID systems in Africa** run by the Centre for
Internet and Society (CIS, India) and Research ICT Africa (RIA). Critical/rights-framed
analysis of Rwanda's national ID ecosystem, its governance, and inclusion/exclusion and
data-protection risks.

**Reference baseline (as of 2021).** Historical reference — its dated figures are **not
promoted** into synthesis pages; fresher country-level data arrives via later clippings.
Cited from [[nida-rwanda]] and [[rwanda--dpi-id]] as a background reference on Rwanda's
digital-ID design and human-rights considerations.
