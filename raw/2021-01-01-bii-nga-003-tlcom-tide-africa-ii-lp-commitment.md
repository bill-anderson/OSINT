## Description

BII committed $10M to TLCom TIDE Africa II LP in November 2021. TLCom is an Africa-focused venture capital investor that supports early-stage tech businesses. TIDE Africa II continues TLCom's mandate to invest in tech-enabled businesses across SSA, with a focus on Nigeria and other high-growth African tech ecosystems. The fund targets fintech, logistics, health-tech, and digital platforms.
