Paystack (owned by The Stack Group) launched Paystack Index, an experimental product letting users in Nigeria check out with supported Paystack merchants using AI agents. Developed with TSG Labs, it builds on Paystack Checkout and Zap to let users complete transactions through AI while keeping payments on Paystack's infrastructure. It works with supported AI clients including Claude, ChatGPT and OpenClaw, with early access launching first with Zap users. A Google-Ipsos survey found 88% of Nigerians surveyed had used generative AI over the past year (62% for everyday tasks). CEO Shola Akinlade: "As AI agents become a more common way for people to search, decide, and take action, we think checkout has to evolve too."
