# NCA Quarterly Statistical Bulletin on Communications — Q2 2024

NCA official telecom statistics for **Q2 (Apr–Jun) 2024**; reference statistical resource (cite, do not promote as current). Key figures as of end-Q2 2024:
- **Mobile voice subscriptions: 37.55m** (from 34.88m in Q1 2024, +7.7%; YoY from 34.15m Q2 2023, +9.95%). **Penetration 114.63%** (up from 107.01% Q1 2024).
- **Market share (3 MNOs):** MTN **75.41%** (28.32m), Telecel **16.97%** (6.37m), AT **7.62%** (2.86m). Prepaid = 98.86% of voice subscriptions.
- Bulletin also covers mobile data subscriptions/penetration, BWA, fixed network, traffic and tariffs, plus authorised FM/TV stations.
