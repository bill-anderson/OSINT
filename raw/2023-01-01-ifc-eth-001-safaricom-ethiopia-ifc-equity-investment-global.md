## Description

IFC made a $157.4 million equity investment in Global Partnership for Ethiopia B.V. (GPE), the consortium holding entity for Safaricom Ethiopia — Ethiopia's second telecoms licence awarded in 2021. The consortium is led by Safaricom PLC (Kenya), Vodafone, Vodacom, Sumitomo Corporation, and others. Safaricom Ethiopia launched commercial services in 2022 and is investing over $8 billion across 10 years. As of 2024 the network provides 4G connectivity to millions of Ethiopians and has launched M-PESA mobile money, significantly advancing digital financial inclusion. IFC holds a 7.25% equity stake via GPE.
