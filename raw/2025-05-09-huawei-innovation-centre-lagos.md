Huawei Nigeria opened a new innovation centre in Lagos, unveiled at Huawei Day Nigeria 2025 ("Accelerating Industrial Intelligence"). Attended by Minister Dr Bosun Tijani, Lagos Deputy Governor Dr Obafemi Hamzat, and Huawei executives. CEO Chris Lu described it as a platform for customers, partners and ICT talent to explore latest technologies, noting Huawei has operated in Nigeria since 1999. Tijani welcomed the centre as "a catalyst for Nigeria's tech sovereignty" that will promote local co-creation above technology consumption. Hamzat cited data sovereignty achieved through localised cloud solutions.
