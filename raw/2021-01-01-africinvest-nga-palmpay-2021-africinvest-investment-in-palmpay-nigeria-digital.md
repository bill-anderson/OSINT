## Description

AfricInvest participates in PalmPay's USD 100 million Series A funding round (closed August 2021) alongside Chuangshi Capital, Yunshi Equity Investment Management, Trust Capital and Chengyu Capital. PalmPay is a Nigerian digital payments and financial services platform offering zero-fee transfers, bill payments, mobile money, merchant POS acquiring, digital wallets, and lending services. Company operates via the Cathay AfricInvest Innovation Fund (CAIF). PalmPay subsequently became one of Africa's fastest-growing fintechs with over 35 million registered users.
