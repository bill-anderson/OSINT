## Description

On August 31, 2018, the Government of Côte d'Ivoire signed a set of loan agreements with Bank of China for Phase 1 of the Digital Education Project (captured via Project ID#89413 and Project ID#91764) and Phase 1 of the Ministry of Interior and Security Unified Communication System Project (captured via Project ID#89415 and Project ID#92201). According to the Government of Côte d'Ivoire’s Aid Management Platform (AMP), Bank of China signed two loan agreements with the Government of Côte d'Ivoire for Phase 1 of the Ministry of Interior and Security Unified Communication System Project: a CFA 42,754,805,764.67 loan agreement (captured via Project ID#89415) and a CFA 7,018,739,900 loan agreement (captured via Project ID#92201). The borrower was expected to use the proceeds of the CFA 42,754,805,764.67 loan to finance 85% of the cost of the commercial contract between Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. and Côte d'Ivoire’s Ministry of Interior and Security. It was expected to use the proceeds of the CFA 7,018,739,900 loan to finance 15% of the cost of the commercial contract between Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. and Côte d'Ivoire’s Ministry of Interior and Security.   As of April 2022, 6 disbursements (worth CFA 42,077,004,262) had been made under the CFA 42,754,805,764.67 loan: a CFA 9,568,049,781 disbursement on June 30, 2019, a CFA 15,478,003,812 disbursement on October 31, 2019, a CFA 10,938,194,160 disbursement on January 31, 2020, a CFA 3,896,402,757 disbursement on March 31, 2020, a CFA 827,825,441 on May 31, 2020, and a disbursement of CFA 1,368,528,311 on August 31, 2020. A single disbursement was made under the CFA 7,018,739,900 loan: a CFA 7,019,988,777 disbursement on April 30, 2019.   The purpose of the project is to establish a unified and secure communication system for Côte d'Ivoire’s Ministry of Interior and Security.
