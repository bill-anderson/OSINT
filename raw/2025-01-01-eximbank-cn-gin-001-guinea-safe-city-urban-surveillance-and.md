## Description

## Notes

Loan agreement signed June 30 2025 between Republic of Guinea and Export-Import Bank of China. RMB 385,329,021 = ~USD 54M at 7.25 rate (2025). Terms: 2% interest, 5-year grace period, 15-year repayment = 20-year total maturity. Scope: 324 surveillance sites in 8 cities for urban security, emergency response, and urban management. President Mamadi Doumbouya promulgated ratification law (L/2026-9/CNT, decree n°2026-201/PRG/SG) on June 16 2026.
