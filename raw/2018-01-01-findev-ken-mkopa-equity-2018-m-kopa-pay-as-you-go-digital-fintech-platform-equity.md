## Description

FinDev Canada's inaugural investment: USD 10 million equity stake in M-KOPA, a Kenyan-based pay-as-you-go fintech platform providing smartphones, solar energy, and consumer appliances to off-grid low-income households via digital micropayments and IoT connectivity. A USD 2 million follow-on equity tranche was disclosed in 2020 as part of the original deal, bringing total to USD 12 million. M-KOPA operates across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Ghana, and Nigeria.
