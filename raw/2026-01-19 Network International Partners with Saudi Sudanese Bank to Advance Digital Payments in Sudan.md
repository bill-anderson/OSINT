Network International, a Middle East and Africa fintech, has signed a strategic processing agreement with Saudi Sudanese Bank, one of Sudan's prominent financial institutions. The partnership marks Network International's expansion into Sudan.

Under the agreement, Network International will provide the bank with end-to-end digital payment processing services, including Mastercard Sponsorship, prepaid issuing, and value-added solutions to support digital transformation. The collaboration aims to modernise Sudan's financial ecosystem, improve trade settlement efficiency, and create new investment channels between markets. It was formalised during Cairo ICT 2025.
