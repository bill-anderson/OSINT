## Description

IFC provided a $100 million senior term loan to Liquid Telecommunications South Africa (Pty) Ltd, the South African subsidiary of Liquid Intelligent Technologies (formerly Liquid Telecom). The financing supports expansion of fibre optic network capacity and data centre operations across South Africa, enabling affordable broadband for businesses and consumers. South Africa is Liquid's largest single market and home to its Johannesburg-based data centres serving as regional hubs for cloud, colocation, and IP transit services.
