# Biometric Digital-ID in Africa: Ten Country Case Studies (Dec 2025)

A **continental reference volume** (December 2025; edited by 'Gbenga Sesan and Tony Roberts) assessing
the **progress and challenges of biometric digital ID across ten African countries** — a rights,
inclusion and governance lens on the continent's digital-ID wave ([[dpi.id]], [[gov.protect]],
[[colonialism]]). The comparative frame against which country ID stories (Liberia, Kenya, Ghana, etc.)
can be read. Reference document; cite, don't absorb.
