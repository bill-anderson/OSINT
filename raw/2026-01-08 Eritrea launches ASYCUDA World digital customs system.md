# Eritrea launches ASYCUDA World digital customs system

The State of Eritrea inaugurated a **digital customs management system** (Jan 2026), developed with **UNDP** and international partners, replacing legacy software in use since 2007. The system — **ASYCUDA World**, UNCTAD's automated customs platform deployed in 100+ countries — came with a **complete transfer of source code to the Eritrean government**, ensuring **national ownership** and letting local IT staff customise it (already under way). Framed as a cornerstone of the government's fiscal and economic reform agenda, moving from a fully paper-based environment to streamline customs, boost revenue collection and improve trade efficiency.
