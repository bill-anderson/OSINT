## Description

EAIF provided USD 33 million in debt financing to Raxio Data Centres, a pan-African data centre platform. Raxio operates carrier-neutral data centres in Uganda, Ethiopia, DRC, Cote d'Ivoire, Mozambique, Angola and Tanzania. The investment reduces latency and improves digital infrastructure across sub-Saharan Africa, supporting cloud adoption and digital transformation.
