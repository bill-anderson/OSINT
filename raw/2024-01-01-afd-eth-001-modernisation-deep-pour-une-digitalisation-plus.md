## Description

Co-financing with EU, EIB and DGT. The project aims to improve access rates and quality of electricity and telecommunications networks, and to strengthen their efficiency and resilience. Sovereign loans: AFD €80M, EIB €83M, DGT €55M+; technical assistance and grants: EU €40M, AFD €1.7M. Hybrid energy + digital project; included as sovereign FR-3 AFD on user instruction (initially excluded by automated cascade per §1.1 energy primary-purpose; user override).
