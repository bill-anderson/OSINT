# Lesotho, US forge AI future

*Public Eye, 17 June 2026. By Mosa Maoeng. New digital programme launched for youth enterprises.*

The Ministry of Gender, Youth and Social Development, in partnership with the United States Embassy in Maseru and the American Corner, launched the Artificial Intelligence (AI) Skills Development Programme at the American Corner Maseru — described as the country's first digital entrepreneurship training integrating e-commerce and advanced AI tools for business practitioners.

The programme expands the Sebabatso Initiative, PM Ntsokoane Matekane's flagship youth empowerment project launched October 2023. Minister of Gender, Youth and Social Development Pitso Lesaoana said the Sebabatso ecosystem has generated over M21 million in revenue over three years, creating 383 jobs across more than 50 companies. US Chargé d'Affaires Thomas Hines said AI is "a present-day bridge connecting local enterprise to global markets," and that the curriculum covers marketing content generation, customer-data analysis, task automation and product translation via platforms like Shopify.

The piece notes the government has approved a National AI Policy and, at the 8th African Science, Technology and Innovation Forum, advanced a framework for AI-ready data management. Hines suggested Lesotho could eventually leverage its energy resources to become a hub for data centres supporting the global AI industry.
