## Description

FCDO grant to the Open Government Partnership to promote open and accountable use of emerging digital technologies, especially ensuring democratic safeguards for AI and automation. Translates global pledges into country-level action. Supports digital governance reforms in priority countries: Ghana, Indonesia, Kenya, Nigeria, Dominican Republic. Accelerates collective norm-setting on digital governance and links global forums on democracy and digital policy. SSA beneficiary countries are Kenya, Ghana and Nigeria.
