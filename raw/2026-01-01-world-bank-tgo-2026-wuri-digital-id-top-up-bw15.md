# World Bank — Togo WURI digital-ID additional financing, 2026

The World Bank signed an additional US$20 million for Togo's WURI digital-identity project on 16 July 2026, within a broader CFA257.4 billion (US$429 million) five-agreement package for Vision 2040.

## Description

"Togo and the World Bank Group signed five financing agreements worth a combined $429 million, or about 257.4 billion CFA francs, in Lomé on Thursday, July 16, 2026." "Under the 'Unite' pillar, the West Africa Unique Identification for Regional Integration and Inclusion Project, known as WURI, will receive an additional $20 million to expand access to secure identification." The other agreements — PRIME-GAS ($40m) and WA-REMP ($100m) energy, COSO social cohesion ($69m) and PASLT logistics ($200m) — fall outside data governance / digital transformation.
