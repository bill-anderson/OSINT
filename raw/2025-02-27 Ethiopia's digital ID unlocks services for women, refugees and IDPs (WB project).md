# Ethiopia's digital ID unlocks services for women, refugees and IDPs (World Bank project)

The World Bank's **[[ethiopia-digital-id-inclusion-project|Ethiopia Digital ID for Inclusion and Services Project]]** ($350m) supports the National ID Program to give **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** IDs to **90 million people, including refugees and IDPs**. Launched 2024, it targets those who currently lack identification (excluded from the formal economy), aiming to cut double benefit-collection and ghost accounts, streamline safety-net payments and grow trust in digital services. **12m+ registered as of February 2025.**
