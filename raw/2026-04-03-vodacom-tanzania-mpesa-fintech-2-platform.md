On 2nd April 2026, Vodacom Tanzania launched a USD 28 million M-Pesa Fintech 2.0 platform, upgrading the service to a new-generation system designed to support higher transaction volumes and system stability. Vodacom stated the platform is built to process over 1,000 transactions per second, equivalent to approximately 86 million transactions per day, and targets 99.999% availability (less than five minutes of downtime per year). The platform integrates AI-based monitoring, auto-scaling, and automatic failover.

Speaking at the launch at Vodacom Headquarters in Dar es Salaam, CEO Philip Besiimire said: "This investment is about futureproofing one of Tanzania's most important financial platforms. We are strengthening the foundation of M-Pesa to ensure it remains secure, reliable, and ready to support innovation at scale."

Launched in 2008, M-Pesa is one of the largest mobile money platforms in Tanzania, accounting for 41% of approximately 76 million mobile money accounts. The platform processes more than TZS 16 trillion in transaction value per month, and its merchant network has grown to over 600,000 merchants nationwide, up from 10,000-15,000 five years ago.
