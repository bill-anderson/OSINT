# Central Bank of Egypt approves first digital bank

The Central Bank of Egypt gave Misr Digital Innovation (MDI), a subsidiary of state-owned Banque Misr, initial approval to establish Egypt's first fully digital bank, "onebank." MDI was founded in 2020; onebank — which eliminates physical branches — was preparing to roll out products in 2026 to promote financial inclusion. Khaled El Attar (former vice minister of Digital Transformation at MCIT) was appointed chairperson.
