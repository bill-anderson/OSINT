Comoros completed construction of a new **public-administration data centre**, with inauguration due **19 May 2025**. Co-managed by the **National Agency for Digital Development ([[anaden]]**) and **Comores Cable**, the **Tier 3** facility has **44.4 terabits** of hosting capacity and will securely host State and private-sector digital platforms and centralise public data to international standards — the physical component of the AfDB-funded **[[project-digitization-comorian-economy]]**.
