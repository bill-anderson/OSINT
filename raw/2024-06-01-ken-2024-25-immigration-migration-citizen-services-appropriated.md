## Description

Passports/biometric visas (1.5m e-passports, 2.3m biometric visas per ministry statement) and the
e-Citizen platform line. The ministry's June-2024 X statement rounded this to "KSh 7.03 billion
for migration and citizen services".

## Notes

**Stage context:** Vote 1024 travelled 14,959.8m (original) -> 14,270.5m (Supp I) -> 20,121.0m (Supp II) -> 20,221.0m (Supp III, final); overall vote absorption 102% - COB attributes the excess to Supplementary III cutting allocations against already-incurred expenditure.
Supp II transformed this programme: Immigration Services 5,982.9m -> 9,492.4m and e-Citizen
607.6m -> 2,607.7m (both recovered from the Supp III comparator), taking the programme to
12,261.9m. BIRR absorption: Immigration 104% (excess), e-Citizen 100%.
