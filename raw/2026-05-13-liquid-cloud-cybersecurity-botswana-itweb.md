# Liquid expands service portfolio in Botswana

By Nsuku Khosa, ITWeb intern, Johannesburg, 13 May 2026

Liquid Intelligent Technologies revitalises access to cloud and cyber security services in support of improved national digital resilience.

Liquid Intelligent Technologies, a business of Cassava Technologies, has announced a major expansion of its service portfolio in Botswana, introducing advanced cloud and cybersecurity solutions aimed at strengthening the country's digital resilience.

The company said these services will be available to existing and potential customers in Botswana, and at the centre of the new offering is Secure360, its integrated security framework that enables organisations to move beyond reactive breach response towards proactive intelligence, protection and assurance.

Liquid said in a statement: "The solution combines local delivery with continental-scale infrastructure and global technology partnerships to provide organisations with enterprise-grade digital security and cloud capabilities aligned with national digital priorities."

Odirile Tamajobe, Managing Director of Liquid Intelligent Technologies Botswana, said that by introducing cloud and cybersecurity services to the country, the company is empowering local businesses with digital solutions, enabling them to compete on a global scale.

The company went on to say the expansion of Liquid's offerings in the market reflects the broader Cassava strategy to deliver integrated digital infrastructure and platforms through its One Cassava approach.

Ziaad Suleman, CEO of Cassava Technologies SA and Botswana, said: "Beyond cloud and cyber security, customers can access data centres, AI readiness reviews, and tailored technology journey roadmaps, all within a unified platform designed to support secure innovation and long-term digital resilience".
