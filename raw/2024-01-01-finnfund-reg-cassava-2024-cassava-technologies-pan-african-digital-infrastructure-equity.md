## Description

Finnfund participated in a USD 90 million equity investment in Cassava Technologies alongside DFC and Google. Cassava is a pan-African ICT platform operating Liquid Intelligent Technologies (fibre), Africa Data Centres, Liquid C2 (cloud), Cassava.ai (AI), and Sasai Fintech across multiple SSA countries. Investment supports digital infrastructure expansion and connectivity for millions across the continent.
