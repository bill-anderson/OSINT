# Senegal launches sovereign cloud initiative with Google

In **late September 2024** Senegal's digital ministry signed an **MoU with [[google]] to establish a
sovereign cloud** ([[google-senegal-sovereign-cloud]]) — keeping storage/management of critical data in local
infrastructure across strategic sectors (finance, health, security) and building local cloud skills. Followed
President Faye's US visit for the 79th UNGA (21–28 Sep 2024) to advance the **[[new-deal-technologique]]**.
Senegal targets the digital sector at 10% of GDP by 2030; scored **0.5142 on the UN E-Government Survey 2024**
(above the continental average 0.4247); ITU cybersecurity Tier 3, score 51.61.
