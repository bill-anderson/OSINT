# EthSwitch — P2P transfers surpass ATM withdrawals (FY2024/25)

For the first time, **P2P interoperable transactions exceeded ATM withdrawals** in volume (Oct 2024) — a cashless-economy marker. In FY ending 30 June 2025, **[[ethswitch]]** processed a record **287.4m interoperable transactions** (birr 741.1bn / ~$12.8bn): **P2P 128.3m** (+158%, birr 577.7bn, ~50% of operating income), ATM 119.3m (+26%), POS 2.78m (+27%). Evidence of Ethiopia's rapid shift to account-to-account digital payments.
