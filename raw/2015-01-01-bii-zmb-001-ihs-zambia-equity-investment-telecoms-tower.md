## Description

BII made two equity investments in IHS Zambia Limited, the sole independent tower operator in Zambia owning and operating over 1,700 telecommunications towers. Investment 1: $40M equity (Oct 2015). Investment 2: $25.5M equity (Jul 2017). IHS Zambia enables mobile network operators to offer 2G/3G/4G connectivity services across Zambia by providing shared infrastructure. Both investments exited by March 2021. IHS Towers is listed on NYSE. This investment directly underpins mobile internet and broadband access across Zambia.
