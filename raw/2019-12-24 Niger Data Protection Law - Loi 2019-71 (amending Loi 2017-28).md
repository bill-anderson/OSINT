Loi No. 2019-71 of 24 December 2019, amending Law No. 2017-28 of 3 May 2017 on the protection of personal data in Niger. As a legal instrument it governs the collection and processing of personal data and underpins the mandate of the data-protection authority (HAPDP). This amending statute was later repealed by Law No. 2022-59 (16 December 2022) in the subsequent overhaul of the regime.

Primary legal-instrument artefact; summary describes what the law does rather than transcribing its provisions.
