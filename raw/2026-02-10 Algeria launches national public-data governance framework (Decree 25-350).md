# Algeria launches national public-data governance framework (Presidential Decree 25-350)

Prime Minister **Sifi Ghrieb** launched Algeria's **national data-governance framework** on **Monday 9 February 2026**, presented as a foundation for state modernisation. It establishes a **sovereign model** treating data as a strategic asset, providing a legal, institutional and technical framework to organise the collection, storage, sharing and use of **public data**; strengthen information-system security; ensure **interoperability between public administrations**; and build a **unified national database of data sources**. Institutions are to exchange information through secure, standardised channels, reducing data silos and improving the reliability of public statistics.

Formally anchored in **Presidential Decree No. 25-350 of 30 December 2025** (see [[algeria-data-governance-framework]]). It aligns with **"Digital Algeria 2030"** ([[digital-algeria-2030]]) and builds on **five regional data centres** commissioned to host and secure public information.
