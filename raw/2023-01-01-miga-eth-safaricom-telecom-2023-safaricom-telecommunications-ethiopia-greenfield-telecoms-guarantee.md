## Description

MIGA 10-year political risk guarantee of $1 billion covering equity investments by Safaricom Ethiopia shareholders (Vodafone Group UK, Vodacom South Africa, Safaricom Kenya, British International Investment UK) in Safaricom Telecommunications Ethiopia Plc. Ethiopia's first privately licensed telecoms operator (greenfield), expected to provide mobile telephony and high-speed internet to tens of millions of people in a market that previously had absolute state monopoly. Total project cost $8 billion over 10 years. MIGA guarantee includes $76M first-loss layer from IDA20 PSW. Risks: Expropriation, War and Civil Disturbance.
