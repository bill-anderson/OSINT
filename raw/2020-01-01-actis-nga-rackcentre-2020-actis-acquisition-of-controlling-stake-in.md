## Description

Actis takes a controlling stake in Rack Centre, Nigeria's leading carrier-neutral co-location data centre in Lagos, as the anchor investment of a US$250 million pan-African data centre platform established jointly with Convergence Partners. Rack Centre hosts over 80 international, regional and local clients including carriers, cloud providers and hyperscalers. Platform strategy: buy-and-build, targeting carrier-neutral data centres across key African markets. Actis partnered with Convergence Partners as ICT infrastructure expert and Tim Parsonson (Teraco co-founder) as chairman.
