## Description

On December 4, 2015, China Development Bank (CDB) and ZTE Corporation co-provided a USD 300 million loan to Econet Wireless Zimbabwe (the Zimbabwe subsidiary of Econet Wireless Global), announced during Chinese President Xi Jinping's visit to South Africa. The group-level facility totalled USD 500 million, with USD 300 million channelled to Econet Zimbabwe. The individual contributions of CDB and ZTE to the USD 300 million loan are unknown; AidData (GCDF v3, Record ID #100875) assumes an equal split of USD 150 million each. Proceeds were intended for ICT market consolidation and new services, including mobile network expansion. ZTE is both a vendor-credit provider and the equipment supplier for this facility. Co-financed by China Development Bank; see china.csv deal-id cdb-zwe-001 for the CDB share (USD 150 million estimated, recorded under financier-id=cdb).
