# Mars Growth Capital (Liquidity/MUFG) — NALA payment-rails credit facility, 2026

Tanzanian fintech NALA secured a US$25 million credit facility (expandable to US$50 million) from Liquidity — a platform backed by Japan's MUFG through Mars Growth Capital — to scale reliable, stablecoin-linked payment infrastructure across international remittance corridors, announced 28 May 2026.

## Description

"On 28 May 2026, NALA announced it had secured a US$25 million credit facility, which can rise to US$50 million, from Liquidity, a platform backed by Japan's MUFG through Mars Growth Capital." NALA said the facility will support "reliable and scalable payment infrastructure across international remittance corridors," including stablecoin-linked payment corridors.
