## Description

MIGA guarantee to The Rise Fund II Aurora S.à r.l (TPG) for its equity investment in Airtel Money Tanzania, the latest addition to the $180 million Airtel Mobile Commerce program. MIGA's first contract in Tanzania since 2014. Services: mobile wallet, airtime recharging, bill payments, merchant payments, international transfers. Risks: war and civil disturbance, expropriation, transfer restriction for up to 3 years.
