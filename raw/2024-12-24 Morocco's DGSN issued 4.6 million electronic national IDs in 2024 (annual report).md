The **[[dgsn-morocco|DGSN]]** (General Directorate of National Security) 2024 annual report details advances in digital identity: the DGSN issued nearly **4.7 million electronic national identity cards in 2024** (3.4m at the Rabat centre, 1.2m at the new Marrakech facility) and implemented the second phase of its electronic identity system through the **"Trusted Third Party Identity Verification"** platform.

The digital transformation yielded **17 framework agreements** (Bank Al-Maghrib, banking groups, healthcare providers); more than **30 public and private institutions** received authorization to use the verification platform (integrating criminal records, prison administration and foreign trade). To reach remote areas, the DGSN deployed 80 mobile units serving 130,000+ citizens and established 71 centres at diplomatic missions (219,408 e-ID cards for expatriates). The new **E-Police** portal launched in 2024 (criminal-record requests, appointment scheduling), and the **E-Blagh** online-crime platform received 12,614 reports since its June-2024 launch.
