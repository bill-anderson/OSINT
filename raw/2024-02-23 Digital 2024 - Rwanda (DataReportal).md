DataReportal's annual reference profile of digital adoption and use in Rwanda, with headline figures for early 2024. (Baseline reference data — figures are as of January 2024 and are superseded by later country-level reporting.)

**Headline figures (early 2024):**

- There were 4.91 million internet users in Rwanda at the start of 2024, when internet penetration stood at 34.4 percent of the total population. Internet users increased by 110 thousand (+2.3 percent) between January 2023 and January 2024. Some 9.35 million people (65.6 percent of the population) remained offline at the beginning of the year.
- Rwanda was home to 930.4 thousand social media users in January 2024, equating to 6.5 percent of the total population. Social media users increased by 286 thousand (+44.3 percent) year on year. 36.2 percent of social media users were female and 63.8 percent male.
- A total of 12.29 million cellular mobile connections were active in Rwanda in early 2024, equivalent to 86.2 percent of the total population.

**Population context:** Rwanda's population stood at 14.25 million in January 2024, up 319 thousand (+2.3 percent) year on year. 51.0 percent female, 49.0 percent male. 18.0 percent lived in urban centres and 82.0 percent in rural areas. Median age 19.5.

**Connection speeds (Ookla, start of 2024):** median mobile internet connection speed 21.11 Mbps; median fixed internet connection speed 29.48 Mbps (fixed speeds up 19.33 Mbps / +190 percent over the prior twelve months).
