## Description

Concessional loan financing for deployment of Intelligent Transport System across Nairobi, including 25 roundabouts with AI-enabled traffic management, CCTV cameras, sensors, and smart city backbone infrastructure. Phase III covers 125 additional intersections.

## Notes

Loan agreement signed November 26 2025 between Kenya Treasury CS John Mbadi and China Eximbank Deputy General Manager Zhu Jia (Sovereign Business Dept). Ksh 24 billion = USD 185 million. Scope: 25 Nairobi roundabouts with digital traffic lights, AI sensors, CCTV cameras; Phase III covers 125 additional intersections to form smart city backbone. Contractor not yet named. China Eximbank confirmed on own website (english.eximbank.gov.cn Dec 2025). Surveillance dimension: real-time CCTV for traffic and road safety enforcement. Status Pipeline as loan signed but implementation not yet commenced. cat-add-codes: 250400 (CCTV/surveillance component), 250200 (smart city data backbone).
