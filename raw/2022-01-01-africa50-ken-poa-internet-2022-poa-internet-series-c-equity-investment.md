## Description

Africa50 led a USD 28 million Series C equity financing round into Poa! Internet, a Kenyan internet service provider delivering affordable unlimited broadband to underserved low- and medium-income communities in Nairobi, Mombasa and Nakuru. Africa50 was the lead investor; other participants included Novastar Ventures, Seedstars Africa Ventures and Taru Capital. Funds used for national rollout across Kenya and planned expansion into additional African markets. As of 2023 Poa! had over 45,000 home internet customers and 10,000 street Wi-Fi users.
