# CRDI (IDRC) — CITADEL AI research centre (Burkina Faso), 2025

Canada's IDRC (CRDI) awarded ~1 million Canadian dollars in competitive international financing to CITADEL, Burkina Faso's AI-for-development centre of excellence at the Université Virtuelle du Burkina Faso, funding a second phase (CITADEL 2.0) from April 2025.
