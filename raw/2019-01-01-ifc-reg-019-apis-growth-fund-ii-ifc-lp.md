## Description

IFC committed $25 million as a limited partner in Apis Growth Fund II LP, targeting $400 million in total commitments. Apis Partners invests in mid-market financial services and fintech companies across Africa and South Asia, focusing on payments infrastructure, digital credit, mobile insurance, and distribution platforms. Africa is a primary focus with significant portfolio exposure to SSA fintech and digital financial services. Co-investors include BII, FMO, Proparco, DEG, Swedfund, and EIB.
