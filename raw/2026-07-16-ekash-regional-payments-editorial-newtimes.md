# eKash is a welcome step towards seamless regional payments

Image caption: Customers at Bank of Kigali main branch. Starting on July 14, customers can now transfer up to Rwf10 million per transaction between bank accounts and mobile wallets. File

Related context (for background, not this editorial's text): from 14 July 2026 the National Bank of Rwanda completed the migration of all domestic interoperable retail payments onto eKash, Rwanda's national instant payment system operated by RSwitch. The change lets customers transfer up to Rwf10 million per transaction between bank accounts and mobile wallets at a capped fee of Rwf20 per person-to-person transfer. eKash, launched in 2022, now connects 22 financial institutions; RSwitch says the next phase will integrate more government payment services and enable cross-border instant payments.
