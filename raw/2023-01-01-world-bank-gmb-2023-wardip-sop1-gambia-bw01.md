# World Bank — WARDIP SOP1, The Gambia, 2023

The World Bank committed an SDR 38.1m (US$50m) IDA grant to The Gambia under WARDIP SOP1 for broadband access and West African digital-market integration.

## Description

The Gambia's national allocation under the Digital Transformation for Africa / Western Africa Regional Digital Integration Program (DTfA/WARDIP) Series of Projects 1 (PAD4756, Operation ID P176932, 7 November 2023): "SDR 38.1 million (US$50.0 million equivalent) to the Republic of The Gambia" as an IDA grant. The SOP objective is to increase broadband access and usage and advance the West African single digital market. Total operation US$293.00m (US$266.50m IDA) across The Gambia, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Mauritania, ECOWAS, the AU and Smart Africa.
