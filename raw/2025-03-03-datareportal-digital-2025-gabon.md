DataReportal's Digital 2025: Gabon report — headlines for early 2025:

- **Mobile connections:** 3.19 million cellular mobile connections in early 2025, equivalent to 124% of the total population (up 139k / +4.6% YoY). GSMA Intelligence indicates 88.0% are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- **Internet users:** 1.84 million internet users in January 2025, an internet penetration of 71.9% (up 39k / +2.2% YoY). This implies 721k people (28.1%) remained offline.
- **Social media:** 782 thousand social media user identities in January 2025, equating to 30.5% of the total population.
- **Population:** 2.57 million in January 2025 (+54k / +2.2% YoY); 91.4% urban, 8.6% rural; median age 21.5.

Source: DataReportal (Kepios / We Are Social / Meltwater), GSMA Intelligence data.
