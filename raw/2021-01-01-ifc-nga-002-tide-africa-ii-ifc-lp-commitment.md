## Description

IFC committed $10 million as a limited partner in TIDE Africa II Limited Partnership, an Africa-focused venture capital fund investing in Seed to Series A tech and tech-enabled companies mainly in East and West Africa. TIDE II is managed by TLCom Capital, one of Africa's most prominent early-stage technology venture investors. The fund targets fintech, logistics tech, health-tech, edtech, and digital platforms. Co-investors include BII, FMO, Proparco, and DEG. This is IFC's second investment in TLCom funds (following the $15M commitment to TIDE Africa I managed by BII).
