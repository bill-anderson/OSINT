## Description

As part of the $1 billion Africa commitment, Google pledged $40 million in cash and in-kind support to nonprofits improving lives across Africa over five years. Confirmed in October 2022 progress report. Includes a $3 million grant to AirQo (Uganda) for air quality monitoring expansion. Supports nonprofits working on digital inclusion, health, education, and economic development.
