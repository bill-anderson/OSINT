The NCC launched a renewed effort to end the sale and use of pre-registered SIM cards and strengthen telecom regulations, announced at a public inquiry on three subsidiary legislations — Licensing Regulations 2019, Enforcement Process Regulations 2019, and Internet Code of Practice 2019. EVC Dr Aminu Maida (represented by Barrister Rimini Makama) described pre-registered SIM cards and call masking as threats to national security and operator revenue. The updated Internet Code of Practice covers cybersecurity, data protection, AI, child online safety and anti-spam. The review followed a six-month consultation.
