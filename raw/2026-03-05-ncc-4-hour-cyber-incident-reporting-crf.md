The NCC introduced a Cyber Resilience Framework for the Nigerian Communication Sector (CRF-NCS) requiring major telecom operators to report significant cybersecurity threats and incidents within four hours of detection. Licensed service providers (including MTN Nigeria, Airtel Nigeria and Globacom) must report breaches to the NCC's Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT). The framework was released February 23, 2026; operators have 12 months to achieve full compliance, though the NCC may begin compliance reviews earlier.
