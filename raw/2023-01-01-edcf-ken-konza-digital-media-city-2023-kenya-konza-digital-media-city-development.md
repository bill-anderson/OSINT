## Description

EDCF/Korea Eximbank concessional financing (~$130-284M, 2023-2024) for 160-acre Digital Media City at Konza Technopolis. Includes multimedia production infrastructure, post-production studios, gaming/animation facilities, media training institutes, and broadcasting stations. 5,000 jobs annually targeted.
