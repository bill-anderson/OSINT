CIO Africa coverage of Raxio Mozambique (Raxio MZ1), the country's first Tier III Uptime-certified data centre, located in Beluluane Industrial Park (MozParks) near Maputo. It is described as foundational for Mozambique's growing digital economy and the government's digital-transformation agenda.

Raxio MZ1 is strategically located near three subsea cable landings, including the high-capacity 2Africa cable, offering strong international connectivity and a redundant route for Southern Africa. Over 80 percent of Mozambique's electricity is produced from renewables (one of the highest in Africa); in keeping with Raxio's sustainability commitment, a high proportion of MZ1's power comes from renewable sources (hydro-grid plus local solar).
