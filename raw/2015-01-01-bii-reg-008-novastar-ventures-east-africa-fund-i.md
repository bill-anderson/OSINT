## Description

BII committed $15M to Novastar Ventures East Africa Fund I LP, an early-stage tech-enabled VC fund focused on East and West Africa. Novastar invests in innovative businesses serving large consumer markets. Portfolio spans agritech, financial services, digital health, digital consumer platforms, and logistics tech. This is BII's anchor LP commitment in Novastar's first fund, which backed companies including Apollo Agriculture (AI-powered smallholder credit), M-Kopa (digital PAYG), and other tech-enabled businesses. Fund remains active.
