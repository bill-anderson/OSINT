TechAfrica News. Intelsat has been selected by Orange Mali SA to bring 3G and 4G connectivity to hard-to-reach areas of Mali via satellite. The agreement marks "a first in Francophone West Africa", the successful deployment of 4G networks over satellite, judged the optimal solution given the country's size and logistics. Orange Mali is the leading MNO (OOKLA-awarded "Western Africa's fastest mobile network" and "Mali's fastest ISP"). A later report (We Are Tech, Nov 2025) states the Intelsat partnership enabled ~360,000 residents in remote areas to gain internet access. Directly addresses the rural connectivity gap (internet penetration ~35%).
