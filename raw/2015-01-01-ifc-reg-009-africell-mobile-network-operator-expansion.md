## Description

IFC provided a $35 million senior loan to Africell Holding, a mobile network operator active in The Gambia, Sierra Leone, DRC, and Uganda. The financing supports network expansion, capacity upgrades, and improved mobile data services in four SSA markets. Africell is a Lebanese-owned MNO providing affordable mobile connectivity to underserved populations across its operating markets. Proceeds finance 3G/4G network rollout and infrastructure improvements to support mobile internet access and digital financial services.
