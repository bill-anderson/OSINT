Washington, 22 May 2024 — Microsoft and G42 announced a package of digital investments in Kenya, with **G42 leading an initial $1 billion**. A priority is a green data center in **Olkaria** (built by G42 and partners) running **Microsoft Azure** in a new **East Africa Cloud Region** (targeted operational within 24 months of definitive agreements), powered entirely by renewable geothermal energy with advanced water conservation.

Four additional pillars: local-language (Swahili/English) AI model development; an East Africa Innovation Lab with AI digital-skills training; international and local connectivity investments; and collaboration on safe cloud services across East Africa. A letter of intent was signed during President Ruto's US state visit (with US and UAE government assistance). Ruto called it "bigger than technology itself"; Microsoft's Brad Smith called it "the single largest and broadest digital investment in Kenya's history"; G42 CEO Peng Xiao highlighted the green data center.
