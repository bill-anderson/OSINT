# Senegal targets 3M ECOWAS biometric ID holders for voter registration

A biometric voter-registration exercise began **1 February 2025**, expected to enrol nearly **3 million
citizens** who hold the national **[[ecowas|ECOWAS]] biometric ID card** but have no electoral-database
entry (a pre-study found **2,900,000** unregistered holders). Senegal contracts **[[iris-corporation]]** for
national ID and voter cards. Run to 31 July 2025 via ~568 administrative commissions (CENA planning up to
600). The **ECOWAS biometric ID card is now the sole accepted proof of identity for voter registration**,
replacing birth-certificate extracts (despite a 2021 national-dialogue recommendation to retain them). The
exercise adds to the 7.3m already on the register; population ~18m; last polls Nov 2024 legislative.
