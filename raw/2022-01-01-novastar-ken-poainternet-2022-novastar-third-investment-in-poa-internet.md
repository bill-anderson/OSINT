## Description

Novastar Ventures makes its third follow-on investment in poa! internet as part of a $28M Series C round led by Africa50, to support poa!'s expansion across Kenya and into additional African markets. poa! internet provides unlimited low-cost WiFi to residents of low-income urban areas in Nairobi and other Kenyan cities, serving a population that has historically been priced out of fixed broadband access. Proparco's FISEA facility has also invested in Novastar Fund (confirmed in Proparco sources).
