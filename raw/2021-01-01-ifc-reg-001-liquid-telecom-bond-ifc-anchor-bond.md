## Description

IFC acted as anchor investor in Liquid Telecom Holdings Ltd's senior unsecured bond issuance, providing $150 million from its own account. Liquid Intelligent Technologies is the largest independent pan-African fibre and data centre operator, with a network spanning 14+ SSA countries including Kenya, South Africa, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Rwanda, Uganda, and DRC. Proceeds finance expansion of fibre optic network and data centre capacity across Sub-Saharan Africa. The bond was part of a broader IFC engagement with Liquid including a separate equity investment and South Africa term loan totalling over $250 million.
