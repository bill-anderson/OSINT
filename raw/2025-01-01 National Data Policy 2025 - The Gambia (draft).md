# National Data Policy 2025 — The Gambia (draft)

The Gambia's draft **National Data Policy 2025** — the data-governance policy whose validation earlier
in 2025 preceded the enactment of the Personal Data Protection and Privacy Act
([[gambia-data-protection-act-2025]]). It frames how public and personal data are governed, shared and
opened ([[gov.policy]], [[gov.protect]], [[dpi.exchange]]), complementing the Open Data Strategy
2023-2026. Draft reference instrument.
