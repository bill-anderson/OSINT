# Benin launches 2025 Digital Week to advance innovation and AI-driven entrepreneurship

Date: 2025-12-03
By: Nana Appiah Acquaye

Benin has opened the 2025 edition of its national Digital Week, SENUM 2025, an initiative led by the Ministry of Digital and Digitisation to accelerate innovation, skills development and digital transformation across the country.

Held under the theme "AI, Media & Digital: Entrepreneurship to Shape Our Common Future," this year's event focuses on the opportunities and challenges brought by artificial intelligence as it reshapes industries, work and economic models.

The opening ceremony which took place at the Sofitel Cotonou Marina Hotel & Spa, drew a wide audience that included government officials, entrepreneurs, industry experts, students and digital professionals. Participants gathered to exchange knowledge and chart pathways for strengthening Benin's digital ecosystem.

SENUM 2025 featured panel discussions, keynote sessions, training programmes, demonstrations, and hands-on workshops. The event also includes the Digital Village, where exhibitors are showcasing innovative products, services and solutions contributing to the country's digital development.
