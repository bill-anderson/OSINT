## Description

Works with the mobile industry and humanitarian organisations to develop cost-effective, scalable solutions and business models for delivering life-enhancing services during disaster preparedness, humanitarian response and recovery. Includes access to information and connectivity via mobile, financial services, and mobile utility access. Africa and SSA countries are the primary delivery region, with work in DRC, Ethiopia, Kenya, Nigeria, Somalia, and South Sudan.
