## Description

EAIF invested XOF 15 billion in a local currency bond issue by Sonatel S.A. (Orange Senegal), raising XOF 100 billion (~USD 174M) total. Proceeds fund extension and enlargement of Sonatel's 4G+ network in urban and rural Senegal, and investment in new activities including energy, banking and multimedia content. The bond was listed on the West African regional BRVM stock exchange.
