## Description

The platform line's first decline after two years of steep growth: 689.9m -> 2,607.7m -> 3,897.5m
-> 5,585.9m (FY2025/26 Supp I) -> **3,612.6m proposed for FY2026/27** - still 5x its June-2024
size, but the surge has crested. Projections hold flat (~3.65bn).
