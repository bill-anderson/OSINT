An **NTU-SBF Centre for African Studies** analysis (July 2025) reviews the DRC's public-private partnership with Singapore's **[[trident-digital-tech]]** to run the nationwide **[[rdc-pass|DRCPass]]** digital-ID / e-KYC system — aimed at preventing identity fraud, enhancing financial inclusion and improving public-service delivery — while **warning it could run into controversy without proper checks and balances**. The piece flags transparency and corruption risks, recalling the cancelled **IDEMIA/Afritech** contract, and questions a **20-year foreign-exclusive** e-KYC concession over the country's identity infrastructure.
