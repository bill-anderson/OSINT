## Description

Finnfund EUR 12 million parallel loan to Wave Mobile Money, a low-cost mobile money platform providing affordable deposit, withdrawal and payment services in West Africa. Wave operates in LDC countries with very low banking penetration, offering free deposit/withdrawal services to increase financial inclusion.
