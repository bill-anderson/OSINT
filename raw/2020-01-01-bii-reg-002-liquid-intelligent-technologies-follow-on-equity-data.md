## Description

Follow-on $40M equity investment by BII in Liquid Telecommunications Jersey Limited in July 2020, to enhance and expand Liquid's data centre business across Sub-Saharan Africa. Liquid Telecom is the largest independent data centre operator in Sub-Saharan Africa, operating facilities in Johannesburg, Nairobi, Harare, Lusaka and elsewhere. This investment supports cloud hosting, colocation services, and internet exchange points across SSA, directly enabling digital infrastructure for businesses.
