DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Kenya (published 3 March 2025), early-2025 headlines:

- Population: 57.0 million (Jan 2025, +2.0% YoY); 30.3% urban, 69.7% rural; median age 20.0.
- Cellular mobile connections: 68.8 million (121% of population, +5.4% YoY; 94.7% broadband).
- Internet users: 27.4 million, internet penetration 48.0%; 29.6 million (52.0%) offline.
- Social media user identities: 15.1 million (26.5% of population).

Source: GSMA Intelligence and Kepios. (Current baseline for the Kenya hub; pairs with Digital 2024.)
