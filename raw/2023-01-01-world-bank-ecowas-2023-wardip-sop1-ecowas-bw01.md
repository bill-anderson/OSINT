# World Bank — WARDIP SOP1, ECOWAS, 2023

The World Bank committed an SDR 8.0m (US$10.5m) IDA grant to ECOWAS under WARDIP SOP1 for regional coordination of the West African single digital market.

## Description

ECOWAS's allocation under the Digital Transformation for Africa / Western Africa Regional Digital Integration Program (DTfA/WARDIP) Series of Projects 1 (PAD4756, Operation ID P176932, 7 November 2023): "SDR 8.0 MILLION (US$10.5 MILLION EQUIVALENT) TO THE ECONOMIC COMMUNITY OF WEST AFRICAN STATES." ECOWAS hosts a Regional Project Coordination Committee for the single-digital-market agenda.
