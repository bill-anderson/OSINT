SIL and Mauritius Telecom deepen collaboration to accelerate digital transformation

SIL and Mauritius Telecom have strengthened their strategic partnership through a working session held in Ebene, Mauritius, aimed at advancing digital transformation and innovation initiatives across the country and the wider African region.

The engagement follows the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding between the two organisations, under which both parties have begun active collaboration on joint projects focused on accelerating digital development.

During the session, experts from both organisations exchanged perspectives on priority areas, aligned strategic objectives, and identified opportunities for joint innovation that can support public and private sector digitalisation in Mauritius.

According to the partners, the collaboration is designed to deliver value-driven digital solutions for citizens, government institutions, parastatal organisations, and private enterprises, with a strong focus on improving service delivery and operational efficiency.

The initiative also places emphasis on supporting small and medium-sized enterprises locally, while exploring opportunities to scale solutions across African markets to enhance competitiveness and foster sustainable economic growth.

Both organisations noted that the partnership builds on shared capabilities and expertise, with the aim of delivering practical digital solutions that respond to evolving technological and economic needs.

SIL and Mauritius Telecom stated that their joint vision is to contribute to shaping the future of digital innovation in Mauritius and beyond, positioning the country as a growing hub for digital transformation in the region.
