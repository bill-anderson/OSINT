# SIL and Mauritius Telecom deepen collaboration to accelerate digital transformation

*Tech Review Africa (PR wire; explicit Date field 2026-05-30).*

**SIL and Mauritius Telecom** held a working session in Ebène following the signing of a **Memorandum of Understanding**, under which both have begun active collaboration on joint projects to accelerate digital development in Mauritius and the wider African region.

The collaboration targets value-driven digital solutions for citizens, government institutions, parastatals and private enterprises, with emphasis on improving service delivery and operational efficiency, supporting local SMEs, and exploring scaling solutions across African markets. *(PR-wire; the underlying MoU and SIL's identity/scope warrant primary confirmation at ingest.)*
