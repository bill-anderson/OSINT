## Description

Partech Africa led a $23M Series A funding round in Kyosk, a Kenya-based B2B e-commerce platform digitizing informal retail supply chains in East and West Africa. Kyosk connects small retailers (dukas) with fast-moving consumer goods brands using mobile technology. Co-investor Quona Capital also participated.
