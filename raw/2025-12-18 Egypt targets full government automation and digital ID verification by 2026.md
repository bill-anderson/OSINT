# Egypt targets full government automation and digital ID verification by 2026

Communications Minister **Amr Talaat** said Egypt is moving toward **fully automating government services** and enabling **digital-identity verification** under the **"[[digital-egypt|Digital Egypt]] 2026"** strategy — digitising state procedures and citizen entitlements. Authorities recently **eliminated the "clearance certificate"** previously required to renew vehicle licences (with Interior and the Public Prosecution), and work is under way on a **digital identity card** enabling remote verification.

Talaat framed frontier technologies (generative AI, IoT, 5G/6G, quantum) as necessary to keep Egypt's lead. **Digital exports** reached **$7.4bn** in the last fiscal year; outsourcing exports doubled over three years ($2.4bn → $4.8bn).
