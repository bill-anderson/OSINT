## Description

Improves efficiency, transparency, and accountability of public expenditure at federal and regional levels in Ethiopia. Primary component is deployment of next-generation FMIS to federal agencies and regions, supporting budget preparation, execution, and reporting. Also includes PFM capacity building and financial accountability reforms.
