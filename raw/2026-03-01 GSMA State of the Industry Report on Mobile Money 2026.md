# State of the Industry Report on Mobile Money 2026 (GSMA)

The GSMA's flagship annual report on mobile money, authored by Gianluca Storchi (GSMA Mobile
Money Data & Insights), based on the Global Adoption Survey 2025 and 2025 GSMA Consumer Survey.

## Headline figures (2025)

- **2.3 billion registered accounts** and **593 million active 30-day accounts**; global monthly
  activity rate **25.7%**.
- **>$2.1 trillion** flowed through mobile money in 2025 — doubling from the first trillion in
  just four years; value scaling faster than volume.
- **Africa's share:** ~$1.432tn of the $2.091tn total value (~66%); ~74% of the ~125bn global
  transactions; ~1.2bn accounts (~52% of global). (Per the Ecofin/GSMA coverage —
  [[2026-03-24 Mobile Money in Africa Strong Growth, but Adoption Still Falls Short]].)
- **Adoption gaps:** SSA smartphone ownership only ~33% of adults; women ~14% less likely to use
  mobile internet; digital-financial-literacy gaps (e.g. Ethiopia: 60% of women / 54% of men
  report not knowing how to use it).
