# Djibouti inaugurates Wingu TO7 Tier-3 data centre and cable landing station

**[[wingu-africa|Wingu Africa]]** inaugurated the **TO7 Technology Park** in Djibouti — attended by President **Ismail Omar Guelleh**. The park integrates a **Tier-3 carrier-neutral data centre** (99.982% uptime), a **cable landing station** for high-speed international transfers, and an **innovation centre** for local tech talent. It is described as the first integrated carrier-neutral facility in Djibouti combining a data centre and a cable landing station — suited to modern open-access cable systems — and **complements Wingu's existing Djibouti data centre** (operating for over a decade).

Co-founder and Chief Strategy Officer **Nicholas Lodge** called the park "a catalyst for Djibouti's digital economy," reinforcing the country's position as an interconnection hub between Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Asia. Together with the [[paix-data-centres|PAIX/FSD JIB1]] project, TO7 is part of the build-out that underpins the DJI hub's "three data centres" baseline.
