# Bitdeer completes 40 MW Oromia bitcoin-mining site in Ethiopia

**Bitdeer** flagged Ethiopia in its Dec-2025 operations update (12 Jan 2026): **40 MW online and complete** at an Oromia crypto-mining data centre, with a further **20 MW in progress** (targeted Q1 2026) tied to deliveries of its proprietary **SEALMINER** equipment.
