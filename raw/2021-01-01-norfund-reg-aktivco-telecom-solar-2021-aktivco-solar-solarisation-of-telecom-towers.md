## Description

Norfund EUR 9 million in EUR 60 million credit facility (FEI lead EUR 27M + Proparco EUR 6M + Norfund EUR 9M + AfricaGoGreen EUR 8M) for AktivCo, Camusat Group's Africa energy services subsidiary. Deploys solar+battery hybrid power at approximately 3,000 telecom tower sites in Chad, Niger, Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso. Enables cleaner, more reliable mobile connectivity across SSA.
