# Tech Diplomacy and Avenues for Digital Development in Africa (CIGI PB232)

> ## Excerpt
> Folashadé Soulé argues "tech diplomacy" — fusing technology into foreign policy and engaging big-tech
> firms directly — is a strategic tool for African states navigating US–China–EU digital rivalry.

> **Provenance note.** Think-tank analysis (secondary); per-figure data-centre-investment claims are the
> author's reporting — trace to AfDB/IFC/Africa50 primaries. "Congo $77m national data centre" is
> ambiguous (COG vs COD).

## Key facts

- **Kenya** is the standout — Africa's first special envoy on technology (Philip Thigo, Mar 2024) and the
  **TechPlomacy Connective** platform (Jul 2025). Data-localisation / data-centre sovereignty is a live
  battleground (Nigeria asked Google/Microsoft/Amazon to open local data centres, Apr 2025). Africa = 18%
  of world population but **<1%** of installed data-centre capacity. Summitry (US/EU/China-Africa, G20,
  UNESCO's Jun-2025 Tech Diplomacy Forum) as venues for agency.
