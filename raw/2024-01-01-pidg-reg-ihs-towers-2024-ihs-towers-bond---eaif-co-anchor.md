## Description

EAIF acted as co-anchor investor alongside IFC and Proparco in IHS Holding Limited's USD 1.2 billion bond issuance (oversubscribed at $2.3B). Proceeds refinance existing debt and support organic growth in African subsidiaries operating in Cameroon, Cote d'Ivoire, Nigeria, Rwanda, South Africa, and Zambia. IHS Towers is a leading independent telecom tower company with over 40,000 towers in Africa.
