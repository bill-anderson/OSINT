## Description

DFID/FCDO strategic partnership with GSMA and its Mobile for Development initiative to identify and support innovative ways mobile phone technologies and networks can improve reach, delivery and affordability of services for poor people in Africa and Asia. Phases include mobile connectivity, mobile money for the poor, utility services (energy, water, agri), and emerging technology applications (AI, big data). Africa is the primary delivery region. Programme phases 2016-2029 cover mobile infrastructure, digital inclusion, and mobile innovation with 14M+ beneficiaries expected. Latest phase (2024-2029) extends digital connectivity and inclusion work across SSA.
