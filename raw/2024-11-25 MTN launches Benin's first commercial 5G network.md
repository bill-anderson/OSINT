**MTN Benin** launched the country's **first commercial 5G network** (November 2024), live initially in **Cotonou and Abomey-Calavi** — beating domestic rivals Moov Africa Benin and Celtiis, both reported to be trialing the technology. MD Uche Ofodile framed 5G as "a catalyst for progress," a foundation for innovation, economic growth, gaming, streaming and IoT. Network vendor partners were not disclosed.
