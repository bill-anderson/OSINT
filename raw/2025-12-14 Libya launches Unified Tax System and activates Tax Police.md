The Libyan Tax Authority announced (12 December 2025) the official launch of the **Unified Tax System**, along with several digital platforms scheduled to become operational in 2026, as part of the Authority's digital-transformation plan and modernization of its technological infrastructure.

The Authority also announced the activation of the **Tax Police's** role in the coming phase, in coordination with the Minister of the Interior, to ensure enhanced oversight, increased compliance and reduced violations. The announcements came during the Authority's Regular 2025 Meeting of department and office directors in Tripoli, where the Head of the Authority commended high revenue-collection rates and announced bonus payments to employees, addressing field challenges and organisational matters to improve service quality and the sustainability of state finances.
