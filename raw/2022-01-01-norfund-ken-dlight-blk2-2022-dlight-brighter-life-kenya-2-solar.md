## Description

Norfund NOK 42.7 million local currency receivables facility (Brighter Life Kenya 2 Limited) for d.light Kenya. BLK2 provides multi-currency financing up to USD 238 million face value of receivables over two years for d.light's PAYG solar home systems in Kenya, with plans to expand to other African markets. Digital scope: IoT-enabled solar kits, mobile money payment collection, digital credit scoring.
