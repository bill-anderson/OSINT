# Egypt's National Elections Authority launches voter app for the 2025 Senate elections

**National Elections Authority (NEA)** President Judge **Hazem Badawi** urged voters to download the NEA's app ("National Elections Authority – Egypt", Google Play / App Store) ahead of the **2025 Senate elections**. Users register with **phone number and national-ID number** to access "first-of-their-kind digital services": locating and **requesting a change of polling station** closer to the national-ID address, and joint requests for family members to vote at the same station. The NEA framed it as digitising voter services, enhancing transparency and improving data accuracy. Egyptians abroad vote at embassies with a national ID or passport bearing the national-ID number.
