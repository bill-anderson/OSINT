## Description

MTN Nigeria Communications PLC acquired a 100 MHz TDD 5G spectrum licence (Lot 1, 3500-3600 MHz) from the Nigerian Communications Commission following the December 2021 auction. The winning bid was $273.6 million; MTN also paid an additional $15.9 million at the assignment stage to secure its preferred Lot 1 band position, bringing total payments to $289.5 million. Full payment was confirmed by NCC on 24 February 2022. MTN became Nigeria's first operator to launch commercial 5G services (August 2022). The licence enables 5G network rollout across Nigeria, supporting mobile broadband, IoT, and enterprise digital services.
