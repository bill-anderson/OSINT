DataReportal's Digital 2026 report for Equatorial Guinea (published 8 November 2025; figures reflect data as of October 2025):

- Population: 1.95 million (Oct 2025, +2.4% YoY); 75.4% urban, 24.6% rural; median age 20.9.
- Cellular mobile connections: 885 thousand, equivalent to 45.4% of the population (+3.2% YoY); 64.5% "broadband" (3G/4G/5G) — an unusually low mobile-penetration figure.
- Internet users: 1.18 million, internet penetration 60.4% at end-2025.
- Social media user identities: 178 thousand, equivalent to 9.1% of the population.

DataReportal changed its publication cycle, publishing Digital 2026 reports at end-2025 (data reflect late-2025). Source: GSMA Intelligence, UN and Kepios.
