# Digital 2024: Chad

Headline numbers for digital adoption and use in Chad in early 2024:

- There were 4.18 million internet users in Chad at the start of 2024, when internet penetration stood at 22.5 percent.
- Chad was home to 973.0 thousand social media users in January 2024, equating to 5.2 percent of the total population.
- A total of 13.06 million cellular mobile connections were active in Chad in early 2024, equivalent to 70.4 percent of the total population.

## Population

The population of Chad stood at 18.56 million in January 2024, up 562 thousand (+3.1 percent) from early 2023. 49.8 percent female, 50.2 percent male. At the start of 2024, 24.6 percent lived in urban centres and 75.4 percent in rural areas. Median age: 15.2.

## Internet use

There were 4.18 million internet users in January 2024, an internet penetration rate of 22.5 percent. Internet users increased by 352 thousand (+9.2 percent) between January 2023 and January 2024. 14.38 million people (77.5 percent of the population) remained offline at the beginning of the year. (DataReportal cautions that published figures typically under-represent reality due to reporting lags.)

## Social media

973.0 thousand active social media user identities in January 2024, equivalent to 5.2 percent of total population (11.4 percent of the population aged 18+). Social media users increased by 511 thousand (+111 percent) between early 2023 and the beginning of 2024. 23.3 percent of Chad's internet user base used at least one social media platform. 25.9 percent of social media users were female, 74.1 percent male.

## Facebook

Meta's advertising resources indicate Facebook had 759.2 thousand users in Chad in early 2024.

*Source: DataReportal Digital 2024 Chad report, produced with We Are Social and Meltwater. Time-varying figures are dated to early 2024.*
