Government Notice No. 28 of 2024 — regulations made by the Minister under section 10 of the National Identity Card Act, cited as the National Identity Card (Mobile ID) Regulations 2024. The instrument establishes the legal basis for the Mauritius Mobile ID: it defines the Mauritius mID Certification Authority System (issuing digital certificates to sign the Mobile Security Object), the Mobile Security Object itself (a signed hash of an applicant's Mobile ID data), the MNIC (Mauritius National Identity Card) and its relationship to MauPass and the National Computer Board Certificate Authority framework, and governs application for and issuance and use of the Mobile ID.

Reference legal instrument underpinning the Mobile ID digital wallet. Published in the 2024 Government Notices; exact promulgation month not established from the text (year precision).
