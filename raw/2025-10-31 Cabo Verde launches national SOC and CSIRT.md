# Cabo Verde launches national SOC and CSIRT

Cabo Verde launched a **Security Operations Center (SOC)** and a **Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT)** within the State's Private Technological Network (**RTPE**), to strengthen the government's ability to monitor, prevent and respond to cyber incidents. The **CSIRT** is a national cyber alert-and-response hub for public institutions and private organisations; the **SOC** detects and responds to threats in real time.

**Eurico Monteiro, Minister of State Modernization and Public Administration**, framed it as part of an integrated vision of public-sector modernisation where digitalisation occurs "in a secure, reliable and resilient environment." In a May 2025 interview, **Pedro Lopes** (Secretary of State for the Digital Economy) cited an update of the national cybersecurity strategy, promotion of data-protection laws, a dedicated training centre and awareness campaigns.

**Benchmarks:** Cabo Verde ranked in the **fourth of five tiers** in the ITU 2024 Global Cybersecurity Index (17.36/20 regulatory pillar; weaker elsewhere), and **111th** in the UN 2024 E-Government Development Index (EGDI 0.6238, above the African average). The digital sector contributes ~5% of GDP.
