## Description

DEG participates in the USD 35 million Series B funding round for Lulalend (rebranded Lula), South Africa's leading digital lender providing working capital and financial services to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). The round was led by Lightrock with DEG, Triodos Investment Management, Women's World Banking, IFC, and Quona Capital participating. Proceeds service surging demand for fast access to working capital and accelerate the rollout of Lula's digital banking offering for SMEs. The company was launched in 2014 and uses digital technology to assess creditworthiness and disburse loans rapidly.
