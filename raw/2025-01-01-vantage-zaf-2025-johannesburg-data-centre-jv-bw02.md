# Vantage Data Centres / Attacq — Johannesburg (Waterfall City) data centre JV, 2025

Vantage Data Centres and property developer Attacq formed a 50:50 joint venture in 2025 to build a Johannesburg data centre, with the shell investment estimated at R628 million (~US$34m) backed by a 20-year lease.

## Description

"Real estate developer Attacq and Vantage Data Centres have partnered in a 50:50 joint venture to build a data center in Johannesburg, South Africa. The project forms the first half of Phase II of Vantage's South Africa data center in Waterfall City… The investment for the data center shell is estimated at R628 million ($34m), backed by a 20-year lease. Attacq is expected to partner on the shell only." The JNB1 campus comprises three facilities and will offer 80MW of IT capacity at full buildout; construction underway with completion expected Q1 2026.

## Notes on amount: the R628m figure is the shell-only estimate for the first half of Phase II, not the whole JNB1 campus (whose planned investment Vantage put at "more than R15-billion").
