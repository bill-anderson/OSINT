# Flutterwave scores Senegal license to power digital payments

ITWeb Africa - 11 July 2025, by Phathisani Moyo

African fintech Flutterwave has secured a Payment Institution License from the Central Bank of West African States (BCEAO), enabling full operations in Senegal and tapping the country's mobile money ecosystem. Co-founder Olugbenga Agboola said the licence allows Flutterwave to offer its complete suite of digital payment solutions to businesses across one of Francophone Africa's fastest-growing economies.

Statista projects Senegal's digital payments market will reach $3.08 billion in 2025, growing at 21.46% CAGR to $8.15 billion by 2030, fuelled by a young population (74% under 40), 60% internet penetration, and mobile money platforms like Wave, Orange Money and Free Money. Flutterwave's presence adds mobile money collections, cross-border payouts in West African CFA franc, and integrated APIs for platforms such as Shopify and WooCommerce.
