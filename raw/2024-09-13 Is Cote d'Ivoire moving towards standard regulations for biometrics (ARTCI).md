An analyst review (Sep 2024) of Côte d'Ivoire's biometrics governance: a **June 2024 press release from the data-protection authority [[artci]]** flagged the growing use of **biometrics for employee access/attendance control** (public and private sectors), warning it must comply with **Law No. 2013-450 (19 June 2013)** on personal-data protection — including sensitive-data rules and prior authorisation. Signals ARTCI as an active regulator and an emerging biometrics-governance question. Cite as analysis.
