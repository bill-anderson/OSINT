## Description

IFC is providing a senior unsecured A-loan of up to 70 million equivalent in local currency (FCFA) to Orange Mali S.A., the leading mobile network operator in Mali. Orange Mali provides 2G/3G/4G connectivity, mobile money (Orange Money), and broadband services across Mali. The IFC financing supports network modernisation, 4G expansion, and mobile money infrastructure improvements. The IATI transaction value of $79.29M reflects the USD equivalent of the FCFA-denominated loan amount (approximately €70M).
