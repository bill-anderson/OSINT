## Description

Wehubit 2.0 builds on the Wehubit 1.0 programme (2018-2023) to continue identifying and supporting scaling of digital and green social innovations in the public and non-profit sectors. Programme is implemented globally but focused on Belgian partner countries in SSA. Budget EUR 4.67M financed by Belgium. Supports digital entrepreneurship, digital governance, and digital social innovation across SSA, with grants of EUR 100K–300K per innovation.
