# EthSwitch reports 97% profit surge (FY2023/24)

Ethiopia's national payment switch **[[ethswitch]]** reported a **97% profit surge** (1.06bn birr pre-tax) for FY2023/24: **94.5m ATM transactions** (123.2bn birr, +32%), **2.18m POS** (5.7bn birr, +125%), and **49.69m P2P transfers** (270.7bn birr, +251%). Paid-up capital rose to 1.79bn birr. Innovations include the **Instant Payment System (IPS)**, the **National Payment Gateway** (launched May 2024), two daily settlement windows, and a **"Request to Pay"** system usable via phone numbers or **national-ID card numbers** with USSD confirmation for non-smartphone users.
