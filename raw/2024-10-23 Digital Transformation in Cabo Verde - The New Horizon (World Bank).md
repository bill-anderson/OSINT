# Digital Transformation in Cabo Verde — The New Horizon (World Bank)

Cabo Verde aims to become a **digital hub in West Africa**, investing in ICT to modernise public services, ease access to information and cut bureaucracy. The government targets **60% of public services digitised by 2026** and over **80% by 2030**.

Highlights:
- Investment in connectivity and education, notably the **WebLabs** programme (nearly **14,000 students** enrolled in web development, robotics and digital marketing).
- **Cabo Verde Digital** fosters a startup innovation ecosystem; the **"Sikabadu"** programme aims to attract diaspora investment in tech businesses.

Backgrounder for the World Bank-supported **[[digital-cabo-verde-project|Digital Cabo Verde Project]]** and the [[nosi|NOSi]]-led digitalisation drive.
