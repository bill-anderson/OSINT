The **SADC Financial Inclusion Subcommittee** met in Johannesburg on **19–20 February 2024** to review the **SADC Strategy on Financial Inclusion and SME Access to Finance 2023-2028** (under SADC Vision 2050), with technology as a primary lever to widen access, increase transaction volumes and support regional financial integrity.

Under the EU-funded **SIBE programme** (11th EDF), 83% of the 2021–2023 Financial Inclusion work plan was implemented. With **FinMark Trust**, the programme tracks inclusion via FinScope surveys: **119 million individuals — 77% of SADC adults — are now financially included** (up from 57% in 2011). A new **SADC Financial Inclusion Information Portal** serves as the central repository and monitoring mechanism for regional FI data.
