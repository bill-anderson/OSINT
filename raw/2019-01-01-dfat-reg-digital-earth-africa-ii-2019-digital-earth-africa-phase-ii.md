## Description

Phase II of Digital Earth Africa expands the African Regional Data Cube (ARDC) pilot into continent-wide satellite remote sensing data infrastructure for 50+ African countries. Converts 30 years of satellite data into ready-to-use Earth observation products covering flooding, drought, soil and coastal erosion, agriculture, forest cover, land use, water availability and quality, and changes to human settlements. Hosted by UNECA; implemented by Geoscience Australia. Provides free, open data products with no cost to African users.
