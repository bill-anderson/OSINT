## Description

JICA technical cooperation project 2022-2026 to establish Rwanda as leading ICT hub in Africa through market creation and innovation model development. Four outputs: entrepreneur supply systems, innovation ecosystem via public-private partnerships, integrated ICT initiative capacity, and Rwanda model for market expansion. Builds on predecessor ICT Innovation Ecosystem Strengthening Project (2017-2022).
