**Binary artefact:** [[2019-06-28 World Bank - Strengthening the National Social Protection System Project (Angola).pdf]] (Project Appraisal Document PAD3340, 63 pages).

World Bank appraisal of a **US$320 million** IBRD loan (28 June 2019) for Angola's Strengthening the National Social Protection System Project (Cash Transfer) — the financing behind the **[[kwenda]]** cash-transfer programme, building the social registry and digital government-to-person (G2P) payment rails that later reached 247,000+ households (targeting 1.6m families). See [[angola--dpi-pay]].
