## Description

EAIF provided a USD 30 million commitment to Eastcastle Infrastructure, a TowerCo platform owning, operating and developing shared telecom tower infrastructure in DRC. The investment enables purchase and development of telecom towers across DRC for mobile network operators (MNOs), supporting digital transformation and last-mile connectivity. DRC's mobile subscriber market projected to grow by 22 million subscribers by 2030.
