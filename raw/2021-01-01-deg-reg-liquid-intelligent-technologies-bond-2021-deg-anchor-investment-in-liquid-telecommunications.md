## Description

DEG invests USD 25 million as anchor investor in the USD 620 million bond issued by Liquid Telecommunications Financing PLC, part of the pan-African telecoms group Liquid Intelligent Technologies (owned by Econet Global). The bond was the first Liquid Telecom capital market issuance since 2017 and was oversubscribed 5x. Liquid Telecommunications operates Africa's largest multi-country fibre optic network across Sub-Saharan Africa including South Africa, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Kenya, Tanzania, DRC, and Botswana, also operating Africa Data Centres (ADC) as a subsidiary. Proceeds used to refinance existing debt and fund growth including new fibre infrastructure, data centres, and new technologies.
