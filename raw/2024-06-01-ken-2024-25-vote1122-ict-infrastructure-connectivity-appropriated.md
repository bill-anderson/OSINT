## Description

Carries the connectivity build: last-mile county connectivity (770 sites maintenance target),
Eldoret 630km fibre uptime, innovation hubs, Digital Superhighway internet connectivity to 11,475
public institutions. Economic classification (Part H): capital transfers KSh 899.54m,
non-financial assets KSh 6,109.6m, financial assets KSh 2,555.76m.
