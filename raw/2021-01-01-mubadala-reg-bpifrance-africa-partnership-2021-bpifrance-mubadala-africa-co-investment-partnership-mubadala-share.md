## Description

Mubadala Investment Company's half of the EUR 350 million Africa co-investment partnership announced jointly with French public investment bank Bpifrance on 30 June 2021 at the 13th UAE-France Strategic Dialogue. The MoU commits both parties to deploy capital through fund and direct investments in high-growth African startups, SMEs and mid-cap companies across regional, multi-country and pan-African private equity and venture capital funds. Mubadala's indicative share is half (~EUR 175M) of the stated EUR 350M total objective; the split is not explicitly 50/50 in official sources but no other split has been disclosed. First deployment under this partnership: Partech Africa II (March 2024).
