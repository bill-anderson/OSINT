## Description

Supports innovation and experimentation with frontier technologies to improve welfare and livelihoods of people in developing countries. Enables FCDO staff and partners to test new technology solutions to development challenges, strengthen adoption of effective innovations, and build local capacity. Activities include piloting new ideas, supporting integration of technologies (including AI and digital tools) into existing FCDO programmes, and generating evidence on what works in frontier technology for development.
