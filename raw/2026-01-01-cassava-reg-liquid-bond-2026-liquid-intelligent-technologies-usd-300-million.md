## Description

In April 2026, Liquid Intelligent Technologies (a Cassava Technologies subsidiary) issued a USD 300 million Eurobond (listed on Euronext Dublin; Rule 144A/Reg S; 2.5x oversubscribed). Part of a broader USD 660M+ refinancing package including: USD 300M Eurobond, USD 210M ZAR syndicated term loan (Nedbank, RMB, Standard Bank, IFC) providing currency hedge on South African revenues, USD 150M USD syndicated term loan (Ninety One/EAAIF, MCB). Cassava simultaneously injected USD 195M fresh equity. DEG was anchor DFI investor in bond. Transaction extended debt maturity profile, reset balance sheet, and provides headroom for further digital infrastructure expansion across 25+ African countries.
