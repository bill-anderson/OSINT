## Description

EAIF anchored the bond issuance of Liquid Telecom to fund expansion of its pan-African telecom business. Liquid Telecom (now Liquid Intelligent Technologies) is a major fibre, cloud and broadband provider across 13+ African countries including DRC, Zambia, Zimbabwe, South Africa, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Mozambique, Botswana, Lesotho and South Sudan.
