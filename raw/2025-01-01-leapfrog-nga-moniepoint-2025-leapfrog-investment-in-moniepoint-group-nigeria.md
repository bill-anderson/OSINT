## Description

LeapFrog Investments participates in the USD 90 million extension of Moniepoint Group's Series C funding round in October 2025, co-investing alongside DPI (round lead), Visa, IFC, Google's Africa Investment Fund, Proparco, Swedfund, and others. Moniepoint is Nigeria's leading business banking, payments and credit unicorn, processing 5.2 billion transactions in 2023. Total Series C reaches USD 200 million (USD 110M in 2024 plus USD 90M in 2025). LeapFrog's specific share within the extension is undisclosed.
