## Description

Swedfund invested in Apis Growth Fund II, a continuation of Apis Growth Fund I targeting financial services and fintech companies providing digital financial services to underserved populations across Africa and South Asia. The fund focuses on mobile payment, savings, insurance and credit services. Fund II closed at USD 563 million hard cap in December 2019. Swedfund's prior commitment referenced as part of its ongoing Apis partnership since 2015.
