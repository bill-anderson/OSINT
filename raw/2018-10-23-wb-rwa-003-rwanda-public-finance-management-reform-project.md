## Description

Improves budget reliability and control of funds for service delivery, enhances budget transparency and increases professionalization of public finance officials in Rwanda. Component 1 focuses on PFM IT Systems Roll Out — deployment and stabilization of IFMIS across government, electronic payroll systems, and financial reporting systems.
