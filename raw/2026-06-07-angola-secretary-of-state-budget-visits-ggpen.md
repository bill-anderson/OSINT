The Secretary of State for the Budget, Juciene Cristiano de Sousa has conducted a working visit to the National Space Programme Management Office, accompanied by the National Director of the Budget, Edilásio Caleia, and the Director General of the Information and Communication Technology Service of Public Finance of Angola, Edílson Coelho.

The delegation was received by GGPEN Director General Zolana João, who presented the office's products and services under the National Space Programme. Highlights included the capabilities of the ANGOSAT-2 satellite, the Conecta Angola project, and Earth observation products that use high-resolution satellite images, artificial intelligence, and geospatial data for infrastructure monitoring, asset management, and land use mapping.

The Secretary of State congratulated the young Angolans involved in developing the programme's products and services and encouraged them to continue their work.

GGPEN has developed solutions using space technology and artificial intelligence to support key sectors of the national economy, including banking and finance, in digital transformation processes as well as asset management and control.
