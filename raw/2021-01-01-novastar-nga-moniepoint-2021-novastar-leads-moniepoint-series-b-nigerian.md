## Description

Novastar Ventures leads a $30M Series B round for TeamApt (now Moniepoint), Nigeria's leading business banking and agency banking platform providing financial services to the mass market. Moniepoint serves 600,000+ businesses and millions of unbanked customers via agency banking, mobile banking, POS and payment processing. Round also includes FMO, Global Ventures, British International Investment, Oui Capital, Kepple Africa Ventures and Endeavor Catalyst. Moniepoint later achieved unicorn status ($1B valuation) in its 2024 Series C.
