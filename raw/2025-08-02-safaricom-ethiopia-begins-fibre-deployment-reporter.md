By Staff Reporter (News in brief), 2 August 2025. Safaricom Telecommunications Ethiopia has commenced installation of a new fibre optic network connecting Afdera to Mekelle, to bolster network resilience and accommodate rising data needs in the region. Launched in a ceremony attended by Afar Regional Administration officials (incl. Deputy Commissioner for Disaster and Risk Management Ali Mohammed). Safaricom Ethiopia also provided emergency relief (500,000 Birr of tenting material, edible oil, rice, flour) to residents affected by flooding in Afdera. (African Wireless Communication.)
