# Vodacom Lesotho Upgrades Network as Group CEO Pledges Continued Investment in Digital Transformation

*Tech Africa News, 25 August 2025. By Joyce Onyeagoro.*

Vodacom Lesotho announced major upgrades to its network and systems, promising clearer voice calls, faster data speeds, wider coverage including underserved areas, and greater resilience. The announcement followed a visit by Vodacom Group CEO Shameel Joosub, who met Prime Minister Sam Matekane to reaffirm the group's commitment to Lesotho's digital transformation.

During the visit, Joosub pledged continued investment to boost the economy through improved connectivity, to facilitate government online payments for public services, and to support youth empowerment through free digital skills training in partnership with **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**. He outlined the Group's 2030 strategic vision prioritising community investment, sustainability, customer satisfaction, expanded connectivity and broader mobile money access.
