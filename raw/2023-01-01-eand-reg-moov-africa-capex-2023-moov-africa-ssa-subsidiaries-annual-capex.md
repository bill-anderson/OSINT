## Description

Maroc Telecom invested MAD 4.54 billion in its Moov Africa sub-Saharan African subsidiaries in 2023, covering network infrastructure including 4G rollout and mobile broadband expansion. Subsidiaries covered: Burkina Faso, Côte d'Ivoire, Benin, Togo, Niger, Gabon, Mauritania, Mali, Chad, Central African Republic.
