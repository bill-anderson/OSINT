Nigeria's active mobile subscriptions rose to 188,009,171 in April 2026, with broadband penetration reaching 55.67% and teledensity 86.73% (up from 85.67% in March), per NCC data. Operator shares: MTN Nigeria 96,391,419 (over half); Airtel Nigeria 64,670,018; Globacom 23,178,597; 9mobile/T2 3,538,021. 4G remained dominant at 54.41% of connections (up from 53.76% March); 5G share rose to 4.34% (from 4.20%); 2G declined to 35.93%.
