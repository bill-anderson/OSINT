# Africa on the move: Boosting mobility and connectivity

> ## Excerpt
> A Mo Ibrahim Foundation flagship report; its digital section frames connectivity as continental-
> integration infrastructure — mobile-communications the most-improved 2024-IIAG governance indicator,
> yet energy access the binding constraint on digital connectivity.

> **Provenance note.** Flagship **reference study** — figures attributed here, not fanned into concept/
> place pages. Note a likely source typo: "fixed broadband access rates the highest globally" should read
> *lowest*. The $1.5tn payments figure is Mastercard-sourced (vendor).

## Key facts (attributed)

- SSA mobile penetration **44%**, mobile-internet as low as **27%** (2023); ~one-third of the continent
  used the internet. Financing vehicles: World Bank **DE4A** ($62–79bn need; universal access by 2030),
  the AfDB–Mastercard–World Bank **MADE Alliance** (100m by 2034). **PAPSS** (2022) + **PASSCARD** (first
  pan-African card scheme, Jun 2025) advance monetary sovereignty / reduced USD dependence; digital
  payments outlook **$1.5tn by 2030**.
