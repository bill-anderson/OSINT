## Description

BII committed $30M to Apis Growth Fund I in August 2015, managed by Apis Partners. The fund supports financial inclusion in Africa and South Asia by targeting mid-market companies in payments infrastructure, credit and savings, insurance, distribution, and service providers. Africa is a primary investment focus (estimated ~50% of portfolio). This is BII's first investment in Apis Partners, an anchor LP position that catalysed the fund to close at $287.5M.
