# Digital 2025: São Tomé & Príncipe

> ## Excerpt
> DataReportal's early-2025 baseline: 61.5% internet penetration (146k users), 170k mobile connections (71.4% of population, of which GSMA rates 93.3% "broadband"), 28.9% social-media reach. Comparator edition to Digital 2026 — a dated connectivity baseline, not current state.

Note on date: comparator (prior) edition to Digital 2026. Data points are as of January/early 2025. Retained as a historical baseline/comparator; do not out-rank the fresher Digital 2026 figures on synthesis pages.

---

## State of digital in São Tomé and Príncipe in 2025 (data as of early 2025)

- **Mobile connections:** 170 thousand active cellular mobile connections at the beginning of 2025, equivalent to **71.4 percent** of the total population. Connections decreased by 726 (-0.4 percent) between start-2024 and start-2025. GSMA Intelligence data suggests **93.3 percent** of connections are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- **Internet users:** 146 thousand individuals using the internet in January 2025, with online penetration at **61.5 percent**. Internet users rose by 2,892 (+2.0 percent) between January 2024 and January 2025; 38.5 percent of the population (91.6 thousand people) remained offline.
- **Social media:** 68.8 thousand social media user identities in January 2025, equating to **28.9 percent** of the total population.

## Population context

- Population stood at **238 thousand** in January 2025, up 4,703 (+2.0 percent) between early 2024 and start-2025.
- Urban 77.3 percent / rural 22.7 percent; female 50.3 percent / male 49.7 percent.
- Median age 19.5.

Report supported by Meltwater and We Are Social; mobile data from GSMA Intelligence; internet-user analysis by Kepios.
