# Digitalization of Land Administration in Ghana (Lands Commission)

Presentation by Ben Arthur (Deputy Executive Secretary, Ghana Lands Commission) on the Commission's programme to digitalize land administration — geographic information, tenure, valuation, surveying and mapping — toward a "fit-for-purpose" cadastral system. Frames modern technology and a digitized land-records/cadastre as the route to reliable, efficient land-services delivery (the land-records digitization priority also named in the US Country Commercial Guide). References a project **expected to commence in 2023**.
