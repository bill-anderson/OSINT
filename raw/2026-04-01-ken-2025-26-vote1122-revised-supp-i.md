# Kenya Vote 1122, FY2025/26 revised (Supplementary I, April 2026): KSh 12,215.1m - BPO/Konza line cut 59%

## Description

Two-thirds of the cut fell on ICT and BPO Development (the Konza/KAIST capital-transfer line),
which lost 59% of its allocation - the second consecutive year the Konza seam absorbed the
deepest in-year cut while identity lines in Vote 1024 gained.
