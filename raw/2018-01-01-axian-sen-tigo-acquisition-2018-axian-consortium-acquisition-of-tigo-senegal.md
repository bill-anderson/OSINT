## Description

On 2 May 2018, a consortium of Axian Group (via Sofima), NJJ Capital (Xavier Niel) and Teyliom (Yerim Habib Sow) — operating as Saga Africa Holdings — acquired 100% of Sentel/Tigo Sénégal from Millicom International. Tigo was Senegal's second-largest mobile operator. The consortium committed an investment programme of approximately 70 billion CFA francs (~USD 107 million at 2018 FX) for network expansion, 4G roll-out, and mobile banking (Free Money). The operator was rebranded as Free Sénégal in October 2019, and later rebranded again to Yas Sénégal. In July 2023 Axian increased its stake from 40% to 80% (no financial figure disclosed for this subsequent transaction).
