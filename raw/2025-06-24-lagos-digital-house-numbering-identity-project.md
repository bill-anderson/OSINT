The Lagos State Government launched the Lagos Identity Project, a digital house-numbering and identification system to transform property mapping, urban planning, service delivery and emergency response. Unveiled June 24 in Alausa by Dr Olajide Babajide (Special Adviser on e-GIS), it introduces QR-coded digital address plates with local-government-specific colour codes enabling instant access to verified property data. Piloted in Eti-Osa LGA, it is expected to scale across all 57 LGs/LCDAs. Developed with geospatial firm Interspatial, backed by two years of aerial mapping and field validation; designed to curb rental fraud, improve postal services and support utilities with geolocation, and to improve tax compliance. Fully funded by the Lagos State Government.
