# Eswatini Data Protection Act, 2022 (Act No. 5 of 2022)

Companion source page for the artefact `2022-03-04 Eswatini Data Protection Act 2022 (Act No. 5 of 2022).pdf`, held in `raw/`.

The Act itself, as gazetted 4 March 2022. Assented by Mswati III. Establishes a Data Protection Commission with sanction powers; Parts I-III cover preliminary provisions, Commission responsibilities and processing of personal data. This is the primary against which the ITLawCo explainer, the ESCCOM mandate page and the APC section-level claims should be verified.
