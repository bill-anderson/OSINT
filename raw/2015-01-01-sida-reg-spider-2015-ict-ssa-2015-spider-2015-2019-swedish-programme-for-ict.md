## Description

SPIDER (Swedish Programme for ICT in Developing Regions) at Stockholm University supports ICT capacity development in sub-Saharan Africa. SSA beneficiary countries include Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda and Zambia. The programme provides technical assistance, research support and training on ICT for development across multiple sectors.
