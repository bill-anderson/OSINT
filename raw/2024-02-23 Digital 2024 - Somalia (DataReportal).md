DataReportal's Digital 2024 report for Somalia (data as of early January 2024):

- Population: 18.42 million (January 2024), up 557,000 (+3.1%) year on year. Urban 48.2% / rural 51.8%. Median age 15.3.
- Internet users: 5.08 million, internet penetration 27.6%; up 154,000 (+3.1%) year on year. 13.34 million (72.4%) remained offline.
- Mobile connections: 10.10 million, equivalent to 54.8% of the population.
- Social media: 2.92 million users, 15.8% of the population.
- Connection speeds (Ookla, early 2024): median mobile 14.88 Mbps (+27.7% YoY); median fixed 11.21 Mbps.

(Historical baseline — note the sharp jump in reported internet penetration to 55.2% in the Digital 2025 report. Reference resource; cite dated, do not absorb as current state.)
