## Description

IFC is considering providing a second loan of up to $350 million total ($150-200 million own account plus $150-200 million mobilised from other DFIs) to Safaricom Telecommunications Ethiopia Private Limited Company (STEP). This second debt tranche supports Safaricom Ethiopia's continued expansion of its 4G/5G network, M-PESA mobile money platform, and broadband connectivity infrastructure across Ethiopia. The Company plans to invest over $8 billion in Ethiopia over 10 years. This follows IFC's first $100M A-loan in 2023.
