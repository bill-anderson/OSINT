## Description

JBIC buyer's credit to Banco de Desenvolvimento de Angola (BDA) to finance Angola Cables S.A. in constructing the South Atlantic Cable System (SACS), a 6,200 km optical submarine cable directly connecting Angola and Brazil. Manufactured and laid by NEC Corporation. First undersea fibre optic cable linking Africa and South America. Cable planned to link onward to a US-bound submarine cable landing in Brazil, connecting Angola to North America via South America. Loan provided under JBIC FAITH (Facility for African Investment and Trade Enhancement). Co-financed with SMBC; NEXI insured the SMBC portion.
