## Description

JICA technical cooperation project to support establishment of inter-ministerial data exchange infrastructure in Senegal. Includes capacity building for system establishment, roadmap creation, and development of a pilot inter-ministerial data exchange platform. Strengthens interoperability of government information systems. Part of New Technology Deal digital public infrastructure programme. Total budget 480M JPY (4.8億円). Duration March 2024 to February 2027 (36 months). Pilot project announced May 2025.
