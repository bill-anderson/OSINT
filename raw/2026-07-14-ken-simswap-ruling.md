By Sam Kiplagat, Court Reporter, Nation Media Group

Diamond Trust Bank (DTB) and Safaricom have been jointly held responsible for a SIM-swap fraud that resulted in a customer losing Sh4.4 million. A court ruled that both institutions failed in their separate duties to protect the client from the fraud.

Dismissing DTB's appeal and a cross-appeal by Safaricom, the High Court affirmed a magistrate's finding that the telco and the lender were negligent, as their failures enabled the fraud.
