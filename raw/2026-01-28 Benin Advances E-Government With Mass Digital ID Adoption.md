Benin's National Agency for the Identification of Persons (**[[anip-benin]]**, ANIP) served **4 million citizens** and issued **10.5 million** civil-status and identity documents in **2025**, per data released on 26 January 2026.

**Online and remote channels handled 75%** of all recorded identification procedures — a marker of the stack's digital maturation. The channel split: **37%** via eservices.anip.bj, **30%** via the ANIP BJ mobile app, **8%** via USSD, and the remaining **25%** at physical service counters.
