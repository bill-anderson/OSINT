## Description

EUR 4.37M (USD 5M) equity in TLcom TIDE Africa Fund, a venture capital fund exclusively focused on technology-enabled services and innovation for sub-Saharan Africa. Fund reached USD 71M final close in February 2020. Invests across the VC cycle in fast-growth digital tech companies serving SSA markets. Co-investors include IFC and South Africa's Sango Capital. Portfolio companies include Andela, Twiga Foods, ULesson. Primary focus countries: Kenya, Nigeria.
