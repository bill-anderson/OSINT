## Description

Delivers innovative technology solutions across FCDO Malawi's portfolio of programmes to improve efficiency and effectiveness of aid interventions. Includes technology investment to support the Malawi Census and a management agent to respond to digital technology needs across the portfolio. Covers digital data collection, mobile-based survey tools, geospatial data applications, and digital platform development for Malawi government programmes. Programme delivered 2018-2021.
