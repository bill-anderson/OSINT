## Description

On May 22, 2015, China donated video conferencing equipment valued at about $2.8 million to Botswana's Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation. The equipment was supplied by Huawei and was installed at the ministry headquarters and Botswana's high commission in Pretoria, South Africa.
