## Description

Norrsken22 co-leads (with Blue Earth Capital) a $77.8M pre-Series C round for TymeBank, South Africa's leading digital-only challenger bank serving the mass market. TymeBank serves over 9 million customers with zero-fee banking and micro-credit. The round will further expand operations in South Africa and the Philippines. Tencent increases its stake in the round alongside new lead investors Norrsken22 and Blue Earth Capital.
