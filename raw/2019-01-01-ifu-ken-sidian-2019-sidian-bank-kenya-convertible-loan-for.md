## Description

IFU provided KSh 1.2 billion (approximately USD 11.2 million) convertible loan to Sidian Bank Kenya for digital banking expansion. Within 36 months, IFU holds the option to convert the loan into 20% equity stake. The investment supports Sidian's strategy to expand digital financial services and mobile banking for unbanked Kenyan customers.
