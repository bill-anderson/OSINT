## Description

Kenya-specific bilateral phase of the Digital Access Programme, launched in 2024. Delivers projects supporting inclusive digital access, connectivity and digital skills for underserved communities in Kenya. Includes both project delivery (GB-1-204963-403: last-mile connectivity and digital inclusion projects) and in-country staffing support (GB-1-204963-402). Extends the DAP Kenya component with dedicated bilateral funding through March 2027. Part of the broader Kenya-UK bilateral digital partnership.
