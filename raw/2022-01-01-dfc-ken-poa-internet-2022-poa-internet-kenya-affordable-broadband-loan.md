## Description

DFC direct loan of $5 million to Poa Internet Kenya Limited to finance capital expenditures including a network of towers and customer equipment to expand broadband internet services in Kenya. Poa Internet provides affordable internet access to low- and middle-income households in Kenya. Six-year tenor. Total project cost $22.8M. The loan expands Poa's tower network and last-mile connectivity infrastructure targeting underserved communities.
