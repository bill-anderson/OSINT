## Description

Improves planning and control of public expenditures, investment management, and accountability in the Kingdom of Eswatini through deployment and expansion of an Integrated Financial Management Information System (IFMIS). Supports IFMIS rollout to line ministries, budget execution systems, and internal audit capacity building.
