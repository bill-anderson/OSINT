## Description

The Action aims at supporting the African Union Commission (AUC), telecommunications National Regulatory Authorities (NRAs) and their Regional Regulatory Organizations (RROs) for the achievement of key strategic EU-AU partnership priorities in the digital domain. This especially pertains to Information, Communication and Technology (ICT) regulation and the facilitation of broadband infrastructure
