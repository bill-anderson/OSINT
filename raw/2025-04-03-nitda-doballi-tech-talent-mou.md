NITDA signed an MoU with Afrovision Technologies Limited (Doballi) to create employment opportunities for Nigerian tech talents by connecting vetted professionals with global opportunities through Doballi's AI-enabled remote work platform. NITDA will provide a pipeline of trained professionals; Doballi will connect them with international enterprises and waive the $150 assessment fee for Nigerian applicants. A dashboard will let NITDA monitor progress, employer feedback and foreign-currency payments into the Nigerian banking system. DG Kashifu Inuwa Abdullahi cited a 2022 NITDA/ccHub IT Talent Gap Assessment estimating about one-third of the 15-35 population as tech-savvy.
