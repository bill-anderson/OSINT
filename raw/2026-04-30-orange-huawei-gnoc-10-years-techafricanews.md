# Telecom Giant Orange Côte d'Ivoire Highlights 10 Years of Network Partnership with Huawei

April 30, 2026
1 min read
Author: Joyce Onyeagoro

Huawei, through its GNOC framework, has worked with the telecom operator to enhance network monitoring and operational efficiency across its regional footprint.

Orange Côte d'Ivoire has marked a decade-long partnership with Huawei's Global Network Operations Center (GNOC), highlighting what it describes as 10 years of collaboration in network innovation and operations management.

In a statement shared on social media, the company said the partnership has played a key role in overseeing operations across more than 12 subsidiaries and supporting services delivered to over 100 million customers.

Orange Côte d'Ivoire expressed appreciation for the collaboration, describing it as a strategic relationship aimed at improving network performance and customer experience.

Huawei, through its GNOC framework, has worked with the telecom operator to enhance network monitoring and operational efficiency across its regional footprint.

The company emphasized that the partnership remains focused on delivering improved connectivity and service quality as demand for digital services continues to grow across its markets.
