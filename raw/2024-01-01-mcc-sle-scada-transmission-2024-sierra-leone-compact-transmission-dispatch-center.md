## Description

Activity 1.1 of MCC's $480.7M Sierra Leone Compact (signed September 2024) establishes a national transmission dispatch center with supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) system for EGTC. The dispatch center provides centralised visibility and remote operating capability over the transmission network, enabling real-time monitoring and control of the 225kV transmission backbone. SCADA upgrades improve grid reliability, accelerate response to network disruptions, facilitate integration of variable renewable generation sources, and support future regional integration with neighbouring countries. The dispatch center also includes a backup facility. The $23.4M activity is part of the $226.7M Transmission Backbone Project. No digital identity or payments components; purely energy sector operational technology / SCADA digitisation.
