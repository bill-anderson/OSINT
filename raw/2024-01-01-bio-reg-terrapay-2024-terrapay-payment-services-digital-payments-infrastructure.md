## Description

USD 10M senior loan to TerraPay Holdings, a UK-based global digital payments infrastructure provider focused on Africa. Part of a USD 75M IFC-arranged syndicated facility (IFC USD 30M + ILX USD 15M + BIO/Finnfund USD 30M combined parallel loan). Supports expansion of TerraPay's interoperable payment rails enabling affordable international remittances and mobile payments across Africa (Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Kenya, Senegal, Tanzania, Uganda).
