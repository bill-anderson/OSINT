# Digital 2026 — Djibouti (DataReportal)

Connectivity baseline (figures reflect the latest data available in October 2025):

- **Mobile connections:** **616,000** active cellular connections — **51.9%** of population; +33,000 (+5.6%) YoY. GSMA Intelligence estimates **92.4%** are broadband (3G/4G/5G).
- **Internet users:** **772,000** — internet penetration **65.0%** (October 2025).

Reference statistics underpinning the DJI hub connectivity baseline; consistent with figures cited in the Feb-2026 [[dpi.id|Mobile ID]] launch coverage.
