# Wingu Africa — Tanzania (Mbezi) data centre Phase II, 2025

Wingu Africa launched a US$50 million second phase of its Tanzania data centre (Mbezi Industrial Area, Dar es Salaam) in March 2025, adding 80 high-density racks to a Tier 3 facility.

## Description

"Wingu Africa has launched the second phase of its data centre in Tanzania, a $50 million investment expected to bolster the country's digital infrastructure… The expanded facility, located in the Mbezi Industrial Area, is now the most connected data centre in Tanzania, with interconnections to 13 operators… The investment adds 80 high-density racks, bringing the total to 110, tailored to the financial and technology sectors."
