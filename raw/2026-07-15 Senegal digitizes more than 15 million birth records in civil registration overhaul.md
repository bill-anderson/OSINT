Senegal's **National Civil Registration Agency (ANEC)** has digitised **more than 15.3 million civil-
registration records** in a nationwide modernisation of birth registration, per figures it released (15 Jul
2026, via APA).

- **629 civil-status centres** are now connected to the national civil-registration network.
- **67%** of centres digitised via the **Civil Registration Management Software (LGEC)**; **27%** connected to
  the **Sama État Civil** platform for **online** civil-registration requests.
- **3,695** registration officers/staff trained.
- A **data-verification campaign runs 13–29 July 2026** across the Fatick, Louga and Diourbel regions to
  validate records and correct inconsistencies.

ANEC frames the overhaul as improving data accuracy/security and expanding access to services that depend on
legal identity (education, health, social protection, financial services) — foundational CRVS beneath the
country's digital-ID and DPI ambitions.
