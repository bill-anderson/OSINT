**GETESA and Huawei signed a collaboration agreement (9 July 2026, Jefatura de Estado, Malabo, chaired by VP Nguema Obiang Mangue)** to modernise the country's network infrastructure and raise the quality of telephony and data services over the next six months. In this first phase, work focuses on modernising existing infrastructure, increasing network capacity, improving data-transmission speed, reinforcing communications stability and raising service-quality standards nationwide. The government reiterated that digital transformation is a strategic axis for economic diversification.
