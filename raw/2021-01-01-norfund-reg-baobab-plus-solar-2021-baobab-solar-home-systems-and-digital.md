## Description

Norfund EUR 10 million equity in Baobab+, leading solar home systems distributor in West Africa and Madagascar. Baobab+ bundles solar kits with digital tablets and mobile financial services for off-grid households in Côte d'Ivoire, Mali, Senegal, Madagascar. Planned expansion to Nigeria and DRC. Digital scope: solar-digital product bundle (solar + tablet), digital financial inclusion, mobile payment collection, digital literacy.
