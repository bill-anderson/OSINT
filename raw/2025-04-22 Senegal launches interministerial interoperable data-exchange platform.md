# Data exchange — Senegal launches an interministerial, interoperable platform

On **22 April 2025** Senegal launched an **interministerial, interoperable platform** for secure data-sharing
between ministries and state institutions — a structuring DPI project under the **[[new-deal-technologique]]**,
implemented with **JICA (Japan)**. It enables "fluid, secure and instant" exchange to cut duplicate data
entry and improve policy steering. Pilot ran 2024–2025; large-scale deployment programmed 2025–2027; the first
phase (targeted by end-Dec 2025) is designed to speed social assistance to vulnerable families. Context:
Senegal scored **0.5162/1 on the UN EGDI (2024)**, above the African average (0.4247), and is among 19 African
"e-governance champions." (Dup fold: Le Soleil launch + Agence Ecofin [23 Apr 2025] + TechAfrica News progress
[19 Sep 2025] — same project.)
