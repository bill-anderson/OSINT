DataReportal's Digital 2024 Burkina Faso baseline (early 2024): **4.69 million internet users**, **19.9% penetration**; internet users grew by 116,000 (**+2.5%**) year on year, leaving **18.85 million (80.1%)** offline — the low-connectivity base that PACTDIGITAL and the "500 underserved localities" target aim to lift.
