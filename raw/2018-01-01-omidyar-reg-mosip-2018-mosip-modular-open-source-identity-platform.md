## Description

Omidyar Network investment in IIIT-B to develop MOSIP, a modular open-source national ID platform adopted by Ethiopia, Morocco, Philippines and others. Omidyar contributed $4M of the ~$28M total investment. Ethiopia is the primary SSA adopter.
