# Ethiopia's Personal Data Protection Proclamation 1321/2024 (overview)

Overview of Ethiopia's first comprehensive **[[ethiopia-pdpp|Personal Data Protection Proclamation No. 1321/2024]]** (the "PDPP") — approved **4 April 2024**, published in the Federal Negarit Gazette **24 July 2024**. It replaces piecemeal regulation (Civil Code, Computer Crimes Proclamation, financial/telecom directives), defines personal data broadly (incl. location and online identifiers), applies to automated and non-automated processing by controllers/processors in Ethiopia (with extraterritorial reach to foreign entities handling Ethiopians' data), and designates the **[[ethiopian-communications-authority|Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA)]]** as regulator. Places significant obligations on controllers.
