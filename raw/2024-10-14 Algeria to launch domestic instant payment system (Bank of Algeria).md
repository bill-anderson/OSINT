# Algeria to launch domestic instant payment system (Bank of Algeria)

The **Bank of Algeria** is preparing a domestic **instant electronic payment system**, per **Nabil Dahia**, Deputy DG of the **Interbank Pre-Clearing Center (CPI)**, speaking at the International Conference on Digital Payments (Ministry of Finance / Bank of Algeria with the Arab Monetary Fund). The system will let transactions complete online instantly — merchants receiving funds immediately, versus the current **up-to-72-hour** delay — aiming at financial inclusion, lower transaction costs/risks, and access for the unbanked.

Context: the ITU's June 2024 ICT development report scored Algeria **80.9/100** (up from 77.8 in 2023). A distinct rail from the **DZ MOB PAY** mobile-wallet app and from cross-border **[[papss|PAPSS]]** — this is the domestic instant switch (which Algeria later joined PAPSS's own IPS as its 18th member).
