Morocco's Minister of the Interior, Abdelouafi Laftit, said the provisional nationwide total of registered voters on the general electoral rolls reached approximately **16.5 million** at the current stage of the annual revision (men 54%, women 46%; 55% urban, 45% rural).

This year's registration drive added **382,170 new registrants — most through digital channels**: 254,740 registrations completed via the dedicated electoral-rolls website, versus 127,430 filed in person. On roll-cleansing, Laftit confirmed ~1.4 million entries were removed for legal reasons (loss of actual residence, duplicate registrations, recorded deaths, and loss of voting eligibility), strengthening the credibility of upcoming elections (scheduled 23 September 2026).
