# Madagascar launches several tenders to advance PRODIGY digital government project

Madagascar has launched a number of tenders within the framework of the **PRODIGY** project. According to a listing from the **Digital Governance Unit (UGD)**, at least **five tenders are currently open**; one calls for supplies for biometric enrollment operations, with a bid deadline of **January 15, 2026** (bid security 25 million Malagasy Ariary, ~US$5,500).

A separate tender for building development works in three lots covers **rehabilitation works on the main data centre and national ID card personalization centre in Tsimbazaza** (Antananarivo), as well as **rehabilitation of the secondary data centre in Iavoloha (Lavoloha)**.

PRODIGY has three principal objectives: a consolidated and interoperable identity management system, digital and mobile government services, and project management support. The overall project was **rejigged in August 2025** over concerns about procurement timelines, with a **new closing date of June 30, 2026**. A preference for national and direct procurement methods aims for more local sourcing and faster execution. Among recent procurements was a tender (closed September 1) to choose a consulting firm to expand the national interoperability system.
