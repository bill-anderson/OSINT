# JICA — Angola DTT broadcasting network development, 2025

The Japanese government, through JICA, committed approximately US$10 million to Angola's Digital Terrestrial Television (DTT) Broadcasting Network Development Project, modernising transmission infrastructure and enabling the analogue-to-digital transition.

## Description

"The Japanese government is investing approximately $10 million in Angola's digital terrestrial television (DTT) project, enabling the Southern African country to transition from analogue television broadcasting while modernising terrestrial transmission infrastructure and equipment in key locations." The Japanese ambassador and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' International Cooperation Directorate "signed an agreement earlier this week to launch the DTT Broadcasting Network Development Project." "The Japan International Cooperation Agency will undertake the project in collaboration with the Angolan Ministry of Telecommunications, Information Technology, and Mass Communications."
