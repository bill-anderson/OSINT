## Description

Strengthens capacity of the National Statistics Institute (INSTAT) to produce quality statistics and enhance statistical dissemination practices. Supports national statistical system modernization including household surveys, national accounts, price statistics, and development of statistical dissemination systems. Includes data quality frameworks and statistical capacity building programs.
