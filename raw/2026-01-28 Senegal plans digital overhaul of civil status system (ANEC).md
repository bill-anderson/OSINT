# Senegal plans digital overhaul of civil status system

Matar Ndao, DG of **[[anec-senegal|ANEC]]**, announced (Ziguinchor, **27 January 2026**) the nationwide
installation of official **pre-declaration software in health facilities** for civil-status records, focused
on births and deaths. Authorised agents will enter information immediately when an event occurs, closing the
gap for events outside facilities and reducing delays/omissions — aiming for a "reliable, secure and
verifiable civil registry" to end fraudulent practices. Part of the **[[new-deal-technologique|$1.7bn New
Deal]]** modernisation; also framed as data protection — a state-certified, standardised, free tool replacing
uncertified local software that could expose sensitive data.
