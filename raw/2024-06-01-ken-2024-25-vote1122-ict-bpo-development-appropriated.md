## Description

The Konza Technopolis / BPO seam: budget-day statements put KSh 5.2bn of this at the Konza data
centre and smart city facilities and KSh 2.3bn at KAIST construction (Techweez and Mwananchi
companions). Delivered mainly as capital transfers to KoTDA/KAIST.

## Notes

**Stage context (the FY of the Finance Bill withdrawal):** gross national budget revised 4.49tn (original) -> 4.37tn (Supp I, Aug 2024) -> 4.64tn (Supp II) -> 4.37tn (Supp III, Jun 2025). Vote 1122 travelled 22,354.9m -> 19,998.0m -> 12,057.9m -> 16,456.8m; overall vote absorption 93% (COB BIRR).
Later stages: Supp I 7,350.58m -> Supp II 4,796.43m (recovered) -> Supp III 7,353.79m -> actual
6,494.56m, **88% absorption** - the vote's laggard (BIRR Table 4.46). Konza Complex reached 76%
completion against a 70% target (Table 4.47).
