Cameroon's **[[st-digital]]** inaugurated its **first Côte d'Ivoire facility** — an eco-responsible **Tier III data centre at VITIB II, Grand-Bassam** (announced 2 October 2025; construction begun November 2024). It marks ST Digital's regional expansion from its Cameroon base into the Abidjan/Grand-Bassam data-centre cluster (alongside Raxio, PAIX, MDXi and Stellarix), framed around data sovereignty and local hosting.
