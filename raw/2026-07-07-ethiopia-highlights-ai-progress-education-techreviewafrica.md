Tech Review Africa, 7 July 2026 (explicit Date field). PM Abiy Ahmed, speaking at the 30th regular session of the House of Peoples' Representatives, highlighted Ethiopia's AI progress, emphasising the achievements of the Ethiopian Artificial Intelligence Institute and the importance of strengthening AI education for future generations. He framed the Institute's creation as a forward-looking move placing Ethiopia among Africa's early AI adopters, and said the government is now focusing on education quality/accessibility and human-capacity building to sustain the AI ecosystem.
