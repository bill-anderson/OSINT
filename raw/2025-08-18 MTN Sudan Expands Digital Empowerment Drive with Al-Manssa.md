MTN Sudan has launched "Al-Manssa" (The Platform), a training initiative to support Sudanese content creators through specialised workshops in digital media and online marketing, aiming to enhance participants' skills and align them with the country's digital transformation.

Noon Al-Rashid, Senior Manager for Corporate Communications and Social Impact, said the platform was developed out of an urgent need to support local creators "especially in the current climate," stressing that young people and digital transformation remain core MTN priorities. Hatem Babiker, General Manager of Digital Services, said the platform offers expert-led workshops helping creators reflect Sudanese identity while creating new income opportunities and overcoming technical challenges. MTN Sudan has previously run workshops for media professionals as part of ongoing efforts to advance digital skills.
