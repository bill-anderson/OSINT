## Description

The overall programme objective is to increase digital-based employment opportunities in high economic potential sectors through innovation and incubation hubs. The scope of the project implementation covers four secondary cities of Rwanda: MUHANGA, NYAGATARE, RUBAVU and RUSIZI.
