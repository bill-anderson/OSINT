## Description

Construction and installation of mobile telecom networks to extend coverage to rural communities of Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) and Cameroon currently not served. Networks using frequency bands under revenue share agreements. By end 2018: coverage to over 3.6M people via 650 sites. Extends mobile population coverage from 75% to 80% in DRC and 85% to 95% in Cameroon.
