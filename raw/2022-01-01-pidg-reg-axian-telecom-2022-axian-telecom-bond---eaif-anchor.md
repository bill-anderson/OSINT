## Description

EAIF was an anchor investor in bond issuances by Axian Telecom, a pan-African telecommunications and digital company. First investment: USD 20M in the February 2022 bond (total USD 420M raised) to fund telecom expansion. Second tranche: USD 30M commitment in 2023 bond. Axian operates in Madagascar, Togo, Tanzania, Uganda, Senegal, Comoros and other markets.
