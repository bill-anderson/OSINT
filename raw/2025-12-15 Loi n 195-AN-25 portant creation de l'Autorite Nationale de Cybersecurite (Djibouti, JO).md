**Loi n° 195/AN/25/9ème L** (Journal Officiel n°23, **15 Dec 2025**; adopted by the National Assembly
**12 Nov 2025**, promulgated by the President) creates the **Autorité Nationale de Cybersécurité (ANC)**:

- **Art. 1** — the **ANC** is an *établissement public à caractère administratif* with legal personality and
  financial/administrative autonomy; HQ Djibouti.
- **Art. 2** — attached to the **Presidency of the Republic**, under the supervision of the **Direction
  Générale de la Sécurité Nationale**.
- **Art. 4** — defines/coordinates/implements national cybersecurity policy (national strategy; protection of
  state and vital-operator systems; national **CERT**; countering digital interference/disinformation;
  technical management of digital identity and e-authentication; prior approval of public-sector digital
  solutions).
- **Art. 6** — a **Conseil National de Cybersécurité** (chaired by the DG de la Sécurité Nationale) sets
  strategic orientations and acts as the ANC's board.
- **Art. 7** — led by a **Director-General** appointed by decree; initial units include a **DJ-CERT**,
  governance/risk/resilience, emerging-tech & digital-identity security, and support.
- **Art. 24** — the **DSSI (Direction de la Sécurité des Systèmes d'Information) of ANSIE** (Loi n°100/AN/15)
  is the ANC's **initial nucleus**; its missions, means and personnel transfer to the ANC and ANSIE's law is
  amended accordingly.
- **Art. 30** — five-yearly revision.
