## Description

DEG acts as anchor investor in the USD 750 million senior bond issued by HTA Group Ltd, a subsidiary of Helios Towers plc, Africa's leading independent telecommunications tower company. The bond, the second in Helios Towers' corporate history, was one of the first capital market issuances by a private-sector company from a developing country since the COVID-19 pandemic outbreak. Helios Towers operates over 7,000 telecommunications towers in Tanzania, the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), Ghana, Congo Brazzaville, and South Africa, serving multiple mobile network operators. Proceeds used for tower expansion and operational growth.
