# DPI Roadmap Playbook (March 2026 updated edition)

> ## Excerpt
> A practitioner framework guiding governments to design national DPI roadmaps across digital identity,
> payments and data exchange. Updated edition of a June-2025 beta launched under South Africa's G20
> Presidency. Standing instrument: [[dpi-roadmap-playbook]].

> **Provenance note.** Gates-funded consortium guidance; advocacy-inflected (pro-DPI, digital-sovereignty
> framing). Its "Malabo Convention adopted 2014, not yet ratified" phrasing is outdated — the Convention
> entered into force June 2023; cite the wiki's [[malabo-convention]] page, not the Playbook, for that.

**Artefact:** ![[2026-03-01 DPI Roadmap Playbook.pdf]]

## Key facts

- Worked case: **South Africa** Digital Transformation Roadmap (Cabinet-approved 26 Mar 2025; MyMzansi
  citizen-portal prototype in 10 weeks by Nov 2025). Supporting cases: Ethiopia (DE2030/Fayda), Nigeria
  (NIN 121m by mid-2025; "Supporting Life Events" DPI Framework, Mar 2025), Rwanda.
- Introduces the **"DPI House"** design/implementation/governance choice model and a **seven-step**
  roadmap process; argues a roadmap is not a prerequisite (India, Rwanda) and a "+1" incremental
  use-case approach can substitute where readiness is low.
