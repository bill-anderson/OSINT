LoGRI Policy Brief 11 (December 2025) — a step-by-step guide to implementing a comprehensive digital addressing system, drawing on the experience of **Kanifing Municipality**, the most densely populated area in The Gambia.

Between July 2021 and February 2022, Kanifing Municipality implemented a **Plus Code** digital addressing system for its 500,000 residents, **georeferencing 36,000 properties**, creating a comprehensive municipal address database and installing address boards. Kanifing Municipal Council now uses the system to improve core public services, including waste management and **property-tax collection**. Digital addressing systems are framed as essential public goods that strengthen city management, improve policy targeting, enhance emergency response, and support delivery services and private-sector innovation.
