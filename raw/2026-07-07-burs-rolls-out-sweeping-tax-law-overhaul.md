# BURS rolls out sweeping tax law overhaul

The country's tax system has undergone its biggest overhaul in decades after the Botswana Unified Revenue Service (BURS) implemented a raft of new tax laws from July 1 aimed at tightening compliance, broadening the tax base and modernising revenue administration.
