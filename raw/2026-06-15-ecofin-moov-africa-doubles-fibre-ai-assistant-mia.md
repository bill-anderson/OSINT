Ecofin Agency (Samira Njoya). Moov Africa Malitel (SOTELMA's commercial brand) announced on 12 June 2026 that it doubled the speed of its fibre-optic internet plans at no extra cost, and launched MIA (Moov Intelligence Artificielle), an AI-powered digital assistant. Moov already offers fibre up to 100 Mbps in Bamako, Kayes, Sikasso, Segou, Mopti and Koulikoro. Context: DataReportal, Mali had 8.91 million internet users at start of 2026 (up from 7.88m in 2024, ~13% growth); internet penetration rose 31.7% to 35.1%. Fixed-broadband competition intensifying between Orange Mali and Moov as data consumption becomes the main growth driver. (Follows Mali's June 2026 move to majority state ownership of SOTELMA.)
