TerraPay partnered with Wave Mobile Money to enable cross-border remittances directly into Wave mobile wallets in Mali. TerraPay's global payment network facilitates remittance flows from multiple money-transfer operators across the US, Canada and Europe to Mali through a single integration, allowing Wave users to instantly receive funds.

Over 80% of Mali's population uses mobile phones, many for mobile money/digital wallets. Wave's Regional Head of Business Development, Karamokho Badiane, cited collaboration with Orabank Mali and TerraPay to expand reach and financial accessibility. TerraPay's VP Sub-Sahara Africa, Willie Kanyeki, said the partnership enables instant, cost-effective, compliant remittances driving financial inclusion in Mali.
