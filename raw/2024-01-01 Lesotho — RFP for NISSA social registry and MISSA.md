The Ministry of Gender, Youth and Social Development (MoGYSD), with EU financial assistance and UNICEF technical assistance, issued a Request for Proposals for the **[[nissa-lesotho|National Information System for Social Assistance (NISSA)]]** social registry and the **Management Information System for Social Assistance (MISSA)** (RFP closing 12 August 2024; Terms of Reference dated 19 July 2024).

An earlier Management Information System was developed November 2014 and completed January 2016 — a web-based system on a Microsoft platform with a mobile application for timely data collection, supporting integrated social safety nets such as the Lesotho Child Grants Programme (CGP), Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVC) Bursary and Public Assistance (PA). UNICEF's Social Policy Section continues to support strengthening of the NISSA social-protection registry and its MISSA management information system.
