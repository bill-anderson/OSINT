Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 14 October 2025. NIDP aims to have at least 63 million Fayda IDs issued before year-end 2025 (toward the 90 million by 2027 target), per Exec Director Yodahe Zemichael - Fayda issued to 23 million so far, hoping to add 40 million by close of year. Expansion pushing beyond cities into rural regions (a Gambella/Dima resident notes registration not yet begun there); Fayda increasingly required for government services incl. social protection benefits.
