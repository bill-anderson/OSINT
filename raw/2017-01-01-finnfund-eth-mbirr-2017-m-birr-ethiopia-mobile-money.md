## Description

Finnfund investment in M-Birr, a mobile money platform providing electronic payment services to microfinance institutions in Ethiopia. M-Birr is the first electronic payment service between financial institutions in Ethiopia and the first electronic distributor of airtime, offering mobile money services for deposits, withdrawals, payroll and aid distribution.
