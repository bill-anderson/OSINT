DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for The Gambia (published 3 March 2025), early-2025 headlines:

- Population: 2.79 million (Jan 2025, +2.3% YoY); 65.4% urban, 34.6% rural; median age 18.6.
- Cellular mobile connections: 3.15 million, equivalent to 113% of the population (+4.5% YoY); 88.7% "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- Internet users: 1.28 million, internet penetration 45.9% (+2.3% YoY); about 1.51 million people (54.1%) remained offline.
- Social media user identities: 449 thousand, equivalent to 16.1% of the population.

Source: GSMA Intelligence and Kepios. (Baseline connectivity figures for the Gambia hub.)
