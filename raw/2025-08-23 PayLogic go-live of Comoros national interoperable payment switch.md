Comoros' **National Interoperable Payment Switch** went live (**23 August 2025**), a unified platform connecting commercial banks, microfinance institutions and mobile-money operators across the Union of Comoros for real-time, interoperable digital transactions. Vendor **[[paylogic]]** (awarded Jan 2025) frames it as a cornerstone of the country's shift away from cash and toward digital financial inclusion, including for the diaspora.

The switch is part of the **World Bank-financed Financial Sector Development Support Project (PADSF)**, under the central bank (**[[bcc-comoros]]**). PayLogic notes Comoros joins other users of its interoperable-switch technology — the **CEMAC** regional switch, **Eswatini**, and **Lesotho (LeSwitch)**.
