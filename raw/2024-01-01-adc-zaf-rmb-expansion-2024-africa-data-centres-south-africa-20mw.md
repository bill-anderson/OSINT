## Description

In June 2024, Africa Data Centres secured a ZAR 2 billion (~US$109.5 million) bespoke financing facility arranged by RMB (Rand Merchant Bank, part of FirstRand Group) to expand hyperscale data centre capacity in South Africa by an additional 20MW. RMB acted as Coordinator, Initial Mandated Lead Arranger, and Bookrunner. The financing enables ADC to accelerate expansion at its existing Midrand (JHB1) and Samrand (JHB2) Johannesburg campuses and Cape Town (CPT1/CPT2) facilities. ADC's South Africa market has capacity of 64MW and the expansion aims to increase this further to serve growing hyperscaler and enterprise demand.
