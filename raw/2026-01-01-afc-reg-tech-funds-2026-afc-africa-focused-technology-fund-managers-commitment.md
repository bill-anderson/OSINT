## Description

AFC's Board approved a commitment of up to USD 100 million to invest in Africa-focused technology fund managers, aiming to address the shortage of long-term institutional capital for African technology companies. Initial deployments: (i) USD 25 million anchor commitment to Lightrock Africa Fund II (Lightrock portfolio includes Moniepoint, Lula, M-KOPA); (ii) USD 15 million to Future Africa Fund III (early-stage venture capital backing technology-enabled solutions). The remaining USD 60 million is earmarked for additional fund managers under evaluation. Sectors covered: fintech, digital infrastructure, consumer technology, education technology. The commitment represents AFC's strategic entry into Africa's venture capital ecosystem to mobilise institutional capital and deepen African ownership of tech investment.
