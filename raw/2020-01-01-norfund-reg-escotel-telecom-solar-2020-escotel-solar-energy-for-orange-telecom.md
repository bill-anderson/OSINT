## Description

Norfund USD 10 million equity in ESCOTEL (USD 35M total: Evolution II Fund USD 20M + Norfund USD 10M + Sagemcom USD 5M). ESCOTEL deploys solar+battery hybrid power at ~900 Orange telecom tower sites in Sierra Leone and Liberia (initial), with DRC over time. Directly enables mobile connectivity and broadband access across rural communities.
