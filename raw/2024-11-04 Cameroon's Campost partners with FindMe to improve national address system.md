Cameroon Postal Services (**[[campost]]**) signed a partnership (**1 November 2024**, Yaoundé) with Cameroonian start-up **[[findme]]** to enhance the **national address system**, adapting FindMe's postal-addressing model (already deployed in Senegal; operational in Cameroon since July 2024). Campost DG Pierre Kaldadak tied it to e-commerce last-mile delivery and improving customer identification (**KYC**) for financial services — a digital-addressing registry layer adjacent to the ID stack.
