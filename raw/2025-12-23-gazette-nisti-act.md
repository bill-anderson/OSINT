# National Institute of Science, Technology and Innovation Act, 2025 (Act 24 of 2025)
Official Gazette — Supplement to Official Gazette, [23 December 2025]; assented 22 December 2025

An Act to provide for the establishment of the National Institute of Science, Technology and Innovation (NISTI) as an umbrella body providing leadership and coordination for research in science, technology and innovation. NISTI is established as a body corporate (s.3). "Minister" means the Minister responsible for Technology.

Objectives (s.4) include: supporting the development of STI; fostering a scientific/innovative culture and coordinating scientists, researchers, innovators and investors; collaborating with international research institutions through Science Diplomacy; making R&D attractive to business; promoting IP-rights awareness; developing human capital in STI; promoting innovation in schools; and devising strategies to promote the use of Artificial Intelligence, other emerging technologies and digitisation as leveraging tools across socio-economic development.

Functions (s.5) include establishing a Science Academy, a digital scientific data repository with open access, and business/technology/innovation incubation centres, science parks and maker-spaces. The Act comes into operation on a date the Minister appoints by Gazette notice.
