Lusaka, September 13th, 2024 – The Zambia Integrated Land Administration System (ZILAS) has revolutionized land administration in Zambia, eliminating queues and making it easier to apply for any land-related matters seamlessly. Deployed by the Electronic Government Division (SMART Zambia Institute), ZILAS has transformed land administration in the country.

ZILAS has reduced paperwork and introduced a seamless application process for land issues, providing an efficient and user-friendly experience. With its 100% digital approach and web-based mobile interfaces, ZILAS is transforming the way users interact with land-related services. With ZILAS, users can access land-related services from anywhere, enjoy a hassle-free experience with no more paper trails, and benefit from modern and efficient service delivery. The system is accessed via the Government Service Bus at gsb.gov.zm.

The SMART Zambia Institute is a Division under the Office of the President mandated to coordinate and implement electronic government (E-Government) for the citizens, businesses and within government for improved service delivery. The Institute was established through a Government Gazette notice No 836 of 2016.
