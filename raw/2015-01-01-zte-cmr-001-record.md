## Description

On December 21st, 2015, ZTE Corporation signed an agreement for a grant worth $12,119,042 USD with Cameroon's Ministry of Economy, Planning and Regional Development (MINEPAT) for the construction of two turnkey Data Centers in Yaounde and Douala on behalf of the National Emergency Telecommunications Network. $7,420,551 will be going to the center in Yaounde, and $4,698,491 will be going to Douala. It is unclear if works ever began/ended.
