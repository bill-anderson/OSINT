## Description

MTN Ghana's Chief Home Officer Richard Acheampong announced (Bright Conversations, Accra,
2026-07-20; reported 2026-07-21) a US$1 billion commitment to digital infrastructure — fibre
broadband networks, digital systems and supporting technologies for home and business
connectivity — under MTN's Mission 2030 Strategy, with home connectivity framed as the growth
centre. Current fibre offering up to 500 Mbps (2026). No disbursement schedule, timeframe or
breakdown was stated.
