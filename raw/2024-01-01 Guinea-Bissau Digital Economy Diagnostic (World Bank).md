# Guinea-Bissau Digital Economy Diagnostic (World Bank)

The World Bank's **Digital Economy Diagnostic** for Guinea-Bissau — assessing the five **DE4A**
pillars (digital infrastructure, digital public platforms, digital financial services, digital
businesses, digital skills) for one of West Africa's least-connected states. It underpins the analysis
that international connectivity is confined to the coast (ACE cable, March 2023) with no national
backbone, ~32.5% internet penetration (2023), and only embryonic middle-mile fibre — the gaps the
[[entd-gw]] strategy 2025-2030 aims to close. Reference document — cite, don't absorb.
