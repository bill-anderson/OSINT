## Description

IFU committed EUR 10 million at the second close of AfricInvest's Financial Inclusion Vehicle (FIVE), a pan-African fintech-focused fund that invests in financial institutions and fintech companies across Africa. FIVE focuses on inclusive fintech including digital lending, mobile payments, insurance tech and other digital financial services.
