Over 66,000 Starlink subscribers in Nigeria risked having internet access restricted after December 31, 2025 if they failed to complete a mandatory biometric update. The requirement, first issued by the NCC in August 2025, extends the regulator's subscriber-verification framework beyond mobile networks to satellite internet providers. The NCC directive (letter dated August 19, 2025) set a deadline of November 19, 2025, extended on November 17 to December 31, 2025. Starlink confirmed via email to customers (December 29), warning non-compliant subscribers would have service suspended. Subscribers must upload a headshot, provide their NIN, and consent to linking the data to their Starlink account.
