# Econet drives digital transformation — launches Wi-Fi Plus

*Lesotho Times, 11 July 2025. By Moroke Sekoboto. Solution integrates fixed broadband and mobile networks.*

TELECOMMUNICATIONS powerhouse Econet Telecom Lesotho (ETL) has launched a Fixed Mobile Convergence (FMC) product, Wi-Fi Plus, uniting fixed and mobile networks into one experience. Designed for home and business, it targets seamless internet access anytime, anywhere.

Speaking at the launch at Thabeng Hotel, ETL CEO Dennis Plaatjies said the innovation marked "a fundamental shift in how our esteemed customers connect, communicate, and do business in Lesotho," merging Econet's fixed broadband and mobile connectivity to reach even the most remote communities. General Manager Sales and Services Lebohang Ramaisa said dedicated packages were rolling out for Residential (up to seven users, each with mobile data, on-net minutes and a free SIM under a shared account) and SMME customers (up to five employees, each with 10GB data and two hours on-net talk time monthly for up to 24 months). The launch builds on Econet's existing fixed infrastructure to bridge the connectivity divide.
