## Description

IFC committed senior secured loans to M-KOPA: up to $50 million (or KES equivalent) to M-KOPA Kenya Limited and up to $15 million (or UGX equivalent) to M-KOPA Uganda Limited. M-KOPA is Africa's leading connected asset financing provider, offering smartphones, solar home systems, and digital financial services (cash loans, mobile insurance) to over-indebted, underserved households through a pay-as-you-go (PAYG) model using mobile money instalments. Approved December 2022 and invested February 2023. IFC is a principal debt financier alongside BII which also holds equity and debt positions.
