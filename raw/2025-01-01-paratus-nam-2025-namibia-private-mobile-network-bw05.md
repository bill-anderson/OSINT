# Paratus — Namibia private mobile network, 2025

Paratus Group invested N$600 million (US$33.7 million) over the year to September 2025 to build and launch Namibia's first privately owned mobile network (LTE and 5G).

## Description

"In the last year, a further N$600 million (US$33.7 million) was invested in the new Namibian mobile network, one-third of which was allocated to build a digital technology stack that integrates all Paratus services into a single platform." "Pan-African telecommunications and network services provider Paratus Group has launched the first private mobile network in Namibia with both LTE and 5G technologies... The new mobile network was developed with Cerillion as the digital technology partner and Nokia as its mobile network partner."
