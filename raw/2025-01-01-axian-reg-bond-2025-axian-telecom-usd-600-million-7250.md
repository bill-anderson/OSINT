## Description

Axian Telecom issued USD 600 million 7.250% Senior Notes due 2030 (priced 25 June 2025; settled July 2025). Proceeds used to refinance outstanding USD 420 million 7.375% notes due 2027 and USD 150 million term credit facility, and to fund the acquisition of Wananchi Group (Kenya/Tanzania/Uganda) and general corporate/digital infrastructure investment. Bond was 2x oversubscribed with over USD 1.3 billion in demand. DFI anchor investors provided USD 160 million of the total: EAAIF/PIDG USD 40M, DEG USD 40M, Proparco USD 25M, IFC, BII (individual amounts for IFC and BII not disclosed in public sources). Axian Telecom operates in 9 SSA markets: Madagascar (Telma), Tanzania (Yas), Togo (Yas), Senegal (Yas), Uganda (Yas), DRC (Yas), Comoros, Réunion and Mayotte.
