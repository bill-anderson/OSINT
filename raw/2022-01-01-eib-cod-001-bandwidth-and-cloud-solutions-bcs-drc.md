## Description

Second phase of construction of a fibre optic backbone in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), focusing on underserved areas of eastern DRC. The fibre links will enable high-speed broadband in nine towns, deployed along railways (1,280 km) and roads (139 km). ~1,400 km of new fibre optics in underserved regions of eastern DRC. First quasi-equity financing from EIB Global.
