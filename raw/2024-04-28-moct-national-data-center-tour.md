THE MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS AND TECHNOLOGY TOURED THE NATIONAL DATA CENTER

April 28, 2024

THE MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS AND TECHNOLOGY TOURED THE NATIONAL DATA CENTER Mogadishu – April 28, 2024… The Honorable MP Mohamed Adam Moalim Ali (Somali), Minister of Communications and Technology, today toured the National Data Center facility currently under construction, which is poised to become a critical hub for governmental data management, accompanied by Mr. Mustafa Yasin Sheikh, Director General of NCA, and Mr. Ahmed Ali Salad, Deputy Director. During the Minister of Communications sightseen, Hon. MP Mohamed Somali, said that the establishment of this center will significantly enhance the technological services provided by government agencies, and it is designed to host various information used by government agencies….. " This center is one of the most important facilities in the country, as it is a center for storing national data for all the different agencies in the country. "….. stated Minister Mohamed Somali. Upon completion of its establishment and equipment, the National Data Center will centralize all data related to various government bodies, and will eliminate dependencies of national data on international data storage solutions, that is stored outside the country, thereby bolstering national data sovereignty, security and accessibility across Somalia. ……………………… Contact for details Information and Public Relations Office Ministry of Communications and Technology.
