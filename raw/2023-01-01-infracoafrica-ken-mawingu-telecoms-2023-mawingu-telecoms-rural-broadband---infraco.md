## Description

InfraCo Africa invested USD 6 million into Mawingu Networks Ltd (Kenya), a rural internet service provider delivering affordable fixed wireless broadband access using solar-plus-battery powered radio towers. Mawingu provides last-mile connectivity for households, businesses, schools and healthcare facilities in rural and peri-urban Kenya. InfraCo Africa made its initial investment in 2022, supporting Mawingu's rural digital inclusion mission.
