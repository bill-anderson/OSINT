## Description

Framework Loan for digital connectivity in Cape Verde. Finances: construction of a branch between a new submarine cable linking Europe to South Africa and Cape Verde's national network; urgent modernisation of Cape Verde's national transmission network; upgrade of information systems and other digital infrastructures of Cabo Verde Telecom.
