# The challenges facing Ethiopia's digital transformation

Analytical review of Ethiopia's **Digital Transformation Strategy** (launched July 2020) three years on. Notes a young population of 128m+ (26m+ in education) as a demographic opportunity if skilled; internet users exceeded 36m in 2023 (~35% of population); digital IDs and mobile payments (40m+ mobile accounts) laying groundwork — against persistent challenges of infrastructure, skills and inclusion.
