# Ghana Policy and Strategy on Digital Health 2023–2027 (GHS)

The Ghana Health Service's five-year digital-health policy and strategy — the reference [[instrument]] framing GHS's health-information-system ([[dpi.mis]]) modernization. Built on a SWOT of GHS's leadership/governance, services/applications, infrastructure and workforce, it sets **ten strategic objectives** including strengthening digital-health governance at national/regional/district levels and **improving interoperability and connectivity of all ICT systems**. GHS is named custodian; developed with WHO/UNICEF/USAID evidence and DANIDA + Global Fund support. COVID-19 exposed vulnerabilities the strategy aims to close.
