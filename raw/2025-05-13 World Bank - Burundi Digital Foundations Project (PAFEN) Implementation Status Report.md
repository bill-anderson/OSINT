World Bank Implementation Status & Results Report (ISR03987, sequence 6; archived **13 May 2025**) for the **Burundi Digital Foundations Project (PAFEN, P176396)** — an IBRD/IDA Investment Project Financing operation from FY2022 (~$92m). Implementing agencies: **MINCOTIM** (Communication, IT and Media), **MFBPE** (Finance, Budget and Economic Planning) and **[[setic-burundi]]** (Executive Secretariat for ICT). Tracks progress on PAFEN's broadband-access, digital-skills and public-service-digitisation components. See [[pafen-burundi]].
