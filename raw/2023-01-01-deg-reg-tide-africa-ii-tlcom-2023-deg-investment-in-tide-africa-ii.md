## Description

DEG signs an equity investment of USD 5 million in TIDE Africa II (TIDE II), a venture capital fund managed by TLcom Capital. TIDE II invests in young companies using tech innovations to disrupt inefficient business models in Africa. The fund is sector-agnostic and invests in market space enablement, agtech, and fintech solutions. TLcom successfully deployed TIDE Africa I across eleven investments and uses the same strategy in TIDE II. Total fund close at USD 154 million (announced April 2024).
