# Djibouti appoints Safia Ali Mohamed Gadileh as Minister Delegate for Digital Economy and Innovation

*Date: 2026-05-22 — By: Nana Appiah Acquaye*

Djibouti has announced the appointment of H.E. Mrs. Safia Ali Mohamed Gadileh as Minister Delegate in charge of the Digital Economy and Innovation within the Ministère Délégué chargé de l'Economie Numérique et de l'Innovation (MDENI), marking a new phase in the country's digital transformation agenda.

The appointment is expected to strengthen national efforts to accelerate digital innovation, expand the digital economy, and enhance the use of technology-driven solutions in public administration and economic development.

Safia Ali Mohamed Gadileh will oversee strategic initiatives aimed at advancing innovation-led growth and supporting the country's broader digital transformation objectives.

The ministry is tasked with promoting digital infrastructure development, fostering innovation ecosystems, and supporting policies that encourage the growth of technology-driven enterprises.

Officials have expressed confidence that the new leadership will contribute to strengthening Djibouti's position as an emerging digital hub in the Horn of Africa, while deepening reforms aimed at modernizing the national economy through technology.

The appointment comes as Djibouti continues to prioritize digital transformation as a key pillar of its national development strategy, with emphasis on innovation, connectivity, and inclusive economic growth.
