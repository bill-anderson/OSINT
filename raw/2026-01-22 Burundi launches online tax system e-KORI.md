The **Office Burundais des Recettes (OBR)** launched the **e-KORI** platform for online tax filing and the payment of taxes and fees on **19 January 2026**, with support from the **World Bank**. Finance, Budget and Digital Economy Minister Dr Alain Ndikumana called it "a key step in the country's digital transformation" that would raise domestic revenue and reduce reliance on foreign aid.

e-KORI digitises internal-tax and non-tax revenue collection — online declarations and payments, remote transaction tracking and fewer visits to tax offices — with the aims of improving revenue tracking, reducing errors and strengthening enforcement. It will roll out in **nine phases**, each deliverable validated before the next, with training for tax officials and taxpayers and cybersecurity as a central focus. The government framed it within Burundi's Vision 2040/2060, while noting that domestic revenue mobilisation remains a challenge in a largely informal economy.
