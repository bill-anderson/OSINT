**[[ethio-telecom]]** and **[[huawei]]** are expanding their collaboration to develop enterprise- and
**SME-focused digital solutions** in Ethiopia, moving beyond telecoms infrastructure into **cloud computing,
AI, cybersecurity, IoT, data-centre services and digital platforms**. Discussions involved Ethio Telecom CEO
**Frehiwot Tamru** and Huawei Northern Africa president **Li Shen**, under Ethio Telecom's **"Next Horizon:
Digital & Beyond 2028"** strategy.

The two also discussed building **local digital capability** (software development, solution customisation,
system integration, R&D collaboration), improving **affordable smartphone/device access** via financing
models, ESG/CSR programmes on digital inclusion and rural connectivity, and customer-adoption campaigns for
Ethio Telecom services including **[[telebirr]]**. Framed as expanded cooperation/discussions rather than a
signed agreement.
