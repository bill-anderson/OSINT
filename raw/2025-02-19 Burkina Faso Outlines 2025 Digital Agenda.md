Burkina Faso's Ministry of Digital Transition unveiled its **2025** priorities at a 14 February 2025 review chaired by PM Ouédraogo. Key projects: completing data-centre construction (then **70% finished**); expanding network coverage to **at least 500 underserved localities**; deepening digitisation of administrative services; large-scale population enrolment for the **WURI** electronic unique-ID project; and launching **PACTDIGITAL** (~$150 million).
