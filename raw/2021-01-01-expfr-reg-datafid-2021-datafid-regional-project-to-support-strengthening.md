## Description

DATAFID aims to increase knowledge about the digital economy and its impacts, and to build the capacities of tax and customs administrations through improved use of data science. Two components: (1) improving knowledge of the digital economy to include it in the tax system; (2) improving the transparency and performance of tax and customs administrations. Beneficiary countries: Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Niger, Senegal, Togo (Component 1); Côte d'Ivoire, Mauritania, Niger, Togo (Component 2). Implemented with AFRISTAT, INSEE, FERDI. Financed by the French Directorate-General of the Treasury (DG Trésor) under the Strategic Investment Plan for Development (PISD) 2020–2023.
