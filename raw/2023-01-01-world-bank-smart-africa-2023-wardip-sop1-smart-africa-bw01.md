# World Bank — WARDIP SOP1, Smart Africa Alliance, 2023

The World Bank committed an SDR 15.3m (US$20.0m) IDA grant to the Smart Africa Alliance under WARDIP SOP1 for continental digital-market coordination.

## Description

The Smart Africa Alliance's allocation under the Digital Transformation for Africa / Western Africa Regional Digital Integration Program (DTfA/WARDIP) Series of Projects 1 (PAD4756, Operation ID P176932, 7 November 2023): "SDR 15.3 MILLION (US$20.0 MILLION EQUIVALENT) TO SMART AFRICA ALLIANCE." Smart Africa hosts a continental project coordination committee and the Smart Africa Digital Academy under the programme.
