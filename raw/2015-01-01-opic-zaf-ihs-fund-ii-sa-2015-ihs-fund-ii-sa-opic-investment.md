## Description

OPIC investment guaranty of up to $80 million to IHS Fund II SA PVE, a fund investing in telecommunications tower infrastructure in South Africa. The DFC provided a participating debt commitment of up to $80 million alongside equity from DFIs. IHS Towers is a leading independent tower company in Africa. Investment supports expansion of telecom infrastructure enabling mobile broadband connectivity in South Africa.
