The **Certificat du numéro personnel d'identification (Cnpi/fID)** — known as the **"C'est moi"** card — is established by article 6 of **décret N° 2020-099 of 26 February 2020**. Built with World Bank support (the WURI project), it is a **free (zero-franc), unlimited-validity** support for each citizen's unique **personal identification number (NPI)**, intended to bring legal identity to the most vulnerable and aligned to SDG 16.9.

The piece frames the NPI as the **base ("socle") for electronic identity in Benin**: a standardised, unique method of identifying individuals that serves as the primary reference for linking electronic-identity data, and as an authentication factor (combined with PINs or biometrics) securing access to online services — though, as of 2024, described as not yet sufficiently exploited.
