## Description

Development Partners International (DPI), via its African Development Partners II/III fund, invests USD 54 million in Channel VAS (rebranded Optasia in 2022), the leading fintech provider of mobile financial and airtime credit services operating in 30+ African countries, the Middle East, and Asia. Channel VAS provides AI-based mobile financial services, nano loans, airtime credit and big data analytics through partnerships with mobile network operators and financial institutions. Over 85% of revenue derived from Sub-Saharan Africa. Convergence Partners co-invested.
