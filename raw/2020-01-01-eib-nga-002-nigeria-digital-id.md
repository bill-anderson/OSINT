## Description

Framework Loan for development of a digital identity (eID) infrastructure in Nigeria and supply of biometric identity to people residing in Nigeria and Nigerians living abroad. Project provides platform for economic development. Identification central to delivering government services: education, health, social safety, transportation, finance, elections. Supports Nigeria's digital economy and fiscal management. Co-financed with World Bank IDA and AFD.
