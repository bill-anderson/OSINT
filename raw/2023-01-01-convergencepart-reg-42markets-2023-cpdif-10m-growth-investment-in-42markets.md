## Description

Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund (CPDIF) acquired a strategic stake in 42Markets Group for $10 million in growth funding. 42Markets Group is an incubator, investor and builder of high-growth specialist businesses in the financial and capital markets sector across Africa. The company focuses on digital transformation and improved efficiency of capital markets in Africa, enabling second-tier capital markets to reduce friction and costs, improve transparency and real-time risk management across financial products, ultimately extending the reach of banking and financial services to a broader cross-section of the population. Operations span South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, and Mauritius.
