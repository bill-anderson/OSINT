DataReportal's Digital 2024 report for Kenya (published 23 February 2024), early-2024 headlines:

- Population: 55.65 million (Jan 2024, +2.0% YoY); 29.8% urban, 70.2% rural; median age 19.7.
- Internet users: 22.71 million, internet penetration 40.8%; 32.93 million (59.2%) offline.
- Social media user identities: 13.05 million (23.5% of population).
- Cellular mobile connections: 66.04 million (118.7% of population).
- Median mobile internet speed: 21.78 Mbps; median fixed speed: 9.78 Mbps.

Source: GSMA Intelligence, Ookla and Kepios. (Comparator baseline for the Kenya hub; pairs with Digital 2025.)
