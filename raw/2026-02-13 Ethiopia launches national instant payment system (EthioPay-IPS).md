# Ethiopia launches national instant payment system (EthioPay-IPS)

**[[ethswitch]]** launched Ethiopia's **National Instant Payment System (EthioPay-IPS)**, powered by **BPC's SmartVista** platform (officially unveiled at the Dec 2025 Digital Payment Conference 2.0). EthSwitch now connects **32 banks, 12 MFIs, 3 PSOs and 3 PIIs**, providing card-switch hosting, centralised reconciliation and shared e-wallet services. EthioPay-IPS gives institutions modern rails for **account-to-account (A2A) and wallet-to-wallet (W2W)** transfers, **ETHQR**, alias and recurring payments — strengthening domestic interoperability.
