## Description

Microsoft announced ZAR 5.4 billion (approximately USD 295 million) to expand cloud and AI infrastructure in South Africa by end of 2027, announced by President Brad Smith on March 6, 2025 alongside President Cyril Ramaphosa. Includes construction of a second cloud infrastructure region in Centurion, expanded data centre capacity, power and water readiness, and AI skills development for government and private sector. Builds on ZAR 20.4B invested 2019-2024.
