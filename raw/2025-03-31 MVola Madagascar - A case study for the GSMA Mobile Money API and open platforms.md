GSMA case study on MVola, a digital bank licensed for mobile money and digital retail banking in Madagascar with over 10 million customers. MVola adopted a digital-first, open-platform strategy — the MVola Developer's Portal for direct merchant integrations, MVola Wallet-to-Bank and QR Code payment — allowing third-party developers to access its APIs.

MVola complied with the GSMA Mobile Money API specification in September 2023. The API is organised around eight use cases; MVola plans to comply with all. In the first phase it complied with transfer use cases (person-to-person transfers and international remittances), adapting its API offering, assessing infrastructure, ensuring data security/privacy per GSMA guidelines, and aligning systems. In October 2023 MVola signed a partnership with MFS Africa to enhance international remittances. Compliance has converged new services within MVola's super-app (P2P transfers, international remittances, bill payments, loans), earning the "GSMA Mobile Money API Compliant" mark.
