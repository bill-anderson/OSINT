Libya Post has begun installing street address signs in the **Hay Al-Andalus** district of Tripoli as part of a nationwide plan to modernize the country's postal system. The first phase will assign official addresses to **42,000 buildings**. The state postal company said the initiative aims to streamline postal services, improve location accuracy and enhance service quality for residents, as part of broader efforts to develop national infrastructure (a national digital-addressing foundation) and facilitate more efficient public services.
