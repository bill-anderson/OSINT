MyLagosApp Goes Live: MTN Nigeria and Lagos State Partner for Digital Innovation

March 14, 2025 2 min read Author: Akim Benamara

MTN Nigeria and Lagos State Government Unveil MyLagosApp to Enhance City Living

MTN Nigeria, in partnership with the Lagos State Government, has launched MyLagosApp, a groundbreaking mobile application designed to improve city exploration and digital transactions for residents and visitors. The unveiling took place today at MTN’s Rooftop Event Centre, marking a significant milestone in the drive toward a more connected and digitally empowered Lagos. The MyLagosApp serves as a one-stop platform for navigating Lagos with ease, offering users a seamless way to discover local attractions, access entertainment, and make digital payments. This initiative reflects MTN Nigeria’s commitment to leveraging technology to enhance urban mobility, tourism, and financial inclusivity.

The launch event was graced by dignitaries, including the Executive Governor of Lagos State, Babajide Sanwo-Olu, represented by his deputy, Femi Hamzat. Their presence underscored the government’s dedication to fostering digital transformation in the state. MTN Nigeria’s CEO, Karl Olutokun Toriola, also played a pivotal role in bringing this vision to life, reinforcing MTN’s commitment to delivering innovative solutions that empower communities.

By integrating city services into a single, user-friendly platform, MyLagosApp aligns with Lagos’ broader vision of becoming a smart city. The collaboration between MTN Nigeria and the Lagos State Government highlights the potential of digital tools in improving urban experiences, driving economic growth, and fostering greater financial inclusion. As Lagos continues to position itself as a hub for innovation, initiatives like MyLagosApp pave the way for a more accessible and efficient digital ecosystem. TechAfrica News will continue to follow the impact of this launch on residents, businesses, and the broader digital landscape.
