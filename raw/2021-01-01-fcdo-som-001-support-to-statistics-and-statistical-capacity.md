## Description

Component of FCDO's Somalia Monitoring Programme Phase 3. Supports statistics and statistical capacity building in Somalia through multilateral implementing organisations. Strengthens the national statistical system in Somalia, improving collection, processing and dissemination of data needed for development planning and poverty reduction. Funded via multilateral organisation channel (FCDO funding type 109). Works across public sector policy and administrative management, with links to health data.
