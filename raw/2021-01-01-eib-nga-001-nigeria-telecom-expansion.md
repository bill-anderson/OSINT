## Description

Capacity and coverage expansion of the promoter's 4G mobile broadband access network to address high growth in mobile data applications. EIB financing to upgrade the 4G core mobile broadband network and expand radio network coverage from 90% to 98% of population in Lagos region. Mobile broadband services to reach remaining areas without access.
