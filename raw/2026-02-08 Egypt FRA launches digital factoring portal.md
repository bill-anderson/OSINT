# Egypt's FRA launches digital factoring portal

Egypt's **[[fra-egypt|Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA)]]** launched a **digital factoring system** on its e-portal (with **e-finance**) to prevent double-financing and streamline oversight. Phase one lets factoring companies **electronically verify whether an invoice has been previously funded**, via a direct link with the Ministry of Finance and the Egyptian Tax Authority. FRA Chair **Mohamed Farid** called it a step toward integrated infrastructure for digitising non-banking financial services. The value of factored papers rose **77.8% in 2025 to EGP 132.2bn** (from EGP 74.5bn in 2024). A second phase will make the full factoring cycle electronic.
