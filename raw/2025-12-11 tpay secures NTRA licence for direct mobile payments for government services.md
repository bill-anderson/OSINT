# tpay secures NTRA licence for direct carrier-billing payments for government services

**[[tpay|tpay]]** signed Egypt's first licence with the **[[ntra-egypt|National Telecom Regulatory Authority (NTRA)]]** to offer **Direct Carrier Billing (DCB) for government payments (P2G)**. The framework lets citizens pay for **electricity/utility bills, traffic fines and civil-registry services** directly from their **mobile balance or monthly mobile bill** — expanding access for those with phones but no bank account or card. Signed by tpay GM **Ahmed Nabil** and NTRA President **Mohamed Shamroukh**; Group CEO **Isik Uman** called it "a turning point" toward "a national digital payment channel." The release cites 76.3% adult bank-account penetration alongside near-universal mobile ownership as the opportunity.
