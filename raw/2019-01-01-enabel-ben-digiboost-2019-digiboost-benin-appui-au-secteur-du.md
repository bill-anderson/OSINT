## Description

Projet DigiBoost: supports Benin's digital sector ecosystem by strengthening and consolidating support structures for digital startups and SMEs. Part of 'Projet d'appui à la compétitivité de l'Afrique de l'Ouest - Volet Benin'. Provides grants, capacity building and technical support to digital innovation structures. Belgium-financed, implemented by Enabel. Start 2019, closed 2023.
