# European Union — Senegal 'Nekkal' civil-registration modernisation, 2020

The European Union financed Senegal's "Nekkal" civil-registration modernisation programme to the tune of 18 billion FCFA, launched 7 May 2020 for 42 months.

## Description

The "Nekkal" civil-registration modernisation programme — "Lancé le 7 mai 2020 pour une durée de 42 mois, ce programme est financé par l'Union européenne à hauteur de 18 milliards de francs Cfa." By June 2024 more than 19 million civil-status records had been digitised and indexed, centralised in the Registre national de l'état civil. The broader Stratégie nationale de l'état civil is budgeted at 68 billion FCFA (a national strategy, not the EU commitment).
