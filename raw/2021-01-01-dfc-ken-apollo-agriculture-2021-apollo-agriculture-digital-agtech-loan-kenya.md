## Description

DFC direct loan of $9.5 million with a 10-year term to Apollo Agriculture Inc., a Kenyan agri-fintech startup that uses satellite data and machine learning to deliver bundled packages of financing, agricultural inputs (seeds/fertilizer), crop insurance, and digitally-delivered advisory services directly to smallholder farmers in Kenya and expanding across Africa. Apollo's innovative use of digital technology to underwrite credit risk for farmers at the base of the pyramid is core to its business model.
