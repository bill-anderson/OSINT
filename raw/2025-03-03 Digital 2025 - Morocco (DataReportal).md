DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Morocco (published 3 March 2025), early-2025 headlines:

- Population: 38.3 million (Jan 2025, +0.9% YoY); 65.9% urban, 34.1% rural; median age 29.8.
- Mobile connections: 54.9 million (143% of population, +4.7% YoY; 84.9% broadband).
- Internet users: 35.3 million, penetration **92.2%**; ~2.98 million (7.8%) remained offline.
- Social media user identities: 21.3 million (55.5% of population).

Source: GSMA Intelligence and Kepios. (Baseline connectivity figures for the Morocco hub — among Africa's highest-penetration markets.)
