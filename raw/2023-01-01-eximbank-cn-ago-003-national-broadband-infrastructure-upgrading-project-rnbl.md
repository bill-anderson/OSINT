## Description

CHEXIM concessional loan (~$249M) for Angola's National Broadband Network project (Rede Nacional de Banda Larga, RNBL). Framework agreement signed January 11, 2023; specific loan agreement signed October 2023 (RMB 1,736,207,810). Covers installation of 2,000 km of terrestrial fiber optic cable, a submarine fiber line connecting Cabinda province, and modernization of core telecommunications infrastructure. Implemented by Sinohydro Corporation Ltd. Expected to extend broadband to remote areas and reduce access costs.
