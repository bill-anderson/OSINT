The Federal Ministry of Communications, Innovation and Digital Economy confirmed it is assessing the proposed acquisition of IHS Towers by MTN Group, citing the strategic importance of telecom infrastructure to Nigeria's economy and national development. The review will be conducted with relevant regulatory authorities to evaluate the transaction's potential impact on the telecommunications sector. The Ministry noted deliberate steps over the past two years to stabilise and strengthen the telecoms industry.
