Banjul, 17 May 2024 (ECA) — a two-day validation workshop (16-17 May 2024) for The Gambia's **National Digital ID and Digital Transformation Strategy 2023-2028**.

Ousman Bah, Minister of Communications and Digital Economy, said the strategy represents The Gambia's commitment to harnessing digital technologies, with pillars aligned to the Recovery-focused National Development Plan; PS Lamin Camara elaborated on government support and Mactar Seck (ECA) outlined the workshop's contribution. The workshop refined and validated action plans for both the Digital ID and Digital Transformation strategies. Stakeholders were drawn from all ministerial offices, immigration departments, the Gambia Police Force, and the **newly established Information Communication Technology Agency (GICTA)**. The initiative was launched by the Government in December 2022 through the Ministry of Communications and Digital Economy, with technical and financial support from ECA (including discussions at the 17th Internet Governance Forum in Addis Ababa).
