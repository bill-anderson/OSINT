Equatorial Guinea officially began implementing the **Sistema Integral de Gestión de Funcionarios (SIGF)**, a strategic tool to transform civil-service management over an estimated **18 months**. The first Steering Committee (Comité de Pilotaje) working session was chaired by Minister Secretary-General Job Obiang Esono Ayingono, who stressed the SIGF is a structural instrument to strengthen administrative governance and ensure more rigorous management of state personnel, not merely a technological innovation.

The session reviewed technical progress, the phased implementation timeline across public institutions, and monitoring/evaluation mechanisms. A project Monitoring Strategy was presented: governance model, execution phases, milestone master plan, risk management, financial control and operational deployment plan. The Steering Committee comprises representatives from various departments.
