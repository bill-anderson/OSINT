DataReportal Digital 2025: Namibia edition (published 3 March 2025) — reference connectivity statistics for early 2025.

Headline figures (early 2025): 2.67 million cellular mobile connections (87.1% of population); 1.97 million internet users, penetration 64.4% (up 41k / +2.1% year-on-year); 754 thousand social media user identities (24.6% of population). Population 3.06 million (Jan 2025), 56.2% urban / 43.8% rural, median age 21.3. Mobile connections fell 161k (-5.7%) year-on-year; GSMA Intelligence indicates 92.9% of connections are broadband (3G/4G/5G); 35.6% of the population (1.09m) remained offline at the start of 2025.

(Reference resource — GSMA Intelligence / Kepios / Ookla-sourced; cite, do not absorb figures into synthesis pages as current state.)
