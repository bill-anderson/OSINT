# Sudan Establishes Three Digital Authorities to Advance E-Govt

PM **Kamil Idris** signed a decree on **5 November 2025** establishing three national agencies under
the Ministry of Digital Transformation and Communications:
- **Digital Transformation Authority**
- **Sudanese Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority**
- **Sudanese Cybersecurity Authority**

Aim: a unified digital system, improved transparency, administrative governance and "digital
sovereignty," and an institutional framework for public digital services, data management and
cybersecurity — pursued amid conflict, weak infrastructure and limited resources.

Dated benchmarks cited:
- **UN EGDI 2024:** Sudan **0.2759/1** (below the African average of 0.4247).
- **Cybersecurity:** **Tier 4**, score **48.17/100**.
- **Oxford Insights AI Readiness Index 2024:** **24.63/100** (sub-Saharan average 32.70).
