## Description

USD 25 million participation in Axian Telecom Holding's USD 600 million public bond issuance. Axian Telecom is a pan-African mobile network operator with operations in Madagascar, Tanzania, Togo, Senegal, Benin, and other SSA markets. Bond proceeds support network expansion, 4G/5G rollout, and digital connectivity across francophone and lusophone Africa.
