At a 31 July 2025 session, **[[anip-benin]]** (DG Aristide Guy Adjinacou Gnahoui) presented new versions of its digital platforms for the electoral process to political parties and civil-society organisations, affirming the **Registre National des Personnes Physiques (RNPP)** as the **single source of electoral data** (per articles 41–42 of law N°2017-08 of 19 June 2017).

New functionality lets citizens find their **NPI**, view or change their polling station, and obtain voting documents; a **USSD portal via *151#** extends the same services to those without a smartphone or internet (digital inclusion), alongside residence declaration and **interoperability with the Register of Beninese Abroad** for diaspora voter transfers. Electoral calendar milestones: electoral statistics to the CENA on 14 July 2025 and display of the definitive computerised electoral list on 12 November 2025, ahead of the **2026** elections.
