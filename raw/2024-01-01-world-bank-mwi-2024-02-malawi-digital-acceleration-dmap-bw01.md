# World Bank — Digital Malawi Acceleration Project (DMAP), 2024

The World Bank appraised a US$70m IDA grant (US$90m total operation with US$20m commercial co-financing) for the Digital Malawi Acceleration Project, part of the regional IDEA MPA.

## Description

Appraisal-Stage Project Information Document (PIDIA00549, 5 May 2024) for the Digital Malawi Acceleration Project (DMAP, P505095), part of the regional IDEA MPA. Total Operation Cost at approval US$90.00m (IDA grant US$70.00m + US$20.00m unguaranteed commercial); estimated approval date 27 June 2024. The appraisal ceiling under the IDEA MPA is US$150m (US$70m IDA Phase 1 + US$80m IDA Phase 2) plus US$50m leveraged commercial. Components: affordable broadband and secure data hosting; interoperable/secure data platforms (next-generation digital ID, Bomalathu data exchange, Data Protection Authority); high-impact digital services; and management.

## Description note

Objective: "Increase access to, and inclusive use of, the internet and improve the Government's capacity to deliver digitally-enabled services."
