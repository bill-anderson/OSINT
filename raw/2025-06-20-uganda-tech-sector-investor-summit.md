Analysis/reported feature on the 2nd Uganda Investor Summit (June 19, 2025, Kololo Independence Grounds, Kampala). The summit was "a quiet statement of intent" rather than a startup pitch event — investors were ferried in an electric bus designed and assembled in Uganda by state-backed Kiira Motors, symbolising how Uganda is "doing tech differently" by starting with the hard stuff: manufacturing, hardware, logistics and high-investment science, rather than startup hype.

Dr Monica Musenero, Uganda's Minister for Science, Technology and Innovation, said the summit "comes at a point when we are really designing Uganda's science, technology, and innovation environment to go to market." The piece frames Uganda's approach as building foundational deep-tech and manufacturing capacity ("preparing the bride") and now seeking investment ("a dowry") to commercialise it.
