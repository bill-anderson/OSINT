# Senegal's National Single Registry (RNU): towards national coverage

IPC-IG Research Brief No. 63 (May 2019; Fatou Ndiaye, Ousseynou Diop, Mansour Sy Sarr) — the institutional
baseline for Senegal's **[[rnu-senegal|Registre National Unique]]**. Government opted to build the RNU in
**2015** (extending the PNBSF Family Security Grant tools, 2013) under the Emerging Senegal Plan (PSE).
Governance: **[[dgpsn-senegal|DGPSN]]** is line/coordination agency; the database is hosted by
**[[senegal-numerique|ADIE]]** (which builds the MIS); legal framework/data use regulated by the
**[[cdp-senegal|CDP]]**; ANSD supports geographic targeting/scoring; World Bank and UNICEF are technical/
financial partners; local targeting-and-monitoring committees handle pre-selection. Historical reference —
figures are 2019 baseline; cite, don't promote as current state.
