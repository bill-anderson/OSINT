# Digital 2025: Sudan (DataReportal)

Recurring reference country digital profile. **Cite, do not absorb** figures as current state —
fresher country-level data supersedes.

Headline numbers (early 2025):
- **21.6m** cellular mobile connections = **42.4%** of population; **down 5.6m (-20.6%) YoY** —
  consistent with conflict-driven connectivity disruption.
- **14.6m** internet users (Jan 2025); penetration **28.7%**; +1.6% YoY; **71.3% (36.3m) offline**.
- **3.68m** social-media user identities = **7.2%** of population.
- GSMA Intelligence: **69.3%** of mobile connections are broadband (3G/4G/5G).
- Population **50.8m** (Jan 2025), urban 37.0% / rural 63.0%, median age 18.5.
