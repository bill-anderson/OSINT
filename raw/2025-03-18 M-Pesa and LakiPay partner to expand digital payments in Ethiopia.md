# M-Pesa and LakiPay partner to expand digital payments in Ethiopia

**M-Pesa** ([[safaricom-ethiopia]]) partnered with Ethiopian fintech startup **LakiPay Financial Technologies** — integrating M-Pesa with LakiPay's payment gateway so LakiPay merchants can accept M-Pesa payments, expanding acceptance points. (Elsa Muzzolini, Safaricom M-Pesa MFS; Habtamu Tadesse, LakiPay founder/CEO.)
