## Description

EUR 6M (two tranches: EUR 4M in 2021 + EUR 2M follow-on) equity in Cathay AfricInvest Innovation Fund (CAIF), a pan-African VC fund. CAIF reached €110M final close in 2023, co-managed by AfricInvest and Cathay Innovation. Invests in digital tech companies from seed to growth stage across Africa (Egypt, Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa). Focus on digital economy, fintech, healthtech, edtech, and agritech startups with scaling potential.
