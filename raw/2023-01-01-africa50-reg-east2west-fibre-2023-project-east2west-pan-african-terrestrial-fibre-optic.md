## Description

Africa50 partnered with Bayobab (wholly owned by MTN Group) in May 2023 to co-develop Project East2West, a terrestrial fibre optic cable network supplying dark fibre and active fibre capacity across ten or more African countries, connecting landlocked countries to subsea cables on both the east and west coasts of Africa. The partnership will invest up to USD 320–325 million (including USD 10 million development costs). Africa50 role: co-lead developer, investing construction equity, co-leading debt raise, and leading ESIA development. Expected to cut east-west latency by up to 65% and create 50,000+ jobs. Countries covered include Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania, DRC, Zambia, South Sudan, Central African Republic, Republic of Congo, Malawi, Angola, Mozambique.
