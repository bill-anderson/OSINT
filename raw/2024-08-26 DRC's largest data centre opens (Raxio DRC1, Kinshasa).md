**Raxio Group** launched **Raxio DRC1** in Kinshasa — described as the **DRC's largest data centre**, with **Tier III** accreditation from the Uptime Institute and a **US$30 million** investment, a milestone in the DRC's National Digital Plan. Distinct from the (first Tier-III) OADC Texaf Digital facility — two Tier-III Kinshasa data centres opened in August 2024. See [[raxio-group]].
