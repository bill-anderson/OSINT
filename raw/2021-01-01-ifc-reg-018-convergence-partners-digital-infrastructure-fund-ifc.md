## Description

IFC committed $25 million as a limited partner in the Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund (CPDIF), a specialist Sub-Saharan African digital infrastructure fund managed by Convergence Partners, targeting $250 million in commitments. CPDIF invests in digital infrastructure across SSA including data centres, fibre networks, broadband connectivity, tech-enabled services, and digital platforms. Portfolio includes CSquared (fibre/data Mauritius), Datacentrix (SA data centre), Merit HoldCo (Nigeria digital infrastructure), Yellow Digital Retailers (device financing across 6 SSA countries). Co-investors: BII ($40M), Proparco, DEG, and others.
