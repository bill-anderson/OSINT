The DRC launched a **blockchain-based e-government initiative** (per Minister of Posts, Telecommunications and Digital Affairs Augustin Kibassa Maliba, May 2025): a blockchain-powered digital ID (**[[rdc-pass|DRCPass]]**), satellite connectivity, a planned **55,000-km national fibre backbone** (about **12,000 km completed**, mainly linking major urban hubs), and a Tier-3 data centre already operational in Kinshasa. Blockchain **land records** are also flagged — an ambitious but early-stage build, with most of the backbone still to lay.
