The Payment System Management Act 14 of 2023, Namibia's primary statute governing the national payment system. It provides for the management, administration, operation, regulation and supervision of payment, clearing and settlement systems, with the Bank of Namibia as the responsible authority, and sets the legal basis for payment-service providers and system participants.
