## Description

IFC provided a $50 million loan to IHS Nigeria Plc to finance the acquisition and upgrade of 2,705 towers from Etisalat Nigeria (now 9mobile) in a Buy and Lease Back (BLB) arrangement. IHS Nigeria is the largest tower company in Nigeria operating over 30,000 towers across the country. The investment enabled IHS to expand its portfolio significantly and provide 2G/3G/4G infrastructure to mobile network operators, supporting connectivity for millions of Nigerians.
