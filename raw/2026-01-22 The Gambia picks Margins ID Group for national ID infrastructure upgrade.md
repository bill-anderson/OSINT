**Margins ID Systems Applications Ltd (MIDSA)**, a subsidiary of Ghanaian identity firm **[[margins-id-group]]**, has won a contract on a build, co-operate and transfer basis to modernize the identity infrastructure of The Gambia and produce national ID cards. The contract was signed between company officials and the Ministry of Interior.

The Gambian government called it the beginning of a strategic partnership "to implement a secure, scalable and integrated National Identity Management System and ICAO-compliant e-identity card infrastructure." Margins Chairman and CEO Moses Kwesi Baiden Jr. pledged three decades of expertise; Margins also produces Ghana's national ID card ([[ghana-card]]). The contract ends "an era of confusion that rocked The Gambia's national ID sector for many years." Interior Minister Abdoulie Sanyang had hinted at the contract in September 2024.
