# Benin Enters the 5G Era as Moov Africa Bénin Launches High-Speed Network at Novotel Orisha Cotonou

April 28, 2026 — 1 min read
Author: Kay-Lyne Wolfenden

The launch of 5G by Moov Africa Bénin signals the operator's commitment to advancing digital infrastructure and delivering next-generation connectivity in Benin.

Moov Africa Bénin has officially launched its 5G network, marking a major milestone in the country's digital transformation. The announcement was made during a dedicated event held at Novotel Orisha Cotonou, attended by company executives, key stakeholders from the ICT sector, media representatives, and strategic clients.

During the event, attendees were given an exclusive preview of the capabilities of 5G technology. Live demonstrations showcased significantly faster speeds, ultra-low latency, and enhanced responsiveness, highlighting the potential of the new network to transform digital experiences across industries.

Following the press conference, guests participated in hands-on demonstrations designed to illustrate real-world use cases of 5G. The sessions emphasized seamless connectivity, instant performance, and the ability to support advanced digital applications, reinforcing the technology's role in enabling innovation.

The launch of 5G by Moov Africa Bénin signals the operator's commitment to advancing digital infrastructure and delivering next-generation connectivity in Benin.

The company described the rollout as a step toward unlocking new opportunities in sectors such as business, media, and digital services, while underscoring that the future of digital connectivity is already taking shape.
