## Description

Axian Telecom issued its debut USD 420 million 7.375% Senior Notes due February 2027 (priced 16 February 2022). Bond was 4x oversubscribed. Proceeds used to fund the acquisition of MIC Tanzania (Tigo Tanzania) completed April 2022, refinance Agou Holding facility (Togo), and fund Uganda tower expansion. DFI anchor investors: DEG USD 23M, CDC Group/BII USD 30M, IFC USD 50–60M, EAIF USD 20M. Axian at issuance operated in: Madagascar (Telma), Togo (Yas), Senegal (Free/Yas), Uganda (Yas), Comoros, Réunion, Mayotte. Tanzania added via proceeds.
