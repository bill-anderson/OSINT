## Description

Africa50 is co-developer and equity co-sponsor of Kigali Innovation City (KIC), a 60-hectare mixed-use, masterplanned innovation city in Kigali, Rwanda, developed under a public-private partnership with the Rwanda Development Board (RDB). The project includes Grade A offices, a technology incubator, retail, hospitality, residential and four planned universities. Africa50 IC approval December 2022; groundbreaking September 2024. Total project value estimated USD 300 million–USD 2 billion at full build-out. Expected to generate USD 150 million in annual ICT exports and attract USD 300 million in FDI.
