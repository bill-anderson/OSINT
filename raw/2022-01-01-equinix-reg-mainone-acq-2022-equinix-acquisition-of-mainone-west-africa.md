## Description

Equinix acquired MainOne Cable Company for US$320 million, completing the transaction in April 2022. The deal provided Equinix with its first African presence: three operational data centres in Lagos (Nigeria), Accra (Ghana), and Abidjan (Côte d'Ivoire), a fourth DC under construction in Lagos, over 64,000 sq ft of gross data centre space, 570,000 sq ft of land for future development, approximately 1,200 km of terrestrial fibre in Nigeria, and the MainOne submarine cable system (approximately 7,000 km, Portugal–Nigeria with landing stations in Nigeria, Ghana, Côte d'Ivoire, and Portugal). Equinix assumed full ownership and operational control. The acquisition was announced December 7, 2021 and closed April 5, 2022.
