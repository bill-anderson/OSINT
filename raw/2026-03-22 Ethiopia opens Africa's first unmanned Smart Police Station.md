# Ethiopia opens Africa's first unmanned Smart Police Station

Ethiopia inaugurated (PM **Abiy Ahmed**) **Africa's first unmanned "Smart Police Station" (SPS)** in Addis Ababa — **AI-driven self-service kiosks** letting citizens report crimes, file complaints, pay fines and request documents 24/7 via touchscreens with officer support by video call, eliminating paperwork and "reducing corruption" in line with **[[digital-ethiopia-2030]]**. Largely self-managed, with one on-site manager and central camera monitoring; submitted cases are assigned to police personnel.
