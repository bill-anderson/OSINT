## Description

Rockefeller Foundation $5.5M collaboration with e-GUIDE (UMass Amherst) and Atlas AI to develop an AI-powered satellite data platform for economic development and climate-resilient infrastructure investment in Kenya, Nigeria, Rwanda, and Uganda.
