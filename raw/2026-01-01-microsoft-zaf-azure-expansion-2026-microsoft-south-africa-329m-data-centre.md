## Description

Microsoft announced a $329 million investment in South Africa in April 2026 to expand data centres, boost infrastructure resilience, and develop AI skills. Covers land acquisition for future data centre expansion, power and water readiness improvements, and capacity increases at existing Johannesburg and Cape Town facilities. Described as building on an earlier $1.2 billion total commitment to South Africa.
