## Description

Norfund USD 30 million debt investment in CREI, backed by Cygnum Capital's Facility for Energy Inclusion (FEI). Total package USD 90 million (FEI USD 60M + Norfund USD 30M), refinancing USD 55M bridge facilities provided in 2024 plus USD 35M new capital. Modernises approximately 4,000 telecom tower sites in Mali, South Sudan, and Central African Republic with solar energy, enabling mobile connectivity and broadband in fragile states.
