# Ethiopia's Fayda digital ID and tax-system transformation (ICTD)

ICTD analysis of integrating **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** with tax administration. Since **December 2023**, taxpayer registration has been integrated with Fayda (a unique **12-digit** number for all eligible residents; NIDP established by **Proclamation 1284/2023** under the PM's Office). Drawing on DIGITAX work in Uganda and Ghana, it identifies five potential benefits — better tax-data quality via verified identity; streamlined registration; a broader tax base (detecting non-registered taxpayers, cross-checking transactions/property); stronger enforcement; and more data-centric revenue governance — while cautioning Fayda is "no silver bullet."
