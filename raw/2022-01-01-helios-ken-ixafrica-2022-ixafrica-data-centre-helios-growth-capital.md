## Description

Helios acquired a majority stake in IXAfrica Data Centre Limited and committed $50 million in growth capital to accelerate development of IXAfrica's hyperscale-ready data centre campus in Nairobi, Kenya. IXAfrica was established in 2021 to meet soaring demand for internet connectivity and cloud services from Kenya's highly digitally-connected population and from global cloud service providers. The investment funds construction of a hyperscale campus with carrier-neutral colocation capacity serving enterprise and hyperscaler tenants in East Africa.
