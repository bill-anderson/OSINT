# WB Policy Note — Automatic Health Insurance Enrollment for Vulnerable Individuals (Egypt UHI)

World Bank policy note (**20 February 2025**) on opportunities to **automatically enrol vulnerable individuals** into Egypt's **Universal Health Insurance (UHI)** system — using existing social registries and data linkages (e.g. **[[takaful-and-karama|Takaful and Karama]]**) to extend coverage to the poor without manual application. Reference/analytical input to the data-integration (registry ↔ health-insurance) leg of Egypt's DPI.
