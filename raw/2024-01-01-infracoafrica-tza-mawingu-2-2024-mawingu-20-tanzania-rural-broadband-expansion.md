## Description

InfraCo Africa invested USD 2 million to support Mawingu's expansion into Tanzania through the acquisition of Habari Node Ltd, a Tanzania-based ISP. As part of the Mawingu 2.0 initiative, the company raised USD 15 million total to acquire Habari and expand its East African network. This extends rural fixed wireless broadband access to Tanzania using solar-powered infrastructure.
