## Description

USD 20 million sustainability-linked loan to WIOCC Group (via its subsidiary Open Access Data Centres / OADC) to finance expansion of core and edge data centres in the DRC, Nigeria, and South Africa. Pricing linked to energy efficiency improvements and EDGE green building certification. Co-financed with IFC (USD 10M + ZAR 200M) and RMB. Total package USD 41M.
