## Description

Digital Frontiers is a $74.4 million seven-year (2017-2024) USAID cooperative agreement administered by DAI that works with USAID Bureaus and Missions to identify and scale digital approaches globally including Africa. Covers digital inclusion, fintech, ICT policy, DCCP, and digital health.
