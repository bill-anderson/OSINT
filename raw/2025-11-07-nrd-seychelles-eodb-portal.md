Seychelles Selects NRD Companies to Develop New Ease of Doing Business Portal to Transform Investment Landscape

The Ministry of Investment, Entrepreneurship and Industry of Seychelles formally launched its Ease of Doing Business (EODB) Portal Project, marking a strategic leap forward in the country’s digital transformation journey.

Over a nine-month project timeline, including six months of implementation followed by a three-month pilot stage, the new system is targeted for full rollout by 30 June 2026.

A Unified Platform for Business Services

Designed as a one-stop digital gateway, the EODB Portal will allow businesses, local and international, to access crucial services, submit regulatory documents, and make payments through a single platform. By consolidating previously fragmented administrative tasks into integrated, end-to-end workflows, the system aims to:

simplify and digitize core business processes,

enhance transparency in government interactions, and

improve the overall business climate in Seychelles.

The solution will be structured around two primary user journeys:

Seamless Government Services Journey – allowing users to complete multiple procedures through a single platform.

Entrepreneur Journey – providing entrepreneurs with centralized access to guidance, resources, and information needed to start, operate, and grow their businesses with greater ease.

Technology Backbone Built for Flexibility and Scale

NRD Companies has been selected as the technology partner, bringing its GxP© – Customer Centric Service Delivery Platform built on a low-code architecture that supports rapid deployment of over 15 essential services. To support rich content and user experience, an Umbraco CMS will complement GxP, delivering an accessible and informative front end with updates on licensing, investment sectors, and business requirements.

This architecture is deliberately flexible, allowing future regulatory changes and process refinements to be accommodated without heavy redevelopment.

Since its establishment, the Ministry of Investment, Entrepreneurship and Industry (MIEI) has worked to foster an enabling environment for business growth, streamline administrative processes, and enhance Seychelles’ investment attractiveness. The EODB Portal represents a natural extension of this mission, providing a modern, digital interface to fulfill the government’s ambition of an efficient, transparent, and accessible business environment.

The Ministry believes that launching the Ease of Doing Business Portal is a pivotal moment in in its commitment to build a competitive, investor-friendly economy. This innovation strengthens the capacity of the many Ministries, Department and Agencies (MDAs) to deliver services aligned with the expectations of both domestic and foreign entrepreneurs.

Mindaugas Glodas, CEO, NRD Companies, is of the view the partnering with the Ministry on this project is a natural alignment of the mission and technology. “NRD Companies’ GxP platform is designed for just this kind of regulatory modernization, enabling disparate agency systems to come together in a unified digital experience. NRD Companies believes the EODB Portal will significantly enhance Seychelles’ standing as a destination for streamlined, transparent investment.”

About The Ministry of Investment, Entrepreneurship and Industry

The Ministry of Investment, Entrepreneurship and Industry of Seychelles is responsible for investment, entrepreneurship and business related affairs. Its core objectives are to promote greater economic activities in the country to create wealth, help increase domestic revenue collection, have a positive impact on Balance of Payments, and reduce level of unemployment in the country.

More information: https://www.miei.gov.sc/
