## Description

Norfund USD 7.5 million equity (co-led with Timon Capital) in USD 20 million Series A for OmniRetail. Nigerian digital B2B commerce platform providing informal retail shops with BNPL credit, digital payments, and fast delivery. OmniPay processed NGN 1.3 trillion (~USD 810M) transactions in 2024. Norfund's first direct equity investment in African fintech. Digital scope: B2B e-commerce, embedded finance (BNPL), POS payments, digital merchant credit scoring.
