## Description

Norfund USD 10.75 million loan (NOK ~130 million) to Greenlight Planet (now Sun King). Part of USD 90 million total facility with CDC, FMO, ResponsAbility, SIMA Funds, Symbiotics, Global Partnerships, and ARCH. Finances PAYG solar home systems with IoT digital metering and mobile money payment collection in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Nigeria. Digital scope: IoT smart metering, mobile money payment rails, digital credit platforms.
