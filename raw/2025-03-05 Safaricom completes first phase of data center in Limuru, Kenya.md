**[[safaricom]]** completed phase one of the first building at its data center in **Limuru (Redhill)**, Kenya — its fourth facility in the country. The campus will comprise three buildings; Safaricom plans **2.8MW** of IT capacity at the end of the second phase (completed January 2026), built to **Tier III** standards.

The facility uses free cooling and supplements grid power with solar, and features an innovation lab. Lead Esther Karuga said it plays "in the same league with Google and Microsoft." Safaricom operates three other facilities in and around Nairobi (Kisumu, Thika, Arena) and two in Addis Ababa; Limuru sits near Nairobi's cluster (Digital Realty, PAIX, Africa Data Centres, MTN).
