## Description

Norfund NOK 128 million receivables purchase facility (Brighter Life Kenya 1) for d.light Kenya. Facility purchases d.light solar product receivables in Kenyan Shillings, enabling up to 1.9 million people to access off-grid electricity. Parallel to DFC NOK 170M facility (Jun 2020). Digital scope: IoT-enabled solar kits, mobile money collection, digital customer credit scoring.
