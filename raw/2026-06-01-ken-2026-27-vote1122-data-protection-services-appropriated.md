## Description

**Kenya's data-protection spend becomes fiscally legible for the first time.** Through FY2025/26
the ODPC sat inside Vote 1122's programmes with no separately visible line - recorded on the KEN
hub as a dated vacuum. The FY2026/27 restructure creates sub-programme 0222020 Data Protection
Services at KSh 917.3m, with a rising three-year trajectory. Single-mandate carve-out applies:
this line is data-governance spend in whole.

## Notes

Whether 917.3m covers the ODPC alone or bundles wider data-protection functions is not stated at
Part F grain - the Recurrent Budget Book head detail would settle it (available, not staged).
