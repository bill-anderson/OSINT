Denmark's cBrain announced (January 14) a partnership with Nigerian technology provider Publica AI to deploy its F2 government software platform across Nigeria's federal ministries and agencies. F2 combines case management, automated workflows and embedded AI as a ready-to-use tool to accelerate administrative modernisation. Publica AI handles implementation and adaptation to Nigerian regulatory requirements; CEO Willie Ignatius said "all sovereign data remains in Nigeria." Nigeria ranked 144th of 193 countries in the UN e-government index (2024) and aims to digitise 75% of public services by 2027.
