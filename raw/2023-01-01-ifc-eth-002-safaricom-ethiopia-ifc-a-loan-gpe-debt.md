## Description

IFC provided a $100 million senior A-loan to Safaricom Telecommunications Ethiopia Private Limited Company (STEP), the operating subsidiary of Safaricom Ethiopia. The loan finances expansion of 4G telecom and mobile money (M-PESA) infrastructure across Ethiopia. This is a companion investment to IFC's $157.4M equity stake in the consortium (GPE). The debt financing supports Safaricom Ethiopia's capital expenditure for building base stations, deploying fibre backhaul, and expanding M-PESA services to underserved populations.
