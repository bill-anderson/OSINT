# Egypt to launch digital platform for social insurance (40 initial services)

Egypt is preparing a **new digital system for the National Social Insurance Authority (NSIA)** offering **95 electronic services** within six months — the **first phase launching 40 services**, with 55 more added in stages (authority head **Gamal Awad**). Announced at a meeting chaired by PM **Mostafa Madbouly** with Social Solidarity Minister **Maya Morsy**, who called it a "national project" letting citizens obtain insurance and pensions quickly. The system implements governance standards, **separates service requesters from providers**, and incorporates **electronic signatures, time-stamping and an electronic payment/collection system**, with electronic links to various entities.
