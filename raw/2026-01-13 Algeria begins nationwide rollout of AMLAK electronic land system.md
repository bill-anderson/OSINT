# Algeria begins nationwide rollout of AMLAK electronic land system

Algeria's **General Directorate of National Domain (DGDN)** announced on **11 January 2026** the nationwide rollout of **AMLAK** ([[amlak]]), a platform replacing paper land titles with a fully electronic system. All land-registry and land-conservation offices are to connect to the unified system, which centralises the **issuance, modification and archiving of land titles**, introduces **full traceability of land transactions** (every stage recorded), and provides real-time cadastral performance indicators. For users it is expected to cut processing times, speed real-estate transactions, strengthen property rights and reduce land disputes.

Context: in 2025 the **National Agency for Land Conservation, Cadastre and Cartography (ANCFCC)** issued **430,000 land titles** (+15% on 2024).
