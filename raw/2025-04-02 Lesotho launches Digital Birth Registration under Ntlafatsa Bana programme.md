The EU Delegation, Government of Lesotho and UNICEF launched the **Digital Birth Registration** system within the **Ntlafatsa Bana** programme (remarks by EU Ambassador Paola Amadei; attended by His Majesty King Letsie III, Prime Minister Sam Matekane and the Minister of Gender, Youth and Social Development, Pitso Lesaoana).

Amadei framed birth registration as the first step in ensuring children's rights and identities are recognised from birth, opening doors to healthcare and education. Digital birth registration is a facet of the broader **CASH Plus** approach under Ntlafatsa Bana, which integrates water access, energy, nutrition and the digitalisation of payments. She noted that **20,000 Child Grants Programme (CGP) beneficiaries** already receive grants directly via digital payments (avoiding long journeys to payment points), enhancing transparency, accountability and cost efficiency. The programme is supported by UNICEF with EU contribution.
