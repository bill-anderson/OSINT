## Description

Strengthens statistical capacity of Gabon, fills key data gaps, improves statistical production, and enhances statistical dissemination practices. Supports national accounts modernization, welfare monitoring statistics, agricultural statistics, and dissemination platform development. Includes statistical agency infrastructure modernization and institutional reform of the national statistical system.
