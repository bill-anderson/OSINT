DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Malawi. Headline figures for early 2025:

- 13.2 million cellular mobile connections at the start of 2025 (60.3 percent of the population; up 1.3 million / +10.8% year-on-year). GSMA Intelligence: 90.1% of connections are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- 3.95 million internet users in January 2025; internet penetration 18.0 percent (unchanged year-on-year; 82.0% offline).
- 1.80 million social media user identities in January 2025 (8.2 percent of the population).

Population: 21.9 million in January 2025 (+558k / +2.6% over the year). 18.8% urban, 81.2% rural. 51.2% female, 48.8% male. Median age 18.1.
