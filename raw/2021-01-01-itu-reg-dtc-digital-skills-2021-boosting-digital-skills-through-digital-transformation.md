## Description

Norway/Norad-funded ITU project (CHF 3.77M total) to boost digital skills through Digital Transformation Centres. Supports 12 countries globally; 6 SSA: DR Congo Ghana Cote d'Ivoire Rwanda Uganda Zambia. Delivers basic and intermediate digital skills training especially for rural and underserved communities; curriculum development; trainer training. Builds on ITU-Cisco DTC Initiative launched September 2019. Norad contribution CHF 3.0M; ITU ICT-DF CHF 759K. Phase 2 continuation project 9GLO23133.
