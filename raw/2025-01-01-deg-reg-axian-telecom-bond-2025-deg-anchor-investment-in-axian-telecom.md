## Description

DEG participates as anchor investor in the USD 600 million 7.250% senior notes due 2030 issued by AXIAN Telecom. The bond is part of a USD 160 million anchor investment by five development finance institutions: EAIF (USD 40M), IFC, BII, DEG, and Proparco (USD 25M). The bond was oversubscribed 2x with over USD 1.3 billion in demand. AXIAN Telecom operates across nine African markets including Madagascar, Senegal, Tanzania, Togo, Uganda, DRC, and Comoros. Proceeds used to refinance existing debt and invest in digital infrastructure, smartphone access, and mobile financial services to expand digital inclusion across Africa.
