## Description

The April-2026 supplementary moved money toward identity while cutting digital infrastructure:
NRB (Maisha Namba issuance) +50%, e-Citizen +43% - in the same instrument that stripped
KSh 3.97bn from Vote 1122. The fiscal priority signal of FY2025/26: registries and the revenue-
collecting platform over connectivity build.
