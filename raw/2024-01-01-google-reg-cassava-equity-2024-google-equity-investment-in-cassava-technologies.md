## Description

Google participated in a $90 million equity round for Cassava Technologies (December 2024) alongside US DFC ($50M, lead) and Finnfund ($15M). Google's share is estimated at approximately $25 million (total $90M minus DFC $50M minus Finnfund $15M). Cassava operates 110,000+ km of fibre, data centres, cloud services, cybersecurity, and AI tools across 30 African countries. Proceeds support data centre expansion in Kenya, Ghana, Nigeria, and other African markets.
