Related coverage of the same development (for context, not this article's text): Broadband Systems Corporation (BSC) has become an authorized Starlink reseller in Rwanda, certified by the Rwanda Space Agency, following a partnership entered into since December 2025. CCO Ndoli Mitali said customers buying Starlink through BSC receive the same quality and technical support as from the provider directly, with tailored packages for healthcare and educational institutions and small non-profits, and planned SME packages offering up to 300 Mbps. The arrangement is expected to strengthen connectivity in underserved rural communities and among small businesses.
