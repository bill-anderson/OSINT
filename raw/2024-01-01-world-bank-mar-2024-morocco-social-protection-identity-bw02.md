# World Bank — Morocco Second Identity and Targeting for Social Protection Project, 2024

The World Bank Board approved US$70 million on 4 December 2024 to reinforce Morocco's National Population Registry (RNP) and Unified Social Registry (RSU) and extend social-protection delivery to rural and remote areas.

## Description

"The World Bank's Board approved the US$70 million Morocco Second Identity and Targeting for Social Protection Project (4 December 2024), building on the first project that designed and implemented the National Population Registry (RNP) and Unified Social Registry (RSU) and enrolled about half the Kingdom's population." The new project has three components — reinforcing/extending the RNP and operationalizing the National Register Agency; expanding the RSU; and project management — extending coverage to rural/remote areas via digital/remote applications, aiming to impact at least five programs by end-2024 and eight by 2028.
