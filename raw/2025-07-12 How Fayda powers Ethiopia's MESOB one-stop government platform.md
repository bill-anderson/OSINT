# How Fayda powers Ethiopia's MESOB one-stop government platform

**[[mesob-platform|MESOB]]** (Modern Ethiopian Service for Organized Benefits) is Ethiopia's one-stop-shop government platform, built on the **[[mosip|MOSIP]]**-based **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** digital ID as its authentication backbone. Citizens validate identity and access **41 essential services across 12 federal institutions** through a single interface — passports, business registration, tax, investment services — **cutting service-delivery time by 60%**, serving 55 providers, with 15m+ registered and a 2027 universal-coverage commitment. Framed as a citizen-centric DPI model for other nations.
