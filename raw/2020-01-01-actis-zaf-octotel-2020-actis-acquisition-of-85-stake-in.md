## Description

Actis acquires an 85% stake in Octotel, a Cape Town-based fibre-to-the-home (FTTH) network operator focused on the Western Cape, South Africa. Investment of approximately USD 167 million from the Neoma Africa Fund III. Octotel's founders remain as minority shareholders. Strategy focused on expanding homes passed with fibre and driving take-up rates. Actis led a ZAR 2bn social loan facility from RMB in 2022. Actis exited to consortium led by AIIM, STOA and Thebe Investment Corporation in September 2024.
