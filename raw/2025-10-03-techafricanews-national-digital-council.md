# Senegal Launches National Digital Council to Guide Digital Transformation

Tech Africa News - 3 October 2025, by Jennifer Onyeagoro

Senegal has launched the National Digital Council (CNN), officially inaugurated on 2 October 2025 by the Prime Minister under the directive of President Bassirou Diomaye Faye. The body brings together 20 experts from the public sector, private enterprises and civil society, mandated to advise the government on global technological trends, propose strategic recommendations for digital policies, and support key structural projects.

Among its missions, the Council will encourage innovation, foster entrepreneurship, boost job creation, promote digital inclusion, strengthen cybersecurity, and ensure the protection of personal data. Its creation is part of the New Technological Deal, which aims to deliver universal internet access, introduce a biometric digital identity, and modernise public services with e-ID, e-signature and e-payment tools, alongside a sovereign cloud to secure national and citizens' data.
