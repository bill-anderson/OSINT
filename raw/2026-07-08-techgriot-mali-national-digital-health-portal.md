Mali's Ministry of Health and Social Development launched a national digital health platform at www.sante.gov.ml, centralising public-health activities, projects, programmes and news, plus practical services such as geolocation of health/administrative facilities; future functions include patient medical-record tracking and faster practitioner access to care data. Part of Mali's National Digital Health Strategic Plan (PSNSN) 2024-2028 (adopted 2024), whose priorities are producing/sharing more health data, strengthening information systems, ensuring interoperability, modernising digital infrastructure and training staff. Flags structural obstacles (power gaps, urban-rural digital divide, weak interoperability). (Complements the OpenClinic HMIS to be linked to NINA and AMO.)
