## Description

BII committed $15M to the Norrsken22 Fund (D) AB in October 2022. Norrsken22 invests in tech-enabled companies operating in fintech, market enablement, and climate tech sectors across Africa, targeting companies at growth stage ($1M-$10M ARR). The fund is a partnership between Norrsken Foundation and various DFIs. It supports the growth of tech startups across SSA including fintech, digital health, and digital market infrastructure.
