## Description

4DX Ventures led a $3M Series A funding round in Tizeti (Wifi.com.ng), a Nigerian Internet Service Provider offering unlimited broadband wifi internet access in Nigeria and Ghana. Tizeti's 4G LTE network uses solar-powered base stations to provide affordable internet to homes and businesses. The funding supports network expansion in Nigeria and growth into Ghana. Co-investors include Y Combinator Continuity, Social Capital, Lynnet Capital, and Golden Palm Investments.
