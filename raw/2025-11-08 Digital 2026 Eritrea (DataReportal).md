# Digital 2026 — Eritrea (DataReportal)

Connectivity baseline (late 2025): **859,000** cellular mobile connections (**23.7%** of population; +8.2% YoY; 49.3% broadband). Still among the lowest penetration in Africa; slow upward trajectory from 22.2% (early 2025).
