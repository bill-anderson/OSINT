## Description

MCF $200M fund-of-funds launched December 2022 to catalyse risk capital through African-owned and led investment vehicles with gender-lens investing. Invests in VC and SME funds targeting women-led enterprises across Ethiopia, Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, Rwanda, Senegal and Uganda. Target: 15,000 direct jobs. Portfolio includes Aruwa Capital, Inua Capital, Chui Ventures, VestedWorld, SME Impact Fund, Sabou Capital and others.
