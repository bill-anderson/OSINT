# Ooredoo Algeria Expands 5G Network Nationwide to Accelerate Digital Transformation

Ooredoo Algeria has announced the nationwide expansion of its fifth-generation mobile network, extending 5G coverage to all wilayas across Algeria. The move marks a major step in the country's ongoing digital transformation and reinforces the operator's commitment to expanding next-generation connectivity.

According to the company, the rollout reflects its strategy to accelerate innovation while widening access to advanced digital technologies. By expanding its 5G footprint nationwide, the operator aims to deliver faster, more intelligent, and more efficient connectivity to users across the country.

With the objective of ensuring equitable access to high-performance connectivity, Ooredoo Algeria has implemented an ambitious nationwide 5G deployment plan. The initiative also responds to the growing demand for high-speed data services across urban and regional markets.

To support the expansion, the operator plans to reinforce its network infrastructure and increase the density of transmission stations. These investments are designed to strengthen coverage and ensure service quality aligned with international standards in every wilaya.

Through this latest deployment phase, Ooredoo Algeria has become the first mobile operator to provide nationwide 5G coverage across the country. Leveraging advanced infrastructure and next-generation technologies, the company has exceeded the deployment targets outlined in regulatory specifications within its first year of rollout.

Commenting on the achievement, Roni Tohme, Chief Executive Officer of Ooredoo Algeria, emphasized:

"By extending 5G coverage nationwide, we reaffirm Ooredoo's commitment to investing in infrastructure and networks to deliver services that meet international standards, support the digital economy, and help young people and businesses seize opportunities created by digital transformation."

Ooredoo Algeria confirmed that all existing 4G USIM cards are compatible with 5G technology, allowing customers to access the new network without replacing their SIM cards. Subscribers can activate 5G services through the MyOoredoo application, via the dedicated online platform choof.ooredoo.dz, or by visiting any Ooredoo customer service center nationwide.

The rollout forms part of the operator's broader digital strategy to simplify customer access to advanced services while strengthening Algeria's digital ecosystem.
