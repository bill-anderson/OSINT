On 18 December 2025 the Ministry of Transport, Telecommunications and Artificial Intelligence Systems met the World Bank to develop a viable strategy to drive Equatorial Guinea's digital transformation. Present: Minister Honorato Evita Oma; Secretary of State for Planning María Ebiaca Moete (alternate World Bank governor); and World Bank Resident Representative Diego Alonso.

Priorities presented: the **digital agenda for Equatorial Guinea (ADIGE)**; planning for **construction of a modern data centre**; and promotion of digital skills with a focus on artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. The minister stressed the urgent need to expand and improve the capacity of the Instituto Superior de Telecomunicaciones. A continuation of the World Bank Digital Economy Diagnostic engagement — cite for the collaboration/direction, not as a source of current figures.
