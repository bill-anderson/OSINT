# EthSwitch unveils 15-year vision and Mastercard partnership

**[[ethswitch]]** launched a second five-year plan and a **15-year roadmap** ("best payment network in Africa by 2035", CEO **Yilebes Addis**), aligned with Digital Ethiopia, the National Financial Access Strategy and the National Digital Payment Strategy 2 ([[ethiopia-ndps]]). A landmark **partnership with [[mastercard]]** integrates EthSwitch into Mastercard's network (via MIP and MPGS), letting Ethiopian businesses accept international payments and banks issue co-branded international cards. Genesis Analytics was selected to develop the next phase.
