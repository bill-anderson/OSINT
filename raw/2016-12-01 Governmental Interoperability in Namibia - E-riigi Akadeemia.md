Project profile: "The Designing and Implementation of Governmental Interoperability Solution (X-Road) in Namibia," implemented by Estonia's e-Governance Academy (eGA). The project delivered a governmental interoperability framework based on the Estonian X-Road system, using the Unified eXchange Platform (UXP) technology implemented by Cybernetica Ltd to securely connect government databases and enable e-services.

eGA also developed the organisational setup, regulatory framework and principle standards for the e-government interoperability system. The project ran in three stages — inventory/readiness assessment, organisational and legal design with a test solution and roadmap, and full-scale implementation.

(Date is a proxy set to the project's completion; the project ran October 2014 – December 2016, per eGA. The project page carries no publication date.)
