## Description

MIGA political risk guarantee covering The Rise Fund II Aurora S.à r.l. (TPG private equity) equity investment in Airtel Mobile Commerce B.V.'s subsidiary providing Airtel Money digital financial services in Niger. Services include mobile wallet (cash-in/cash-out), airtime recharging, bill payments, merchant payments; with international transfers, savings and microloans in some markets. Risks: war and civil disturbance, expropriation, transfer restriction and inconvertibility for up to 5 years.
