**Birhane, Isaac, Prabhakaran, Díaz, Elish, Gabriel & Mohamed (2022), *Power to the People?
Opportunities and Challenges for Participatory AI*** (ACM EAAMO '22; authors at Mozilla / UCD /
Google / DeepMind). Full text: `raw/2022-10-01 birhane-6.pdf`.

- Examines **participatory approaches to AI** — who is consulted, how, and with what power —
  interrogating whether "participation" delivers genuine agency or merely legitimises decisions
  already made.
- Global ML-ethics scholarship; relevant here for the **"who gets a say" / sovereignty** thread
  that connects to African critiques of externally-designed AI (cf. [[alice-munyua]] on the
  [[au-continental-ai-strategy]], and Birhane's [[2020-08-21 Algorithmic Colonisation of Africa]]).
- Not an African development — recorded as reference scholarship, figures/claims kept on this
  page, not promoted into place/concept synthesis.
