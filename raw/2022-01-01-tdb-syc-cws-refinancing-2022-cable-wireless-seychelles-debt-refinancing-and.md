## Description

TDB provided a USD 50 million funding package to refinance Cable & Wireless Seychelles' outstanding debt of USD 48.1 million from the original 2019 acquisition loan. The package includes bridging and term loan facilities, a USD 5 million convertible note (convertible to CWS equity at time of share listing), and a term loan in Seychelles Rupees. The transaction also supports CWS's digital transformation activities including eBill and self-care platforms, online registrations, and bill payments. CWS is exploring fintech innovations through Nebula Fintech Limited. Following the restructuring, TDB also acquired approximately 2.9% equity stake in CWS via an off-market transaction (December 2022). CWS subsequently listed on MERJ Exchange Seychelles in September 2022.
