## Description

BII committed $44.4M to Paradigm Infrastructure in September 2025, established in 2019 to pursue opportunities to develop shared telecoms tower infrastructure across Sub-Saharan Africa. Paradigm operates and leases tower sites to mobile network operators, enabling expanded network coverage especially in underserved rural areas. The investment directly supports mobile broadband connectivity and digital inclusion across multiple SSA markets.
