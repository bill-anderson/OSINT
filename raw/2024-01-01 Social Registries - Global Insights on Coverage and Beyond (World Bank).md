# Social Registries: Global Insights on Coverage and Beyond (World Bank)

A World Bank global reference report on **social registries** — coverage, design and use across
countries — the comparative frame against which national social registries (including The Gambia's
**[[gambia-social-registry|GamSR]]**) can be read ([[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]). Reference
document; global scope, filed alongside the Gambian social-registry material in this batch. Cite,
don't absorb.
