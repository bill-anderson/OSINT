# SYSROAD — GAMTEL national broadband modernisation PPP, The Gambia, 2026

SYSROAD SA HOLDING entered a US$50 million public-private partnership with GAMTEL to upgrade The Gambia's national broadband backbone from 40Gbps to 800Gbps.

## Description

"The Gambia Telecommunications Company Ltd (GAMTEL) has entered a US$50 million Public-Private Partnership (PPP) with SYSROAD SA HOLDING to upgrade, expand, and modernize the country's national broadband and telecommunications infrastructure." "The project will modernize the national internet backbone, increasing capacity from 40Gbps to 800Gbps, a nearly 20-fold expansion." Signed at the Bakadaji Hotel in Bijilo.
