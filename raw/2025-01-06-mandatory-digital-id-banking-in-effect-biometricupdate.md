Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 6 January 2025. A government policy mandating use of the Fayda national digital ID for banking transactions (e.g. opening an account) went into effect in Addis Ababa from 1 January 2025, phased through 31 December 2026. Fayda then had 11 million+ registrations. The policy extends to bank branches in other major cities from 1 July 2025, with nationwide implementation from 1 January 2026; all account holders must link accounts to Fayda by 31 December 2026. Aims: financial inclusion, better KYC/fraud reduction, data security. The foundational start of the Fayda-banking mandate thread. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
