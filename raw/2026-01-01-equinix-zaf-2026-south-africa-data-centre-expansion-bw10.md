# Equinix — South Africa data centre expansion, 2026

Equinix announced a US$438 million (R7.5 billion) expansion of its South African data centres in Johannesburg and Cape Town, self-funded from its balance sheet, adding roughly 160 MW of capacity; it has already bought R890 million of land.

## Description

"Global data centre operator Equinix Inc. is expanding in South Africa with a planned $438 million investment, as it seeks to tap into growing demand for artificial intelligence and cloud services across Africa." "The company... said it will build additional facilities in Johannesburg and Cape Town." "Sandile Dube, Equinix's managing director for South Africa, said the company has secured about 327,000 square metres of land for future development. The expansion is expected to add roughly 160 megawatts of data centre capacity." "All investments are funded by our own balance sheet." Per the companion report, Equinix "has bought more land in that city and also in Cape Town, totalling R890 million."
