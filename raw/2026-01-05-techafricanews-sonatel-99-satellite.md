# Sonatel Orange Achieves 99% Coverage in Senegal with New Satellite Internet Offerings

Tech Africa News - 5 January 2026, by Jennifer Onyeagoro

Sonatel Orange has launched Senegal's first satellite internet offers, in partnership with Eutelsat/Konnect, providing unlimited high-speed internet to households and professionals. Combined with existing fibre, 4G and 5G networks, the satellite deployment enables Orange to cover 99% of Senegal, including remote and rural areas. The service is immediately available and installed by local teams.

In alignment with the Senegal Horizon 2050 Plan, Orange has committed to deploying one million additional fibre optic sockets between 2026 and 2028, reaching nearly two million optical sockets nationwide by end-2028. Initial December 2025 deployments were completed in isolated localities such as Moudery, Sare Bidji, Ndiedieng, Ogo and Koungheul. Orange positions itself as a partner for the digitalisation of state services, education and healthcare institutions, and secure interconnection for government services.
