## Description

ITP 316 ICT Regulation: Policy and Practice programme 2017-2024, implemented by SPIDER (Stockholm University) in partnership with PTS (Swedish Post and Telecom Authority). Provides training and capacity support to ICT regulators in 27 sub-Saharan African countries on telecom policy, spectrum management and broadband regulation.
