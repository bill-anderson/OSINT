## Description

MCC's $536.7M Côte d'Ivoire Compact (signed November 2017, entered force August 2019, closed August 2025) included an Education Management Information System (EMIS, French: Système d'Information et de Gestion de l'Education — SIGE Intégré) as the Management Systems for Decision-Making sub-activity within the Secondary Education Activity of the Skills for Employability and Productivity Project. MCC funded development and implementation of an integrated EMIS for the Ministry of National Education (MENET-FP), centralising data across HR, school management, examinations, and student assessments into a single platform. The system supports national and international student assessments (ENIA), monitoring of ~80 new secondary schools, and data-driven decision-making. Implementation contract QCBS/011 was valued at approximately $4.3 million for the consultant services component; additional hardware/software goods procurement was issued separately. Development Gateway / IREX provided data assessment and requirements support. System designed with five functional components: unified education reference data, HR management, sector statistics and monitoring, school management, and examination management.
