## Description

DEG participates in the USD 17 million IFC-led equity round for Naked Insurance, South Africa's first fully digital insurance platform. The round is led by IFC with DEG, Hollard, and Yellowwoods as co-investors. Naked uses AI and automation to provide comprehensive and instant vehicle, home, contents, and standalone item insurance. Customers can get a final insurance quote in under 90 seconds and switch or pause cover online. The investment supports Naked's strategy to expand its digital insurance offering to first-time insurance buyers and continue innovation in customer experience. DEG first invested in Naked in 2023; in January 2025 DEG participated in follow-on Series B2 round of USD 38M.
