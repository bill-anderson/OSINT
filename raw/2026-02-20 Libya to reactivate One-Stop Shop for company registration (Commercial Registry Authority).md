A meeting was held (16 February 2026) at the **Commercial Registry Authority** to discuss activating the **"One-Stop Shop"** system, implementing the Prime Minister's directives to simplify company registration and improve the business environment. Attendees included the Head of the Commercial Registry Authority and the Director General of the National Economic and Social Development Board (NESDB).

Discussion focused on consolidating the entities responsible for issuing licenses and registering companies in one location (per Law No. 23 of 2010) to ensure swift, transparent procedures. The meeting reviewed the Authority's **digital-infrastructure readiness** and its integration with the One-Stop Shop project to standardise procedures, reduce complexity and enhance investor confidence — against a backdrop of repeated past failures to reform company-registration bureaucracy.
