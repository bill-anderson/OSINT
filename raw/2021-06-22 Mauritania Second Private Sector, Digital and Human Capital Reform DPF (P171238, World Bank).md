World Bank program document (Report No. PGD146, 22 June 2021) for a proposed US$30.0 million (SDR 20.8 million) IDA development policy grant to the Islamic Republic of Mauritania — the second Development Policy Financing operation in a programmatic series of three. Its reform pillars span the business environment and financial inclusion, competitive broadband connectivity, and human capital and climate resilience.
