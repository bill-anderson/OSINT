DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Somalia (data as of early January 2025):

- Population: 19.3 million (January 2025), up 647,000 (+3.5%) year on year. Urban 48.8% / rural 51.2%. Median age 15.6.
- Mobile connections: 11.3 million cellular mobile connections, equivalent to 58.3% of the population; up 887,000 (+8.5%) year on year. GSMA Intelligence indicates 72.7% of connections are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- Internet users: 10.7 million, internet penetration 55.2%; up 357,000 (+3.5%) year on year (adoption rate unchanged). Roughly 8.66 million people remained offline.
- Social media: 3.05 million user identities in January 2025, equating to 15.8% of the population.

(Reference resource; figures are dated and should be cited, not absorbed as current state. Note the large single-year jump in reported internet penetration from 27.6% in the Digital 2024 report — sanity-check before presenting.)
