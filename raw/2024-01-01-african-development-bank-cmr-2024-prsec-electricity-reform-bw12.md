# African Development Bank — Cameroon PRSEC electricity-sector reform (AMI smart metering / Douala data centre), 2024

The African Development Bank provided CFA48 billion in financing to Cameroon's Electricity Sector Reform / Recovery Programme (PRSEC-PforR, 2024–2026), which includes Advanced Metering Infrastructure (20,000 smart meters) and a Douala data centre to centralise and secure meter data.

## Description

"The initiative is part of the Electricity Sector Recovery Plan (PRSEC), a broader reform agenda running from 2024 to 2026 and valued at nearly 400 billion CFA francs ($710 million). The programme is supported by 180 billion CFA francs in World Bank financing and an additional 48 billion CFA francs from the African Development Bank." The PRSEC's digital components include AMI smart metering (automated consumption-data transmission) and a data centre under construction in Douala.
