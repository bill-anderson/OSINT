## Description

Norrsken22 co-leads (with Costanoa Ventures) a $20M Series B round for Smile ID, Africa's leading KYC and identity verification platform. Smile ID enables African fintechs and enterprises to verify identities against government databases in real time across 30+ countries. The round brings total funding to $31M. Norrsken22 GP Lexi Novitske joins the board. Smile ID serves Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Ghana and 30+ SSA markets.
