## Description

KOICA grant ($6.3M, 2021-2026) to modernise Senegal's National Assembly ICT systems including e-voting, simultaneous interpretation, conference management, and parliamentary record digitalisation. Improves public access to parliamentary activities and strengthens women MPs' ICT capacity.
