Prime Minister Abdul Hamid Dabaiba approved the draft **Unified Social Registry** document, submitted by the Minister of Social Affairs and High Committee chair Wafaa Al-Kilani (Tripoli, 23 June 2025).

The project relies on a **unified database** integrating citizens' social and economic information to accurately and transparently identify those eligible for support, with a classification system that sets priorities by social criteria (income level, health status, family size) and periodic data-update mechanisms. The **[[libya-unified-social-registry|Unified Social Registry]]** is described as a fundamental pillar for structuring a social-security system protecting the most vulnerable, as part of efforts to develop social protection and promote fairness in the distribution of support and services.
