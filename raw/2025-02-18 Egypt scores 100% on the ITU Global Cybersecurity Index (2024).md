# Egypt scores 100% on the ITU Global Cybersecurity Index (2024)

Egypt scored **100% on the ITU Global Cybersecurity Index (GCI) 2023–2024**, placing it among the **top 12 countries globally** (per a Cabinet release). The GCI evaluates countries on five pillars — legal, technical, organisational, cooperation and capacity-building. (Contrast with neighbouring Djibouti's Tier-4 GCI standing — Egypt is one of Africa's most cyber-mature states.)
