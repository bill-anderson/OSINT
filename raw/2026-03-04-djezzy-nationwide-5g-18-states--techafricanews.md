# Djezzy Launches Nationwide 5G Network, Offering High-Speed Connectivity Across 18 Algerian States

March 4, 2026 — 1 min read
Author: Editorial Team
Category: Infrastructure

The rollout offers users faster speeds, dedicated internet upon activation, and easy setup with a single-click activation pool.

Djezzy, a leading telecommunications provider in Algeria, has officially launched its 5G network, now available across 18 states in the country. The rollout offers users faster speeds, dedicated internet upon activation, and easy setup with a single-click activation pool.

Customers can activate the service through the new Djezzy app or via the company's website at my.djezzy.dz. The provider emphasizes that its 5G network will deliver enhanced connectivity and improved internet experiences, reinforcing Djezzy's commitment to bringing the latest digital innovations to Algerian users.

Djezzy invites subscribers to experience the future of mobile connectivity with its expanded 5G network.
