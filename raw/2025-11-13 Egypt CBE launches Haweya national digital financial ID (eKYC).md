# Egypt's CBE launches Haweya national digital financial ID (eKYC)

The **[[central-bank-of-egypt|Central Bank of Egypt (CBE)]]** unveiled **[[haweya|Haweya]]**, a mobile app for **digital-ID verification and remote eKYC** — the cornerstone of a **national digital financial identity system**. Citizens register with **face + fingerprint biometrics and a digital signature**, creating a trusted digital-ID profile enabling remote **bank-account opening (37 banks)**, document verification, SIM-card purchase and access to other government/private services. Haweya CEO **Tamer Gadalla** leads; system integrator **Raya IT** recently obtained funding to contribute to Egypt's DPI expansion.

Announced at the **12th PAFIX conference** (Governor **Hassan Abdullah**), the eKYC project lets Egyptians open accounts without visiting branches. Progress cited (end-2024): **Meeza** national cards **43.5m**; **InstaPay** 16m users (1.1bn transactions, EGP 2.4tn); mobile wallets **55.5m** accounts; Apple Pay (launched Dec 2024) 40m contactless transactions. Financial inclusion reached **76.3%** (June 2025), up from 27.4% in 2016; ~53m adults hold a bank account.
