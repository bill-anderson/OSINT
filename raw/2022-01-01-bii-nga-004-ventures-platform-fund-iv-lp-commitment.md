## Description

BII committed $5M to Ventures Platform Fund IV in July 2022, a seed-stage venture fund investing in tech-enabled businesses predominantly in Nigeria. The fund addresses structural challenges in healthcare, commerce, and finance through technology. Ventures Platform portfolio includes notable Nigerian tech companies such as Paystack, Mono, 500 Chow, and others.
