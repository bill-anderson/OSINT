Mali's Autorite de Protection des Donnees a caractere Personnel (APDP) opened a regional antenna in Segou (launch ceremony 4 Dec 2025 at the governorate; national APDP delegation led by 2nd rapporteur Selly Ouane). The APDP is an independent administrative authority created by Law No. 2013-015 of 21 May 2013, charged with protecting personal data of all persons on national territory and helping regulate the sector. The Segou antenna aims to bring the institution closer to citizens and institutions, provide guidance/compliance support to data controllers, and intensify awareness, oversight and training missions in the region, with emphasis on protecting youth exposed to digital risks. Establishes APDP as an active, territorially-expanding DPA (the wiki previously held no APDP entity).
