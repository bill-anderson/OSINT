## Description

ITU is implementing agency for the regulatory and policy component of the EU-funded VaMoz Digital! programme in Mozambique. Project strengthens enabling environment for inclusive digital transformation through: policy and regulatory framework reform; institutional capacity development for telecom regulators; digital skills curriculum; and fostering a digital innovation ecosystem. ITU component (Outcome 1) of broader EUR 10M VaMoz Digital! programme (EU Global Gateway flagship). AICS implements complementary youth digital skills component.
