# Cabo Verde advances digitalisation of the National Health System (SNS)

Minister of Health **Jorge Figueiredo** (Praia, 18 Dec 2025) announced Cabo Verde is advancing digitalisation of the **National Health System (SNS)** — a **unique patient identification code**, **digital medical records**, and **electronic prescriptions**, centralising clinical data across facilities. Each citizen will have a unique code granting access to their medical history (lab tests, imaging, treatments); professionals will have full access regardless of hospital or health centre, reducing duplicate tests and optimising treatment and public-health planning.

The announcement came at the handover of **24 telemedicine units**. Electronic prescriptions are intended to ease access to medicines in pharmacies, cut unnecessary travel and reduce dispensing errors. The unique patient identifier links health-system digitalisation to Cabo Verde's broader **[[dpi.id|digital-identity]]** and interoperability agenda (see [[nosi|NOSi]]).
