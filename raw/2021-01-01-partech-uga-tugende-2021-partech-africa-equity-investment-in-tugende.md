## Description

Partech Africa and Enza Capital provided $3.6M in fresh equity to Tugende, a Uganda-based technology-enabled asset finance company that provides productive asset loans (motorcycle taxis, smartphones) to MSMEs in East Africa. The round, led by Partech, was agreed and structured in 2020 and extends Tugende's Series A. Brings total Series A equity to $9.9M.
