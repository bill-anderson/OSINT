# Govt developing new digital system to enhance public service delivery

Related coverage of the same announcement (for context, not this article's text): on 14 October 2025, Minister of Local Government Dominique Habimana told the Chamber of Deputies that Rwanda would launch a new AI-powered digital system, "Mbaza" (meaning "ask me"), to help citizens report and track their issues, integrating with Irembo and the CRVS system and replacing the old e-Citizen platform. He said records from all 416 sectors had been fully digitalized. Mbaza later entered a trial phase in the City of Kigali from 27 January 2026.
