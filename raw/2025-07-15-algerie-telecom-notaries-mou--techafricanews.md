# Algeria Strengthens E-Governance with New Notarial Sector Digital Partnership

TechAfrica News, 15 July 2025

The signing ceremony took place in the presence of the President of the National Chamber of Notaries and the President and CEO of Algeria Telecom.

In a strategic move to accelerate digital transformation in Algeria's public service sectors, Algeria Telecom has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the National Chamber of Notaries. The signing ceremony took place in the presence of the President of the National Chamber of Notaries and the President and CEO of Algeria Telecom.

This partnership is part of ongoing national efforts to strengthen institutional collaboration and support the modernization of administrative functions through technology. The MoU sets the foundation for enhanced cooperation between the two entities, with the aim of integrating digital tools and services into the documentation and notarial sector.

The agreement focuses on supporting the digitalization of notarial processes, facilitating more efficient communication between notaries and other government bodies, and improving the overall accessibility and reliability of notarial services. Through this cooperation, both parties intend to promote innovation and enhance operational efficiency within the legal documentation system.

This initiative forms part of a broader national strategy to modernize key sectors and reinforce Algeria's commitment to digital transformation.
