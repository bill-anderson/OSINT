# Egyptian mobile operators — US$3.5bn 5G spectrum award to NTRA, 2026

Egypt signed a US$3.5 billion agreement in 2026 with its four mobile operators (Telecom Egypt, Vodafone Egypt, Orange Egypt, e& Egypt) for an additional 410 MHz of spectrum — doubling their holdings — to support 5G expansion under the National Spectrum Strategy 2026–2030.
