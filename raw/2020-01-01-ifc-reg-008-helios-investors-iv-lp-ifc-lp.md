## Description

IFC committed $50 million as a limited partner in Helios Investors IV L.P., the fourth flagship private equity fund managed by Helios Investment Partners, Africa's largest dedicated Africa-focused PE firm. The fund targets investments across sectors in Sub-Saharan Africa with significant allocation to digital infrastructure, fintech, and technology-enabled businesses. Helios IV has invested in companies including Vantage Towers Africa and other digital and telecoms assets across SSA, continuing the firm's track record in Africa's digital economy.
