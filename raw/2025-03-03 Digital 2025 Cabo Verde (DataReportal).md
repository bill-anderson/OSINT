# Digital 2025 — Cabo Verde (DataReportal)

Connectivity baseline (early 2025):

- **Mobile connections:** **604,000** — **115%** of population; +13,000 (+2.3%) YoY. GSMA Intelligence estimates 91.4% broadband (3G/4G/5G).
- **Internet users:** **387,000** — internet penetration **73.5%** (high by regional standards); ~139,000 (26.5%) remained offline.

Reference statistics underpinning the CPV hub connectivity baseline.
