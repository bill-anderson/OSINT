Libya's National High Electoral Commission (**HNEC**) launched an electronic verification mechanism to confirm voter identity as part of the municipal council elections, coinciding with the distribution of voter cards. The project initially targeted four municipalities: Zliten, Al-Sbiaa, Brak and Al-Abrak.

The commission said the step enhances the electoral process to the highest standards of integrity and professionalism; electronic verification of voter identities is conducted using advanced devices that register voters' **biometric data** during the collection of their voter cards at designated election centres.
