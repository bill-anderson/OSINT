## Description

AFC provided and successfully syndicated funded participations under a USD 95 million senior debt facility to finance the supply of equipment to a leading telecoms company in Nigeria. This is disclosed on AFC's syndications page as a completed transaction, with AFC acting as arranger. The borrower's identity is not publicly disclosed but is described as a 'leading telecoms company in Nigeria'. The facility is distinct from the MTN Nigeria equipment deal and supports mobile network infrastructure deployment.
