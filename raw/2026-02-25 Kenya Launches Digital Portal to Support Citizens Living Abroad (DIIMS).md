Kenya launched a unified digital portal — the **Diaspora Integrated Information Management System (DIIMS)** — on the **eCitizen** platform, introduced by the State Department for Diaspora Affairs, enabling citizens abroad to register (individually or as associations), report missing persons and deaths, access crisis-mapping tools, and submit distress alerts in cases of abuse, overwork or unsafe conditions.

A 24-hour diaspora response centre (phone, WhatsApp or email) handles emergencies including medical situations, arrests and lost travel documents. The launch coincides with the **Kazi Majuu** programme (regulated overseas employment — e.g. Germany, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, the UK) and forms part of wider efforts to modernise Kenya's digital public services.
