## Description

EUR 10M equity in AfricInvest Financial Inclusion Vehicle (FIVE), formerly AfricInvest Financial Sector Holding. An evergreen pan-African investment fund focused on financial inclusion across Africa. Initial strategy targeted established banks, insurers, and NBFIs; evolved to include fintech companies with digital financial services. BIO partner for 20+ years with AfricInvest, investing EUR 76M total across 9 AfricInvest funds.
