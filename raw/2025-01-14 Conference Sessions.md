## Fintech Festival

## Empowering East Africa

The Fintech Festival Tanzania 2025 focuses on Innovation, Inclusion, Governance, and Advancement, reflecting the transformative nature of f nancial technology in today’s world and exploring how emerging technologies like digital payments, AI, and blockchain are transforming financial services and promoting financial inclusion in East Africa. The event will highlight the latest fintech innovations, regulatory frameworks, and strategies for fostering growth, with a focus on good governance, sustainable solutions, and cybersecurity. The aim is to foster an inclusive, resilient, and sustainable financial ecosystem that drives meaningful change in the industry
