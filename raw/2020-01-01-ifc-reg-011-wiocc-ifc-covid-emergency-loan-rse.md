## Description

IFC provided a $20 million senior loan to WIOCC under the Real Sector Emergency (RSE) COVID-19 Response Facility in 2020. WIOCC is a carrier-of-carriers providing wholesale fibre and data centre connectivity across 20+ African countries. The COVID-19 emergency facility supported WIOCC's liquidity and continued capital investment during the pandemic, enabling sustained operation and expansion of digital infrastructure at a time of sharply increased demand for broadband and cloud services as economies shifted online.
