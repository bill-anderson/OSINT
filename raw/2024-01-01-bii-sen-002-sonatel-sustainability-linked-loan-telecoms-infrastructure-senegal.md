## Description

BII provided €25M (~$26.7M) as part of an €87M sustainability-linked loan to Sonatel, the leading telecoms operator across West Africa (Senegal, Mali, Guinea, Guinea Bissau, Sierra Leone). Total package: €32M IFC + €25M BII + €30M Proparco, arranged May 2024. The investment supports improvements to essential telecommunications infrastructure and expansion of reliable, affordable mobile and fixed broadband services in Senegal, directly advancing digital transformation. Sonatel subsidiary of Orange Group.
