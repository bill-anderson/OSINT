# Gates Foundation — Senegal New Deal Technologique digital ID / AI, 2025

The Gates Foundation committed US$10 million to Senegal's New Deal Technologique, funding a universal digital identity, an AI hub (health and agriculture) and a Delivery Unit, announced on the margins of UNGA80 in September 2025.
