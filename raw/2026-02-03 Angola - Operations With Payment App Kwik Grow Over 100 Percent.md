At least **35 million transactions totalling 590 billion kwanzas** were processed through the KWiK instant payment system in 2025 — over 100% growth in volume and value versus 2024. December 2025 alone saw 3.5 million transfers worth 74.5 billion kwanzas, establishing KWiK as a pillar of the modernisation of the Angolan Payment System and financial inclusion.

The data was disclosed at the 7th Ordinary Session of the Coordination Committee of the National Financial Inclusion Strategy (CCENIF), led by Minister of State for Economic Coordination José de Lima Massano. KWiK's growth advances the ENIF 2025-2027 objectives — expanding digital-financial-service access, interoperability between institutions and lower consumer costs. Currently 22 financial institutions (banking and non-banking) are part of the KWiK ecosystem; the roadmap adds QR-code payments, service and state payments, and merchant management.
