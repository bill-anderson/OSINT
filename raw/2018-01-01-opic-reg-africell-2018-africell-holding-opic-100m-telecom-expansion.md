## Description

OPIC loan of $100 million to Africell Holding Limited to finance upgrades and expansion of 2G, 3G, and 4G mobile networks in The Gambia, Sierra Leone, Uganda, and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Up to $40M may refinance existing debt. OPIC commitment letter signed November 2018; finance agreement concluded May 2019. Africell is a US-owned mobile operator and the first to receive DFC/OPIC financing. The facility supported over 12 million subscribers and provided affordable mobile voice and data services in frontier markets. Africell repaid the loan three years ahead of schedule in December 2024.
