# Orange Expands 4G Network to Provinces in the Central African Republic

May 26, 2025 · 1 min read
Author: Aayushya Ranjan

*Orange launches 4G in CAR provinces, introducing new mobile internet offers with CEO Max Francisco.*

[Orange Centrafrique](https://www.orangerca.com/) is expanding 4G coverage across the Central African Republic. Since May 22, Orange teams have been visiting provincial towns to introduce new mobile internet offers tailored for 4G users.

The rollout continues with the CEO of RCA, Max Francisco, joining teams in Boali, Bossembélé, and Bouar to promote the service.

This expansion aims to bring faster and more reliable mobile internet to more Central Africans outside the capital.
