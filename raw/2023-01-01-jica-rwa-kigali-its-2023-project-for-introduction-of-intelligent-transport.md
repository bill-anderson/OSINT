## Description

JICA grant aid for deployment of intelligent transport system at 20 intersections in Kigali City. Includes traffic control equipment (traffic lights and traffic control consoles), road-surface markings, detailed design and supervision, and technical assistance for facility management including development of manuals, operation of traffic control and signal control systems, and analysis of traffic data. Grant agreement signed October 5, 2023.
