Côte d'Ivoire approved a decree (**18 June 2025**, Council of Ministers) to **digitize nationality certificates** — a joint Justice + Digital Transition proposal — modernising a document previously prone to fraud and hard to obtain. It builds on the e-Justice platform (online nationality-certificate applications) and **[[oneci]]** identity systems, part of the country's e-services modernisation.
