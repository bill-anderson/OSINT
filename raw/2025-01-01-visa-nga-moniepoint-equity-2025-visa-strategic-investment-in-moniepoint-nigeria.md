## Description

Visa made a strategic equity investment in Moniepoint Inc., Nigeria's fastest-growing fintech unicorn (valued >$1 billion), as part of Moniepoint's Series C funding round in January 2025. The investment exceeded $10 million. Moniepoint serves 2.5+ million business owners in Africa with payment solutions, business banking, and financial tools. Visa's investment is intended to accelerate financial inclusion for African SMEs and expand Moniepoint's operations across Africa.
