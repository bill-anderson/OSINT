## Description

Context:   The East African Community (EAC) is one of the fastest-growing economic regions in Africa. Despite digital advancements, higher education and research face significant challenges. STEM education lacks practical orientation, collaboration with industries is insufficient, and women are severely underrepresented in technology-related programs. Additionally, harmonized standards and policies for the sustainable use of emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI) are missing.
