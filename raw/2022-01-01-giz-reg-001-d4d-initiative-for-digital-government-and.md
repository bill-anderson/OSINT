## Description

Multi-donor project jointly co-financed by the European Union and BMZ. Aims to support Kenya, Somalia and Djibouti to enhance public sector service delivery through improved secure digital channels. GIZ leads e-government; Expertise France leads cybersecurity; FIIAPP leads change management.
