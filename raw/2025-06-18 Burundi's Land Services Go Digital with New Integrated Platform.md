Burundian company **Mediabox** launched (17 June 2025) an **Electronic Document and Records Management System (EDRMS)** for the Directorate of Land Titles and National Cadastre (**DTFCN**), funded by the World Bank — an internal tool for land-administration agents providing structured file consultation, secure archiving and full operation traceability.

The EDRMS integrates with two other DTFCN platforms: the **Building Permit System (BPS)** (permit applications and issuance of initial land titles) and the **Property Management System (PMS)** (updates to existing titles — transfers, corrections, mortgages) — together forming a land-administration digital ecosystem addressing bureaucratic delays, absent central archives and land-title legal insecurity. The initiative sits within the **Digital Foundations Project (PAFEN)**, a $92 million World Bank programme for rural broadband, digital skills and public-service digitisation. Burundi ranked **183rd of 193** on the UN's **2024** E-Government Development Index.
