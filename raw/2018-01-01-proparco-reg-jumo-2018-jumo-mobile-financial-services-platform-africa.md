## Description

USD 3 million equity investment in JUMO, a fintech startup offering mobile loans, savings, and financial services to unbanked populations in SSA via mobile phone networks. JUMO's platform uses digital credit scoring algorithms. Proparco's first investment in an African fintech. Operates in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, Ghana.
