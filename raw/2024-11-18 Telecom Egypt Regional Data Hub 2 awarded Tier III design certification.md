# Telecom Egypt's Regional Data Hub 2 awarded Tier III design certification

**[[telecom-egypt|Telecom Egypt]]** received **Uptime Institute Tier III Design Certification** for its **Regional Data Hub 2 (RDH 2)**, building on RDH 1 (full utilisation within a year). RDH 2 has an estimated **IT load of 4.6 MW** (vs 2.5 MW for RDH 1) and is registered for LEED. Execution was expected to complete by end-2025. Once operational, RDH 2 leverages the **13 submarine cable systems** then landing on Egypt's eastern coast (plans to expand to **18**), reinforcing Egypt's role as a global connectivity hub. CEO **Mohamed Nasr** committed to pursuing all three Uptime certifications.
