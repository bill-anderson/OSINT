# Gambia National Digital Identity Strategy (Strategy Report)

The Government of The Gambia's **National Digital Identity Strategy** report — the strategy document
(validated May 2024; part of the National Digital ID and Digital Transformation Strategy 2023-2028)
setting out the country's plan for a secure, inclusive digital-identity ecosystem ([[dpi.id]]). The
policy frame behind the Margins/MIDSA national-ID build ([[gambia-national-id-contract]]) and the
eCRVS unique-national-ID backbone. Reference instrument.
