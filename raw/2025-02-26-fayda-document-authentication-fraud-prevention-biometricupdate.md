Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 26 February 2025. At a "Fayda for Ethiopia" public discussion (organised by the state Ethiopian News Agency with NIDP), Fayda was cited as a vital tool for document authentication and fraud prevention across public/private services. As of 26 February, 9.1 million authentications had been completed using Fayda. Attendees included CRRSA, Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE), the Documents Authentication and Registration Service (DARS) and the Ministry of Revenue.
