[Cybastion](https://www.cybastiontech.com/) is reinforcing its role in digital transformation across Africa through a suite of government-focused technology solutions designed to deliver measurable public value, improve service delivery, and strengthen revenue systems. The company’s Digital Solutions portfolio spans electronic payments, taxation, visas, and healthcare, aimed at helping governments modernize operations while expanding accessibility for citizens and businesses.

At the core of Cybastion’s offering is its electronic payment management platform, which enables governments to streamline transactions, enhance transparency, and broaden financial inclusion. The system supports the modernization of public payment infrastructure, making it easier for citizens and businesses to interact with government services digitally.

Its eTax platform further strengthens public finance systems through an integrated e-filing and e-payment portal. The solution includes a full tax management application covering assessment, taxation, collection, and audit processes, helping authorities improve compliance, efficiency, and revenue generation.

Cybastion also provides an eVisa platform, allowing countries to shift from paper-based visa processing to secure digital applications. The platform simplifies procedures for travelers while improving border management, data accuracy, and processing efficiency for governments.

In the healthcare sector, Cybastion’s eHealth platform, developed in collaboration with IQVIA, delivers a mobile digital health solution designed to enhance healthcare delivery across Africa. The system supports better data management, improved patient services, and more efficient healthcare administration.

Meanwhile, Cybastion has announced a major milestone in Benin, where the Ministry of Economy and Finance is expanding the country’s digital infrastructure to ensure full interoperability with SECUROUTE, the national platform for managing and securing road enforcement data.

Cybastion is partnering with the Government of Benin on the technology upgrade, which forms part of broader efforts to modernize public administration, particularly in road safety and traffic management. Developed in collaboration with the Ministry of Transport, the National Police, and national road safety agencies, the SECUROUTE platform enables secure and efficient data exchange across government systems.

The platform centralizes, secures, and tracks information generated by traffic monitoring and regulatory systems, providing authorities with a unified and reliable source of road enforcement data. By consolidating this information, SECUROUTE improves coordination among agencies, enhances operational efficiency, and supports more effective responses to traffic, safety, and compliance challenges.

Together, Cybastion’s digital platforms and its work in Benin highlight the growing role of integrated digital infrastructure in strengthening governance, improving citizen services, and enabling data-driven public sector operations across the continent.
