[DISCOVER THE MISSION](https://commission.aiforgood.itu.int/about/ "About Us")

Artificial intelligence is the most transformative technology of our lifetime, reshaping how knowledge is generated and enabling software to reason, decide, and act autonomously. While the potential is extraordinary, the challenge is to ensure these systems are trusted and deliver benefits for people and societies worldwide.

The AI for Good Global Commission brings together global leaders from government, business, and international institutions to engage directly with the defining technical, socio-economic, and policy questions surrounding AI. New Commissioners are set to join, further expanding the breadth of expertise and stakeholder perspectives.  
**  
Its purpose is not simply to advance dialogue, but to help identify practical pathways to strengthen trust, support responsible innovation, and enhance human capability.**
