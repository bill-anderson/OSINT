## Description

USD 10 million subscription to a green bond arranged by Symbiotics Investments and on-lent to Sun King (Greenlight Planet / GLP), a leading off-grid solar kit provider in SSA. Proceeds support distribution of solar home systems and lanterns with digital pay-as-you-go (PAYG) financing via mobile money. Sun King is one of the world's largest off-grid solar PAYG providers in SSA.
