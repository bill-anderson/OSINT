## Description

MIGA guarantee of $81 million to Orange Cote d'Ivoire Participations (OCIP) covering existing equity investment and future retained earnings for telecom operations and expansion in Orange Liberia Inc., including construction of telecom towers, installation of fiber optic cable, and provision of telecom services and mobile finance services. Risks: Expropriation and War and Civil Disturbance for up to 8 years. Issued September 8, 2023 (FY2024). MIGA's first contract in Liberia since 2016.
