# AfricaNenda Foundation — Annual Report 2025

> ## Excerpt
> The year "from preparation to execution": record-speed IIPS launches (Rwanda eKash, Liberia in 73
> days), AU-Commission cross-border harmonisation work, and SIIPS 2025.

> **Provenance note.** Self-published NGO report; several figures are self-reported (eKash volumes) or
> rest on AfricaNenda's own SIIPS dataset (circular within its corpus). A SIIPS launch date printed as
> "November 13, 2024" is almost certainly a typo for November 2025.

**Artefact:** ![[2026-03-01 AfricaNenda Annual Report 2025.pdf]]

## Key facts

- **Rwanda eKash relaunch** (Feb 2025, on Mojaloop; operator RSwitch): 1.5m tx/month, $3.08m/month,
  +40% vs 2024. **Liberia** mobile-money IPS launched **73 days** after a Nov 2025 kick-off (G2P first;
  958,014 tx / $11.3m by 12 Feb 2026). Ethiopia's EthSwitch "EthBridge" middleware due mid-2026;
  Cabo Verde IIPS strategy approved 2025; nine IIPS projects total.
- **AU Commission cross-border harmonisation**: Sept 2025 AACB Assembly of Governors **Decision 21**
  tasked its Task Force with a Regulatory Harmonization Framework for Cross-Border Payment Services;
  feasibility study (benchmarking EU PSD1–3, ASEAN) to complete Q1 2026.
- Org: 25 staff in 13 countries; goal of a live IPS in every African country by 2030.
