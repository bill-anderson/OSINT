# Regulation Governing SIM Card Registration in Rwanda (RURA No. 18/2024)

Companion source page for the binary artefact
`2024-08-09-Regulation-SIM-Card-Registration-Rwanda-RURA-18-2024.pdf`
(Regulation No. 18/R/SM-ICT/RURA/2024 of 09/08/2024).

Primary legal instrument establishing Rwanda's SIM-registration regime, including
identity-verification against the national-ID database, anti-fraud controls (no proxy
registration, OTP validation, SIM-swap safeguards, SIM-count limits), operator-database
security and data-protection duties, and administrative fines. Full analysis on the
instrument page [[rwanda-sim-card-registration-regulation-2024]]; issuer
[[rura-rwanda]].
