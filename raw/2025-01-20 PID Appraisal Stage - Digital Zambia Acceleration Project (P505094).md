# Project Information Document (Appraisal Stage): Digital Zambia Acceleration Project - DZAP (P505094)

Companion source page for the artefact `2025-01-20 PID Appraisal Stage - Digital Zambia Acceleration Project (P505094).pdf`, held in `raw/`.

Report No. PIDIA00880. Borrower: Republic of Zambia. Implementing agency: Smart Zambia Institute.

**ESTIMATED DATES — NOT AN APPROVAL RECORD.** The PID prints "Estimated Appraisal Date: 13-Jan-2025" and
"Estimated Approval Date: 27-Mar-2025". These are **planned** dates in an appraisal-stage document. The
only actual date it carries is its own preparation date, **20-Jan-2025**; its decision line says merely
that "the review did authorize the team to appraise and negotiate". **Do not file 27-Mar-2025 as an
approval.** The wiki already holds the DZAP **PAD** (Report No. PADHI00683) dated **2025-09-11** — later
than the PID's estimated approval, which shows the estimate did not hold.

**HISTORICAL BASELINE.** This is the same project as the held PAD, at an earlier stage, and it must not
out-rank it. What it uniquely records is the January-2025 appraisal-stage plan and its estimated
timeline. Financing: **US$120.00m total = US$100.00m IDA Grant + US$20.00m unguaranteed commercial**,
financing gap zero; flagged MFD-Enabling and Private Capital Enabling. PDO: "Increase access to, and
inclusive use of, the internet and digitally enabled services." Four components: affordable broadband;
interoperable and secure data platforms; digital skills and capabilities; programme management. It is the
**fifth operation of the IDEA MPA**, which the Board approved **27 June 2024**, following Angola, DRC,
Malawi and COMESA as coordinating REC.
