## Description

LeapFrog Investments acquires a controlling stake in Goodlife Pharmacy Africa, Kenya's leading modern retail pharmacy chain, for USD 22 million. Investment supports expansion of a digital health platform across Kenya and Uganda, providing affordable access to genuine medicines, prescription drugs, and health services through an omnichannel model. Goodlife grew to approximately 150 pharmacy and health hub locations across Kenya and Uganda. LeapFrog exited fully to CFAO Healthcare Group in July 2025 after an 8-year holding period.
