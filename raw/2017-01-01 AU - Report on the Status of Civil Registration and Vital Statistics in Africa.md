**Binary artefact:** [[2017-01-01 AU - Report on the Status of Civil Registration and Vital Statistics in Africa.pdf]] (42 pages).

African Union / UNECA continental report on the status of **civil registration and vital statistics (CRVS)** across Africa, produced under the APAI-CRVS monitoring framework (based on the October 2016 monitoring round). Documents the shift from fragmented to integrated CRVS improvement, with more than half of countries having conducted assessments but persistent capacity gaps. A regional ([[XAF]]) CRVS reference; country figures not promoted to place pages.
