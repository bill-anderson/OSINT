CHiLi (MTML) step-by-step customer guide to the electronic Know-Your-Customer (eKYC) process for mandatory SIM re-registration in Mauritius. Under the ICTA (Registration of SIM) Regulations 2023 — in force from 31 October 2023 — all SIM holders in the Republic of Mauritius must re-register their SIM(s), with operator deadlines running to 30 April 2024; the guide walks users through online eKYC re-registration (e.g. via operator eKYC apps/websites) and in-person options.

Reference/how-to artefact tied to the 2023-2024 SIM re-registration drive. No explicit publication date on the artefact; dated by proxy to the re-registration window (year precision).
