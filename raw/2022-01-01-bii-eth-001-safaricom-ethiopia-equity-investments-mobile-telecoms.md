## Description

BII is a founding investor in the Global Partnership for Ethiopia (GPE) consortium, which holds equity in Safaricom Ethiopia — the second telecoms licence in Ethiopia, led by Safaricom PLC (Kenya). BII committed multiple equity tranches to the consortium: $11.1M (Apr 2024, F331601-03), $11.6M (Oct 2024, F331601-04), $12.1M (Oct 2024, F331601-05). Additionally, BII invested $10.9M (Jun 2023) in SafariCom Telecommunications Ethiopia Private Limited directly, and $30.5M (Apr 2023) in Safaricom PLC (Kenya-listed parent). Safaricom Ethiopia launched in 2022, providing 4G connectivity, mobile money (M-PESA), and digital services. As of 2024, 4G coverage doubled and mobile data prices fell by up to 70%.
