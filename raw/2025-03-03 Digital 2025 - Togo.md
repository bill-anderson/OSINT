DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Togo (data as of early January 2025):

- Population: 9.62 million in January 2025, up 206,000 (+2.2%) year-on-year. Urban 45.3%, rural 54.7%. Female 49.7%, male 50.3%. Median age 19.1.
- Mobile connections: 7.53 million cellular mobile connections in early 2025, equivalent to 78.2% of the total population (per GSMA Intelligence). Connections rose by 502,000 (+7.2%) year-on-year. 63.5% of connections are "broadband" (3G/4G/5G). Note: some connections are voice/SMS only; the figure is not a proxy for mobile internet use.
- Internet users: 3.56 million in January 2025, an internet penetration rate of 37.0% of the population. Users increased by 76,000 (+2.2%) year-on-year; adoption rate unchanged. 6.06 million people (63.0%) remained offline.
- Social media: 934,000 social media user identities in January 2025, equal to 9.7% of the total population.

Note: internet-use figures may under-represent current realities owing to data-collection lags. Age breakdown of population: 0-4 (14.1%), 5-12 (20.3%), 13-17 (11.4%), 18-24 (13.0%), 25-34 (14.3%), 35-44 (11.0%), 45-54 (7.9%), 55-64 (4.8%), 65+ (3.3%).
