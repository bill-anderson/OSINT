[Axiz,](https://info.axiz.com/) a leading distributor of digital technologies and services in Africa, has welcomed the recent investment announcement from [Equinix Inc.,](https://www.equinix.com/) noting it as a strong signal of continued momentum in the continent’s digital infrastructure landscape.

The investment reinforces South Africa’s role as a key data centre hub and expands access to globally connected infrastructure for businesses and partners across the region.

> “For the channel, this means greater access to global platforms, interconnection ecosystems and the ability to build solutions that can extend beyond local markets.”
> 
> – **Terence Barter, Executive, Axiz**

Demand across Africa is shifting quickly. The growth of artificial intelligence workloads, increased hybrid cloud adoption and rising data sovereignty requirements are driving the need for infrastructure that is both more capable and more accessible.

The expansion of providers such as Equinix strengthens the foundation needed to support this demand, bringing additional capacity, improved connectivity and environments designed for modern workloads.

As Equinix’s distribution partner in South Africa, Axiz plays an important role in helping partners turn this infrastructure into practical, market-ready solutions. This includes commercial support, technical expertise and access to broader solution stacks across cloud, data, security and AI.

South Africa’s role is also evolving beyond its domestic market, increasingly supporting regional demand and acting as a gateway for digital services across the continent.

> “We are seeing a more practical environment take shape. Partners can move faster, deploy with confidence and deliver solutions that meet both global standards and local requirements.”
> 
> – **Terence Barter, Executive, Axiz**

While Africa still represents a smaller share of global data centre capacity, ongoing investment, the presence of hyperscalers such as Microsoft and Amazon, and growing enterprise demand continue to strengthen the market.

For Axiz, this points to a shift towards deeper ecosystem enablement.

Barter concluded that the real value of infrastructure comes through the channel.

> “Our focus is on ensuring partners are ready to take advantage of this growth and deliver meaningful outcomes for customers across Africa.”
> 
> – **Terence Barter, Executive, Axiz**
