# Orange Burkina Faso — white-zones mobile coverage PPP (phase 1), 2022

Under an ARCEP-run universal-service PPP, Orange Burkina Faso financed over 60% of the ~13 billion FCFA phase-1 build of 183 mobile sites covering ~300 previously unserved localities.

## Description

Under a public-private partnership run by the regulator ARCEP, Orange Burkina Faso was awarded (by tender) implementation of phase 1 of the white-zones ("zones blanches") coverage programme, installing 183 mobile sites (each ~6 km range) that newly covered about 300 of the 11,000 white zones nationally. ARCEP president Tontama Charles Millogo stated "L'opérateur a supporté à plus de 60% le coût"; project chief Soumanan Sanou put the phase-1 cost at "un peu plus de 13 milliards F CFA."
