## Description

ITU technical assistance to Uganda for developing and implementing a National ICT Development Strategy. Activities include strategy formulation; institutional capacity building for NITA-U and Ministry of ICT; training workshops on digital economy regulatory frameworks; and support for digital transformation roadmap. Implemented January 2021 to December 2023.
