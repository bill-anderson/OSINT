# Ethio Telecom sets 1M-per-month Fayda registration target

State telco **[[ethio-telecom|Ethio Telecom]]** is using its service centres, franchise outlets and partners across **29 cities** to drive **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** registration — targeting **1 million digital IDs per month** and 32m of the 90m the country aims to reach by 2028. It urged all Ethiopians aged five and above — urban and rural residents, students, civil servants, displaced persons and legally-resident foreigners — to enrol with proof of address (Kebele ID, driver's licence or passport).
