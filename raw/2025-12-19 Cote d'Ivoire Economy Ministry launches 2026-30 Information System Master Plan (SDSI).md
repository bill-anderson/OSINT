Côte d'Ivoire's **Ministry of Economy, Planning and Development (MEPD)** adopted a **2026–2030 Information System Master Plan (SDSI)** (announced Dec 2025, Abidjan) — a framework to modernise digital tools, strengthen **data security**, and improve public-service management and digital governance. Chief of staff Yéo Nahoua called it "a strategic tool to strengthen digital governance and improve the effectiveness of public action" — a ministry-level IT-governance plan alongside the national digital strategy.
