# South Sudan's NCA establishes Gateway Services and Data Centre Oversight Committee

> ## Excerpt
> South Sudan's NCA formed a Gateway Services and Data Centre Oversight Committee (Jan 2026) with Swiss operator MGI Communications AG, to digitise SSIGW gateway revenue and bring the national data centre under sovereignty/security oversight.

South Sudan's communications regulator, the National Communication Authority (NCA), has established a new oversight body to manage the country's international gateway services and national data centre. NCA Director General Rizig Dominic Samuel chaired a meeting with MGI Communications AG (also styled "mgi communications ag") on Friday 17 January 2026 to formally constitute the **Gateway Services and Data Centre Oversight Committee** (styled by Africa Arbitration as the "Supervisory Committee for Gateway Services and the National Data Centre").

The committee is mandated to strengthen governance, accountability, and institutional control over South Sudan's gateway services and national data centre infrastructure. Its priorities include:

- Digitizing revenue streams from the **South Sudan International Gateway (SSIGW)** to improve transparency and efficiency;
- Modernizing existing infrastructure to support the country's broader digital transformation agenda;
- Ensuring operations comply with national sovereignty and security requirements.

The NCA says enhanced regulatory oversight and digitized revenue collection will ensure national communication resources contribute directly to economic growth, technological independence/self-reliance, and a more connected digital ecosystem.

**MGI Communications AG** is a Swiss-based international telecommunications company that manages South Sudan's international gateway services and critical data centre infrastructure. It specializes in integrated voice, data and infrastructure management for emerging markets, and is currently working on digitizing national revenue systems, upgrading gateway technology, and expanding digital inclusion to underserved regions across the country.
