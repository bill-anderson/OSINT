CBL Governor **Naji Issa**, with PM **Aldabaiba**, inaugurated the **Second Libya International Exhibition for Electronic Payment and Financial Technologies (EPAIX 2026)** on 17–18 June (Tripoli International Fair, 40+ participating entities). Three new services were launched:

1. **Acceptance of international Visa cards** in foreign currency through local POS.
2. **SoftPOS** technology for the first time in Libya — converting NFC smartphones into authorised POS.
3. Start of the **National Stand-In Processing Service** — ensuring continuity of e-payment services during communications or banking-system disruption.
