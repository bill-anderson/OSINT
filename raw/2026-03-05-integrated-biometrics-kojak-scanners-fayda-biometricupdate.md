Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 5 March 2026. A case study reports U.S. firm Integrated Biometrics' MOSIP-compliant Kojak fingerprint scanners are being used by NIDP for fingerprint capture during Fayda enrollment - lightweight (725g), rapid capture from dry fingers, operable in direct sunlight/extreme temperatures and low power (kits run long periods off-grid), aiding rural/underserved inclusion. Since 2023 Ethiopia has run the World Bank-funded Digital ID for Inclusion and Services Project; 30 million+ registered, 90+ agencies integrated, 90 million target by end-2027.
