The U.S. ITA Country Commercial Guide (May 2026) frames Côte d'Ivoire's push to be **francophone West Africa's digital hub** — expanding infrastructure and connectivity, financial inclusion and a secure digital environment under the **National Digital Development Strategy**. It names **[[artci]]** (regulator/DPA), **ANSUT** (universal service), the **National Cybersecurity Agency** and **BCEAO**. Internet penetration was cited as projected to reach **67% in 2025** (31.9% of the population as of 2024, per Statista) — a reference snapshot of the CIV digital-economy landscape.
