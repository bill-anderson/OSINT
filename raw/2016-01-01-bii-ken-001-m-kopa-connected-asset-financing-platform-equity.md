## Description

Series of equity investments by BII in M-KOPA, Africa's leading connected asset financing provider. M-KOPA sells smartphones, solar home systems, and digital financial services (cash loans, mobile insurance) to over-indebted, underserved households in Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria, and Ghana through a pay-as-you-go (PAYG) model using mobile money instalments. BII first invested in 2016 when M-KOPA was primarily a solar company; subsequent rounds tracked its transformation into a digital platform. Rounds: $11.65M equity (Sep 2016), $7M equity (Nov 2017), $3M equity (Dec 2019), $7.5M equity (Jun 2021), $4.93M equity (Dec 2022).
