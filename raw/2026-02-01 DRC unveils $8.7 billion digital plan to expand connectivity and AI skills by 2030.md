The DRC's **2026-2030 digital transformation plan** (PNN2) is reported here at **$8.7 billion** — to expand connectivity, modernise public services and build a skilled tech workforce. Targets: **650 additional communities** gaining broadband (connecting ~**30 million** people and 1,000 public institutions), a projected **+$4.1 billion to GDP by 2029**, **700,000 jobs**, and mobile internet for **9.7 million** new users, alongside a national interbank payment platform. Funders cited include the World Bank IDEA programme and AFD.

*(The $8.7bn headline conflicts with the $1.5bn government-investment figure and a ~$10.4bn total reported elsewhere — flagged as REV-COD-001.)*
