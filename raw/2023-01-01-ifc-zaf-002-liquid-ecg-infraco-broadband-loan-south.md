## Description

IFC provided a senior loan of approximately $23.89 million to Liquid ECG Infraco (Pty) Ltd, a Liquid Intelligent Technologies subsidiary operating broadband infrastructure in South Africa. The project supports expansion of open-access fibre and broadband connectivity to underserved communities and businesses in South Africa, advancing digital inclusion and supporting the country's digital economy targets.
