# Senegal and Finland Expand Cooperation on Cybersecurity, Digital Infrastructure, and AI Development

Tech Africa News - 1 April 2026, by Jennifer Onyeagoro

Senegal and Finland strengthened cooperation in digital transformation following high-level political consultations held on 26 March 2026 in Dakar, on the sidelines of the second session of political consultations between the two countries. The Finnish delegation was led by Outi Holopainen of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Discussions focused on strengthening sovereign and resilient digital infrastructure in Senegal, including modernisation of data centres, improved government connectivity, and AI-ready infrastructure, plus enhancing the capacity of the Share Cable. The two countries explored technical and financial cooperation under frameworks such as the New Deal for Technology and the EU's Global Gateway initiative. Cybersecurity was highlighted as a key concern, alongside the challenge of misinformation and promotion of media literacy.
