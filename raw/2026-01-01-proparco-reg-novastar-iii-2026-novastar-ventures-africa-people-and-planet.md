## Description

USD 5 million commitment to Novastar Ventures Africa People and Planet Fund III (NVIII), a USD 147 million pan-African venture capital fund (final close 2026). Fund invests from seed to growth stage ($1M–$8M per company) in tech-enabled solutions combining economic growth with positive environmental impact. Sectors: delivery, e-mobility, smartphone access, sustainable agriculture. Part of Proparco's Choose Africa VC programme (EU/EFSD+).
