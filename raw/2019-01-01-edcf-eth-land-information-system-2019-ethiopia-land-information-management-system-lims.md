## Description

EDCF concessional loan ($30M, 2019) to build a modern Land Information Management System in Ethiopia. Implemented in Adama, Hawassa, Wolaita Sodo, and Bahir Dar cities. Digitises urban cadastral records, establishes CORS stations, and provides digital LIS for land transactions.
