## Description

FCDO grant through FSD Africa to the Digital Frontiers Institute for capacity building in digital finance. Supports professionals in Sub-Saharan Africa and globally through digital finance certification programmes including the Certificate in Digital Money (CIDM) and Certified Digital Finance Practitioner (CDFP). Phase I and Phase II ran from 2016 to 2022. Builds regulatory and practitioner capacity for digital financial services ecosystems across SSA priority countries. Part of FCDO's support to financial sector deepening in Africa through FSD Africa.
