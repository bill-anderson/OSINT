## Description

BII provided a $20M senior secured loan to TerraPay in March 2024, a global cross-border payments processor focused on remittance transfers into Africa. BII financing is structured through Lendable's senior secured facility. The investment pre-funds increasing remittance volumes to Africa via key corridors: Kenya, Ghana, Uganda, Tanzania, Cameroon, Mali, Benin, Côte d'Ivoire, Senegal, Mozambique. TerraPay processes digital remittances lowering costs and increasing speed for African diaspora sending money home.
