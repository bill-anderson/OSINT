## Description

TLcom Capital co-led a $10M Series B investment in Twiga Foods, a Kenya-based mobile-based food distribution platform connecting smallholder farmers to urban food vendors. TLcom committed up to $4M per IFC investment disclosure, alongside IFC as co-lead. Twiga uses mobile technology to eliminate middlemen in food distribution, improving farmer incomes and reducing food prices for retailers.
