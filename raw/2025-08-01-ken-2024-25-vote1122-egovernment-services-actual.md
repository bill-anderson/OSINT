## Description

The COB's own words: "E-Government Services recording excess expenditure over its budget
allocation at 105 per cent, indicating possible irregular budget reallocation."

## Notes

**Stage context (the FY of the Finance Bill withdrawal):** gross national budget revised 4.49tn (original) -> 4.37tn (Supp I, Aug 2024) -> 4.64tn (Supp II) -> 4.37tn (Supp III, Jun 2025). Vote 1122 travelled 22,354.9m -> 19,998.0m -> 12,057.9m -> 16,456.8m; overall vote absorption 93% (COB BIRR).
The excess follows a 51% cumulative cut to the programme across the year's three supplementaries
(4,687.5m -> 2,290.8m) - money was spent faster than the estimates were cut. A governance flag,
not just an execution number.
