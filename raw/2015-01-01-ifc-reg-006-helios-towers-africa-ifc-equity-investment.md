## Description

IFC made a $35 million equity investment in Helios Towers Africa Ltd in 2015, an independent tower company operating in Tanzania, DRC, Republic of Congo, Ghana, and other SSA markets. Helios Towers provides passive telecom infrastructure including towers, shelters, and power systems to mobile network operators. The investment supported expansion of the company's tower portfolio across Sub-Saharan Africa, enabling greater rural connectivity and lowering the cost of mobile network deployment. Helios Towers listed on the London Stock Exchange in 2019.
