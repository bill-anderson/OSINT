## Description

Mastercard invested $100 million to acquire a minority stake (~3.775%) in Airtel Mobile Commerce BV (AMC BV), the holding company for Airtel Africa's mobile money operations across 14 African countries. The investment was made at a $2.65 billion valuation of the mobile money business. Alongside the equity stake, Mastercard and Airtel extended commercial agreements across card issuance, payment gateway, processing, merchant acceptance, and remittance solutions. AMC BV at the time had 21 million mobile money users.
