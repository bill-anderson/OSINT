## Description

Improves collection, management, analysis and use of geospatial and demographic data to improve lives of the poorest in developing countries. Enables better understanding of population distribution and location of key infrastructure (roads, hospitals, schools, water points). Geospatial data platform supports government planning, census operations, vaccination campaigns, and humanitarian response. SSA primary partner countries include Nigeria, DRC, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Madagascar, Mozambique, South Sudan, and others.
