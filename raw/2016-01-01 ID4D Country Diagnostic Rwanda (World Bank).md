# ID4D Country Diagnostic: Rwanda (World Bank, 2016)

Companion source page for the binary artefact
`2016-01-01-ID4D-Country-Diagnostic-Rwanda-WorldBank.pdf` (cited as *World Bank. 2016.
ID4D Country Diagnostic: Rwanda*; web version released 2018).

The World Bank ID4D **diagnostic** of Rwanda's identity ecosystem: civil registration
and the National Population Register (NPR), the National Identity (NID) system and NIN,
the production database, authentication readiness, governance and NIDA capacity.

**Reference study (as of 2016).** Cite, don't absorb — a dated baseline diagnostic; its
figures are **not promoted** into synthesis pages. Establishes the pre-SDIS baseline for
[[nida-rwanda]] and [[rwanda--dpi-id]].
