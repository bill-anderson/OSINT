## Description

IFC made a $30 million equity investment in WIOCC Holding Company Ltd (WHCL), the restructured parent of WIOCC, to support expansion of core and edge data centres and fibre networks in DRC, Nigeria, and South Africa. WIOCC's Open Access Data Centres (OADC) strategy targets Tier III data centres across Africa serving cloud providers, enterprises, and internet exchanges. The IFC equity stake aligns with WIOCC's $200M combined debt and equity raise in 2021-2022 to fund the OADC strategy. Proparco, EAAIF (Ninety-One), and other DFIs also participated.
