Open Access Data Centres (OADC), a subsidiary of WIOCC Group, has announced plans to expand its Lagos data centre to 24 megawatts by 2027. Multiple reports confirm the large-scale project is expected to require an investment of $240 million, aligning with the company's strategy to strengthen digital infrastructure in Nigeria.

The expansion will be carried out in phases as part of OADC's broader $500 million investment plan, unveiled in 2021, to develop data centre infrastructure across Africa.
