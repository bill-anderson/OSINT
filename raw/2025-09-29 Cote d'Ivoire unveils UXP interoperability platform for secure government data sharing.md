Côte d'Ivoire's Minister of Digital Transition and Digitalization, **Ibrahim Kalil Konaté**, launched a **public-administration interoperability platform** (Abidjan, **25 September 2025**) for secure, real-time data exchange between state information systems. It runs on **[[cybernetica]]**'s **UXP** (Unified eXchange Platform, X-Road family); the **Société Nationale de Développement Informatique ([[sndi-cote-divoire]]**) was commissioned by the Ministry (MTND), with a **20-month** implementation alongside Digital Afrique Telecom (DAT). Part of the National Digital Development Strategy and the "zero paper 2030" goal — the same UXP model Benin uses.
