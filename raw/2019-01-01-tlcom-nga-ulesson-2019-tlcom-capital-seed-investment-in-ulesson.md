## Description

TLcom Capital led a $3.1M seed round in uLesson, a Nigerian edtech startup founded by serial entrepreneur Sim Shagaya (founder of Konga). uLesson delivers affordable, structured K-12 digital learning content across Africa, targeting learners in Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Rwanda, Uganda, Sierra Leone, and Liberia via mobile app. This was TLcom's first education sector investment in Africa.
