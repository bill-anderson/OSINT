## Description

In February 2021, Liquid Telecommunications Financing PLC (a subsidiary of Liquid Intelligent Technologies, itself owned by Cassava Technologies) issued a USD 620 million bond oversubscribed 5x. Bond proceeds used to refinance existing debt and fund new fibre infrastructure, data centres, and new technologies across Liquid's 14+ SSA country network (ZAF, ZWE, ZMB, KEN, TZA, COD, UGA, RWA, BWA, ETH, MOZ, LSO, SSD, plus cross-border links). Liquid's network at issuance spanned approximately 100,000 km of fibre. DFI anchor investors: IFC USD 150M (bond) + USD 90M (equity) + USD 100M (ZAF term loan), DEG USD 25M, EAIF/PIDG USD 9.77M. This records the Cassava/Liquid corporate issuance.
