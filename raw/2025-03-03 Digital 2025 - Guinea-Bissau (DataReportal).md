DataReportal's Digital 2025 report for Guinea-Bissau (published 3 March 2025), early-2025 headlines:

- Population: 2.23 million (Jan 2025, +2.2% YoY); 46.1% urban, 53.9% rural; median age 19.4.
- Cellular mobile connections: 2.62 million, equivalent to 118% of the population (+13.9% YoY); 81.8% "broadband" (3G/4G/5G).
- Internet users: 723 thousand, internet penetration 32.5% (+2.2% YoY); about 1.50 million people (67.5%) remained offline.
- Social media user identities: 443 thousand, equivalent to 19.9% of the population.

Source: GSMA Intelligence and Kepios. (Baseline connectivity figures for the Guinea-Bissau hub.)
