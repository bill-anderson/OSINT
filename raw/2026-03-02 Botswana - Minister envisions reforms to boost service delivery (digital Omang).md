Botswana's **Ministry of Labour and Home Affairs** (Minister Maj Gen Pius Mokgware), presenting 2026/27 budget estimates, outlined reforms to boost service delivery — including 24-hour operations at strategic border posts (Kazungula, Martins Drift, Pioneer Gate, Tlokweng) and advancing a **digital Omang** national ID, an **Electronic Identification System**, an e-wallet and e-government/biometric services — the digital-identity and e-government modernisation strand for Botswana.
