## Description

ADC invested significantly in South Africa as its largest market, operating data centres in Johannesburg (JHB1 Midrand: 14.4MW; JHB2 Samrand: 10MW expanding to 20MW) and Cape Town (CPT1: 12MW expanding to 20MW with new halls; CPT2: 20MW). The first DFC disbursement of $83 million in August 2022 primarily supported South Africa expansion. In June 2024, RMB arranged a ZAR 2 billion (~$109.5 million) bespoke financing facility for ADC to accelerate South Africa hyperscale capacity by an additional 20MW. Combined South Africa capacity from JHB1, JHB2, CPT1, and CPT2 represents 64MW+, the largest ADC market. The July 2024 Cape Town CPT1 expansion added three new halls with 6MW additional IT load.
