## Description

UNDP timbuktoo: pan-African innovation platform launched January 2024 at WEF Davos. Aims to mobilize $1 billion (catalytic + commercial) over 10 years to support 10,000 startups and create 10M jobs. Establishes 8 thematic hubs: Fintech (Lagos), HealthTech (Kigali), GreenTech (Nairobi), AgriTech (Accra), TradeTech (Dakar), Creatives (Cape Town), Smart Cities (Morocco), EdTech/TourismTech (Dakar). IATI shows $6.78M committed 2024-2026 for core programme operations.
