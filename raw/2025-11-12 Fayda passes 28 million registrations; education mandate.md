# Fayda passes 28 million registrations; education enrolment mandate

Ethiopia's **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** National ID Program had enrolled **28 million people** by 12 November 2025, integrating with institutions across sectors. The **Ministry of Education** now requires **all students from Grade 1** to obtain a Fayda ID for school registration, and it is **mandatory for national-exam candidates** — to build an organised student data profile and detect fraudulent educational documents.
