The UNDP and Libya's **[[gia-libya|General Information Authority (GIA)]]** signed a Memorandum of Understanding in Tripoli to strengthen cooperation on human development, data systems and sustainable-development planning, as Libya prepares to launch its **Seventh National Human Development Report (2025)**.

The MoU covers joint support for the report, research and consultation, knowledge exchange, technical training, and measures to improve energy efficiency in GIA facilities. GIA head Dr Abdulbaset AlBaour said the partnership sits within the Government of National Unity's vision that "has made digital transformation and human development central pillars," affirming the GIA's commitment to building "an advanced information system"; UNDP Resident Representative Dr Sophie Kemkhadze said it will reinforce national ownership and deepen the role of data in Libya's development.
