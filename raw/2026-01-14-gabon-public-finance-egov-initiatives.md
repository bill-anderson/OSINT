The Ministry of Economy, Finance, Debt and Holdings convened a strategic meeting on Tuesday, 13 January 2026, chaired by the Ministers of Economy, Finance, and Digital Economy, focused on modernizing public administration and accelerating Gabon's digital transformation.

Key outcomes: the launch of the **Integrated Public Finance Management System (SIGFIP)** to streamline government financial operations, and the **MADIGIPAIE** project introducing electronic payments for public services. The **Unique Participation File (FUP)** was established to centralize government-participation data, while the **Gabon Digital** loan program is to be extended to support digital projects across the country.

Other initiatives: regulation of the Gabonese counterparty under the **e-Gabon** project, implementation of electronic invoicing, and a national platform for the payment and collection of public revenue. The ministers reaffirmed commitment to these structural reforms with regular follow-up.
