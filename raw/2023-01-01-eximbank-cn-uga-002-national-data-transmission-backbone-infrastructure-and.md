## Description

CHEXIM loan of approximately RMB 1.049 billion (~$148M) for Phase V of Uganda's National Data Transmission Backbone Infrastructure and E-Government Infrastructure project. Extends high-speed fiber optic connectivity to sub-county level, connecting government and public sector offices and institutions. Phase agreement signed March 2023. Builds on Phases I-III (2006-2013) and Phase IV (2018). Aims to provide e-government services across all sub-counties.
