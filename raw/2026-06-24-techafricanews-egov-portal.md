# Seychelles Launches Revamped eGov Portal to Expand Digital Public Services | 24 June 2026
By Jennifer Onyeagoro — TechAfrica News

Vice-President and Minister for Information, Technology and Innovation, Mr Sebastien Pillay, officially launched the revamped Seychelles Government Portal, egov.sc, at a ceremony at the Savoy Resort and Spa. The event was hosted by the Department of Information under the Office of the Vice-President.

The relaunch — a joint initiative of the Department of Information and the Department of Information and Communication Technology (DICT), under the theme "Access Government Services, With Just One Click" — coincided with the 50th Anniversary of Independence. Pillay described the milestone as a decisive stride in President Patrick Herminie's vision to position Seychelles among the world's leading digital economies within five years. He noted the portal had not undergone meaningful review for over a decade and had become little more than a way for public servants to access government email.

A project overview was delivered by DICT Principal Secretary Mr Benjamin Choppy. Going forward, the Department of Information will manage content and DICT the technical functions.
