## Description

Mubadala Investment Company LP commitment to Partech Africa II, the largest venture capital fund dedicated to technology startups in Africa (hard cap EUR 280M, final close above EUR 300M). Announced jointly with Bpifrance on 6 March 2024 as the first deployment under their EUR 350M Africa co-investment partnership. The fund invests initial tickets of USD 1M-15M from Seed to Series C across African tech and digital companies covering fintech, healthtech, edtech, mobility, logistics and other digital sectors in Sub-Saharan Africa and pan-African markets covering 27+ countries. Other LPs include KfW (anchor), EIB, IFC, FMO (EUR 25M), BII (USD 15.61M), DEG, Proparco. Mubadala's ticket size is undisclosed.
