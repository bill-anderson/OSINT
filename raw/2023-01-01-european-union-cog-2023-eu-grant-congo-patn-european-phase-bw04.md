# European Union — Republic of Congo, PATN European-phase grant, 2023

The European Union committed a 10 billion CFA franc grant to the European component of Congo's Project for Accelerating Digital Transformation (PATN), under the Global Gateway initiative.

## Description

"The European component of the project brings in 27 billion CFA francs—10 billion in EU grants and 17 billion as a loan from the EIB—while the World Bank contributes an additional 61.4 billion CFA francs."

Per EU Ambassador Anne Marchal, "This project is part of the Global Gateway initiative, which aims to promote quality infrastructures for human and economic development." The European phase focuses on administrative modernisation, economic development, youth skills, data governance and cybersecurity/data protection.

"The project is underpinned by a series of binding agreements, including grant convention FI 96262 (signed December 30, 2023)."
