## Description

DEG provides USD 4 million in equity capital to South African insurtech Naked Insurance as part of its USD 38 million Series B2 funding round. The round is led by global impact investor BlueOrchard with continued support from Hollard Insurance, Yellowwoods, IFC, and DEG. Naked's digital platform uses AI and automation to revolutionise insurance access, providing vehicle, home, and other insurance products. Proceeds support Naked's growth strategy including investment in automation and AI, product and market expansion, and enhanced advertising. DEG first invested in Naked in 2023 (Series B).
