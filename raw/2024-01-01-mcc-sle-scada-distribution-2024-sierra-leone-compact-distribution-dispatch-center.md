## Description

Activity 2.1 of MCC's $480.7M Sierra Leone Compact (signed September 2024) constructs and operationalises a main and backup distribution dispatch center for EDSA, providing centralised visibility and remote operating capability over the distribution network. The dispatch center improves EDSA's operations and maintenance performance, enables real-time monitoring of the distribution system, and supports efficient management of an increasingly complex network including integration of variable renewable generation. The $30.6M activity is part of the $123.6M Distribution and Access Project. This is distinct from the Transmission Dispatch Center (Activity 1.1) which serves EGTC; this center serves the distribution utility EDSA.
