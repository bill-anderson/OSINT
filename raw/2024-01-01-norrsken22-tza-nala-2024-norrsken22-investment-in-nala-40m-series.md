## Description

Norrsken22 participates in NALA's $40M Series A round, one of the largest Series A transactions in Africa. NALA (headquartered in Tanzania, UK-registered) provides remittance services from US, UK and EU to Africa and is building a B2B cross-border payments platform for African businesses. Round led by Acrew Capital, with Norrsken22, DST Global Partners, and HOF Capital. NALA serves Tanzania, Uganda, Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda and other SSA markets.
