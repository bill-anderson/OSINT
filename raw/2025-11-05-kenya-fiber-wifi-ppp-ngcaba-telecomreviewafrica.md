# Kenya Advances Fiber and Wi-Fi Rollout with Public-Private Push

As Kenya accelerates the rollout of 100,000 kilometers of fiber infrastructure, 25,000 public Wi-Fi hotspots, and a nationwide network of digital hubs, sustained collaboration between the government and the private sector remains vital to achieving the country's digital transformation goals.

During a meeting at the Ministry Headquarters, Hon. William Kabogo Gitau met with Dr. Andile Ngcaba, a prominent African technology investor and founder known for spearheading major infrastructure projects such as SEACOM, the first undersea fiber-optic cable system linking Africa's East Coast.

Their discussion centered on Kenya's National Connectivity Programme, a key component of the Digital Superhighway under the Bottom-Up Economic Transformation Agenda (BETA). The two leaders explored strategies for expanding broadband infrastructure, building local capacity, and engaging communities in the deployment and maintenance of digital infrastructure.
