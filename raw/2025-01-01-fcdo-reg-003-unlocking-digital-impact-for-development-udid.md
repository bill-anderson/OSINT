## Description

Continuation phase of the FCDO-DIAL partnership for digital development activities, launched September 2025. Supports ongoing digital public goods ecosystem development, digital transformation advisory, and open-source solutions for development challenges. Maintains FCDO engagement with the Digital Impact Alliance for dissemination of digital development principles and supporting DPI adoption in low- and middle-income countries, with Africa as a primary region of focus.
