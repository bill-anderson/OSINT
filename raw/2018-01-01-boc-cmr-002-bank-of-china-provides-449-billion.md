## Description

In June 2018, Cameroon's Minister of the Economy, Planning, and Regional Development (MINEPAT) signed a contract with the Bank of China for the extension of a 44.9 billion CFA franc credit line to finance Phase 1 of the Intelligent Urban Video Surveillance System Expansion Project. This credit line is in addition to the $41,780,769 commercial loan agreement that the Government of Cameroon and the Bank of China signed on February 22, 2018, for Phase 1 of the Intelligent Urban Video Surveillance System Expansion Project (captured via Project ID#85647). AidData has not yet identified any drawdowns under the credit line. Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. is responsible for the implementation of Phase 1 of the Intelligent Urban Video Surveillance System Expansion Project.
