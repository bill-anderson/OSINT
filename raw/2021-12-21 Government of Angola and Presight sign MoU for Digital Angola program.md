During President João Lourenço's visit to the UAE (received by Sheikh Hamdan bin Zayed Al Nahyan at Al Nakheel Palace), the **Government of Angola and Presight** — a **G42** company specialising in big-data analytics and AI — signed a Memorandum of Understanding to develop a transformation program for **"Digital Angola"**, aimed at advancing Angola's digital transformation using big-data analytics and artificial intelligence.

*(The Presight page dateline is 21 Dec 2021; "Digital Angola 2024" is the program name. The available clip body is brief — MoU scope/valuation not detailed.)*
