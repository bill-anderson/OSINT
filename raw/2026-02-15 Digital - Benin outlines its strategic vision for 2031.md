Benin drafted a **strategic digital vision to 2031** following a Cotonou seminar on **5–6 February 2026**, run by the Ministry of Digital and Digitalisation with **Deloitte** (partner Karim Koundi), to align public and private stakeholders around a shared "Benin digital nation by 2031."

Minister Aurélie Adam Soulé Zoumarou acknowledged that, despite progress, major challenges remain — still-limited connectivity, high-speed network coverage and quality, a persistent digital divide, personal-data protection, and support for digital SMEs and start-ups. The strategy is to span infrastructure, digitised public services, cybersecurity, local innovation, human capacity and enterprise financing, culminating in a strategic reference document to guide state, business and partner investment — a successor framing to Benin's 2021–2026 government digital plan.
