## Description

Africa50 co-led a USD 14 million Series A.5 equity funding round into Zeepay Ghana Ltd, a fast-growing payment infrastructure company that facilitates digital settlement of international remittances into mobile money wallets across Africa and the Caribbean. Zeepay settled over 10 million transactions worth over USD 3 billion in 2023. Co-investors: Oikocredit (USD 5M per separate disclosure), Injaro Ghana Venture Capital Fund, Verdant Capital Hybrid Fund, and Investisseurs & Partenaires. The investment supports expansion of cross-border remittances and mobile financial services to a minimum of 10 countries.
