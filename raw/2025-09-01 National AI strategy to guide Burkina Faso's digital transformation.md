Burkina Faso began drafting a **national AI Action Plan for 2026–2028**, launched via a consultation workshop by the Ministry of Digital Transition, Posts and Electronic Communications. Permanent Secretary for Innovation and Digital Emerging Technologies Dr Yaya Traoré said AI is central to digital-economy growth and aligns with the country's digital-economy development plan **2018–2027**.

The plan highlights six focus areas: infrastructure and connectivity; **data governance**; human-capital development through AI skills training; legal and ethical frameworks for responsible AI; innovation and entrepreneurship; and sustainable funding and international partnerships.
