## Description

IFC provided a senior debt facility of €25 million (A-loan) to Wave Mobile Money S.A. (Senegal entity) and Wave Côte d'Ivoire S.A. as part of a €130 million total financing package. Wave is Francophone Africa's first fintech unicorn ($1.7B valuation), operating a low-cost interoperable mobile money platform across Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Gambia, Mali, Niger, and Uganda. IFC's participation mobilised €65M in B-loans from impact lenders (Symbiotics, BlueOrchard, responsAbility, Lendable). Proparco/Finnfund/Norfund provided €24M in parallel loans. Total €130M package.
