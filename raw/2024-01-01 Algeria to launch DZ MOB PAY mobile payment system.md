# Algeria to launch DZ MOB PAY mobile payment system (January 2025)

Algeria was set to introduce **"DZ MOB PAY"**, a **QR-code smartphone payment system**, in **January 2025** — announced by **Mohand Bourai**, President of the Association of Banks and Financial Institutions (ABEF). Three institutions lead: **BNA (National Bank of Algeria)**, **Al Salam Bank Algeria** and **Algeria Post**; the platform is **free for users and merchants** to encourage adoption.

Context: mobile payments expanded after the **Switch Mobile** interoperability platform launched June 2024; by October 2024 Algeria recorded **>14.8 million online transactions** and **>75 million** smartphone-based transactions.
