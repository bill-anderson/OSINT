# Liberia ID4D Country Diagnostic (World Bank)

identification ecosystem (the NIR/NBIS, civil registration, functional IDs and coverage gaps) behind the
later GREAT project and the mandatory-enrollment drive ([[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]]). Reference document;
cite, don't absorb.
