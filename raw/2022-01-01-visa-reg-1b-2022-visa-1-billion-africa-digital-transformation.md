## Description

During the US-Africa Business Forum in December 2022, Visa announced a pledge to invest $1 billion in Africa by 2027 to advance resilient, innovative and inclusive economies. The five-year commitment covers: scaling Visa operations across Africa (now 10 offices up from 1); deploying new payment innovations and technologies; deepening collaboration with governments, financial institutions, MNOs, fintechs, and merchants. Includes the Africa Fintech Accelerator, investments in African fintechs, and new data infrastructure. Countries include all 54 African nations; SSA represents the majority of operations.
