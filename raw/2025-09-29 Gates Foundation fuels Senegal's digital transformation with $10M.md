# Gates Foundation fuels Senegal's digital transformation with $10M

The **[[gates-foundation]]** committed **$10m** to Senegal's **[[new-deal-technologique]]**, announced when
President Bassirou Diomaye Faye met Bill Gates on the margins of **UNGA80 (New York, Sep 2025)**. Per the
presidency, the partnership funds a **universal digital identity**, an **AI hub** (health and agriculture)
and a **Delivery Unit** for transparency and efficiency. Senegal needs ~**$1.7bn** for the 2025–2034
strategy; the Foundation is a major DPI backer (leading a $500m-over-5-years DPI call and the 50-in-5
campaign) and works with Nigeria and Kenya on digital ID. New Deal (launched Feb 2025) four pillars:
sovereignty, public-service digitisation, digital economy, regional leadership; 2034 targets include ≥94%
low-cost connectivity, all sensitive data hosted onshore, 500 startups, 150,000 jobs, 80% services digital.
