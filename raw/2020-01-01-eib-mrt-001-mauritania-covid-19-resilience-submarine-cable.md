## Description

EIB loan to finance the submarine cable connecting Mauritania directly to the global internet backbone. Provides first direct high-speed fibre optic link for Mauritania to Europe and West Africa, significantly increasing internet capacity and reducing costs. EUR 25M commitment, signed Dec 2020.
