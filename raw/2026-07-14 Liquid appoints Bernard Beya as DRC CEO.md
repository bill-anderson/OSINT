Pan-African technology group **Liquid Intelligent Technologies** has appointed **Bernard Beya** as CEO of its
**Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC)** operations, effective **1 April 2026**, following his tenure as acting
CEO since November 2025.

Liquid said the appointment comes as the DRC accelerates its national digital-transformation agenda, drawing
investment into infrastructure, connectivity and skills; it described the DRC as one of the fastest-growing and
most strategic markets in its African footprint.

Beya has more than 20 years of leadership experience in telecommunications, including extensive experience in
the DRC market. He joined Liquid in September 2022 as chief financial officer and, as acting CEO, oversaw the
organisation's strategic, operational and commercial direction.

Commenting on the appointment, Beya said his immediate mandate is to steer the DRC business through its next
phase of growth: "As a business of **Cassava Technologies**, we can expand access to reliable connectivity,
cloud, cyber security, colocation and **AI compute** for our customers … accelerating digital transformation
for the country's enterprises, government and communities."
