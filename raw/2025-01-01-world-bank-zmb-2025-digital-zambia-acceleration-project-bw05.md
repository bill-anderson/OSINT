# World Bank (IDA) — Digital Zambia Acceleration Project (DZAP), 2025

The World Bank's International Development Association approved a US$100 million-equivalent grant (SDR 73.9 million) to Zambia for the Digital Zambia Acceleration Project (DZAP, P505094), board-approved 11 September 2025, implemented by the Smart Zambia Institute.

## Description

Project Appraisal Document (Report No. PADHI00683): "PROPOSED GRANT IN THE AMOUNT OF SDR 73.9 MILLION (US$100 MILLION EQUIVALENT) TO THE REPUBLIC OF ZAMBIA FOR A DIGITAL ZAMBIA ACCELERATION PROJECT (DZAP) (P505094)". Total Operation Cost US$120m (IDA US$100m grant + US$20m unguaranteed commercial financing). Components: (1) Affordable broadband US$50m; (2) Interoperable and secure platforms US$38m; (3) Digital Skills and Capabilities US$5m; (4) Program management US$7m. Implementing Agency: Smart Zambia Institute. Expected approval 11-Sep-2025; expected closing 31-Oct-2030. Part of Phase 2 of the Inclusive Digitalization in Eastern and Southern Africa (IDEA) MPA (P512859), overall envelope US$2.48bn.
