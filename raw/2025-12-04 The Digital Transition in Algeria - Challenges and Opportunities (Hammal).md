# The Digital Transition in Algeria — Challenges and Opportunities (Hammal)

Peer-reviewed analytical article (Vol. XI, n°03, **published 4 December 2025**; received 9 Oct, accepted 8 Nov 2025) by **Farida Hammal** (University of Algiers 3). Frames Algeria's digital transition as a strategic imperative for economic diversification and competitiveness, examining infrastructure, public-service modernisation and the structural challenges (skills, regulation, adoption) constraining it. Analytical reference for the [[digital-algeria-2030|Digital Algeria 2030]] agenda — cite for framing, not as primary evidence.
