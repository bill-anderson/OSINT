# World Bank — Eswatini Health System Strengthening (CBHIS), 2020

The World Bank committed US$20m to Eswatini's Health System Strengthening for Human Capital Development project, financing the electronic Community-Based Health Information System (CBHIS).

## Description

Project Appraisal Document (PAD3539, 29 May 2020) for the Health System Strengthening for Human Capital Development project in Eswatini. The US$20m project finances, among health-system components, the electronic Community-Based Health Information System (CBHIS).
