## Description

BII committed $40M total to the Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund (CPDIF), a specialist SSA digital infrastructure fund managed by Convergence Partners. Investment 1: $30M (Jul 2021). Investment 2: $10M top-up (Jun 2022). The fund invests in digital infrastructure across Sub-Saharan Africa including data centres, fibre networks, broadband connectivity, and tech-enabled services. Portfolio companies include: 42Markets (SA digital trading), C Squared InvestCo (fibre/data Mauritius), CTrack Africa (fleet telematics), Datacentrix (data centre SA), Merit HoldCo (Nigeria digital infrastructure), Yellow Digital Retailers (device financing: Madagascar, Malawi, Rwanda, Uganda, Zambia).
