## Description

The FY2025/26 vote, down from the prior year's original 22.35bn but slightly below its final
revised 16.46bn - the baseline column of this PBB reconciles exactly with the FY2024/25 Supp III
finals held from the KEN/2024 extraction. Budget-day thematic framing: the "digital superhighway"
package at KSh 12.7bn (-22% YoY), Konza DC 3.1bn, DEAP 3.7bn (up), KAIST 2.3bn, e-GP 700m.
