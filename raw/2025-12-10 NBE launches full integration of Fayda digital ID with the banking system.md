# NBE launches full integration of the Fayda digital ID with the banking system

The **[[national-bank-of-ethiopia|NBE]]** issued a directive requiring **all banks and financial institutions to harmonise customer account information with the [[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]] digital ID** — integration running **1 December 2025 to 30 March 2026**. Customers link all accounts under a single Fayda ID, enabling cross-bank transfers, fraud prevention, streamlined credit access and protection against fake-document account movements; banks received **2,000 biometric kits** for enrolment. Aligned with **[[digital-ethiopia-2030]]**, positioning Fayda as foundational DPI.
