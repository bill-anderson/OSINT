## Description

World Bank Program Document (Report PGD168) for a "Development Policy Loan of EUR 459.8 million (~US$500 million)" to the Kingdom of Morocco for the First Financial and Digital Inclusion Development Policy Financing (26 May 2020), spanning the Finance/Competitiveness/Innovation, Digital Development and Macroeconomics/Trade Global Practices in the MENA Region. The DPF underpins Morocco's financial- and digital-inclusion reform agenda, a baseline behind the later social-protection registry (RNP/RSU) and Digital Morocco 2030.
