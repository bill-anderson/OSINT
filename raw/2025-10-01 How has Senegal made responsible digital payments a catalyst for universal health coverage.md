# How has Senegal made responsible digital payments a catalyst for universal health coverage?

Better Than Cash Alliance case study on **[[sen-csu|SEN-CSU]]**, Senegal's Universal Health Coverage agency.
After decades below ~10%, health coverage **passed 50% of citizens in 2022**, driven by SEN-CSU's plan to
digitise operations and payments. Developed in-country with **[[better-than-cash-alliance]]** technical
assistance, aligned to the UN 9 Principles for Responsible Digital Payments (five integrated). Results:
digital payments **halved enrolment costs**, expanded nationwide coverage, and empowered women (**85% of
beneficiaries**); the platform enables diaspora, rural-mutual and partner sponsorship (incl. DER/FJ).
