## Description

Government of India Line of Credit to Eswatini for construction of a Disaster Recovery Site (DRS) for the national data centre as part of the Royal Science and Technology Park (RSTP) Phase 2. The LoC agreement was signed between India Exim Bank and the Government of Eswatini. The project builds a secondary data centre facility providing business continuity and disaster recovery capabilities for government IT systems and national data assets.
