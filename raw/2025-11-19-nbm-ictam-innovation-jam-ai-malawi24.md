# K75.5m to help Malawian young innovators turn ideas into thriving solutions

National Bank of Malawi (NBM) plc has donated **K75.5 million** to support the **2025 Innovation Jam**, running alongside the annual ICT Association of Malawi (ICTAM) conference from 19–22 November 2025 under the theme **"AI for Sustainable Development"** — part of NBM's six-year partnership with ICTAM.

NBM Head of Digital Financial Services William Kaunda said many promising ideas fail because innovators lack structured guidance, and announced a **three-year MoU** with ICTAM to provide mentorship, incubation and technical support. Kaunda stressed AI is now indispensable for fraud prevention and system security: "To combat modern fraud and protect our customers, we need advanced AI tools." ICTAM President Clarence Gama welcomed the support. The Innovation Jam features pitching sessions judged by experts to identify ideas for incubation.
