## Description

Novastar Ventures co-leads (with AfricInvest via Cathay AfricInvest Innovation Fund) a $10M Series A equity round for Turaco, an insurtech company providing affordable health insurance and life insurance to low-income workers via embedded distribution through digital platforms, agribusinesses and logistics companies. Turaco operates across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Ghana and Nigeria. Novastar's investment follows its initial investment in 2020. Turaco grew 300%+ since Novastar's first investment.
