# EIB — Mauritania EllaLink submarine-cable connection, 2025

The European Investment Bank, with EU support, financed Mauritania's more than €43.5 million connection to the EllaLink international submarine cable, launched at Nouadhibou in July 2025.

## Description

"President Mohamed Ould Cheikh El Ghazouani supervised, on Monday, in Nouadhibou, the launch of the project to connect to the international submarine cable 'EllaLink'... The cable extends 669 km at sea within Mauritanian territorial waters, directly linking the country to internet networks in Europe and America."

"The total project cost, financed by the European Investment Bank and supported by the European Union, is estimated at more than €43.5 million, of which €25.6 million is allocated to execution and €17.9 million to maintenance over a 25-year period."
