## Description

AfricaGrow fund-of-funds commits €15M (~$17.5M) to Cathay AfricInvest Innovation Fund (CAIF), a pan-African VC fund investing in Series A/B tech companies across Africa. CAIF focuses on digital sectors: fintech, marketplace, B2B digital. Final close at €110M in 2022. AfricaGrow is first institutional LP. SSA coverage: Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, South Africa, West/East Africa.
