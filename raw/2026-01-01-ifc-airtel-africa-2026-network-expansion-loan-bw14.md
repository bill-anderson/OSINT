# IFC — Airtel Africa network expansion loan, 2026

IFC committed a US$150 million loan to two of Airtel Africa's subsidiaries to expand and upgrade mobile networks and broaden high-speed data access in underserved communities across sub-Saharan Africa.

## Description

"IFC's $150 million loan to two of Airtel Africa's subsidiaries will help the company expand and upgrade mobile networks over the coming years. In doing so, it will broaden access to high-speed data connectivity in underserved communities, enabling small businesses to grow, entrepreneurs to reach new markets and young people to access digital opportunities." IFC notes its support includes longer-tenor and local-currency financing.
