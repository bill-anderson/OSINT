## Description

KOICA grant to Nigeria ($13M, 2020-2026) to enhance capacity for e-government master plan execution, improve e-government service delivery competencies, activate the Government Service Portal (www.services.gov.ng), and establish additional NIN enrolment centres. Phase 1 (2013-2019) established the e-Government Training Centre.
