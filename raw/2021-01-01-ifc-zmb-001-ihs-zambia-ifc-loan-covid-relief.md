## Description

IFC provided a $75 million senior loan to IHS Zambia Ltd under the Real Sector Emergency COVID Facility (RSE). IHS Zambia is the sole independent tower operator in Zambia with over 1,700 towers providing passive telecom infrastructure to all major mobile network operators. The RSE COVID financing enabled IHS Zambia to refinance existing debt on more favourable terms during the pandemic and maintain capital investment in tower upgrades. IHS Zambia enables mobile internet and data services for millions of Zambian consumers and businesses.
