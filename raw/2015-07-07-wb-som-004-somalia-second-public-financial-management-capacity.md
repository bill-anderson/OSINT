## Description

Strengthens systems of domestic revenue mobilization, expenditure control, and accountability in the Federal Government of Somalia, Puntland State, and Somaliland. Supports deployment and roll-out of IFMIS systems for budget management and financial reporting, revenue administration system upgrades, and financial accountability mechanisms.
