## Description

MEDUSA (Mediterranean-Equatorial Digital United SEALINK for Africa) submarine cable system. EUR 38.5M EIB loan + EUR 40M EU investment grant. Cable connects Portugal to multiple African countries including Mauritania (SSA). Total cable EUR 342M. This record covers SSA-attributable portion (Mauritania landing). EIB backed with Neighbourhood Investment Platform grant from European Commission.
