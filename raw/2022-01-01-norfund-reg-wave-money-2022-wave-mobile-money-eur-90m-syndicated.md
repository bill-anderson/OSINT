## Description

Norfund EUR 12 million parallel bilateral loan in EUR 90 million IFC-led syndicated debt facility for Wave Mobile Money. Wave provides low-cost mobile wallet, transfers, and merchant payment services in Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Gambia, Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger. Pure-play mobile money unicorn in Francophone West Africa. Digital scope: mobile money platform, digital payments, financial inclusion.
