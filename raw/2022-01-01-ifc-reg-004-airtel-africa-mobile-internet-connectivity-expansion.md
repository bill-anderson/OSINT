## Description

IFC committed $150 million from its own account plus $44 million from the Managed Co-Lending Portfolio Program (MCPP) — a total of $194 million — to Airtel Africa to support expansion of mobile internet connectivity across six Sub-Saharan African countries: DRC, Kenya, Madagascar, Niger, Republic of Congo, and Zambia. Airtel Africa is one of the continent's leading mobile network operators. The financing enables rollout of 4G networks, upgrading of existing infrastructure, and expansion into underserved rural and peri-urban areas, directly contributing to affordable mobile internet access for millions of users.
