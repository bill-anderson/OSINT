## Description

USD 10M (EUR 8.83M) equity commitment to Convergence Partners Digital Infrastructure Fund LP (CPDIF), a private equity fund investing in ICT infrastructure across sub-Saharan Africa. Fund targets fibre networks, wireless broadband, data centres, 5G infrastructure, and digital service provider platforms. Portfolio countries include Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa (DRC, Ethiopia, Côte d'Ivoire per DFC filing). Fund size ~USD 250M+ with co-investors including EIB, DFC, Finnfund.
