## Description

MCF $360M investment over 7 years (2024-2031) expanding partnerships with CAMFED (Campaign for Female Education) and FAWE (Forum for African Women Educationalists). CAMFED: support 62,000 girls in Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Ghana, Malawi into secondary/tertiary education and employment. FAWE: 10,500+ youth in Uganda, Rwanda, Zambia, Ethiopia, Malawi, Ghana, Liberia, Tanzania, Zimbabwe, Senegal. Digital skills component included.
