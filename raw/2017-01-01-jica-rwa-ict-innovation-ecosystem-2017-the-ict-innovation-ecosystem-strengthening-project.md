## Description

JICA technical cooperation project to strengthen Rwanda's ICT innovation ecosystem. Duration October 2017 - March 2022 (66 months). Established 250STARTUPS early-incubation mechanism, three innovation hubs in secondary cities, supported 58 startups, matched 17 Rwandan-Japanese company MOUs/LOIs, supported 10 proof-of-concept projects. Aimed to position Rwanda as Africa's leading ICT hub.
