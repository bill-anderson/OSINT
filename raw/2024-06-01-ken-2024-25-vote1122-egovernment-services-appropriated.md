## Description

Government digital-services delivery under BETA - includes the ICTA Presidential Digital Talent
Programme (4,800 interns target) and cyber-security services training.

## Notes

**Stage context (the FY of the Finance Bill withdrawal):** gross national budget revised 4.49tn (original) -> 4.37tn (Supp I, Aug 2024) -> 4.64tn (Supp II) -> 4.37tn (Supp III, Jun 2025). Vote 1122 travelled 22,354.9m -> 19,998.0m -> 12,057.9m -> 16,456.8m; overall vote absorption 93% (COB BIRR).
This programme took the year's deepest proportional cut: Supp I 3,594.90m -> Supp II 2,309.20m
(recovered) -> Supp III 2,290.79m - then spent **KSh 2,408.26m, 105% of allocation**, which the
COB flags as "indicating possible irregular budget reallocation" (BIRR 4.4.7.2). Actual-stage
record: [[2025-08-01-ken-2024-25-vote1122-egovernment-services-actual]].
