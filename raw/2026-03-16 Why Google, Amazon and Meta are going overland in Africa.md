Meta and Google want to open a new chapter in Africa. The two tech giants have already strengthened the continent’s international high-speed connectivity with their submarine cables, 2Africa and Equiano.

Now, alongside their ‘coopetitors’, Amazon and Microsoft, they are working to build a future ultra-high-speed fibre-optic network across the African hinterland. The goal: to enhance digital infrastructure and connectivity across the region.
