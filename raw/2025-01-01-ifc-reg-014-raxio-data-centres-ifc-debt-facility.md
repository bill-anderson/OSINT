## Description

IFC committed $100 million to Raxio Group to develop a leading Sub-Saharan African data centre platform. The financing comprises $60 million senior debt and $40 million subordinated debt. Raxio operates carrier-neutral Tier III data centres in Ethiopia, Mozambique, DRC, Côte d'Ivoire, Tanzania, Angola, and Uganda, addressing the critical shortage of colocation and cloud infrastructure across the continent. The investment enables Raxio to accelerate construction of new data centres and expand capacity in existing facilities, supporting digital transformation across multiple SSA markets.
