Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 22 May 2025. Ethiopia launched the FaydaPass wallet (announced 21 May at ID4Africa 2025), a DPI move to speed financial inclusion and verification for government/other services. Co-developed with TECH5 (providing T5-AirSnap and T5-OmniMatch) and Visa, addressing digital KYC needs for "a diverse and inclusive user base." Users download the official app and request their digital-ID credential; framed as transforming access to financial services in Ethiopia. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
