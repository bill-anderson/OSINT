Vodacom Mozambique inaugurated its new Business Data Centre at the end of March 2025 in Matola — a Tier 3 (Uptime Institute-certified) facility for national and international companies. It occupies 2,600 m² and starts with 128 racks, expandable to over 500 racks.

Features: a neutral connectivity model (customers freely choose their telecom provider); advanced cooling, redundant power and UPS; a 24/7 control centre; strict physical security (perimeter walls, dedicated security team, biometric and electronic-card access control, surveillance cameras); high-speed fibre connectivity at national and global level; and one of the best energy-efficiency indices in Africa. Positioned as a catalyst for cloud computing, AI and big-data solutions and for the growth of the Mozambican technology sector. [Same facility as the DCD Vodacom Maputo report; cluster.]
