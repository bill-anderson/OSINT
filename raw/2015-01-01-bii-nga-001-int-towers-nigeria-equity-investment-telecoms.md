## Description

BII invested $20M equity in INT Towers Limited in February 2015. INT Towers owns and operates over 9,100 telecoms towers across seven regions in Nigeria and is a wholly owned subsidiary of the IHS Towers Group. The investment enables mobile network operators to provide 2G/3G/4G connectivity to Nigerian consumers and businesses. Investment exited by September 2019.
