## Description

On 7 April 2022, a consortium led by Axian Telecom (together with Rostam Azizi) completed the acquisition of Millicom's entire Tanzanian telecom operations: MIC Tanzania Ltd (Tigo Tanzania) and Zanzibar Telecom (Zantel). The deal price was USD 100 million. The acquisition gave Axian strategic ownership in the EASSy cable landing station in Dar es Salaam, a consortium network of over 3,200 km backbone and 600 km metro fibre. The business was subsequently rebranded as Yas Tanzania. Axian announced a USD 500 million investment plan for Tanzanian network expansion over subsequent years.
