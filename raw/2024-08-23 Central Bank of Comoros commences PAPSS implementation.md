The **Banque Centrale des Comores ([[bcc-comoros]]**) and **[[papss]]** (Pan-African Payment and Settlement System) announced the official commencement of PAPSS implementation in Comoros (Moroni, **23 August 2024**), supported by **[[afreximbank]]** and the African Union under **AfCFTA**. The BCC signed the membership agreement in July 2024; a workshop (21 Aug 2024) gathered commercial-bank and microfinance CEOs, with BCC Governor Dr Younoussa Imani and PAPSS Deputy CEO John Bosco Sebabi.

PAPSS lets Comorians pay other African countries **directly in Comorian francs** (settlement in ~120 seconds), cutting FX-conversion cost and delay; commercial banks are to begin integrating.
