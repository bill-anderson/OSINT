# OGE 2025 — Crédito Adicional Suplementar USD 218,5 M for the Bilhete de Identidade universalisation

Presidential Decree 169/25 opened a supplementary credit of USD 218,500,000 inside Angola's enacted 2025 state budget, assigned to the Ministry of Justice and Human Rights, to fund the Strategic Plan for the Universalisation of the National Identity Card.

## Description

"É autorizada a abertura de Crédito Adicional Suplementar no montante de USD 218 500 000,00 (duzentos e dezoito milhões e quinhentos mil dólares dos Estados Unidos da América), para cobrir as despesas referentes à Implementação do Plano Estratégico para a Universalização do Bilhete de Identidade." (Artigo 1.º)

"O Crédito Adicional Suplementar, aberto nos termos do presente Diploma, é afecto à Unidade Orçamental — Ministério da Justiça e dos Direitos Humanos, e deve ser disponibilizado em função das necessidades de pagamento e disponibilidade de tesouraria." (Artigo 2.º)

## Notes

- **Currency.** The decree states the amount in **USD**, not kwanza, and gives no Kz equivalent; carried as the announcing party's own currency per `CLAUDE.md` → *Currency*. `amount_usd` therefore repeats the stated figure with no FX rate applied (rate basis not applicable).
- **Stage and version.** `revised` / `supplementary-1`, `supplementary_basis: increment` — the decree opens an *additional* credit inside the enacted OGE 2025, the Angolan pattern where the Assembly does not re-vote a *revisão*. It does not restate the ID programme's total, so it must not supersede any original-stage line.
- **`published` = the gazette date** (19-Sep-2025, DR I.ª Série n.º 178), day precision. The decree was **signed 8-Sep-2025** and entered into force the day after publication; the signature date is recorded here rather than used as the event date.
- **funding source unstated** — the decree names the budget-law basis but not whether the credit is resourced from domestic revenue, domestic borrowing or external financing, and names no external funder. Origin `domestic-state` by the gate's default, flagged.
- **`source_tier: official-statement`**, not `budget-document`: the instrument is the primary appropriating act, but the text is held via LEX.AO's verbatim reproduction rather than the gazetted PDF.
