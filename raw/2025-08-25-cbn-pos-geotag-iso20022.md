The Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) issued a circular (dated August 25, 2025) mandating all licensed payments operators to migrate to the ISO 20022 standard for payment messaging and to implement mandatory geo-tagging of payment terminals. It targets Deposit Money Banks, Microfinance Banks, Mobile Money Operators, switching/processing companies, PTSPs, PSSPs and super agents. All domestic and international payment messages must conform to ISO 20022 (per CBN and SWIFT specs) with accurate identifiers for payers, payees, merchants and agents; full compliance by October 31, 2025. All existing and new payment terminals must be geo-tagged with double-frequency GPS receivers and registered. CBN begins compliance validation from October 20, 2025.
