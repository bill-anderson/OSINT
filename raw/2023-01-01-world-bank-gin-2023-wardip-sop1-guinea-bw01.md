# World Bank — WARDIP SOP1, Guinea, 2023

The World Bank committed a US$60.0m IDA credit to Guinea under WARDIP SOP1 for broadband access and West African digital-market integration.

## Description

Guinea's national allocation under the Digital Transformation for Africa / Western Africa Regional Digital Integration Program (DTfA/WARDIP) Series of Projects 1 (PAD4756, Operation ID P176932, 7 November 2023): "IN THE AMOUNT OF US$60.0 MILLION TO THE REPUBLIC OF GUINEA" as an IDA credit. The SOP raises broadband access and usage and advances the West African single digital market. Implementing agency: Ministère de la Poste, des Télécommunications et de l'Économie Numérique (MPTEN).
