## Description

ICT project to build out high-speed information networks connecting 22 major cities across Burkina Faso, deploying approximately 800 km of fibre optic infrastructure. Implemented by Huawei and CITCC. Financed by a Chinese concessional loan of approximately 47 billion CFA francs. Project completed August 2025.
