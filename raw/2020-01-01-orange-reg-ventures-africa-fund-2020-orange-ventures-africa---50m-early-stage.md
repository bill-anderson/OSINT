## Description

Orange Digital Ventures Africa (later Orange Ventures MEA) is a €50 million investment fund focused on early-stage tech startups in Africa. Launched in 2020, the fund targets sectors including fintech, connectivity, IoT, e-health, and energy. Portfolio companies include Africa's Talking (Kenya), Gebeya (Ethiopia), Yoco (South Africa), Terragon (Nigeria), YouVerify (Nigeria), Julaya (Côte d'Ivoire), ToumAI (Sudan/Chad), SudPay, Afrikamart, Moja Ride, and others across SSA.
