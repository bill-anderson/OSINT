## Description

Deepens work with African Science Granting Councils (SGCs) covering research management, monitoring, learning and evaluation, knowledge transfer to the private sector, and enhanced networks. Extends into new cross-cutting dimensions. SGCs in Zimbabwe (85% of budget), Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Botswana, Rwanda, Sierra Leone, and South Africa receive direct support. Digital research infrastructure, data management systems, and ICT knowledge transfer are central components (32182 + 22040 + 22030 = 51% digital sectors).
