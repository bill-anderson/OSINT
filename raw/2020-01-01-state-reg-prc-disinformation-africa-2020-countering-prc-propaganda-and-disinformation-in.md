## Description

Partner African journalists and newsrooms with researchers and civil society advocates to identify, counter, and report on the PRC's information manipulation toolkit. The program builds African media capacity to expose and counter state-sponsored disinformation and propaganda from China operating in Sub-Saharan Africa.
