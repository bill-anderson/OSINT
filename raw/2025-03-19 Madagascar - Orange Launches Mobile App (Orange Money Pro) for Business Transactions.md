Orange Madagascar introduced Orange Money Pro on 18 March 2025, a mobile application for merchants and distributors to streamline financial-transaction management and improve the mobile-money experience for professionals.

The app offers faster transaction processing, real-time tracking, payment management and improved financial-flow visibility; for distributors it simplifies point-of-sale management, banking connections and customer support. Merchants can use QR-code payments and generate remote payment links. Currently Android-only, the app had been downloaded more than 10,000 times per Google Play statistics. Orange aims to bolster financial inclusion and may later add credit and integrated accounting. Adoption challenges include internet access (32.57% in 2023 per the regulator), merchant digital literacy and compatibility with other financial services.
