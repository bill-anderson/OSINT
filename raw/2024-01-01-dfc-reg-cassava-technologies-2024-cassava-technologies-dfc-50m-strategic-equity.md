## Description

DFC direct equity investment of $50 million in Cassava Technologies Ltd., one of Africa's largest pan-African digital infrastructure and services platforms. Cassava operates over 110,000 km of fibre optic networks across 30 countries, data centres, cloud services, cybersecurity, and AI tools. DFC led the equity round alongside Google LLC and Finnfund. Investment supports data centre expansion in Kenya, Ghana, Nigeria (and Morocco), providing directed use of proceeds for new data centre construction. Counters risks from strategic competitor digital ecosystems. Cassava equity round total $90M.
