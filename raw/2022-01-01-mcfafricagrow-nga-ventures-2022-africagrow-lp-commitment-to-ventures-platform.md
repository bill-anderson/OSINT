## Description

AfricaGrow commits to Ventures Platform's $46M Pan-African Fund I, a leading early-stage VC firm focused on Nigeria and broader Africa. Fund invests in fintech, insurtech, health tech, edtech, agritech, enterprise SaaS. AfricaGrow confirmed LP alongside IFC, BII, Proparco, Standard Bank. Fund closed Dec 2022 above $40M target.
