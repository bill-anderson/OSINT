## Description

Microsoft launched the Africa Development Centre (ADC) in May 2019 with initial hubs in Nairobi, Kenya and Lagos, Nigeria — Microsoft's 7th global development centre and first in Africa. Committed $100 million over the first 5 years to recruit 500 full-time engineers building cloud, AI/ML, mixed reality, and Windows products with global impact. By 2022, ADC employed ~570 engineers. Lagos facility expanded March 2022 in Ikoyi. Note: Nigeria ADC closed in 2024 per Bloomberg.
