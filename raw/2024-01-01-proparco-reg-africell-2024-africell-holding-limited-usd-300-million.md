## Description

DFI participation in Africell Holding Limited's debut USD 300 million public senior secured bond (10.5% coupon, due 2029). Africell operates mobile networks in DRC, Sierra Leone, Uganda, and Gambia. Bond proceeds refinanced existing capital structure and fund network expansion. EAIF participated as sole impact investor (USD 28M). Proparco and IFC also participated as DFI anchor investors. Proparco's specific allocation not publicly disclosed.
