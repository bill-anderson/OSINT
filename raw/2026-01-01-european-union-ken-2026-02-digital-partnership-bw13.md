# European Union — Kenya Digital Partnership commitment, 2026

The EU–Kenya Digital Partnership committed KSh 15.3 billion to strengthen Kenya's digital infrastructure, connectivity and innovation, with a further €37 million of the broader EU support package allocated to the Blue Raman submarine cable.

## Description

"Kenya's digital transformation agenda has received a major boost following a Ksh.15.3 billion commitment from the EU–Kenya Digital Partnership. The funding is aimed at strengthening digital infrastructure, improving connectivity, and accelerating innovation across key sectors of the economy." The agreement followed high-level discussions between President William Ruto and senior EU officials. "A significant portion of the broader EU support package includes €37 million allocated to the Blue Raman submarine cable project," linking Djibouti, Somalia, Kenya and Tanzania to enhance regional internet capacity.
