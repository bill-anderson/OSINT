Telkom subsidiary BCX teamed up with Alibaba to bring Alibaba Cloud to Mozambique. The Africa Local Public (ALP) Cloud services are hosted in a BCX data centre in Maputo — the second African country where Alibaba launched ALP Cloud (after South Africa in October 2023).

BCX CEO Jonas Bogoshi and chief solutions officer Jan Bouwer emphasised a local approach and data sovereignty — keeping local data within Mozambique's/Africa's borders, with redundancy through neighbouring availability zones and on-the-ground support; ALP Cloud services are billed in local currency to avoid exchange-rate issues. BCX (a Telkom Group subsidiary, founded 1996 as Business Connexion, acquired by Telkom 2015) is one of Africa's largest systems integrators and holds exclusive rights to distribute Alibaba Cloud products in South Africa.
