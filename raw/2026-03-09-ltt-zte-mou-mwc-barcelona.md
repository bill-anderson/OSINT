# LTT and ZTE Sign MoU to Accelerate Digital Transformation and Network Evolution in Libya at MWC Barcelona 2026

Release Time: 2026-03-09

- The partnership aims to drive innovation, 4G/5G expansion, and the development of a comprehensive digital service ecosystem
- The collaboration will prioritize sustainability by incorporating energy-efficient solutions and green network technologies

Barcelona, Spain, March 9, 2026 - ZTE Corporation (0763.HK / 000063.SZ), a global leading provider of integrated information and communication technology solutions, and LTT, the leading telecommunications operator in Libya, have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) at MWC Barcelona 2026. This MoU establishes a strategic partnership between the two parties to accelerate network infrastructure modernization and foster the growth of Libya's digital economy.

Under this MoU, ZTE and LTT are committed to working together to:

• Advance Network Infrastructure: Collaborate on the planning and deployment of 4G and 5G networks to enhance coverage, connectivity, and performance across Libya.

• Expand Digital Service Ecosystems: Jointly develop and integrate innovative services, including E-wallet solutions, E-commerce platforms, and Content Delivery Networks (CDN) to enrich user experiences.

• Boost Terminal Business: Accelerate the availability and adoption of high-performance devices, including FWA (Fixed Wireless Access), CPE, and MiFi solutions, to bridge the digital divide.

• Drive Strategic Growth: Explore new business models to strengthen LTT's market position and accelerate Libya's overall digital transformation.

Zhang Wanchun, Senior Vice President of ZTE, commented: "ZTE is honored to deepen its strategic collaboration with LTT. We believe that the combination of our cutting-edge technological innovations and LTT's strong market presence will accelerate Libya's digital evolution. This MoU reflects our shared vision of bridging the digital divide and empowering the local market with next-generation communication tools and diversified digital services."

Ammar Albouzaidi, Chairman of LTT, stated: "Our partnership with ZTE at MWC Barcelona 2026 marks a transformative moment for the cooperation between us. By integrating ZTE's world-class 4G/5G technology and digital new business solutions, LTT is not only enhancing connectivity but also building the foundation for a robust digital economy. With the new business mode, we are committed to delivering the best-in-class digital experience to our customers."

This collaboration marks a significant milestone in the long-standing partnership between LTT and ZTE. By leveraging ZTE's global expertise in cutting-edge technologies and LTT's deep local market presence, both companies aim to deliver superior connectivity, drive technological innovation, and provide next-generation digital services to the people of Libya.
