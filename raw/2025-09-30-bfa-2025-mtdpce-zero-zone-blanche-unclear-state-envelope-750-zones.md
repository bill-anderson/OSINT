# Chantier zéro zone blanche — enveloppe d'État d'environ 37,5 milliards FCFA (750 zones blanches)

The Government of Burkina Faso, at the 2025-09-30 Ouahigouya launch of the project to
cover 750 white zones under the "Chantier zéro zone blanche", committed a state envelope
of approximately FCFA 37.5 billion, with telecom operators to carry the remaining
investment; target is zero white zones by end-2027.

## Description

« La ministre de la Transition digitale, des Postes et des Communications électroniques,
Aminata Zerbo, a lancé le projet de couverture de 750 zones blanches sur l'ensemble du
territoire national, lors d'un atelier, mardi 30 septembre 2025, à Ouahigouya. Cette
initiative s'inscrit dans le cadre du « Chantier zéro zone blanche » visant l'amélioration
de la couverture des réseaux mobiles et de la réduction de la fracture numérique. »

« L'objectif ultime est d'atteindre zéro zone blanche à l'horizon fin 2027. « Pour y
parvenir une enveloppe d'environ 37,5 milliards F CFA sera mobilisée par le gouvernement
et les opérateurs auront à leur charge le reste des investissements », a informé Mme
Zerbo. »

« Pour pallier la situation, l'Etat a déjà subventionné, aux dires de la ministre, la mise
en service de 223 sites depuis 2020. »

## Notes

- **Translation (amount sentence):** "To get there, an envelope of about FCFA 37.5
  billion will be mobilised by the government, and the operators will bear the rest of
  the investment."
- **scope_basis:** explicit named programme with a dedicated envelope — coverage of 750
  unserved localities with electronic-communications services; wholly a digital
  (connectivity/rural inclusion) activity. `scope_confidence: whole`.
- **Funding source:** the cited source says only "mobilisée par le gouvernement" —
  **funding source unstated** in the cited source. The same-day companion report
  (Burkina24, above) states the State mobilises the 37.5bn via **FASU** (universal
  access/service fund — own-source levy income, domestic-state) **and PACTDIGITAL**
  (a World Bank-financed project, i.e. external money). The parts are **not stated
  separately**, so per the driver's combined-figure rule the line is recorded once at
  its stated origin (state mobilisation → `domestic-state`) with the blending noted,
  not apportioned. **Warning:** the PACTDIGITAL share overlaps World Bank financing
  already held on the non-state side (PACTDIGITAL, FCFA 96bn total project) — a
  compile pass should treat this record's total as blended, not purely own-resource.
- **Budget stage:** none stated — an envelope announced at project launch, not tied to
  an appropriation, release or outturn; `budget_stage: unclear`. Judgment call: this is
  more than a bare pledge (named funding instruments, competitive-tender allocation
  mechanism, prior FASU subsidy track record of 223 sites since 2020), so it passes
  fact 3 as a committed programme envelope; flagged borderline.
- **Fiscal year:** none stated; the envelope spans the project horizon (launch
  2025-09-30 to end-2027). `fiscal_year_label` left blank; the deal_id stem uses the
  commitment year 2025 as label surrogate.
- **Amount:** "environ" — approximate. No capital/recurrent split stated. No USD
  conversion stated by the source — `amount_usd` left blank.
- The Burkina24 companion also gives a cumulative project investment of FCFA 75
  billion (state + operators). Only the state's 37.5bn is recorded here; the operators'
  share is private money, out of this dataset's scope.
