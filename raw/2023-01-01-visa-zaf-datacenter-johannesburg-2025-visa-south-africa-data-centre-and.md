## Description

Visa opened its first African data centre in Johannesburg, South Africa in July 2025. The data centre — Visa's fifth globally — is part of a R1 billion (~$54–57 million) investment in South Africa over three years (2023–2025+). It localises processing of South African transactions on VisaNet and forms part of Visa's broader $1 billion Africa digital transformation pledge made in 2022. Investment also covers SME enablement, youth training and merchant digitisation.
