## Description

DFC direct loan of up to $300 million to Africa Data Centres Holdings Limited under a master finance agreement to finance the acquisition and expansion of existing data center assets in South Africa and Kenya, and entry into new markets through development, construction, and operation of carrier-neutral data centers across DFC-eligible African countries. First disbursement of $83M announced August 2022 primarily for South Africa expansion. Africa Data Centres is Africa's largest network of interconnected data facilities. Total project cost $428.6M. Supports seven existing and greenfield data centers in five countries.
