## Description

IFC provided a senior loan of up to €50 million from its own account (A-loan) to Togocom, Togo's privatised incumbent telecoms operator and a subsidiary of Axian Group, to support modernisation and expansion of its mobile network. IFC also mobilised up to €72 million in co-financing (B-loans) for a total package exceeding €120 million. The investment supports 4G network rollout, mobile data capacity expansion, and mobile money infrastructure improvements in Togo and is the largest private telecoms financing in the country. IATI records $59.51M reflecting the combined A+B portion reported by IFC.
