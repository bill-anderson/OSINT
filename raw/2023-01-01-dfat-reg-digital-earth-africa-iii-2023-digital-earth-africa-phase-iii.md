## Description

Funding to Geoscience Australia to support Digital Earth Africa which provides African countries with access to free and open information from satellite images relating to flooding, drought, soil and coastal erosion, agriculture, forest cover, land use and land cover change, water availability and quality, and changes to human settlements. Phase III creates additional value from Phase II by driving uptake and delivering impact at scale across 50+ African countries. Activity period 2022-07-01 to 2025-06-30.
