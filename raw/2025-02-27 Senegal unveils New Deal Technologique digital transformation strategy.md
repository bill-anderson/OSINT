# Senegal unveils digital transformation strategy to enhance economic growth

President Bassirou Diomaye Faye launched the **[[new-deal-technologique|New Deal Technologique]]** on **24
February 2025** — a digital-transformation strategy running to 2034 within the "Senegal 2050" agenda. Core:
universal affordable internet; a **unique biometric-based digital ID** able to generate secondary/functional
identities for sectoral uses (health, education, justice, economy) across all life events; a **single citizen
portal**; and a centralised integrated services platform (business creation, tax, land registry, payments).
Enablers: updated Startup Act, DPI, legal/regulatory/governance frameworks, informal-economy digitisation, AI
and cybersecurity. Estimated **$1.7bn** budget (per Ecofin), targeting 95% internet coverage, 150,000+
digital jobs and ≥500 startups by 2034. (Founding launch of the New Deal frame.)
