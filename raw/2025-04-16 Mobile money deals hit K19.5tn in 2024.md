Per the RBM 2024 National Payment Systems Report, mobile money subscribers rose 27.6 percent from 12.7 million (2023) to 16.2 million (2024), transacting K19.5 trillion. Transaction value nearly doubled (+80.9 percent from K10.8tn in 2023) and volume rose 40.4 percent to 1.8 billion. Use remains skewed to airtime top-ups (43.6 percent) and cash in/out (33.0 percent), with person-to-person at 9.1 percent and merchant payments 6.9 percent; RBM notes rising cash usage challenges the digitalisation agenda.

Subscribers remain male-skewed (58.3 percent vs 41.7 percent female), though the gender gap narrowed to 16.6 percent. Activity is low (46.6 percent over 30 days). Registered agents surged 48.4 percent to 491,591. Malawi's two main platforms are Airtel Money and TNM Mpamba.
