## Description

MIGA guarantee covering Sonatel SA (Senegal) equity investment and retained earnings in Orange Finances Mobiles Guinee SA, the mobile money subsidiary in Guinea. Part of the combined Orange Guinea and Guinea Bissau guarantee (EUR 433.7M total). This tranche (PID 15192) covers $26.5M for the mobile money operations. Risks: Expropriation, War and Civil Disturbance, Transfer Restrictions & Currency Inconvertibility. Issued August 2023 (FY2024).
