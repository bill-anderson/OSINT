# Bank of Namibia, IPN and NamClear Sign Pact to Strengthen Instant Payments

The Bank of Namibia has joined Instant Payments Namibia (IPN) and NamClear in signing a Declaration of Intent (DoI) to strengthen collaboration and advance the country's digital payments infrastructure. The agreement aims to integrate Namibia's instant payment capabilities with the country's existing clearing infrastructure, improving efficiency, resilience and interoperability of the national payments ecosystem.

Signatories: Bank of Namibia Governor Ebson Uanguta, IPN COO Marsorry Ickua, and NamClear MD Fabian Tait. The parties will leverage NamClear's established role in Namibia's clearing system and its relationship with the central bank through the Namibia Interbank Settlement System (NISS). The initiative supports a more inclusive, modern financial system and the resilience of the national payments infrastructure.
