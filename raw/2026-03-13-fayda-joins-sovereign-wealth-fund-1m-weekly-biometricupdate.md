Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 13 March 2026. NIDP is registering an estimated one million people per week toward the 90 million Fayda target, with 37 million+ enrolled (Exec Director Yodahe Zemichael), intensifying efforts in rural/hard-to-reach areas. Notably, Fayda/NIDP has joined the Ethiopian Investment Holdings (EIH) portfolio - Ethiopia's sovereign wealth fund and public-enterprise commercialisation engine - with authorized capital of 10 billion birr (~US$64m) and 2.5 billion birr (~$16m) paid-up. Signals a shift from project to commercialised, revenue-generating entity (authentication-as-a-service). [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
