# PAIX Data Centres breaks ground on Dakar data center

**[[paix-data-centres]]** broke ground in **January 2025** on a data centre in **Les Mamelles, Dakar** —
**1.2MW critical power across 918 sqm** white space, operational **in 2026**; ~200 construction jobs, ~20
full-time operational staff. CEO Wouter van Hulten cited Dakar's aggregation of submarine-cable landings
(ACE, MainOne, SAT-3, SHARE, with 2Africa to join). PAIX pledged 100% renewable construction energy by 2030;
it also operates in Accra and Nairobi, plus a Djibouti JV (2026). Africa50 invested $20m+ in PAIX (2022).
Senegal's data-centre market is small and Dakar-concentrated (existing: Onix, Sonatel, Diamniadio, Free au
Sénégal). (Dup fold: DCD "breaks ground" [23 Jan 2025] + Connecting Africa [27 Jan 2025] — same announcement.)
