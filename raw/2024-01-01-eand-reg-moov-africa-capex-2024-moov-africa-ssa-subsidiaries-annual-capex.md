## Description

Maroc Telecom's capital expenditures in its sub-Saharan African subsidiaries operating under the Moov Africa brand reached MAD 7.96 billion ($800 million) in 2024, up 75.6% from MAD 4.54 billion in 2023. Of the total, MAD 3.549 billion was for frequency and licence acquisitions. Subsidiaries covered: Burkina Faso, Côte d'Ivoire, Benin, Togo, Niger, Gabon, Mauritania, Mali, Chad, Central African Republic. e& (Etisalat Group) owns 53% of Maroc Telecom which in turn owns these subsidiaries.
