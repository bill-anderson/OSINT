# MTN Cameroon — three-year network capex pledge, 2025

MTN Cameroon pledged US$225 million over three years to strengthen its operations in Cameroon — particularly its fintech division — expanding national network coverage, reported alongside an infrastructure/energy partnership with Camusat and its subsidiary Aktivco.

## Description

"MTN Cameroon has entered into a partnership with Camusat Group … through its energy and infrastructure subsidiary Aktivco. … Camusat will provide end-to-end telecom infrastructure and energy solutions, allowing the telco to expand national coverage and improve network reliability. The telco has previously pledged $225 million over three years to strengthen its operations in the country, particularly its fintech division."
