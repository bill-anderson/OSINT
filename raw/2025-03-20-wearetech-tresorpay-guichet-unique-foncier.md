We Are Tech Africa (Samira Njoya). On Tuesday 18 March 2025, PM Abdoulaye Maiga launched two digital platforms. "Tresor Pay", a collaboration with all Malian mobile-money operators, streamlines and secures payment of national ID card and passport fees, traffic violations and fixed fines; developed by the Support Unit for Computerisation of Tax and Financial Services (CAISFF) with the National Directorate of Treasury and Public Accounting; already operational at the Directorate General of Transport and Commune IV city hall. The Land Information System / One-Stop Land Office (SIF/GUF) modernises land and property procedures in a single location, slated for rollout across Bamako's seven districts within the year. Mali rose 13 places to 141st/193 on the 2024 UN EGDI. (Tresor Pay is the payment rail later linked to Sigui-Dolo biometric driving licences.)
