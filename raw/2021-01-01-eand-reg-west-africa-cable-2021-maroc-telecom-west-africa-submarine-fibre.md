## Description

Maroc Telecom invested €150 million ($157 million) in the construction of the West Africa submarine fibre optic cable, a 9,414 km system connecting the group's Moov Africa SSA subsidiaries to the international network via Morocco. The cable has 8 landing points across 7 countries including Côte d'Ivoire (Abidjan), Togo (Lomé), Benin (Cotonou), Gabon (Libreville), Mauritania (Nouadhibou), Morocco (Casablanca, Dakhla), and Portugal (Lisbon). South segment operational July 2021; North segment operational April 2022. Capacity: 20-60 Tbps. Financed by a consortium of Maroc Telecom and its African subsidiaries.
