## Description

Supports governments to implement trusted, secure and inclusive digital identification and civil registration systems. Finances the World Bank Group's Identification for Development (ID4D) initiative 2019-2025 to: (1) produce evidence-based guidance on preferred approaches for digital identification and illustrate development value; (2) establish and support global platforms, convening and peer-to-peer exchange; (3) provide technical assistance to over 30 governments and regional institutions to roll out digital identification and civil registration. Also funds independent evaluation of the ID4D programme. ID4D priority countries include Nigeria, Ethiopia, DRC, Kenya and others in SSA.
