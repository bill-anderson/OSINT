## Description

MCF $275.7M partnership with Carnegie Mellon University and Government of Rwanda to expand engineering, AI and technology education at CMU-Africa in Kigali. Includes $175M endowment for CMU-Africa and $100.7M for the new Center for the Inclusive Digital Transformation of Africa (Upanzi Network). Target: 10,000 young Africans in digital skills and digital transformation careers.
