The IEBC participated in an interagency meeting (11 November 2025) to explore **data-sharing framework** strategies supporting the ongoing **Continuous Voter Registration (CVR)** exercise. The team seeks a collaborative framework and a draft MoU between the IEBC and the State Department of Immigration and Citizen Services for secure, lawful data sharing — covering national ID and passport data, the **Integrated Population Registration System (IPRS)**, records of deceased persons and other datasets for updating the Register of Voters.

The IEBC team was led by Moses Sunkuli, Chrispine Owiye and Michael Ouma. NRB Secretary Dr Christopher Wanjau said the bureau targets registering over 6.7 million Kenyans by 2027 and proposed registering youth collecting IDs at bureau offices. The meeting agreed to form a joint secretariat to actualize an **API to the IPRS** for the pre-registration module.
