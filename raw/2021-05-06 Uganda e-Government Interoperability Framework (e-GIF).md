# Uganda e-Government Interoperability Framework (e-GIF)

Companion source page for the artefact `2021-05-06 Uganda e-Government Interoperability Framework (e-GIF).pdf`, held in `raw/`.

UGANDA's, confirmed on the cover. Twelve underlying principles (subsidiarity/proportionality, openness, transparency, reusability, technological neutrality and data portability, user-centricity, inclusion and accessibility, security, privacy) and six interoperability layers (governance, integrated public service governance, legal, organisational, semantic, technical). Conceptual model includes base registries, open data and catalogues. Project duration printed as 10 Nov 2020 - 9 Sep 2021; no version number on the cover, unlike its companion Digital Government Strategy v1.1.
