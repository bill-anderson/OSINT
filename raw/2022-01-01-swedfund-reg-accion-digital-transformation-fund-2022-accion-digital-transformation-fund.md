## Description

Swedfund invested up to USD 15 million in the Accion Digital Transformation Fund, focused on the digital transformation of financial institutions in developing countries in Africa and South and Southeast Asia. Accion supports digital transformation through capital, strategic and technical advice and governance support to microfinance institutions and financial service providers. The fund raised USD 152.5 million in its 2024 close with co-investors BII, FMO, IFC, Mastercard, and OeEB.
