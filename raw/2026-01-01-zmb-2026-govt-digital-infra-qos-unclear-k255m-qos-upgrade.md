# Zambia approves K255 million to upgrade digital infrastructure and Quality of Service

The Government of Zambia approved K255 million to upgrade digital infrastructure and improve Quality of Service, particularly in rural areas, announced by Minister of Technology and Science Felix Mutati following the Third Digital Economy Roundtable (DERT III) in Lusaka.
