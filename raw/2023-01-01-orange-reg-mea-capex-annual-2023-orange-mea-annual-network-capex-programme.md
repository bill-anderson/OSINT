## Description

Orange invested €1.2 billion across its MEA region in 2023 for network deployment including 4G/5G rollout, fibre extension, and mobile broadband. SSA countries include Senegal (Sonatel), Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon, Mali, Burkina Faso, Guinea, Madagascar, DRC, Liberia, Sierra Leone, Botswana. Orange has stated an annual CapEx commitment of over €1 billion per year to Africa and Middle East.
