Singapore's **[[trident-digital-tech]]** (NASDAQ: TDTH) signed the **definitive PPP agreement** with the DRC on **25 June 2025**, capping a collaboration framework begun December 2024 — becoming the DRC's **exclusive provider of e-KYC services** for its **80 million+ mobile subscribers** on a **20-year term**, delivering the Web 3.0 / **Tridentity**-based **[[rdc-pass|DRCPass]]** national digital ID.

Phased nationwide deployment and a public-education campaign were underway by August 2025; Singapore's Minister Grace Fu highlighted DRCPass at the **Africa Singapore Business Forum 2025** as a model of Singapore–Africa collaboration. Use cases: government-service access, **biometric SIM registration**, and identity verification for **digital payments and credit assessment**.
