## Description

Sonatel Group (Orange subsidiary in West Africa) invested XOF 253.5 billion in 2023 across Senegal, Mali, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, and Sierra Leone. Investments covered fibre optic deployment, 4G extension, 5G licence acquisition in Senegal (XOF 34 billion), mobile network densification, and digital services infrastructure. Investments represent 15.6% of Group revenue.
