## Description

DIRECCT (Digital Response Connecting Citizens) Senegal components: (1) digital transformation of health systems and (2) digital transformation of micro/small/medium enterprises (MPMEs) in Senegal. EU-funded programme co-managed by Enabel and AFD. Senegal health component: digitisation of health facility data and DHIS2. MPME component: distribution of digital tools and training to 202 SMEs in Thiès/Diourbel/Kaolack region for agri and green economy.
