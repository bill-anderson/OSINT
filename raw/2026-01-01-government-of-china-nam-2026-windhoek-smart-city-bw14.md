China committed ¥98 million (about US$14.4 million) towards Namibia's Smart City pilot project, supporting digital infrastructure, AI capabilities and skills development under Windhoek's decade-long modernisation programme.

## Description

"China has committed ¥98 million (about US$14.4m) towards Namibia's Smart City pilot project, with the funding set to support the country's digital infrastructure, artificial intelligence (AI) capabilities and skills development under a decade-long modernisation programme." "The contribution follows the signing of the Smart City arrangement at the end of June." The project forms part of the Windhoek People-Centred Smart City Strategy (2026-2036).
