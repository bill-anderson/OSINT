## Description

On 5–7 November 2025, Axian Telecom Fibre Ltd (trading as Yas) completed the acquisition of 99.63% of Wananchi Group Holdings Ltd for approximately USD 63 million (KES 9.6 billion). Wananchi Group operates Zuku (consumer broadband/IPTV, Kenya) and Simbanet (enterprise connectivity, Tanzania). The acquisition extends Axian's fixed broadband footprint into Kenya, Tanzania (integrated with existing Yas Tanzania) and Uganda. The deal was partly financed by the USD 600M 2025 bond (part of proceeds) and separately by an IFC loan of USD 25M plus USD 37.5M from partner lenders.
