## Description

LeapFrog Investments and Tana Africa Capital make a joint strategic investment of USD 110 million in Interswitch Group, one of Africa's leading technology-driven integrated digital payments companies. The investment involves both primary capital and acquisition of shares from existing shareholders including Ignite Holdco (Helios Investment Partners and TA Associates). Interswitch provides payment processing, switching services, card issuance and digital financial services across Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa, supporting financial inclusion for Nigeria's unbanked consumers.
