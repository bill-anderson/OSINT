## Description

MCF Scholars Program committed $1.7B over first decade (2012-2022) to nearly 40,000 scholars. Target 100,000 scholars by 2030 across 33 partner institutions in Africa. Primarily serves SSA countries. Digital focus includes technology and engineering degrees at CMU-Africa, AIMS, and partner institutions. University of Rwanda 10-year $55M partnership also included.
