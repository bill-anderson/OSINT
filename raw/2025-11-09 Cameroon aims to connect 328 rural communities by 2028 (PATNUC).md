Cameroon's Posts and Telecommunications Minister Minette Libom Li Likeng opened bidding (**31 October 2025**) for the **"rural connectivity"** component of the **Digital Transformation Acceleration Project (PATNUC)** — World Bank-backed, funded with **55 billion XAF** — to connect **328 currently-unserved 3G localities by 2028**.

The limited national tender runs in two phases (an initial 120 sites plus an optional 208), with work expected by 30 December 2026. Implementation involves the ministries of Public Procurement (Minmap) and Economy/Planning (Minepat), the regulator **ART**, **Camtel**, **MTN Cameroon** and **Orange Cameroun**. Rural connectivity is one of three PATNUC components ([[patnuc]]).
