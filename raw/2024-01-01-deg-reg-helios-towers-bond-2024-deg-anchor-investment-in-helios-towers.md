## Description

DEG acts as anchor investor in the USD 850 million 7.500% senior notes due 2029 issued by HTA Group Ltd, a subsidiary of Helios Towers plc. The bond was co-anchored by BII (USD 30M), IFC, DEG, and the Emerging Africa Infrastructure Fund. The bond refinances Helios Towers' existing 2025 notes and extends average debt maturity from three to five years. Helios Towers operates over 14,000 sites across nine SSA countries: Tanzania, DRC, Ghana, Congo Brazzaville, South Africa, Senegal, Madagascar, and Malawi. The investment supports Africa's digital transformation through the backbone of mobile network infrastructure.
