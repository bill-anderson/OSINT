## Description

Sonatel Group invested approximately XOF 300 billion in 2024 (representing 17% of turnover of XOF 1,776.4 billion) across Senegal, Mali, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, and Sierra Leone. Investments included 5G launch in Senegal, extension of fibre optic network, mobile broadband coverage expansion (4G reaching 98% of Senegal population), and satellite internet launch.
