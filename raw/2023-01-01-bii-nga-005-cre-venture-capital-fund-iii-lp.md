## Description

BII committed $8M to CRE Venture Capital Fund III A LP in August 2023, an early-stage venture fund investing in tech-enabled businesses predominantly in Nigeria. CRE (formerly Chrysalis) focuses on fintech, digital health, and logistics tech businesses solving structural challenges for consumers and businesses in Nigeria. The fund complements BII's direct investments in Nigerian tech ecosystem.
