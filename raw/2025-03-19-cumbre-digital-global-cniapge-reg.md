Equatorial Guinea participated in the **World Bank Global Digital Summit 2025** (Washington DC, 17–21 March 2025), theme "Digital Pathways for All". The country was represented by **Melanio Ebendeng Oyana, Director General of the Centro Nacional para la Informatización de la Administración Pública de Guinea Ecuatorial (CNIAPGE)** — the national body for informatising public administration — standing in for Minister Honorato Evita Oma. Sessions addressed the role of technology in transforming public administration and Central African economic development, and highlighted regional digital integration including the **CARDIP** project (Central African regional digital integration).
