# US EXIM Bank — Côte d'Ivoire sovereign data centre and digitisation, 2026

Côte d'Ivoire authorised a US$170 million US Export-Import Bank-financed project, implemented by US firm Cybastion, to build a sovereign national data centre, a government digitisation platform and a smart border-surveillance system.

## Description

"U.S. technology company Cybastion has received formal authorization from the Ivoirian government to proceed with a $170 million U.S. Export-Import Bank financed project that will bring a sovereign national data center, government digitization platform, and a cutting-edge smart border surveillance system to Côte d'Ivoire. U.S. tech companies Cisco and Cybastion are partnering with the U.S. Embassy to deepen American engagement in Côte d'Ivoire's growing digital sector." Announced 16 July 2026 by Assistant Secretary of State for African Affairs Frank R. Garcia at the U.S. Embassy in Abidjan.
