## Description

EAIF acted as sole impact investor anchoring Africell's debut USD 300 million international public bond (oversubscribed at $550M+). The financing supports roll-out of digital infrastructure across Angola, DRC, Gambia and Sierra Leone, enhancing connectivity for Africell's 14 million+ customers. EAIF committed USD 28 million (sole DFI/impact investor in the bond).
