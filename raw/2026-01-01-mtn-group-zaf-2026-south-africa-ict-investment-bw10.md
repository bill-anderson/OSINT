## Description

"Telecommunications operator MTN has pledged to invest 21.8 billion South African rand (US$1.3 billion) over the next three years to expand ICT infrastructure and advance the digital economy in South Africa." "The announcement was made at the annual South Africa Investment Conference in Sandton, Johannesburg, this week." "By the end of 2028, it expects to invest a total of almost R22 billion ($1.3 billion) in the country." MTN SA spent R6.8 billion ($404 million) on its network in 2025.
