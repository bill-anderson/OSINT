## Description

TLcom Capital participated as an existing investor in uLesson's $7.5M Series A round, approximately 11 months after leading the seed. uLesson had grown to cover K-12 content across six African countries. The Series A supports product development, content expansion, and market growth across East and West Africa. Other investors: Owl Ventures (new lead), Founder Collective.
