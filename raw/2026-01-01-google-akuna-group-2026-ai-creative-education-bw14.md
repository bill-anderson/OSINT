# Google.org — Akuna Group AI creative-education programme (Africa), 2026

At its inaugural Africa Cloud Summit (Johannesburg, July 2026), Google committed more than US$1 million (R17 million) in Google.org funding to a partnership with The Akuna Group delivering AI creative education to underrepresented creators across Africa.

## Description

"Google is partnering with The Akuna Group to empower underrepresented creators in Africa. Backed by more than $1 million (R17 million) in Google.org funding, the program delivers AI creative education alongside advanced digital tools. The program's goal is to equip African creators to tell locally rooted stories in new ways and forge professional advancement pathways."
