## Description

EAIF acted as anchor investor in bond issuances by Helios Towers Africa (HTA Group Ltd), a pan-African tower company owning and operating over 7,000+ telecom towers. The 2020 bond raised USD 750 million (EAIF: USD 30 million) to fund tower expansion in Tanzania, DRC, Congo Brazzaville and Ghana. EAIF made an additional USD 25 million commitment in 2024 as co-anchor investor alongside IFC and Proparco in the 1.2 billion bond alongside IHS Towers.
