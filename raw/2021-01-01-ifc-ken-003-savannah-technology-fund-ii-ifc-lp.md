## Description

IFC committed up to $3 million as a limited partner in Savannah Fund II LP, a venture capital fund raising up to $25 million to invest in early-stage technology companies in Eastern and Southern Africa. The fund targets pre-seed and seed stage tech startups including fintech, edtech, agritech, and digital platforms. Savannah Fund is a Nairobi-based VC fund making early bets on Africa's technology ecosystem.
