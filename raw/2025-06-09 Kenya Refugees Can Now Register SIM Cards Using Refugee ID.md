The Government of Kenya published the **Kenya Information and Communications (Registration of Telecommunications Service Subscribers) Regulations, 2025** under **Legal Notice No. 90**. A key provision, Section 5(e), officially recognizes the **Refugee Identity Card** as a valid document for SIM card registration, allowing refugees to legally acquire SIM cards and access mobile services across Kenya.

Other highlights: mandatory biometric and ID verification; SIM registration allowed for children by parents/guardians; a ban on proxy registrations (except for minors); a six-month compliance window for existing subscribers; and revocation of the 2015 SIM registration regulations.
