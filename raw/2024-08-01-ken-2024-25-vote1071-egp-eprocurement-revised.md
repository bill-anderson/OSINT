## Description

The e-GP system build (10 government institutions onboarding target for 2024/25, rising to 50 by
2026/27 per PBB Part E). One of the few large digital capital lines to survive the
post-Finance-Bill-withdrawal cuts untouched.

## Notes

Cross-vote catch (Block 4b): this line sits in the Treasury vote, not the ICT vote. Sister lines
in the same schedule were wiped: KRA ERP/CRM (KSh 3,436.2m -> 0), Government Data Centre DRC
equipment (600m -> 0), IFMIS Oracle licences (50m -> 0), IFMIS county connectivity (150m -> 0);
Single Window Support cut 258.3m -> 100m. See
[[2024-08-01-ken-2024-25-vote1071-kra-erp-crm-zeroed]].
