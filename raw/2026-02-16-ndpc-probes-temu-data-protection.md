The Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) launched an immediate investigation into the data processing activities of e-commerce platform Temu following complaints over alleged poor data management. National Commissioner Vincent Olatunji said preliminary findings indicate Temu processes personal information of approximately 12.7 million data subjects in Nigeria (about 70 million daily active users globally). The investigation concerns online surveillance, accountability, data minimisation, transparency, duty of care and cross-border data transfers. Olatunji warned that processors handling personal data must verify compliance with the NDPA or face liability, including fines and sanctions.
