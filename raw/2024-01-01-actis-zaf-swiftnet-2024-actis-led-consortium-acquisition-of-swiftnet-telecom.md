## Description

Actis, together with Royal Bafokeng Holdings as empowerment partner, acquires 100% of Swiftnet, Telkom South Africa's masts and towers portfolio, at an enterprise value of ZAR 6.75 billion (c. USD 355 million). Actis leads the consortium via Towerco Bidco with a 70% stake; Royal Bafokeng Holdings holds 30%. Swiftnet operates approximately 4,000 towers and masts across South Africa. Transaction completed March 2025 following regulatory approvals from Competition Tribunal (September 2024) and ICASA (December 2024).
