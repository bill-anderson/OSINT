Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 7 January 2026. Ethiopia's Government Communication Service (GCS) underscored Fayda's role in the Digital Ethiopia 2030 strategy (launched at the close of 2025), casting digital identity as shaping national economic-development paths. GCS framed Fayda as enabling equal access to social services, strengthening national security via biometric data (making identity fraud "impossible"), eliminating intermediaries between government and citizens, and helping prevent corruption/misuse of public resources - "the foundation for a competitive digital [economy]." Official pro-Fayda framing (contrast with Addis Standard critiques). [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
