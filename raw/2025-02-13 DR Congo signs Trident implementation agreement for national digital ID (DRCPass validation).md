The DRC and Singapore's **[[trident-digital-tech]]** announced (**12 February 2025**) an **implementation agreement** with the Ministry of Posts, Telecommunications and Digital Affairs to deploy a national digital-ID system — following a December 2024 collaboration framework with the Office of the President.

The **[[rdc-pass|DRCPass]]** **validation phase launched 15 March 2025** in Kinshasa, led by Minister Augustin Kibassa Maliba, using Trident's flagship **Tridentity** platform — combining blockchain, biometrics, AI and zero-knowledge proofs, integrated into sovereign-cloud systems and national data centres.
