## Description

AFC approved a USD 230 million senior debt facility (super senior debt investment) to 9mobile (Emerging Markets Telecommunications Services / EMTS, formerly Etisalat Nigeria), Nigeria's fourth-largest mobile network operator. The facility is structured in two tranches: (i) to repay historic vendor obligations, finance costs, and interest reserve account; and (ii) quick-win capex initiatives for network infrastructure upgrade. Purpose is to support 9mobile's turnaround strategy and reposition it for growth and profitability in Nigeria's competitive telecoms market.
