State daily; capture is not endorsement. Justice Minister Mamoudou Kassogue welcomed the operationalisation of the criminal chamber within Mali's National Pole for the fight against Cybercrime (Pole national de lutte contre la cybercriminalite). At the reported session, 4 cases involving 8 accused were on the roll, to be examined over a week; charges included insults, threats, undermining state security via an information system, ICT-enabled fraud, and conspiracy to commit computer offences. Kassogue said the jurisdiction is playing an essential role in "pacifying the digital space," noting "everything that happens in the virtual world has repercussions in the real world." Evidence of Mali building operational cyber-justice capacity alongside its cybersecurity strategy and the new ANSSI agency.
