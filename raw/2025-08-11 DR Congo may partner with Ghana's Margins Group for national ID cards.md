The DRC is finalising a contract with Ghana's **[[margins-id-group|Margins Group]]** (maker of the "Ghana Card") to **produce national identity cards**, the Presidential Strategic Oversight Council (CPVS) said on 25 July 2025 — replacing the terminated **Afritech/IDEMIA** deal. CPVS coordinator François Muamba Tshishimbi and **[[onip-drc]]** DG Richard Ilunga confirmed the partnership but gave no details on the selection process.

This physical-**card** track (via ONIP) runs **parallel to** Trident's **DRCPass** digital-ID/e-KYC system — two distinct identity efforts underway at once, with the card-vendor selection process left opaque.
