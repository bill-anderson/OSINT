# KOICA — Cameroon COLEPS e-procurement grant, 2025

The Korea International Cooperation Agency provided a US$12.5 million grant that funded the upgraded Cameroon Online e-Procurement System (COLEPS), launched by the Ministry of Public Contracts in June 2025.

## Description

"Public Contracts Minister, Ibrahim Talba Malla, recently presided over the launching ceremony of the platform which was set up with the support of grant funding from the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA) to the tune of $12.5 million." The upgrade makes online contract signing and real-time monitoring possible, integrating with banks and financial institutions for bid securities and performance guarantees.

"This grant has enabled the materialization of public contract procedures under the first and second phases of the e-procurement project, hence the advent of this new and elaborate version of COLEPS."
