# Digital 2025 — Algeria (DataReportal)

Connectivity baseline (early 2025):

- **Mobile connections:** **54.8m** — 116% of population; +3.0m (+5.8%) YoY; 91.4% broadband (3G/4G/5G).
- **Internet users:** **36.2m** — penetration **76.9%**; +488,000 (+1.4%) YoY; 10.9m (23.1%) offline.
- **Social-media identities:** 25.6m (54.2%).
- **Population:** 47.1m (Jan 2025); 76% urban; median age 28.6.
