World Bank Program Document (Report PGD168) for a proposed **Development Policy Loan of EUR 459.8 million (~US$500 million)** to the Kingdom of Morocco for the **First Financial and Digital Inclusion Development Policy Financing** (26 May 2020) — spanning the Finance/Competitiveness/Innovation, Digital Development and Macroeconomics/Trade Global Practices in the MENA Region.

The DPF operation underpins Morocco's financial- and digital-inclusion reform agenda — a baseline behind the later social-protection registry build (RNP/RSU) and the Digital Morocco 2030 strategy. Reference document; cite, don't absorb.
