# Congo Telecom becomes first operator in Republic of Congo to receive ISO 9001:2015 certification

Date: 2026-03-29. By: Nana Appiah Acquaye.

*Sweep note — PR-wire: Tech Review Africa serves no date to search, but the body carries `Date: 2026-03-29` and a named byline. The item reads as Congo Telecom corporate communications; the **primary worth sourcing at ingest** is the Congo Telecom / Bureau Veritas certification announcement itself (congotelecom.cg carries an "Congo Telecom obtient la certification ISO 9001:2015" news item).*

Congo Telecom has become the first telecommunications operator in the Republic of Congo to obtain ISO 9001:2015 certification, issued by Bureau Veritas. The certification recognizes the company's structured processes, customer-focused approach, and commitment to continuous improvement.

Congo Telecom described the achievement as an external validation of its daily efforts but emphasized that it is not an endpoint.

The company affirmed its ongoing commitment to maintaining high-quality standards and fostering continuous improvement across all operations, positioning itself to meet both current and future customer expectations.
