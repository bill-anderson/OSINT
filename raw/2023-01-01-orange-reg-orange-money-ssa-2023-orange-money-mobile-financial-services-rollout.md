## Description

Orange Money is a mobile financial services platform operating across 17 countries in Africa and Middle East. In 2023, the platform recorded over €130 billion in transactions with 34.1 million active customers and revenue growth of 25.8% YoY. SSA countries include Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon, Mali, Burkina Faso, Guinea, Madagascar, DRC, Liberia, Sierra Leone. The 2024 figure exceeded €160 billion in transactions.
