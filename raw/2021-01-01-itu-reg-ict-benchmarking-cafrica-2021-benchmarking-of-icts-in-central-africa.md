## Description

ITU project to conduct ICT benchmarking for Central African countries. Covers data collection analysis and dissemination of ICT indicators for policy and regulatory decision-making. Supports evidence-based digital transformation planning through standardized measurement methodologies. All target countries are SSA: Cameroon Central African Republic Chad Congo DRC Equatorial Guinea Gabon South Sudan.
