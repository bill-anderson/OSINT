## Description

DFC loan of up to $5 million to Twiga Foods Ltd. to strengthen food security in Kenya by increasing farmers' access to markets through a digital B2B food distribution platform. Twiga uses mobile technology and data analytics to connect farmers directly to urban retailers, digitising the agricultural supply chain. DFC loan finances expansion of cold storage and logistics technology. First disbursement March 2020. Twiga connects over 17,000 vendors to 30,000+ farmers using its mobile platform.
