At a national **digital-identity workshop** in Brazzaville (**16 July 2025**, "Toward an inclusive and secure digital identity"), Posts, Telecoms and Digital Economy Minister **Léon Juste Ibombo** stressed the need for a secure digital-identity system to protect personal data and guarantee **national digital sovereignty** — under the World Bank-financed **[[patn-congo]]** and "Congo Digital 2025."

The government committed to a legal/institutional framework enabling a **Digital Wallet**; **Thales**'s Laurent Jutard called trusted digital identity the first pillar of transformation. The Ministry of Interior (Bonsang Oko Letchaud) said a **unified, reliable, sovereign digital identity** is being built with WB support, and civil-status reform is progressing via **SIFEC** (Système d'information des faits d'État Civil), digitising civil-status records — modern interoperable ID framed as national security amid cross-border threats.
