Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 4 June 2025. Interview with NIDP Executive Director Yodahe Zemichael at the close of ID4Africa 2025 (hosted by Ethiopia): the roadmap to give every citizen/legal resident a Fayda digital ID before end-2027 ("Digital ID is not optional; it is a necessity"). As of 4 June, 16.4 million enrolled. Covers the significance of hosting ID4Africa, the push for cross-border digital-ID interoperability across Africa (movement/trade), inclusion and financial inclusion via a digital wallet, tackling mistrust/misconceptions, data privacy/security, and Africa's technology sovereignty.
