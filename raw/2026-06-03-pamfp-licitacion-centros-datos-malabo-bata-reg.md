The **Proyecto de Apoyo a la Modernización de las Finanzas Públicas (PAMFP)** launched an open national tender to acquire IT equipment for the server rooms of the Ministry of Finance, Planning and Economic Development. The tender covers two strategic facilities: the **Primary Data Centre in Malabo** and the **Backup Data Centre in Bata**, split into two independent lots (max four months' execution each). Open to companies/consortia legally established in GNQ with proven experience; financed by PAMFP counterpart funds. Proposals in French with a Spanish copy. Evaluation criteria include equipment quality/origin, experience in data-centre implementation and **solar-energy systems**, and technical staff qualifications. Aim: strengthen technological infrastructure for storage, processing and backup of public-financial information, with an operational backup centre for continuity/resilience.
