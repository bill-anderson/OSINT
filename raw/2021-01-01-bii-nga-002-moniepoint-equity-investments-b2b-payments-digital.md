## Description

Three equity investments by BII in Moniepoint (formerly TeamApt), a Nigerian fintech providing an all-in-one payments, banking, and operations platform for businesses. Moniepoint processes millions of transactions monthly for small businesses across Nigeria. Investment 1: $3.6M (Sep 2021, via VC Scale-up programme). Investment 2: $1.5M equity (Jan 2022, follow-on). Investment 3: $5M equity (Jun 2022, Moniepoint Inc. later exited Oct 2024). BII investment improved access to financial services for millions of unbanked individuals and micro-entrepreneurs.
