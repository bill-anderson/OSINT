## Notes

The **audited stage** for FY2024/25. Covers all PFMA auditees: 417 auditees, 151 clean
audits (36%, managing only 12% of the R2.21tn expenditure budget); 266 auditees managing
88% of the budget lack capability for credible financial/performance reporting. Contains a
**dedicated section on the government ICT function** (per the AG's briefing) plus
high-impact-auditee analyses — the entry point for Vote 30/DCDT, SITA and Home Affairs
audit findings. Fruitless and wasteful expenditure R1.4bn (down from R3.5bn);
accruals R51.25bn; claims against departments R116bn.
