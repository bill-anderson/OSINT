# Kenya Programme Based Budget FY2024/25 (original estimates)

Companion page for the artefact (1,149 pp, June 2024 per cover). The **cross-vote instrument**:
all national-government votes at programme and sub-programme level with economic classification,
plus FY2025/26 and 2026/27 projections. Original estimates as tabled/approved before the Finance
Bill 2024 withdrawal; superseded in-year by Supplementary I (Aug 2024), II and III. **Scale and
currency: absolute KShs per table (no thousands scale observed on sampled tables — verify per
table at extraction).** Digital targets for extraction: Vote 1122 (State Dept for ICT and Digital
Economy), Vote 1123 (Broadcasting and Telecommunications), the Interior/Immigration votes (Maisha
Namba, population registration, passports), the ODPC head, Konza/KAIST lines, e-government shared
services; cross-vote scan per Block 4b. Body is a structured extract of a tabular document, not a
withheld text.
