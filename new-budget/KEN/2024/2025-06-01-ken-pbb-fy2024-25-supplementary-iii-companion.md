# Kenya PBB FY2024/25 Supplementary Estimates III (approved, June 2025)

Companion page for the artefact (740 pp, "JUNE 2025" per cover, approved). The **final in-year
revision** — per the COB's annual BIRR, the gross budget moved 4.49tn (original) → 4.37tn (Supp I)
→ 4.64tn (Supp II) → 4.37tn (Supp III). This volume is the closest instrument to final revised
estimates for FY2024/25 and the comparator for the BIRR's actuals. Budget stage: **revised
(final)**. Scale and currency: absolute KShs per table (verify at extraction). Body is a
structured extract of a tabular document, not a withheld text.
