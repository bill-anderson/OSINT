# Kenya Development Supplementary I FY2024/25 Vol. I (Votes D1011–D1083)

Companion page for the artefact (1,099 pp, August 2024). Revised project-level development lines
for votes D1011–D1083 — the range holding **Interior/Immigration and Citizen Services (identity:
Maisha Namba, population registration, passports)** and other Block-4b ministries. Staged in place
of the original Development Book Vol. I (D1011–D1083), which is a **dead link on Treasury's own
site** (all three URL paths serve a Drupal 404 as of 2026-07-23 — in acquisitions). Budget stage:
revised. Scale and currency: absolute KShs. Body is a structured extract of a tabular document,
not a withheld text.
