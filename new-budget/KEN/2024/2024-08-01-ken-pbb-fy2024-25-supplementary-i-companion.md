# Kenya PBB FY2024/25 Supplementary Estimates I (approved, August 2024)

Companion page for the artefact (977 pp, "AUGUST 2024" per cover, marked approved). The
**post-Finance-Bill-withdrawal revision**: the Finance Bill 2024 was rejected under Art. 115 after
the June 2024 protests (revenue shortfall ~KSh 344.3bn); this supplementary implements the revised
fiscal framework — national government cut KSh 145.7bn (40bn recurrent / 105bn development),
enacted as the Supplementary Appropriation (No. 2) Act, 2024, assented 2024-08-05. Budget stage:
**revised**. Scale and currency: absolute KShs per table (verify at extraction). Body is a
structured extract of a tabular document, not a withheld text.
