## The gap this document does not fill

Burundi's audit-side instrument for a given fiscal year is not the RPGA but the
**rapport sur le projet de loi de règlement (PLR)**, published at
`courdescomptes.bi/assets/images/PLR<YYYY>_<YYYY>.pdf`. That series runs to
**PLR2022_2023 and stops**: `PLR2023_2024.pdf` and `PLR2024_2025.pdf` both 404 as at
2026-07-23. So **no Cour des comptes report on the FY2024/25 loi de règlement is
published**, even though the Assembly adopted that law on 13 May 2026. Read that as
the finding, not as a fetch failure.
