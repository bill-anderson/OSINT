## Extraction note

This is the **revised** side of the FY2024/25 appropriated pair, on domestic
resources only. It is markedly smaller than the initial PTBA (44 MB vs 265 MB) at more
page objects, so the two are **differently produced scans, not the same volume
re-issued** — do not assume identical layout, column set or scale between them.

The pair (initial + modifié) is what makes an *appropriated → revised* comparison
possible for Burundi at programme grain. The parliamentary scrutiny staged in `new/`
(Burundi Eco, 7 October 2025) establishes that crédits were also moved **between**
programmes during execution beyond what the rectificative captured, so
`revised` here is still an estimate, never outturn evidence.
