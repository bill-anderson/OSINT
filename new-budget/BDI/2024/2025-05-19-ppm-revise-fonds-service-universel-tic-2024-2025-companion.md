# PPM révisé du Fonds de Service Universel des TIC, exercice 2024-2025

The **universal service fund's own procurement plan** for FY2024/25 — block 6
material, and the only artefact in this run that shows an own-source ICT levy body
spending its money. Held awaiting the budget extraction pass; **not an admitted
source**.

## The body

The **Fonds de Service Universel (FSU)** was created by **décret n°100/186 du 16
octobre 2017** and restructured by **décret n°100/054 du 29 mars 2024** — that
restructuring falls inside FY2024/25's run-up and gave the FSU its own directorate
and website (`fsu.gov.bi`); before it, the ARCT executed the fund's financial
operations under article 12 of the 2017 decree. It is financed by a **levy of 1% of
operators' turnover**, not by appropriation — so its spending is domestic and
**non-fisc own-source**, which the driver's origin gate must handle deliberately.

The FSU carries the government's stated commitment to connect **at least 1 000 health
facilities and 1 000 schools** to the internet (minister, 11 June 2026 — undated as to
delivery).

## Related, not staged

An FSU tender captured during the run states its own funding basis explicitly —
*Relance de l'AAO ouvert national n° DNCMP/444/F/2024-2025, fourniture de tablettes
portables*, published 18 June 2025: **« Le présent Marché est financé à 100% Par le
Fonds de Service Universel sur le budget général de l'exercice 2024-2025. »**
(`esoko.bi`). That phrasing — own-source fund, *sur le budget général* — is exactly
the origin ambiguity to settle at extraction.
