## Digital relevance

The minister gave the **shortfall in electronic invoicing machines — 500 of 10 000
ordered delivered** — as a named cause of the revenue miss that forced the
rectificative (IWACU, staged in `new/`). The machines' own procurement cost is not
stated in reporting; look for it in this act's annexes and in the OBR's lines.
