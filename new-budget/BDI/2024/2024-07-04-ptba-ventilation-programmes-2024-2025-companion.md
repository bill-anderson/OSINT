# PTBA 2024-2025 — ventilation des dépenses par programme ou dotation

**This is Burundi's estimates volume for FY2024/25 and the priority-0 artefact of the
run** — the only instrument that carries the per-programme split needed for a
cross-vote digital scan. Held awaiting the budget extraction pass; **not an admitted
source**.

## What a PTBA is

The **Plan de Travail et Budget Annuel** is the operational instrument of Burundi's
programme-budget reform (loi organique n°1/20 du 20 juin 2022). Each ministry's vote
is organised into ministerial **programmes** — the Cour des comptes notes roughly five
per ministry, one of them a transversal coordination/steering programme — while some
institutions carry simple **dotations** rather than programmes. This volume ventilates
FY2024/25 expenditure by programme or dotation, **on domestic resources only**
(*sur les ressources nationales*) — externally financed expenditure is out of it,
which is exactly the origin cut the domestic-state driver wants.

The PM reports to Parliament in congress on PTBA execution each semester; the
**second-semester FY2024/25 report** (7 October 2025, 2.7% overrun) is staged as prose
in `new/`.

de l'Economie Numérique** (which holds the *économie numérique* portfolio — Burundi's
