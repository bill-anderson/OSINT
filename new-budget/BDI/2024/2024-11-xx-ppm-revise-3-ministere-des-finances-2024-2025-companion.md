All **« Budget de l'Etat »** — domestic fisc, FY2024/25. These are running-cost IT
lines, not systems: **the SIGFP_BI IFMIS and e-KORI are not in this plan**, because
they are procured through PAFEN (World Bank) on its own procurement track at
`pafen.gov.bi`. That split — fisc buys the hardware and the maintenance, the donor
buys the systems — is the finding, and it is what a Burundi domestic-state total will
understate if the PAFEN side is not stated alongside it.
