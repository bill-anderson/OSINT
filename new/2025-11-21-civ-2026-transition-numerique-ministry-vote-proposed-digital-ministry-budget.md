## Description

"The Committee on Economic and Financial Affairs of the National Assembly has unanimously approved the 2026 budget for the Ministry of Digital Transition and Digitalisation. The budget, presented by Minister Ibrahim Kalil Konaté, totals FCFA 83,275,503,595 and reflects the government's strong commitment, under the leadership of President Alassane Ouattara, to strengthen the country's digital infrastructure and ensure broader connectivity across Côte d'Ivoire."

"The 2026 budget is structured around four major programs: general administration, the digital economy and postal services, support for the development of new technologies in urban areas, and the regulation of the electronic communications sector."
