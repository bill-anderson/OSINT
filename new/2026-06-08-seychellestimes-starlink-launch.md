# Starlink Satellite Internet Service Launches in Seychelles | 8 June 2026
The Seychelles Times

Starlink satellite internet service has officially launched in Seychelles, bringing high-speed broadband connectivity to homes, businesses, and institutions across the islands. According to the Department of Information Communications Technology, Starlink will complement existing fixed and mobile networks, providing additional capacity and resilience, particularly for remote areas and outer islands. The service is expected to support remote work, online education, telemedicine, and digital government services.

Starlink uses a constellation of low-earth orbit satellites to deliver low-latency broadband suitable for applications that traditionally required fibre or cable, and can be deployed quickly where terrestrial infrastructure is challenging or uneconomic. Initial coverage is available across Mahé, Praslin, and La Digue, with expansion to outer islands planned.
