MTN Nigeria announced plans to expand its fibre network to reach over eight million homes nationwide by 2028, supporting the federal government's National Broadband Plan and Digital Economy Policy. The expansion is driven by Nigeria's growing population, urbanisation and demand for low-latency connectivity. MTN is partnering with infrastructure providers, state governments and local contractors, and plans to integrate fibre into new housing projects while addressing challenges such as vandalism, right-of-way constraints and road-construction damage.
