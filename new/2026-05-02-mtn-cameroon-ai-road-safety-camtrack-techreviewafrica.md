# MTN Cameroon showcases AI-driven road safety solution at National Forum

*Date: 2026-05-02 — By: Nana Appiah Acquaye*

MTN Cameroon has highlighted the role of artificial intelligence in improving road safety during the country's first National Forum on Road Safety, held under the theme "Artificial Intelligence in the Service of Road Safety."

At the event, the telecommunications company presented an innovative solution developed in partnership with Camtrack, aimed at monitoring and analysing driver behaviour in interurban transport and heavy-duty passenger mobility.

The solution leverages AI and connected technologies to enhance driver accountability, improve fleet monitoring, and support safer transportation systems. According to MTN Cameroon, the initiative is designed to reduce road risks and contribute to more efficient mobility across communities.

The forum brought together stakeholders to discuss the future of road safety and explore how digital innovation can be applied to address challenges in the transport sector.

MTN Cameroon said the platform provided an opportunity to exchange ideas on the integration of technology into road safety strategies, particularly through data-driven approaches and real-time monitoring systems.

The company was also recognised with an award during the forum for its contribution to advancing road safety through technology.

MTN Cameroon reaffirmed its commitment to developing solutions that promote safety, connectivity, and smarter mobility, as part of its broader efforts to leverage digital innovation for social impact.
