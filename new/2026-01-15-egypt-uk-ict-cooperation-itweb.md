# Egypt, UK strengthen ICT cooperation

Egyptian Minister of Communications Amr Talaat met British Ambassador Mark Bryson-Richardson to explore boosting investment and attracting British companies to Egypt's offshoring industry. Talks covered digital transformation, digital-skills development, AI, cloud computing, data centres, cybersecurity and fintech, positioning Egypt as a regional hub and gateway to African markets. The meeting followed Egypt's recognition as Africa's leader in AI readiness in the 2025 Government AI Readiness Index (Oxford Insights).
