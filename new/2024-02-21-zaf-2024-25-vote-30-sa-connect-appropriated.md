## Description

ENE 2024, Table 30.14: the Broadband subprogramme carries R1,894.6 million in 2024/25 — up from R1,196.1 million adjusted in 2023/24, with MTEF estimates collapsing to R267.4 million (2025/26) and R279.4 million (2026/27). The chapter identifies this subprogramme as the carrier of the SA Connect broadband programme.
