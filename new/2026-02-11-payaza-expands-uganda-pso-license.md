Payaza has secured a Payment System Operator (PSO) license in Uganda, part of its plan to build regulated payment infrastructure across Africa. The license authorises Payaza to operate directly within Uganda's regulated financial system rather than relying on third-party intermediaries, giving it a stronger foothold in East Africa and deepening cross-border trade support. Capabilities unlocked include local and international payment processing (accepting global card payments alongside Uganda's dominant local methods). The company frames the approval as reflecting confidence in its compliance systems, governance and security standards.
