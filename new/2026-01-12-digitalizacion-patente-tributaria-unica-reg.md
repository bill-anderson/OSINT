On 12 January 2026 Vice-Prime Minister and Minister of Commerce Gaudencio Mohaba Messu chaired a working session on the **Patente Tributaria Única Anual** (single annual business tax/licence), focused on presenting and refining its new **digital application**. With technicians from the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Commerce, the session worked to optimise business categorisation, tax settlements and field-officer control, aiming to improve efficiency and reduce paper use. Framed as a key step to modernise tax administration, ease compliance for taxpayers and reinforce transparency in revenue collection. Adjustments were introduced before definitive rollout.
