The Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC) began strategic efforts to develop a comprehensive cybersecurity framework to protect the nation's communications infrastructure, classified as critical national infrastructure. Part of the Commission's agenda to strengthen national digital resilience. The proposed framework is expected to establish clear guidelines, enforce industry best practices, and foster collaboration between government agencies, private-sector operators and international partners to detect, prevent and respond to cyber incidents. It aligns with Nigeria's National Cybersecurity Policy and Strategy, reinforcing the NCC's role as a frontline institution in the country's digital security ecosystem.
