TechAfrica News, 23 February 2026. Ethio Telecom announced a major expansion of its digital-transformation efforts, launching new digital platforms - including teleStream and a Zero-Touch Broadband offering - under its "Next Horizon: Digital & Beyond 2028" strategy, aimed at accelerating digital inclusion, job creation and inclusive growth. The company framed the strategy as moving beyond basic connectivity to position itself as a global competitor in digital solutions and telebirr-enabled financial services.
