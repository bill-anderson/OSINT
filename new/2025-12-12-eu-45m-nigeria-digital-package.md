The European Commission announced a €45 million commitment under the EU–Nigeria Digital Economy Package, at the EU–Nigeria Digital Open Day in Brussels, with Nigeria's delegation led by Minister Dr Bosun Tijani. Discussions focused on three areas: secure connectivity and digital infrastructure (nationwide fibre backbone expansion, enhanced subsea cable capacity, 5G deployment, last-mile access to underserved areas); plus digital services and skills. The funding supports Nigeria's efforts to build a more connected, innovative and inclusive digital economy.
