Municipality of Tripoli Centre announced on 19 April the start of development of **BaladiyaBOT**, a digital assistant to enhance citizens' access to municipal services. It is developed within Germany's **GIZ** "Support to Municipalities in Libya" (SML III) programme, **funded by the European Union in Libya** and Germany's BMZ, implemented with the Libyan Ministry of Local Government and 30 municipalities.

BaladiyaBOT is conceived as the digital extension of the physical Citizen Service Centre. It will be piloted in Tripoli Centre in cooperation with the Ministry of Local Government, with a view to scaling nationally.
