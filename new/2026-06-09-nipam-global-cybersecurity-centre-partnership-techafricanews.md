# Namibia Boosts Cybersecurity Capacity Through NIPAM–Global Cybersecurity Centre Partnership

The Namibia Institute of Public Administration and Management (NIPAM) has signed an MoU with the Global Cybersecurity Centre to enhance cybersecurity awareness and capacity building across Namibia's public sector. The agreement, signed on 4 June [2026], establishes a framework to equip public servants with knowledge and skills to navigate the cyber threat landscape, focused on raising awareness and promoting best practices.

Under the MoU, NIPAM and the Global Cybersecurity Centre will jointly develop and deliver training programs to help participants identify, prevent and respond to cyberattacks — targeting public-sector employees and extending to private-sector professionals. NIPAM said the collaboration reflects its commitment to skills development and institutional capacity building critical to Namibia's digital transformation agenda.
