Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 11 December 2025. The National Bank of Ethiopia (NBE) issued a directive requiring all banks/financial institutions to align customer account information with the Fayda digital ID, with all active accounts to be linked between 1 December 2025 and 30 March 2026 (NBE press statement 10 December). Aim: support the digital-transformation agenda and seamless citizen-data integration under Digital Ethiopia 2030, enabling single-ID banking, inter-bank transfers and accurate records; customers integrate via mobile app or branch.
