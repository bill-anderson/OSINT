# Smart Africa and Algeria hold discussions on AI adoption and digital integration

Date: 2026-07-13

By: Nana Appiah Acquaye

Smart Africa has held high-level discussions with Algeria's Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, H.E. Sid Ali Zerrouki, focusing on advancing artificial intelligence adoption, digital integration and innovation across Africa.

The engagement, held in Geneva, brought together Smart Africa Chief Executive Officer Lacina Koné and the Algerian minister to discuss progress under the Africa AI Council and Algeria's participation in the Smart Market Initiative.

The discussions highlighted the importance of coordinated efforts to accelerate the adoption of artificial intelligence technologies across the continent while strengthening collaboration among African countries in building inclusive digital ecosystems.

Smart Africa said the meeting reaffirmed a shared commitment to leveraging AI, digital transformation and innovation as key drivers of Africa's economic development and technological advancement.

The engagement forms part of Smart Africa's ongoing efforts to build partnerships with governments and stakeholders to support continental digital integration and promote the development of emerging technologies across African markets.
