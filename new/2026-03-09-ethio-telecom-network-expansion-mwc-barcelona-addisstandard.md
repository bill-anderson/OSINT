Addis Standard, 9 March 2026. Ethio Telecom announced a series of strategic network expansion/modernisation agreements with Ericsson, ZTE, Huawei and Nokia at Mobile World Congress 2026 (Barcelona). CEO Frehiwot Tamru led the delegation. Ethio Telecom-ZTE: a major expansion covering 647 network sites (incl. 115 rural), lifting 4G population coverage to 95.5%. Ethio Telecom-Ericsson: a "historic" agreement to expand/modernise 1,500 mobile sites (4G layering, new tech), incl. 75 rural localities, modernising 502 3G sites to 4G. Aligned to the "Next Horizon: Digital & Beyond 2028" strategy. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
