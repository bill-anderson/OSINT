# Central Bank of Nigeria Introduces Initiatives to Streamline Operations

By Kay-Lyne Wolfenden, TechAfrica News, January 21, 2025

CBN Governor Olayemi Cardoso launches DocFlow System for digitized document management and MDAs Naira Payment Solution for financial transparency.

The Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) Governor, Mr. Olayemi Cardoso, has launched two key initiatives to enhance operational efficiency: DocFlow and MDA Naira Payment Solutions, the Nigerian Tribune has reported.

The initiatives reportedly intend to digitize document management, reduce paper dependency, and expedite internal approval processes within the bank.
