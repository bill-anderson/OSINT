# Projet de villages intelligents de l'ANSI à Tabelot : le numérique au service de l'inclusion et du développement local

*(Le Sahel / ONEP is the Niger state daily. Capture is not endorsement.)*

As part of the national digital-transformation strategy, the **Agence Nationale pour la Société de l'Information (ANSI)** is deploying its "**villages intelligents**" (smart villages) project nationwide. At **Tabelot** — a remote rural commune in the Agadez region — a **digital centre (centre numérique)** is progressively reducing the digital divide and bringing services closer to the population. A supervision mission visited the centre with local authorities in April 2026.

The centre provides online-service access (saving travel/time), educational resources and basic IT training for pupils and youth, and runs awareness sessions for women and girls on income-generating digital opportunities (commerce, information, job search). Centre facilitator Ghaicha Bouha describes teaching users — particularly women and girls — what phones and the internet are for.
