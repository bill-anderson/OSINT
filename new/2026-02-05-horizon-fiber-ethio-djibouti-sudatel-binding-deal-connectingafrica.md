Connecting Africa, 5 February 2026. Ethio Telecom, Djibouti Telecom and the Sudatel Group turned their earlier cross-border optical-fibre MoU (the "Horizon Fiber" initiative, signed December 2024) into a binding agreement. The deal establishes a resilient terrestrial fibre corridor connecting the international submarine cable landing stations in Djibouti, traversing Ethiopia, and extending to Sudan's landing stations - a scalable, secure, diversified regional pathway linking East Africa to global networks and supporting data, cloud, hyperscale connectivity and cross-border data flows. CEOs: Frehiwot Tamru (Ethio Telecom), Mohamed Assoweh Bouh (Djibouti Telecom), Magdi Abdalla Taha (Sudatel). Positions Ethiopia as a strategic digital transit hub.
