## Description

"CFA9.7bn operating and CFA17.4bn capital, across three programmes — Piloting and Support (CFA2.1bn), the Digital Programme (CFA12.3bn, nationwide digital transformation) and the Media Programme (CFA12.6bn, audiovisual/DTT)."

"Despite the cut, the government said it would still fund AI integration in public services, nationwide high-speed coverage, and a national digital-skills school."
