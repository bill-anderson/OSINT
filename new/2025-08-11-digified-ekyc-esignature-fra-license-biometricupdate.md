# Digified licensed to offer digital KYC, e-signature services in Egypt

Egyptian regtech firm Digified (founded 2019) received clearance from Egypt's Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA) to provide AI-powered digital KYC (eKYC) and e-contracting services to non-banking financial institutions (lending, investment, insurance). Its suite covers biometric/non-biometric eKYC, government data authentication via national ID and mobile-registry databases, and legally binding e-contracts with digital signatures.

Digified reports over 25 million annual verifications across Egypt's lending, investment and insurance sectors.
