The Federal Ministry of Communications, Innovation and Digital Economy directed that regulatory status quo be maintained on matters relating to regulation of internet platforms, online intermediaries and other cross-cutting digital economy issues, pending development of a harmonised national policy and governance framework. The decision follows a high-level strategic meeting convened by Minister Dr Bosun Tijani with the leadership of the Nigerian Communications Commission and other agencies. The Minister noted regulatory coordination is essential to legal certainty, investment, innovation and Nigeria's competitiveness as Africa's leading digital economy.
