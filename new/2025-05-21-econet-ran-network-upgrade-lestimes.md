# Econet rolls out major network upgrade

*Lesotho Times, 21 May 2025. By Moroke Sekoboto. To boost connectivity across the country.*

TELECOMMUNICATIONS giant Econet Telecom Lesotho has launched the Maseru RAN (Radio Access Network) and Transport Network Upgrades Project, an ambitious rollout set to improve mobile and internet services nationwide. It is being run under the "Re le Utloile" Campaign — signalling Econet has "heard the nation's outcry over network problems."

The project kicked off in February 2025; the first phase is complete, with major milestones expected between 20 May and 30 July 2025. CEO Dennis Plaatjies said the upgrade replaces outdated radio systems with high-performance equipment and reinforces the transport network to handle growing demand. Customers can expect stronger transport infrastructure (faster data transmission, better routing) and upgraded 4G and 5G capabilities (reduced congestion, expanded coverage). The rollout is phased site by site to minimise disruption.
