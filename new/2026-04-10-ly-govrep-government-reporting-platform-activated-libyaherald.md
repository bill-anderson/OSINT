Libya's General Information Authority (GIA) announced the activation of the Government Reporting Platform (**LY_GOVREP**) as a unified national tool for monitoring performance and measuring achievement levels within public entities. The GIA said this comes as part of the Tripoli government's efforts to enhance good governance and transparency, with follow-up from the Office of the Secretary General of the Council of Ministers.

The GIA said it commenced actual operation of the platform within its role of developing and operating government digital systems, analysing data, and issuing dashboards, supporting an integrated digital government system based on data and evidence-based decision-making.
