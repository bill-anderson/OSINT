# Entretien accordé à la RTN par le ministre Sidi Mohamed Raliou : « Nous sommes en train de construire un Data center (…) au plus tard le 30 septembre, nous allons réceptionner cet outil où toutes les données du Niger seront stockées et sécurisées »

*(Le Sahel / ONEP is the Niger state daily — government voice. Capture is not endorsement.)*

Interview of the Minister of Communication, Posts and the Digital Economy, Sidi Mohamed Raliou, given to state broadcaster RTN, reviewing the ministry's record since the 26 July 2023 events (CNSP transition).

The Minister recounts finding a "very tense" situation on taking office after 9 August 2023 — structures (RTN, ONEP, ANP, Niger Télécoms) barely able to pay salaries, telecom operators with serious quality-of-service problems, and network saturation at weekends. He describes attributing additional frequencies early to decongest traffic, saying congestion/saturation has since been resolved though quality "is not yet satisfactory."

Key stated commitments in the interview:

- **National Data Center under construction.** "Nous sommes en train de construire un Data Center national, c'est une banque de bases de données … une base de données où toutes les données de notre pays sont stockées." The Minister said he expects to take delivery of the facility **by 30 September at the latest**, where "all Niger's data will be stored and secured."
- **National digital-development policy.** The Minister — himself an IT specialist ("je suis informaticien") — said the ministry had drafted a *politique nationale de développement du numérique* with an action plan, not yet adopted; once adopted it would be rolled out. He noted that "aujourd'hui, nous ne disposons pas d'une politique en matière de numérique."
- **"Zéro papier" agenda.** Work with ANSI, ARCEP and HAPDP toward a paperless administration to reduce workload and improve efficiency.
