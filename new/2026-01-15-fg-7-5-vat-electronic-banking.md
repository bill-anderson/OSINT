The Federal Government directed banks and fintech firms to begin collecting and remitting a 7.5% value-added tax (VAT) on selected electronic banking services, effective January 19, 2026. It applies to charges on services such as mobile money transfers, USSD transactions and card issuance fees — on the service fee, not the amount transferred (e.g. a N100 transfer fee attracts 7.5% VAT on the N100). VAT is remitted to the Nigerian Revenue Service (NRS, formerly FIRS). Moniepoint clarified this is not a price increase but statutory compliance. Interest earned on savings/deposit accounts is exempt.
