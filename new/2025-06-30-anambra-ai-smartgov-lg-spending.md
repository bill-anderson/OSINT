Anambra State is using AI to tackle inflated budgets, payroll fraud and inefficient service delivery via SmartGov Suite, an AI-powered platform built in-house by the Anambra State ICT Agency (created 2019). Led by Nonso Okoye (Governor's Special Assistant on Cybersecurity), the tool is being deployed across ministries starting with the 21 local governments. It flagged a ₦2.1 billion unspent education allocation lacking disbursement records. For payroll verification, by analysing NINs, BVNs and other identifiers it detects ghost workers (found one account used by seven people). Context: NITDA DG Kashifu Inuwa told TechCabal in May that less than 10% of government operations nationwide have been digitised.
