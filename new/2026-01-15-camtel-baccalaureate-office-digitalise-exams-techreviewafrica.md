# CAMTEL, Baccalaureate Office partner to digitalise and secure Cameroon's national examinations

*Date: 2026-01-15 — By: Nana Appiah Acquaye*

Cameroon's leading network operator, CAMTEL has entered into a strategic partnership with the Cameroon Baccalaureate Office (OBC) to support the digitalisation and security of the country's national examinations, marking a significant step in the use of sovereign digital technologies to modernise public services.

The agreement was formalised on January 15, 2026, and is designed to strengthen the technological infrastructure underpinning the administration of official examinations across the country. It focuses on improving connectivity, enhancing data security, and deploying reliable digital platforms to support examination processes.

Under the partnership, CAMTEL will provide high-speed and redundant interconnection for OBC sites nationwide, ensuring operational continuity and reducing the risk of service disruptions. Sensitive examination data will be hosted and secured within Cameroon's sovereign national cloud, reinforcing data protection and national control over critical information.

The collaboration also will ensure the deployment of transparent and reliable digital platforms aimed at improving efficiency, traceability, and trust in the management of national examinations.

The Director of the Cameroon Baccalaureate Office described the partnership as a major technological advancement for the institution, noting that it is supported by CAMTEL's technical expertise and experience in delivering large-scale digital infrastructure.

With this agreement, CAMTEL further consolidates its role as a key technological partner to public administration, contributing to the sustainable digital transformation and modernization of essential state services in Cameroon.
