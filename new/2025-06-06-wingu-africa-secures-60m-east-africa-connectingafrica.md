Connecting Africa, 6 June 2025. Data-centre operator Wingu Africa raised US$60 million (from Rand Merchant Bank, RMB) to fast-track expansion across its East African markets - Djibouti, Ethiopia and Tanzania - for hyperscale, cloud and enterprise connectivity. Wingu launched a second Djibouti site (first integrated cable landing station + data centre; access to 12 operational submarine cables; AMS-IX Djibouti IX). In Ethiopia, Wingu's Tier III-certified campus hosts the ADDIX exchange, a foundation for cloud and content acceleration. CEO Anthony Voscarides framed it as "an investment in Africa's digital independence." [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
