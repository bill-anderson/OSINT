Nigeria's Federal Government commenced full rollout of its sovereign 1Government Cloud (1Gov), implemented through Galaxy Backbone Limited, to reduce reliance on foreign digital systems and drive paperless administration. Deployed across ministries including Communications, Justice, Foreign Affairs, Solid Minerals and Intergovernmental Affairs. It aims to strengthen data protection, unify inter-ministerial collaboration and cut billions in recurrent operational costs. The platform integrates GovDrive (cloud storage), GovECMS (automated workflows), GovMail (encrypted communications) and GovE-Sign (electronic signatures). Galaxy Backbone MD Professor Ibrahim Adeyanju called it "a crucial turning point in Nigeria's digital journey."
