# Malawi Slashes Spectrum Fees by 20% to Boost Digital Inclusion

MACRA has announced a **20% reduction in spectrum fees across all frequency bands**, aimed at lowering operational costs for operators, attracting new entrants and accelerating the digital economy. Director General Daud Suleman described the revision — which includes Radio Frequency Spectrum licence fees — as "leveling the playing field" toward the MW2063 vision.

The revision follows a review of the **Administrative Incentive Pricing (AIP)** model (introduced to promote efficient spectrum use); the updated pricing schedule, rolled out in 2024, accounts for location, bandwidth, frequency and market conditions. MACRA expects the revised fees to lower operational costs (freeing capital for network expansion), reduce entry barriers for startups and smaller players, and support MW2063's digital-infrastructure agenda (e-commerce, e-learning, e-health, e-governance).
