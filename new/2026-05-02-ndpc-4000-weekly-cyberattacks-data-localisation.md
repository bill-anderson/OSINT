Nigeria records over 4,000 cyberattacks weekly, prompting urgent calls for stronger data protection, local data storage and stricter compliance, NDPC National Commissioner Dr Vincent Olatunji said at the IoT West Africa 2026 conference in Lagos. He warned the scale of attacks highlights critical vulnerabilities as the digital economy expands, stressing the need for regulatory and physical infrastructure to keep pace.
