# Kenya FY2024/25 budget — State Department for Immigration, population management services (KES 6.88bn)

Kenya's FY2024/25 budget allocated KES 6.88 billion (US$53.3m, source's conversion) to the State Department for Immigration's population-management services — digital/third-generation ID cards, biometric passports, birth certificates and biometric visas, and rollout of the Unique Personal Identifier (UPI).

## Description

"Kenya allocated **KES 15.13 billion (US$117 million)** to digital ID and related projects for FY2024/25, disbursed by the State Department for Immigration."

"Of the total, KES 6.88bn ($53.3m) goes to population management — issuing 7.5m digital/third-generation ID cards, 1.5m biometric passports, 6.3m birth certificates and 2.3m biometric visas, and rolling out the **Unique Personal Identifier (UPI)** from birth" (Biometric Update, 2024-06-17).

Corroborated by Techweez (2024-06-13): "Population-management services receive **KES 6.88 billion**, funding 7.5 million (digital, third-generation) ID cards — meaning issuance of the **Unique Personal Identifier (UPI)** may finally begin — plus 1.5m e-passports, 6.3m birth certificates and 2.3m e-visas."
