## Description

AENE 2025: the 2024/25 SA Connect underspend returns as a R1.005bn rollover into the Broadband subprogramme — vote total 2,545,626 → 3,550,413 (delta exactly 1,004,787). The appropriation-side answer to the year's suspension: the money was re-voted in November 2025; per the DG's later account (TechCentral, 2026-06-18) cash became available only in January 2026, hardware arrived end-March, and the department closed the year at R3.61bn spent of R3.74bn.
