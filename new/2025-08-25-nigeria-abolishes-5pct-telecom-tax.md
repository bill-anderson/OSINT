The NCC approved the total removal of the 5% excise duty on telecommunications services under new tax legislation, aimed at lowering consumer costs and supporting industry growth. EVC Dr Aminu Maida said the fee — introduced in 2022 by the former administration and paused by President Tinubu on taking office — has now been entirely removed. Maida also announced a public network performance map (to be released in September) and quarterly performance reports, plus a joint NCC-CBN task force to standardise electronic top-up operations; audits found no systemic billing manipulation despite data-depletion complaints.
