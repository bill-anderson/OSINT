# Guinea launches GovStack Workshop to advance digital public services

Guinea has taken a significant step in its digital transformation agenda with the launch of the GovStack Workshop in Conakry, aimed at strengthening the delivery of interoperable and citizen-centric public services. The initiative, coordinated under the WARDIP project (West Africa Regional Digital Integration Program), focuses on laying the foundation for a modern digital administration capable of delivering efficient, accessible, and seamless e-services across government institutions.

Fodé Youla, Senior Advisor to the Minister at the Ministère des Postes, highlighted the importance of the workshop in driving institutional collaboration. The GovStack framework emphasizes interoperability, enabling different government systems to communicate and operate cohesively. The initiative is being supported by development partners including the World Bank.
