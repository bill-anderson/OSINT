Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 16 January 2026. UNHCR Country Representative Aissatou M. Ndiaye (to ENA) praised Fayda for easing refugees' access to services and giving a sense of inclusion. Ethiopia began issuing digital ID to refugees in 2024 with UNHCR; it hosts 1 million+ refugees/asylum seekers, mainly from four neighbouring countries. Officials say Fayda has helped refugees run formal businesses, seek medical care, enrol children in school and apply for formal employment - though the piece also flags concerns.
