# Congo: additional US$3 million government allocation to upgrade PATN connectivity sites to 4G

The Congolese government allocated an additional US$3 million of its own money — separate from the World Bank/EU-financed US$100 million PATN envelope — to upgrade rural connectivity-site technology, prioritising 4G, as stated by Minister Léon Juste Ibombo during the World Bank's mid-term review of PATN.
