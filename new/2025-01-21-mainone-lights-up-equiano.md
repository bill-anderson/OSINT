# MainOne lights up on Equiano, offering customers extra redundancy

By Staff Writer, ITWeb, Johannesburg, 21 Jan 2025

MainOne says this move adds another layer of resiliency for its enterprise customers, providing uninterrupted service at no additional cost.

MainOne, a West African data centre and connectivity services provider, today announced an extra tier of service for its customers via the Equiano submarine cable.

According to the company, this move adds another layer of resiliency for MainOne's enterprise customers, providing uninterrupted service at no additional cost.

In a statement, MainOne said after recognising the problems its customers face in guaranteeing business continuity in the event of network disruptions such as the submarine cut earlier this year, it has taken proactive actions to secure its network.

The company said by incorporating capacity from the Equiano undersea cable into its network, MainOne is providing consumers with an additional level of redundancy, ensuring reliable access at all times.

"We understand the technical complexities and cost associated with building and managing redundant network infrastructure," said Oluwasayo Oshadami, director, solutions architect at MainOne, Solutions by Equinix.

"Our customers can remain focused on the core of their business operations, not on the intricacies of managing multiple networks, fail-overs or resilience. With the integration of Equiano, we are simplifying and eliminating the stress of managing multiple cable connections for our end users. Hence, we give them the peace of mind they deserve."
