# ANSI — Startups nigériennes : le numérique au service d'une économie plus résiliente

*(Le Sahel / ONEP is the Niger state daily. Capture is not endorsement.)*

Le Sahel profiles Niger's emerging tech-startup ecosystem, backed by the **Agence Nationale pour la Société de l'Information (ANSI)**:
- **Qwiper** (Niamey) — custom development, cybersecurity, sovereign cloud, AI, WhatsApp marketing; publisher of the all-in-one "Qwiper" super-app (news, social, messaging, marketplace, jobs, AI assistant); works with the State, Niger Poste, STM Transport, NGOs.
- **E-Mobility Niger SARL** (founded 2024) — multichannel PCI-DSS/EMV payments bridging telcos, banks and card networks, with a green urban-mobility ambition.
- **CodeLoccol** — an ANSI-subsidised, inclusive digital school ("learning by doing"); its learners won the 2024 digital-week programming contest and 3rd prize at a July-2025 international robotics competition.
- Others: **ADN** (community digital training with PLAN), **Africa Ycone** (blood-donation tracking app), **Maria Server** (locally-built offline server for zones without network).
