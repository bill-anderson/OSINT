# Niger : Banque Atlantique et NITA renforcent l'interopérabilité entre services bancaires et paiement mobile

*(Communiqué de presse carried by Le Sahel / ONEP — PR-wire; primary announcement, source the underlying event at ingest.)*

Niamey, 29 June 2026 — **Banque Atlantique Niger** (subsidiary of the pan-African Groupe Banque Centrale Populaire, BCP) and **NITA** (a major mobile financial-services player in Niger) announced the launch of **"Bank to Wallet" and "Wallet to Bank"** services, enabling **instant interoperable transfers between bank accounts and electronic wallets**. Officially launched **26 June 2026 in Niamey** at a ceremony with Banque Atlantique Niger DG **Mamadou Koné** and NITA senior management.

The services are framed as advancing financial inclusion and digital-usage adaptation. Banque Atlantique is a subsidiary of Groupe BCP present across all eight WAEMU/UEMOA member states (Benin, Burkina Faso, Côte d'Ivoire, Guinea-Bissau, Mali, Niger, Senegal, Togo).
