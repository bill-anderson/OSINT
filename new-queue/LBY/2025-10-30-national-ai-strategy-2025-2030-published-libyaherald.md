Libya's **General Information Authority (GIA)** announced the public availability of the full **National Strategy for Artificial Intelligence in the State of Libya 2025–2030**. The GIA called it the "highest reference strategy of the Libyan state," a roadmap toward a knowledge economy: training young Libyan competencies in data science and AI; developing frameworks and legislation for safe, ethical AI; implementing AI in health, education and energy; and supporting university R&D.

*(GNU/Tripoli-side. Prepared with ESCWA (see 2025-10-16 workshop); the strategy notably targets a data-protection law by 2026 to fill Libya's legislative vacuum. Superseded/renamed as the 2026–2030 strategy formally adopted 2026-06-01 — see structural note. Excerpt body.)*
