Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 26 June 2026. At the MESOB Innovation Exhibition in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia showcased how digital ID, payments and cloud infrastructure are converging into a unified national platform - a "sovereignty stack" around identity, finance, data governance and infrastructure for national autonomy. MESOB now has 185 government services integrated with the super app. Ethiopia runs the second-largest digital ID project in Africa (after Nigeria), 40 million+ registered for Fayda, and is integrating further DPI (e.g. passport applications) via the foundational identity. Cites an Institute of Foreign Affairs analysis. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
