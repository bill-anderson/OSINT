TechAfrica News, 16 July 2026. Ethio Telecom is advancing plans to deepen Ethiopia's digital transformation following high-level strategic discussions with Chinese technology firm Inspur Software Technology Co., Ltd. and infrastructure company Shandong Hi-Speed Road & Bridge International Engineering, on a potential long-term partnership combining advanced digital technologies with modern infrastructure development under the "Next Horizon" strategy. Meeting hosted by CEO Frehiwot Tamiru. Extends the Ethio Telecom-Shandong data-centre thread and deepens China-Ethiopia digital dependency. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
