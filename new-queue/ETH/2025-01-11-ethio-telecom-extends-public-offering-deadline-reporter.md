By Elias Tegegn, January 11, 2025.

Ethio telecom executives announced a five-week extension of the deadline for the public offering of a 10 percent stake in the state-owned telecom giant; the offer now runs until 14 February 2025. Ethio telecom began offering equity in October 2024, putting 100 million ordinary shares up for sale at a par value of 300 birr. Prospective investors must buy a minimum of 33 shares, ceiling 3,333 shares (~1m birr); buyers pay a 1.5% service fee and 15% VAT. The shares are sold on Telebirr, Ethio telecom's mobile money platform. The offering sparked expert skepticism over whether the operator could hit its target before the original 3 January deadline.
