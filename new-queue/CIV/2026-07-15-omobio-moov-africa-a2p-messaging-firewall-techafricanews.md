# Omobio and Moov Africa Côte d'Ivoire Partner to Secure and Monetise International A2P Messaging

July 15, 2026
2 min read
Author: Editorial Team

The partnership establishes a framework for both organisations to strengthen oversight of international enterprise messaging traffic entering the network while improving commercial control, messaging security, operational visibility, and long-term monetisation opportunities.

Mobile operators have spent years investing in network infrastructure, customer acquisition, and digital services. Yet a significant portion of international enterprise messaging traffic often remains outside their direct commercial control.

Recognising the importance of building a more secure, transparent, and commercially sustainable messaging environment, Omobio, a global telecom technology and services company, and Moov Africa Côte d'Ivoire have entered into an exclusive International A2P Messaging and Firewall Solution agreement.

The partnership establishes a framework for both organisations to strengthen oversight of international enterprise messaging traffic entering the network while improving commercial control, messaging security, operational visibility, and long-term monetisation opportunities.

As enterprise messaging continues to support authentication, customer engagement, notifications, and digital services across industries, operators increasingly require greater transparency into how international traffic is delivered, managed, and commercialised. This partnership reflects a shared commitment to creating a trusted messaging ecosystem that delivers value for operators, enterprises, aggregators, carriers, and ecosystem partners alike.

"As mobile network operators continue to place their trust in our vision, capabilities, and operational expertise, we remain focused on helping them build stronger, more secure, and commercially sustainable messaging businesses. This partnership with Moov Africa Côte d'Ivoire reflects a shared commitment to creating a transparent and trusted international messaging ecosystem. We look forward to supporting their long-term objectives through our integrated technology, carrier services, and operational framework."

– Yogesh Lulla, CEO – Telecom Services, Omobio

Delivering these outcomes requires more than standalone technology. Omobio combines in-house firewall technology, messaging infrastructure, fraud intelligence, international traffic aggregation, and carrier services within a unified operational framework. This enables operators to strengthen commercial control, enhance network protection, improve operational visibility, and create sustainable value from international enterprise messaging.

The agreement marks another important step in Omobio's continued expansion across Africa and reinforces the company's commitment to enabling Connectivity, Monetisation and Security – Worldwide through long-term operator partnerships built on technology, expertise, and operational excellence.
