The Minister of Digital Transition and Technological Innovation of Côte d'Ivoire, Djibril Ouattara, has held discussions with a delegation from VISA on strengthening collaboration in key areas of the country's digital transformation agenda.

The meeting took place in Abidjan during a working visit by the VISA delegation led by Tareq Muhmood.

According to the ministry, discussions focused on several strategic priorities including digital transformation, the development of postal financial services, startup financing and support, as well as cybersecurity.

The engagement forms part of ongoing efforts by the Ivorian government to accelerate digital innovation and expand access to digital services across the country.

During the meeting, the VISA delegation expressed its commitment to supporting the ministry's initiatives aimed at advancing Côte d'Ivoire's digital transformation agenda.

The discussions also highlighted the growing role of partnerships between governments and global technology and financial service providers in driving digital inclusion, innovation ecosystems, and secure digital infrastructure across Africa.
