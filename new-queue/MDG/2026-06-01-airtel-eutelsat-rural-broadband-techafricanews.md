# Airtel Madagascar and Eutelsat Deepen Push for Rural Broadband Access

**Airtel Madagascar and Eutelsat OneWeb** are continuing to expand satellite-powered internet connectivity across Madagascar, bringing high-speed broadband to remote and underserved areas. Airtel Business is leveraging a constellation of **low Earth orbit (LEO) satellites** positioned between 160 and 2,000 km above Earth to deliver reliable, high-speed internet with low latency.

The satellite technology **integrates with existing fibre optic and 4G networks**, creating a resilient, secure connectivity ecosystem that ensures service continuity and redundancy. Beyond enterprise connectivity, the initiative aims to reduce the digital divide in rural communities by expanding access to online education, e-commerce, financial services and public information.

A notable development: **successful railway connectivity tests achieved download speeds of up to 100 Mbps aboard a moving train**, signalling potential for mobile broadband in the transport sector. Airtel Madagascar also provides installation, onboarding and dedicated technical support as part of its mission to democratise high-speed internet access.
