# UNIPOD Initiative Aims to Bring High-Speed Internet and AI to Universities in Madagascar

Under the leadership of the Prime Minister, Madagascar's Ministry of Digital Development, Posts and Telecommunications (MNDPT) and four partner ministries officially signed a **strategic cooperation agreement for the UNIPOD project** at Mahazoarivo. The initiative aims to digitalize higher education, enhancing access to quality learning and preparing young people for the evolving job market.

The MNDPT's responsibilities include:
- **Digital integration in universities**: installing high-speed internet across all universities and connecting them to the national digital platform.
- **Research and innovation**: supporting research in Big Data and Artificial Intelligence by providing access to data centres.
- **Digital business support**: creating a conducive environment for university-based startups.
- **Competence valuation**: implementing digital competence frameworks aligned with local and international standards.

The UNIPOD project involves collaboration with the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, the Ministry of Industry Development and Private Sector Development, the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock, and the Ministry of Fisheries and Blue Economy.
