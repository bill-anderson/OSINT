# AXIAN rebrands fintech arm in digital banking push across Africa

**AXIAN**, a pan-African infrastructure and services group, has rebranded its fintech cluster, formerly Axian Open Innovation & Fintech, to **AXIAN Digibank & Fintech**, marking its shift from a mobile-money operator into a **full digital banking ecosystem**.

The cluster brings together AXIAN's financial services operations, including **MVola and Mixx**, its flagship fintech products, which help individuals and businesses send money, pay merchants and access short-term credit across **Madagascar, Comoros, Tanzania, Togo and Senegal**. AXIAN says the new structure will let it build long-term financial products — credit and savings, insurance, investments and cross-border transactions.

"With AXIAN Digibank & Fintech, we are entering a new chapter, one where mobile money evolves into full digital banking," said Erwan Gelebart, CEO of AXIAN Digibank & Fintech.

The company constituted a new board including former N26 executive Georg Hauer, mobile-money pioneer Brad Jones and **former Madagascar central bank governor Henri Rabarijohn**. AXIAN says it already serves **nearly 500,000 merchants monthly** and wants to reach millions by 2030 through restocking credit, improved payment terms and cross-border settlement tools. It plans to scale longer-tenor loans, insurance, investments and eventually mortgages, using AI-driven underwriting.
