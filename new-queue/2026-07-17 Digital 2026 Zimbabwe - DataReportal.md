Note on dates: DataReportal published its Digital 2026 reports at the end of 2025; the data points below reflect the latest available data in October 2025 and are stated for late 2025.

## The "state of digital" in Zimbabwe in 2026 (headline figures)

- 16.2 million cellular mobile connections were active in Zimbabwe in late 2025, equivalent to 95.0 percent of the total population. (Some connections may only include voice and SMS.)
- 6.54 million individuals were using the internet in Zimbabwe at the end of 2025, when online penetration stood at 38.4 percent.
- 2.60 million social media user identities in October 2025, equating to 15.3 percent of the total population.

## Population

- Population of Zimbabwe stood at 17.0 million in October 2025, up 319 thousand (+1.9 percent) between the end of 2024 and the end of 2025.
- In late 2025, 32.9 percent lived in urban centres, 67.1 percent in rural areas.
- 52.3 percent female, 47.7 percent male.
- Median age 18.1 years at end of 2025.

## Mobile connections

- 16.2 million cellular mobile connections at the end of 2025 (GSMA Intelligence), equivalent to 95.0 percent of the total population in October 2025.
- Mobile connections increased by 1.1 million (+7.0 percent) between the end of 2024 and the end of 2025.
- 93.3 percent of mobile connections can now be considered "broadband" (3G, 4G or 5G).

## Internet use

- 6.54 million internet users in October 2025; internet penetration rate 38.4 percent of the total population at the end of the year.
- Number of internet users increased by 122 thousand (+1.9 percent) between October 2024 and October 2025; internet adoption rate (percentage of population using the internet) remained unchanged during that period.
- 10.5 million people (61.6 percent of the population) remained offline at the end of 2025.

## Internet connection speeds (Ookla, end of 2025)

- Median fixed internet download speed: 37.69 Mbps.
- The download speed of the typical fixed internet connection increased by 15.87 Mbps (+72.7 percent) in the twelve months to August 2025.

## Social media

- 2.60 million active social media user identities in October 2025, equivalent to 15.3 percent of Zimbabwe's total population at the end of 2025.
