# Lesotho Explores Child-Focused Digital Public Infrastructure with UNICEF Support

*Tech Africa News, 8 April 2026. By Jennifer Onyeagoro.*

UNICEF Lesotho is strengthening efforts to promote child-safe and inclusive digital transformation through a high-level Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) workshop held at the Avani Lesotho Hotel & Casino. The workshop brought together stakeholders to explore how digital systems can better support child-focused services — birth registration, education, healthcare and social protection — with emphasis on protecting children's data and ensuring vulnerable groups are not excluded.

Representing MICSTI, Kanono Ramashamole outlined the government's vision for a digitally inclusive Lesotho supported by interconnected platforms linking key public services. He said integrating systems such as birth registration, education and healthcare would enhance planning, improve service delivery and enable data-driven decision-making for children's welfare, and stressed the importance of early-stage integration.
