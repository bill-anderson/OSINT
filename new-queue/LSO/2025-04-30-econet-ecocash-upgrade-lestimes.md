# Econet enhances EcoCash system

*Lesotho Times, 30 April 2025. By Moroke Sekoboto.*

THE Sasai Econet Financial Services (SEFS), a subsidiary of Econet Telecom Lesotho, has upgraded its mobile money platform, EcoCash, to deliver a better, faster and safer experience and to build a platform capable of supporting innovation and long-term growth.

SEFS General Manager Moeketsi Mafereka said the company migrated EcoCash to a new system in October 2024. Speaking at a press conference at Lancers Inn, he described the migration as one of the most significant upgrades in EcoCash's history, acknowledging it had at times caused considerable inconvenience but was now very stable, and apologised for the disruption.

Improvements include the integration of Mokhatlo services (for stokvels, savings clubs and burial societies) into the EcoCash App, allowing members to pay contributions directly and monitor transactions in real time. Enhanced payroll lets businesses process salaries via EcoCash. Mafereka cited improved fraud detection and stronger authentication, and an upgraded self-service portal for business clients. He also announced a forthcoming feature: interest payouts to EcoCash wallets for eligible customers.
