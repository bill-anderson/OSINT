# MTN Digital Infrastructure Secures 15-Year License to Expand Fibre Network in South Sudan

*TechAfrica News, 25 November 2025 (Jennifer Onyeagoro)*

MTN Digital Infrastructure's South Sudan subsidiary, Bayobab Infra Solutions Ltd, has secured a 15-year license from the National Communications Authority of South Sudan to construct, install, and operate electronic communications systems, effective 16 October 2025.

The license empowers MTN Infra Solutions to deploy and manage national and cross-border fibre networks. It establishes terrestrial and satellite systems, provides national and international leased circuits, delivers IP transit services, and extends high-capacity fibre connectivity across South Sudan.

The South Sudan license is a pivotal step in the expansion of Project East 2 West (E2W), which aims to create a resilient digital corridor connecting East and West Africa. Project E2W is supported by key partners including Africa50. MTN Digital Infrastructure's entry supports the country's ambitions to modernise telecommunications and integrate more fully into the regional and global digital economy. (Related coverage notes E2W is a US$320m initiative launched 2023 and that this is the second South Sudan licence to an MTN company in 2025, after MTN South Sudan's new 15-year mobile operator licence.)
