# MTN Cameroon engages regulator on strategy, investment and sector priorities

*Date: 2026-04-13 — By: Nana Appiah Acquaye*

MTN Cameroon has hosted a high-level delegation from the Telecommunications Regulatory Agency Cameroon as part of ongoing engagement between the operator and the country's telecom regulator.

The delegation was led by Director General, Philemon Zoo Zame and focused on reviewing the company's performance and strategic direction.

During the meeting, MTN Cameroon presented an overview of its key achievements for 2025, alongside its strategic priorities and investment plans for the current year. Discussions also addressed broader sector issues, with both parties seeking to strengthen alignment on regulatory and industry developments.

The engagement forms part of continued efforts to enhance collaboration between operators and regulators in Cameroon's telecommunications sector.

MTN Cameroon reaffirmed its commitment to advancing digital inclusion, driving innovation, and contributing to socio-economic development, in line with the country's broader digital transformation agenda.
