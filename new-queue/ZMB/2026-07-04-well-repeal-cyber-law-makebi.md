NRPUP presidential running mate Makebi Zulu says if elected, his government will repeal the Cyber Crimes and Cyber Security Act, as it has gagged Zambians from expressing their views. Speaking at a rally in Mpulungu, Friday, Zulu said residents should have freedom to communicate on issues such as not having enough fertilizer in their area. He said Zambians have been "gagged" through the enactment of the cybercrime law. Meanwhile, Zulu says the UPND cancelled all the projects that were started by late former presidents Michael Sata and Edgar Lungu.
