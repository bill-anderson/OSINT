The Law Association of Zambia (LAZ) signalled it would mount a legal challenge against the newly enacted cyber laws (Cyber Security Act No. 3 of 2025 and Cyber Crimes Act No. 4 of 2025), warning that the surveillance and interception provisions represent a democratic backslide. Part of the contentious surveillance / free-expression thread around Zambia's 2025 cyber legislation signed by President Hichilema on 8 April 2025.
