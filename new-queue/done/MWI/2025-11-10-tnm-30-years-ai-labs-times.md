# TNM turns 30, sets ambitious plans

Telekom Networks Malawi (TNM) commemorated **30 years** as a pioneer of Malawi's digital transformation. CEO Michel Hebert said TNM covers **85 percent of the population** and is working to reach the remaining 15% through cost-effective technologies such as **solar-powered infrastructure**. He announced plans to establish **artificial intelligence (AI) laboratories** and innovation hubs "to empower Malawians to create AI-driven services tailored to our needs." Hebert cited **foreign-exchange shortages** as TNM's biggest challenge, affecting payments to international and equipment suppliers.

ICT Minister Shadric Namalomba praised TNM's role in financial inclusion (via Mpamba), sports sponsorship (TNM Super League) and CSR, and urged alignment with the government's Malawi 2063 digital-economy agenda (inclusive growth, smart cities, last-mile connectivity).
