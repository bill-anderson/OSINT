# Malawi Moves to Expand Rural Connectivity with 90 New Telecom Towers

MACRA has announced progress in expanding digital connectivity through the **Digital Malawi Acceleration Project (DMAP)**, as plans advance to procure service providers for the rollout of **more than 90 new communication towers** nationwide. The development was highlighted at a consultative meeting in Blantyre engaging mobile network operators, ISPs, satellite companies and other stakeholders.

Supported by the World Bank, the initiative addresses connectivity gaps in underserved and rural areas. A study cited during the meeting revealed **Malawi requires more than 1,000 communication towers** to achieve nationwide coverage — underscoring the infrastructure deficit. MACRA Director General Daud Suleman said while DMAP is committed to deploying around 100 towers, the regulator will continue allocating funds from its annual budget to the **Universal Service Fund** for further expansion, acknowledging existing funding remains insufficient.
