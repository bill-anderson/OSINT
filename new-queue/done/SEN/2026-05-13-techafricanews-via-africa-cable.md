# Via Africa Project to Launch New Subsea Cable Strengthening Europe-Africa Connectivity

Tech Africa News - 13 May 2026, by Jennifer Onyeagoro

Investors including Canalink, GUILAB, International Mauritania Telecom, Orange Group, Orange Cote d'Ivoire, Sonatel and Silverlinks signed a Memorandum of Understanding to initiate the Via Africa submarine cable project. The new system aims to connect Europe to South Africa - with landing points in the UK, France and Portugal - and destinations along the Atlantic coastline including the Canary Islands, Mauritania, Senegal, Guinea, Cote d'Ivoire and Nigeria, with extensions further south for connectivity diversity and resilience.

The Via Africa system is managed as an open consortium, enabling partners seeking autonomy and sovereignty to co-invest and take part in governance. In the initial phase, members will jointly finance a cable route study and prepare the procurement process for a cable supplier. The project is explicitly positioned as a resilience/diversity play against West Africa's history of simultaneous subsea disruptions.
