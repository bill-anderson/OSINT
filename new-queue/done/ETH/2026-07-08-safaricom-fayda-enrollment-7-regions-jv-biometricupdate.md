Biometric Update (Chris Burt), 8 July 2026. Safaricom Ethiopia signed a Joint Venture Agreement with the National ID Program (NIDP) to take over biometric Fayda enrollment for residents in seven regions - "Lots 3 and 4" of the National Fayda Digital ID Registration Project - expanding the private sector's role (Safaricom is Ethio Telecom's only competitor; Ethio Telecom was an early registration partner). Enrollment captures fingerprint, face and iris biometrics. NIDP Executive Director Yodahe Zemichael said Ethiopia has reached 46.5 million Fayda registrations, up from 16.4 million as of 4 June [2025].
