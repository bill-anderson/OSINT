TechAfrica News, 20 April 2026. At the EU-Ethiopia Business Forum in Addis Ababa, Safaricom Ethiopia highlighted the scale of investment required to support Ethiopia's expanding telecom sector, pointing to a large, youthful population (130m+, ~10,000 births daily) as a driver of future digital demand and one of Africa's fastest-growing consumer markets. It tied the opportunity to ongoing reforms and Digital Ethiopia momentum (~$5bn investment need cited). [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
