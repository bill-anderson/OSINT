Biometric Update (Ayang Macdonald), 11 November 2025. The Ethiopian Bankers Association (EBA) distributed 2,000 biometric enrollment kits to 31 banks (ceremony at Commercial Bank of Ethiopia HQ) to support NIDP's Fayda expansion; 6,000 kits total planned (remaining 4,000 under a re-issued tender), cost ~$17 million - each portable kit holds a facial-recognition camera, fingerprint and iris scanners, laptop and lighting. Aligns with the start-of-year government directive making Fayda a requirement for all banking operations (phased, 31 December deadline). [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
