Connecting Africa, 2 December 2025. Safaricom Ethiopia's mobile-money service M-Pesa launched M-Pesa LeHulum, a fully telco-agnostic platform (developed in-house) that works with a SIM from any network, removing long-standing network barriers. Users register with any phone number and verify via the Fayda Digital ID to begin transacting within minutes - send/receive money, pay merchants and bills, buy airtime, QR payments and short-term loans; transfers to banks and wallets via ETHSwitch integration. Follows an Awash Bank overdraft-service agreement five months earlier. M-Pesa went live in Ethiopia in August 2023. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
