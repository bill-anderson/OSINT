TechAfrica News, 14 July 2026. Raxio Group surpassed US$380 million in committed capital as shareholders Meridiam and Roha increased support following a sixfold surge in contracted capacity this year. The additional equity extends Raxio's capital base from a previous US$350 million, building on a US$100 million IFC (World Bank Group) financing package secured last year, plus debt funding from Proparco and the Emerging Africa & Asia Infrastructure Fund (EAAIF). Raxio operates a flagship Tier III data centre in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia (and facilities across several African markets). [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.
