Biometric Update (Lu-Hai Liang), 7 April 2026. Dashen Bank introduced a digital onboarding system allowing bank accounts to be opened remotely using Fayda - the first commercial bank in Ethiopia to link account opening directly to the national biometric identity database. Customers create accounts with only a mobile number and Fayda credentials (no in-person branch visit), streamlining access for unbanked populations. Paired with a Mastercard-enabled virtual card for international online payments/remittances. Launched at Addis Ababa University's School of Commerce. Fayda registrations at 40 million; MOSIP acknowledged the milestone. [Excerpt captured at sweep; full clip on promotion.]
