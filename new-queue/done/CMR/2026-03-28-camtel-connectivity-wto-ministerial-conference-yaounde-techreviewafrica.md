# Camtel provides connectivity infrastructure for WTO Ministerial Conference in Yaoundé

*Date: 2026-03-28 — By: Nana Appiah Acquaye*

Cameroon Telecommunications is providing digital connectivity services for participants at the 14th Ministerial Conference of the World Trade Organization, currently taking place in Cameroon's capital.

At the Yaoundé Convention Centre, the national telecommunications operator has deployed a dedicated infrastructure, including a secure and high-capacity WiFi network to support seamless communication throughout the event.

As part of its service offering, Camtel introduced a tailored package known as the "WTO blue fleet" for foreign delegations. The package includes unlimited calls and text messaging within the network, communication credit valued at 50,000 CFA francs, and 10 gigabytes of data per line.

Camtel teams are expected to remain on-site until March 29, 2026, providing technical support and ensuring uninterrupted connectivity for participants.

Officials say the initiative underscores Camtel's capacity to support major international events and reinforces Cameroon's positioning as an emerging hub in the global digital economy.
