# Mauritius Telecom Expands Fibre, AI, and Financial Services Across Region

*TechAfrica News, 8 December 2025.*

At the **Mauritius Telecom Connect event on 5 December 2025**, MT (CEO Veemal Gungadin) unveiled its strategy to position Mauritius as a digital hub linking Africa and Asia, structured around **four corridors**:
- **Connectivity Corridor** — expanding fibre, deploying **5G-Advanced**, and new submarine cables (**T4 and SEACOM 2.0**, complementing SAFE/LION/T3).
- **AI/Compute Corridor** — GPU clusters, Tier IV data-centre expansion, sovereign cloud.
- **Innovation & Scale-Up Corridor** — startups ("Build in Mauritius, Scale across continents").
- **Financial Corridor** — scaling **my.t money** (130,000+ active users) into a cross-border payment solution.

Reinforced by cybersecurity, digital-skills programmes, greener infrastructure and the ambition of "AI for every Mauritian." Reported elsewhere as a **three-year Rs 20 billion (~US$434 million)** investment programme. *(Trade-journal record of the MT Connect launch; the newspaper account is lexpress node/553579.)*
