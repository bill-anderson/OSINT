# Sublime Seas to Storage Sphere: Mauritius Expands Hyperscale Data Hub

*Telecom Review Africa (feature), 16 July 2025.*

Feature on Mauritius positioning as an Indian Ocean data and connectivity hub under the **Digital Mauritius 2030** strategy, via hyperscale data centres (Tier III/IV) and advanced connectivity.

**Data-centre inventory (as of mid-2025):** 10 operating data centres across five locations (Port Louis, Reduit, Arsenal, Quatre Bornes, Rose Belle), including:
- **Port Louis:** Aphelion DC3; Rogers Capital Port Louis; Harel Mallac Technologies (Tier III); Rogers Capital La Tour Koenig (disaster recovery); Rogers Capital Les Cascades (HA, 50-50 load balancing).
- **Reduit:** Bhumishq Data Center (Tier IV-designed, carrier-neutral); Rogers Capital Ebene Data Center (hosts the extended **Mauritius Internet Exchange Point / MIXP**).
- **Arsenal:** Emtel Data Center (flood-free).
- **Quatre Bornes:** BIRGER Candos Recovery Center.
- **Rose Belle:** 1,500 m² secured rack space, 3-MW power.

Connectivity: linked to **SAFE, LION/LION2, and METISS** cables; the new **T4** cable will replace SAFE (retiring ~2027). Reports total internet subscriptions ~1.01 million (Jan 2025); Mauritius 3rd in Africa on ITU ICT development; Tier 1 cybersecurity rating; participates in Smart Africa Alliance.
