The Ministry of Communication, Technology and Innovation (MoCTI) announced plans to develop Sierra Leone's first-ever National Artificial Intelligence (AI) Strategy in collaboration with the World Bank Group and other partners.

As part of the process, the ministry produced a Draft Sierra Leone AI Readiness Diagnostic Report assessing the country's preparedness to implement AI in a responsible, inclusive, and citizen-focused manner. The report evaluates institutional, technological, and social readiness.

To support strategy development, the ministry convened a three-day stakeholder engagement bringing together media organisations, civil society, government institutions, and the private sector. The consultations aim to ensure the National AI Strategy reflects the realities, priorities, and voices of Sierra Leone's people, including local languages and cultural contexts. The strategy will serve as a foundation for guiding the ethical and inclusive use of AI.
