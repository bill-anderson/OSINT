# Cybercriminals Attack Seychelles — Offshore Banking as a Target | 29 July 2025
Resecurity (cybersecurity firm — first-hand analysis of the Seychelles Commercial Bank breach)

Analysis by Resecurity, the Los Angeles-based firm that engaged directly with the threat actor "ByteToBreach" in the Seychelles Commercial Bank (SCB) incident. Resecurity reviewed a sample of the stolen dataset, reporting it appears to contain numerous customer names, email addresses, phone numbers, account types and account balances, with multiple entries labelled "current accounts - government." The piece frames the SCB compromise within the broader targeting of offshore/tax-haven banking jurisdictions.
