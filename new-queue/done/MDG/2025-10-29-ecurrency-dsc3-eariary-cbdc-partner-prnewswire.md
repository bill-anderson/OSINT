# eCurrency CBDC technology in Madagascar for eAriary Project at Banky Foiben'i Madagasikara (BFM)

*(PR-wire item — primary vendor announcement; verify against BFM at ingest.)*

**eCurrency Mint** is set to begin the pilot phase of the **eAriary project** in Madagascar. The **eCurrency DSC3 technology** was selected for its ability to integrate into Madagascar's existing financial ecosystem **in partnership with PayLogic SA**. The solution will be piloted to align with the specific requirements of **BFM, the central bank of Madagascar**.

The solution enables secure and efficient transactions through a **post-quantum digital bearer instrument**, and the eAriary CBDC operates as a true general-purpose digital bearer instrument for a wide range of use cases.

"We are truly honored to be selected as the preferred technology partner for Madagascar's CBDC initiative," said Jonathan Dharmapalan, CEO, eCurrency.

**BFM will preserve its exclusive authority as the sole issuer** of the national currency, while distribution to the public will be handled by private sector entities, including commercial banks, e-money providers, microfinance institutions and fintech companies.

eCurrency uses Digital Symmetric Core Currency Cryptography (DSC3) technology to enable central banks to issue digital fiat currency as a digital bearer instrument alongside notes and coins.
