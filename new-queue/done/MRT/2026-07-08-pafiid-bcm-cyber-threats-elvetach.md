# Mauritania — 10,000+ cyber threats to the digital financial system, H1 2026 (BCM AI platform)

*Off-assigned-host capture (Elvetach, Arabic). Body in Arabic; English gloss follows. Corroborated by lakhbar.com (same date). Rare operational cybersecurity data point.*

The **Innovative, Inclusive and Sustainable Finance Support Programme (PAFIID)** reported detecting **more than 10,000 electronic security threats** against Mauritania's digital financial system in the **first half of 2026**, of which **470 were confirmed and removed**.

The programme explained that the **Central Bank of Mauritania (BCM) uses an AI-supported "digital reputation protection" platform** to monitor cyberattacks — including phishing, identity impersonation, and fraud via websites and apps. The platform has helped protect **20 digital identities**, including those of the central bank, PAFIID and national mobile-banking services, to strengthen trust in digital financial services.
