In March 2026 the CBN issued Baseline Standards for Automated Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Solutions, a major shift from largely manual compliance to technology-driven monitoring. All financial institutions must implement automated systems capable of detecting and preventing money laundering, terrorism financing and proliferation financing (ML/TF/PF), emphasising demonstrable effectiveness over feature-based compliance and requiring proactive real-time identification of suspicious activity. Institutions must begin implementation immediately and submit an implementation roadmap to the CBN Compliance Department within three months.
