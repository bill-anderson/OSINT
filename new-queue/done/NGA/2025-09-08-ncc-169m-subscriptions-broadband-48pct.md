The NCC published industry statistics as of July 2025. Active telephony subscriptions were 169.3 million in July 2025, down from 171.7 million in June, with teledensity at 78.11% (down from 79.22%). The Commission linked the decline to enforcement of SIM-NIN linkage regulations removing unverified SIM cards, plus rectification of discrepancies by a mobile network operator. Teledensity is calculated on a projected national population of 216 million (since September 2023). MTN remains the dominant operator by market share. Broadband penetration was reported at 48%.
