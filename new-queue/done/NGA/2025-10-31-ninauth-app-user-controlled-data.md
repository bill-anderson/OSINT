President Tinubu officially unveiled the NIMC NINAuth Application, placing data control directly in the hands of Nigerian citizens (part of the Renewed Hope Agenda). The app gives individuals full authority over personal data linked to the NIN — users can grant, revoke or review access permissions, ensuring privacy, security and transparency in how information is shared. For businesses it introduces a secure, efficient identity-verification mechanism, fostering trust and regulatory compliance. (Formal citizen-facing launch of NINAuth, first introduced May 2025.)
