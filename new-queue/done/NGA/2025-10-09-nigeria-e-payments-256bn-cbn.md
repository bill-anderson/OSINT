Nigeria's electronic payment transactions reached $256 billion by July 2025, up from $187 billion in August 2024, per the CBN. Volume increased from 3.9 billion transactions in 2024 to 4.12 billion by July 2025. CBN Governor Olayemi Cardoso (represented by Opemi Yusuf) announced this at Nigeria Fintech Week 2025 in Lagos: "Over the past year, we've witnessed a remarkable adoption of digital channels, with transaction values rising from $187 billion to $256 billion." The CBN is enhancing integrity/security standards, cyber security frameworks and fraud detection, working with the Nigeria Electronic Fraud Forum and law enforcement.
