Nigeria's active mobile subscriptions rose to 175,322,398 in October 2025 (teledensity 80.87%), per NCC data, up from 173.5m in September and 171.6m in August. Broadband penetration rose to 49.89% in October (from 49.34% September, 48.81% August). Operator market shares (October): MTN Nigeria 91,077,821 lines (52.02%); Airtel Nigeria 59,039,563 (33.72%); Globacom 21,833,946 (12.47%); 9mobile/T2 3,131,570 (1.79%). Analysts attributed growth to sustained demand despite infrastructure gaps, high operating costs and unreliable power.
