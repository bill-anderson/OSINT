President Tinubu directed the Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC) to investigate major global technology companies and Generative AI platforms over allegations of anti-competitive practices and unlawful exploitation of Nigerian media content. The directive, issued July 6, 2026, follows a joint petition by the Nigerian Press Organisation (NPO) — comprising the Newspaper Proprietors' Association (NPAN), Nigeria Union of Journalists (NUJ), Nigerian Guild of Editors (NGE) and broadcasting bodies. The Commission said the investigation addresses growing concerns over the impact of global digital platforms on Nigeria's media industry and the sustainability of local news organisations.
