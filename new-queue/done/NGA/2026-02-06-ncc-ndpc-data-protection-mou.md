The NCC and NDPC signed an MoU to strengthen enforcement of data privacy and protection in the telecommunications industry, providing a structured framework for regulatory synergy. NCC EVC Dr Aminu Maida said effective regulation of data within the sector is critical to sustaining trust, innovation and growth as Nigeria transitions deeper into the digital and AI era, noting Nigeria has evolved from about 500,000 connected telephone lines to over 170 million active connections.
