Google announced a $37 million commitment to strengthen AI research, education, infrastructure and innovation across Africa (announced July 24 in Accra, Ghana; continental scope, widely covered in Nigerian tech press). Includes the launch of an AI Community Center in Accra; $25 million toward the AI Collaborative: Food Security; and $3 million to the Masakhane African Languages AI Hub (building datasets, translation and voice tech for more than 40 African languages). Google framed it as ensuring African languages, cultures and contexts are represented in AI, reducing reliance on foreign tech systems.
