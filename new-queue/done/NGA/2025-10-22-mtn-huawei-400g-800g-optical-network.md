MTN Nigeria and Huawei launched Nigeria's first high-rate 400G/800G Hybrid Automatically Switched Optical Network (ASON) in Lagos (in June 2025), now live in the Lagos district — marking Nigeria's entry into a new era of ultra-broadband and high reliability. MTN said the higher-capacity optical channels enable it to meet growing capacity demands and improve wireless, home broadband and enterprise services, reducing cost per bit while boosting service reliability.
