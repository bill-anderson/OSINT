The Nigerian government is preparing to launch the Nigerian Data Exchange Platform (NGDX), digital infrastructure to centralise and secure data sharing between government agencies and businesses, expected operational by end of 2025. Discussed at an August 25 workshop in Abuja with international partners including the EU, Finland and Estonia. Minister Bosun Tijani said NGDX will facilitate secure data sharing, stimulate innovation and inclusive growth, and prevent citizens from repeatedly entering the same information for different government services: "We've seen how interoperability transformed financial services through NIBSS. NGDX will extend that power across our entire economy." Nigeria aims for ICT to contribute 22% of GDP by 2027.
