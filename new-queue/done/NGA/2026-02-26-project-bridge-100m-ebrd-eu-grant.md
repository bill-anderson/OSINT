Project BRIDGE, Nigeria's national digital infrastructure initiative, received a $100 million investment from the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD), alongside a €22 million grant from the European Union. This follows the World Bank's $500 million financing, supporting deployment of 90,000km of fibre nationwide. The EU's Digital Economy Package contributes €45 million total: €22 million earmarked for Project BRIDGE, €18 million for Digital Public Services, and €5 million for the 3MTT programme.
