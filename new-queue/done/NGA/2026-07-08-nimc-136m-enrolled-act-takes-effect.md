NIMC announced that more than 136 million Nigerians and legal residents have been enrolled into the National Identity Database (NIDB). DG Engr Abisoye Coker-Odusote disclosed this during stakeholder engagement on implementation of the newly enacted NIMC Act 2026 (which repeals the 2007 Act). The act reinforces the NIN as the country's single foundational identity under a "one person, one identity" policy, designates NIMC as the root certificate authority for Nigeria's digital identity infrastructure, and strengthens data protection, cybersecurity and secure digital credentials.
