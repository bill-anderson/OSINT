NIMC assumed responsibility as Nigeria's designated certification authority for electronic signatures, expected to strengthen the security of digital transactions. DG Engr Abisoye Coker-Odusote said recent legislation (the NIMC Act 2026) expanded the commission's mandate beyond identity management to include digital trust services and authentication. The role provides the legal and technical framework for trusted electronic signatures, reduces identity fraud, strengthens cybersecurity and improves confidence in digital transactions across public and private sectors. She described digital identity as the backbone of digital governance.
