The IFC announced a $150 million loan to two of Airtel Africa's subsidiaries to help expand and upgrade mobile networks over the coming years, broadening access to high-speed data connectivity in underserved communities. (Continental Airtel Africa financing covered in Nigerian trade press.) By strengthening network capacity and widening coverage, the partnership builds on a longstanding IFC–Airtel collaboration to expand access to connectivity and support job creation.
