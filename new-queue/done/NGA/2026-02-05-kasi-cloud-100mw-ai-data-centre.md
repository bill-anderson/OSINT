Kasi Cloud is building Nigeria's first data centre campus purpose-built for AI — a 42-hectare site facing the Atlantic along the Lagos–Calabar coastal road. Nigeria has about 17 operational data centres, none with capacity higher than 20MW; hyperscale AI campuses target 50-100MW+ due to dense GPU racks (50-150 kW per rack). Founder/CEO Johnson Agogbua (site tour January 25, 2026): "This is not a retrofit. This was designed for AI from day one." Kasi Cloud broke ground on the $250 million hyperscale data centre in Lekki in April 2022; construction began Q2 2023.
