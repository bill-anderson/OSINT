Nigeria's internet penetration hit a record 53% in January 2026, per NCC data, driven by the four largest operators. Airtel Nigeria recorded the biggest monthly gain, adding over 1.5 million internet subscribers between December 2025 and January 2026 (its largest single-month increase since April 2016); its base rose to 53.5% (from 52% in December), ~10.5% YoY growth. Airtel added 2,242 new network sites in 2025 (+15.5%, to nearly 16,711), expanded its fibre backbone by 25%, and more than doubled active 5G sites across the top 20 cities. MTN Nigeria added 1.2 million subscribers to reach 81.1 million in January (10% YoY). Nigeria still missed its 70% broadband target in 2025.
