NITDA officially took over the Nigeria Government Enterprise Architecture (NGEA) infrastructure at a handover ceremony in Abuja, marking a milestone in digital governance. The transition follows a strategic collaboration between NITDA and the Korea International Cooperation Agency (KOICA), which helped develop and implement the NGEA framework — a core component of Nigeria's e-Government strategy providing structured, integrated technology systems for government.
