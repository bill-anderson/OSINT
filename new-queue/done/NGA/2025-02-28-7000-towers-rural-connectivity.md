# Nigeria to Build 7,000 Telecom Towers to Boost Rural Connectivity

February 28, 2025 — Aayushya Ranjan, TechAfrica News

Nigeria plans 7,000 telecom towers to expand rural connectivity, enhance digital inclusion, boost economic growth, and strengthen nationwide telecommunications infrastructure.

Multiple reports have confirmed that the Federal Government has unveiled plans to construct 7,000 communication towers in rural areas.

The initiative aims to bridge the digital divide by improving connectivity in underserved regions, fostering economic growth, and expanding access to digital services.
