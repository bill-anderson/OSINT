The Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) launched an investigation into an alleged data breach involving Remita Payment Services Ltd, Sterling Bank and other related entities. Per a press release dated April 5, 2026, a Notice of Investigation was issued on April 1, 2026; relevant parties have begun providing information. The investigation forms part of NDPC's mandate to enforce compliance with data protection regulations and strengthen trust in the digital and financial services sector, ensuring data subjects are protected through appropriate technical and organisational measures.
