# Somalia Advances Digital Governance with Approval of Public Data Protection Regulation

By Jennifer Onyeagoro — TechAfrica News, January 8, 2026

An extraordinary meeting of the Council of Ministers of the Federal Government of Somalia, chaired by President Hassan Sheikh Mohamud and attended by PM Hamza Abdi Barre, approved by majority vote the Regulatory Framework for the Public Data Protection Law. The framework introduces essential provisions required for effective implementation of the Public Data Protection Law and aligns Somalia's legal framework with its digital transformation agenda.

The Ministry of Communications and Technology — which drafted the regulatory framework — confirmed the regulation underwent all constitutional, legal and technical procedures mandated by national law prior to approval. Minister of Communications and Technology Mohamed Somali said approval marks a critical step toward building public trust in government digital services. The adoption is expected to play a pivotal role in ensuring data privacy, strengthening information security and reinforcing good governance.
