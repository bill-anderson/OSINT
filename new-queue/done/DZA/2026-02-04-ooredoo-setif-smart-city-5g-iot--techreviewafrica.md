# Ooredoo Algeria launches smart city pilot in Setif with 5G and IoT solutions

Date: 2026-02-04

By: Nana Appiah Acquaye

Ooredoo Algeria has partnered with DEO Electronique to launch the smart city "Ganzat" in the upper province of Setif, marking a pioneering step in the gradual rollout of 5G technology in the country.

The pilot project leverages Internet of Things (IoT) solutions to enable intelligent management of equipment and infrastructure, improve energy efficiency, and enhance the quality of public services. By integrating these technologies, the initiative aims to create a model for more connected, efficient, and sustainable local communities.

The Ganzat project represents a key milestone in Algeria's digital transformation strategy, demonstrating how 5G and IoT can be applied to urban planning and public service delivery to support smarter, more resilient cities.
