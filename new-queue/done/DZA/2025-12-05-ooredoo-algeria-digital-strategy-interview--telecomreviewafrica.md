# Leading Algeria's Digital Leap: Ooredoo's Strategic Vision in the Era of Digital Transformation

In an exclusive interview with Telecom Review Africa, Chief Marketing Officer of Ooredoo Algérie, Isabelle Hajri, revealed how the company is driving Algeria's digital future by merging cutting-edge technologies with a human-centered customer experience, championing digital inclusion, empowering youth and startups, and turning innovation into a catalyst for sustainable national growth.

**How is 5G reshaping customer experiences and unlocking innovative digital services that drive both business growth and national economic development?**

At Ooredoo Algeria, we view 5G not merely as the next step in mobile technology, but as a true catalyst for digital transformation across the nation. 5G will redefine connectivity, enabling new opportunities in smart cities, connected industries, e-health, education, and entertainment.

Our vision is perfectly aligned with Algeria's digital transformation roadmap and the government's ambitions to modernize the national telecommunications infrastructure. Beyond deploying cutting-edge technology, our priority is to create a sustainable ecosystem that empowers innovation, supports startups, and drives economic growth.

Ooredoo Algeria is actively investing in network readiness and capacity-building initiatives. Our recent partnerships with leading Algerian universities are a strong example, as they foster research, innovation, and the development of digital skills among young talents. Through such collaborations, we aim to make 5G a driving force for the country's digital and socio-economic progress.

**How are data, personalization, and advanced digital tools transforming the way companies deliver unified, seamless, and customer-centric experiences?**

Customer experience is at the very core of Ooredoo Algeria's marketing strategy. In today's fast-evolving digital landscape, customers expect more than connectivity; they expect personalized, seamless, and human-centered experiences.

To deliver on that expectation, we leverage data intelligence and advanced digital tools to understand our customers' preferences and anticipate their needs. Our My Ooredoo App, for instance, is a key enabler of this vision, allowing customers to manage their services effortlessly, access exclusive offers, and interact with the brand in a fluid and intuitive way.

What truly sets us apart, however, is the emotional connection we maintain with our customers. Ooredoo's communication has always reflected values that resonate deeply with Algerians: youth, solidarity, and innovation.

For more than two decades, we have built a trusted relationship with our customers, positioning Ooredoo not only as a telecom provider, but as a daily partner in their digital lives.

**In what ways can a well-executed omnichannel strategy enhance brand perception, ensure message consistency, and deepen customer trust across all digital touchpoints?**

Digital transformation has been at the center of our brand modernization journey. Ooredoo Algeria has evolved from a traditional telecom operator into a digital lifestyle brand, one that enables people and businesses to thrive in a connected world.

Our approach is guided by an omnichannel strategy that ensures message consistency and coherence across all touchpoints, from digital platforms and social media to retail and customer care. This unified experience strengthens our identity as a modern, innovative, and customer-focused brand.

Internally, we have embraced digitalization as a key driver of performance and collaboration. We have developed a digital workplace environment that encourages agility, innovation, and data-driven decision-making, aligning our internal culture with our external promise of being a digital-first brand.

Ultimately, digitalization allows us to remain close to our customers, listen to them, and evolve with them, reinforcing Ooredoo's reputation as a forward-looking, responsible, and relevant brand in today's digital age.

**How is artificial intelligence redefining customer engagement and empowering marketing teams to boost efficiency, agility, and overall performance?**

Artificial intelligence plays a pivotal role in Ooredoo Algeria's marketing and customer experience strategies. We are using AI to make our interactions smarter, faster, and more personalized, ensuring that every customer feels understood and valued.

Through predictive analytics and machine learning, we can better anticipate customer needs, tailor offers in real time, and enhance satisfaction. Our AI-powered virtual assistants and chatbots provide 24/7 customer support, helping us deliver efficient service while maintaining the human touch that defines our brand.

AI also contributes to optimizing our marketing effectiveness by allowing us to measure impact, improve targeting, and refine our communication strategies.

At Ooredoo, we believe that technology should empower, not replace human connection. AI is, therefore, an enabler of empathy and excellence, ensuring that our customers' journey is both intelligent and meaningful.

**How is Ooredoo promoting entrepreneurship and innovation?**

Entrepreneurship and innovation are deeply embedded in Ooredoo Algeria's purpose and corporate identity. We firmly believe that fostering local talent and supporting startups is essential to building a sustainable digital economy.

This belief drives our flagship initiatives such as T-Start, our incubator program that provides young entrepreneurs with mentorship, access to technology, and business development support. We are also proud of our Machrou3i initiative, which empowers women entrepreneurs across Algeria by helping them transform their ideas into viable projects.

Additionally, our partnership with the TV show Ma3i fi Machrou3i, which represents the Algerian version of Shark Tank, reflects our continued commitment to inspiring and mentoring youth with creative and impactful business ideas. Through these programs, Ooredoo plays an active role in cultivating a culture of innovation, resilience, and ambition.

For us, entrepreneurship is not only about creating startups; it's about shaping a generation of digital leaders who will drive Algeria's future growth. As a responsible corporate citizen, Ooredoo is proud to be a trusted partner in that journey, contributing to the nation's economic and digital development.
