The Makerere Innovation and Incubation Center (MIIC), in partnership with Afriventia Group, has unveiled Fintech Fridays Uganda Edition, a platform to accelerate innovation, investment and collaboration within Uganda's growing fintech ecosystem. It aims to bring together fintech startups, investors, regulators, financial institutions, development partners and policymakers to showcase solutions and unlock investment.

Speaking at the launch (Four Points by Sheraton), Diana Nannono Mugagga (Head of the Private Sector Development Unit, MoFPED; MIIC board member) said a regular forum will help position Uganda as a competitive regional fintech destination. The initiative comes as Uganda's digital economy expands via smartphone penetration, mobile money adoption and digital payments. Sessions will cover digital payments, AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, financial inclusion, regulatory innovation and digital lending.
