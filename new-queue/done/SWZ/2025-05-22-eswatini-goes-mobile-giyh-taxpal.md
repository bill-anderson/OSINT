Eswatini launches GIYH and TaxPal apps, revolutionizing access to government and tax services with convenience, dignity, and digital inclusion.

Minister of ICT Savannah Maziya has officially launched the Government In Your Hand Mobile Application, bringing essential public services directly into the hands of emaSwati. This launch was held in collaboration with the Eswatini Revenue Service (ERS), who also launched the ERS TaxPal App, a digital solution to revolutionise tax and customs services.

Minister of ICT Savannah Maziya said that they are restoring the dignity of emaSwati by collapsing barriers of time, distance, and cost, and bringing Government services to the hands of the people. "This is not just a mobile App — it is a transformation of how the Government serves. This App is a game-changer. It brings Government closer. It saves time. It cuts costs. It removes barriers. Most importantly, it restores dignity."

Through the GIYH mobile App, emaSwati can already check the status of their IDs, passports, and travel documents. They can register a business name, pay for vehicle licences using Mobile Money, and access educational financing information. "These services are just the beginning. Every six months, we will be adding more services, building a comprehensive, one-stop platform for all Government services."

The Government of Eswatini remains committed to ensuring digital inclusion, transforming public service delivery, bridging the digital divide, empowering the young or old and differently abled and creating jobs through innovation.
