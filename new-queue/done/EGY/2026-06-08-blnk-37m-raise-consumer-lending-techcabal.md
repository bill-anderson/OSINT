# Blnk raises $37 million to target Egypt's underserved credit market

Blnk, an Egyptian fintech offering point-of-sale consumer loans, raised $37.1 million in equity and debt — a $12.5m Series A led by Algebra Ventures (with SANAD Fund, Endeavor Catalyst, Emirates International Investment Company) plus $24.6m in local-currency debt from National Bank of Egypt, Suez Canal Bank and Bank Al Baraka Egypt.

As of June 2025 about 16.7 million Egyptian adults had no formal access to credit. Founded 2021, Blnk lends through 3,000+ merchants, has onboarded 1m+ customers and a loan book over EGP 1bn ($21m); it became profitable in 2025 (revenue +173% YoY).
