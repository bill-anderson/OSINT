# How Egypt's startup ecosystem performed in 2025

Egypt raised over $339 million in H1 2025 (behind only South Africa among the "Big Four"). VC funding grew 31% YoY in H2 (excluding December), hitting $524 million from 71 deals, concentrated in large strategic rounds (proptech Nawy's $75m package the year's largest; wealthtech Thndr $15.7m; MoneyFellows $13m). In November 2025 GlobalFoundries acquired Cairo deep-tech semiconductor startup InfiniLink (reported 400% return for Egypt Ventures).

The government is preparing the Egypt Startup Charter — a unified financing initiative valued at EGP 50 billion ($1bn) targeting 5,000 startups, aiming to attract $5bn over five years and create 500,000 jobs. Egypt ranks as North Africa's lead startup ecosystem (65th globally).
