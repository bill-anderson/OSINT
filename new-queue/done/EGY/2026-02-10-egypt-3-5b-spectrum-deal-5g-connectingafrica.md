# Egypt seals $3.5B spectrum deal with telcos

Egypt signed a US$3.5 billion agreement with its four operators — Telecom Egypt, Vodafone Egypt, Orange Egypt and e& Egypt — to double the country's mobile spectrum and support 5G expansion, adding 410MHz. Minister Amr Talaat said fixed-line speeds have doubled over seven and a half years (first in Africa), with plans to expand to 40,000 towers by 2028.

The deal aligns with the National Spectrum Strategy 2026–2030. NTRA launched 5G services in June 2025, a year after awarding 5G licences to the three private operators; Telecom Egypt secured its 5G licence in January 2024 for $150 million.
