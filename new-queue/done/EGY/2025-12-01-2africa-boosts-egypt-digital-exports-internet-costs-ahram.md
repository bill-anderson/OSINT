# 2Africa cable boosts Egypt digital exports amid rising internet cost complaints

The 2Africa subsea system (~45,000km, 33+ countries, 45+ landing stations; consortium including Meta, Vodafone, Orange, China Mobile, MTN) is accelerating Egypt's shift from expanding basic access to monetizing connectivity through exports, data centres and higher-value services — even as consumer complaints over package prices and data caps rise.

Fixed broadband subscriptions rose to 12.60 million in November 2025 (up from 11.51m in 2024). Minister Amr Talaat said Egypt's digital exports reached $7.4 billion, with outsourcing exports at $4.8 billion in 2025 (up from ~$2.4bn in 2022). Egypt hosts ~15 active international submarine cable systems — the largest concentration in Africa — but Africa still holds under 2% of global data-centre capacity.
