# Egypt Invests Over $6 Billion in Telecom Infrastructure Since 2019

In a review with PM Madbouly, Minister Raafat Hindi said Egypt has invested more than $6 billion in telecom infrastructure since 2019, directed at fixed and mobile internet, fibre-optic expansion, internet-quality improvements and 5G rollout. The government plans to add ~3,000 new telecom towers this year and more than 9,000 over the next three years. Recent spectrum allocations worth $3.5 billion were issued to the four operators.
