# Egypt and SAP Deepen Ties to Boost AI, Cloud and Outsourcing Growth

Minister Raafat Hindi met SAP's Regional President for EMEA, Augusta Spinelli, to discuss expanding SAP's outsourcing operations and investment in Egypt. A key focus was cloud computing to support Egypt's push for greater digital sovereignty over data and applications hosted on SAP platforms, alongside advanced AI rollout and digital-skills training for youth.
