Libya's second-largest state mobile operator, **Almadar Aljadid**, announced on 27 January the launch of its **5G service**, becoming the first Libyan national operator to offer it. Phase one covers the capital **Tripoli** first, with the rest of Libya to follow. Initial reports to Libya Herald indicated the service was not yet widely available in Tripoli, confined to a few central parts of the capital.
