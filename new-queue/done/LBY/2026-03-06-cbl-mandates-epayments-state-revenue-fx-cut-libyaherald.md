PM **Aldabaiba** issued a decree mandating the inclusion of **e-payment as an approved method for collecting state revenues and public dues**, requiring Ministry of Finance treasuries and other ministries and agencies to accept e-payments alongside cash and cheques. In parallel, the CBL directed commercial banks (letter dated 4 March) to activate the **LYPay** instant-payment system enabling **QR-code POS purchases by Sunday 8 March**, and to launch a joint awareness campaign.

The CBL also amended the maximum FX commission for official bureaux to 1% (cash) and 0.5% (cheques, transfers, e-payment).
