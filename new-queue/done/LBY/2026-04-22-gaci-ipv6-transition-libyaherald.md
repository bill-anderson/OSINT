Abdelbaset Al-Baour, Chairman of the **General Authority for Communications and Informatics (GACI)**, met on 20 April with major Libyan telecom companies and the committee responsible for the transition, to activate Libya's move from **IPv4 to IPv6**. He described it as a strategic national project addressing IPv4 address depletion, offering scalability, enhanced security and reliability, and building a sustainable digital infrastructure supporting the digital economy — aiming to increase usage indicators in 2026.
