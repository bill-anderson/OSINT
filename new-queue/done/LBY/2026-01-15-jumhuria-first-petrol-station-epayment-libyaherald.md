Libya's largest state bank, **Jumhouria Bank**, launched the country's first-ever e-payment service at **petrol stations**, via the "OnePay – Mini Pay" platform accessible through the "Masrafi Plus" app, with no commission fees. The launch comes within a broader CBL initiative to enhance financial inclusion and expand Libya's e-payments systems.
