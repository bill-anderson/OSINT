# Connectivité et Innovation : Airtel Niger lance sa solution « Starlink by Airtel »

At the Radisson Blu, Niamey, on the evening of **Saturday 16 January 2026**, **Airtel Niger** officially launched commercialisation of "**Starlink by Airtel**" — a service from the **strategic partnership between Bharti Airtel (African subsidiary) and SpaceX/Starlink**. Niger is billed as one of the first countries on the continent to experiment with the solution, offering Airtel's customers better network coverage and stable, secure connectivity. Airtel Niger, the country's leading telecom operator (marking 25 years), was represented by board chair Ibrahim Idi Ango and DG Dina Amadou; the ARCEP DG attended.

The DG reviewed 2025 accomplishments — launch of eSIM, a SPAM Alert security service and the My Airtel app.
