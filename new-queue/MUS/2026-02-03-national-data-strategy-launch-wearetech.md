# Mauritius launches 2025-2029 national data strategy

*We Are Tech Africa, 3 February 2026.*

Mauritius launched its **National Data Strategy 2025-2029** on **28 January 2026** (World Data Protection Day), under the theme "a new era of trust." Led by the **Ministry of Information Technology** and the **Data Protection Office**, the roadmap prioritises **responsible data governance, cybercrime prevention, and stronger protections for citizens**, especially young people vulnerable online.
