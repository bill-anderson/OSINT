Mali Actuel (verify against Council of Ministers communique). The Council of Ministers of 10 June 2026 adopted draft texts creating, organising and operating the Agence nationale de la Securite des Systemes d'Information (ANSSI). Presented by the Minister of Communication, Digital Economy and Administration Modernisation, implementing Mali's National Cybersecurity Strategy and its 2026-2030 Action Plan (per this report "adopted in February" 2026; the strategy was taken note of at Council on 5 Dec 2025). ANSSI's mission: ensure national cybersecurity and protect information systems and electronic communications; coordinate response to cyberattacks; secure critical infrastructure and services. Framed as a major step in Mali's digital sovereignty. NOTE: minor date nuance between the strategy's Dec-2025 Council note and this report's "February" adoption reference, not a substantive conflict.
