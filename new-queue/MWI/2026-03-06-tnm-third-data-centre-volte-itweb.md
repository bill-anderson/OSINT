# TNM Malawi mulls third datacentre as it launches VoLTE service

Telekom Networks Malawi (TNM) has announced plans to construct a **third datacentre** within the next three months to support growing demand for digital services and improve network performance. The announcement was made on 5 March 2026 in Lilongwe during the launch of **Voice over LTE (VoLTE)**, which enables voice calls over the operator's high-speed LTE network.

TNM CEO Michel Herbert said the planned datacentre will strengthen infrastructure as demand for data and digital communication rises, improving service reliability and customer experience. VoLTE offers clearer voice quality, faster call connection and lets users continue browsing/streaming while on a call. Herbert said TNM has upgraded its internet services in recent months to improve connectivity and extend coverage.
