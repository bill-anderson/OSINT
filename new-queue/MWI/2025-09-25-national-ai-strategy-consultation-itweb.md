# Malawi initiates dialogue on AI strategy

Malawi has launched regional consultative workshops to develop its **first-ever National Artificial Intelligence Strategy** and a **National Digital Transformation Strategy**. The Department of E-Government is leading the strategy development, in collaboration with the UNDP under the **Inclusive Digital Transformation for Malawi (IDT4M) Project** and the Public Private Partnership Commission through the **Digital Malawi Acceleration Project (DMAP)**.

The two documents will serve as blueprints for Malawi's adoption and governance of AI and digital technologies — to establish a robust digital economy, improve service delivery and promote fair access to digital services. As consultations continue across regions, the strategies are expected to outline how Malawi can responsibly embrace AI, bridge the digital divide and achieve long-term digital transformation.

(Sept 2025 launch of the consultation process that reached the February 2026 validation workshop already held in the wiki.)
