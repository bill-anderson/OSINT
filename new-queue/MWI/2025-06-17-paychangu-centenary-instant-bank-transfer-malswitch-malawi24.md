# PayChangu, Centenary Bank launch instant bank transfer to transform business payments in Malawi

Malawi fintech **PayChangu** (founded 2021 by Morgan Tembo) launched an **Instant Bank Transfer** solution in partnership with **Centenary Bank**, letting merchants receive real-time payments from any bank in the country — supporting transactions of up to **MWK 500 million per transfer**, targeting energy, agriculture, gaming, construction and wholesale trade.

Crucially, the service is **powered by the national payment switch (Malswitch)**, enabling instant transfers to any bank in Malawi, and is accessible via **\*632#** on Centemobile (a USSD platform requiring no app) — beneficial to rural users and micro-entrepreneurs without smartphones. The PayChangu platform consolidates **Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba and bank transfers** into a single system with 24/7 access, virtual accounts and simplified KYC. Centenary Bank MD Godfrey Byekwaso and PayChangu CEO Morgan Tembo framed it as advancing financial inclusion and a 24-hour economy.
