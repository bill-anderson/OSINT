# Chinguitel–Huawei network modernisation, Nouakchott

Chinguitel completed the modernisation of its entire network in Nouakchott, in collaboration with **Huawei**, as part of an initiative to enhance digital infrastructure and high-speed internet services. The upgrade is a key component of Chinguitel's digital-transformation strategy.

The project supports the country's digital-government initiatives and enables e-learning, digital banking and smart-city applications, while improving service quality for subscribers. Chinguitel plans to expand its network stations in Nouakchott and extend modernisation to other Mauritanian cities by the end of 2025.
