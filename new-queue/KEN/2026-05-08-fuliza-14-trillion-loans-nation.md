# Kenyans 'Fuliza' record Sh1.4 trillion loans for food, rent and fees

man holds a
...
Loans disbursed on Safaricom’s mobile-money overdraft facility crossed the Sh1 trillion mark for the first time in the year ended March 2026, as borrowers increasingly seek survival loans for basic items such as food, rent and medicine.
...
The telecoms operator reported that the value of disbursements on the service, which allows M-Pesa customers to complete transactions even when they do not have sufficient funds in their mobile-money wallets, had hit Sh1.46 trillion, jumping by 49.3 per cent from Sh981.6 billion in the year to March 2025.
