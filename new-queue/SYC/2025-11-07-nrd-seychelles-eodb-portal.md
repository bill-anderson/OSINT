# Seychelles Launches Ease of Doing Business Portal — NRD Companies selected as technology partner | 7 November 2025
NRD Companies (press release — vendor-issued; primary should be cross-checked against the Ministry announcement at ingest)

The Ministry of Investment, Entrepreneurship and Industry (MIEI) of Seychelles formally launched its Ease of Doing Business (EODB) Portal Project. Over a nine-month timeline (six months implementation plus a three-month pilot), the system is targeted for full rollout by 30 June 2026.

Designed as a one-stop digital gateway, the EODB Portal will allow local and international businesses to access services, submit regulatory documents, and make payments through a single platform, consolidating fragmented administrative tasks into end-to-end workflows. It is structured around two user journeys: a Seamless Government Services Journey and an Entrepreneur Journey.

NRD Companies was selected as technology partner, bringing its GxP Customer Centric Service Delivery Platform (low-code, supporting rapid deployment of 15+ services), complemented by an Umbraco CMS front end. CEO Mindaugas Glodas framed the project as regulatory modernization unifying disparate agency systems into a single digital experience.
