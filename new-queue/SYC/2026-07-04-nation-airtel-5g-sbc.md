# Airtel Seychelles launches 5G network and partners with SBC | 04 July 2026
By: Vidya Gappy — Seychelles Nation

Airtel Seychelles has officially launched its highly anticipated 5G services, unveiling next-generation mobile and home internet solutions at a press conference held yesterday at Airtel House, Ile Perseverance. The new network promises faster speeds, stronger connectivity, and greater value for customers.

Alongside the launch, Airtel announced a landmark partnership with the Seychelles Broadcasting Corporation (SBC). This collaboration combines advanced telecommunications infrastructure with local cultural content, ensuring that the benefits of high-speed technology directly enrich the daily lives of the Seychellois community.

[Body truncated at Seychelles Nation paywall. See The Seychelles Times (2026-07-06, staged) for the fuller sourced account: Airtel is the second 5G operator after CWS (2020, Huawei); up to 500 Mbps; built on a 2021 Airtel Africa–Ericsson turnkey deal.]
