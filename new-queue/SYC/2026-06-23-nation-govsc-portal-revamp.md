# Seychelles launches revamped gov.sc portal to strengthen digital access | 23 June 2026
By: Patrick Joubert — Seychelles Nation

In a clear move to place communication at the heart of public service, the government of Seychelles has launched a revamped gov.sc website, the official Government Portal, designed to provide quick and seamless access to services and information. Vice-President Sebastien Pillay launched the portal.

The launch, aligned with the 50th Anniversary of Seychelles' Independence, underscores the government's commitment to a modern, citizen-centred digital future.
