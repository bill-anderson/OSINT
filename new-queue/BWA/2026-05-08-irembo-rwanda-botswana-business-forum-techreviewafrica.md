# Irembo joins Rwanda-Botswana Business Forum to discuss digital government collaboration

Tech Review Africa — Date: 2026-05-08 — By: Nana Appiah Acquaye

Irembo has participated in the Rwanda–Botswana Business Forum held as part of the Rwandan delegation's state visit to Botswana, with discussions focusing on digital governance, sovereign infrastructure and citizen-centred public service delivery.

The company's Chief Executive Officer, Israel Bimpe, joined engagements involving the Botswana Investment and Trade Centre, government officials and private sector stakeholders to explore opportunities for collaboration in digital transformation.

According to Irembo, the discussions centred on how African governments can build digital systems that effectively serve citizens while remaining rooted in local institutions and infrastructure.

Participants also examined the importance of coherent service delivery models, sovereign digital infrastructure and stronger pan-African partnerships in advancing public sector innovation and digital inclusion across the continent.

The engagements reflected growing interest among African governments in leveraging technology, operational systems and institutional collaboration to improve the accessibility and efficiency of public services.

Irembo said the exchanges underscored a shared commitment to building citizen-focused digital ecosystems that support broader economic and social development objectives across Africa.
