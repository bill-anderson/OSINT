# MTN MoMo and Nikulipe Expand Digital Finance Across Africa

*Telecom Review Africa (Telecom Operators), published 8 September 2025.*

MTN MoMo has made significant strides in its partnership with Nikulipe, marking a key advancement in Africa's digital financial ecosystem. The collaboration focuses on expanding financial inclusion and delivering convenient, secure, and innovative mobile financial services across the continent.

The partnership has already reached important milestones, including the rollout of services in new markets such as Cameroon, Zambia, and Congo-Brazzaville. These expansions provide millions of users with seamless mobile financial solutions, improving daily transactions and increasing access to digital finance.

MTN MoMo and Nikulipe are also empowering merchants by facilitating international online commerce. By connecting businesses with broader customer bases across Sub-Saharan Africa, the partnership fosters economic growth and strengthens the regional digital economy.

This initiative highlights MTN MoMo's commitment to accelerating Africa's digital economy and equipping communities with the tools to thrive in the digital era. Further market expansions are planned as the company continues to build a more inclusive and accessible financial ecosystem.

Through collaborations like this, MTN MoMo and Nikulipe are not just imagining a digitally empowered Africa; they are actively shaping it.
