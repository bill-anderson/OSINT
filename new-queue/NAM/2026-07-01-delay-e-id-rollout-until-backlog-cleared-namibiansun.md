# Delay E-ID rollout until backlog is cleared, activists say

*Documentation crisis a constitutional issue.*

[CLIP — paywalled; free lede only:]

Thousands of undocumented Namibians have called on government to suspend the rollout of electronic national identity (E-ID) cards until it clears a years-long backlog of citizenship and identity documents. A petition argues that thousands remain excluded from jobs, education and public services while waiting for citizenship and identity documents — framing the documentation crisis as a constitutional issue.
