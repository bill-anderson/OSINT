# Telecom Namibia, Angola Telecom and SATCAB Sign SARSSy Deal to Deliver 1 Tbps Connectivity Across Southern Africa

Telecom Namibia, Angola Telecom, and SATCAB – Satélite e Cabo TV, S.A. signed the Master Service Agreement (MSA) for the Southern African Regional Submarine System (SARSSy) on 12 June 2026, integrating the Equiano Cable System. The tripartite agreement builds on the MoU and Commercial Terms Agreement signed in April 2026 in Swakopmund, marking a transition from strategic intent to practical implementation.

Concluded within the framework of bilateral cooperation between Namibia and Angola, it aims to advance regional integration, strengthen economic cooperation, and promote "digital sovereignty and connectivity" within the SADC region. The parties intend to facilitate the landing and operationalisation of SARSSy, including technical integration of Equiano and SARSSy, to establish a resilient, high-capacity regional digital corridor enhancing international connectivity across Namibia, Angola and the broader SADC region.
