# Nigerian Vice President Welcomes Visa's Investment in Nigerian Data Centre Infrastructure

March 24, 2025 — Joyce Onyeagoro, TechAfrica News

Visa's planned investments and technological advancements reflect its confidence in Nigeria's economic potential and its commitment to strengthening the country's digital payments ecosystem.

Multiple reports have confirmed that the Nigerian Vice President Kashim Shettima has welcomed Visa Inc.'s commitment to establishing data centre infrastructure in Nigeria.

This potentially marks a significant investment pledge from the global payment solutions giant.
