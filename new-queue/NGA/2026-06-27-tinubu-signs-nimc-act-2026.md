President Tinubu signed the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC) Act, 2026 into law, repealing the 2007 legislation and creating a new legal framework for a secure, interoperable and inclusive digital identity ecosystem. Senate President Godswill Akpabio said the strengthened national identity management system is yielding security dividends — integration of government identity databases enabled security agencies to identify and apprehend suspected terrorism kingpins attempting to enter the country. Interior Minister Olubunmi Tunji-Ojo and NIMC DG Dr Abisoye Coker-Odusote applauded the assent. The legislation significantly expands NIMC's powers.
