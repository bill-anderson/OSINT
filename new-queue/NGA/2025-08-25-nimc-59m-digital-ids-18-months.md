NIMC must issue at least 59 million NINs by December 31, 2026 under revised terms of the Nigeria ID4D project (funded by the World Bank, French Development Agency and European Investment Bank, ~$430 million). The project was revised after Nigeria missed targets; conditions include increasing database capacity to 250 million entries, raising the issuance target to 180 million, and revising the NIMC Act 2007. NIMC must issue at least 3.3 million NINs monthly (far above the current ~1.08 million/month average). As of June, 121 million NINs had been issued (about 1.5 million to Nigerians abroad). NIMC upgraded its Automated Biometric Identification System (ABIS) and expanded enrolment centres.
