Google, through Google.org, announced a N3 billion commitment to Nigeria to accelerate advanced AI skills development and strengthen digital safety, supporting the National AI Strategy and the FG's plan to create one million digital jobs. Google said the dual strategy will help Nigeria unlock a projected $15 billion in economic value from AI by 2030 (per Public First research). Minister Bosun Tijani welcomed it as a timely boost. The N3bn will be channelled to five expert organisations (including FATE Foundation with AIMS, embedding an advanced AI curriculum into universities; and the African Technology Forum launching an innovation challenge).
