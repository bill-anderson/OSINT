# MTN Partners with NIMET, Tomorrow.io to Launch Digital Climate Advisory for Farmers

By Aayushya Ranjan, TechAfrica News, February 3, 2025

MTN, NIMET, and Tomorrow.io partner to launch a mobile weather advisory service, enhancing climate resilience and food security for Nigerian farmers.

On January 24, 2025, MTN, alongside the Nigerian Meteorological Agency (NIMET) and Tomorrow.io, signed a memorandum of understanding to develop a Digital Climate Advisory Services (DCAS) system. This initiative aims to deliver location-specific weather advisories to Nigerian farmers via mobile SMS, enhancing climate resilience in the agricultural sector.

"I am optimistic about what this collaboration will do for the agricultural sector, emergency management, and the Nigerian economy as a whole. We are grateful to NiMet for allowing MTN to partner with them." – Karl Olutokun Toriola, CEO, MTN

This partnership reflects MTN's commitment to supporting government initiatives and contributing to food security in Nigeria.
