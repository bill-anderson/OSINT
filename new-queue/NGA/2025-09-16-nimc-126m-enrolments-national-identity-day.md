President Tinubu declared that over 126 million citizens are now enrolled in the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC) database, describing the achievement as "126 million stories of visibility, empowerment and inclusion." Represented by SGF Senator George Akume at the 7th National Identity Day Celebration in Abuja (September 16, 2025). He stressed that digital identity is central to Nigeria's development and inclusion agenda.
