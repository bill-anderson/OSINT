NIMC released NIN figures for March: 118.4 million digital IDs issued as of March 31 (Lagos leading with 12.6m, Kano 10.4m, Kaduna 6.9m; 66.9m male vs 51.5m female registered). Under the World Bank's restructured funding arrangement, Nigeria is now required to issue 180 million NINs by December 31, 2026 — meaning NIMC must deliver 62 million digital IDs in 21 months. Total project funding is $430 million via a tripartite arrangement (World Bank, French Development Agency, European Investment Bank).

A bill to amend the NIMC Act 2007 to make digital ID registration mandatory scaled second reading in the Senate; a similar move would make enrolment mandatory for foreigners. NIMC warned NIN holders against fraudsters who fool holders into disclosing NIN numbers for monetary compensation (complaints from Anambra State).
