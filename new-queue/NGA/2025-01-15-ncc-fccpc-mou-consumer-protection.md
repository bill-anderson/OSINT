# NCC and FCCPC Sign MoU to Enhance Consumer Protection in Telecommunications

By Aayushya Ranjan, TechAfrica News, January 15, 2025

NCC and FCCPC sign MoU to promote a transparent, competitive telecommunications industry focused on consumer protection and accountability.

The Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC) and the Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC) have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to promote a transparent and consumer-centric telecommunications industry.

The agreement, signed by NCC Executive Vice Chairman Dr. Aminu Maida and FCCPC Chief Executive Mr. Tunji Bello, underscores the commitment of both agencies to fostering competition, safeguarding consumer rights, and ensuring industry-wide accountability.
