Airtel Nigeria unveiled plans for a 38-megawatt hyperscale data centre in Lagos, branded under the "Nxtra by Airtel" name, sited in the high-security Eko Atlantic district and expected operational by Q1 2026. It will feature over 3,000 racks across six floors and ~300,000 square feet, engineered for high power density (up to 25kW per rack) suitable for GPU-intensive and AI workloads, serving hyperscalers, government agencies and enterprises. CEO Dinesh Balsingh said the facility reflects a long-term commitment to digital infrastructure supporting cloud, AI and the broader digital economy.
