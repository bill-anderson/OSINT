The CBN unveiled the Payments System Vision 2028 (PSV 2028), a strategic framework to reshape Nigeria's financial ecosystem to be more secure, inclusive, innovative and globally competitive. It builds on earlier reforms under PSV 2020 and PSV 2025 and supports Nigeria's transition toward a digitally driven economy. At its core: strengthening regulatory oversight, deepening financial inclusion, and improving efficiency and safety of payment systems, with projected expansion in formal financial access. Success depends on collaboration across regulators, financial institutions, fintechs and technology providers.
