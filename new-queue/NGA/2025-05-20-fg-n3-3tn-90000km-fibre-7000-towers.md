Minister Dr Bosun Tijani disclosed that rollout of the N3.3 trillion, 90,000km fibre lines and 7,000 telecom towers nationwide (Project Bridge) will formally commence in Q4 2025. The sector attracted $191 million FDI in Q1 2024, a ninefold increase from $22 million in Q1 2023. Finance Minister Wale Edun underscored the capital market's role in the $1 trillion economy vision by 2030. The 3MTT programme has trained over 117,000 Nigerians (target 3 million), with 35,000 more in training. (Companion to the Vanguard $2bn Project Bridge report of 2025-05-18; both draw on the same State House second-anniversary documentary interview, with differing headline figures — $2bn vs N3.3tn.)
