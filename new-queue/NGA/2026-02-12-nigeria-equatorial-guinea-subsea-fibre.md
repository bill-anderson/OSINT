Nigeria's Minister of Foreign Affairs Ambassador Yusuf Maitama Tuggar signed a landmark digital infrastructure agreement in Malabo, Equatorial Guinea, for a subsea fibre-optic cable to expand broadband capacity, strengthen digital communications and enhance regional cybersecurity collaboration. The agreement builds on President Tinubu's State Visit to Malabo in August 2024. The principles agreement was signed by Backbone Connectivity Network Nigeria Ltd (BNC), represented by Alhaji Ibrahim Dikko, and Equatorial Guinea's Minister of Transport, Telecommunications and AI Systems, Honorato Evita Oma.
