A new secure platform for verification and authentication of Nigeria's NIN — the NIN Authentication app (NINAuth) — was unveiled, its launch approved by President Tinubu (statement issued May 6 by NIMC). NINAuth is "a cutting-edge suite of services that include web, API and mobile verification designed to enhance data security, protect privacy, and simplify access to government services." Users can undergo verification for immigration/passport processing, driver's licenses, SIM registration, tax filing and financial transactions, and government social protection programs. It requires explicit consent before data is shared for KYC, offering real-time authentication of NIN records, database harmonization and compliance with national ID policy. NIMC reported over 118 million NINs issued, targeting 180 million by December 31, 2026.
