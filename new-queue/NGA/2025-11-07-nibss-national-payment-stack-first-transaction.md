NIBSS executed the first live transaction on the National Payment Stack (NPS), the next-generation infrastructure to unify and modernise Nigeria's electronic payment ecosystem. The transaction completed at 11:56 a.m. on November 7, 2025 between PalmPay and Wema Bank, processed in milliseconds with instant settlement. Built on the ISO 20022 international messaging standard, aligning with the CBN's directive to enhance data quality, regulatory transparency and global compatibility. The NPS lays a foundation for Nigeria's emerging Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI), enabling instant payments, seamless bank/fintech integration, enhanced data protection (digital signatures, multi-layer authentication) and cross-border capability. The NPS was launched in June 2025.
