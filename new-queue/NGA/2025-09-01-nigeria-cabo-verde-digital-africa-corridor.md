The Digital Africa Corridor (DAC), convened by SheCode.ai and facilitated in Nigeria by Kryterion Limited, launched its first pilot corridor between Nigeria and Cabo Verde with a high-level roundtable in Abuja on September 1, 2025, co-hosted with Cabo Verde's Ministry of Digital Economy. 35 leaders from government, private sector, investors and innovators explored AI, youth empowerment and bilateral digital cooperation. A key milestone was Cabo Verde's endorsement of SheCode.ai's "Code the Future – Cabo Verde Rising" (empowering 500+ secondary school girls across three islands with AI/coding skills). Cabo Verde charged the organisers to lead a Nigeria–Cabo Verde Business & Innovation Mission to Praia in Q4 2025.
