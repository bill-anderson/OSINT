The TELECEL Group, represented by Mr. Moh Damush, CEO of the TELECEL Group, and the Government of Guinea-Bissau, represented by His Excellency Dr. Florentino Mendes Pereira, held a ceremony on May 6, 2026, to sign a Joint Decree formalizing the creation of the Coordination and Monitoring Committee for the STARTOCODE Platform, as part of the implementation of STARTOCODE in Guinea-Bissau.

The Committee is composed of representatives from the Ministries of Education; Youth; Women and Social Solidarity; Public Administration and Labor; Transport, Telecommunications and Digital Economy; as well as Telecel Guinea-Bissau. Its mission will be to supervise, coordinate, and monitor the implementation of the STARTOCODE program.

The signing marks a further step in strengthening collaboration between TELECEL and the Government of Guinea-Bissau in empowering Guinean youth with programming and digital skills, with special emphasis on women and people with disabilities. Under the STARTOCODE Program, one thousand young people will be trained annually over a four-year period in programming, data analysis, and cloud computing.
