# Egypt pilots selfie biometrics to streamline access to digital govt services

Egypt is piloting MOIEG-PASS, described as the country's first unified biometric authentication platform, through a collaboration between the Ministries of Interior and Communications. Users scan their national IDs and face biometrics to authenticate for a selected number of government services, available first on Android with iOS to follow and further government-service integration planned.

The push aligns with the ICT 2030 Strategy and the Digital Egypt vision's digital-innovation pillar — a sustainable GovTech ecosystem and easier access to public/private services.
