# Huawei Cloud Strengthens Egypt's FinTech Ecosystem

At the Huawei Cloud FinTech Summit 2026 in Cairo, Huawei Cloud reaffirmed its commitment to Egypt's financial-services sector. Since launching its first public cloud region in Egypt in 2024, Huawei Cloud keeps data inside Egypt — supporting digital sovereignty. The platform holds NTRA certification and a Tier 3 licence, and adheres to Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA) data-localisation requirements for non-banking financial entities.

CEO Joe Xu highlighted local cloud advantages: lower latency, data sovereignty, 24/7 local support and regulatory compliance. A related MoU was signed with digital-investment platform Thndr (16 June 2026) to expand cloud/AI in fintech.
