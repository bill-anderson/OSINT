# UNDP and Senegal Government Strengthen Ties on Digital Development Agenda

Tech Africa News - 16 April 2026, by Jennifer Onyeagoro

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and Senegal's Ministry of Communication, Telecommunications and Digital Economy (MCTN) have signed a memorandum of understanding to strengthen collaboration on digital transformation. According to UNDP Senegal, the partnership will focus on key areas including digital governance, digital inclusion, artificial intelligence and innovation.

The agreement aims to support Senegal's broader digital transformation agenda by leveraging technology to enhance public services, expand access to digital opportunities and foster innovation across sectors, positioning Senegal as a growing hub for digital innovation in the region.
