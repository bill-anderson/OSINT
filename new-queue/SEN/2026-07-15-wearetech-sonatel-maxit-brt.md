# Senegal: Sonatel Connects Max it App to Dakar Bus Rapid Transit Network

We Are Tech Africa - 15 July 2026, by Samira Njoya

Dakar Mobilite, the BRT concessionaire, and Sonatel signed a partnership agreement on 14 July to integrate Sonatel's Max it application into Dakar's Bus Rapid Transit network. Passengers will progressively gain access to ticket purchases, subscription top-ups and additional digital mobility services through a single platform. Beyond ticketing, the partners plan transport-card top-ups, financing solutions through Orange Bank, Wi-Fi in stations and data analytics to optimise passenger flow.

Dakar Mobilite said digital payments on the BRT network have risen to 39% of transactions from 10% at launch, while user satisfaction reached 99%. Dakar's BRT is the first fully electric BRT network in sub-Saharan Africa, designed to carry up to 300,000 passengers per day. For Sonatel, the deal extends its Max it super-app ecosystem (payments, transfers, service purchases) into mobility as Senegal prepares to host the Dakar 2026 Summer Youth Olympics.
