# CNAMGS and ANINF partner to accelerate digital modernisation of public services in Gabon

Agence Nationale des Infrastructures Numériques et des Fréquences and Caisse Nationale d'Assurance Maladie et de Garantie Sociale have signed a memorandum of understanding aimed at strengthening the digital transformation and modernization of strategic public services in Gabon.

The agreement, signed on May 8, 2026, will see ANINF support CNAMGS in auditing its information systems to improve the security, resilience and performance of its digital infrastructure.

Speaking during the signing ceremony, Alberto Mounguengui Moudoki reaffirmed the agency's commitment to supporting strategic public administrations in their digital transformation projects, with a focus on cybersecurity, data governance and digital sovereignty.

For her part, Béatrice Yvette Nguema Edzang said the partnership marks the beginning of a new phase of modernization and operational performance for CNAMGS, adding that the institution's digital transformation ambitions require strong technical and strategic collaboration with ANINF.

The partnership aligns with the national digital transformation agenda championed by Brice Clotaire Oligui Nguema, which prioritises the modernization of public administration and the strengthening of digital sovereignty.

Both institutions said the collaboration reflects a shared commitment to building more efficient, secure and citizen-focused public services through digital innovation.
