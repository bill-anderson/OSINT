# Kenya Budget Statement FY2024/25 (13 June 2024)

Companion page for the artefact (128 pp). The budget speech as delivered — **pre-withdrawal**: the
Finance Bill 2024 it rested on was rejected on 2024-06-26, so its revenue side never took effect
and its allocations were revised within two months (Supplementary I). Digital seam: the "digital
superhighway and creative economy" thematic (KSh 16.3bn) with named lines (Konza Data Centre and
Smart City 5.2bn; KAIST 2.3bn; Digital Economy Acceleration Project 2.8bn; Last Mile County
Connectivity 2.8bn; shared services). Budget stage: **proposed**.
