## Notes

Statistical annexure to the May-2025 Budget Review (the third, enacted 2025 budget tabling). Table 4 gives, for FY2024/25, each vote's budget estimate, adjusted appropriation AND revised estimate side by side, plus actual outcomes for earlier years — the nearest thing to a per-vote outturn the Treasury publishes ahead of audited AFS. Caveat printed in the document: historical data adjusted for function shifts, so figures may differ from departments' own financial statements.
