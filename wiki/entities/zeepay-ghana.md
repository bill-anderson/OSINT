# Zeepay (Ghana)

A Ghanaian fintech / **Dedicated Electronic Money Issuer (DEMI)**. The **[[bank-of-ghana|Bank of Ghana]] revoked
its DEMI licence on 14 July 2026** over multiple regulatory breaches (under **s.13 of Act 987**), reportedly
because issued e-money was **not fully cash-backed**; BoG officials and police restricted access to its HQ the
next day — a notable enforcement action in Ghana's fintech-supervision drive. See [[ghana--dpi-pay]].
