# KICTANet

The **Kenya ICT Action Network** — a multi-stakeholder ICT-policy think tank and convener
(Kenya-based, active continentally) working on internet governance, data protection and
digital-rights capacity building. Appears here as a technical/facilitation partner on
continental data-governance capacity work.

## Activity in this wiki

- **São Tomé data-governance workshop (2026-07).** Named an implementing partner (with
  **[[giz]]** and **[[auda-nepad]]**) of the **[[d4d-hub]]** #D4DataGOV workshop helping
  **[[STP]]** adopt the **[[au-data-policy-framework]]** nationally — a Kenyan civil-society
  body exporting data-governance facilitation across the continent. See [[gov.protect]],
  [[capacity.training]], [[KEN]]. Source: [[2026-07-13 D4D Hub on Sao Tome and Principe]].
