# INE (National Statistics Institute, Cabo Verde)

Cabo Verde's national statistics office — the subject of a **live data-integrity controversy** that the
wiki records **from both sides (capture is not endorsement; neither claim is adopted)**.

## The data-manipulation controversy (2025–26)

- **The allegation.** Anonymised INE technicians (via A Nação) alleged **GDP/VAB methodology manipulation
  from 2016** under DG **João de Pina Cardoso** — the origin of the thread, echoed by the opposition
  PAICV. Source: [[2025-08-10-estatisticos-alertam-enganados-pelo-ine-anacao]].
- **The rebuttals.** INE **repudiated** the accusations (Sept 2025, invoking Eurostat/IMF/World Bank/ILO),
  then issued a **detailed rebuttal reaffirming technical independence** (Jun 2026 — citing IDRF 2023, the
  World-Bank SWIFT methodology, ERETES for GDP, and a fully-digital IDRF for Jul 2026), and finally **filed
  a formal complaint** with the authorities over the anonymous accusations and alleged **"misuse of INE
  digital platforms."** Sources: [[2025-09-17-ine-repudia-acusacoes-manipulacao-dados-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-06-01-ine-rejeita-acusacoes-manipulacao-independencia-tecnica-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-06-02-ine-apresenta-denuncia-autoridades-acusacoes-expressodasilhas]].
- **Independent-cooperation move.** Separately, INE signed a **protocol with the University of Coimbra** on
  microdata/metadata access with data-protection and statistical-secrecy safeguards (Mar 2026). Source:
  [[2026-03-29-ine-universidade-coimbra-cooperacao-estatistica-expressodasilhas]].

*This is an unresolved allegation-vs-rebuttal controversy, not a value the wiki adjudicates; both sides are
held, dated and attributed.*
