# BMCI

A Mauritanian commercial bank, cited as an illustrative beneficiary of the **[[pamif|PAMIF]]** financial-
infrastructure modernisation: with real-time clearing in place, BMCI processed **5,000 transfers in all of
2023 versus 94,000 in H2 2025 alone** — a marker of the leap in interbank settlement volume under the
**[[central-bank-mauritania|Central Bank of Mauritania]]** reforms.
