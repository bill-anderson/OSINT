# Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019 (Uganda)

Uganda’s data-protection statute — Act No. 9/2019 (Cap 97), with the Data Protection and Privacy
Regulations 2021 (SI 21/2021); enforced by the [[personal-data-protection-office-uganda]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **s.35 in the 2026 polls (2026-03).** The Act’s criminal-offence provision (s.35) was used against
  civil-society actors during the electoral cycle, while state controllers went largely unenforced —
  central to the "selective enforcement" finding. See [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-31 Uneven Enforcement of Data Protection Laws Ugandas 2026 Polls]].
