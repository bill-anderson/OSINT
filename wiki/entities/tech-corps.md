# Tech Corps

A US "Peace Corps for AI" — a Peace-Corps-style volunteer program to help partner nations
adopt the American AI stack. It is one prong of the broader US AI-export push and is
explicitly aimed at steering Global-South countries toward American AI hardware and
software, and away from cheaper Chinese ([[deepseek]]) alternatives.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Announced at the India AI Impact Summit (New Delhi, Feb 2026).** White House science &
  technology adviser **Michael Kratsios** launched Tech Corps, positioning it as "bringing
  America's historic Peace Corps into the 21st century": thousands of US technical/scientific
  volunteers to provide "last-mile support" deploying American AI applications in partner
  nations across **health, education, and agriculture**. See [[geopol.usa]],
  [[capacity.training]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-02 The U.S. Launches a Peace Corps for AI to Compete With China]].
- **Part of the US AI-stack export campaign.** Tech Corps is the latest measure in a
  sequence — the July 2025 "Winning the AI Race: America's AI Action Plan," the AI-export
  executive order, and the Commerce Department's [[american-ai-exports-program]] — to
  diffuse the "full-stack American AI technology package" abroad, alongside financing
  initiatives, a "National Champion Initiative," and the [[pax-silica]] trusted-supply-chain
  alliance (which India recently joined). Frames the US–China AI-stack contest in the Global
  South. See [[geopol.usa]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-02 The U.S. Launches a Peace Corps for AI to Compete With China]].
- **Critiqued as commercial diplomacy.** Analysts note the program is a "striking
  divergence" from the original Peace Corps intent given its clear commercial and
  geopolitical incentives, and question whether it can overcome a decade of accumulated
  Chinese customer relationships in emerging markets. See [[colonialism]], [[sovereignty]].
  Source: [[2026-03-02 The U.S. Launches a Peace Corps for AI to Compete With China]].
