# Togo Digital Economy Diagnostic Report (World Bank, 2019)

**A [[world-bank]] Group country diagnostic of Togo's digital economy — a standing reference
object rather than a report of an event.** It is the baseline assessment against which later
Togolese digital-policy activity (the 2024 [[togo-digital-acceleration-financing]] series, the
public-service digitisation of [[services-publics-togo]]) can be read. Held in `raw/` as a PDF
artefact with a companion source page. See [[TGO]], [[gov.policy]].

**Reference study — cite, do not absorb.** This is a country-level reference work. **Its dated
figures are not promoted onto concept or place pages as current state**, and none are lifted
here. Cite the study; leave its numbers in it. Fresher country-level data arrives via clippings.
A specific dated figure later pulled from the diagnostic becomes a normal source citing this
instrument.

**Date conflict, printed on the document itself.** The **copyright page reads "© 2020"** while
the **suggested citation reads "World Bank Group. 2019."** The item is dated **2019 per the
printed citation**, at **year precision**. This is a genuine on-page conflict in the published
document, not an extraction artefact — recorded rather than resolved.
