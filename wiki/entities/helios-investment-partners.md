# Helios Investment Partners

Africa-focused private-equity firm, active as a financial sponsor in African
telecoms and digital-infrastructure assets. Not to be confused with
**[[helios-towers]]**, the separately-listed tower company.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Regional Data Center Hub stake purchase cancelled (2026-07-17).** Helios would have
  acquired a **75–80% interest** in the unit owning **[[telecom-egypt]]**'s
  **[[regional-data-center-hub]]** under an agreement announced **2025-09-07**. Telecom
  Egypt's board withdrew, saying the transaction's conditions had not been met, and opted
  to carve RDH out as a **wholly-owned subsidiary** instead
  ([[telecom-egypt-helios-rdh-stake-sale]]). See [[EGY]], [[infra.store]],
  [[egypt--infra-store]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-telecom-egypt-cancels-helios-data-center-stake-sale]].
