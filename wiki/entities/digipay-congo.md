# DigiPay (Congo-Brazzaville)

A **Congo-B-founded remittance fintech** that, with **Belmoney** (licensed by the National Bank of
Belgium), opened a **DigiTransfer** instant-transfer corridor **France/Belgium → Congo-B (+ DR Congo)** on
a Remittance-as-a-Service model, framed around the SDG **3% cost-cap** target. Sources:
[[2026-05-28-belmoney-digipay-digitransfer-remittances-connectingafrica]],
[[2026-05-28-belmoney-digipay-instant-transfer-app-wearetech]].
