# MaPoste (digital super-app, Côte d'Ivoire)

"MaPoste" is a Côte d'Ivoire digital "super application" that links national digital identity to
postal and administrative services. Any citizen holding a National Identification Number (NNI)
can use it to reach a digital mailbox from a smartphone and transact with public administration.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched 2026-01-16.** [[oneci]] and [[la-poste-cote-divoire]] launched MaPoste, integrating
  ONECI's National Register of Natural Persons (RNPP) and the NNI into La Poste's digital
  platform. It aims to dematerialise administrative procedures including national ID-card
  delivery and the request and tracking of civil-status certificates, nationality certificates
  and criminal records, plus a digital mailbox — reducing travel, speeding procedures and
  strengthening transaction security under the national digital-transformation strategy. Built on
  a phygital model (200+ [[la-poste-cote-divoire]] agencies plus digital). Tags [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-19 Ivory Coast Launches MaPoste to Digitize Postal and Administrative Services]].

- **National-press/second-outlet detail (Jan 2026).** Coverage adds that MaPoste's **digital post boxes
  become mandatory from 1 Mar 2026**, CNI home delivery runs through **200+ post offices**, and the app
  is explicitly backed by the biometric **RNPP/NNI**. See [[civ--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-20-maposte-app-postal-digital-id-biometricupdate]].
