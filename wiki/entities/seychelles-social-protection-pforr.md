# Seychelles Strengthening the Quality of the Social Protection System (PforR)

**Parties:** the Government of Seychelles ([[SYC]]) and the **[[world-bank]]**. **What:** a
**US$30m Program-for-Results** operation to strengthen the quality of Seychelles' social
protection system. **When:** appraised in the Project Appraisal Document **PAD3541**, dated
**2021-03-03**. **Instrument:** Program-for-Results — disbursement against results achieved
rather than inputs reimbursed. See [[finance.new]], [[include.access]], [[dpi.registry]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **US$30m PforR appraised (2021-03-03).** This wiki holds the World Bank Project Appraisal
  Document (PAD3541) as a PDF artefact with a companion source page. The **US$30m** figure and
  the PforR instrument are what the source page establishes; the PAD's programme design, results
  framework, indicators and disbursement-linked indicators are **not summarised in this wiki** —
  read the artefact. Source:
  [[2021-03-03 PAD - Seychelles Strengthening Quality of the Social Protection System PforR (PAD3541)]].
- **Status unknown — this is a 2021 appraisal, not current state.** A PAD documents a programme
  **as appraised at approval**. No source held here records the programme's approval,
  effectiveness, disbursement, restructuring, results or closure in the **five-plus years since
  2021-03-03**. Do not read the appraisal forward as a description of what the programme is doing
  as of 2026-07-17.
- **Why it sits in this wiki.** Social-protection quality programmes typically rest on
  beneficiary registries and identity-linked targeting, connecting this operation to
  [[dpi.registry]] and to Seychelles' identity stack ([[seyid]],
  [[department-of-immigration-and-civil-status-seychelles]]). **No source held here establishes
  such a link** — it is the reason to look at the PAD, not a finding drawn from it.
