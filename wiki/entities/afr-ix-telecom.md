# AFR-IX Telecom

**The operator of the [[medusa-submarine-cable|Medusa]] system — and, in this wiki, the announcer of record whose own newsroom settled a Tunisian ownership claim that both national operators' PR had blurred.**

*Held for the Medusa file. The wiki holds nothing on AFR-IX's wider network or corporate structure, as of **2026-07-20**.*

## Activity in this wiki

- **Medusa lands at Bizerte (2025-10-31).** The cable's **first African landing**, marked by a ceremony both Tunisian operators' CEOs attended — which is precisely what produced the ownership confusion that followed. Announced by **AFR-IX/Medusa's own newsroom** with both CEOs present and EU Ambassador **Giuseppe Perrone** placing the landing under **[[global-gateway|Global Gateway]]**. Source: [[2025-11-01-medusa-lands-in-bizerte]] (the announcer of record); TAP/Zawya re-report: [[2025-11-03-zawya-tunisie-telecom-medusa-landing-bizerte]].
- **The ownership question it settled (resolved 2026-07-20).** Against AFR-IX/Medusa's own account, **[[orange-tunisia|Orange]]** is the landing party and **owns and operates the Bizerte landing station** and the ~1,050 km Tunisian segment (**ViaTunisia**); **[[tunisie-telecom|TT]]** is a **capacity buyer** (a 20 Tbps pair, 2025-02), not a co-owner. Full case: [[tunisia--infra-connect]].
- **The system.** Medusa is an **8,760 km carrier-neutral** Mediterranean system operated by AFR-IX — carrier-neutrality being the structural point: capacity is sold to operators rather than owned by a consortium of them.

## Reads

- `sovereignty`: a carrier-neutral operator changes who holds the leverage on a route. Tunisia's international capacity is landed by a national incumbent but *supplied* by a third party under an EU flagship programme — a different dependency shape from the consortium cables, not the absence of one.
- A methodological note the wiki keeps returning to: **when two national operators attend the same ribbon-cutting, prefer the cable operator's own announcement**. It is the party with no reason to blur which of them owns what.
