# CAIMEx

CAIMEx is [[cassava-ai]]'s "Multi-Model LLM Exchange for Telcos" — a unified gateway to multiple large language models. It matters as Cassava's sovereignty-and-latency pitch for governed, onshore access to global and regional models.

## Profile
- A single OpenAI-compatible unified API/gateway to access, govern, route and switch between multiple global and regional LLMs.
- Fully-managed LLMOps, running on Cassava's GPU/AI-factory infrastructure across Africa and the Middle East.
- Pitched on ultra-low latency plus data localisation, residency and sovereignty.
- Telco-grade.
- Launched **2025**, powered by NVIDIA Blueprints, Models and NIM microservices; supported models include Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini. Source: [[2026-03-18 Cassava Technologies Launches AI Factory in South Africa, Plans Expansion Across Africa]].
- Hosts the **Cassava Autonomous Network** blueprint (agentic RAN self-optimisation for mobile operators). Source: [[2026-03-03 Cassava unveils NVIDIA-backed autonomous network solution]].
