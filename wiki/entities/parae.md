# PARAE

An **AfDB-supported programme (~49bn FCFA)** launched alongside the 13 Mar 2025 handover of Côte
d'Ivoire's AI and Data Governance strategies — including a **data centre** — the implementation vehicle
the hub had listed only as an unhubbed donor programme. Paired in the record with **PADCI** (the World-
Bank-cofinanced digital-transformation project behind the 232-service mapping). Source:
[[2025-03-14-bad-strategies-gouvernance-donnees-ia-fratmat]].
