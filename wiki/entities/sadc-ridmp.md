# SADC Regional Infrastructure Development Master Plan (RIDMP)

The **[[sadc]]** bloc's foundational regional-infrastructure blueprint (**Executive Summary, August
2012**), setting a long-horizon master plan for Southern-African infrastructure across six sectors:
**energy, transport, ICT, meteorology and water** (plus tourism). Sponsored via DFID/TradeMark
Southern Africa with EU and **[[dbsa]]** contributions.

## What it is (as of 2012-08)

- **Scope.** A cross-sector regional integration plan; the transport chapter builds on SADC's 2008
  **Spatial Corridor Development Strategy** (flagship **North–South Corridor**), while the **ICT
  sector plan** set out the "**Digital SADC 2027**" framing later carried into the
  [[sadc-digital-transformation-strategy]].
- **Relevance here.** The predecessor infrastructure framework that Southern-African connectivity,
  energy and cross-border digital-corridor items trace back to. See [[infra.connect]],
  [[infra.energy]], [[gov.regional]], [[XSA]].
