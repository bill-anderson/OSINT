# MTN South Sudan

Leading mobile operator in South Sudan (part of the **[[mtn-group]]**), running **MTN MoMo** mobile
money. Launched the country's first **eSIM** (Jan 2025), secured a new **15-year mobile operator
licence** (Apr 2025), and cut tariffs **25%** (Dec 2025). Its infrastructure sibling
**[[bayobab-south-sudan]]** holds the national fibre-backbone licence. See [[south-sudan--infra-connect]],
[[SSD]].
