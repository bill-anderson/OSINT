# John Mwendwa

Chief executive of the **[[kenya-investment-authority|Kenya Investment Authority (Invest Kenya)]]**.

On the record (interview with *Business Daily*, reported 2026-07-17) confirming that the agency is
**exploring residency by investment** — *"Directionally, that's the way investors would like it"* —
while stating plainly that thresholds and qualifying sectors are not yet set, that any programme
**would require legislation** because immigration policy falls outside his agency's mandate, and that
*"we have to have parameters that make commercial sense."*

The useful signal is the candour about limits: the promotion agency is the proposer, not the decider.
See [[KEN]], [[kenya-investment-authority]], [[gov.policy]].
