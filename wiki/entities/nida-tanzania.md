# National Identification Authority (NIDA), Tanzania

NIDA runs [[TZA]]'s national digital ID system, Jamii Namba, the country's foundational [[dpi.id]] registry.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Phase II procurement (2026-04-22).** NIDA is seeking biometrics contractors for Phase II of the national digital ID project, which adds iris and facial recognition, builds a new NIDA headquarters in Dodoma, and expands registration to 31 districts. Phase II is backed by an approximately $70m loan from the Export-Import Bank of Korea and sits within the Digital Tanzania Project, [[world-bank]]-funded since 2021. Source: [[2026-04-22 Tanzania seeks biometrics contractors for Phase II of national digital ID project]].
