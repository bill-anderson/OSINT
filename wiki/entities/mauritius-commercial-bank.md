# Mauritius Commercial Bank (MCB)

Mauritius's largest commercial bank, whose **Juice** mobile app and **MauCAS QR** acceptance are a bellwether for
retail digital-payment adoption. Figures presented for **2025** (Stephanie Ng Tseung, Head of Payments):

- **QR-code payments +67%** year-on-year (via Juice and the central bank's **[[maucas|MauCAS]]** QR);
- contactless card payments +4% (cap raised to Rs 2,500; >6 in 10 card transactions);
- **Tap to Pay** emerging (1.4% of card transactions via the Juice wallet);
- **~18,000 MCB-affiliated merchants** accepting card or MauCAS QR by end-2025;
- **cash share of consumer spend fell from 30% to 27%** (by value);
- festive season (Nov–Dec 2025): 17.3M transactions (+13%); Black Friday ~399,000 in a day.
