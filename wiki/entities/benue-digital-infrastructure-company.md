# Benue Digital Infrastructure Company (BDIC)

State-run digital-infrastructure vehicle of **Benue State, Nigeria**, chaired (2024) by Deputy
Governor **Sam Ode**, driving the state's ambition to become a "digital hub" — sub-national
digitalisation ([[digital.localgov]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **2024-07-12** — signed an MoU with [[unicloud-africa]] to build a data centre in **Makurdi** (with
  an [[africa-data-centres]] disaster-recovery site in Abuja). Source:
  [[2024-07-12 UniCloud Africa and Benue Digital Infrastructure to build data center in Makurdi]].
