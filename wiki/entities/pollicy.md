# Pollicy

Kampala-based feminist civic-technology and data organisation working on data governance, DPI and
digital rights across Africa.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Afro-feminist DPI analysis (2026-01).** With the African Internet Rights Alliance (AIRA), argued DPI
  in Uganda/Kenya/South Africa is engineered for **state-centric** goals (revenue, surveillance) and
  reproduces gendered/minority exclusion absent "gender-by-design" governance — shutdowns and spyware
  (Uganda), ethnic-minority citizenship precarity and protest-era tracking via Safaricom (Kenya), the
  "Thandi" universal-user critique of MyMzansi (South Africa). See [[dpi.id]], [[include.divides]].
  Source: [[2026-01-01 Systemic Barriers Gendered Consequences An Afro-feminist Analysis of DPI]].
