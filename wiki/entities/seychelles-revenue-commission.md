# Seychelles Revenue Commission (SRC)

Seychelles' tax and customs authority, pursuing a digital-transformation plan across trade facilitation
and revenue. Launched the **electronic Certificate of Origin (e-CO)** with COMESA (Jul 2026) and set
**cheque thresholds** en route to a full **cheque phase-out by 31 Dec 2028**. See
[[seychelles--dpi-govtech]], [[seychelles--dpi-pay]], [[SYC]].
