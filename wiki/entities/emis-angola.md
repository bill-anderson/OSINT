# EMIS (Empresa Interbancária de Serviços)

Angola's **interbank payments switch** — operator of the national payment system, the **Multicaixa** card/ATM/POS network and the **[[kwik-angola|KWiK]]** instant-payment system (built with the **[[bna-angola|BNA]]**). The backbone of Angola's bank-centric digital-payments rails.

## Activity in this wiki

- **KWiK instant payments scaling fast.** KWiK processed **35m transfers / 590bn kwanzas in 2025** (>100% growth y/y); **22 banking and non-banking institutions** in the ecosystem; roadmap adds QR, service, state and merchant payments — a pillar of the National Financial Inclusion Strategy (ENIF 2025-2027). See [[kwik-angola]], [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Sources: [[2026-02-03 Angola - Operations With Payment App Kwik Grow Over 100 Percent]], [[2026-01-29-operacoes-no-kwik-crescimento-acima-dos-100]].
- **Market structure (Feb 2026 monthly report).** EMIS's report shows a wallet-led, concentrated KWiK: **[[paypay-angola|Paypay]] 52.2%**, BAI 21.9%, **[[unitel-money|Unitel Money]] 12.6%**; **12.3m+ registered accounts**, ~24.8m active keys, 66.4% of transfers under 10,000 Kz. Source: [[2026-03-05-paypay-ultrapassa-16-milhoes-transferencias-no-kwik]].
- **ISO 20022 migration (completed 2026-07-10).** EMIS was a core operator in the **[[bna-angola|BNA]]**'s migration of domestic payments to ISO 20022, alongside BODIVA and SPTR participants. See [[gov.standards]]. Source: [[2026-07-16-bna-conclui-migracao-iso-20022]].
- **Multicaixa** remains the dominant card/merchant network; mobile-money ([[africell]]'s Afrimoney) is the newer inclusion layer beside it.
