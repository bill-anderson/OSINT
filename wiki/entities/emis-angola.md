# EMIS (Empresa Interbancária de Serviços)

Angola's **interbank payments switch** — operator of the national payment system, the **Multicaixa** card/ATM/POS network and the **KWiK** instant-payment system. The backbone of Angola's bank-centric digital-payments rails.

## Activity in this wiki

- **KWiK instant payments scaling fast.** KWiK processed **35m+ transactions / 590bn kwanzas in 2025** (>100% growth y/y); 22 banking and non-banking institutions in the ecosystem; roadmap adds QR, service, state and merchant payments — a pillar of the National Financial Inclusion Strategy (ENIF 2025-2027). See [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-02-03 Angola - Operations With Payment App Kwik Grow Over 100 Percent]].
- **Multicaixa** remains the dominant card/merchant network; mobile-money ([[africell]]'s Afrimoney) is the newer inclusion layer beside it.
