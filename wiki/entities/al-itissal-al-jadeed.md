# Al Itissal Al Jadeed

A **Libyan** identity/document-technology firm contracted to help **digitise Niger's passports and national
identity card** under **two MoUs signed 27 June 2025**. The choice of a fellow-African (Libyan) supplier over
a Western vendor fits Niger's and the **[[alliance-of-sahel-states|AES]]** framing of identity production as a
matter of technological independence and data sovereignty. *(Also rendered "Al Itissan Al-Jadeed" in some
reporting.)*
