# National Office for Identification (Office National d'Identification, ONI)

The Madagascar body managing **biometric enrolment** for the national digital-ID programme, working under the
**[[mg-ministry-digital-development|Ministry of Digital Development, Posts & Telecom]]**. Under
[[prodigy-madagascar|PRODIGY]], the ONI runs the pilot enrolment that began **27 January 2026** in the
Antsimondrano district, assigning each citizen a **Unique Identification Number (NUI)** from fingerprints,
facial image and iris. Operational planning references ~2,500 enrolment kits and 1,729 enrolment centres,
targeting 2m urban adults (Apr–Jun 2026) then ~8m rural residents.
