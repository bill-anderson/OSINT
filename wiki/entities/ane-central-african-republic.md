# Autorité Nationale des Élections (ANE)

The Central African Republic's **National Elections Authority**, responsible for the
biometric electoral register and the conduct of elections.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometric voter registration (2024–25).** ANE ran the biometric register revision ahead
  of CAR's first local elections in ~36 years (Bangui phase 24 Nov–7 Dec 2024; nationwide to
  mid-December), using a **[[genkey]]** system — ~3,000 tablets, ~1,469 agents; >70% of funds
  mobilised; supported by **[[undp]]**. Spokespeople: Théophile Momokoama, Saint-Régis
  Zoumiri. The exercise both re-verified 2020 registrants and enrolled first-timers.
- **ID-document dependency.** Registration requires a CNI, birth certificate or passport —
  all hard to obtain — so ANE fell back on verbal testimony from traditional chiefs; the
  finalisation of the electoral roll became entangled with the **[[al-madina]]** CNI price
  dispute. See [[caf--dpi-id]].
