# Magdi Amin

Managing Partner at **African Renaissance Ventures**; advocate for rebuilding Sudan's digital
infrastructure to enable humanitarian aid and economic recovery. Argues for digital public
infrastructure — notably [[mojaloop]]-based interoperable payments — to reduce Sudan's dependence on
a single provider ([[bank-of-khartoum]]'s Bankak) and to deliver cash transfers efficiently in a
fragile environment, working with the central bank and development partners. Interviewed on VoxDevTalks
(2025). See [[dpi.pay]].
