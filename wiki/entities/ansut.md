# ANSUT (National Universal Telecom Service Agency)

Côte d'Ivoire's **universal-service fund/agency** — the vehicle for rural and underserved connectivity,
previously "mentioned, not hubbed." Runs the rural-connectivity programmes (PNCR, "Nos villages
connectés") and frames connectivity as the base for digital and financial inclusion. Target:
**smartphone adoption from 41% to 80% by 2030** (APIDE 2026). Sources:
