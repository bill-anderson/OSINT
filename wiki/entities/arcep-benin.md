# ARCEP Benin

Benin's **electronic-communications and postal regulator**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory SIM registration (2025).** ARCEP ruled that SIM cards unregistered after 30 March 2025 would be **deactivated from 30 April 2025**, with daily operator SMS notifications — framed as anti-fraud/anti-cybercrime subscriber identification and to secure mobile-financial-services transactions. Reporting flagged that SIM-ID requires a strict data-protection framework (citing Mauritius cancelling its own scheme) — tying SIM registration to the digital-ID / data-protection nexus. See the [[BEN]] hub, [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2025-02-22 Benin - unregistered SIM cards to be deactivated from 30 April 2025]].
