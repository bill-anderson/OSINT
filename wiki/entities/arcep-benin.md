# ARCEP Benin

Benin's **electronic-communications and postal regulator** — SIM registration, tariff/quality regulation,
regional roaming and market statistics.

## Market snapshot (ARCEP 2025 report)

- Mobile-money penetration **88.09%** (**11.65m** active accounts, 31 Dec 2025); **MTN ~51%** of accounts
  and ~**83%** of revenue. Data prices fell **4,000 FCFA/GB (2016) → 995 FCFA (2023)** under ARCEP's
  tariff-capping role, though a rural access gap persists. Benin placed **4th in Africa (76 pts)** on the
  Cloudwards **2026 internet-freedom** ranking. Sources:
  [[2026-05-19-arcep-benin-essor-usages-numeriques]], [[2025-04-08-internet-benin-acces-qualite-cout]],
  [[2026-07-11-liberte-internet-afrique-benin-top-5]].
- Benin explored **Starlink** (talks led by then-minister Romuald Wadagni, 2025-02) — the same article
  carried **two irreconcilable 2023 internet-user figures**: ARCEP **7.0m (55.4%)** vs GSMA **3.8m (28%)**,
  both attributed, neither reconciled (different measurement bases). Source:
  [[2025-02-07-wearetech-benin-explores-starlink]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory SIM registration (2025).** ARCEP ruled that SIM cards unregistered after 30 March 2025 would be **deactivated from 30 April 2025**, with daily operator SMS notifications — framed as anti-fraud/anti-cybercrime subscriber identification and to secure mobile-financial-services transactions. Reporting flagged that SIM-ID requires a strict data-protection framework (citing Mauritius cancelling its own scheme) — tying SIM registration to the digital-ID / data-protection nexus. See the [[BEN]] hub, [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2025-02-22 Benin - unregistered SIM cards to be deactivated from 30 April 2025]].
- **BEN/SEN/TGO free roaming (2026-04-24).** [[gov.regional]] ARCEP Benin, with Senegal and Togo,
  launched community **free roaming** at the 23rd ARTAO AGM in Lomé (under ECOWAS reg. C/REG.21/12/17) —
  free incoming calls for the first 30 days, local rates outgoing — a building block of the West African
  single digital market. Sources: [[2026-04-26-benin-senegal-togo-roaming-sans-frontieres]],
  [[2026-04-27-techreviewafrica-senegal-togo-benin-free-roaming]].
- **5G market (2024→26).** [[infra.connect]] After [[mtn-benin]] launched Benin's first commercial 5G
  (Nov 2024), **Moov Africa Bénin** launched 5G (24 Apr 2026, seven cities) as the second operator — the
  underlying operator/ARCEP releases behind thin PR coverage. Source:
  [[2026-04-28-techafricanews-moov-africa-benin-5g-launch]].
