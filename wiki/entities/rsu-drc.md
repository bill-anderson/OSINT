# RSU (Registre Social Unique)

DR Congo's **single social register** — created by decree **25 Jul 2025**, targeting poor/vulnerable
households for social protection. A **second population register** running alongside ONIP's identity file.
Source: [[2025-07-27-adoption-decret-registre-social-unique-actualite]].
