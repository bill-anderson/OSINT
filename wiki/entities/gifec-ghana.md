# GIFEC (Ghana Investment Fund for Electronic Communications)

Ghana's **universal-access fund** (CEO Tanko Rashid-Computer) — finances connectivity in underserved
communities. Its **Rural Telephony Project** (RTP masts, in partnership with **[[huawei]]**) remains its top
priority for closing the rural digital divide. See [[ghana--infra-connect]].
