# National Data Protection Commission (CNPD, Cabo Verde)

**Comissão Nacional de Protecção de Dados** — Cabo Verde's independent data-protection supervisory authority, established under the **[[data-protection-law-cabo-verde|Data Protection Law (Law 133/V/2001)]]**. Monitors and enforces data protection; may impose administrative fines (**CVE 50,000–3,000,000**) and ancillary measures (cessation, blocking, deletion or destruction of data). No case law yet on data infringements in a metaverse context.

## Activity in this wiki

- **A decade under review (2025).** President **Faustino Varela** marked the CNPD's tenth anniversary:
  Cabo Verde acceded to **Convention 108 and the Malabo Convention**, the CNPD has handled **deepfake cases
  since 2021**, and its 10th-anniversary international conference put **AI/Big-Data harms and constitutional
  rights** on the agenda (the PM speaking on AI harms). Sources:
  [[2025-02-02-cnpd-faustino-varela-ia-big-data-direitos-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2025-03-19-cnpd-dialogo-governanca-dados-regulacao-ia-expressodasilhas]].
- **Surveillance-access debate (2026).** The **Judicial Police (PJ) sought inter-institutional access to
  the national CCTV (videovigilância) system** (backed by the Justice Minister; system expanding across
  islands) — a surveillance-governance question under the CNPD's regime. Source:
  [[2026-05-13-pj-acesso-videovigilancia-investigacao-criminal-anacao]].
