# National Data Protection Commission (CNPD, Cabo Verde)

**Comissão Nacional de Protecção de Dados** — Cabo Verde's independent data-protection supervisory authority, established under the **[[data-protection-law-cabo-verde|Data Protection Law (Law 133/V/2001)]]**. Monitors and enforces data protection; may impose administrative fines (**CVE 50,000–3,000,000**) and ancillary measures (cessation, blocking, deletion or destruction of data). No case law yet on data infringements in a metaverse context.
