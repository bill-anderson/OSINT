# Paysmart (Tunisia payments platform)

**A Tunisian payments platform integrated with the national digital identity system
[[e-houwiya]], credited by the [[central-bank-of-tunisia]] as one of the principal enablers of the
country's 2025 mobile-payments expansion.** The single source held names the integration and its
function — secure, seamless authentication for users — but does not describe Paysmart's ownership,
operator, launch date or architecture; this page records what is evidenced and no more.

## Activity in this wiki

- **E-Houwiya integration named as a growth enabler (reported 2026-02-23).** In its 2025 payments
  bulletin the [[central-bank-of-tunisia]] attributed what it called "explosive growth" — mobile
  payments and transfers up **81%** year-on-year in **2025**, on **8.4 million** transactions worth
  **TND 1,769 million** — to deliberate financial-inclusion policy, of which "among the most
  significant enablers was the integration of the national E-Houwiya digital identity system with
  the Paysmart platform, enabling secure, seamless authentication for users." Paysmart is thus
  positioned as the point where Tunisia's identity layer meets its payments layer. See [[dpi.pay]],
  [[dpi.id]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-23 Central Bank Of Tunisia Reports 81% Surge In Mobile Payments As Tunisia Accelerates Its Digital Transformation In 2025]].
