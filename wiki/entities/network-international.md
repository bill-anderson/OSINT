# Network International

Dubai-based Middle East and Africa digital-payments processor. Provides
end-to-end card/payment processing, issuing and Mastercard sponsorship services
to banks across the MEA region.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Entry into Sudan via Saudi Sudanese Bank (2026-01).** Signed a strategic
  processing agreement with **[[saudi-sudanese-bank]]** — its first move into
  Sudan — to provide end-to-end digital payment processing including
  **[[mastercard]]** sponsorship, prepaid issuing and value-added services.
  Formalised at Cairo ICT 2025 and framed as modernising Sudan's wartime payment
  ecosystem and improving trade settlement. An external Gulf/MEA actor entering
  Sudan's rebuild. See [[dpi.pay]], [[finance.mou]], [[geopol.gulf]], [[SDN]].
