# CAIMSA (Centre for AI and Multidisciplinary Solutions in Africa)

A pan-African organisation (CEO **Baraka Matinde**) whose stated mission is to advance
AI in Africa through research, innovation, entrepreneurship, capacity building and
partnerships. Surfaced in this wiki via a Tanzanian-media announcement; its base country
and institutional backing are **not specified** in the source.

> **Profile is a stub / capture-is-not-endorsement.** The only activity on record is an
> aspirational announcement with no timeline, partners, location, funding figure or specs.

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Africa AI Data Centre" vision (2026-07).** CAIMSA announced plans for a
  **multi-million-dollar AI data centre** to close Africa's **compute-access gap** —
  advanced **GPU** resources, connectivity, reliable power, secure storage and AI research
  environments for universities, startups, governments and industry — framed as building a
  "more inclusive and self-reliant African AI ecosystem," "from Africa, for Africa." No
  implementation timeline, strategic partners, host country, investment figure or technical
  specs were disclosed ("to be announced as the project progresses"). Sits within the wider
  **compute/intelligence-divide** and sovereign-AI-infrastructure themes. See [[tech.ai]],
  [[infra.store]], [[infra.capacity]], [[include.divides]], [[XAF]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 CAIMSA unveils Africa AI data centre plan]].
