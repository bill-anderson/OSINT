**Parties:** [[liquid-c2]] (a business of [[cassava-technologies]] / Liquid Intelligent Technologies) and [[google]] Cloud.

**What:** A strategic MoU-based partnership to deliver Google Cloud data-analytics/AI, cybersecurity and collaboration services to enterprise, public-sector and SME customers across Africa.

**When / where:** Founding MoU **signed 10 November 2023** (announced 14 Nov 2023), pan-African scope. Extended in **June 2024** when Liquid C2 became the first Google Cloud **Interconnect** provider on the continent.

**Value:** Not disclosed; positioned under Google's **$1 billion** commitment to Africa's digital economy.

**Status:** Active and expanding.

## Scope

The original 2023 MoU set out three solution areas:
1. **Data & AI** — analytics and AI/ML tools, breaking down data silos (Google Cloud data-analytics and generative-AI stack).
2. **Cybersecurity** — Google's own security tooling for customers, including **Chronicle** and **Mandiant**.
3. **Collaboration** — Google Workspace enhanced with generative AI (Duet AI).

Liquid C2 CEO **Oswald Jumira** tied the partnership to Cassava's pan-African data-centre and fibre footprint and its multi-cloud strategy; Google Cloud's Umesh Vemuri framed it as accelerating digital enablement of African businesses and governments.

## Hybrid network / Interconnect milestone (June 2024)

Liquid C2 became the **first Google Cloud Interconnect provider in Africa**, offering customers a **private, high-availability, low-latency direct connection** to the Google Cloud network via its Cloud Connect portfolio. This reduces reliance on the public internet, improving performance and security for wholesale, enterprise and government customers.

## Context

This partnership is distinct from, but related to, the separate 2024 [[google-cassava-90m-round]] equity investment. It also connects to the earlier Liquid C2 × Google Cloud × Anthropic collaboration bringing advanced cloud, cybersecurity and generative-AI capabilities to Africa. As a US-hyperscaler dependency embedded in African connectivity, it carries a **geopol.usa** dimension.
