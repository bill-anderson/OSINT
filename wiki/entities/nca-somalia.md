# NCA (National Communications Authority, Somalia)

Somalia's telecom/communications regulator (DG **Mustafa Yasiin Sheikh**). Licensed **[[starlink]]**
(Apr 2025) after ~2.5 years of talks, runs the national **IPv6** migration (National IPv6 Center,
Jamhuriya University), and convened Somalia's first **Submarine Cable Landing Technical Working Group**
with the IFC (Broadband Infrastructure Project). See [[somalia--infra-connect]], [[SOM]].
