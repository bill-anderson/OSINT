# Unitel Money (USPM)

The mobile-money / payment-service arm of Angolan incumbent **[[unitel]]**, operating as **USPM** — a bank-adjacent participant in the **[[kwik-angola|KWiK]]** instant-payment ecosystem.

## Activity in this wiki

- **KWiK participant (Feb 2026).** USPM/Unitel Money handled **395,505 KWiK transactions** in February 2026 for a **12.6% share** — third behind [[paypay-angola|Paypay]] (52.2%) and BAI (21.9%). See [[kwik-angola]], [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-03-05-paypay-ultrapassa-16-milhoes-transferencias-no-kwik]].
