# VENEEM

Burkina Faso's **digital civil-registration (CRVS) platform** for the systematic declaration of civil-status events — capturing births and other events at their point of occurrence (health facilities), on PC or smartphone, with notifications and level-by-level validation under the Code des personnes et de la famille.

## Activity in this wiki

- Underpins the **unique identifier (identifiant unique)** and secure civil-status documents — the CRVS foundation beneath Burkina's ID stack (WURI enrolment and the **[[cib-aes]]** card). Directed by Commissaire principal Ousmane Diallo Nacanabo. See [[burkina-faso--dpi-id]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2024-11-13 Burkina Faso - digitalisation of birth declarations and civil status (VENEEM)]].
- **CRVS baseline (2024–25).** [[data.statistics]] The national civil-registration strategy (SNEC) hit
  **64.11%** physical execution in 2024 — **3,391,907 acts digitised across 54 communes**, 145,655 birth
  certificates issued; by end-2025 **30 communes held fully digitised registers**, and a 2025 objectives
  contract delivered **121,000 civil-status acts to internally displaced persons** (nearly 2× the 65,000
  target) — civil registration as a resilience tool in a security crisis. Sources:
  [[2025-06-19-snec-taux-execution-physique-6411-2024]],
  [[2026-02-02-contrat-objectifs-121000-actes-etat-civil-deplaces]].
