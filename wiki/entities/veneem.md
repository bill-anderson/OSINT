# VENEEM

Burkina Faso's **digital civil-registration (CRVS) platform** for the systematic declaration of civil-status events — capturing births and other events at their point of occurrence (health facilities), on PC or smartphone, with notifications and level-by-level validation under the Code des personnes et de la famille.

## Activity in this wiki

- Underpins the **unique identifier (identifiant unique)** and secure civil-status documents — the CRVS foundation beneath Burkina's ID stack (WURI enrolment and the **[[cib-aes]]** card). Directed by Commissaire principal Ousmane Diallo Nacanabo. See [[burkina-faso--dpi-id]], [[dpi.registry]].
