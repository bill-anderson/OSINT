# VaulFi

Fintech startup (co-founders Safa Korti, CEO; Karim Khattaby, CTO) building a **stablecoin-powered super-app
for North Africa** (2026-02) — payments, savings and transfers on stablecoin rails, positioned against the
region's cash dependence and currency constraints. Sits in tension with Algeria's state-led "digital dinar" /
financial-sovereignty framing. See [[algeria--dpi-pay]].
