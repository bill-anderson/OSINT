# Liberia Telecommunications Authority (LTA)

Liberia's telecommunications regulator, responsible for regulating the sector, attracting
investment and supporting the country's digital agenda. Acting Chairperson: Commissioner
**Patrick R. Honnah**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Lead on the Digital Liberia strategy (~2026-02-03).** The LTA is a lead body, alongside the
  Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, on the [[digital-liberia-strategy]] 2025–2029. At
  the launch, acting chair Honnah framed the plan as timely direction as technology reshapes
  economies and reaffirmed the LTA's commitment to collaboration, arguing a more modern ministry
  would enhance regulation, attract investment and deliver benefits to citizens. Tags
  [[gov.legislate]], [[infra.connect]], [[gov.policy]], [[LBR]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-03 Liberia Unveils 5-Year 'Digital Liberia' Strategy to Boost Sovereignty and Innovation]].
