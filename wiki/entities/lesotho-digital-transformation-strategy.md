# Lesotho National Digital Transformation Strategy (Agenda 2030) & National Digital Policy 2024

Lesotho's twin 2024 digital-governance instruments — the **National Digital Transformation Strategy:
Agenda 2030** and the **National Digital Policy 2024** — the policy frame under which Lesotho pursues DPI
(X-Road interoperability, MOSIP digital ID), LeSwitch payments, NISSA social protection and 50-in-5
commitments ([[gov.policy]], [[dpi.govtech]]). Led by MICSTI ([[micsti-lesotho]]). See [[LSO]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Strategy + policy (2024).** Set out Lesotho's digital-transformation vision to 2030 (connectivity,
  digital government, digital ID, data). Sources:
  [[2024-01-01 Lesotho National Digital Transformation Strategy - Agenda 2030]],
  [[2024-01-01 Lesotho National Digital Policy 2024]].
