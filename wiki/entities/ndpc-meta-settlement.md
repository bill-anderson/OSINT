# NDPC–Meta settlement & M-SIDP

A **2025 court-approved settlement** between Nigeria's **[[ndpc]]** and
**[[meta]]** over Meta's handling of Nigerian users' data, giving rise to the
**Meta-Supported Initiatives for Data Protection (M-SIDP)** — a **two-year**
Meta-funded programme (launched 2026-06).

- **Four focus areas:** governance/research/institutional development; platform
  safety & sustainability; DPO and Compliance-Organisation capacity-building;
  public-awareness campaigns (children, older persons, underserved communities).
- **Basis:** Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023. Meta's funding amount not disclosed.
