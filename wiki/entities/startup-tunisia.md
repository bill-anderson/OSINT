# Startup Tunisia

**Tunisia's national startup initiative — the vehicle credited with taking Tunisian firms into
foreign markets through its Global Program.** It appears here through a single roundup, which
carries its headline expansion figure and situates it in Tunisia's wider innovation-financing
picture; no founding date, legal basis or operator is established in any source held.

## Activity in this wiki

- **International expansion (as of 2025-03).** **Over 40 firms** had expanded across **Europe, MENA
  and Sub-Saharan Africa** via the **Startup Tunisia Global Program**. See [[tech.innovate]],
  [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2025-03-01 Tunisia drives digitization agenda forward while navigating risks]].
- **The financing context it sits in (as of 2025-03).** Collaborations with the **EU**, the **AfDB**
  and accelerators such as **Flat6Labs** and **Seedstars** supported fintech, agritech and healthtech
  innovation; **fintech raised US$150 million**. See [[finance.new]], [[dpi.pay]], [[geopol.eu]].
  Source: [[2025-03-01 Tunisia drives digitization agenda forward while navigating risks]].
- **Adjacent bilateral cooperation (discussed, as of 2025-03).** Tunisia and **China** discussed
  digital transformation cooperation (telecom infrastructure and AI); Tunisia and **Italy** explored
  collaboration on innovation and startups. Discussions only — no agreement is reported. See
  [[geopol.china]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source:
  [[2025-03-01 Tunisia drives digitization agenda forward while navigating risks]].
