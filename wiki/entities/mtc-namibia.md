# MTC (Mobile Telecommunications Company)

Namibia's largest, state-owned **mobile network operator**. Beyond connectivity, its **mobile-money** service
is used to deliver **UNHCR cash-based assistance** to refugees at the Osire settlement — a financial-inclusion
channel for an otherwise excluded population. MTC's rural network (e.g. its Kunene towers) also features in
Namibia's connectivity/device-affordability debate, where regulator CRAN reported 4G utilisation below 50%.
