# IGCA / land-and-property registry platform — Angola

The **Instituto de Gestão Cadastral de Angola (IGCA)** and the digital land-and-property
registry platform being built around it — Angola's move to bring urban housing into the
formal register. Anchors the *land/address registry* strand of [[dpi.registry]] alongside
the civil-identity strand.

## Current state (2026-04)

- The Executive set a target to **legalise 40,000 properties by end-2026**, via a new
  **digital platform** built in partnership with the tax authority
  (**[[agt-angola|AGT]]**) that links **IGCA, AGT, the
  *cadastro matricial* and the Conservatória do Registo Predial** (property registry). The
  aim: cut duplication, speed up sale/registration acts, and support mortgage access.
  Spokesperson: Paulo Tecas. Source:
  [[2026-04-17-plataforma-digital-legalizacao-de-40-mil-imoveis]].
- Reads as an interoperability/data-exchange play across previously siloed
  land-administration bodies — the property-register counterpart to the BDIC↔BDCM
  integration on the civil-identity side. See **[[angola--dpi-registry]]**.
