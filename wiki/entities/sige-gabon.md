# SIGE (Education Management Information System, Gabon)

Gabon's **Système d'Information pour la Gestion de l'Éducation** — the education-sector MIS (EMIS)
being built out by the Ministry of National Education as its planning, management and
monitoring-evaluation backbone.

## Key facts

- **Data-collection push (Mar 2025).** A 10 March 2025 working session under Minister of State
  Carmelita Ntoutoume Leclercq made school-data collection a priority; the **[[world-bank]] supplied
  tablets** and **UNESCO proposed field-collection software**, with data-protection safeguards flagged
  and consolidation of existing exam-management and school-asset databases identified as the gap
  ([[2025-03-11-gabon-school-data-collection-priority]]).
- **Health-indicator integration (Aug 2025).** The Ministry began integrating **school health and
  well-being indicators** into the SIGE, enabling pedagogical data to be crossed with health
  indicators (hygiene, nutrition, water access, safety) to identify determinants of school performance
  ([[2025-08-09-gabon-school-health-sige-integration]]).

Gabon's sectoral MIS effort, parallel to the whole-of-government DPI under [[digital-gabon-project]].
