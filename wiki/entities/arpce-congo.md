# ARPCE (Postal and Electronic Communications Regulatory Agency)

The Republic of Congo's **posts and electronic-communications regulator** — also a builder/operator of state data-centre infrastructure. DG: Louis-Marc Sakala.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Pointe-Noire data centre + 2Africa landing (2024).** ARPCE built the Pointe-Noire data centre commissioned alongside Congo's activation of the **2Africa** subsea cable (28 Feb 2024) — the state's second DC (after Brazzaville 2021), for "sovereign data" storage/security (CFA3.8bn, 54 racks). See [[infra.store]]. Source: [[2024-03-11 Republic of Congo launches Pointe-Noire data centre for 2Africa cable]].
- Source of Congo's connectivity statistics (3.4m internet subscribers / 59.7%, 2023).
