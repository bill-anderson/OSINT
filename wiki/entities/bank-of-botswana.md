# Bank of Botswana

Botswana's **central bank**, leading the digitisation of government payments and revenue collection.

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Digital BoB2024" (2025).** The Bank of Botswana reported steady progress digitising government revenue collection and payments under its Digital BoB2024 initiative — the central-bank-led strand of the country's digital-payments agenda.
