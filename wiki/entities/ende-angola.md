# Angola National Statistics Development Strategy 2026–2032 (ENDE)

Angola's **Estratégia Nacional de Desenvolvimento da Estatística** for the **2026–2032 cycle** — the planning instrument meant to strengthen the country's statistical capacity and production and bring more actors into the **National Statistical System**.

## Profile

- **Status (2026-06-12).** Announced by INE board chair **Joel Futi** at the 6th ANGOTIC forum as expected to be **approved by 2032** (i.e. for the 2026–2032 cycle); the first session of the **Conselho Nacional de Estatística** was scheduled for the following month to receive both the strategy and a proposed **revision of the statistics law**. Paired with a new online National User Satisfaction Survey.
- Runs alongside INE's broader modernisation ([[ine-angola]] — Censo 2024 portal/app, ArcGIS/Field Maps field methods). See [[data.statistics]], [[gov.policy]]. Source: [[2026-06-12-nova-estrategia-de-desenvolvimento-da-estatistica-2026-2032]].
