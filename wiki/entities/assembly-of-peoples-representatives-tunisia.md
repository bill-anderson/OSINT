# Assembly of People's Representatives (ARP), Tunisia

Tunisia's **legislature** (Assemblée des Représentants du Peuple / مجلس نواب الشعب) — where the country's
digital-governance legal frameworks are being written and revised.

- **Data-protection organic law (Feb 2026).** The ARP's **Rights and Freedoms Committee** (chair **Thabet
  Abed**) reviewed the draft opinion on the 123-article personal-data-protection bill. Source:
  [[2026-02-20-africanmanager-data-protection-law-parliament]].
- **E-invoicing amendment (Feb 2026).** The Finance & Budget Committee and the Administration/Digitisation/
  Governance & Anti-Corruption Committee held a joint session on bill 2026/012 amending **Article 53** of
  the 2026 Finance Law (compulsory e-invoicing). Source:
  [[2026-02-10-assabah-parliament-e-invoicing-law-amendment]].
