# MOIEG-PASS (Egypt Interior Ministry biometric verification app)

Egypt's **Ministry of Interior** biometric-verification app for citizens accessing **government digital services** — described as the country's first unified national platform of its kind (Android pilot). Users authenticate by **scanning the national ID card + facial recognition** (technology described as Egyptian-developed), registering with mobile number and national-ID details; features include identity verification, request tracking and notifications. The government-services identity gateway, distinct from the CBE's **[[haweya]]** financial-ID app; part of the **[[digital-egypt]]** transformation.
