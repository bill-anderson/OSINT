# Bayobab Infra Solutions (South Sudan)

MTN Digital Infrastructure's South Sudan fibre subsidiary. Holds a **15-year NCA licence** (eff. 16 Oct
2025) to build and operate national and cross-border fibre, IP transit and leased circuits (part of MTN's
**East 2 West** corridor). With **NCT South Sudan**, executing the **National Backbone Fiber Network
Project** linking the eastern and western corridors through Juba. See [[south-sudan--infra-connect]],
[[mtn-south-sudan]], [[SSD]].
