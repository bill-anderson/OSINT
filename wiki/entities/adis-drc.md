# ADIS (Africa Digital Innovation Summit)

DR Congo's flagship digital-innovation summit — **rebranded from the Africa Digital Expo** (previously run
with Morocco's One Africa) into a **wholly Congolese-run event under ANDIS**, a marker of the country's
regional digital-governance ambition. The 2025 edition featured Raïssa Malu on the AI-driven transformation
of the education system. Sources: [[2025-10-24-lancement-adis-4e-edition-actualite]],
[[2025-11-27-adis-2025-raissa-malu-education-numerique-actualite]].
