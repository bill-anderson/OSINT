# DPI Roadmap Playbook

A practitioner framework (March 2026 updated edition; beta June 2025) guiding governments to design
national DPI roadmaps across the three building blocks — digital identity, digital payments, data
exchange. Authored by a Gates-Foundation-funded consortium ([[africanenda]], Better Than Cash Alliance,
CDPI, [[dial]], Integral: Governance Solutions). Anchored on South Africa's Digital Transformation
Roadmap; introduces the "DPI House" choice model and a seven-step process. See [[dpi.exchange]].
