# Malawi Revenue Authority (MRA)

Malawi's national tax-collection agency, whose Electronic Invoicing System (EIS) has become a
working example of digital public infrastructure boosting domestic revenue mobilisation —
capturing transactions in real time to widen the tax base.

## Activity in this wiki

- **EIS digital tax gains (2026-06-03).** The EIS is now generating more than $1.2m/day, with
  May collections above ~$28m. Around 7,500 of ~9,000 registered VAT operators have migrated
  to the system, which replaced older Electronic Fiscal Devices and captures transactions in
  real time. Constraints cited: power blackouts, connectivity gaps and business resistance.
  Tags [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.mis]], [[data.statistics]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-03 Malawi reaps digital tax gains]].
- **Municipal counterpart — Blantyre local tax (2026-03-06).** At the sub-national level, the
  **[[blantyre-city-council]]** (Malawi's second city) partnered with NBS Bank to digitise
  council revenue and tax collection and enable electronic payment of council services, under the
  **Digital Malawi Acceleration Project** — a local-government complement to MRA's national EIS.
  See [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-06 Malawi's second city digitises tax collection]].
