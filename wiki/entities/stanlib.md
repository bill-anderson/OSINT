# STANLIB

South African asset manager; its **Infrastructure Fund II** (digital infrastructure, renewables, logistics)
is investing in data centres.

- **Africa Data Centres stake (2026).** CompCom recommended **unconditional approval** of STANLIB
  Infrastructure Fund II taking a **minority stake in Cassava ADC** ([[africa-data-centres]], a
  [[cassava-technologies]] unit; control of ADC Dev/ADC Sam), following an Oct-2025 partnership — accelerating
  **AI-ready** DC expansion in Johannesburg and Cape Town and deleveraging Liquid Intelligent Technologies.
