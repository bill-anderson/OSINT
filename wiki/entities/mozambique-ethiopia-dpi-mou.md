# Mozambique–Ethiopia DPI MoU

South-South DPI cooperation agreement signed in Addis Ababa in the week of 2026-04-28, for an initial three-year term.

- **Parties.** [[atdi-mozambique]] (chaired by Adilson Gomes) and Ethiopia's National ID Program ([[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]], executive director Yodahe Zemichael).
- **Scope.** Digital-ID platforms and authentication, DPI pilots, interoperability, capacity-building, and joint cybersecurity ([[dpi.id]], [[dpi.exchange]]).
- **Support.** NIDP's commercial arm FaydaVerse provides technical support; framed as [[sovereignty]]-driven South-South cooperation.
- **Status.** Active, initial three-year term (as of 2026-04-28).

Source: [[2026-04-28 Mozambique taps Ethiopia's digital ID expertise in DPI partnership]].

An in-scope Club of Mozambique account (27 Apr, Addis Ababa signing) confirms **ATDI chair Adilson Gomes**
framing digital identity as key to financial inclusion and public-service access, with a commitment to
open-standard, interoperable, cybersecure ID platforms. See [[mozambique--dpi-id]]. Source:
[[2026-04-27-mozambique-ethiopia-mou-digital-identity-clubofmozambique]].
