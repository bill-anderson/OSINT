## Why it is on file

The deal itself is minimally documented — the held account discloses **no amount, no valuation, no
stake and no round stage** — but it is the first funding event on file for AI-driven alternative-data
credit scoring in Nigeria, a technique that turns mobile-app payment traces into an underwriting input
for borrowers with no bank record. The company's claim that the system has **supported eight million
loans in Nigeria (as of 2026-07-17)** is the scale figure attached to the round; it is a company
statement, undated as to start, and uncorroborated.
