# UAE AI-for-Development Initiative ($1bn)

A **$1bn** UAE fund to expand AI infrastructure and AI-enabled services across
Africa, announced by UAE Minister of State Saeed bin Mubarak Al Hajeri at the
**G20 leaders' summit in Johannesburg, 22 November 2025**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Scope.** Targets **education, healthcare and climate adaptation**. Context:
  UAE–Africa bilateral trade ~$107bn in 2024 (+28%); UAE building one of the
  world's largest data-centre hubs with US technology. See [[XAF]], [[tech.ai]],
  [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2025-11-22 UAE announces $1 billion initiative to expand AI in Africa]].
- Cited in [[2026-06-23 A Kenya Technology Prosperity Deal Could Help Washington Secure Durable AI Partnerships with Africa]] as evidence of the UAE's role at the
  intersection of US-aligned AI architecture and African AI development.

Distinct from the **$1bn Microsoft–G42 Kenya data centre**
([[microsoft-g42-kenya-datacenter]]) — both $1bn and UAE-linked, but separate.

See [[uae]], [[g42]], [[tech.ai]].
