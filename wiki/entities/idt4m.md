# IDT4M (Inclusive Digital Transformation for Malawi)

A **[[undp|UNDP]]**-led programme (with **EU** support, ~€10m) advancing Malawi's digital public
infrastructure on top of the country's near-universal foundational ID
(**[[national-registration-bureau-malawi|NRB]]**). IDT4M supports the National Registration and Identification
System foundation, a **Digital Readiness Assessment**, membership of the **50-in-5** DPI campaign, and a
catalogue of digital systems (identity, government services and data exchange) to move Malawi from initial DPI
deployment into operations and interoperable service delivery.
