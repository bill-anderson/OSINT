# Central Bank of Liberia (CBL)

Liberia's central bank. Launched a national mobile-money instant payment system in **73 days** (from a
November 2025 kick-off) on open-source [[mojaloop]], with a G2P use case and zero downtime — cited by
[[africanenda]] as evidence IPS can be stood up in months. See [[LBR]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
[[2026-03-01 AfricaNenda Annual Report 2025]].
