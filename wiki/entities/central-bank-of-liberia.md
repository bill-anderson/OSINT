# Central Bank of Liberia (CBL)

Liberia's central bank and payments regulator. It designed, launched and supervises the **Inclusive
Instant Payment System** — branded **[[pay-na-na]]** — Liberia's national mobile-money interoperability
rail, stood up in **73 days** on open-source [[mojaloop]] and cited by [[africanenda]] as evidence IPS
can be deployed in months. Executive Governor (2025–26): **Henry F. Saamoi**. See [[LBR]], [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **IIPS launch + operating agreements (2025-12).** The CBL signed Participation and Service Level
  Agreements (12 Dec 2025) with Lonestar Cell/MTN Mobile Money, Orange Money and switch operator
  ThitsaWorks, then officially **launched the IIPS on 16 December 2025**. Under the framework the CBL
  holds USD/LRD settlement accounts, runs daily settlement, and enforces SLA penalties; it credited the
  Gates Foundation, [[africanenda]], [[mojaloop]], ThitsaWorks and the [[world-bank]] (backing a full
  National Electronic Payment Switch). Sources:
  [[2025-12-15-cbl-formalizes-iips-agreements-observer]],
  [[2025-12-17-cbl-launches-iips-mobile-money-silos-fpa]].
- **Statutory independence preserved under EO 163 (2026-04).** [[gov.policy]] Liberia's
  [[otdi-liberia|EO 163 digital-government reform]] explicitly does not diminish the CBL's statutory
  independence. Source: [[2026-04-29-executive-order-163-digital-government-ndmi-observer]].
