# mytGPT (Mauritius Telecom sovereign AI platform)

**[[mauritius-telecom|Mauritius Telecom]]**'s enterprise-grade **sovereign AI platform**, presented as the
country's first, keeping data under Mauritian jurisdiction and running on MT's Tier IV data centre / GPU compute
(the "AI & Compute Corridor" of MT's 2026-2029 strategy). CEO **Veemal Gungadin** frames it around
**democratising AI access** ("an AI reserved for specialists is not an AI that truly serves a country").

**mytGPT Education** — an AI personal-tutor pilot launched **26 January 2026** (demonstrated at James Burty David
Secondary School; with the Ministry of Education) — is evolving toward **my.t Learn** and is folded into the
2026-2027 Budget's **50,000-trainee** AI-skilling plan (the MyT-GPT Education pilot scaling from 10 schools into
secondary). See [[mauritius--tech-ai]], [[capacity.training]].

*PR-wire provenance for the pilot launch; MT AI announcements at the AllMyT Summit (Jul 2026) await primary
confirmation — see `reviews/gaps.md` GAP-MUS-001.*
