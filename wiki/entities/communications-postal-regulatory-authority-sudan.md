# Communications and Postal Regulatory Authority (Sudan)

Sudan's telecom and postal regulator, coordinating with the digital-transformation
ministry and the central bank on the mobile/telecom side of digital financial
services.

## Activity in this wiki

- **USSD banking (2026-03).** Coordinated with the
  **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** and the
  **[[central-bank-of-sudan]]** on the launch of USSD-based banking (works
  without internet) as a wartime resilience/inclusion rail. See [[infra.connect]],
  [[dpi.pay]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-18 Sudan Launches USSD Banking Services to Expand Digital Financial Access]].
