# Wananchi Group (Holdings) Ltd

**A leading East African provider of pay TV, broadband and internet services**, operating
in **Kenya and Uganda** — best known for **Zuku** (retail broadband) and **Simbanet**
(enterprise connectivity). Since November 2025, **99.63% owned by [[yas]]**, a subsidiary
of [[axian-telecom]]. Group CEO: **Genue Mwaura**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Acquired by Yas / AXIAN (completed, announced 2025-11-10).** Yas completed the
  acquisition of **99.63% of Wananchi Group (Holdings) Ltd** for **approximately US$63
  million**, announced at a **Kampala press conference on 10 November 2025**; all country
  and regional regulatory approvals, **including [[comesa]]**, were completed. **No job
  losses**; **Zuku and Simbanet retain their brand identities**. Mwaura said the company
  now needs *"to move to the attacking side."* See [[axian-wananchi-acquisition]] for the
  deal. Source:
  [[2025-11-11 YAS completes $63m acquisition of parent company of Zuku and Simbanet]].
- **Planned investment (as of 2025-11-11).** Over the **next 12 to 24 months** Yas plans to
  **upgrade Wananchi's fibre network in Uganda** — speed, reliability and coverage —
  expanding **fibre-to-the-home (FTTH)** plus enterprise cloud services and IoT
  connectivity. Projection, not achievement. See [[infra.connect]]. Source: as above.
