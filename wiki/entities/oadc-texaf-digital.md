# OADC Texaf Digital

The **DRC's first Tier-III certified data centre** — a Kinshasa joint venture between **Open Access Data Centres (OADC)**, a WIOCC company, and Congolese group **TEXAF**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **First Tier-III DC (Aug 2024).** Open-access, carrier-neutral, ~2 MW-capable; Uptime Institute Tier-III certified (ISO 27001 targeted Q3 2024) — one of two Tier-III Kinshasa facilities that launched August 2024 (alongside the larger [[raxio-group]] DRC1). See [[infra.store]]. Source: [[2024-08-14 DRC's first Tier-III data centre goes live (OADC Texaf Digital, Kinshasa)]].
- **ST Digital partnership (Sep 2024).** Cameroon-HQ cloud provider **[[st-digital]]** entered the DRC by partnering with OADC Texaf for content delivery and cloud services. Source: [[2024-09-17 ST Digital expands into DRC, partners with OADC Texaf Kinshasa (press release)]].
