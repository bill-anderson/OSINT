# Smart Africa Data Exchange (SADX)

SADX is [[smart-africa]]'s federated, decentralised architecture for cross-border data exchange, designed to enable sovereign cross-border digital interactions between African states without centralising member-country data. It is Smart Africa's flagship vehicle for continental [[dpi.exchange]] interoperability, framed explicitly around data [[sovereignty]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Technical Assessment Mission in Kigali (2026-04-27).** Smart Africa convened a hands-on SADX mission in Kigali with [[RWA]], [[BEN]] and [[GHA]], moving from architecture to practical implementation. Work focused on PKI trust chains, API contracts, verification-request processing, certificate management and cross-jurisdiction incident response — the operational plumbing for sovereign cross-border exchange spanning [[dpi.id]], [[gov.standards]] and [[infra.cybersec]]. Source: [[2026-04-27 Smart Africa drives cross-border data interoperability through SADX Technical Mission]].
