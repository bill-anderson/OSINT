# General Information Authority (GIA, Libya)

Libya's national information/statistics and data-governance authority (also rendered General Authority
for Information), the Government of National Unity's lead on **government data governance, open data and
digital-transformation strategy** ([[gov.policy]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[data.open]]). Head (2025-26):
Dr Abdulbaset AlBaour. It runs an Open Data Platform (91 datasets), the LY-GOVREP government-reports
platform, and the Seventh National Human Development Report with UNDP. See [[LBY]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **LY-GOVREP government-reports platform (2025-07).** [[dpi.govtech]] Launched a unified digital
  platform for official government reports (60 Cabinet entities) with the Inspection and Follow-Up
  Department — supporting transparency and oversight. Source:
  [[2025-07-20 Libya's GIA launches national government-reports platform LY-GOVREP]].
- **Government Data Governance vision (2026-02).** [[dpi.exchange]] Presented "Government Data
  Governance Policies" at a GACI cloud-computing workshop, treating data as a strategic resource; had
  launched indicators covering 91 datasets via its Open Data Platform. Source:
  [[2026-02-16 Libya's GIA presents Government Data Governance vision at cloud-computing workshop]].
- **UNDP MoU (2025-07).** Cooperation on human development, data systems and the Seventh National Human
  Development Report. Source: [[2025-07-02 UNDP and Libya's GIA sign MoU on data systems and human development]].
