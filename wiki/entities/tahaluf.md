# Tahaluf

**A UAE-based IT company** — **the service provider for Uganda's new national digital ID
system** ([[ndaga-muntu]]), the system into which [[nira-uganda]] migrated more than 28
million identity records.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Prime provider for Uganda's new national ID (as of 2026-02-11).** Tahaluf is named as
  the service provider for the new national ID system. **Five modules are
  [[mosip]]-based** — ID registration, renewals, updating and correction, issuance, and
  lost-ID replacement. Uganda's other technology providers include **[[tech5]],
  Technoforte, Entrust and Hyf** (per NIRA ED [[rosemary-kisembo]] at MOSIP Connect 2026).
  See [[dpi.id]], [[geopol.gulf]], [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-11 Uganda migrates more than 28M records to new national ID system]].

**Worth noting for the sovereignty read:** an open-source core ([[mosip]]) integrated by a
**Gulf-based** prime — the vendor-lock-in problem MOSIP was designed to defeat reappears
one layer up, at the integrator. The source does not argue this; capture is not
endorsement.
