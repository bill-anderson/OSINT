# Ministry of Human Resources and Social Welfare (Sudan)

Sudan's ministry for human resources and social welfare, owner of the
civil-service HR modernisation and the **[[unified-social-registry-sudan]]** —
the social-protection data infrastructure for targeting vulnerable groups.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Social registry + civil-service system (2026-04).** Minister **Muatasim
  Ahmed Saleh** met the **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** minister to
  review a new civil-service (HR) system and progress on the unified social
  registry, framed as building data-driven modern institutions for post-war
  recovery. See [[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-19 Ministerial meeting on digital transformation, civil-service reform and unified social registry (SUNA)]].
