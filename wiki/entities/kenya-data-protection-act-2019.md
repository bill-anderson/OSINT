# Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019

Kenya’s omnibus data-protection statute, enforced by the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner
([[odpc-kenya]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Amendment push (2026-03).** Reform is driven by implementation gaps and the 2023 WorldCoin
  parliamentary inquiry — proposals include requiring foreign companies to prove local registration
  before processing, a data-protection appeals tribunal, and repealing the s.54 exemption power. See
  [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-12 Africa’s Data Protection Reforms A Continental Perspective on the Drivers of Change in Legal Frameworks]].
