# SMART Zambia–Huawei National AI Data Centre MoU

An MoU (2026 Zambia Mobile Congress, Lusaka) between the **[[smart-zambia-institute]]** and **[[huawei]]**
for **AI infrastructure, sovereign cloud hosting, DPI support for 25 ministries, and a co-invested national
data centre** keeping critical data in-country, plus **~5,000 youth** trained via the Huawei Global Academy
(3 years). VP Nalumango backing.

- **The sovereignty paradox.** Framed as a **digital-sovereignty** move — yet delivered by a Chinese vendor
  with a 2-decade footprint (Huawei built the earlier Zambia National Data Centre), the `colonialism` lens
  the wiki flags on Chinese-supplied "sovereign" infrastructure. Ties to the year-end national digital ID
  (DZAP). See [[ZMB]], [[geopol.china]]. Source: [[2026-06-08-zambia-huawei-ai-sovereignty-partnership]].
