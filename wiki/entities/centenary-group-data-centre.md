# Centenary Group Data Centre (Masaka)

**A green Tier 3 data centre under construction in Masaka City, Uganda**, developed by
[[centenary-group]] through Centenary Technology Services. Billed (2025-10-03) as
**Uganda's first Green Tier 3 Data Centre**.

## Current state

**Official launch: Q1 2026** (as of **2025-10-03**) — rebased from a **completion in late
2025** target (as of **2025-08-11**). Same publisher, same facility: this is
**supersession, not contradiction**.

*Worth one line: the October piece describes the rebased schedule as **"on schedule"**
without acknowledging the earlier commitment — a rhetorical tell, not a source conflict.*

## Design and purpose

- **40% less power** than conventional data centres, through innovative cooling and power
  management; targeting **Tier 3 certification**; built with technology designed for
  African conditions.
- **Open-access connectivity** — all internet service providers can offer services through
  the facility, promoting competition. Cloud-ready services.
- **To host critical government systems** alongside enterprise and financial services (as
  of 2025-10-03), extending its reach to the wider East African region.
- Construction sourced most supplies and materials from **local Masaka businesses**,
  strengthening supply chains and building technical capacity.
