# NITA Transfert (Niger)

Niger's leading money-transfer / fintech operator, increasingly a **connective tissue between digital identity
and digital payments**.

- **CNIB-AES enrolment kiosks (2026-04).** In the AES biometric-ID mass-enrolment drive, NITA deployed
  **payment kiosks** at Niamey commissariats (from Yantala, 25 Apr 2026) so citizens could pay the **4,500-FCFA**
  enrolment fee on the spot and receive the receipt needed for their file — the payments/logistics layer of the
  ID rollout. See [[niger-national-eid]], [[niger--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-27-cnib-aes-enrolment-nita-payment-kiosks-actuniger]].
- **Bank-to-wallet interoperability with Banque Atlantique (2026-06).** NITA partnered **Banque Atlantique
  Niger** to enable **instant bank↔wallet transfers**, positioning NITA as a fintech interoperability actor. See
  [[dpi.pay]], [[finance.mou]]. Source: [[2026-06-29-banque-atlantique-nita-bank-to-wallet-interoperability-lesahel]].
