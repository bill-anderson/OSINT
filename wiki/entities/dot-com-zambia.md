# Dot Com Zambia PLC

Zambian technology firm (founded 2009; CEO **Mawano Kambeu**) providing payment systems, revenue-collection
platforms and digital business solutions (**eToll**, **eLevy**) — one of the **first local tech firms listed
on the Lusaka Securities Exchange**.

- **Digital-economy PPP (May 2026).** Met Minister of Technology & Science **Felix Mutati** on building on
  government DPI (Government Service Bus, PACRA integration) and a national digital-ID **"single source of
  truth"** for KYC/fraud/social protection (Ghana lessons). See [[zambia--dpi-pay]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-14-zambia-accelerate-digital-economy-ppp]].
