# INS (National Institute of Statistics, Congo-Brazzaville)

Congo-Brazzaville's national statistics office. Launched an **ILO-backed quarterly labour-force survey — a
national first** — harmonising to ICLS standards. Against that improvement sits an admitted collapse in
statistical production elsewhere: **BEAC's balance-of-payments data-collection rate fell from 98% (2024,
136 declarants) to 41.81% for 2025**. Sources: [[2026-07-11-statistiques-marche-du-travail-bit-ins-ldb]],
[[2026-04-22-beac-balance-des-paiements-collecte-donnees-ldb]].
