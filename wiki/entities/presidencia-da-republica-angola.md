# Presidência da República — Angola

The Angolan presidency as an **institution of budget and regulatory authority**, distinct from
President **[[joao-lourenco]]** personally. Minted 2026-07-23 by lint (9 referrers) — the slug had
been tagged as the authorising party on Angolan expenditure records without a page behind it.

## Why it matters here — the executive instrument is where the money surfaces

Angola's digital spending is legible less through its budget tables than through **presidential
decrees and despachos**, which name an amount, an órgão and a purpose in a way the enacted volumes
do not (and which, unlike the volumes, are published as text). Two instrument types recur:

- **`Decreto Presidencial`** — opens a *crédito adicional suplementar* inside an enacted budget,
  under the Lei-Quadro do OGE (Lei 15/10, arts. 26.º–27.º). The Assembly does not re-vote a
  *revisão*; the executive appropriates in-year. Worked example: **DP 169/25** (19-Sep-2025),
  **USD 218 500 000** for the *Plano Estratégico para a Universalização do Bilhete de Identidade*,
  assigned to [[ministerio-da-justica-e-dos-direitos-humanos-angola|MINJUSDH]] — the largest single
  dated domestic commitment the wiki holds to Angolan digital ID.
  Source: [[2025-09-19-ago-dp-169-25-bilhete-identidade-credito-adicional]].
- **`Despacho Presidencial`** — authorises a specific expenditure and opens (or waives) its
  procurement, under Lei 41/20 (Contratos Públicos). These carry the vendor and the procurement
  route, which the vote structure never does. Held examples: **DP 12/26** (Kz 9,2 mil milhões,
  telecoms and hosting for [[ministerio-das-financas-angola|MINFIN]]); **DP 11/26** (Kz 8,6 mil
  milhões, **Microsoft** licences for the whole public administration via [[ima-angola|IMA]]);
  **DP 38/26** (US$150m, the IMA–[[tis-angola|TIS]] "Digitais Públicas" DPI build); a 2026 despacho
  of Kz 76 mil milhões for the industry ministry's network, by *ajuste directo*; and **DP 206/24**,
  which authorised the **€225m Société Générale** financing for the ANGEO-1 satellite.

Two consequences the wiki records rather than infers. **Stage is often `unclear`**: a despacho
authorising expenditure is not an appropriation line, a treasury release or an outturn, and names no
stage in the budget-cycle vocabulary. And **sole-source awards recur** — several of the largest
digital instruments are direct awards or unspecified procurement routes, noted on
[[angola--dpi-govtech]].

The presidency is also the **regulator by decree**: the National Cybersecurity Strategy (DP 256/25),
the Conselho Nacional de Cibersegurança (DP 258/25) and the
[[centro-nacional-de-ciberseguranca-angola|Centro Nacional de Cibersegurança]] (DP 263/25) all
entered force by presidential decree while the cybersecurity *bill* stalled in parliament — the
executive track outrunning the legislative one. See [[angola--infra-cybersec]].
