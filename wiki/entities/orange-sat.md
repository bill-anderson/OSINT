# Orange Sat (Côte d'Ivoire satellite internet)

Orange Sat is [[orange-cote-divoire]]'s satellite-internet service, launched in January 2026 to
extend connectivity to remote and underserved areas — the "white areas" beyond existing 4G and
fibre coverage — for households, businesses and public institutions.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched 2026-01 as a strategic partnership with [[eutelsat]].** Orange Sat uses Eutelsat's
  KONNECT satellite and complements [[orange-cote-divoire]]'s fibre and mobile-broadband
  portfolio. Positioned as a GEO satellite play in Africa's broader LEO-vs-GEO connectivity mix
  (cf. [[starlink]]), it targets economic development and access to education, healthcare and
  digital administration in geographically hard-to-serve regions. Tags [[infra.connect]],
