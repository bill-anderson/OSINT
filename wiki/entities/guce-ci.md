# GUCE-CI (Single Window for Foreign Trade)

Côte d'Ivoire's **foreign-trade single window** — a concrete, in-use node of the national
interoperability layer.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Inter-agency data exchange with the DGI (Dec 2025).** GUCE-CI and the tax directorate (DGI) moved to
  exchange data directly — the interoperability layer put to work (trade window ↔ tax). Source:
  [[2025-12-23-guce-ci-dgi-echange-donnees-fratmat]].
- **GUCE CONNECT (May 2026).** Hosted the first edition of a foreign-trade digitalisation ecosystem event.
  Source: [[2026-05-12-guce-connect-digitalisation-commerce-exterieur-linfodrome]].
