# Gabon–SGG start-up credit-guarantee accord

**Parties:** the **[[ministere-economie-numerique-gabon]]** and the
**[[societe-gabonaise-de-garantie]]** (SGG). **What:** an agreement to establish **guarantee
mechanisms** that reduce the risk borne by financial institutions when extending credit to innovative
young companies. **When/where:** signed **2026-07-14**, on the margins of the launch of
**[[kimba-connect]]**. **Status:** active.

- SGG director-general **Alban Etho**: **risk-sharing is a key condition** for stimulating innovation
  and improving young firms' access to bank finance.
- Minister **[[mark-alexandre-doumba]]** framed this and the parallel
  **[[gabon-huawei-kimba-connect-accord]]** as accompaniment going beyond simple project financing.

**Terms not published (as of 2026-07-17):** no guarantee ceiling, coverage ratio, eligibility test,
term, participating banks or budget envelope is on the record. Source:
[[2026-07-17-gabon-garantie-publique-huawei-start-up]].
