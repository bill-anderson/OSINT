# ANEC (National Civil Registration Agency, Senegal)

Senegal's **Agence Nationale de l'État Civil** — the government body modernising and digitising the country's
**civil registration (CRVS)** system, the foundational identity layer beneath digital ID and DPI.

## Activity in this wiki

- **15.3m records digitised (2026-07).** ANEC reported digitising **more than 15.3 million** civil-registration
  records, with **629 civil-status centres** connected to the national network — **67%** via the **Civil
  Registration Management Software (LGEC)** and **27%** connected to the **Sama État Civil** online-request
  platform; **3,695** officers trained. A **data-verification campaign (13–29 July 2026)** across Fatick, Louga
  and Diourbel validates records and corrects inconsistencies. Framed as improving data accuracy/security and
  access to identity-dependent services. See [[SEN]], [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-15 Senegal digitizes more than 15 million birth records in civil registration overhaul]].
