# São Tomé and Príncipe National Framework for Interoperability (Law 1/2024)

STP's **National Framework for Interoperability**, enacted as **Law 1/2024**, the legal
basis for **data sharing and governance across public institutions**. It is the
statutory underpinning for the cross-institution data exchange that the country's
digital-ID and digital-government ambitions require (see
[[sao-tome-national-digital-id]] — officials note a citizen-data system exists but
institutions are "not yet connected").
