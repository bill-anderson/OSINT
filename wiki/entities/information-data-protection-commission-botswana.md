# Information and Data Protection Commission (Botswana)

Botswana's **data-protection authority**, whose powers were substantially expanded by the **[[botswana-data-protection-act-2024]]** (effective 14 Jan 2025).

## Activity in this wiki

- Empowered under the 2024 Act to oversee controllers/processors, enforce DPIAs, breach notification and cross-border-transfer rules — the enforcement body for a regime that only became operative in 2025 (the 2018 Act it replaced never took effect). See [[botswana--gov-protect]]. Source: [[2025-01-08 Understanding Botswana's New Data Protection Act 2024]].
- **First Commissioner + enforcement turn (2026-02).** The first **Information and Data Protection
  Commissioner, Kepaletswe Somolekae**, took a firmer stance on non-compliant **Non-Bank Financial
  Institutions** and on **cross-border data flows** — the Commission moving from paper to active
  enforcement. Source: [[2026-02-11-new-data-commission-tightens-compliance-oversight]].
