# NIU — Numéro d'Identification Unique

The Republic of Congo's **unique identification number** — a **tax ID that has become a foundational biometric ID**. Instituted by Décret 2004-469 (2004) as mandatory taxpayer registration for the tax authority (DGID), it was modernised in 2020 (the "new NIU") with **biometric capture (iris + fingerprints)**.

## Profile

- **Universal & mandatory.** Now issued to every foreign resident and Congolese citizen **from age 6**, valid **10 years**, free for individuals/non-commercial entities. Order 25550 (2022) makes it **mandatory for 8 economic transactions + 5 administrative/social formalities** — bank accounts, transfers abroad, utility/telecom subscriptions, invoicing/contracts; tax/customs declarations, Treasury collection, nationality/criminal-record certificates, social security.
- **Relaunch (Sept 2025).** Production relaunched; Finance Minister Christian Yoka framed the NIU as permanent unique digital identification to curb duplicates/fraud and to populate economic databases; it remains free nationwide.
