# Rwanda Information Society Authority (RISA)

Rwanda's ICT implementation agency (CEO Innocent Bagamba Muhizi); drives the national DPI strategy and
digitalisation. Hosts the **Center for Digital Public Infrastructure (CDPI)**, established within RISA in
**February 2025**. Seconds staff into data-governance roles (e.g. the [[nisr]] Data Governance Unit).
See [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
