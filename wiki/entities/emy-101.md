# EMY 101

Côte d'Ivoire's **government AI assistant** — a chatbot on **WhatsApp/Messenger** plus a toll-free **101**
line giving citizens guidance on **civil-status and tax** procedures and a channel to report issues
(launched Mar 2026). The conversational front door to the e-government stack ([[civ--dpi-govtech]]).
