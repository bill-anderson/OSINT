# SNIA (Cameroon National AI Strategy)

Cameroon's **National Artificial Intelligence Strategy**, framed around digital sovereignty — to be rolled out with local operator **[[st-digital]]** (sovereign cloud + AI infrastructure).

## Activity in this wiki

- The government tapped **[[st-digital]]** as a strategic partner for the SNIA rollout after a July 2025 ministerial visit to its Douala Tier 3 datacentre (sovereign cloud, AI equipment) — positioning domestic infrastructure as the base for national AI. See [[tech.ai]] and the [[CMR]] hub. Source: [[2025-07-16 Cameroon taps ST Digital to lead National AI Strategy (SNIA)]].
