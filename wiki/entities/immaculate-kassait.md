# Immaculate Kassait

Kenya's **Data Protection Commissioner**, head of the **[[odpc-kenya|ODPC]]** — the primary on-record
voice of Kenya's data-protection regime. See [[gov.protect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **ODPC 5-year privacy plan (2025-07).** [[gov.policy]] Kassait launched a **KES 12.6bn** ODPC
  strategic/privacy plan, signalling enforcement-capacity build-out. Source:
  [[2025-07-05-odpc-sh126bn-privacy-plan-standard]] *(summary-only clip)*.
- **Public data-rights advocacy (2026-02).** [[gov.protect]] An op-ed ("Why every Kenyan must protect
  their personal data") — an attributable primary voice on individual data rights. Source:
  [[2026-02-28-every-kenyan-protect-personal-data-kassait-standard]] *(summary-only clip)*.
- **Ray-Ban Meta probe (2026-03).** [[tech.ai]] Kassait confirmed the ODPC had commenced **suo moto
  investigations** into the Ray-Ban Meta AI glasses' surveillance/consent risks and Meta-AI training
  data. See [[kenya--gov-protect]]. Source: [[2026-03-31-ray-ban-meta-ai-glasses-probe-nation]].
