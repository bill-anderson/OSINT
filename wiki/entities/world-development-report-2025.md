# World Development Report 2025

The World Bank Group's flagship "World Development Report 2025: Standards for Development" — themed on standards (measurement, quality, compatibility) as a "quiet catalyst" for development via an adapt–align–author progression. It is a standing reference in this wiki: a dated fact drawn from it becomes a normal source citing this instrument.

## Summary
- Frames standards — measurement, quality, compatibility — as a "quiet catalyst" for development.
- Structured around an adapt–align–author progression.
- Chapters on quality infrastructure, and standards for the economy, trade, FDI and technology diffusion, human capital, environment and governance.
- Includes "Spotlight 5: Standards and Digital Public Infrastructures" — the section most directly relevant here.
- Includes Sub-Saharan Africa material.
- Reference document: `2026-07-10 World Development Report 2025.pdf`.
