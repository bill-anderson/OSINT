# Balcão Único de Atendimento ao Público (BUAP) — Angola

Angola's network of **one-stop public-service counters**, sited in municipal and communal
administrations (and some diplomatic/consular missions) — run by the
**[[ministerio-da-administracao-do-territorio-angola|MAT]]**. In 2026 the BUAPs became the
front line of two converging drives: the **[[registo-eleitoral-oficioso-angola|electoral
proof-of-life update]]** and **[[bilhete-de-identidade-angola|BI]]** data capture.

## Current state

- **Rollout for the 2027 update.** **254 BUAPs live in the first phase** (across all 21
  provinces) of a planned **634**, staffed by 1,200+ *brigadistas* rising to a total of
  **10,747** people, running to 2027-03-31. Source:
  [[2026-06-15-arranca-prova-de-vida-para-actualizacao-do-registo-eleitoral]].
- **BUAPs double as BI capture points**, extending identification reach; and the civic
  campaign to collect the 120,000+ uncollected BIs runs through them. Source:
  [[2026-07-15-mais-de-120-mil-bilhetes-aguardam-levantamento]].
- **Hardening ordered.** The 2026-06-08 decree tasks the commission with reinforcing BUAP
  **energy, computing, communications and digital-security** infrastructure — a tacit
  admission the network's connectivity and power are the binding constraint (weak coverage
  in remote areas flagged as the chief registration risk). Sources:
  [[2026-06-08-pr-cria-comissao-actualizacao-dados-cidadaos-maiores-bdcm]],
  [[2026-06-20-fraca-cobertura-de-internet-pode-afectar-registo-eleitoral]].
