# James Kaji

**Director General of [[nida-tanzania]]** (the National Identification Authority), Tanzania — as
of **2026-01**. The senior official voice on **[[jamii-namba]]** and the pending
**[[tanzania-infant-registration-bill]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Infant registration Bill still pending (2026-01-04, Kibaha).** Speaking at the **28th session
  of the NIDA Staff Council**, Kaji set out the government's preparation to **table the Infant
  Registration Bill in the next Parliament**, which would let NIDA register children from birth.
  He argued that registering infants would ensure children are **legally recognised from the start
  of their lives**, eliminate the challenges that arise when registering adults, ease access to
  healthcare, education and insurance, and give government accurate population data for planning.
  **Note the source's headline says "rolls out"; Kaji's own account is that the Bill is
  pending** — the body governs. See [[gov.legislate]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-04 Tanzania rolls out infant registration for stronger citizen ID]].
- **300,000+ uncollected ID cards (2026-01-04).** Kaji urged **more than 300,000 citizens whose
  ID cards have already been processed to collect them** from district offices, noting that
  delays cause **losses for the government** and deny citizens use of the documents. A telling
  counterpoint to headline coverage figures: cards personalised and cards in hand are not the
  same thing — the same collect-your-document gap that appears in the addressing programme
  (**[[napa-tanzania]]**, uncollected placards, 2024-07). See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-04 Tanzania rolls out infant registration for stronger citizen ID]].
