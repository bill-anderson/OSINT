# Ministry of Lands and Natural Resources (MLNR), Zambia

**Zambia's land ministry** — the policy and service owner for land administration, title
deeds and ground rent, and the client ministry for **[[zilas]]**, the land-administration
system deployed for it by **[[smart-zambia-institute]]**'s E-Government Division. It is the
sectoral counterpart in land to what [[dnrpc-zambia]] is in population registration: SZI
builds and hosts the platform, MLNR runs the service.

**Provenance caveat:** everything the wiki holds on MLNR's digitisation comes from **SMART
Zambia's own portal pages** — the implementing agency's account of its client ministry.
Attribute; no independent evaluation is held.

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Goes digital" (SZI, 2024-09-13).** MLNR concluded a **four-day exhibition** at
  **Cosmopolitan Mall, Lusaka (10–13 September 2024)**, **attending to over 250 clients**.
  ICT Officer **Ms. Gwendoline Hamabele** described the ministry as having **transitioned to
  digital platforms for all its services**: a **Zamportal account is now a prerequisite to
  pay ground rent**, **title deeds are now digitally issued**, and services run over the
  **Government Service Bus (gsb.gov.zm)**, which hosts [[zilas]]. Framed by the ministry and
  SZI as efficiency, transparency and accessibility gains — a claim, not a measurement.
  Source: [[2024-09-13 Ministry of Lands and Natural Resources Goes Digital (SMART Zambia)]].
- **Platform (2024-09).** [[zilas]] — deployed by SZI's Electronic Government Division,
  100% digital, web and mobile. Source:
  [[2024-09-13 ZILAS - Your Gateway to Modern Land Administration (SMART Zambia)]].
