# Digital-Y

A **German development-cooperation project** (launched January 2025, **€4m**), delivered via **[[giz|GIZ]]**,
integrating digital tools into Mauritanian public administration. Digital-Y supports **[[mtnima-mauritania|
MTNIMA]]**'s work on **public-digital-services standards** (the March 2026 interoperability/standards-framework
workshop) and is cited as context for the country's wider digital-transformation financing (the
**[[islamic-development-bank|IsDB]]** $50m programme).
