# GovGuide (Nigeria)

An AI chatbot that gives Nigerian citizens conversational access to government
services, positioned as a public-facing [[dpi.govtech]] layer over the state's
service offerings.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched (2026-05-22).** GovGuide went live as a web voice-and-text
  assistant for accessing public services, built by [[meta]] with Nigeria's
  Federal Ministry of Communications, Innovation and Digital Economy (Minister
  [[bosun-tijani]]), the National Centre for AI and Robotics (NCAIR) and local
  firm Publica AI ([[tech.ai]], [[include.access]]). Source: [[2026-05-22 Nigeria launches GovGuide, an AI chatbot for public service access]].
- **Runs on open-source Llama; multilingual.** The service runs on Meta's
  open-source Llama models and supports English, Hausa, Igbo and Yoruba; Meta's
  NLLB-200 translation model now covers 50+ African languages ([[capacity.literacy]]).
  Source: [[2026-05-22 Nigeria launches GovGuide, an AI chatbot for public service access]].
- **Adoption caveat.** Reach is constrained by rural broadband and smartphone
  gaps across [[NGA]]. Source: [[2026-05-22 Nigeria launches GovGuide, an AI chatbot for public service access]].
