# Inwi

One of Morocco's **three main telecom operators** (with Maroc Telecom and Orange Maroc). See
[[infra.connect]], [[morocco--infra-connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **FiberCo / TowerCo JVs with Maroc Telecom (2025-04).** [[infra.connect]] Inwi and **[[maroc-telecom]]**
  formed two joint ventures — **FiberCo** (fibre) and **TowerCo** (towers) — to fast-track fibre and 5G
  rollout on shared assets (a long-running dispute settled). See [[morocco--infra-connect]]. Source:
  [[2025-04-02-connectingafrica-maroc-telecom-inwi-fiber-5g]].
- **5G launch (2025-11).** Inwi was one of the three operators switching on 5G; its ANRT licence fee was
  **600m dirham**. Source: [[2025-11-07-en-hespress-what-5g-could-mean-for-morocco]].
