# Dashen Bank

An Ethiopian commercial bank — the **first in Ethiopia to link account opening directly to the national
biometric identity database** (2026-04). Customers open accounts **fully remotely** with only a mobile number
and **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** credentials (no branch visit), paired with a **Mastercard-enabled
virtual card** for international online payments/remittances — a concrete "utility-first" Fayda use case for the
unbanked (launched as Fayda passed 40m registrations; MOSIP flagged the milestone). See [[ethiopia--dpi-pay]].
