# Ministry of Finance (Sudan)

Sudan's finance ministry, driving the wartime digitisation of state revenue —
electronic collection and tax computerisation — from Port Sudan, to raise
revenue efficiency, cut cash dependence and improve fiscal transparency.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Isali e-collection (2026-04).** Contracted the
  **[[national-workers-bank-sudan]]** to deliver **[[isali]]**, digital
  government-revenue collection. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-30 Finance Ministry-National Workers Bank contract for Isali e-collection service (SUNA)]].
- **Tax e-invoicing restoration (2026-05).** Oversees the
  **[[tax-authority-sudan]]**, whose electronic-invoicing project (advanced
  before the war) has been restored, with PM backing. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-24 PM backs Tax Authority; e-invoicing project restored after war (SUNA)]].
