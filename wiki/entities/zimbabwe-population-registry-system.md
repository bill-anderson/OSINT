# Zimbabwe Population Registry System (ZPRS)

The modernised national **population registry** (upgrade begun ~2023), cited by Home Affairs as the platform
behind e-passports and the goal of **online, real-time** birth-certificate and passport applications. Its
performance is the subject of the ID-backlog debate (Minister Kazembe denying backlogs against complaints of
undocumented children). See [[zimbabwe--dpi-id]].
