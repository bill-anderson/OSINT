# Trésor Pay (Guinea)

Guinea's digital gateway for **public payments** — a platform to dematerialise the payment of administrative fees and charges owed to the state, run by the public treasury under the Ministry of Economy and Finance ([[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.govtech]]). Launched **22 December 2025** in Conakry following a presidential directive, it lets citizens pay state fees via mobile phone (relying on electronic-money services, alongside the traditional circuit) to reduce queues, curb informal practices, ensure traceability and strengthen domestic revenue mobilisation. A G2P/P2G complement to the [[bcrg]]-led payments push, in a country where financial inclusion is ~30%. See [[GIN]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched (2025-12-22).** Finance Minister Mourana Soumah framed Trésor Pay as improving public-service access and non-tax revenue collection, with a dedicated call centre; over time it could extend to mass payments and other dematerialised public financial instruments.
