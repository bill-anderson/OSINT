# UNCTAD

The UN Conference on Trade and Development — producer of the **eTrade Readiness Assessments** and digital-
economy benchmarking cited across African country profiles. In Mauritania, UNCTAD's **eTrade Readiness
Assessment (2024)** flagged barriers across seven pillars: limited payments interoperability/acceptance, high
logistics costs and weak addressing, patchy legal frameworks (consumer protection, data, e-signatures), and
constrained SME finance for digitalisation. A reference/benchmarking body — cite its assessments, don't absorb
their figures as current state.
