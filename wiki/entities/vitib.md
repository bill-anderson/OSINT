# VITIB (Grand-Bassam technology park)

Côte d'Ivoire's **ICT/biotech technology park at Grand-Bassam** — the physical cluster hosting the
country's sovereign-cloud build ([[st-digital]] Tier III, [[raxio-group]] CIV1, PAIX, MDXi). Sought
**$311m** (Indian investors / Exim Bank of India) for a 2028 development plan — a third external financier
alongside the US (EXIM) and Gulf (G42). Sources:
[[2025-04-28-vitib-seeks-311m-wearetech]], [[2025-10-03-data-center-inaugure-vitib-fratmat]].
