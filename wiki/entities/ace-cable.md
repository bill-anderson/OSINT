# ACE (Africa Coast to Europe)

The **Africa Coast to Europe** submarine cable, linking West African coastal states to Europe. Mauritania has
been **connected since 2011** and — until the second **[[ellalink|EllaLink]]** cable is commissioned (expected
Q1 2027) — depends on it heavily as effectively its only international link. Outages and even scheduled
maintenance on ACE have repeatedly disrupted internet and mobile services nationwide, the single-landing
fragility that the second cable is intended to resolve.
