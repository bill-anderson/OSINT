# SADC cross-border DPI / digital-ID framework

Billed as **"Africa's first" regional cross-border digital-ID framework** — a federated **digital eKYC** ecosystem across the **16 SADC member states** to fix siloed national ID systems and enable cross-border banking and payments.

## Profile

- **Partners:** the **SADC Committee of Central Bank Governors (CCBG)**, **Co-Develop** (a DPI fund; 50-in-5 campaign partner) and ****FinMark Trust**** (technical support). Components: federated digital ID, interoperable data exchange and enhanced digital payments. Follows a real-time cross-border ID-verification-for-payments pilot between **South Africa and Lesotho**.
- **Problem:** siloed national IDs block **>3.7 million** nationals living outside their home countries from banking/payments; a shared, reusable rail replaces 16 bilateral agreements. Covers Angola, Botswana, Comoros, DRC, Eswatini, Lesotho, Madagascar, Malawi, Mauritius, Mozambique, Namibia, Seychelles, South Africa, Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe.
