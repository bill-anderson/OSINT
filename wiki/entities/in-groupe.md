# IN Groupe

French identity-services company (formerly Imprimerie Nationale), a supplier of national ID documents
and digital-identity systems across Francophone Africa.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gabon CNIE supplier (from June 2024).** IN Groupe supplies both the **physical electronic national
  ID card and the digital-ID management software/hardware** for Gabon's new-generation CNIE, launched
  25 June 2024 — the flagship legal-identity component of the [[digital-gabon-project]]. EVP Yann
  Haguet named. Source: [[2024-09-25-gabon-digital-id-forefront-transformation]].

A French vendor at the core of a sovereign national-ID build invites the [[colonialism]] /
[[sovereignty]] reading also flagged on Gabon's [[gabon--dpi-id]] story — the ID stack is
domestically framed but foreign-supplied (IN Groupe now; [[thales]] historically).
