# Airtel Tanzania

The Tanzanian operating company of **[[airtel-africa]]** — a major MNO, market leader in fixed lines
(**213,010** subscribers, Mar 2026). Managing Director (2025): **Charles Kamoto**.

- **Zanzibar broadband MoU (Aug 2025).** MoU with **[[zictia]]** to expand broadband and digital inclusion.
  Source: [[2025-08-19-airtel-tanzania-zictia-broadband-zanzibar]].
- **Starlink context.** Parent Airtel Africa's SpaceX/[[starlink]] distribution deal (see [[TZA]] hub and
  [[tanzania--infra-connect]]) covers Tanzania as one of 14 markets.
