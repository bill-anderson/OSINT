# Tanzania Posts Corporation (TPC)

Tanzania's **national postal operator** — and, in the government's addressing programme, a
system of record rather than a delivery service. TPC is named among the **29 systems
interconnected** to the National Physical Addressing database (**[[napa-tanzania]]**), which
places the post office inside the country's registry infrastructure rather than downstream of
it.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Interconnected to the national address registry (2026-02-09).** Deputy Permanent Secretary
  **Nicholas Mkapa** (**[[micit-tanzania]]**) told the Annual Residential Address Conference and
  Postal Services Technology Exhibition that since the addressing programme began (2010),
  **29 systems have been interconnected, including the Tanzania Posts Corporation**,
  facilitating more efficient service delivery. See [[napa-tanzania]], [[dpi.registry]],
  [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-09 Tanzania registers 12 million housing addresses to boost digital governance]].
- **Post as a service channel, per the regulator (2026-02-09).** **[[tcra]]** DG **Dr Jabiri
  Bakari** said the national residential addressing system has **strengthened postal services,
  making them a key channel for delivering financial, social and government services** more
  efficiently — with the gain framed as **particularly benefiting rural communities**. The
  addressing layer is what converts a postal network into a public-service delivery rail. See
  [[include.access]], [[digital.rural]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-09 Tanzania registers 12 million housing addresses to boost digital governance]].
- **A continental benchmark, per PAPU (2026-02-09).** **[[papu]]** Secretary General **Dr
  Sifundo Chief Moyo** cited **"Tanzania's achievements in addressing, including its Universal
  Postal Union (UPU) S42 certification"** as setting **"a benchmark for the continent."** See
  [[upu-s42]], [[upu]], [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-09 Tanzania registers 12 million housing addresses to boost digital governance]].
