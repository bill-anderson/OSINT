# TrendAI

Cybersecurity / AI company operating in sub-Saharan Africa (country MD for SSA:
**Gareth Redelinghuys**); vendor of the **TrendAI Vision One** platform.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Locally-governed sovereign-AI data centre (2026).** TrendAI operates a South African
  data centre with its own data lake, running [[anthropic]]'s **Claude Opus 4.7**, governed
  under South African law with audit trails "a South African regulator can actually read."
  Pitched squarely at [[popia]] exposure: keeping AI workloads, queries and logs in
  jurisdiction so an SA-domiciled controller is not shipping personal data to Virginia or
  Dublin. Framed as **phase one** of a broader African rollout. Vision One uses Claude for
  agentic workflows, automation and AI-native security operations (Zero Day Initiative,
  Pwn2Own). See [[ZAF]], [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]], [[infra.cybersec]],
  [[south-africa--gov-protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-09 Anthropic's Claude to bolster data sovereignty in SA]].
