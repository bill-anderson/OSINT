# Alcatel Submarine Networks (ASN)

Global submarine-cable manufacturer and marine-installation contractor. In Mauritania, ASN implements the
second international submarine cable alongside **[[ellalink|EllaLink]]** — the deep-ocean cable and the
coastal-landing works (PLSE / Bu7 junction, phase-1 completed May 2026; commissioning expected Q1 2027).
