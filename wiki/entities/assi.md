# ASSI (Information Systems Security Agency)

Algeria's **information-systems security agency**, operating under the **Ministry of National Defence** — the
executive core of the cyber framework: implements national cybersecurity policy, monitors cyberspace and
defends critical state infrastructure. Unveiled the **National Strategy for Information Systems Security
2025–2029** (3 Mar 2026) and runs the **RSSI** security-officer training cohorts (Sidi Abdellah). See
[[algeria--infra-cybersec]].
