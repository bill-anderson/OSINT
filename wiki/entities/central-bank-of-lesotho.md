# Central Bank of Lesotho (CBL)

Lesotho's central bank — driver of the national payment-system modernisation, leading the
**[[leswitch|LeSwitch]]** national payment switch with the commercial banks to localise domestic payments
and advance financial inclusion. See [[dpi.pay]], [[lesotho--dpi-pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **LeSwitch POS localisation (2025-05).** [[include.access]] [[sovereignty]] The CBL, with the **four
  commercial banks**, launched **local POS transactions on LeSwitch** (11 May 2025) — any-bank cards on
  any POS, processed **domestically** rather than through costly international clearing (Director of
  Payments & Settlements Mothetsi Sekoati). The switch's two streams (instant + card) build toward an
  e-commerce/retail phase. See [[leswitch]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-14-leswitch-pos-financial-inclusion-lestimes]].
