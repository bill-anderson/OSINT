# Payments Association of Zambia (PAYZ)

Industry body representing Zambia's mobile-money and digital-payments sector, positioned by
the government as a partner in expanding financial inclusion and reducing cash dependency.
PAYZ frames its value as extending financial services to unbanked rural communities and
creating agency-network micro-entrepreneurship for young people.

## Activity in this wiki

- **State House engagement on digital financial services (~2026-02-26).** President Hakainde
  Hichilema hosted PAYZ at State House to advance the government's digital financial services
  agenda — accelerating digital payments (including within government agencies), shifting
  consumer behaviour away from cash, and boosting inclusion through policy and infrastructure.
  Officials from the Ministry of Technology and Science, the [[bank-of-zambia]] and [[zicta]]
  attended; ZedMobile, named Zambia's fourth mobile network operator, pledged support for the
  National Financial Inclusion Strategy. PAYZ holds regular quarterly discussions with the
  Ministry to harmonise regulation and transform national payment systems. Tags [[dpi.pay]],
