# World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO)

**The UN specialised agency for intellectual property**, and — in this wiki — the **publisher of
the [[global-innovation-index]]**. Its footprint here is narrow and index-shaped: WIPO appears as
the institution behind the GII country profiles this wiki cites, not yet as an actor in any
African digital-governance development. See [[tech.innovate]], [[data.statistics]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Publishes the Global Innovation Index.** WIPO is the source institution for the
  [[global-innovation-index]], the annual comparative ranking of economies by innovation
  performance, issued with per-economy country profiles. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 Global Innovation Index 2025 - Seychelles country profile (WIPO)]].
- **Seychelles GII 2025 profile held.** This wiki holds WIPO's **GII 2025 Seychelles country
  profile** (PDF artefact plus companion source page), which places **Seychelles 75th of 139 (GII
  2025)**. Per the reference-study rule, that figure stays with the index — see
  [[global-innovation-index]] and [[SYC]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 Global Innovation Index 2025 - Seychelles country profile (WIPO)]].
- **Nothing else held.** No source in this wiki records WIPO programmes, technical assistance,
  treaties or country engagements in Africa. Its IP-registry and treaty-administration work —
  which would plausibly touch the [[registrar-general-office-seychelles]]'s Intellectual Property
  department — is **not** evidenced here and should not be assumed.
