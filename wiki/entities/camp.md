# Canadian Anti-Monopoly Project (CAMP)

Competition-policy organisation (Executive Director **Keldon Bester**); publisher of the
**"Parting Clouds"** report on cloud-market concentration.

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Parting Clouds: Creating a Competitive Marketplace for Compute" (2025).** Bester &
  **[[david-eaves]]** document hyperscaler dominance (three firms ~66% of global public
  cloud, **85% in Canada**) and its costs — lock-in, opaque pricing, gatekeeping, systemic
  risk, geopolitical coercion — arguing for interoperability and procurement-led market
  shaping over bespoke sovereign stacks. Canada-focused but the analysis generalises. See
  [[tech.industry]], [[gov.legislate]], [[infra.store]], [[david-eaves]].
