# Sudan Customs Authority

Sudan's customs force, operator of the automated customs-clearance system and a
front line of the post-war restoration of digital trade/government systems.

## Activity in this wiki

- **ASYCUDA reactivation, Gezira (2025-06).** Reactivated the **[[asycuda]]**
  (UNCTAD automated customs) system at Gezira state's Hantoub customs yard after
  war looting/damage — restoring generators, technical equipment and (with
  **[[sudani-telecom]]**) internet/network connectivity, described as the basis
  for all modern customs procedures. Earliest in-window sweep item for Sudan.
  See [[dpi.govtech]], [[infra.connect]], [[SDN]]. Source:
