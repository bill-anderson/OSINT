# ARTCI (Telecoms/ICT Regulatory Authority of Côte d'Ivoire)

Côte d'Ivoire's telecoms/ICT regulator, which also serves as the country's **personal-data-protection authority** under Law No. 2013-450 (19 June 2013). The national-press record shows a **regulator in active motion in 2026** — new leadership, new compliance tooling, and a visible international profile — after the hub had treated it as static since 2024.

## Activity in this wiki

- **New Regulatory Council seated (2026).** A new ARTCI Regulatory Council under **Roger Félix Adom** was sworn in (7 May 2026), then officially installed (12 May), setting mandate priorities — leadership turnover the hub had missed. Sources: [[2026-05-07-conseil-regulation-artci-prestation-serment-fratmat]], [[2026-05-08-artci-conseil-regulation-pretent-serment-linfodrome]], [[2026-05-12-artci-nouveaux-regulateurs-installes-linfodrome]], [[2026-05-12-artci-regulatory-council-roadmap-techreviewafrica]].
- **CERTINUM — DP-compliance goes paperless (Jul 2026).** ARTCI launched **[[certinum]]** (live 2 Jul 2026, slipped from 25 Jun), a platform to digitise personal-data-protection authorisations/compliance — nothing comparable was in the hub. See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.standards]].
- **Compliance regime + operator data reporting.** Strengthened a conformity framework with operators (Apr 2026) and, as postal regulator, **mandated operator data transmission** (Apr 2026). A ministerial DP stocktake (May 2026) and a call for **regional DP coordination** followed. Sources: [[2026-04-27-artci-operateurs-cadre-conformite-linfodrome]], [[2026-04-08-artci-regulation-postale-donnees-fratmat]], [[2026-05-18-protection-donnees-progres-cote-divoire-fratmat]], [[2026-05-18-regulation-numerique-afrique-action-collective-donnees-fratmat]].
- **International profile.** An Ivorian (**Bessou**) was elected **Chair of the ITU's TDAG** — a global ITU seat; ARTCI represented CIV at the 2nd Francophone Forum on Digital Governance & AI (Geneva) and pushed an **anticipatory/evidence-based ("data-driven") regulatory doctrine** at WSIS 2026. Sources: [[2026-03-31-artci-delegation-meets-digital-minister-techreviewafrica]], [[2026-07-04-artci-francophone-forum-digital-governance-ai-geneva-techreviewafrica]], [[2026-07-10-data-driven-digital-regulation-wsis-forum-techreviewafrica]].
- **Public education & rights outreach.** Ran DP rights-and-duties education for thousands of young people (Jan 2026) and gendered data-rights outreach to schoolgirls on IWD (Mar 2026). Sources: [[2026-01-29-artci-protection-donnees-jeunes-fratmat]], [[2026-03-13-artci-jif-2026-lyceennes-fratmat]].
- **e-transactions law amendment (2025).** Parliament adopted a bill ratifying **Ordinance n°2017 of 2 Aug 2017** (arts. 3 & 17 amended) on electronic exchanges — a `dpi.exchange` legislative instrument the hub lacked. Source: [[2025-04-24-echanges-electroniques-loi-ratification-fratmat]].
- **Biometrics governance (2024).** A June 2024 ARTCI notice flagged **biometrics for employee access control**, warning it must comply with the 2013 data-protection law. Source: [[2024-09-13 Is Cote d'Ivoire moving towards standard regulations for biometrics (ARTCI)]].
- *(Note: cybersecurity mandate moved from ARTCI to the new [[anssi-cote-divoire|ANSSI]] under Ordinance 2024-950 — see the cybersecurity thread.)*
