# ARTCI (Telecoms/ICT Regulatory Authority of Côte d'Ivoire)

Côte d'Ivoire's telecoms/ICT regulator, which also serves as the country's **personal-data-protection authority** under Law No. 2013-450 (19 June 2013).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometrics governance (2024).** A June 2024 ARTCI notice flagged the growing use of **biometrics for employee access/attendance control**, warning it must comply with the 2013 data-protection law (sensitive-data rules, prior authorisation) — signalling an active DPA and an emerging biometrics-regulation question. See [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2024-09-13 Is Cote d'Ivoire moving towards standard regulations for biometrics (ARTCI)]].
- Named as regulator/DPA in the US ITA digital-economy guide (alongside ANSUT and the National Cybersecurity Agency). See the [[CIV]] hub.
