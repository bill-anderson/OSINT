# Malawi Low Resource Language Data Trust (Chichewa AI)

A government initiative to build **AI models in Chichewa**, launched June 2026 (AI for Malawi National Language
Data Trust workshop, Lilongwe). It develops core AI frameworks and assembles **licensed Chichewa-language
content** as training data, so AI systems — digital assistants, translation, educational platforms — can serve
local needs. Supported by the **[[world-bank]]** and the **Gates Foundation**, it places Malawi among states
building AI for underrepresented ("low-resource") languages as a **digital-inclusion** lever. See
**[[malawi--tech-ai]]**.
