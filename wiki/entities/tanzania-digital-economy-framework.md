# Tanzania Digital Economy Strategic Framework 2024-2034

Tanzania's **ten-year national digital economy strategy**, published by **[[micit-tanzania]]**
(the ICT ministry), with a **foreword by President Samia Suluhu Hassan** in her capacity as
**Chairperson of the Tanzania National Business Council** — a framing that places the strategy in
a business-council setting rather than a purely ministerial one.

**Anchoring.** The Framework is anchored to the **National ICT Policy**, the **National Five-Year
Development Plan 2021/22–2025/26**, the **SDG Agenda 2030** and the **AU Agenda 2063** — the
standard four-way alignment of national, continental and global frames that African digital
strategies of this period tend to assert.

**Dating.** The imprint reads **© MICIT 2024** with **no month printed**, so `date_precision` is
**year** and the 2024-01-01 prefix is padding for sorting. A separate document in the same
ingest batch states the President **launched it on 29 July 2024** — external corroboration, not
something printed in the Framework itself; recorded here as such rather than promoted to the
document's date.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Published (2024).** MICIT issued the Framework as Tanzania's ten-year digital economy
  strategy. It is the policy companion to the **[[tanzania-e-government-strategy-2022]]**
  (2022–2027): the e-Government Strategy addresses how the **state** digitises its own services;
  this Framework addresses the **economy** the state wants to build around them. See
  [[gov.policy]], [[tech.industry]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Tanzania Digital Economy Strategic Framework 2024-2034 (MICIT)]].
