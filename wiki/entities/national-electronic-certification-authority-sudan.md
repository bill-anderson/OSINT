# National Electronic Certification Authority (Sudan)

Sudan's public-key/electronic-certification authority (Arabic: السلطة القومية
للمصادقة الإلكترونية), responsible for the trust and digital-signature layer
underpinning Sudan's e-government stack.

## Activity in this wiki

- **SUDAPASS implementation (2026-05).** Co-implemented the launch of the
  **[[sudapass]]** national digital ID alongside the
  **[[sudanese-cybersecurity-authority]]** and, for identity data, the Interior
  Ministry's **[[civil-registry-sudan]]** — providing the electronic
  certification and legally-recognised digital-signature capability that lets
  SUDAPASS support e-transactions across government, banking and telecoms. See
  [[dpi.id]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-21 Sudan launches national digital identity SUDAPASS (SUNA)]].
