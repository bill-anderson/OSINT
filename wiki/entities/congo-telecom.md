# Congo Télécom

Congo-Brazzaville's **state telecom incumbent** — owner of the national backbone (whose outages Airtel
blamed for rural 4G gaps) and the vehicle for the country's **Central-African digital-transit-hub**
ambition.

## Activity in this wiki

- **ISO 9001:2015 (Mar 2026)** — first COG operator to earn the standard (Bureau Veritas). Source:
  [[2026-03-29-congo-telecom-iso-9001-certification-techreviewafrica]].
- **Transit-hub bid (Jun 2026).** Courted **FAGACE / Africa50** at the AfDB Annual Meetings in Brazzaville
  and sent a Kinshasa delegation to Orange DRC / Airtel / Vodacom on **interconnection + roaming** — the
  COG-as-transit-hub claim, sourced. Source:
  [[2026-06-21-congo-telecom-central-africa-digital-hub-ambition-techreviewafrica]].
