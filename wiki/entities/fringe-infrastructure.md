# Fringe Infrastructure Limited

Nigerian **carrier-neutral edge data-centre** operator building the **Metro EDGE** platform — compute
and data services placed close to users in dense urban centres for low latency and local data
residency ([[infra.store]], [[sovereignty]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **2026-01-08 — Ikoyi Metro EDGE launch (Lagos).** Fringe launched the **Ikoyi Metro EDGE** data
  centre in Ikoyi, Lagos — the first regional node of its Metro EDGE platform, sited in the city's
  financial/tech corridor near banks, startups and government to support faster connectivity and
  data-sovereignty/compliance needs. Positioned as the start of a wider carrier-neutral rollout across
  major African cities. Source:
  [[2026-01-08 Nigeria's digital future gets a boost with Fringe's Ikoyi Metro EDGE launch]].
