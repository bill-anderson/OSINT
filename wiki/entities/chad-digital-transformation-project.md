# Chad Digital Transformation Project (P180000 / PAD00070)

**Parties:** the [[world-bank]] and the Republic of Chad ([[TCD]]). **What:** a digital
transformation operation of **US$120 million total**, of which an IDA grant of **SDR 69.5 million
(US$92.2 million)**. **Implementing agency:** the **Ministry of Telecommunications and Digital
Economy**. **Status:** active.

**This is a third, separate financing** — distinct from [[pmice]] (the €175.1m State Connectivity
Infrastructure Modernization Project) and from [[tchad-connexion-2030]] (the US$1.5bn national
umbrella strategy). The three are not merged here, and no source held states how they relate.

## Project timeline

- **27 February 2023** — Concept stage; Project Information Document (P180000, PIDC34946). Source:
  [[2023-02-27 PID Concept Stage - Chad Digital Transformation (P180000)]].
- **6 November 2023** — Appraisal.
- **19 December 2023** — Board.
- **4 September 2024** — Project Appraisal Document (PAD00070). Source:
  [[2024-09-04 PAD - Chad Digital Transformation Project (PAD00070)]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Held as project documents, not reporting.** Both source pages are companions to World Bank
  PDFs in `raw/`. They establish the financing envelope, the approval chronology and the
  implementing agency; they are not press accounts of a development. See [[finance.new]],
  [[dpi.govtech]].
- **A 2023 World Bank document is the origin of the superseded PMICE costing.** Separate
  reporting (2026-01-06) cites *a 2023 World Bank document detailing wider digital transformation
  plans for the country* as the source of PMICE's original **$180m / Eximbank** estimate, and of
  references to a Chadian **Internet Exchange Point** and the **EU-funded Trans-Saharan Corridor
  Project**. The sources held do not identify which World Bank document that was — it is not
  attributed to P180000. See [[pmice]], [[trans-saharan-fibre-backbone]].
