# SIGF (Sistema Integral de Gestión de Funcionarios)

Equatorial Guinea's **civil-servant management system** — implementation began early 2026 over an estimated
**18 months**, built with Morocco's **M2M Group**. It centralises a **digital registry of civil servants** with
**biometric attendance control**, **automated payroll**, digital administrative history and real-time personnel
data — a single secure database to strengthen transparency, cut duplication/errors and improve public-spending
rigour (a functional-ID / anti-ghost-worker use case). Overseen by a Steering Committee (2 sessions, Feb–Mar
2026). See [[gnq--dpi-govtech]].
