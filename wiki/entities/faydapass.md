# FaydaPass

Ethiopia's **digital-ID wallet app** — announced at **ID4Africa 2025** (21 May 2025) as the consumer front-end
to **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]**. Users download the official app and request their digital-ID
credential for **digital KYC** and verification across government and other services. Co-developed with
**TECH5** (providing T5-AirSnap and T5-OmniMatch) and **Visa**, framed as accelerating financial inclusion. See
[[ethiopia--dpi-id]], [[ethiopia--dpi-pay]].
