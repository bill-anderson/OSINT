# Baladna

Sudan's national **e-government platform** ("Our Country"), run under the
**[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]**, providing the shared digital
delivery and network-integration layer for government services. It is the
umbrella programme that also hosts the **[[sudapass]]** national digital ID.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Electronic investment licensing (2026-02).** The
  **[[national-investment-authority-sudan]]** announced completion of network
  integration with Baladna and launched fully-electronic investment-project
  licensing (online application, document upload, status tracking), aimed at
  attracting reconstruction investment. Evidence of Baladna expanding from
  identity into transactional service delivery. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[finance.new]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-23 National Investment Authority launches electronic investment licensing via Baladna (SUNA)]].
