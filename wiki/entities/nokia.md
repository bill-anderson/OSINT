# Nokia

Finnish global **telecoms-equipment vendor** — a supplier of network and subsea-cable infrastructure
across Africa. See [[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Medusa subsea cable (2025-07).** [[infra.connect]] Nokia was selected to **power the Medusa Submarine
  Cable System** (owned by Spain's AFR-IX Telecom; first announced 2022) — an Atlantic–Mediterranean–Red
  Sea high-capacity corridor linking **Europe and North Africa** (including Morocco). See
  [[morocco--infra-connect]]; cf. the Medusa Misrata landing in [[LBY]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-28-itweb-nokia-subsea-cable-europe-north-africa]].
