# Côte d'Ivoire National Data Governance Strategy 2030

The **National Data Governance Strategy 2030** — the data-side companion to the
[[civ-ai-strategy|National AI Strategy]], handed to the PM on **13 Mar 2025** and presented to the private
sector (May 2025). Anchors the country's **`data.open`** agenda (open data, data-sharing governance,
sovereign cloud) inside the aligned strategy stack. Sources:
[[2025-03-14-bad-strategies-gouvernance-donnees-ia-fratmat]],
[[2025-03-14-ai-data-governance-strategy-wearetech]],
[[2025-05-15-strategies-ia-donnees-secteur-prive-fratmat]].
