# Posta Uganda

Uganda's national postal operator — a physical-network partner for decentralised government service delivery.

- **Service Uganda Centres (2026).** Partnered **[[nita-uganda|NITA-U]]** to host regional **Service Uganda
  Centres** (one-stop government-service hubs) on Posta premises — the **UGX 4bn Gulu** centre (40+
  workstations for URSB, URA, NIRA; completion Jun 2026), with Mbarara and Tororo planned. See
  [[uganda--dpi-govtech]]. Source: [[2026-03-13-service-uganda-centre-construction-gulu]].
