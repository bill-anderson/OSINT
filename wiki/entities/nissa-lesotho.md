# NISSA (National Information System for Social Assistance, Lesotho)

Lesotho's national **social registry**, which categorises households by
vulnerability to help government target social-protection support (child grants,
food assistance, health services).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coverage.** NISSA holds data on **more than 90% of Lesotho's households**,
  making it the backbone of the country's social-protection targeting. See
  [[LSO]], [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.mis]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-16 Lesotho's Digital Leap How Child-Centred Systems are Reshaping Social Protection]].
- **Child-centred redesign (as of 2026-01).** With backing from **UNICEF** and
  the **Co-Develop Fund**, the government is redesigning its DPI to put children's
  rights at the centre — applying the **[[universal-dpi-safeguards-framework]]**
  to prevent exclusion of children lacking documentation, living with
  disabilities or in hard-to-reach areas, and to strengthen protection of
  children's data. Lesotho is also developing its own local safeguards framework.
  See [[include.access]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-16 Lesotho's Digital Leap How Child-Centred Systems are Reshaping Social Protection]].
