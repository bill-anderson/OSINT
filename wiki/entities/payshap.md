# PayShap

South Africa's **instant interbank retail payment system**, launched **13 March 2023**, operated by
**[[payinc]]** (formerly BankservAfrica). Being repositioned as **shared national payment infrastructure**
after the **[[sarb]]** took a 50% stake in the operator.

- **Scale (Mar–May 2026).** **~R403bn across 461m transactions**; **6m registered users, 905m transactions**
  by May 2026; **4.9m+ proxy identifiers**. Small relative to Pix/UPI (India's PhonePe adds 1m users every
  6 days).
- **The fee problem.** Bank-owned rail with **inconsistent, often high fees** (R1–R10; Discovery 0.5% capped
  R35; GoTyme free) and **no USSD** for feature-phone users — a bank-ownership conflict of interest widely
  blamed for slow adoption. Now **pivoting P2P → merchants/e-commerce** (PayShap Request RTP since Dec 2024).
- Adopting the SARB **QR+** interoperable QR standard as its first rail.
