# Seychelles–NPCI UPI MoU

An MoU signed during **PM Modi's 29 Jun 2026 visit** between **NPCI International** (global arm of India's
National Payments Corporation) and the **[[central-bank-of-seychelles]]** to roll out India's **Unified
Payments Interface (UPI)** in Seychelles by **end-2026** — instant QR/mobile bank-account payments, with
regulatory prep and merchant training. Aimed at India-source tourism and financial inclusion; Seychelles
would be among the first small island economies in Africa to deploy UPI. See [[seychelles--dpi-pay]], [[SYC]].
