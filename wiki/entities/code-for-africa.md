# Code for Africa

The continent's largest network of **civic-technology and open-data / investigative-data
organisations** (DataLab, sensors.AFRICA, and the [academy.AFRICA](https://academy.africa/) training
initiative for digital investigations, data literacy and open-source intelligence). Of standing
OSINT relevance both as a **publisher** of dated, primary-citing data-governance analysis and as a
builder of the OSINT/data-journalism capacity this wiki's method depends on.

Its 2026 data-protection review
([[2026-05-05 Africa's data protection laws began to bite in 2025]], by Christine Kandeo) corroborates
the **44 laws / 38 operational DPAs** end-2025 figures (citing [[digital-policy-alert]]) and documents
the 2025 turn from "paper law" to active enforcement.
