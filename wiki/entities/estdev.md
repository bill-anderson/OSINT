# ESTDEV (Estonian Centre for International Development)

Estonia's international development agency, exporting the country's e-government
and digital-public-infrastructure experience abroad. Executive director Andrea
Kivi. It is one channel through which European digital-governance models —
interoperability, reusable state platforms — reach African partners, positioned
under a [[geopol.eu]] rather than hyperscaler framing.

## Activity in this wiki

- **MoU with Smart Africa on DPI and cybersecurity (2026-05-28).** ESTDEV signed
  a memorandum of understanding with [[smart-africa]] in Brussels to build
  interoperable and reusable [[dpi.exchange]] across Smart Africa's 42 member
  states, targeting cross-border integration, reduced vendor lock-in, and
  stronger [[infra.cybersec]] resilience. It pairs Estonia's e-government
  experience with Smart Africa's continental programme architecture and adds
  [[capacity.training]] for public-sector leaders, ICT professionals,
  entrepreneurs and youth, plus support for startups, women-led innovation and
  STEM for girls. Kivi framed the goal as "secure, interoperable, people-centred
  digital systems." See [[estdev-smart-africa-mou]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-28 Estonia backs Africa digital infrastructure]].

- **Team Europe Digital Public Services — Nigeria–Finland MoU (2026).** A Nigeria–Finland
  digital-innovation MoU (DPI, digital government, cybersecurity) complements Finland's
  lead in the **EU Team Europe Initiative on Digital Public Services**, which is implemented
  with Estonia's **ESTDEV** — extending the Estonian e-government model into Nigeria. See
  [[NGA]], [[nigeria-finland-digital-mou]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-01 Nigeria Signs MoU With Finland On Digital Innovation, Deepens Bilateral Ties]].

- **Kenya Cyber Resilience Project (2026-01-27).** ESTDEV is co-implementer (with
  **[[expertise-france]]**) of the EU-funded **[[kenya-cyber-resilience-project]]** — €3m
  ($3.56m) over 36 months to harden critical infrastructure and build cyber skills. See
  [[KEN]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Kenya launches project to boost cyber security]].
