# National Population Commission (NPC)

Nigeria's constitutional census and population-data authority. Preparing the country's **first
biometric population census** (last census 2006), with digital maps, tablets and demarcation
complete pending budget approval (~₦980bn) and a presidential proclamation; census biometrics are to
feed the [[nimc]] national identity database. See [[nigeria--dpi-registry]].
