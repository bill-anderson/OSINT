# MINPOSTEL (Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications)

Cameroon's lead ministry for the digital sector (Minister **Minette Libom Li Likeng**) — sponsor of
the National AI Strategy ([[snia-cameroon]]) and the CONIA consultations ([[conia]]), and the political
owner of much of the country's digital-transformation, connectivity and digital-inclusion agenda.
Oversees agencies including [[antic-cameroon|ANTIC]], the regulator ART, Camtel and Campost.

## Activity in this wiki

- **AI strategy sponsor.** Minister Libom Li Likeng unveiled the SNIA at CONIA 2 (7–8 Jul 2025) and
  drives its "responsible AI / digital sovereignty" framing. Source:
  [[2025-07-08-snia-2040-ai-roadmap-seven-pillars-techafricanews]].
- **Digital-inclusion agenda (National Assembly, Apr 2025).** In a content-rich address the Minister
  set out the enacted **data-protection law (Loi n°2024/017 of 23 Dec 2024)**, the online
  child-protection charter (Loi n°2023/009), PATNUC ($100m World Bank), and headline penetration
  figures (**87% mobile, ~46% internet, 12,000 km backbone**). Source:
  [[2025-04-08-leconomie-minpostel-minister-national-assembly-digital-inclusion]].
