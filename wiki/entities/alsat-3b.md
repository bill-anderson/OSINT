# ALSAT-3B

Algeria's **second Earth-observation satellite launched in 2026** (ALSAT-3B) — extending national
remote-sensing capacity for agriculture, land and resource monitoring, part of the sovereign space/data
programme. See [[algeria--infra-connect]] (satellite-ISP tender) for the connectivity leg.
