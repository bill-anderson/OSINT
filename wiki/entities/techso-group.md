# TECHSO-GROUP

**The private security firm mandated to audit and certify Chad's National Data Center** before
launch. Rendered *TECHSO-GROUP* by TechAfrica News and *Techso Group* by We Are Tech Africa —
the same company. Co-founder: **Hamza Waraki**. The sources establish the company only through
this mandate: nationality, size, ownership and prior track record are not reported.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Tripartite audit mandate (2026-02-05).** TECHSO-GROUP signed with [[adetic]] and [[ansice]]
  to conduct the security audit and certification of the [[chad-national-data-centre]] — see
  [[adetic-ansice-techso-audit-agreement]]. We Are Tech Africa frames the arrangement as the
  Chadian authorities *assigning Techso Group the task of securing the national data center ahead
  of its launch*; TechAfrica News frames it as ANSICE and ADETIC conducting the audit
  *"leveraging TECHSO-GROUP's expertise."* The agreement also covers the operation of 100
  micro-data centres across government institutions. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[infra.store]],
  [[gov.standards]], [[tech.industry]]. Sources:
  [[2026-02-06 ADETIC, ANSICE, and TECHSO-GROUP Join Forces to Protect Chad's Digital Infrastructure]],
  [[2026-02-06 Chad Moves to Secure National Data Center Ahead of Launch]].
