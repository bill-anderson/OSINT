# Google–Senegal sovereign cloud MoU (2024)

An **MoU signed late September 2024** between Senegal's Ministry of Communication, Telecommunications and the
Digital Economy and **[[google]]** to establish a **sovereign cloud in Senegal** — keeping storage and
management of critical data (finance, health, security) within local infrastructure and building local cloud
skills. Signed following President Bassirou Diomaye Faye's US visit for the 79th UNGA (21–28 Sep 2024), where
he met several tech firms to advance the **[[new-deal-technologique]]**.

Part of the onshore-cloud / data-sovereignty thread the [[SEN]] hub tracks, distinct from the earlier
**[[amazon-web-services]] Wavelength** data-residency zones in Orange DCs. See [[infra.store]],
[[finance.mou]], [[geopol.usa]]. Source: [[2024-10-04 Senegal launches sovereign cloud initiative with Google]].
