# Globalstar

US satellite-communications firm operating a low-Earth-orbit constellation and spectrum
assets for voice, data and IoT services. **Apple holds ~20%** of the company.

## Activity in this wiki

- **African footprint.** Globalstar holds spectrum authorisations and partnerships in
  **South Africa, Rwanda, Mozambique and Gabon**, focused on enterprise connectivity,
  conservation tracking and industrial IoT — an established regulatory presence that could
  offer a shortcut into markets where rivals have spent years negotiating approvals (as of
  2026-04). See [[ZAF]], [[RWA]], [[MOZ]], [[GAB]], [[infra.connect]], [[data.satellite]].
  Source: [[2026-04-14 Amazon's $9bn satellite deal set to benefit Africa]].
- **Amazon acquisition target (2026-04).** Globalstar is the target of the proposed
  **~$9bn acquisition by [[amazon-web-services]]/Amazon** to accelerate Project Kuiper
  ("Leo") against [[starlink]]. Apple's ~20% stake is a reported sticking point; the deal was
  not finalised as of 2026-04-14. See [[amazon-globalstar-acquisition]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-14 Amazon's $9bn satellite deal set to benefit Africa]].
