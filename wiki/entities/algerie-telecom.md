# Algérie Télécom

Algeria's **state-owned incumbent telecom operator**; its mobile subsidiary **Mobilis** competes with Djezzy and Ooredoo. Central to the national connectivity build-out:

- **4G expansion:** 345 new base stations across 44 provinces announced Feb 2026, plus a rural programme connecting villages of 500–2,000 people (4,500 localities by 2027).
- **Fibre:** **2.9m FTTH subscribers** by late 2025 (from 53,000 in 2020); legacy copper to be phased out by 2027.
- **5G:** Mobilis among the three operators awarded 5G licences (Dec 2025); tests reached 1.2 Gbps.
- **Innovation:** established a ~1.5bn DZD (~$11m) fund for AI, cybersecurity and robotics startups; new **Sparkle (Italy)** subsea-cable partnership adds redundancy alongside **Medusa**.
