# Sierratel

Sierra Leone's state-owned telecommunications company. After failed privatisation attempts, the
government relaunched it (Apr 2026) as an **MVNO** with **[[africell]]** as investment partner
(revenue-share, no heavy capex), keeping the state brand to serve underserved segments. Carries ~$6.3m
in employee obligations and ~$35m external debt. See [[sierra-leone--infra-connect]], [[SLE]].
