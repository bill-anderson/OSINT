# Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB)

**Uganda's business and legal registry** — the authority with which companies and other
legal entities are registered, and the original home of the country's mobile
vital-records system before civil registration moved to [[nira-uganda]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **URSB numbers become business tax identity (2025-11-11).** Under the **Tax Procedures
  Amendment Bill**, as individuals' TINs give way to the NIN, **businesses registered with
  URSB will use their URSB registration numbers as TINs** — the corporate half of Uganda's
  single-identifier reform. See [[ura-uganda]], [[ndaga-muntu]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2025-11-11 URA Launches Tax Filing Season, Announces Shift from TIN to NIN]].
- **Origin of the MVRS (built from 2011; moved 2015).** Uganda's **Mobile Vital Records
  System** ([[mvrs-uganda]]) was built by the former Uganda Registration Services Bureau
  **from 2011**; an institutional change in **2015** moved civil registration — and the
  system — to [[nira-uganda]]. See [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2020-01-01 Maintaining civil registration amid COVID-19 - Uganda's mobile notification system (IDRC)]].
