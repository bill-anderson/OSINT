# Paradigm Initiative (PIN)

Pan-African digital-rights organisation; runs the **[[ripoti]]** reporting platform and pursues
strategic data-privacy litigation.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Data Privacy Week stock-take (2026-01).** Applauded new/amended data-protection laws — **Djibouti**
  (Jun 2025), **The Gambia** (Sep 2025), **Burundi** (Jan 2026), **Botswana** (in force Jan 2025),
  **Algeria** (amended Jul 2025) — and named gap countries (DRC, Mozambique, South Sudan, Sudan,
  Guinea-Bissau, Eritrea, Liberia, Namibia, Sierra Leone, Libya). Cited its litigation wins (UBA
  ₦8m; Domino’s/Eat’n’Go ₦3m under NDPA). See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-29 Press release Paradigm Initiative applauds countries with data protection laws]].
