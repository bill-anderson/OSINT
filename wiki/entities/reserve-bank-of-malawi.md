# Reserve Bank of Malawi (RBM)

Malawi's central bank and payments overseer, and the publisher of the quarterly **National Payment Systems
(NPS) reports** that chart the country's fast-growing digital-payments economy. Its data show **mobile-money
subscribers passing 20.1m** (Q2 2025, up from ~16.2m in 2024) and **mobile-money transactions of ~K19.5tn in
2024**, with the interbank settlement running on the MITASS platform — the payments counterpart to Malawi's
foundational-ID and e-government build-out.

**2025 NPS Report (published 2026-03).** Mobile-money **value rose 94% to K37.9tn**, **volumes to 2.4bn**, and
**subscriptions to 19.9m** (only **44.8% active**). Usage is still cash-substitution — **merchant payments just
7.7%** — the key challenge the RBM's strategy targets. The Ministry of Finance raised **wallet limits K1m→K5m**.

**NPS Vision & Strategy 2026–2030 (2026-04).** A payments-modernisation roadmap (Deputy Governor Kisu Simwaka):
affordability for low-income users, cybersecurity/resilience, regulatory modernisation, regional integration,
and (per companion coverage) a **CBDC Proof of Concept by 2030** and **ISO 20022** migration — atop MITASS
(2002) and the **National Switch (2015)**. Full picture: **[[malawi--dpi-pay]]**.
