# Somalia Ministry of Communications and Technology

Somalia's federal ICT ministry (Minister **Mohamed Adam Moalim**), lead body for the
country's digital-transformation strategy, data-protection regime and DPI coordination.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Public Data Protection regulatory framework (~2026-02).** The ministry **drafted** the
  **Regulatory Framework for the Public Data Protection Law**, which the **Council of
  Ministers approved** (reported 2026-02-06). The framework operationalises the existing
  Public Data Protection Law and ties the legal structure to Somalia's digital-transformation
  strategy; the ministry affirmed it passed all constitutional, legal and technical
  procedures. See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-06 Somalia adopts data regulatory framework]].
- **National DPI Summit (2026-01-29).** Co-organiser, with the Office of the PM, of
  Somalia's **first National DPI Summit** (Mogadishu, 14–15 Feb 2026), bringing together
  [[central-bank-of-somalia]], [[nira-somalia]], the National Communications Authority and
  the Data Protection Authority to build an interoperable national digital vision. See
  [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-29 Mogadishu to Host First National Digital Public Infrastructure Summit; Key Agencies to Map Out Digital Transformation Strategy]].
