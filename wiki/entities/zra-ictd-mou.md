# ZRA–ICTD MoU and NDA (2026)

A **Memorandum of Understanding plus a Non-Disclosure Agreement** signed by the
**[[zambia-revenue-authority|Zambia Revenue Authority]]** and the
**[[international-centre-for-tax-and-development|International Centre for Tax and Development]]**,
announced **17 July 2026**.

- **Signatories / principals.** ZRA Commissioner General **Dingani Banda**; ICTD Executive Director
  **Giulia Mascagni**.
- **Scope as announced.** Research, capacity building and innovation initiatives to support
  **evidence-based tax administration** and **domestic resource mobilisation**, explicitly aimed at
  the "growing complexities of the digital economy."
- **Not disclosed.** Value, duration, governance, and — materially — **what taxpayer data the NDA
  covers** and under what safeguards.
