# Econet Wireless Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe's dominant mobile operator (the **opco**, distinct from the wider Econet Group / [[econet-wireless]]
— see ISSUE-008/009). CEO **Douglas Mboweni**.

- **Market dominance (Q4 2025, POTRAZ).** **7,143 base stations** (340 5G) — more than NetOne + Telecel
  combined; **73.75% of subscribers**, ~**81.2% of data traffic**. Parent of [[ecocash]] and
  [[econet-infraco]]. See [[zimbabwe--infra-connect]]. Source: [[2026-04-30-econet-5g-exceeds-netone-telecel-combined]].
