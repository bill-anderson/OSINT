# Gabon Connect (single digital window)

Gabon's planned **unified national digital portal / single window (guichet unique)** to centralise all
dematerialised public services online — presented to President **Brice Clotaire Oligui Nguema** (Feb 2026) by
**IKA Technology** DG **Alexandre Reina**, and positioned as the cornerstone of public-administration
modernisation under **[[digital-gabon-project|Digital Gabon]]**. See [[gabon--dpi-govtech]].
