# Bank of the Republic of Burundi (BRB)

Burundi's central bank and payments regulator (Banque de la République du Burundi). Governor (2026):
**Édouard Normand Bigendako**. The BRB is the driving institution behind Burundi's payments
modernisation — the national switch, the **[[burundipay]]** instant-payment rail, ISO-20022 migration and
an AML/CFT overhaul — even as **cash still dominates** and account dormancy is high. See [[BDI]],
[[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **BurundiPay instant-payment launch (2026-04-23).** [[dpi.pay]] The BRB launched **[[burundipay]]**, a
  national instant-payment platform connecting banks, microfinance and mobile money (bank accounts,
  wallets or phone numbers), ~1,000 TPS, 24/7 — funded by the World Bank's [[pafen-burundi]]. Governor
  Bigendako framed it as "a bridge for interoperability." Source:
  [[2026-04-25-burundipay-platform-connects-banks-microfinance-mobile-money]].
- **ISO 20022 migration (2026-02-05).** [[gov.standards]] The BRB upgraded its Automated Transfer System
  (ATS) to **ISO 20022**, aligning with global/regional payment networks; interbank operations (incl. the
  Régie Nationale des Postes) resumed on the new system 6 Feb. Source:
  [[2026-02-05-burundi-upgrades-payment-system-iso-20022]].
- **AML/CFT overhaul (2026-01-30).** [[gov.legislate]] A new regulation implementing an amended AML/CFT
  law plus **five implementing circulars** (KYC, large-value/cash thresholds, suspicious-transaction
  typologies, internal controls, a sanctions matrix) aligned to [[fatf]] and ESAAMLG standards. Source:
  [[2026-02-16-burundi-central-bank-new-aml-cft-rules]].
- **Payments still cash-heavy (2025 Q2).** [[include.access]] ~97.5m mobile/digital transactions worth
  >4.4tn BIF in Q2 2025, but **deposits/withdrawals were 45%** of value and P2P just 3%; of **7.6m
  registered mobile wallets only 1.6m were active**. The BRB flagged patchy internet, low smartphone
  penetration, high fees and weak digital literacy. Source:
  [[2025-09-29-burundi-digital-payments-growth-cash-dominates]].
- **"Full digitalisation" ambition (2025-04).** The BRB's Payment Systems Management Department set a
  (later-slipping) **Nov 2025** target to fully digitise the financial system on an enhanced national
  switch, with USSD/offline support for low-connectivity users. Source:
  [[2025-04-21-burundi-central-bank-aims-full-digital-transformation]].
