# Synectics Technologies

**The developer of the [[aeonian-project]]** — Uganda's planned hyperscale data and
high-performance computing centre at the [[karuma-hydropower-plant]]. CEO: **Oladele
Oyekunle**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Developing Aeonian with Schneider Electric (2025-09-15).** Synectics is developing the
  facility in partnership with **[[schneider-electric]]**, sited at the Karuma hydropower
  plant on the Nile, housing several AI modules and the **USIO** supercomputer. Oyekunle:
  *"By digitalizing clean energy through best-in-class infrastructure, we are empowering
  Africa to control its data backbone responsibly, sustainably, and sovereignly."*
  **Rollout begins 2026 with the first AI module, full capacity by 2028.** See
  [[tech.ai]], [[infra.store]], [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-15 Synectics, Schneider to build AI center in Uganda starting 2026]].
- **The company's own description (per its website, cited 2025-09-11).** *"Synectics
  Technologies is developing The Aeonian Project, which is a hybrid Tier-4 Plus (4+)
  off-grid green energy regional Hyperscale Data & High-Performance Computing Centre (DHPC)
  powered by 100MW of excess pre-transmission electricity from the government-owned 600MW
  Karuma Hydro Power Plant (K-HPP)."* Data is sent via two leased core dark fibre-optic
  cables to the company's **Kampala headquarters** for remote surveillance and to the
  submarine cable in Kenya for global connection. Proposed services: colocation,
  blockchain, data storage, AI, on-demand cloud, and **DRaaS** for existing regional data
  centres. Source:
  [[2025-09-11 Another 'first AI factory in Africa' - Uganda is said to have made its entry into the AI race]].
- **Cost — single-sourced.** The **$1.2 billion** project cost is reported only by Watchdog
  Uganda (2026-01-28). Flag as single-sourced. Source:
  [[2026-01-28 Uganda to Launch Africa's First Home-Grown AI Supercomputer Center at Karuma Hydropower Plant]].

**Status caution:** USIO is **planned / under development as of 2025-09-15**, not built.
See [[aeonian-project]] for the tense discrepancy across accounts and the contested "first
AI factory in Africa" label.
