# Digitax Gabon / e-tax (DGI revenue digitalization)

Gabon's tax-administration digitalisation programme run by the **Direction Générale des Impôts
([[dgi-gabon]])**. Two linked pieces: the **"Digitax Gabon"** back-office platform (launched
**April 2024**) for integrated management of fiscal processes — tax-base management, recovery,
control, dispute processing and payment by teleprocedure — and the taxpayer-facing **e-tax** portal
(`e-tax.dgi.ga`, announced **18 August 2025**) for online declaration and payment.

## Key facts

- **Uptake:** per the DGI's 2024 report, **at least 80% of tax revenues are now collected via the
  system** ([[2025-09-15-gabon-2026-budget-revenue-digitalization]]).
- **Revenue outturn (2024):** DGI achieved CFA1,021.58bn in treasury revenues against an CFA862.3bn
  target (18% overshoot) and 107% of its CFA969.38bn budgetary target.
- **Policy role:** the anchor of the revenue-digitalisation push behind Gabon's ~$13bn (CFA7,233.3bn)
  2026 budget — Digital Economy Minister Mark-Alexandre Doumba's strategy to widen the tax base into
  the informal economy (40-50% of GDP), promote electronic payments to capture VAT, and secure
  administration payments.

Feeds the treasury-side [[sigfip-gabon]]; complements the payments-side [[madigipaie-gabon]].
