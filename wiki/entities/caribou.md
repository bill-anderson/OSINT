# Caribou (Caribou Digital)

Research and advisory firm on digital economies and digital identity in the Global South; publishes
fieldwork-based analysis of DPI inclusion/exclusion.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital Identity and Migration (2026-02).** Synthesis of the *Identity in the Age of Migration*
  project (fieldwork in Kenya and Germany): digital-ID systems are political infrastructures that
  include and exclude migrants/refugees. Kenya cases — the **Social Health Authority** excluding refugee
  IDs; SIM-registration cutting refugees off from **M-PESA**; double-registration denying tens of
  thousands of citizens national IDs. Cites Ethiopia’s **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]]** UNHCR
  joint-committee as a rare participatory model. See [[dpi.id]], [[include.divides]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-01 Digital Identity and Migration Struggles for Equitable Technology Governance]].
