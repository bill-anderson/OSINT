# Kenya School of Government (KSG)

Kenya's statutory public-service training institution, established by Parliament in 2012
(**KSG Act No. 9**) to build the knowledge, skills and competencies of public servants.
Director General **Prof. Nura Mohamed**. It is the government's main vehicle for equipping
civil servants with digital and governance capabilities.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Prospective Huawei digital-skills MoU (as of 2026-01-12).** Following a high-level
  meeting, KSG and **[[huawei]]** Kenya opened **discussions** (not a signed agreement) on a
  Memorandum of Understanding to train public servants in **AI, cybersecurity, cloud
  computing and digital governance**, with a stated aim of integrating practical AI/cloud
  applications into KSG executive programmes and bolstering compliance with the Computer
  Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018 and the Data Protection Act 2019. The MoU is prospective
  only — no [[finance.mou]] deal entity is created pending signature. Huawei Public Affairs
  Director Yuta Leng represented the vendor ([[geopol.china]]). See [[capacity.training]],
  [[tech.ai]], [[KEN]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-12 Kenya, Huawei to boost public sector digital skills]].
