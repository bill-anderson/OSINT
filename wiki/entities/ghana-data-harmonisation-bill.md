# Ghana Data Harmonisation Bill 2025

A **[[nita-ghana|NITA]]**-drafted bill establishing a comprehensive legal framework for **data sharing,
standardisation and interoperability** across public and private institutions — opened for **public
consultation 19–27 Nov 2025**. Part of Ghana's "15 digital bills" tech-law reform agenda; the interoperability
backbone beneath the Ghana-Card-integrated GovTech stack. See [[ghana--gov-protect]], [[ghana--dpi-govtech]].
