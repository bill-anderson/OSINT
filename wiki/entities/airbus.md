# Airbus (Defence and Space)

European aerospace group; its **Defence and Space** division (Toulouse) is the prime contractor for
national space programmes across Africa. In this wiki it appears as the builder of Angola's first
Earth-observation satellite.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Prime contractor for [[angeo-1]] (2026-03).** Airbus Defence and Space is building Angola's first
  Earth-observation satellite on its **S250** optical platform (>1,000 high-res images/day), under a
  **~€225m/$259m** contract with **[[ggpen]]** kicked off at Toulouse on 16 March 2026 (Airbus EVP
  Space Systems Alain Fauré; VP Christophe Lemercier). SKP milestone validated May 2026, first ground
  infrastructure from June 2026. Airbus also participates in Angola's space-skills training (e.g.
  University of Namibe VSAT programme). See [[AGO]], [[data.satellite]], [[angola--data-satellite]].
