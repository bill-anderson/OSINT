# Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC), Tanzania

Tanzania's national **data-protection authority**, the regulator established under the country's **Personal
Data Protection Act** to oversee the lawful handling and safeguarding of personal information. Director General:
**Dr. Emmanuel Mkilia**. Tanzania's counterpart to Kenya's [[odpc-kenya]], the Ugandan PDPO
([[personal-data-protection-office-uganda]]) and Congo-Brazzaville's commission
([[national-personal-data-protection-commission-congo]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Certified Data Protection Officer (CDPO) Programme (2026-07).** PDPC partnered with the **Open University
  of Tanzania (OUT)** to launch a national **CDPO Programme** professionalising Data Protection Officers — OUT
  delivers and assesses the training; **PDPC provides official certification**. Aimed at building compliance
  capacity across government, finance, telecoms, healthcare, education and technology as Tanzania's digital
  services expand. A build-out of enforcement/compliance capacity rather than new statute (cf. the ID4Africa
  "DPA-capacity" thread in [[gov.protect]]). See [[capacity.training]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-14 OUT, PDPC Launch National Data Protection Programme]].
