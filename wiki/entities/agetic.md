# AGETIC

Mali's **government ICT agency** (Agence des Technologies de l'Information et de la
Communication), under the Ministry of Communication, Digital Economy and Administration
Modernisation (minister Alhamdou Ag Ilyène; DG Mohamed Assalia Boncana). Builder and operator
of the state's shared digital infrastructure — the government network, the **.ml** ccTLD, and
e-service portals — framed explicitly as **digital sovereignty**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Government Intranet + .ml domain (2025-12).** At AGETIC's 22nd board session, the agency
  is progressively interconnecting all public and para-public services into a private, secured
  **government Intranet** (13 services already integrated), pushing a single-window services
  portal, and moving a draft decree to **make the government messaging system mandatory**. On
  the **.ml** country-code domain, AGETIC managed **13,441 domain names** across **30
  registrars** and urged wider .ml adoption for Mali's cyberspace visibility. 2026 budget down
  ~0.67% vs 2025. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[infra.connect]], [[sovereignty]].
  Source: [[2025-12-30-lessor-agetic-intranet-point-ml]].
- **Justice e-services + E-verif (2026-02-10).** Built the online **criminal-record**
  (ecasierjudiciaire.gouv.ml) and **nationality-certificate** (enationalite.gouv.ml) platforms
  launched during the Week of Justice — citizens apply with their **NINA** and civil-status
  details, documents returned by email (accessible to the diaspora and remote areas), plus an
  **E-verif** authenticity tool giving each document a unique code to combat forgery in job
  applications and tenders. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-12-maliweb-justice-casier-nationalite-en-ligne]].
