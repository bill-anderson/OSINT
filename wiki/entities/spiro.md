# Spiro

**The largest electric-mobility operator in Africa** by unit count — electric motorcycles and
battery-swap infrastructure.

## Key facts (as reported 2026-07)

- **Over 100,000 electric motorcycles deployed.**
- **2,500 battery-swap stations.**
- **Seven African markets** (individual markets not enumerated in the source the wiki holds — a
  provenance gap).

Spiro's scale establishes the baseline of the continent's EV story: it is a **two-wheeler** story, and
the swap-station network is the real asset. Four-wheeler electric ride-hailing (see [[gocab]]) has
been the harder segment to unlock — higher upfront vehicle cost, longer payback, and dependence on a
customer base willing to pay premium fares.
