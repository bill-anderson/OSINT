# PayLogic

A payments-technology firm supplying **national and regional interoperable payment switches** across African markets — the infrastructure that connects banks, microfinance institutions and mobile-money operators. Executive Chairman: Mohamed Mekouar.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Comoros National Payment Switch (2025).** PayLogic delivered the Comoros switch (awarded Jan 2025, go-live Aug 2025) under the World Bank PADSF — a unified interoperable platform for real-time transactions across banks, MFIs and mobile wallets. See [[COM]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source: [[2025-08-23 PayLogic go-live of Comoros national interoperable payment switch]].
- **Regional footprint.** Its switch technology also underpins the **CEMAC** regional switch, **Eswatini** ([[SWZ]]), and **Lesotho** ([[LSO]], "LeSwitch").
