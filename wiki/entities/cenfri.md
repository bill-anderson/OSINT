# Cenfri

South Africa-based financial-sector and data-governance think tank (Centre for Financial Regulation
and Inclusion).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Reassessing African DPAs (2026-03).** Argued that AfCFTA Digital Trade Protocol obligations lock
  African states into a GDPR-style "omnibus" model their under-resourced DPAs cannot enforce (the
  "transplant effect"), and that integrating **sectoral regulators** (esp. financial) is the pragmatic
  fix. See [[gov.protect]], [[afcfta-digital-trade-protocol]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-23 Reassessing the role of data protection authorities in Africa]].
- **Rwanda data-governance push (2026-05).** Profiled the NISR Data Governance Unit and the shift from
  data ownership to stewardship; interview with DGU lead Maryse Gwiza. See [[RWA]], [[nisr]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-07 What will determine the success of Rwanda’s data governance push]].
