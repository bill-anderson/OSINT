# ARCEP Burkina Faso

Burkina Faso's **electronic-communications and postal regulator**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DNSSEC for ".bf" (Dec 2025).** ARCEP launched DNSSEC for the national ".bf" domain (5 Dec 2025), with ICANN support — a DNS-integrity/trust measure. Source: [[2025-12-09 Burkina Faso Targets Online Trust with New Internet Security Measures (DNSSEC)]].
- **Operator revenue data / local-HQ decree (Feb 2026).** ARCEP's 2024 operator-revenue figures (Onatel CFA117.19bn, Orange CFA332.91bn, Telecel CFA40.44bn) underpin a decree requiring large firms — the three mobile operators — to build local headquarters. Source: [[2026-02-13 Burkina Faso Orders Large Firms to Build Local Headquarters]].
