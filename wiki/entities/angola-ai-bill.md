# Angola draft Artificial Intelligence law (2025)

Angola's **draft AI law** — a comprehensive framework governing the development, supply and use of AI, structurally influenced by **OECD principles** and the **EU AI Act's** risk-based approach. Among the first wave of specific African AI bills (with Egypt, Ghana, Morocco, Nigeria).

## Profile

- **Dual objective:** build a domestic AI industry and **technological sovereignty** while establishing human-centric safeguards. Broad scope with strong **extraterritorial jurisdiction** (activities outside Angola affecting Angolan public interests or persons); excludes purely personal/family use and scientific R&D.
- Reviewed alongside a **concurrent draft amendment to the Data Protection Act** ([[angola-data-protection-law-22-11]]) — the AI/DP-law linkage. Analysis: TechHive Advisory (cite as analysis). See [[tech.ai]]. Source: [[2025-12-29 Review of Angola's Proposed Law on Artificial Intelligence]].
