# Hikvision

Chinese surveillance-camera and facial-recognition vendor, a leading supplier in
African "smart city"/"safe city" deployments. Its cameras are the typical
hardware in the bundled Chinese surveillance packages financed by China Eximbank.

## Activity in this wiki

- **African surveillance market (per Mar 2026 IDS report).** The Institute of
  Development Studies ("Smart City Surveillance in Africa", March 2026) found
  African states spent **>$2bn** on facial-recognition and ANPR systems, mostly
  Chinese-supplied, and documented cases of the technology being repurposed
  against political opposition (e.g. facial-recognition fears in Zimbabwe
  ([[ZWE]]); cameras concentrated in opposition strongholds in Mozambique
  ([[MOZ]])). See [[infra.cybersec]], [[tech.ai]], [[geopol.china]].
  Source: [[2026-04-04 Chinese surveillance rolled out in Africa ZTE, Hikvision, Huawei at the helm]].
- **Nigeria deployments.** In Nigeria ([[NGA]]), Lagos deployed ~**10,000**
  high-definition cameras, and Hikvision was part of the [[zte]]/Hikvision Abuja
  security network ($470m spend, $399m financed by China Exim Bank). See [[XAF]],
  [[zte]], [[huawei]].
  Source: [[2026-04-04 Chinese surveillance rolled out in Africa ZTE, Hikvision, Huawei at the helm]].
