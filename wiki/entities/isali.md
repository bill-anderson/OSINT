# Isali (إيصالي)

A Sudanese **electronic government-revenue collection** service (person-to-
government / P2G payments), letting citizens and traders pay government fees and
dues digitally via bank apps and certified service points. Part of the
Port-Sudan government's wartime drive to digitise state revenue, cut cash
reliance and improve fiscal transparency, delivered through the "unified
collection apparatus."

## Activity in this wiki

- **National contract (2026-04).** The **[[ministry-of-finance-sudan]]** signed a
  contract with the **[[national-workers-bank-sudan]]** to deliver Isali. See
  [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-30 Finance Ministry-National Workers Bank contract for Isali e-collection service (SUNA)]].
- **Sub-national diffusion, Gadarif → Kassala (2026-05).** Gadarif state's
  finance directorate hosted a Kassala delegation to transfer its Isali
  experience, including digitised crop-market collection and real-time revenue
  monitoring — state-to-state spread of the DPI at local-government level. See
  [[digital.localgov]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-03 Gadarif transfers Isali electronic-collection experience to Kassala state (SUNA)]].
