# GSMA Mobile Gender Gap Report

An **annual flagship index** from the **[[gsma]] Connected Women** programme (fieldwork by Ipsos;
funded in part by UK Aid, Sida and the [[gates-foundation]]) tracking the gender gap in **mobile
ownership, smartphone ownership, mobile internet adoption and mobile money** across low- and
middle-income countries (LMICs), Sub-Saharan Africa among them. A standing OSINT reference for
gender-disaggregated connectivity and financial-inclusion figures ([[include.divides]],
[[include.access]]).

**Cite, don't absorb.** Pull a specific dated figure into a place or concept page only as a citation
of the relevant edition; leave the report's numbers in it. Country deep-dives (e.g. the GSMA's
Nigeria mobile-gender-gap work) arrive as their own sources that cite this series.
