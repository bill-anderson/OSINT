# Chinguitel

One of Mauritania's mobile-network operators (a Sudatel-group subsidiary), alongside **[[mauritel]]** and
**[[mattel]]**. In **November 2025** Chinguitel completed a full network modernisation in Nouakchott with
**[[huawei|Huawei]]** — a Chinese vendor footprint in Mauritanian mobile infrastructure ([[geopol.china]]),
set against the EU-financed cable and data centre. A **provisional 5G licence winner** (April 2026, bid
MRU 260.6m + 5% of revenue) and among the operators previously served biometric-SIM enforcement notices by
**[[are-mauritania|ARE]]**.
