# Digital Algeria 2030 (SNTN-2030)

Algeria's **National Digital Transformation Strategy 2025–2030**, unveiled **May 2025** and overseen by the **[[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria|High Commission for Digitalisation]]**. Built around **seven pillars** (digital infrastructure, cybersecurity, technological sovereignty, education/innovation/skilled jobs, and making digital a growth driver). Headline target: **100% of public services online by 2030**.

Delivery vehicles and milestones threading through this strategy:
- **GovTech:** [[dzair-services|Dzair Services]] single-window; Bawabatak portal; [[amlak|AMLAK]] land registry; Tabioucom e-payments.
- **Legal:** [[algeria-digital-id-trust-law|digital-ID & trust-services law]] (2025); [[algeria-data-governance-framework|public-data governance framework]] (Decree 25-350).
- **AI:** [[algeria-national-ai-strategy|National AI Strategy]] (7% of GDP by 2027); Oran AI data centre.
- **Infrastructure:** five regional data centres; sovereign cloud; 5G licences (Dec 2025); FTTH build-out. Sovereignty is reinforced by **mandatory data localisation** (see [[anpdcp]]).
