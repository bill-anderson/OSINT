# Public Procurement and Disposal of Assets Authority (PPDA, Malawi)

Malawi's public-procurement regulator (Acting Director General **Timothy Kalembo**), operator of the **Malawi
National Electronic Procurement System (MANePS)**. From **1 April 2026**, under a March-2026 government
directive, MANePS became **mandatory for all public entities** — a shift from paper to a fully digital
procurement platform aimed at transparency, value-for-money and lower supplier logistics (remote submission/
registration), aligned to Malawi 2063. It advances the earlier World Bank Fiscal-Governance eGP work.
