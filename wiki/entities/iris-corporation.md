# Iris Corporation Berhad

Malaysian identity-technology company; producer of **Senegal's national ID card** (the ECOWAS biometric
CNI) for roughly a decade. Initial contract **2016 (~CFA50bn / ~US$90m)**, renewed **2021 at US$19.5m for
three million cards**. Supplies both national ID and voter cards.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Named in the Feb 2026 DAF breach.** A leaked email attributed to an Iris senior GM (Quik Saw
  Choo / "Choo") warned Senegalese officials that attackers breached **2 [[daf-senegal|DAF]] servers on
  2026-01-19**, stealing card-personalisation data; Iris reportedly cut server access, changed passwords,
  blocked foreign-mission connections and dispatched a technical team to Dakar (planned **2026-01-22**).
  Reporting (Gambia Journal) tied the incident to an **unpaid-invoices dispute** between Iris and the state,
  which requested systems be shut down — raising unconfirmed questions of a link between the commercial
  dispute and the attack. The government is examining whether the supplier could bear liability. See
  [[SEN]], [[dpi.id]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Sources:
  [[2026-02-10 Senegal data breach disrupts national ID issuance]],
  [[2026-02-10 Senegal shuts National ID office after ransomware attack]].
- **ID/voter-card supplier for 2025 electoral enrolment.** Iris named as Senegal's contractor for national
  ID and voter cards during the ECOWAS-biometric-ID voter-registration exercise (Feb–Jul 2025). Source:
  [[2025-02-18 Senegal targets 3M ECOWAS biometric ID holders for voter registration]].
