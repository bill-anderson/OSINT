# Ministry of Digital Transition & Technological Innovation (MTND)

Côte d'Ivoire's lead digital ministry — the political owner of the country's strategy stack (National
Digital Development, AI, Data Governance 2030, Cybersecurity), of [[sndi-cote-divoire|SNDI]],
[[anssi-cote-divoire|ANSSI]] and the govtech build, and the driver of the "technological sovereignty"
framing that runs through the CIV record.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Leadership change (Jan 2026).** **Djibril Ouattara** (ex-MTN Côte d'Ivoire CEO) replaced **Ibrahim
  Kalil Konaté**; the portfolio was renamed **Digital Transition and Technological Innovation**. Source:
  [[2026-01-26-djibril-ouattara-appointed-minister-wearetech]].
- **Seven priorities / first 100 days (2026).** Ouattara's first-100-days articulation set out **seven
  priority areas** under a technological-sovereignty banner, feeding the NDP/PND 2026–2030 target of
  lifting digital from ~6% to **15% of GDP by 2030**. Source:
  [[2026-05-02-digital-minister-first-100-days-techreviewafrica]].
