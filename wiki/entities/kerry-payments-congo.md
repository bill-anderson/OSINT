# Kerry Payments (Congo-Brazzaville)

A **PawaPay subsidiary** (Kerry Payments Brazzaville SARLU) that secured a **VAS payment-aggregator licence
from ARPCE** (Jun 2026) — offering a **unified API across MNOs, banks and MFIs**. Concrete evidence on the
ARPCE/BEAC mobile-money co-regulation question GSMA flagged. Source:
[[2026-06-16-pawapay-kerry-payments-vas-aggregator-licence-techafricanews]].
