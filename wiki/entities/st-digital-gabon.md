# ST DIGITAL GABON

Gabonese datacenter developer/operator, led by **Laïka Mba**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gabon's first Tier III datacenter (inaugurated 2026-07-04).** Built to Tier III
  international standards to host sensitive government and business data within Gabon's
  borders, "reducing reliance on foreign digital infrastructure" — explicitly framed as
  digital sovereignty by President **Brice Clotaire Oligui Nguema**. To support public
  services, cybersecurity, AI applications and business hosting.
- **Startup-hosting MoU.** Signed with the **Ministry of Digital Economy, Digitalization
  and Innovation** (Minister **Mark-Alexandre Doumba**) for preferential hosting rates to
  ministry-supported startups. See [[finance.mou]], [[tech.innovate]].
- **Wider ST Digital group — Togo arm (2026-06-02).** ST Digital markets sovereign cloud,
  sovereign AI and digital solutions **across West Africa**; its **ST Digital Togo** arm
  (DG **Justine Adebiyi**) signed a partnership with state-owned **[[sin-togo]]** to launch
  private cloud services at the Tier III **[[lome-data-centre]]**, framed as building
  Togo's digital sovereignty. This indicates ST Digital Gabon is one national arm of a
  broader ST Digital group operating region-wide. Deal:
  [[sin-st-digital-lome-cloud-deal]]. See [[TGO]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-02 SIN, ST Digital to deploy cloud services at Lomé data center]].

See [[GAB]], [[infra.store]], [[infra.cybersec]].
