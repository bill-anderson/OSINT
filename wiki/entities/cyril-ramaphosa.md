# Cyril Ramaphosa

President of South Africa; host and convenor role in the 2025–26 wave of
hyperscaler and Gulf investment into SA's data-centre/AI economy.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Google Cloud Summit (2026-07-01).** Opened Google's first Africa Cloud Summit
  in Sandton; his SONA cited **55 data centres** built in SA. See [[ZAF]],
  [[google]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-01 Google Cloud Summit in Africa Highlights the Continent's Digital Transformation and Unveils New Agentic AI and Infrastructure Investments]].
- **G20 host (2025-11).** As G20 host, invited the **[[uae]]** to announce its
  **$1bn** African AI initiative in Johannesburg. Source:
  [[2025-11-22 UAE announces $1 billion initiative to expand AI in Africa]].
- Presides over the government reframing data centres as critical infrastructure.
  Source: [[2026-02-25 South Africa puts data centres on par with energy, ports in big policy shift]].

- **4IR Commission and the hype critique (as of 2019).** Ramaphosa's **Presidential Commission
  on the 4IR** (2019) and the WEF Centre for the 4IR at the CSIR are the backdrop to a series of
  Alison Gillwald ([[research-ict-africa]]) op-eds arguing that SA's 4IR/AI framing diverts
  resources from the connectivity, skills and affordability basics needed to close digital
  inequality — historical framing, dated to 2019/2020, not current data. See [[include.divides]],
  [[tech.ai]], [[gov.policy]]. Sources:
  [[2019-08-20 South Africa is caught in the global hype of the fourth industrial revolution]],
  [[2019-10-03 South Africa must harness technology in a way that helps fix its problems]],
  [[2019-10-03 Addressing SA's extreme digital inequality]].

See [[ZAF]], [[tech.ai]], [[infra.store]].
