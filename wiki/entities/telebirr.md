# telebirr

[[ethio-telecom]]'s mobile-money and fintech platform — the dominant digital-payments
rail in [[ETH]], embedded in daily economic activity and anchoring payments, savings and
micro-loans at national scale. Distinct from [[safaricom]]'s M-PESA Ethiopia, its main
private-sector challenger.

## Activity in this wiki

- **72m customers (mid-2025).** telebirr recorded **72 million customers** by mid-2025,
  making it the largest single mobile-money base in Ethiopia and a core layer of the
  country's fintech growth. See [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-07 Why Ethiopia, Somalia and Djibouti are starting to matter in digital Africa]].
- **DE2030 pillar.** Named alongside the National Payment Gateway as a driver of
  [[digital-ethiopia-2030]]'s projected **sevenfold growth** in digital payments toward a
  cash-light economy, unlocking credit, savings and insurance access. See
  [[digital-ethiopia-2030]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Digital Ethiopia 2030 Charting a Sovereign Path to Prosperity]].
- **National-scale anchor (2026-01).** [[ethio-telecom]] continues to build telebirr into
  its expansion into financial technology, supporting payments, savings and micro-loans;
  used to deliver the Awash Bank "Tila" microfinance service and smart-device financing.
  Source: [[2026-01-30 Inside Ethio Telecom's bold TechCo transformation]].
- **Competitive context (Q3 FY26).** As telebirr scaled, rival M-PESA Ethiopia
  ([[safaricom-ethiopia]]) reached 5.2m active customers (+258.5% YoY) after
  [[ethswitch]] integration — the two now compete across Ethiopia's interoperable
  payments ecosystem. Source:
  [[2026-02-02 Safaricom Ethiopia Surpasses 12 Million Users as M-PESA Adoption Grows]].
- **Huawei integration workstream (announced 2026-07-17).** Under the
  **[[ethio-telecom-huawei-digital-partnership]]**, **[[huawei]]** would integrate business solutions with
  telebirr to enable digital payments, financial services, e-commerce and value-added services for
  enterprises — putting a Chinese vendor's enterprise stack directly on Ethiopia's dominant payments rail.
  Reported as scoping discussions, not an executed agreement. See [[dpi.pay]], [[geopol.china]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-ethio-telecom-huawei-digital-infrastructure]].
