# Tech Hive Advisory

An Africa-focused (Nigeria-based) technology, data-protection and privacy advisory firm. Of OSINT
value here as a **recurring reference publisher**: it originates the **"Roundup on Data Protection in
Africa"** series and the bimonthly **"Update on Privacy in Africa,"** now produced with
[[digital-policy-alert]] — the datable, primary-citing reference reports this wiki draws on for
continental data-protection state-of-play and enforcement chronology.
