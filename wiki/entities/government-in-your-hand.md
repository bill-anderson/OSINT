# Government in Your Hand (GIYH)

**Eswatini's digital-government flagship** — both the theme of the
[[eswatini-digitalisation-strategy-2024-2028]] and the delivery platform meant to put
public services on citizens' phones. Owned by [[ministry-of-ict-eswatini]]; its
implementation roadmap is scoped under the [[world-bank]]
[[digital-eswatini-project]]. See [[SWZ]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Scope (ToR, 2025, year precision).** The Digital Government Implementation Roadmap
  covers **digital ID, data exchange, social registries, PKI, payments and ~200
  services**. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 ToR - Government in Your Hand Digital Government Implementation Roadmap (Digital Eswatini)]].
- **App launched 2025-05-21 at Ezulwini.** [[savannah-maziya]] unveiled the **GIYH mobile
  app** alongside the [[eswatini-revenue-service]]'s **TaxPal** app, with Finance Minister
  **Neal Rijkenberg** and ERS Commissioner General **Brightwell Nkambule** in attendance.
  Launch-day services: check the status of IDs, passports and travel documents; register a
  business name; **pay vehicle licences via Mobile Money**; access educational-financing
  information — with Maziya promising new services "every six months." Login is **national
  ID plus an OTP by SMS**. **Over 2,000 downloads by 2025-06-11.** See [[dpi.pay]],
  [[include.access]]. Source: [[2025-05-21-eswatini-launches-new-apps-giyh-taxpal]].
- **The privacy criticism, substantiated (2025-07-04).** The local **Sunday Observer**
  reported that **its own reporter was able to locate public data on the system**, calling
  it **"a glaring security risk"** — the specific finding behind the general "criticism over
  data privacy" the wiki previously carried undated. The government **acknowledged rather
  than denied it**: **Bongani Ndlangamandla**, Director of eGovernment Services, said *"we
  are aware of some gaps, hence the need for a proper digital ID and digital signatures."*
  That is an admission that GIYH was **launched ahead of its own foundational layer** —
  digital ID and PKI are exactly what the [[digital-eswatini-project]] ToR scopes, and what
  the Google workstream later moved toward (2025-10). See [[gov.protect]],
  [[infra.cybersec]], [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-04-biometricupdate-eswatini-giyh-privacy-criticism]].
- **The critique reached the Prime Minister (2026-03-18).** PM **Russell Dlamini**, denying
  he opposed GIYH, told Parliament his office is *"concerned about the protection of people's
  data should our systems not be robust enough, hence the emphasis that Government In Your
  Hand must be **watertight**"* — adding that his office **does not support outsourcing data
  to global IT companies** and that any MoU signed without proper data-protection
  consideration is *"meaningless."* This is the same argument as the
  [[eswatini-google-partnership]] cabinet row; see [[eswatini--dpi-govtech]].
- **Service ambition (2026-05).** The [[digital-eswatini-project|Eswatini Digital Transformation Project]]
  negotiations frame GIYH as the channel for passport applications, business
  registration, vehicle licensing, health services, education systems and social
  services. See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-18 Eswatini promotes digital transformation project through World Bank engagements]].
- **Aligned to the DRA (2024-11-22).** [[savannah-maziya]]: "It is encouraging that the
  findings of this DRA are aligned with the Government in Your Hand Strategy" — though
  the [[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]] itself found DPI, connectivity and
  regulation lagging, and **half of stakeholders (2024)** perceiving strategy
  implementation as delayed. Sources:
  [[2024-11-22 Eswatini unveils readiness report for digital ID, transformation push]],
  [[2024-12-05 Eswatini Advances Toward Digital Transformation - Key Findings from the Digital Readiness Assessment]].
- **Google tooling (launched on or before 2025-01-20).** The [[eswatini-google-partnership]]
  puts [[google]] Workspace, AI and digital-addressing work into GIYH; it became a cabinet
  controversy in Apr 2026. See that page and [[eswatini--dpi-govtech]]. Sources:
  [[2025-01-20-insidebiz-eswatini-partners-with-google]],
  [[2025-01-21-google-works-with-eswatini-to-accelerate-digital-economy]].
