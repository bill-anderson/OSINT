# Bilhete de Identidade (BI) — Angola

Angola's national civil identity document — the foundational credential the state's
[[dpi.id]] rebuild is organised around. The BI derives from the birth-registration record
"through interoperability between the respective information systems"
([[2026-07-01-impressao-do-bi-abrange-todas-as-provincias]]); it does **not** replace the
birth certificate (*certidão de nascimento*), which remains the first proof of juridical
existence.

## Current state

- **Coverage is low and unequal.** Per the **Censo 2024 definitive results (reported
  2025-11)**, only **52.4% of the population held a BI**, split **63% urban vs 30.9%
  rural**; **14.9m people had no BI** (2.4m+ in Luanda alone), and holding rises by age
  (30% of 5–14s, 55% of 15–24s, 66% of 25+). Birth registration stood at **63% (2024)**,
  up from 53% (2014). Source: [[2025-11-26-quase-metade-dos-angolanos-sem-bilhete-de-identidade]].
- **Target:** 15 million BIs issued by 2027 ([[2026-03-17-governo-fixa-bi-original-para-registo-de-obitos]]).
- **Toward sole-legal-ID status.** A Council of Ministers bill (2026-04-29) would make the
  BI the **single valid identification document**, mandatory for opening bank accounts,
  telecoms, school enrolment, property registration and contracts — ending the historical
  acceptance of the voter card, driving licence or passport as substitutes. It consolidates
  BI and criminal-record rules into one diploma. First issue is **free**; a second copy
  costs **4,500–8,000 Kz**. Security features: **QR code, barcode and microchip**. Went to
  the National Assembly. Source: [[2026-04-29-bi-documento-unico-valido-identificacao]].
- **Death registration** now requires the deceased's **original BI** (effective
  immediately, 2026-03). Source: [[2026-03-17-governo-fixa-bi-original-para-registo-de-obitos]].
- **Fraud on the record.** The minister concedes fraudulent issuance occurs "with human
  interference" — foreigners obtaining BIs through complicit officials; such BIs are
  cancelled and staff dismissed and referred for prosecution. Source:
  [[2026-07-02-nj-bi-tratado-a-partir-do-domicilio-mediante-taxa]].

Production decentralisation, the 48h target, BI-at-birth and home delivery are treated on
**[[angola--dpi-id]]** and **[[angola--dpi-registry]]**.
