# Taslim Okunola

Nigerian ([[NGA]]) Global Strategy & Operations Manager at [[google]], from
Ibadan. An advocate of building products **"from scratch"** for African users
rather than scaling global products down for emerging markets.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Localising Google products (2026-03-28).** In a Business Insider Africa
  profile, Okunola described helping bring **Nigerian English** and
  **Yoruba, Hausa and Igbo** into Google Assistant and Google Maps, closing the
  gap between global products and how Nigerians actually speak. He argues local
  companies win because "they are building from scratch for their environment and
  their users," and stresses that trust and data discipline are foundational.
  Cites ITU (~36–38% of Africans online) and GSMA (400m+ Sub-Saharan mobile-
  internet users). See [[tech.ai]], [[include.access]], [[XSS]], [[google]].
  Source: [[2026-03-28 Meet Taslim Okunola, the Nigerian behind Google's push for localised technology in Africa]].
