# Centre for Digital Public Infrastructure (CDPI)

A DPI advisory body within the [[gates-foundation]] DPI ecosystem that convenes
cross-country peer-learning to advance DPI implementation. It runs webinars where
implementing governments share their trajectories, governance and funding models.
See [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Benin peer-learning session (June 2025).** CDPI hosted the virtual
  peer-learning webinar where [[asin-benin]] shared [[BEN]]'s nine-year,
  X-Road-inspired DPI experience — as of mid-2025 **1,500+ services integrated,
  55+ institutions connected and 70M+ data-exchange transactions** on the
  [[uxp-benin]] platform. Attendees included officials from Ghana, Lesotho, South
  Africa and Zambia, part of CDPI's wider cross-country learning effort. See
  [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-23 Benin shares its X-Road-inspired DPI interoperability experience in peer learning webinar]].
