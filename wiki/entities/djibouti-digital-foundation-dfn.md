# Djibouti Digital Foundation (DFN)

The **World-Bank-financed national fibre programme** (aligned with the regional **[[eardip|EARDIP]]** project)
extending connectivity from Djibouti-ville and Balbala to the **five interior regions** — the vehicle for
closing Djibouti's urban–rural digital divide.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Reaching the interior (2025).** Fibre reached **Ali-Sabieh regional hospital** (>200 km deployed;
  enabling **instant inter-hospital medical-record sharing** vs a full day before) and **Damerjog** (Arta:
  a school with 1,200+ pupils, sub-prefecture and clinic online); a stakeholder/grievance-mechanism
  workshop covered the rollout across the capital and five regions. Sources:
