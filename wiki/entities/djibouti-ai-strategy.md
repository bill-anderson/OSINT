# Djibouti National AI Strategy

Djibouti's **first national AI Strategy** — launched **Jan 2026** with **UNESCO RAM + UN ESCWA** support
(27 Jan workshop), under [[vision-djibouti-2035|Vision 2035]]'s "Smart Nation" goal. Minister Dawaleh
separately pitched Djibouti as an **"emerging platform for AI / digital innovation" to Chinese capital**
(China Exim Bank, Beijing, Jun 2026) — a China-patron vector. Sources:
[[2026-01-29-national-ai-strategy-wearetech]], [[2026-06-22-djibouti-china-industrial-ai-roadmap-beijing-adi]].
