# TouchNet

A pan-African cloud/digital-transformation provider (from a B2B ISP origin, ~15 years, 300+ business
clients/MSPs) offering **decentralised Sovereign AI Cloud services** to data-centre operators, carriers,
MSPs, CSPs and ISPs across Africa. See [[infra.store]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Zadara Sovereign AI Cloud in Kenya (2025-07).** [[tech.ai]] [[sovereignty]] TouchNet brought the
  **Zadara AI Sovereign Cloud** — locally hosted at **[[ixafrica-data-centres|iXAfrica]]**'s Nairobi One
  (NBOX1) campus and managed by a Kenya service provider — offering compute, networking, storage and
  **GPUaaS** without reliance on offshore services. An earlier sovereign-AI-cloud entrant predating the
  later Servernah/Baobab launches. See [[kenya--infra-store]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-17-touchnet-sovereign-ai-cloud-ixafrica-techafricanews]].
