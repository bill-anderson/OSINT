# Lusaka City Council (LCC)

Zambia's capital-city local authority — the **first of 116 councils** to go live on **[[zamportal]]** (May
2026), leading the e-Council rollout (pilot councils Ndola, Kitwe, Solwezi, Chingola, Kalulushi, Kalumbila,
Chilanga to follow by 30 Jun 2026). See [[zambia--dpi-govtech]].
