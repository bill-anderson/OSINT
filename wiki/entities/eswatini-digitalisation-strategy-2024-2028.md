# Eswatini Digitalisation Strategy 2024-2028

**Eswatini's national digital strategy**, owned by [[ministry-of-ict-eswatini]] and
themed **"Government in Your Hand"** — the frame every other digital initiative in the
country is fitted to. See [[SWZ]], [[gov.policy]].

*The wiki holds no copy of the strategy document itself; everything below is reported
by sources that cite it. Titles vary across sources — "Eswatini Digitalization
Strategy (2024-2028)", "Eswatini Government Digital Transformation Strategy", "the
Kingdom of Eswatini's 2024-2028 digital strategy" — all appear to denote this
instrument.*

## Activity in this wiki

- **The strategy and its theme.** [[savannah-maziya]]: "Eswatini has embarked on a new
  drive to transform public services through the implementation of the Eswatini
  Digitalization Strategy (2024-2028) themed 'Government in Your Hand'" **(2024-12-10)**
  — see [[government-in-your-hand]] for the programme. Source:
  [[2024-12-10 Unveiling Eswatini's Digital Path]].
- **Implementation is the weak point (2024).** The
  [[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]] found that a national digital strategy is in
  place but carries only **moderate political support**: **at least 64% of stakeholders
  (2024)** acknowledged its implementation, yet **half perceived it as delayed**, citing
  weak design, insufficient political backing, lack of financial support and limited
  institutional coordination. See [[eseparc]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-05 Eswatini Advances Toward Digital Transformation - Key Findings from the Digital Readiness Assessment]].
- **Anchored to fiscal reform (2025-07-31).** Minister for Economic Planning and
  Development Thambo Gina tied the [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]] to it: "This report
  aligns with the Kingdom of Eswatini's 2024-2028 digital strategy... how digital
  transformation can contribute to accelerating our ongoing efforts to boost inclusive
  economic growth and domestic revenues and in so doing reduce reliance on **SACU
  transfers**." See [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-31 Eswatini's Digital Transformation Crucial to Unlocking Growth, Jobs, and Economic Resilience]].
