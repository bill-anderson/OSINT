# Malswitch (national payment switch, Malawi)

The operator of Malawi's **National Switch** — the interbank/mobile-money/ATM interoperability rail (established
**2015**) that underpins retail payments and, increasingly, fintech aggregation. **[[paychangu]]**'s Instant
Bank Transfer runs on Malswitch to move money in real time to any Malawian bank. It sits alongside the RBM's
**MITASS** interbank settlement system (2002) in the national payments stack. See **[[malawi--dpi-pay]]**.
