# Nigeria–Angola space cooperation agreement (2026)

A space-science and technology cooperation agreement signed **2026-06-13** at **ANGOTIC 2026** (CCTA Talatona Convention Center, Luanda, 11–13 June) between the Director General of Nigeria's **National Space Research and Development Agency (NASRDA)**, Matthew Olumide Adepoju, and the Director General of Angola's **National Space Programme Management Office ([[ggpen]])**, Zolana João.

## Terms

- Cooperation on **space research, capacity building** and the application of space-based technologies to national-development priorities.
- Welcomed by Angola's Minister **[[mario-oliveira]]**; witnessed by the **Portuguese Space Agency** (President Ricardo Conde), **SANSA** (CEO Humbulani Mudau) and the **African Space Agency** — situating the deal in a wider African/Lusophone space-diplomacy network.

## Reads

- `sovereignty`: an **Africa–Africa** space partnership (continent's two largest economies-by-ambition in space) rather than a North–South transfer — Angola using its GGPEN/ANGOSAT platform as a diplomatic asset. See the exporter reading on [[AGO]].
