# MobileMoney Fintech Ltd (MTN Ghana MoMo)

**MTN Group's Ghana mobile-money business, spun off into a standalone fintech** (2026-04) — turning MoMo into a
separate growth engine and complying with Ghana's **Payment Systems and Services Act 2019 (Act 987)** (which
requires a dedicated fintech entity). MTN's Ghana MoMo earned **$549m in 2025** — a core node of Ghana's deep
mobile-money market. See [[ghana--dpi-pay]], [[mtn-ghana]].
