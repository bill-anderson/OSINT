# Africell

US/UK-backed mobile operator active in the DR Congo, Sierra Leone, The Gambia and — since 2022 — **Angola**, where it broke the state-telco duopoly as the fourth operator and quickly took ~25% market share. Chairman/CEO: Ziad Dalloul; Africell Angola CEO: Christopher Lundh.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Luanda data centre (Oct 2021).** Ahead of its mobile launch, Africell opened a cloud-based enterprise data centre at its Kings Tower HQ — the cornerstone of its Angola network and open low-cost hosting for local developers; suppliers HP, Nokia, Dell, Oracle; partners incl. [[angola-cables]], MSTelcom, Angola Telecom. First phase of a 5G-enabled network. See [[angola--infra-store]]. Source: [[2021-10-20 Africell opens high-tech data center in Angola]].
- **Afrimoney mobile money (Apr 2023).** Africell launched **Afrimoney** in Angola — the mobile-money inclusion play in a ~50%-unbanked market, alongside the bank-centric [[emis-angola]]/Multicaixa rails. See [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2023-06-26 Mobile Money in Angola - How Afrimoney is paving the way]].
