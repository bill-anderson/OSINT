# Unitel

Angola's **largest mobile operator** and long-dominant incumbent — majority state-controlled since 2012. Runs enterprise data-centre capacity in Luanda (Unitel Luanda Sul, Unitel FILDA). Its market dominance is the backdrop to Africell's 2022 entry.

## Activity in this wiki

- The **"single state-owned player"** that dominated Angolan mobile before **[[africell]]**'s 2022 entry (Africell took ~25% share within eight months) — the competitive context for mobile-money rollout. Source: [[2023-06-26 Mobile Money in Angola - How Afrimoney is paving the way]].
- Part of Angola's data-centre estate (Unitel Luanda Sul, FILDA enterprise facilities) alongside [[angola-cables]]/AngoNAP, [[paratus]], [[raxio-group]] and MSTelcom. See [[angola--infra-store]].

*A 2025 Unitel–Ericsson network-modernisation deal appears in the source datasets but is not yet backed by a dated source page in the wiki; tracked for follow-up.*
