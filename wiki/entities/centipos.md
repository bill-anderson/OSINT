# CENTIPOS

Togo's national **GNSS correction network** — **25 reference stations** delivering **centimetre-precision**
positioning (corrections transmitted in real time over mobile). Underpins the national infrastructure-
mapping single-window, and also serves cadastre, precision agriculture and environmental mapping (launched
Apr 2026). See [[togo--dpi-govtech]], [[TGO]].
