# CIB-AES (AES Biometric Identity Card)

The **harmonised biometric national ID card of the Alliance of Sahel States (AES)** — the identity counterpart to the AES common biometric passport, rolled out first in Burkina Faso from late 2025.

## Profile

- In **Burkina Faso**: unveiled by **[[oni-burkina-faso]]** on 1 Dec 2025 (decree 6 Nov 2025), replacing the CNIB as the **5th generation** of national ID; deliverable from **age 5**, mandatory from **age 13**, cost **3,500 FCFA**, polycarbonate. Bears the national name/flag **and** the AES logo with a bilingual (FR/EN) "AES Identity Card" label — aligned to ICAO standards.
- Part of the **[[alliance-of-sahel-states]]** (Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger) integration agenda after their ECOWAS exit — "harmonised at AES level while keeping national sovereignty." A pointed sovereignty/geopolitical statement (AES-wide identity + travel documents replacing the ECOWAS frame). See [[burkina-faso--dpi-id]], [[gov.regional]].
