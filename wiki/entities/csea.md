# Centre for the Study of the Economies of Africa (CSEA)

Abuja-based economic-policy think tank. Publishes the **CSEA Digital Preparedness Index**, a **Data
Governance Index**, and country data-governance snapshots.

## Activity in this wiki

- **South Africa Data Governance Snapshot 2026 (2026-04).** Mapped SA’s data-governance landscape:
  POPIA (+17 Apr 2025 amendment), National AI Policy Framework, Digital Transformation of Government
  Roadmap, DPGA accession (Sep 2025), and structural constraints (rural connectivity, data cost,
  skills, power). Ranked SA 3rd of 43 on its Digital Preparedness Index. See [[ZAF]], [[gov.policy]].
  Source: [[2026-04-07 CSEA South Africa Data Governance Snapshot 2026]].
