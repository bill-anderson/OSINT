# Gamswitch (national payment switch, The Gambia)

The Gambia's **national payment switch**, operator of **BANTABA 2.0** — the country's first Inclusive
Instant Payment System, a Mojaloop-based national interoperability platform ([[dpi.pay]]). Under the
Gambia Interoperability Switch Program, Gamswitch runs the shared national backbone connecting banks,
mobile-money operators, MFIs, fintechs, PSPs and public institutions through a single interoperable
switch with real-time clearing and settlement — with **domestic ownership** an explicit design choice.
See [[GMB]], [[gambia--dpi-pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **BANTABA 2.0 launched (2025-12-15).** [[include.access]] The Gambia launched **BANTABA 2.0**
  ("common meeting place" in Mandinka), led by the **Central Bank of The Gambia** and the Ministry of
  Communications and Digital Economy, with the Institute for Inclusive Digital Africa (IIDiA) and the
  **[[mojaloop]]** Foundation. Built after a year of preparation (from October 2024) and a pilot; PS
  Hassan M. M. Jallow framed a low-cost interoperable national switch as "foundational DPI." Source:
  [[2025-12-15 Gambia launches BANTABA 2.0, first real-time interoperable payment system]].
