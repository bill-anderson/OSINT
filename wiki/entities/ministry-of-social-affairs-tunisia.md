# Ministry of Social Affairs (MoSA, Tunisia)

**The implementing ministry for Tunisia's flagship social-assistance programme,
[[amen-program-tunisia]] — and therefore the custodian of the AMEN social registry, the country's
largest operational beneficiary database.** Its digital significance in this wiki is registry-plus-
payments: targeting the poorest households and moving their transfers from cash to digital rails.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Implements the AMEN Program (launched 2019).** AMEN is a social assistance initiative launched
  in **2019** with support from the **[[world-bank]]** and implemented by MoSA, targeting the
  country's most vulnerable citizens with cash transfers, medical services and economic empowerment
  opportunities. See [[amen-program-tunisia]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-14 Tunisians Share How their Life Changed Through the AMEN Program]].
- **Registry and reach (as of December 2023).** The programme reached **over 333,000 poor
  households** — **more than 10% of the population** — with monthly cash transfers, and more than
  **620,000 low-income households** with free and subsidised healthcare. **54%** of cash-transfer
  beneficiaries are women-headed households. Almost **156,000 children under 5** receive allowances
  (about **30 dinars per month**), from **103,000 households** enrolled in the **AMEN social
  registry**. See [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-14 Tunisians Share How their Life Changed Through the AMEN Program]].
- **Payment digitalisation (as of December 2023).** **44.4%** of cash transfers digitalised, and
  **over 76%** of women beneficiaries receiving payments digitally. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-14 Tunisians Share How their Life Changed Through the AMEN Program]].

*Register caution: MoSA's registry work should not be conflated with the
[[subsidies-compensation-platform-tunisia]], which [[access-now]] alleges skipped data-protection
impact assessments. The two are adjacent but their relationship is **not established** in any source
held.*
