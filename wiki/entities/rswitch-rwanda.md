# RSwitch (Rwanda National Payment Switch)

Rwanda's national payment switch — the domestic instant-payment infrastructure connecting
banks and mobile-money wallets under the central bank's oversight. It **powers [[ekash]]**,
Rwanda's national instant/interoperable payment system (see [[rwanda-dpi-strategy]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **eKash launch — RSwitch as the engine (2025-12).** Rwanda's instant/interoperable payment
  system **[[ekash]]** was officially launched **5 December 2025**, powered by RSwitch and
  connecting **20+ financial institutions**; it builds on the earlier RIPPS bank-to-bank rail and
  the P2P (May 2022) and P2M (Feb 2025) layers. Provenance: the launch account is by an RSwitch
  insider, so adoption/volume figures are project-stated. See [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]],
  [[RWA]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-05 From Access to Empowerment Introducing Rwanda's Instant Payment System — eKash]].
- **First EAC cross-border interlink pilot (as of 2026-07-10).** RSwitch is being interlinked
  with Tanzania's **[[tips-tanzania]]** in a proof of concept — the first cross-border
  corridor under the **[[eac-cross-border-payment-masterplan]]** — advanced at a week-long
  technical meeting in Zanzibar. Initial scope is person-to-person transfers; no launch date
  announced. See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]], [[RWA]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-10 New Rwanda-Tanzania Payment Pilot could transform trade across East Africa]].
