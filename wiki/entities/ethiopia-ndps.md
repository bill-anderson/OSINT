# Ethiopia National Digital Payments Strategy (NDPS 2026–2030)

Ethiopia's second **National Digital Payments Strategy**, launched **9 December 2025** (succeeding the 2021 NDPS), charting a 2026–2030 roadmap for interoperability, trust and innovation in digital finance — led by the **[[national-bank-of-ethiopia|NBE]]**. Notable proposal: enabling **low-value outbound cross-border transfers** via cards, wallets and digital banking for the first time. Context at launch: Ethiopia processes **18.5tn+ birr** in annual digital transactions, with the **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** digital ID as an enabler. Complements the **[[ethswitch]]** instant-payment build-out and **[[digital-ethiopia-2030]]**.

- **Policy → execution (2026-07).** The Ministry of Finance, the **[[national-bank-of-ethiopia|NBE]]** and
  **[[ethswitch|EthSwitch]]** convened public institutions and infrastructure providers to operationalise a
  **unified implementation framework** for nationwide **interoperable government digital payments** via
  EthSwitch's **EthioPay** national ecosystem — marking the shift from strategy to delivery. Source:
  [[2026-07-10-ethiopia-interoperable-government-digital-payments-techafricanews]].
