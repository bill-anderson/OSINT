# Afriland First Bank

Major Cameroonian bank — partner in the first **[[visa|Visa]]** entry recorded in CMR holdings.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Visa partnership (Mar 2026).** Partnered **Visa** to expand card issuance and merchant acceptance in
  Cameroon — a **US card network** growing in a market otherwise dominated by domestic/regional rails
  (Mobile Money, Blue Money, GIMACPAY, PAPSS); the tension between foreign card rails and sovereign
  payment infrastructure is the point. *(PR-wire item; capture is not endorsement.)* Source:
