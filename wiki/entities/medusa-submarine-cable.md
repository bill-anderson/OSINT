# MEDUSA submarine cable

MEDUSA is a Mediterranean subsea cable network spanning more than 8,000 km and connecting close to
13 countries across Europe, North Africa and the Middle East. It is supported by the European
Union and led by the Spanish company AFR-IX, and is described as a Global Gateway flagship for
Mediterranean connectivity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Tunisia connection financed via the [[ebrd]] loan (2026-01).** [[tunisie-telecom]]'s
  up-to-€190m EBRD development-linked loan includes financing to connect Tunisia to MEDUSA,
  enhancing the country's international connectivity and positioning it as a Mediterranean digital
  hub. Tags [[infra.connect]]. Source:
