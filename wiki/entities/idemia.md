# IDEMIA

French biometric-identity and secure-credentials multinational — a major global vendor of national ID and biometric systems, and a recurring foreign supplier in African ID programmes.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DRC $1.2B contract cancelled (2024).** IDEMIA (with local intermediary **Afritech**) held the DRC's ~$1.2bn national biometric-ID contract until it was **terminated on 12 August 2024** — after a Bloomberg / Lighthouse Reports investigation alleged financial mismanagement and cost irregularities (overbilling). The DRC subsequently pivoted to Singapore's Trident (DRCPass) and Ghana's Margins Group (cards). See [[drc--dpi-id]].
