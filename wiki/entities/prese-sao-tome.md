# PReSE (Projeto de Reforma do Sistema Eleitoral)

EU-funded electoral-system reform project in São Tomé and Príncipe (**€500k**, implemented by Portugal's
**Camões, I.P.**; platform by the **University of Aveiro**). Built **Africa's first Lusophone automatic
and permanent voter registration** — enrolling voters via **interoperability between the civil register
(SIGA/BI) and the electoral roll (CEN/GTE)** — responding to EU Election Observation recommendations,
applied to the July/Sep 2026 elections, saving ~**€2m/yr**. Also financed the free "BI para Todos"
national-ID field campaign (Starlink-powered off-grid emission). See [[sao-tome--dpi-id]], [[STP]].
