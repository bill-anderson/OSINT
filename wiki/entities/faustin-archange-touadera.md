# Faustin-Archange Touadéra

**President of the Central African Republic**, personally associated with the country's
digital-transformation and biometric-identity agenda.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital transformation as economic strategy (2025).** Frames digital (fibre rollout,
  rural 4G, startup support incl. a University of Bangui startup training centre, service
  digitalisation to cut corruption) as a lever to revitalise the economy and position CAR in
  the regional digital economy. Source:
  [[2025-05-22 RCA - Touadera mise sur la transformation numerique pour revitaliser l'economie]].
- **Biometric enrolment figurehead.** First person biometrically enrolled in the 2020
  electoral register (**[[genkey]]** system) and launched the 2024–25 register revision in
  Bangui. Patron of the MEPCI **DUNIA** platform launch (Feb 2026). Also personally backed
  the **[[al-madina]]** decentralised CNI centre at Bossangoa (2023). See [[caf--dpi-id]],
  [[caf--dpi-govtech]].
