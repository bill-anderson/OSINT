# CAIDP (Access to Public-Interest Information Commission)

Côte d'Ivoire's **access-to-information authority** — the institution behind the country's transparency /
right-to-information regime, and the `data.open` anchor the hub previously lacked (it rested only on the
National Data Governance Strategy). Its **ACCESS** initiative marks "a turning point for transparency and
access to information." Source: [[2025-07-25-access-caidp-transparence-acces-information-fratmat]].
