# Publish What You Fund

The **global campaign for aid and development transparency** — an NGO that advocates for,
and independently assesses, the open publication of aid data. It publishes the recurring
**Aid Transparency Index** (next edition **November 2026**) ranking major donors on the
quality and openness of their reporting, much of it against the
**[[international-aid-transparency-initiative]]** (IATI) standard.

## What it is / what it does

- Standing methodological authority on open aid data; consulted as a reference on IATI data
  quality and donor transparency.
- In a 2026 first-hand experiment (source below), PWYF tested an **LLM agent** against raw
  IATI data, finding it strong on **retrieval** (totals within **0.3%** of established IATI
  tools) but unreliable on **interpretation** — hallucinated explanations, missed
  double-counting, missed USAID transaction-level sector coding and UK/FCDO 2025 aid cuts.
