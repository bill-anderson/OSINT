# NINAuth (NIN Authentication app)

A secure verification/authentication suite for Nigeria's National Identification Number,
launched by **[[nimc]]** with a web, API and mobile stack. Presented as a **consent-based**
layer designed to enhance data security and privacy and simplify access to services.

- **2025-05-06** — NIMC unveiled NINAuth (approved by President **[[bola-tinubu]]**), available on
  Google Play and the App Store, described as the official service for NIN verification and
  authentication across immigration/passport processing, driver's licences, SIM registration,
  tax filing, financial transactions and government social-protection programmes.

Sits alongside the **[[ngdx]]** data-exchange backbone in Nigeria's identity-assurance layer.
See [[dpi.id]], [[nigeria--dpi-id]].
