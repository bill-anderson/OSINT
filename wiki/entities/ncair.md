# National Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (NCAIR)

Nigerian government AI/robotics research centre under [[nitda]]. A co-developer (with [[nitda]] and
[[awarri-technologies]]) of the sovereign local-language LLM ****N-ATLAS**** (Sep 2025). Part of the
institutional layer under the National AI Strategy and National AI Trust.

See [[nigeria--tech-ai]], [[NGA]], [[tech.ai]].
