# PATNUC (Project for the Acceleration of the Digital Transformation of Cameroon)

A [[world-bank]]-funded programme (Projet d'Accélération de la Transformation
Numérique du Cameroun) run through Cameroon's **Ministry of Posts &
Telecommunications** to accelerate the country's digital transformation,
including its cybersecurity and DPI infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Funded ANTIC cybersecurity procurement (2026-01).** PATNUC financed
  [[antic-cameroon]]'s **~XAF735m (US$1.3m)** intrusion detection/prevention
  system to strengthen the national CIRT, commissioned in Yaoundé on 20 January
  2026. The underlying cybersecurity tender was announced in 2024. See [[CMR]],
  [[infra.cybersec]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[world-bank]]. Sources:
  [[2026-01-22 Cameroon boosts cyber defense systems]],
  [[2026-01-27 Cameroon deploys cybersecurity system to protect DPI, boost digital trust]].
