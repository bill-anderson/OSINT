# Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (Zambia)

Zambia's **data-protection regulator** under the Data Protection Act (2021) — now operational (activation
reported early 2025), even as Cabinet has signalled intent to repeal/replace the 2021 Act for AI analytics
(see [[ZMB]] hub). Part of the multi-sectoral digital-ID delegation.

- **ID4Africa Abidjan (May 2026).** Joined SMART Zambia, Home Affairs, the Bank of Zambia and Agriculture in
  Zambia's ID4Africa delegation, on digital-ID data-protection frameworks. See [[zambia--dpi-id]],
  [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2026-05-18-zambia-id4africa-multi-sectoral-engagement]].
