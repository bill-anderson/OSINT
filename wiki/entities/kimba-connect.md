# Kimba Connect

Presented at launch as **Gabon's first national open-innovation programme**
(*premier programme national d'open innovation*), run under the
**[[ministere-economie-numerique-gabon]]**. Its design is corporate-demand-led: connect established
companies to local start-ups so that concrete business problems become the specification for digital
solutions, rather than start-ups building speculatively.

## Scope and terms (as of 2026-07-17)

- **Target ecosystem: an estimated 150–200 start-ups** (organisers' figure, 2026-07).
- **Support offered:** financial accompaniment, technology support, **labelling** (*labellisation*) and
  facilitated market access.
- **Timetable:** applications open **until 2026-07-24**; final **2026-08-04**.

The **labelling** component is the notable one — a state-conferred quality mark is a governance
instrument as much as a support measure, since it makes the ministry the arbiter of which firms count
as credible counterparties.

## Attached agreements

Two accords were signed on **2026-07-14**, on the margins of the launch, to supply the programme's two
declared missing inputs:

- **Finance** — **[[gabon-sgg-startup-guarantee-accord]]** with the
  **[[societe-gabonaise-de-garantie]]**.
- **Skills** — **[[gabon-huawei-kimba-connect-accord]]** with **[[huawei]]** Technologies Gabon.
