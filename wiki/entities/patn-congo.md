# PATN — Digital Transformation Acceleration Project

The Republic of Congo's flagship **World Bank-financed digital-transformation programme** (Projet d'Accélération de la Transformation Numérique) — the WB names it the **Congo Digital Acceleration Project (P175592)**, ~**$100m** (FY2022), launched January 2023.

## Profile

- **Delivery:** an FY2024 budget of **CFA38.7bn ($63.9m)** funded **57 projects** — high-speed internet to public administrations, universities, schools and rural areas — aligned to the 2022-2026 National Development Plan and "Congo Digital 2025." Per ARPCE, Congo had 3.4m internet subscribers (59.7%) in 2023. Source: [[2024-05-28 Congo allocates $63.9m for 2024 digital transformation (PATN)]].
- **Digital identity strand.** PATN frames the country's secure-digital-identity push (Brazzaville workshop, Jul 2025; Digital Wallet; SIFEC civil-status digitisation). See [[congo--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2025-07-17 Congo - national workshop launches secure digital-identity system push]].

*(Distinct from Cameroon's PATNUC and DRC's Digital Acceleration Project.)*
