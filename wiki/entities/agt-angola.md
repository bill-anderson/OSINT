# AGT (Administração Geral Tributária / General Tax Administration)

Angola's **tax authority**, driving the digitalisation of tax administration — the online tax portal and the mandatory electronic-invoicing regime.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory e-invoicing (phasing in 2025–2026).** Under **Presidential Decree 71/25** (technical standards in Executive Decree 2683/25), AGT postponed the mandate to 1 Oct 2025 with a grace period to 31 Dec 2025; from **1 Jan 2026** mandatory for large taxpayers, government suppliers and transactions >AOA 25m, extending to all VAT-registered taxpayers by Sept 2026. Requires AGT-certified software, real-time transmission and SAF-T format. See [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2025-10-02 Angola - Mandatory e-invoicing implementation postponed]].
- Also cleared equipment for Africell's Luanda data centre (2021) — a supporting-role mention.
