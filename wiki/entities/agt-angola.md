# AGT (Administração Geral Tributária / General Tax Administration)

Angola's **tax authority**, driving the digitalisation of tax administration — the online tax portal, mandatory electronic-invoicing, and (as vanguard of the "paperless state") the e-signature and time-stamp trust layer. President of the board **José Leiria**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory e-invoicing — now live (1 Jan 2026).** Under the Regime Jurídico de Facturas (**Presidential Decree 71/25**, March 2025; technical standards in Executive Decree 2683/25), the September–December 2025 transition was **not extended**: from **1 Jan 2026** e-invoicing is mandatory for large taxpayers and state suppliers (Leiria called it a "paradigm shift"), extending to all VAT-registered taxpayers within 12 months. As of Dec 2025, **500+ software packages registered, 21 certified**. See [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Sources: [[2025-10-02 Angola - Mandatory e-invoicing implementation postponed]], [[2025-12-09-agt-facturacao-electronica-a-partir-de-janeiro-2026]].
- **Paperless trust layer (2026-05-23).** At the 7th IMA Forum, Leiria set out **electronic signatures + time-stamping** as the trust pillars of a fully digital state — validating public acts and contracts without physical presence — with **all e-invoices auto-reported to AGT, which generates a verification code** for authenticity checks and secure payment. IMA (Meick Afonso) provides the certification infrastructure; capacity-building is the main constraint. See [[angola--dpi-govtech]], [[gov.standards]]. Source: [[2026-05-23-agt-eliminacao-gradual-documentos-em-papel]].
- **FATCA cross-border data flow to the US IRS (2026-06-08).** President Lourenço authorised **€552,000** (36-month licence, simplified/sole-source procurement) for tax-evasion software under the **US–Angola FATCA** intergovernmental agreement: Angolan financial institutions identify US-person accounts and report them to AGT, which **transmits the data to the US Internal Revenue Service** — a standing cross-border financial-data flow to a foreign authority. See [[angola--dpi-govtech]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[geopol.usa]], [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2026-06-08-agt-licenca-software-fatca-acordo-com-eua]].
- Also cleared equipment for Africell's Luanda data centre (2021) — a supporting-role mention.
