# Morocco National Population Registry (RNP) & Unified Social Registry (RSU)

Morocco's social-protection identity backbone — the **National Population Registry (RNP)**, the **Unified
Social Registry (RSU)**, and the **National Register Agency**, all established by **Law 72-18** ([[dpi.id]],
[[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]). Born of King Mohammed VI's 2018 call for harmonized social
protection, the RSU is the single entry point for social programmes; ~half the population is enrolled,
and it underpins **AMO-TADAMON** (replacing RAMED) and the December-2023 **Direct Social Benefit** program
(>half the population, ~1.6% of GDP). Built on MOSIP ([[mosip]]; ~22m of 39m registered). See [[MAR]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **World Bank $70m second project (2024-12).** [[finance.new]] The WB approved the Second Identity and
  Targeting for Social Protection Project — reinforcing the RNP, operationalizing the National Register
  Agency, and expanding the RSU to rural/remote areas (digital/remote applications; 8 programmes by 2028).
  Source: [[2024-12-04 World Bank approves $70M for Morocco's social protection delivery (RNP and Unified Social Registry)]].
