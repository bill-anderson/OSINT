# NINETEC (Nigérienne du Net et des Technologies)

A **"100% Nigerien" approved Starlink distributor** (DG **Ousseini Garba Bechir**), partnering with SpaceX to
distribute Starlink kits under its **NINE-Starlink** campaign (launched 30 Apr 2025) — pitched as local
ownership pairing with a global satellite leader to connect health centres and rural communes. Kit sales are
governed by **décret N°2020-331 (8 May 2020)** KYC rules: each kit registered/activated in the final client's
name, personal/professional use only (no sharing/resale). See **[[niger--infra-connect]]**, [[starlink]].
