# MTN Côte d'Ivoire

Local opco of [[mtn-group]] — a leading CIV mobile operator (source of the current Digital minister
Djibril Ouattara, its ex-CEO). At **20 years** (2025): **16m subscribers, 4.9m MoMo users, 93% coverage**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Network investment & satellite.** Pledged **~200bn CFA by 2028**; partnered **Eutelsat (KONNECT)** for
  a hybrid GEO+LEO satellite-broadband play framed defensively against **Starlink**; and pushed **MoMo
  Soutra** (mobile-money overdraft). Sources: [[2025-09-02-mtn-cote-divoire-20-years-itweb]],
