# Africa Policy Research Institute (APRI)

**Policy research institute** publishing comparative analysis on African governance and
technology policy. Held here as the publisher of a cross-country e-government study the wiki
cites but does not absorb.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Comparative e-government study (2024, year precision).** APRI published *What African
  Countries Can Learn from Each Other in E-Government Implementation* (DOI 10.59184/pb024.05),
  a pan-African ([[XAF]]) comparative treatment of e-government implementation. Held as a
  **reference study — cited, not absorbed**: its cross-country figures are not promoted onto
  concept or place pages as current state, since fresher country-level data reaches the wiki
  via clippings. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 What African Countries Can Learn in E-Government (APRI)]].
