# PI-SPI (Plateforme Interopérable du Système de Paiement Instantané, UEMOA)

The **[[bceao|BCEAO]]**'s regional **instant-payment rail** for the eight-state West African Economic and
Monetary Union — the UEMOA analogue of a national IPS, launched **30 September 2025**. PI-SPI lets users
send and receive money **instantly, 24/7, across any bank, e-money issuer, microfinance institution or
payment institution and across borders** (a Senegalese merchant can be paid instantly by a Beninese
customer), **free** for domestic transfers, built with regional expertise. See [[dpi.pay]],
[[gov.regional]].

## What we know

- **Launch + interconnection (2025–26).** Launched simultaneously across the union (BCEAO principal
  agencies); by **May 2026** banks and mobile money were **100% interconnected** on the platform. Sources:
  [[2025-10-07-lancement-pi-spi-uemoa]], [[2026-05-29-pi-spi-banques-mobile-money-interconnectes]].
- **Connection deadlines extended (2026-06).** BCEAO pushed integration deadlines to **30 Sep 2026**
  (banks / e-money / payment institutions) and **30 Jun 2027** (microfinance) for a "progressive and
  robust" onboarding; **80 participants connected, 74 in live testing**. Source:
  [[2026-06-29-pi-spi-nouveaux-delais-connexion]].
- **Context.** Sits atop a fast-growing UEMOA digital-money base — **248.7m e-money accounts** (2024,
  BCEAO), 76.8m active — and is the payments pillar the [[2025-11-19-declaration-cotonou-transformation-numerique|Cotonou
  Declaration]] names as interoperable DPI. In **[[BEN]] Benin**, mobile-money penetration reached
  **88.09%** (11.65m active accounts, end-2025; MTN ~51% of accounts).

- **Rollout in [[BFA]] Burkina Faso (2025–26).** A live-test phase opened **Aug 2025** ahead of the
  **30 Sep 2025** union launch; **nine banks/institutions** were authorised in Burkina by Feb 2026; per the
  Jul-2026 deadline extension **80 institutions** were connected union-wide (deadlines to 30 Sep 2026 /
  30 Jun 2027). Sources: [[2025-08-03-pi-spi-uemoa-phase-de-test-lancee]],
  [[2026-03-08-pi-spi-neuf-banques-autorisees-burkina]],
  [[2026-07-07-bceao-prolonge-delais-pi-spi]].
