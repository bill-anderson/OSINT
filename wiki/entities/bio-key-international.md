# BIO-key International

US biometric identity and access management (IAM) vendor whose products —
the PortalGuard IAM platform, PIN:You tokenless authentication and Single
Sign-On — target the banking and financial sector. It expands in Africa
through regional integrators rather than direct presence.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometric IAM into Mozambique's national payments network (2026-02-24).**
  With regional integrator [[runlevel]], BIO-key signed a multi-year strategic
  agreement to deploy PortalGuard, PIN:You and SSO into the environment of
  [[simo-mozambique]] (SIMO), operator of the SIMOrede national electronic
  payments network. The deployment is pitched to strengthen governance,
  cybersecurity resilience and secure access for banks participating in
  SIMOrede's interoperability, clearing and settlement. It builds on a prior
  BIO-key/RunLevel deployment for a major Mozambican national bank in May 2025,
  and marks BIO-key's 11th banking/financial-sector customer globally
  ([[MOZ]], [[dpi.id]], [[infra.cybersec]]). Source: [[2026-02-24 BIO-key and RunLevel Deploy Biometric IAM Across Mozambique's National Payments Network]].
