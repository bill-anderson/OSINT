# Malawi Electoral Commission (MEC)

Malawi's election-management body, which maintains the **electronic voter register**. In 2025 the MEC
**rejected** an observer coalition's (Chisankho Watch) request for machine-readable access to the voter roll —
a live **transparency-versus-privacy** tension over how much of a state-held identity dataset independent
monitors may inspect, and on what terms.
