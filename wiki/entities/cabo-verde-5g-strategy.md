# Cabo Verde National 5G Strategy

Cabo Verde's **National 5G Implementation Strategy** — World-Bank-funded (DGTED), **launched Sept 2025** and
**formally approved by Resolution 33/2026 (20 Feb 2026)** — targeting **90% internet penetration by 2026**
and part of a "digital nation by 2030" vision, bundled with a **universal digital ID and a data strategy**.
