# KS-Wallet (BGB Solutions)

An **independent Guinean e-wallet** from **BGB Solutions S.A** (2026-04) — deposit/withdraw via the **KS-PAY**
agent network, **QR-code payments**, **fee-free P2P transfers**, instant remittance to **200+ countries** (to
bank accounts, wallets or cash), plus **EDG utility / prepaid-meter / TV / airtime** bills and a savings vault.
A non-bank, non-telco entrant in Guinea's fast-widening e-money field. See [[guinea--dpi-pay]].
