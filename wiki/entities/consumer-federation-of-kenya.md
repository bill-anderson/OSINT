# Consumer Federation of Kenya (COFEK)

A Kenyan consumer-rights advocacy organisation. Appears in this wiki as the **litigant** who took
the Kenya–US health cooperation deal to court to contest cross-border transfer of Kenyan health
data.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Petition against the Kenya–US $2.5bn health deal (Dec 2025).** Per [[zuria-nagadya]]
  ([[2026-06-08 Kenyan Citizens Took A $2.5 Billion Deal With America To Court To Fight For Their Health Data]]),
  COFEK filed a petition roughly eight days after the deal was signed (2025-12-04), arguing it
  violated Kenya's **Data Protection Act** and **Digital Health Act** — that once medical and
  epidemiological data is transferred abroad the harm is permanent and beyond Kenyan regulators'
  reach. The **High Court suspended the data-transfer component**; the **Court of Appeal later
  reversed** the suspension while the case continues. Cited as data sovereignty exercised through
  domestic law. See [[us-kenya-health-framework]], [[kenya--gov-protect]].
