# Africa CEO Forum

A pan-African business and investment platform (annual summit) that convenes the continent's
corporate and political leadership. Appears in this wiki as the co-author of a 2026 analysis of
satellite-internet economics and the African digital divide.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Satellite revenue-leakage report (2026-05).** With **Askya Investment Partners**, and based on
  interviews with 30+ telecom executives and regulators, warned that offshore LEO operators (led by
  [[starlink]]) capture Africa's highest-value customers under far lighter regulatory and tax
  burdens than licensed telcos — so the continent "risks losing billions." Recommends a hybrid model
  (satellites as wholesale / underserved-area providers). See [[infra.connect]], [[data.satellite]],
  [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-26 Africa risks losing billions to satellite internet operators, report says]].
- **The divide is affordability, not coverage.** The same report is cited in Semafor's SpaceX
  analysis for reframing Africa's connectivity gap as an affordability problem rather than a
  coverage one. Source: [[2026-05-22 View How SpaceX is making a quiet bet on Africa]].
