# ADAPT (Africa Digital Access and Public Infrastructure for Trade)

An **AfCFTA-led** initiative (launched Nov 2025) to build **shared, interoperable digital rails** — identity,
payments and trusted data exchange — underpinning the continental single market (rather than individual
government systems). African-led with the [[tony-blair-institute]], [[iota-foundation]] and WEF, on the
**TWIN** infrastructure; explores stablecoins.

- **First pilot trio (May 2026).** **Kenya, Morocco and Nigeria** selected via a two-stage evaluation; work
  focuses on connecting digital-ID systems, integrating payment rails, continental interoperability standards
  and live cross-border data exchange. SG **[[wamkele-mene]]** ties it to +80% intra-African exports / $450bn
  by 2035. See [[XAF]]. Source: [[2026-05-26-nigeria-kenya-morocco-afcfta-adapt]].
