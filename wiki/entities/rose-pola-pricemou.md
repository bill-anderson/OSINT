# Rose Pola Pricemou

Guinea's **Minister of Posts, Telecommunications and the Digital Economy** ([[ministry-digital-economy-guinea]]) — the public face of the country's digital-transformation and digital-sovereignty agenda under the Doumbouya transition and [[simandou-2040]]. She presented Guinea's 2025 sector review and championed local data hosting, the .gn ccTLD, e-government and the Transform Africa Summit 2025 host role. See [[GIN]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **2025 sector review + 2026 targets (2026-01).** Announced the backbone quadrupling to 200 Gbps, first Tier III data centre, 39 public-service apps, TELEMO/FUGAS, and 2026 priorities (white zones, cybersecurity, local hosting). Source: [[2026-01-14 Guinea Reviews 2025 Digital Sector Gains, Targets Faster Rollout in 2026]].
- **TAS 2025 host messaging (2025-11).** Framed Guinea's digital sovereignty around local data hosting and .GN, and flagged the foundation of a national AI strategy (UNDP-supported Oct-2025 workshop). Sources: [[2025-11-13 Transform Africa Summit 2025 opens in Conakry, epicentre of digital cooperation in Africa]], [[2025-11-19 Transform Africa Summit 2025 (Conakry) - Simandou 2040 Pillar 3, TELEMO, SANIA-SADX and the closing wrap]].
