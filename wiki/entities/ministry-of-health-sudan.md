# Ministry of Health (Sudan)

Sudan's health ministry, partner in the health-sector digital-transformation
strategy and the **[[afia-platform-sudan|Afia]]** health platform.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Health-digital strategy with WHO (2026-05).** Joined the
  **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** and the
  **[[world-health-organization]]** to develop a comprehensive health-sector
  digital-transformation strategy, referencing the launch of the **Afia**
  platform (health MIS/e-health). See [[dpi.mis]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-20 Comms Ministry and WHO discuss health-sector digital transformation; Afia platform (SUNA)]].
