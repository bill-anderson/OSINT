# Project Pangolin (Zimbabwe National AI & Data Platform)

Zimbabwe's proposed centralised National AI and Data Platform under the [[zimbabwe-national-ai-strategy]],
consolidating fragmented government databases for AI development. Critics ([[misa]]) warn centralisation
without safeguards enables mission creep and surveillance (Zimbabwe Constitution s.57 privacy). See
[[infra.store]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
[[2026-06-23 Implications of Zimbabwe’s National AI Strategy on Freedom of Expression Privacy and Access to Information]].
