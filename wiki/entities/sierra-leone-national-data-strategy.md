# Sierra Leone National Data Strategy

Sierra Leone's national framework for how data is collected, managed and used across
sectors — defining data-governance frameworks, interoperability standards and policies.

## History

- **First National Data Strategy** developed off DIAL's 2022 work with the government on the
  country's digital transformation strategy — Sierra Leone's first ever.
- **2025 update/refinement.** [[dial]] supported a focused **5-month 2025 engagement** to
  update and refine the strategy, prioritising alignment with the **AU Data Policy
  Framework**, via stakeholder engagement, benchmarking and key-informant interviews.
  Source: [[2025-01-01 Strengthening Sierra Leones national data strategy (DIAL)]].

See [[SLE]], [[gov.policy]], [[gov.standards]]. (Distinct from the 2024 Sierra Leone
National Innovation and Digital Strategy, a separate MoCTI instrument.)
