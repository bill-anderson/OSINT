# Equity Group Holdings

Kenya-headquartered pan-African financial-services group; positioning itself as the
private-sector implementation partner for continental DPI.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DPI partnership (2026-07-07).** Signed an MoU with **[[africanenda]]** and the
  **[[gates-foundation]]** to build and scale interoperable DPI (payments, ID, data
  exchange) across **20–30 African markets** — deal:
  [[equity-africanenda-gates-dpi-partnership]].
- **James Mwangi named Continental DPI Champion.** Group MD/CEO **[[james-mwangi]]**
  appointed inaugural continental DPI Champion. His framing: "we are not asking
  governments to surrender ownership of public infrastructure" — the private sector as
  accelerant, not owner. See [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]], [[KEN]].
