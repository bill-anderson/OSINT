# Peach Payments

Pan-African online payments provider (payment gateway and orchestration) operating directly
in 11 African markets and reaching 15 via partnerships. Its Mauritius entity has become a
flagship case for the island's emergence as a regional payments hub.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mauritius growth surge (2024→2025).** Peach's Mauritius operation reported transaction
  volume up more than 300% year-on-year and merchant acquisition up 42%. The company credits
  Mauritius' exit from the FATF grey list for lifting counterparty confidence, and is
  integrating with commerce platforms including Oracle ([[oracle]]), Shopify and WooCommerce.
  Group COO Sandeep Chagger. Tags [[dpi.pay]], [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-18 Mauritius emerges as an African payments hub]].
