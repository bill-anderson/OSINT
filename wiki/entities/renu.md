# Research and Education Network for Uganda (RENU)

Uganda's **national research and education network** — the connectivity/compute backbone for universities.

- **National AI Research Cloud (Mar 2026).** Co-developed the **National AI Research Cloud** hosted at
  **[[makerere-university|Makerere]]** (CEDAT + CoCIS) — local compute + storage for training AI models,
  keeping data **in-country** (cheaper, and a data-sovereignty answer to Amazon/Google). See
  [[uganda--tech-ai]]. Source: [[2026-03-16-musenero-launches-national-ai-research-cloud]].
