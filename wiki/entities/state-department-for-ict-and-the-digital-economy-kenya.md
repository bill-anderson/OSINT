# State Department for ICT and the Digital Economy (Kenya)

The ICT arm of Kenya's Ministry of Information, Communications and the Digital Economy — the state
department carrying the **Digital Transformation Agenda** and the
**[[kenya-national-digital-masterplan]]** (2022–2032). Headed by Principal Secretary
**[[john-tanui]]**; **[[ict-authority-kenya]]** sits under the same portfolio as the delivery agency.

The department is the standing counterparty for Kenya's bilateral digital-cooperation engagements —
the recurring node through which AI, e-commerce and infrastructure partnerships are negotiated and
staffed.

## Activity in this wiki

- **18 officers to Shandong, China (2026-07-17).** Flagged off an **18-officer delegation** to the
  **2026 Silk Road E-Commerce: Connecting the World Industry Matching Activity** in Shandong Province.
  Tanui framed it as building on the **[[kenya-china-ecommerce-mou]]** and as capacity-building in
  service of the Digital Master Plan — *"the programme underscores the critical role of people in
  driving digital transformation."* **No cost, funding source or deliverable was published**; the
  stated return is "practical knowledge" and "strategic partnerships." Source:
  [[2026-07-17-kenya-ict-officers-china-digital-trade]].
