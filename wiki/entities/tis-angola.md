# TIS Tech Angola

The private contractor awarded Angola's **Projecto de Infra-estruturas Digitais Públicas** — the concrete build-out of the country's core DPI, contracted by the **[[ima-angola|IMA]]**. Executive director **Filipe Monteiro**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **$150m "Digitais Públicas" contract (Despacho Presidencial 38/26, signed 2026-06-17).** IMA (director-general Meick Afonso) and TIS Tech Angola formalised the contract for the **US$150m** Public Digital Infrastructure project — a **12-month execution** inscribed in the 2026 Public Investment Programme (PIP). It builds the **technological backbone for cross-ministry interoperability**: a **critical-computing component in a secure zone** hosting the **interoperability platform** and **PKI-based electronic signatures**, plus **digital identity** and a more structured approach to **cybersecurity** and continuity of digital public services. This is the physical execution of the "Infra-estrutura Nacional de Dados"/"Identidade Digital Única" pillar that the World Bank **PADA** ([[ima-angola|IMA]]-implemented) has underwritten. See [[angola--dpi-govtech]], [[ima-angola]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2026-06-17-ima-tis-contrato-projecto-infra-estruturas-digitais-publicas]].
