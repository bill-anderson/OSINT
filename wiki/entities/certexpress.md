# CertExpress

**Seychelles' online civil-registration certificate service** — a digital platform through which
Seychellois citizens request **birth, marriage and death certificates** without an in-person
visit. Launched by the [[department-of-immigration-and-civil-status-seychelles]] with
[[dict-seychelles]], announced alongside [[seyid]] on **2024-09-18**. It is the citizen-facing
delivery layer over the civil register constituted under the [[seychelles-civil-status-act]].
See [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[SYC]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (2024-09-18).** Introduced at a press conference at the Ministry of Internal Affairs
  by the ICS team with DICT, as one of two new digital solutions for public services (the other
  being the SeyID virtual ID card for permit holders). Source:
  [[2024-09-18 Launch of New Digital Solutions for Public Services]].
- **Service terms (as announced 2024-09-18).** Certificates are requested online and delivered
  **within 2 working days** through the platform; a requested own birth or marriage certificate
  is saved to the user's **E-Doc wallet in the [[seyid]] app**, giving ongoing access without
  reapplication. The **price of certificates was unchanged at SCR 50**. *(A price is a
  time-varying figure — this is the SCR 50 stated at launch in September 2024, not a
  verified-current fee.)* Source:
  [[2024-09-18 Launch of New Digital Solutions for Public Services]].
- **Diaspora reach.** Secondary reporting frames CertExpress as cutting wait times for both
  local residents and the **Seychellois diaspora**, with certificates delivered by email and
  retained in the digital wallet. See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2024-09-18 Seychelles Makes Digital ID Mandatory for Work Permit Holders]].
