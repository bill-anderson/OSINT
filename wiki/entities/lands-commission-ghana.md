# Lands Commission (Ghana)

Ghana's statutory land-administration body, responsible for geographic information, land-tenure guarantee, property valuation, surveying and mapping. It is pursuing a **digitalization of land administration** programme toward a "fit-for-purpose" cadastre — the institutional owner of the land-records digitization named as a government priority in the US Country Commercial Guide ([[dpi.registry]], [[digital.localgov]], [[data.satellite]]). Deputy Executive Secretary (as presented): Ben Arthur. See [[GHA]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Land-administration digitalization programme (~2023).** [[dpi.registry]] A Lands Commission presentation set out the digitization of land records and a fit-for-purpose cadastral system as the route to reliable, efficient land-services delivery, referencing a project expected to commence in 2023. Date is a proxy. Source: [[2023-01-01 Digitalization of Land Administration in Ghana (Lands Commission)]].
