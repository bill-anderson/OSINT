# Digital Eswatini Project (P508948)

**The [[world-bank]] project intended to finance Eswatini's digital-government build.**
**US$65.16m** proposed; implementing agency [[ministry-of-ict-eswatini]]. Project ID
**P508948** (PID **PIDDC01229**), Concept Stage Project Information Document published
**2025-06-28**. The PID gives an *Estimated* Appraisal Date of **2025-08-01** and an
*Estimated* Approval Date of **2025-09-01** — these are **planned dates in a concept-stage
document, not a record of approval**, and nothing held confirms either occurred. See [[SWZ]],
[[finance.new]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **The project record behind the "Eswatini Digital Transformation Project."** The
  wiki's [[eswatini-digital-transformation-project]] page rests on a thin 2026-05
  secondary report of Eswatini "moving closer to securing funding" through World Bank
  negotiations, with **no funding figure**. P508948 is the World Bank record that report
  was flagged to be verified against: same country, same [[world-bank]] financier, same
  implementing ministry, same [[government-in-your-hand]] delivery platform. Note the
  chronology, which the secondary account does not fit cleanly — the PID puts **approval
  at 2025-09-01**, some eight months *before* the 2026-05 report described negotiations
  as ongoing. The wiki holds no source reconciling the two, and no source stating they
  are the same project. Treat the identification as **probable, not established**.
  Sources: [[2025-06-28 PID Concept Stage - Digital Eswatini (P508948)]],
  [[2026-05-18 Eswatini promotes digital transformation project through World Bank engagements]].
- **Scope via the GIYH roadmap ToR (2025, year precision).** The Digital Government
  Implementation Roadmap procured under Digital Eswatini covers **digital ID, data
  exchange, social registries, PKI, payments and ~200 services** — see
  [[government-in-your-hand]]. This is the fullest statement of project scope the wiki
  holds. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 ToR - Government in Your Hand Digital Government Implementation Roadmap (Digital Eswatini)]].
- **What is not held.** No appraisal-stage PAD, no financing breakdown (IDA/IBRD split),
  no results framework, no effectiveness or disbursement status. The dates above are
  the PID's **planned** appraisal and approval dates; the wiki holds nothing confirming
  either occurred.
