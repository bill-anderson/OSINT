# Cashi (Chad)

A fintech deploying an **interoperable digital-payments platform for Central Africa, Chad-first**, in
partnership with the **IFC** (Apr 2026) — accessible by mobile, POS and SMS — to reduce cash dependence
and widen access to formal finance for small businesses and the poorly-connected. Named in the
government's state-payments convention-cadre. See [[chad--dpi-pay]], [[TCD]].
