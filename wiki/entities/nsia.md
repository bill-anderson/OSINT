# Nigeria Sovereign Investment Authority (NSIA)

Nigeria's ~$1.6bn sovereign wealth fund, increasingly deploying state capital into
domestic digital infrastructure rather than purely financial assets.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Backing Kasi Cloud's $250m Lagos hyperscale campus (2026-02-26).** NSIA is a
  backer of [[kasi-cloud]]'s $250 million hyperscale data-centre campus in Lekki,
  Lagos — an instance of sovereign capital directed into domestic [[infra.store]]
  ([[finance.new]]) and a state bet on onshore compute in [[NGA]].
  Source: [[2026-02-26 Lagos Is Getting a $250 Million Hyperscale Data Center. Here's Why Nigeria Still Can't Power the Cloud]].
