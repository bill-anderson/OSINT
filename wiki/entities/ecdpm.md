# ECDPM (European Centre for Development Policy Management)

An independent policy think tank (Maastricht/Brussels) working on Europe–Africa relations
and international cooperation. Its **digital economy & governance team** is a recurring
source of named analyst commentary on AU–EU digital and AI-governance relations relevant to
the African data landscape.

## Activity in this wiki

- **AU–EU AI-governance analysis (2026-01-21).** ECDPM analysts **Melody Musoni** and
  **Verengai Mabika** argued that the AU and EU should co-create a mutually beneficial,
  human-centric AI-governance framework — blending the EU AI Act with the
  [[au-continental-ai-strategy]] and the [[au-data-policy-framework]] — and move beyond a
  "standards-setter versus standards-taker" dynamic, explicitly warning against "digital
  colonialism." External expert commentary (tagged as analysis). See [[tech.ai]],
  [[gov.regional]], [[geopol.eu]], [[colonialism]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-21 How Africa and Europe could create global AI governance rules]].
