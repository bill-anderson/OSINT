# CMIL (Commission Malagasy de l'Informatique et des Libertés)

Madagascar's **data-protection authority — provided for by law n°2014-038 (9 Jan 2015) but never
established**. Its continuing absence is a structural governance gap as biometric enrolment and
interoperability accelerate. See [[gov.protect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **AFAPDP mission urges CMIL creation (2025-02).** [[gov.protect]] [[sovereignty]] The Francophone
  data-protection network **AFAPDP** assessed Madagascar's data protection and stressed the **urgent
  creation of the CMIL** — still unestablished despite the 2014 law — a "worrying delay" as digital
  transformation exposes personal data. Under the PREA reform programme with **[[oif-la-francophonie|OIF]]**
  support (expertise exchange, study visits incl. Morocco). Source:
  [[2025-02-25-gouvernance-numerique-donnees-personnelles-cmil-lexpress]].
