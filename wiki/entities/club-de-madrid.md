# Club de Madrid

Club de Madrid is the network of former democratic heads of state and government. It matters here as the convenor of a policy dialogue on governing global digital public goods.

## Profile
- Its 2026 Annual Policy Dialogue commissioned a case study on governing global digital public goods for DPI, authored by Alison Gillwald of [[research-ict-africa]].
- The study argues the multilateral trust deficit is structural and that regional DPI is the most viable Global-South pathway.
