# Kiro'o Games

**Central Africa's first professional video-game studio** (founder/CEO **Olivier Madiba**), making
cross-media cultural products (games, comics, colouring books, Rebuntu) "rooted in African cultural
references" against a market dominated by Western/Asian models — the `colonialism`-lens framing is
Madiba's own.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Collateral victim of the InTouch payment monopoly (2025).** Forced onto **[[intouch]]** within three
  weeks by the state's online-gaming payment-aggregation directive, Kiro'o lost **10+ days of all customer
  payments** across its whole catalogue; Madiba warned the decision "could push us straight into
  bankruptcy" for a studio dependent on the Cameroonian market, and argued the state should have consulted
  the Finance and Posts/Telecoms ministries and defined "online gambling" to avoid conflating it with
  entertainment gaming. The clearest evidence in CMR holdings of a **domestic digital-policy decision
  harming a domestic firm**. Source:
  [[2025-02-27-kiroo-games-payment-aggregation-monopoly-intouch-wearetech]].
