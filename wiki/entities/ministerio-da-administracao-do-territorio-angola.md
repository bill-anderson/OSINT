# Ministério da Administração do Território (MAT) — Angola

Angola's ministry of territorial administration — the body running the network of local
one-stop offices (**[[balcao-unico-de-atendimento-ao-publico-angola|BUAP]]**), the
adult-citizen database (**[[base-de-dados-de-cidadaos-maiores-angola|BDCM]]**) and the
2027 electoral-register update, and coordinator of the World Bank
**[[projecto-njila-angola|Njila]]** local-governance project.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Budget more than doubled for the registry push (2026-02).** The President opened a
  **$275m supplementary credit** in the 2026 state budget for MAT — on top of its ~209.2bn
  Kz (~$226m) allocation — to fund the **BDCM proof-of-life action plan**. The price tag on
  the registry drive. Source: [[2026-02-26-credito-suplementar-275-milhoes-usd-mat-bdcm]].
- **2027 electoral-register / proof-of-life (from 2026-06-15).** MAT runs the update through
  the BUAP network; it set the **target universe of 16,707,455 citizens** for the BDCM
  handed to the electoral commission. See **[[angola--dpi-registry]]**,
  [[registo-eleitoral-oficioso-angola]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-15-arranca-prova-de-vida-para-actualizacao-do-registo-eleitoral]].
- **Connectivity gap conceded (2026-06).** The Secretary of State for Local Authorities,
  **Fernando da Paixão**, flagged weak internet and mobile coverage in hard-to-reach areas
  as the chief risk to registration, with 634 BUAPs planned (254 in the first phase) and
  10,000+ *brigadistas*. Source: [[2026-06-20-fraca-cobertura-de-internet-pode-afectar-registo-eleitoral]].
- **Projecto Njila (World Bank, $250m).** MAT coordinates the eight-province local-governance
  project whose Component III expands birth registration and BI issuance. See
  [[projecto-njila-angola]]. Source: [[2026-04-06-projecto-njila-reabilita-postos-de-registo-bie-huambo]].
