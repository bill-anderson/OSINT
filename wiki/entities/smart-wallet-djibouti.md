# Smart Wallet (Djibouti national digital wallet)

Djibouti's planned **national digital wallet**, subject of a **16 September 2025 MoU with [[visa]]** — a digital-payments channel and gateway to government services and digital identity. State-owned **[[djibouti-telecom]]** backed the announcement.

- **Biometric layer:** **[[tech5]]** (face, iris, fingerprint onboarding).
- **Inclusion:** access for the unconnected via post offices and "Maisons du Citoyen"; targets reducing cash usage by **60% within three years**; addresses ~25% bank-account penetration.
- **Legal basis:** the **[[djibouti-digital-code]]**. Stores the **[[mobile-id-djibouti|Mobile ID]]**.

Status: **MoU only** — no detailed rollout calendar published as of late 2025.
