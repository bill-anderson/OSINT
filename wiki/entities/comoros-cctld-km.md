# .km ccTLD (Comoros)

Comoros' **national country-code top-level domain**. In 2025 its management was **transferred from the
incumbent [[comores-telecom|Comores Télécom]] to the state digital agency [[anaden|ANADEN]]** (arrêté
n°24-02), with an ICANN re-delegation step — a digital-sovereignty move putting the national domain under
a public registry. Sources: [[2025-05-08-cctld-km-transfer-comores-telecom-to-anaden-alwatwan]],
[[2025-05-12-cctld-km-transfer-anaden-registry-lagazette]].
