# Rosemary Kisembo

**Executive Director of [[nira-uganda]]** — Uganda's National Identification and
Registration Authority. She appears in this wiki on both sides of the ID system's public
argument: rebutting the surveillance charge, and setting out the vendor stack behind the
new register.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Refuted the surveillance-access claim (2024-06-05).** In comments to Bloomberg,
  Kisembo **refuted claims that law enforcement could readily access the NIRA database** —
  the claim advanced by the Bloomberg / Lighthouse Reports investigation, which reported
  that underlying software and datasets are easily accessed by individuals at all levels
  of law enforcement. The investigation itself is not held (GAP-UGA-002). See
  [[nick-opiyo]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2024-06-05 Ugandan human rights lawyer's arrest exposes use of national ID for surveillance]].
- **Named the technology providers at MOSIP Connect 2026 (reported 2026-02-11).** Kisembo
  said Uganda's technology providers for the new national ID system include **[[tech5]],
  Technoforte, Entrust and Hyf** — alongside service provider **[[tahaluf]]** and the
  five **[[mosip]]**-based modules. See [[dpi.id]], [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-11 Uganda migrates more than 28M records to new national ID system]].
