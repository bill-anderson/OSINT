# Bank of Algeria

Algeria's **central bank** (governor **Mohamed Lamine Lebbou**, 2026) — the regulator behind the digital-payments
build-out. It stood up the domestic **instant payment system**, joined **[[papss|PAPSS]]** (2025-08), set
licensing rules for **fully digital banks** (Instruction 02-2025), and issued a **binding KYC instruction
04-2026** (30 Apr 2026) enforcing AML/CTF and PEP scrutiny. The new monetary-and-banking law opened the market
to **digital banks and payment-service providers**, several of whose dossiers are under review. See
[[algeria--dpi-pay]].
