# GITGE (Gestor de Infraestructuras de Telecomunicaciones de Guinea Ecuatorial)

Equatorial Guinea's **state telecoms-infrastructure manager** — operator of the country's international
connectivity (the **ACE** and Ceiba subsea-cable landings) and its recurring representative in cable governance.
GITGE **hosted the 36th ACE Operation & Maintenance Committee** (Malabo, May 2026) — its **second** ACE technical
meeting — confirming its standing role in managing internationally significant digital infrastructure. It also
**authored a digital-maturity study of the ministries** (2026-07), quantifying a wide gap. See
[[gnq--infra-connect]].
