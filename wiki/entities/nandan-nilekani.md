# Nandan Nilekani

Founding chairman of UIDAI (which built India's Aadhaar); principal advocate of taking
India's DPI model to the world as an open public good.

## Positions / arguments

- Aadhaar itself is "a strategic asset of India" and proprietary — but its *ideas* can be
  rebuilt as open source ([[mosip]]) with stronger privacy/standards.
- The DPI bet: countries seeking inclusive growth need "low-cost, interoperable public
  rails on which private innovation can flourish" — Western proprietary models seen as
  prohibitive. Co-chaired the **G20 Task Force on DPI** (2022–23), which coined "DPI"
  (previously "IndiaStack"). See [[geopol.india]], [[gov.standards]].
