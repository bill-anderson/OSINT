# Xalam Analytics

**A research and analysis firm covering African digital infrastructure markets, and the author
of the data-centre market briefings held in this wiki as reference works.** Its output is a
standing OSINT input on the continent's data-centre demand, supply and investment landscape. See
[[infra.store]], [[tech.industry]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Togo Data Center Market Briefing (July 2025).** A country market briefing produced by
  **Xalam Analytics / DIF**, held in `raw/` as a PDF artefact with a companion source page. See
  [[TGO]], [[infra.store]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-01 Togo Data Center Market Briefing (Xalam Analytics)]].
- **The 2026 DIF brief series.** Xalam also produced the **22-country data-centre market brief
  series** commissioned by the **Digital Investment Facility (DIF)** under the Data Governance in
  Africa Initiative (January 2026) — catalogued separately as
  **[[xalam-dc-market-briefs-2026]]**, which includes a Togo brief. The July-2025 Togo briefing
  above is a **distinct, earlier item**; the two should not be collapsed.

**Reference study — cite, do not absorb.** The briefings are country-level reference works.
**Their dated figures are not promoted onto concept or place pages as current state**, and none
are lifted here — consult the relevant brief for a country's live/planned MW, operator landscape
and power/regulatory constraints when building a place hub. A specific dated figure pulled from a
brief becomes a normal source citing the resource.

**Scope note.** The evidence held here establishes Xalam Analytics through its briefings only;
no ownership, headquarters, size or methodology is asserted from it.
