# William Ruto

President of Kenya; central to Kenya's positioning as an East African AI/digital
hub and its balancing of US, Gulf and multilateral partners.

## Activity in this wiki

- **OpenAI Academy talks (2026-06).** Disclosed G7-sidelines discussions with
  **[[openai]]** CEO Sam Altman on a Nairobi OpenAI Academy; framed Kenya as
  seeking to "shape" rather than "adopt" the digital future. Source:
  [[2026-06-18 Kenya Eyes First OpenAI Academy in East Africa Following Ruto–Sam Altman Talks]].
- **Microsoft–G42 grid warning (2026-07).** Said the stalled **1 GW**
  **[[microsoft-g42-kenya-datacenter]]** would force cutting power to half the
  country; targeting **10 GW by 2030**. See [[infra.capacity]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-09 Microsoft and G42 data center in Kenya stalled due to lack of power capacity]].
- **First public cloud region (announced 2024).** Ruto announced Kenya's first public cloud
  region, now confirmed (Jan 2026) as the **Oracle OCI Nairobi** region hosted at
  [[ixafrica-data-centres]] ([[ixafrica-oracle-oci-nairobi]]). See [[infra.store]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-28 iXAfrica, Oracle to launch Kenya's first public cloud region]].
- Associated with Kenya's data-as-asset agenda ([[kenya-data-marketplace]]).

See [[KEN]], [[tech.ai]], [[geopol.usa]].
