# Ministry of Home Affairs, Immigration, Safety & Security (MHAISS, Namibia)

Namibia's ministry responsible for **civil registration and identity** — home of the Registrar-General and
custodian of the Civil Register under the **[[namibia-civil-registration-identification-act-2024|CRIA 2024]]**.
It is leading the **[[namibia-digital-id|national e-ID rollout]]** (targeted for July 2026; executive director
Etienne Maritz) and a stepped-up **civil-registration drive** backed by a ~**US$9m** budget allocation. It
works with **[[undp|UNDP]]** on digital-legal-identity governance.
