# Telecom Egypt–Helios RDH majority stake sale (cancelled)

Proposed sale by **[[telecom-egypt]]** of a majority interest in the unit owning its
**[[regional-data-center-hub]]** to **[[helios-investment-partners]]**.

|---|---|

| Stake | **75–80%** of the RDH-owning unit |

| Consideration | Not disclosed in the wiki's held sources |
| Status | **Cancelled — board withdrawal reported 2026-07-17** |

## Outcome

Telecom Egypt's board withdrew from the agreement, stating that **the conditions for the
proposed transaction had not been met**. Rather than seek another buyer, the operator said it
would **carve RDH out as a wholly-owned subsidiary**, citing the data-centre sector's growth
and rising Egyptian demand as requiring a specialised operating model, and framing the move as
enhancing "the value of the asset" and "long-term returns for shareholders."

No consideration, valuation or breakdown of the unmet conditions appears in the source — the
commercial terms are a provenance gap.
