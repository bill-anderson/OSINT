# ANTIC (Agence Nationale des Technologies de l'Information et de la Communication, Cameroon)

Cameroon's national ICT agency, led by Director General **Ebot Ebot Enaw**. ANTIC
runs the country's **Computer Incident Response Team (CIRT)** and its Public Key
Infrastructure (PKI), and leads the drive to secure Cameroon's digital
transformation.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cybersecurity procurement (2026-01-20).** ANTIC took delivery of intrusion
  detection/prevention hardware and software worth **~XAF735m (US$1.3m)**,
  commissioned in Yaoundé to fortify its CIRT — five servers, three
  investigation platforms, 20 storage consoles, 30 workstations, and vulnerability
  scanning / penetration-testing platforms. The procurement ran through the
  Ministry of Posts & Telecommunications under the World Bank-funded [[patnuc]]
  (a 2024 tender). See [[CMR]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[world-bank]]. Sources:
  [[2026-01-22 Cameroon boosts cyber defense systems]],
  [[2026-01-27 Cameroon deploys cybersecurity system to protect DPI, boost digital trust]].
- **Threat surface (as of 2026-01).** ANTIC reports identifying **at least 8,502
  vulnerabilities since January 2024** through scans and audits across public and
  private entities; its CIRT collects and analyses **~200 GB of data daily** and
  responds to **~200 urgent requisitions**. It processed **32,500 cybercrime
  complaints in 2025** (up 30% on 2024). See [[dpi.govtech]]. Sources:
  [[2026-01-22 Cameroon boosts cyber defense systems]],
  [[2026-01-27 Cameroon deploys cybersecurity system to protect DPI, boost digital trust]].
