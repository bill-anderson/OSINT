# ANTIC (Agence Nationale des Technologies de l'Information et de la Communication, Cameroon)

Cameroon's national ICT agency, led by Director General **Ebot Ebot Enaw**. ANTIC
runs the country's **Computer Incident Response Team (CIRT)** and its Public Key
Infrastructure (PKI), and leads the drive to secure Cameroon's digital
transformation.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cybersecurity procurement (2026-01-20).** ANTIC took delivery of intrusion
  detection/prevention hardware and software worth **~XAF735m (US$1.3m)**,
  commissioned in Yaoundé to fortify its CIRT — five servers, three
  investigation platforms, 20 storage consoles, 30 workstations, and vulnerability
  scanning / penetration-testing platforms. The procurement ran through the
  Ministry of Posts & Telecommunications under the World Bank-funded [[patnuc]]
  (a 2024 tender); a third account dates the **handover to 20 Jan 2026** and values it at **FCFA 735m+**,
  naming PATNUC/World Bank as funder. See [[CMR]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[world-bank]]. Sources:
  [[2026-01-22 Cameroon boosts cyber defense systems]],
  [[2026-01-27 Cameroon deploys cybersecurity system to protect DPI, boost digital trust]],
  [[2026-01-21-antic-cirt-cybersecurity-systems-patnuc-techafricanews]].
- **DNS sovereignty — DNSSEC/RPKI (Dec 2025 → 2026).** ANTIC (DG Ebot Ebot Enaw) **cryptographically
  signed the national `.cm` zone on 8 Apr 2025** — framed as "an act of digital sovereignty" — then set a
  hard roadmap. National **DNSSEC validation stood at 56.60%** (Dec 2025), masking wide disparities:
  **Orange Cameroun just 2.75%**, most operators **0%**. Targets (with ICANN + AFRINIC support): **98%
  DNSSEC validation and 100% RPKI** across all operators by H1 2026, later **raised to 100% DNSSEC by
  end-2026** at the 6th National DNS Forum (17–19 Jun 2026), with an explicit **IPv4→IPv6** push framed as
  traceability + cybersecurity. Sources:
  [[2025-12-09-leconomie-antic-dnssec-rpki-cyberspace-defence]],
  [[2025-12-10-dnssec-validation-cm-domain-antic-techafricanews]],
  [[2026-06-19-antic-national-dns-forum-sovereign-internet-techafricanews]].
- **Threat figures & fintech readiness.** ANTIC reported blocking **>19 million cyber-threats in 2024**;
  and ran a **fintech cyber-risk workshop** with The Change Engine (Nov 2025). Sources:
  [[2025-02-13-lejour-19-million-cyber-threats-blocked-2024]],
  [[2025-11-17-leconomie-antic-fintech-cyber-risk-workshop]].
- **Threat surface (as of 2026-01).** ANTIC reports identifying **at least 8,502
  vulnerabilities since January 2024** through scans and audits across public and
  private entities; its CIRT collects and analyses **~200 GB of data daily** and
  responds to **~200 urgent requisitions**. It processed **32,500 cybercrime
  complaints in 2025** (up 30% on 2024). See [[dpi.govtech]]. Sources:
  [[2026-01-22 Cameroon boosts cyber defense systems]],
  [[2026-01-27 Cameroon deploys cybersecurity system to protect DPI, boost digital trust]].
