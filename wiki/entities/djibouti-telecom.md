# Djibouti Telecom

**State-owned incumbent operator**, central to Djibouti's role as a submarine-cable and data-residency hub. Operates the **D-Money** mobile-payment service (launched 2020; extended to port workers via Djibouti Port Community Systems). Amplified the government's 2025 **[[smart-wallet-djibouti|Smart Wallet]]** MoU with **[[visa]]**, signalling national buy-in.
