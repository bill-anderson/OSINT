# Djibouti Telecom

**State-owned incumbent operator**, central to Djibouti's role as a submarine-cable and data-residency hub. Operates the **D-Money** mobile-payment service (launched 2020; extended to port workers via Djibouti Port Community Systems). Amplified the government's 2025 **[[smart-wallet-djibouti|Smart Wallet]]** MoU with **[[visa]]**, signalling national buy-in.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Horizon Fiber corridor (2026-02).** Partnered with **[[ethio-telecom]]** and
  **[[sudatel]]** to launch the **[[horizon-fiber]]** cross-border terrestrial
  fibre corridor from Djibouti's subsea landing stations through Ethiopia to
  Sudan (MoU Dec 2024 → binding Feb 2026); its CEO framed the corridor as
  strengthening the region's "digital sovereignty." See [[infra.connect]],
  [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-10 Ethio telecom, Djibouti Telecom, Sudatel Seal Binding Deal on Cross-Border Fiber Network]].
