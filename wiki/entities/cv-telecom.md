# CV Telecom (Cabo Verde Telecom)

Cabo Verde's **telecom incumbent** — owner of the inter-island backbone. The state extended a **€37M
sovereign guarantee** for its telecom infrastructure (incl. an **inter-island submarine cable**), which
drew a formal open-access/neutrality objection from competitor **[[unitel|UNITEL T+]]** (see
[[cabo-verde--infra-connect]]). Source:
[[2026-04-21-estado-aval-37-milhoes-cvtelecom-telecomunicacoes-expressodasilhas]].
