# CNDP (National Commission for the Control of Personal Data Protection, Morocco)

Morocco's **data-protection authority** (president Omar Seghrouchni), enforcing **Law 09-08** on personal
data — and increasingly a co-author of the country's AI-governance agenda (responsible-AI platform,
Idarati X.0 wallet oversight). See [[gov.protect]], [[morocco--tech-ai]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Responsible-AI platform MoU (2025-09).** [[tech.ai]] [[sovereignty]] The CNDP and the Ministry of
  Digital Transition signed a deal to build a **national responsible-AI platform** based on **sovereign
  Moroccan LLMs** — incorporating Moroccan language, culture, legal framework and digital identity — to
  give citizens, business and administrations rights-respecting generative-AI tools. A "strategic step"
  toward Moroccan AI defined by "sovereignty, ethics and responsibility." Source:
  [[2025-09-11-en-hespress-morocco-responsible-ai-platform-deal]].
- **Idarati X.0 co-lead.** [[dpi.id]] The CNDP co-leads the [[idarati-x0]] wallet's privacy-by-design
  governance with the digital-transition ministry. See [[morocco--dpi-id]].
