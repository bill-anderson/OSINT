# Algeria biometric national ID card

Algeria's **existing biometric national identity card** — the foundational credential the 2025
**[[algeria-digital-id-trust-law|digital-ID & trust-services law]]** builds on, consolidating citizens'
biometric data into a unified digital-identity platform. Increasingly a **prerequisite for public
transactions** — e.g. mandatory for vehicle-registration transfers under the all-digital system (2026-04).
See [[algeria--dpi-id]].
