# Maturity Assessment of DHIS2 Implementation in Ethiopia

Peer-reviewed article by **Yilma, Taddese, Mamuye, Tilahun et al.**, *JMIR Medical
Informatics* **12:e50375 (2024)**. Assesses national **[[dhis2]]** maturity using the **SOCI
(Stages of Continuous Improvement) tool**. Cited here as literature; a later dated fact drawn
from it becomes a source citing this page. Part of the HMIS/[[dhis2]] evidence cluster
([[dpi.mis]]).

## Argument

- Ethiopia's DHIS2 implementation scores at the **"defined" stage (2.81)** against a
  **"managed" (4.09) target for 2025** — meaningful adoption, but short of an
  institutionalised, data-driven system ([[data.statistics]]).
- **ICT infrastructure is the weakest dimension** — "repeatable," **2.14** ([[infra.connect]]);
  **interoperability and data use are only "emerging"** ([[dpi.exchange]], [[gov.standards]]).
- Flags that **Ethiopia lacks a national digital ID / client registry**, an **interoperability
  gap** for linking health records — the layer later addressed by **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]]**.
