# National Personal Data Protection Commission (Congo-Brazzaville)

The Republic of Congo's independent data-protection authority, tasked with
overseeing and regulating personal-data processing as the country digitises
public services and expands its digital economy.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Origin — draft law approved (2024-07-03).** The Council of Ministers approved the bill creating the
  commission (presented by Minister Léon-Juste Ibombo) on 3 July 2024 — to strengthen legislation, build
  trust in digital services and ensure rigorous control of personal-data use. Source: [[2024-07-05 Congo - Council of Ministers approves bill creating National Personal Data Protection Commission]].
- **Established (~2026-01-22).** ICT minister **Léon Juste Ibombo** installed the
  commission's members, framing the body as central to consolidating the
  country's **"digital rule of law"** amid rapid digital transformation
  (digitisation of public services, growth of online platforms and social
  networks). See [[COG]], [[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-22 Congo-Brazzaville intros data protection commission]].
- **Mandate.** Positioned as a reliable, independent and authoritative body
  empowered to **advise, warn and, where required, sanction** in line with the
  law; charged not only with monitoring and framing data processing but with
  instilling a "genuine culture of data protection" across the public sector,
  companies, civil society and citizens — striking a balance between innovation
  and rights protection. See [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-22 Congo-Brazzaville intros data protection commission]].

- **Legal instruments named (2026-01).** A second-outlet account supplies the **two instruments the held
  source lacked: Decree No. 2025-538 (31 Dec 2025) and Law No. 29-2019 (10 Oct 2019)**, plus the election of
  President/VP/Rapporteur. And the judiciary is building capacity to *apply* digital law (2nd Magistrates
  Open Days, Pointe-Noire, Nov 2025) — the enforcement layer under the commission. Sources:
  [[2026-01-22-congo-launches-data-protection-commission-techafricanews]],
  [[2025-11-13-congo-arpce-judiciary-digital-governance-techafricanews]].
