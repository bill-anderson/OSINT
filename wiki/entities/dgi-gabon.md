# DGI Gabon (Direction Générale des Impôts)

Gabon's **tax authority** (Directorate-General of Taxes), the operator of the country's
revenue-digitalisation programme and the institutional driver behind the ~$13bn 2026 budget's
revenue-collection targets.

## Activity in this wiki

- Runs the **[[digitax-gabon]]** platform (launched April 2024) and the taxpayer-facing **e-tax**
  portal (`e-tax.dgi.ga`, Aug 2025).
- Per its **2024 report**, at least **80% of tax revenues are now collected digitally**; it reported
  CFA1,021.58bn in treasury revenues (18% above target) and 107% of its budgetary target.
- Its revenues feed the treasury-wide **[[sigfip-gabon]]** platform launched January 2026.
