# Niger national e-ID / AES biometric ID & e-Passport

Niger's programme to modernise foundational identity with a **biometric national ID card and electronic
passport**, aligned to the **[[alliance-of-sahel-states|Confederation of Sahel States (AES)]]** regional
credential. The Council of Ministers approved the enabling **decrees on 26 December 2025** (following the AES
biometric-ID framework), with President Tiani the first enrollee at the March-2026 launch. Production is being
digitised under **two MoUs signed 27 June 2025 with the Libyan firm [[al-itissal-al-jadeed|Al Itissal Al
Jadeed]]**, framed as a digital-sovereignty and technological-independence move.
