# CST (Companhia Santomense de Telecomunicações)

São Tomé and Príncipe's incumbent telecom operator (35 years in 2025). Expanding fibre and mobile to
communities and **schools** (with the Education Ministry); reached **35 antennas** nationwide with the
Água Izé tower (Mar 2026), closing rural-coverage gaps. See [[sao-tome--infra-connect]], [[STP]].
