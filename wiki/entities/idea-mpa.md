# IDEA — Inclusive Digitalization in Eastern and Southern Africa (MPA)

A World Bank **Multiphase Programmatic Approach** (P512859) financing national digital
transformation across Eastern & Southern Africa. Board-approved envelope **US$2.48bn**
(program financing ~US$2.58bn), approved **27 June 2024**. Coordinating Regional
Economic Community: **COMESA**.

## Program development objective

"Increase access to, and inclusive use of, the internet and digitally enabled services
in Eastern and Southern Africa." Emphasis on cross-border regulatory harmonisation and
a Single Digital Market — see [[gov.regional]].

## Operations (per the Zambia PAD, 2025-09-11)

- Earlier operations: **COMESA** (REC), DR Congo (COD), Angola (AGO), Malawi (MWI).
- **[[ZMB]] Zambia — [[dzap]]** is the **5th** operation (US$100M IDA + US$20M UCF).
- Further phases named: Eswatini (SWZ), Tanzania (TZA), Botswana (BWA), Madagascar
  (MDG), Lesotho (LSO), South Sudan (SSD), and a second Malawi operation.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DRC digital-transformation blueprint (2026-01-30).** The IDEA program is named among the
  external financiers (with the French Development Agency) expected to fund **[[COD]] DR Congo**'s
  2026–2030 digital blueprint — a ~**US$10.4bn** plan (government committing ~$1.5bn) targeting a
  functional digital-government platform in Q1 2026, an interbank payment platform, tax/customs
  portals, and the **[[rdc-pass]]** digital ID. Confirms IDEA's role in the COD operation flagged
  above. See [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-30 DRC envisages a functional digital government platform by March]].
