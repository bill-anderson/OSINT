# Algeria–Russia cybersecurity MoU

MoU (2025-10/11) between Russia's **Positive Technologies** and Algeria's **EPE Proxylan SPA** — a subsidiary
of **[[cerist]]** — for joint cybersecurity research, joint training programmes, "cutting-edge technology
transfer" and durable technological cooperation (extending, per the Russo-Algerian business council, to smart
cities and e-government). A notable **non-Western** cyber tie; the wiki has **no `geopol.russia` slug** (see
ISSUE-014). See [[algeria--infra-cybersec]].
