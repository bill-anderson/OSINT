# Telecom Egypt

Egypt's **state-owned integrated telecom operator** (CEO **Mohamed Nasr**), central to the country's role as a **submarine-cable and data-centre hub**. Operates the **Regional Data Hub** data centres — **RDH 1** (2.5 MW) and the Tier-III-certified **RDH 2** (4.6 MW) — leveraging the **13 subsea cable systems** landing on Egypt's eastern coast (expanding toward 18). One of the four operators in Egypt's June-2025 5G rollout.
