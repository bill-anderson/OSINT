# BRICS Pay

A **proposed cross-border digital payment and settlement system** for the BRICS grouping,
designed to enable settlement in national currencies (and future CBDCs), reducing
dependence on the US dollar and SWIFT/dollar-clearing. Positioned as an interoperability
layer linking members' domestic rails — China's CIPS, Russian SWIFT-alternatives, India's
UPI.
