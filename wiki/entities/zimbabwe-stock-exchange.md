# Zimbabwe Stock Exchange (ZSE)

**Zimbabwe's primary domestic securities exchange — in this wiki, the disclosure regime whose Listings Requirements produced the documentary record behind the Econet restructuring.** The wiki holds the ZSE not as a market but as a source of primary corporate filings: cautionary announcements, circulars and pre-listing statements that settle facts trade press gets wrong.

*Paged for that evidentiary role. The wiki holds no ZSE market data — no index level, capitalisation or listing count — as of **2026-07-20**.*

## Activity in this wiki

- **The EWZL voluntary delisting (resolved 2025-12-15).** [[econet-wireless-zimbabwe]]'s board resolved to pursue a **voluntary delisting from the ZSE under Section 11.5 of the ZSE Listings Requirements**, following a first cautionary announcement of **2025-12-03**. Shareholders were offered a **voluntary Exit Offer** settled **partly in cash and partly in [[econet-infraco]] shares** before delisting took effect, with up to **30%** of InfraCo earmarked. Source: [[2025-12-15-econet-wireless-zimbabwe-further-cautionary-announcement]].
- **The stated rationale — a valuation gap.** EWZL "has traded at a significant discount to its peers across Africa which trade at **6–8x EV/EBITDA**", peers having already separated and realised value from their towers. Post-delisting, EWZL's Memorandum and Articles were to be **amended to reinstate shareholder rights of pre-emption** that had been removed on listing — i.e. the delisting reverses a governance concession the listing itself required. Source: same.
- **The ZSE-to-[[victoria-falls-stock-exchange|VFEX]] migration.** The restructuring moves the group's infrastructure vehicle onto VFEX (InfraCo trading from **2026-03-31**) while taking EWZL off the ZSE — the two exchanges functioning as different regimes rather than rivals for the same paper. See [[econet-infraco]], [[victoria-falls-stock-exchange]].
- **The filings as evidence.** The **2026-02-04** EWZL circular and InfraCo Pre-Listing Statement, and the **2026-03-26** Supplementary PLS, supplied the incorporation date, original registration number and founding shareholding that resolved the **DPA-identity** contradiction. See [[distributed-power-africa]]. Sources: [[2026-02-04-econet-wireless-zimbabwe-circular-and-econet-infraco-pre-listing-statement]], [[2026-03-26-econet-infraco-supplementary-pre-listing-statement]], [[2025-12-24-econet-notice-completion-infrastructure-restructuring]].

## Reads

- A listing requirement is a disclosure engine. Almost everything the wiki can state precisely about the Econet group's structure exists because the ZSE and VFEX regimes compelled its publication — a reminder that securities regulation, not transparency policy, is often the best-functioning corporate-data source in a market.
