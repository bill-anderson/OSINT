# Airtel Malawi Plc

One of Malawi's two mobile network operators (Chairperson **Kayisi Sadala**, MD **Aashish Dutt**), and operator
of the **Airtel Money** mobile-money service (see [[dpi.pay]]). At its **27th AGM** (Mzuzu, 15 May 2026) it
reported FY2025 network capex of **MK50.8bn** — **58 new sites** and a **5G footprint of 111 sites across 10
districts** (Airtel launched 5G in Nov 2025, after TNM) — and signalled interest in **satellite connectivity**
for hard-to-reach areas. Financials: gross revenue +34%, EBITDA +29.4%, PBT +54.9%; a **MK38.5bn** dividend
(+75%). See [[malawi--infra-connect]].

Its **Airtel Money** arm partnered the state **Admarc** to digitise agricultural-value-chain payments into
farmers' wallets (Jun 2026 — see [[malawi--dpi-pay]]), and the **Airtel Africa Foundation** launched the **Nzeru
Fund**, funding **25 low-income STEM undergraduates** (IT, CS, software engineering, data science, AI,
cybersecurity) at the Malawi University of Science and Technology (see [[malawi--tech-ai]], [[capacity.training]]).
