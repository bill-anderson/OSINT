# Tanzania Communications Regulatory Authority (TCRA)

Tanzania's **converged communications regulator** — telecoms, broadcasting and **postal
services** — and, through its sector statistics, one of the country's standing sources of
dated communications data. Director General: **Dr Jabiri Bakari** (as of 2026-02).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Addressing as postal-sector policy (2026-02-09).** At the Annual Residential Address
  Conference and Postal Services Technology Exhibition, DG **Dr Jabiri Bakari** framed the
  national residential addressing system (**[[napa-tanzania]]**) as **strengthening postal
  services into a channel for financial, social and government services** — improving
  location-identification accuracy and positioning post as a strategic driver of economic
  growth, **particularly benefiting rural communities**. This is the regulator reading an
  address registry as service-delivery infrastructure, not merely as mail logistics. See
  [[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]], [[digital.rural]], [[tanzania-posts-corporation]].
  Source:
  [[2026-02-09 Tanzania registers 12 million housing addresses to boost digital governance]].
- **Sector statistics feeding payments analysis.** TCRA's **Q4 2024** statistics supply the
  **63.2m active mobile money wallets at end-2024** figure used in the Bank of Tanzania / FSDT
  TIPS case study — TCRA data underpinning **[[bank-of-tanzania]]**'s account of
  **[[tips-tanzania]]**. See [[data.statistics]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-16 Building an Inclusive Payments Ecosystem in Tanzania through TIPS (BOT and FSDT)]].
  *(That case study is `date_source: proxy` — no printed date; the day precision is unverified.
  The TCRA Q4-2024 attribution is stated within it.)*
