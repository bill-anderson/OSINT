# Development Gateway

Development Gateway, an IREX venture, is a data and digital-development nonprofit. It matters here for its analysis of how external funding shocks ripple through national data systems.

## Profile
- An IREX venture focused on data and digital solutions for international development.
- Co-authored (with Open Data Watch) the analysis "A year after: reclaiming control of data systems," on the impact of the January-2025 US aid freeze on national data systems.
