# Gates Foundation–Anthropic partnership

Multi-year partnership between the [[gates-foundation]] and [[anthropic]],
announced 2026-05-14, to build AI tools and shared public goods for low- and
middle-income countries.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Parties.** [[gates-foundation]] and [[anthropic]] ([[tech.ai]]).
- **Announced.** 2026-05-14.
- **Commitment.** $200m over four years — grants, API credits and technical
  support — for AI tools and shared public goods such as datasets and benchmarks
  across health, education and agriculture in LMICs ([[include.access]],
  [[capacity.research]]).
- **Health work.** Collaboration with IHME on the Global Burden of Disease study;
  Africa relevance is illustrative, with Kenya [[KEN]] and Nigeria [[NGA]]
  examples cited.
- **Status.** Active.

Source: [[2026-05-14 Making AI work for more people]].
