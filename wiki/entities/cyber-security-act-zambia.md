# Cyber Security Act No. 3 / Cyber Crimes Act No. 4 of 2025 (Zambia)

Zambia's 2025 cyber legislation, **signed 8 April 2025** by President Hichilema. The **Cyber Security Act
No. 3** establishes the **[[zambia-cyber-security-agency|ZCSA]]** and a critical-information-infrastructure
regime (and, per the US Embassy alert, **requires ICT providers to proactively intercept all electronic
communications** to detect "critical information" — a Central Monitoring Centre). The **Cyber Crimes Act
No. 4** criminalises unauthorised access, identity offences, computer fraud, "deceptive electronic
communications," malware and "false information."

The Act is the axis of a live democratic-vs-security debate around the **13 August 2026 election** — see
[[zambia--infra-cybersec]] (US alert, LAZ challenge, opposition repeal pledge, ZCSA's protective framing).
