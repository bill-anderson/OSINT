# TICEF (National ICT-for-Education policy)

DR Congo's **national ICT-for-education-and-training policy** (adopted 12 Jun 2026; 19 principles),
explicitly meant to make the sector's **scattered digital initiatives interoperable** — paired with a
MINEDU-NC/FDSU MoU to connect schools into an interoperable education data ecosystem. Sources:
[[2026-06-15-politique-nationale-ticef-education-actualite]],
[[2026-04-03-memorandum-digitalisation-systeme-educatif-fdsu-actualite]].
