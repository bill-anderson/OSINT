# Digital Land Administration Platform (DLAP), Zimbabwe

The national **digital deeds-registry platform** under **[[zimbabwe-si-76-2025|SI 76 of 2025]]** — the
operational system for "securitised deeds."

- **Validation pilot (Apr 2026).** A **16-law-firm pilot** (notice 4 Apr 2026) tests/refines DLAP's
  processes; paper-deed holders must validate through conveyancers within **24 months of the SI's publication
  → deadline ~18 July 2027** (fingerprints + notarized ID required). See [[zimbabwe--dpi-registry]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-07-title-deed-validation-reissuance-pilot-madzima]].
