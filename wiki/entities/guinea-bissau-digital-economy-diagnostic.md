# Guinea-Bissau Digital Economy Diagnostic (World Bank)

The World Bank's **Digital Economy Diagnostic** for Guinea-Bissau — the reference assessment of the five **DE4A** pillars for one of West Africa's least-connected states, documenting coast-confined international connectivity (ACE cable, 2023), no national backbone, ~32.5% internet penetration (2023), and only embryonic middle-mile fibre. The evidence base behind the [[entd-gw]] strategy. Reference figures — cite, don't absorb. See [[GNB]].
