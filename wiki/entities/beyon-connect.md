# Beyon Connect

**Bahraini digital-identity/govtech firm** (Beyon group) — a **third foreign digital-ID vector** into
Côte d'Ivoire, alongside the US (Cybastion/EXIM) and Gulf (G42/Presight) pushes.

## Activity in this wiki

- **La Poste OneID/OneBox MoU (Nov 2025).** Signed an MoU with **[[la-poste-cote-divoire]]** for a
  proof-of-concept of its **OneID** (digital identity) and **OneBox** (secure digital mailbox) products,
  via the postal operator — a Gulf-origin identity layer routed through La Poste, overlapping the
  ONECI/MaPoste national stack. Sources:
  [[2025-11-27-beyon-connect-la-poste-mou-oneid-onebox-techafricanews]],
