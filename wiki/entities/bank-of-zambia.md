# Bank of Zambia (BoZ)

Zambia's central bank and the regulator of the national payment system. It sits at the centre
of the country's financial-inclusion architecture, overseeing the rules under which mobile
money and digital payments operate.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Government–PAYZ digital-payments push (~2026-02-26).** BoZ participated, alongside the
  Ministry of Technology and Science and [[zicta]], in the State House engagement between
  President Hichilema and the [[payz]] on accelerating digital financial services and reducing
  cash dependency. The engagement sits against the backdrop of the National Financial Inclusion
  Strategy, with newly-licensed ZedMobile named Zambia's fourth MNO. Tags [[dpi.pay]],
