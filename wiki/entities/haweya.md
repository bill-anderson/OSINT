# Haweya (Egypt national digital financial ID)

The **[[central-bank-of-egypt|CBE]]**'s mobile app for **digital-ID verification and remote eKYC** — the cornerstone of Egypt's **national digital financial identity system**. Citizens register with **face + fingerprint biometrics and a digital signature**, creating a trusted profile that enables remote **bank-account opening (37 banks)**, document verification, SIM purchase and access to other government/private services. Led by CEO **Tamer Gadalla**; system integrator **Raya IT**. Framed as scaling **financial inclusion** (76%+ of adults by mid-2025). Complements Interior's **[[moieg-pass]]** government-services identity app within Egypt's DPI.
