# SIREC (Integrated System of Citizen Registers and Statistics, Guinea-Bissau)

Guinea-Bissau's new civil-registration digitization platform (Sistema Integrado de Registos e Estatísticas do Cidadão), run by the Civil Registration Office of the Ministry of Justice ([[dpi.registry]]). It is the foundation for an integrated digital-identity system in a country where birth registration is **below 50%** (~46% of under-fives). Launched in 2025 with UNDP support (officer training on enrollment kits), it is being developed alongside a UNDP "Building National ID" project (Swiss-financed) that scans and digitizes the paper civil-registry books. The government is exploring integration with the health and education sectors. See [[GNB]], [[guinea-bissau--dpi-id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **SIREC rollout + civil-registry digitization (2025).** [[dpi.id]] Civil Registration head Raul Gomes (MoJ) described SIREC as the digitization backbone; UNDP trained officers, and health technicians are being trained for facility birth registration. In parallel the UNDP "Building National ID" project sets up a digitization centre and software to digitize births/deaths/marriages. Sources: [[2025-06-10 Guinea-Bissau deploys efforts to digitize birth registration, national ID systems]], [[2024-01-01 Building a National Identification System - Scanning and Digitizing the Civil Registry Books in Guinea-Bissau]].

- **Civil-registry/notary digitization workshop (2025-06).** [[dpi.registry]] A two-day workshop
  (50 technicians) opened by Justice Minister Albino Gomes launched the modernisation of civil
  identification, registry and notary services under **DGICRAN** (DG José Alves Té). It set out an
  EU-backed digitization of registry books, credited a UNICEF/Health partnership with lifting birth
  registration **from 23.4% to 46%** (target ≥90%), a Bissau-then-regions rollout, and a link to help
  the National Elections Commission (CNE) update electoral rolls. Source: [[2025-06-01 Guinea-Bissau to digitize civil identification, registry and notary services]].
