# ZIPIT

Zimbabwe's interbank instant-transfer service (on [[zimswitch]]) — enables some cross-platform mobile-money
transfers, but not yet **seamless interoperability** (a gap that preserves EcoCash's network advantage). Its
per-transaction limits were raised **ZiG 8,000 → 13,000** in the 2026 MPS. See [[zimbabwe--dpi-pay]].
