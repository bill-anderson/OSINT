# MTN Rwanda

Rwandan subsidiary of the MTN Group and a leading mobile operator, with the **MoMo** mobile-money
platform. In **Q1 2026** MTN Rwanda returned to profit (**Rwf10.8bn / ~$6.8m** after tax; service
revenue +14.7% to Rwf295.7bn), led by digital growth: **data subscribers 2.8m (+14.1%)** and **MoMo
monthly active users 6.2m (+17.3%)**, fintech revenue +27.6%. See [[rwanda--infra-connect]],
[[rwanda--dpi-pay]].
