# Gabon–Huawei Kimba Connect skills accord

**Parties:** the **[[ministere-economie-numerique-gabon]]** and **Huawei Technologies Gabon**
([[huawei]]). **What:** an agreement giving Huawei a **technical accompaniment** role in
**[[kimba-connect]]**. **When/where:** signed **2026-07-14**, at the programme's launch.
**Status:** active.

## Commitments

- Create **"salles intelligentes"** (smart rooms) dedicated to innovation.
- Deploy **training programmes** in **networks, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence and software
  development**.
- Give beneficiaries access to **certifying training kits** used in Huawei's international programmes.

**Terms not published (as of 2026-07-17):** no number of rooms, trainee target, cost, funding split or
duration is on the record.

## Reading

This is **distinct from — and later than — the [[gabon-huawei-mou]]** signed 2025-12-23, which covered
national digital transformation broadly; this accord is scoped to the start-up programme. Together they
mark Huawei as the standing technical partner to Gabon's digital agenda at **both** the state-
infrastructure and the skills-pipeline layers. The certification dependency is the sovereignty point:
where the curriculum, the certificate and the equipment are a single vendor's, capacity-building and
vendor lock-in are the same programme. Capture is not endorsement.
