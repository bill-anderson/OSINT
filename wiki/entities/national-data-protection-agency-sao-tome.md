# National Personal Data Protection Agency (ANPDP, São Tomé and Príncipe)

STP's data-protection regulator — the **Agência Nacional de Protecção de Dados Pessoais
(ANPDP)** — the enforcement authority under the [[sao-tome-data-protection-law-2016]]
(Personal Data Guarantee and Protection Law, Law 03/2016).

## Powers

- Imposes **fines** and **ancillary measures**: data cessation, blocking, deletion or
  destruction.
- Handles **controller notifications**; failure to notify, or providing fraudulent
  information, triggers fines (STN 50m–120m for individuals; STN 250m–500m for legal
  entities).
