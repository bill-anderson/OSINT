# Central Bank of Mauritania (BCM)

Mauritania's monetary authority (Banque Centrale de Mauritanie), the anchor of the country's financial-market
infrastructure modernisation. Under **[[pamif|PAMIF]]** (AfDB-financed) the BCM deployed real-time gross
settlement for large-value transfers, **télécompensation** (remote cheque clearing), and an automated
refinancing facility — helping more than double financial inclusion (**21% in 2019–20 → 55% in 2026**) and
bring inflation from double digits to below 2%. It also operates an **AI-supported "digital reputation
protection" platform** (via **[[pafiid|PAFIID]]**) that detected **10,000+ cyber threats** to the digital
financial system in H1 2026 (470 confirmed and removed).
