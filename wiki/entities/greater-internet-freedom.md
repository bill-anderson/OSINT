# Greater Internet Freedom (Internews)

A **digital-rights programme run by Internews**, publishing country research on digital
rights, biometrics and digital identity. A recurring OSINT source for the Central African
Republic's data-sovereignty and identity story.

## Activity in this wiki

- **CAR digital-ID country report (2023).** Commissioned "Rebuilding Identification and
  Stakeholder Engagement in the Central African Republic" (author Dr. Dércio Tsandzana) —
  documenting the 2013 destruction of 60–80% of ID documents, the opaque **[[al-madina]]**
  contracting, and foreign-control perceptions. See [[caf--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2023-06-27 Country Report - Rebuilding Identification and Stakeholder Engagement in the Central African Republic]].
- **CAR AI / digital-space baseline (2022).** Published the digital-rights baseline noting
  CAR's absent national data centre, ARCEP, and 2020 France/Russia-targeting disinformation
  campaigns. Source:
  [[2022-10-21 La Republique centrafricaine et l'intelligence artificielle dans l'espace numerique]].
