# Lesotho–India DPI MoU (India Stack)

An MoU (July 2025) between Lesotho's **[[micsti-lesotho|MICSTI]]** and India's Ministry of Electronics &
Information Technology to build **Digital Public Infrastructure** drawing on **[[india-stack|India Stack]]**
— signed during a visit by India's MoS External Affairs, Shri Pabitra Margherita. See [[dpi.id]],
[[geopol.india]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Three DPI priorities (2025-07).** [[dpi.id]] [[dpi.pay]] PS **Kanono Ramashamole** set the year's
  priorities: **(1) digital ID** ("the foundation of everything"; pilots already underway with India);
  **(2) a unified payment environment** modelled on India's QR-code payments (for vendors/informal
  businesses); and **(3) once-only cross-ministry information exchange**. Framed on India's "scalable,
  inclusive, secure" model and 50+ years of India–Lesotho ties. See [[lesotho--tech-ai]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-29-lesotho-india-dpi-mou-publiceye]].
