# Fintech Festival Tanzania

A recurring **fintech / insurtech / digital-identity conference** for East Africa, convened
by the Fintech Society of Tanzania. The **2025 edition** ran **12–13 June 2025 in Dar es
Salaam** on themes of innovation, inclusion, governance and cybersecurity.
