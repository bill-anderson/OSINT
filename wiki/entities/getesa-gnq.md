# GETESA (Equatorial Guinea state telecom operator)

Equatorial Guinea's incumbent **state telecom operator**. In 2026 a new DG launched a **modernisation plan**:
**reactivating fibre** (previously used only by the power utility SEGESA), **introducing 5G**, **public Wi-Fi**
in Malabo/Bata/Oyala, **eSIM**, reactivating **roaming**, and a **Getesa Money** e-payment platform, with
government pushing tariff cuts. A **[[huawei|Huawei]]** contract (signed **9 July 2026**, six months) is to
modernise the deteriorated network — though the independent press flagged that the **value, financing and
selection procedure were not disclosed**, and that GETESA's role vis-à-vis new operator **[[telgesa-gnq|Telgesa]]**
and MUNI is unclear (`geopol.china`). See [[gnq--infra-connect]].
