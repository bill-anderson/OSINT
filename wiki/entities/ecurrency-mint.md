# eCurrency Mint

A **CBDC technology vendor** (CEO Jonathan Dharmapalan), supplier of the **DSC3** digital-bearer-instrument
platform. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **eAriary CBDC pilot partner (2025-10).** [[dpi.pay]] eCurrency's **DSC3** technology was selected for
  Madagascar's **[[e-ariary|eAriary]]** pilot (with **PayLogic SA**), to integrate into the existing
  financial ecosystem for **BFM** (Banky Foiben'i Madagasikara, the central bank) — a **post-quantum digital bearer
  instrument** where BFM remains sole issuer and private entities (banks, e-money, MFIs, fintechs) handle
  distribution. See [[madagascar--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-29-ecurrency-dsc3-eariary-cbdc-partner-prnewswire]] *(vendor PR-wire)*.
