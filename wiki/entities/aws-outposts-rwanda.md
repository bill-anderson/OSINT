# AWS Outposts Rwanda

The AWS Outposts deployment in Rwanda (announced 2026-04-17) brings hybrid cloud — local compute and storage tied back to AWS regions — for latency- and data-residency-sensitive workloads in healthcare, financial services and public administration. It matters as a concrete test of local data residency framed explicitly around data sovereignty.

## Profile
- Delivered by [[amazon-web-services]] with [[sand-technologies]], the Government of Rwanda and RISA (Rwanda Information Society Authority).
- First in the region and third on the African continent.
- Ecosystem partner: Norrsken East Africa (Kigali House).
- Framed around Rwanda's Vision 2050 and data sovereignty.
- Quotes from Jyoti Ball (AWS GM Sub-Saharan Africa), Fred Swaniker (Sand CEO) and Antoine Sebera (RISA CEO).
- AWS did not disclose deployment counts.
