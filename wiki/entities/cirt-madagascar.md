# CIRT Madagascar (Computer Incident Response Team)

Madagascar's **national computer incident-response team** (DG **Éric Rakotomaniraka**), under the Ministry
of Digital Development — driving the national cybersecurity strategy and the overhaul of the 2014 cyber
law. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[madagascar--infra-cybersec]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **National cybersecurity strategy (2025-12).** [[gov.policy]] [[sovereignty]] Launched via **PRODIGY**,
  a multisectoral strategy (child protection, GBV, justice, forensic cooperation) targeted for delivery in
  **three months**, with youth cyber-skills training and a donor conference. Source:
  [[2025-12-15-cybersecurite-strategie-nationale-midi]].
- **2014-law overhaul (2026-06/07).** [[gov.legislate]] At the **first Cybersecurity Symposium** (22–23
  Jun) the Ministry said the new framework — defining a **security incident** and criteria for **Critical
  Infrastructure Organisations (OIVs)** — would publish shortly; the **Council of Ministers adopted the
  cybercrime bill on 8 Jul 2026** (three axes: protect the vulnerable, end digital impunity via revised
  e-evidence/data-retention/asset-freeze tools, and mandatory operator cooperation with justice). Sources:
  [[2026-06-25-madagascar-new-cybersecurity-law-wearetech]],
  [[2026-07-09-cybercriminalite-projet-loi-adopte-agenceecofin]].
