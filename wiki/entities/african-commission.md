# African Commission on Human and Peoples’ Rights (ACHPR)

African Union human-rights body; its **Special Rapporteur on Freedom of Expression and Access to
Information in Africa** leads standard-setting on data access and digital rights.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Draft African Guidelines on Data Access (2026, consultation to 14 Jul 2026).** Under **Resolution
  620 (2024)**, the Commission circulated a draft soft-law instrument reframing data access as a
  right-to-information/right-to-development tool binding on public *and* private actors. Instrument:
  [[african-guidelines-data-access]]. See [[gov.regional]], [[data.open]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-07 African Guidelines on Data Access ACHPR Res 620 draft]].
