# Sonatel

Senegal's incumbent telecom operator and the dominant West African unit of the **[[orange]]** group
(also Mali, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Sierra Leone). Runs **Orange Money** (13m active users, 2025) and
the **Max it** super-app. **2025 group revenue 1,923.1bn CFA (+8.3%)**, net profit 413.6bn; mobile data
22.5m, fixed broadband 1.16m (+26%), **FTTH >640,000**. Owner of Senegal's **2Africa** landing station,
partner in the **[[via-africa-cable]]**, operator of the **Gandoul** teleport (Eutelsat OneWeb LEO
gateway), and host of **AWS Wavelength** in Dakar — central to Senegal's connectivity and onshore-cloud
build ([[senegal--infra-connect]], [[senegal--infra-store]]).
