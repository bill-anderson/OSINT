# Development Bank of Southern Africa

The Development Bank of Southern Africa (DBSA) is a national development finance institution. It matters here as the commissioner and data owner of South Africa's most granular digital-infrastructure investment analysis.

## Profile
- With the National Planning Commission, commissioned the South Africa Digital Infrastructure Investment Study (Sadis) 2025 and the accompanying national broadband map (built by Africa Analysis).
- The underlying data is DBSA-owned and not yet public.
