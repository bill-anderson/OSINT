# Ghana.gov

Ghana's **national digital-services / payments platform**. In 2025 Minister **Samuel Nartey (Sam) George**
announced a major revamp to move **~16,000 government services** onto Ghana.gov with **full Ghana Card
integration** (NITA re-engineering; upgraded system targeted for Q4 2025), backed by the **World Bank's $200m
[[ghana-digital-acceleration-project|Ghana Digital Acceleration Project]]**. A 2026 **shake-up** (Peduase
workshop with the World Bank, TBI and Public Digital, modelled on **GOV.UK / Irembo**) aims to make the portal
citizen-simple. See [[ghana--dpi-govtech]].
