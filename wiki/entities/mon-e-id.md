# Mon e-ID (Morocco national digital identity)

Morocco's **national digital-identity system** — the "Mon e-ID" mobile app and citizen portal built by
[[idakto]] on IDEMIA/IN Group ([[idemia]], [[in-groupe]]) biometric foundations, under contract to the
DGSN ([[dgsn-morocco]]) and ADD ([[add-morocco]]) ([[dpi.id]]). It uses a **hybrid model** (server-side
1:N for proofing, on-device biometrics for 1:1; the vendor never holds biometric data) and reached
**2.5m active users / 7m+ transactions** since April 2022. It is the authentication layer for the
Idarati X.0 e-government wallet ([[idarati-x0]]). See [[MAR]], [[morocco--dpi-id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Fast-tracked after cyberattacks (2025-09).** Adopted by ministries (Justice, Habitat, Youth), CNSS,
  DGI, TGR; banks/telecoms following. Source:
  [[2025-09-24 Morocco fast-tracks 'Mon e-ID' digital identity after cyberattacks (iDAKTO)]].
- **National digital wallet (2026-02).** iDakto + ShareID engaged for the wallet architecture within
  Idarati X.0. Source: [[2026-02-13 Morocco signs agreements with iDakto and ShareID for national digital identity wallet]].
