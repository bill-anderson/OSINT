# ANSSI (Congo-Brazzaville national cybersecurity agency)

Congo-Brazzaville's **national cybersecurity agency** — placed under the **Conseil national de sécurité**
(not the ICT ministry) — an institution the wiki previously lacked.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Budget & mandate.** A **2025 budget line of ~$1.3m (FCFA 800m)**; Congo scored **ITU GCI Tier 4 (2024)**.
  Sources: [[2025-04-04-republic-of-congo-invests-1-3m-cybersecurity-anssi-telecomreviewafrica]].
- **ARPCE–ANSSI MoU (25 Apr 2025)** — dual-agency cybersecurity collaboration. Source:
  [[2025-04-29-congo-arpce-anssi-cybersecurity-pact-wearetech]].
- **13th Regional Cyberdrill (Kintélé, 1–4 Jul 2025).** Congo hosted the ITU/Interpol regional Cyberdrill,
  opened by the PM. Sources: [[2025-07-01-cyberdrill-regional-2025-brazzaville-ldb]],
  [[2025-06-25-congo-to-host-regional-cyberdrill-techafricanews]].
- **First Central-African cybersecurity conference (Jun 2026).** An ANSSI × **Osiane** accord (30 Apr 2026)
  set the first regional conference of Central African cyber agencies (2–5 Jun 2026, Brazzaville). Source:
  [[2026-05-02-brazzaville-premiere-conference-cybersecurite-afrique-centrale-ldb]].
