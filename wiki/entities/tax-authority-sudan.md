# Tax Authority (Sudan)

Sudan's tax administration (ديوان الضرائب, Diwan al-Daraib), one of the state's
principal revenue units, pursuing tax computerisation led by an electronic-
invoicing project.

## Activity in this wiki

- **E-invoicing restored (2026-05).** The Secretary-General reported that tax
  computerisation — headed by the **electronic-invoicing** project, which had
  advanced substantially before the April-2023 war — has been **restored and is
  resuming at pace**, for revenue growth and transparency. PM **Kamil Idris**
  affirmed full support. A post-war digital-government system restoration
  overseen by the **[[ministry-of-finance-sudan]]**. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[dpi.pay]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-24 PM backs Tax Authority; e-invoicing project restored after war (SUNA)]].
