# TogoTech

A national association of Togolese technology startups (launched Oct 2025; president Gaëlle Matina
Egbidi), formed to "speak with one voice" and bridge startups, public institutions and investors. Members
include **[[gozem]]**, **[[semoa]]**, Édolé, MiaPay, Clinicaa and others; claims CFA2bn+ cumulative
revenue and ~100 direct jobs. See [[togo--tech-ai]], [[TGO]].
