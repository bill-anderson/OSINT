# Togo Anti-Money Laundering Law (2025)

**Togo's anti-money-laundering statute, adopted by the National Assembly during the budget
session (reported 2025-12-30).** It targets money laundering, terrorist financing and the
proliferation of weapons of mass destruction, and **replaces a 2018 statute**. See [[TGO]],
[[gov.legislate]].

## What it does

- **Aligns to two external standard-sets.** It brings national legislation into line with
  **updated [[fatf]] recommendations** and a **2023 [[waemu]]/UEMOA directive** — a national law
  whose content is substantially set by regional and global standard-setters. See
  [[gov.standards]], [[gov.regional]].
- **Introduces a risk-based approach.**
- **Extends due-diligence obligations to virtual asset service providers** — bringing crypto
  intermediaries inside the identification perimeter. See [[dpi.id]].
- **Strengthens identification requirements related to beneficial ownership** — the
  registry-facing core of the reform. See [[dpi.registry]].
- **Includes penalties**, as part of what the government presents as a harmonised framework.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Framing (2025-12).** Finance and Budget Minister **Essowe Georges Barcola** said the reform
  forms part of a national strategy to safeguard the integrity of the financial system, and that
  the harmonised framework, with its penalties, would help **reduce the opacity of financial
  flows often linked to organised crime**. The government said the reform should ultimately
  improve **transparency at a time of heightened regional security threats**. The measure passed
  as parliament prepared to close its budget session, having already approved a supplementary
  2025 budget and the 2026 finance law. See [[gov.policy]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-30 Togo Adopts New Law to Strengthen Anti-Money Laundering Rules]].

**Coverage note.** The single account held here reports **adoption by the National Assembly**;
it gives **no law number, promulgation date or entry-into-force date**, and no text of the
provisions beyond the summary above.
