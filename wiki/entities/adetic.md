# ADETIC (Agence de Développement des Technologies de l'Information et de la Communication)

**Chad's ICT development agency** — rendered in English as the *Agency for Information and
Communication Technologies Development* and, more tersely, the *Agency for ICT Development*.
ADETIC is the operational arm behind [[TCD]]'s state digital infrastructure: it co-signs and
co-runs the security audit of the [[chad-national-data-centre]], speaks for the government on
the 100 planned micro-data centres, and fronts Chad's bilateral and regional digital
cooperation. Director General: **Adoum Djimet Saboun**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National Data Center audit (2026-02-05).** ADETIC signed a tripartite agreement with
  [[ansice]] and [[techso-group]] for a security audit and certification of the National Data
  Center — see [[adetic-ansice-techso-audit-agreement]]. Saboun framed the step as *"the final
  milestone before the inauguration of the Data Center,"* a project he attributed to President
  Mahamat Idriss Déby Itno, and described the facility as *"a strategic base for sovereign
  housing of state data and continuity of public digital services."* See [[infra.cybersec]],
  [[infra.store]], [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-06 ADETIC, ANSICE, and TECHSO-GROUP Join Forces to Protect Chad's Digital Infrastructure]].
- **Micro-data centre architecture.** ADETIC stated that 100 micro-data centres deployed across
  government institutions and interconnected with the National Data Center *"will establish a
  unified, secure and high-performance digital architecture to support the dematerialization of
  administrative procedures and improve public service quality."* See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[dpi.exchange]]. Source: [[2026-02-06 Chad Moves to Secure National Data Center Ahead of Launch]].
- **Azerbaijan partnership talks (Nov 2025).** At the World Telecommunication Development
  Conference (17–28 Nov 2025), Saboun led discussions with Azerbaijan on three priorities — a
  modern national data centre, digital-infrastructure upgrades, and e-government services —
  saying Chad aims to draw on Azerbaijani technical expertise to strengthen local skills. The two
  sides agreed to formalise the partnership by MoU, with **no timeline announced** as of
  2025-12-02. See [[gov.policy]], [[finance.mou]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].
- **CADNAC founding (25–26 Nov 2025).** ADETIC represented Chad at the drafting meeting for the
  foundational texts of [[cadnac]], and described the planned platform as a vehicle to pool
  regional expertise and support **regional digital sovereignty**. See [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].
