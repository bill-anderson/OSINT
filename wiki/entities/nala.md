# NALA

**NALA** is a Tanzanian-founded fintech scaling from a consumer remittance app into **cross-border payment
infrastructure** (a "product model to network model" shift), including **stablecoin-linked corridors**.

- **$50m debt facility (May 2026).** Secured a **$25m credit facility, expandable to $50m**, from
  **Liquidity** (backed by Japan's **MUFG** via Mars Growth Capital) — debt rather than equity — for
  "reliable and scalable payment infrastructure across international remittance corridors." Cited as
  evidence investors now view African fintechs as **payment-systems builders**, not consumer apps. See
  [[finance.new]], [[tanzania--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-05-29-nala-raises-50m-payment-rails]].
