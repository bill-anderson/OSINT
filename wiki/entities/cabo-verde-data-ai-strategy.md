# Cabo Verde National Data & AI Strategies

Paired instruments kicked off **28 Apr 2026 (EU-supported)**: a **Data Strategy for Public Administration**
and a **national AI Strategy** — framed around **interoperability / de-siloing** to modernise public
administration. Source: [[2026-05-04-national-data-ai-strategies-techafricanews]].
