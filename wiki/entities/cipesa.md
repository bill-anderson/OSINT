# CIPESA

Collaboration on International ICT Policy for East and Southern Africa — a Kampala-based digital-policy
and rights think tank; runs public-dialogue and research programmes on DPI and data governance.

## Activity in this wiki

- **App-based health-data governance brief (2026-06).** Flagged the absence of health-specific AI
  regulation, fragmented frameworks and weak consent; proposed seven governance principles aligned with
  the Africa CDC Health Data Governance Framework and WHO Digital Health Strategy. See [[gov.protect]],
  [[dpi.mis]], [[africa-cdc]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-23 Towards Regulation of App-Based Health Data in Africa]].
