# INIC (Instituto de Inovação e Conhecimento)

São Tomé and Príncipe's institute for innovation and knowledge (est. 2008), a strategic body for digital
skills and public-administration modernisation. Co-leads (with DGRN) the WB-financed foundational digital
ID and launched the single **Portal do Cidadão** (beta, Mar 2026). See [[sao-tome--dpi-govtech]],
[[sao-tome--dpi-id]], [[STP]].
