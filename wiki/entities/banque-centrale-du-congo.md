# BCC (Banque Centrale du Congo)

DR Congo's central bank (Governor **André Wameso**) — the policy anchor of the country's digital-payments
story, pursuing financial inclusion *and* **de-dollarisation** at once.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Inclusion push + de-dollarisation deadline (Apr 2026).** **58% financial access vs 25–30% bank-account
  penetration**; policy rate cut **15% → 13.5%**; and a hard rule — **from 9 Apr 2027, no foreign-cash
  transactions at all (scriptural only)** — a direct tension with the USD-denominated and stablecoin rails
  spreading in the market. Source: [[2026-05-04-bcc-inclusion-financiere-58-pourcent-actualite]].
- **Visa × BCC MoU (Apr 2026)** tied to the **BCC 2026–2030 Strategic Plan** (Wameso / Aminata Kane) —
  material context for the de-dollarisation-vs-stablecoin tension. Source:
  [[2026-04-16-visa-bcc-agreement-financial-inclusion-payments-techafricanews]].
