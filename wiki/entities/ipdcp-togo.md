# IPDCP (Instance de Protection des Données à Caractère Personnel, Togo)

**Togo's personal data protection authority.** The IPDCP was created as part of the legal
scaffolding built around the [[e-id-togo]] foundational identity programme — a package the
World Bank's WURI implementation report dates to a **data protection law of September 2019**,
a **foundational unique identification law of September 2020** (creating [[anid-togo]]) and
**June 2022** cybersecurity/cybercrime amendments (creating **ANCy**). See [[TGO]],
[[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Institutional cooperation accord with the HAAC (2025-12-10).** The IPDCP and Togo's
  broadcast regulator **[[haac-togo]]** signed a memorandum framing media regulation and data
  protection as a joint problem — the two bodies arguing it is urgent to protect personal data
  "in an audiovisual landscape where information circulates faster than vigilance." The HAAC
  undertakes to build data protection into its procedures, monitoring and awareness work; the
  IPDCP supplies expertise. The operative mechanism: **the HAAC authorises the IPDCP to access
  its control and monitoring room** (*salle de contrôle et de monitoring*), enabling tracking of
  broadcast content and coordinated reaction to a personal-data breach. Deal:
  [[haac-ipdcp-accord]]. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[finance.mou]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-12 Personal data and digital rights in Togo - HAAC and IPDCP seal institutional cooperation accord]].
- **Statutory origin (WURI ISR, 2023-12-21).** The IPDCP's creation is recorded in the World
  Bank's account of Togo's identification-law package, alongside a **February 2022** decree
  establishing e-ID project governance bodies. Source:
  [[2023-12-21 ISR - WURI Phase 2 Seq 7 (P169594)]].

**Provenance note.** The December-2025 accord is reported by Africa Cybersecurity Mag
**sourced to the IPDCP itself**. The account carries **no critical framing** of a data
protection authority obtaining access to a broadcast-monitoring room; the absence is recorded
here rather than filled in.
