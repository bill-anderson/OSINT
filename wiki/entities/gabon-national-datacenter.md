# Gabon National (Nkok) Data Centre

Gabon's **first national / sovereign data centre** — a **Tier III** facility built by **[[st-digital-gabon|ST
Digital]]** in the **Nkok Special Economic Zone** (Libreville), inaugurated by President **Brice Clotaire Oligui
Nguema** on **3 July 2026**. The state's answer to the fact that **~95% of Gabon's data is hosted abroad**.

- **Figures:** **8bn CFA (~$14m)** investment; **92 racks**, **3,000 m²**, **1 MW** IT load.
- **Technical spec:** dual **15 kV** feeds with **N+1** (150/100 kVA) redundancy, **~22% photovoltaic**,
  **65 m² colocation**, and peering to **ACE + SAT-3/WACS** subsea cables.
- **Trajectory:** announced **Feb 2025** (ST Digital, eco-responsible design) → **groundbreaking Aug 2025** →
  launch date set for **June 2026** → inaugurated **3 Jul 2026**. Framed for **data localisation** and reviewed
  with the **[[apdpvp-gabon|APDPVP]]** for compliance (Min. Économie numérique **Mark-Alexandre Doumba**; ST
  Digital DG **Laïka Mba**). See [[gabon--infra-store]].
