# Paul Kagame

President of Rwanda; long associated with the country's digital-transformation branding.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Co-chair, [[ai-for-good-global-commission]] (2026).** Co-chairs the ITU commission
  (with Marc Benioff) "aimed at promoting inclusive governance, expanding access and
  narrowing the digital divide" (ITWeb Africa, 2026-07-03). A visible African seat in
  global AI governance. See [[RWA]], [[tech.ai]].

> Note: the ITWeb clipping captured only the headline/lede; substance drawn from the
> commission's own profile ([[2026-05-21 AI for Good Global Commission]]).

- **Anthropic–Rwanda MoU context (2026-02).** Rwanda's three-year MoU with [[anthropic]]
  ([[anthropic-rwanda-mou]]) — embedding Claude across health, public sector and education —
  is read as part of the deliberate, first-mover technology-adoption strategy associated with
  Kagame's government; analysts warn the arrangement creates an accountability vacuum absent
  external oversight. See [[RWA]], [[tech.ai]], [[paula-ingabire]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-17 Anthropic is Becoming the Backbone of Rwanda's Government. But Who is Accountable]].
