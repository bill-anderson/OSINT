# AngoREN (National Research and Education Network)

Angola's **National Network for Education and Research** — the country's NREN — presented by the
**Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Technology and Innovation (MESCTI)** on **13 May 2026** in
Benguela, alongside an **Executive Roadmap for the Digital Transformation of Higher Education**.

- Part of the **Higher Education, Science and Technology Development Project (TEST)** (World
  Bank-adjacent), aimed at connecting universities and research institutions and integrating Angola
  into the **global research-and-education network** ecosystem.
- Complements the satellite-led university connectivity under **[[conecta-angola]]** with a dedicated
  academic backbone.

See [[infra.connect]], [[capacity.research]], [[AGO]], [[angola--infra-connect]].
