# Ministry of Digital Transformation and Communications (Sudan)

Sudan's lead ministry for digital transformation, telecommunications and e-government (rendered in
sources as the **Ministry of Telecommunications and Digital Transformation** and the **Ministry of
Digital Transformation and Communications**). Owner of the **Baladna** e-government programme and
the **[[sudapass]]** unified national digital ID.

## Key facts

- **Nov 2025 institutional build-out.** Under a decree by PM **Kamil Idris** (5 Nov 2025), three new
  national agencies were established under the ministry: the **[[digital-transformation-authority-sudan]]**,
  the **[[sudanese-data-and-ai-authority]]** and the **[[sudanese-cybersecurity-authority]]**.
- **Minister Ahmed Dirdiri Ghandour** presented Sudan's digital-transformation pillars at the 29th
  session of the Arab Telecommunications and Information Ministers Council (early 2026).

## Activity in this wiki (2026, the Port-Sudan digital-state build)

- **SUDAPASS launched (2026-05-21).** Formally launched the **[[sudapass]]**
  national digital ID via the **[[sudanese-cybersecurity-authority]]**, the
  **[[national-electronic-certification-authority-sudan]]** and the
  **[[civil-registry-sudan]]**. See [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-21 Sudan launches national digital identity SUDAPASS (SUNA)]].
- **Health-sector digital strategy with WHO (2026-05).** Met a
  **[[ministry-of-health-sudan]]** + **[[world-health-organization]]** delegation
  to set a health-digital-transformation strategy and the
  **[[afia-platform-sudan|Afia]]** platform. Source:
  [[2026-05-20 Comms Ministry and WHO discuss health-sector digital transformation; Afia platform (SUNA)]].
- **Unified social registry + civil-service HR system (2026-04).** Reviewed the
  **[[unified-social-registry-sudan]]** and a new civil-service system with the
  **[[ministry-human-resources-social-welfare-sudan]]**. Source:
  [[2026-04-19 Ministerial meeting on digital transformation, civil-service reform and unified social registry (SUNA)]].
- **USSD banking (2026-03).** Launched offline USSD banking with the
  **[[central-bank-of-sudan]]** and the
  **[[communications-postal-regulatory-authority-sudan]]** as a wartime
  resilience/inclusion rail. Source:
  [[2026-03-18 Sudan Launches USSD Banking Services to Expand Digital Financial Access]].
- **Business digital transformation (2026-01).** Minister Ghandour praised
  operators **[[sudani-telecom]]** and **[[sudatel]]** at a Port-Sudan
  business-digital event. Source:
  [[2026-01-16 Minister praises Sudani-Sudatel role in business digital transformation (SUNA)]].

Operating in a conflict-fragmented state (no census since 2008; fragmented registries) with limited
infrastructure and skills. Runs the **[[baladna-platform-sudan|Baladna]]** e-government platform.
See [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]].
