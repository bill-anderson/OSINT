# IGN FI (Institut Géographique National France Internationale)

French geomatics company (international arm of France's national geographic institute), a supplier of
cadastre, land-administration and geographic-information systems, with pan-African land-reform
experience (it cites projects in Uganda and Côte d'Ivoire).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gabon cadastre modernisation (24 Nov 2025).** IGN FI submitted a strategic proposal to Gabon's
  Ministry of Housing, Habitat, Urbanism and Cadastre for an **Integrated Urban Land Management System
  (SIG)** centralising land data to secure transactions (cut double sales and fraud), streamline
  parcel registration and land-title issuance, and support urban planning on a single platform.
  Engagement: [[gabon-ign-fi-cadastre-deal]]. Source:
  [[2025-11-24-gabon-cadastre-modernization-ign-fi]].

A French firm proposing to run a sovereign land register carries the [[colonialism]] reading common
to Francophone-Africa digital-infrastructure supply.
