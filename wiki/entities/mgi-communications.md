# MGI Communications AG

A Swiss-based international telecommunications company that manages South Sudan's
**international gateway services** and **national data centre** infrastructure,
specialising in integrated voice, data and infrastructure management for emerging
markets. In South Sudan it is working on digitising national revenue systems,
upgrading gateway technology and expanding digital inclusion to underserved
regions.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gateway/data-centre oversight (2026-01).** Partnered with the
  **[[national-communication-authority-south-sudan]]** in the newly constituted
  Gateway Services and Data Centre Oversight Committee, tasked with digitising
  South Sudan International Gateway (SSIGW) revenue and bringing gateway and
  national data-centre operations under sovereignty/security oversight. Source:
  [[2026-01-17 South Sudan NCA establishes gateway services and data centre oversight committee]].

_Capture is not endorsement. A foreign private operator holding both the national
gateway and national data centre is an analytically notable sovereignty
concentration; the wiki records the fact without endorsing the arrangement._
