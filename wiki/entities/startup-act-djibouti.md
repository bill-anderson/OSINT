# Djibouti Startup Act

A dedicated **startup law** — a "Startup" label, a **fund-of-funds** and a **Startup Visa** — **presented
9 Nov 2025** and later promulgated as **Loi n°179/AN/25** (Journal Officiel 08/01/2026). Positions Djibouti
alongside the wave of African startup acts. The **primary legal text (eJO / journalofficiel.dj) is a standing
acquisition gap** — coverage here rests on trade-press reporting. Sources:
[[2025-11-12-startup-act-advances-techafricanews]], [[2025-11-14-startup-act-djibouti-wearetech]].
