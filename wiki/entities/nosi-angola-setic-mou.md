# NOSi–SETIC FP Angola govtech MoU

A **memorandum of understanding** (2026) under which Cabo Verde's **[[nosi|NOSi]]** exports its govtech
stack to **Angola's SETIC FP** — another instance of NOSi as a **South-South GovTech exporter** (alongside
Equatorial Guinea, Mozambique, Burkina Faso). Source:
[[2026-03-06-nosi-setic-fp-angola-memorando-entendimento-expressodasilhas]].
