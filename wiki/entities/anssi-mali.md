# ANSSI — Mali

Mali's **national cybersecurity agency** (Agence nationale de la Sécurité des Systèmes
d'Information), **created by draft texts adopted at the Council of Ministers of 10 June 2026**
to implement the country's **National Cybersecurity Strategy 2026–2030** and its Action Plan.
Its mission: ensure national cybersecurity, protect information systems and electronic
communications, coordinate cyberattack response, and secure critical infrastructure — framed
as a step in Mali's **digital sovereignty**.

The agency is the **institutional capstone** of a cyber build-out that also includes the
December-2025 cybersecurity strategy, **[[agetic]]**'s secured government Intranet, and the
operational cybercrime criminal chamber (see [[MLI]] → [[infra.cybersec]]).

*Provenance note:* reported via Mali Actuel against the Council of Ministers communiqué; a
minor date nuance (strategy "taken note of" 5 Dec 2025 vs this report's "adopted February"
2026) is not a substantive conflict.
