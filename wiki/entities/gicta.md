# GICTA (Gambia Information & Communication Technology Agency)

The Gambia's ICT agency, established in the early 2020s under the Ministry of Communications and Digital
Economy to drive e-government and the delivery of the country's digital-transformation agenda
([[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]]). It participated in validating the National Digital ID and Digital
Transformation Strategy 2023-2028. See [[GMB]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Stakeholder in the digital-ID/transformation strategy (2024-05).** GICTA (described as newly
  established) took part in the strategy validation workshop. Source:
  [[2024-05-17 Validation of The Gambia's National Digital ID and Digital Transformation Strategy]].
