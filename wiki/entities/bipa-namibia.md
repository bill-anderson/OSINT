# Business and Intellectual Property Authority (BIPA)

Namibia's statutory **registrar of businesses and intellectual property** — the authority for company,
close-corporation, trademark, patent and design registration and administration. As a functional registry it
is part of Namibia's digital-government and business-registration infrastructure, complementary to the civil
and identity registers held by the **[[ministry-home-affairs-namibia|Ministry of Home Affairs]]**.
