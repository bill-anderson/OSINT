# CEET (Togo's national electricity utility)

**Togo's electricity utility.** The sources held here render the body only by its acronym,
**CEET**, and it is left unexpanded rather than guessed at. Its relevance to this wiki is
twofold: it is a state utility under **ongoing funding challenges** (as reported 2025-05), and
it is among the first service providers whose administrative procedures moved onto the national
public-services portal. See [[TGO]], [[infra.energy]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Tariff rise to shore up utility finances (effective 2025-05-05).** The government
  increased electricity prices by an **average of 12.5%**, effective **5 May 2025**, via an
  interministerial order dated **24 March 2025** — the stated aim being to strengthen CEET's
  financial stability amid ongoing funding challenges. **The detailed tariff schedule stays in
  the source page** and is deliberately not promoted here: it is a price list without a data- or
  digital-governance payload. Source:
  [[2025-05-16 Togo Increases Electricity Prices to Boost Utility's Finances]].
- **BOAD emergency facility (reported 2025-05-16).** CEET "recently secured **CFA40 billion**"
  from the West African Development Bank (**[[boad]]**) to handle energy emergencies. See
  [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-16 Togo Increases Electricity Prices to Boost Utility's Finances]].
- **29 procedures onto the national portal (2025-12).** The **Ministry of Energy** added **29
  administrative procedures** to the [[services-publics-togo]] national portal, covering services
  provided by **CEET, AT2ER and ARSE** — a utility's counter moving online as part of the
  government's public-service digitisation. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-17 Togo Expands Public Service Digitisation With Online Civil Registry Platform]].
