# Online company-creation platform (Djibouti)

Djibouti's **fully-online business-registration platform** (launched by PM Kamil Mohamed, Jan 2026) — part
of the **G2B one-stop** digital push under Vision 2035. The government cites **8,446 firms created 2017–24**
and a "2nd worldwide on investor protection" ranking (undated methodology, a government claim). Sources:
[[2026-01-27-djibouti-online-company-creation-platform-lanation]],
[[2025-12-18-g2b-platform-techafricanews]].
