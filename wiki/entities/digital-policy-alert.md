# Digital Policy Alert

An open-access, independent **regulatory-change tracker** for the global digital economy — data
protection, platform regulation, AI, competition and cross-border data flows — maintained with
per-change entries (each carrying a `digitalpolicyalert.org/change/<id>` reference). A standing OSINT
**resource** for this wiki: it is the tracker most frequently cited for African data-protection
legislative activity, and the source counted on for continental head-counts (laws enacted,
authorities operational).

Its annual **"Data Protection in Africa Roundup"** is produced in collaboration with
[[tech-hive-advisory]]; the 2025 edition
([[2026-01-11 The Year of the Teeth Data Protection in Africa Roundup, 2025, Projections for 2026]])
is the authority behind the **44 laws / 38 operational DPAs / 11 states yet to legislate** end-2025
figures used across [[gov.protect]].
