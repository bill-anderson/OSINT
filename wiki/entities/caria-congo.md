# CARIA (Congo)

Congo-Brazzaville's **AI-and-digital-training institution** — the "clé de voûte" of the PATN digital
programme and the most-cited entity the wiki lacked. Notable for being built with **both Chinese and
Russian** backing — a distinctive dual-patron sovereignty play.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Regulatory framework & training platform (2025).** CARIA's regulatory framework was **adopted in the
  Council of Ministers on 2 Jul 2025**; it launched a **free training platform** (~3,000 courses; 10,000-
  youth target). Sources: [[2025-07-07-cyberdrill-2025-closes-kintele-techafricanews]],
  [[2025-05-15-plateforme-caria-formation-jeunes-numerique-ldb]].
- **China (CAICT).** CARIA is built **with China's CAICT** and was showcased in **Xiamen** (2025-09);
  Sassou N'Guesso is a **FOCAC African co-chair** — direct China–AI–sovereignty material. Source:
  [[2025-09-18-congo-brics-xiamen-caria-ai-techafricanews]].
- **Russia (Sberbank debt-swap).** **Russia is funding CARIA phases 3–4 via a Sberbank debt-to-development
  swap** (plus a "School 21" programme and an HSE MoU; CARIA joins the AI Alliance) — a wholly new
  geopolitical vector. *(The taxonomy has no `geopol.russia` slug — this rides the open ISSUE-014.)* Source:
  [[2026-04-21-congo-russia-digital-ai-cooperation-caria-techafricanews]].
