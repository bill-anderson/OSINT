# DGSN (General Delegation for National Security)

Cameroon's national police / security body, the **issuer of the national ID card (CNI)**. Delegate-General: Martin Mbarga Nguele.

## Activity in this wiki

- **New biometric CNI system (2025).** DGSN runs the new-generation biometric CNI under a 15-year BOT contract with German firm **[[augentic]]** — online pre-enrolment from 17 Feb 2025, biometric capture from 24 Feb, 48-hour issuance promise; issuance began March 2025 after a presidential decree. Opposition MPs criticised its **cost and complexity** (long biometric-capture waits) amid the push for voter registration before the Oct 2025 presidential election. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]].
