# DGSN (General Delegation for National Security)

Cameroon's national police / security body, the **issuer of the national ID card (CNI)**. Delegate-General: Martin Mbarga Nguele.

## Activity in this wiki

- **New biometric CNI system (2025).** DGSN runs the new-generation biometric CNI under a 15-year BOT contract with German firm **[[augentic]]** — online pre-enrolment via **[[idcam]]** (www.idcam.cm) from 17 Feb 2025 for a **10,000 FCFA (~$16) fee**, biometric capture from 24 Feb; issuance began **3 Mar 2025** after Biya's decree N°2025/059 of 28 Feb 2025 (which also covers residence, refugee and disability cards). The **48-hour issuance promise became real** — first 48h-produced CNIs handed over at Yaoundé's 10th arrondissement (3 Mar 2025), citizens contrasting them with cards uncollected since 2020. Opposition MPs criticised the system's **cost and complexity** amid the push for voter registration before the Oct 2025 presidential election. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]]. Sources: [[2025-02-05 Cameroon's new-generation biometric national ID card system (Augentic) goes live]], [[2025-02-20-idcam-pre-enrolment-plateforme-carte-identite-wearetech-fr]], [[2025-03-04-lejour-first-48h-national-id-cards-issued]], [[2025-03-05-issuance-of-new-national-id-card-takes-off-biometricupdate]], [[2025-06-24 Cameroon lawmakers criticize cost, complications in new national ID system]].
- **Second production centre — Garoua (Aug 2025).** A second Augentic production centre opened in Garoua (Far North), after Douala (Mar 2025), producing **~2,000 cards/day** with 15 security features; adds IOM data collection and a CMR–UNHCR refugee biometric-ID track. Source: [[2025-08-07-new-biometric-id-card-center-garoua-northern-cameroon-biometricupdate]].
- **Sectoral ID verticals keyed to the CNI (2026).** The national CNI is becoming a spine for sector-specific IDs — a mandatory QR-coded **digital ID for Yaoundé taxi owners/drivers** (GIZ-supported, 3 Jul–5 Oct 2026) and an MTN-run **student digital ID** (Ministry of Secondary Education) — increasingly operated by private/municipal actors. Sources: [[2026-07-01-digital-id-system-taxi-owners-drivers-biometricupdate]], [[2026-06-11-student-digital-id-program-mtn-biometricupdate]].
