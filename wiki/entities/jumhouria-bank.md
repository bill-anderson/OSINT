# Jumhouria Bank

**Libya's largest state-owned bank**, a lead retail channel for the [[central-bank-of-libya|CBL]]'s
e-payments drive. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **First petrol-station e-payment (2026-01).** [[dpi.pay]] Jumhouria launched Libya's **first e-payment
  service at petrol stations** — the **"OnePay – Mini Pay"** platform via the **Masrafi Plus** app, with
  no commission — within the CBL's broader financial-inclusion/e-payment push. See [[libya--dpi-pay]].
  Source: [[2026-01-15-jumhuria-first-petrol-station-epayment-libyaherald]].
