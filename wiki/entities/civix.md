# CIVIX (Côte d'Ivoire Internet Exchange Point)

Côte d'Ivoire's **third national internet exchange point (IXP)** — a traffic-localisation asset central to
[[artci|ARTCI]]'s **local-peering / interconnection** push. Colocated in **[[raxio-group|Raxio]]**'s
Tier III Grand-Bassam facility (ARTCI–Raxio MoU, 2025). ARTCI ran peering forums (Aug 2025) and Peeringday
2026 to grow local interconnection and keep domestic traffic in-country. Sources:
