# Sibanye-Stillwater

JSE-listed mining company — subject of a **landmark PAIA enforcement notice**.

- **PAIA disclosure order (May 2026).** The **[[information-regulator-south-africa|Information Regulator]]**
  ordered Sibanye (s.77J(1)(a), PAIA) to grant a complainant access to its **2019–2023 Social & Labour Plan
  compliance reports** (MPRDA) for the Eastern/Western Platinum Mines — a significant precedent for **private
  bodies'** access-to-information obligations, with imprisonment among the consequences of non-compliance.
  See [[south-africa--gov-protect]]. Source: [[2026-06-16-paia-enforcement-mining-house-sibanye]].
