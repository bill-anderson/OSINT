# Instance Nationale de Protection des Données Personnelles (INPDP)

Tunisia's **national personal-data-protection authority**, established under the **2004 organic law
No. 2004-63** ([[organic-law-2004-63-tunisia]]). It is the regulator the reform debate turns on: the
sponsors of the 123-article data-protection bill cite the INPDP's **limited role** as the institutional gap
the new law must close (see [[tunisia--gov-protect]]).

- **Reform pending (Feb 2026).** The draft organic law before parliament would give the authority a
  strengthened mandate, a **Data Protection Officer** regime, mandatory declarations/authorisations for
  public-institution processing, and a **dedicated sanctions department** with heavier financial penalties.
  Source: [[2026-02-20-africanmanager-data-protection-law-parliament]].
