# Co-Develop

Global fund financing safe, inclusive digital public infrastructure in the Global South; co-funds DPI
deployments alongside the World Bank and technical advisers such as [[cdpi]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **São Tomé Citizen Portal (2026-04).** Co-funded (with World Bank digitisation financing) São Tomé
  and Príncipe’s Citizen Portal launch (27 Mar 2026), advised by [[cdpi]] — a growing Lusophone-Africa
  DPI footprint moving toward verifiable credentials. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[STP]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-07 São Tomé and Príncipe Launches Citizen Portal]].
