# TTCL (Tanzania Telecommunications Corporation)

Tanzania's **state-owned telecom operator** — custodian of much of the national backbone and, since a 2023
presidential steer, refocused on broadband. Managing Director (2026): **CPA Moremi Marwa**.

- **Kilimanjaro One submarine cable (Nov 2025).** TTCL unveiled **KO-SCS**, a **1,900 km** Dar es Salaam–
  **Mauritius** cable (branches to Unguja/Mafia, potential Madagascar), framed as making Tanzania "East
  Africa's true digital gateway"; construction from 2026, completion late 2027. See
  [[tanzania--infra-connect]]. Source: [[2025-11-12-tanzania-kilimanjaro-one-submarine-cable]].
- **1m fibre subscribers by 2028 (2026-05).** Targeting 280,000 new connections this FY (200,000 done),
  400,000 in 2026/27, ~300,000 in 2027/28. Market position (Mar 2026): **2.78% of mobile internet**; **3rd
  in fixed lines (63,843)** behind Airtel (213,010) and Vodacom (114,109). Source:
  [[2026-05-06-ttcl-targets-1-million-fiber-subscribers-2028]].
