# Ericsson

Swedish telecoms-equipment vendor. In the Algeria context, its study warned that national **5G deployment
could cost $3–8bn** (plus 20–35% to extend coverage beyond profitable urban areas) — a rural-gap caution on
the [[algeria-5g]] programme. See [[algeria--infra-connect]].

In **Angola**, Ericsson announced on **2025-03-06** that it had completed the modernisation of
[[unitel]]'s core network — dual-mode 5G Core, IMS and cloud-native infrastructure — and had run a
trial data call over 5G Standalone in a live environment; a partnership it dates back over 20 years.
See [[angola--infra-connect]]. Source:
[[2025-03-06-unitel-ericsson-angola-network-modernization]].
