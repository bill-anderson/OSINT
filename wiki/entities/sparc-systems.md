# Sparc Systems Limited

A Malawian ICT company (Group MD **Wisely Phiri**) working across **Malawi, Zambia, Tanzania, Mozambique and
Rwanda** in enterprise infrastructure, cloud, cybersecurity, software, data-centre services and ICT training —
a face of Malawi's ICT-services export sector (exports **US$70m in 2020 → US$154m in 2023**).

- **Commercial data centre (Lilongwe, 2026-05).** Launched to provide **affordable local hosting** and cut
  reliance on offshore data (a **data-sovereignty / forex** argument) — see **[[malawi--infra-store]]**.
- **Home-grown platforms.** Comply Quest (AML/compliance), **Saris** (student academic records), **Farmers
  Payment Systems**, Sparc CRM/HR/Force, Procomos (project/contractor monitoring) — pitched for
  scaling across African markets.
