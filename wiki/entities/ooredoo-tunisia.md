# Ooredoo Tunisia

One of Tunisia's three mobile network operators (with **[[tunisie-telecom]]** and **[[orange-tunisia]]**),
part of the Qatar-headquartered Ooredoo group — the Gulf link in the Tunisian telecoms market.

- **5G launch (Feb 2025).** Launched 5G with an FWA offer ("Fix Jdid 5G", up to 100 Mbps) for homes/offices.
  CEO **Mansoor Rashid Al-Khater**: 5G is "the key to a more competitive digital economy." Licence from the
  Ministry of Communication Technologies, end-Nov 2024. Source:
  [[2025-02-17-developingtelecoms-three-operators-launch-5g]].
- **walletii fintech (Mar 2026).** Ooredoo Fintech won a CBT licence for the **walletii** wallet (with QNB
  Group and [[smt-tunisia|SMT]]) targeting the underbanked. See [[tunisia--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-07-menastartupdigest-ooredoo-walletii-license]].
