# National Identification and Registration Authority (NIRA), Somalia

Somalia's national identification and registration authority — the federal agency
responsible for foundational digital identity and civil registration.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National DPI Summit (2026-01-29).** Named a collaborating institution — with the
  **[[central-bank-of-somalia]]**, the National Communications Authority and the Data
  Protection Authority — for Somalia's **first National DPI Summit** (Mogadishu, 14–15 Feb
  2026), convened to build a cohesive national vision rooted in interoperability of
  digital systems. Organised by [[somalia-ministry-communications-technology]] and the
  Office of the PM. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-29 Mogadishu to Host First National Digital Public Infrastructure Summit; Key Agencies to Map Out Digital Transformation Strategy]].
