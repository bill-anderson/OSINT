# National Identification and Registration Authority (NIRA), Somalia

Somalia's national identification and registration authority — the federal agency
responsible for foundational digital identity and civil registration, issuing the
[[snid]] (Somali National ID) since September 2023. Its origins trace to **DADSOM** (Law
No. 41, Dec 2020), consolidated in **March 2023** as NIRA under the Identification and
Registration Act. Director General: **Abdiwali Ali Abdulle** (also rendered "Abdiwali
Timacadde"). Technology partner: Pakistan's **[[nadra]]**; principal financier: the World
Bank.

## Activity in this wiki

- **PM mandate (2025-04-07/08).** PM Hamza Abdi Barre directed all government and
  private-sector providers to integrate the national ID for verification, ahead of mass
  enrollment. See [[dpi.id]].
- **Use-case expansion (2025).** ID made mandatory for passport applications (1 Sep 2025),
  domestic travel (1 Jan 2026) and bank-account opening (CBS, 29 Jul 2025).
- **Mass registration drive (2025-09-16).** Foundational-ID mass-registration campaign
  launched on International Identification Day; pilot began 18 Aug 2025 in Shangani and
  Boondheer, targeting all 3.5m Mogadishu residents, then Banadir, then **15m nationwide by
  end-2029**. Backed by the World Bank ([[somalia-spring-project]] / SCALED-UP).
- **SPRING pillar (2025-12).** By late 2025, **>1m enrolled** and **>24 public services
  integrated**; NIRA named a key institution in the >$100m SPRING project.
- **NADRA partnership (2025-01 / 2026-01).** NIMS rolled out 2024 (maiden ID conference,
  Mogadishu, 16 Sep 2024); CRMVS civil-registration system handed over Nov 2024; Interior
  Minister + NIRA DG visited Islamabad (~20-21 Jan 2026) to deepen cooperation. The
  foreign-build / data-residency question drives a live sovereignty and data-security
  debate (lens: sovereignty; see [[somalia--dpi-id]]).
- **Platforms.** Operates HUBIYE (verification), [[eaqoonsi]] (mobile digital ID) and the
  Certificate Delivery System (CDS).
- **National DPI Summit (2026-01-29).** Named a collaborating institution — with the
  **[[central-bank-of-somalia]]**, the National Communications Authority and the Data
  Protection Authority — for Somalia's **first National DPI Summit** (Mogadishu, 14–15 Feb
  2026). Organised by [[somalia-ministry-communications-technology]] and the Office of the
  PM. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]].
