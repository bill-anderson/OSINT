# IIT Madras Zanzibar (IITMZ)

**IIT Madras Zanzibar** is the **first international campus** of India's IIT Madras — an India–Zanzibar
partnership anchoring the archipelago's tech-education and innovation-hub ambition. Managing Director:
**Prof Prabhu Rajagopal**; governing council chair **Prof Mohamed Sheikh**.

- **Second graduation (Jul 2026).** Graduated MSc cohorts in **Data Science & AI** and MTech in Ocean
  Structure. IITMZ reports **129 students** (~50% East African, ~30% women) and aims to spin out **100
  startups** from Zanzibar (of 565 across the IITM ecosystem). Zanzibar Minister **Mudrik Ramadhan Soraga**
  framed it within a bid to be **"a leading centre for technology and innovation in Africa."** See
  [[tanzania--dpi-govtech]]. Source: [[2026-07-05-zanzibar-positions-africas-technology-innovation-hub]].
