# Djibouti Digital Code

Djibouti's **first comprehensive Digital Code**, passed by the National Assembly on **30 June 2025** — developed over 2+ years by the Ministry of Digital Economy and Innovation and **drafted by Jones Day** (which also drafted Benin's 2017 Digital Code). Described as **eight volumes and nearly 800 articles**, it consolidates all digital-sector provisions into a single instrument.

- **Coverage:** data protection, cybersecurity/cybercrime, e-commerce, cryptology, digital consumption, innovative services.
- **Data-protection regime:** **156 articles**; establishes the **[[cndp-djibouti|National Commission for the Protection of Personal Data (CNDP)]]**; 72-hour breach notification; tiered prior formalities (prior CNDP authorisation for biometric/genetic ID data and processing involving the national identification number).
- **Cybersecurity:** provides for a cybersecurity body (see **[[national-cybersecurity-authority-djibouti]]**).

Foundational to **[[mobile-id-djibouti|Mobile ID]]** and the **[[smart-wallet-djibouti|Smart Wallet]]**. Sits under **[[vision-djibouti-2035]]**. Awaiting full promulgation and implementing decrees.
