# Mauritius Mobile ID

Mauritius's **mobile digital-identity wallet**, launched **4 September 2024** and built with
**[[thales|Thales]]** — presented as the **first African ISO-standard digital ID wallet** (an
ISO/IEC-18013-style mobile credential). It lets citizens carry a verifiable digital version of the national
identity card on a smartphone for online and in-person verification.

Its legal basis is the **National Identity Card (Mobile ID) Regulations 2024** (Government Notice No. 28 of
2024). A February 2025 analysis in *Le Mauricien* ("a balanced approach") welcomed the convenience but raised
**privacy and surveillance** concerns, urging safeguards. The digital-ID push runs alongside the country's
**eKYC / SIM re-registration** drive (ICTA SIM Regulations 2023).
