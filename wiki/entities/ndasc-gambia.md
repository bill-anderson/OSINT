# NDASC (National Digital Addressing Steering Committee)

Governance body inaugurated (Oct 2025) to steer The Gambia's **digital-addressing** rollout under the **National
Digital Addressing Policy 2023** — a foundational layer (addresses/geolocation) for public services, logistics
and DPI. See [[gambia--dpi-govtech]].
