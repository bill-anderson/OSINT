# Mauritel

Mauritania's incumbent/largest mobile operator (Moov Mauritel; part of the Maroc Telecom group), one of the
three long-standing operators with **[[mattel]]** and **[[chinguitel]]**. A **provisional 5G licence winner**
(April 2026), submitting the highest bid — **MRU 305m + 5% of annual 5G revenue**. Among the operators served
biometric-SIM enforcement notices by **[[are-mauritania|ARE]]**.
