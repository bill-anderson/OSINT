# Convalt Energy

A **US-based energy/infrastructure company** (CEO Hari Achuthan; board member and former US House Majority
Leader **Richard Gephardt**), evolving toward **integrated infrastructure for AI-driven, energy-intensive
industries**. Counterparty to Lesotho's **[[project-kobong|Project Kobong]]** — the country's largest-ever
proposed FDI. See [[infra.energy]], [[infra.store]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Project Kobong — $6.2bn hydropower + AI data centre (2026-06).** [[geopol.usa]] Convalt agreed a
  **~$6.2bn (98bn maloti)** deal with Lesotho's Ministry of Energy pairing **1.2 GW hydropower** (plus
  4.6 GW solar, up to 4 GWh BESS) with an **AI data centre** in **Mokhotlong** — powering AI workloads,
  cutting Lesotho's electricity-import dependence on South Africa, and positioning it as a regional power
  exporter. Signed after a **King Letsie III–Gephardt** meeting; associated US exports could exceed $2bn;
  construction from ~2029. The deal's **legal status is disputed** across outlets (binding MoA vs
  non-binding MoU) — see [[project-kobong]], [[lesotho--tech-ai]]. Sources:
  [[2026-06-09-convalt-kobong-hydropower-ai-datacenter-techafricanews]],
  [[2026-06-30-lesotho-first-ai-data-center-connectingafrica]].
