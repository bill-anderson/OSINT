# Vision Djibouti 2035

Djibouti's long-term national development plan, the framing under which the government
pursues its transformation into a **"Smart Nation."** Its digital leg centres on
leveraging Djibouti's position as a Red Sea submarine-cable and internet-transit hub,
expanding digital public services and building a national AI ecosystem.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mobile ID launched (2026-02-09).** The **[[mobile-id-djibouti|Mobile ID]]** national digital
  identity platform — a single citizen identifier for authentication, e-signature and service
  access — was launched by President Guelleh as "a cornerstone of Vision Djibouti 2035," built on
  the **[[djibouti-digital-code]]**, a sovereign national cloud and cybersecurity reforms. See
  [[dpi.id]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-10 Djibouti launches Mobile ID national digital identity platform]].
- **Digital Code adopted (2025-06-30).** Djibouti's first comprehensive **[[djibouti-digital-code|Digital Code]]**
  (data protection, cybersecurity, e-commerce) sits within the Vision 2035 road map. Source:
  [[2025-07-01 Djibouti Digital Code adopted by National Assembly]].
- **G2B one-stop platform (2025-12).** A government-to-business digital one-stop shop for MSMEs
  under Vision 2035. Source: [[2025-12-18 Djibouti begins one-stop G2B digital platform for businesses]].
- **First national AI strategy (2026).** Djibouti's first national AI strategy — launched
  Jan 2026 by the Ministry Delegate for the Digital Economy and Innovation with **ESCWA**
  and **UNESCO** support — forms part of Vision Djibouti 2035, aiming to strengthen AI
  skills and expand practical public-service applications. See [[tech.ai]], [[gov.policy]].
  Source: [[2026-01-29 Djibouti develops national AI strategy with UN support]].
- **Partnership push (2026-07).** At WSIS 2026 Djibouti sought **[[itu]]** and
  **[[smart-africa]]** support (plus Chinese technical support) to digitalise public
  services, build AI capacity, close the urban–rural digital divide, and deepen its role
  in regional digital governance (**[[eardip]]**, Djibouti Digital Foundation). Source:
  [[2026-07-13 Djibouti Seeks ITU, Smart Africa Support for Telecoms and AI Development]].
