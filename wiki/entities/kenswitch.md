# Kenswitch

A Kenyan **shared payments-switch operator** (established 2002 in Kenya's payments-modernisation drive),
connecting **30+ financial institutions** across thousands of ATMs, POS terminals and agent-banking
outlets. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Visa alliance for the national switch (2026-04).** [[finance.mou]] Kenswitch struck a **framework
  agreement with [[visa]]** to co-develop next-generation processing/settlement infrastructure and
  bid for a role in Kenya's planned **national switch / fast payment system (FPS)** — a contest also
  drawing in **[[pesalink]]** (Kenya Bankers Association / IPSL), Nigeria's **NIBSS** (with local firm
  Ceva) and telco/bank interests. The **[[central-bank-of-kenya|CBK]]** wants a real-time, interoperable
  network with **interoperability and domestic processing** central. See [[kenya--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-03-kenswitch-visa-national-switch-techcabal]].
