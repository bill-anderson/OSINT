# "7.77" National Digital Empowerment programme

A **nationwide digital-skills programme** (Ministry of Post & Telecommunications) — the name signals citizens
**aged 7 to 77**. By 2026-05 it had **64,508 enrollees across all 58 wilayas** (80.4% remote, 19.6% in-person);
**AI was the top training choice (30%, 19,691)**, tied with productivity tools, ahead of digital discovery,
professional cybersecurity and digital entrepreneurship. Ages 23–45 were 61.4% of registrants; Algiers led by
volume. Course languages: 58.7% Arabic, 20.9% French, 20.4% English. See [[algeria--tech-innovate]].
