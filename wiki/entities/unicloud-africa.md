# UniCloud Africa

Pan-African **sovereign-cloud provider** (founded 2023), headquartered in Nigeria, positioning itself
as "the first connected sovereign cloud platform" for data residency/in-country storage (pitching
NDPR/GDPR compliance). Operated in Nigeria, South Africa and Ghana, with projects going live in
Kenya, Uganda, DRC and Tanzania (as of 2024). Partners with hyperscalers on connectivity/scalability.
A domestic-sovereignty play in [[NGA]] [[infra.store]] ([[sovereignty]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **2024-07-12 — Makurdi data centre MoU (Benue).** UniCloud and the state-run
  [[benue-digital-infrastructure-company]] (BDIC) signed an MoU to build a data centre in Makurdi,
  Benue State, with UniCloud providing cloud infrastructure; [[africa-data-centres]] to build a
  complementary disaster-recovery site in Abuja. Project ~₦10.8bn ($6.6m), targeted for completion in
  six months; other partners Cedarview Communication (network) and SIT Consulting (software). Source:
  [[2024-07-12 UniCloud Africa and Benue Digital Infrastructure to build data center in Makurdi]].
