# ESTDEV–Smart Africa MoU

Memorandum of understanding between [[estdev]] (Estonia's development agency) and
[[smart-africa]], signed in Brussels and reported 2026-05-28.

- **Parties:** [[estdev]] and [[smart-africa]].
- **What:** Cooperation on interoperable and reusable [[dpi.exchange]],
  [[infra.cybersec]], and platform interoperability across Smart Africa's 42
  member states, combining Estonia's e-government experience with Smart Africa's
  continental programme architecture. Includes support for startups, women-led
  innovation and STEM education for girls, plus joint capacity-building.
- **Aims:** Cross-border integration, reduced vendor lock-in, reusable DPI
  components adoptable across countries rather than rebuilt per programme.
- **On the record:** ESTDEV director Andrea Kivi — "secure, interoperable,
  people-centred" systems; Smart Africa CEO Lacina Koné — a digital future built
  on "trust, innovation and digital sovereignty."
- **Status:** Signed (framework MoU; no monetary value disclosed).
