# Information Network Security Administration (INSA)

Ethiopia's national cybersecurity and information-security agency (INSA), a core
government body in the country's data-governance and cyber-resilience architecture.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National data-governance strategy (as of 2025-09-18).** INSA sits on the **technical
  committee** — alongside the **Ministry of Innovation and Technology (MiNT)**, the
  **[[ethiopian-ai-institute]]** and **Addis Ababa University** — developing Ethiopia's
  **first-ever national data-governance strategy** under the **Office of the Prime
  Minister**. The committee assessed the national data ecosystem (management, sharing,
  classification, access, and safety/security principles) and drafted a high-level
  framework, later expanded into a full draft with [[dial]], drawing on the AU Data Policy
  Framework. The work feeds the transition to [[digital-ethiopia-2030]]. See
  [[gov.protect]], [[gov.policy]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-18 Ethiopia's approach to their National Data Strategy offers lessons for Africa's digital future]].
