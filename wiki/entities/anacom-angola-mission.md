# ANACOM–INACOM regulatory cooperation mission (2026)

An institutional cooperation mission carried out **2026-03-16** in Luanda by Portugal's telecoms regulator **ANACOM** (Autoridade Nacional de Comunicações) — delegates Manuel Cabugueira (Director) and Manuel Costa Cabral — aimed at strengthening Angola's **regulation and development of the digital sector**.

## Terms

- Meetings with the **Angolan Institute of Communications (INACOM)**, the **Embassy of Portugal** and the **Delegation of the European Union to Angola** on strengthening regulatory capacity.
- Centred on the training initiative **"ICT Policy and Regulation – Institutional Strengthening,"** supporting the institutional and technical development of communications regulation.

## Reads

- `colonialism`: the **ex-metropole shaping the regulatory layer** — Portugal's regulator (backed by the EU Delegation) training the former colony's institutions in how to regulate its digital sector. Where Angola courts China/India/Gulf/US capital for infrastructure, the **rulemaking** layer is being socialised toward a European template. This is the European regulatory pole of the multi-pole contest read on [[AGO]], and belongs beside the Airbus/Société Générale (ANGEO-1) European industrial pole.
