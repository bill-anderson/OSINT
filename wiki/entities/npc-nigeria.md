# National Population Commission (NPC), Nigeria

Nigeria's statutory body for civil registration and vital statistics (births, deaths, marriages)
and population data. Operator of the **[[e-crvs-nigeria]]** electronic Civil Registration and
Vital Statistics System, moving registration off paper.

- **2025-11** — President **[[bola-tinubu]]** launched the **e-CRVS** platform managed by NPC,
  covering birth/stillbirth, attestation, adoption, marriage, divorce, migration and death.
- **2026-02-20** — **[[unicef]]** reported **14 million** children registered over two years,
  with the system being digitalised across health facilities; supportive legislation before the
  National Assembly. Birth registration underpins legal identity and, in the absence of a recent
  census, reliable population data.

See [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.id]], [[e-crvs-nigeria]].
