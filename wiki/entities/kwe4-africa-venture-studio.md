Venture studio backing African technology ventures. **[[codar]]**, the Nigerian digital-skills
training company, is described as a KWE4 portfolio venture.

*Thin by design.* The wiki holds KWE4 only through a single **sponsored TechCabal Partner /
Press Room placement** about its portfolio company, which states the relationship but gives no
fund size, vintage, other portfolio companies, geography or ownership. No independent account is
held. See [[finance.new]], [[tech.innovate]].
