# GABIX (Gabon Internet Exchange Point)

Gabon's **national Internet Exchange Point** — created in **2014** but little-used (only **two operators since
2019**). In May 2026 the Ministry of Digital Economy launched a **relaunch phase**, drawing new members back and
lifting **local traffic** — framed as improving connection quality, **cutting international-transit costs** and
strengthening **digital sovereignty / autonomy** through local peering. See [[GAB]], [[infra.connect]].
