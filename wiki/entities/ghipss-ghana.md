# GhIPSS (Ghana Interbank Payment and Settlement Systems)

A **[[bank-of-ghana|Bank of Ghana]]** subsidiary and the **central rail** of Ghana's payments system — operator
of the **national switch**, **GhIPSS Instant Pay (GIP)** and **mobile-money interoperability** (the backbone
behind Ghana's GH¢4.5tn (2025) MoMo market). At the **3i Africa Summit (2026-05)** it set out **cross-border
interoperability** plans — **ISO 20022** migration and **VASP** integration — positioning Ghana as a hub for
continental digital-financial integration. See [[ghana--dpi-pay]].
