# William Kabogo Gitau

Kenya's **Cabinet Secretary for Information, Communications & the Digital Economy** — the political head
of the ICT ministry driving eCitizen, digital-economy and data-governance policy. See [[KEN]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Governance-and-accountability pivot (2026-04).** [[gov.policy]] Kabogo framed Kenya's digital economy
  as now **"established"**, with the policy focus shifting to **governance, accountability, trust and
  cybersecurity** — signalling the maturation narrative around eCitizen and DPI. Source:
  [[2026-04-25-kabogo-digital-economy-governance-standard]] *(summary-only clip)*.
- **Data-protection enforcement.** [[gov.legislate]] Kabogo has publicly pressed enforcement — e.g.
  ordering **betting firms holding children's data** to register or face shutdown (see
  [[kenya--gov-protect]]).
