# Congo-Brazzaville National AI Strategy

The **national AI strategy** GSMA recommended — **now underway (May 2026)** with UNDP support: a technical
committee, 6-month horizon, **11 deliverables, FCFA 150–200m**, with a distinctive **national-languages
flagship — digitising lingala and kituba** against "extinction numérique." New ICT minister **Frédéric
Malik Nguema Nzé** appears. Source: [[2026-05-06-strategie-nationale-ia-langues-nationales-pnud-ldb]].

## The regulation-first run-up (2026)

Ahead of the strategy, ARPCE (DG Sakala: "technologies evolve faster than our capacity to regulate them")
ran **digital-regulation / AI-governance seminars** (Jan 2026, with an EU AI-committee expert and HEC Paris
advising) — the closing session of which stated **in passing two facts** the wiki lacked: **the National
Data Centre was inaugurated** and **5G launched in Brazzaville** (both asserted only here — primaries
needed; see the hub). Earlier, a **UNDP** AI/cyber youth-training partnership (Mar 2025) seeded the
UNDP–Congo relationship that now funds the strategy. Sources:
