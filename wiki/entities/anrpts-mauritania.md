# National Agency for the Population Register and Secure Titles (ANRPTS)

Mauritania's foundational-identity authority (Agence Nationale du Registre des Populations et des Titres
Sécurisés), sitting under the **Ministry of Interior**. The ANRPTS developed and manages the national
**biometric ID system** introduced in 2020, which had enrolled **more than 94% of the population by 2025**.
Its **Integrated System of Population Management and Secured Titles (SIGPTS)** integrates the government
structures that issue ID documents. Director (2025): Sidi Ali Nafee.

The ANRPTS biometric register is the identity layer beneath Mauritania's citizen-facing digital-ID and
e-government platform, **[[houwiyeti]]**.

**Register growth and the exclusion question (2026).** The Interior Minister told the National Assembly (June
2026) that the National Population Register rose **from 3.4m (2019) to over 5.1m today** (1.7m+ new
enrolments), with enrolment now a **permanent, continuously-available public service** (no longer time-limited
campaigns) under a **2025 legal reform** — 36,516 registered in under a year under the new mechanism, a survey
capturing 613,000+ citizens facing enrolment difficulties, and **238 local identification-and-statistics
commissions** installed since end-September. But named opposition deputies (Kadiata Malick Diallo, Almamy Ilo
Ba) pressed the government over **citizens left without identity papers** — a de-facto-statelessness charge
that, in the wider debate, falls disproportionately on Haratine and Afro-Mauritanian groups. The ~94%
aggregate-coverage claim and the contested residual sit in tension: high enrolment totals do not settle who
remains outside the register ([[sovereignty]], [[colonialism]]).

**A PKI layer is being procured over — not beside — this register.** [[mtnima-mauritania|MTNIMA]]'s
[[wardip|WARDIP]] tender AP-01_DP-15_WARDIP-2026 (**2026-04-28**, unawarded as of 2026-07-20) puts
Lot 02's e-signature **integration with the ANRPTS's own PKI** at the centre of the scope. Read
alongside its title's digital-ID framing, the clauses describe a **trust layer over the existing
ANRPTS register, not a second identity rail**. Source:
[[2026-04-28-wardip-mauritanie-ap01-identite-numerique-pki]].
