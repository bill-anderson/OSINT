# Eswatini National FinTech Strategy (2025–2030)

Eswatini's national fintech roadmap, launched Dec 2025 by the **[[central-bank-of-eswatini|CBE]]** (Gov.
Phil Mnisi) and Finance Minister Neal Rijkenberg. Aims to reduce cash reliance, expand MSME credit /
savings / insurance for underserved communities, create youth jobs, and position Eswatini as a **regional
fintech hub** by "leapfrogging" legacy systems. Targets **500,000 e-KYC users** and **80% payment
interoperability by 2028**. See [[eswatini--dpi-pay]], [[SWZ]].
