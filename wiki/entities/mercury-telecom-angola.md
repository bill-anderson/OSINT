# Mercury (Angola)

Angolan connectivity provider ("Mercury Beyond Connection"), CEO **Francisco Pinto Leite**, serving
enterprises, public institutions, telecom operators and the **offshore (oil-and-gas) sector**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Multi-year LEO agreement with [[eutelsat]] (2026-06-12).** Signed at ANGOTIC 2026 with Eutelsat's
  Africa VP Philippe Baudrier, deepening a long-standing relationship to deliver **low-Earth-orbit
  (OneWeb) connectivity** — secure, resilient, low-latency links nationwide, including remote areas
  and offshore clients. Eutelsat remains the **only licensed LEO operator in Angola**, with a local
  gateway and point of presence. See [[infra.connect]], [[data.satellite]], [[AGO]],
  [[angola--infra-connect]], [[eutelsat]].
