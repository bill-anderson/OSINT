# Yas (AXIAN Telecom operating brand)

Pan-African telecoms operator, the network-operating brand of **[[axian-telecom]]**
(part of the Madagascar-headquartered [[MDG]] AXIAN group; Group CEO **Hassan Jaber**).
Yas runs **mobile and fixed networks, fintech and digital infrastructure** (towers,
backbone, data centres) across **11 markets** in Africa and the Indian Ocean. In Kenya
it operates the fibre/broadband business built on AXIAN's **2025 acquisition of
Wananchi** (see [[axian-telecom]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **EBRD €270m network-expansion loan (2026-07).** Yas is the borrower in the
  **[[ebrd-yas-network-loan]]** — a senior facility of up to **€270m** from **[[ebrd]]**
  to expand **4G, 5G and fibre** in **[[SEN]] Senegal** (network modernisation + fibre
  rollout) and **[[KEN]] Kenya** (fibre expansion on the Wananchi base). Jaber called it
  the largest financing the group has raised, framed around closing Africa's mobile-coverage
  gap (~1 in 10 still excluded). See [[infra.connect]], [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 EBRD Extends €270m Financing to Yas for Kenya, Senegal Network Expansion]].
