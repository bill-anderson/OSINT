# National Institute of Statistics of Rwanda (NISR)

Rwanda’s national statistics office; hosts the **Data Governance Unit (DGU)**, the operational arm of
the [[rwanda-national-data-sharing-policy]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Data Governance Unit rollout (2026-05).** DGU (led by Maryse Gwiza, seconded from RISA) is building
  classification standards, cataloguing tools and a central data-sharing platform to move government
  from data *ownership* to *stewardship*. Success is measured by routine cross-institutional data use
  over 3–5 years. See [[dpi.exchange]], [[data.statistics]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-07 What will determine the success of Rwanda’s data governance push]].
