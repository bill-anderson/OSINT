# Avanti Communications

UK satellite-connectivity firm exploring a partnership with **[[camtel|CAMTEL]]** — one of the foreign
satellite/cloud approaches (alongside AWS) that sit in tension with the CMR sovereign-cloud line and the
[[artac|ARTAC]] data-residency position.

## Activity in this wiki

- **CAMTEL × Avanti satellite talks (8 Apr 2026).** Exploratory partnership to strengthen connectivity
  (rural/underserved coverage), with the **UK High Commissioner present** — the second foreign
  satellite/cloud approach in 2026 after the CAMTEL–AWS engagement, and squarely within the frame ARTAC
  wants regulated. Source: [[2026-04-09-camtel-avanti-satellite-partnership-techafricanews]].
