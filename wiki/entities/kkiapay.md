# Kkiapay

A **100% Beninese** payment-aggregation platform — a home-grown fintech letting merchants accept mobile
money, cards and bank transfers through one integration. PCI-DSS certified, with **13,000+ merchants**,
Kkiapay is a rare Beninese-owned rail in a market dominated by telco mobile money, and is expanding into
Côte d'Ivoire, Togo, Senegal and Niger. See [[BEN]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
[[2025-01-24-kkiapay-solution-beninoise-digitalisation-paiements]].
