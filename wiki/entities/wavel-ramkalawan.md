# Wavel Ramkalawan

**President of Seychelles** in the period this wiki's Seychelles material covers (2022-2025
appearances). He functions here as the **political sponsor of digital public services** — present
at the launch of the country's digital-ID platform and publicly committing his administration to
digitalising public services. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]], [[SYC]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **At the SeyID launch (2022-11-01).** Attended the official launch ceremony of the [[seyid]]
  national digital-identity platform, alongside Vice-President **Ahmed Afif** — the presidential
  imprimatur on the [[wisekey]]-built ID platform. See [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2024-09-18 Seychelles Makes Digital ID Mandatory for Work Permit Holders]].
- **Registrar General's Office visit (2025-02-07).** Visited the
  [[registrar-general-office-seychelles]], touring Business Registration and Intellectual
  Property and meeting senior management on achievements, challenges and modernisation. Speaking
  alongside Registrar General **Wendy Pierre**, he **praised the office's digitalization
  progress**, emphasised its role in service accessibility, and **announced upcoming legislative
  reforms** to support modernised operations. The visit was reported as reaffirming his
  administration's commitment to digitalising public services. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[gov.policy]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2025-02-10 Seychelles Pledges to Modernize Public Services Through Digital Transformation]].
- **Reading the record.** Both appearances are **ceremonial and declaratory** — a launch and a
  visit. No source held here attributes a specific policy instrument, budget, strategy document
  or delivered reform to Ramkalawan personally. Note also that the AfCFTA Digital Trade Corridor
  material (2026-07) refers to the **Herminie administration**, so the 2022-25 Ramkalawan framing
  should not be read forward as current: see [[SYC]].
