# Apolitical

Apolitical is a **global learning platform for government innovation** — an
organisation that delivers online training and professional development to public
servants worldwide.

## Why it matters here

Apolitical is the **delivery partner** for the AI-readiness training programme for
African public officials announced alongside the **[[au-google-mou|AUC–Google
MoU]]** (17 Feb 2026). Google's Regional Director Charles Murito named Apolitical
as the partner through which African officials would receive AI fundamentals,
ethical frameworks and leadership training.

## Sovereignty concern

Analyst [[alice-munyua]] ("The Gatekeepers Part XII," 2026-03-05) singles out
Apolitical as "the Apolitical vector" in her critique of the Google MoU, arguing
it is **embedded in Google's philanthropic ecosystem**:

- Apolitical is described as sitting within **Google.org's partner ecosystem**.
- In the **2026 Google.org Impact Challenge for AI in Government**, Apolitical is
  designated as **one of only two external partner review organisations**
  evaluating which nonprofits and institutions receive between **$1 million and
  $3 million** in Google.org grants plus Google Cloud Credits.

Munyua's argument is that officials trained via a Google.org-funded curriculum
will carry those frameworks into procurement, evaluation and regulation-writing —
"ecosystem conditioning" rather than neutral capacity building. This is a
named-analyst characterisation, captured here for context; Apolitical itself is
profiled as a standing govtech-training organisation.
