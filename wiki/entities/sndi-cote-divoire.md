# SNDI (Société Nationale de Développement Informatique)

Côte d'Ivoire's **national IT development company**, the implementer of the state's digital-government systems.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Government interoperability platform (UXP, 2025).** Commissioned by the Ministry of Digital Transition (MTND) to set up a secure data-exchange layer, SNDI signed with **[[cybernetica]]** for its **UXP** (Unified eXchange Platform, X-Road family) — a 20-month implementation with Digital Afrique Telecom (DAT), for real-time inter-ministry data exchange under the "zero paper 2030" agenda. See [[civ--dpi-govtech]]. Source: [[2025-09-29 Cote d'Ivoire unveils UXP interoperability platform for secure government data sharing]].
