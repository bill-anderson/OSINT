# La Poste de Côte d'Ivoire

Côte d'Ivoire's national postal operator. It operates a "phygital" model, combining a physical
network of more than 200 agencies with fully digital services, positioning its branch footprint
as a delivery channel for digital public administration.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Co-launched the [[maposte]] super-app (2026-01-16).** With [[oneci]], La Poste launched
  "MaPoste," an application linking digital identity (the NNI/RNPP) to postal and administrative
  services. La Poste supplies the phygital delivery layer — its 200+ agencies plus digital
  channels handle ID-card delivery and the request and tracking of civil-status, nationality and
  criminal-record certificates. Tags [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-19 Ivory Coast Launches MaPoste to Digitize Postal and Administrative Services]].
