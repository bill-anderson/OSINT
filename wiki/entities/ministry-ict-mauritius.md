# Ministry of Information Technology, Communication and Innovation (MITCI)

Mauritius's lead ministry for the digital agenda, headed by **Minister Avinash Ramtohul**. MITCI is the owner of
the country's flagship digital instruments and coordinates delivery across government:

- the **[[mauritius-digital-blueprint-2025-2029|Digital Transformation Blueprint 2025-2029]]** (launched May 2025);
- the **[[mauritius-national-data-strategy|National Data Strategy 2025-2029]]** (with the
  [[data-protection-office-mauritius|DPO]], Jan 2026);
- the **National AI Strategy** and FAIR guidelines (Apr 2026);
- the 2025 wave of **new digital regulations** and the inter-ministerial coordination committee.

Earlier (Apr 2025), Minister Ramtohul signalled the reform runway — flagging a **slip in Mauritius's UN EGDI
ranking**, dispersed personal-data holdings, and a lagging legal framework, and calling for a **citizen "data
hub"** ("which should already have existed"). Source:
[[2025-04-09-ict-data-protection-strategy-in-preparation-lexpress]].
