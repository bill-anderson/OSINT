# Verint Systems

Israeli-origin surveillance and communications-interception vendor. In **Mauritius**, a Verint mass-interception
system was procured by the then MSM-led government in **2017**, with a further batch in **2022**, and deployed
across major telecom operators — branded locally **"PertSol"** — feeding the **Counter Terrorism Unit** and
**National Security Services** with access to calls, internet traffic and social/messaging platforms **without
judicial oversight**. Parliamentary revelations in 2025 (confirmed by PM Ramgoolam) tied the system to illegal
wiretapping and a reported post-2019-election data wipe — see the **[[mauritius--gov-protect]]** surveillance
saga.
