# École 42 Djibouti

A **peer-learning coding school** on the global **42 Network** model, brought to Djibouti with **EU /
Expertise France** and the **Association 42** support — free, selective, no-teacher software training aimed
at building a local developer pool. Source: [[2026-04-06-ecole-42-djibouti-techreviewafrica]].
