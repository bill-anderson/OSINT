# ENCORE (Enhancing Collection of Revenue and Expenditure Management)

A **World Bank-funded DRC public-finance reform project** supporting revenue collection and expenditure management — including the digitisation of the civil-service payroll.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital civil-servant registry (2025).** ENCORE backed the DRC's first clean, digital directory of civil servants (central government + Kinshasa province) — addressing an unknown headcount and ghost-worker/wage-bill problem — implemented with the Ministry of Civil Service and the public-finance reform committee **COREF**; launch chaired by PM Judith Suminwa and Minister Jean-Pierre Lihau. See [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.mis]]. Source: [[2025-07-17 DR Congo launches digital civil servant registry to tackle ghost workers (ENCORE)]].
