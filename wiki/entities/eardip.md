# Eastern Africa Regional Digital Integration Project (EARDIP)

A **World Bank-supported regional programme** to build a connected single digital
market across the Horn/East Africa — cross-border connectivity, data infrastructure
and harmonised digital rules — the eastern counterpart to West Africa's [[wardip]].
It appears in this wiki via Djibouti's alignment of national projects to it.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Djibouti alignment (2026-07).** In WSIS-2026 sideline talks, Djibouti's
  communications minister and **[[smart-africa]]** CEO Lacina Koné discussed aligning
  Djibouti's national digital projects — including the **Djibouti Digital Foundation
  (DFN)** — with EARDIP and broader continental data-sovereignty / AI initiatives,
  building on Djibouti's role as a Red Sea connectivity hub. See [[DJI]], [[XEA]],
  [[gov.regional]], [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 Djibouti Seeks ITU, Smart Africa Support for Telecoms and AI Development]].
