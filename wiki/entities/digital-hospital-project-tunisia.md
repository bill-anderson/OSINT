# Digital Hospital Project (Tunisia)

**A Tunisian government project to digitalise hospital services, named as the focus of the
[[ministry-of-health-tunisia]]'s partnership with the national operator [[tunisie-telecom]].** The
single source held names the project and credits the partnership as key to advancing it, but
supplies **no scope document, no budget, no timeline and no launch date**; this page records the
project's existence and its stated institutional context, and nothing further.

**Written as intention, not achievement.** The source reports stated plans from one meeting — no
amounts, no dates, no named agreement, no "as of" figures.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Named as the government project the Health–Tunisie Telecom partnership advances (reported
  2026-02-13).** Health Minister **Dr Mustafa Al-Furjani** praised **[[tunisie-telecom]]**'s
  technical and logistical support, **describing the partnership as key to advancing the
  government's Digital Hospital Project**, and said the collaboration will help translate digital
  transformation efforts into faster, more efficient and citizen-focused healthcare services.
  Source: [[2026-02-13 Tunisia Advances Digital Health Reform With AI and National Cloud Strategy]].
- **The surrounding plans (stated 2026-02).** The same meeting set out priorities the project sits
  among: **high-speed internet across health institutions, particularly primary care facilities**;
  enhanced **data exchange** and **telemedicine**; an **advanced digital call centre**; **AI** for
  data analysis and decision support; and adoption of **fully Tunisian cloud solutions to safeguard
  national health data**, for local control over sensitive medical information. Whether these are
  components of the Digital Hospital Project or parallel workstreams is **not established** by the
  source. See [[ministry-of-health-tunisia]], [[infra.connect]], [[tech.ai]], [[infra.store]],
  [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-13 Tunisia Advances Digital Health Reform With AI and National Cloud Strategy]].
