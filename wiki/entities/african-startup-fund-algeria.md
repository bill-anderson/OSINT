# African Startup and Young Innovators Financing Fund (Algeria)

An Algeria-backed continental fund launched by President **Tebboune** in **September 2025**, to which Algeria committed **USD 1 billion** (announced 21 Oct 2025 by Foreign Trade Minister **Kamel Rezig** at UNCTAD16, Geneva). Targets African startups and young innovators in technology, education, healthcare and humanitarian services with strong social/economic impact.

Part of Algeria's outward, continental innovation posture — alongside domestic vehicles (Algeria Venture, the National Startup Fund; goal of **20,000 startups by 2029**) and North–Sub-Saharan infrastructure links (Trans-Saharan highway/pipeline, high-speed fibre). A notable case of a North-African state deploying capital continentally.
