# Mojaloop

Open-source instant-payments software (a digital public good) used to build interoperable national
instant payment systems. In Africa it underpins Rwanda's **[[ekash]]** and Liberia's 2025 mobile-money
IPS — cited as evidence that IPS can be built in months at low cost, reducing reliance on proprietary
switches. See [[dpi.pay]].
