# Mojaloop

Open-source instant-payments software (a digital public good) used to build interoperable national
instant payment systems. In Africa it underpins Rwanda's **[[ekash]]** and Liberia's 2025 mobile-money
IPS — cited as evidence that IPS can be built in months at low cost, reducing reliance on proprietary
switches. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **[[GMB]] The Gambia — BANTABA 2.0 (2025-12).** The Gambia's first Inclusive Instant Payment System,
  **BANTABA 2.0**, is built on Mojaloop and operated by **[[gamswitch]]** (with the Central Bank, IIDiA
  and the Mojaloop Foundation) — a domestically-owned national switch. See [[gambia--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-15 Gambia launches BANTABA 2.0, first real-time interoperable payment system]].
- **[[GIN]] Guinea — SPI (2025-12).** Guinea's central bank ([[bcrg]]) selected Mojaloop for its
  instant payment system to limit technological dependence and favour local innovation. See
  [[guinea--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2025-12-22 Guinea central bank starts work on instant payment system]].
