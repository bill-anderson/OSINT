# Sudanese Cybersecurity Authority

National cybersecurity body established by decree of PM **Kamil Idris** on **5 November 2025**,
one of three agencies created under the **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** (alongside the
**[[digital-transformation-authority-sudan]]** and the **[[sudanese-data-and-ai-authority]]**).
Charged with building a national cybersecurity framework and protecting public data and systems.

Context: Sudan ranked **Tier 4** (score 48.17/100) on cybersecurity in 2024; the authority is
stood up amid conflict, weak infrastructure and a skills shortage. See [[infra.cybersec]].
