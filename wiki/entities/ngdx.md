# Nigeria Data Exchange (NGDX)

A centralised **data-exchange platform** developed by **[[nitda]]** to let authorised
institutions verify and share verified records on the back end, ending the burden of Nigerians
re-presenting the same personal details and biometrics across NIN, BVN, SIM, driver's-licence
and passport registration. Positioned as the interoperability backbone for identity and KYC —
faster fintech onboarding, lower cost and time, privacy safeguards.

- **2025-08-25** — Unveiled at a stakeholders' workshop in Abuja (with **[[european-union]]**,
  Finland and Estonia as partners); Minister **[[bosun-tijani]]** framed it as enabling secure
  data-sharing, innovation and inclusive growth, and NITDA DG **[[kashifu-inuwa]]** described it
  as a unified backbone for identity and data verification. Targeted to be **operational by end
  of 2025**.

NGDX is the concrete platform behind Nigeria's wider push against **eight siloed government
databases** (NIN, BVN, NCC, NIS, FIRS, FRSC, CAC, INEC). Relates to the **[[ngea]]** enterprise
architecture and the National Cloud Policy. See [[dpi.exchange]], [[nigeria--dpi-id]].
