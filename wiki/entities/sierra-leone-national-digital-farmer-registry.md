# Sierra Leone National Digital Farmer Registry

A planned foundational database for Sierra Leone's digital-agriculture systems, being built
under the Ministry of Agriculture and Food Security (**MAFS**) and the Food Systems
Resilience Project (**FSRP**), aligned to President Bio's **Feed Salone** flagship. Vendor:
**Tiwai Memory Masters**.

## Design

- **Integrated with the [[ncra]] NIN system (agreed 2025-05).** Farmer identities are
  authenticated and de-duplicated against the biometric NIN database, addressing years of
  fragmented, inconsistent farmer records. Intended to serve as the base layer for
  e-extension and other agtech platforms. Hosting at NCRA's **Tier-3 data centre** under
  discussion (infrastructure-sharing among MDAs). Source:
  [[2025-05-19 NCRA, Ministry of Agriculture partner on National Digital Farmer Registry integration]].
- The World Bank frames the registry as a route to connect farmers to **subsidies, credit,
  markets and mobile money** through ID linkage. Source:
  [[2025-12-09 Unlocking opportunity - How digital ID can transform lives in Sierra Leone]].
