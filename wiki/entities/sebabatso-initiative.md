# Sebabatso Initiative

PM **Ntsokoane Matekane**'s flagship **youth-empowerment programme** (launched Oct 2023) — by 2026 it
reported generating **over M21m in revenue over three years and 383 jobs across 50+ companies**. See
[[capacity.training]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **AI Skills Development Programme (2026-06).** [[tech.ai]] [[geopol.usa]] The Ministry of Gender, Youth
  & Social Development, with the **US Embassy Maseru** and the American Corner,
  launched an **AI Skills Development Programme** under Sebabatso — described as Lesotho's **first digital
  entrepreneurship training** integrating e-commerce and advanced AI tools (marketing content, customer-
  data analysis, automation, product translation via Shopify). US Chargé d'Affaires Thomas Hines framed
  AI as "a present-day bridge connecting local enterprise to global markets." See [[lesotho--tech-ai]].
