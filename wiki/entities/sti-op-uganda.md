# Science, Technology and Innovation Office of the President (STI-OP), Uganda

**Uganda's presidential science, technology and innovation office** — the state vehicle
behind the country's Deep Tech Hub and Deep Tech Centre of Excellence, and a convenor of
the domestic cloud and innovation ecosystem. Minister of Science, Technology and
Innovation (as of 2026-03): **Monica Musenero Masanza**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **ABQ Cloud launch (2026-03-02).** STI-OP took part in the launch of **[[abq-cloud]]**,
  billed as Uganda's indigenous sovereign cloud ecosystem, at the **Deep Tech Hub and
  [[raxio-group]] Data Centre, Namanve, Kampala** — officiated by Minister Musenero, who
  called ABQ Cloud *"a strategic pillar for Uganda's future."* Other participants: ABQ
  Cloud, Raxio, **Roke Cloud**, **AfriQloud** and EDiC. **David Gonahasa** represented
  STI-OP on the "Driving Innovation using AI Sovereign Cloud" panel. A hackathon hosted by
  STI's **Industry 4.0+ Bureau** awarded its top five winners **2.5 million shillings**
  starting capital, a year of free cloud credits, incubation and mentorship at the **Deep
  Tech Centre of Excellence**, and a year of free internet. See [[abq-cloud]],
  [[tech.innovate]], [[infra.store]], [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-02 ABQ Cloud launches Ugandan sovereign cloud ecosystem]].
