# Airtel Madagascar

A Madagascar **mobile operator** (Airtel Africa), pushing **satellite-augmented connectivity** and mobile
money (Airtel Money). See [[infra.connect]], [[madagascar--infra-connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Direct-to-Cell with SpaceX/Starlink (2026).** [[include.access]] SpaceX filed an FCC notice (13 May)
  to launch **Direct-to-Cell (Supplemental Coverage from Space)** in Madagascar using **Airtel's licensed
  spectrum**; the two then ran Madagascar's **first Direct-to-Cell satellite trial** (Sadabe, 11 Jul) —
  the **third African country** to test it (after Kenya, Uganda) — completing the first satellite WhatsApp
  video call. Commercial deployment pending **ARTEC** approval. Sources:
  [[2026-05-19-starlink-direct-to-cell-fcc-airtel-spaceinafrica]],
  [[2026-07-15-direct-to-cell-satellite-trial-telecomreviewafrica]].
- **Eutelsat OneWeb rural broadband (2026-06).** [[infra.connect]] Airtel Madagascar and **[[eutelsat|Eutelsat
  OneWeb]]** expanded **LEO** satellite broadband integrated with fibre/4G — including a **100 Mbps
  moving-train** test. Source: [[2026-06-01-airtel-eutelsat-rural-broadband-techafricanews]].
- **Airtel Money–Baobab Bank (2026-07).** [[dpi.pay]] [[finance.mou]] Airtel Money integrated
  **[[baobab-bank|Baobab Bank]]** into its **Mbanking** service (bidirectional wallet↔account, USSD
  \*436\*5# / My Baobab) — bridging mobile money and formal banking. See [[madagascar--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-airtel-money-baobab-bank-partnership-techafricanews]].
