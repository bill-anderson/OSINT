# CEMAC (Communauté Économique et Monétaire de l'Afrique Centrale)

**The Central African Economic and Monetary Community** — the economic and monetary bloc of
[[XCA]] states. Per the wiki's rule that blocs are entities rather than places, CEMAC is held here
as an organisation and tagged to the geographic region plus the states the sources connect it to.

**This page is thin by design: the sources currently held touch CEMAC only once.** Its membership,
institutions, treaty basis and digital mandate are not established by any source in the wiki, and
are not asserted here.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Free roaming with Chad (reported 2025-12-02).** Among the partnerships [[TCD]] expanded from
  January 2025, Ecofin lists *"CEMAC member states on free roaming"* — alongside Guinea-Bissau,
  the UAE, Greece, Cameroon, the UNDP, Kenya, India, Canada and the [[itu]]. No further detail on
  the roaming arrangement — scope, status, effective date — is reported. See [[gov.regional]],
  [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].

## Distinguish from

- **[[cadnac]]** — the *Central African Digital Development Entities Conference*, a separate,
  agency-level platform whose foundational texts were being drafted in November 2025. No source
  held places CADNAC under CEMAC.
- **BEAC** (Bank of Central African States), which appears in the wiki's Cameroon telecom material
  as a lender, is a distinct institution and is not hubbed.
