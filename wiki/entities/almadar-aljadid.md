# Almadar Aljadid

A Libyan state mobile operator (subsidiary of **[[lptic|LPTIC]]**), and the **first national operator to
launch 5G** in Libya. See [[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Libya's first 5G (2026-01).** [[infra.connect]] Almadar launched **5G service in central Tripoli**
  (27 Jan), first nationally, with a countrywide rollout to follow (initial coverage thin). See
  [[libya--infra-connect]]. Source: [[2026-01-29-almadar-launches-first-5g-tripoli-libyaherald]].
