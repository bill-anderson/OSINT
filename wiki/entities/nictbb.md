# National ICT Broadband Backbone (NICTBB), Tanzania

Tanzania's **state-owned terrestrial fibre backbone** — the spine of national and regional connectivity,
operated in connection with **[[ttcl]]** under **[[micit-tanzania]]**.

- **Scale (as of Dec 2025).** ~**14,000 km** laid; cumulative **13,820 / 16,280 km** target; reaching
  **85–109 of 139 districts**; **capacity raised tenfold, 200 → 2,000 Gbps**; connected to **Mombasa
  (Kenya)** via the Horohoro–Tanga route. Sources:
  [[2025-05-16-tanzania-allocates-sh73-billion-ict-backbone]], [[2026-04-10-communications-sector-surges-400pc]].
- **2025/26 spend (Sh73bn).** DRC extension, Wi-Fi in 50 public spaces, last-mile fibre to 100
  justice/defence/security institutions, 90,000 FTTx connections, two national data centres (Dodoma +
  Zanzibar). Source: [[2025-05-16-tanzania-allocates-sh73-billion-ict-backbone]].
- **2026/27 plan.** DRC link (20 stations), 7 IXPs, Wi-Fi in 100 public spaces, 100 schools; the ministry
  expects **Sh160.3bn revenue** from NICTBB capacity sales. Source:
  [[2026-04-30-kairuki-aomba-222-bilioni-bajeti-micit]].
