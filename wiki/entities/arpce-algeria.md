# ARPCE (Algeria)

Algeria's **post & electronic-communications regulator**. Ran the **5G licence tender** (2025) leading to the
$492m three-operator award, launched the **two-licence satellite-ISP (NGSO) tender** (2026-04), and operates
**electronic authentication & signature** services (2025-09). Not to be confused with the identically-named
Congolese regulator ([[arpce-congo]]). See [[algeria--infra-connect]], [[algeria-5g]].
