# EXIM Bank (Export-Import Bank of the United States)

The **official export-credit agency of the United States** — the US counterpart to China Eximbank — using
guarantees under the **China and Transformational Exports Program (CTEP)** to finance US-vendor digital
infrastructure abroad as a geopolitical instrument.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Côte d'Ivoire — first SSA data-centre backing (2025).** Approved **$66m** for the CIV
  **[[national-data-centre-civ|National Data Centre]]** (**the first data centre EXIM has backed in
  sub-Saharan Africa**, implemented by **[[cybastion]]**, displacing a Chinese bidder) and **$47m** for
  Ministry of Commerce digitisation, consolidated into a **$100m+ CTEP guarantee** (Cisco, HPE, Schneider)
  — later scaled to **$170m** with smart-border surveillance added — explicitly framed as countering the
  PRC. See [[civ--infra-store]], [[geopol.usa]], [[geopol.china]]. Sources:
