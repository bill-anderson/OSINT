# Jack Mwiimbu

**Zambia's Minister of Home Affairs and Internal Security** — the political principal over
**[[dnrpc-zambia]]**, and therefore over the NRC, civil registration, **[[inris]]** and the
refugee-ID work under **[[zrhcp]]**. He is the ministerial voice in this batch; Registrar
General **[[davies-chikalanga]]** is the operational one, and the two do not give the same
digital-ID date.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Nationwide NRC enrolment campaign launched (2025-04-10).** At **Buseko Primary School
  Grounds, Kitwe**, Mwiimbu launched a mobile enrolment exercise expected to last **three
  months**, targeting **~3.5 million** citizens across all **ten provinces** — aimed
  specifically at far-flung areas where national registration offices do not exist.
  **Free for first-time registrants aged 16+**, with a **K2 (~$0.072) replacement fee** for
  card replacement. He directed the Registrar General accordingly: *"I have given a directive
  to the Registrar General to ensure that every Zambian accesses an NRC as long as they are
  above 16."* Traditional rulers and local authorities were urged to support it. Source:
  [[2025-04-11 Zambia commences nationwide mobile campaign for issuance of 3.5M ID documents]].
  *(Scope wobble: the April piece scopes the 3.5m to NRCs; the June piece by the same author
  scopes it to birth certificates **and** NRCs — the June framing is taken as corrected.)*
- **8th Africa CRVS Day launched, Lusaka (2025-08-10).** Speaking ahead of the continental
  observance — theme **"Civil Registration as the Foundation for Digital Public
  Infrastructure and Digital Legal Identity Systems in Africa"** — Mwiimbu framed civil
  registration as **"the bedrock of legal identity"**, not paperwork: it confirms citizenship
  and gates access to social protection. He set out **three pillars** toward **SDG 16.9**
  (legal identity for all, including birth registration, **by 2030**): **integration**
  (linking CRVS to health, education, identity management and social protection),
  **modernization** (digitisation and upgrade to expand coverage), and **decentralization**
  (bringing registration close to remote communities). Without inclusive CRVS, he argued,
  African states cannot build trustworthy digital identity systems. See
  [[dpi.registry]], [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2025-08-12 Zambia sees civil registration as bedrock of inclusive digital transformation]].
- **Refugee biometric NRCs (2025-10).** On **[[zrhcp]]**, Mwiimbu said the biometric cards
  are intended *"to improve access to socio-economic opportunities for both refugees and host
  communities."* Source:
  [[2025-10-03 Zambia plans domestic digital ID development with help from international partners]].
