# Econet AI

An AI unit of **[[econet-wireless-zimbabwe]]** / the Econet Group (ISSUE-009) — products **Yamurai AI** and
**OmniContact AI**, plus consulting and a stated **sovereign cloud** for government/institutions and
**Shona local-language AI**.

- **Compute via Cassava (Apr 2026).** Draws GPU compute from **[[cassava-technologies]]**'s "AI factory" —
  though **whether the compute is physically in Zimbabwe is unclear** (may be offshore), a tension against the
  "sovereign cloud" framing. See [[zimbabwe--tech-ai]]. Source: [[2026-04-17-econet-ai-launch-cassava-compute]].
