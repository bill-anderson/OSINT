# ePass (Benin diaspora passport-renewal platform)

Benin's self-service biometric passport-renewal platform for citizens abroad
(epass.gouv.bj), run by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and its Direction de
l'émigration et l'immigration. It lets the diaspora renew travel documents from
home without visiting an embassy — part of Benin's "**4D Diplomacy**" agenda.
See [[BEN]], [[dpi.id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (Oct 2025).** Rolled out via mobile app and web; registration keyed
  to national digital ID ([[anip-benin]]) and consular number. Contactless
  **fingerprint + face biometrics with liveness detection**, ICAO-compliant,
  guaranteed **4-week** delivery, with document submission, application tracking
  and online **payment** ([[dpi.pay]]). Source:
  [[2025-10-24 Benin unveils platform to ease biometric passport applications for diaspora citizens]].
- **Built on Seamfix GovSmart (revealed 2025-12).** Developed by [[seamfix]] on
  its **GovSmart** platform, integrated with Benin's national ID and immigration
  systems; now available in **80+ countries**, renewals ~15 minutes. Framed as a
  potential blueprint for other African governments. Source:
  [[2025-12-18 Seamfix revealed as developer of Benin's biometric passport renewal app]].
