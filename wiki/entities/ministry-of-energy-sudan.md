# Ministry of Energy (Sudan)

Sudan's energy ministry, coordinating (through the electricity companies) the
reconstruction of a national grid heavily damaged by the war — the upstream
dependency for telecom and all digital infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National-grid reconstruction (2026-05).** Stated that thermal generation had
  collapsed to "unprecedented" lows because of large-scale damage to generation,
  transmission and distribution infrastructure from the war and "systematic
  targeting" of facilities (the Port-Sudan government's framing; capture is not
  endorsement). Priority: restoring power to water stations, hospitals and
  service/education institutions, plus solar projects at critical sites. Included
  as the physical-destruction dimension and the upstream cause of Sudan's
  connectivity collapse. See [[infra.energy]], [[infra.connect]], [[SDN]].
