# DIODE Network (publications list)

Curated publications bibliography of DIODE (Development Implications of Digital
Economies), a research network based at the University of Manchester's Global
Development Institute and associated with Richard Heeks and collaborators. The
list spans briefings, DIODE working papers and member publications on the digital
economy, digital labour and the [[include.divides|digital divide]] in the Global
South, read here through a [[colonialism]] lens.

Filed as a single `resource` with `status: needs-review`. Per the wiki's
bibliography rule, **capture is not endorsement**: individual entries are **not**
promoted at intake. Named scholarly works within it — e.g. the Bukht & Heeks
working papers on defining and measuring the digital economy, *Digital Economies
at Global Margins*, the Fairwork studies, and Heeks' *ICT4D* — become
`publication` entities only on deliberate later review, or when a specific work
actually earns a citation elsewhere in the wiki. Within the list,
framework/standard entries would map to `instrument`, institutional/agency reports
(UNCTAD, World Bank, OECD, ILO, GSMA) to `source`, and only named scholarly works
to `publication`.
