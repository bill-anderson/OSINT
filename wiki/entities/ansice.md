# ANSICE (National Agency for Information Security and Electronic Certification)

**Chad's information-security and electronic-certification authority** — the state body that
certifies critical digital infrastructure and carries the government's cybersecurity brief.
ANSICE is one of the two Chadian agencies auditing the [[chad-national-data-centre]] before
commissioning. Deputy General Manager (also rendered *deputy director general*):
**Nadjma Saleh Kebzabo**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National Data Center audit (2026-02-05).** ANSICE co-signed the tripartite agreement with
  [[adetic]] and [[techso-group]] to audit and certify the National Data Center — see
  [[adetic-ansice-techso-audit-agreement]]. Kebzabo said the aim is to *"guarantee the
  reliability, integrity and resilience of the national data center,"* and framed the
  collaboration as the state's response to *"growing challenges related to cybercrime and the
  protection of sensitive data."* ANSICE draws on TECHSO-GROUP's expertise for the audit itself.
  See [[infra.cybersec]], [[infra.store]], [[gov.standards]], [[sovereignty]]. Sources:
  [[2026-02-06 ADETIC, ANSICE, and TECHSO-GROUP Join Forces to Protect Chad's Digital Infrastructure]],
  [[2026-02-06 Chad Moves to Secure National Data Center Ahead of Launch]].
- **Certification against critical-infrastructure standards.** The audit is scoped to compliance
  with *national and international standards for critical infrastructure protection* — the
  certification step separating a built facility from a commissioned one. See [[gov.standards]].
  Source: [[2026-02-06 ADETIC, ANSICE, and TECHSO-GROUP Join Forces to Protect Chad's Digital Infrastructure]].
