# onebank

**Egypt's first fully digital bank** — the **[[central-bank-of-egypt|CBE]]** gave **Misr Digital Innovation
(MDI)**, a subsidiary of state-owned **Banque Misr**, initial approval to establish it (2025-08). Branchless,
with products rolling out in 2026 to promote financial inclusion; chaired by **Khaled El Attar** (former MCIT
vice-minister for Digital Transformation). MDI was founded in 2020. See [[egypt--dpi-pay]].
