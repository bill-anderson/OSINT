# Flutterwave

Nigerian-origin pan-African **payments company** — notable in CMR holdings as the **first BEAC-licensed
fintech entry** recorded for Cameroon.

## Activity in this wiki

- **BEAC payment licence via Ecobank (2025-06).** Flutterwave obtained a **[[beac]]** payment licence for
  Cameroon **through a technical partnership with [[ecobank]]** — a regional regulatory-access pattern
  (a foreign fintech entering the CEMAC zone on the rails of an established regional bank). Source:
  [[2025-06-23-flutterwave-digital-payment-license-beac-ecobank-connectingafrica]].
