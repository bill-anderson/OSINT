# BOAD (Banque Ouest-Africaine de Développement / West African Development Bank)

**The West African Development Bank — the regional development-finance institution of the
[[waemu]]/UEMOA states.** In this wiki it appears as a lender of last resort to a
state-owned utility under financial strain. See [[XWA]], [[finance.new]],
[[gov.regional]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **CFA40bn to CEET for energy emergencies (reported 2025-05-16).** Togo's electricity
  utility **[[ceet]]** "recently secured **CFA40 billion** from the West African Development
  Bank (BOAD) to handle energy emergencies," reported alongside the **12.5% average tariff
  rise** that took effect **5 May 2025** — both framed as responses to the utility's ongoing
  funding challenges. See [[TGO]], [[infra.energy]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-16 Togo Increases Electricity Prices to Boost Utility's Finances]].

**Scope note.** The single account held here is a Togolese tariff report; it establishes the
BOAD only through this facility. No further mandate, portfolio or terms are asserted.
