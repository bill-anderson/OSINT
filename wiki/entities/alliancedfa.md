# Alliance of Digital Finance and Fintech Associations (AllianceDFA)

A network of national fintech associations (Ghana, Nigeria, Rwanda, South Africa, Zimbabwe and others).
Co-published, with [[africanenda]], a 2026 focus note arguing non-bank direct participation in instant
payment systems is essential to inclusion. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
[[2026-04-01 Making Non-bank Participation in Inclusive Instant Payment Systems a Reality in Africa]].
