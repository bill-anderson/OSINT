# Centro Nacional de Cibersegurança (Angola)

Angola's planned **National Cybersecurity Centre** — a specialised body to be established (alongside a **National Cybersecurity Council**) to protect national cyberspace, critical infrastructure and public/private institutions. **Not yet operational**: it is a creature of two still-incomplete tracks.

## Status in this wiki

- **Legislative track.** The [[angola-cybersecurity-law]] (Proposta de Lei de Cibersegurança) that would create the Council and Centre was **approved in general terms 23 Jan 2026 (105–1–75)** but its committee-stage discussion was **suspended in July 2026** over drafting non-conformities — so the Centre has no statutory basis yet. See [[angola-cybersecurity-law]].
- **Decree track.** The draft-Presidential-Decree [[national-cybersecurity-strategy-angola]] (Dec 2025) also provides for a National Cybersecurity Council and incident-response teams.
- **Presidential framing.** President [[joao-lourenco]] listed the *Centro Nacional de Cibersegurança* among the digital-sovereignty investments (with the National Broadband, National Data Centre and National Cloud) at the 2026 Judicial Year opening (2026-03-03). See [[angola--infra-cybersec]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Sources: [[2026-01-23-proposta-lei-ciberseguranca-aprovada-generalidade]], [[2026-03-03-joao-lourenco-cybercrime-desafio-da-justica]].
