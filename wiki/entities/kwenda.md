# Kwenda (Angola cash-transfer programme)

Angola's flagship **social cash-transfer / G2P programme**, launched **May 2020** and implemented by the **Social Support Fund (FAS)** under the World Bank-financed Strengthening the National Social Protection System Project.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital G2P at scale.** Delivers quarterly payments (~25,500 kz / ~$40) digitally to most beneficiaries; 247,000+ households enrolled early on, targeting **1.6 million** low-income families (60% female-headed) — Angola's first cash-transfer programme paying via cash and digital, a financial-inclusion and women's-economic-empowerment lever. Part of the WB **G2Px** digitizing-G2P initiative. See [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Sources: [[2022-03-15 Q&A - How is Angola Reaching the Poor and Vulnerable During Covid-19]], [[2019-06-28 World Bank - Strengthening the National Social Protection System Project (Angola)]].
- Rests on a **social registry** of poor/vulnerable households — the registry layer under Angola's DPI.
