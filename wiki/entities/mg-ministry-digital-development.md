# Madagascar Ministry of Digital Development, Posts & Telecommunications

The Malagasy government ministry (Ministère du Développement Numérique, des Postes
et des Télécommunications) leading Madagascar's national digital identity
programme and public-administration digitisation.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National biometric ID pilot (~2026-01-27).** The ministry launched the pilot
  phase of a national biometric-registration programme, starting with senior
  government officials to stress-test the technical infrastructure. The system
  captures **fingerprints, facial images and iris scans** to give each citizen a
  **single, verifiable ID** and unique identification number for accessing public
  services, cutting duplication and fraud. See [[MDG]], [[dpi.id]],
  [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Madagascar Tests Biometric ID System, Aims to Register 2 Million People by Mid-2026]].
- **Nationwide rollout (target Apr–Jun 2026).** A full launch is planned for
  **April 2026**, targeting registration of **2 million adults** (aged 18+)
  between April and June 2026, alongside improved civil-registry reliability and
  expanded online public services. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Madagascar Tests Biometric ID System, Aims to Register 2 Million People by Mid-2026]].
