# Eswatini Digital Economy Assessment (World Bank)

**The [[world-bank]]'s country diagnostic of Eswatini's digital economy**, published
**2022** (held at year precision) — the Bank's baseline assessment preceding the
digitalisation push that the [[eswatini-digitalisation-strategy-2024-2028]] and the
[[eswatini-economic-update-2025]] later carried. See [[SWZ]], [[gov.policy]].

> **Reference study — cite, don't absorb.** A country-level reference work. Its figures
> are dated to it and are **not** current state; the 2022 baseline must not out-rank the
> 2025–26 material the wiki holds. Cite the study; leave its numbers in it.

## Standing in this wiki

- **A historical baseline, not a development.** The assessment is held as an artefact
  with a companion source page; no clip in the wiki reports on its launch or findings,
  so the wiki carries no extracted results from it. It is the pre-strategy datum against
  which later analysis — the [[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]] (2024) and the
  [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]] (2025) — should be read.
- **Scope.** Digital economy diagnostic across policy, connectivity ([[infra.connect]]),
  government digitisation ([[dpi.govtech]]) and the ICT industry ([[tech.industry]]).
