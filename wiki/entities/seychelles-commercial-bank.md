# Seychelles Commercial Bank (SCB)

A Seychelles bank. In **2025** it suffered a major **data breach**: attacker "ByteToBreach" breached its
network (5 Feb 2025) and exfiltrated **~2.2 GB** of client data (names, contacts, account balances,
including "current accounts – government"), advertised on DarkForums; SCB suspended internet banking. The
attacker cited an **Oracle WebLogic** vulnerability (Flexcube Private Banking) and keys accessible in the
IT environment — cited by Resecurity as an instance of offshore/tax-haven banking being targeted. See
[[seychelles--gov-protect]], [[SYC]].
