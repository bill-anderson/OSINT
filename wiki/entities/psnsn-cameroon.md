# PSNSN (Digital Health Strategic Plan 2026–2030)

Cameroon's **national digital-health strategic plan, 2026–2030** — **~29bn FCFA / $51.3m, 8 axes** — the
state's response to a weak digital-health baseline (**the prior plan scored 1.8/5**). It directly answers
the wiki's held **"digital health gap (2025 study)"** (85% unaware of guidelines, 45% DHIS2
interoperability problems). Source:
[[2026-03-10-leconomie-digital-health-strategic-plan-2026-2030]].
