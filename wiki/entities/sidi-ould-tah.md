# Sidi Ould Tah

President of the [[african-development-bank]] and the leading advocate of "African financial
sovereignty" — the argument that the continent should fund its own growth from domestic
capital as the aid model frays.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Presented NAFA at the AU summit (2026-02-14/15, Addis Ababa).** Ould Tah launched the
  [[nafa]] (New African Financial Architecture) framework around four "Cardinal Points":
  mobilising African capital, rebuilding financial sovereignty, harnessing the demographic
  dividend, and building resilient infrastructure. Tags [[finance.new]], [[gov.regional]].
  Source: [[2026-02-26 The AfDB's push for African financial sovereignty, and a tech investment rebound]].

- **"Fund your own growth" advocacy (2026-05-26).** Frames domestic resource mobilisation —
  tax digitisation, pension-fund redeployment, closing the public-investment efficiency gap —
  as the alternative to declining external aid. Source:
  [[2026-05-26 AfDB says Africa must fund its own growth as aid model frays]].
