# Jane Munga

Technology-policy scholar at the **Carnegie Endowment for International Peace**
(Africa Program), working on African AI/digital governance and US–Africa tech
engagement.

## Activity in this wiki

- **US–Kenya TPD framework (Carnegie, 2026-06).** Author of the argument that a
  **[[technology-prosperity-deal]]** with Kenya could be a replicable baseline for
  structured US–Africa AI partnership. Also associated with Carnegie's **Africa
  Technology Policy Tracker (AfTech)**. See [[technology-prosperity-deal]],
  [[KEN]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-23 A Kenya Technology Prosperity Deal Could Help Washington Secure Durable AI Partnerships with Africa]].

- **AFGHS stress-test analysis (Carnegie, 2026-03).** Co-authored (with Rose Mosero, ex-Kenya
  deputy data commissioner) the Carnegie analysis arguing Kenya's court-suspended
  **[[us-kenya-health-framework]]** is an early stress test of the
  **[[america-first-global-health-strategy]]**: its data-sharing annex institutionalises US
  access to Kenyan health and genomic data, conflicting with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019,
  the Digital Health Act 2023, and the remit of the **[[odpc-kenya]]** — positioning Kenya as a
  data supplier while value accrues elsewhere. See [[KEN]], [[gov.protect]], [[geopol.usa]].
  Source: [[2026-03-27 Kenya's Health Deal Is a Stress Test for the America First Global Health Strategy]].

- **Undersea-cable vulnerabilities (Carnegie reference paper).** Munga authored a Carnegie
  paper on Africa's subsea fragility: **77 cable systems** land in Africa (37 of 38 coastal
  states, Eritrea excepted), with hubs in Egypt/SA/Djibouti/Nigeria/Kenya; the **March 2024**
  outage hit 10 countries (Nigeria lost **>$590m** in four days) and only **three repair
  ships** serve the continent. Flags the ITU+ICPC advisory body (Nov 2024) co-chaired by
  [[bosun-tijani]]. See [[infra.connect]], [[gov.policy]]. Source:
  [[2025-04-03 Beneath the Waves Addressing Vulnerabilities in Africa's Undersea Digital Infrastructure]].
