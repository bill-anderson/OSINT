# Airtel Nigeria (Nxtra) — Eko Atlantic hyperscale data centre

A planned carrier-neutral **38 MW** hyperscale data centre in **Eko Atlantic City**, Lagos, part
of **[[airtel-africa]]**'s Nxtra network, built for cloud and **GPU-powered AI** workloads.

- **2025-08-08** — Announced by Airtel Nigeria CEO Dinesh Balsingh: **>US$120m** cost, **3,000+
  racks** across six floors, densities up to **25 kW/rack**, target **PUE < 1.4**, redundant
  substations; completion scheduled **Q1 2026**. Eko Atlantic (reclaimed land by Victoria Island)
  offers proximity to subsea-cable landing stations and dense fibre; tenants include First Bank,
  Dangote and MTN Nigeria.

Announced a month after the **[[mtn-sifiso-dabengwa-data-centre]]** launch — part of the Lagos
AI-ready data-centre build-out. See [[infra.store]], [[nigeria--infra-store]].
