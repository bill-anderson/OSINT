# Somalia Data Protection Authority (DPA)

Somalia's data-protection regulator, established under the [[somali-data-protection-act]]
(Law 005/2023) and **inaugurated on 24 February 2024** (it had commenced some activities
earlier). It oversees implementation of the Act — issuing regulations and guidance,
investigating, imposing fines, registering controllers/processors of major importance,
accrediting compliance organisations and advising government.

- In early Feb 2024 the DPA published implementation **guidelines** (staff awareness, data
  inventory, risk assessment, compliance action plan) and, under Art 41, the Implementation
  Regulation, DPA Guidance and an Alternative Dispute Resolution Procedure.
- Independence is qualified: the head (General Manager) is proposed by the Minister,
  confirmed by Cabinet and appointed by the President; the Authority must defer to
  executive direction in national-security/public-order matters (lens: sovereignty).
- Named a collaborating agency for Somalia's first **National DPI Summit** (Feb 2026).
