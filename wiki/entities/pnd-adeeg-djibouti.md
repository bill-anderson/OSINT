# PND-ADEEG 2025–2030

Djibouti's **third National Development Plan** — **formally adopted by decree** at the Council of Ministers
(30 Jun 2026) — the successor development frame under **[[vision-djibouti-2035|Vision 2035]]** cited across
the digital strategy stack (the same session also advanced an anti-corruption bill, CNIPLC/FATF). Source:
