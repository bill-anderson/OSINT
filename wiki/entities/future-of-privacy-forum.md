# Future of Privacy Forum (FPF)

US-based privacy think tank with a dedicated Africa programme (Policy Manager for Africa **Mercy
King’ori**). Publishes comparative analysis of African data-protection reform.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Continental data-protection reform mapping (2026-03).** FPF traced a "second wave" of reform across
  **Nigeria, Kenya, Angola, Mauritius, Botswana, Seychelles and Ghana**, plus AU/REC-level moves —
  local-nexus/accountability rules, AI-driven drivers, and Ghana’s move to codify property rights over
  personal data. See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-12 Africa’s Data Protection Reforms A Continental Perspective on the Drivers of Change in Legal Frameworks]].
