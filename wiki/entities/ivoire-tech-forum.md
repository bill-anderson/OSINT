# Ivoire Tech Forum

Côte d'Ivoire's **flagship tech-innovation gathering** (inaugural 2025) — the launchpad for the country's
startup-and-investment ambition after the National Digital Strategy 2025, framed as "digitally
sovereign."

## Activity in this wiki

- **A FCFA 100bn technological-innovation fund** was announced at the inaugural forum; the government also
  called for a **~$178m (CFA 100bn) private-sector startup fund** against ~300 active startups; **Ivoire
  Tech 2025** reported **>250bn FCFA invested for 2,965 direct jobs**. Sources:
