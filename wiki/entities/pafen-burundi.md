# PAFEN — Digital Foundations Project for Burundi

The **World Bank Digital Foundations Project** (P176396) — Burundi's flagship digital-transformation programme, approved **2022** (FY2022 IBRD/IDA), an estimated **$92 million**, launched September 2024 by VP Prosper Bazombanza.

## Profile

- **Three components:** expand digital access/inclusion (broadband, esp. underserved/rural communities), build enablers of digital public-service delivery, and strengthen institutional coordination. Digital-skills building is a core aim.
- **Implementing agencies:** MINCOTIM (Communication/IT/Media), MFBPE (Finance/Budget/Economic Planning) and **[[setic-burundi]]**.
- Funds concrete deliverables including the **[[mediabox]]** land-administration (DTFCN) EDRMS. Context: Burundi ranked **183rd of 193** on the UN **2024** EGDI. See [[BDI]], [[infra.connect]]. Sources: [[2025-05-13 World Bank - Burundi Digital Foundations Project (PAFEN) Implementation Status Report]], [[2025-06-18 Burundi's Land Services Go Digital with New Integrated Platform]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mid-term review — 61% committed (2026-03-26).** [[finance.new]] A World Bank mission put PAFEN at a
  **61% budget-commitment** rate at mid-term, prioritising public-finance-management modernisation and the
  **national digital ID** rollout, and provided for a **national data centre**; target completion **2028**.
  Source: [[2026-03-27-world-bank-reviews-burundi-pafen-reform-program]].
- **Rural-connectivity contracts (2026).** [[digital.rural]] PAFEN signed lots 1–3 to connect ~**178 rural
  communes** to 4G (>800,000 people): **[[lumitel]]** (>$10m PPP, 86 hills / 14 provinces / ~300 schools)
  and **[[onatel-burundi]]** ($5.9m, 92 hills / 372,035 people). Sources:
  [[2026-03-04-lumitel-burundi-10m-high-speed-internet-project]],
  [[2026-05-05-onatel-5-9m-deal-digital-access-rural-burundi]].
- **Funds BurundiPay + e-KORI + BERNET.** [[dpi.pay]] PAFEN financed the [[bank-of-the-republic-of-burundi|BRB]]'s
  **[[burundipay]]** instant-payment rail and the [[obr-burundi|OBR]] e-KORI tax platform, and supports the
  **BERNET** network linking 14 universities. Sources:
  [[2026-05-01-internet-haut-debit-pafen-couvrir-80-du-territoire]],
  [[2026-06-12-burundi-eco-digitalisation-services-publics]].
- **PFM modernisation — the SIGFP_BI IFMIS, contested (2026-04).** [[dpi.mis]] PAFEN's public-finance-management
  sub-component finances a **Système intégré de Gestion des Finances Publiques (SIGFP_BI)**, envelope put at
  **~US$30m**. Its gré-à-gré procurement drew allegations of favouritism/conflict of interest around awardee
  **[[mediabox]]** from the NGO King Umurundi Freedom (reported, not established) — the governance risk sitting
  inside the World Bank money. Source: [[2026-04-20-digital-business-africa-burundi-sigfp-bi-gre-a-gre]].
