# Eswatini National Health Sector Strategic Plan 2024/25-2027/28 (NHSSP)

**Eswatini's health-sector strategy**, published by [[ministry-of-health-eswatini]]
(held at **2024-08**, month precision) — the policy frame under which the country's
community-health digitisation sits. See [[SWZ]], [[gov.policy]], [[dpi.mis]].

## Standing in this wiki

- **The strategic home of CBHIS/CMIS.** The NHSSP is the strategy under which the
  [[eswatini-cbhis]] and [[eswatini-cmis]] work reported in the World Bank's
  **2026-02-23** feature sits. Source:
  [[2026-02-23 From Paper to Pixels - Digital Transformation of Community Health Services in Eswatini]].
- **Held as an artefact.** The plan itself is in `raw/` with a companion source page; no
  clip in the wiki reports on its launch or extracts its targets, so no NHSSP indicators
  are carried here. Its four-year window (FY2024/25–FY2027/28) overlaps the
  [[eswatini-digitalisation-strategy-2024-2028]], and health is one of the sectors the
  [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]] says an interoperability layer should integrate. See
  [[dpi.exchange]].
