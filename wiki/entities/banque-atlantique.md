# Banque Atlantique

A pan-African bank, subsidiary of Morocco's **Groupe Banque Centrale Populaire (BCP)**, present across all
**eight WAEMU/UEMOA states** (Benin, Burkina Faso, Côte d'Ivoire, Guinea-Bissau, Mali, Niger, Senegal, Togo).

- **Bank-to-wallet interoperability with NITA (Niger, 2026-06).** Banque Atlantique Niger (DG **Mamadou Koné**)
  and **[[nita-niger|NITA]]** launched **"Bank to Wallet" / "Wallet to Bank"** services (26 Jun 2026) — instant
  interoperable transfers between bank accounts and mobile wallets — framed as advancing financial inclusion. See
  [[dpi.pay]], [[niger--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2026-06-29-banque-atlantique-nita-bank-to-wallet-interoperability-lesahel]].
