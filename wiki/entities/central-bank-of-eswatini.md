# Central Bank of Eswatini (CBE)

**Eswatini's central bank and the sponsor of its domestic payment switch** ([[SWZ]]).
Governor: **[[phil-mnisi]]** (as of 2024-12-11), who also chaired the [[comesa]]
Committee of Governors of Central Banks at that date. The CBE drove
[[eswatini-payment-switch]] with the domestic payments industry under the **National
Payment Systems Strategy 2022–2025**. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Payment switch sponsor.** The CBE, with the Eswatini payments industry, embarked on
  a project to implement a domestic payment switch, aligned to the National Payment
  Systems Strategy 2022–2025. Stated objectives: interconnectedness and
  interoperability among banks and non-banks; payments processed within seconds toward a
  **cash-lite economy**; 24/7 availability. Implementation service provider
  [[paylogic]], with technical assistance from the [[world-bank]]. See
  [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-14 Phil Mnisi - Eswatini Payment Switch Fast Payments product launch]].
- **Mandated participation.** The CBE mandates participation in Fast Payments for all
  payment service providers offering local payments — a regulator-forced
  interoperability, not a voluntary scheme. Detail on phasing, limits and participants
  is on [[eswatini-payment-switch]]. See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-14 Phil Mnisi - Eswatini Payment Switch Fast Payments product launch]].
