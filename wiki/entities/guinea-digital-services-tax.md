# Guinea 3% foreign-digital-services tax (RCN) & Digital Sovereignty Fund

A **decree signed 21 May 2026** (President Doumbouya) introduces a **Digital Compliance Fee (RCN)** — a **3% tax
on foreign digital services** used in Guinea: **streaming, cloud, SaaS subscriptions, digital advertising and
e-commerce**. A flat **3% for the first 12 months**, then a **tiered 1.5%–7%** by service category; providers
below **250m GNF (~$28,600)** annual taxable revenue (and some educational/non-profit services) are exempt. The
decree also creates a **Digital Sovereignty Fund (FSN)** and a **PARN monitoring platform** — a
revenue-and-sovereignty play targeting foreign Big Tech. See [[GIN]].
