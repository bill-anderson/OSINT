# Finnfund

Finnish development financier that invests in private-sector projects in emerging
markets, with a focus on climate resilience and digital inclusion. In Africa it
channels finance alongside EU instruments such as the European Fund for
Sustainable Development Plus (EFSD+) under the Global Gateway strategy.

## Activity in this wiki

- **$5m follow-on for solar-hybrid telecom energy in South Sudan (2026-02-20).**
  Finnfund committed a $5m follow-on investment in [[crei-south-sudan]]
  (Communication and Renewable Energy Infrastructure), taking its total
  investment to $10m. The financing — backed by the EU's EFSD+ under the Africa
  Connected programme — funds the modernisation and expansion of solar-hybrid
  energy systems across [[mtn-group]] South Sudan's 499 telecom sites, under a
  10-year Energy-as-a-Service contract ([[SSD]], [[finance.new]],
  [[infra.energy]]). Source: [[2026-02-20 South Sudan green network gets $5m boost]].
- **Cassava $90m equity round (2024-12).** Finnfund joined **[[us-dfc]]** and
  **[[google]]** (a direct stake) in a **$90m** equity round into
  **[[cassava-technologies]]** — part of a **$310m** raise to expand data centres, fibre
  and AI infrastructure across Africa (including the Google-linked **Umoja** terrestrial
  cable). See [[cassava-technologies]], [[infra.store]], [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-17 Google joins $90M investment into Cassava to bolster Africa's digital infrastructure]].
- **Nigeria–Finland digital MoU (2026).** Finnfund (with Finnvera) featured in the Helsinki
  engagements underpinning a Nigeria–Finland digital-innovation MoU
  ([[nigeria-finland-digital-mou]]) on DPI, digital government and cybersecurity. See
  [[NGA]], [[finance.mou]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-01 Nigeria Signs MoU With Finland On Digital Innovation, Deepens Bilateral Ties]].
