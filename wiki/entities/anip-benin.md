# Agence Nationale d'Identification des Personnes (ANIP)

Benin's national identity authority, created by the **2017 Identification Law**
(which also introduced a unique personal identifier for every resident, citizen
and expatriate). Directed by **Aristide Guy Adjinacou**, ANIP runs the National
Register of Natural Persons (RNPP) and is the coordinating body for [[BEN]]'s
foundational-ID and civil-registration systems. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coverage (as of ID4Africa 2025, June 2025).** RNPP covers **~98% of the
  population**; **10m+** identity documents issued; **~200,000 biometric
  authentications/day** via mobile operators; register connected to **80+**
  public and private institutions. Under the 2017 law, ANIP enrolled **10m+**
  people in six months. Goal: legal identity for every citizen by **2028**.
  See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-18 Benin envisages mobile wallet launch in inclusive national digital ID push]].
- **Digital-channel shift (2025 figures, reported 2026-02).** ANIP reports
  **≥75%** of identification services accessed digitally in 2025 — split **37%**
  government portal / **30%** ANIP BJ app / **8%** USSD; **10.5m** civil-status
  and ID services issued; 4m citizens reached; 3.8m telecom authentications and
  **25m eKYC** verifications. Sources:
  [[2026-02-02 Benin reports 75% of identification services accessed through digital platforms]],
  [[2026-01-28 Benin Advances E-Government With Mass Digital ID Adoption]] (corroborating outlet; channel split 37% portal / 30% app / 8% USSD / 25% counters).
- **Foundational ID via WURI (2024–25).** Through the [[wuri-programme]] the
  QR-code fID certificate ("Certificat NPI/fID" / "C'est moi") plus 2.2m extra
  enrolments brought biometric enrolment to **near-99%**. [[world-bank]] donated
  **2,050 mobile biometric terminals** to ANIP (Oct 2024) to feed births,
  marriages and deaths into the RNPP/RNEC. Sources:
  [[2024-06-27 WURI digital ID project improving access to public services in Benin]],
  [[2025-11-01 Identification System in Benin Increasing Access to Services]],
  [[2024-10-28 Benin receives 2,050 biometric terminals from World Bank to boost civil registration]].
- **Mobile wallet planned (~end 2026).** ANIP flags a citizen mobile wallet as
  the missing module of its architecture — a secure tool to store and present
  credentials — targeted for end-2026. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-18 Benin envisages mobile wallet launch in inclusive national digital ID push]].
- **ID backbone for ePass (2025).** ANIP's national ID system underpins the
  [[epass-benin]] diaspora passport-renewal app (built by [[seamfix]]),
  registration keyed to national digital ID. Sources:
  [[2025-10-24 Benin unveils platform to ease biometric passport applications for diaspora citizens]],
  [[2025-12-18 Seamfix revealed as developer of Benin's biometric passport renewal app]].
- **Electoral platforms modernised (2025).** ANIP presented (Jul 2025) new digital platforms making
  the **RNPP the single source of electoral data** (law 2017-08, arts. 41–42): citizens find their NPI,
  view/change polling station and obtain voting documents, with a **USSD *151#** channel for the
  unconnected and **interoperability with the Register of Beninese Abroad** for diaspora transfers.
  Definitive computerised electoral list displayed 12 Nov 2025, ahead of the 2026 elections. See
  [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2025-08-02 Benin - ANIP unveils digital platforms to modernise the electoral process]].
- **Foundational instrument (backfill).** The free, unlimited-validity **Certificat du NPI (Cnpi/fID)**
  — the "C'est moi" card — is established by article 6 of **décret 2020-099** (26 Feb 2020) as the base
  for electronic identity. Source: [[2024-05-06 Benin - the NPI certificate as the base for electronic identity]].
