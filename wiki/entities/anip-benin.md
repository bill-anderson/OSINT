# Agence Nationale d'Identification des Personnes (ANIP)

Benin's national identity authority, created by the **2017 Identification Law**
(which also introduced a unique personal identifier for every resident, citizen
and expatriate). Directed by **Aristide Guy Adjinacou** (also **National Civil
Registrar**), ANIP runs the National Register of Natural Persons (RNPP) and is the
coordinating body for [[BEN]]'s foundational-ID and civil-registration systems. Its
defining trait is **governance**: **directly attached to the presidency**, it is the
**single institution** for all identity matters and holds the population register,
civil registry and electoral register as **one integrated system**. See [[dpi.id]],
[[dpi.registry]], [[benin--dpi-id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coverage (as of ID4Africa 2025, June 2025).** RNPP covers **~98% of the
  population**; **10m+** identity documents issued; **~200,000 biometric
  authentications/day** via mobile operators; register connected to **80+**
  public and private institutions. Under the 2017 law, ANIP enrolled **10m+**
  people in six months. Goal: legal identity for every citizen by **2028**.
  See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-18 Benin envisages mobile wallet launch in inclusive national digital ID push]].
- **Digital-channel shift (2025 figures, reported 2026-02).** ANIP reports
  **≥75%** of identification services accessed digitally in 2025 — split **37%**
  government portal / **30%** ANIP BJ app / **8%** USSD; **10.5m** civil-status
  and ID services issued; 4m citizens reached; 3.8m telecom authentications and
  **25m eKYC** verifications. Sources:
  [[2026-02-02 Benin reports 75% of identification services accessed through digital platforms]],
  [[2026-01-28 Benin Advances E-Government With Mass Digital ID Adoption]] (corroborating outlet; channel split 37% portal / 30% app / 8% USSD / 25% counters); origin national-paper coverage in [[2026-01-29-anip-digitalisation-quatre-millions-beninois]] and [[2026-01-28-anip-numerisation-etat-civil-4-millions]].
- **Foundational ID via WURI (2024–25).** Through the [[wuri-programme]] the
  QR-code fID certificate ("Certificat NPI/fID" / "C'est moi") plus 2.2m extra
  enrolments brought biometric enrolment to **near-99%**. [[world-bank]] donated
  **2,050 mobile biometric terminals** to ANIP (Oct 2024) to feed births,
  marriages and deaths into the RNPP/RNEC. Sources:
  [[2024-06-27 WURI digital ID project improving access to public services in Benin]],
  [[2025-11-01 Identification System in Benin Increasing Access to Services]],
  [[2024-10-28 Benin receives 2,050 biometric terminals from World Bank to boost civil registration]].
- **Mobile wallet planned (~end 2026).** ANIP flags a citizen mobile wallet as
  the missing module of its architecture — a secure tool to store and present
  credentials — targeted for end-2026. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-18 Benin envisages mobile wallet launch in inclusive national digital ID push]].
- **ID backbone for ePass (2025).** ANIP's national ID system underpins the
  [[epass-benin]] diaspora passport-renewal app (built by [[seamfix]]),
  registration keyed to national digital ID. Sources:
  [[2025-10-24 Benin unveils platform to ease biometric passport applications for diaspora citizens]],
  [[2025-12-18 Seamfix revealed as developer of Benin's biometric passport renewal app]].
- **Electoral platforms modernised (2025) → the 2026 poll under regional scrutiny.** ANIP presented
  (Jul 2025) new digital platforms making the **RNPP the single source of electoral data** (law 2017-08,
  arts. 41–42): citizens find their NPI, view/change polling station and obtain voting documents, with a
  **USSD *151#** channel and **interoperability with the Register of Beninese Abroad**. For the **12 Apr
  2026 presidential poll**, **ECOWAS** (Senegal/Nigeria/Guinea experts, 6 Apr) and an **African Union**
  mission (led by ex-Burundi president Ntibantunganya, 10 Apr) assessed the integrated register and
  pronounced satisfaction; ANIP waived CIP "C'est Moi" fees and opened photo-update/web/mobile/USSD to
  polling morning. Sources:
  [[2025-08-02 Benin - ANIP unveils digital platforms to modernise the electoral process]],
  [[2026-04-11-techreviewafrica-ecowas-au-benin-electoral-roll]],
  [[2026-04-08-anip-voter-identification-scrutin]].
- **Governance-first model (ID4Africa 2026-05).** [[gov.policy]] Adjinacou framed Benin's system as "a
  structural decision, not a technical one" — one agency owning all identity, presidency-attached, the
  DG doubling as National Civil Registrar. On that basis Benin joined the UNDP/World Bank **50-in-5**
  campaign (Jun 2026) as the **16th African member** (via [[asin-benin]]); it has issued the
  **ECOWAS-standard biometric card since 2020** and inaugurated an **AFIS** (30 Sep 2025) wired into
  ECOWAS **SIPAO**. Sources: [[2026-05-12-biometricupdate-id4africa-2026-benin-anip-governance]],
  [[2026-06-02-biometricupdate-benin-joins-50-in-5]],
  [[2025-10-02-afis-empreintes-digitales-sipao-cedeao]].
- **Register extended to foreigners/Afro-descendants — Law 2025-12 (2025-06).** [[gov.legislate]] Amends
  art. 6 of the 2017 ID law to enrol **foreigners and naturalised-by-recognition persons** in the
  biometric register (a **résidents file**, distinct foreigner and Afro-descendant PINs) for migration
  control and civic inclusion. Source: [[2025-06-23-article-6-loi-identification-retouche]].
- **CRVS — the birth-registration gap and the guichet unique (2025→26).** [[dpi.registry]] Behind
  ~98–99% *biometric* coverage sits weak *civil* registration: **2021–2025 ~2.5m births declared but
  <1m registered** (parents had to make a second trip to ANIP). A **guichet unique** at maternities
  (WURI/UNICEF/World Bank) lifted the rate to **76.56%** in a 10-maternity pilot (vs ~22% nationally,
  2025); generalisation slipped 3–4 months into consolidation. Also RNPP opened to **banks** for KYC/AML
  (BCEAO/APBEF/CENTIF, Mar 2025). Sources: [[2026-07-16-adjinacou-maternite-identite-legale]],
  [[2026-02-26-guichet-unique-naissances-bilan-six-mois]], [[2025-03-27-bceao-apbef-anip-centif-rencontre]].
- **Foundational instrument (backfill).** The free, unlimited-validity **Certificat du NPI (Cnpi/fID)**
  — the "C'est moi" card — is established by article 6 of **décret 2020-099** (26 Feb 2020) as the base
  for electronic identity. Source: [[2024-05-06 Benin - the NPI certificate as the base for electronic identity]].
