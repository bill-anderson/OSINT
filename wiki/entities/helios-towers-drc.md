# Helios Towers (DR Congo)

The independent **towerco** underpinning DRC mobile coverage. A **$100m+ ANAPI investment convention**
(Mar 2026); ~**3,000 towers**, >**$0.7bn** invested since 2011; **>50 sites in mining zones**; pushing
**infrastructure-sharing** regulation with ARPTC and francophone regulators (FRATEL seminar 2026). *Note:
coverage is self-reported as **32m (May 2026) then 39m (Jun 2026)** on a static tower count — two
differently-constructed claims, not a real jump; write both dated.* Sources:
