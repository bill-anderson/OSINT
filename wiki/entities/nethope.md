# NetHope

NetHope, through its Center for the Digital Nonprofit, is a nonprofit-sector technology collaborative. It matters here for its evidence base on AI adoption in humanitarian work.

## Profile
- Published "Harnessing AI for Humanitarian Impact" (March 2026, with the UK Humanitarian Innovation Hub; author Meheret Takele Mandefro), synthesising 11 AI-in-humanitarian case studies.
- Headline finding: "data readiness trumps model sophistication," and responsible-AI governance is fragmented.
