# ENERCA (Énergie Centrafricaine)

The Central African Republic's national **electricity utility**. Only ~**14.3%** of CAR's
population has electricity access (~35% in Bangui, ~0.4% rural) — the binding constraint
behind CAR's weak connectivity and absent data-centre infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **World Bank PARSE project (2022).** The $138m Electricity Sector Strengthening and Access
  Project (PARSE) targets ENERCA's performance and capacity alongside five 10 MW mini-grids
  (Nola, Bouar, Bossembélé, Bangassou), transmission strengthening, expansion of the **Danzi
  solar plant** (25→40 MW), and off-grid solar for households, 300 schools and 300 health
  centres. Source:
  [[2022-06-03 Central African Republic - Increasing Electricity Supply and Access and Supporting the Health System]].
