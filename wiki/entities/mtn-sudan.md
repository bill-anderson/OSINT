# MTN Sudan

Sudanese subsidiary of the pan-African **MTN Group**; one of Sudan's three mobile
network operators alongside **[[zain-sudan]]** and **[[sudani-telecom]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Al-Manssa" digital-skills programme (2025-08).** Launched "Al-Manssa" (The
  Platform), a training initiative for Sudanese content creators — workshops in
  digital media and online marketing — developed, in the words of communications
  manager **Noon Al-Rashid**, out of "an urgent need to support local creators
  especially in the current climate" (i.e. the war). A rare
  **[[capacity.training]]** / creative-economy data point for Sudan. See
  [[include.access]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2025-08-18 MTN Sudan Expands Digital Empowerment Drive with Al-Manssa]].
