# Cassava AI

Cassava AI (also "Cassava Intelligence") is the AI arm of [[cassava-technologies]]. It matters as the operator of the group's sovereign AI-factory compute and its data-residency-focused AI products.

## Profile
- Runs GPU-accelerated AI-factory infrastructure across Africa and the Middle East.
- Owns the [[caimex]] LLM exchange and the [[national-sovereign-cloud]] product.
- Emphasises data localisation, residency and sovereignty.
- References Anthropic Claude among its models.
- Positions Cassava as the **continent's first NVIDIA Cloud Partner**; ships the **Cassava Autonomous Network** (an NVIDIA-backed agentic RAN-optimisation blueprint that runs on [[caimex]]).
