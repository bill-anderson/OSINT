# Bloggers of Zambia

Zambian digital-rights non-profit (est. 2017); publishes the annual *State of Internet Freedom in Zambia*
report.

## Activity in this wiki

- **State of Internet Freedom 2026 (6th ed.).** Tracks a contested new legal architecture ahead of the
  Aug-2026 elections: the **Cyber Security Act No. 3 / Cyber Crimes Act No. 4 of 2025** (ISP real-time
  interception; a Central Monitoring Centre) and a CCTV (Public Protection) Bill; the newly operational
  Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (even as Cabinet plans to repeal the 2021 Data Protection
  Act); plus a post-election **digital-ID deferral** (INRIS), rising fintech/deepfake fraud, and a
  ZANACO ransomware claim. Rated "Partly Free" (62/100). See [[ZMB]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-01 State of Internet Freedom in Zambia 2026 6th Edition]].
