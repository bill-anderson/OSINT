# Cloudoor (Door)

A local vendor marketing **"Door — the African sovereign cloud"** (GOS) across Côte d'Ivoire and Senegal —
the domestic-vendor entry in the CIV sovereign-cloud contest, alongside the state's US-financed National
Data Centre and the foreign operators at VITIB. *(Advertorial source; primaries named in file.)* Source:
