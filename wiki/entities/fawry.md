# Fawry

Egypt's **largest mobile-money / e-payment platform** — launched **2008** as an e-payment switch, now processing
**~6m transactions/day** and serving nearly half of Egypt's population. **2025 financials:** revenue **EGP 8.65bn
($166.7m)**, net revenue EGP 3.1bn, profit EGP 2.89bn; **market cap EGP 67.72bn ($1.3bn)**, up **1,029% since its
2019 EGX listing** — Egypt's **second-largest listed fintech** after state-owned **e-finance**. Expanded into
microfinance/consumer lending, an EGP 8bn securitisation programme and stakes across logistics/e-commerce (Bosta,
Waffarha, Roaderz), consolidated under **Fawry Holding for Financial Investments** (Nov 2025, FRA-approved). See
[[egypt--dpi-pay]].
