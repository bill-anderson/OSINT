# CENI (Commission Électorale Nationale Indépendante)

Burundi's **Independent National Electoral Commission** — operator of the voter register and the country's largest identity-data exercise, in a political context dominated by the ruling CNDD-FDD.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometric ID slipped past the 2025 vote.** In May 2024 CENI (president Prosper Ntahorwamiye) announced the **biometric national ID card would not be ready** for the 2025 voter enrolment, so registration used the current, easily-falsifiable ID card; the 2025 elections were budgeted at **114.4 billion FBu**, with population projected from the 2008 census (~8m) at 3%/yr to ~**13.2m**. See [[burundi--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2024-05-24 Burundi - biometric national ID card will not be available for 2025 voter enrolment]].
- **Coercion around registration (2025).** Card collection was extended by a day (to 15 May 2025) amid low turnout; SOS Médias documented citizens compelled to register (services conditioned on proof of registration; Imbonerakure checking documents) — a caveat on voter-data integrity. Source: [[2025-05-16 Burundi - CENI extends voter registration card collection by one day]].
