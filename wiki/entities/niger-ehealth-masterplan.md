# Niger National e-Health Master Plan 2025-2029

Niger's sectoral **digital-health strategy** (Schéma Directeur National de la e-Santé 2025-2029), a component of
the "Programme de la Refondation de la République 2025-2029" (Axis 4, social reforms). Its **budgeted roadmap**
comprises **27 structuring projects** across governance, health information systems, infrastructure, community
health, hospitals, laboratories, supply chain, **epidemiological surveillance**, human resources, and —
notably — **cybersecurity, interoperability and data use for decision-making**. Health Minister **Garba Hakimi**
framed it as a move "from dispersed projects to a genuine national policy of digital transformation of health."
A first substantive **[[dpi.mis]]** anchor for Niger. Source:
[[2026-07-09-national-e-health-master-plan-2025-2029-roadmap-lesahel]].
