# Zanzibar ICT Infrastructure Agency (ZICTIA)

The **Zanzibar Information and Communication Technology Infrastructure Agency** — the semi-autonomous
government's vehicle for broadband and digital-infrastructure PPPs on Unguja and Pemba. Director General
(2025): **Shukuru Awadh Suleiman**.

- **Airtel MoU (Aug 2025).** MoU with **[[airtel-tanzania]]** to expand broadband, digital inclusion and
  e-government/e-health/e-learning in Zanzibar. Source: [[2025-08-19-airtel-tanzania-zictia-broadband-zanzibar]].
- **Yas Fibre PPP (Jun 2026).** A **20-year, TZS 300bn (~$114.5m)** deal with **[[yas-tanzania|Yas
  Fibre]]** to connect ~100,000 households. Source: [[2026-06-25-yas-zictia-expand-broadband-zanzibar]].
