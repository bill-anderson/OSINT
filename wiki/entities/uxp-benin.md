# UXP Benin (national data-exchange dashboard)

UXP Benin is [[BEN]]'s national data-exchange platform, an instance of [[cybernetica]]'s UXP (Unified eXchange Platform, from the X-Road family). Its public directory dashboard (statistiques.xroad.bj) is a standing OSINT asset for Benin [[dpi.exchange]] metrics, exposing live counts of members, systems, services and requests.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Live directory statistics (as of 2026-07-11).** 138,174,337 total requests exchanged (32.7m in the last 30 days) across 73 members, 176 information systems and 512 services. Top providers were ANATT (transport), ANIP (identity agency) and DGI (tax); the top single service was SECUROUTE. Illustrates Benin's operational [[dpi.govtech]] backbone and generates ongoing [[data.statistics]]. Source: [[2026-07-11 UXP Directory]].
- **CDPI peer-learning webinar (2025-06-23).** In a Centre for Digital Public Infrastructure ([[cdpi]]) session, [[asin-benin]] officials detailed the UXP interoperability layer: **1,500+ services integrated, 55+ institutions connected, 300+ services fully digitized and 70M+ data-exchange transactions** completed by mid-2025. The webinar clarified that UXP is a Cybernetica commercial product *inspired by* Estonia's X-Road, not a fork of it. See [[dpi.exchange]], [[gov.regional]]. Source: [[2025-06-23 Benin shares its X-Road-inspired DPI interoperability experience in peer learning webinar]].
- **Downstream platforms (2025).** UXP underpins Benin's wider DPI cluster — the ePass diaspora biometric-passport app ([[seamfix]] GovSmart, Oct 2025), ASIN's Cyber Africa Forum cooperation deals, and the GIZ "Beninnovation" e-gov tender — all riding the shared data-exchange rails. Sources: [[2025-10-24 Benin unveils platform to ease biometric passport applications for diaspora citizens]], [[2025-09-01 Benin boosts digital inclusion, govt service access with cooperation deals]], [[2025-01-29 Deadline extended for Benin digital government transformation tender]].
