# Emptech (Emperor Technology Co., Ltd)

Chinese supplier of biometric-passport systems to the **[[alliance-of-sahel-states|Confederation of Sahel
States]]**. Emptech supplies **Mali's "E-AES" biometric passport**, launched 27 October 2025 (ICAO-compliant,
chip carrying fingerprints and photograph), **replacing France's [[idemia|Idemia]]** after a 2023 dispute in
which Mali accused Idemia of refusing to transfer biometric data — a change Malian officials framed as
"regained sovereignty" and local data management. Emptech is also reported to supply **Burkina Faso's** AES
passport (though one source says it is not behind Niger's or Mali's earlier documents).
