# Angola Cables

Angolan wholesale connectivity operator focused on **Atlantic submarine routes**, IP transit and cloud — a rare African-owned intercontinental carrier and a data-sovereignty talking point.

## Profile

- **Submarine systems:** operates/participates in the **SACS** (South Atlantic Cable System — the first subsea cable directly linking Africa to the Americas, Angola↔Brazil), plus WACS and Monet, giving an Angola–Brazil–USA corridor.
- **Facilities:** the **AngoNAP** data centre and the **Angonix** internet exchange point (IXP) in Luanda; a data centre in **Fortaleza, Brazil**; cloud/CDN services (Clouds2Africa, Host2Africa) and IP transit.
- Positions Luanda as an Atlantic interconnection hub; a partner in Africell's Angola build. Reference collateral: [[2022-11-01 Angola Cables - Commercial Catalogue 2022]].
- Claims its subsea + partner networks carry **>70% of Africa's internet/data traffic flows** (2026).
  CMO Samuel Carvalho pitches **"distributed, sovereign-compliant infrastructure"** — data localisation
  as a product — while CEO Ângelo Gama drives its US/Atlantic corridor.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Uniti Wholesale — the Africa–US corridor (2026-05).** Signed in Washington, D.C. with US fibre
  provider **[[uniti-fiber|Uniti Wholesale]]**, combining SACS/MONET/WACS with Uniti's **386,000 km**
  US fibre / 300+ metro markets for wholesale, enterprise, content and (notably) upstream oil-and-gas
  customers. See [[geopol.usa]], [[angola--infra-connect]]. Sources:
  [[2026-05-27-angola-cables-uniti-wholesale-parceria-de-conectividade]],
  [[2026-06-12-angola-cables-uniti-partnership-connectingafrica]].
- **Accelera Cloud Program (2025-12).** Free cloud credits + full migration assistance onto
  **Clouds2Africa** (billed as the continent's largest cloud), priced in **local currency** — a
  domestic-cloud demand play for SMEs/startups. See [[angola--infra-store]], [[infra.store]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-03-angola-cables-unveils-accelera-cloud-programme]].
- **Megaport NaaS on-ramps (2025-03).** Interconnection at NY/Miami/London giving access to
  **930 data centres / 300 cloud nodes** — the "super corridor." Source:
  [[2025-03-20-angola-cables-and-megaport-expand-global-reach]].
- **MEO Wholesale (Portugal) Atlantic corridor (2025-06).** A Portugal–Brazil–Africa–US "super
  business corridor" tying MEO's data centres to **AngoNAP Fortaleza** via the South Atlantic ring.
  See [[geopol.eu]]. Source: [[2025-06-23-angola-cables-meo-international-connectivity-deal]].
