# Angola Cables

Angolan wholesale connectivity operator focused on **Atlantic submarine routes**, IP transit and cloud — a rare African-owned intercontinental carrier and a data-sovereignty talking point.

## Profile

- **Submarine systems:** operates/participates in the **SACS** (South Atlantic Cable System — the first subsea cable directly linking Africa to the Americas, Angola↔Brazil), plus WACS and Monet, giving an Angola–Brazil–USA corridor.
- **Facilities:** the **AngoNAP** data centre and the **Angonix** internet exchange point (IXP) in Luanda; a data centre in **Fortaleza, Brazil**; cloud/CDN services (Clouds2Africa, Host2Africa) and IP transit.
- Positions Luanda as an Atlantic interconnection hub; a partner in Africell's Angola build. Reference collateral: [[2022-11-01 Angola Cables - Commercial Catalogue 2022]].
