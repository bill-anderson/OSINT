# Gabon–Visa Financial Inclusion Partnership

**Parties:** the Government of Gabon ([[GAB]]) and **[[visa]]** (US payments network). **What:** a
partnership to accelerate financial inclusion — reported to cover **modernising tax collection,
automating disbursements, and introducing a secure digital-identity system**. **When/where:**
concluded at the **Africa CEO Forum in Côte d'Ivoire, May 2025**. **Status:** active.

## Notes

- **Public-services focus (2025-07 follow-up).** A Visa delegation (VP & GM West/Central Africa **Ismahill
  Diaby**) met Gabon's VP **Alexandre Barro Chambrier** and Minister Doumba in Libreville (18 Jul 2025) to
  deepen the accord toward **digitalising public services** and modernising the administration — corroborating
  the May-2025 Africa CEO Forum signing with a named primary account. Source:
  [[2025-07-20-gabon-visa-partnership-public-services]].
- Otherwise reported second-hand in the December 2025 [[uncdf]] and MADIGIPAIE coverage; the **digital-identity
  element** in particular should still be **corroborated against a primary source** before hardening. Visa also
  appears among private partners approached for Gabon's 2026 infrastructure build
  ([[2025-10-24-gabon-2026-digital-budget-156pct]]).
- Part of a dense late-2025 financial-inclusion cluster with the [[gabon-uncdf-financial-inclusion-deal]]
  and [[madigipaie-gabon]] — US ([[geopol.usa]]) and UN channels arriving together.
