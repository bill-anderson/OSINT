# UGPass

**Uganda's government digital authentication and e-signature platform**, run by
[[nita-uganda]] — the trust layer that sits alongside the [[ughub]] data-exchange
platform. It provides authentication over **OpenID Connect** and **OAuth**, and an
**e-seal signing portal**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Platform description (undated deck, `date_source: proxy`).** In the joint
  [[nira-uganda]] / NITA-U deck, UGPass is presented as the digital authentication and
  e-signature platform (OpenID Connect, OAuth, e-seal signing portal), companion to UGHub
  as Uganda's government data and integration platform. Governance for the pair runs
  through a Cabinet Technical Sub-Committee Working Group headed by the Ministry of ICT.
  *The deck carries no date on any slide and no recoverable PDF metadata; it is dated to
  the ingest date as a proxy and is not a publication date.* See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[dpi.id]], [[gov.standards]]. Source:
