# Diacente Group

Ugandan company developing **green industrial zones** — local partner in the **[[global-settlement-network]]**
tokenised-finance / CBDC-pilot plan.

- **$5.5bn plan (Oct 2025).** With GSN, a plan to build national digital infrastructure linking farms,
  mines, power and factories, and pilot Uganda's first **CBDC ("digital shilling")**, rolling out first in
  the **Karamoja Green** industrial zone. See [[uganda--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-08-uganda-5-5bn-digital-project-cbdc-shilling]].
