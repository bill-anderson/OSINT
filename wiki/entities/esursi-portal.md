# ESURSI (higher-education digital portal)

DR Congo's **higher-education digital portal + first national digital library** (live 30 Mar 2026, WB/AFD-
backed) — student ID, transcripts, equivalences — with DRC joining the UNESCO/UNICEF **"Gateways"**
initiative. A whole sector newly digitised. (Dates internet users at 34.7m / 30.5%, end-2025.) Source:
[[2026-04-02-rdc-portail-numerique-universitaire-esursi-wearetech]].
