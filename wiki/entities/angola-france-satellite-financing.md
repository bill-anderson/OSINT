# Angola–France satellite financing (Société Générale €225m)

The financing package behind **[[angeo-1]]**. Signed **23 January 2025** at the **Angola–France
Economic Forum** (concluding President [[joao-lourenco]]'s state visit) by Finance Minister **Vera
Daves de Sousa** with French bank **Société Générale**.

- **€225,257,201** total for the Earth-observation satellite system — design, manufacture, launch and
  in-orbit installation — carried out with **[[airbus]]** (split **€35,268,507 + €189,988,694**).
- Two further technical instruments were signed the same day: **Phase 2 of the National Meteorological
  Service Modernization Project** (seismology network) and rehabilitation/expansion of the **Luanda
  Medicine Quality Control Laboratory**.

The European financing pole (`geopol.eu`) in Angola's foreign-stack contest, alongside the Chinese
(civil registration, Sinohydro broadband), Indian (DPI credit) and Gulf/UAE (data-centre) tracks.

See [[angeo-1]], [[data.satellite]], [[AGO]], [[finance.new]].
