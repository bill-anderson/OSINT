# BNA (Banco Nacional de Angola)

Angola's **central bank** and operator of the national payments architecture — the Sistema de Pagamentos de Angola (SPA) and its real-time settlement rail (SPTR). Under governor **Manuel Tiago Dias**, the BNA is pushing a standards-and-sovereignty agenda: instant payments, cross-border integration and international messaging standards, framed explicitly around **monetary sovereignty**. It co-developed the **[[kwik-angola|KWiK]]** instant-payment system with **[[emis-angola]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **ISO 20022 migration completed (2026-07-10).** The BNA concluded migration of domestic payment operations to the **ISO 20022** international messaging standard on 10 July 2026, coordinated across [[emis-angola]], **BODIVA** (Bolsa de Dívida e Valores de Angola), the banking institutions and other SPTR participants — pitched as raising interoperability and transaction-data quality. Announced 2026-07-16, the most recent item in the Angola batch. See [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-07-16-bna-conclui-migracao-iso-20022]].
- **Kwanza to join SADC-RTGS (H2 2026).** At the Angola Banking Conference (2026-05-27) Tiago Dias announced the kwanza will integrate the **SADC Real-Time Gross Settlement** system from the second half of 2026, framed as **reinforcing monetary sovereignty** by cutting FX intermediation and letting Angolan firms transact regionally in national currency. He also flagged an **Open Banking** framework and an **AI-usage diagnostic** ahead of regulation. See [[angola--dpi-pay]], [[gov.regional]], [[sadc]]. Source: [[2026-05-27-kwanza-integra-sistema-de-pagamento-sadc-rtgs]].
- **BNA–DRC cross-border MoU (2026-07-08).** The BNA and the **Banque Centrale du Congo** (governor André Nkualoloki) signed an MoU on payment-system interoperability and cross-border payments — technical assistance, digital payment instruments, joint supervision — so Angolan and Congolese payment systems can communicate directly. See [[gov.regional]], [[COD]], [[finance.mou]]. Source: [[2026-07-08-bna-banco-central-rdc-memorando-de-entendimento]].
- **KWiK co-developer and inclusion driver.** KWiK is a BNA–EMIS build; its growth (35m transfers / 590bn Kz in 2025, >100% YoY) is tracked by the financial-inclusion committee (CCENIF) as a pillar of the **ENIF 2025–2027** strategy. See [[kwik-angola]], [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-01-29-operacoes-no-kwik-crescimento-acima-dos-100]].
