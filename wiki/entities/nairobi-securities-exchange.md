# Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE)

Kenya's principal **securities exchange** (CEO Frank Mwiti), regulated by the Capital Markets Authority.
See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Ziidi Trader — equities on M-Pesa (2026-02).** [[finance.new]] The NSE and **[[safaricom]]** launched
  **Ziidi Trader**, a platform on the M-Pesa app letting Kenyans buy/sell listed shares and corporate
  bonds from **as little as one share**, under Capital Markets Authority oversight — extending M-Pesa
  from payments/lending/savings into **retail capital-markets access**. See [[kenya--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-10-safaricom-nse-share-trading-mpesa-connectingafrica]].
