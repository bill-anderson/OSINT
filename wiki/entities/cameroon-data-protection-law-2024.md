# Cameroon Personal Data Protection Law (Law 2024/017)

Cameroon's **personal-data-protection law**, **Loi n°2024/017, enacted 23 Dec 2024** — the same day as
the civil-registration law ([[cameroon-civil-registration-law-2024]], Loi 2024/016), the two being the
paired late-2024 digital-governance statutes.

## Status — resolves an earlier open question

The wiki previously held the data-protection reform only at the **Nov-2024 draft stage**, and a separate
Jul-2025 source loosely referred to "the recent adoption of a data protection law" (MANIFEST Flag 3). The
**Minpostel's April-2025 National Assembly address names the enacted law by number and date — Loi
n°2024/017 of 23 Dec 2024** — which **confirms enactment**: the Nov-2024 draft became law, and the wiki
was simply stale. Recorded here as a staleness correction, not a contradiction. Source:
[[2025-04-08-leconomie-minpostel-minister-national-assembly-digital-inclusion]].

*Provenance note:* the confirmation is a cabinet minister's on-the-record citation before parliament; the
**official gazette text of Loi 2024/017 is not yet held** — sourcing it is a standing gap.
