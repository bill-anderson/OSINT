# Namibia Cyber Security Incident Response Team (Nam-CSIRT)

Namibia's national cyber-incident response team, **housed at and managed by [[cran]]** — the country's central
cyber-threat monitor, statistics publisher and breach-confirmation authority. Its **Namibia Cybersecurity
Landscape Report 2025** (released 11 Apr 2026) recorded **1.7 million cyberattacks** (up ~**37%** YoY) and
**2,231,970 vulnerabilities**; in **Q1 2026** it logged **103,085 threats and 367,670 vulnerabilities**, and it
issued the **FortiBleed** alert to 13 organisations (Jun 2026). It confirms major national breaches (e.g. the
Namibia Airports Company INC ransomware attack) and warns that expanding **digital ID and services** raise the
urgency of a whole-of-society cyber posture. A **government-sector CSIRT + SOCs** was approved by cabinet (Nov
2025) to sit alongside it. See **[[namibia--infra-cybersec]]**.
