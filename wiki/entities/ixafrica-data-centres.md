# iXAfrica Data Centres

Nairobi-based carrier-neutral, AI-ready data-centre operator, chaired by Guy
Willner. It positions its campus as sovereign compute capacity that keeps AI
workloads and regulated data within African borders.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Hosts Kenya's "first sovereign-hosted" AI cloud (2026-03-12).** iXAfrica's
  Nairobi campus hosts [[servernah-cloud]], launched in partnership with Atlancis
  Technologies and Everse Technology. The platform runs AI workloads near where
  African data is generated, combining EverseTech's AI-as-a-Service with
  iXAfrica's carrier-neutral, AI-ready infrastructure. Chairman Guy Willner
  framed it as letting "Africa shape its own digital destiny," with sovereignty
  cast as retaining talent, revenue and skills rather than local storage alone
  ([[KEN]], [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]], [[infra.capacity]]). Source:
  [[2026-03-12 iXAfrica hosts Kenya's first sovereign-hosted AI cloud]].
- **Host for OCI Nairobi — Kenya's first public cloud region (2026-01-28).** iXAfrica is
  named host/colocation partner for the **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) region in
  Nairobi**, Kenya's first public cloud region ([[ixafrica-oracle-oci-nairobi]]) — the region
  President Ruto announced in 2024. iXAfrica is described as East/Central Africa's largest
  hyperscale carrier-neutral AI-ready facility, with construction, power and connectivity in
  advanced stages (CEO Snehar Shah; David Bunei, [[oracle]] Kenya). See [[KEN]],
  [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-28 iXAfrica, Oracle to launch Kenya's first public cloud region]].

- **"First GPU infrastructure in Africa" (Russell Southwood, 2026-05-19).**
  Southwood's demand-side AI survey credits iXAfrica with installing **Africa's
  first GPU infrastructure in November 2025** — an early supply-side marker in the
  thin specialist-AI compute market. See [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-19 Trying to make sense of who will use Artificial Intelligence in Africa and how – Going beyond the hype]].
