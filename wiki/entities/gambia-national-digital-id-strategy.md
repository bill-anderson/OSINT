# Gambia National Digital ID and Digital Transformation Strategy 2023-2028

The Gambia's national strategy for a **secure, inclusive digital-identity ecosystem** and broader
digital transformation, initiated December 2022 by the Ministry of Communications and Digital Economy
with UN ECA support and **validated May 2024** ([[dpi.id]], [[gov.policy]]). Its pillars align with the
Recovery-focused National Development Plan; it is the policy frame behind the eCRVS unique-national-ID
backbone and the Margins/MIDSA national-ID build ([[gambia-national-id-contract]]), and sits under the
wider National Digital Economy Master Plan 2024-2034 ([[gambia-national-digital-economy-masterplan]]).
See [[GMB]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Validated (2024-05-17).** [[gov.policy]] A two-day Banjul workshop refined and validated action
  plans for both the Digital ID and Digital Transformation strategies, with stakeholders from all
  ministries, immigration, the Gambia Police Force and the newly-established **[[gicta]]**. Source:
  [[2024-05-17 Validation of The Gambia's National Digital ID and Digital Transformation Strategy]].
- **Evidence base.** A World Bank high-level assessment of The Gambia's identity-management system
  underpins the reform. Source: [[2025-10-20 ID4D-Diagnostic of ID Systems in The Gambia - Presentation (World Bank)]].
