# CERIST

Algeria's **scientific & technical information research centre** (under Higher Education & Scientific
Research; Minister Kamel Baddari inaugurated the platforms). In Mar 2025 it launched a **cloud-computing
platform** (OpenStack/Kubernetes/Linux), a **drone design & control system**, and a **business incubator**
(20 startups by end-2025, targeting 100/yr from 2027) — framed as sovereign, "autonomous and high-performance"
infrastructure. Also a cybersecurity R&D actor (its Proxylan/CERIST arm signed a 2025 MoU with Russia's
Positive Technologies). See [[algeria--infra-store]].
