# Rwanda National Data Sharing Policy

Rwanda’s public-sector data-sharing policy — positions government data as **open by default** (subject
to classification and access controls) and institutions as **stewards, not owners**. Operationalised
through the Data Governance Unit at the [[nisr]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Implementation phase (2026-05).** Requires institutions to catalogue and classify datasets, set
  access rights, and staff internal data teams (chief data officers, stewards, custodians) before
  sharing via a central platform. The hard shift is behavioural — ownership to stewardship. See
  [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-07 What will determine the success of Rwanda’s data governance push]].
