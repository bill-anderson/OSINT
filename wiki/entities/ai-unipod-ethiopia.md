# AI UniPod Ethiopia

The Artificial Intelligence University Innovation Pod (AI UniPod) in Addis Ababa — a
national innovation centre for AI research, talent development and locally-built
technological solutions, established under [[digital-ethiopia-2030]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (2026-02-19).** Ethiopia's Deputy PM **Temesgen Tiruneh** announced the AI
  UniPod as a collaborative initiative of the **[[ethiopian-ai-institute]]**, **Addis
  Ababa University** and **[[undp]]**, in partnership with the **Timbuktoo Initiative**.
  Framed within DE2030 and the economic-reform agenda as a hub for innovation, research
  and youth talent. See [[tech.ai]], [[tech.innovate]], [[capacity.research]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-19 Ethiopia launches AI UniPod to drive innovation]].
- **Sovereignty framing.** Tiruneh cast the centre as a sovereignty project — "in this
  century, sovereignty means more than borders, it means **owning our data, designing our
  algorithms** and solving our challenges with our own solutions." See [[sovereignty]].
  Source: [[2026-02-19 Ethiopia launches AI UniPod to drive innovation]].
