# MTN Uganda

**Uganda's largest mobile operator** and, through **MTN MoMo**, the dominant mobile-money
platform — the private rail on which much of Uganda's payment and now tax collection runs.
Part of [[mtn-group]]. MTN MoMo Uganda Managing Director (2025-12): **Richard Yego**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Share of e-money (FY2024/25, reported 2025-10-06).** Per [[bank-of-uganda]]'s
  Integrated Annual Report, MTN Uganda **handles 40% of the country's e-money
  transactions** and reported a **28.6% rise in value to UGX 319.6 trillion**.
  **INTERNAL INCONSISTENCY IN THE SOURCE — carried as reported:** national e-money is
  given as **UGX 326tn (+28.6%)** and MTN's as **UGX 319.6tn (+28.6%)** while MTN is said
  to handle **40%** — two near-identical values that cannot both hold with a 40% share.
  **Do not compute derived shares from these figures.** See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-06 BoU's New Payment System Cuts Delays and Boosts E-Money by 28.6%]].
- **Tax collection on MoMo (2025-12-23).** With [[ura-uganda]], MTN launched enhanced tax
  payment services on MTN MoMo — USSD ***165*18#**, free, on any phone, routing directly
  from MoMo to URA and the bank; the existing ***285#** retained. MD Richard Yego said the
  integration aligns with MTN's ambition of a **cash-light economy**, placing modern
  financial tools *"directly in the hands of over 14 million customers and thousands of
  small businesses"* (as of 2025-12-23). See [[john-musinguzi]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-23 URA Launches Enhanced Tax Payment Services on MTN MoMo to Boost Digital Compliance]].
- **Data-centre footprint (as of 2024-08-12).** **MTN claims six data centers in Uganda
  with capacity for 972 racks across them** — reported in coverage of [[liquid-c2]]'s Azure
  Stack launch, as context on where Ugandan cloud capacity sits. Company claim —
  attribute. See [[infra.store]]. Source:
  [[2024-08-12 Liquid Intelligent Technologies launches Azure Stack in Uganda]].
