# Google DeepMind

[[google]]'s AI research division. In the Africa context it is the lead
developer of the [[google-ai-research-foundations]] curriculum, the anchor of
Google's advanced AI-education push on the continent.

## Activity in this wiki

- **AI Research Foundations rollout in Africa (2026-03-17).** Google DeepMind
  announced additional courses of its [[google-ai-research-foundations]]
  curriculum for technical learners across Africa — free on Google Skills,
  co-developed with University College London pedagogy experts, localised to
  African contexts with the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS),
  and scaled via a "train-the-trainer" model with Google.org-funded lecturer
  toolkits. The programme moves beyond AI literacy to hands-on work building and
  fine-tuning language models. Framing statistic: Africa holds ~1/5 of the world
  population but contributes only 1.8% of AI research (SCImago). AI in Society
  Pillar lead **Melanie Eusebe** authored the announcement. See [[tech.ai]],
  [[capacity.research]], [[capacity.training]], [[XAF]].
  Source: [[2026-03-17 Broadening advanced AI education across Africa]].
