# ILX Fund

Amsterdam-based **impact private-credit fund** that co-invests alongside multilateral
development banks in emerging-market infrastructure, mobilising institutional capital into
development-finance loan syndications (CIO **Kirstine Damkjaer**).

## Activity in this wiki

- **B-loan participant in the EBRD–Yas facility (2026-07).** ILX takes a "sizeable position"
  in the **up-to-€50m B-loan** syndicated under the **[[ebrd-yas-network-loan]]** (Kenya/Senegal
  4G/5G/fibre) — the institutional-capital-mobilisation leg of the transaction. Damkjaer cited
  "continued confidence in African digital infrastructure." See [[finance.new]], [[infra.connect]],
  [[KEN]], [[SEN]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 EBRD Extends €270m Financing to Yas for Kenya, Senegal Network Expansion]].
