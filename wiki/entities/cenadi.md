# CENADI (National Centre for Computer Development)

Cameroon's **National Centre for Computer Development** (Director Chantal Marguerite Mveh; chaired by
Finance Minister Motazé) — an actor new to the wiki that produced the **evidentiary base under the
country's data-sovereignty rhetoric**, and a damning one.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Data-centre ecosystem study (May 2025).** A **three-year, 269-page study** of Cameroon's data-centre
  ecosystem, restituted May 2025, found the reality behind the sovereign-cloud ambition to be weak:
  **uncertified small facilities, inadequate power, thin bandwidth** — cooling and energy constraints
  chief among them. It is the sober counterweight to the CAMTEL/Zamengoe and ST Digital sovereignty
  claims: Cameroon wants sovereign hosting but the physical base is fragile. Sources:
