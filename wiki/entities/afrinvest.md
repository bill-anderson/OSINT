# Afrinvest

Nigerian investment firm running retail-facing digital investment apps — **PlutusNeo** (offering
US mutual funds) and **Afrinvestor 2.0** (Nigerian equities).

On file (2026-07-18) as part of the incumbent-digitisation pattern in Nigerian retail investing: the
apps let salaried users run small fixed monthly contributions — one investor on record puts **₦20,000
a month into US mutual funds via PlutusNeo and another ₦20,000 into Nigerian equities via Afrinvestor
2.0** — with domestic and offshore exposure held side by side in the same phone.
