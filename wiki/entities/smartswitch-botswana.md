# SmartSwitch Botswana

A **fintech operator** (subsidiary of **Lesaka Technologies**) running biometric smart-card payments on Botswana's Universal Electronic Payment System (UEPS).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometric social-benefit payments (2025).** More than **75,000 food-voucher beneficiaries** completed digital purchases at local stores via SmartSwitch's biometrics-enabled smart-card system on UEPS — biometric-linked G2P payments advancing financial inclusion (the ID-payments nexus). Fuller account: **1,200+ shops**, the UEPS platform **accredited by the central banks of Namibia, Ghana and South Africa**, a new tender pending; procured via the Ministry of Local Government. See [[dpi.pay]].
