# LEAP (Livelihood Empowerment Against Poverty, Ghana)

Ghana's flagship social-protection cash-transfer programme, run by the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection (MoGCSP) and a pillar of the Government's Reset Agenda. It pays bi-monthly, household-size-structured grants to poor and vulnerable households, increasingly on **mobile-money-enabled government-to-person (G2P) rails** ([[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]]) — a concrete instance of Ghana's payments DPI carrying welfare delivery. LEAP has also been used as an integration point for the [[ghana-card]] and NHIS (the LEAP 1000 sub-programme). See [[GHA]], [[ghana--dpi-pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **99th payment cycle (2025-12-22).** Nationwide disbursement from 22 Dec 2025: **GH¢139.3m** released to **~350,580 households (~1.5m people)**; grants GH¢320-530 by household size. The Ministry of Finance allocated **GH¢1.1bn** to LEAP for the period (highest since inception), with coverage set to expand to **400,000 households in 2026**.
