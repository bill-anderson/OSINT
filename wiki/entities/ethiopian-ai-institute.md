# Ethiopian Artificial Intelligence Institute

Ethiopia's state AI body; developer of the **MESOB** unified digital-services
platform, positioned as homegrown/indigenous digital-infrastructure capacity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **MESOB Platform launch (2026-06).** The Institute built the **[[mesob-platform]]**,
  an AI-driven, integrated public-service platform inaugurated by PM
  **[[abiy-ahmed]]**, framed to cut reliance on external platforms and serve as a
  model for other African states. DG Worqou Gatchena. See [[ETH]],
  [[dpi.govtech]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-20 Ethiopia launches unified MESOB Platform to accelerate national digital transformation]].
- **AI UniPod launched (2026-02).** With Addis Ababa University, [[undp]] and the Timbuktoo
  Initiative, the Institute launched an **AI University Innovation Pod**
  ([[ai-unipod-ethiopia]]) in Addis Ababa — a national centre for AI innovation, research
  and talent under [[digital-ethiopia-2030]]. Deputy PM Temesgen Tiruneh framed sovereignty
  as "owning our data, designing our algorithms." See [[tech.ai]], [[tech.innovate]],
  [[capacity.research]]. Source: [[2026-02-19 Ethiopia launches AI UniPod to drive innovation]].
- **Data-strategy technical committee (2025).** The Institute sat on the technical committee
  (with [[insa-ethiopia]], MiNT and Addis Ababa University) that drafted Ethiopia's first
  national data-governance strategy under the Office of the PM, later expanded into a full
  draft with [[dial]]. See [[gov.policy]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-18 Ethiopia's approach to their National Data Strategy offers lessons for Africa's digital future]].

See [[ETH]], [[mesob-platform]], [[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]], [[tech.ai]].
