# Paratus Eswatini

A joint venture (launched 25 Jul 2025) between the pan-African **Paratus Group** and local ISP **Real
Image Internet**, and an **authorised Starlink Business reseller** offering fully-managed satellite
connectivity (hardware, install, 24/7 support) — extending reach "from remote schools and clinics to
urban enterprises" (EduLINK schools). See [[eswatini--infra-connect]], [[SWZ]].
