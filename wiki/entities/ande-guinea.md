# ANDE (Agence Nationale de Digitalisation de l'État)

Guinea's **state digitalisation agency** — one of the delivery bodies behind the public-administration
modernisation drive under **[[simandou-2040]]**, cited among the **nine digital projects** (GUSP, Gouv-Lab,
e-Tax, HR-system modernisation, the [[fugas|FUGAS]] payroll) presented May 2026. See [[guinea--dpi-govtech]].
