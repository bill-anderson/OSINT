# Sociedade Interbancária de Moçambique (SIMO)

Operator of SIMOrede, Mozambique's unified national electronic payments network,
which handles interoperability, clearing and settlement across the country's
financial sector. As a national payments-rail operator it carries a governance
mandate over shared banking infrastructure serving a population of 36.6 million.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Adopting biometric IAM across its environment (2026-02-24).** SIMO signed a
  multi-year agreement with US vendor [[bio-key-international]] and integrator
  [[runlevel]] to deploy biometric identity and access management (PortalGuard,
  PIN:You tokenless authentication, SSO) into its environment, securing access
  for banks participating in SIMOrede. CISO Juna Chiloveque framed it as
  strengthening governance and supporting "the secure digital transformation of
  our national payments infrastructure" ([[MOZ]], [[dpi.pay]],
  [[infra.cybersec]]). Source: [[2026-02-24 BIO-key and RunLevel Deploy Biometric IAM Across Mozambique's National Payments Network]].
