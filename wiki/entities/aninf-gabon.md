# ANINF (National Agency for Digital Infrastructure and Frequencies, Gabon)

Gabon's **Agence Nationale des Infrastructures Numériques et des Fréquences** — the state agency
responsible for national digital infrastructure, spectrum/frequencies and, in practice, the technical
delivery of cross-government digital systems.

## Activity in this wiki

- **RSU (2025-08-28).** ANINF is a core partner in delivering the **[[registre-social-unique-gabon]]**
  social registry, providing the digital-infrastructure backbone alongside the World Bank and the
  relevant social ministries.
- **Electoral-roll platform (2025).** ANINF/SSI published the online electoral-roll verification
  service (`consultation.listelectorale.ga`), keyed on the national **NIP** — linking the register to
  the CNIE / national digital-ID system ([[2025-01-01-gabon-electoral-roll-verification-platform]]).
