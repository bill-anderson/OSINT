# ODI Global

London-based global-affairs think tank (formerly Overseas Development Institute) publishing on African
digital trade, DPI, payments/identity and infrastructure finance.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cross-border payments & identity in Nigeria under the AfCFTA (2026-03).** Nigeria's constraint is
  misalignment (a corporate-ID "noodle bowl"; only 561 LEIs vs 3m+ firms), not infrastructure. See
  [[NGA]], [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2026-03-01 Cross-border digital payments and identity in Nigeria under the AfCFTA]].
- **DPI at the G20 (2026-05).** Traced DPI through the India–Brazil–South Africa troika; African
  pushback on exporting India's model; data governance as the neglected "third pillar." Source:
  [[2026-05-29 Digital Public Infrastructure at the G20 tensions commitments and continuities]].
- **China's infrastructure finance in Africa (2026-02).** See [[geopol.china]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-05 Chinas infrastructure finance in Africa impacts and lessons]].
