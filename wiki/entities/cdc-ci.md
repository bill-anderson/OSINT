# CDC-CI (Caisse des Dépôts et Consignations)

Côte d'Ivoire's **state deposit-and-investment fund** — a public investment vehicle now taking **equity in
fintechs** to drive financial inclusion. Took a stake in **[[green-pay|Green-Pay]]** (alongside Orange;
protocol Dec 2025 / Jan 2026). Sources: [[2026-01-06-cdc-ci-green-pay-fratmat]],
[[2026-01-15-cdc-ci-green-pay-investment-protocol-techafricanews]].
