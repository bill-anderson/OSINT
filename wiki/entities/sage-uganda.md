# Social Assistance Grants for Empowerment (SAGE), Uganda

**Uganda's cash-transfer programme for vulnerable elderly people** — and the clearest case
in this wiki of the national ID operating as a gate on social protection: **SAGE benefits
only those on the National Identification Register with a national ID**
([[ndaga-muntu]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **ID as eligibility condition (2025-09-13, advocacy analysis — attribute).** In DRAA's
  account of the dismissed [[iser-uganda]] litigation, SAGE is named as one of the
  services conditioned on a NIN or National ID under the
  [[registration-of-persons-act-2015]]. The **High Court held that the applicants had not
  adduced sufficient evidence of "actual and systemic exclusion" from SAGE benefits**, and
  that the deponents *"appear to me to be few and isolated cases."* See [[iser-uganda]],
  [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-13 Uganda's Digital ID Barrier Worsens as Court Delivers Blow to Access]].
- **Exclusion at registration (2025-05-28).** At the launch of NIRA's mass registration,
  Tororo District chairperson **John Okeya** urged officials to collect accurate data,
  citing **older persons whose misrepresented biodata blocked their access to SAGE** — the
  same failure mode, arising upstream in enrolment rather than in law. See
  [[nira-uganda]], [[include.divides]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-28 Huge turnout as Nira starts mass registration]].
