# ZESCO

Zambia's **state-owned electricity utility**, and — as a holder of national grid rights of way and
fibre — a partner in the state's connectivity build-out.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Partnership with SMART Zambia (2025).** [[smart-zambia-institute]] signed a partnership with
  ZESCO to **extend digital access to schools, health facilities and other public administrations**,
  alongside SZI's connection of **all 116 local authorities to the Government Wide Area Network
  (GWAN) in 2025**. Source: [[2026-07-17-zambie-lance-reseau-4g-prive-govlink]].

The wiki holds **only this single, second-hand mention** of the partnership: no contract, scope,
value, coverage target or delivery evidence is on file. Utility-fibre partnerships are a standard
route to national backbone capacity, but nothing here evidences delivery.
