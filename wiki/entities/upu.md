# Universal Postal Union (UPU)

The **United Nations specialised agency for the postal sector** — the global standards body whose
technical standards govern, among much else, how **postal addresses are structured and rendered**.
Its **S42** international addressing standard (**[[upu-s42]]**) is the certification against
which national addressing systems are measured.

Held here for its **standards-setting** role: UPU is one of the quiet global bodies whose
technical specifications shape what a national registry must look like — a standards channel
running parallel to, and largely independent of, the digital-ID and DPI standards ecosystems.
