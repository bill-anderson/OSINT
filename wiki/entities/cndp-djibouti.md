# National Commission for the Protection of Personal Data (CNDP, Djibouti)

Djibouti's **independent data-protection authority**, created by the data-protection regime embedded in the **[[djibouti-digital-code]]** (enacted June 2025). Has administrative and financial autonomy and a multi-stakeholder composition.

**Functions/powers:** receives 72-hour breach notifications; maintains a public **national register** of processing; administers a **tiered prior-formalities system** — declarations for routine processing and **prior authorisation** for higher-risk processing (sensitive data for research, genetic/biometric data for identification, offence/conviction data, certain interconnections, and any processing involving the **national identification number**). A specific regime governs health-data processing.

Directly relevant to **[[mobile-id-djibouti]]** (national-identifier processing sits in the prior-authorisation tier).
