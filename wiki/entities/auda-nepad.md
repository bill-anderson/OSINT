# AUDA-NEPAD (African Union Development Agency)

The **African Union's development agency** (formerly the NEPAD Planning and Coordinating
Agency), the AU's technical body for coordinating and implementing continental development
priorities — including digital-transformation and data-governance programmes that
operationalise AU frameworks at national level.

## Activity in this wiki

- **São Tomé data-governance workshop (2026-07).** Named an implementing partner (with
  **[[giz]]** and **[[kictanet]]**) of the **[[d4d-hub]]** #D4DataGOV workshop helping
  **[[STP]]** adopt the **[[au-data-policy-framework]]** nationally. See [[gov.regional]],
  [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2026-07-13 D4D Hub on Sao Tome and Principe]].
