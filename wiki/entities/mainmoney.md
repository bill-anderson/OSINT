# MainMoney

A DR Congo **palm-vein biometric payment** technology (Visa-backed; built with **AI.Tech / Keyo**), pitched
to spare the population "the weight of cash" and **integrated into national payments infrastructure**. Named
against the **2023–2028 National Financial Inclusion Strategy** (~70% by 2028). *(Launch carried a Visa /
Silikin Village sponsorship disclosure; CEO still cited 29m mobile-money accounts, Apr 2026.)* Sources:
