# Digital Economy for Chad — Country Diagnostic (P177120)

**A [[world-bank]] country diagnostic of Chad's digital economy**, published **2023** (precision:
year — the source page records `date_precision: year`), project code **P177120**. It belongs to
the Bank's Digital Economy for Africa (DE4A) diagnostic series and is held in the wiki as a
standing reference object for [[TCD]]: the assessment against which later Chadian strategy,
financing and reporting can be read.

## Reference study — cite, do not absorb

Per the wiki's currency discipline, **this diagnostic's figures are not promoted onto concept or
place pages as current state.** It is cited as a study; its numbers stay in it. Fresher
country-level data on Chad arrives via dated clippings — see [[tchad-connexion-2030]],
[[chad-national-data-centre]], [[2024-02-23 Digital 2024 - Chad]]. That guard matters here: a
2023 baseline sits upstream of Chad's entire 2025–26 digital push, and is exactly the kind of
document whose statistics get quietly recycled as though current.

## What it is

- **Type.** Country-level digital-economy diagnostic — a reference work, not a financing
  instrument and not a dated news source. It is a distinct object from the
  [[chad-digital-transformation-project]] (P180000 / PAD00070), the Bank's US$120m operation, and
  from Chad's own [[pmice]] and [[tchad-connexion-2030]] programmes.
- **Holding.** The wiki holds the PDF in `raw/` with a companion source page carrying the
