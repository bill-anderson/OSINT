# SNIAM (Stratégie Nationale en IA et Mégadonnées, Benin, 2023–2027)

Benin's **National Strategy for AI and Big Data (2023–2027)** — the policy frame under which the country
positions itself as an early African AI mover and, since 2026, an AI-first digital agenda. Benin rose from
**145th globally to sub-Saharan top-5** on the Oxford Insights AI-readiness index in three years; the new
minister **Mahuna Akplogan** was an AI figure (SNIAM contributor, GPT-BJ). See [[BEN]], [[tech.ai]],
[[benin-digital-strategy-2031]].

## Under SNIAM

- **Skills pipeline.** [[capacity.training]] The first **National AI Olympiad** (2026, under SNIAM) ran
  via [[seme-city|Sèmè City]] — 16 laureates, a bootcamp and 8 representing Benin in Kazakhstan; Benin had
  placed **30th of 78** at IOAI Beijing 2025 (financing beyond edition one undisclosed). Source:
  [[2026-06-29-wearetech-benin-first-national-ai-olympiad]].
- **AI in the administration — a strict frame.** [[gov.protect]] ASIN's June-2026 legal note urges
  **sovereign hosting, full encryption, no-training contractual clauses, no extraterritorial transfers,
  DPIAs and APDP declarations, audits and supplier diversification** against dependence — mapping GenAI
  use and migrating civil servants off personal accounts onto a harmonised **AI-use charter**. Source:
  [[2026-06-16-asin-encadrement-ia-administration]].
- **Regional AI governance.** [[gov.regional]] Benin co-adopted a **six-country francophone West African
  AI-governance framework** (with BFA/CIV/GIN/MLI/SEN; Niyel-led) — ethical, inclusive AI, data
  protection and **digital sovereignty**, aligned to the AU continental AI strategy and submitted to the
  UN's inaugural **Global AI Dialogue** (Jul 2026) — an explicit anti-dependence framing. Benin also
  hosted its first **Law and Tech Meetings** on AI regulation and cross-border data governance (Dec 2025,
  with the APDP, MTN Benin and ISOCEL). Sources:
  [[2026-07-09-wearetech-six-francophone-ai-governance-framework]],
  [[2025-12-12-techreviewafrica-benin-law-and-tech-meetings]].
