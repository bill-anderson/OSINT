# Sand Technologies

Sand Technologies is an AI and engineering firm operating across eight African countries. It matters here as the local delivery partner that brought AWS hybrid-cloud infrastructure into Rwanda.

## Profile
- Supported the AWS Outposts deployment in Rwanda, handling infrastructure design and AI experimentation environments — see [[aws-outposts-rwanda]].
- Operates across eight African countries.
- CEO is Fred Swaniker, also founder of African Leadership University.
