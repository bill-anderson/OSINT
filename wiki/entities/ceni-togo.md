# CENI (Commission Électorale Nationale Indépendante, Togo)

**Togo's Independent National Electoral Commission — custodian of the electoral roll
(*listes électorales*).** The CENI runs electoral censuses and roll revisions, and trains
the field workforce that carries them out. See [[TGO]], [[dpi.registry]].

**A register distinct from the NIU.** The electoral roll is **not** the [[e-id-togo]]
identity register run by [[anid-togo]]. The two have different custodians, different
eligibility rules (voters aged 18+ vs. all residents including children aged 0–5) and
different revision cycles. **Voter-roll totals must never be read as, or added to, e-ID
enrolment.** The distinction matters historically too: [[novissi]] was built on Togo's voter
registry precisely because no social registry existed.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Roll revision for the 2025 municipal elections (7–23 April 2025).** A presidential
  decree announced on **1 April 2025** fixed the modalities for a nationwide revision of the
  electoral rolls running **7–23 April 2025**. It covered citizens not yet registered
  (notably young people who had recently turned 18), voters who had changed domicile, and
  those who had lost their voter card. Existing electoral districting served as the
  framework; centres opened daily 7am–5pm. See [[digital.localgov]], [[gov.policy]].
  Source: [[2025-04-02 Municipal elections in Togo - revision of electoral rolls begins 7 April]].
- **Training cascade.** Ahead of the operation the CENI trained **324 trainers** in Lomé,
  who in turn trained **nearly 6,000 data-entry operators** for field implementation — a
  cascade model for register maintenance. See [[capacity.training]]. Source:
  [[2025-04-02 Municipal elections in Togo - revision of electoral rolls begins 7 April]].
- **Roll size (April 2024).** At the close of the electoral census organised for the
  **April 2024** legislative and regional elections, the CENI had registered **more than 4.2
  million Togolese voters**. Source:
  [[2025-04-02 Municipal elections in Togo - revision of electoral rolls begins 7 April]].
