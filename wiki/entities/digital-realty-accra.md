# Digital Realty ACR2 (Accra data centre)

**Ghana's largest data centre** — **ACR2** in Accra, commissioned Oct 2025 by **Digital Realty** (a global
carrier-neutral data-centre REIT): **1.7 MW**, **500 racks**, connected to the **2Africa** subsea cable.
Framed as anchoring local hosting capacity for Ghana's digital-sovereignty ambitions. See
[[ghana--infra-connect]].
