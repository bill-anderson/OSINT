# International Finance Corporation (IFC)

The private-sector arm of the **[[world-bank]]** Group; a major development
financier of African digital connectivity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **$150m Airtel Africa loan (2026-07-01).** IFC lent **$150m** to two
  **[[airtel-africa]]** subsidiaries to expand and upgrade mobile networks in
  underserved communities across its 14-country Sub-Saharan footprint, with
  longer-tenor and local-currency financing. Deal: [[ifc-airtel-africa-loan]].
  IFC has mobilised **>$12bn** in emerging-market telecoms/tech over the past
  decade (FY2025 total commitments a record $71.7bn). See [[XSS]],
  [[infra.connect]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-01 IFC and Airtel Africa Expand Digital Connectivity to Strengthen Jobs and Inclusion]].
