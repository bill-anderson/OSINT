# Eswatini Health System Strengthening for Human Capital Development Project

**A [[world-bank]] health project for [[SWZ]], appraised 2020-05-29 (PAD3539),
**US$20m** — the financing behind the country's community-health digitisation. See
[[finance.new]], [[dpi.mis]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Finances CBHIS.** The [[ministry-of-health-eswatini]] introduced the electronic
  [[eswatini-cbhis]] "with World Bank support under the Health Systems Strengthening for
  Human Capital Development Project" — the offline-capable handheld system taking Rural
  Health Motivators off paper, integrated with [[eswatini-cmis]]. Pilot completed in
  Mpolonjeni Inkhundla, Lubombo, with national rollout starting **(as of 2026-02-23)**.
  See [[digital.rural]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-23 From Paper to Pixels - Digital Transformation of Community Health Services in Eswatini]].
- **Note the gap between the documents.** The Project Appraisal Document is dated
  **2020-05-29**; the digitisation it financed surfaces in reporting only in
  **2026-02**. The wiki holds no implementation status report, restructuring paper or
  closing date for the project, and no disbursement figures. Source:
  [[2020-05-29 PAD - Eswatini Health System Strengthening for Human Capital Development (PAD3539)]].
