# Daraja subsea cable (Meta–Safaricom)

**Meta's second submarine cable to Kenya** (after 2Africa), announced Oct 2025. A **4,108km, 24-fibre-pair**
system linking **Salalah (Oman)** to **Mombasa (Kenya)**, funded (**~$23m / KES 2.98bn**) by Meta's
subsidiary **Edge Network Services** and built by **Alcatel Submarine Networks (ASN)**, ready for service
**2026**. **[[safaricom]]** was contracted to operate the in-territory segment and associated in-country
infrastructure — reinforcing Kenya's role as an East-Africa landing hub and US-hyperscaler capacity
dependence. See [[kenya--infra-connect]]. Source:
[[2025-10-30-meta-safaricom-daraja-subsea-cable-telecomreviewafrica]].
