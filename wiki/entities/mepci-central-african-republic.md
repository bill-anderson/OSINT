# MEPCI (Ministère de l'Économie, du Plan et de la Coopération Internationale)

The Central African Republic's **economy, planning and international-cooperation ministry**,
custodian of the **[[pnd-2024-2028-central-african-republic]]** (National Development Plan)
and its digital-governance agenda. Minister (2026): Pr Richard Filakota.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DUNIA platform (launch 23 Feb 2026).** MEPCI launched the **DUNIA** digital platform to
  dematerialise administrative procedures, under President Touadera's patronage — positioned
  as a flagship of the PND 2024-2028 digitalisation of public services. Source:
  [[2026-02-11 La digitalisation des services publics et la gouvernance numerique du PND 2024-2028]].
- Distinct from the Ministry of Finance's **"Gouvernemental Centralisé"** unified services
  portal (World Bank / EY Tunisie Medianet, Dec 2025) and DGID's **e-Tax** (Mar 2025) — three
  parallel e-gov systems under the same modernisation drive. See [[caf--dpi-govtech]].
