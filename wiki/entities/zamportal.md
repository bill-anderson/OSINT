# ZamPortal

Zambia's **single national e-services portal** — a 24-hour government digital operations platform (run by
the **[[smart-zambia-institute]]** e-Government Division) through which citizens access and pay for public
services online, paired with the **[[zampay]]** payment gateway and the Government Service Bus. It carries
~300 of ~1,500 targeted services and is the front end for the ID, land, property, council and passport
systems compiled across the ZMB pages.

- Councils, property (PMIS), land (ZILAS) and other systems onboard to ZamPortal; a **ZamPortal account is a
  prerequisite to pay ground rent**. See [[zambia--dpi-govtech]]. Sources:
  [[2026-05-13-zambia-launches-pmis-property-management]], [[2026-05-29-councils-digital-lcc-first-zamportal]].
