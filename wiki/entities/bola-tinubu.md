# Bola Ahmed Tinubu

Bola Ahmed Tinubu is the President of Nigeria, and a recurring actor across the country's
digital-economy and data-governance decisions. His **Renewed Hope Agenda** is the framing
under which several digital-inclusion and sovereignty initiatives are advanced.

## What matters now

- **Big-tech investigation directive (2026).** Tinubu ordered the [[fccpc]] to investigate
  [[meta]], Alphabet/[[google]], [[x-corp]] and generative-AI firms over uncompensated use of
  Nigerian news content — the boldest move yet in Nigeria's push to hold global platforms to
  local rules. See [[gov.legislate]], [[tech.ai]], [[nigeria--gov-protect]].
- **Renewed Hope / digital inclusion.** [[nitda]]'s digital-inclusion work (e.g. the
  [[dl4all-nitda]] programme) is positioned as delivery of the Renewed Hope Agenda's
  inclusivity priority. See [[include.access]].

Tinubu also recurs in the identity-governance thread (database audits, NIN–SIM linkage probe)
carried on the [[NGA]] hub.
