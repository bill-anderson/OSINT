# Bayobab Zambia

The Zambia fibre unit of MTN's Bayobab fibre arm ([[mtn-group]]), which develops cross-border and
national fibre infrastructure. In Zambia it partners with government and other operators to extend
the national backbone to neighbouring countries.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Chanida–Cassacatiza cross-border interconnector launched (2026-02-23).** With [[vodacom]],
  Bayobab Zambia commissioned the Chanida–Cassacatiza fibre-optic interconnector linking Zambia's
  national backbone to Mozambique's submarine-cable network, strengthening redundancy and
  cross-border data exchange. Minister Felix Mutati said the link makes Zambia fibre-connected to
  eight neighbours and "the most digitally connected country within Southern Africa." Tags
