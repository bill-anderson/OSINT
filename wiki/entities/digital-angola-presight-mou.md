# Digital Angola — Government of Angola × Presight (G42) MoU (2021)

An MoU signed during President João Lourenço's **December 2021 UAE visit** between the **Government of Angola** and **Presight**, a **G42** company (big-data analytics + AI), to develop a **"Digital Angola"** transformation programme using big-data analytics and artificial intelligence.

## Significance

- The **UAE/G42-facing leg** of Angola's DPI/AI courtship — alongside the **[[angola-india-dpi-credit-line]]** (India DPI/Aadhaar) and the **Chinese**-devised civil-registration system. G42's involvement carries the Gulf-AI ([[geopol.gulf]]) and data-sovereignty dimensions seen elsewhere on the continent. See [[tech.ai]]. Source: [[2021-12-21 Government of Angola and Presight sign MoU for Digital Angola program]].
- Scope/valuation beyond the MoU is not detailed in the available source; the "Digital Angola 2024" programme name recurs in later government framing.
