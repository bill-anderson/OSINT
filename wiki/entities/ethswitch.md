# EthSwitch

Ethiopia's national payment switch, regulated by the National Bank of Ethiopia — the
interoperability backbone linking banks and mobile wallets for real-time transfers and
interoperable merchant payments (EthQR).

## Activity in this wiki

- **M-PESA integration drives adoption (October 2025).** [[safaricom]]'s M-PESA integrated with
  EthSwitch, connecting the wallet to more than 30 banks and NBE-regulated digital wallets and
  enabling real-time wallet-to-bank and bank-to-wallet transfers plus interoperable merchant
  payments via EthQR (acceptance at 50,000+ merchants). The integration helped drive M-PESA
  Ethiopia active users up 258.5% year-on-year to 5.2 million in the quarter to December 2025,
  with total M-PESA transaction value up 192%. EthSwitch is framed as aligning with Ethiopia's
  national digital-payments strategy of interoperability and reduced cash reliance.
  [[safaricom-ethiopia]] separately reported crossing **12.2m** three-month active customers
  in Q3 FY26 (period to 31 Dec 2025), with M-PESA transaction value more than doubling to
  **ETB 20.37bn** on the back of the EthSwitch integration. Tags [[dpi.pay]],
