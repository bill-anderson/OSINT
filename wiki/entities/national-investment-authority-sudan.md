# National Investment Authority (Sudan)

Sudan's national investment authority (الجهاز القومي للاستثمار), the body
licensing investment projects and a target of e-government service digitisation
to improve the business environment and attract reconstruction investment.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Electronic investment licensing (2026-02).** Secretary-General **Ahlam Madani
  Mahdi** announced completion of network integration with the
  **[[baladna-platform-sudan|Baladna]]** e-government platform and launched
  fully-electronic investment-project licensing (online submission, document
  upload, status tracking). See [[dpi.govtech]], [[finance.new]], [[SDN]].
