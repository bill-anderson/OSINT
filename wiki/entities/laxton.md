# Laxton

A **citizen-registration / biometric-enrolment vendor** (recently a **MOSIP full-service partner**),
supplying enrolment kits and mass-enrolment solutions for national-ID programmes. See [[dpi.id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **PRODIGY biometric-kit contract (2025-05).** [[dpi.registry]] Laxton **won the biometric registration
  kit contract** under Madagascar's **[[prodigy-madagascar|PRODIGY]]** (beating Augentic/Thales for the
  ~$15m equipment lot), alongside **[[in-groupe|IN Groupe]]**'s database/identity-management award. Source:
  [[2025-05-16-madagascar-selects-in-groupe-8-5m-digital-id-biometricupdate]].
- **Mass-enrolment delivery amid crisis (2026-04).** [[dpi.id]] Laxton designed/supplied/deployed the mass
  biometric-enrolment solution (iris/face/fingerprint, online-or-offline) and **2,500 kits** — navigating
  **regime change/civil unrest and a cyclone**. The Jan-2026 pilot enrolled **~130,000 people across 116
  centres in 26 communes** with **100% data completeness, 99% kit availability**, <10-min average. See
  [[madagascar--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2026-04-21-madagascar-biometric-enrollment-laxton-biometricupdate]].
