# IDCAM (Cameroon national ID pre-enrolment platform)

**www.idcam.cm** — the DGSN's online pre-enrolment portal for the new-generation biometric
national ID card ([[dgsn-cameroon]] / [[augentic]]). Citizens fill an information form, pay
online and book a physical enrolment appointment through it. It is the digital front door to the
CNI stack, and the point where the ID's **priced gateway** becomes concrete.

## Profile

- **Launched 17 Feb 2025** for online pre-enrolment; regional enrolment centres in all ten
  regions began **24 Feb 2025**. Pre-enrolment carries a **fee of 10,000 FCFA (~US$16)** — a real
  barrier in a country whose ID access is already uneven, and the figure opposition lawmakers
  objected to when criticising the system's cost (mid-2025). Sequence: IDCAM live 17 Feb → regional
  enrolment 24 Feb → Biya decree N°2025/059 of 28 Feb 2025 (CNI characteristics/distribution) →
  issuance began 3 Mar 2025. Sources:
