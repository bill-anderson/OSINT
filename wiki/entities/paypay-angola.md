# Paypay (Paypay Africa)

Angolan **digital wallet** and the single most active participant in the **[[kwik-angola|KWiK]]** instant-payment ecosystem — the leading non-bank consumer face of Angola's digital payments.

## Activity in this wiki

- **KWiK market leader (Feb 2026).** Paypay processed **1,637,275 instant transfers in February 2026** for a **52.2% share** of the KWiK ecosystem, total value **24.5bn Kz** (+142% y/y). Average transfer ~**14,956 Kz** — everyday payments, small purchases and family support. **625,000+ active accounts** (+146.7% y/y); new users +5.2% in February alone. See [[kwik-angola]], [[angola--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-03-05-paypay-ultrapassa-16-milhoes-transferencias-no-kwik]].
