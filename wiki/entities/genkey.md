# GenKey

Dutch biometric-identity firm supplying **biometric voter-registration** systems (hardware
and software) in Africa. In the Central African Republic, GenKey is the vendor behind the
**[[ane-central-african-republic]]** (ANE) electoral-register system.

## Activity in this wiki

- **CAR biometric voter registration.** GenKey supplied the biometric enrolment system used
  for CAR's **2020** general-election register and again for the **2024–25** revision ahead
  of the country's first local elections in ~36 years — ~3,000 enrolment tablets, ~1,469
  registration agents. President Touadera was the first person biometrically enrolled in
  2020. See [[caf--dpi-id]]. Sources:
  [[2024-10-08 Central African Republic in full biometric voter registration preparations]],
  [[2024-12-10 Central African Republic enrols citizens for first local elections in 36 years]].
