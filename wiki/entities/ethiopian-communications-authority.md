# Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA)

Ethiopia's telecommunications regulator, designated by the **[[ethiopia-pdpp|Personal Data Protection Proclamation 1321/2024]]** as the country's **data-protection regulatory authority**. As of late 2025, enforcement had **yet to gather momentum** — the secondary directives to operationalise the PDPP were still pending, leaving data-subject rights largely unrealised in practice.
