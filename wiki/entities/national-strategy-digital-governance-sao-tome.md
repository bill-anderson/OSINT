# National Strategy for Digital Governance (São Tomé and Príncipe)

São Tomé and Príncipe's whole-of-government **digital-transformation strategy**, the
national policy framework for aligning public-sector digital services across
governance sectors. The underlying strategy dates to **2020**; it is documented by a
**[[unu-egov]]** (United Nations University Operating Unit on Policy-Driven Electronic
Governance) project page, with UNU-EGOV Deputy Director **Luís Soares Barbosa** as
project manager.

## What it sets out

- **Objective:** align public-policy goals across governance sectors for digital
  transformation, and mobilise material and human resources to improve delivery of
  **digital public services**.
- **Structure:** digital-transformation initiatives organised across **nine thematic
  axes**, with sector-specific implementation measures and a monitoring-and-evaluation
  mechanism benchmarked to international best practice.
