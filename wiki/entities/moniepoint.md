# Moniepoint

Nigerian fintech unicorn (founded 2015 by Tosin Eniolorunda and Felix Ike), **Nigeria's largest merchant
acquirer and payment processor** — 20m+ business and personal customers, **>$250bn** in annual digital
payments value, unicorn status Oct 2024 (($60m Series C, >$1bn valuation)). Its full-stack platform spans
banking, payments, credit, cross-border and business-management tools for MSMEs. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Kenya market entry (2026-04).** [[KEN]] [[finance.new]] Moniepoint completed the **acquisition of
  Sumac Microfinance Bank** (a [[central-bank-of-kenya|CBK]]-licensed MFI) — its **first major African
  acquisition** and East-Africa entry — to bring integrated banking/payments/credit to Kenyan MSMEs.
  Framed against Kenya's **$67.3bn** mobile-payments ecosystem and an under-served SME segment — a
  cross-border challenge to [[safaricom|M-Pesa]]'s dominance. See [[kenya--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-02-moniepoint-enters-kenya-connectingafrica]].
