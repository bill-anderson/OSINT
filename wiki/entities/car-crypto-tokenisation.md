# CAR crypto & tokenisation track (Sango, $CAR, land)

The Central African Republic's serial crypto experiments — **Sango Coin**, the **$CAR meme coin**, and
**tokenised land** — repeatedly pitched as sovereign wealth-creation and repeatedly failing, while
independent research (**GI-TOC**) reads the whole track as a vehicle for **criminal capture**
with **Russia-linked** figures at its centre. It is the sharpest expression of CAR's sovereignty-anxiety
theme, and a track the wiki previously held nothing on. See [[CAF]], [[dpi.pay]].

## What we know

- **Sango — "the sell-off of sovereignty" (2022→25).** [[gov.legislate]] The **Sango Project**, launched
  after CAR briefly made Bitcoin legal tender, sold sovereignty-branded tokens and an "**île aux Singes**
  crypto city" that was never built; the Sango site went **dead (29 Apr 2025)**, **<10% of tokens sold**,
  **<$2m raised**. A **July 2023 resource-tokenisation law** provided the legal base. Sources:
  [[2025-12-01-gitoc-behind-the-blockchain-crypto-criminal-capture-globalinitiative]],
  [[2025-12-21-rfi-gitoc-crypto-organised-crime-allafrica]].
- **The $CAR meme coin (2025-02).** [[dpi.pay]] President Touadéra launched a **$CAR meme coin** on X; it
  **fell >90% in 24 hours** (23,559 buyers vs 20,330 sellers) and the official X account was suspended —
  the Le Monde launch report is bot-blocked (a manual-clip gap). Source:
  [[2025-02-11-car-launches-meme-coin-techcabal]].
- **Tokenised land (2025-06).** [[dpi.registry]] By presidential decree, from **21 Jun 2025** $CAR buys
  **tokenised land concessions** — **1,700 ha at Bossongo**, a $20/yr charge, sold to global investors,
  with **mineral rights** flagged next: a crypto token wired into the **land registry**, with no
  land-administration governance. *(Government-sourced throughout — treat the framing with suspicion; the
  decree itself is worth sourcing.)* Source: [[2025-06-23-car-land-ownership-cryptocurrency-itweb]].
- **Read as criminal capture (GI-TOC, Dec 2025).** [[gov.protect]] The GI-TOC report frames the schemes as
  **"public losses, private gains"** — named intermediaries, **Russia-based figures** central, land
  tokenised without safeguards — published (with the RFI interview) days before the **28 Dec 2025**
  election. Source: [[2025-12-01-gitoc-behind-the-blockchain-crypto-criminal-capture-globalinitiative]].
