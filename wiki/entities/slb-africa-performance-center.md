# SLB Africa Performance Center (Luanda)

An AI/analytics and digital-workflow hub opened by global energy-technology company **SLB** (formerly Schlumberger) in Luanda on **28 January 2025** — a **3,200 sq ft** collaborative facility for oil-and-gas, new-energy and digital solutions. Notable in the corpus as the point where **Angola's actual AI/advanced-analytics capacity is landing: anchored in oil and gas**, not consumer tech.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Opening (2025-01-28).** MD for Angola, Central and East Africa Miguel Baptista pitched "localized solutions" drawing on SLB's global portfolio; the centre is positioned around AI-powered digital workflows, advanced analytics and automation, plus capacity-building and local-talent development, aligned with Angola's economic-diversification and digital-transformation agenda.
