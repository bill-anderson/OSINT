# Zimbabwe National AI Strategy 2026–2030

Zimbabwe's national AI strategy, positioning AI as a driver of inclusive growth and "national
sovereignty," grounded in Ubuntu principles and Heritage-Based Education 5.0; drawn on UNESCO's AI
Readiness Assessment Methodology; eight principles incl. data sovereignty. Proposes a National AI Act,
technical standards, a regulatory sandbox and statutory bodies (National AI Council). Critics
([[misa]]) flag high-risk provisions — [[project-pangolin]], an AI–Cybersecurity Fusion Centre, a
National Reality Check Platform — lacking legal safeguards. See [[tech.ai]]. Source:
[[2026-06-23 Implications of Zimbabwe’s National AI Strategy on Freedom of Expression Privacy and Access to Information]].
