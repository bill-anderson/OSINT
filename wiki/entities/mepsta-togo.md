# MEPSTA (Ministère de l'Enseignement primaire, secondaire, technique et artisanal, Togo)

**Togo's Ministry of Primary, Secondary, Technical and Artisanal Education — the owner of the
country's education management information system (EMIS / *système d'information de gestion de
l'éducation*, SIGE).** It collects statistical data from Togo's **16,000 nursery, primary and
secondary schools** and, as of the accounts held here, runs **[[stateduc]]** for collection with
**[[dhis2]]** layered on for processing and analysis. See [[TGO]], [[dpi.mis]],
[[data.statistics]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **The legacy collection chain.** MEPSTA historically relied on a **hybrid paper-and-digital
  system**: data from the 16,000 schools were first collected on **paper forms**, forwarded to
  **education inspectorates** for review and entry into **StatEduc** — a statistical application
  with **limited analytical tools**, leaving sub-national levels dependent on central analyses.
  See [[include.divides]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-19 HISP WCA develops DHIS2 EMIS for education data analysis and visualization in Togo]].
- **2020 education sector plan.** MEPSTA developed a sector plan centred on teaching/learning
  quality, **equitable access to basic education, improved information systems,
  decentralisation, empowerment and accountability-based transparency** — the policy the
  DHIS2-based SIGE was built to support. Source:
  [[2024-11-19 HISP WCA develops DHIS2 EMIS for education data analysis and visualization in Togo]].
- **Status: under review (as of 2024-11-19).** The DHIS2 EMIS was reported as **"currently
  under review by Togo's Ministry of Education"**, and separately described as being **piloted**
  alongside StatEduc — a deployment not evidenced here as fully institutionalised, despite a
  companion DHIS2 profile page describing national scale. See the start-date contradiction on
  [[hisp-wca]] and [[stateduc]]. Sources:
  [[2024-11-19 HISP WCA develops DHIS2 EMIS for education data analysis and visualization in Togo]],
  [[2024-11-05 Togo - Integrating DHIS2 with existing EMIS systems]].
- **One of six education ministries in the DHIS2 for Education research project**, with
  Eswatini, Mozambique, The Gambia, Sri Lanka and Uganda. See [[capacity.research]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-05 Togo - Integrating DHIS2 with existing EMIS systems]].

**Provenance note.** Both accounts are published by **DHIS2 (education.dhis2.org)** — the
implementer's own platform. Progress claims are the implementer's.
