# Yonna Wallet (Yonna Group, The Gambia)

A Gambian fintech (subsidiary of Yonna Group) providing a mobile wallet with 200+ branches/outlets, and
the **first Gambian fintech authorized to collect payments for all government services** ([[dpi.pay]],
[[dpi.govtech]]). Its Yonna Global Wallet extends government-fee payments to the diaspora. Related to the
telecom incumbent restructuring (Gamtel/Gamcel's 80% sale to "Y Cell (Yoona)" appears in the incumbent
story). See [[GMB]], [[gambia--dpi-pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Government-payments partnership (2026-03-02).** [[dpi.govtech]] Yonna Wallet signed a partnership
  with the Accountant General's Department to conduct payments for all government services (ID cards,
  passports, business registration, rates) — auto-generating traceable receipts, cutting fraud and
  leakages, with diaspora payment via the Yonna Global Wallet. Source:
  [[2026-03-02 Gambia Gov't payments now digitalised live on Yonna Wallet app]].
