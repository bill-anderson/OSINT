# Xalam Analytics Data Center Market Briefs 2026

A series of **country data-center market briefs** produced by **Xalam Analytics** at the behest of
the **Digital Investment Facility (DIF)** under the **Data Governance in Africa Initiative**
(January 2026). Each brief assesses a country's data-center demand/supply, business landscape,
regulatory impact and investment returns — a standing OSINT reference for the Sub-Saharan Africa
data-centre investment map. Reference documents: `Data-Center-Market-Brief(s)-<Country>-2026.pdf`.

## Coverage

- **22 country briefs:** Angola, Botswana, Cameroon, Congo (Republic), Côte d'Ivoire, DR Congo,
  Ethiopia, Ghana, Kenya, Madagascar, Mauritius, Mozambique, Namibia, Nigeria, Rwanda, Senegal,
  South Africa, Tanzania, Togo, Uganda, Zambia, Zimbabwe.
- Consult the relevant brief for a country's live/planned MW, operator landscape and power/regulatory
  constraints when building or updating a place hub ([[infra.store]], [[infra.energy]]). A specific
  dated figure pulled from a brief becomes a source citing this resource.
