# Garsu Pasaulis

**Lithuanian supplier of Zimbabwe's border management system — and, on the same source's
account, Zimbabwe's passport provider.** The wiki holds one source on this company, and
it is entirely about the Zimbabwe engagement; nothing about the firm's ownership, size,
other markets or contract terms is held.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Supplies OBMS (as of 2024-05-15).** Garsu Pasaulis supplies Zimbabwe's
  [[obms-zimbabwe]] Online Border Management System — fingerprint and **iris** biometric
  e-gates, self-service barriers and kiosks, verifying travellers against the national
  immigration database, designed on **Dubai's Smart Gates** platform. As reported, the
  system was a **pilot** at three ports of entry plus inland stations; **its outcome is
  not held (GAP-ZWE-002)**. See [[dpi.id]], [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2024-05-15 Zimbabwe implements biometric border posts for automating immigration processes]].
- **Also Zimbabwe's passport provider (as of 2024-05).** The same source, attributing to
  The Sunday Mail, states Garsu Pasaulis "is also Zimbabwe's passport provider". Source:
  as above.

## Worth noting

The concentration is the fact the source does not argue: **a single foreign vendor holds
both the passport supply and the border database** — the credential and the system that
checks it — on a **Dubai-derived architecture**, feeding a platform described as giving
**all border and security agencies** shared access, with **no oversight arrangement
mentioned**. Recorded as an observation; the evidence held does not support more.

*(No `geopol.eu` tag: a commercial vendor contract with a Lithuanian firm is not EU
geopolitics on this evidence.)*
