# AMLAK (Algeria electronic land-title system)

Algeria's **electronic land-registration platform**, replacing paper land titles — nationwide rollout begun **11 January 2026** by the **General Directorate of National Domain (DGDN)**. Centralises the issuance, modification and archiving of land titles across all land-registry and land-conservation offices, with **full transaction traceability** and real-time cadastral indicators, aiming to cut processing times, strengthen property rights and reduce land disputes.

Context: the **ANCFCC** (National Agency for Land Conservation, Cadastre and Cartography) issued **430,000 land titles in 2025** (+15% YoY). Part of the **[[digital-algeria-2030]]** GovTech agenda; the [[dpi.registry|registry]] leg of Algeria's DPI.
