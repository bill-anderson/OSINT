# AFNIC

France's internet domain-name registry (operator of the `.fr` ccTLD and related registry
services), Director-General **Pierre Bonis**. Appears here as a technical-capacity partner
to African internet infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Smart Africa MoU (2026-07).** Partnered with [[smart-africa]] to build the capacity of
  African **ccTLD** operators, strengthen **DNS** security/resilience, and modernise
  national domain registries across the continent. See [[smart-africa-afnic-mou]],
  [[infra.connect]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-09 Smart Africa and AFNIC Partner to Strengthen Africa's Internet Infrastructure]].
