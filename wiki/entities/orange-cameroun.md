# Orange Cameroun

Local opco of the **[[orange|Orange]]** group — with ~**50% mobile share** the market-leading CMR mobile
operator (see [[art-cameroon]]'s 2024 Observatory).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Skills — sixth digital centre, Maroua (May 2026).** Orange opened its **sixth Orange Digital Center**
  in **Maroua (Far North)** — the north being the point: state ID apparatus (Garoua) and a private
  operator's CSR arm both extending north in 2025–26 (company-reported 481 trained, 50 certifications).
  Source: [[2026-05-10-orange-cameroon-sixth-digital-center-maroua-techreviewafrica]].
- **Regulatory & security exposure.** Among operators **fined CFA 1.5bn+** by ART (Jul 2025); and the DNS
  laggard flagged by ANTIC — **just 2.75% DNSSEC validation** (Dec 2025). Sources:
  [[2025-07-11-art-fines-orange-mtn-4-6-million-telecomreviewafrica]],
  [[2025-12-09-leconomie-antic-dnssec-rpki-cyberspace-defence]].
