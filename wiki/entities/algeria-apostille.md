# Algeria Apostille (Hague Convention e-legalisation)

Algeria's **online apostille system** for document legalisation — live **9 July 2026** following formal
accession to the **Hague Apostille Convention on 25 August 2025**. It replaces the complex multi-step manual
chain (university → platform → ministry → foreign affairs) with a single digital mechanism giving **instant
recognition of Algerian documents in ~126 member states** (with a few objecting/non-member exceptions).
Citizens buy an apostille stamp from tax offices or post offices and register on the **[[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria|HCN]]** platform. Especially significant for **higher-education
diplomas**. See [[algeria--dpi-govtech]].
