# Gambia Communications Bill 2025

A telecoms/digital-sector reform bill that **passed second reading** in the National Assembly (Mar 2026),
covering **e-commerce, electronic signatures and cybercrime** — a modernisation of the communications legal
framework. **Distinct** from the **[[gambia-data-protection-act-2025|Personal Data Protection & Privacy Act
2025]]**, though reported alongside it in the "protect personal data in the digital age" framing. See
[[gambia--dpi-govtech]].
