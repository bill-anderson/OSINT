# Ministry of External Relations (MINREX)

Cameroon's foreign ministry (Minister Lejeune Mbella Mbella), which holds **exclusive authority
over consular services** and owns the country's **e-Visa / biometric-visa programme**, delivered
by Ivorian vendor [[impact-palmares]] under a 2022 consular-modernisation contract.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital consular services & e-Visa.** MINREX runs the biometric visa system (live 2023) and is
  building a Digital Transformation Centre (CTDM, due June 2026). Reported volumes:
  **411,439 paid visas / FCFA 46.8bn to 30 Sep 2025**, and **540,000+ visas over three years**
  since the 30 Apr 2023 launch. Sources:
  [[2025-12-10-impact-palmares-digital-visa-deal-printing-contract-biometricupdate]],
  [[2026-05-08-lejour-evisa-540000-visas-three-years]].
