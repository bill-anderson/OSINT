# Cybastion

A pan-African digital-government technology company selling a Digital Solutions
portfolio to public administrations — electronic payments, **eTax** (assessment,
collection, audit), **eVisa**, and **eHealth** (built with IQVIA). It positions
itself around modernising public operations, revenue systems and service delivery
across the continent. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[XAF]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Benin SECUROUTE upgrade (Jan 2026).** Cybastion is partnering with Benin's
  **Ministry of Economy and Finance** to expand digital infrastructure for full
  interoperability with [[securoute]], the national road-enforcement data
  platform — part of a broader push to modernise public administration in road
  safety and traffic management, enabling secure data exchange across government
  systems. See [[BEN]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Cybastion Advances Digital Government Solutions, Supports Benin's SECUROUTE Infrastructure Upgrade]].
