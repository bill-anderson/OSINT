# Cybastion

A pan-African digital-government technology company selling a Digital Solutions
portfolio to public administrations — electronic payments, **eTax** (assessment,
collection, audit), **eVisa**, and **eHealth** (built with IQVIA). It positions
itself around modernising public operations, revenue systems and service delivery
across the continent. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[XAF]].

Angolan reporting characterises Cybastion as a **US-based** technology firm (CEO Dr. Thierry Wandji Ketchiozo), the same company; the "pan-African" framing above is its own market positioning.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Angola Telecom $170m investment (Jun 2025).** At the **US–Africa Business Summit** in Luanda (24 Jun 2025, hosted by the Corporate Council on Africa), Cybastion signed a **$170 million** investment agreement with incumbent **[[angola-telecom]]** to modernise digital infrastructure and cybersecurity capacity, under its **"Digital Fast Track"** initiative (building on a March 2025 MWC Barcelona MoU). A **$25m first phase** funds the **establishment of a national cybersecurity agency** and training via the **Cisco Networking Academy**, plus a **Luanda–Lobito undersea cable**. This makes a **US vendor** a builder of Angola's cyber-institution stack — a fourth external pole (alongside China/India/UAE) in the country's digital-sovereignty courtship. Angola scored **ITU GCI Tier 4 (2024)**, the second-lowest of five bands. See [[AGO]], [[angola--infra-cybersec]], [[geopol.usa]], [[finance.new]]. Sources: [[2025-06-24-cybastion-angola-telecom-acordo-soberania-digital]], [[2025-06-25-cybastion-angola-telecom-170m-digital-sovereignty]], [[2025-06-30-angola-taps-us-firm-cybastion-to-boost-cybersecurity]].
- **Côte d'Ivoire — the flagship US-EXIM build (2025–26).** Cybastion is the implementer of CIV's
  **US [[exim-bank-usa|EXIM]]-financed** digital-sovereignty package: the **[[national-data-centre-civ|
  National Data Centre]]** ($66m, the first EXIM data centre in SSA), Ministry of Commerce digitisation
  ($47m; ERP + GUCII business registration), consolidated to a **$100m+ CTEP guarantee** and later
  **$170m** (adding smart-border surveillance) — plus the **DigiEmpower** skills programme (200k / 5 yrs),
  a **[[digital-city-civ|Digital City]]** data-centre core, and **eTourism** ("Sublime Côte d'Ivoire")
  under its Digital Fast Track programme. Its single largest country engagement in the wiki, and the
  vehicle for US "digital sovereignty" framing against Chinese vendors. See [[civ--infra-store]],

  Meetings** — adding DRC to the US-courtship thread (beside PayServices and Pax Silica). See [[COD]],

- **Benin SECUROUTE upgrade (Jan 2026).** Cybastion is partnering with Benin's
  **Ministry of Economy and Finance** to expand digital infrastructure for full
  interoperability with [[securoute]], the national road-enforcement data
  platform — part of a broader push to modernise public administration in road
  safety and traffic management, enabling secure data exchange across government
  systems. See [[BEN]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
