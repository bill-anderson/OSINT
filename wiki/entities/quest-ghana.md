# Quest Ghana Limited

A Ghana-based company acting as a private-sector delivery partner in the AfCFTA digital-trade
agenda. It is a signatory, alongside the [[afcfta]] Secretariat and the Government of
Seychelles, to the arrangements establishing Seychelles as a continental digital-trade hub.

## Activity in this wiki

- **AfCFTA digital-trade-hub MoU (2026-03-05).** Quest Ghana was one of three parties to the
  **[[seychelles-afcfta-mou]]** to develop Seychelles as a digital trade hub, and is named as
  a continuing partner (with the AfCFTA Secretariat) in standing up the
  **[[afcfta-digital-trade-corridor]]** headquarters in Victoria approved 2026-07-08. See
  [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[SYC]], [[GHA]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-10 Seychelles to Host AfCFTA Digital Trade Corridor Headquarters]].
