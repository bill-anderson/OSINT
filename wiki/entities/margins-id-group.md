# Margins ID Group

A Ghanaian-owned identity and secure-document company, founded in 1990 and headquartered in
Accra, led by founder and CEO Moses Kwesi Baiden Jnr. Over 35 years it has grown from a
print and document-finishing business into an end-to-end identity provider, holding one of
the continent's most extensive certification portfolios (ISO/IEC 20000, ISO/IEC 27001,
ISO 22301 at group level; eight global certifications overall). It positions itself as a
locally-owned, standards-aligned alternative to overseas card production — a sovereignty
pitch of African capability serving African needs, aligned to AfCFTA interoperability and
SDG 16.9 (legal identity for all).

Key subsidiaries: **Intelligent Card Production Systems (ICPS)** — Africa's first domestic
production of secure identity cards, payment cards and government documents, and the only
West African facility holding ISO 14298:2022 for government-level security printing
(Mastercard/Visa/Europay certified); it produces the [[ghana-card]]. **Margins ID Systems
Applications (MIDSA)** — digital-identity platforms, biometric verification and secure
databases. Also operates Identity Management Systems.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Profiled as Africa's certified-identity leader (2026-01-28).** Positioned as a fully
  certified provider of identity and payment infrastructure for governments, banks and
  development partners. Architect and operator of Ghana's National Identification System.
  Tags [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.standards]]. Source: [[2026-01-28 Margins ID Group emerges as Africa's leading provider of certified digital identity solutions]].

- **SIM re-registration goes fully online, at ICPS Accra (2025-04-15).** [[dpi.id]] Communications
  Minister [[samuel-nartey-george]] announced (8 Apr 2025) — during a visit to Margins ID Group's
  ICPS plant in Accra — that from June 2025 SIM re-registration would run fully online via the
  [[ghana-card]] with automatic verification. Underlines ICPS as the domestic production/verification
  hub for Ghana's ID stack. Source: [[2025-04-15 Ghana's SIM Registration Goes Fully Online from June 2025]].
- **Wins The Gambia national-ID contract (2026-01-20).** Selected under a design-build-
  finance-co-operate-transfer (DBFOT) contract for The Gambia's national ID and integrated
  digital ID — becoming the first African-owned firm to export a full national ID system
  beyond its home market. As of the signing, its Ghana system had registered 19.2 million
  Ghanaians (aged 15+) and integrated 262 institutions. See [[gambia-national-id-contract]].
  Tags [[GMB]], [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2026-01-20 Margins takes Ghana's ID success to The Gambia]].
