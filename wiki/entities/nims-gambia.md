# NIMS (National Identity Management System, The Gambia)

The Gambia's **National Identity Management System** — launched by President **Adama Barrow** on **1 July 2026**
(Barrow the **first enrollee**), built by **[[margins-id-group]]** on a **build-operate-transfer (BOT)** model
after ~5 years of negotiation. It enables **instant biometric "GAM ID"** issuance and is pitched as the
foundation of a **trusted, sovereign digital-public-infrastructure** ecosystem. Runs on the co-inaugurated
**National Data Centre in Abuko** (the phase-2 economic/security-IT integration for verification services);
**nationwide enrolment from 4 August 2026**. See [[gambia--dpi-id]], [[gambia-national-id-contract]].
