# T-Connect Lesotho

A Lesotho **digital-transformation group** (a consortium of local firms, chaired by former MultiChoice
Group CEO **Nolo Letele**; Group CEO **Phelane Phomane**) and the **exclusive [[starlink|Starlink]]
reseller** in the country. See [[infra.connect]], [[lesotho--infra-connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Starlink launch (2025-06/07).** [[data.satellite]] T-Connect launched Starlink in Lesotho (AVANI
  Maseru) under the LCA's 10-year licence, pledging **10,000 direct / 50,000+ indirect jobs** and a
  disruptive **M10-per-session** (time-based, uncapped) pricing model plus community hotspots in all 10
  districts — positioned to reach rural areas where 80% of the population is offline. Sources:
  [[2025-06-30-starlink-launch-tconnect-lestimes]], [[2025-07-01-starlink-launch-publiceye]].
- **Rural community hotspots (2025-09).** [[digital.rural]] Handed over first Community Hotspots at
  **Matsieng, Thaba Bosiu and Mokhotlong** (via principal chiefs' offices), prioritising rural villages
  over cities. Source: [[2025-09-12-tconnect-community-hotspots-publiceye]].
- **T-Connect Impact — schools & clinics (2026-01).** [[include.access]] Launched **T-Connect Impact**
  (Starlink Priority Connectivity for schools/clinics/community centres) at **Soofia International School,
  Butha-Buthe** — digital education, telehealth, e-administration. Source:
  [[2026-01-17-tconnect-impact-soofia-school-publiceye]].
- **AI-data-centre ambition.** [[infra.store]] Phomane floated (with the DBSA) plans for **AI data
  centres across Southern Africa** including "the highest data centre in the world" in Lesotho — distinct
  from the separate [[project-kobong|Convalt Project Kobong]] deal.
