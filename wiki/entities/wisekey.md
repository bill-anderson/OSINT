# WISeKey

**Swiss cybersecurity company**, the technology partner behind Seychelles' national digital-ID
platform [[seyid]]. Its role in this wiki is confined to the Seychelles engagement: WISeKey
International Holding developed SeyID on its own **WISeID** digital-identity and online-security
product. See [[dpi.id]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[SYC]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Selected as Seychelles' digital-signature partner (2014).** The Seychelles government
  selected WISeKey to supply technology and expertise on **public-key digital signatures for
  electronic transactions** — the origin point of the SeyID programme. See [[infra.cybersec]].
  Source: [[2024-09-18 Seychelles Makes Digital ID Mandatory for Work Permit Holders]].
- **Built SeyID on WISeID.** The [[seyid]] platform was developed by WISeKey International
  Holding based on **WISeID**, its digital-identity and online-security platform; SeyID launched
  officially on **2022-11-01**. The resulting stack carries WISeKey-typical features — KYC
  onboarding, OTP authentication, QR-code login and a cloud signature service for legally valid
  digital document signing. A foundational national ID built on a foreign vendor's proprietary
  identity platform is a **[[sovereignty]]** question this wiki holds open: no source held here
  addresses the licensing, hosting or exit terms. See [[dpi.id]], [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2024-09-18 Seychelles Makes Digital ID Mandatory for Work Permit Holders]].
