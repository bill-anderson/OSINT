# Mattel

Mauritanienne de Télécommunications (Mattel) — one of Mauritania's three long-standing mobile operators with
**[[mauritel]]** and **[[chinguitel]]**. A **provisional 5G licence winner** (April 2026, bid
**MRU 252.6m + 5%**, the lowest of the four). Among the operators served biometric-SIM enforcement notices by
**[[are-mauritania|ARE]]**.
