# Nexus AI Factory (Morocco sovereign AI data centre)

A landmark **sovereign AI-compute build** unveiled at **GITEX Africa 2026**, led by **[[nexus-core-systems|Nexus
Core Systems]]** with the digital-transition ministry (MTNRA), MICEPP and AMDIE — integrating a
**high-performance (HPC) data centre**, a Center of Excellence (skills) and an Innovation Hub, positioned
to bridge Africa, Europe and global markets under **[[digital-morocco-2030|Digital Morocco 2030]]**. See
[[morocco--infra-store]].

*(A GITEX-2026 deal round-up put the build at ~**12bn dirham ($1.28bn)**, TAQA-powered with Nvidia/Lloyds
involvement — see the GITEX chunk.)*
