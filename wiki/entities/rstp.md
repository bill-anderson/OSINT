# Royal Science and Technology Park (RSTP)

**Eswatini's state science-and-technology park**, and the operator of the
[[eswatini-national-data-centre]] — the country's principal domestic hosting asset and
its nearest thing to a sovereign compute base. See [[SWZ]], [[infra.store]].

*Capture is not endorsement.* Nearly everything the wiki holds on RSTP comes from RSTP's
own facility page, which is marketing-inflected and **discloses no capacity, tenant,
uptime-tier or utilisation figures**. Treat its claims as the operator's, not as
verified state.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Operates the National Data Centre.** The NDC is described as an entity of RSTP,
  positioned to provide ICT services to enterprises, act as the e-government
  development platform, support research and high-performance computing, and promote
  digital inclusion. Detail on the facility itself is on
  [[eswatini-national-data-centre]]. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[capacity.research]].
  Source: [[2021-01-01 The National Data Centre - Royal Science and Technology Park]].
- **A locus for unlegislated digital-ID work (2024-07-22).** [[apc]] reports
  "indications that, through the Royal Science and Technology Park (RSTP), initiatives
  are being conceptualised" on biometrics and digital identity — noted as
  conceptualisation without a public mandate, at a point when national government "has
  not started rolling out digital identity in earnest." See [[dpi.id]],
  [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2024-07-22 Risk of human rights infringement as Eswatini rushes to regulate the ICT sector]].
