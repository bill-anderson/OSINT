# ANRT (National Telecommunications Regulatory Agency, Morocco)

Morocco's **telecoms regulator**, running licensing and coverage/quality oversight under Maroc Digital
2030. See [[infra.connect]], [[morocco--infra-connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **5G licensing round (2025).** [[gov.legislate]] ANRT launched the **5G licence tender** (Jul 2025) and
  set **moderate one-off fees — 900m dirham (Maroc Telecom), 600m each (Orange, Inwi)** — with coverage
  targets of **~25% by 2026 and 70% by 2030**, driving rollout via **regulatory stability rather than
  public funds**; it will assess operator progress after the first operational year. Sources:
  [[2025-07-16-telecomreviewafrica-morocco-5g-license-tender]],
  [[2025-11-07-en-hespress-what-5g-could-mean-for-morocco]].
