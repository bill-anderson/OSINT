# Gest-exo

**The [[otr-togo]] digital platform for managing tax exemption and waiver requests**, launched
in Lomé on **30 September 2025** and **mandatory from 1 January 2026**. It lets economic
operators file requests remotely and track them from submission through validation in real
time, and is presented as simplifying, securing and automating a previously manual process. See
[[TGO]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Design: traceability plus automatic customs handoff.** The platform provides **full
  traceability**, **real-time monitoring**, and **automatic integration with the customs software
  [[sydonia]]** — the exemption decision flowing straight into the customs system rather than
  being re-keyed. **Atta-Kakra Essien**, OTR Commissioner for Customs and Indirect Taxes: "This
  platform responds to an imperative of efficiency. It ensures smooth management of requests,
  from submission to automatic transmission to the SYDONIA customs system." A system-to-system
  handoff inside the revenue administration, and the clearest [[dpi.exchange]] case in the
  Togolese material held here. Source:
  [[2025-10-01 Togo Launches Online Platform for Tax Exemptions and Waivers]].
- **Transition to mandatory use.** Launched **30 September 2025**, with a **three-month
  transition** to complete requests under the old procedures; use became **mandatory on 1
  January 2026**. See [[gov.policy]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-01 Togo Launches Online Platform for Tax Exemptions and Waivers]].
- **Part of a wider OTR digital estate.** Gest-exo joins existing OTR services including online
  management of **laissez-passer permits** and **motor vehicle tax payments**. See
  [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-01 Togo Launches Online Platform for Tax Exemptions and Waivers]].
