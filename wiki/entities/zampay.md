# ZamPay

Zambia's **government digital payment gateway** — the payment layer behind **[[zamportal]]** and the
Government Service Bus (GSB), through which the state collects fees for public services.

- Integrated with ZamPortal-onboarded systems (PMIS, councils, etc.). Government revenue collected via
  GSB/ZamPortal/ZamPay reached **K8.8bn (~$488m)** with 400+ services onboarded (2026). See
  [[zambia--dpi-govtech]], [[zambia--dpi-pay]]. Sources: [[2026-05-13-zambia-launches-pmis-property-management]],
  [[2026-07-10-digital-services-strengthen-zambia-finances]].
