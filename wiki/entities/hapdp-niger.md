# High Authority for the Protection of Personal Data (HAPDP)

Niger's **data-protection authority** (Haute Autorité de Protection des Données à Caractère Personnel), the
regulator responsible for supervising personal-data processing under the country's
**[[niger-data-protection-law|data-protection law]]**. It sits within a legal chain running from **Loi 2017-28**
(as amended by Loi 2019-71) through subsequent ordinances, giving Niger a formal, if young, privacy-enforcement
institution.
