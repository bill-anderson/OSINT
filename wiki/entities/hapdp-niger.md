# High Authority for the Protection of Personal Data (HAPDP)

Niger's **data-protection authority** (Haute Autorité de Protection des Données à Caractère Personnel), the
regulator responsible for supervising personal-data processing under the country's
**[[niger-data-protection-law|data-protection law]]** (President **Iro Adamou**). Created in 2017 under Loi
2017-28, it now operates under **Loi n° 2022-59 (16 Dec 2022)** — which **repealed and replaced** the
2017-28/2019-71 arsenal — and under Niger's **ratification of the Malabo Convention (23 May 2022)**, giving Niger
a formal, if young, privacy-enforcement institution. Its powers include *délibérations*, receiving
declarations/complaints, sworn-agent controls, referral of offences and opinions on draft laws. Source:
[[2025-07-28-hapdp-president-interview-loi-2022-59-lesahel]].
