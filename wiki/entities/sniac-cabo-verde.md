# SNIAC (Sistema Nacional de Identificação e Autenticação Civil), Cabo Verde

**Cabo Verde's national civil identification and authentication system — the statutory frame under which the *Cartão Nacional de Identificação* (CNI) and the *Chave Móvel Digital* (CMDCV) sit.** SNIAC is the credential layer beneath the CRVS stack, and it was designed from the outset as an **authentication system, not a document-issuance scheme**.

## The statutory chain

| Instrument | Date | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Decreto-Lei n.º 19/2014** | **17 Mar 2014** | creates the CNI; governs issuance, replacement, use and cancellation |
| **Decreto-Lei n.º 68/2014** | **22 Dec 2014** | fee regime for issuance, replacement and delivery |
| **Portaria n.º 21/2017** | **15 Jun 2017** | regulates biometric data capture |
| **Portaria n.º 35/2017** | **2 Sep 2017** | sets the CNI's validity period |
| **Decreto-Legislativo n.º 5/2020** | **21 Jul 2020** | creates the **Chave Móvel Digital de Cabo Verde (CMDCV)** |
| **Portaria n.º 27/2022** | **24 Jun 2022** | CMDCV implementing rules |

*These are structural facts — a law's provisions — and are not dated as time-varying figures.*

## The credential

- **CNI launched 23 January 2018**, in Paúl, Santo Antão, replacing the older *Bilhete de Identidade*.
- A **polycarbonate contact smart card** whose chip carries a digital certificate plus **identification, authentication and digital-signature applications**. Using the digital functions requires a **smart-card reader, CNI middleware and a PIN letter** collected from the issuing service — a meaningful friction on actual use.
- The **CMDCV** is the mobile alternative: a permanent PIN plus a one-time code per authentication. In the phase the source describes ("nesta primeira fase"), it **can only be obtained by first activating the CNI's digital functions** — binding the mobile credential to the card rather than replacing it. *That phase description is undated on the source page and may no longer hold.*
- Both feed **[[autentika-cabo-verde|Autentika]]**, the state's electronic-identity and authentication platform.

## Not established

- **No enrolment count, no CMDCV activation figure, and no dated uptake figure for CNI or CMDCV is held from any source, as of 2026-07-20.** The reference pages behind this material are themselves **undated**, and the source is filed `date_source: proxy` at the retrieval date — that date carries **no evidential weight** about when the facts became true.

## Reads

- `sovereignty`: a smart card that needs a reader, middleware and a collected PIN letter is a sovereignty asset with a usability tax. The CMDCV was the answer, but binding it to prior CNI activation preserves the bottleneck it was meant to bypass.
