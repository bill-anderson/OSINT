# SADC Digital Transformation Strategy and Action Plan (SADC-DTS, draft)

A **draft** regional digital strategy for the **[[sadc]]** bloc (document ref
**SADC/CM/2/2023/5.2.3B**, dated **12 October 2022**), setting a harmonised digital-transformation
agenda and action plan for Southern Africa.

## What it is (as of 2022-10, draft)

- **Policy alignment.** Explicitly nested within the **SADC Regional Indicative Strategic
  Development Plan (RISDP 2020-30)**, the **2012 SADC RIDMP ICT sector plan** ([[sadc-ridmp]]; its
  "Digital SADC 2027" framing), the **[[au-digital-transformation-strategy]]** (AU-DTS 2030), UN
  2030 universal-meaningful-connectivity targets, and the EU–AU digital partnership.
- **Approach.** Articulates regional vs national/sectoral digital interventions and a theory of
  change — achieving targets via **harmonised, effective and transformative policies** and regional
  support for national implementation. See [[gov.regional]], [[gov.standards]], [[dpi.exchange]].
- **Status.** Draft (Council-of-Ministers document); recorded here as the regional instrument
  behind Southern-African digital-harmonisation items.
