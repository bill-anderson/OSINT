# DRC National Artificial Intelligence Strategy

The DRC's **first National AI Strategy**, launched for drafting (8 Oct 2025) alongside the **[[drc-national-digital-plan-pnn2|PNN2]]** — framed around technological sovereignty and leveraging the country's critical minerals as both an investment catalyst and an AI-solutions input.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Congolese Academy of AI planned (2025-10).** The Strategy provides for creating a **Congolese Academy
  of Artificial Intelligence** to train young talent, encourage applied research and stimulate local
  innovation — the human-capital arm of the sovereignty framing. Financing sits within the PNN2 **$1.5bn**
  envelope ($1bn public + $500m secured partners). See [[tech.ai]], [[capacity.training]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-11 La RDC prevoit 1,5 milliard $ de budget pour son Plan national du numerique et de l'IA 2026-2030 (Agence Ecofin)]].
