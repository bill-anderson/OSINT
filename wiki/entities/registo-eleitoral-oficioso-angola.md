# Registo Eleitoral Oficioso — Angola

Angola's **official (automatic) electoral registration** — the process by which the
adult-citizen database (**[[base-de-dados-de-cidadaos-maiores-angola|BDCM]]**) is updated
and validated ahead of an election, now paired with a mandatory biometric **proof-of-life**
(*prova de vida*) to purge deceased or rights-suspended names.

## Current state (2027 general elections)

- **The update runs 2026-06-15 → 2027-03-31**, in-country and in the diaspora, through the
  **[[balcao-unico-de-atendimento-ao-publico-angola|BUAP]]** network (254 of a planned 634
  live in the first phase) and mobile brigades. Accepted documents: **voter card, BI or
  passport (diaspora), even if expired**; those without any document must first obtain a BI
  (late registration where needed). No new voter cards are issued. Source:
  [[2026-06-15-arranca-prova-de-vida-para-actualizacao-do-registo-eleitoral]].
- **Target universe: 16,707,455 citizens.** The validated BDCM is handed to the electoral
  commission for voting-assembly mapping and the electoral rolls. Source:
  [[2026-06-15-arranca-prova-de-vida-para-actualizacao-do-registo-eleitoral]].
- **BI issuance is the gating dependency.** Because the update rests on holding a BI,
  MINJUSDH reinforced BI issuance (decentralised printing, mobile brigades, BUAP capture)
  specifically to feed the register; 120,000+ issued BIs remain uncollected. Sources:
  [[2026-06-12-justica-reforca-emissao-do-bi-registo-eleitoral]],
  [[2026-07-15-mais-de-120-mil-bilhetes-aguardam-levantamento]].
- **Governance.** Coordinated by the presidential proof-of-life commission (chair
  [[dionisio-da-fonseca]]). Source:
  [[2026-06-08-pr-cria-comissao-actualizacao-dados-cidadaos-maiores-bdcm]].
