# Autentika (CRVS-ID interoperability platform)

Cabo Verde's **civil-registration ↔ identity interoperability platform** — it assigns a **Unique
Identification Number (UIN) at birth** and links civil registration to the ID system, credited with
driving **birth registration to ~99% in five years**. Showcased at **ID4Africa 2026**. Source:
[[2026-05-19-crvs-id-reforms-birth-registration-biometricupdate]].
