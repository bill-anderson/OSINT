# Fédération Nationale des Entreprises du Numérique

Djibouti's **national federation of digital enterprises** — a private-sector body constituted (Dec 2025) as a
**founding member of the pan-African digital-enterprises federation** (Marrakech Declaration), giving the
country's tech firms an organised voice in the strategy stack. Source:
[[2025-12-15-federation-nationale-entreprises-numerique-wearetech]].
