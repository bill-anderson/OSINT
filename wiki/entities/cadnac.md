# CADNAC (Central African Digital Development Entities Conference)

**A planned Central African platform for cooperation between national digital-development
agencies** — in formation as of late 2025. Per [[adetic]], CADNAC is intended to promote
inter-state cooperation, **pool expertise**, strengthen public digital policies and support
**regional digital sovereignty** across [[XCA]].

CADNAC is captured here as an **organisation**, not a place: per the wiki's rule, blocs and
regional bodies are entities. It is an agency-level conference — a body of digital-development
entities — rather than a treaty organisation of states, and is distinct from [[cemac]].

## Status

**Foundational texts in drafting.** Chad ([[TCD]]) joined the drafting meeting for CADNAC's
foundational texts on **25–26 November 2025**. As of the single source held (2025-12-02) the
platform is *planned*: no founding date, membership list, secretariat, host state or legal form is
reported. Source:
[[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Part of a wider Chadian cooperation push.** The CADNAC drafting meeting sits alongside Chad's
  November 2025 [[itu]] World Telecommunication Development Conference engagement, its
  partnership talks with Azerbaijan, and — as reported for the year from January 2025 — expanded
  collaboration with Guinea-Bissau, the UAE, Greece, Cameroon, the UNDP, Kenya, [[cemac]] member
  states on free roaming, India and Canada. Ecofin's own caveat is worth keeping: **"many
  cooperation initiatives remain at an early stage."** See [[gov.regional]], [[gov.policy]].
  Source: [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].
- **The sovereignty framing is the agency's own.** "Regional digital sovereignty" is ADETIC's
  characterisation of CADNAC's purpose, not an independent assessment. See [[sovereignty]].
  Source: [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].
