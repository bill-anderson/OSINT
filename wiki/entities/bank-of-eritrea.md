# Bank of Eritrea

Eritrea's **central bank and monetary authority**, anchoring a state banking monopoly
in a largely **cash-based, under-banked** economy (banks are confined to towns and
cities). Its posture is relevant to the wiki as context for the **near-total absence
of mobile money and digital payments** in Eritrea (see [[dpi.pay]], [[ERI]]).

On **~30 June 2026** it issued a **legal notice** ordering individuals and
institutions to deposit cash held outside banks "in excessive amounts" by
**31 July 2026** or face legal action — offering no definition of "excessive amount"
or enforcement mechanism. Diaspora/opposition outlet Awate framed it against past
currency/demonetisation measures (Eritrea last changed its Nakfa notes in 2015); see
[[gov.legislate]].
