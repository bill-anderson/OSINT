# East African Payment System (EAPS)

**The East African Community's cross-border payment system**, through which regional
transfers settle between member central banks. Uganda's participation is reported by
[[bank-of-uganda]]; the wiki holds EAPS only from the Ugandan side, so its figures below
are Uganda inflows, not system-wide totals.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Uganda EAPS activity, FY2024/25 (reported 2025-10-06).** Per BoU's Integrated Annual
  Report, EAPS **inflows reached UGX 1.3 trillion, up 17%**, with **Kenyan and Tanzanian
  currency transactions making up 99% of activity** — a regional payment rail that is, on
  these figures, effectively a two-corridor system for Uganda. Reported alongside BoU's
  **RTGS Replacement and ISO 20022 Migration** (go-live set for **November 2025**), which
  is framed as making payments *regionally compatible*. See [[dpi.pay]],
  [[gov.regional]], [[bank-of-uganda]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-06 BoU's New Payment System Cuts Delays and Boosts E-Money by 28.6%]].
