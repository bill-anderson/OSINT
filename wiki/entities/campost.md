# CAMPOST (Cameroon Postal Services)

Cameroon's public postal operator, modernising last-mile delivery, addressing and postal financial services.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National address system with FindMe (2024).** Campost partnered Cameroonian start-up **[[findme]]** (Nov 2024) to enhance the **national address system** (FindMe's model, already deployed in Senegal), tied to e-commerce last-mile delivery and improved customer identification (**KYC**) for financial services — a digital-addressing registry layer. DG: Pierre Kaldadak. See [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2024-11-04 Cameroon's Campost partners with FindMe to improve national address system]].
