# Bokeli (FOGEC AI platform)

A **live AI-assisted state platform** (FOGEC, launched 3 Feb 2026) that generates **bankable business
plans** for entrepreneurs — built by Congolese firm **Quantum Vertex**, an example of an *endogenous*
AI service rather than a foreign one. Source: [[2026-02-04-bokeli-plateforme-fogec-actualite]].
