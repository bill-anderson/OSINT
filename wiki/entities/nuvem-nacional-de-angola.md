# Nuvem Nacional de Angola (Government Cloud)

Angola's **unified government cloud**, built on the **[[data-center-e-cloud-do-governo-angola]]**
(Camama) as its base platform. Its purpose is to **centralise and integrate** the information
systems of ministries, public institutions and sovereign organs.

## What we know

- Operationalisation stated as planned **for 2026** (the year of the data centre's April inauguration).
- On commissioning, a **progressive and mandatory migration** of public services onto the platform
  begins — the localisation mechanism at the heart of Angola's sovereignty push. (An earlier account,
  Nov 2025, framed this as a **30-day** mandatory service-migration window; the April 2026 inauguration
  reporting restated it as "progressive and mandatory.")
- Designed to host **four structuring cloud platforms**, cut computing costs for firms, incentivise
  local application development, and keep **strategic state and citizen data under national
  jurisdiction** ([[mario-oliveira]]).
