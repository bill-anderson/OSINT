# ICT Authority (Kenya)

Kenya's state agency (icta.go.ke) responsible for building and coordinating public-sector
ICT infrastructure, standards and digital-skills programmes. It is **distinct from the
[[communications-authority-of-kenya]]**, which is the sector **regulator**; the ICT
Authority is a delivery/implementation body sitting under the State Department for ICT &
Digital Economy.

## Activity in this wiki

- **2026 infrastructure targets (as of 2026-01-05).** The Authority announced plans to
  extend Kenya's national fibre-optic network to **100,000 km** and deploy **25,000 public
  WiFi hotspots** during 2026, alongside digital-literacy/skills work with the ICT industry,
  responsible-data-management practices, and e-waste reduction. The round-number targets are
  aspirational plans rather than delivered figures. See [[infra.connect]], [[include.access]],
  [[capacity.training]], [[KEN]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-05 Kenya Targets 100,000 km Fiber Network, 25,000 Public WiFi Hotspots in 2026]].
