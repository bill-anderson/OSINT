# African Centre for Media Excellence (ACME)

**A Ugandan media-research and journalism organisation** — author of the 2023 study
linking Uganda's biometric and digital identity programmes to surveillance of journalists.

## Activity in this wiki

- **The 2023 BDI study (reported 2024-06-05).** A study by ACME concludes that the
  implementation of **biometric and digital identity (BDI) programmes in Uganda has given
  room for surveillance and intrusion on journalism and media**, with **journalists
  targeted** due to the mass collection of data under the government's biometric and
  digital ID programmes and its ability to engage in communications surveillance. Reported
  alongside the [[nick-opiyo]] case and the Bloomberg / Lighthouse Reports investigation
  (the primary, not held — GAP-UGA-002). See [[gov.protect]], [[dpi.id]],
  [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2024-06-05 Ugandan human rights lawyer's arrest exposes use of national ID for surveillance]].
