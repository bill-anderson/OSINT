# Digital X.0 law (Morocco)

Morocco's proposed **Digital X.0** national law — the legal foundation of Digital Morocco 2030
([[digital-morocco-2030]]), integrating **AI governance, data governance and digital identity** into one
framework ([[gov.legislate]], [[tech.ai]]). Presented by Minister Delegate Amal El Fallah Seghrouchni;
under General Secretariat review before parliament. Three pillars: **data governance, digital identity,
interoperability** — ensuring secure/ethical data use under **Law 09-08**, sectoral digital IDs keeping
data control with users, and a traceable consent mechanism for public↔private data exchange, plus
cybersecurity and transparency. Morocco's **first attempt to embed AI governance** into national policy.
See [[MAR]].
