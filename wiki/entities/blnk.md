# Blnk

Egyptian **point-of-sale consumer-lending** fintech (founded 2021). Raised **$37.1m** (2026-06) — a **$12.5m
Series A** led by **Algebra Ventures** (with SANAD Fund, Endeavor Catalyst, EIIC) plus **$24.6m local-currency
debt** (National Bank of Egypt, Suez Canal Bank, Bank Al Baraka Egypt). Lends through **3,000+ merchants**,
**1m+ customers**, loan book **>EGP 1bn ($21m)**; profitable in 2025 (revenue +173% YoY). Targets a large
credit gap — **~16.7m Egyptian adults had no formal credit access (Jun 2025)**. See [[egypt--dpi-pay]].
