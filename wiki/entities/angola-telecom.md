# Angola Telecom

Angola's **state-owned incumbent fixed-line / wholesale operator** (Chairman Adilson dos Santos). It
is emerging as the state's vehicle for **international-capacity and infrastructure-sharing** deals.

## Activity in this wiki

- **SARSSy / Equiano capacity (2026-06).** With **[[telecom-namibia]]** and **SATCAB**, Angola Telecom
  signed the **[[sarssy]]** master service agreement, gaining **1.0 Tbps** of dedicated Equiano
  capacity landed at Swakopmund, with the option to **assume ownership** of its infrastructure
  components over time. See [[equiano-cable]], [[telecom-namibia-angola-deals]].
- **Infrastructure-sharing with [[unitel]] (2026-06).** An infrastructure-sharing agreement signed at
  ANGOTIC to extend broadband to **underserved border regions**, improve coverage, redundancy and
  resilience.

See [[angola--infra-connect]], [[infra.connect]], [[AGO]], [[sarssy]].
