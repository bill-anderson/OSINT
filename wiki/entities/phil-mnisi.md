# Phil Mnisi

**Governor of the [[central-bank-of-eswatini]]** and, at the same date, **Chairman of
the [[comesa]] Committee of Governors of Central Banks (as of 2024-12-11)** — a
dual role that puts Eswatini's payments modernisation in a regional frame. See
[[SWZ]], [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched Fast Payments (2024-12-11, Ezulwini).** Mnisi delivered the launch speech
  for the [[eswatini-payment-switch]] Fast Payments product, marking completion of the
  first of the project's three phases (Fast Payments, then Open Banking, then POS and
  ATM Switching). He set out the switch's objectives — interoperability across banks
  and non-banks, sub-second processing toward a **cash-lite economy**, 24/7 availability
  — acknowledged implementation provider [[paylogic]] and [[world-bank]] technical
  assistance, and introduced the new EPS logo. *(Speech delivered 2024-12-11; published
  in BIS Review 2025-01-14.)* See [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-14 Phil Mnisi - Eswatini Payment Switch Fast Payments product launch]].
