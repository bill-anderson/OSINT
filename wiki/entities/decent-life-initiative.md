# Decent Life (Haya Karima) initiative

Egypt's **presidential rural-development programme**, which the
**[[mcit-egypt|Ministry of Communications and Information Technology]]** uses as the delivery
vehicle for rural connectivity: extending **high-speed fibre-optic infrastructure** and upgrading
telecommunications services in targeted villages.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Fibre to 1,250+ villages (as of 2026-07).** MCIT said it has extended high-speed fibre-optic
  infrastructure to **more than 1,250 rural villages** through Decent Life, and named the
  initiative's villages as a focus of its expanded digital cooperation with the
  **[[world-bank]]**. Source: [[2026-07-17-egypt-deepens-world-bank-digital-partnership]].

The figure is a **ministry self-report** with no independent verification, no denominator for the
programme's total village target, and no service-quality or uptake data on file — the recurring
shape of Egypt's rural-connectivity evidence in this wiki.
