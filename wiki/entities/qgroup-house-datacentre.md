# QGroup House data centre (The Gambia)

**The Gambia's first (private) data centre** — inside **QGroup House**, a "smart" facility inaugurated by
President **Adama Barrow** on **30 Apr 2026**, developed by the **QGroup / QCell** (with **QFiber**). Framed as
**Gambian-owned digital sovereignty**; **[[huawei]]** (QCell's 17-year partner) called it "the digital backbone
of the nation" (`geopol.china`). Complements the state's **[[nims-gambia|National Data Centre]]** (Abuko, Jul
2026). See [[gambia--infra-connect]].
