# Biometric Digital-ID in Africa: Ten Country Case Studies (2025)

A continental reference volume (December 2025; edited by Gbenga Sesan and Tony Roberts) assessing the
**progress and challenges of biometric digital ID across ten African countries** through a rights,
inclusion and governance lens ([[dpi.id]], [[gov.protect]]). A comparative frame for the wiki's many
country digital-ID stories (Liberia, Kenya, Ghana, etc.). See [[XAF]].
