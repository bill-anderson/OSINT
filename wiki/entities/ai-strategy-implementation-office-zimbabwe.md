# AI Strategy Implementation Office (AISIO), Zimbabwe

The body overseeing implementation of the **[[zimbabwe-national-ai-strategy|National AI Strategy 2026–2030]]**
— Treasury- and USF-funded, coordinating the Sovereign National Cloud, HPC national assets, a regulatory
sandbox and the forthcoming National AI Act. See [[zimbabwe--tech-ai]].
