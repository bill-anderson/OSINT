# Privacy Scorecard Report (Unwanted Witness)

Annual assessment by [[unwanted-witness]] of privacy-compliance maturity across African organisations
and sectors — a recurring OSINT benchmark for the continent’s enforcement gap.

## Latest edition

- **2025 (5th edition).** 286 organisations across 9 countries and 8 sectors. Continental score 46% (up
  from 40% in 2024). Country ranking: Kenya 46 (top), Nigeria 44, Uganda 43, Mauritius 40, Ghana 36,
  Botswana 36, Rwanda 33, Tanzania 33, Zimbabwe 28 (lowest). Sectors: Banking 52 (best), Health 22
  (worst), Government 25. 14 of 20 top apps flagged "potentially dangerous"; Google + Meta > two-thirds
  of observed trackers. See [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-18 Africa’s Digital Economy Is Expanding Faster than Its Privacy Protections]].
