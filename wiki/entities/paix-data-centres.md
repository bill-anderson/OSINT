# PAIX Data Centres

Pan-African **carrier- and cloud-neutral data-centre operator** (CEO **Wouter van Hulten**), operating in **Accra (Ghana)** and **Nairobi (Kenya)**. In **May 2024** PAIX announced a joint venture with the **[[fonds-souverain-djibouti|Fonds Souverain de Djibouti]]** to build **JIB1** in Djibouti — ~50,000 sq ft, up to 5 MW, ~$50m investment, first phase expected 2026 — positioned as a regional interconnection hub tapping Djibouti's ~10 subsea cable landings.

Part of Djibouti's data-centre build-out alongside **[[wingu-africa]]**.
