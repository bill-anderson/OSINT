# Federal Ministry of Education, Nigeria

Nigeria's Federal Ministry of Education is the federal authority for education policy and
system data. It is the owner of Nigeria's new national education management information
system, [[dnemis-nigeria]]. Minister: **Dr Maruf Tunji Alausa**.

## What matters now

- **DNEMIS launch (2026).** The ministry launched **DNEMIS**, a [[dhis2]]-based national
  education MIS, at an event in Abuja (~2026-07-05), built with the [[hisp-centre]]
  (University of Oslo), HISP Nigeria, UNICEF Nigeria and the [[european-union]]. Minister
  Alausa set out a vision for **data-driven resource allocation** across all levels of the
  education system and explicitly framed the project around **data sovereignty** — data
  domiciled in Nigeria. See [[dpi.mis]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[sovereignty]].

- **Japan/UNESCO teacher-capacity project (handover 2026-06-02).** The ministry is the implementing
  partner, with **[[unesco-iicba]]** and funding from **[[japan]]**, for *Capacity Building of
  Teachers to Promote Continuous and Inclusive Access to Safe and Quality Education for Girls in
  West Africa*. Under it, **15 Colleges of Education** across Nigeria received laptops, tablets,
  desktop computers, smart boards, projectors, printers and backup hard drives, handed over at the
  ministry in Abuja by the Japanese Chargé d'Affaires **Hitoshi Kozaki** — aimed at strengthening
  teacher education, improving digital literacy and supporting gender-responsive teaching. **No
  monetary value, recipient-college list, connectivity/maintenance provision or project timeframe
  is disclosed.** Note the pairing: this ministry is simultaneously building the national education
  **data** layer (DNEMIS) and receiving the **device** layer from a bilateral donor — the
  information system and the hardware that would feed it arriving through entirely separate
  channels. See [[capacity.training]], [[capacity.literacy]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-japan-ict-equipment-nigerian-colleges]].
