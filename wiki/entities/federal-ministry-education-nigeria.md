# Federal Ministry of Education, Nigeria

Nigeria's Federal Ministry of Education is the federal authority for education policy and
system data. It is the owner of Nigeria's new national education management information
system, [[dnemis-nigeria]]. Minister: **Dr Maruf Tunji Alausa**.

## What matters now

- **DNEMIS launch (2026).** The ministry launched **DNEMIS**, a [[dhis2]]-based national
  education MIS, at an event in Abuja (~2026-07-05), built with the [[hisp-centre]]
  (University of Oslo), HISP Nigeria, UNICEF Nigeria and the [[european-union]]. Minister
  Alausa set out a vision for **data-driven resource allocation** across all levels of the
  education system and explicitly framed the project around **data sovereignty** — data
  domiciled in Nigeria. See [[dpi.mis]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[sovereignty]].
