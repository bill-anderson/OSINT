# Gozem

A Lomé-based super-app startup (mobility/commerce), which launched **Gozem Money** (Oct 2025) — a mobile-
money wallet (bills, online purchases, transfers, PFM) with NSIA Banque handling BCEAO regulatory
compliance, built on its acquisition of Beninese fintech Moneex. A founding member of TogoTech. See
[[togo--dpi-pay]], [[TGO]].
