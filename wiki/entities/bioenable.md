# BioEnable Technologies

An Indian biometric-technology vendor (CEO/CTO **Mahesh Ghatge**), **MOSIP-compliant** since end-2024. In
**Niger** it powered a **[[mosip|MOSIP]]**-based national-digital-ID **pilot** (Feb 2025) under **[[wuri-programme|
WURI-Niger]]** and the Interior Ministry — deploying its **IriUniverse-Two dual-iris scanners** for ISO-compliant
registration across **four communes** (Tanout, Mayahi, Doutchi, Tagazar), **3,000+ enrolled** — the open-source
ID track distinct from Niger's Al-Itissal/AES card. See [[niger--dpi-id]].
