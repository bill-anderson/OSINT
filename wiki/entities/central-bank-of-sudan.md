# Central Bank of Sudan (CBoS)

Sudan's monetary authority and banking/payments regulator, setting the rules for
digital wallets, bank interoperability and mobile financial services during the
wartime rebuild of the payments system.

## Activity in this wiki

- **USSD banking rollout (2026-03).** Coordinated with the
  **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** and the
  **[[communications-postal-regulatory-authority-sudan]]** on the launch of
  USSD-based banking (offline transfers, balance and transaction queries) as a
  wartime connectivity-resilience/inclusion rail. See [[dpi.pay]],
  [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-18 Sudan Launches USSD Banking Services to Expand Digital Financial Access]].
- **Digital-wallet oversight (2025-04).** The **[[bede-wallet|Bede]]** wallet
  operates under CBoS digital-wallet policy, with trust accounts overseen by
  several banks. See [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2025-04-18 Zain Launches Bede Digital Wallet to Boost Financial Inclusion in Sudan]].
