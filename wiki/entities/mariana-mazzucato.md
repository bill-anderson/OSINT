# Mariana Mazzucato

Mariana Mazzucato is an economist and founder/director of the UCL Institute for Innovation and Public Purpose ([[ucl-iipp]]). She matters here for bringing her public-value frameworks to bear on digital public infrastructure.

## Profile
- Known for market-shaping, public value and the "entrepreneurial state" / common-good frameworks.
- Co-authored (with [[david-eaves]] and Beatriz Vasconcellos) the 2026 paper applying those frameworks to DPI, "what is 'public' about DPI."
