# Moov Africa Chad

One of Chad's two main mobile operators (Maroc Telecom group), alongside [[airtel-chad]]. Repeatedly
reprimanded by government over poor network quality; ordered (Jun 2026) to migrate transmission sites to
**solar/wind** after fuel-supply excuses were rejected. Runs **Moov Money** mobile money. See
[[chad--infra-connect]], [[chad--dpi-pay]], [[TCD]].
