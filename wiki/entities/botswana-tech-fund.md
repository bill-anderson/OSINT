# Botswana Tech Fund

A Gaborone-based **venture-capital vehicle** for Southern African startups — anchored by **Pula
Investments** (Stephen Lansdown) with **Launch Africa Ventures** as investment adviser and a Botswana
Innovation Hub partnership — pitched as filling a gap where African VC "rarely flows." See [[BWA]],
[[finance.new]].

## What we know (as of 2026-04)

- **Size is reported inconsistently.** [[finance.new]] Press-release coverage puts the fund at a
  **£50m (~$67.5m) target**; a bylined account reports only **£10m committed / £5m first close** (Phase One
  £5m, £1m pre-seed) and a **Guernsey domicile** — a target-vs-committed gap flagged for review
  (`reviews/contradictions/open/`). Sources: [[2026-04-24-botswana-tech-fund-67m-vehicle-wearetech]],
  [[2026-04-27-botswana-tech-fund-southern-africa-digital-gap-techcabal]].
