# Botswana Tech Fund

A **venture-capital vehicle** for Southern African startups, **domiciled in Guernsey** with its
**operational base in Gaborone** — anchored by **Pula Investments** (Stephen Lansdown) with **Launch Africa
Ventures** as Phase One investment adviser and a Botswana Innovation Hub partnership — pitched as filling a
gap where African VC "rarely flows." See [[BWA]], [[finance.new]].

## Size — target, committed and first close are three different figures

- **Target: £50m (~$67.5m)** in total commitments, as of **2026-04-21** — stated by the fund itself as a
  *target*, never as capital raised.
- **Committed: £10m (~$13.5m)** from anchor investor Pula Investments, as of **2026-04-27**.
- **First close: £5m (~$6.7m)**, deploying over the following six months, of which £1m to a pre-seed
  cohort — as of **2026-04-27**. Leaves £45m still to raise.

Headline "$67.5m / $64m fund" figures in launch coverage restate the £50m **target**, not capital raised;
the defect is in the promotional coverage, not in the fund's own release, which says "a **targeted** £50
million… with a first close in Q2/2026." Sources: [[2026-04-24-botswana-tech-fund-67m-vehicle-wearetech]],
[[2026-04-27-botswana-tech-fund-southern-africa-digital-gap-techcabal]].

*Domicile note:* Guernsey rests on the managing partner's on-record statement, not a register entry — no
Guernsey Financial Services Commission filing was found (checked 2026-07-20). The fund's own footer records
Botswana Tech Ltd as an FCA Appointed Representative of Thema Capital Limited — a UK regulatory wrapper over
a Guernsey vehicle with a Botswana operating base.
