# ANSUTEN (Agence Nationale du Service Universel des Télécommunications et du Numérique)

Guinea's **universal-service agency** for telecoms and digital. It ran a **white/grey-zone feasibility study**
(since 2023, with SFM Technologies) and produced a **national GIS** to target a **90% rural-population coverage
by 2030**. It also delivers digital inclusion beyond Conakry — the **N'Zérékoré digital hub** (forest region,
2025) and the **Univ Connect** higher-education fibre project (17 universities/institutes). See
[[guinea--infra-connect]]. *(One of the agencies flagged in GAP-GIN-001.)*
