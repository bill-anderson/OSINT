# Jan Hnizdo

**Chief Executive Officer of [[teraco]]**, South Africa's largest data-centre operator and a
**[[digital-realty]]** company — and therefore the executive voice behind the largest single
component of the South African data-centre power build-out.

## Activity in this wiki

- **On the JB7 build (2024-11-13).** Announcing Teraco's **40 MW hyperscale JB7** at the Isando
  campus — its **ninth** data-centre development, funded by an **R8-billion syndicated loan** and
  lifting Teraco's total critical power to **228 MW** — Hnizdo framed demand as driven by "growing
  hybrid cloud deployments and the adoption of cloud services in Africa," with JB7 serving "Teraco's
  long-term vision of enabling digital transformation across Africa." Source:
  [[2024-11-13 Teraco to build 40 MW JB7 data centre at Isando Campus]].
- **The regional-hub thesis, in his words (2024-11-13).** "Teraco is focused on growing its capacity
  footprint across its core hubs. We ensure our clients have the flexibility to scale and take
  advantage of the digital transformation across sub-Saharan Africa. We continue to invest
  significantly in the region's ICT infrastructure and have **built Africa's largest data centre
  platform**." He positions **South Africa as the springboard for cloud provision into Africa** and
  the data-centre hub for sub-Saharan Africa, citing "massive global investments into undersea
  cables, like **[[equiano-cable]]** and 2Africa" as further strengthening that position. *A vendor
  first-party framing — attribute it.* Source: same.
