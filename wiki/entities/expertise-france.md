# Expertise France

France's public international technical-cooperation agency (part of the Agence Française de
Développement group), delivering governance, digital and security capacity-building
programmes abroad. It is one channel through which European ([[geopol.eu]]) digital-governance
and cybersecurity models reach African partners.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Implementer of the Kenya Cyber Resilience (KCR) Project (2026).** Expertise France is
  the implementing agency for the EU-funded, €3m / 36-month
  **[[kenya-cyber-resilience-project]]** (launched Nairobi, week of 2026-01-27), working
  alongside **[[estdev]]** to protect Kenya's critical information infrastructure and build
  cyber skills. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[capacity.training]], [[KEN]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Kenya launches project to boost cyber security]].
