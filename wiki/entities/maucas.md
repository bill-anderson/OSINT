# MauCAS (Mauritius Central Automated Switch)

The **Bank of Mauritius**'s national **instant-payment switch**, enabling interoperable, real-time retail
payments across banks and payment providers (including QR-code and mobile payments). MauCAS is profiled in the
AfricaNenda **State of Inclusive Instant Payment Systems (SIIPS) 2024** case study as one of the continent's
more mature instant-payment rails, underpinning Mauritius's position as a digitally advanced financial
jurisdiction.
