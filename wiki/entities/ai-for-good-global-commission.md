# AI for Good Global Commission

ITU-convened commission of leaders from government, business and international
institutions on trusted, inclusive AI — "practical pathways to strengthen trust, support
responsible innovation, and enhance human capability."

## Leadership

- **Co-chairs:** **[[paul-kagame]]** (President of Rwanda) and **Marc Benioff**
  (Chair/CEO, Salesforce).
- **Vice-chair:** **Doreen Bogdan-Martin**, Secretary-General, ITU.

## Significance

Rwanda's co-chair seat is a rare African hand on the tiller of global AI-governance
norm-setting — read alongside the affordability/agency gap documented under [[tech.ai]]
and [[capacity.research]]. Commission profile published 2026-05-21; Kagame's role
reported 2026-07-03. See [[RWA]], [[gov.regional]].
