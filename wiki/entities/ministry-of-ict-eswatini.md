# Ministry of Information, Communications and Technology (MICT), Eswatini

**The lead ministry for Eswatini's digital agenda** ([[SWZ]]) — owner of the
[[eswatini-digitalisation-strategy-2024-2028]] and its
[[government-in-your-hand]] programme, implementing agency for
[[digital-eswatini-project]], and the political sponsor of the
[[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]]. Minister: **[[savannah-maziya]]**
(as of 2025-01-22). See [[gov.policy]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Owns the strategy and the flagship.** MICT pursues the digital agenda through
  [[government-in-your-hand]] and implementation of the
  [[eswatini-digitalisation-strategy-2024-2028]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-05 Eswatini Advances Toward Digital Transformation - Key Findings from the Digital Readiness Assessment]].
- **Commissioned the DRA with UNDP and ESEPARC (2024).** MICT conducted the
  [[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]] with [[undp]] and [[eseparc]], making
  Eswatini among the first African countries to implement the UNDP DRA instrument.
  Sources: [[2024-11-22 Eswatini unveils readiness report for digital ID, transformation push]],
  [[2024-12-10 Unveiling Eswatini's Digital Path]].
- **Implementer of Digital Eswatini (P508948).** MICT is named implementer of the
  **US$65.16m** [[world-bank]] [[digital-eswatini-project]] (appraisal 2025-08-01,
  approval 2025-09-01), and appears in the 2026-05 negotiations reported around the
  [[digital-eswatini-project|Eswatini Digital Transformation Project]] alongside the Ministry of
  Finance and the Ministry of Economic Planning and Development. See [[finance.new]]. Sources:
  [[2025-06-28 PID Concept Stage - Digital Eswatini (P508948)]],
  [[2026-05-18 Eswatini promotes digital transformation project through World Bank engagements]].
- **Mandate overlap flagged (2025-07-31).** The [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]]
  recommends clarifying mandates between MICT and regulators ([[esccom]]) to eliminate
  overlaps, and fully operationalising the Cybersecurity Agency and Data Protection
  Office through a national change-management programme. Source:
  [[2025-07-31 Eswatini Economic Update - Digital Transformation Key to Unlocking Growth]].
- **SIM-registration rulemaking.** The Minister of ICT published **Legal Notice No. 26
  of 2016**, framing registration of all mobile subscribers — names, addresses,
  identity numbers and facial images, retained up to five years post-contract. See
  [[dpi.id]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2024-07-22 Risk of human rights infringement as Eswatini rushes to regulate the ICT sector]].
- **Google partnership (2025-01-22).** MICT announced the
  [[eswatini-google-partnership]] to bring [[google]] tooling into
  [[government-in-your-hand]] — on a single secondary source; see that page's
  provenance caveat. Source:
  [[2025-01-22 Eswatini partners with Google to drive digital transformation and economic growth]].
