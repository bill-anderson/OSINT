# DouIA2 / Manifesto of Conakry

Guinea's **national AI-and-data initiative, phase 2 (DouIA2)** — launched with the signing of the **"Manifesto
of Conakry"** at the Cité des Sciences et de l'Innovation (2026-04), concluding DouIA1. It sets a shared vision
for integrating **AI and digital data** into national development, framed around **economic growth and digital
sovereignty** (Rose Pola Pricemou; initiated by Diaka Sidibé and former minister Alpha Bacar Barry). See
[[GIN]], [[tech.ai]].
