# Meristem Securities

Nigerian investment firm operating **Meritrade**, its online stockbroking platform.

Meristem is on file (2026-07-18) as an example of the *incumbent* response to app-based retail
investing: an established investment house expanding its own digital offering — alongside Stanbic
IBTC — rather than ceding the retail channel to entrants such as **[[bamboo]]** and
**[[cowrywise]]**. Meritrade users on record include self-directed traders running their own
fundamental analysis.
