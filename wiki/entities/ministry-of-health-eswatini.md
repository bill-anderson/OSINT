# Ministry of Health, Eswatini

**The health ministry running Eswatini's community-health digitisation** ([[SWZ]]) —
owner of the [[eswatini-nhssp]] strategy, of the [[eswatini-cmis]] national electronic
health record, and of the [[eswatini-cbhis]] rollout that is taking Rural Health
Motivators off paper. Its digital work is financed through the [[world-bank]]
[[eswatini-health-system-strengthening-project]]. See [[dpi.mis]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **CBHIS pilot and rollout (2026-02-23).** The Ministry introduced the electronic
  Community-Based Health Information System to digitise Rural Health Motivator
  reporting — offline-capable handheld devices syncing when connectivity allows,
  integrated with [[eswatini-cmis]]. A pilot in the **Mpolonjeni Inkhundla, Lubombo
  region**, was completed and RHM training begun as national rollout started **(as of
  2026-02-23)**. See [[digital.rural]], [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-23 From Paper to Pixels - Digital Transformation of Community Health Services in Eswatini]].
- **Why the community tier matters.** An estimated **77% of the population lived in
  rural areas (2026-02-23)** per the World Bank feature, and RHMs accounted for
  **nearly 22% of the national health workforce (2026-02-23)**. See [[include.access]].
  Source:
  [[2026-02-23 From Paper to Pixels - Digital Transformation of Community Health Services in Eswatini]].
- **Strategic frame.** The digitisation sits under the [[eswatini-nhssp]] (National
  Health Sector Strategic Plan 2024/25–2027/28). Source:
  [[2024-08-01 Eswatini National Health Sector Strategic Plan 2024-25 to 2027-28]].
