# Gabon–IGN FI Cadastre Modernisation Engagement

**Parties:** Gabon's Ministry of Housing, Habitat, Urbanism and Cadastre (Minister Ludovic Megne
Ndong) and **[[ign-fi]]** (French geomatics). **What:** an early-stage engagement toward digitising
Gabon's land sector via an **Integrated Urban Land Management System (SIG)**. **When:** delegation
received **24 November 2025**. **Status:** proposal stage — a strategic proposal submitted, not yet a
signed contract.

## What it covers

- Digital centralisation of land data to secure transactions (reduce double sales / fraud), streamline
  parcel registration and land-title issuance, and support urban planning on a single platform.
- Rationale: eliminate land disputes and optimise territorial planning; IGN FI cited its Uganda and
  Côte d'Ivoire land-reform experience.
