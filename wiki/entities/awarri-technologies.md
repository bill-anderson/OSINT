# Awarri Technologies

Nigerian AI-infrastructure company positioned as the government's homegrown sovereign-AI partner.
President Tinubu cited Awarri as a "key player" in Nigeria's AI ecosystem during the Feb-2025 Google
alliance talks ([[nigeria--tech-ai]]). Awarri built ****N-ATLAS**** — the Nigerian Atlas for Languages
& AI at Scale — with [[nitda]] and [[ncair]]: an open-source multilingual/multimodal LLM (Yoruba,
Hausa, Igbo, Nigerian-accented English) on Meta's Llama-3 8B, fine-tuned on 400m+ tokens (launched
UNGA80, Sep 2025).
