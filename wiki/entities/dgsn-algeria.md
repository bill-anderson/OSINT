# DGSN (Directorate General of National Security)

Algeria's **national police**, led by Director-General **Ali Badaoui**. Increasingly a digital-identity /
cyber actor: signed a **2025 MoU with the UK Home Office** for digital-forensics and biometric
identity-verification training, and hosted a **2026-07 study day** framing personal-data protection as core
to national security (*"national security is now cybersecurity first"*), with the Justice and Interior
ministers and the ANPDP president. See [[algeria--dpi-id]], [[algeria--infra-cybersec]].
