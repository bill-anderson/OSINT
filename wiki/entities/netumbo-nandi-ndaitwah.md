# Netumbo Nandi-Ndaitwah

President of Namibia; here as the principal in Namibia's digital-infrastructure
engagement with China.

## Activity in this wiki

- **State visit to China (2026-07).** Acknowledged China's **¥98m (~US$14.4m)**
  commitment to Namibia's Smart City pilot during a meeting with [[huawei]] in Shenzhen,
  and commended Huawei's work with Namibia on smart-city solutions and an AI-ready
  national data centre. Framed the partnership around a "secure, inclusive and
  knowledge-based economy," digital inclusion and workforce skills. See [[NAM]],
  [[china-namibia-smart-city]], [[geopol.china]].
- **China hands over first satellite ground station (2026-02).** Her statement (read
  by acting head of state PM Ngurare) marked China's handover of Namibia's **first
  satellite data-receiving ground station** (Telecom Earth Station outside Windhoek) for
  agriculture, disaster and environmental monitoring — wholly Namibia-run, with China
  training the technicians. Framed Namibia's clear skies as a "sovereign advantage" and a
  premier global hub for space observation; cabinet approved drafting a **Space Science &
  Technology Bill** (2025). See [[NAM]], [[geopol.china]], [[infra.connect]],
  [[data.satellite]]. Source: [[2026-02-13 Namibia gets  Chinese satellite ground station]].
