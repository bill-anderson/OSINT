# FindMe

Cameroonian start-up providing a **digital postal-addressing system** — a local player in the address-registry layer of DPI, deployed in Senegal and (from 2024) Cameroon.

## Activity in this wiki

- Partnered **[[campost]]** (Nov 2024) to adapt its addressing model for Cameroon's national address system (operational in Cameroon since July 2024), supporting e-commerce last-mile delivery and KYC. See [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2024-11-04 Cameroon's Campost partners with FindMe to improve national address system]].
