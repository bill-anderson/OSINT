# National Business Register (RNE), Tunisia

The **Registre National des Entreprises (RNE)** is Tunisia's national companies registry — the
authoritative record for business creation, name reservation and company extracts. Director-General
(2026): **Mohamed Adel Chouari**.

- **Fully digital from 1 July 2026.** Paper filing permanently discontinued; the whole business-creation
  chain (name reservation → tax ID → RNE extract) goes online with **no physical travel**, and **access
  requires a digital identity** ([[e-houwiya]]) for individuals, companies and regulated professions
  (lawyers, chartered accountants). Electronically submitted documents carry the **same legal/evidentiary
  value** as physical ones. Online tax-identification-card issuance targeted end-Sept 2026 (with the
  General Directorate of Taxes / Finance Ministry IT Centre). Under the Finance Minister's decree of
  12 Feb 2026. Source: [[2026-04-27-africanmanager-national-business-register-digital]].
