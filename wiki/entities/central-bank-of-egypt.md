# Central Bank of Egypt (CBE)

Egypt's central bank and the driving regulator of its **digital-payments and financial-inclusion** agenda (Governor **Hassan Abdalla**). Its systems and initiatives dominate Egypt's fintech DPI:

- **Digital financial identity:** the **[[haweya|Haweya]]** app / national **eKYC** project — a biometric digital-ID for remote bank-account opening and services.
- **Rails:** **InstaPay** (instant payments), **Meeza** national cards (43.5m), mobile wallets (55.5m accounts), and a new **SoftPOS** contactless-acceptance service.
- **Inclusion:** **77.6%** of adults financially included by end-2025 (from 27.4% in 2016); developing a **Second Financial Inclusion Strategy 2026–2030** with CAPMAS, the World Bank and IFC.

Frames its work around a **cashless-society** goal under **[[egypt-vision-2030|Vision 2030]]**.
