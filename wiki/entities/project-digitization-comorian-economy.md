# Project to Support Digitization of the Comorian Economy

The Comoros' flagship **AfDB-financed digital-government programme** — improving the governance, quality, affordability and accessibility of public services.

## Profile

- **Financing:** AfDB approved **€9.51m (~$10.4m)** (1 Oct 2024; African Development Fund + Transition Support Facility); total project est. **€22.53m (~$25m)**.
- **Components:** construction/operationalisation of a **new data centre** (+ the existing secondary DC — see **[[anaden]]**) and an **incubator**, run by a **PPP** private operator; a digital-administration platform; a **digital public payment system**; an **interoperability and data-exchange** platform; and creation of a **National Authority for the Protection of Personal Data and Access to Information**. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[infra.store]] and the [[COM]] hub. Source: [[2024-10-06 Comoros to implement digital govt program with AfDB grant (Digitization of the Comorian Economy)]].
