# A Blueprint for Mauritius — Digital Transformation 2025-2029

Mauritius's national digital strategy, **"A Blueprint for Mauritius — A Bridge to the Future: Digital
Transformation 2025-2029,"** published by the **Ministry of Information Technology, Communication and
Innovation (MITCI)** in **May 2025**. It sets the direction for e-government, digital identity, connectivity
and a modernised regulatory framework.

Follow-through in 2025: the government announced **new digital regulations** (Minister Ramtohul, Aug 2025) and
established an **inter-ministerial committee** (26 Aug 2025) to coordinate delivery across ministries. The
Blueprint is the policy umbrella above initiatives such as the **[[mauritius-mobile-id|Mobile ID]]** wallet.
