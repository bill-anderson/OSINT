# Algeria 5G programme

Algeria's national 5G rollout. **[[arpce-algeria|ARPCE]]** ran a three-licence tender in 2025; the licences —
**$492m / DZD 63.9bn** — were confirmed Dec 2025 to **[[mobilis]]**, **[[djezzy]]** and **[[ooredoo-algeria]]**.
A **six-year plan** from **eight pilot wilayas** to nationwide by **2031**; the government projects the digital
ecosystem contributing **$9bn → $13bn to GDP by 2030**. An **[[ericsson]]** study warns full deployment could
cost **$3–8bn**. By 2026-03 Djezzy was live in 18 states and Ooredoo across all wilayas. See
[[algeria--infra-connect]].
