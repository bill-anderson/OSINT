# National Electoral Commission (NEC), Somalia

Somalia's electoral management body (chair Abdikarim Ahmed Hassan, who also chairs the
Borders Committee), tasked with delivering the country's shift to **"one person, one
vote."**

- **18 Mar 2025** — unveiled **digital voter-registration equipment** in Mogadishu, to be
  tested at **local government elections scheduled for 30 June 2025** — the first direct
  election of local leaders in five decades (previous polls used a delegate system).
- Public voter registration is the first phase; regional parliamentary and presidential
  elections were envisaged to follow. The reform follows a 2024 federal/regional agreement
  introducing a presidential "one person, one vote" system.

Note: some Somalis have voiced concern that the national ID ([[snid]]) rollout and the
electoral card could be conflated without adequate public awareness (see
[[2025-11-17 A smart national ID powers Somalia's Digital Public Infrastructure vision]]).
