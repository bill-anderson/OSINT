# openIMIS

An **open-source management information system** for health financing and social protection — a global digital public good governed with backing from the World Bank, Germany's BMZ and Switzerland's SDC. Its **CORE-MIS** variant (built by the World Bank during COVID-19, integrated into openIMIS in 2023) administers cash transfers and social-registry functions.

## Use in this wiki

- **Burundi's Merankabandi (2025).** Powers Burundi's **[[merankabandi]]** Cash for Jobs programme via CORE-MIS — 1.25m beneficiaries across five provinces (since May 2025), with integrated payment providers and KoboToolbox, and social-registry features positioned for Burundi's planned Unified Social Registry. See [[dpi.mis]], [[BDI]]. Source: [[2026-01-01 Burundi's growing social protection system now runs on openIMIS]].
