# US Department of Commerce (International Trade Administration)

US government body; the ITA administers the **[[american-ai-exports-program]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **AI Exports Program update (2026-07-07).** ITA announced conclusion of the application
  window (78 applications); the Secretary of Commerce will issue final designation
  determinations after interagency consultation. See [[geopol.usa]], [[tech.ai]].
- **Underwhelming turnout (Politico, 2026-07-11).** The **78** first-round applications fell far
  short of the "hundreds" expected (ITA disputes a shortfall); **Phase 2** (deal bidding) had not
  yet launched, and majors were unnerved by the administration's abruptly-imposed-then-reversed
  order restricting [[anthropic]]'s foreign model access. See [[geopol.china]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-11 'Not where they hoped it'd be' Launch of Trump AI promotion program underwhelms]].
- **AI exports & Africa (2026).** Commerce manages the **American AI Exports Program** (central to Carnegie's Kenya-TPD argument) and hosted the Chad delegation on digital-transformation cooperation. See [[technology-prosperity-deal]], [[TCD]], [[geopol.usa]]. Sources: [[2026-06-23 A Kenya Technology Prosperity Deal Could Help Washington Secure Durable AI Partnerships with Africa]], [[2026-07-01 Chad and US Explore Partnerships on Satellite Connectivity and Cybersecurity]].
