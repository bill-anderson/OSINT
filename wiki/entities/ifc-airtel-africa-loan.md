# IFC–Airtel Africa $150m connectivity loan

A **$150m** loan (announced 2026-07-01, Nairobi) from the **[[ifc]]** to two
**[[airtel-africa]]** subsidiaries to expand and upgrade mobile networks in
underserved communities across Airtel's 14-country Sub-Saharan footprint, with
longer-tenor and local-currency financing.

- **Parties:** IFC (World Bank Group) · Airtel Africa (CEO Sunil Taldar).
- **Rationale:** high-speed data access, jobs, digital inclusion; continuation of
  a longstanding IFC–Airtel partnership.

See [[ifc]], [[airtel-africa]], [[XSS]], [[finance.new]].
