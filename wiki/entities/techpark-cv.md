# TechPark CV

Cabo Verde's flagship **technology-park programme** and centrepiece of its "tech islands" digital-diversification strategy — two campuses: **Praia (Santiago)** and **Mindelo (São Vicente)**. Backed by the **[[african-development-bank|AfDB]]**. Each campus houses a **data centre**, incubation hub and training/conference facilities.

## Value — resolved 2026-07-21: all three figures are the same money at different aggregations

The three readings the wiki carried as a conflict **reconcile exactly**, and reading (3) — the Bank's
money as a *component* of a larger project total — is the correct framing:

| | Total cost | AfDB financing | Other |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase I** (approved 2013) | **€35.90m** | **€31.59m** | Cabo Verde counterpart ~€4.31m |
| **Phase II** (approved Nov 2022) | **€15.95m** | **€14.00m** | KOAFEC grant €0.10m; counterpart €1.85m |
| **Combined** | **€51.85m** | **€45.59m** | |

€35.90m + €15.95m = **€51.85m**; €31.59m + €14.00m = **€45.59m**. Both sum precisely. So **€45.59m is
the Bank's financing across both phases**, not the project — press accounts that call it "the project"
mislabel it. **€45.5m is the same number truncated in the Bank's own press release**, not a different
quantity, and not an envelope-vs-disbursed distinction. Per the Phase II appraisal, the Bank finances
**87.75%** of that phase, counterpart funding **11.62%** and KOAFEC **0.63%**; Bank loans to Cabo Verde
are **denominated in EUR**, which removes exchange risk because the escudo is pegged to the euro — so
**euro is the announcing party's own currency and is what the wiki carries**.

**The USD figures are the Bank's own and are mutually unreconciled — dropped, not converted.** The
Bank's president said **US$50m** at the Praia inauguration on 2025-05-05; its press office said
**US$57m** on 2025-05-07. €45.59m at plausible May-2025 rates is about **US$51.5m**, matching neither.
The likeliest explanation is phase-by-phase conversion at each approval date (2013 and late-2022 rates
differ sharply), but **the Bank states no rate and no date**, so that is arithmetic, not a finding.

**"Nearly 2% of Cabo Verde's GDP" is struck, not re-based.** It appears in no AfDB or Cabo Verdean
government document — only in press and member-news write-ups. It is also methodologically unsound:
it sets a **cumulative 2013–2025 capital programme** against a **single year's GDP**. For context
only, and not carried as a claim: €45.59m is ~1.8% of 2024 GDP and ~2.0% of 2023 GDP — so the framing
"works" only against an unstated year.

**Still not established (2026-07-21):** any **disbursement** figure — every number above is a
commitment or approval — and the **Phase I appraisal report** itself, which is inferred from the Phase
II report's back-reference plus three independent corroborations rather than read directly. Both are
**queued for acquisition**. Sources:
[[2025-05-07-afdb-techpark-cv-inauguration-press-release]] (the financier's own release, carrying the
€51.85m/€45.5m framing and the unreconciled $57m).

- **Mindelo opening (6 May 2025):** 10 companies from four countries, 125 young professionals; tenants in AI, digital health, fintech, cybersecurity.
- **Incentives:** designated **Special (Digital) Economic Zone** — 2.5% corporation-tax discount, VAT/import-tax exemptions; **Remote Working Program** digital-nomad visa (12 months, min. €1,500/month income).
- **Goal:** raise the digital economy's share of GDP from **7% to 25%**; attract the diaspora and reduce dependence on tourism.
