# TechPark CV

Cabo Verde's flagship **technology-park programme** and centrepiece of its "tech islands" digital-diversification strategy — two campuses: **Praia (Santiago)** and **Mindelo (São Vicente)**. Backed by the **[[african-development-bank|AfDB]]** — but **the project's value is unresolved as at
2026-07-20**: press accounts of the May 2025 inauguration give **€45.59m** as the Bank's whole
investment, and **€51.85m** as the project total with **€45.5m** of Bank financing inside it, while the
Bank is separately said to have provided **$57m across Phases 1 and 2**. The "~2% of Cabo Verde's GDP"
framing depends on which figure is taken. No AfDB appraisal or approval document is on file. Each campus houses a **data centre**, incubation hub and training/conference facilities.

- **Mindelo opening (6 May 2025):** 10 companies from four countries, 125 young professionals; tenants in AI, digital health, fintech, cybersecurity.
- **Incentives:** designated **Special (Digital) Economic Zone** — 2.5% corporation-tax discount, VAT/import-tax exemptions; **Remote Working Program** digital-nomad visa (12 months, min. €1,500/month income).
- **Goal:** raise the digital economy's share of GDP from **7% to 25%**; attract the diaspora and reduce dependence on tourism.
