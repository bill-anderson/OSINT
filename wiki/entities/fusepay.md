# Fusepay

A **Seychelles-founded** B2B fintech for frontier markets, launched in Victoria with its first live
transaction on **19 Nov 2025**. Provides **virtual business accounts**, structured accounts-payable/
receivable, instant network transfers and **post-dated digital payments** as a cheque replacement (amid
the national cheque phase-out). Raised a **$350k pre-seed** (Aug 2025; Hustle Fund, Everywhere Ventures,
First Check Ventures, Startup Istanbul), 13-person team, planning expansion to adjacent under-digitised
markets. See [[seychelles--dpi-pay]], [[SYC]].
