# Baobab Cloud Services (Mitsumi)

An **AI-enabled, locally hosted sovereign public cloud** platform (powered by **Mitsumi Distribution**),
purpose-built for African organisations. See [[infra.store]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Sovereign public cloud launch (2026-05).** [[sovereignty]] [[gov.protect]] Launched at **AI
  Everything–GITEX Kenya** with **[[ixafrica-data-centres|iXAfrica]]** (Tier III, Nairobi) hosting and
  Mitsumi's regional distribution. It targets the barriers to African cloud adoption — **foreign-currency
  billing, offshore data hosting, data-sovereignty concerns** — offering **Kenya-Shilling billing**, 24/7
  local engineering, low latency, **99.9% uptime SLA**, flat monthly charges and **zero egress fees**;
  framed around **data residency**. See [[kenya--infra-store]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-22-ixafrica-baobab-sovereign-public-cloud-techafricanews]].
