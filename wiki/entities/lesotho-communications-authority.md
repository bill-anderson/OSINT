# Lesotho Communications Authority (LCA)

Lesotho's **telecoms/communications regulator** — licensor of operators and satellite services, and the
body at the centre of the Starlink foreign-ownership debate and Vodacom regulatory scrutiny. See
[[infra.connect]], [[lesotho--infra-connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Starlink licence (2025-04).** [[data.satellite]] [[sovereignty]] After two rounds of public
  consultation (from Starlink's Apr-2024 application), the LCA granted Lesotho's **first satellite
  internet licence** — a **10-year** operating licence (14 Apr 2025) — over objections about **100%
  foreign ownership** (Section 2 rights group) and a Vodacom appeal for local shareholding. Sources:
  [[2025-04-22-starlink-licence-mixed-reactions-publiceye]], [[2025-03-11-starlink-opposition-section2-connectingafrica]].
