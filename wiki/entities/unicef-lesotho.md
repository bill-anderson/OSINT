# UNICEF Lesotho

The UNICEF country office — a key **DPI and civil-registration partner** to the Government of Lesotho
(digital birth registration, national-ID ecosystem, child-focused DPI). See [[dpi.id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **MOSIP national-ID work plan (2026-03).** [[dpi.registry]] UNICEF and **[[micsti-lesotho|MICSTI]]**
  signed the **2026 Action Plan** (under the Lesotho–UNICEF Programme of Cooperation 2025–2028) to build
  an **integrated national digital-ID ecosystem on [[mosip|MOSIP]]** — passport/ID renewal, biometric
  registration, proof-of-life, and updating expired documents — strengthening identity verification for
  financial services and AML/fraud prevention. Aligns with NSDP II, the National Social Protection
  Strategy II, and the **50-in-5** campaign. See [[lesotho-national-id]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-03-lesotho-unicef-mosip-national-id-biometricupdate]].
- **Child-focused DPI workshop (2026-04).** [[include.access]] [[gov.protect]] A DPI workshop (Avani
  Maseru) explored **child-safe, inclusive** digital systems — birth registration, education, healthcare
  and social protection — stressing children's-data protection and early-stage integration (MICSTI's
  Ramashamole). Source: [[2026-04-08-child-focused-dpi-unicef-techafricanews]].
