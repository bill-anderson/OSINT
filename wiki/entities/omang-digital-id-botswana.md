# Omang digital ID / Electronic Identification System (Botswana)

Botswana's move to **digitise the Omang national ID** into an Electronic Identification System — the foundational-ID modernisation strand of the country's e-government agenda, led by the Ministry of Labour and Home Affairs.

## Activity in this wiki

- Flagged in the 2026/27 budget (Minister Maj Gen Pius Mokgware) alongside an **e-wallet**, biometric/e-government services and 24-hour border-post operations — advancing a digital Omang and Electronic Identification System. Early-stage/planned as of Mar 2026. See [[dpi.id]] and the [[BWA]] hub. Source: [[2026-03-02 Botswana - Minister envisions reforms to boost service delivery (digital Omang)]].
