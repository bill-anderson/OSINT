# Open Access Data Centres (OADC)

A carrier-neutral data-centre operator, subsidiary of the **WIOCC Group**, building colocation and
edge facilities across Africa; landing party/host for **Google's Equiano** cable at its Lagos
facility. A core operator in the Lagos data-centre cluster alongside Equinix (MainOne), Rack
Centre, Digital Realty, MTN and Airtel.

- **2025-03-24** — Announced expansion of its **Lagos** facility from 1.5 MW to **24 MW by 2027**
  (~**$240m**), phased under a broader **$500m** pan-African investment plan unveiled in 2021.
