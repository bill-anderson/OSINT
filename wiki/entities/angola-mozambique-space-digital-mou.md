# Angola–Mozambique space & digital cooperation MoUs (2026)

Two memoranda signed **2026-06-23** on the sidelines of Mozambique's **5th National Communications Conference** in Maputo, between Mozambique's Minister of Communications and Digital Transformation, **Américo Muchanga**, and Angola's Minister of Telecommunications **[[mario-oliveira]]** ([[ministerio-das-telecomunicacoes-tecnologias-de-informacao-e-comunicacao-social-angola]]).

## Terms

- **Space MoU** — a framework for sharing knowledge, building technical capability and joint use of space technologies for socio-economic development.
- **Communications/ICT/meteorology MoU** — technical and institutional cooperation, exchange of expertise and best-practice promotion.
- **The concrete substrate:** the **Boane Earth Station** (Maputo Province) has been **technically integrated with [[angosat-2]] and is already receiving signal**, with operational tests underway toward full activation of remote terminals — a joint **GGPEN–INCM** deployment. The Angolan delegation included GGPEN DG **Zolana João** and INACOM CEO Joaquim Muhongo.

## Reads

- `sovereignty`: Angola **exporting** its own space infrastructure to a fellow Lusophone state — the sovereignty stack acquiring an outbound, regional dimension rather than only importing capacity. See the Angola-as-exporter reading on [[AGO]].
