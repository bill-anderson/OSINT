# LeSwitch (Lesotho National Payment Switch)

Lesotho's **national payment switch**, led by the Central Bank of Lesotho with the banking sector and
PSPs — a single central hub connecting POS terminals, ATMs and mobile money for near-real-time, 24/7,
interoperable domestic payments ([[dpi.pay]]). Conceptualized in 2016, its fast-payments phase launched
20 March 2024; by early 2025 **all local debit/credit card transactions are processed within the
country**, cutting fees and reducing reliance on international schemes (Visa/Mastercard still handle
cross-border). See [[LSO]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Journey and milestones.** [[include.access]] 2016 conceptualized; 2021 instant payments bank↔mobile
  money (M-Pesa, Khetsi); 2023 domesticated ATM cards; Oct 2024 ATM localization; **11 May 2025** localized
  **POS** (any-bank debit cards on any POS). Next phase: e-commerce/retail. The switch runs **two streams
  — instant payments and card payments** — led by the **[[central-bank-of-lesotho|CBL]]** with the four
  commercial banks (Standard Lesotho Bank's Samuel Koatla; CBL's Mothetsi Sekoati). Full thread:
