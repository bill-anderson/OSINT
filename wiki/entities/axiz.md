# Axiz

African distributor of digital technologies and [[equinix]]'s South Africa
distribution partner, positioned in the channel layer that resells and integrates
data-centre and cloud capacity across the continent.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Welcomed Equinix's South Africa investment (2026-04-28).** As Equinix's SA
  distribution partner, Axiz publicly framed [[equinix]]'s South Africa investment as
  momentum for [[ZAF]]'s role as a continental [[infra.store]] hub, citing AI
  workloads, hybrid cloud and data-sovereignty demand as the drivers. The clip is thin
  and establishes the channel relationship rather than a transaction — no Equinix
  investment figure was disclosed.
