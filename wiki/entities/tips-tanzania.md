# TIPS (Tanzania Instant Payment System)

Tanzania's national instant-payment system, operated within the Bank of Tanzania's National
Payments Directorate — the domestic real-time rail linking banks and mobile-money wallets.

## Activity in this wiki

- **First EAC cross-border interlink pilot (as of 2026-07-10).** TIPS is being interlinked
  with Rwanda's national switch **[[rswitch-rwanda]]** in a proof of concept — the first
  cross-border corridor under the **[[eac-cross-border-payment-masterplan]]** — following a
  week-long technical meeting in Zanzibar chaired by Nestory Nicholas Maro (Bank of Tanzania).
  Initial scope is person-to-person transfers; no launch date announced. See [[dpi.pay]],
  [[gov.regional]], [[TZA]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-10 New Rwanda-Tanzania Payment Pilot could transform trade across East Africa]].
