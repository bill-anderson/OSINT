# Telecel (Guinea-Bissau)

The mobile operator serving Guinea-Bissau, part of the Telecel Group (led by CEO Moh Damush).
Telecel took over the former **MTN Guinea-Bissau** operation in 2024, and is now the private-sector
counterpart in the government's digital-skills agenda. See [[GNB]], [[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **STARTOCODE skills committee (2026-05-06/07).** [[capacity.training]] Telecel and the Government of
  Guinea-Bissau signed a **Joint Decree** creating a Coordination and Monitoring Committee for the
  **STARTOCODE** digital-skills platform — spanning the ministries of Education, Youth, Women/Social
  Solidarity, Public Administration/Labour, and Transport/Telecommunications/Digital Economy plus
  Telecel Guinea-Bissau. The programme is to train **1,000 young people per year over four years** in
  programming, data analysis and cloud computing, with emphasis on women and people with disabilities.
  Company release (flagged pr-wire). Source:
  [[2026-05-07 Telecel and Guinea-Bissau Government launch STARTOCODE Coordination Committee]].
