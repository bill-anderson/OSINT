# Sama

San Francisco-headquartered data-labelling and content-moderation
business-process outsourcer (BPO) with a large Kenyan [[KEN]] workforce, an
emblematic case of global-margins AI-data labour ([[tech.ai]], [[capacity.training]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **1,108 Kenya layoffs after Meta contract ends (2026-04).** Sama issued a
  redundancy notice for 1,108 Kenya-based employees after [[meta]] ended a major
  content-moderation / AI data-annotation contract. Source: [[2026-04-17 Meta contract end sparks 1 100 Sama job cuts]].
- **Motaung exploitation case (since 2022).** Sama is tied to the landmark Daniel
  Motaung case over content-moderator working conditions; Kenyan courts have
  ruled that [[meta]] can be sued locally, a precedent for platform accountability
  in African jurisdictions ([[gov.protect]]). Source: [[2026-04-17 Meta contract end sparks 1 100 Sama job cuts]].
