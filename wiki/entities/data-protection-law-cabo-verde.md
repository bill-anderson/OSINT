# Cabo Verde Data Protection Law (Law 133/V/2001)

Cabo Verde's general **personal-data protection framework**, which **closely mirrors the EU GDPR**. Covers data-subject rights, controller/processor duties, special categories, international transfers, security measures and DPIAs; applies within Cabo Verde and **extraterritorially** to processing of data of individuals located in the country. Establishes the **[[cnpd-cabo-verde|National Data Protection Commission (CNPD)]]**.

**Sanctions:** administrative fines CVE 50,000–3,000,000 plus cessation/blocking/deletion; criminal penalties (unauthorised access, copying, transfer, interconnection, or false information) up to **two years' imprisonment** or a fine up to 240 days. Private life is constitutionally protected.
