# Sudani

Sudan's domestic mobile and fixed operator, the retail arm of **[[sudatel]]**
(Sudatel Telecom Group). One of the country's national operators (alongside
**[[zain-sudan]]** and **[[mtn-sudan]]**), presented by the Port-Sudan
government as core infrastructure kept running through the civil war.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Business-digital-transformation offers (2026-01).** Sudani announced a
  package of enterprise services (for companies, institutions, banks and
  organisations) at a Port-Sudan event; COO **Mohamed Al-Rayah** pledged
  reconstruction-phase support. The digital-transformation minister praised the
  company's infrastructure as a "solid, strong foundation" with nationwide
  reach. See [[tech.industry]], [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-16 Minister praises Sudani-Sudatel role in business digital transformation (SUNA)]].
- **Customs connectivity restoration (2025-06).** Cooperated with Sudan Customs
  to restore internet/network links reactivating the **[[asycuda]]** system at
  Gezira state's Hantoub customs yard after war damage. Source:
  [[2025-06-18 ASYCUDA customs system reactivated at Gezira state customs after war damage (SUNA)]].
