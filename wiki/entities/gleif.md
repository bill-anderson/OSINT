# Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation (GLEIF)

Issues and governs the Legal Entity Identifier (LEI), a global corporate-ID standard. ODI's 2026 Nigeria
study found only **561 LEIs** (541 active) against 3m+ registered firms — a corporate-identity gap
blocking cross-border B2B payments under the AfCFTA. See [[dpi.id]]. Source:
[[2026-03-01 Cross-border digital payments and identity in Nigeria under the AfCFTA]].
