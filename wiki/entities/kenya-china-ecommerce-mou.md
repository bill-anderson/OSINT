# Kenya–China Memorandum of Understanding on E-Commerce Cooperation

**Parties:** the Government of Kenya ([[KEN]]) and the People's Republic of China.
**What:** a bilateral memorandum of understanding on **e-commerce cooperation**, cited by Kenya's
**[[state-department-for-ict-and-the-digital-economy-kenya]]** as the standing framework under which
its digital-trade engagement with China proceeds. **Status:** active and being drawn on as of
**2026-07**.

## What the wiki holds

- **Cited as the basis for the Shandong delegation (2026-07-17).** Principal Secretary
  **[[john-tanui]]** said the dispatch of **18 ICT officers** to the *2026 Silk Road E-Commerce:
  Connecting the World Industry Matching Activity* "builds on" the MoU, presenting it as the vehicle
  for deepening bilateral ties, exchanging best practice, exploring investment and strengthening
  institutional capacity in support of the **[[kenya-national-digital-masterplan]]**. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-kenya-ict-officers-china-digital-trade]].

**Provenance note.** The wiki holds no signing date, text, term or published scope for this MoU — it is
so far known only by reference from the Kenyan side. See [[geopol.china]].
