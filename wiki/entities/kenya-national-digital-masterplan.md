# Kenya National Digital Masterplan 2022-2032

Kenya's overarching ten-year digital strategy — the umbrella instrument under which the DPI roadmap
([[kenya-dpi-roadmap]]), the National AI Strategy 2025-2030, a planned National Data Governance policy,
eCitizen and the Digital Superhighway (BETA) all sit ([[gov.policy]], [[dpi.govtech]]). See [[KEN]].
