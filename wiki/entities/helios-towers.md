# Helios Towers

Pan-African telecommunications-tower (passive infrastructure) company that builds and
operates shared tower sites for mobile operators across the continent. Managing Director for
Tanzania: **David Dzigba**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **133 UCSAF rural sites completed (Tanzania, commemorated Dodoma, April 2026).** Helios
  Towers completed **133 telecommunications sites** under Tanzania's **Universal
  Communications Service Access Fund (UCSAF)** rural-connectivity programme — a government-led
  effort to extend infrastructure to areas historically limited or commercially unviable. The
  milestone was commemorated in Dodoma with **President Samia Suluhu Hassan** and ICT Minister
  **Angela Kairuki**. See [[TZA]], [[digital.rural]], [[infra.connect]],
  [[include.access]], [[digital.localgov]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-13 Helios Towers completes 133 UCSAF sites to expand rural connectivity in Tanzania]].
- **Rural focus.** The company said nearly **45% of its Tanzania sites** are in rural areas,
  reflecting a sustained focus on bridging the digital divide, with the new infrastructure
  expected to support access to financial services, education, healthcare and digital
  government platforms. Source:
  [[2026-04-13 Helios Towers completes 133 UCSAF sites to expand rural connectivity in Tanzania]].
