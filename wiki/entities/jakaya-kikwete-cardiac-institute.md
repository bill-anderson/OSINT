# Jakaya Kikwete Cardiac Institute (JKCI)

Tanzania's national **cardiac specialist institute** — a digital-health early adopter. Executive Director
(2026): **Dr Peter Kisenge**.

- **Digital patient tracking + AI diagnostics (Jul 2026).** JKCI's system tracks and follows up patients
  treated at medical camps, letting doctors access prior diagnoses/tests/treatments on return — praised as
  a continuity-of-care model. Tools include **AI-enabled echocardiography**, the contactless **"Dozee"**
  under-mattress vital-signs monitor, robotic-assisted surgery, and the **"Know Your Numbers"** NCD
  campaign. See [[tanzania--tech-ai]], [[dpi.mis]]. Source: [[2026-07-16-jkci-digital-patient-tracking-system]].
