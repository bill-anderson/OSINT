# Schneider Electric

**A global energy-management and automation company.** In this wiki it appears as
[[synectics-technologies]]' build partner on Uganda's [[aeonian-project]] — the
energy-and-infrastructure half of a data centre whose entire premise is surplus
hydroelectricity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Aeonian build partner (2025-09-15).** Uganda's Aeonian project — a large-scale
  sovereign AI centre at the **[[karuma-hydropower-plant]]** on the Nile — is **developed
  by Synectics Technologies in partnership with Schneider Electric**, housing several AI
  modules and the **10 MW USIO** supercomputer. Powered by renewable energy from the Karuma
  dam and **cooled by Nile water**; connected to a **2,500 km fibre-optic network** linking
  Uganda to submarine cables in Kenya and Tanzania. **Rollout begins 2026 with the first
  AI module, full capacity by 2028.** See [[aeonian-project]], [[infra.energy]],
  [[infra.store]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-15 Synectics, Schneider to build AI center in Uganda starting 2026]].
