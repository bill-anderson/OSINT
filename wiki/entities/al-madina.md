# Al Madina

Private security-printing company contracted to produce the **Central African Republic's
secure national ID card (CNI)** under a public-private arrangement — the private
infrastructure provider at the centre of CAR's post-2013 identity rebuild, and a lightning
rod for sovereignty concerns about foreign control of citizens' identity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **CNI production contract.** Al Madina holds the concession to manufacture CAR's secure
  national ID card. The **[[greater-internet-freedom]]** country report (2023) finds the ID
  **contracting process opaque** — the Al Madina award was not scrutinised by the relevant
  public-procurement body — and situates it within popular perceptions of *foreign control*
  of local identity. A predecessor arrangement, **Centrafrique Carte System (CCS)**, held ID
  contracting in 2016–2017. See [[caf--dpi-id]].
- **Illegal pricing (2020–2025).** Under the public-private deal Al Madina imposed CNI
  prices of **6,000–6,750 FCFA** against the legal **4,500 FCFA** set by the 2020 finance
  law — sparking litigation (Bangui administrative tribunal ruled the pricing "arbitrary",
  Sep 2020) and the **M4500** citizens' collective (three members arrested at a 13 Oct 2020
  sit-in). The government finally restored the 4,500 FCFA tariff by interministerial order of
  **26 June 2025**. Source:
  [[2025-07-27 Centrafrique - le prix des cartes d'identite reduit apres cinq ans de violations de la loi]].
- **Decentralised mobile enrolment (2023–2025).** Because CNIs were long issued only in
  Bangui, Al Madina ran town-by-town enrolment missions (photo, fingerprint, signature):
  **Bossangoa** (Ouham, Oct 2023, 2,500 applicants targeted), later **Bouar** and
  **Berbérati**, and **Birao** (Vakaga, Dec 2025). Coordinator general: Ulrich Perrin
  Marandji Moholo. Sources:
  [[2023-10-07 Centrafrique - la delivrance de la carte nationale d'identite facilitee pour les habitants de Bossangoa]],
  [[2025-12-08 RCA - la societe Al-Madina deploie sa mission d'identite a Birao]].
