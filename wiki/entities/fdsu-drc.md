# FDSU (Universal Service Development Fund)

DR Congo's **universal-service fund** (legal base **loi n°20/17 of 5 Nov 2020**) — long dormant, now
operational.

## Activity in this wiki

- **From unfunded to first sites.** As of **Feb 2025** the fund "n'est toujours pas disponible" (Internet
  Society masterclass established its legal base and funding model); it then **launched its first pilot
  connectivity sites at Nkamba, Kongo Central (30 May 2026)** — the **first state-financed rural telecom
  build**. Sources: [[2025-02-19-internet-society-masterclass-fdsu-actualite]],
  [[2026-06-02-fdsu-sites-pilotes-nkamba-actualite]].
