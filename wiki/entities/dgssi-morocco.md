# DGSSI (Directorate General of Information Systems Security, Morocco)

Morocco's **national cybersecurity authority** (under the National Defence Administration), operating the
**maCERT** monitoring/response centre. Its Director General, **General Abdellah Boutrig**, is vice-president
of the **African Network of Cybersecurity Authorities (ANCA)** — positioning Rabat as a regional cyber
hub. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[morocco--infra-cybersec]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **FortiBleed alert (2026-06).** [[infra.cybersec]] maCERT warned that **several Moroccan institutions**
  were among global victims of the **FortiBleed** leak (Fortinet SSL-VPN firewalls; ~**75,000 devices in
  ~200 countries**), with offline password-hash cracking, lateral movement and ransomware risk —
  recommending immediate password rotation, **MFA**, PBKDF2 hardening (FortiOS 7.2.11/7.4.8/7.6.1) and
  blocking public FortiGate admin interfaces. Source: [[2026-06-21-hespress-morocco-fortibleed-macert-cyber-alert]].
- **ANCA regional CERT & leadership programme (2026-06).** [[gov.regional]] [[sovereignty]] DGSSI hosted
  the **ANCA Cybersecurity Executive Leadership Programme** in Rabat (1–5 Jun) for African decision-makers;
  the royal council (9 Apr 2026) had approved agreements establishing an **ANCA regional incident-response
  support & coordination centre** in Morocco and an **AUDA-NEPAD national office** — Boutrig: cybersecurity
  is "a pillar of digital sovereignty." Source: [[2026-06-02-assabah-rabat-cybersecurity-leadership-program]].
