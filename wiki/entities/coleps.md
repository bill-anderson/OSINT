# COLEPS (Cameroon Online E-Procurement System)

Cameroon's **digital public-procurement system**, upgraded with a **$12M Korean (KOICA) grant** (2025) —
a govtech track (Korea as a donor) the wiki did not previously carry for CMR; a further **$12.5m** was due
in 2026 for interoperability. Source:
[[2025-06-30-coleps-digital-procurement-korean-grant-biometricupdate]].
