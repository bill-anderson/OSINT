# CIL (Commission de l'Informatique et des Libertés, Burkina Faso)

Burkina Faso's **data-protection authority** (origin loi 010-2004/AN; operative framework loi
001-2021/AN). President: **Dr Halguièta Nassa-Trawina**. The CIL's defining trait in this period is
**weakness turned into political momentum**: it lacks its own enforcement teeth (the *décrets
d'application* are missing) but has been elevated by a Prime-Minister-led, ministry-by-ministry
compliance drive — and its data-protection message is folded into the government's **sovereignty and
counter-insurgency** framing. See [[BFA]], [[gov.protect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Pleading for enforcement powers (2025-03).** [[gov.legislate]] On National Data Protection Day the
  CIL asked the PM for support, noting it has **no repressive powers** without the missing implementing
  decrees, and handed over a **2022 activity report in 2025** — a candid picture of an under-equipped
  regulator. Source: [[2025-03-19-cil-sollicite-accompagnement-premier-ministre-jnpdp]].
- **PM-led, ministry-by-ministry campaign (2025).** [[gov.protect]] The Prime Minister personally
  launched a sensitisation campaign across the administration, raising the CIL's political standing;
  sessions ran at **Territorial Administration, Fonction Publique, Infrastructures, Alphabétisation and
  Enseignement supérieur**, plus diplomats. The **MTDPCE–CIL** working session (Sep 2025, ministers
  Zerbo-Sabané and CIL president Nassa-Trawina) made **data-protection compliance mandatory before
  platform deployment**. Sources: [[2025-07-01-premier-ministre-lance-campagne-sensibilisation-administration]],
  [[2025-09-04-burkina-faso-strengthens-data-protection-digital-transition]].
- **The one concrete enforcement act on record (2025-07).** [[gov.legislate]] The Ministry of Territorial
  Administration formally **noticed (mise en demeure) an association** that had collected citizens'
  personal data and **hosted it outside the territory**; the CIL publicly welcomed the action — the
  clearest datable enforcement in the period, and a data-localisation signal. Source:
  [[2025-07-28-cadres-ministere-administration-territoriale-outilles]].
- **Protection framed as security + sovereignty.** Data protection is pitched against **transfer to
  foreign powers** (diplomats' sensitisation) and folded into the **counter-insurgency** effort (social-
  media use by ~500 troops at Kaya). A first national **cybercrime week** (Nov 2025) created the **Galian
  prize**. Sources: [[2025-08-07-diplomates-burkinabe-sensibilises-protection-donnees]],
  [[2025-04-01-cil-sensibilise-forces-defense-securite-kaya-reseaux-sociaux]],
  [[2025-11-04-semaine-nationale-lutte-cybercriminalite-cloture]].
