# Jamii X-Change

**Tanzania's citizen-facing interoperability platform, built by [[micit-tanzania|MICIT]] with [[ega-tanzania|e-GA]] under the [[digital-tanzania-project|Digital Tanzania Project]] — comprising three modules: Jamii Namba (identity), Jamii Kadi (card) and Jamii Malipo (payments).**

**Do not conflate it with GovESB.** Two distinct exchange builds run in parallel: **GovESB**, e-GA's system-to-system bus (mandatory from **30 Jul 2025**), and **Jamii X-Change**, the MICIT + e-GA platform under the Digital Tanzania Project. The primaries treat them as separate objects; this wiki keeps them separate.

## The three modules

- **[[jamii-namba]]** — identity. Per PM **Kassim Majaliwa** (**2024-10-17**, published 2024-10-18), the government directed that **the NIDA number *is* the Jamii Namba** for delivering and receiving services.
- **Jamii Kadi** — the card layer.
- **Jamii Malipo** — payments; **still to be built** as of **2024-08-27**, intended to link via the card to **[[tips-tanzania|TIPS]]**. *Jamii Malipo sits with MICIT/e-GA, not the [[bank-of-tanzania]]* — a distinction that matters, since BoT owns TIPS, which Jamii Malipo would only *connect to*.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Under construction (2024-08-27).** MICIT Permanent Secretary **Mohammed Khamis Abdulla**, opening a Digital Tanzania Project review meeting in **Arusha**, said MICIT with e-GA was building Jamii X-Change to execute **President Samia's directive** that systems interoperate and exchange information, with **"much money" directed into it through the Digital Tanzania Project**. On identity he was explicit: *"every Tanzanian will have a Jamii Namba."* Source: [[2024-08-27-mfumo-jamii-x-change-kuunganisha-mifumo-tehama]].
- **The interoperability order (2024-10-17).** Closing the 8th ICT Congress at JNICC, Dar es Salaam, PM Majaliwa ordered **all public and private institution heads to make their systems interoperable so Jamii X-Change completes as planned**. ICT Minister **Jerry William Silaa** said the ministry was coordinating **Jamii Namba, Jamii X-Change and Jamii Portal**, plus **[[napa-tanzania|NaPA]]**. Source: [[2024-10-18-hakikisheni-mnapata-namba-ya-nida-jamii-namba-majaliwa]].

## Not established (as of 2026-07-20)

- **No go-live date, no completion date and no uptake figure** for Jamii X-Change or any of its three modules is held. The most recent held statement of status is *under construction*, dated **2024-08-27**, with Jamii Malipo unbuilt.

## Reads

- `sovereignty`: the module naming (*jamii* — community) frames an exchange layer as a citizen entitlement rather than a back-office bus, which is also how the mandate was enforced — by prime-ministerial directive on institutions, not by technical standard. Whether the plumbing followed the rhetoric is not establishable from what the wiki holds.
