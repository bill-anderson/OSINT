# Mo Ibrahim Foundation

African governance foundation (founder Sir Mo Ibrahim); publishes on governance, mobility and
digital/financial inclusion.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cash-dependency report (2026-06).** With [[affinity-africa]] and Yale ILC — Africa runs ~74% of
  global mobile-money volume yet >90% of value is cashed out immediately; frames finishing the digital
  transition as economic sovereignty. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-01 The State of Cash Dependency and Digital Financial Inclusion in Africa]].
