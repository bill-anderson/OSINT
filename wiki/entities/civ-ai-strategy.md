# Côte d'Ivoire National AI Strategy

Côte d'Ivoire's **National AI Strategy** — handed to the Prime Minister on **13 Mar 2025** together with
the **[[civ-data-governance-strategy|National Data Governance Strategy]]**, the paired instruments of the
country's data/AI stack. Framed around **technological sovereignty**, a **sovereign cloud**, a **"Safe AI"
label** and a **National Committee**, and adopting the **UNESCO RAM** readiness methodology (a
letter of intent HABG/ministry/UNESCO was signed 30 Sep 2024).

## Profile

- **Costed and scoped.** **912bn FCFA, 15 objectives, 109 projects over 5 years** (presented at SIADE 2025)
  — figures the hub named the strategy but lacked. Delivery leans on the **[[parae]]** support programme
  (49bn FCFA incl. a data centre) and AfDB backing. Presented to the private sector (May 2025) and framed
  by government as making AI "an engine of development." Sources:
