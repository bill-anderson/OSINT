# Electoral Commission of Zambia (ECZ)

Zambia's electoral management body — its **voter registration** is a recurring reference point in the case
for a permanent biometric national ID (rather than repeated mass registration on paper voter's cards).

- **Voter-card reuse (2025).** ECZ (Chief Electoral Officer **Brown Kasaro**) said 2021 voters / 2020-card
  holders need not re-register for the mass registration ahead of the **13 Aug 2026** election — prompting
  op-ed calls for a **permanent biometric NRC** serving voting and other purposes. See [[zambia--dpi-id]].
  Source: [[2025-09-08-why-we-need-biometric-national-id-cards]].
