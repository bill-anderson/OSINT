# Orabank

**A West African commercial bank with a Togolese subsidiary, Orabank Togo.** Its CEO (as of
2025-10), **Guy Martial Awona**, also serves as **president of the UEMOA banking federation** —
giving the bank a voice in regional payments policy as well as a place on the rails. See
[[TGO]], [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **On the BCEAO instant-payment platform — dated states.** Orabank was one of **three banks
  operational** on the [[waemu-instant-payment-platform]] in Togo **as of 2025-10-01** (with
  Coris Bank and [[ecobank]]), and appears among the **seven institutions committed** or
  preparing interconnection **as of 2025-11-18**. See [[bceao]]. Sources:
  [[2025-10-01 BCEAO Launches Instant Payments Platform in Togo with Three Banks]],
  [[2025-11-18 BCEAO Launches Developer Sandbox for Instant Payments]].
- **Convergence framing (2025-10-01).** Awona called the platform launch a "decisive step
  toward an ecosystem where banks, microfinance institutions, and fintechs converge on a single
  infrastructure" — the incumbent-bank case for shared public rails, made by the banking
  federation's president. See [[gov.regional]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-01 BCEAO Launches Instant Payments Platform in Togo with Three Banks]].
