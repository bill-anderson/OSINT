# Takaful and Karama (Egypt social-protection programme)

Egypt's flagship **conditional/unconditional cash-transfer programme** (Ministry of Social Solidarity), launched 2015. Rests on a **unified social registry** covering **~12 million households**, reaching **4.7m active households (~17m people, ~20% of families)**. Payments run via **Meeza cards**; a **national ID-based system** and 3,000+ offices enable real-time monitoring; targeting combines proxy means testing with home verification. The grievance system handled 6m inquiries (99.8% response). Backed by **$1.4bn+** of World Bank financing.

A reference case of DPI-for-social-protection: registry + digital ID + digital payments — and a candidate base for **automatic enrolment** into Universal Health Insurance.
