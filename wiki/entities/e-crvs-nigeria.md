# e-CRVS (Nigeria)

Nigeria's electronic Civil Registration and Vital Statistics System, managed by the
**[[npc-nigeria]]**, digitalising the previously paper-based registration of births,
stillbirths, birth attestation, adoption, marriage, divorce, migration and deaths.

- **2025-11** — Launched by President **[[bola-tinubu]]**, moving Nigeria's long paper-based
  civil-registration process onto a national digital platform.
- **2026-02-20** — **[[unicef]]** reported 14 million children registered in two years, with
  rollout across health facilities.

The foundational CRVS layer beneath NIN-based digital identity ([[nimc]]) — birth registration
is the entry point to legal identity. See [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.id]], [[nigeria--dpi-id]].
