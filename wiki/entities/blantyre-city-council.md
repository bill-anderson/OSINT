# Blantyre City Council

Local-government authority for Blantyre, Malawi's second city and commercial hub. It illustrates
digital transformation reaching the municipal tier, complementing national-level reforms.

## Activity in this wiki

- **NBS Bank revenue-digitisation partnership (~2026-03-06).** Under Mayor **Isaac Jomo Osman**,
  the council partnered with NBS Bank to digitise revenue and tax collection, introducing
  digital payment systems that let residents pay for council services electronically and cutting
  reliance on manual, in-person transactions. The council frames it as modernising tax
  collection and improving financial management and service delivery. It sits within the
  national **Digital Malawi Acceleration Project** and acts as a local-government counterpart to
  the national [[malawi-revenue-authority]] e-invoicing/e-taxation drive. Tags
  [[digital.localgov]], [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[MWI]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-06 Malawi's second city digitises tax collection]].
