# Djibouti Open Data Platform (SGDD-a / IMF e-GDDS)

Djibouti joined the **IMF enhanced General Data Dissemination System (SGDD-a / e-GDDS)** — the ~76th country
— launching a **National Summary Data Page** and hosting the PNRD on the **"Plateforme Open Data de
Djibouti"** (IMF + AfDB support) — a distinctive open-data signal for the country. Source:
[[2025-06-15-sgdd-a-imf-open-data-national-summary-page-lanation]].
