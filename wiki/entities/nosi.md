# NOSi (Núcleo Operacional da Sociedade de Informação)

Cabo Verde's **digital-government agency** (CEO **Carlos Tavares Pina**), established ~25 years ago; the operational engine of the country's e-government. Three stated pillars: **digital identity and authentication, interoperability, and disruptive innovation**.

- **Flagship systems:** the open-source **Integrated Government Resource Planning (IGRP)** microservices framework; the **Justice Information System (SIJ)**; the **Consular Portal / Digital Diaspora** (2020). Underpins the **[[gov-cv|Gov.CV]]** unified portal.
- **Regional reach:** provides **data-centre hosting to other African states** (Equatorial Guinea, Mozambique, Burkina Faso and more) — an unusual case of a small state **exporting** GovTech.
- **Skills:** NOSiAkademia (2016) and WebLab school labs.

Central to the target of **60% of public services online by 2026 / 80% by 2030** and to the World Bank **[[digital-cabo-verde-project]]**.
