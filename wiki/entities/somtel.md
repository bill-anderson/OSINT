# Somtel (Somtel International)

Somali telecom operator (part of the Dahabshiil group), active in connectivity across
Somalia and Somaliland. A landing/consortium partner in the **[[dare1-cable]]** submarine
system (with [[hormuud-telecom]], [[djibouti-telecom]] and Telkom Kenya), which lands at
Bosaso and Mogadishu and is being extended south along the East African coast (2025).

> Somtel operates in Somaliland as well as federal Somalia; mapped to SOM for the place
> facet only (see the [[SOM]] hub sensitivity note).
