# BEAC (Banque des États de l'Afrique Centrale)

The **Central African States Bank** — the common central bank of the **CEMAC** monetary
union, issuing the Central African CFA franc for its six member states: Cameroon
([[CMR]]), the Central African Republic ([[CAF]]), Chad ([[TCD]]), the Republic of Congo
([[COG]]), Equatorial Guinea ([[GNQ]]) and Gabon ([[GAB]]). As the regional monetary
authority it is the natural counterparty for integrating Central Africa into continental
payment infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Joined PAPSS (2026-07-11).** BEAC officially joined the **[[papss]]** (Pan-African
  Payment and Settlement System), extending the continental real-time cross-border
  settlement rail into the CEMAC zone and, by extension, all six member states. Framed as a
  lever to boost intra-African trade and financial integration in Central Africa under
  **[[afcfta]]** — parallel to PAPSS's earlier West and East African expansion. The
  accession date and terms are not yet fully confirmed (source paywalled beyond the lede);
  corroborate against a BEAC/[[afreximbank]] primary release before hardening. See
  [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]], [[XCA]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-11 BEAC joins PAPSS and accelerates cross-border payments integration in Central Africa]].
