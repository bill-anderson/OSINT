# SoleCrypt

**SoleCrypt** (launched 2022) is an **Anglo-Tunisian data-centre and solar developer** pursuing sustainable
digital infrastructure in Tunisia. CEO **Amir Ben Gacem** frames the company's ambition as making Tunisia a
"**Mediterranean super hub** for sustainable digital infrastructure" and a "digital bridge between Africa
and Europe."

- **Bizerte 20 MW solar-AI data centre (Feb 2026).** SoleCrypt plans a **20 MW data centre in Bizerte** —
  sited at the landing point of **three submarine cables including [[medusa-submarine-cable|Medusa]]** —
  powered **100% renewably** from its own **60 MW Tozeur desert solar project ("site T60")** (base-camp
  operational Nov 2025). Under a non-binding **[[solecrypt-schneider-electric-mou|MoU with Schneider
  Electric]]** for AI-dedicated, high-efficiency/low-water data centres, promising sub-millisecond latency
  to Europe. See [[tunisia--infra-store]]. Source: [[2026-02-06-dcd-solecrypt-schneider-bizerte-data-center]].
