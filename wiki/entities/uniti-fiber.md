# Uniti Wholesale (Uniti Group)

US fibre infrastructure provider (Uniti Group, NASDAQ: UNIT); its **Uniti Wholesale** unit builds and
sells dark fibre and high-capacity wavelengths across **>300 metro markets** and **386,000 km** of US
terrestrial fibre.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Transatlantic partnership with [[angola-cables]] (2026-05).** Signed in Washington, D.C., combining
  Angola Cables' **SACS / MONET / WACS** subsea systems with Uniti's US terrestrial network to build a
  direct **Africa–US** corridor for wholesale customers, carriers, content providers and enterprise
  (notably upstream oil-and-gas, aviation, logistics, healthcare). Angola Cables frames the value as
  **"distributed, sovereign-compliant infrastructure"** keeping sensitive data in-jurisdiction. See
  [[angola--infra-connect]], [[infra.connect]], [[geopol.usa]], [[AGO]], [[angola-cables]].
