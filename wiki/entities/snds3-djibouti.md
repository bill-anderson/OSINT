# SNDS-3 (National Statistics Development Strategy 2025–2029)

Djibouti's **third National Statistics Development Strategy (2025–2029)**, validated Apr 2025 (INSTAD,
World-Bank-financed) — three axes including **administrative-database interoperability and data
digitisation**, tying the statistics system into the DPI/interoperability agenda. Source:
[[2025-04-30-instad-snds3-national-statistics-strategy-lanation]].
