# AMECC (civil-registry modernisation)

Comoros' **civil-registry modernisation/computerisation programme** (France/UNICEF-funded) — the
foundational-ID layer distinct from biometric card production. A preliminary study launched at **Mohéli
(Oct 2025)**; a validation workshop followed (Mar 2026); and it introduces a **unique Personal
Identification Number (PIN)** under the **27 Jul 2023 law**. Sources:
