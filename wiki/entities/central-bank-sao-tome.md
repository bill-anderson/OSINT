# Banco Central de São Tomé e Príncipe (BCSTP)

STP's central bank and financial regulator. Runs the **National Financial Inclusion Strategy** and
launched the country's first **FinTech Sandbox** (2025, five-phase) to grow digital financial services
and reduce cash use; strengthening payment systems, supervision and digital banking. See
[[sao-tome--dpi-pay]], [[STP]].
