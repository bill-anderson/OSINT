# COMESA — Common Market for Eastern and Southern Africa

A **21-member regional economic community (REC)** spanning Eastern and Southern Africa (Secretariat in Lusaka),
one of the AU's building-block RECs. In this wiki it matters as a **regional harmonisation** actor for digital
policy — the coordinating REC for the World Bank **[[idea-mpa]]** programme and the sponsor of an emerging
**Regional AI Strategy** and **Regional Digital Inclusion Strategy**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coordinating REC for IDEA.** COMESA is the coordinating Regional Economic Community for the **US$2.48bn
  Inclusive Digitalization in Eastern and Southern Africa (IDEA)** Multiphase Programmatic Approach — a
  World Bank vehicle financing national digital transformation and cross-border regulatory harmonisation
  across 15+ member states. See [[idea-mpa]], [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.exchange]].
- **Regional AI Strategy in development (2026-07).** With Zambia's Ministry of Technology and Science and the
  **[[world-bank]]**, COMESA launched an **AI Sensitisation Workshop** in Lusaka (under IDEA) — the first
  structured activity under the Stakeholder Engagement Plan for its AI & Emerging Technologies Consultancy,
  feeding a **COMESA Regional AI Strategy** and **Regional Digital Inclusion Strategy**. Secretariat rep
  **Leonard Chitundu** framed it around evidence-based, harmonised policy addressing data governance, ethical
  AI, consumer protection, cybersecurity and skills. See [[tech.ai]], [[gov.policy]], [[ZMB]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-14 Zambia and COMESA Launch AI Initiative to Advance Regional Digital Transformation]].

- **Digital Retail Payments Platform — first Malawi–Zambia cross-border payments (2025-10).** COMESA's
  **Digital Retail Payments Platform (DRPP)** went live with a first **cross-border, local-currency retail
  payment** pilot between **Malawi ([[MWI]])** and **Zambia ([[ZMB]])** — a regional interoperability rail
  distinct from the continental [[papss|PAPSS]]. See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-13 Comesa payment system connects Malawi, Zambia]].
