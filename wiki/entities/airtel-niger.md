# Airtel Niger

Niger's **leading telecom operator** (~40%+ share; a **Bharti Airtel** subsidiary; chair Ibrahim Idi Ango, DG
Dina Amadou), marking 25 years in 2026.

- **"Starlink by Airtel" (2026-01).** Airtel Niger launched a **telco-integrated satellite** service from the
  strategic **Bharti Airtel × SpaceX/Starlink** partnership — Niger among the first African markets to trial it —
  offering customers better coverage and stable connectivity (a distinct model from direct Starlink retail). It
  also launched **eSIM**, a **SPAM Alert** security service and the **My Airtel** app in 2025. See
  **[[niger--infra-connect]]**, [[starlink]], [[geopol.india]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-18-airtel-niger-starlink-by-airtel-launch-actuniger]].
