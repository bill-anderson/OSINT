# AfCFTA Digital Trade Corridor

A planned continent-wide programme to serve as the **operational nerve centre for cross-border
digital commerce** under the [[afcfta]] framework, built on the
**[[afcfta-digital-trade-protocol]]**. Its **headquarters will be based in Victoria,
Seychelles**, following a Seychelles Cabinet approval on **2026-07-08**, sitting **alongside
(not replacing)** the AfCFTA Secretariat in Accra.

## Profile

- Rooted in the AfCFTA Protocol on Digital Trade (ministers approved February 2024), whose
  eight annexes — digital products, cross-border payments, digital identity, data transfers,
  cybersecurity and emerging technologies — remain under negotiation.
- Intended to facilitate cross-border commerce, support the Africa Commodities Exchange,
  strengthen supply-chain connectivity, and integrate Seychellois MSMEs into African digital
  value chains.
- Origin: a precursor **[[seychelles-afcfta-mou]]** signed 2026-03-05 between the AfCFTA
  Secretariat, **[[quest-ghana]]** Ltd and the Government of Seychelles during a visit by
  Secretary-General **[[wamkele-mene]]**.
- Announced just after the **AfCFTA Digital Trade Forum 2026** concluded in Lagos
  (2026-07-02).
