# GUILAB (Guinéenne de Large Bande)

Guinea's **national broadband / submarine-cable landing party** (DG **Ahmed Karifa Diawara**) — the state
vehicle diversifying Guinea's international connectivity beyond its **single ACE cable** (~13–15 years old and
**saturated**, versus up to six cables in some neighbours). GUILAB is the national signatory for the **MEDUSA
Africa** second cable (C&MA, May 2026) and a **co-investor in the Via Africa** third-cable consortium (Paris,
June 2026) — "co-invest, co-decide, co-operate" as digital sovereignty. See [[guinea--infra-connect]].
