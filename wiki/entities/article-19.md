# ARTICLE 19

Freedom-of-expression organisation (ARTICLE 19 Eastern Africa is its regional office); engages the
African Commission ([[african-commission]]) on media freedom, assembly and digital rights.

## Activity in this wiki

- **ACHPR civic-space interventions (2026-05).** At the ACHPR's 2026 sessions, ARTICLE 19 Eastern Africa
  flagged **Kenya** (14th Periodic Report: police brutality at protests, targeted digital surveillance,
  internet-throttling threats, misuse of cybercrime laws against journalists) and **Rwanda** (systemic
  censorship; weaponised national-security, cyber-surveillance and anti-defamation laws). See [[KEN]],
  [[RWA]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-15 African Commission Highlighting pressures on civic space in Kenya and Rwanda]].
