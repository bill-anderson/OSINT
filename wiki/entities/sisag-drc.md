# SISAG (government programme-tracking MIS)

A **government-wide programme-tracking management information system**, introduced during the roll-out
("vulgarisation") of the **PNSD 2024–2028** national strategic development plan (May 2025) — the
planning-side counterpart to the PNN2 digital plan. Source:
[[2025-05-30-pnsd-2024-2028-vulgarisation-sisag-actualite]].

## Related context — Kibassa's "sovereignty / 2035" doctrine

Minister Kibassa's economic-governance doctrine (May 2026, PR-wire) frames a **structured recapture of
economic flows**: **DGI/DGDA/DGRAD/BCC interoperability**, a sovereign cloud, **30M active mobile-money
users**, a claimed (unsourced) **$100bn annual digital flows**, and a **2035 hub** target. Source:
[[2026-05-28-souverainete-numerique-horizon-2035-actualite]].
