# Centre for Intellectual Property and Information Technology Law (CIPIT)

Research centre at Strathmore University, Nairobi, working on data protection, IP and AI governance.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital lending-app privacy study (2021).** Empirical analysis (7 apps, traffic capture) finding
  Kenyan digital lenders non-compliant with the Data Protection Act — excess data harvesting and silent
  third-party trackers (Google, Facebook Graph). See [[KEN]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2021-01-01 Privacy and Data Protection Practices of Digital Lending Apps in Kenya]].
