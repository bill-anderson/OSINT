# ReDIV (Angola digital immunisation registry)

Angola's **digital platform for individual, real-time immunisation records** — the front line of the Ministry of Health's digital-health stack, run under the National Development Plan 2023–2027 and the Angola 2050 long-term strategy.

## Activity in this wiki

- **From COVID-19 to all national campaigns (as of 2026-02-14).** ReDIV began in the COVID-19 response and now records doses in **all national vaccination campaigns** (polio, measles, HPV), enabling near-real-time coverage monitoring, rapid gap-finding and accountability — credited with reaching children and adolescents historically missed. It is being made **interoperable with Angola's [[dhis2|DHIS2]]-based national HMIS**, and sits beside **IOTA**, the national real-time stock-management platform for vaccines, medicines and medical supplies (central warehouses to peripheral units). Both feed the wider telemedicine and hospital-digitisation push (Angola was the second Sub-Saharan country to introduce robotic surgery; a robot-commanded operation was run from Orlando, USA, ~17,000 km away, at ANGOTIC 2025).
