# Data Protection Africa

A standing **reference registry of data-protection regimes across Africa**, maintained by
**ALT Advisory** — per-country factsheets covering domestic law, scope, cross-border transfer,
supervisory authorities and applicable regional instruments. A recurring OSINT asset for
tracking data-protection status and enforcement gaps.

## Use in this wiki

- **CAR factsheet (updated 28 Jan 2026):** records CAR as having a data-protection *law in
  place* but *no data-protection authority appointed* — the enforcement gap around
  **[[car-data-protection-law-24-001]]** — and lists the applicable CEMAC-level instruments.
  See [[caf--gov-protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-28 Central African Republic Data Protection Factsheet]].

Consult per country for data-protection law/authority status; a dated fact pulled from it
becomes a normal source citing this resource.
