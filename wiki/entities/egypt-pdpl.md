# Egypt Personal Data Protection Law (Law 151/2020 + Executive Regs 816/2025)

Egypt's **Personal Data Protection Law (Law No. 151/2020)**, finally operationalised by **Executive Regulations No. 816/2025** (issued 1 Nov 2025), with **enforcement expected from 1 November 2026**. Establishes the **[[pdpc-egypt|Personal Data Protection Center (PDPC)]]** as supervisor.

Key features (per the Regulations): purpose limitation; a structured PDPC approvals model; **cross-border transfers requiring a PDPC licence**; extra approvals for e-marketing and certain public-space surveillance; possible local representative for foreign processors; PDPC-registered DPOs; children's-data safeguards (guardian consent under 15); **72-hour breach notification** to the PDPC.

**Enforcement already emerging pre-Regulations:** the Alexandria Economic Court imposed **EGP 10m** liability on Orange Egypt (Feb 2025) on tort/"custodian's liability" grounds even before the Regulations existed.
