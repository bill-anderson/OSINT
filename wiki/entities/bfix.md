# BFIX (Burkina Faso Internet Exchange)

The **national internet exchange point (IXP)** for landlocked Burkina Faso, started **June 2015** — the resilience backbone that keeps local traffic flowing (rather than routing to Europe and back) during subsea-cable cuts.

## Activity in this wiki

- With Internet Society support (2019 network-engineering training; a CDN cache-fill grant and equipment via the Sustainable Peering Infrastructure program), BFIX raised peak capacity **from 0.5 to 6 Gbit/s**, improving service quality and cutting operator costs. Executive director: Jean-Baptiste Millogo. See [[infra.connect]]. Source: [[2025-04-11 Local Businesses Benefit from Burkina Faso's Internet Development (BFIX)]].
