# Media Rights Agenda (MRA)

Nigerian freedom-of-expression and digital-rights CSO.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Horizontal-accountability call (2026-06).** Led a 13-CSO coalition arguing Nigeria’s data-protection
  problem is one of **implementation, not law** — despite the NDPA 2023, NDPC and a 121m+ digital-ID
  system, oversight between agencies is weak (INEC breach via leaked credentials; NINs sold for ₦100).
  Urged equal application of the NDPA to public bodies and Cybercrimes Act s.24 reform. See [[NGA]],
  [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-22 MRA, Civil-Society Coalition Call on Nigerian Government to Strengthen Horizontal Accountability for Citizens Data]].
