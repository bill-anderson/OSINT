# National Audit Office of Tanzania (NAO)

Tanzania's **supreme audit institution**, headed by the **Controller and Auditor General**
(**Charles E. Kichere**, as of 2021-03). NAO conducts value-for-money (performance) audits under
the **Public Audit Act No. 11 of 2008**, reporting to the President and, through her, to
Parliament.

**Why it matters here.** Nearly all published state-of-play on Tanzania's national ID comes from
**[[nida-tanzania]]** itself or from its parent ministry's budget speech. An **independent state
audit is a materially different kind of evidence** — an external body with statutory access,
auditing the programme against its own objectives rather than reporting its progress. Where NAO
findings bear on the same questions as NIDA's coverage claims, they should be weighed
accordingly rather than merged into a single narrative.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Performance audit of national ID registration and issuance (2021-03).** The Controller and
  Auditor General conducted a **value-for-money audit of NIDA's registration and issuance of
  national ID cards to all eligible citizens**, under **s.28 of the Public Audit Act No. 11 of
  2008**, submitted to **President Samia Suluhu Hassan** and through her to Parliament. The
  audited entities were the **[[ministry-of-home-affairs-tanzania]]** and **[[nida-tanzania]]**.
