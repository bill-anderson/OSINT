# KWiK (Angola instant-payment system)

Angola's **instant-payment rail** — 24/7 real-time transfers across banks and mobile wallets, developed by **[[emis-angola]]** in partnership with the **[[bna-angola|BNA]]**. Users associate an identifier (phone, e-mail) with an account; the system interoperates across all banks and payment-service providers, and is the interoperability pillar of the **National Financial Inclusion Strategy (ENIF 2025–2027)**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **2025 scale (as of 2026-01-29).** KWiK processed **35m transfers / 590bn kwanzas in 2025**, **>100% growth** in both count and value y/y; December 2025 alone reached **3.5m transfers / 74.5bn Kz**. **22 banking and non-banking institutions** are in the ecosystem. Roadmap adds QR-code payments and purchases, service payments, state payments, merchant management (via the Multicaixa network) and KWiK cash withdrawals at Multicaixa terminals. Source: [[2026-01-29-operacoes-no-kwik-crescimento-acima-dos-100]].
- **Market structure (Feb 2026).** Per EMIS's monthly report, the ecosystem is **wallet-led and concentrated**: **[[paypay-angola|Paypay]] 52.2%** (1,637,275 transfers), **BAI 21.9%** (687,441), **[[unitel-money|Unitel Money]]/USPM 12.6%** (395,505), 16 banks the remaining ~11.6%. Angola held **12.3m+ registered accounts** and ~**24.8m active payment keys** (annual growth >180%); **66.4% of transfers were under 10,000 Kz** — a low-value, high-frequency, informal-worker pattern. Jan–Feb 2026 combined: **6.1m operations / 144.68bn Kz**. Source: [[2026-03-05-paypay-ultrapassa-16-milhoes-transferencias-no-kwik]].
