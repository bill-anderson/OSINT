# PAFIID

The **Innovative, Inclusive and Sustainable Finance Support Programme** (Programme d'Appui à la Finance
Inclusive, Innovante et Durable), a Mauritanian digital-finance programme working with the
**[[central-bank-mauritania|Central Bank of Mauritania]]**. In **H1 2026** PAFIID reported detecting
**more than 10,000 electronic security threats** against Mauritania's digital financial system — of which
**470 were confirmed and removed** — using the BCM's AI-supported "digital reputation protection" platform
(monitoring phishing, identity impersonation and app/website fraud; **20 digital identities protected**,
including the central bank, PAFIID and national mobile-banking services). A rare operational cybersecurity
data point for the country.
