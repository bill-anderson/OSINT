# Satu Kahkonen

**The [[world-bank]]'s senior country voice on Eswatini** ([[SWZ]]) across the Bank's
2025 project and analytics announcements. Titled **Country Director (2025-02-13)** and
**Division Director for Eswatini (2025-05-12, 2025-07-31)** in Bank press releases —
the wiki records the variation rather than picking one. See [[gov.policy]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **On telecom reform (2025-07-31).** Launching the [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]]:
  "Realizing this will require bold reforms... including the restructuring of Eswatini
  Posts and Telecommunications Corporation (EPTC)." See [[eptc]], [[infra.connect]].
  Source:
  [[2025-07-31 Eswatini's Digital Transformation Crucial to Unlocking Growth, Jobs, and Economic Resilience]].
- **Welcomed EYEOP (2025-05-12).** Kahkonen and Minister of Economic Planning and
  Development Dr Thabo Gina welcomed the
  [[eswatini-youth-employment-opportunities-project]] on Board approval. See
  [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-12 New World Bank Project Supports Job Creation for Eswatini's Youth]].
- **Welcomed ASCENT Eswatini (2025-02-13).** Kahkonen and Minister Thambo Gina welcomed
  the [[ascent-eswatini]] energy-access project. See [[infra.energy]]. Source:
  [[2025-02-13 World Bank Expands Support to Electrify Rural Eswatini, Reaching 200,000 People]].
