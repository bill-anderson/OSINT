# Togo Digital Acceleration Financing (World Bank, $100m IDA series)

**Parties:** the **[[world-bank]]** (International Development Association) and the **Government
of Togo**. **What:** the **first round of financing, $100 million (around FCFA 62 billion)**, in
a **series of IDA projects** supporting the use of digital technologies to accelerate growth, job
creation and sectoral competitiveness — a **programmatic** World Bank engagement covering
broadband connectivity, digital skills, digital entrepreneurship and climate resilience through
technology. **When:** approved **18 December 2024**. **Status:** active. See [[TGO]],
[[finance.new]], [[infra.connect]].

**Not WURI. Do not sum.** This operation is **distinct from** the **$72 million** reported for
Togo under the foundational-ID programme [[wuri-programme]] (see [[e-id-togo]]). Different
instruments, different objects — a broadband/skills/entrepreneurship series versus a foundational
identity project. The two figures must never be added or conflated.

**Date note.** The press release carries the dateline **"WASHINGTON, December 18, 2024"** while
its URL path reads `/2024/12/19/`. **The dateline is authoritative** and is used here. Press
release no: 2025/044/AFW.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Targets.** Connect approximately **8,000 public institutions** to broadband and provide
  **more than one million people** with new or improved internet access, leveraging private-sector
  financing to extend connectivity to nearby households and businesses. See
  [[include.divides]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-18 Togo - $100 Million in Funding to Accelerate Digital Transformation]].
- **Skills and enterprise.** In collaboration with the **IFC**, the project finances **digital
  skills** to improve employability of key population groups and boost the entrepreneurial
  ecosystem. See [[capacity.training]], [[tech.innovate]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-18 Togo - $100 Million in Funding to Accelerate Digital Transformation]].
- **Climate dimension.** Investments in **climate-resilient connectivity** and
  **climate-informed guidelines for the digital sector**, aimed at strengthening energy
  efficiency and infrastructure resilience. See [[infra.capacity]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-18 Togo - $100 Million in Funding to Accelerate Digital Transformation]].
- **Policy alignment.** Aligned with the Government's **Development Roadmap** and the World Bank
  Group's new **Country Partnership Framework for Togo** (which identifies digital technology as
  a key cross-cutting enabler), and with the WBG **Global Challenge Program "Accelerating
  Digitalization"**. **[[cina-lawson]]** called it "a game-changer for Togo"; **Fily Sissoko**,
  World Bank Resident Representative for Togo, tied it to productivity, innovation and access to
  educational content "especially [for] young people and women". See [[gov.policy]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-18 Togo - $100 Million in Funding to Accelerate Digital Transformation]].

**Coverage note.** The announcement reports approval, not disbursement or delivery; the 8,000
institutions and 1m+ people are **targets as of 2024-12-18**, not achieved figures.
