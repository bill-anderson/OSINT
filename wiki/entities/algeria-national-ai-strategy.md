# Algeria National Artificial Intelligence Strategy

Algeria's **National AI Strategy**, adopted **December 2024** by the **National AI Council** led by Professor **Merouane Debbah** (founding Director, 6G Research Center, Khalifa University). Organised across **six pillars**: scientific research; talent development; hardware/infrastructure investment; investment promotion and ecosystem-building; data protection and regulatory framework; and sector-specific implementation (agriculture, healthcare, cybersecurity).

Headline ambition: **AI = 7% of GDP by 2027**. Algeria's AI market is projected to grow from **$498.9m (2025) to $1.69bn (2030)**. Backed by the **Oran AI data centre** (ground broken Mar 2025; GPU/HPC). Sits under **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**.
