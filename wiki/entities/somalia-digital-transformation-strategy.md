# Somalia Digital Transformation Strategy 2025-2030

Somalia's national digital-transformation policy framework, led by the
[[somalia-ministry-communications-technology]] (MoCT) with **ITU** support and aligned to
the country's **Vision 2060** agenda. A two-day **national multi-stakeholder validation
workshop** was held **12-13 October 2025** in Mogadishu with government institutions,
licensed operators and development partners.

- **Purpose** — a single national framework coordinating all ICT initiatives across
  sectors; priorities include broadband expansion, cybersecurity, digital skills, online
  public services and digital inclusion in education, health, agriculture and finance.
- **Builds on** the National ICT Policy and Strategy 2019-2024 (achievements: e-visa
  platform, universal QR payment standard, improved internet access) and a **July 2025 MoU
  with UNDP** on digital governance, innovation, service delivery and human capital.
- **Baseline** — Somalia ranks **191st of 193** on the UN E-Government Development Index
  (2024, score 0.1468 vs African average 0.4247) and near the bottom of the ITU ICT
  Development Index (33.7/100, July 2025).
