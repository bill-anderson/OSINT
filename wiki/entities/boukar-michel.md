# Boukar Michel

**Chad's telecommunications and digital economy minister** across the sources held (Sept 2025 –
Feb 2026) — the public face of [[tchad-connexion-2030]], the [[pmice]] rollout and Chad's digital
legal reform. He is the government's most consistently quoted voice on **digital sovereignty**,
framing infrastructure as a matter of national security and territorial justice rather than
service delivery alone.

**Title variants.** The same post is rendered differently by nearly every source; one person, one
portfolio:

## Activity in this wiki

- **PMICE launch, Sarh (Dec 2025).** At the inauguration ceremony in Sarh, Moyen-Chari, Michel
  said: *"Digital must not stop at capitals, it must irrigate territories, connect regions, and
  unite the nation,"* and *"The sovereignty of a modern state is also measured by its ability to
  master its digital infrastructures."* He called fibre optic networks a national security issue
  as well as *"a tool of modern governance and a lever for territorial justice."* See [[pmice]],
  [[digital.rural]], [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-06 Chad invests 175m in national data center and networks]].
- **New digital law (2025-12-09).** Announcing at the third Chadian School of Internet Governance
  (TCSIG) that Chad is finalising a digital law incorporating international standards on
  cybersecurity, data protection and internet governance: *"The future belongs to those who
  identify opportunities before they become obvious. In the digital age, a country that does not
  control its data does not control its destiny."* See [[gov.legislate]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-10 Chad Finalizes Digital Law to Align National Framework With Global Standards]].
- **Huawei talks (2025-12-24).** Met a [[huawei]] North Africa delegation led by VP Jason Ye on
  telecom infrastructure, connected-services access and public-administration digitalisation,
  exploring concrete projects aligned with Tchad Connexion 2030. See [[geopol.china]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-29 Chad Steps Up Rollout of Tchad Connexion 2030 in Talks With Huawei]].
- **US engagement (2025-11-26).** Met **William Flens**, chargé d'affaires of the United States in
  Chad, to reaffirm plans to expand cooperation and attract greater participation from American
  technology companies in local digital initiatives. See [[geopol.usa]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].
- **Regional interconnection advocacy (17–18 Nov 2025).** At the Regional Summit on Digital
  Transformation in Cotonou, Michel advocated stronger interconnection between **Central and West
  Africa** through fibre-optic infrastructure, data centres and digital corridors, flagging AI and
  agritech as levers for youth employment. See [[gov.regional]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-02 Chad Accelerates Digital Cooperation to Support Its Transformation Agenda]].
- **N'Djamena network announcement (2026-02-11).** Announced **50 new telecom sites** in N'Djamena
  and **50 km of FTTH** via [[sotel-tchad]] — **without details on timeline, financing or
  coverage areas**, at a ceremony organised by ministry staff and affiliated agencies, and against
  continued public criticism of service quality. See [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-13 Chad Plans 50 Telecom Sites, 50 km of Fiber in N'Djamena]].

The wiki's later Chad reporting (2026-07-01) names **Haliki Choua Mahamat** as *Minister of
Telecommunications, Digital Economy and Digitalization* — the same portfolio. **No source held
reports a handover, a date, or Michel's current position.** Treated as an open question, not an
inference. Source:
