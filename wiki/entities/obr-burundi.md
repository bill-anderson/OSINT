# OBR (Office Burundais des Recettes)

Burundi's **revenue authority**, operator of the **e-KORI** online tax and non-tax revenue platform.

## Activity in this wiki

- **e-KORI launched (19 Jan 2026).** World Bank-backed online platform for tax/fee declaration and payment, to raise domestic revenue and cut reliance on foreign aid (Finance/Budget/Digital-Economy Minister Dr Alain Ndikumana). Nine-phase rollout with cybersecurity as a central focus; framed under Vision 2040/2060. See [[dpi.govtech]] and the [[BDI]] hub. Source: [[2026-01-22 Burundi launches online tax system e-KORI]].
