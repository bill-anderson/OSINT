# OBR (Office Burundais des Recettes)

Burundi's **revenue authority**, operator of the **e-KORI** online tax platform and the electronic
**Guichet Unique** (single window) for imports — the most active engine of Burundi's govtech
digitalisation, [[world-bank]]/[[pafen-burundi|PAFEN]]-backed and framed around mobilising domestic
revenue "without relying exclusively on external aid."

## Activity in this wiki

- **e-KORI launched (19 Jan 2026).** [[dpi.govtech]] World Bank-backed online platform for tax/fee
  declaration and payment (Commissioner General **Emmanuel Mbonihankuye**; Minister Alain Ndikumana):
  real-time revenue monitoring, a **nine-phase** rollout with a technical security partner, framed under
  Vision 2040/2060. Sources: [[2026-01-20-burundi-revenue-authority-e-kori-digital-tax-platform]],
  [[2026-01-22 Burundi launches online tax system e-KORI]].
- **Guichet Unique + #ABREMA pharma module (2026-02-17).** [[dpi.exchange]] The OBR put its web
  **Electronic Single Window** into definitive production, adding the **ABREMA** module for pharmaceutical
  imports (interactive dashboard, multi-currency auto-fees), with TradeMark Africa
  funding and [[unctad]] technical support, integrated toward the **[[asycuda]]** customs system and a
  single customs territory. Source: [[2026-02-19-burundi-guichet-unique-importation-wearetech]].
- **Regional tax-digitalisation exchange (2025-06).** [[gov.regional]] The OBR ran learning exchanges
  with Sierra Leone's NRA, Liberia's LRA and Benin on digital tax collection, ahead of a May-2025 tender
  for an integrated internal-tax system. Source:
  [[2025-06-25-burundi-regional-cooperation-digital-tax-reforms]].
