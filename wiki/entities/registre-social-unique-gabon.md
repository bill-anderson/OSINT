# Registre Social Unique (RSU, Gabon)

Gabon's **Unique Social Registry** — an integrated database to target social programmes and reach the
most vulnerable, replacing fragmented legacy mechanisms. **Technical work finalised** at a closing
workshop on **28 August 2025**, chaired by the Minister of Women, Family and Child Protection.

## Key facts

- **Purpose:** rationalise social spending (avoid duplication/waste), improve coordination of social
  programmes across government, and enable precise beneficiary targeting — with explicit attention to
  vulnerable children, women and families, including in remote areas.
- **Partners:** Ministry of Women, Family and Child Protection; Ministry of Social Affairs and
  Inclusion; **[[aninf-gabon]]** (digital infrastructure); technical/financial partners, notably the
  **[[world-bank]]** — consistent with the social-protection access aims of the
  [[digital-gabon-project]].
- **DPI linkage:** a social registry sits downstream of the CNIE/NIP legal-identity layer, the
  intended key for targeting once ID coverage scales (see [[gabon--dpi-id]]).
