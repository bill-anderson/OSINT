# Baobab Bank (Madagascar)

A Madagascar bank (part of the pan-African **Baobab Group**, microfinance/SME focus), integrating with
mobile money to widen banking access. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Airtel Money Mbanking integration (2026-07).** [[finance.mou]] [[include.access]] Baobab Bank
  integrated into **[[airtel-madagascar|Airtel Money]]**'s **Mbanking** service — Baobab customers access
  banking via Airtel Money, and Airtel Money users transfer to Baobab accounts (USSD **\*436\*5#** or the
  My Baobab app). See [[madagascar--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-airtel-money-baobab-bank-partnership-techafricanews]].
