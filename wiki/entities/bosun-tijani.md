# Bosun Tijani

Nigeria's **Minister of Communications, Innovation and Digital Economy** — the principal
directing federal digital-economy policy (created here on the same "principals" bar as
other ministers/heads of state in the wiki).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Regulatory harmonisation directive (2026-07).** Tijani suspended the implementation
  and enforcement of newly-introduced regulations on internet platforms, online
  intermediaries and cross-cutting digital-economy issues, **pending a harmonised national
  policy framework**. He established a **joint technical coordination committee** of the
  **[[ncc]]**, **[[nitda]]** and **[[ndpc]]** to run stakeholder consultations, citing the
  convergence of telecoms, digital platforms, AI, online safety and data governance
  creating overlapping regulatory mandates. Existing statutory powers of the agencies are
  unaffected. See [[NGA]], [[gov.policy]], [[gov.legislate]], [[nigeria--gov-protect]].
  Source: [[2026-07-08 FG Halts Enforcement of New Regulations on Internet Platforms]].
- Context: the directive came <24h after President Tinubu asked the **FCCPC** to
  investigate major tech / generative-AI platforms over anti-competitive practices and
  "exploitation of Nigerian media content." See [[tech.ai]].
- **NUCAP / China Industrial Bank (2026-06).** Tijani announced that **China Industrial
  Bank** — its first investment in Nigeria — will back at least **1,000 of [[nucap]]'s
  3,700 planned telecom towers** before end-2026, extending "green" connectivity to 20m+
  underserved Nigerians. See [[NGA]], [[geopol.china]], [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-04 Chinese bank backs Nigeria towers project]].
- **GovGuide launch (2026-05).** Tijani launched **[[govguide-nigeria]]**, a Meta-built,
  Llama-based multilingual (English/Hausa/Igbo/Yoruba) AI chatbot for public-service
  access, developed with his ministry (FMCIDE), NCAIR and Publica AI. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-22 Nigeria launches GovGuide, an AI chatbot for public service access]].
- **Digital Postcode System approved (2026-03).** Tijani announced approval of a GIS-enabled,
  alphanumeric **[[nigeria-digital-postcode-system]]** (geographic intelligent addressing) as
  foundational DPI for planning, emergency response, logistics/e-commerce and government service
  delivery. See [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-05 Nigeria approves modern digital postcode system]].
- **Finland digital-innovation MoU (2026-03).** Tijani and Finland's Under Secretary Jarno Syrjälä
  signed the **[[nigeria-finland-digital-mou]]** in Abuja covering digital government, DPI,
  cybersecurity, emerging tech and capacity building — following February engagements in Helsinki
  (Finnvera, Finnfund), with Finnish interest in the Data Exchange Platform and **Project BRIDGE**;
  it complements Finland's lead in the EU **Team Europe Initiative** on digital public services
  (implemented by HAUS with Estonia's ESTDEV). See [[finance.mou]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[geopol.eu]].
  Source: [[2026-03-01 Nigeria Signs MoU With Finland On Digital Innovation, Deepens Bilateral Ties]].
- **AI-governance push (2026-03).** As champion of the National AI strategy, Tijani has warned that
  without a clear national approach to governing AI, deepfakes carry "significant implications for
  society" — the framing behind [[ndpc]]'s parallel entry into the Global Privacy Assembly enforcement
  coalition. See [[tech.ai]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-04 Nigeria takes new measures to regulate AI]].
