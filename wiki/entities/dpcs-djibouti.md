# DPCS (Djibouti Port Community System)

Djibouti's **port-community single-window / trade-data-exchange system** (legal base **Décret 216/2023**) —
showcased at Dar es Salaam as an **IMO / World Bank best practice** alongside Mauritius, KenTrade and Busan.
A cross-border trade-data interoperability signal for the region's premier transit port. Source:
[[2026-04-29-dpcs-port-digitalisation-imo-worldbank-best-practice-lanation]].
