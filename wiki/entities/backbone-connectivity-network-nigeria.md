# Backbone Connectivity Network (BCN) Nigeria

A Nigerian subsea and connectivity operator, led by CEO Ibrahim Dikko. It is pursuing
cross-border fibre links framed around regional data sovereignty and reduced dependence on
external (European) routing.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Signs Nigeria–Equatorial Guinea subsea cable agreement (2026-02).** BCN, represented by
  Dikko, signed a landmark subsea fibre-optic agreement in Malabo with Equatorial Guinea's
  Minister of Transport, Telecommunications and AI Systems, Honorato Evita Oma. The deal
  includes a commercial data-centre component; it is cast as building cross-border
  redundancy and regional data sovereignty. See [[nigeria-eqguinea-subsea-cable]]. Tags
  [[infra.connect]], [[gov.regional]]. Source: [[2026-02-24 Nigeria, Equatorial Guinea subsea cable strengthens connectivity]].

- **Compute co-location at a hydropower site.** BCN is also cited co-locating compute at a
  hydropower site with Mainstream Energy. ⚠ Detail from a single source; verify against a
  primary release before citing.
