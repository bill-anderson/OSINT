# Communication and Renewable Energy Infrastructure (CREI)

South Sudanese telecom-energy company that delivers hybrid (solar-hybrid) energy
solutions to mobile network operators, powering telecom towers where the grid is
absent or unreliable. Its model treats reliable energy as the backbone of
digital connectivity in a low-infrastructure market.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Solar-hybrid energy for MTN South Sudan's tower fleet (as of 2026-02-20).**
  Under a 10-year Energy-as-a-Service contract, CREI is modernising and expanding
  solar-hybrid energy across [[mtn-group]] South Sudan's 499 telecom sites,
  having renovated 490 sites serving over four million people. Over three years,
  network coverage rose from 69% to 80% of the population. The work is backed by
  Finnish financier [[finnfund]] ($10m total, including a $5m follow-on backed by
  the EU's EFSD+ / Africa Connected) ([[SSD]], [[infra.energy]],
  [[infra.connect]]). Source: [[2026-02-20 South Sudan green network gets $5m boost]].
