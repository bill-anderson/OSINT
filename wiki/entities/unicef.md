# UNICEF

United Nations Children's Fund. In this wiki's domain, UNICEF is a recurring actor in **civil
registration and vital statistics (CRVS)** — especially **birth registration** — and child-focused
digital-inclusion work across Africa, frequently partnering with national population/registry bodies
([[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.id]], [[include.access]]).

## Activity in this wiki
- **2026-02 (Nigeria)** — reported **14m births** registered over two years, supporting the national
  electronic CRVS drive ([[e-crvs-nigeria]], [[npc-nigeria]]). Source:
  [[2026-02-20 UNICEF records 14 million Nigeria birth registrations in two years]].
