# eAqoonsi (Somalia digital ID app)

**eAqoonsi** is [[nira-somalia]]'s digital ID mobile application — the digital foundation
for online interactions with the Somalia National ID System (SNIDS / [[snid]]). It serves
citizens and non-citizens as a secure alternative to the physical national ID card, with
the same legal authority, usable online and offline.

## Key features

- **Verifiable digital credential** with the same authority as the physical card.
- Streamlined onboarding by entering personal details and capturing data on a mobile
  device; facial recognition, basic info and OTP verification at registration.
- **Robust authentication** — OTP for critical actions (login, profile changes, ID
  verification); login via username/password and QR codes for quick verification.
- **Virtual ID card** — a government-approved secure virtual card in the app.
- Profile management (users update personal and biometric information), stated to comply
  with data-protection regulations.

Launched alongside NIRA's HUBIYE verification platform as part of Somalia's foundational
digital-ID stack. One of "three formats" of the [[snid]] (physical card, eAqoonsi digital
wallet, printable QR certificate).
