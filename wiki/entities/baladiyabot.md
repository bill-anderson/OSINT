# BaladiyaBOT (Tripoli municipal digital assistant)

A **municipal digital assistant** for citizen access to local-government services, launched (development
start 19 Apr 2026) by the **Municipality of Tripoli Centre** as the digital extension of the physical
Citizen Service Centre. See [[digital.localgov]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Development launch (2026-04).** [[geopol.eu]] Built within Germany's **GIZ** "Support to
  Municipalities in Libya" (SML III) programme, **EU-funded** (with Germany's BMZ), implemented with the
  Ministry of Local Government across 30 municipalities — piloted in Tripoli Centre with a view to
  national scaling. A concrete EU/German development-actor footprint in Libyan local e-government. Source:
  [[2026-04-25-tripoli-baladiyabot-municipal-digital-assistant-libyaherald]].
