# Information Regulator (South Africa)

South Africa’s data-protection authority, enforcing **[[popia]]** and the Promotion of Access to
Information Act (PAIA). One of the better-resourced African DPAs, yet it reports capacity, staff-
retention and funding strain. Issues sector **codes of conduct** (e.g. Banking Association, Credit
Bureau Association) under POPIA s.61.

## Activity in this wiki

- **POPIA monitoring exercise (2026-02).** Launched a proactive compliance-monitoring drive, compelling
  selected organisations to submit electronic compliance reports (incl. Legitimate Interest
  Assessments) within 14 days, with premises inspections possible. See [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-23 Bimonthly Update on Privacy in Africa (January & February 2026)]].
- **Cited in DPA-capacity debate (2026-03).** Held up by Cenfri as an example of even a well-resourced
  DPA facing severe capacity constraints; its POPIA s.61 sector-code mechanism is offered as a model for
  integrating sectoral expertise. See [[afcfta-digital-trade-protocol]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-23 Reassessing the role of data protection authorities in Africa]].
