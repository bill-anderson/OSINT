# MTN Benin

Benin subsidiary of **[[mtn-group]]** and the market's connectivity front-runner.

## Activity in this wiki

- **First commercial 5G in Benin (Nov 2024).** MTN Benin launched the country's first commercial 5G network, live initially in **Cotonou and Abomey-Calavi**, ahead of rivals Moov Africa Benin and Celtiis (then trialing). MD: Uche Ofodile. See [[infra.connect]] and the [[BEN]] hub. Source: [[2024-11-25 MTN launches Benin's first commercial 5G network]].
