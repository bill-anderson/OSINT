# MTN Benin

Benin subsidiary of **[[mtn-group]]** and the market's connectivity front-runner.

## Activity in this wiki

- **First commercial 5G in Benin (Nov 2024).** MTN Benin launched the country's first commercial 5G network, live initially in **Cotonou and Abomey-Calavi**, ahead of rivals Moov Africa Benin and Celtiis (then trialing). MD: Uche Ofodile. See [[infra.connect]] and the [[BEN]] hub. Source: [[2024-11-25 MTN launches Benin's first commercial 5G network]]. (Moov Africa Bénin became the second operator on 5G in Apr 2026.)
- **Huawei Future Tech Summit (2026-05).** [[geopol.china]] MTN Benin and **Huawei** co-hosted a Future Tech Summit 2026 — the first Huawei-in-Benin item on record here, thickening an otherwise thin `geopol.china` thread on the Benin pages. Source: [[2026-05-17-techreviewafrica-mtn-benin-huawei-future-tech-summit]].
