# Tunisia Investment Authority (TIA)

The **Tunisia Investment Authority (TIA)** is the national body handling investment-project registration
and declarations.

- **Mobile ID mandatory for investment declarations (from 1 July 2026).** TIA made **[[e-houwiya|Mobile
  ID]]** compulsory for all investment-project declarations — investors must hold a digital identity
  before accessing investment services — framed as streamlining procedures and cutting bureaucracy.
  Source: [[2026-06-26-techafricanews-mobile-id-mandatory-investors]].
