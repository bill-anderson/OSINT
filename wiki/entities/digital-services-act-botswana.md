# Digital Services Act / Digital Services Authority / 1Gov-1Citizen (Botswana)

Botswana's **e-government backbone reform** under Minister of Communications and Innovation **David Tshere**
(appointed Nov 2024): a **Digital Services Act** and a new **Digital Services Authority** meant to make
government "**digital-by-default**" — setting standards for service interoperability, cybersecurity and
governance across all ministries — anchored on the **1Gov-1Citizen** e-services platform. It replaces a
history of stalled govtech (a P500m e-government programme the Auditor General judged a fiasco). See
[[BWA]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## What we know (as of 2026-03)

- **1Gov-1Citizen.** [[dpi.govtech]] E-services expanded **33 → 52** (teacher registration, appointment
  scheduling, business facilitation) in FY2026/27; migration of government systems to the
  [[digital-delta-data-centre|Delta Digital Data Centre]], with a **second data centre planned outside
  Gaborone**. Source: [[2026-03-18-botswana-1gov-1citizen-digital-services-act-techafricanews]].
- **The network under it.** [[infra.connect]] An **SD-WAN across 600 priority sites**, Government Data
  Network expansion, and a **Local Access Network** connecting **13,400+** facilities (a Johannesburg–
  Tlokweng fibre link on the backbone). Source:
  [[2026-03-18-botswana-1gov-1citizen-digital-services-act-techafricanews]].
- **Legislative pipeline.** [[gov.legislate]] The Act sits with a **Cybersecurity Act (Nov 2025)** and the
  standing data-protection regime; a **Draft Digital Services Bill** and debate over whether a standalone
  **Digital Services Agency** is even needed ran through 2025. Sources:
  [[2025-12-14-botswana-ict-trajectory-2025-2026]], [[2025-08-18-no-need-for-a-new-digital-services-agency]].
- **Foreign blueprints.** Botswana drew on **Estonia** (President Boko's 2025 state visit; Nortal and
  Baltic Computer Systems already operating in-country) and courted **Rwanda's Irembo** e-gov operator
  (2026). Sources: [[2025-05-22-botswana-taps-estonia-egovernance-blueprint-itweb]],
  [[2026-05-08-irembo-rwanda-botswana-business-forum-techreviewafrica]].

- **Funding + regional framing.** [[finance.new]] Under **NDP 12** the Finance Ministry earmarked **P8.8bn**
  for digital-discipline priorities (a national payment switch, fintech sandbox, e-procurement, BURS
  e-VAT); Botswana's VP Ndaba Gaolathe pressed a **federated African digital-transformation** agenda
  (harmonised data-protection/e-signature/digital-ID, shared data centres) at the Digital Government Africa
  Summit. Sources: [[2025-11-03-finance-ministry-bets-p8-8bn-on-digital-discipline]],
  [[2025-10-09-botswana-vp-digital-government-africa-summit-techafricanews]].
