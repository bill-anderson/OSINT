# James Mwangi

Managing Director and CEO of **[[equity-group-holdings]]**; appointed inaugural
**Continental Digital Public Infrastructure Champion** (2026-07).

## Positions / arguments

- Private sector as DPI accelerant, not owner: "we are not asking governments to
  surrender ownership of public infrastructure… we are demonstrating how the private
  sector can partner with governments to accelerate" digital ID and interoperable
  payments.
- "Infrastructure alone is not enough; its value comes from the solutions innovators…
  build on top of it." See [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]], [[tech.innovate]].
