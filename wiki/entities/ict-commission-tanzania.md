# Tanzania ICT Commission (ICTC)

Tanzania's **ICT Commission** — the government body coordinating ICT-sector development, skills and
innovation. Director General (2025): **Dr Nkundwe Mwasaga**.

- **First National AI Forum (Jul 2025).** ICTC convened Tanzania's inaugural **National AI Forum** (Dar,
  28–29 Jul 2025), theme *"Reimagining Tanzania's Future through Inclusive and Ethical AI."* Mwasaga:
  **"we must embed our Tanzanian values into these systems — without ethical frameworks, our platforms
  will not be trusted."** See [[tanzania--tech-ai]]. Source: [[2025-07-29-tanzania-charts-path-responsible-ai]].
