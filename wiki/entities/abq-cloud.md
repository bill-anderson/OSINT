# ABQ Cloud

**A Ugandan cloud provider**, launched **2026-03-02** and described as the country's
**indigenous sovereign cloud ecosystem** — a cloud built *"by Ugandans for Uganda and
Africa,"* hosted within Uganda and delivered through local partners. CTO: **Eric
Mugerwa**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (2026-03-02).** Unveiled at the **Deep Tech Hub and [[raxio-group]] Data
  Centre, Namanve, Kampala**, officiated by **Monica Musenero Masanza**, Minister of
  Science, Technology and Innovation, who called it *"a strategic pillar for Uganda's
  future."* Participants: **[[sti-op-uganda]]**, ABQ Cloud, Raxio Data Centre, **Roke
  Cloud**, **AfriQloud** and **EDiC**, with Uganda's innovation community. The platform is
  positioned as a step toward securing Uganda's digital independence, providing
  affordable, secure and locally hosted cloud infrastructure for startups, enterprises,
  government institutions and innovators. **The sales proposition: local-currency
  pricing, reduced latency, enhanced data sovereignty, predictable costs.** CTO Eric
  Mugerwa described ABQ Cloud as *"a foundation for Uganda's digital independence."*
  Raxio's General Manager **Caroline Kamaitha** said Raxio provides *"secure, reliable,
  and globally certified infrastructure that enables platforms like ABQ Cloud to thrive"*;
  Roke Cloud MD **James Byaruhanga** said the partnership is *"democratizing access to
  digital infrastructure."* See [[infra.store]], [[tech.innovate]], [[sovereignty]].
  Source: [[2026-03-02 ABQ Cloud launches Ugandan sovereign cloud ecosystem]].

**Worth noting:** the sovereignty claim rests on hosting in a **[[raxio-group]]** facility
— a sovereign cloud layer atop foreign-owned colocation. Capture is not endorsement; the
source does not examine the tension.
