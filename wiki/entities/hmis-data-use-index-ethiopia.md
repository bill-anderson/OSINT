# Data for local decision-making: an index to measure facility-level use of HMIS data

Peer-reviewed article by **Qian, Shiferaw, Seme et al.**, *Journal of Global Health Reports*
**(2023)**. Develops and applies a **10-item, facility-level HMIS data-use index** across
**305 Ethiopian facilities**, measured in **2019 and 2020**. Funded by the
**[[gates-foundation]]** through **PMA Ethiopia** (Addis Ababa University with Johns Hopkins).
Cited here as literature; a later dated fact drawn from it becomes a source citing this page.
Part of the HMIS/[[dhis2]] evidence cluster ([[dpi.mis]]).

## Argument

- Argues HMIS data should drive **local decision-making**, "not a mere reporting
  requirement," and operationalises this with a measurable index ([[capacity.research]]).
- Mean index score **rose 7.2 → 7.9** between 2019 and 2020 (**P<0.01**) — a small but
  significant improvement in facility-level data use ([[data.statistics]]).
- **>95%** of facilities use **DHIS-2** ([[dhis2]]), introduced in Ethiopia in **2018** —
  confirming near-universal platform adoption, with data *use* the remaining frontier.
