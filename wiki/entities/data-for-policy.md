# Data for Policy

A UK-registered non-profit **global community** (founded **2014**) fostering discourse on
data, AI and their use in government, governance and policy. Established by **Dr Zeynep
Engin and Prof. Jon Crowcroft** via the Cambridge Centre for Science and Policy (CSaP), it
became a **UK Community Interest Company (CIC)** in **2018** and convenes a network of
**5,000+ experts** (as of the 2023 About-Us page). A standing OSINT object: the community
and journal behind a body of governance/data-transformation scholarship the wiki cites.

## What it is / what it does

- Publishes the open-access **Data & Policy journal** with Cambridge University Press
  (launched **2019**) — home of the **[[digital-data-driven-transformations-landscape-review-2025]]**.
- In **2022** launched a proactive inclusion / **"Equity Initiative"** with
  **[[gates-foundation]]** support, initially focused on **Sub-Saharan Africa**
  participation and inclusion scholarships (hence the XSS tag).
- Board members include Zeynep Engin, Jon Crowcroft, David Hand, Emily Gardner and
  **[[stefaan-verhulst]]** ([[the-govlab]], NYU).
