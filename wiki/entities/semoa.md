# Semoa Group

A Togolese fintech (founded 2016 by engineer Edem Adjamagbo) building digital-payment and transaction-
digitisation solutions. On **29 Jan 2026** it obtained a **BCEAO Level 3 accreditation** — the most
complete licence the central bank grants a non-bank — under Instruction 001-01-2024. See
[[togo--dpi-pay]], [[TGO]].
