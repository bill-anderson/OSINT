# Payment Service Directive for Africa (PSDA, proposed)

A **proposed** harmonising framework (floated by African finance experts convened by [[africanenda]],
benchmarked on the EU's PSD1–3) to regulate payment services continent-wide and unlock instant
cross-border payments under the AfCFTA — a response to fragmentation across 54 jurisdictions and 40+
currencies. Proposal, not yet an instrument in force. See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]].
