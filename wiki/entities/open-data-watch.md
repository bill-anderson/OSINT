# Open Data Watch (ODW)

An international non-profit that works to **strengthen open and official data systems** in
low- and middle-income countries — monitoring the coverage, openness and capacity of national
statistical and administrative data. Co-author of PARIS21's [[2026-07-11 data-systems-crossroads]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Rebuilding Global Health Data" (2025).** ODW authored *Rebuilding Global Health Data:
  Scale, Risks, and Paths to Recovery*, warning that the routine health-data systems of
  **>75 LMICs** — most built on [[dhis2]] and covering >40% of the world's population — face
  acute **funding-shock risk**. It names three risks (over-reliance on donor funding, weak
  domestic financing/governance, insufficient country coordination) and calls for governments
  to fund **permanent HMIS/CRVS roles** in recurrent budgets rather than short-term
  consultancies. See [[dpi.mis]], [[data.statistics]], [[finance.new]]. Reported via
  [[hisp-centre]]. Source: [[2025-11-05 New report highlights urgent need to rebuild and sustain health data systems]].
