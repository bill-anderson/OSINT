# Registre Social Unique (RSU)

Côte d'Ivoire's **single social registry** — centralising socio-economic data on poor and vulnerable households to share with social programmes for targeting. Managed via the SIG-RSU information system, under the Ministry of National Cohesion (Filets Sociaux programme).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Operationalisation (2025).** As of April 2025, **more than 200,000 households** were in the process of registration — the social-registry (dpi.registry) layer under Côte d'Ivoire's social-protection system. See [[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]. Source: [[2025-04-12 Cote d'Ivoire - Registre Social Unique (RSU) operationalisation, 200,000+ households]].
