# Senegal Numérique SA (SENUM SA)

Senegal's **state digital-infrastructure and e-government agency** (formerly the **Agence de l'Informatique
de l'État — ADIE**), leading the country's digital transformation and hosting core state platforms and data.
Institutional home for much of the DPI build-out under the **[[new-deal-technologique]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Reported compromised alongside the DAF breach (Feb 2026).** SeneWeb reported that SENUM SA — "guardian
  of state data" — suffered compromises around the same time as the **[[daf-senegal|DAF]]** national-ID
  breach (early Feb 2026), suggesting possibly coordinated attacks; authorities issued no consolidated
  technical accounting of the second claim. See [[SEN]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-24 Senegal ID System Breach – 139TB Hack Claim]].
- **Historic role (ADIE).** As ADIE, hosted the **[[rnu-senegal|RNU]]** social-registry database and its MIS
  (per IPC-IG, 2019). Source: [[2019-05-01 Senegal National Single Registry RNU (IPC-IG research brief 63)]].
