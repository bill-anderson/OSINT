# Senegal Numérique SA (SENUM SA)

Senegal's **state digital-infrastructure and e-government agency** (formerly the **Agence de l'Informatique
de l'État — ADIE**), leading the country's digital transformation and hosting core state platforms and data.
Institutional home for much of the DPI build-out under the **[[new-deal-technologique]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cyberattack *attempts* acknowledged (communiqué, 9 Feb 2026) — the company never confirmed a breach.**
  SENUM SA's own communiqué of **9 Feb 2026** reports **"tentatives de cyberattaques"** on its information
  systems and sets out containment: **temporary restriction of access to certain services**, reinforced
  threat detection, cyberdefence/systems/network teams mobilised in coordination with the national
  cybersecurity authorities, and a helpdesk for affected customers. It names **no attacker, no exfiltration
  and no affected dataset**. Source: [[2026-02-13-enqueteplus-senum-tentatives-cyberattaques]].
- **Press had reported it as a compromise three days earlier — the discrepancy is unreconciled.**
  **L'Observateur** (6 Feb 2026, relayed by SeneWeb and others) reported SENUM SA — "guardian of state data" —
  compromised alongside the **[[daf-senegal|DAF]]** national-ID breach, and raised a possible **coordinated
  offensive**. That coordination framing is **press inference from simultaneity**, attributed to the
  newspaper's own reading and never to the DSC, the company or the ministry; **no actor claimed SENUM**,
  unlike the DAF. No consolidated technical accounting has been published *(as of 2026-07-20)*. See [[SEN]],
  [[infra.cybersec]], [[senegal--infra-cybersec]]. Sources:
  [[2026-02-24 Senegal ID System Breach – 139TB Hack Claim]],
  [[2026-02-13-enqueteplus-senum-tentatives-cyberattaques]].
- **Historic role (ADIE).** As ADIE, hosted the **[[rnu-senegal|RNU]]** social-registry database and its MIS
  (per IPC-IG, 2019). Source: [[2019-05-01 Senegal National Single Registry RNU (IPC-IG research brief 63)]].
