# Registration, Insolvency and Trusteeship Agency (RITA), Tanzania

The **civil-registration authority for Tanzania Mainland** — the body responsible for
registering births, deaths and other vital events, and therefore the upstream half of any
birth-to-ID pipeline. RITA sits at the join between civil registration and the foundational ID
run by **[[nida-tanzania]]**: birth certificates are RITA's, national ID numbers are NIDA's, and
the **[[jamii-namba]]** universal-ID design promises both at birth.

## Activity in this wiki

- **CRVS reference snapshot (IDRC Centre of Excellence, 2019).** The
  **[[idrc-crvs-centre-of-excellence]]**, with UNECA, published a country snapshot of Tanzania
  Mainland's civil registration and vital statistics systems, **built from a questionnaire
  completed by RITA in December 2018** plus desk review (author Yacob Zewoldi; funded by Global
  Affairs Canada and IDRC) — establishing RITA as the responding authority for mainland CRVS.
  **Reference study — cited, not absorbed:** its dated figures are deliberately left in the study
  rather than promoted here as current state. See [[dpi.registry]], [[data.statistics]]. Source:
  [[2019-01-01 Snapshot of CRVS Systems of Tanzania Mainland (IDRC Centre of Excellence)]].
- **The Zanzibar boundary.** The snapshot covers **Tanzania Mainland** only — a reminder that
  Tanzania's registration architecture is not uniform across the union. The same fault line
  appears in the **[[tanzania-pdpa]]**, which reaches Zanzibar only on 'Union Matters'. Source:
  [[2019-01-01 Snapshot of CRVS Systems of Tanzania Mainland (IDRC Centre of Excellence)]].
