# The Global Fund (to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis and Malaria)

A global health-financing partnership that mobilises and invests funding to fight AIDS,
tuberculosis and malaria and to strengthen health systems in low- and middle-income
countries. In the African data landscape it appears primarily as a **financier of
health-data and public-health-surveillance infrastructure** ([[dpi.mis]]), making it a
recurring funding node behind continental health-information systems.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Financed the Africa CDC Central Data Repository (2026).** The Global Fund **directly
  financed** the **[[africa-cdc-cdr]]** and engaged the
  **[[public-health-informatics-institute]]** (PHII) to conduct the needs assessment, cost
  analysis, operational planning and prototype development for the continental federated
  health-data platform launched by **[[africa-cdc]]** in January 2026. See [[dpi.mis]],
  [[finance.new]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-28 Africa CDC Establishes Central Data Repository to Strengthen Public Health Surveillance]].
