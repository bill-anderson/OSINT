# Instance Nationale des Télécommunications (INT)

Tunisia's **telecommunications regulator** — the independent authority overseeing the
telecoms market and publishing sector statistics.

- **Data-driven shift (2025).** INT data showed mobile internet usage and subscriptions rising sharply
  while voice plateaued (OTT displacement); fixed internet growing steadily but **mobile broadband
  dominant** as the primary access point, with rising active SIMs (multi-device / IoT). Source:
  [[2025-04-11-techafricanews-telecom-data-driven-growth]].
