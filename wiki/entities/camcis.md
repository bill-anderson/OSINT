# CAMCIS (Cameroon Customs Information System)

Cameroon's **digital customs platform**, run under the Finance Ministry / Customs (Minister **Motazé**) —
the system behind the **IMEI-based mobile-device import-duty regime**. (Note: **CAMCIS ≠ CAMCIH**, a
separate climate-innovation NGO.)

## Profile

- **What it answers.** Device customs revenue collapsed from ~CFA 2bn/month in the 2000s to **~CFA 100m
  in 2025**; estimated leakage ~**$21.5m/yr**. Source:
  [[2026-03-23-camcis-digital-customs-platform-mobile-devices-wearetech]],
  [[2026-03-31-unlock-stalled-mobile-phone-tax-revenue-camcis-itweb]].
- **Mechanics & legal basis.** IMEI-gated network access, grounded in **Article 6 of the 2023 Finance
  Law** and framed **"not a new tax"** but enforcement of existing duties; a pre-1-Apr-2026 fiscal
  amnesty, a two-month stock-regularisation window, roaming/temporary-admission exemptions, and vendor
  liability — officials' "digital border." A **2022 attempt collapsed** under public resistance when the
  burden fell on end users. Sources:
  [[2026-04-01-imei-system-mobile-device-import-duties-camcis-techcabal]],
  [[2026-03-31-unlock-stalled-mobile-phone-tax-revenue-camcis-itweb]].
- **Enforcement contested — the block did *not* land on schedule.** Blocking of undeclared devices was set
  for **25 May 2026** (order dated 1 Apr 2026, operator liability for unpaid duties; **51,000+ devices
  declared, >CFA 200m raised; projection CFA 25bn/yr** vs CFA 1.3bn before). But by **9 Jun 2026** the
  operators had **refused** — MTN/Orange jointly objected that technical/operational/legal prerequisites
  were unmet and warned of competitive distortion; Motazé wrote demanding compliance and clarified blocking
  targets **IMEIs, not SIMs** (~700,000 uncleared connections in Apr 2026). *The May item must not stand as
  evidence the block succeeded.* Sources:
  [[2026-05-22-blocks-undeclared-mobile-devices-imei-customs-itweb]],
  [[2026-05-25-orders-telcos-block-unregistered-mobile-devices-connectingafrica]],
  [[2026-06-09-motaze-warns-telcos-block-illicit-devices-tax-penalties-connectingafrica]].
