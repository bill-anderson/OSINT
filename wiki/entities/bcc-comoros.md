# Banque Centrale des Comores (BCC)

The Comoros' **central bank** — driving the country's digital-payments modernisation. Governor: Dr Younoussa Imani.

## Activity in this wiki

- **PAPSS accession (2024).** The BCC signed the PAPSS membership agreement (Jul 2024) and launched implementation (Aug 2024, Moroni workshop) — enabling cross-border payments to other African states directly in Comorian francs (~120-second settlement) under AfCFTA. See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]]. Source: [[2024-08-23 Central Bank of Comoros commences PAPSS implementation]].
- **National Payment Switch.** Oversees the World Bank-financed (PADSF) national interoperable payment switch delivered by **[[paylogic]]** (go-live Aug 2025). Source: [[2025-08-23 PayLogic go-live of Comoros national interoperable payment switch]].
