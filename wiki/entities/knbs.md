# Kenya National Bureau of Statistics (KNBS)

Kenya's **national statistics office**, custodian of official statistics and a key data source for the
proposed **[[kenya-data-marketplace|State Data Marketplace]]**. See [[data.statistics]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Safaricom mobile-data-for-statistics partnership (2026-06).** [[data.statistics]] [[sovereignty]]
  KNBS and **[[safaricom]]** announced a collaboration (Global Data Festival / Kenya Space Expo) to use
  **anonymised, aggregated mobile-phone data** to complement traditional statistics — population trends,
  mobility, socio-economic patterns — framed around privacy-by-design and "ethical insights" (Safaricom's
  Sharon Holi). A live case of **private data feeding public planning**. Source:
  [[2026-06-08-safaricom-knbs-mobile-data-planning-techafricanews]].
- **Data-marketplace supplier (2026-06).** [[data.open]] KNBS datasets are among those the state plans to
  licence via the **[[kenya-data-marketplace|State Data Marketplace]]** (non-personal, anonymised). See
  [[kenya-data-marketplace]]. Source: [[2026-06-07-state-plans-ecitizen-data-sale-nation]].
