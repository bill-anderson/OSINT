# Studia Inc

A firm delivering **AI-powered digitisation of civil-registration archives** in Madagascar under the
**EC-MADA programme**. See [[dpi.registry]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **~10m civil records digitised (2026-05).** [[tech.ai]] Studia inventoried, digitised and indexed
  **nearly 10 million civil-registration records** across **1,695 communes / 11 priority regions** —
  **500 people over seven months**, autonomous mobile units (satellite + solar), and specialised **OCR +
  AI-driven indexing** to extract handwritten data from paper archives. Partnered with **Senegal-based
  D.IA Advisory** on interoperability and data-sovereignty compliance. See [[madagascar--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-08-studia-ai-digitisation-civil-records-intelligentcio]].
