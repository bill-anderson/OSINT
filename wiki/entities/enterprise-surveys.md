# World Bank Enterprise Surveys

**The [[world-bank]] Enterprise Analysis Unit's firm-level survey programme** — a
standing, comparable instrument measuring the business environment as private firms
experience it, published as per-country **Country Profiles**. Its ICT-adoption,
connectivity and finance indicators are the wiki's main firm-side evidence on the
digital economy. See [[tech.industry]], [[data.statistics]].

> **Reference study — cite, don't absorb.** Country Profiles are reference works. Their
> figures are dated to the **survey year** and belong in the profile; do not promote
> them onto concept or place pages as current state.

## Country profiles held

- **Eswatini** ([[SWZ]]) — **2024 survey year**; the profile carries a 2025 IBRD
  copyright and prints no publication date, so it is held at **2025, year precision**.
  Distinguish survey year from publication year when citing. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 Enterprise Surveys - Eswatini 2024 Country Profile (World Bank)]].
- **Benin** ([[BEN]]) — 2024 Country Profile. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 World Bank Enterprise Surveys - Benin 2024 Country Profile]].
