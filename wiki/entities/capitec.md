# Capitec

Major South African retail bank — the **first bank to go live** with Home Affairs Smart-ID applications
under the Digital Partnership Model (Orange Farm, 9 Mar 2026), and a participant in the payments ecosystem
(it absorbs, rather than passes on, the DHA ID-verification fee — see [[south-africa--dpi-id]]).

- Part of the four-bank Smart-ID network (with Absa, Standard Bank, FNB) across **296 participating
  branches** (Jul 2026). Source: [[2026-07-16-absa-joins-home-affairs-smart-id]].
