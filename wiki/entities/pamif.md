# PAMIF (financial-infrastructure modernisation)

A **$4.78m** African Development Fund (concessional **[[african-development-bank|AfDB]]** arm) initiative that
rebuilt Mauritania's financial market infrastructure with the **[[central-bank-mauritania|Central Bank of
Mauritania]]**. It **more than doubled financial inclusion — 21% (2019–20) → over 50% (55% in 2026)** — and
underpins the QR-code payments now common in Nouakchott.

Components deployed simultaneously: **télécompensation** (a cheque now settles same-day vs up to two weeks
before), a **real-time system** for large-value transfers ("funds within five minutes"), and an **automated
refinancing facility**. Illustrative impact: **[[bmci|BMCI]]** processed 5,000 transfers in all of 2023 vs
**94,000 in H2 2025 alone**; inflation fell from double digits to below 2%; a bond market now offers up to
ten-year maturities. A **second PAMIF phase** targets deeper inclusion, rural financial services, green/social
finance and the emergence of a Mauritanian fintech ecosystem.
