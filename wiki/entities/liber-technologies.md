# Liber Technologies

Luxembourg-based firm (est. 2017) specialising in **secure document digitisation, digital-government services and fiducial printing** for African states.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cameroon CRVS (2025).** Partners **[[bunec]]** to implement Cameroon's digital civil-registration reform (Law 2024/016) — digital certificates, UPIN, interoperable administrative-data systems. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2025-10-27 Cameroon's BUNEC partners with Liber Technologies for CRVS digitization]].
- Also provides digital-government services elsewhere in Africa, including **Chad** ([[TCD]]) — an e-visa system and digital land registration.
