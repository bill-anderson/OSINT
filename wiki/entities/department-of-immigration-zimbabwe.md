# Department of Immigration (Zimbabwe)

**Zimbabwe's border and immigration authority**, and the operator of the
[[obms-zimbabwe]] Online Border Management System — the biometric border programme
supplied by [[garsu-pasaulis]]. Everything held here is **dated 2024 and describes a
pilot**; the wiki holds nothing on its outcome (GAP-ZWE-002).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometric border pilot (as of 2024-05-15).** Zimbabwe was implementing **fingerprint
  and iris biometrics** at its borders to automate passport checks, visa verification and
  entry/exit records. The automation was to be **"initially tested"** at **Robert Gabriel
  Mugabe International Airport**, **Victoria Falls International Airport** and
  **Beitbridge Border Post**, plus several inland stations. **A pilot, dated — do not read
  as current operation.** See [[dpi.id]], [[obms-zimbabwe]]. Source:
  [[2024-05-15 Zimbabwe implements biometric border posts for automating immigration processes]].
- **Cross-agency data sharing (as of 2024-05).** **Respect Gono**, Chief Director of
  Immigration, told The Sunday Mail the new border management system would **improve the
  coordination of all border agencies** — implying a **centralised platform on which
  different border-control and security agencies access and share information**. Traveller
  identity is verified against **biometric data stored in the national immigration
  database**. See [[dpi.exchange]], [[gov.regional]]. Source: as above.

## Worth noting

The source does not argue it, but on its own facts: **a single Lithuanian vendor
([[garsu-pasaulis]]) supplies both Zimbabwe's passports and its border database**, on an
architecture derived from **Dubai's Smart Gates**, with **centralised cross-agency
biometric sharing** and **no oversight mechanism mentioned anywhere in the account**.
Recorded as an observation, not a conclusion.
