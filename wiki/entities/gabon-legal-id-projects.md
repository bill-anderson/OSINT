# Gabon legal-identity projects (RBPP, SNID, CNEC)

The three legal-identity pillars under **Gabon Digital** component 2 (World-Bank-financed), launched via
technical workshops at Ntoum on **23 June 2025** and framed within the **Plan National de Développement pour la
Transition 2024–2026**:

- **RBPP** — extension of the **Registre Biométrique des Personnes Physiques** (biometric population register).
- **SNID** — the **Système National d'Identité Digitale** (national digital-identity system). *(Note: distinct
  from Somalia's [[snid|SNID]] — same acronym, different country.)*
- **CNEC** — the **Centre National d'État Civil** (national civil-registration centre).

Together they build on the 2024 **CNIE / NIP** relaunch (see [[gabon--dpi-id]]) toward legal identity for every
citizen, better public services and social protection. **[[aninf-gabon|ANINF]]** DG Alberto Wenceslas
Mounguengui Moudoki is Gabon's ID4Africa ambassador (ID Day 2025 marked the governance milestone). See
[[digital-gabon-project]].
