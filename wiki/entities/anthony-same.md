# Anthony Same

Founder and CEO of **[[st-digital]]**, the pan-African data-centre and cloud operator — the clearest
**African-owned** counterweight in Cameroon's data-centre picture (otherwise the state via CENADI /
CAMTEL-Zamengoe, or foreign hyperscalers via the CAMTEL–AWS talks).

## Profile

- Founded ST Digital in **2017**; the firm operates in **Cameroon, Gabon, Republic of the Congo and Côte
  d'Ivoire**, with **500+ clients** and (company-reported) **three certified datacenters** — *certification
  unspecified; do not harden into a Tier claim* (as of May 2026).
- His pitch is the same **"keep African data hosted on the continent"** sovereignty claim CAMTEL makes,
  from a private pan-African firm — with the irony worth keeping in view that his credibility was built
  **inside Microsoft (from 2006) and SAP (2011–2015, head of Central Africa)**, after Toshiba and IBM.
  KEDGE MSc Finance (2003); ESSEC IT-management diploma (2005). Source:
  [[2026-05-13-anthony-same-st-digital-african-datacenter-network-wearetech]].
