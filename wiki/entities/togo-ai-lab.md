# Togo AI Lab

An initiative of Togo's Ministry of Public-Service Efficiency & Digital Transformation, developed with
Berkeley's **CEGA**, the **Patrick J. McGovern Foundation**, the Innovation for Development Fund and GIZ
**ProDigiT**. With **Zindi** and the startup **Umbaji**, it is building **open-source AI models for Togo's
50 national languages** — framed by Minister Cina Lawson as "essential public infrastructure" for a
sovereign, inclusive AI. See [[togo--tech-ai]], [[TGO]].
