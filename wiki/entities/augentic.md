# AUGENTIC

German biometric-identity firm (Darmstadt) providing national ID and secure-document systems in Africa on build-operate-transfer models — a European vendor at the core of a foreign-run identity build.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cameroon national ID (15-year BOT, from 2024).** Under a build-operate-transfer contract signed May 2024 with the **[[dgsn-cameroon]]**, Augentic overhauls Cameroon's national ID infrastructure — biometric enrolment, card production and secure issuance. The new-generation biometric CNI (e-chip, MRZ, QR, 10 fingerprints; ICAO/ISO-IEC 19303; Cameroon the first African adopter of the **ID-1 portrait format**) went to pre-enrolment 17 Feb 2025, with a 48-hour issuance promise; a Far North rapid-enrolment campaign followed (Aug 2025). A **second production centre opened in Garoua** (Aug 2025) after Douala, running ~2,000 cards/day with 15 security features. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]]. Sources: [[2025-02-05 Cameroon's new-generation biometric national ID card system (Augentic) goes live]], [[2025-08-04 Cameroon Launches Rapid Biometric ID Program in Far North to Address Displacement]], [[2025-08-07-new-biometric-id-card-center-garoua-northern-cameroon-biometricupdate]].
- The 15-year BOT (a foreign firm owning core ID infrastructure) is the sovereignty/dependence dimension of Cameroon's ID modernisation.
