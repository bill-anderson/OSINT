# Cina Lawson

**Togo's minister responsible for the digital economy and digital transformation — the
political principal of the country's digital agenda across the period covered here (2024–2025).**
Her ministry is the parent of **[[anid-togo]]**, the agency implementing [[e-id-togo]]. Title
renderings vary by source: **"Togolese Minister of Digital Economy and Transformation"** (World
Bank, 2024-12) and **"Digital Transformation Minister"** (Togo First, 2025-12). See [[TGO]],
[[gov.policy]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **On the $100m World Bank digital operation (2024-12-18).** Lawson framed the financing
  ([[togo-digital-acceleration-financing]]) as connective infrastructure first: **"This
  initiative is a game-changer for Togo, as we connect our schools, health facilities, and public
  institutions to the very high-speed internet, unlocking opportunities for innovation and
  progress. By empowering our youth with digital skills and fostering an environment for
  entrepreneurship, we are laying the groundwork for a more competitive and resilient economy."**
  See [[infra.connect]], [[capacity.training]], [[tech.innovate]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-18 Togo - $100 Million in Funding to Accelerate Digital Transformation]].
- **On public-service digitisation (2025-12).** With Territorial Administration Minister
  **Hodabalo Awate**, Lawson reaffirmed a commitment to extend the online civil-status service
  ([[services-publics-togo]]) **gradually to all municipalities nationwide**, aiming to improve
  administrative efficiency while reducing costs for users. See [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-17 Togo Expands Public Service Digitisation With Online Civil Registry Platform]].

**Scope note.** The evidence held here covers Lawson only through these two developments; no
biography, tenure dates or wider portfolio are asserted from it.
