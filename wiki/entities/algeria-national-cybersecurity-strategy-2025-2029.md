# Algeria National Strategy for Information Systems Security 2025–2029

Algeria's **first National Strategy for Information Systems Security (2025–2029)** — unveiled by **[[assi|ASSI]]**
(Ministry of National Defence) on **3 Mar 2026** and approved by President **[[abdelmadjid-tebboune]]**. Three
objectives: protect critical infrastructure, secure sensitive state data, ensure continuity of public services.
Priorities: technical capacity, inter-agency coordination, prevention/incident response, and public awareness.
Context: **70m+ cyberattacks on Algeria in 2024** (Kaspersky); Algeria at **ITU GCI Tier 3** (2024). See
[[algeria--infra-cybersec]].
