# CARDIP (Central African Digital Integration Program)

A **World Bank regional DPI programme** for Central Africa — the CEMAC-region analogue of West Africa's
WARDIP and East Africa's EARDIP — spanning **Cameroon, Chad, Congo, Gabon, Equatorial Guinea and the
Central African Republic**. It is the regional digital-integration vehicle CAR is seeking to board. See
[[XCA]], [[gov.regional]].

## What we know

- **CAR exploring accession (2026-03).** [[CAF]] CAR is in talks with the World Bank over **CARDIP**
  support — connectivity, cross-border digital services and DPI — a route out of its structural isolation
  as a landlocked, single-route state. Source:
  [[2026-03-06-cardip-world-bank-digital-integration-talks-africabusinessinsight]].
