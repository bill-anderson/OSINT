# ICJ Kenya

The **Kenyan Section of the International Commission of Jurists** (Nairobi) — a jurists'
and rule-of-law NGO whose **Digital Rights, Civic Space and Independent Media** portfolio
covers digital-rights and data-governance advocacy in Kenya. A standing civil-society actor
the wiki returns to on Kenyan digital-rights, data-protection and DPI-accountability
questions, alongside the regulator **[[odpc-kenya]]**.

## Relevant output

- ***Mapping Global Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI): A Human Rights Perspective with
  Focus on Kenya*** (2025) — a report evaluating DPI (digital ID, digital payments, data
  exchange) through a human-rights lens, with Kenya (M-Pesa, eCitizen, Huduma Namba, the
  Worldcoin controversy) as the focus country. Research consultant Henry Omusundi Maina;
  Deputy Executive Director Demas Kiprono. See the source record for facts drawn from it.
