# GAMTEL–SYSROAD $50m broadband PPP

A **US$50m Public-Private Partnership** between **GAMTEL** (state telecom) and **SYSROAD SA Holding** (Senegal,
[[SEN]]) to modernise The Gambia's **national broadband backbone** — upgrading core-network capacity from **40
Gbps to 800 Gbps (~20×)** to eliminate bottlenecks. Signed early 2026; one of the largest network investments
in GAMTEL's history, framed as **digital sovereignty**. See [[gambia--infra-connect]].
