# Ministry of Health (Zambia)

Zambia's health ministry — an early adopter in the SMART Zambia digitisation programme: it hosts the
**SMART Office e-registry** pilot (from MoH HQ) and runs the **[[smartcare-pro]]** national EHR (~80% of
facilities). See [[zambia--dpi-govtech]], [[ZMB]].
