# Ministry of Health (Zambia)

Zambia's health ministry — an early adopter in the SMART Zambia digitisation programme: it hosts the
**SMART Office e-registry** pilot (from MoH HQ) and runs the **[[smartcare-pro]]** national EHR (~80% of
facilities). See [[zambia--dpi-govtech]], [[ZMB]].

It is also a **launch partner in [[govlink|GovLink Private 4G]] (2026-07)**, the government-owned
network on which the health sector is the flagship: **Care 2.0** and **SmartCare Pro** migrate onto
it, and PS **Dr Kennedy Lishimpi** framed it as the connectivity that makes EHRs, telemedicine and
real-time clinical decision support workable nationally.
