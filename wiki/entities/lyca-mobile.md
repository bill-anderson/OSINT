# Lyca Mobile

British telecom group (founder/chairman **Subaskaran Allirajah**) expanding into African markets, already
operating in Uganda and Tunisia. In Burundi it obtained a telecom licence by presidential decree in
**October 2025** and is moving to deployment, pairing network build-out with a **Lyca Health** e-health
push (telemedicine, medical-infrastructure modernisation). See [[BDI]], [[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Burundi telecom + e-health partnership (2026-02-27).** [[infra.connect]] President Ndayishimiye met
  Allirajah to lay the groundwork for a "strategic digital partnership" — universal connectivity, digital
  integration of public services, and health-sector solutions — aligned to Vision 2040–2060. Source:
  [[2026-03-02-lyca-mobile-telecom-ehealth-burundi]].
