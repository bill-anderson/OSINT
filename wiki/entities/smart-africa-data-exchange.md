# Smart Africa Digital Xchange (SADX)

A **[[smart-africa]]** continental platform for **secure, trusted cross-border data sharing and digital-ID
verification** — built on **open standards**, scalable and interoperable, respecting trust/security/
sovereignty — toward the Single Digital Market. Builds on the **Smart Africa Trust Alliance (SATA)** (six
signatories).

- **Unveiled + piloted (Nov–Dec 2025).** Announced at Transform Africa Summit for **Benin, Ghana and
  Rwanda**; partnered **[[ascend-digital-solutions]]** to run the pilot. See [[XAF]]. Sources:
  [[2025-11-18-smart-africa-ai-dpi-initiatives-single-market]], [[2025-12-19-smart-africa-ascend-sadx-pilot]].
