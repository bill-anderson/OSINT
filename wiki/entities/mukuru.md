# Mukuru

A pan-African **remittance/payments fintech**, a channel for cross-border money transfers used by
Southern-African migrant workers. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Lesotho migrant remittances (2026-06).** [[finance.new]] [[gov.regional]] As South Africa's
  remittance/banking rules tightened, Lesotho migrant workers (enclaved within ZAF) shifted from banks to
  fintechs such as **Mukuru** for faster, more reliable cross-border transfers home — a cross-border
  dependence/sovereignty angle. See [[lesotho--dpi-pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-12-lesotho-migrants-fintech-remittances-techcabal]] *(paywalled — free lede only, full clip
  at promotion)*.
