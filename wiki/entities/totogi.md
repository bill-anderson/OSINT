# Totogi

US-based telecom-software vendor offering an AWS-hosted, public-cloud **Charging-as-a-Service** (SaaS)
platform. Profiled here for its role in **[[zain-sudan]]**'s 2024 connectivity restoration: per Totogi's
own case study, it migrated >20 million Zain Sudan subscribers to the public cloud and restored charging
services ~18 days after contract signing during the civil-war blackout. *(Vendor-sourced claims; capture is
not endorsement.)* See [[infra.connect]].
