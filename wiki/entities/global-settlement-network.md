# Global Settlement Network (GSN / GSX / GSH)

US-based blockchain-infrastructure group (Global Settlement Network / Global Settlement Exchange; investment
arm **Global Settlement Holdings**) betting on **Uganda as a hub for tokenised finance in Africa**. CEO:
**Ryan Kirkley**.

- **$5.5bn digital-infrastructure + CBDC pilot (Oct 2025).** Partnered **[[diacente-group]]** on a **$5.5bn**
  plan to link farms/mines/factories and pilot a bond-backed **CBDC "digital shilling"** (Karamoja Green
  SEZ), financed by **tokenised loans** from Korea/Middle East. Source: [[2025-10-08-uganda-5-5bn-digital-project-cbdc-shilling]].
- **AKIBA acquisition (Apr 2026).** Moving to a **majority stake in [[akiba-international]]** for the
  licences to run a broker-dealer off-ramp, a **regulated tokenised-asset exchange** and a mining platform —
  under the BoU sandbox with a licensed local settlement bank; up to **$1.5bn** planned. Source:
  [[2026-04-08-gsh-acquire-akiba-tokenised-capital-markets]].
