# Libyan Post, Telecommunications & Information Technology Company (LPTIC)

Libya's **state telecoms holding company** (chairman Ali Bin Gharbia) — parent of the mobile operators
(**[[almadar-aljadid|Almadar Aljadid]]**, Libyana) and the lead vehicle for national connectivity, subsea
capacity and 5G. GNU/Tripoli-side. See [[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Medusa subsea cable (2025-05).** [[geopol.eu]] LPTIC inaugurated the **8,700 km Medusa** system to
  Europe (Misrata), framing Libya as a "digital gateway to Africa" and citing digital sovereignty. See
  [[trans-saharan-fibre-backbone]], [[libya--infra-connect]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-12-medusa-submarine-cable-inaugurated-misrata-libyaherald]].
- **5G build & foreign vendors (2026).** [[finance.mou]] LPTIC launched Libya's first 5G via Almadar
  (Tripoli, Jan), signed an infrastructure/5G MoU with US firm **KBR** (Jan), and met **Nokia** (Finland)
  to accelerate 5G modernisation (Jul). Sources:
  [[2026-01-29-almadar-launches-first-5g-tripoli-libyaherald]],
  [[2026-01-25-lptic-kbr-mou-5g-infrastructure-libyaherald]], [[2026-07-16-libya-nokia-5g-cooperation-alwasat]].
