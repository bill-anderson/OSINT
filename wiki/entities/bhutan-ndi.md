# Bhutan National Digital Identity (Bhutan NDI)

Bhutan's state digital-identity company, exporting its national self-sovereign identity
architecture as an **open-source foundational framework** for other governments — a
South–South DPI-transfer actor.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Sierra Leone (2026-07).** Provides the foundational open-source identity framework
  and national digital-identity-architecture experience, with **Bhutan's NDI programme as
  the template** for Sierra Leone's build; contributes to institutional capacity building
  and knowledge transfer. See [[sierra-leone-digital-id-mou]], [[SLE]], [[dpi.id]],
  [[gov.standards]].
