# CONIA (National Consultations on Artificial Intelligence)

Cameroon's **national multi-stakeholder consultation process on AI**, convened by
[[ministry-of-posts-and-telecommunications-cameroon|MINPOSTEL]] — the venue in which the National AI
Strategy ([[snia-cameroon]]) was conceived and then unveiled.

## Profile

- **1st edition: 25–26 June 2024** — where Cameroon resolved to build an AI strategy (integrating AI
  "responsibly and effectively" across sectors). This is the SNIA's true origin point.
- **2nd edition: 7–8 July 2025, Yaoundé** — opened with a pitch for a **sovereign AI platform**, and
  the venue at which the SNIA was formally unveiled; close-out framed the challenge as *regulated* AI
  use. Sources: [[2025-06-26-leconomie-conia-2025-sovereign-ai-platform]],
  [[2025-07-07-leconomie-conia-2025-opens-yaounde]],
  [[2025-07-09-lejour-conia-2025-national-ai-strategy-consultations]].
