# John R. Musinguzi

**Commissioner General of the [[ura-uganda]]** (as of 2025-12) — the official who states,
most plainly of anyone in this batch, what digitalising payments is *for* in revenue
terms.

## Activity in this wiki

- **The visibility rationale (2025-12-23).** Launching enhanced URA tax payment services
  on **MTN MoMo** with [[mtn-uganda]], Musinguzi said: *"By shifting transactions from cash
  to digital platforms, economic activity becomes visible and traceable. Informal traders
  can be identified and gradually integrated into the tax net,"* adding that digital
  payments reduce leakages, lower enforcement costs and create reliable audit trails. *The
  source offers no counterpoint to this logic — it is recorded as stated.* It is also
  the logic the rights material in this wiki reads against (see [[iser-uganda]],
  [[nick-opiyo]]).
- **Performance and platform (2025-12-23).** Musinguzi reported URA collected **UGX 15.25
  trillion net in H1 FY2025/26 against a UGX 14.93tn target — 102 percent, a UGX 322bn
  surplus**. The upgraded platform supports both PRN and non-PRN tax services including
  high-volume transactions: *"Today's upgrade goes beyond what was previously possible. It
  translates into faster processing, greater reliability, and enhanced convenience for
  taxpayers."* Payment runs via the free USSD code ***165*18#** on any phone, with
  ***285#** retained. See [[ura-uganda]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-23 URA Launches Enhanced Tax Payment Services on MTN MoMo to Boost Digital Compliance]].
