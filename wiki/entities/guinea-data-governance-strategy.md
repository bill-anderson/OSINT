# Guinea National Data Governance Strategy

Guinea's **National Data Governance Strategy** — drafting launched **19 June 2026**, led by the **National
Directorate of Information Technology and the Digital Economy (DNTIEN)** (with UNECA/GIZ, aligned to the AU
Data Policy Framework). It will set rules for **collecting, sharing, securing and using** data from public
institutions, businesses and citizens; improve public-data quality and **interoperability**; create an
**AI-enabling environment**; and safeguard **national digital sovereignty**. Sits alongside Guinea's
**cybersecurity law** (which the HAC clarified governs web-media offences, 2025-07). See [[guinea--dpi-govtech]].
