# Onafriq–PAPSS Ghana cross-border payments launch

A June 2025 launch of live cross-border payments in Ghana by **[[onafriq]]** in partnership with **[[papss]]**, letting Ghanaian banks, fintechs, mobile-money operators and traditional institutions send and receive funds directly to mobile wallets and bank accounts across the region ([[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.exchange]]). The [[bank-of-ghana]] approved a **six-month pilot** (to evaluate volumes, FX outcomes and engagement), with **[[afreximbank]]** as settlement agent. Framed as operationalising the Onafriq–PAPSS partnership and advancing AfCFTA financial integration; it precedes the Nigeria→Ghana naira wallet rail later reported for Feb 2026 (see [[ghana--dpi-pay]]). See [[GHA]], [[gov.regional]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Service launched, BoG six-month pilot (2025-06-23).** Real-time transactions on PAPSS rails; SME/retail emphasis; addresses high fees and opaque FX while formalising informal flows. Onafriq spans 43 African countries, 1bn+ wallets, 500m accounts. Source: [[2025-06-23 Onafriq and PAPSS launch cross-border payments in Ghana]].
