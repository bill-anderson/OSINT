# UN-Habitat

**The United Nations human settlements programme.** In this wiki it appears through its
**Land, Housing and Shelter Section**, which houses the **[[gltn]]** Global Land Tool
Network — the route by which UN-Habitat works on land registration and tenure security in
African states.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Uganda digital land certificates (year-precision, proxy date — implementer's own
  account, attribute).** Through GLTN, UN-Habitat has partnered with **[[mlhud-uganda]]**
  since **2014** to support Uganda's 2013 National Land Policy: strengthening customary
  land rights, improving women's access to land, and developing the digital **Certificate
  of Customary Ownership** template on the **[[stdm]]** Social Tenure Domain Model —
  gazetted **18 September 2020**, with **over 7,684 parcels registered** for CCOs. See
  [[gltn]], [[stdm]], [[dpi.registry]], [[digital.rural]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Uganda Moves to Digital Certificates of Customary Ownership to secure land rights and improve land use]].
