# Open Government Partnership (OGP)

A multilateral initiative (launched 2011) through which member governments make and are held to
commitments on transparency, accountability, civic participation and open government, via biennial
national action plans. In this wiki OGP appears as the **governance frame proposed for Digital Public
Infrastructure** — the argument that DPI (identity, payments, data exchange) should be brought under
open-government principles of transparency, oversight and participatory design rather than run as an
opaque delivery project.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DPI-governance framing (as of 2026-02-11).** Analysis argues DPI governance is a blind spot in
  open-government reform and should be explicitly included in frameworks like OGP. Attributed analysis,
