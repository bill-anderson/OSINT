# Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA, Egypt)

Egypt's regulator of **non-banking financial services** (Chair **Mohamed Farid**). Driving digitisation of the sector — e.g. a **digital factoring portal** (with e-finance) allowing electronic verification of whether invoices have been previously funded, linked to the Ministry of Finance and the Egyptian Tax Authority to curb double-financing. The value of factored papers rose 77.8% in 2025 to EGP 132.2bn. Part of building an integrated digital infrastructure for non-banking finance.
