# Egypt Startup Charter

A unified government financing initiative (in preparation, 2025) valued at **EGP 50bn (~$1bn)**, targeting
**5,000 startups**, aiming to attract **$5bn over five years** and create **500,000 jobs**. Context: Egypt is
**North Africa's lead startup ecosystem (65th globally)**, raising **>$339m in H1 2025** and **$524m from 71
deals in H2** (Nawy $75m, Thndr $15.7m, MoneyFellows $13m); in Nov 2025 **GlobalFoundries acquired** Cairo
deep-tech semiconductor startup **InfiniLink** (a reported 400% return for Egypt Ventures). See
[[egypt--tech-ai]].
