# Qubit Hub (Kenya AI research/innovation lab)

An **African AI research, innovation and development lab** incubated by **Qhala** under **QTrust**,
bringing together researchers, technologists, data scientists, policymakers and builders working on
practical AI for the continent. See [[tech.ai]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **AI-infrastructure ecosystem launch (2026-05).** [[infra.store]] [[sovereignty]] Announced at AI
  Everything GITEX Kenya 2026, a collaboration to **host Qubit Hub at [[ixafrica-data-centres|iXAfrica]]
  NBOX1**: **Qhala** (innovation/research/ecosystem), **Amini AI** (sovereign compute + AI/data
  infrastructure), **[[angani]]** (local cloud for testing/deployment/scale) and **iXAfrica** (AI-ready,
  carrier-neutral DC). Positioned as a regional (not Kenya-only) foundation "solving for compute, talent
  and community." See [[kenya--infra-store]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-25-qhala-amini-angani-ixafrica-ai-hub-techafricanews]].
