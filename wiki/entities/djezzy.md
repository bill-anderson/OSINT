# Djezzy (Optimum Télécom Algérie)

State-controlled Algerian mobile operator. Shared the **$492m** 5G award (Dec 2025) and launched **5G across
18 states** by 2026-03. Deepened a **Qvantel** managed-services hub (2026-03), signed an **INATEL** industrial
partnership (2026-04), and unveiled a sovereign **AI cloud, AventureCloudz** (with Algeria Venture & Taubyte,
2026-04). See [[algeria--infra-connect]], [[algeria-5g]].
