# BOCRA (Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority)

Botswana's **communications regulator**, established under the Communications Regulatory Authority (CRA) Act 2012.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory SIM registration (2024).** BOCRA required SIM-card registration for all users, directing licensed operators to comply — the subscriber-identification (SIM-ID) layer of Botswana's identity/communications regulation. Source: [[2024-03-11 Botswana - Mandatory SIM Card Registration public notice (BOCRA)]].
- Named as sector regulator in the US ITA digital-economy guide (satellite openness, pending Digital Services and Cybersecurity bills). See the [[BWA]] hub.
- **Consumer pricing + SADC roaming (2026).** [[infra.connect]] BOCRA approved BTC **data-price cuts**
  (publishing a comparative operator-pricing table after a May-2025 cost study), expanded the **One Network
  Area** roaming framework to Malawi, Lesotho, Mozambique, Zambia and Zimbabwe (cuts **10–98.6%**, e.g.
  BWA–Zambia data **−94%**, free inbound SMS; building on BWA–Namibia Aug 2024), and kicked off **Mobile
  Number Portability** (Dec 2025). Sources: [[2026-02-18-btc-reduces-data-prices-following-regulator-approval]],
  [[2026-03-13-bocra-sadc-one-network-area-roaming-tariffs-techafricanews]],
  [[2025-12-16-btc-sets-sights-on-market-growth-mnp]].
- **Standards + global spectrum governance (2026).** [[gov.standards]] BOCRA ran an **IPv6 training**
  drive (with AFRINIC/Mascom/ICT Dynamix), hosted the **ITU/CRASA Southern-Africa broadband-mapping GIS
  training** (BWA/MWI/ZMB/ZWE), backed a national **e-waste strategy** (BOCRA–DEP MoU), and put forward
  **Basebi Mosinyi** for the **ITU Radio Regulations Board 2027–30** — Botswana seeking a seat in global
  spectrum governance. Sources: [[2026-05-21-bocra-ipv6-training-workshop-techreviewafrica]],
  [[2026-06-01-itu-africa-broadband-maps-gis-training-botswana-techreviewafrica]],
  [[2026-06-15-botswana-itu-radio-regulations-board-seat-bid-techreviewafrica]].
