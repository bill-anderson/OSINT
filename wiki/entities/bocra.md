# BOCRA (Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority)

Botswana's **communications regulator**, established under the Communications Regulatory Authority (CRA) Act 2012.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory SIM registration (2024).** BOCRA required SIM-card registration for all users, directing licensed operators to comply — the subscriber-identification (SIM-ID) layer of Botswana's identity/communications regulation. Source: [[2024-03-11 Botswana - Mandatory SIM Card Registration public notice (BOCRA)]].
- Named as sector regulator in the US ITA digital-economy guide (satellite openness, pending Digital Services and Cybersecurity bills). See the [[BWA]] hub.
