# Rwanda Utilities Regulatory Authority (RURA)

Rwanda's multi-sector regulator, including for telecommunications and ICT. RURA sets
the rules for SIM registration, spectrum and network competition. See [[RWA]],
[[infra.connect]], [[gov.legislate]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Biometric SIM-registration regime (2024).** RURA issued **Regulation No.
  18/R/SM-ICT/RURA/2024 of 09/08/2024**
  ([[rwanda-sim-card-registration-regulation-2024]]) requiring SIM registration to be
  verified against the **[[nida-rwanda]]** biometric database (fingerprint/face matched
  to the national ID, SIM linked to the holder's unique ID number), with KYC duties on
  operators — framed as anti-fraud (mobile-money scams) and national security. A
  **pilot** followed in Sept 2024. See [[dpi.id]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Sources:
  [[2024-09-17 Rwanda conducts pilot to implement biometric SIM registration, verification]],
  [[2024-08-09 Regulation Governing SIM Card Registration in Rwanda (RURA No.18-2024)]].
- **4G market liberalisation (2023).** RURA modified the **KTRN (Korea Telecom
  Rwanda Networks)** exclusive-wholesaler arrangement to let MTN and Airtel build their
  own 4G LTE networks, ending KT Corp's 4G infrastructure monopoly and cutting retail
  prices. Source:
  [[2024-09-30 Rwanda's digital transformation an effort at intergovernmental collaboration]].
