# Data Protection Office (Mauritius)

Mauritius's independent data-protection regulator, headed by **Data Protection Commissioner Drudeisha
Madhub**, enforcing the **Data Protection Act 2017** (GDPR-aligned). Among the most active DPAs in Africa:
per the ID4Africa 2026 AGM it issues roughly **3,000 enforcement actions a year (99% resolved amicably)**,
mandates **chief data officers** across controllers, and was amending the law to add **administrative fines**.

The DPO is a co-owner of the **[[mauritius-national-data-strategy|National Data Strategy 2025-2029]]** (launched
with [[ministry-ict-mauritius|MITCI]], 28 Jan 2026) and positions itself as a facilitator of transparency —
Madhub has announced a **Freedom of Information Bill** in preparation. It signed a data-protection/cybercrime
**MoU with the Mauritius Police Force and CERT-MU** (Jan 2026).
