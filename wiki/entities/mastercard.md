# Mastercard

Global US payments network expanding into African domestic and cross-border rails through switch and bank partnerships. In Ethiopia it signed a landmark partnership with the national switch **[[ethswitch]]** (Nov 2025), integrating EthSwitch into Mastercard's network via the Mastercard Interface Point (MIP) and Global Payment Gateway Services (MPGS) — enabling Ethiopian businesses to accept international payments and banks to issue co-branded international cards.
