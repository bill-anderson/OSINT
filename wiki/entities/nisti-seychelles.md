# NISTI (National Institute of Science, Technology and Innovation, Seychelles)

Statutory STI umbrella body, established as a **body corporate** by the **NISTI Act 2025** (Act 24 of
2025, assented 22 Dec 2025; commences on a Minister-appointed date). Coordinates STI research, with an
explicit mandate to promote **AI, emerging technologies and digitisation**, plus a **Science Academy**, an
**open-access digital scientific-data repository**, and innovation incubation (science parks, maker-
spaces). See [[seychelles--tech-ai]], [[SYC]].
