# Seamfix

Nigerian/UK digital-identity company (Group CEO **Chimezie Emewulu**) building
the **GovSmart** government-services platform and **BioSmart** biometric eKYC
product. UK-based operations give it access to global banking and payment
infrastructure for cross-border deployments across Africa. See [[dpi.id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Developer of Benin's ePass (revealed 2025-12).** Seamfix was named as
  developer of [[epass-benin]], the diaspora biometric passport-renewal app,
  built with Benin's Ministry of Foreign Affairs on **GovSmart** and integrated
  with [[BEN]]'s national ID and immigration systems (real-time verification,
  face biometrics + liveness). Launched Oct 2025, now in **80+ countries**;
  renewals ~15 minutes, 4-week delivery. Sources:
  [[2025-12-18 Seamfix revealed as developer of Benin's biometric passport renewal app]],
  [[2025-10-24 Benin unveils platform to ease biometric passport applications for diaspora citizens]],
  [[2026-02-02 Benin reports 75% of identification services accessed through digital platforms]].
- **MTN Côte d'Ivoire eKYC (2025).** MTN's Ivorian operation registered **8m SIM
  subscribers in 10 months** using Seamfix's BioSmart biometric KYC platform.
  See [[CIV]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-18 Seamfix revealed as developer of Benin's biometric passport renewal app]].
- **Funding (2024).** Raised **$4.5m** to expand its digital-identity services
  across additional African markets. Source:
  [[2025-12-18 Seamfix revealed as developer of Benin's biometric passport renewal app]].
