# CNTR (fiscal-transparency body)

Congo-Brazzaville's transparency body, building a **digital platform for online fiscal-transparency (ECTR)
assessment** covering 2023–25 under the Transparency Code — with focal points asking for laptops and
rechargeable routers, a marker of the base-level infrastructure gap. Source:
