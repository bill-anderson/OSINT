# Faso Arzêka

Burkina Faso's **government digital-payment platform**, run by the Ministry of Economy and Finance via the Directorate General of Treasury and Public Accounting (DGTCP) — a person-to-government (P2G) payments backbone.

## Activity in this wiki

- Launched (early 2025) to centralise government/financial services: a mobile app (iOS/Android), web, and a **USSD *700#** channel for the unconnected; payments via **Arzêka Money**, mobile money, bank cards and transfers. Services include **eTimbre** (electronic tax stamps), motor-vehicle and residence taxes, and customs duties — categorised by ministry. Aims: revenue collection, transparency, rural financial inclusion. See [[dpi.pay]] and the [[BFA]] hub. Source: [[2025-01-30 Burkina Faso launches Faso Arzeka digital payment platform]].
- **Treasury goes online — LANAYA + e-BDT (2026-04).** [[dpi.govtech]] The DGTCP launched **LANAYA**
  (treasury teleprocedures) and **e-BDT** (an online bank), framed as in-country-built "œuvres nationales"
  with World Bank backing. A **certified electronic invoice** became mandatory from **1 Jul 2026** (replacing
  the 2017 paper norm), and **e-Dimaakia** digitised official-travel procurement — extending state digital
  finance beyond Faso Arzêka. Sources: [[2026-04-03-lanaya-e-bdt-plateformes-tresor-public]],
  [[2026-04-06-tresor-public-lanaya-e-bdt-plateformes]], [[2026-01-06-facture-electronique-certifiee-lancee]],
