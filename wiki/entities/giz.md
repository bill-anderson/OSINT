# Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ)

Germany's federal development agency and a recurring DPI donor across Africa —
co-funding health-information systems ([[dhis2]]), Ghana's AI strategy via its
**FAIR Forward** programme, and [[microsoft]] Elevate's Ikamva Digital, alongside
direct e-government capacity-building. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[capacity.training]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Benin "Beninnovation" e-gov tender (Jan 2025).** GIZ runs Benin's
  **Beninnovation** public-service-digitalisation programme — a tender to stand
  up a modern **Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS)** development environment for public
  services and to build capacity among civil servants, ministry IT directorates
  and local private providers. The bid deadline was extended to **3 Feb 2025**.
  The notice cites Benin's biometric RNPP (10m), the [[smart-africa]] Digital ID
  Blueprint, its X-Road implementation ([[uxp-benin]]) and the [[wuri-programme]]
  fID certificate. See [[BEN]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-29 Deadline extended for Benin digital government transformation tender]].
- **DPI co-funder (context).** GIZ (the German Agency for International
  Cooperation) is cited as a dual-source funder — with the [[world-bank]] — of
  Benin's core DPI infrastructure, a pattern of donor involvement it repeats
  across the continent. See [[XAF]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-29 Deadline extended for Benin digital government transformation tender]].
- **Digital Investment Facility (DIF) implementer.** GIZ (with HAUS) implements the
  EU/Germany/Finland-funded **[[digital-investment-facility]]** under Global Gateway / the
  D4D Hub — de-risking revenue-generating **data-centre and IXP** projects across
  sub-Saharan Africa (minimum €5m, ESG-screened) and publishing the Xalam data-centre
  market briefs. See [[infra.store]], [[finance.new]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-01 Data Governance in Africa - Digital Investment Facility]].

- **EAC cross-border payment PoC (2026-07-10).** GIZ was among the partners (with the RWA/TZA
  central banks, EAC Secretariat and [[world-bank]]) at the Zanzibar technical meeting on a
  proof-of-concept interlinking Tanzania's **[[tips-tanzania]]** and Rwanda's
  **[[rswitch-rwanda]]** under the [[eac-cross-border-payment-masterplan]]. See [[RWA]],
  [[TZA]], [[XEA]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-10 New Rwanda-Tanzania Payment Pilot could transform trade across East Africa]].

- **São Tomé AUDPF adoption workshop (2026-07).** As "GIZ African Union," an implementing
  partner (with **[[auda-nepad]]** and **[[kictanet]]**) of the **[[d4d-hub]]** #D4DataGOV
  Data Governance Capacity Building Workshop helping **[[STP]]** adopt the
  **[[au-data-policy-framework]]** nationally. See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 D4D Hub on Sao Tome and Principe]].
