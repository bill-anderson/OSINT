# CSquared

Pan-African **wholesale digital-infrastructure** company (originally Google-incubated) that builds and
operates **open-access metro fibre and terrestrial backbone**, plus subsea capacity, across West, Central
and East Africa — selling wholesale to operators and ISPs rather than to end users.

## Activity in this wiki

- **2Africa West integration for resilience (2026-07-15).** CSquared added capacity on the
  **[[2africa-cable|2Africa West]]** submarine system to its own West Africa subsea + terrestrial network,
  adding an **independent international route** and **automated rerouting** to reduce the region's exposure to
  single-cable outages (three West Africa multi-country disruptions in 28 months). Its backbone also carries
  **[[equiano-cable|Equiano]]** capacity inland across Burkina Faso, Mali, Guinea, Sierra Leone and Liberia.
  CTO Samuel Owusu Yeboah. See [[infra.connect]], [[XWA]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-15 CSquared boosts internet by integrating with 2Africa West cable]].
