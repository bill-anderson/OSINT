# Global Land Tool Network (GLTN)

**A land-tools network housed within the Land, Housing and Shelter Section of
[[un-habitat]]** — developer of the **[[stdm]]** Social Tenure Domain Model and a
long-running partner of Uganda's land ministry on customary tenure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Uganda customary land partnership since 2014 (year-precision, proxy date —
  implementer's own account, attribute).** GLTN has partnered with **[[mlhud-uganda]]** to
  support implementation of Uganda's **2013 National Land Policy** — strengthening
  customary land rights, improving women's access to land, developing and implementing the
  national gender strategy for the NLP, and building partner capacity on **pro-poor,
  fit-for-purpose and gender-responsive land tools**. **Over 7,684 parcels were registered
  for Certificates of Customary Ownership** through these efforts. GLTN developed the
  digital CCO template on **[[stdm]]**, approved by MLHUD and **gazetted 18 September
  2020**. Context: approximately **80% of Uganda's land is held under customary tenure**,
  registered under the **Land Act 1998**. *The GLTN blog is undated; the 2024 year rests
  on an intake assertion — do not reason about it as a 2024 datapoint.* See
  [[dpi.registry]], [[digital.rural]], [[include.divides]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Uganda Moves to Digital Certificates of Customary Ownership to secure land rights and improve land use]].
