# Microsoft Elevate

[[microsoft]]'s AI-skilling initiative. In South Africa ([[ZAF]]) it is
positioned as the national **"connective tissue"** linking government, industry,
educators and communities around AI literacy, aiming to turn AI skills from a
privilege into a public good.

## Activity in this wiki

- **South Africa positioning (2026-03-27).** In a profile interview, National AI
  Skills Director [[tiara-pathon]] framed Elevate as the coordinating layer
  addressing three gaps — accessibility, relevance and scalability — in South
  Africa's AI-skills ecosystem, emphasising localisation to South African
  contexts and mobile-first delivery. See [[tech.ai]], [[capacity.training]],
  [[capacity.literacy]].
  Source: [[2026-03-27 Tiara Pathon on Turning AI Literacy From a Privilege Into a Public Good]].
- **Delivery partners.** Named delivery partners include **SABC+** (mobile-phone
  delivery), **Civic AI** (upskilling non-profits) and **Ikamva Digital**
  (launched with GIZ), reflecting a community-partnership model.
  Source: [[2026-03-27 Tiara Pathon on Turning AI Literacy From a Privilege Into a Public Good]].
- Connects to Microsoft's broader South Africa skills commitments — see
  [[microsoft-sa-2026-expansion]].
