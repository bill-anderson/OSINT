# Digital for Development (D4D) Hub

A **Team Europe** multi-stakeholder platform coordinating EU and European member-state
support for digital transformation in partner regions, aligned to the **Global Gateway**
strategy. In Africa it runs the **Data Governance in Africa (#D4DataGOV)** initiative —
supported by the **[[african-union]]**, the **[[european-union|European Commission]]**,
Germany, France, Belgium, Estonia and Finland — to help states operationalise continental
data-governance norms.

## Activity in this wiki

- **São Tomé and Príncipe data-governance workshop (2026-07).** Under #D4DataGOV, the D4D Hub
  ran a **Data Governance Capacity Building Workshop** (9–10 Jul 2026) helping **[[STP]] São
  Tomé and Príncipe** explore national adoption of the **[[au-data-policy-framework]]** (AUDPF),
  with implementing partners **[[giz]]**, **[[auda-nepad]]** and **[[kictanet]]**. See
  [[gov.protect]], [[gov.policy]], [[gov.regional]], [[capacity.training]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 D4D Hub on Sao Tome and Principe]].
