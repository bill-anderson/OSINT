# Ministry of Education and Training, Eswatini

**Eswatini's education ministry** ([[SWZ]]), publisher of the *National General
Education Progress Report* and the sectoral owner of education management information
and school connectivity. See [[dpi.mis]], [[capacity.training]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Reports on general education.** The Ministry published *The National General
  Education Progress Report 2024* (held at year precision), the wiki's sector baseline
  for Eswatini education data. See [[data.statistics]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Eswatini National General Education Progress Report 2024]].
- **School connectivity is a named gap.** The [[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]]
  found poor internet connectivity **especially in schools** to be a critical
  limitation **(2024)**, and the [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]] frames education as
  one of the sectors an interoperable DPI layer should integrate. Ministry-specific
  reporting on either is thin in the wiki. See [[infra.connect]], [[dpi.exchange]].
  Source:
  [[2024-12-05 Eswatini Advances Toward Digital Transformation - Key Findings from the Digital Readiness Assessment]].
