# DGI (Direction Générale des Impôts)

Benin's **tax authority**, operator of the **e-services.impots.bj** online tax portal (first launched 2017) — part of Benin's bid to be West Africa's digital hub.

## Activity in this wiki

- **New online tax services (2024).** In May 2024 the DGI (DG Nicolas Yenoussi) added online services for small businesses and property owners in Cotonou (nationwide expansion planned), building on the 2017 portal. Reported that gradual digitisation contributed to a **13.1% rise in tax revenue over 2017–2021** (vs 5.7% in 2012–2016). See [[dpi.govtech]] and the [[BEN]] hub. Source: [[2024-05-27 Benin Launches New Online Tax Services for Businesses and Property Owners]].
