# United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO)

The UN specialised agency for inclusive and sustainable industrial development (French:
**ONUDI**). Relevant to this wiki chiefly where industrial policy meets the digital
economy — electronics manufacturing and repair capacity, e-waste and circular-economy
programmes, and the local industrial base underneath device affordability.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Egypt electronics-refurbishment unit (reported 2026-07-17).** A partnership with
  **[[orange]]** established a **local unit dedicated to refurbishing electronic equipment**
  in Egypt, part of Orange Middle East & Africa's circular-economy programme. See [[EGY]],
  [[tech.industry]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-orange-reconditionne-164000-box-internet]].
