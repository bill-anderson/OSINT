# Egypt National AI Strategy (2nd edition, 2025)

Egypt's **second National AI Strategy (2025)** — six pillars (governance, technology, data, infrastructure,
ecosystem, skills) — the latest step in a timeline running **National Council for AI (2019) → 1st AI Strategy
(2021) → Responsible AI Charter (2023) → 2nd Strategy (2025)**. The council was **renamed to cover AI, quantum
computing and emerging technologies**. Framed by the (then) minister to the Senate as core to **digital
sovereignty and national security**. Delivery assets: the locally-contextual **"Karnak" Arabic LLM** and the
**Egyptian Center for Responsible AI (ECRAI)** (late 2025). Egypt ranks **1st in Africa / 51st globally** on the
Government AI-Readiness Index and scored **100% on the ITU cybersecurity index**; the government estimates AI
could add up to **$42.7bn** (7.7% of GDP by 2030). See [[egypt--tech-ai]].
