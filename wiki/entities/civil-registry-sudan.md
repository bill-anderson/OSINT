# Civil Registry (Sudan)

Sudan's civil registration body (السجل المدني), under the Ministry of Interior —
the authoritative source of foundational identity records (passport / national
number) that anchor the country's digital-ID build.

## Activity in this wiki

- **SUDAPASS identity base (2026-05).** Partnered (via the Interior Ministry) in
  the launch of the **[[sudapass]]** national digital ID, which is activated with
  passport/national-number details plus face biometrics — making the Civil
  Registry the identity-data foundation for the platform, alongside the
  **[[sudanese-cybersecurity-authority]]** and the
  **[[national-electronic-certification-authority-sudan]]**. See [[dpi.id]],
  [[dpi.registry]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-21 Sudan launches national digital identity SUDAPASS (SUNA)]].
