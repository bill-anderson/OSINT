# Somali National ID (SNID)

Somalia's foundational national identity credential, issued by [[nira-somalia]] since
September 2023 — the first national ID in ~30 years. Each citizen or legal resident
receives a unique, lifetime **11-digit National Identification Number (NIN)**, known in
Somali as **Tirsi Aqoonsi**, underpinned by biometrics (fingerprints via a 4-4-2 scan
and facial recognition; iris planned). The credential is issued in **three formats**: a
physical card, a digital mobile ID (see [[eaqoonsi]]), and a downloadable/printable
verifiable credential (QR-coded paper certificate).

## Features (NIRA)

- Unique lifetime number; biographic data per Article 8 of the ID Law (full name incl.
  father's/grandfather's and mother's names, DOB, place of birth, sex, legal status,
  residence, contact).
- Biometric deduplication before issuance; processing stated to follow Somalia's
  [[somali-data-protection-act]].
- Machine-readable with QR code; real-time online verification/authentication (via the
  HUBIYE platform) for e-government, banking and other services.
- Centralised government database; **10-year validity** with periodic biometric updates;
  tamper-proof design.

## Use cases

Government services and social protection; civil registration; tax; banking and financial
inclusion (account opening, loans, remittances); **SIM registration**; education;
immigration and travel (mandatory for **passport applications from 1 Sep 2025**, and for
**domestic travel from 1 Jan 2026**); healthcare; legal/notary; employment. The Central
Bank of Somalia mandated the SNID for opening/updating bank accounts from 29 Jul 2025.

## Scale and targets

~85% of Somalis (pop. ~19.7m) lacked any legal ID; fewer than 16% held a government ID
(2022 SIHBS). Target: **15 million IDs by end-2029** (NIRA's registration portal states
"over 15 million by 2030" — a variant). By late 2025, **>1 million enrolled** with **>24
public services integrated**. Built with World Bank finance and Pakistan's **NADRA** as
technology partner — the foreign-build/data-residency question is a live sovereignty
debate (see [[somalia--dpi-id]]).

## Related components

- **HUBIYE** — NIRA's real-time identity verification/authentication platform.
- [[eaqoonsi]] — the digital ID mobile app / wallet.
- **Certificate Delivery System (CDS)** — credential delivery.
- **NIMS** (National Identity Management System) — foundational system rolled out 2024;
  **CRMVS** civil-registration system handed over Nov 2024 (both built by NADRA).
