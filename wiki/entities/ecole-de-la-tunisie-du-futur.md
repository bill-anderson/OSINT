# L'école de la Tunisie du futur (tarbia.tn)

**Tunisia's national education platform — "The School of Future Tunisia" — launched by the
[[ministry-of-education-tunisia]] on 3 January 2024 at tarbia.tn, a comprehensive digital platform
for students, parents and teachers.** Its stated purpose pairs administrative transparency with
service delivery: modernising education while making the system legible to parents.

**Not Madrassati.** **Madrassati** is a separate education portal, listed among the government
services reachable through [[e-houwiya]]'s single sign-on. No source held connects it to tarbia.tn;
do not conflate the two.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (3 January 2024, reported 2024-01-08).** The Ministry of Education launched the platform
  with **dedicated portals for each of students, parents and teachers** — streamlining
  administrative processes, facilitating distance learning and offering real-time student
  monitoring. **Parents** can track attendance, assignments and progress reports; **educators** can
  manage classes and engage with students remotely. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.mis]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-08 Tunisia Unveils Digital Platform to Boost Education Transparency, Equity]].
- **Stated purpose (2024-01).** Per Minister **Mohamed Ali Boughdiri**, with the digital transition
  crucial to improving education quality, the platform promotes transparency and efficiency and
  streamlines essential services — **school meals, scholarships and guidance**. Source:
  [[2024-01-08 Tunisia Unveils Digital Platform to Boost Education Transparency, Equity]].
- **Sits inside a connectivity strategy — whose target is unverified.** The initiative dovetails
  with the ministry's broader strategy of integrating technology into schools and closing the
  digital divide. The **"Modern School on Every Hill"** project **aimed to connect 3,300 schools to
  broadband internet by the end of 2024**, bridging the rural connectivity gap. That is the target
  as stated in **January 2024**; **no source held confirms the outcome**. A platform for parents
  who can reach it presupposes the schools that can. See [[include.divides]], [[digital.rural]],
  [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-08 Tunisia Unveils Digital Platform to Boost Education Transparency, Equity]].
