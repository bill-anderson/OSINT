# Orange Côte d'Ivoire

The Côte d'Ivoire operating subsidiary of [[orange]], the French telecoms group. It runs a
portfolio of fibre and mobile broadband services and, from 2026, satellite connectivity aimed at
closing the country's urban–rural coverage gap.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched Orange Sat satellite internet (2026-01, with [[eutelsat]]).** Orange Côte d'Ivoire
  unveiled [[orange-sat]], a satellite-broadband service using Eutelsat's KONNECT satellite,
  targeting rural "white areas" beyond the reach of 4G and fibre. The operator frames it as a
  digital-inclusion move complementing its existing fibre and mobile broadband, responding to a
  landscape where urban hubs (Abidjan, Bouaké, San-Pedro) have near-universal 4G and early 5G
  pilots while rural provinces still rely mainly on 3G. Tags [[infra.connect]], [[digital.rural]].
