# Morocco FinTech Center / Offre Startup Maroc

Morocco's flagship **fintech-ecosystem initiatives** — the **Morocco FinTech Center** and **"Offre
Startup Maroc"** — providing structured support for fintech entrepreneurs (ideation to scale), to reduce
financial exclusion (**~44% of adults**) and position Morocco as a regional fintech leader. Championed by
MTNRA Minister **Seghrouchni** on a government–private–academia model. See [[dpi.pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Visa Fintech Day — inclusion focus (2025-06).** [[include.access]] The 2nd **Visa FinTech Day**
  ("AI and Financial Inclusion") launched around the FinTech Center + Offre Startup Maroc, with
  **[[visa]]**, the US Consul General and academia — ML in credit scoring, fraud prevention and
  personalised services to widen access. Source:
  [[2025-06-10-techreviewafrica-morocco-fintech-ecosystem-ai-inclusion]].
- **Visa Fintech Day — AI in payments (2026-06).** [[tech.ai]] The 3rd edition ("Reshaping Payments in
  the Era of AI", Rabat) framed AI's role in fraud detection, risk assessment and process automation,
  tied to Digital Morocco 2030 and "AI Made in Morocco" (sovereign infra + data protection). Source:
  [[2026-06-04-techreviewafrica-morocco-visa-fintech-day-ai-payments]].
