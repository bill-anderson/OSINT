# Herotel

One of South Africa's largest fixed/wireless ISPs — **350,000+ customers across 550+ towns**. CEO: **Van Zyl
Botha**.

- **Amazon Leo distributor (Jul 2026).** Chosen as the local partner for **[[amazon-leo]]** (ex-Project
  Kuiper), which launches in SA in **2027** through a new service **"evry"** — Amazon supplies the satellite
  network, Herotel does installs/support/sales. The model by which a foreign satellite operator **enters via
  a licensed local partner rather than seeking its own licence** — the workaround [[starlink|Starlink]]
  refused. See [[south-africa--infra-connect]]. Source: [[2026-07-16-why-amazon-partnered-where-starlink-pushed-back]].
