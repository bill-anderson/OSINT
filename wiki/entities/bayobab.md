# Bayobab

MTN Group's **pan-African digital-connectivity / fibre-infrastructure** arm (formerly MTN GlobalConnect),
building terrestrial and subsea capacity across the continent. See [[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Uganda–Kenya fibre route (2025-05).** [[gov.regional]] Bayobab completed a **260km Kampala–Tororo**
  route along the Uganda Railway corridor, interconnecting to Kenya's **National Long Distance (NLD)**
  fibre (Mombasa–Malaba) — giving Uganda **>1 Tbit/s** low-latency access to Mombasa subsea landings and
  regional data centres. See [[kenya--infra-connect]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-08-bayobab-uganda-kenya-fiber-connectingafrica]].
