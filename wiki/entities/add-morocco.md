# ADD (Agence de Développement du Digital, Morocco)

Morocco's **Digital Development Agency**, the executing agency for Digital Morocco 2030 — driving
e-government, cloud-first standards and the national digital-ID platform ([[digital-morocco-2030]],
[[dpi.govtech]]). It contracts the [[mon-e-id]] digital-identity build (with the DGSN) and, continentally,
**chairs Smart Africa's Council of African IT Agencies (CAITA)** — giving Morocco a lead role in
African data-exchange standardisation. DG: Amine El Mezouaghi. See [[MAR]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cloud-first + digital-ID execution.** [[infra.store]] Runs the cloud excellence centre and the
  Mon e-ID contract (with DGSN, IDEMIA/IN Group). Sources:
  [[2025-01-01 Morocco adopts Cloud First Policy under Digital Morocco 2030]],
  [[2025-09-24 Morocco fast-tracks 'Mon e-ID' digital identity after cyberattacks (iDAKTO)]].
- **Chairs CAITA — Africa's first Cross-Border Data Exchange Guidelines (2025-07).** [[gov.regional]]
  ADD (chairing [[smart-africa]]'s CAITA) hosted 11 states in Rabat to draft the guidelines under SATA.
  Source: [[2025-07-14 Smart Africa co-creates Africa's first Cross-Border Data Exchange Guidelines (Rabat, ADD-chaired CAITA)]].
