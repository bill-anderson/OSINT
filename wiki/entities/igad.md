# Intergovernmental Authority on Development (IGAD)

East African regional bloc — co-implementer (with the [[eac]] and [[world-bank]]) of the **[[eardip|Eastern
Africa Regional Digital Integration Project]]**, advancing cross-border payment-system interoperability and
broadband integration across nine Eastern African states. See [[XAF]].
