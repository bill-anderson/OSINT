# Mediabox

A **Burundian software company** — a rare local systems integrator building government digital platforms.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Land-administration digital ecosystem (2025).** Developed the **EDRMS** (Electronic Document and Records Management System) for the Directorate of Land Titles and National Cadastre (DTFCN), World Bank-funded under **[[pafen-burundi]]**, integrated with the Building Permit System (BPS) and Property Management System (PMS) — secure archiving, traceability and faster title processing. See [[BDI]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2025-06-18 Burundi's Land Services Go Digital with New Integrated Platform]].
