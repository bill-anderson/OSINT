# Kepios

Analytics advisory founded by **Simon Kemp** that compiles the global and per-country digital-adoption
data published as the **[[datareportal]]** "Digital {year}" report series (with We Are Social and
Meltwater). In this wiki Kepios appears as the analysis house behind DataReportal figures; the reports
themselves are the citable [[datareportal]] resource.
