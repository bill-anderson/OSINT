# Uganda Digital Government Strategy, Version 1.1

**Uganda's digital government strategy**, version 1.1, dated **26 February 2022**, issued
by the Government of Uganda (MoICT&NG / [[nita-uganda]]). It was issued as a **formal
addendum to the Government Enterprise Architecture (GEA) and [[uganda-e-gif]] consultancy
engagement** — not as a free-standing strategy — with project duration printed as
**10 Nov 2020 – 8 Feb 2022**.

## Anchoring

The strategy is anchored to **Digital Uganda Vision**, **NDPIII**, the **National
Information Security Framework (NISF)**, and the **EU eIDAS** regulation as a reference
regime. Source: [[2022-02-26 Uganda Digital Government Strategy v1.1]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Companion to the e-GIF.** The strategy and the [[uganda-e-gif]] (**6 May 2021**) are
  the paired outputs of the same GEA engagement; the e-GIF carries the twelve principles
  and six interoperability layers, this document the strategic frame. Both are now the
  subject of a NITA-U renewal process with the [[e-governance-academy]] (see
  [[uganda-e-gif]]). See [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]], [[geopol.eu]]. Sources: as
  above and [[2021-05-06 Uganda e-Government Interoperability Framework (e-GIF)]].
