# BANGE (Banco Nacional de Guinea Ecuatorial)

Equatorial Guinea's **national bank** — a lead actor in the cash-lite drive. It launched **BANGE MOBILE GE**
(free iOS/Android, 2026-02) for balances, transfers, payments, card-less cash withdrawal and bill payment
(trial cap 2m FCFA/month), designed to **integrate with regional systems via the G-MAT Pay CEMAC gateway** and
**Visa prepaid** cards — framed as financial inclusion for the unbanked. See [[gnq--dpi-pay]].
