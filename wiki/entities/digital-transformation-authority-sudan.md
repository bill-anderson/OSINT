# Digital Transformation Authority (Sudan)

National agency established by PM **Kamil Idris**'s decree of **5 November 2025**, one of three
bodies created under the **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** (with the
**[[sudanese-cybersecurity-authority]]** and **[[sudanese-data-and-ai-authority]]**). Tasked with
building a unified digital system and regulating public digital services to improve administrative
governance and "digital sovereignty." Stood up amid conflict and limited resources. See
[[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]].
