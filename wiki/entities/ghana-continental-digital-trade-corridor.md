# Continental Digital Trade Corridor (Ghana-led)

A **Ghana-championed AfCFTA digital-trade pilot** — with **Rwanda and Zambia** — to cut delays and the high
cost of intra-African transactions. Announced by Vice-President **Naana Jane Opoku-Agyemang** at the **3i Africa
Summit 2026** (hosted by the Bank of Ghana), it links **mobile-money interoperability, cross-border digital-ID
KYC and e-invoicing** into a shared corridor — positioning Ghana as a hub for continental digital-financial
integration. See [[ghana--dpi-pay]].
