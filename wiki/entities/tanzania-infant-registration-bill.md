# Infant Registration Bill, Tanzania

A **proposed statute** that would allow **[[nida-tanzania]]** to **register children from
birth** — the enabling legislation for the birth-to-ID design of **[[jamii-namba]]**. If passed,
children would be registered and **assigned identification numbers** to access social services,
and issued national ID cards **using the same numbers upon turning 18**. **Status as of
2026-01-04: not passed** — "preparing to be tabled in the next Parliament."

**Headline/substance caution.** The source reporting this is headlined *"Tanzania rolls out
infant registration"*, but its body says only that the government **is preparing to table** the
Bill. **The body governs**: as of January 2026 the enabling legislation was still pending, and
nothing had been "rolled out" in the statutory sense.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Still pending (2026-01-04, Kibaha).** Speaking at the **28th session of the NIDA Staff
  Council**, NIDA Director General **[[james-kaji]]** said the government is **preparing to table
  the Infant Registration Bill in the next Parliament**. He argued that registering infants would
  ensure children are **legally recognised from the start of their lives** and eliminate the
  challenges that arise when registering adults, easing access to healthcare, education,
  insurance and other social services while providing accurate population data for government
  planning. Permanent Secretary **Ally Gugu** (**[[ministry-of-home-affairs-tanzania]]**) also
  addressed the session. See [[gov.legislate]], [[dpi.id]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-04 Tanzania rolls out infant registration for stronger citizen ID]].
- **The pilot preceded the Bill.** An operational **[[unicef]]**-backed infant-registration pilot
  was slated for **July 2025** — roughly **six months before** this Bill was still awaiting
  tabling in January 2026. NIDA was candid about the sequencing at the time:
  **[[edson-guyai]]** said the authority was **"reviewing our legal frameworks to align with
  these new goals"** (2025-06). The two accounts are internally consistent — this is a finding
  about capability preceding statutory basis, **not** a contradiction between sources. See
  [[jamii-namba]]. Sources:
  [[2025-06-17 Tanzania launching infant biometric registration pilot with UNICEF support]];
  [[2026-01-04 Tanzania rolls out infant registration for stronger citizen ID]].
- **Budgetary anticipation (2025-05-27).** The FY2025/26 Home Affairs budget already named the
  **commencement of registration and identification of under-18s**, to be issued national
  identification numbers, as a critical new initiative — money moving ahead of the statute too.
  Source: [[2025-05-27 Home Affairs unveils 2.06tri- budget]].
