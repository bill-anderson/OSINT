# CNIA (National Commission for Artificial Intelligence, Mozambique)

Mozambique's **national AI advisory body** — created by a Council-of-Ministers decree (in force April
2026), Maputo-headquartered, subordinate to the Council of Ministers. See [[tech.ai]],
[[mozambique--tech-ai]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Begins advising government (2026-05).** [[gov.policy]] [[sovereignty]] The CNIA started advising on the
  **responsible/ethical use of AI** — ethics, legal, security and social issues; the regulatory framework;
  education/research; and AI's impact on the **labour market** — issuing opinions on AI policies, standards,
  research institutions and international cooperation. Created alongside **[[atdi-mozambique|ATDI]]** and the
  CTD in the digital-transformation institutional build-out. Source:
  [[2026-05-06-cnia-begins-advising-government-on-ai-clubofmozambique]].
