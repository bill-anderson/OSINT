# Registrar General's Office (Seychelles)

**Seychelles' business and intellectual-property registry** — the office holding Business
Registration and Intellectual Property among its departments, and one of the two registry bodies
in this wiki's Seychelles material (the other being the
[[department-of-immigration-and-civil-status-seychelles]], which holds the civil register). Led
by Registrar General **Wendy Pierre** (as at 2025-02). See [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.govtech]],
[[SYC]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Presidential visit (2025-02-07).** President **[[wavel-ramkalawan]]** visited the Registrar
  General's Office, touring key departments including **Business Registration** and
  **Intellectual Property**, and met senior management to discuss institutional achievements,
  ongoing challenges and modernisation efforts. Speaking alongside Registrar General **Wendy
  Pierre**, he **praised the office's progress in digitalization** and announced **upcoming
  legislative reforms** to support the transition to modernised operations. No detail of those
  reforms — scope, instrument or timetable — is held here. See [[gov.policy]],
  [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-02-10 Seychelles Pledges to Modernize Public Services Through Digital Transformation]].
- **What is not held.** The reporting is at the level of political commitment: no source here
  records which registry services are actually online, their uptake, or whether the announced
  legislative reforms were tabled. Treat the office's digitalisation as **asserted as of
  2025-02**, not measured.
