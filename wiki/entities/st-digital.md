# ST Digital

**Cameroon-headquartered regional data-centre and cloud operator** (Tier III facilities, sovereign cloud, AI infrastructure), founded **2017** by **[[anthony-same]]**, expanding across Central and West Africa — a rare African-owned sovereign-cloud player (500+ clients; company-reported three certified datacenters across CMR/GAB/COG/CIV — certification unspecified, not hardened here). (Its Gabon arm is also profiled at [[st-digital-gabon]].)

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cameroon: SNIA / sovereign cloud (2025).** ST Digital is the government's **sovereign-cloud/infrastructure partner** for the **National AI Strategy ([[snia-cameroon]])** — *not* the strategy's author (that is [[cabinet-danielle-moukouri]]) — a role the wiki previously conflated. A 14 Jul 2025 ministerial visit to its **Douala** Tier-3 datacentre is the event behind the "taps ST Digital" line; the same visit carried an ST Digital × ANINF **Gabon** MoU (9 Jul 2025). Sources: [[2025-07-16 Cameroon taps ST Digital to lead National AI Strategy (SNIA)]], [[2025-07-17-leconomie-minpostel-visits-st-digital-datacenter]].
- **The African-owned counterweight.** In the CMR data-centre picture — otherwise the state (CENADI, CAMTEL/[[zamengoe-data-centre]]) or foreign hyperscalers (the CAMTEL–AWS talks) — ST Digital is the private, pan-African "keep African data on the continent" pole. See [[cameroon--infra-store]]. Source: [[2026-05-13-anthony-same-st-digital-african-datacenter-network-wearetech]].
- **Côte d'Ivoire: Grand-Bassam DC (2025).** Inaugurated an eco-responsible **Tier III** data centre at VITIB II, Grand-Bassam (Oct 2025; construction begun Nov 2024) — its first CIV facility, joining the Abidjan/Grand-Bassam cluster ([[raxio-group]], PAIX, MDXi). See [[civ--dpi-govtech]] context on the [[CIV]] hub. Source: [[2025-10-03 ST Digital opens eco-responsible Tier III data centre in Cote d'Ivoire (Grand-Bassam)]].
- **DR Congo (2024).** Entered the DRC by partnering **[[oadc-texaf-digital]]** (Kinshasa) for content delivery/cloud (Sep 2024). Source: [[2024-09-17 ST Digital expands into DRC, partners with OADC Texaf Kinshasa (press release)]].
- Also active in **Gabon** ([[st-digital-gabon]]) and **Togo** (Lomé cloud).
