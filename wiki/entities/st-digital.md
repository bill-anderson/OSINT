# ST Digital

**Cameroon-headquartered regional data-centre and cloud operator** (Tier III facilities, sovereign cloud, AI infrastructure), expanding across Central and West Africa — a rare African-owned sovereign-cloud player. (Its Gabon arm is also profiled at [[st-digital-gabon]].)

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cameroon: SNIA / sovereign cloud (2025).** The government tapped ST Digital to help roll out its **National AI Strategy ([[snia-cameroon]]**) and boost digital sovereignty, after a July 2025 ministerial visit to its **Douala** Tier 3 datacentre (sovereign cloud + AI equipment). Source: [[2025-07-16 Cameroon taps ST Digital to lead National AI Strategy (SNIA)]].
- **Côte d'Ivoire: Grand-Bassam DC (2025).** Inaugurated an eco-responsible **Tier III** data centre at VITIB II, Grand-Bassam (Oct 2025; construction begun Nov 2024) — its first CIV facility, joining the Abidjan/Grand-Bassam cluster ([[raxio-group]], PAIX, MDXi). See [[civ--dpi-govtech]] context on the [[CIV]] hub. Source: [[2025-10-03 ST Digital opens eco-responsible Tier III data centre in Cote d'Ivoire (Grand-Bassam)]].
- **DR Congo (2024).** Entered the DRC by partnering **[[oadc-texaf-digital]]** (Kinshasa) for content delivery/cloud (Sep 2024). Source: [[2024-09-17 ST Digital expands into DRC, partners with OADC Texaf Kinshasa (press release)]].
- Also active in **Gabon** ([[st-digital-gabon]]) and **Togo** (Lomé cloud).
