# Seychelles–AfCFTA–Quest Ghana digital-trade-hub MoU

A memorandum of understanding signed **2026-03-05** in Victoria — during a visit by AfCFTA
Secretary-General **[[wamkele-mene]]** — between the **[[afcfta]]** Secretariat, **[[quest-ghana]]
Ltd** and the **Government of Seychelles**, committing the three parties to develop Seychelles
as a **digital trade hub** within the continental bloc, with a focus on integrating
Seychellois MSMEs into African digital value chains.

## Profile

- The MoU was the precursor to the **[[afcfta-digital-trade-corridor]]**: the Seychelles
  Cabinet's **2026-07-08** decision to host the corridor's headquarters in Victoria turned
  the MoU framework into a permanent institutional seat.
- Follow-up work (legal, technical, staffing arrangements) is to be led by Seychelles'
  Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Diaspora with the AfCFTA Secretariat and Quest Ghana.
