# Algeria Digital Identity & Trust Services Law (2025 draft)

Draft law on **trust services for electronic transactions and digital identification**, approved by Algeria's **Council of Ministers on 2 November 2025** (President Tebboune presiding) and taken to the **National People's Assembly (APN)** by Dec 2025. It modernises the **2015 electronic-signature framework**, granting electronic documents, signatures, seals and timestamps legal validity equal to physical counterparts, and formally recognising digital signatures, electronic seals, secure time-stamping and web-authentication services.

Its core is a **national digital identification system linked to the existing [[algeria-biometric-id-card|biometric national ID card]]**, consolidating citizens' biometric data into a unified digital-identity platform. **Trust-service providers must be certified** (the certification base being **[[arpce-algeria|ARPCE]]** as national e-certification authority + AGCE). Framed as a pillar of **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**. Still progressing through the legislative process (draft as of late 2025). See [[algeria--dpi-id]].
