# Algeria Digital Identity & Trust Services Law (2025 draft)

Draft law on **trust services for electronic transactions and digital identification**, approved by Algeria's **Council of Ministers on 2 November 2025** (President Tebboune presiding). It modernises the **2015 electronic-signature framework**, granting electronic documents, signatures, seals and timestamps legal validity equal to physical counterparts, and formally recognising digital signatures, electronic seals, secure time-stamping and web-authentication services.

Its core is a **national digital identification system linked to the existing biometric national ID card**, consolidating citizens' biometric data into a unified digital-identity platform. **Trust-service providers must be certified.** Framed as a pillar of **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**. Still progressing through the legislative process (draft as of late 2025).
