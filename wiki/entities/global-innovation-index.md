# Global Innovation Index (GII)

**[[wipo]]'s annual global ranking of economies by innovation performance**, published with
per-economy country profiles. It is a **standing reference index** this wiki consults and cites —
a comparative framework, not a report of a development. See [[tech.innovate]],
[[data.statistics]].

> **Reference study — cite, don't absorb.** GII rankings and scores are **not** to be promoted
> onto concept or place pages as current state. Cite the index; leave its numbers in it. Fresher
> country-level data arrives via clippings.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coverage held: Seychelles (GII 2025).** This wiki holds the **WIPO Global Innovation Index
  2025 Seychelles country profile** as a PDF artefact with a companion source page. **Seychelles
  ranks 75th of 139 (GII 2025).** That is the single ranking held here — it is the index's own
  figure, dated to the 2025 edition, and is not a statement of Seychelles' current innovation
  standing. See [[SYC]], [[tech.innovate]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 Global Innovation Index 2025 - Seychelles country profile (WIPO)]].
- **Dating.** The source is filed `published: 2025-01-01` with **`date_precision: year`** — the
  January date is padding for sortability. The GII 2025 edition's actual publication date is not
  established here.
- **How to use it.** Consult the country profile for comparative innovation-ecosystem indicators
  and as a pointer to the underlying data. A dated fact drawn *from* the index becomes a normal
  source citing this entity.
- **Scope of holdings.** Only the **Seychelles** profile is held. No other GII country profile,
  no edition beyond 2025, and no methodology note is in this wiki — do not treat the index as
  broadly available here.
