# Megaport

Global **Network-as-a-Service (NaaS)** provider running a software-defined network with on-ramps to
**930+ data centres** and **300+ cloud nodes** across five continents.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Interconnection with [[angola-cables]] (2025-03).** A dual arrangement interconnecting the two
  networks at **New York, Miami and London**, giving Angola Cables customers self-service access to
  Megaport's global ecosystem of data centres, clouds, internet exchanges and 300+ service providers
  (including its AI-Exchange and Financial Services Exchange). Framed by Angola Cables/TelCables as a
  **"super corridor"** for east–west data transit. See [[angola--infra-connect]], [[infra.connect]],
  [[infra.store]], [[AGO]], [[angola-cables]].
