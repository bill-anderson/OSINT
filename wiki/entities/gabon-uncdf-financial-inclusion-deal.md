# Gabon–UNCDF Financial Inclusion Partnership

**Parties:** the Government of Gabon ([[GAB]]) and the **[[uncdf]]** (UN Capital Development Fund),
with **UNDP** as facilitator. **What:** a partnership to modernise Gabon's financial-services
ecosystem and raise financial inclusion. **When:** announced **8 December 2025**. **Status:** active.

## What it covers

- Policy support to strengthen financial governance, aligned with the **PNCD 2026-2030** national plan
  and **CEMAC's Regional Financial Inclusion Strategy (2023-2027)** ([[gov.regional]]).
- Focus areas: competition among financial-service providers, essential digital-payment infrastructure,
  and consumer protection.
- **Baseline:** ~54% of Gabonese adults unbanked (World Bank Global Findex 2025) despite growing mobile
  money. Framed by Minister Mark-Alexandre Doumba as making digital financial services a pillar of
  inclusion.
- **Sequencing:** landed the day before the [[madigipaie-gabon]] public-payments launch (9 Dec 2025),
  and months after Gabon's May 2025 [[gabon-visa-partnership-deal|Visa partnership]].
