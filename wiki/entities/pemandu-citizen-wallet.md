# Botswana Citizen Wallet + Cryptographic Card (PEMANDU Digital Ecosystem Model)

The closest thing Botswana has to a **foundational DPI programme** — a **Digital Ecosystem Model Project**
run with Malaysia's **PEMANDU Partners** (Vinod Naidu), pairing a **Citizen Wallet** with a **Cryptographic
Card**. It is the payments-plus-identity layer beneath Botswana's e-government push, distinct from the
legacy Omang card and aimed squarely at subsidy delivery and inclusion. See [[BWA]], [[dpi.pay]],
[[dpi.id]].

## What we know (as of 2025-09)

- **Citizen Wallet.** [[dpi.pay]] A **unified e-wallet integrating multiple payment providers**, designed to
  channel **subsidies directly to beneficiaries**, cut fraud, strengthen accountability and expand
  financial inclusion — especially for citizens outside traditional banking. Presented at the 168th Rural
  Development Council meeting.
- **Cryptographic Card.** [[dpi.id]] A **secure smart card** safeguarding sensitive citizen data and
  enabling **trusted, verifiable approvals** — a trust/credential layer for digital service delivery.
- **Context.** Complements the digital **[[omang-digital-id-botswana|Omang]]** ID and the
  **[[digital-services-act-botswana|1Gov/Digital Services Act]]** stack; the sweep found this the most
  concrete Botswana digital-ID/DPI programme on record. Source:
  [[2025-09-17-pemandu-digital-ecosystem-citizen-wallet-techafricanews]].
