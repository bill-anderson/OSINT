# Knight Frank

**A global real-estate consultancy advising [[cavaleros]] on its [[cosmas-data-cities]] data-centre
campuses in South Africa.** Its presence marks the arrival of international property advisory capital
in the African data-centre siting market — the intermediaries who decide where hyperscale demand
lands.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Adviser to the Cavaleros data-centre entry (2025-11-11).** Cavaleros is working with **Knight
  Frank** and data-centre engineering services provider **Future-tech** on its two planned campuses:
  Johannesburg (**200 MW** via a **450 MW self-built switching station**) and Cape Town (**100
  acres**, up to **360 MW**). Penny Cavaleros credits the combination of "our real estate expertise
  with Knight Frank's industry insight and Future-Tech's technical excellence." Source:
  [[2025-11-11 South African real estate firm Cavaleros Group launches data center unit, plans two campuses]].
- **Oscar Matthews on South Africa as gateway (2025-11-11).** Knight Frank's **head of EMEA data
  centre investment & development**: "South Africa holds immense importance as a **gateway for
  hyperscalers, neoclouds, and global enterprises**. With strong cloud regions and fiber
  connectivity, it is the continent's **primary destination for large-scale deployments**. Cavaleros
  has positioned itself as a clear market leader with unmatched expertise." *An adviser's framing of
  a client — attribute it, and note the commercial interest in the claim.* Source: same.
