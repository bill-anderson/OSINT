# PARIS21

The **Partnership in Statistics for Development in the 21st Century** — an OECD-hosted global
partnership that works to strengthen statistical systems in least-developed, low-income, fragile
and small-island developing states, so they can deliver quality data for sustainable development.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Publishes on the official-statistics crisis.** Co-authored (with Open Data Watch) the 2026
  discussion paper **[[2026-07-11 data-systems-crossroads]]**, diagnosing the funding, AI, mandate and trust
  pressures reshaping national statistical offices — acutely felt across African NSOs after the
  2024–25 aid shocks. See [[data.statistics]], [[capacity.research]].
