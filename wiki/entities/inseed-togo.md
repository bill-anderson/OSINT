# INSEED (Institut National de la Statistique et des Études Économiques et Démographiques, Togo)

**Togo's national statistics institute — the National Institute of Statistics, Economic and
Demographic Studies — and the field-operations partner in the [[e-id-togo]] biometric
enrolment.** INSEED sits under the **Ministry of Planning, Development and Cooperation** and
works alongside [[anid-togo]], which implements the e-ID project itself. See [[TGO]],
[[data.statistics]], [[dpi.id]].

**A wire-copy error not propagated.** The Biometric Update piece of **2024-03-06** renders
INSEED as "the Ministry of Planning, Development, and Cooperation (INSEED)" and ANID as a
ministry likewise. Both are **agencies under** those ministries, not the ministries. The
institutional structure recorded here follows the Togo First account of **2025-11-19**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Field personnel for the Maritime enrolment (2025-11).** INSEED manages **deployment of
  field personnel** for the biometric enrolment drive as it expanded into the Maritime
  region's Zone 1 (Vo, Yoto, Bas-Mono, Lacs) from **22 November 2025** — the statistics
  institute supplying enumeration capacity to an identity programme run by [[anid-togo]].
  See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2025-11-19 Togo Advances Digital ID Program With New Enrollment Phase in Maritime Region]].
- **WURI test registration (March 2024) — historical baseline.** INSEED was reported
  co-managing the WURI project in Togo with ANID at the point of test registrations planned
  for **5–15 March 2024**. See [[wuri-programme]]. Source:
  [[2024-03-06 1 step forward, 3 steps back for West Africa's regional digital ID projects]].
