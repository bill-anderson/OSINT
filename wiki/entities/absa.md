# Absa

Major South African bank — a participant in the Home Affairs bank Smart-ID Digital Partnership and the
national payments ecosystem.

- **Smart ID via banks (Jul 2026).** Went live as the **fourth bank** (after Capitec, Standard Bank, FNB)
  offering Home Affairs **Smart ID** applications — 12 branches live, 60 more by end-2026, plus a **"Bank on
  the Move"** mobile service. See [[south-africa--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2026-07-16-absa-joins-home-affairs-smart-id]].
