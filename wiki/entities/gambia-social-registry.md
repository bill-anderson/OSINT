# Gambia Social Registry (GamSR)

The Gambia's national **social registry**, initiated in 2021, that maintains a household database for
targeting social-protection programmes ([[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]). Within two years it
reached **40 of 43 districts and 203,022 households (~70% of the population)** — a World Bank-cited
systems-building success. Built on a digitized questionnaire + **proxy means test**, CAPI tools and a
**Social Registry Information System (SRIS)** with automated data-quality checks, using the Gambia
Bureau of Statistics (GBoS). See [[GMB]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coverage + downstream use (2024).** GamSR underpinned the 'Nafa Quick' COVID response (78,422
  households) and the Nafa cash-transfer programme (16,966 households), and serves the National Disaster
  Management Agency, the ROOTS agriculture project and the WFP. Challenges: remote populations, data
  accuracy, infrastructure, data security. Source: [[2024-04-23 The Gambia Social Registry - A Success Story in Systems Building]].
