# SIN–ST Digital Lomé cloud partnership

Strategic partnership (signed ~June 2026) between [[sin-togo]] (DG Attia Byll) and ST
Digital Togo (DG Justine Adebiyi) to launch a private cloud at the [[lome-data-centre]],
so Togolese companies' data is hosted and processed in-country — a digital-sovereignty
play for [[TGO]].

## Deal terms

- **Parties.** [[sin-togo]] (Togolese state-owned, DG Attia Byll) and ST Digital Togo
  (DG Justine Adebiyi). ST Digital appears to be the same group as [[st-digital-gabon]],
  operating national arms across West and Central Africa — a link to note.
- **What / when.** Deployment of an in-country private cloud at the [[lome-data-centre]],
  signed ~June 2026, offering cloud and [[tech.ai]] services.
- **Status.** Partnership signed; deployment underway. No financial terms disclosed.
