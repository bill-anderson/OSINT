# Personal Data Protection Office (PDPO, Uganda)

Uganda’s data-protection authority under the **[[data-protection-and-privacy-act-uganda-2019]]**;
operational since August 2021.

## Activity in this wiki

- **2026 polls enforcement asymmetry (2026-03).** The Electoral Commission registered with the PDPO only
  *after* polling (21 Jan 2026; ref PDPO-202601-9760) and no political party registered at all, even as
  civil-society actors were pursued under DPPA s.35 — a documented "selective enforcement" pattern. See
  [[UGA]], [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-31 Uneven Enforcement of Data Protection Laws Ugandas 2026 Polls]].
