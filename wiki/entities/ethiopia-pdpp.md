# Ethiopia Personal Data Protection Proclamation (No. 1321/2024)

Ethiopia's **first comprehensive personal-data protection law (the "PDPP")**, approved by parliament **4 April 2024** and published in the Federal Negarit Gazette **24 July 2024**. Before it, data protection was piecemeal (Civil Code, Computer Crimes Proclamation, financial/telecom directives). Defines personal data broadly (incl. location and online identifiers); applies to automated and non-automated processing by controllers/processors established in Ethiopia or using local equipment, with extraterritorial reach to foreign entities handling Ethiopians' data; exempts personal/household activity, need-to-know inter-agency exchange and mere transit.

Designates the **[[ethiopian-communications-authority|Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA)]]** as regulator. **Enforcement is lagging (2025):** the secondary directives needed to operationalise it remain unissued and public awareness is low — a gap between law-on-paper and effect.
