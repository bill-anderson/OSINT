# X Corp (X / Twitter)

X Corp operates the social platform **X, formerly Twitter**. It appears in the wiki as a
subject of African regulatory action over platform dominance and the use of local content.

## What matters now

- **Nigeria FCCPC investigation (2026).** X is a named primary subject — alongside [[meta]]
  and Alphabet/[[google]] — of a [[fccpc]] investigation ordered by President [[bola-tinubu]]
  into whether the platforms breached the Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Act 2018
  by scraping, training on and profiting from Nigerian news content without compensation
  (abuse of dominance + copyright). See [[gov.legislate]], [[tech.ai]],
  [[nigeria--gov-protect]].
