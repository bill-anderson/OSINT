# Mark-Alexandre Doumba

**Minister of the Digital Economy, Digitalisation and Innovation, Gabon**
(**[[ministere-economie-numerique-gabon]]**) — the recurring signatory across Gabon's digital
partnership agreements and the public voice of its digitalisation agenda.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Start-up financing and skills accords (2026-07-14).** Oversaw the signing of two agreements — with
  the **[[societe-gabonaise-de-garantie]]** and with **Huawei Technologies Gabon** — at the launch of
  **[[kimba-connect]]**, arguing the state's role should extend past project finance into
  accompaniment. Source: [[2026-07-17-gabon-garantie-publique-huawei-start-up]].
- **Gabon–Huawei digital transformation MoU (signed 2025-12-23).** Oversaw the national digital
  transformation MoU with Huawei. See [[gabon-huawei-mou]].
- **Recurring across Gabon's deal set.** Named in connection with [[st-digital-gabon]],
  [[gabon-national-datacenter]], [[gabon-visa-partnership-deal]],
  [[gabon-uncdf-financial-inclusion-deal]] and [[digitax-gabon]].

See [[GAB]] · [[tech.innovate]] · [[gov.policy]].
