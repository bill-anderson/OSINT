# DRC sovereign satellite (Monacosat)

A **sovereign satellite programme** for DR Congo under a **Nov-2024 DRC–Monaco MoU with Monacosat**, cost
estimated at **$400m** reported already mobilised — a state-owned space asset for connectivity, named
(with Starlink) as a PNN connectivity instrument. Source:
[[2025-08-31-projet-acquisition-satellite-monacosat-actualite]].
