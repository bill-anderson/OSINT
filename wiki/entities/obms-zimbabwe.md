# Online Border Management System (OBMS, Zimbabwe)

**Zimbabwe's biometric border-automation system**, supplied by [[garsu-pasaulis]] and run
by the [[department-of-immigration-zimbabwe]]. **Everything held is dated 2024 and
describes a pilot — the wiki holds nothing on its outcome (GAP-ZWE-002). Do not read as
currently operating.**

## Design (as of 2024-05-15)

- **Biometrics:** fingerprint and **iris** verification at e-gates, checked against
  biometric data stored in the **national immigration database**.
- **Architecture:** designed on **Dubai's Smart Gates** platform, which offers a
  contactless immigration process.
- **Components:** digital gate platforms with **automated self-service barriers** drawing
  on passport data, biometrics and pre-submitted travel details; a **kiosk model** for
  travellers who do not use pre-clearance.
- **Pilot sites:** Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport, Victoria Falls
  International Airport, Beitbridge Border Post, plus several inland stations.
- **Cross-agency:** presented as improving coordination of **all border agencies** — a
  centralised platform for sharing information across border-control and security bodies.
  No oversight arrangement is described.

## The "first in SADC" claim — not asserted

OBMS was **"reportedly the first in the SADC region"**. This is a state-media claim (The
Sunday Mail) that the reporting author himself hedges with "reportedly". **The wiki does
not assert it**; it is recorded as a claim, unverified.

## Worth noting

On the source's own facts, and without the source drawing the link: **one Lithuanian
vendor supplies both Zimbabwe's passports and its border database**, on a **Dubai-derived
architecture**, with **centralised cross-agency biometric access** and no mentioned
oversight. Recorded, not editorialised.
