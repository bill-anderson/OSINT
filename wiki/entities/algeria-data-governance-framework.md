# Algeria National Data Governance Framework (Presidential Decree of 30 Dec 2025)

> ⚠ **Decree-number dispute.** The held English source (We Are Tech) gives the number as **25-350**; two
> Algerian-press sources (El Moudjahid 245982; Echorouk market-supply IS, 2026-04-27) give **25-320**, same JO
> date (30 Dec 2025). Under reconciliation — see `reviews/contradictions/open/algeria-data-governance-decree-number`.

Algeria's **national framework for the governance of public data**, launched by PM **Sifi Ghrieb** on **9 February 2026** and anchored in a **Presidential Decree of 30 December 2025** (number disputed — see above). Establishes a **sovereign data model** — data as a strategic asset — with a legal, institutional and technical basis for collecting, storing, sharing and using public data; stronger information-system security; **interoperability between public administrations**; and a **unified national database of data sources**.

Aims to reduce data silos, improve public-statistics reliability, and support data-driven digital services. Builds on **five regional data centres** and aligns with **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**; complements the **[[anpdcp|Law 18-07]]** personal-data regime and mandatory data localisation.
