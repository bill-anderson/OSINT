# Algeria National Data Governance Framework (Presidential Decree 25-320 of 30 Dec 2025)

Algeria's **national framework for the governance of public data**, established by **Presidential Decree n° 25-320 of 30 December 2025** (*Journal Officiel* n° 87, 30 December 2025, pp. 4–6, signed by President Tebboune) and launched by PM **Sifi Ghrieb** on **9 February 2026**. It runs to 20 articles across four chapters, and was published alongside Decree n° 25-321 approving the National Information-Systems Security Strategy 2025–2029. Establishes a **sovereign data model** — data as a strategic asset — with a legal, institutional and technical basis for collecting, storing, sharing and using public data; stronger information-system security; **interoperability between public administrations**; and a **unified national database of data sources**.

Aims to reduce data silos, improve public-statistics reliability, and support data-driven digital services. Builds on **five regional data centres** and aligns with **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**; complements the **[[anpdcp|Law 18-07]]** personal-data regime and mandatory data localisation.

## Commencement: gazetted, launched, but not yet in force on its core duties

The decree is **commencement-deferred**, and this is the most consequential fact about it. Art. 8 provides
that the two *référentiels* — data classification, and data-source cataloguing — are **published by
decision of the High Commissioner for Digitalisation**, not by decree. Art. 19 then makes the classification
and cataloguing obligations on public institutions run **"à compter de la date de publication des deux
référentiels cités à l'article 8"**. Until that decision issues, no public body is under a live duty.

**As of 2026-07-20, no publication of either référentiel has been traced** — not in the *Journal Officiel*,
not in any [[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria|HCN]] announcement, on a French-language search of the
Algerian press and gazette. So more than six months after gazetting, and five months after the political
launch, **the framework's operative obligations are not shown to have commenced.** Stated as a negative
finding, not a proven absence: a *décision* could be gazetted in the JO's *arrêtés, décisions et avis*
section without press notice, and a full JORADP 2026 index sweep has not been run.

Two things must not be mistaken for that publication. The **9 February 2026 launch** installed a national
follow-up commission and switched the dispositif on politically — it did not trigger Art. 19. And the
*"première version du Référentiel national de la gouvernance des données"* presented by
**Meriem Benmouloud** at an HCN seminar on **20 February 2025** predates the decree by ten months and is a
different object — a draft framework tied to the IRIES sovereign-network project, not the Art. 8 decision.

Companion instrument **Decree n° 25-321** (same gazette issue, p. 6) approves the
[[algeria-national-cybersecurity-strategy-2025-2029|ISS Strategy 2025–2029]] but states the strategy is
*"annexée à l'original du présent décret"* — **the strategy text is therefore not printed in the JO**: the
instrument is public, its content is not.
