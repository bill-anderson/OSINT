# Algeria National Data Governance Framework (Presidential Decree 25-350)

Algeria's **national framework for the governance of public data**, launched by PM **Sifi Ghrieb** on **9 February 2026** and anchored in **Presidential Decree No. 25-350 of 30 December 2025**. Establishes a **sovereign data model** — data as a strategic asset — with a legal, institutional and technical basis for collecting, storing, sharing and using public data; stronger information-system security; **interoperability between public administrations**; and a **unified national database of data sources**.

Aims to reduce data silos, improve public-statistics reliability, and support data-driven digital services. Builds on **five regional data centres** and aligns with **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**; complements the **[[anpdcp|Law 18-07]]** personal-data regime and mandatory data localisation.
