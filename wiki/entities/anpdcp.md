# National Authority for the Protection of Personal Data (ANPDCP, Algeria)

Algeria's **data-protection supervisory authority**, established under **Law No. 18-07** on the protection of personal data (enacted **10 June 2018**; in force from **10 August 2023**). Law 18-07 governs the collection and processing of personal data and is paired with a **mandatory data-localisation** rule — personal data must be stored within Algeria. The ANPDCP oversees enforcement.

Algeria's data-protection regime is being extended by the November-2025 **[[algeria-digital-id-trust-law|digital-ID and trust-services draft law]]** and the 2025 **[[algeria-data-governance-framework|public-data governance framework]]**, both of which lean on this base and its localisation stance.
