# SKLD Integrated Services Limited

Nigerian education-supplies and services company; organiser of the
**[[nigeria-education-supplies-summit|Nigeria Education Supplies Summit (NESS)]]**. CEO: **Tayo
Osiyemi**; Founder & Executive Vice Chairman: **Temilola Adepetun (2026-07)**.

> **Provenance.** The wiki holds SKLD only through a **TechCabal Partner / Press Room
> placement** about its own summit — sponsored content, admissible as a first-hand organiser
> statement, not independent reporting. No revenue, size, ownership or client information is on
> file.

## What matters now

- **NESS 2026 (2026-07-16, Civic Centre, Victoria Island, Lagos).** SKLD convened school leaders,
  education executives, policymakers and technology suppliers under the theme *"Resilience in
  Motion, Schools Leading Change"*. Osiyemi framed it as an attempt to build a standing annual
  platform rather than a one-off problem-discussion forum: *"NESS is our contribution to creating
  an annual platform where regulators, school leaders, policymakers, financiers, technology
  providers, and education suppliers can come together not merely to discuss challenges but to
  build practical solutions together."* See [[nigeria-education-supplies-summit]].

## Why it matters here

SKLD is a **supplier convening its own market's regulators and buyers** — the commercial interest
is disclosed by the format, and the wiki records the summit's content accordingly: as positions
taken by named speakers at a vendor-organised event, not as findings.
