# ITIDA (Information Technology Industry Development Agency)

Egypt's **IT-industry development agency** (under **[[mcit-egypt|MCIT]]**), the lead body for the **outsourcing /
digital-services export** drive that underpins Egypt's **$7.4bn digital exports** ($4.8bn outsourcing, 2025).
In 2026-07 it signed an **MoU with EY MENA** to establish a regional **IT-Outsourcing (ITO) delivery centre**
serving MENA — **1,000+ jobs** over three years in cybersecurity, digital engineering, data analytics, AI and
business platforms. See [[egypt--tech-ai]].

ITIDA was represented at MCIT's **2026-07-17** talks with the [[world-bank|World Bank Group]] on
digital skills, AI and innovation — by **CEO Ahmed Elzaher** and **VP for ICT markets development
Mahmoud Sofrata**. Source: [[2026-07-17-egypt-deepens-world-bank-digital-partnership]].
