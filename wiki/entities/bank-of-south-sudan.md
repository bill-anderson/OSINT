# Bank of South Sudan

South Sudan's central bank, responsible for the country's monetary system and
its nascent interbank payments infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Inter-Bank Payment and Settlement System (2025-10).** The bank launched an
  interoperable payment and settlement system enabling **instant transfers, bulk
  salary payments and electronic government transactions**, reducing reliance on
  physical cash. Uptake remains limited: **not all banks are fully integrated**,
  outages and manual workarounds persist, and confidence is undercut by
  governance concerns that key tax/revenue systems are controlled by individuals
  rather than institutionally owned. See [[SSD]], [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.exchange]].
  Source: [[2026-01-30 Can a digital economy exist in South Sudan without reliable DPI]].
- **Context — no national digital ID.** The settlement system sits atop a wider
  DPI gap: South Sudan lacks a national digital identity layer, leaving mobile
  money, biometric payroll and humanitarian registries siloed and its de-facto
  DPI donor-driven and proprietary. See [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-30 Can a digital economy exist in South Sudan without reliable DPI]].
