# DGPSN — Délégation générale à la Protection sociale et à la Solidarité nationale (Senegal)

Senegal's social-protection coordination body, mandated to drive and streamline social-protection strategies.
**Line and coordination agency of the [[rnu-senegal|RNU]]** social registry and manager of safety nets
including the **Family Security Grant** (Bourse de sécurité familiale), the "Yook Kom Kom" project and the
Agricultural Resilience Programme.

## Activity in this wiki

- Reported the **RNU's 2024 expansion (542,000 → 1 million households)** and gender/disability-inclusion work
  with UNICEF (regional workshop, Ziguinchor, Dec 2025); ~64,000 people with disabilities receive the Family
  Security Grant (2025). See [[SEN]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-09 Senegal's RNU social registry rises from 542,000 to one million households]].
