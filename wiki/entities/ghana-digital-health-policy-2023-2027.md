# Ghana Policy and Strategy on Digital Health 2023-2027

The [[ghana-health-service|Ghana Health Service]]'s five-year digital-health policy and strategy — the reference instrument framing GHS's health-information-system ([[dpi.mis]]) modernization. Built on a SWOT of GHS leadership/governance, services/applications, infrastructure and workforce, it sets **ten strategic objectives**, prominently (1) strengthening digital-health governance at national/regional/district levels and (2) improving **interoperability and connectivity of all ICT systems**. Developed through expert consultation on WHO/UNICEF/USAID evidence, with DANIDA and Global Fund support; COVID-19 exposed the vulnerabilities it aims to close. See [[GHA]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Issued 2023.** Establishes the general framework for coordinating digital-health interventions within GHS and ensuring applications can interoperate and share information over 2023-2027. Source: [[2023-01-01 Ghana Policy and Strategy on Digital Health 2023-2027 (GHS)]].
