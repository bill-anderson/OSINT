# Bank of Namibia

Namibia's central bank and payments overseer. Under the
**[[namibia-payment-system-management-act-2023|Payment System Management Act 2023]]** it regulates the national
payments system and is developing — with the Ministry of Finance — an **Instant Payment Solution (IPS)** to
enable real-time retail and **government-to-person** payments (e.g. social grants), with a launch flagged for
**Q3 2026**. It is also weighing membership of the pan-African **[[papss|PAPSS]]** for local-currency
cross-border settlement, and anchors the Ministry of Finance's 2023 digital-finance ecosystem strategy.

The IPS is being built by **[[instant-payments-namibia|Instant Payments Namibia (IPN)]]** (COO Marsorry Ickua,
seconded from the BoN), likely using **biometric authentication** on the new digital ID (India Stack / M-Pesa as
models). In **July 2026** the BoN (Gov. **Ebson Uanguta**), IPN and **NamClear** signed a Declaration of Intent
to integrate the IPS with NamClear's clearing infrastructure and the **Namibia Interbank Settlement System
(NISS)**. Sources: [[2025-07-01-namibia-weighs-biometrics-instant-payment-biometricupdate]],
[[2026-07-02-bon-ipn-namclear-instant-payments-pact-techafricanews]].
