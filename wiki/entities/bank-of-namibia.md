# Bank of Namibia

Namibia's central bank and payments overseer. Under the
**[[namibia-payment-system-management-act-2023|Payment System Management Act 2023]]** it regulates the national
payments system and is developing — with the Ministry of Finance — an **Instant Payment Solution (IPS)** to
enable real-time retail and **government-to-person** payments (e.g. social grants), with a launch flagged for
**Q3 2026**. It is also weighing membership of the pan-African **[[papss|PAPSS]]** for local-currency
cross-border settlement, and anchors the Ministry of Finance's 2023 digital-finance ecosystem strategy.
