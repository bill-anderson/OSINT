# Dzair Services (Algeria public-services single window)

Algeria's national **single-window platform** centralising public digital services — announced **16 October 2025** by High Commissioner for Digitalisation **Meriem Benmouloud** (see [[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria]]). A single access point aggregating existing services to simplify procedures, ensure traceability and reduce institutional overlaps, positioned as the central interface of Algeria's online administration.

Runs on an **interoperability system** for automatic verified-data exchange (46 ministries/agencies on fibre; national data centres at El Mohammedia and Blida; a sovereign cloud in preparation), laying groundwork for AI integration. Complements the older **Bawabatak** portal (342 services). Part of **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**.
