# Bede

A telco-led **mobile-money digital wallet** launched in Sudan by Zain Fintech,
the financial-services arm of Zain Group, for **[[zain-sudan]]** customers. Lets
users transact directly from a mobile phone without a bank account or card,
working across both basic feature phones and smartphones — a wartime
financial-inclusion/survival rail complementing Bank of Khartoum's
**[[bankak]]**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (2025-04, Port Sudan).** Launch ceremony held in Port Sudan attended
  by Sovereign Council and ministry representatives. Initial phase covers
  transfers, airtime, bill payments, merchant purchases and agent
  deposits/withdrawals. First deployed in Bahrain; Sudan rollout planned in
  three phases (add banks + electricity/government payments → banking services,
  international remittances and savings, with full inter-bank interoperability).
  Operates under **[[central-bank-of-sudan]]** digital-wallet policy and national
  AML/data-protection standards. See [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]], [[SDN]].
