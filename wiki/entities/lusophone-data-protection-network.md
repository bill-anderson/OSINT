# Lusophone Data Protection Network (Rede Lusófona de Proteção de Dados, RLPD)

A cooperation network of data-protection authorities of Portuguese-speaking jurisdictions, established
**2024**. Founding members: Portugal (CNPD), Brazil, Angola, Cabo Verde and **São Tomé and Príncipe
([[national-data-protection-agency-sao-tome|ANPDP]])**. Brazil holds the presidency; Portugal's CNPD runs
the permanent secretariat. **Macau's DSPDP joined Apr 2026**, extending the network into a China–Lusophone
data-diplomacy bridge (cross-border data flows, AI supervision). See [[sao-tome--gov-protect]].
