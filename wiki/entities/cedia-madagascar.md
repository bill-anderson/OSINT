# CEDIA (Centre of Excellence for Data & AI, Madagascar Customs)

An **in-house data-science / AI laboratory** within Madagascar Customs (launched **May 2025**),
centralising data-science knowledge, automating anomaly detection and building risk-targeting algorithms.
See [[tech.ai]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **IMF's Africa AI-in-customs pilot (2025-06).** [[dpi.govtech]] The **IMF selected Madagascar Customs as
  its Africa pilot** for AI in customs operations; DG Zafivanona Ernest Lainkana presented the **Enhanced
  Risk Assessment (ERA)** intelligent-targeting system and a **Data Warehouse** at an IMF Washington panel
  (alongside Google/Microsoft/IBM/Meta/OpenAI, and Australia) and at Dubai's Innovate the Future forum.
  CEDIA anchors the customs' 2025–2029 strategy. Source: [[2025-06-07-douanes-madagascar-modele-ia-cedia-midi]].
