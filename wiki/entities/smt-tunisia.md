# Société Monétique Tunisie (SMT)

**Société Monétique Tunisie (SMT)** is Tunisia's interbank electronic-payments company — the national
switch/monétique operator that sits under the **[[central-bank-of-tunisia|CBT]]**'s payments architecture,
alongside the **[[paysmart]]** mobile rail. It is a recurring counterparty in the country's payments build.

- **TUNPAY (May 2026).** SMT partnered the CBT to launch **TUNPAY**, the national mobile-payment label that
  banks, PSPs and the postal service integrate for harmonised identification and interoperability. Source:
  [[2026-05-07-wearetech-central-bank-tunpay-label]].
- **walletii (Mar 2026).** SMT is a partner (with **QNB Group**) in **Ooredoo Fintech**'s CBT-licensed
  **walletii** wallet. Source: [[2026-03-07-menastartupdigest-ooredoo-walletii-license]].
