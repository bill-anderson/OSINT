# Bank of Mauritius (BoM)

Mauritius's central bank (Governor **Priscilla Muthoora Thakoor**), driving the shift from cash and cheques to
digital payments. It operates the **[[maucas|MauCAS]]** national instant-payment switch and, per a Jan-2026
industry workshop, is:

- modernising via a **Know-Your-Customer (KYC) System** for digital onboarding, compliance and anti-fraud;
- aligning to the **G20 Roadmap for Enhancing Cross-border Payments** (2020) and the Indian G20 presidency's DPI
  push;
- cautioning on **payment-system fragmentation, stablecoins and cybersecurity risk**, strengthening its
  regulatory framework with IMF support and exploring **AI for proactive fraud detection**.

**UPI-RuPay (India).** In **February 2024** the BoM launched the **UPI-RuPay partnership with India** — the
country's **first bilateral cross-border payments agreement** — opening a path to interoperability and eventual
peer-to-peer transfers via a future UPI Global Hub. See [[geopol.india]], [[mauritius--dpi-pay]].
