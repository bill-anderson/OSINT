# Digital Governance Unit (Unité de Gouvernance Digitale, UGD)

Madagascar's special unit for accelerating e-governance, **established in 2022**. The UGD operationalises the
country's **national interoperability platform** — built on **X-Road** (or equivalent), in collaboration
with **Estonia's e-Governance Academy** — creating a secure, trusted data-exchange ecosystem for public and
private service providers. In August 2025 it issued a call for a consulting firm to help scale the platform
(bidders required prior X-Road deployments).

The interoperability system is one of [[prodigy-madagascar|PRODIGY]]'s three pillars, and the national
digital ID is to be integrated with ministries and public services **through the interoperability system
overseen by the UGD**.
