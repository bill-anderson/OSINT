# ONIP (Office National d'Identification de la Population)

The DRC's **national population-identification office** — responsible for the physical national ID card and the population register. Director-General: Richard Ilunga.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Cancelled the $1.2B IDEMIA/Afritech contract (Aug 2024).** ONIP DG Richard Ilunga confirmed the DRC called off the $1.2bn national biometric-ID contract with **[[idemia]]** (via intermediary Afritech) on 12 Aug 2024, after a Bloomberg/Lighthouse investigation into overbilling. See [[drc--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2024-09-04 DR Congo cancels $1.2B IDEMIA-Afritech biometric ID contract]].
- **Pivot to Ghana's Margins Group (2025).** The Presidential Strategic Oversight Council (CPVS) and ONIP announced (Jul 2025) a partnership with **[[margins-id-group]]** to produce national ID cards — the physical-card track, parallel to Trident's DRCPass digital-ID/e-KYC. Selection process undisclosed. Source: [[2025-08-11 DR Congo may partner with Ghana's Margins Group for national ID cards]].
