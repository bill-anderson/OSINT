# Ripoti (Paradigm Initiative)

A [[paradigm-initiative]] platform for documenting and responding to digital-rights and data-privacy
violations across Africa; feeds pro-bono legal representation and strategic litigation (e.g. the
Domino’s/Eat’n’Go direct-marketing case). See [[gov.protect]]. Source:
[[2026-01-29 Press release Paradigm Initiative applauds countries with data protection laws]].
