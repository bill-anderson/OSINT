# INE (Instituto Nacional de Estatística, Angola)

Angola's **national statistics office** — producer of official demographic, social and economic data, custodian of the **Censo 2024** (Recenseamento Geral da População e Habitação), and the institutional home of the national statistical system. Board chair (PCA) **Joel Futi**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Censo 2024 portal + mobile app (2026-07-02).** INE launched the **Portal do Censo 2024** (census indicators, maps, tables and charts by geographic disaggregation) and the **"INE Angola" mobile app** (macro indicators — GDP, inflation, employment, business statistics — in PDF/Excel). Both were **built in-house** by INE technical staff, framed as open, universal access to official statistics. Source: [[2026-07-02-ine-lanca-portal-do-censo-2024-e-aplicacao-movel]].
- **Digital field methods (2026-02, Benguela).** INE uses **ArcGIS + Esri Field Maps** for geo-referenced field collection, cutting data-gathering time **from 6–8 months to ~2 months** and enabling real-time monitoring/correction; combined with 2023 cartographic updates and satellite imagery to map schools, banking and health facilities. Source: [[2026-02-10-tecnologias-digitais-dados-estatisticos-em-benguela]].
- **Strategy & law reform.** INE is steering the **[[ende-angola]]** (National Statistics Development Strategy 2026–2032) and a **statistics-law revision** to the Conselho Nacional de Estatística, plus a new online **National User Satisfaction Survey**. Source: [[2026-06-12-nova-estrategia-de-desenvolvimento-da-estatistica-2026-2032]].
