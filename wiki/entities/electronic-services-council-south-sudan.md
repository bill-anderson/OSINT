# Electronic Services Council — South Sudan

The government body mandated to drive digitisation of South Sudan's public
institutions, chaired by **Thomas Gatkuoth Nyak**, Undersecretary for
Telecommunications. It sits alongside the Ministry of Information, Communications
Technology and Postal Services, which introduced an e-government system as early
as 2019 and operates under a National E-Government Framework.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Full-digitisation decree (2025-12).** President Salva Kiir granted the
  council full authority to digitise all government institutions; announced
  17 Dec 2025 with a **January 2026** adoption deadline for institutions not yet
  digitised. Nyak claimed the economy had improved ~40% since e-services began.
  South Sudan ranked **192/193** on the UN E-Government Development Index (EGDI
  0.1191). Source:
  [[2025-12-19 South Sudan orders full digitization of public institutions]].
