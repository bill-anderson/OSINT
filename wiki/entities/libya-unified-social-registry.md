# Libya Unified Social Registry

Libya's planned **unified social registry** — a single database integrating citizens' social and
economic information to identify those eligible for social support, with a priority-classification system
(income, health, family size) and periodic updates ([[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]). Approved in
draft by PM Dabaiba (June 2025) as a foundational pillar for a social-security system. A natural consumer
of the Civil Registry's National ID Number ([[civil-registry-authority-libya]]). See [[LBY]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Draft approved (2025-06-23).** Submitted by Social Affairs Minister / High Committee chair Wafaa
  Al-Kilani; part of developing social protection and fairness in support distribution. Source:
  [[2025-06-23 Libya's PM Dabaiba approves Unified Social Registry project]].
