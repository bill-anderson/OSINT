# NatCA (National Communications Authority, Sierra Leone)

Sierra Leone's telecoms regulator (DG **Amara Brewah**; also styled NATCOM). Drives cross-border
integration — an ECOWAS **free-roaming** deal with Guinea (CAR-15 / Transform Africa 2025) and the
Liberia–Gambia–Sierra Leone roaming MoU — and spectrum reform: NGSO/satellite guidelines, technology-
neutral licensing, longer (15–20yr) licence durations, staggered fees, and an LS Telcom Automated
Spectrum Management System. Runs the Universal Access Development Fund (UADF) solar community sites.
See [[sierra-leone--infra-connect]], [[SLE]].
