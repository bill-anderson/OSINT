# Lumitel (Viettel Burundi)

Burundi's largest mobile operator by many measures, the local arm of Vietnam's **Viettel** group.
Director-General: **Phan Truong Son**. Lumitel is the private partner in the World Bank
[[pafen-burundi|PAFEN]] rural-connectivity drive and a would-be 5G first-mover. See [[BDI]],
[[infra.connect]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **$10m PAFEN rural broadband contract (2026-03-02).** [[digital.rural]] Lumitel signed a **>$10m** PPP
  (World Bank **$4.37m** + Lumitel **>$5.62m**) to bring high-speed internet to **86 hills across 14
  provinces** over 14 months — **>400,000 people**, ~**300 schools** — deploying 2G/4G, with Lumitel
  operating and maintaining. Source: [[2026-03-04-lumitel-burundi-10m-high-speed-internet-project]].
- **5G plans (2025).** [[infra.connect]] Lumitel planned to deploy 5G equipment on **10 sites**, cited by
  the ICT minister as Burundi's near-term 5G start. Source:
  [[2025-01-21-burundi-embraces-eac-digital-integration-ict-growth]].
