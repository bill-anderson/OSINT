# IMA (Instituto de Modernização Administrativa)

Angola's **administrative-modernisation institute**, the implementing agency for the country's digital-government and interoperability agenda. Director-general **Meick Afonso**. Convenes the annual **IMA Forum** on digital transformation.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Governo Digital 2027 — the umbrella agenda (2026-03-05).** IMA anchors the **Agenda de Transição Digital da Administração Pública – "Governo Digital 2027"**, now counting **218 structuring projects** across six strategic objectives and seven intervention axes (up from **214 projects** presented at the World Bank Digital Summit in Washington, March 2025). The government (Casa Civil minister Dionísio da Fonseca) is pressing for a **Lei-Quadro do Governo Digital** (framework law) to align institutions, standardise technology and coordinate the rollout, and signed three MoUs (broadband connectivity, digital inclusion, the **national digital-identity system**) at the 6th IMA Forum. See [[angola--dpi-govtech]], [[gov.policy]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source: [[2026-03-05-governo-aposta-reorganizacao-administracao-publica-governo-digital-2027]].
- **The $150m "Digitais Públicas" DPI build (2026-06-17).** IMA formalised the **US$150m** contract (Despacho Presidencial 38/26, 12-month execution) with **[[tis-angola|TIS Tech Angola]]** for the concrete DPI backbone: a secure-zone critical-computing component hosting the **interoperability platform**, **PKI-based e-signatures**, **digital identity** and structured **cybersecurity**. See [[tis-angola]], [[angola--dpi-govtech]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2026-06-17-ima-tis-contrato-projecto-infra-estruturas-digitais-publicas]].
- **Digital-certification trust layer (2026-05-23).** At the 7th IMA Forum, IMA reaffirmed its role providing the **e-signature / time-stamp** certification infrastructure the rest of the state (e.g. **[[agt-angola|AGT]]** e-invoicing, the Serviço Nacional de Contratação Pública) builds on — naming technical capacity as the main challenge. See [[gov.standards]]. Source: [[2026-05-23-agt-eliminacao-gradual-documentos-em-papel]].
- **Angola Digital Acceleration Project (PADA).** IMA implements PADA — a **$300m** World Bank (IBRD) loan under the regional **[[idea-mpa]]** (IDEA) programme — targeting internet access, data infrastructure and **cross-ministry interoperability**, expected to mobilise ~$80m private investment. It underwrites the **Identidade Digital Única** and the **Infra-estrutura Nacional de Dados** that the TIS contract now executes. See [[dpi.govtech]]. Source: [[2024-06-27 Inclusive Digitalization in Eastern and Southern Africa Program - Angola]].
- Works alongside **[[infosi]]** (national DC/cloud) on the state digital stack.
