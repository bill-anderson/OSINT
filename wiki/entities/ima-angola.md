# IMA (Instituto de Modernização Administrativa)

Angola's **administrative-modernisation institute**, the implementing agency for the country's digital-government and interoperability agenda.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Angola Digital Acceleration Project (PADA).** IMA implements PADA — a **$300m** World Bank (IBRD) loan under the regional **[[idea-mpa]]** (IDEA) programme — targeting internet access, data infrastructure and **cross-ministry interoperability**, expected to mobilise ~$80m private investment. The interoperability goal (databases/systems across ministries) is the stated next challenge for modernising public administration. See [[angola--dpi-pay]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source: [[2024-06-27 Inclusive Digitalization in Eastern and Southern Africa Program - Angola]].
- Works alongside **[[infosi]]** (national DC/cloud) on the state digital stack.
