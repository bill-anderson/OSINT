# Google AI Research Foundations

A free, research-grade AI curriculum from [[google-deepmind]], Google.org and
Google Skills, designed to move learners beyond AI literacy into hands-on work
building and fine-tuning language models and transformers. Co-developed with
pedagogy experts at University College London and localised to African contexts
with the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Africa expansion (2026-03-17).** [[google-deepmind]] launched additional
  courses aimed at technical university students and community learners across
  Africa, available at no cost on Google Skills for self-paced or classroom use.
  Google.org funds lecturer training and instructional toolkits, using a
  **"train-the-trainer"** model to translate the online material into in-person
  instruction. Source: [[2026-03-17 Broadening advanced AI education across Africa]].
- **$4m to FATE Foundation (2026-03).** Google.org provided **$4 million** to
  Nigeria's [[NGA]] FATE Foundation to bring the curriculum into African
  classrooms, in partnership with AIMS — launching specialised lecturer toolkits
  and train-the-trainer programmes. See [[capacity.training]].
  Source: [[2026-03-17 Broadening advanced AI education across Africa]].
- **Framing stat.** The initiative is pitched against the gap that Africa is
  ~1/5 of the world population but contributes only **1.8%** of AI research
  contributions (SCImago). See [[tech.ai]], [[capacity.research]], [[XAF]].
