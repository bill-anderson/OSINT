# ARPT (Autorité de Régulation des Postes et Télécommunications, Guinea)

Guinea's posts-and-telecommunications regulator ([[infra.connect]]). Beyond the domestic mandate, ARPT **chairs the Smart Africa Council of African Regulators (CAR)** — the Alliance's consultative body for harmonising ICT policy across the continent — giving Guinea a continental regulatory-coordination role ([[gov.regional]]). Director-General (2025): Mamady Doumbouya. See [[GIN]], [[smart-africa]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Chairs Smart Africa's Council of African Regulators.** [[gov.regional]] Guinea, through ARPT, holds the CAR presidency, promoting collaboration and harmonised ICT policies across Smart Africa member states. Source: [[2025-09-09 Get to know The Republic of Guinea Home of Transform Africa Summit 2025]].
- **Partner in the .gn / Tier III launch (2025-09).** Listed among the institutions supporting the national-domain and data-centre launch. Source: [[2025-09-05 Guinea Launches National Domain .gn and Unveils First Tier III Data Center]].
