# MPT-CERT

The **sectoral cyber-incident monitoring, alert and response centre** for Algeria's **post & telecom sector** —
inaugurated **2026-06-15** by Minister **[[sid-ali-zerrouki]]** with **South Korea's KOICA** technical support
(Korean ambassador Min Kyung-tae present), alongside a Cyber Security Forum. Tasked with continuous monitoring,
threat analysis and incident-response coordination across the sector's bodies. See [[algeria--infra-cybersec]].
