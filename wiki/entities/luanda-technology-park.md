# Luanda Science and Technology Park

A research-and-innovation hub in Luanda, inaugurated **29 June 2026** by the Angolan government and the **[[african-development-bank|AfDB]]**, meant to link academia, research, companies and young entrepreneurs and to help diversify the economy away from oil under the **National Development Plan 2023–2027**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Part of a $100m AfDB Science and Technology Development Project (STDP).** Alongside the park, the STDP has (per AfDB) **equipped 54 science laboratories in 18 secondary schools**, **trained 1,500+** faculty, researchers, technicians and counsellors, **funded 73 research projects** (nearly a third led by women), provided **161 international scholarships**, and funded secondary science education for **1,204 girls** from disadvantaged backgrounds. President João Lourenço framed it as strengthening national research capacity; higher-education minister Albano Lopes Ferreira called it a milestone in the national science-technology-innovation policy.
