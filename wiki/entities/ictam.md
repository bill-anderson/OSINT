# ICT Association of Malawi (ICTAM)

Malawi's ICT-industry membership body (General Secretary **Exile Njoka**), a recurring advocacy voice on the
country's digital agenda — pressing for **national-ID integration** with digital platforms as a verification
base for financial inclusion, health, education and welfare (against the NSFI III 95%-by-2028 target), for
**ICT-policy enforcement**, and for growth of Malawi's ICT-services exports. It co-runs the annual **Innovation
Jam** (with NBM) and convenes the **ICTAM Expo**.
