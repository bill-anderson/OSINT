# Higher Education Students' Loans Board (HESLB), Tanzania

Tanzania's **student-loans board** — a downstream consumer of the national ID and addressing stack.

- **Digital ID/address-enabled loans (2025).** Since **national ID became a requirement for student loans
  (May 2025)** and Zanzibar's National Address Programme went operational (June 2025), **over 489,409
  students applied for loans by digital means** — an example of the ID + addressing layers converting into
  service access. See [[tanzania--dpi-registry]], [[tanzania--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2025-08-19-tanzania-digital-id-address-student-loans-zanzibar]].
