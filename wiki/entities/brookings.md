# Brookings Institution (Africa Growth Initiative)

US think tank; its Africa Growth Initiative (AGI) publishes on African digital transformation, AI and
trade (incl. Foresight Africa).

## Activity in this wiki

- **"Sequence, not rush" AI commentary (2026-02).** Gabon's digital-economy minister Mark-Alexandre
  Doumba argues Africa risks *premature automation* — "once raw minerals, now raw data" — and should
  sequence AI behind data/infrastructure/skills (Africa: 38% internet penetration, <1% of global
  data-centre capacity and AI research). See [[tech.ai]], [[colonialism]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-10 Why Africa should sequence not rush into AI]].
