# AFICTA (Africa ICT Alliance)

The **Africa ICT Alliance** — a continental private-sector-led alliance of national ICT
associations, companies and stakeholders that convenes multistakeholder dialogue on
Africa's digital transformation, WSIS follow-up and digital-economy policy. A recurring
convener at global internet-governance fora.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Co-organised the WSIS Forum 2026 Africa session (2026-07-10).** With Nigeria's
  **[[nitda]]**, AFICTA convened a WSIS Forum 2026 session arguing Africa already has the
  strategies for digital transformation but must shift from declarations to **measurable
  implementation** of the WSIS+20 outcome document. The session surfaced UNECA's
  ([[uneca]]) "Africa 2035 Digital Implementation Roadmap" and a proposed WSIS
  Implementation Maturity Index, alongside infrastructure-, skills- and inclusion-gap
  arguments. See [[gov.policy]], [[gov.regional]], [[capacity.training]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-10 Africa urged to turn WSIS+20 commitments into measurable digital progress]].
