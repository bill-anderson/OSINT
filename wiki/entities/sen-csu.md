# SEN-CSU — Senegalese Agency for Universal Health Coverage

Senegal's **universal-health-coverage agency** (Agence de la Couverture Maladie Universelle), which digitised
its operations and payments to expand coverage. Developed in-country with technical assistance from the
**[[better-than-cash-alliance]]**, aligning to the UN's 9 Principles for Responsible Digital Payments (five
integrated to date).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Coverage past 50% (2022), driven by digital payments.** After decades stuck below ~10%, health coverage
  passed **50% of citizens in 2022**. Digital payments **halved enrolment costs** and expanded coverage
  nationwide; **women are 85% of beneficiaries**; the platform supports diaspora, rural-mutual and partner
  sponsorship (incl. DER/FJ). Framed as a model for responsible, inclusive digital transformation. See
  [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]], [[SEN]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-01 How has Senegal made responsible digital payments a catalyst for universal health coverage]].
