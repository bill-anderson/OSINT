# Intelsat

Global **satellite communications operator**, appearing in the wiki as a supplier of
satellite-backhaul connectivity for African MNOs seeking to close rural coverage gaps.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Orange Mali 4G-over-satellite (2025-05).** Selected by **[[orange-mali]]** to deliver
  3G/4G to hard-to-reach regions of Mali via satellite — described as "a first in Francophone
  West Africa" for 4G-over-satellite, judged the optimal solution given the country's size and
  logistics; a later account credits it with reaching ~360,000 remote residents. See
  [[MLI]], [[data.satellite]], [[include.divides]], [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-07-techafricanews-orange-mali-intelsat-4g-satellite]].
