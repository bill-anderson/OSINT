# Fonds Souverain de Djibouti (FSD)

Djibouti's **sovereign wealth fund**, an investment vehicle for the country's digital and strategic infrastructure. In May 2024 it entered a joint venture with **[[paix-data-centres|PAIX Data Centres]]** to build the **JIB1** carrier-neutral data centre. FSD's Jean-Andre Gbarssin framed the project as making Djibouti "the heart of Africa's digital economy."
