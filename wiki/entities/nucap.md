# Nigeria Universal Communication Access Project (NUCAP)

Nigerian government connectivity programme to extend mobile coverage to underserved
populations, targeting the country's rural and unconnected [[digital.rural]]
[[include.access]] gap through mass tower deployment.

## Activity in this wiki

- **China Industrial Bank backs ≥1,000 tower sites (2026-06-04).** The bank committed to
  back at least 1,000 of NUCAP's planned 3,700 nationwide towers before end-2026 — its
  first Nigeria investment. NUCAP aims to extend coverage to >20m underserved Nigerians.
  Source: [[2026-06-04 Chinese bank backs Nigeria towers project]].
- **Governance.** The project sits under Minister [[bosun-tijani]] and relates to the
  regulatory remit of the [[ncc]].
  Source: [[2026-06-04 Chinese bank backs Nigeria towers project]].
