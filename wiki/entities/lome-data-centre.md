# Lomé Data Centre (LDC)

Tier III-certified data centre in Lomé, Togo, wholly owned by the Togolese government
via [[sin-togo]] — infrastructure positioned to make [[TGO]] a leading West Africa
digital hub by hosting government and business data in-country.

## Activity in this wiki

- **In-country private cloud + AI services (~June 2026).** The facility is the site of a
  [[sin-togo]]–ST Digital private cloud deployment (see [[sin-st-digital-lome-cloud-deal]]),
  positioned to host government and business data domestically and to offer cloud and
  [[tech.ai]] services rather than routing Togolese workloads offshore.
  Source: [[2026-06-02 SIN, ST Digital to deploy cloud services at Lomé data center]].
