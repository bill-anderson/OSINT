# Hormuud Telecom

Somalia's largest telecom operator (CEO Ahmed Mohamud Yusuf) and a central player in the
country's privately-built digital economy, including the EVC Plus mobile-money service that
underpins Somalia's ~$8bn/yr mobile-money market. Operates in a high-security-risk,
infrastructure-scarce environment.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Green data centres (2024-12-09).** Plans to build more "green" data centres for the
  local and global AI economy; already operates **eleven data centres** with a combined
  **10 MWh** capacity (Bloomberg, via CEO). See [[infra.store]], [[infra.energy]].
- **DARE1 cable (2025-09-04).** A partner (with [[somtel]] and [[djibouti-telecom]]) in the
  36 Tbps **[[dare1-cable]]** submarine system landing at Bosaso and Mogadishu, now being
  extended south toward South Africa. See [[infra.connect]].
