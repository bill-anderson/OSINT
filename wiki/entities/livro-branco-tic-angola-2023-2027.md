# Angola ICT White Paper 2023-2027 (LBTIC)

Angola's **ICT sector strategy**, the *Livro Branco das Tecnologias de Informação e Comunicação* (**LBTIC 2023-2027**), approved **5 December 2024** — "the path to digital acceleration and transformation in Angola." Repeals the 2019-2022 White Paper.

## Profile

- Sets the ICT sector's political/strategic measures, nested within the long-term strategy **ELP Angola 2050**, the **National Development Plan 2023-2027**, AU **Agenda 2063**, ITU **Connect 2030** and **SADC Digital 2027**. Custodian ministry: **MINTTICS** (public consultation Mar 2024). Five 2027 objectives, led by faster/cheaper communications and strategic ICT-infrastructure investment.
- The policy umbrella over Angola's digital-acceleration agenda — the [[infosi]] national DC/cloud and the IMA-led PADA/[[idea-mpa]] project. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]] and the [[AGO]] hub. Source: [[2025-02-18 White Paper on ICT 2023-2027 - Strategic Vision for ICTs in Angola]].
