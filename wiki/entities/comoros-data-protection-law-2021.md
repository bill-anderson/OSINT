# Comoros Protection of Personal Data Law (2021)

The Union of Comoros' **data-protection law**, enacted **29 June 2021** — a comprehensive, GDPR-aligned framework with broad extraterritorial scope. Its defining feature in practice is the **enforcement gap**: the authority it creates is not yet operational.

## Key provisions & status

- Standard principles and lawful bases; privacy notices, **data-protection by design/default**, processor agreements, security, **72-hour breach notification**, a mandatory **DPO** (public bodies + large-scale/sensitive processing), records of processing. Full data-subject rights (access, erasure, portability, no solely-automated decisions). Risk-based **international transfers** (adequacy / safeguards / derogations).
- Establishes a **National Authority for the Protection of Personal Data** — but **as of mid-2024 it had not been constituted or made operational**. The AfDB **[[project-digitization-comorian-economy]]** separately funds standing up such an authority. See [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2025-07-23 Review of the Comoros Data Protection Law]].
