# SoleCrypt–Schneider Electric data-centre MoU

**Parties:** **[[solecrypt]]** (Anglo-Tunisian developer) and **[[schneider-electric]]**.
**What:** a **non-binding MoU** to design and develop **next-generation, AI-dedicated data centres** across
Tunisia — high-energy-efficiency, low-water-consumption architectures. **When:** reported **Feb 2026**.

- **Roles.** SoleCrypt provides strategic, well-connected sites (sub-millisecond latency to Europe);
  **Schneider acts as key technology advisor**, supplying **AI data-centre reference architectures
  co-developed with Nvidia**, plus electrification, secure power, **liquid cooling** and EcoStruxure IT
  infrastructure management.
- **First project.** The **20 MW solar-powered Bizerte data centre** (100% renewable via the 60 MW Tozeur
  T60 solar site) is the flagship instance — see [[solecrypt]], [[tunisia--infra-store]].

*Non-binding MoU — capture is not endorsement; no committed capex or firm timeline is stated.* Source:
[[2026-02-06-dcd-solecrypt-schneider-bizerte-data-center]].
