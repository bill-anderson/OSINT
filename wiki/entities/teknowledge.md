# TeKnowledge

Global expert technology-services company specialising in helping organisations "become AI
First." In Africa it operates as an implementation and delivery partner for [[microsoft]]'s
AI-skilling programs.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Phase 2 delivery partner, Microsoft AI National Skilling Initiative in Nigeria
  (2026-03-09).** TeKnowledge was named delivery/implementation partner for Phase 2 in
  Nigeria ([[NGA]]), committing to **directly train 10,000 participants** (youth, women,
  developers, decision-makers), scaling through **universities and the National Youth Service
  Corps (NYSC)**. Phase 1 (2025) reached **50,000+** Nigerians with foundational/intermediate
  AI skills and produced **3,000+** who earned advanced Microsoft AI certifications; it was
  coordinated with the national **3 Million Technical Talent (3MTT)** initiative and included
  an Agentic AI Hackathon (Microsoft Semantic Kernel) and a career fair. See
  [[capacity.training]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-09 Over 10,000 Nigerians to Receive Advanced AI Training Through TeKnowledge-Microsoft Partnership]].

- **Equinix partnership for AI-ready infrastructure (2026-07).** TeKnowledge and [[equinix]]
  announced a strategic partnership ([[teknowledge-equinix-partnership]]) to expand cloud adoption,
  cybersecurity and **AI-ready, locally-hosted infrastructure** across [[NGA]] and West Africa —
  pitched on data residency and alignment with Nigeria's Digital Transformation Agenda and National
  AI Strategy. See [[infra.store]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[XWA]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 TeKnowledge, Equinix partner to accelerate digital transformation]].
