# Agence Togo Digital (ATD)

Togo's digital-development agency. Launched the **BoD-TPME** SME digital-maturity toolbox (Jun 2026, with
GIZ ProDigiT / Germany-Luxembourg-EU) under the **Togo Digital 2025** strategy (target: 3,000 firms
digitised). See [[togo--dpi-govtech]], [[TGO]].
