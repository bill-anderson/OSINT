# Powertel–Paratus Botswana–Zimbabwe–Zambia fibre corridor

A cross-border fibre spine under a **June 2025 PPP** between **Powertel
Communications** (ZESA's telecom arm; a Mutapa Investment Fund company) and
**[[paratus]]** Zimbabwe.

- **Phase 1 (Plumtree–Bulawayo):** live 2026-07, carrying traffic; **DWDM >10
  Tbps-capable**, initial equipped **800 Gbps**.
- **Phase 2 (Bulawayo–Livingstone, Zambia):** to light up **September 2026**,
  completing the Botswana–Zimbabwe–Zambia corridor (onward to South Africa and the
  wider Paratus network).
- **Model:** equal-investment PPP via an **IRU**, keeping operational control with
  Powertel. Supports Zimbabwe Vision 2030 / Digital Economy Strategy.
