# Ministry of Technology and Science (Zambia)

Zambia's technology ministry (Minister **Felix Chipota Mutati**) — championing local tech firms and a
digital-ID **"single source of truth"**, and regional tech partnerships (Mauritius, Ghana). See
[[zambia--dpi-pay]], [[ZMB]].

Source: [[2026-05-14-zambia-accelerate-digital-economy-ppp]].

## AI mandate (2026-07)

The ministry is the lead department for Zambia's AI agenda. At the **2026-07-17** national
recognition ceremony for Team Zambia's advance to the global finals of the AI for Good / Robotics
for Good Youth Challenge — which it organised with the
**[[presidential-delivery-unit-zambia|PDU]]**, **[[e-mark-innovation-hub]]** and the Education and
Youth ministries — a ministry representative (for the Permanent Secretary, Assistant Director for
Digital Technologies) made three disclosures:

- Commitment to advancing AI through the forthcoming **[[zambia-national-ai-strategy]]** **and a
  Startup Bill** — neither published; no text, date or budget on file.
- Zambia has received an **invitation to the 2027 AI for Good Global Summit**.
- An **opportunity to establish an [[itu]] AI for Good Lab** in Zambia, expected to support AI
  policy development, innovation, skills and digital infrastructure. **An opportunity, not an
  agreement.**
