# Karuma Hydropower Plant (K-HPP)

**Uganda's government-owned 600MW hydropower plant on the River Nile** — and, in this
wiki, the physical and economic premise of the [[aeonian-project]]. It is held here as a
standing asset the compute story keeps returning to: the plant's **surplus
pre-transmission electricity** and its **river water** are what the data centre is being
built to consume.

## Why it matters here

- **Power.** The Aeonian facility is to use **100MW of surplus pre-transmission
  electricity** from the **600MW** plant — power that exists upstream of the transmission
  network, i.e. capacity the grid is not taking. See [[infra.energy]].
- **Cooling.** The facility is **cooled by Nile water**, with modular heat-recycling
  technology.
- **Siting.** The data centre is to be built **at / inside** the plant, on the Nile.
- **Connectivity.** It is to be linked to a **2,500km fibre-optic backbone** reaching
  submarine cables via **Kenya and Tanzania**.
