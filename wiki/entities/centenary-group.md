# Centenary Group

**A Ugandan financial-services group** — parent of **Centenary Bank Uganda** and
**Centenary Technology Services (CTS)** — and the developer of the
**[[centenary-group-data-centre]]** in Masaka City, a bank moving into digital
infrastructure.

## People on the record

- **Prof. John Ddumba Ssentamu** — Chairman, Board of Directors (2025-10).
- **Bwoch Gustavio** — Centenary Bank Board Chairperson (2025-08).
- **Dr Grace Ssekakubo** — Chief Executive, Centenary Technology Services.
- **Peter Kahiigi** — Chief Technology Officer, CTS. **Kimanthi Mutua** — Group Director.
- **Bishop Callist Rubaramira** — Centenary Bank Board Director.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Data-centre delivery (as of 2025-10-03: Q1 2026 launch).** The Board reaffirmed the
  Masaka Tier 3 green data centre as **on schedule for official launch in the first
  quarter of 2026**, billed **Uganda's first Green Tier 3 Data Centre**, and intended to
  **host critical government systems** alongside enterprise and financial services,
  extending beyond Uganda to the wider East African region. Ssentamu: *"This project is a
  foundation for Uganda's digital future."* Source:
  [[2025-10-03 Centenary Group set to launch its Green Tier 3 Data Centre in 2026]].
- **Prior target (2025-08-11): late 2025.** Two months earlier the same publisher reported
  the Board confirming the project **on schedule for completion in late 2025**, targeting
  Tier 3 certification, consuming **40% less power** than conventional data centres,
  **open-access** so all ISPs can offer services through it, and built on a local Masaka
  supply chain. Source:
  [[2025-08-11 Centenary Group affirms data centre will be ready by late 2025]].
- **Supersession, not contradiction.** The delivery date slipped **end-2025 → Q1 2026**.
  Current value plus the one dated prior; see [[centenary-group-data-centre]] for detail.
