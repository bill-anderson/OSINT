# Konoom

A Chadian fintech startup (N'Djamena, launched 2025; CEO Brahim Moussa Hassan), **COBAC-licensed** as a
payment institution. Offers a **multichannel electronic wallet** — mobile app, web, **USSD** (offline)
and **WhatsApp** — for transfers, merchant/bill payments, mass payouts and **QR** acceptance across
wallets, bank accounts and agents. Named in the government's state-payments convention-cadre. See
[[chad--dpi-pay]], [[TCD]].
