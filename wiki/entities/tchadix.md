# TCHADIX (Chad Internet Exchange)

Chad's national internet exchange point, operated by **[[adetic]]**. Marked its first year (May 2026)
localising national internet traffic to reduce dependence on international routes and improve cost and
quality — data governance flagged as a strategic priority. See [[chad--infra-connect]], [[TCD]].
