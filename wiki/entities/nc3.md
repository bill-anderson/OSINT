# NC3 (National Cybersecurity Coordination Centre, Sierra Leone)

Sierra Leone's national cybersecurity coordination body, partnering MoCTI and [[dsti]] on whole-of-
government cyber/AI leadership training ("strong digital armor to protect citizen data and institutional
integrity"). See [[sierra-leone--tech-ai]], [[SLE]].
