# Abiodun Adetona

Founder of **[[decide-ai|Decide]]**, the Nigerian spreadsheet-AI startup he launched in **2025**.
Previously a software engineer at **[[flutterwave]]**.

## Positions on record

- **On the adoption gap (2026-07-17).** *"AI agents are improving rapidly, but their adoption and
  integration into everyday work have not caught up."* His stated strategy follows from it: embed
  the agent **both** where work happens online — "inside spreadsheets, inboxes, and existing
  business tools" — **and physically**, "through the companies, co-working spaces, universities,
  and communities where people work and learn every day."
- **On the [[cafeone]] partnership (2026-07-17).** Framed as a membership-match rather than a
  channel deal: CafeOne's members "closely match the people Decide was built for." Said the
  rollout is a first step, with further co-working spaces, universities, professional communities
  and companies in discussion.
