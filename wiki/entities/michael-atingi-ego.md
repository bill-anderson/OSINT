# Michael Atingi-Ego

**Governor of the [[bank-of-uganda]]** (as of 2025-10) — the voice on the record for
Uganda's payments-modernisation programme.

## Activity in this wiki

- **On the third ACH session (reported 2025-10-06).** Presenting BoU's Integrated Annual
  Report for FY2024/25, Atingi-Ego said: *"We have reduced the time it takes to complete
  electronic fund transfers (EFTs), especially for government payments."* The driver was a
  **third Automated Clearing House session started February 2025**, closing **3:30 p.m.**
  and enabling same-day receipt. Reported in the same account: electronic money
  transactions **+28.6% to UGX 326 trillion**; the **RTGS Replacement and ISO 20022
  Migration** set to go live **November 2025**; the **National Payment Switch** in
  progress; and BoU's plan to **study a CBDC**. See [[bank-of-uganda]], [[eaps]],
  [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-06 BoU's New Payment System Cuts Delays and Boosts E-Money by 28.6%]].
