# Statistics South Africa (Stats SA)

South Africa's national statistics agency (census, CPI, employment).

- **Ransomware attack (Mar 2026).** The **XP95** group breached a Stats SA server, claiming **453,362 files
  / 154 GB** stolen and demanding **$100k (~R1.7m)** — the second major government cyber incident that month,
  raising re-identification concerns over census/survey data. See [[south-africa--gov-protect]],
  [[infra.cybersec]]. Source: [[2026-03-30-statssa-ransomware-attack]].
