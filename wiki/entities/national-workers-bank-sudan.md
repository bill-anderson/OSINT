# National Workers Bank (Sudan)

Sudanese bank (Bank of the National Workers) contracted by the state as the
delivery channel for digital government-revenue collection.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Isali e-collection contract (2026-04).** Signed a contract with the
  **[[ministry-of-finance-sudan]]** to deliver **[[isali]]**, an electronic
  government-revenue collection service letting citizens pay government fees via
  the bank's apps and certified service points — cutting cash reliance and
  improving revenue governance. See [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[SDN]].
