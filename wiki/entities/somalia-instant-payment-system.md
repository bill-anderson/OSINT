# Somalia Instant Payment System (SIPS)

Somalia's first nationwide instant payment system, led by the [[central-bank-of-somalia]]
(CBS) and operated by scheme manager **Somalia Payment Switch (SPS)** in collaboration with
the Somalia Bankers Association. SIPS lets consumers make instant transfers and QR-based
payments from mobile or banking apps, aiming to reduce cash reliance and bridge previously
isolated wallets and the banking sector.

- **Launched January 2025** via a cloud deployment, with plans to move to a **local data
  centre** for resilience.
- Runs on **BPC's SmartVista** platform (Swiss paytech), with participant/dispute portals,
  an integration platform and BPC's fraud-management solution; CBS acts as clearing and
  settlement agent.
- The national QR standard **SOMQR** (launched 2023) routes contactless transactions
  through SmartVista.
- **Seven financial institutions** connected at launch, with six more banks and major
  mobile-money operators expected to join later in 2025.

The state's first credible regulatory foothold over one of Africa's most sophisticated but
long-informal mobile-money economies (~$8bn/yr, ~36% of GDP). See [[somalia--dpi-pay]].
