# Zamengoe Data Centre (CAMTEL)

**[[camtel|CAMTEL]]'s data centre at Zamengoe** — the state's proposed sovereign-hosting pole for
government and institutional data, distinct from the private ST Digital (Douala) facility. Presented by
DG Judith Yah Sunday at **SAGO 2026** as the anchor of Cameroon's digital-sovereignty pitch.

## Profile

- **Corporate claims (unverified).** CAMTEL bills it as **"largest in Central Africa" and Tier III
  certified** — both **PR-wire claims** carried by a byline-free outlet, not independently confirmed. The
  facility is real and tourable (an Ethio Telecom delegation visited, Dec 2025), but the "largest"/"Tier
  III" claims should **not be hardened** without an Uptime Institute record or equivalent (MANIFEST
  Flag 6; a CAMTEL SAGO 2026 primary is the source to obtain). *Capture is not endorsement.* Sources:
  [[2026-06-12-lejour-camtel-zamengoe-datacenter-digital-sovereignty]],
  [[2026-06-12-camtel-zamengoe-data-centre-sovereignty-sago-techreviewafrica]].
- **Institutional buyers courted (Dec 2025).** The BEAC National Director inspected Zamengoe **with a
  view to hosting** — potentially a six-state CEMAC data-sovereignty development, not just CMR's. Source:
  [[2025-12-17-beac-national-director-visits-camtel-data-center-techreviewafrica]].
