# DIAL Digital ID Assessment

The Digital ID Assessment is a self-assessment framework and toolkit for national digital-identity systems, authored by the [[dial]] (Digital Impact Alliance). It comprises a suite of instruments — a Guidebook, a Principles Inventory and a Solution Evaluation — scoring systems across 12 categories on a maturity scale.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Framework structure (as of 2024-01-16).** The toolkit pairs a Guidebook, Principles Inventory and Solution Evaluation, assessing digital-ID systems across 12 categories with maturity scoring. Source: [[2024-01-16 Digital ID]].
- **Ethiopia an early adopter (first used early 2025).** Ethiopia's National ID Program ([[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]]) was among the first to use the assessment and made it an official monitoring mechanism, re-running it in 2026 to track its improvement journey. Source: [[2026-05-12 Strengthening Fayda Ethiopia's improvement journey through the Digital ID Assessment]].
