# Ministry of Education (Tunisia)

**The ministry behind Tunisia's education-sector digitalisation — operator of the
[[ecole-de-la-tunisie-du-futur]] platform at tarbia.tn and sponsor of the "Modern School on Every
Hill" school-connectivity project.** Its stated framing pairs administrative transparency with
closing the rural digital divide.

**Minister on the record here:** **Mohamed Ali Boughdiri** (as of 2024-01).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched "L'école de la Tunisie du futur" (3 January 2024).** The ministry launched a
  comprehensive digital platform for students, parents and teachers at **tarbia.tn**, with dedicated
  portals for each group — streamlining administrative processes, facilitating distance learning and
  offering real-time student monitoring; parents can track attendance, assignments and progress
  reports, educators can manage classes and engage students remotely. See
  [[ecole-de-la-tunisie-du-futur]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.mis]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-08 Tunisia Unveils Digital Platform to Boost Education Transparency, Equity]].
- **Stated rationale (2024-01).** Per Minister **Mohamed Ali Boughdiri**, with the digital
  transition crucial to improving education quality, the platform both promotes transparency and
  efficiency and streamlines essential services — school meals, scholarships and guidance. Source:
  [[2024-01-08 Tunisia Unveils Digital Platform to Boost Education Transparency, Equity]].
- **"Modern School on Every Hill" — a target, not an outcome (as of 2024-01).** The project **aimed
  to connect 3,300 schools to broadband internet by the end of 2024**, to bridge the connectivity
  gap in rural areas. This is the target as stated in January 2024; **no source held confirms
  whether it was met**. See [[infra.connect]], [[include.divides]], [[digital.rural]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-08 Tunisia Unveils Digital Platform to Boost Education Transparency, Equity]].
