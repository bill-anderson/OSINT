# Econet Group

**Strive Masiyiwa**'s pan-African group — parent of **[[econet-wireless-zimbabwe]]**, **[[cassava-technologies]]**
and **[[liquid-c2]]** (distinct from the Zimbabwe opco; ISSUE-008/009). Its **Liquid C2** launched Africa's
first **Google Cloud Partner Experience Centre** (Johannesburg, 2026), exporting Zimbabwean-owned digital
infrastructure across Africa. See [[zimbabwe--tech-ai]], [[ZWE]].
