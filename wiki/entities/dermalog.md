# DERMALOG

German biometric-identity firm — the DRC's **biometric passport supplier** (a track the wiki had no
material on), replacing Semlex.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DRC biometric passport (2022 contract, $48m).** Cut the passport price to **$75** (from Semlex's $185,
  then $99 in 2019), added **online enrolment**, deployed **50 fixed/mobile stations + 50 at diplomatic
  missions**, to **ICAO ISO/IEC 39794**, capacity **2,400 passports/day**. Source:
  [[2025-06-02-drc-cuts-biometric-passport-price-dermalog-biometricupdate]].
