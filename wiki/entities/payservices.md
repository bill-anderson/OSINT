# PayServices

Idaho-based US fintech that markets electronic collections, interoperable
payment rails and public-sector digitisation. It says it has worked on digital
payments and government-finance modernisation in markets including Nigeria,
Ghana and Rwanda, and was contracted in late 2023 to modernise the Democratic
Republic of the Congo's state banking and payments systems.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Suing the DRC over a collapsed banking-modernisation project (~2026-01).**
  PayServices filed suit in US federal court against the DRC and senior
  officials, alleging its flagship $72m project to modernise state banking and
  payments was deliberately sabotaged. Details and claims are tracked on the deal
  page [[payservices-drc-banking-deal]] ([[COD]], [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.govtech]]).
  Source: [[2026-01-19 US fintech sues DR Congo for $400m over failed banking deal]].
