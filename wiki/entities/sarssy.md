# SARSSy (Southern African Regional Submarine System)

A regional subsea system linking Southern Africa's west coast, built to **interconnect with Google's
[[equiano-cable]]** via **[[telecom-namibia]]**'s **Swakopmund** landing station and add international
capacity to Namibia, Angola and neighbours.

## The tripartite MSA (2026)

- **Master Service Agreement signed 12 June 2026** by **[[telecom-namibia]]**, **[[angola-telecom]]**
  and **SATCAB** (Satélite e Cabo TV, S.A.), building on an **April 2026 MoU/CTA** at Swakopmund
  (witnessed by both countries' ICT ministers) — moving from intent to implementation.
- **Telecom Namibia** is the **landing party**: it hosts and supports the infrastructure at
  Swakopmund, including **Angola Telecom's equipment**, and provides an **initial dedicated 1.0 Tbps**
  of capacity on the Equiano system.
- The model lets **Angola Telecom expand capacity and potentially assume ownership** of its
  infrastructure components over time — a South–South, sovereignty-framed regional corridor aligned
  with Namibia's Vision 2030/NDP6 and Angola's digital-sovereignty ambitions.
