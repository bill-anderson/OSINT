# Liquid Intelligent Technologies

Pan-African connectivity, cloud, cybersecurity, data-centre and digital-payments provider, a subsidiary of
**[[cassava-technologies]]**. Liquid Tanzania CEO (2026): **Manish Govindji**.

- **Government alignment (Jun 2026).** Liquid Tanzania reaffirmed support for Tanzania's **Digital Economy
  Strategic Framework 2024–2034** after a meeting with Minister **Angellah Kairuki** (Dodoma, 16 Jun 2026)
  spanning connectivity, cloud, cybersecurity, data centres and payments. See [[tanzania--infra-store]].
  Source: [[2026-06-19-liquid-tanzania-government-align-digital-transformation]].
- **$855m recapitalisation (Apr 2026).** Completed an **$855m** recapitalisation/debt-refinancing — fresh
  equity from parent **[[cassava-technologies]]** plus a Eurobond listed on Euronext Dublin. Source:
  [[2026-06-19-liquid-tanzania-government-align-digital-transformation]].
