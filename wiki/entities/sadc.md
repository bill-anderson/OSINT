# Southern African Development Community (SADC)

The **Southern African Development Community** — the 16-member Southern-African regional economic
community (region [[XSA]]) coordinating regional integration across infrastructure, trade and,
increasingly, the digital economy. In this wiki it is the issuing body for two reference instruments
and the regional frame for Southern-African connectivity/payments items.

## Reference instruments (held in this wiki)

- **[[sadc-ridmp]]** — the Regional Infrastructure Development Master Plan (Aug 2012), the
  foundational SADC infrastructure blueprint (energy, transport, ICT, water, meteorology).
- **[[sadc-digital-transformation-strategy]]** — the draft SADC Digital Transformation Strategy &
  Action Plan (12 Oct 2022), aligning the regional digital agenda to the RISDP 2020-30 and the
  AU-DTS 2030.
