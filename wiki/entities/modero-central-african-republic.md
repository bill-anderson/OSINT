# MODERO (EU/CIVIPOL civil-registration modernisation, CAR)

An **EU-funded civil-registration modernisation programme** — *Appui à la Modernisation, à la Sécurisation
et à l'Accessibilité du Système d'État Civil en RCA* — implemented by **CIVIPOL** (the French interior
ministry's operator) with CAR's territorial-administration ministry (**MATDDL**). It is an order of
magnitude larger than the wiki's previously-known CRVS thread (the OIF's 3,000-certificate project) and
brings the **EU** onto CAR's identity stack. See [[CAF]], [[caf--dpi-id]], [[dpi.registry]].

## What we know (as of 2026-02)

- **Scale + target.** [[include.access]] A steering committee (**CTAS**) validated CIVIPOL's 2025 work
  (~90% complete) — digital case-management tools, data-security hardening, in-/out-of-time birth-act
  attribution, wider access — moving from a Bangui-corridor pilot toward national scale with a **target of
  400,000 birth certificates by 2028**. DG Décentralisation/État Civil **Jean Symphorien Ngama-Piault**
  chairs; diaspora civil-status demand is a live workstream. Source:
  [[2026-02-23-modero-etat-civil-eu-civipol-oubanguimedias]].
