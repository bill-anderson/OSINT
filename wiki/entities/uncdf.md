# UNCDF (United Nations Capital Development Fund)

UN agency providing capital financing and technical assistance for financial inclusion and
digital-finance development in least-developed and low-income countries.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gabon financial-inclusion partnership (8 Dec 2025).** UNCDF agreed to support Gabon in modernising
  its financial-services ecosystem and raising financial inclusion — **~54% of Gabonese adults are
  unbanked** (World Bank Global Findex 2025) despite growing mobile money. Work aligns with the
  **PNCD 2026-2030** national plan and **CEMAC's Regional Financial Inclusion Strategy (2023-2027)**,
  focusing on provider competition, digital infrastructure and consumer protection; UNDP facilitates.
  Deal: [[gabon-uncdf-financial-inclusion-deal]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-12-gabon-uncdf-financial-inclusion-deal]].
