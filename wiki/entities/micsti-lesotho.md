# MICSTI (Ministry of Information, Communications, Science, Technology & Innovation, Lesotho)

Lesotho's ICT ministry, leading the design of the country's digital public
infrastructure, working with the **National University of Lesotho (NUL)**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DPI review with NUL (2026-02).** MICSTI (delegation led by Principal
  Secretary **Kanono Ramashamole**) convened with NUL's Department of Mathematics
  and Computer Science to review progress on three core systems: **X-Road** for
  secure government-to-government interoperability and information sharing;
  **[[mosip]]** for a biometric digital ID improving access to public services;
  and a **Decision Support System (DSS)** for data-driven planning and budgeting.
  The engagement stressed government–academia collaboration to build secure,
  citizen-focused digital systems. See [[LSO]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.id]],
  [[dpi.govtech]], [[sovereignty]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-20 MICSTI AND NUL ADVANCED LESOTHO DIGITAL PUBLIC INFRASTRUCTURE]].
