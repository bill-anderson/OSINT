# Eutelsat

European satellite operator supplying connectivity capacity across Africa, notably through its
KONNECT satellite. In the continent's connectivity market it represents a GEO alternative in the
LEO-vs-GEO mix, positioning satellite as a bridge for the digital divide via partnerships with
mobile operators (cf. [[starlink]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Partner behind Orange Sat in Côte d'Ivoire (2026-01).** Eutelsat is the satellite partner for
  [[orange-cote-divoire]]'s [[orange-sat]] service, providing KONNECT capacity to reach rural
  areas beyond 4G and fibre. Philippe Baudrier, Eutelsat's VP for connectivity in Africa, framed
  the deal as an example of satellite bridging the digital divide through alliances. Tags
