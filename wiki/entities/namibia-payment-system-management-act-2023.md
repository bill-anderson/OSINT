# Namibia Payment System Management Act 14 of 2023

Namibia's **payments statute** (Act 14 of 2023, in force 8 August 2023), providing for the establishment,
management, regulation, oversight and supervision of **payment, clearing and settlement systems** in Namibia.
It gives the **[[bank-of-namibia|Bank of Namibia]]** the legal basis to designate and supervise payment
systems and underpins the country's move toward an **instant-payment solution** and modernised retail rails.
