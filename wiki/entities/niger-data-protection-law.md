# Niger Data Protection Law (Loi 2017-28, as amended)

Niger's **personal-data-protection statute** — **Loi n° 2017-28** of 3 May 2017 relating to the protection of
personal data, **amended by Loi n° 2019-71** of 24 December 2019 (with further ordinances since). It sets the
principles and obligations for processing personal data and establishes the supervisory
**[[hapdp-niger|High Authority (HAPDP)]]**. It is the legal backdrop against which Niger's fast-moving
**[[niger-national-eid|biometric e-ID and e-passport]]** programme collects and processes citizen data.
