# iXAfrica–Oracle OCI Nairobi region

A host-partner agreement (announced **2026-01-28**) under which **[[ixafrica-data-centres]]**
will host the **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI)** region in Nairobi — **Kenya's first
public cloud region**. President **[[william-ruto]]** first announced the region in 2024;
[[oracle]] selected iXAfrica as its colocation partner.

## Profile

- iXAfrica is described as **East and Central Africa's largest hyperscale, carrier-neutral,
  AI-ready** facility; construction, power and connectivity infrastructure were in advanced
  stages of deployment at announcement.
- Framed around building a **"sovereign, globally competitive digital economy"** in Kenya —
  local hosting to cut latency and keep AI-ready workloads onshore.
- Quotes: **Snehar Shah** (iXAfrica CEO), **David Bunei** (Oracle country leader, Kenya).
- Sits within Kenya's broader hyperscale-cloud drive (cf. the stalled
  [[microsoft-g42-kenya-datacenter]]; [[servernah-cloud]] sovereign AI cloud).
