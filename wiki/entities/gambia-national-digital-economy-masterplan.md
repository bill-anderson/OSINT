# Gambia National Digital Economy Master Plan 2024-2034

The Gambia's overarching ten-year **digital-economy strategy** (Ministry of Communications and Digital
Economy) — the umbrella instrument tying together connectivity, digital government, digital ID, payments
and digital skills ([[gov.policy]]). It frames the country's 2024-26 wave of concrete reforms: the
BANTABA payment switch ([[gamswitch]]), the digital ID strategy ([[gambia-national-digital-id-strategy]]),
GRA e-invoicing, and the data-protection regime ([[gambia-data-protection-act-2025]]). See [[GMB]].
