# Banco de Moçambique (BdM)

Mozambique's **central bank** (Governor Rogério Zandamela) and regulator of the national payment system —
driving the instant-payments rail (**METIX/SPIM**) and the modernised National Payments System (SNP) law.
See [[dpi.pay]], [[mozambique--dpi-pay]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **METIX / SPIM instant payments (2026-03).** [[dpi.pay]] The BdM established the **Instant Payment
  System (SPIM, Notice 1/GB/2026)** and launched it as **METIX** — integrating banks and mobile wallets
  (M-Pesa, e-Mola, m-Kesh) for **real-time transfers**, **free interbank P2P** for individuals; operated
  by **[[simo-mozambique|SIMO]]**. Source: [[2026-03-16-central-bank-launches-metix-clubofmozambique]].
- **National Payments System (SNP) law (2026).** [[gov.legislate]] Parliament **unanimously approved** the
  revised SNP law (adjusting BdM powers, reducing risk, adding **AI/technology** security provisions), then
  promulgated alongside the cyber laws (Jun). New **National Switch connection rules (Notice 2/GBM/2026)**
  require institutions to **report payment-service failures within 60 minutes**. Sources:
  [[2026-04-15-national-payments-system-law-approved-parliament-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-06-16-financial-institutions-60-minutes-report-failures-clubofmozambique]].
- **Financial-inclusion via e-money (2025).** [[include.access]] E-money accounts reached **109.8% of
  adults (Q1-2025, from 28.6% in 2015)** — 100% e-money coverage despite 26 regions with no bank branch —
  at the launch of the **National Financial Inclusion Strategy 2025-2031**. Source:
  [[2025-08-06-e-money-accounts-109pct-adult-population-clubofmozambique]].
