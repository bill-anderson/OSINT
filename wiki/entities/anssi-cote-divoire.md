# ANSSI (National Information Systems Security Agency)

Côte d'Ivoire's **national cybersecurity agency** — created to centralise the country's cyber-defence,
and the institutional gap the hub previously had under "National Cybersecurity Strategy." Under the
Minister of Digital Transition (Djibril Ouattara).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Creation & launch (2024–25).** Created by decree of the **30 Oct 2024** Council of Ministers and
  **officially launched Feb 2025**, centralising the existing **PLCC** (cybercrime-fighting platform),
  **CI-CERT** (incident response) and **Alertes100**. Source: [[2025-02-11-anssi-lancement-fratmat]].
- **Mandate shift from ARTCI (Ordinance 2024-950).** Parliament (National Assembly then Senate, Apr–Jun
  2025) ratified **Ordinance n°2024-950**, moving the cybersecurity mandate **from [[artci|ARTCI]] to
  ANSSI** and repealing parts of the 2013 e-transactions law — a hard institutional/legislative event.
  Sources: [[2025-04-28-digital-security-framework-wearetech]], [[2025-06-17-senate-approves-cybersecurity-egov-bill-techreviewafrica]].
- **Threat & enforcement magnitudes (2024).** Cybercrime enforcement handled **12,100 dossiers for a
  ~7bn FCFA loss**; Kaspersky reported **>27 million attack attempts detected in CIV in 2024**
  (vendor-sourced). Sources: [[2025-05-22-cybercriminalite-rapport-2024-anssi-fratmat]],
  [[2025-06-11-kaspersky-27-millions-attaques-fratmat]].
- **Capability build-out (2026).** A real-time threat-hunting **SOC** (May 2026); a **national CISO/RSSI
  network** launched (Jun 2026) to institutionalise security roles across the administration; and a
  governance/accountability framing of cyber-defence (Jul 2026). Sources:
  [[2026-05-19-cybersecurite-centre-soc-cybermenaces-temps-reel-fratmat]],
  [[2026-06-10-lancement-reseau-national-rssi-fratmat]],
  [[2026-06-11-premier-atelier-reseau-national-rssi-linfodrome]],
  [[2026-07-11-cybersecurite-gouvernance-responsabilite-fratmat]].
- **Regional cooperation.** Togo–CIV talks (Lawson–Konaté) on regulation, cybersecurity and
  critical-infrastructure protection. Source: [[2025-02-10-togo-digital-cooperation-wearetech]].
