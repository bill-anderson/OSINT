# National Registration Bureau (Malawi)

Malawi's statutory identity and civil-registration authority (Principal Secretary
**Patrick Machika**), custodian of the national ID and registration system and the
lead agency for the country's foundational-identity drive. Operates under the
**National Registration Act** and is a core participant in the World Bank-funded
**[[dmap]]** (Digital Malawi Acceleration Project); its registers are among the
services already onboarded to the **Bomalathu** single digital-services platform.

## Activity in this wiki

- **13.5m trusted digital IDs; ~99% of adults (2026-07).** The NRB's enrolment drive
  has issued national IDs to **>13.5m** citizens, with **~99% of Malawians aged 16+**
  now holding one — near-universal foundational coverage. Over **150,000** were enrolled
  April–June 2026, **53% of them women** (narrowing the gender gap). Backed by **[[undp]]**,
  the EU, Norway and Irish Aid; builds on the 2017 mass-registration campaign (~9.2m) and a
  five-year universal-legal-identity blueprint launched in 2025. See [[MWI]], [[dpi.id]],
  [[include.access]], [[malawi--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 Malawi reaches 13.5M digital IDs as universal coverage comes into view]].
- **Transition to mobile-accessible digital ID cards (2026-07).** The NRB is steering a
  move to **digital national ID cards accessible via mobile phone**, framed as reducing
  delays in issuing physical IDs and improving registration efficiency. It is concurrently
  driving a review of the **National Registration Act** and the **Marriage, Divorce and
  Family Relations Act** (stakeholder consultation, Lilongwe) to underpin a modern civil-
  registration and identification system — work sitting inside the **[[dmap]]**. See
  [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-13 Malawi outlines plans to transition to digital IDs - CAJ News Africa]].

- **Mass birth-registration campaign (2026-02).** With the **EU** (a 10-year civil-registration
  partnership begun 2016), Norway, Ireland and **[[undp]]**, the NRB launched phase 1 of a **mass birth-
  registration campaign** to register **4.6m children** and close the gap to universal coverage — after
  achieving **full adult registration in 2024**. The national ID already anchors voter registration, social-
  protection targeting and payroll. See [[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-04 10 years of a fruitful partnership with the Government of Malawi on civil registration]].
- **ID4Africa 2025 milestone; IDT4M.** At the ID4Africa 2025 AGM the NRB reported **~12.5m** enrolled and a
  planned full digital-ID (wallet) rollout by 2026; the drive is supported by the EU/UNDP
  **[[idt4m]]** programme (€10m to the registration foundation). Sources:
  [[2025-05-28 Malawi nears full scale digital ID rollout to streamline access to services]],
  [[2025-05-21 NRB to roll out digital ID by 2026]],
  [[2024-05-14 Malawi on path to inclusion with digital ID project supported by EU, UNDP]].
