# National Bank of Ethiopia (NBE)

Ethiopia's central bank and the architect of its digital-payments transformation (Governors **Mamo Mihretu**; **Eyob Tekalign**). Launched the **National Digital Payments Strategy** (2021, and the 2026–2030 successor), licenses non-bank **Payment Instrument Issuers / Payment System Operators** (Telebirr, M-PESA, Kacha, Arif Pay, Chapa, etc.), and owns a stake in the national switch **[[ethswitch]]**. Drove the **mandatory integration of all bank accounts with the [[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]] digital ID** (directive Dec 2025, deadline 30 Mar 2026). Anchors payments policy under **[[digital-ethiopia-2030]]**.
