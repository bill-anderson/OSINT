# Africa AI Council

A continental **AI governance and innovation body** unveiled by the **[[smart-africa]]** Board (Transform
Africa Summit, Conakry, Nov 2025) — chaired by Rwanda's President **Kagame**, with **seven African ICT
ministers** (incl. Cina Lawson, Bosun Tijani, William Kabogo, Paula Ingabire) and **eight independent members**
(from Gates Foundation, Orange, Microsoft, Google). Provides "strategic, actionable recommendations" for an
"AI-driven future rooted in African values" (Smart Africa CEO Lacina Koné). See [[XAF]].
