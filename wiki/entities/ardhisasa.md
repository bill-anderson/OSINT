# Ardhisasa (Kenya digital land-administration platform)

Kenya's **homegrown digital land-administration platform**, built on the **National Land Information
Management System (NLIMS)** — enabling land searches, transfers, subdivisions and other services online
([[dpi.registry]], [[digital.localgov]]). It is being rolled out county by county under the State
Department for Lands, framed as streamlining land governance and addressing historical land injustices.
See [[KEN]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **County rollout (2025).** Isiolo County onboarded (Blocks 1-10, 31 Jul 2025), following Mombasa
  Island Blocks XVI/XVII; Marsabit and Baringo (Koibatek) next — with national-county data sharing
  emphasised. Source: [[2025-07-31 Isiolo County Onboarded onto Ardhisasa as More Counties Embrace the Platform]].
