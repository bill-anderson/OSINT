# Registrar-General of Zimbabwe

**The office responsible for Zimbabwe's national population registry and the issuance of
identity documents**, under the [[ministry-of-home-affairs-zimbabwe]], and the operational
owner of a digital ID rollout that had not begun as of the last date held.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Stated readiness ahead of digital ID (2025-07-15).** **Registrar-General Henry
  Machiri** said personnel **had already been trained** and **other material items were
  already in place** ahead of the digital ID exercise. This is a readiness claim, not a
  delivery: the same piece records **no official indication of when issuance would start**,
  after a May 2025 announcement that it would begin in July 2025. See [[dpi.id]],
  [[capacity.training]]. Source:
  [[2025-07-15 Zimbabwe looks forward to digital ID rollout after population registry reform]].
- **Population registry reform (~2023 onward).** The digital ID plan rests on a
  modernisation begun around **2023** — upgrading the national population registry and
  linking it to other services, with an integrated digital system announced to be built on
  the upgraded register. **The register's state, size and completeness are not held.** See
  [[dpi.registry]]. Source: as above.
- **Mobile registration campaigns.** The office's route to ID adoption has been mobile
  registration campaigns; the Binga exercise (July 2025) run by the
  [[civil-registry-department-zimbabwe]] with [[unicef]] and the Embassy of Sweden is the
  one instance held in detail. Source: as above.

## Provenance warning

All of the above is relayed second-hand by Biometric Update from local media. **No
coverage figure for national ID exists in held material**, and the readiness quote is the
office's own account of itself.
