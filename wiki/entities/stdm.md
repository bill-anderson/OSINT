# Social Tenure Domain Model (STDM)

**A GLTN land-information tool conforming to international land administration
standards**, which provides a flexible way of **recording people-to-land
relationships** — rather than only the surveyed-parcel-and-title relationships that
conventional cadastres capture. That design is what makes it usable for customary tenure,
where rights are social before they are cartographic.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Uganda's digital Certificate of Customary Ownership (year-precision, proxy date —
  implementer's own account, attribute).** [[gltn]] / [[un-habitat]] and
  [[mlhud-uganda]] built Uganda's **digital CCO template on STDM**, replacing manually
  typewritten certificates. The template was approved by MLHUD and **gazetted in the
  Uganda Gazette (notice of 18 September 2020)**, adding a **unique QR code**, a parcel
  map, provision for Area Land Committee signatures and stamp, and a Uganda Coat of Arms
  watermark. Context: approximately **80% of Uganda's land is held under customary
  tenure**, and **over 7,684 parcels were registered for CCOs** through the GLTN–MLHUD
  partnership since **2014**. *The GLTN blog is undated; the 2024 year rests on an intake
  assertion, and the only hard in-body date is the 2020 Gazette notice — do not reason
  about it as a 2024 datapoint.* See [[dpi.registry]], [[digital.rural]],
  [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Uganda Moves to Digital Certificates of Customary Ownership to secure land rights and improve land use]].
