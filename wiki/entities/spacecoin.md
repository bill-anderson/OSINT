# Spacecoin

A satellite network operator building **blockchain-integrated, decentralised** satellite
communications infrastructure aimed at low-cost "permissionless" connectivity in areas where
terrestrial networks are limited. Founder **Tae Oh**. It positions itself as an open-source
alternative to dominant LEO players — notably **[[starlink]]** ([[spacex]]), present in 26
nations.

## Profile

- Launched **three additional satellites** in late November 2025.
- **Kenya (as of 2026-01-13):** secured a transmission licence with the
  **[[communications-authority-of-kenya]]** for satellite IoT monitoring and connectivity
  expansion.
- **Nigeria:** continues work under an existing **[[ncc]]** licence.
- **Asia (non-African, [[XGL]]):** connectivity work in Indonesia and an alliance with
  Cambodia's MekongNet for rural satellite internet.
- Model: Spacecoin provides the core satellite technology/infrastructure while local partners
  handle ground operations and user support; proof-of-concept demonstrations of decentralised
  telecoms.
