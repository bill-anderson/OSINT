# LINX Nairobi (Internet Exchange Point)

The **London Internet Exchange (LINX)**'s African IXP presence, entered via **Nairobi in 2023** (plus a
Mombasa exchange from 2025) — interconnected peering points accessible from multiple Nairobi data centres,
keeping East-African traffic local. See [[dpi.exchange]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Peering expansion (2026-07).** [[infra.connect]] LINX added regional cloud provider **[[angani]]** to
  its Nairobi hub. The Nairobi platform now supports **55+ active networks** (incl. **TikTok** and
  **Starlink**); combined Nairobi+Mombasa traffic grew **20× in a year, peaking >330Gbps**. Mombasa
  serves as a subsea-cable content gateway. Source: [[2026-07-10-linx-kenya-peering-network-itweb]].
