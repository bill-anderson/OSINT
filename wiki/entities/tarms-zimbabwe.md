# TaRMS Self-Service Portal (Zimbabwe)

**[[zimra]]'s online taxpayer registration and management portal**, at
`mytaxselfservice.zimra.co.zw` — the digital front door to Zimbabwe's tax system and the
issuing point for the **Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN)**. A standing government
platform worth returning to, profiled here from ZIMRA's own undated guidance page.

## What it does

- **Issues the TIN.** A Taxpayer Identification Number is allocated on successful
  registration, after review by a ZIMRA official with approval rights; applicants can
  trace the approval on the applications page.
- **Registration is mandatory** for all persons carrying out any trade or business in
  Zimbabwe, including Authorised Economic Operators.
- **Four registration categories:** Register as an Individual; as an Organization; as a
  **Representative of a Deceased Taxpayer**; or **with an existing BP Number** — the last
  confirming **migration from a legacy Business Partner numbering system**.
- **Flow:** sign up (personal details, username, email, phone, password) → complete the
  category form and attach supporting documents → submit for approval → obtain TIN →
  select the bank used for payments.
- **Unlocks** e-filing, e-payments and automated tax clearance issuance.
