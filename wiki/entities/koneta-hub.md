# Koneta Hub

A South Sudanese technology and policy hub / civil-society organisation engaged
in digital-rights and data-governance advocacy. Executive Director: **Denaya
Dennis**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Data-protection convening (2025-10).** Co-hosted, with
  **[[paradigm-initiative]]**, a two-day Juba convening on South Sudan's draft
  Data Protection Bill under PIN's Digital Policy Engagement Series (DiPES).
  Denaya Dennis noted the country lacks a comprehensive legal and institutional
  framework for personal-data protection and that existing ICT regulations are
  limited. Source:
  [[2025-10-17 Paradigm Initiative partners with Koneta Hub on data protection law in South Sudan]].
