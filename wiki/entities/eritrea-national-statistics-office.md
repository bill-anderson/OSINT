# Eritrea National Statistics Office (NSO)

Eritrea's **national statistical authority**, responsible for the country's population,
health and economic data. Under the **Joint Programme on Data for Development (D4D)** —
with **[[undp]]** and **UNECA** support and technical input from Mikro International
Consultancy Service (Oslo) — the NSO reported accelerated momentum in 2025–26 in
modernising national data systems (see [[data.statistics]], [[capacity.research]],
[[ERI]]).

Its flagship achievement was completion of the **Fourth Round Eritrea Population and
Health Survey (EPHS 2025)** — the most comprehensive such survey in over a decade,
covering all six regions, **9,794 households across 405 Enumeration Areas** at a
**98.4% completion rate**, using **CAPI** digital data collection to replace decades of
paper. It also **compiled 2023 national accounts** and began 2024 national-accounts
work, with a planned **Eritrea Household Income and Expenditure Survey (EHIS)** next.
