# ZEEDE-GE (Djibloho digital economic zone)

A **digital economic zone in Djibloho** (draft decree, Mar 2026) to make it Equatorial Guinea's "digital
epicentre" — offering global connectivity plus integrated digital services around **AI, big data and
high-performance data centres**, to accelerate national digitalisation and e-commerce. Presented alongside sector
revenue figures (transport/telecom revenue up 48% YoY; ambition to grow the sector from XAF 3bn to 12bn/yr) and
the Maritime / Foreign-Trade single windows. See [[gnq--infra-store]].
