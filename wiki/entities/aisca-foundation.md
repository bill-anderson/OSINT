# AISCA Foundation

The AI Skills & Compute Africa (AISCA) Foundation launched in Kigali (aiscafrica.org) with [[cassava-technologies]] as founding technology partner. It matters as the skills-and-access layer sitting atop Cassava's sovereign compute.

## Profile
- Launched in Kigali; [[cassava-technologies]] is founding technology partner.
- Four pillars: Compute (sovereign compute on African soil); Curated data (African-language datasets across agriculture, health, finance, education and the creative economy); Capacity (tiered AI skills to move 1 million youth into work); Community (universities and ventures via "AISCA Houses and Studios").
- Grantees receive "AI Tokens" for compute access rather than cash; an inaugural cohort of 10 grantees was shortlisted.
- Sits atop the [[cassava-ai-factories]] compute.
