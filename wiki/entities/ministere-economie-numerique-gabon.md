# Ministère de l'Économie numérique, de la Digitalisation et de l'Innovation (Gabon)

Gabon's digital-economy ministry — the policy principal for the country's digitalisation and
innovation agenda, above **[[aninf-gabon]]** as the implementing agency. Minister:
**[[mark-alexandre-doumba]]**.

The ministry is the counterparty on Gabon's recent run of partnership agreements
(**[[gabon-huawei-mou]]**, **[[st-digital-gabon]]**, **[[gabon-visa-partnership-deal]]**,
**[[gabon-uncdf-financial-inclusion-deal]]**) — a signature-led pattern in which the state convenes
external technical and financial partners around a domestic ecosystem it does not itself fund.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Two start-up accords signed (2026-07-14).** Signed **two separate agreements** — with the
  **[[societe-gabonaise-de-garantie]]** on credit guarantees
  (**[[gabon-sgg-startup-guarantee-accord]]**) and with **Huawei Technologies Gabon** on technical
  capacity (**[[gabon-huawei-kimba-connect-accord]]**) — on the margins of the launch of
  **[[kimba-connect]]**. Doumba framed the pair as support going *beyond simple project financing*.
  Source: [[2026-07-17-gabon-garantie-publique-huawei-start-up]].
