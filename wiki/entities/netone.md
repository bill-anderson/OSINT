# NetOne

Zimbabwe's **state-owned** mobile operator (2nd by network; placed the country's first mobile call, 1996),
under the **[[mutapa-investment-fund]]** sovereign-fund portfolio. CEO **Raphael Mushanawani**.

- **Turnaround (2025).** From technical insolvency (US$40m loss, 2022) to **US$210m revenue (+62%)**, EBITDA
  +68%, mobile data +110% — targeting **US$1bn by 2029**; **46%+ of rural mobile base stations**; flagship
  **OneHealth** telemedicine (20+ centres, virtual hospital Ntunungwe, with the Office of the First Lady). See
  [[zimbabwe--infra-connect]]. Source: [[2026-07-15-netone-at-30-digital-future]].
- **Network (Q4 2025):** 4,838 base stations (POTRAZ). Source: [[2026-04-30-econet-5g-exceeds-netone-telecel-combined]].
