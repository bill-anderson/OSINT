# Bank of Sierra Leone (BSL)

Sierra Leone's central bank and the driver of financial inclusion since 2008 — the **National Financial
Inclusion Strategy**, the **National Switch** (the interoperable **[[sierra-leone-instant-payment-switch|Instant
Payment Switch]]**), and a **Regulatory Sandbox** that incubates fintech products in real time. Inclusion
rose from **18% (2018) to ~40% (2025)**, with 1m+ IPS transactions. See [[sierra-leone--dpi-pay]], [[SLE]].
