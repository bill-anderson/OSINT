# Maroc Telecom

Moroccan telecom group operating across Africa under the **Moov Africa** brand (present in
~11 countries, ~77m customers). In the wiki it appears chiefly through its Malian subsidiary
**[[sotelma]]** (Moov Africa Malitel).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Reduced to minority in SOTELMA (2026-06).** After buying **51%** of **[[sotelma]]** for
  ~€275m in **2009**, Maroc Telecom was cut to a **minority shareholder** when Mali's state
  raised its stake to 56% in a ~$277m 2026 deal (**[[sotelma-state-majority-2026]]**) — an
  AES-aligned economic-sovereignty reclaim. See [[MLI]], [[tech.industry]], [[sovereignty]].
  Source: [[2026-06-25-businessinsider-mali-regains-sotelma-majority-stake]].
- **Moov Money–Mastercard card, Mali (2026-04).** Through Moov Africa Malitel, launched a
  co-branded prepaid Mastercard at GITEX Africa 2026 (with Mastercard's DG North-West Africa),
  positioned within BCEAO/WAEMU digital-payment interoperability. See [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-09-le360-moov-money-mali-carte-prepayee-mastercard-gitex]].
