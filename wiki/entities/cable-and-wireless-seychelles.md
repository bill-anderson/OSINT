# Cable & Wireless Seychelles (CWS)

Seychelles' pioneering incumbent quad-play operator; first to launch **5G** (Mahé, 2020, via Huawei).
Signed an MoU with China's **Hytera** (MWC 2025) to build a **resilient emergency-communications
redundant network** (PoC/MCX) for public safety and disaster resilience. See
[[seychelles--infra-connect]], [[SYC]].
