# tpay (TPay Mobile)

Mobile-payments company (Group CEO **Isik Uman**) that secured **Egypt's first Direct Carrier Billing (DCB) licence** from the **[[ntra-egypt|NTRA]]** (Dec 2025) to enable **government payments (P2G)** — utility bills, traffic fines, civil-registry services — charged to a user's **mobile balance or monthly bill**, extending payment access to phone owners without bank accounts or cards.
