# International Centre for Tax and Development (ICTD)

A research organisation working on **tax policy and administration in lower-income countries**, led
(2026-07) by Executive Director **Giulia Mascagni**. It partners with revenue authorities on
research, capacity building and the use of administrative tax data for evidence-based policy.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Zambia Revenue Authority partnership (2026-07-17).** ICTD signed an **MoU and NDA** with the
  **[[zambia-revenue-authority|ZRA]]** ([[zra-ictd-mou]]) focused on **research, capacity building
  and innovation** to strengthen tax administration and **domestic resource mobilisation**. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-zambia-revenue-authority-ictd-deal]].

*Capture is not endorsement.* The wiki holds a single source on ICTD, from the counterparty
announcement; no independent account of its funding, governance or data-handling arrangements with
African revenue authorities is on file.
