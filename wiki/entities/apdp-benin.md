# APDP (Autorité de Protection des Données à caractère Personnel, Benin)

Benin's independent **data-protection authority**, created by the **Code du numérique** (loi 2017-20, as
amended by loi 2020-35 of 6 Jan 2021). President: **Me Luciano Hounkponou**. The APDP is among Africa's
more **operational and outward-facing** DPAs — a working enforcer with hard annual numbers, an explicit
AI stance, and a visible regional/international leadership role — the rights-side counterweight to Benin's
fast digital-ID and e-government build. See [[BEN]], [[gov.protect]], [[benin--dpi-id]].

## Profile

- **Mandate + powers.** An independent administrative authority (legal personality, no political
  instruction), composed of 8 members (3 deputies, 2 electronic-communications experts, 1 presidential
  appointee, 1 magistrate, 1 senior lawyer). Powers span **control and sanction** — administrative
  (warning, formal notice, **fines**, processing injunctions, data locking, authorisation withdrawal) and
  **penal** (6 months–10 years' imprisonment; fines 10m–50m FCFA). Source:
  [[2025-05-05-benin-loi-protection-donnees-2021]].
- **2025 enforcement bilan.** [[data.statistics]] Four axes (conformity, control, sanction, training):
  **907 files examined, 87.4% validated** (793 — 606 authorisations + 187 declarations; 90 deferred);
  **782** contentious files; **64 control missions**; **20 privacy complaints**; **75 conformity
  certificates**; **37 DPOs** registered; **14 major contentious cases** yielding formal notices and
  pecuniary sanctions (+99 formal-notice files). Source: [[2026-06-25-apdp-bilan-2025-conformite]].
- **An AI-forward regulator.** [[tech.ai]] The APDP maintains a **specific AI policy** with strict
  purpose-limitation, set its 2026 Data Protection Day theme on **CCTV + AI / algorithmic
  discrimination**, and (per its own rapporteur) made the electoral agency **strip extraneous fields from
  the electoral roll before publication** — a concrete DPA enforcement act bearing on ANIP's integrated
  register. Its president flags real capacity gaps: **no algorithmic-audit tooling**, budget constraints.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Regional + international leadership (2025–26).** [[gov.regional]] Hounkponou was elected **AFAPDP
  Vice-President**; Benin joined the **INPDPA executive committee** (with Mali, Morocco, Somalia, Saudi
  Arabia; Türkiye, Jun 2026); signed a five-pillar convention with **Senegal's CDP** (cross-border data
  flows, a "concerted African position"; Apr 2026) and a cooperation agreement with **Mali's** DPA
  (2025); and led a delegation to the **9th RAPDP** conference (Abidjan). Sources:
  [[2026-04-17-benin-senegal-apdp-cdp-convention]], [[2026-06-19-apdp-inpdpa-comite-executif-turkiye]],
  [[2026-06-05-benin-59e-reunion-bureau-afapdp]], [[2025-04-16-wearetech-benin-mali-data-protection-cooperation]].
- **Public outreach + child safety.** [[capacity.literacy]] With **UNICEF** on children's online safety
  (schools campaign, Nov 2025; expanded partnership Mar 2026), and first sub-national awareness stops in
  **Couffo/Aplahoué** (Apr 2026) and **Ouémé/Porto-Novo** (Jun 2026). Benin presented Code numérique
  **Book V** at the EU-facing **Privacy Symposium in Venice** (Apr 2026). Sources:
  [[2026-03-26-techreviewafrica-apdp-unicef-digital-safety]], [[2026-04-16-apdp-sensibilisation-couffo-aplahoue]],
  [[2026-04-23-apdp-privacy-symposium-venise]].
