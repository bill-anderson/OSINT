# Carnegie Mellon University Africa (CMU-Africa)

Kigali-based engineering/ICT research campus of Carnegie Mellon University; a recurring source of
Africa-specific ICT4D, digital-ID and AI-fairness research.

## Activity in this wiki

- **ID-required mobile money fieldwork (2026, CHI ’26).** 72 interviews (Kenya + Tanzania) documenting
  "third-party SIM" use — banking under someone else’s ID because SIM-registration mandates an official
  ID many lack — and arguing mandatory SIM-ID rules fail their anti-fraud purpose ("adverse digital
  incorporation"). See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-01 My Money, Your Name Challenges and Workarounds in ID-Required Mobile Money in East Africa]].
