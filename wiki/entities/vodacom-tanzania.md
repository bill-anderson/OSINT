# Vodacom Tanzania

The Tanzanian operating company of the **[[vodacom]]** group — a major mobile network operator and
operator of the **[[mpesa|M-Pesa]]** mobile-money platform. CEO (2026): **Philip Besiimire**.

- **M-Pesa Fintech 2.0 (Apr 2026).** Launched a **$28m** core upgrade — see [[mpesa]] and
  [[tanzania--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-04-03-vodacom-tanzania-mpesa-fintech-2-platform]].
- **M-Pesa × PayPal (May 2026).** Wallet interoperability with [[paypal]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-26-vodacom-mpesa-tanzania-paypal-alliance]].
