# Cauridor

A **Guinea-founded cross-border payments infrastructure** company (founded 2022 by Oumar Barry and Abdoulaye
Bah; **$13m raised**). It sits in the middle of cross-border flows, integrating **mobile-money providers, banks
and international money-transfer operators** via API across **36 African countries** (Guinea, Sierra Leone,
Liberia, Senegal, Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Mali, The Gambia…), now eyeing the DRC. In 2026-04 it hired
**ex-Flutterwave** Global Head of Operations **Awa Koné** as COO — a notable Guinea-origin fintech scaling
regionally. See [[guinea--dpi-pay]].
