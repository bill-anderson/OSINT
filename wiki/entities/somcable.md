# Somcable (Somaliland Cable)

Telecom provider and internet service provider based in Somaliland (CEO **Mohammed
Ibrahim Ahmed**; Chairman Mohamed Said Guedi). Somaliland is a self-declared state with no
ISO 3166 code; it is mapped here to **[[SOM]]** for tagging purposes only, and the
political sensitivity of that mapping should be noted in any published treatment.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Ethio Telecom talks (2026-01-29).** Somcable's delegation met [[ethio-telecom]] CEO
  Frehiwot Tamru to discuss a **strategic cross-border digital and telecom partnership**,
  aimed at sharing infrastructure and experience across the Horn of Africa. Talks only —
  no signed deal reported. See [[infra.connect]], [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-29 Ethio Telecom, Somcable talk cross-border collaboration]].
