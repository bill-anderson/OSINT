# TELEMO (Guinea e-procurement platform)

Guinea's **national digital public-procurement platform**, launched in Conakry **22 May 2026** — developed in
**collaboration with Rwanda**. It centralises the **full procurement cycle** (planning, tenders, bid
submission, evaluation, contract award, archiving) online, moving away from paper. Significant because
**public procurement is 11–15% of Guinea's GDP**. See [[guinea--dpi-govtech]].
