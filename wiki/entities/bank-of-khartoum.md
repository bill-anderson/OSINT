# Bank of Khartoum

Sudan's largest bank; its **[[bankak|Bankak]]** mobile app became, during the 2023– civil war, the
**dominant channel for digital transactions and remittances** in Sudan after most other
providers were unable to operate. Concentration on a single provider is flagged as a systemic
vulnerability — not all Sudanese have bank accounts or smartphones — which motivates the push for
interoperable rails ([[mojaloop]]) to reduce single-point dependency. See [[dpi.pay]],
[[include.access]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Bankak-fraud conviction (2026-02).** A Dinder court convicted a criminal
  network for scamming citizens and traders using **forged Bankak payment
  notifications** — the payments-integrity/cyber dimension of the dominant
  wartime DFS rail. See [[bankak]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-22 Fraud network convicted in Dinder for forging Bankak mobile-money notifications (SUNA)]].
