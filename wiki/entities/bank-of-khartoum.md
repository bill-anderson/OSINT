# Bank of Khartoum

Sudan's largest bank; its **Bankak** mobile app became, during the 2023– civil war, the
**dominant channel for digital transactions and remittances** in Sudan after most other
providers were unable to operate. Concentration on a single provider is flagged as a systemic
vulnerability — not all Sudanese have bank accounts or smartphones — which motivates the push for
interoperable rails ([[mojaloop]]) to reduce single-point dependency. See [[dpi.pay]],
[[include.access]].
