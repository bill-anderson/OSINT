# High Commission for Digitalisation (HCD/HCN, Algeria)

Algeria's central digital-transformation authority, **reporting directly to the Presidency**, created by **Presidential Decree No. 23-314 (6 September 2023)**. Led by **High Commissioner Meriem Benmouloud** (ministerial rank). Oversees implementation of **"Digital Algeria 2030" ([[digital-algeria-2030]])** and the portfolio of 500+ digital-transformation projects (2025–26).

Anchors the GovTech build-out: the **[[dzair-services|Dzair Services]]** single-window platform, the **Bawabatak** portal (342 services), and the interoperability layer connecting 46 ministries/agencies.

*Acronym note: sources render it both **HCD** (High Commission for Digitalisation) and **HCN** (Haut-Commissariat à la Numérisation) — the same Presidency-level body.*
