# CafeOne

Nigerian co-working network, **>30 locations across Nigeria (2026-07)**, serving professionals,
founders, freelancers, operators, analysts and small teams. Subscription tiers run from
**₦8,525 ($6.15) daily to ₦109,950 ($79.36) monthly (2026-07)**.

## What matters now

- **First deployment partner for [[decide-ai|Decide]]'s enterprise arm (2026-07-17).** Under the
  Decide for Work partnership, CafeOne members with an active subscription get **premium access
  to Decide's spreadsheet AI agent bundled into their membership**, activated by instructions
  from CafeOne or on request at any location. See [[tech.ai]], [[nigeria--tech-ai]].

## Why it matters here

CafeOne is tracked not as a property business but as a **distribution channel for AI tooling**.
Bundling an AI agent into a workspace subscription makes the co-working operator the party that
mediates which AI tools its members meet, on what terms, and with what handling of the business
data those members put through them — a governance surface that sits outside both the vendor's
and the user's own contracting.
