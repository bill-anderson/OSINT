# Abdelmadjid Tebboune

President of Algeria. Digital transformation is run **from the Presidency** under his direction — he
inaugurated the sovereign **[[national-digital-services-center-algeria|National Digital Services Center]]**
(2026-07-05) and ordered administrations to interconnect to the National Data Center **within one month**
(cabinet, 2026-07-12). Ministers routinely credit his "strategic vision" for the digital-sovereignty agenda.
See [[DZA]], [[digital-algeria-2030]].
