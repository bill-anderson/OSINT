# Botswana Data Protection Act No. 18 of 2024

Botswana's operative **data-protection law** — published **29 October 2024** (presidential assent), effective **14 January 2025** — which finally replaced the stillborn **Data Protection Act No. 32 of 2018**.

## Key provisions & context

- The **2018 Act never came into force**: its effective date was repeatedly extended by ministerial orders, and it did not bind the state or controllers outside Botswana. The 2024 Act closes those gaps.
- Strengthens the **[[information-data-protection-commission-botswana]]**'s powers and adds fuller obligations on controllers, processors and sub-processors: children's-data processing, DPIAs, breach notification, records of processing (RoPA), a data-protection representative, and cross-border-transfer rules. See [[botswana--gov-protect]]. Source: [[2025-01-08 Understanding Botswana's New Data Protection Act 2024]].
