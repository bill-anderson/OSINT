# Cowrywise

Nigerian digital savings and investment platform, offering retail users app-based access to mutual
funds, equities and ETFs at low minimum ticket sizes.

Named (2026-07-18) among the platforms — with **[[bamboo]]**, Trove and Risevest — that displaced the
traditional route into Nigerian equities, where buying shares meant opening an account with a broker,
completing paperwork and investing sums large enough to justify the process for both sides. The
low-minimum design is the substantive change: one investor on record made a first equity purchase of
**₦5,000** through Cowrywise, a ticket size no conventional brokerage relationship would have carried.
