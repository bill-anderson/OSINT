# HAAC (Haute Autorité de l'Audiovisuel et de la Communication, Togo)

**Togo's High Authority for Audiovisual and Communication — the broadcast and media
regulator.** The HAAC operates a **control and monitoring room** (*salle de contrôle et de
monitoring*) from which it tracks broadcast content. Its point of contact with this wiki is
its December-2025 cooperation accord with the data protection authority
**[[ipdcp-togo]]**. See [[TGO]], [[gov.policy]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Accord with the IPDCP (2025-12-10).** The HAAC and the IPDCP signed an institutional
  cooperation memorandum on the future of media regulation, aiming to promote press freedom
  while guaranteeing personal data protection and digital rights. The HAAC pledged to
  integrate data protection into its **procedures, its monitoring and its awareness-raising
  actions**, and — the accord's operative mechanism — **authorises the IPDCP to access its
  control and monitoring room**, allowing more precise tracking of broadcast content, faster
  control of possible abuses, and coordinated reaction in the event of a personal-data
  breach. The two presidents argued that press freedom cannot hold if the protection of
  digital rights remains fragile. Deal: [[haac-ipdcp-accord]]. See [[gov.protect]],
  [[finance.mou]]. Source:
  [[2025-12-12 Personal data and digital rights in Togo - HAAC and IPDCP seal institutional cooperation accord]].

**Provenance note.** The single account held here is sourced to the IPDCP and carries no
critical framing of the monitoring-room access arrangement.
