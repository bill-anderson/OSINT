# Waka

Africa-Asia cross-border payments company (formerly **Pyxis**), founded by
April Long. Waka provides an integrated corridor service — local relationship
managers and client onboarding, FX liquidity management/market-making, a
stablecoin settlement rail, and compliant onshore CNY settlement into mainland
China with the SAFE/PBOC documentation package attached — targeting large
enterprise importers rather than SME retail flows.

> **Provenance caveat.** This page draws on a single source, *#117 — Payments
> Follow Trade*, which is a **partner piece co-written with Waka's CEO** and
> illustrated from the "Waka Seed Deck." All traction figures below are
> **company-stated**, not independently verified; treat with caution and seek
> corroboration before reuse.

## Company-stated facts (as of 2026-04, per partner piece)

- >US$100m annual flow processed across eight African markets.
- >100 liquidity providers connected across 20 currencies.
- Backers include Visa Ventures, Coinbase Ventures and Tether.
- Founder **April Long** — ex-Standard Chartered Kenya and Gulf African Bank,
  10+ years in Africa-Asia corridor banking (attributed in source frontmatter;
  no separate person page).
