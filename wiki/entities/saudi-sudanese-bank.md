# Saudi Sudanese Bank

A Sudanese commercial bank with Saudi ties, described as one of Sudan's prominent
financial institutions.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Card-processing deal with Network International (2026-01).** Signed a
  strategic processing agreement with **[[network-international]]** for
  end-to-end digital payment processing, **[[mastercard]]** sponsorship and
  prepaid issuing — modernising its payments capability amid Sudan's wartime
  reconstruction. See [[dpi.pay]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-19 Network International Partners with Saudi Sudanese Bank to Advance Digital Payments in Sudan]].
