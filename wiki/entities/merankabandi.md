# Merankabandi (Cash for Jobs)

Burundi's flagship **social-protection cash-transfer programme** — a World Bank-financed Government of Burundi initiative, national since **2022**, and the cornerstone of the country's social-protection system.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Migrated to openIMIS (2025).** Since **May 2025** the programme runs on the **CORE-MIS** solution powered by **[[openimis]]**, managing cash transfers and income-generating-activity financing for **1.25 million beneficiaries** across five provinces (>1m records migrated from the legacy system). Supports 200,000+ vulnerable households and has added emergency transfers and benefits for **360,000** refugees/host-community members; payment providers and KoboToolbox integrated. See [[dpi.mis]]. Source: [[2026-01-01 Burundi's growing social protection system now runs on openIMIS]].
- Feeds the government's planned **Unified Social Registry** under the National Social Protection Strategy — a registry layer for coordinating access across sectors.
