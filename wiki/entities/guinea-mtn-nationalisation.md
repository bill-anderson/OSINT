# State acquisition of MTN Guinée (87.5%)

The **State of Guinea acquired MTN's Guinea-Conakry operation** — a **telecom nationalisation**. MTN Group
made the exit official **30 Dec 2024** (part of its "Ambition 2025" portfolio simplification; ~3m
subscribers), and the government later stated it took **87.5%** of MTN Guinée — framed by Minister **Rose Pola
Pricemou** as "taking back control of our telecom future" and a foundation of **digital sovereignty**. See
[[guinea--infra-connect]].
