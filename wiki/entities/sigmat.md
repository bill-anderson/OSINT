# SIGMAT (ECOWAS Interconnected System for the Management of Goods in Transit)

An [[ecowas]] flagship regional platform, launched in 2019, that modernises customs
transit through end-to-end digitalisation and cross-border system interconnectivity —
harmonising regional procedures, dematerialising transit workflows and securing electronic
data exchange between member-state customs administrations. Deployed states report tangible
reductions in border friction, and interest has extended beyond ECOWAS.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Presented at WCO 2026 Technology Conference, Abu Dhabi (2026-01-30).** The ECOWAS
  Commission's Directorate of Customs showcased SIGMAT at the World Customs Organization's
  2026 Technology Conference (28–30 January 2026, Abu Dhabi, UAE), presenting measurable
  progress, implementation challenges and its forward roadmap. The roadmap adds enhanced
  risk-management mechanisms, broader mobile/field capabilities, a regional guarantee-
  management system, and the SIGMAT e-Hub — a secure, scalable infrastructure for
  electronic message exchange enabling interoperable corridor operations. Tags
