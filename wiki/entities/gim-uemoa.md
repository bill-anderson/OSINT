# GIM-UEMOA

The **interbank monétique/card-interoperability body** for the 8 UEMOA/[[waemu|WAEMU]] states, under
[[bceao]] supervision — complementary to BCEAO's PI-SPI instant-payment platform. Runs an academy supporting
the mandatory **CAF-STI/SSI cybersecurity certification** for payment-ecosystem operators. See [[XAF]].
