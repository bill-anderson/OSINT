# Google for Startups Accelerator Africa

[[google]]'s equity-free accelerator programme for African startups, providing
mentorship and technical support without taking a stake ([[tech.innovate]],
[[finance.new]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Class 10 selected (2026-04-21).** Google announced 15 startups chosen from
  roughly 2,600 applications for a three-month hybrid programme running 13 April
  to 19 June 2026, with every selected company applying AI ([[tech.ai]]). Source:
  [[2026-04-21 Meet the 15 Startups Joining the Google for Startups Accelerator Africa Class 10]].
- **Cumulative reach since 2018.** The accelerator has supported 106 startups
  from 17 countries; alumni have raised more than $263m and created over 2,800
  jobs across [[XAF]] (as of 2026). A Google-commissioned Public First report earlier
  cited **120 companies** graduated over five years, plus a **2023 AI-First Accelerator**
  strand — sitting within a wider Google startup portfolio (Black Founders Fund: 135
  entrepreneurs, >$10m non-equity, since 2021). Sources:
  [[2026-04-21 Meet the 15 Startups Joining the Google for Startups Accelerator Africa Class 10]],
  [[2024-01-01 Google's Impact in Sub-Saharan Africa – Google's Impact in Sub-Saharan Africa]].
- **Eligible markets.** Applications open to startups in 17 countries: Algeria, Botswana,
  Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Egypt, Ethiopia, Ghana, Kenya, Morocco, Nigeria, Rwanda, Senegal,
  South Africa, Tanzania, Tunisia, Uganda and Zimbabwe. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Google Hustle Academy]].
