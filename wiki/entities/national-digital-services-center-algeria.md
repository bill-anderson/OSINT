# National Digital Services Center (Algeria)

Algeria's **first sovereign digital infrastructure** — inaugurated by President **[[abdelmadjid-tebboune]]**
on **2026-07-05** (Mohammadia, Algiers). Two **active-active data centres in Algiers (Mohammadia) and Blida**,
built by **[[huawei]]**, **Uptime Tier III Design** certified, **99.98% availability** (<2 h/yr downtime),
with real-time synchronisation. It **hosts all public-administration data on national territory**, runs the
**national cloud** and the future **[[dzair-services|Dzair Digital Services]]** portal, and enables
inter-ministerial interoperability toward **40 digitised public services** accessed via a national digital
identity. Supervised by the **[[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria|HCN]]**. On 2026-07-12 Tebboune ordered
administrations to interconnect within **one month**. See [[algeria--infra-store]].
