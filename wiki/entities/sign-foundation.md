# Sign Foundation

Digital-infrastructure firm (CEO **Claire Ma**) building blockchain- and
open-standards-based digital identity systems, positioned on open-source stacks, W3C
verifiable credentials and national ownership rather than proprietary lock-in.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Sierra Leone (2026-07).** Signed a tri-party MoU with the Government of Sierra Leone
  (MoCTI) and [[bhutan-ndi]] to design and implement Sierra Leone's national digital ID
  platform, leading **system design, solution architecture and technical implementation**.
  Builds on an existing Nov 2025 SL–Sign MoU for a blockchain-powered digital ID. See
  [[sierra-leone-digital-id-mou]], [[SLE]], [[dpi.id]].
- Ma points to the rapid spread of **MOSIP-based** deployments ([[mosip]]) as evidence
  that African / Global-South governments are moving faster on foundational ID than many
  advanced economies.
