# Bankak

The mobile-money application of **[[bank-of-khartoum]]**, Sudan's largest bank.
During the 2023– civil war Bankak became the **dominant channel for digital
transactions and remittances** after most other providers could not operate —
making it both the survival rail of Sudan's payments system and a systemic
single-provider concentration risk (the recurring motive for interoperable-rail
advocacy; see [[mojaloop]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Fraud-network conviction, Dinder (2026-02).** A Dinder (Sennar state) court
  convicted a criminal network (case 620/2025; forgery art.123 + fraud art.178,
  1991 Penal Code) for scamming citizens and traders using **forged Bankak
  payment notifications** — the payments-integrity/cyber dimension of Sudan's
  dominant wartime DFS rail. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[gov.protect]], [[SDN]].
