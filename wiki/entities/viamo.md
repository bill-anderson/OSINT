# Viamo

A social-enterprise provider of **mobile-based information services** for underserved
populations, best known for reaching users on **basic and feature phones** via voice
(IVR), SMS and USSD. In this wiki it appears as a leading example of **AI-as-access-layer**
digital inclusion ([[include.access]], [[tech.ai]]).

## Activity in this wiki

- **Ask Viamo Anything (AVA), Zambia.** AVA lets users **dial a number on any phone** and
  receive **AI-generated voice responses**, extending digital services to feature-phone
  users. In early studies with the **[[gsma]]**, **59% of AVA users were women**, with
  engagement significantly higher than legacy automated (IVR) systems — especially for
  sensitive topics such as health. See [[ZMB]], [[include.access]], [[include.divides]],
  [[capacity.literacy]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-15 Leveraging AI to Bypass the Smartphone Barrier and Advance Digital Inclusion in Africa]].
