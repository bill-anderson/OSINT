# Better Than Cash Alliance

A **UN-based partnership** (housed at UNCDF) of governments, companies and international organisations
accelerating the transition from cash to **responsible digital payments** to advance financial
inclusion and the SDGs ([[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]]). A recurring reference actor in African
digital-payments and government-payments-digitisation work.
