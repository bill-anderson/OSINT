# Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA)

Zambia's national tax administration, headed (2026-07) by **Commissioner General Dingani Banda**.
Within this wiki it appears as a **domestic-revenue-mobilisation body pursuing data-driven tax
administration** — the fiscal-capacity face of the state digitisation programme run elsewhere by
[[smart-zambia-institute]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **ICTD partnership (2026-07-17).** ZRA signed a **Memorandum of Understanding and a
  Non-Disclosure Agreement** with the **[[international-centre-for-tax-and-development|ICTD]]**
  ([[zra-ictd-mou]]) covering **research, capacity building and innovation** in tax administration.
  Banda framed it as a milestone toward "a fully data-driven tax administration capable of
  addressing the growing complexities of the digital economy"; ICTD Executive Director **Giulia
  Mascagni** tied it to **domestic resource mobilisation**. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-zambia-revenue-authority-ictd-deal]].

The NDA alongside the MoU is worth noting: **taxpayer microdata is the asset a research partnership
of this kind runs on**, and the wiki holds no detail of what data is shared, under what safeguards,
or whether Zambia's data-protection regime ([[office-of-the-data-protection-commissioner-zambia]])
is engaged. That is an absence, not a finding.
