# Gov.CV (Cabo Verde unified government portal)

Cabo Verde's **unified government portal** (web + mobile app), launched under PM **Ulisses Correia e Silva** (early 2026) as the **single gateway** to public services — civil registration and identity, financial and tax operations, higher education, transportation and social services. Built for efficiency, interoperability and transparency, with more services being onboarded; delivered on the **[[nosi|NOSi]]** digital-government stack. Framed by Minister Eurico Monteiro as a driver of citizen participation under Cabo Verde's open-governance strategy.

Launched alongside a parallel Central African Republic digital-government platform (see [[CAF]]).
