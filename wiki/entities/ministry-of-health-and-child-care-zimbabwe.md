# Ministry of Health and Child Care (Zimbabwe)

Zimbabwe's health ministry (Minister **Douglas Mombeshora**).

- **Unactivated Starlink kits (Jul 2026).** **400+ rural health facilities** received Starlink kits under the
  government digitisation programme but **cannot use them** — the ministry says subscriptions are **too high**
  to pay and is negotiating fees / seeking a USF extension. The "architecture vs operation" gap made literal.
