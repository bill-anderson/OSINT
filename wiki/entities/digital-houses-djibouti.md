# Digital Houses / E-SKILLS

Djibouti's **E-SKILLS "Digital Houses"** initiative — physical digital-training centres across **5 inland
regions**, targeting **3,000 people trained by 2029** at a stated **€7M**, aimed squarely at the country's
very high youth unemployment (~76% in 2024). Complemented by a civil-servant **digital-skills programme**
(DCO / Oracle University / INAP). Sources: [[2026-05-16-digital-houses-e-skills-wearetech]],
[[2025-02-24-digital-skills-program-govt-officials-wearetech]].
