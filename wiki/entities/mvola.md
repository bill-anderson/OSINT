# MVola

Madagascar's leading mobile-money service and licensed **digital bank** (Telma group), with **over 10m
customers** (2023) — against roughly 3m traditional bank accounts. MVola pursues a digital-first,
**open-platform** strategy: the MVola Developer's Portal for direct merchant integrations, MVola
Wallet-to-Bank, and QR-code payment, exposing APIs to third-party developers.

MVola became **GSMA Mobile Money API compliant** in September 2023 (first phase: P2P transfers and
international remittances), earning the "GSMA Mobile Money API Compliant" mark and enabling faster partner
onboarding — e.g. an October 2023 tie-up with MFS Africa for remittances. Its super-app converges P2P
transfers, international remittances, bill payments and loans. MVola is one of the private rails alongside
which the central bank is piloting the **[[e-ariary]]** CBDC.
