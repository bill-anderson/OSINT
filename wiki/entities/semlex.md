# Semlex

Belgian identity-solutions company supplying biometric national-ID and secure-document systems to African governments ([[dpi.id]]). In Guinea-Bissau it produces the biometric national ID card and has helped **decentralize** civil-identification through a network of production centres. Semlex is a recurring (and, in some markets, controversy-attracting) foreign ID vendor on the continent — captured here per capture-is-not-endorsement. See [[GNB]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Safim production centre (2026-01-30).** A new biometric national-ID production centre opened in Safim (Biombo region), inaugurated by Justice Minister Carlos Pinto Pereira — the third centre in Biombo and part of a network of **18 production centres nationwide** (capacity 100 cards/day at Safim). Source: [[2026-02-11 Semlex Guinea-Bissau strengthens access to biometric identification]].
