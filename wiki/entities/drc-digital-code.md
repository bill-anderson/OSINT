# DRC Digital Code (Loi 20/017)

The Democratic Republic of Congo's **Digital Code** — the framework law for the digital sector, including personal-data protection and the mandate for a national **Data Protection Authority**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Data Protection Authority still on paper (2024).** Think tank Law & Technologies urged the government (Aug 2024) to **operationalise the Data Protection Authority required by Article 262** of the Digital Code — by drafting and signing the decree defining its organisation and functioning. The authority remains non-operational, a pointed enforcement gap as the **[[rdc-pass]]** 20-year exclusive e-KYC concession over citizens' identity data proceeds. See [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2024-08-07 RDC - government urged to operationalise the Data Protection Authority]].
- **DPO network — first concrete step under Art. 262 (Mar 2026).** The government launched a **network of
  Data Protection Officers** (Thu 12 Mar 2026, Kinshasa) — the **first concrete step since the 2024 "still
  on paper" finding**, and one that **precedes** creation of the national DPA (still only "planned"),
  alongside a named **National Data Governance Strategy** (not yet held). Moves the standing enforcement
  gap, without closing it. Sources: [[2026-03-16-rdc-reseau-responsables-protection-des-donnees-wearetech]],
  [[2026-03-17-drc-launches-data-protection-officers-network-techafricanews]].
- **Livre III now governs data protection extensively (Apr 2026).** Legal scholar Henrotte dates the shift:
  the 2020 telecom law was "embryonnaire"; **Livre III of the Digital Code now governs data protection "de
  façon très extensive"** — the closest live status check on the enforcement gap. Source:
  [[2026-04-21-ouvrage-protection-donnees-personnelles-actualite]].
- **The Code invoked as a repression instrument (Mar 2026).** A four-tier **state-document classification**
  regime (82nd Council of Ministers, Justice Minister Ngefa) **invokes the Digital Code as a repression
  instrument** — a use the wiki had not recorded, and a counterpoint to the rights framing. Source:
  [[2026-03-20-regime-classification-documents-officiels-actualite]].
- The Code also underpins the DRC's e-commerce and mobile-money-interoperability framework (per the US ITA guide).
