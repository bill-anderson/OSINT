# Eswatini Economic Policy Analysis and Research Centre (ESEPARC)

**Eswatini's national economic policy research centre** ([[SWZ]]), and the domestic
technical partner on the [[eswatini-digital-readiness-assessment]] alongside
[[ministry-of-ict-eswatini]] and [[undp]]. See [[capacity.research]].

**Name, and a name error worth holding.** The centre publishes under its own name —
Eswatini Economic Policy Analysis & Research Centre, at `separc.co.sz`. Biometric
Update's DRA write-up (2024-11-22) calls it the **"Southern Africa Research and
Policy Centre (SEPARC)"**; that is an outlet error, not an alternative name. Treat
*SEPARC* as an alias to recognise, never to reproduce.

## Activity in this wiki

- **DRA technical partner and publisher of the findings (2024-12-05).** ESEPARC set
  out the DRA's headline results: digital readiness at the **"systematic" stage, an
  overall score of 2.6 out of 5 (2024)**, with strengths in the people, economy and
  government pillars and lags in digital public infrastructure, connectivity and
  regulation. ESEPARC's **Tengetile Hlophe** reported that, under the government
  pillar, **at least 64% of stakeholders (2024)** acknowledged the
  [[eswatini-digitalisation-strategy-2024-2028]]'s implementation while **half
  perceived it as delayed** — citing weak design, insufficient political backing, lack
  of financial support and limited institutional coordination. See [[gov.policy]],
  [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2024-12-05 Eswatini Advances Toward Digital Transformation - Key Findings from the Digital Readiness Assessment]].
- **Credited by Biometric Update (2024-11-22)** for the DRA's technical support,
  under the mistaken "SEPARC" name above. Source:
  [[2024-11-22 Eswatini unveils readiness report for digital ID, transformation push]].
