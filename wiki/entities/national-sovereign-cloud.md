# National Sovereign Cloud

Cassava's National Sovereign Cloud (NSC) is a [[cassava-ai]] product for African governments, announced 2026-03-05. It matters as a packaged, locally-governed platform for AI-enabled public services.

## Profile
- Announced 2026-03-05; targets African governments with secure, locally-governed infrastructure for AI-enabled public services.
- Bundles cloud, cyber security, compute/AI, local-language AI models, an "AI Institute", plus a payments and remittances layer.
- Delivered through Cassava's Cloud Partner Programme (targeting MNOs/system integrators to resell), offering NVIDIA Cloud Partner solutions, the Cassava AI Factory, and [[caimex]] — which supports both Anthropic's Claude and Google's Gemini.
- Explicitly pitched against **US CLOUD Act** extraterritorial reach (Cassava citing that "~33–45% of the world's data is hosted in the USA"), arguing a sovereign cloud keeps model weights and public-AI training data under national custody. Surfaced at **MWC Barcelona (Mar 2026)**. Source: [[2026-03-06 Cassava launches sovereign cloud for Africa's public sector]].
- Quoted: Ahmed El Beheiry (Cassava Group COO / CTO & AI Officer).
