# Department of Information, Communications and Technology (DICT), Seychelles

**Seychelles' central ICT department** — the government's digital-transformation implementer and
the connective body across the country's e-government projects. In the material this wiki holds,
DICT appears in three distinct roles: **policy convenor** on data-protection legislation,
**delivery partner** on citizen-facing digital services, and **technical committee member** on
address-data infrastructure. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.policy]], [[SYC]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Ran the data-protection bill consultation (2023-08).** DICT issued the call for public
  comments on the draft Data Protection Bill, the consultation that preceded enactment of the
  [[data-protection-act-2023-seychelles]] in December 2023 — placing DICT, not the
  [[information-commission-seychelles]], at the drafting end of the law the Commission now
  enforces. See [[gov.legislate]], [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2024-06-04 Review of Seychelles DPA]].
- **Co-delivered SeyID and CertExpress (2024-09-18).** The
  [[department-of-immigration-and-civil-status-seychelles]] launched [[seyid]] virtual ID cards
  and the [[certexpress]] certificate platform "in partnership with" DICT. See [[dpi.id]],
  [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2024-09-18 Launch of New Digital Solutions for Public Services]].
- **Partner on the National Addressing System.** DICT sits in the inter-agency collaboration
  behind the [[seychelles-national-addressing-system]], alongside the
  [[seychelles-postal-regulator]], Seychelles Postal Services, the Ministry of Lands and
  Housing, the National Bureau of Statistics, the Attorney General's Office, the Land Transport
  Division and the Local Government Department. *(Status as described on an undated portal page
  proxy-dated to 2025 — see the source page for the provenance caveat.)* See [[dpi.registry]],
  [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 National Addressing System - Seychelles Postal Regulator]].
