# SECUROUTE

Benin's national platform for managing and securing **road-enforcement data**.
It centralises, secures and tracks information generated by traffic-monitoring
and regulatory systems, giving authorities a unified, reliable source of road
enforcement data across the Ministry of Transport, the National Police and
national road-safety agencies. See [[BEN]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Interoperability expansion (Jan 2026).** [[cybastion]], with Benin's
  Ministry of Economy and Finance, is expanding the digital infrastructure to
  ensure **full interoperability** of SECUROUTE with other government systems —
  improving inter-agency coordination on traffic, safety and compliance. See
  [[dpi.exchange]], [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Cybastion Advances Digital Government Solutions, Supports Benin's SECUROUTE Infrastructure Upgrade]].
- **Top service on Benin's UXP dashboard.** SECUROUTE ranks as the leading
  service on the [[uxp-benin]] interoperability platform run by [[asin-benin]].
  Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Cybastion Advances Digital Government Solutions, Supports Benin's SECUROUTE Infrastructure Upgrade]].
