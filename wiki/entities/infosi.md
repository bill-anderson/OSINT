# INFOSI (National Institute for the Promotion of the Information Society)

Angolan government body driving the **sovereign national data centre and unified government cloud** — the host and operator of the state's core digital infrastructure. Director General: **André Mpumba Pedro**. The facility (Camama, Luanda) is the physical anchor of Angola's digital-sovereignty posture and, increasingly, a regional showcase.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National data centre (operational H1 2026).** ~$89–90m sovereign facility in Luanda; modular 12-container design, 336 racks; ~75% complete Nov 2025. All government services must migrate within 30 days of inauguration — a data-localisation/sovereignty posture. Inaugurated end-April 2026. See [[angola--infra-store]]. Source: [[2025-11-14 Angola Nears Completion of National Data Center, Set for 2026 Launch]].
- **A regional model (2026-06-13).** During ANGOTIC 2026, Namibia's ICT minister **[[emma-theofelus]]** and her delegation toured the Camama data centre and cloud, received by DG André Mpumba Pedro; Theofelus cited it as a reference for Namibia's own planned sovereign data centre — the sovereignty play acquiring **regional prestige** and positioning Angola as an infrastructure exemplar to its neighbours. See [[NAM]], [[gov.regional]]. Source: [[2026-06-16-namibia-ict-minister-visits-angolas-national-data-centre]].
- **National cloud platform (first stone Sept 2025)** developed alongside the DC; part of the shift from pilots to scalable state infrastructure. Source: [[2025-09-10 Angola advances technological development for digital transformation]].
