# INFOSI (National Institute for the Promotion of the Information Society)

Angolan government body driving the **sovereign national data centre and unified government cloud** — the host and operator of the state's core digital infrastructure. Director General: André Pedro.

## Activity in this wiki

- **National data centre (operational H1 2026).** ~$89–90m sovereign facility in Luanda; modular 12-container design, 336 racks; ~75% complete Nov 2025. All government services must migrate within 30 days of inauguration — a data-localisation/sovereignty posture. Inaugurated end-April 2026 and later cited by Namibia as a regional model (see [[AGO]] hub). See [[angola--infra-store]]. Source: [[2025-11-14 Angola Nears Completion of National Data Center, Set for 2026 Launch]].
- **National cloud platform (first stone Sept 2025)** developed alongside the DC; part of the shift from pilots to scalable state infrastructure. Source: [[2025-09-10 Angola advances technological development for digital transformation]].
