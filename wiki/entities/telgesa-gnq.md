# Telgesa (Equatorial Guinea 2nd state operator)

A **new second state telecom operator** launching in 2026 — budgeted at **~US$50m**, with **260 telecom
towers** and a planned **90% national coverage** (per Ecofin). Its arrival, alongside the **[[getesa-gnq|GETESA]]**
–Huawei rescue and the existing state firms **MUNI** and **ORTEL**, raises unresolved questions (flagged by the
independent outlet **RadioMacuto**) about how the state operators will coexist and about accountability for prior
telecom investments. In **May 2025** the government commissioned **Cyberteq** to assess coverage and produce an
optimisation plan. See [[gnq--infra-connect]].
