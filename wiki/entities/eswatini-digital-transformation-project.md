# Eswatini Digital Transformation Project

Eswatini's planned, **[[world-bank]]**-financed national digital-transformation
programme, advancing through inter-ministerial negotiations (ICT, Finance,
Economic Planning).

## Activity in this wiki

- **WB engagement (2026-05).** Aims to modernise government systems and deliver
  services — passports, business registration, vehicle licensing, health,
  education, social services — via the **"Government in Your Hand" (GIYH)**
  platform, plus cybersecurity frameworks, digital skills and rural broadband.
  Funding amount not yet stated (negotiations ongoing). See [[SWZ]],
  [[dpi.govtech]], [[infra.connect]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-18 Eswatini promotes digital transformation project through World Bank engagements]].
