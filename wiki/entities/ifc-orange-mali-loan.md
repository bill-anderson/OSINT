# IFC €80m social loan to Orange Mali (2025)

Announced **17 Nov 2025** (Bamako). A **€80 million (~XOF 52.5bn / ~$92.6m) Social Loan** from
**[[ifc]]** and co-investors to **[[orange-mali]]** to expand digital connectivity and women's
digital inclusion.

- **Structure:** ~€50m equivalent in XOF from IFC's own account + up to €30m mobilised from the
  **West African Development Bank (BOAD)**, backed by the **[[world-bank|World Bank Group IDA
  Private Sector Window]]**.
- **Use of proceeds:** **300 new 4G towers** (half in rural areas) and fibre to reach
  **~300,000 households and small businesses**.
- **Social/environmental terms:** raise women's participation in Orange Mali's digital-training
  programmes to **70% by 2032**; replace diesel generators with **solar**, cutting >8,000
  tonnes CO₂/year.
- **Context:** first investment under the **IFC–Orange Middle East & Africa (OMEA)** partnership
  signed at the Africa CEO Forum (Abidjan, May 2025, targeting 8 countries); supports Mali
  Digital 2020 and the WBG Digital Economy for Africa initiative.
