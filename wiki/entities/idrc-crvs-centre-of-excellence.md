# IDRC Centre of Excellence for CRVS Systems

**Research and knowledge centre on civil registration and vital statistics**, hosted by
Canada's International Development Research Centre (IDRC). Held here as the publisher of
standing reference material on the relationship between CRVS and foundational identity —
the design question underlying much of the wiki's [[dpi.id]] and [[dpi.registry]] coverage.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Linking National ID and CRVS Systems (2019, year precision).** A reference work (author:
  Mitra) on how national identity systems and civil registration interoperate — whether
  foundational ID is built on the civil register, alongside it, or independently of it.
  Global in scope ([[XGL]]) rather than Africa-specific. Held as a **reference study — cited,
  not absorbed**; its figures stay in the study. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]],
  [[gov.standards]]. Source:
  [[2019-01-01 Linking National ID and CRVS Systems (IDRC Centre of Excellence)]].
