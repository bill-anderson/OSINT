# Presidential Delivery Unit (Zambia)

Zambian centre-of-government unit charged with driving delivery of the President's priorities.
Head: **Kusobile Kamwambi (2026-07)**.

## What matters now

- **Co-organiser of the national AI/robotics recognition ceremony (2026-07-17).** The PDU
  co-hosted the ceremony for Team Zambia's advance to the global finals of the 2026 AI for Good /
  Robotics for Good Youth Challenge, with the
  **[[ministry-of-technology-and-science-zambia|Ministry of Technology and Science]]**,
  **[[e-mark-innovation-hub]]**, the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Youth, Sport and
  Arts.
- **Announced the [[zambia-national-ai-strategy]] is nearing completion (2026-07-17).** Kamwambi
  described ICT as **one of Zambia's fastest-growing economic sectors** and a critical pillar of
  national development, credited government–private-sector collaboration, and called for
  increased investment in AI, robotics and youth-focused innovation programmes. *No sector growth
  rate or GDP-contribution figure is given for the "fastest-growing" characterisation.*

## Why it matters here

The PDU's involvement puts Zambia's AI agenda **inside the centre of government** rather than
solely in the technology ministry — the same pattern as the SMART Zambia Institute's
whole-of-government mandate ([[smart-zambia-institute]]). It is the office announcing the
strategy's status, which makes it, alongside the ministry, one of the two voices the wiki has on
that instrument's timing.
