# Sèmè City (Benin innovation & knowledge city)

Benin's flagship **innovation and higher-education hub** — a government-backed "knowledge city" and
network of **Sèmè City Hubs** delivering skills, incubation and applied research, central to the country's
AI and startup ambitions (and to the Wadagni government's "hub technologique" manifesto). See [[BEN]],
[[capacity.training]], [[tech.ai]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Sorbonne framework convention (2026-06).** SCITI/Sèmè City and **Sorbonne Université** formalised a
  framework convention (building on a 2021 partnership) across **AI, materials science and
  innovation-management** degrees. Source: [[2026-06-07-sciti-sorbonne-convention-cadre]].
- **Host of the National AI Olympiad (2026).** [[tech.ai]] Sèmè City ran Benin's first **[[sniam-benin|National
  AI Olympiad]]** (16 laureates, 8 to Kazakhstan). Source:
  [[2026-06-29-wearetech-benin-first-national-ai-olympiad]].
- **Innovation-ecosystem magnet.** Quantum Surgical founder **Bertin Nahum** toured Benin to explore
  siting high-level innovation centres at the **GDIZ** special economic zone. Source:
  [[2026-04-24-bertin-nahum-ecosysteme-technologique-beninois]].

- **Startup-ecosystem policy (2025).** Then-minister Zoumarou's long-form interview set out Sèmè City,
  API-An and the startup "label" law as pillars of Benin's tech-talent strategy. Source:
  [[2025-07-04-techcabal-benin-digital-future-minister-interview]].
