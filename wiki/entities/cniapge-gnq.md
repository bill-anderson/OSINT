# CNIAPGE (Centro Nacional para la Informatización de la Administración Pública)

Equatorial Guinea's **body for informatising public administration** — the e-government arm, **distinct from
[[cnedoge|CNEDOGE]]** (document issuance). Its DG **Melanio Ebendeng Oyana** represented GNQ at the **World Bank
Global Digital Summit 2025** (Washington), which highlighted Central-African digital integration and the
**CARDIP** regional project. See [[gnq--dpi-govtech]].
