# Equity–AfricaNenda–Gates DPI Partnership (MoU)

**Signed 2026-07-07.** Strategic partnership among **[[equity-group-holdings]]**,
**[[africanenda]]** and the **[[gates-foundation]]** to accelerate development and
implementation of Digital Public Infrastructure across Africa.

## Terms / scope

- Build "practical DPI models" and scale across **20–30 African markets**.
- Focus areas: interoperable payment systems, government/merchant payment solutions,
  cross-border payments, digital ID and secure data exchange, regulatory engagement,
  technical assistance, knowledge sharing and policy advocacy.
- Explicitly linked to **AfCFTA** implementation (cross-border payments, trusted digital
  identity). See [[afcfta]].
- Accompanied by the appointment of **[[james-mwangi]]** as Continental DPI Champion.
