# Bill Anderson

Independent **systems architect** specialising in digital transformation, data
governance policy and data standards, working through **Data Landscapers Ltd** (a
UK-registered company) — see [[data-landscapers]]. His stated focus is **Africa's
digital sovereignty**: "the struggle to ensure that the continent's data
infrastructure is owned, governed and controlled by Africans."

## Profile

- **Former roles** (per the [About page](https://data-landscapers.com/about/)):
  data-standards advisor and technical lead at the **International Aid Transparency
  Initiative (IATI)**; **data ecosystems programme lead at Development Initiatives**
  for 14 years, until that organisation's liquidation in **October 2024**.
- A veteran of the **South African liberation struggle** and a former **ANC military
  intelligence data-processing officer** (his own account).
