# Civil Status Division (CSD, Mauritius)

Mauritius's **civil-registration authority** (within the Prime Minister's Office), responsible for registering
births, marriages, deaths and other civil events and — with the National Identity Card system — anchoring the
country's identity ecosystem. It operates a network of **Civil Status Offices** (33) plus National Identity
Card centres, and maintains a long-standing, largely complete CRVS system (a strength that underpins the
national ID and the newer **[[mauritius-mobile-id|Mobile ID]]** wallet).
