# National Data Centre (Côte d'Ivoire)

Côte d'Ivoire's **sovereign National Data Centre** — financed by a **US [[exim-bank-usa|EXIM]] guarantee
of $66m (~39.6bn FCFA)** and built by **[[cybastion]]** — the **first data centre EXIM has backed in
sub-Saharan Africa**, and the physical anchor of the country's US-financed "digital sovereignty." The US
ambassador framed it as backing "the digital sovereignty of Côte d'Ivoire"; the deal reportedly displaced
a Chinese competitor. Sources:
[[2025-08-26-centre-national-donnees-exim-bank-fratmat]],
[[2025-08-25-us-exim-66m-data-center-guarantee-techafricanews]].
