# Centre for Social and Economic Progress (CSEP)

New Delhi think tank; co-produced *The Africa–India Blueprint for Growth* with the AUDA-NEPAD Africa
Policy Bridge Tank. Its digital chapter (Research ICT Africa's Chetty & Timcke) argues the AU Data Policy
Framework and Malabo Convention "remain largely undomesticated," and positions well-governed **DPI** —
adapting India's Aadhaar/UPI/DigiLocker stack — as an alternative to frontier-AI dependence. See
[[geopol.india]], [[dpi.exchange]]. Source: [[2026-01-01 The Africa-India Blueprint for Growth]].
