# AfriLabs

A **pan-African network of technology and innovation hubs** — a standing continental body connecting incubators, accelerators and innovation spaces across Africa's regions.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Network expansion (2024).** Admitted **18 new member hubs** in Q2 2024 across all African regions, including its **first hub in Stellenbosch** (South Africa) and several in Tunisia. A marker of the continental innovation-ecosystem's growth. See [[tech.innovate]], [[capacity.training]]. Source: [[2024-08-22 AfriLabs opens 18 new tech hubs across Africa]].
