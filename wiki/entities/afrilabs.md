# AfriLabs

A **pan-African network of technology and innovation hubs** — a standing continental body connecting incubators, accelerators and innovation spaces across Africa's regions.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Network expansion (2024).** Admitted **18 new member hubs** in Q2 2024 across all African regions — bringing the network to **496 hubs across 261 cities in 53 African nations** — including its **first hub in Stellenbosch** (South Africa), several in Tunisia and Egypt, East Africa additions (Nairobi, Nakuru, Kisumu) and Central Africa (DRC, Cameroon). A marker of the continental innovation-ecosystem's growth. See [[tech.innovate]], [[capacity.training]].
