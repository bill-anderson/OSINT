# Seychelles Postal Regulator (Postal Regulator Services)

**Seychelles' postal regulatory body**, and the lead agency for the
[[seychelles-national-addressing-system]] — the national addressing and postcode programme that
makes an ostensibly postal regulator the custodian of a foundational **address registry**. It
convenes the inter-agency technical committee behind the system and publishes its status through
its portal. See [[dpi.registry]], [[gov.standards]], [[SYC]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Leads the National Addressing System.** Postal Regulator Services, in collaboration with
  Seychelles Postal Services, the **Ministry of Lands and Housing (MLH)**, the **National Bureau
  of Statistics (NBS)**, **[[dict-seychelles]]**, the **Attorney General's Office**, the **Land
  Transport Division** and the **Local Government Department**, **received Cabinet approval** to
  implement a National Addressing and Postcode System, aimed at improving the structure,
  accessibility and management of address data in line with international standards. See
  [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 National Addressing System - Seychelles Postal Regulator]].
- **Legal framework in development (as described on an undated portal page, proxy-dated 2025).**
  The regulator's technical committee is developing the addressing system's legal framework in
  close consultation with the Attorney General's Office; once finalised, **draft legislation is
  to be submitted to Cabinet for approval**. **This status has no reliable as-of date** — the
  source page is `date_precision: year`, `date_source: proxy`, with no publication date stated —
  and may well have moved since. See [[gov.legislate]]. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 National Addressing System - Seychelles Postal Regulator]].
