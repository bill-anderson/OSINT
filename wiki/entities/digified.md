# Digified

Egyptian **regtech** firm (founded 2019) — **licensed by the [[fra-egypt|FRA]]** (2025-08) to provide AI-powered
**digital KYC (eKYC)** and **e-contracting / e-signature** services to non-banking financial institutions
(lending, investment, insurance). Its suite covers biometric/non-biometric eKYC, government data authentication
against the national-ID and mobile-registry databases, and legally binding e-contracts. Reports **25m+ annual
verifications** — the private-sector leg of Egypt's digital-ID stack alongside CBE's [[haweya]] and the interior
ministry's [[moieg-pass]]. See [[egypt--dpi-id]].
