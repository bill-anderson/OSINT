# John Tanui

**Principal Secretary at Kenya's Ministry of Information, Communications and the Digital
Economy** — a recurring senior interlocutor for Kenya's international digital-cooperation and
infrastructure engagements.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Kenya–Germany AI cooperation (2026-02-03, Nairobi).** Hosted a delegation from the German
  Federal Ministry of Research, Technology and Space to explore a joint **AI centre**
  ([[kenya-germany-ai-centre]]), positioning **[[kenya-aist]]** as a core element alongside
  responsible-AI governance, talent development and data ecosystems. See [[tech.ai]],
  [[capacity.training]], [[giz]].
- **Sovereign-cloud / hyperscaler talks.** Named in Kenya's exploratory discussions with
  **[[oracle]]** on cloud, AI and sovereign-cloud infrastructure, and cited across Kenya's
  connectivity and cyber-resilience engagements. See [[infra.store]], [[KEN]].
