# SETIC (Executive Secretariat for ICT)

Burundi's **ICT coordination body** (Secrétariat Exécutif des TIC), a lead agency for the country's digital-transformation agenda and a co-implementer of the **[[pafen-burundi]]** Digital Foundations Project (with MINCOTIM and the finance ministry MFBPE).

## Activity in this wiki

- Its Executive Secretary met the **[[ifc]]** in Feb 2025 to explore public-private-partnership routes to Burundi's digital agenda, following a Vietnam digital-cooperation MoU. See [[BDI]].
