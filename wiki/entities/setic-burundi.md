# SETIC (Executive Secretariat for ICT)

Burundi's **ICT coordination body** (Secrétariat Exécutif des TIC), a lead agency for the country's digital-transformation agenda and a co-implementer of the **[[pafen-burundi]]** Digital Foundations Project (with MINCOTIM and the finance ministry MFBPE). Executive Secretary **Bienvenu Irakoze** doubles as PAFEN coordinator, so SETIC signs the project's flagship contracts.

## Activity in this wiki

- Its Executive Secretary met the **[[ifc]]** in Feb 2025 to explore public-private-partnership routes to Burundi's digital agenda, following a Vietnam digital-cooperation MoU. See [[BDI]]. Sources: [[2025-02-18 Burundi sorts out collaboration terms with IFC for digital transformation project]], [[2025-05-13 World Bank - Burundi Digital Foundations Project (PAFEN) Implementation Status Report]].
- **Signs the rural + payments deliverables (2026).** [[digital.rural]] SETIC/Irakoze signed the [[onatel-burundi|ONATEL]] ($5.9m/92 hills) and [[lumitel|Lumitel]] (>$10m) rural-4G contracts and announced the **[[burundipay]]** launch. Burundi's **AI strategy** also tasks SETIC with **modernising its data centre** and building sovereign-cloud/data hubs. Sources: [[2026-05-05-onatel-5-9m-deal-digital-access-rural-burundi]], [[2026-04-22-lia-au-coeur-de-la-strategie-numerique-burundi]].
