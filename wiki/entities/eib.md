# European Investment Bank (EIB)

The European Union's lending arm, a recurring financier of African digital infrastructure. In Mauritania the
EIB financed the **Nouakchott Tier III national data centre** (~€15m, inaugurated May 2025) and, with the EU,
the **second submarine cable** (**[[ellalink|EllaLink]]** branch — >€43.5m, of which €25.6m execution). A
principal vehicle of the EU's ([[geopol.eu]]) infrastructure engagement, financing the EU-backed alternative
to Chinese and Gulf vendor footprints.
