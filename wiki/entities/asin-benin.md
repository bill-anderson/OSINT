# Agence des Systèmes d'Information et du Numérique (ASIN)

Benin's national digital agency, the central coordinator of the country's
X-Road-model digital public infrastructure. Directed by **Marc-André Loko**,
ASIN owns interoperability, the citizen portal, the national PKI and e-government
delivery for [[BEN]]. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.exchange]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **DPI operator (peer-learning webinar, June 2025).** ASIN officials set out
  Benin's nine-year DPI trajectory (begun ~2016 under President Talon, modelled
  on Estonia), built on the [[uxp-benin]] interoperability platform —
  [[cybernetica]]'s proprietary UXP, X-Road-inspired but not X-Road. As of
  mid-2025: **1,500+ services integrated, 55+ institutions connected, 300+
  services digitized, 70M+ data-exchange transactions**. ASIN enforces that
  foreign partners collaborate with a local company to build national capacity.
  See [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-23 Benin shares its X-Road-inspired DPI interoperability experience in peer learning webinar]].
- **Cooperation deals + first cyber report (Sept 2025).** At the **5th Cyber
  Africa Forum** (Cotonou), ASIN signed **three** cooperation agreements — with
  **IIDiA** (modernise national systems), **Quality Corporate** (e-signatures,
  seals, digital ID) and **Orbus Digital Services** (PKI software for e-gov). At
  the same event Benin released its **maiden national cybersecurity report
  (2021–24)**, flagging vulnerabilities across state platforms. See
  [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-01 Benin boosts digital inclusion, govt service access with cooperation deals]].
- **SECUROUTE context (Jan 2026).** [[securoute]], the top service on Benin's
  UXP dashboard, sits within the ASIN-coordinated interoperability layer;
  [[cybastion]] is expanding its infrastructure with the Ministry of Economy and
  Finance. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Cybastion Advances Digital Government Solutions, Supports Benin's SECUROUTE Infrastructure Upgrade]].
