# Agence des Systèmes d'Information et du Numérique (ASIN)

Benin's national digital agency, the central coordinator of the country's
X-Road-model digital public infrastructure. Directed by **Marc-André Loko**,
ASIN owns interoperability, the citizen portal, the national PKI and e-government
delivery for [[BEN]]. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.exchange]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **DPI operator (peer-learning webinar, June 2025).** ASIN officials set out
  Benin's nine-year DPI trajectory (begun ~2016 under President Talon, modelled
  on Estonia), built on the [[uxp-benin]] interoperability platform —
  [[cybernetica]]'s proprietary UXP, X-Road-inspired but not X-Road. As of
  mid-2025: **1,500+ services integrated, 55+ institutions connected, 300+
  services digitized, 70M+ data-exchange transactions**. ASIN enforces that
  foreign partners collaborate with a local company to build national capacity.
  See [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-23 Benin shares its X-Road-inspired DPI interoperability experience in peer learning webinar]].
- **Cooperation deals + first cyber report (Sept 2025).** At the **5th Cyber
  Africa Forum** (Cotonou), ASIN signed **three** cooperation agreements — with
  **IIDiA** (modernise national systems), **Quality Corporate** (e-signatures,
  seals, digital ID) and **Orbus Digital Services** (PKI software for e-gov). At
  the same event Benin released its **maiden national cybersecurity report
  (2021–24)**, flagging vulnerabilities across state platforms. See
  [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-01 Benin boosts digital inclusion, govt service access with cooperation deals]].
- **SECUROUTE context (Jan 2026).** [[securoute]], the top service on Benin's
  UXP dashboard, sits within the ASIN-coordinated interoperability layer;
  [[cybastion]] is expanding its infrastructure with the Ministry of Economy and
  Finance. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Cybastion Advances Digital Government Solutions, Supports Benin's SECUROUTE Infrastructure Upgrade]].
- **Service delivery + local capacity (2025).** [[dpi.govtech]] Benin reports **1,000+** digitised and
  **210+ fully dematerialised** services on service-public.bj (**>85% of public services online**, 2025).
  ASIN builds local capacity via the EU-funded **Beninnovation** PaaS programme (advised by Estonia's
  e-Governance Academy and [[cybernetica]]) and pushes govtech to communes — the **e-Commune** pilot
  (Porto-Novo, Lokossa, Djougou under PRSCG2), the **GEC** correspondence platform, and the **"229 Cadre
  de Vie"** citizen-reporting app (free 144 line, local languages) — and hosted the **WSIS+20 Africa
  summit** (Cotonou, May 2025). Sources:
  [[2025-01-02-wearetech-benin-seeks-expert-beninnovation]], [[2025-10-01-projet-e-commune-lance]],
  [[2025-02-18-gec-courriers-administration]], [[2026-02-02-benin-229-cadre-de-vie-plateforme]],
  [[2025-04-08-benin-ere-numerique-revolution-administrative]], [[2025-05-15-smsi20-benin-cas-decole-numerique]],
  [[2025-05-13-cotonou-accueille-smsi20]].

- **"Diversified portfolio of best-in-class partners" (2024, DG Loko).** ASIN frames Benin's
  strategy as deliberate multi-vendor diversification against single-partner dependence: Estonia/Rwanda
  expertise ([[cybernetica]], eGA); **Chinese** financing (Huawei, CITCC via China Development Bank /
  EximBank); Microsoft/Oracle-certified firms; French feasibility houses; Tunisian firms; and delegated
  management of Benin's digital-infrastructure company to Senegal's **Sonatel** (2021). Benin **leads
  [[smart-africa]]'s digital-ID interoperability project** (cross-border ID: Senegal, Togo, soon Ghana).
  See [[benin--dpi-id]], [[geopol.china]]. Source: [[2024-05-29 Marc-Andre Loko - Benin's digital strategy involves a diversified portfolio of partners]].
- **Strategy & budget (2025–26).** Custodian of the **[[benin-digital-strategy-2031|digital strategy
  stack]]** (Vision 2060 pillar + Deloitte-facilitated 2031 roadmap; Benin a **2025 GovTech Maturity
  Index** top performer with >250 services and >3,000 km fibre); the **2026** digital budget was
  **CFA27.2bn (~$48m), down 6.32%** on 2025. **Ministerial change (2026-05):** minister **Aurélie Adam
  Soulé Zoumarou** handed to **Mahuna Akplogan** under the new Wadagni government; the ministry was
  renamed **Transformation Digitale et Innovation** with an explicit national-AI mandate. Sources:
  [[2025-11-27 Benin Cuts Digital Budget by 6.32% Despite Expanding Tech Ambitions]],
  [[2026-02-15 Digital - Benin outlines its strategic vision for 2031]].
- **Trust services + a National Root CA (2025).** [[gov.standards]] ASIN's Cyber-Africa-Forum MoUs
  (IIDiA / Quality Corporate / Orbus) build out **PKI, e-signatures/seals and digital-ID trust
  services**, with ASIN operating the **National Root Certification Authority** — DG Loko: "we no longer
  want to be mere consumers of technology." Source:
  [[2025-07-17-techafricanews-benin-asin-trust-services-agreements]].
- **Code du numérique implementing decrees (2025-07).** [[gov.legislate]] The Council of Ministers
  adopted **eight long-awaited implementing decrees** of the Code du numérique — covering **e-ID assurance
  levels, cryptology licensing, lawful interception and e-waste** — operationalising the legal base under
  ASIN's trust services and digital ID. Source: [[2025-07-02-code-numerique-huit-decrets]].
- **Govtech coordination localised (2026-03).** [[giz]] handed the **eServices Community of Practice** to
  **Club DSI Bénin** — a donor-to-local handover of a govtech coordination body. Source:
  [[2026-03-02-cop-eservices-club-dsi]].
- **"Urbanising" the state IS — PASI + PDAIE (2026-07).** [[dpi.exchange]] After years of siloed,
  ministry-by-ministry platform-building, ASIN opened consultation on two structuring referentials — the
  **Politique d'Architecture des Systèmes d'Information de l'État (PASI)** and the **Politique de
  Développement et d'Intégration des Applications de l'État (PDAIE)** — to harmonise standards,
  interoperability and procurement and stop duplicative solutions. Also **50-in-5** representative for
  Benin (2026, see [[benin--dpi-id]]). Source: [[2026-07-01-pasi-pdaie-architecture-systemes-etat]].
