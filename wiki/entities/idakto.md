# iDAKTO

French digital-identity technology company that built Morocco's **[[mon-e-id]]** national digital-identity
app and citizen portal, and (with ShareID) the national digital-identity **wallet** under Idarati X.0
([[dpi.id]]). Its architecture uses a privacy-preserving hybrid model (server-side 1:N proofing +
on-device biometrics; iDAKTO never holds citizens' biometric data). See [[MAR]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Morocco Mon e-ID + wallet (2025-26).** Built the Mon e-ID app on IDEMIA/IN Group foundations; engaged
