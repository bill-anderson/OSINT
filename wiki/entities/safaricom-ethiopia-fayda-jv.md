# Safaricom Ethiopia–National ID Program Fayda JV

**Deal / Joint Venture Agreement (2026-07).** [[safaricom]] (Safaricom Ethiopia), the
Ethiopian **National ID Program** and **Africom Technologies PLC** signed a JV to deliver
**Lots 3 & 4** of the [[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]] Digital ID Registration Project.

## Terms

- **Scope.** Large-scale citizen registration across seven regions — **Afar, Amhara,
  Tigray, Sidama, South West Ethiopia, Central Ethiopia and South Ethiopia**.
- **Rationale.** Part of the **Digital Ethiopia** strategy; pitched to advance digital
  inclusion, service access, financial inclusion and governance, and to create
  **short-term registration jobs for thousands** of Ethiopians.
- **Principals.** Wim Vanhelleputte (Safaricom Ethiopia CEO); Yodahe Zemichael (National
  ID Program ED). See [[ETH]], [[dpi.id]], [[include.divides]].
