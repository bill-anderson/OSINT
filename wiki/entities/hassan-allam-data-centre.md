# Hassan Allam Digital Infrastructure data-centre licence

**[[ntra-egypt|NTRA]]** granted **Hassan Allam Digital Infrastructure and Data Center Solutions** a licence to
build and operate a data centre — with VC/tech firm **A15** — on a **$400m first-phase** investment, providing
hosting, cloud and **AI-ready** infrastructure for government, financial institutions and business (2026-06).
The **tenth DC licence NTRA has issued in two years**, set against Egypt's **$42.7bn AI-value / 7.7%-of-GDP-by-2030**
targets. See [[egypt--infra-store]].
