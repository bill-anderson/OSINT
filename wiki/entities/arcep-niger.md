# ARCEP Niger

Niger's electronic-communications and postal regulator (DG **Colonel-major Idrissa Chaibou**), the enforcement
arm of the junta's telecoms agenda under President Tiani's "refondation nationale."

- **Continuous mobile-network monitoring platform (2026-01).** ARCEP inaugurated a **permanent QoS/regulatory
  supervision platform** (28 Jan 2026) — tools **TRAFSCAN, QUALCOP, NETFIX** for automated technical/economic
  data collection integrated with operators' network cores — framed as a "lever of digital sovereignty." Deployed
  by Tunisia-based **INFOCOM INTERNATIONAL** (also active in Mali, Togo, Mauritania, Guinea-Bissau, CAR). See
  [[gov.standards]], [[niger--infra-cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-29-arcep-continuous-mobile-network-monitoring-platform-lesahel]].
- **Starlink enforcement (2025-09).** ARCEP cracked down on unlicensed "Wifi Zones" reselling Starlink under
  **Loi 2018-45**, with sanctions and an ultimatum — even as authorised Starlink service launched. See
  [[niger--infra-connect]], [[starlink]]. Source: [[2025-09-09-arcep-crackdown-pirate-starlink-wifi-zones-actuniger]].
