# DGSN (Direction Générale de la Sûreté Nationale, Morocco)

Morocco's national-security directorate and **issuer of the electronic national identity card** and the
national digital identity — operating a **Trusted Third Party Identity Verification** platform used
across banks, healthcare, tax and justice ([[dpi.id]]). It is the identity backbone of Digital Morocco
2030 and the [[mon-e-id]] wallet, built with IDEMIA/IN Group and the Digital Development Agency
([[add-morocco]]). See [[MAR]], [[morocco--dpi-id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **4.6m e-national IDs issued in 2024.** [[dpi.govtech]] DGSN's 2024 report: ~4.7m e-ID cards (Rabat +
  new Marrakech centre); **17 framework agreements** + **30+ institutions** on the verification platform;
  E-Police and E-Blagh portals; 219,408 e-IDs for expatriates. Source:
  [[2024-12-24 Morocco's DGSN issued 4.6 million electronic national IDs in 2024 (annual report)]].
- **Mon e-ID + government-service partnerships (2025).** [[dpi.id]] Signed with the digital-transition
  ministry to make services accessible via the national biometric digital ID; the **[[mon-e-id]]** app
  (2.5m active users, 7m+ transactions since Apr 2022) was fast-tracked after cyberattacks. Sources:
  [[2025-05-05 Morocco boosts digital ID for government services (DGSN–Ministry partnership)]],
  [[2025-09-24 Morocco fast-tracks 'Mon e-ID' digital identity after cyberattacks (iDAKTO)]].

- **CNIE renewal online (2026-05/06).** [[dpi.govtech]] DGSN digitised most of the CNIE-renewal process —
  online pre-fill, document upload, residency-certificate tracking and stamp-fee payment via **Epolice.ma**
  / **cnie.ma**, biometric capture in person (Rabat open house 18–22 May; Commissioner Loubna Kikou) — to
  cut travel and processing time. See [[morocco--dpi-id]]. Sources:
  [[2026-05-23-wearetech-morocco-digitizes-national-id-renewal]],
  [[2026-06-02-biometricupdate-morocco-national-id-renewal-online]].
- **New biometric passport — decree 2.26.551 (2026-07).** [[gov.legislate]] The government council
  approved a decree overhauling the 2008 passport: four languages (Arabic, Amazigh, French, English),
  encrypted **ICAO-aligned** chip, secured signature-page photo, **address removed**, **temporary
  passports abolished**, and removal of the minor-ID-via-passport exception; rollout from **Aug 2026**.
  Sources: [[2026-07-10-en-hespress-morocco-new-biometric-passport-amazigh]],
  [[2026-07-15-biometricupdate-morocco-biometric-passport-legal-framework]].
