# TELEMO (national e-procurement platform, Guinea)

Guinea's national **electronic public-procurement platform**, born of a **Guinea–Rwanda South-South partnership** and officially launched on **12 November 2025** at the Transform Africa Summit ([[transform-africa-summit-2025]]) by Djiba Diakité on behalf of the Head of State. It fully digitises public procurement — a transparency/anti-graft deliverable under [[simandou-2040]] and part of the broader public-administration digitization drive ([[dpi.govtech]]). The Rwanda link ([[RWA]]) is a notable instance of intra-African govtech transfer ([[gov.regional]]). See [[GIN]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launched at TAS 2025 (2025-11-12).** Officially rolled out; the 2025 sector review reports TELEMO "fully digitized public procurement."
