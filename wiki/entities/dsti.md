# DSTI (Directorate of Science, Technology and Innovation, Sierra Leone)

Sierra Leone's science/technology/innovation directorate, central to the government's AI, data-science
and digital-service agenda. Co-ran the whole-of-government AI/cyber leadership training (with MoCTI,
[[nc3]] and Qhala), runs a Data Science Unit, and signed a geospatial MoU with Catholic Relief Services
(the CVP platform / SDS4D). Also a partner in the Giga/UNICEF school-connectivity programme. See
[[sierra-leone--tech-ai]], [[SLE]].
