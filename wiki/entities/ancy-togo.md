# ANCy (Agence Nationale de la Cybersécurité, Togo)

Togo's national cybersecurity agency (created via June-2022 cybersecurity/cybercrime amendments). Runs
the **CERT.tg** incident-response centre and the **National Cybersecurity Strategy 2024–2028**; co-ran
(with the [[ipdcp-togo]]) a 2025 campaign training ~3,000 youth in digital hygiene. See
[[togo--gov-protect]], [[TGO]].
