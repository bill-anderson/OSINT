# Plan National de Développement (PND-RCA) 2024-2028

The Central African Republic's **National Development Plan for 2024–2028**, within which
**digital integration and the digitalisation of public services** are treated as priority,
structuring projects — the policy umbrella over CAR's e-government drive.

## Activity in this wiki

- Frames the government's push to dematerialise administrative procedures, speed processing,
  improve transparency and the business climate. Custodian ministry: **[[mepci-central-african-republic]]**
  (Minister Pr Richard Filakota). The **DUNIA** platform (launch 23 Feb 2026) is presented as
  a flagship deliverable. See [[caf--dpi-govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-11 La digitalisation des services publics et la gouvernance numerique du PND 2024-2028]].
- **The financing vehicle: the Casablanca roundtable, September 2025.** The PND's
  **"Ambition28"** financing roundtable in **Casablanca** is where CAR's largest digital
  commitments were signed — including the **$150m [[socatel]] privatisation MoU** with US firm
  Greenline Technologies (**18 Sep 2025**, before President [[faustin-archange-touadera]]),
  promising a **Tier 3 data centre** and a national connectivity ecosystem. Greenline's own
  announcement puts the roundtable's headline at *"nearly $9 billion in development commitments"*
  from the World Bank, IFC and the EU plus **36 private-sector infrastructure MoUs** — a vendor's
  characterisation, not an audited figure. The SOCATEL MoU entered an **operational phase on
  16 July 2026** with the takeover agreements still unfinalised. **As of 2026-07-20 the wiki holds
  no independent accounting of what share of the roundtable's pledges has been contracted or
  disbursed.** See [[car-national-datacentre]], [[caf--infra-connect]]. Sources:
  [[2025-09-18-greenline-socatel-privatisation-mou-greenline]],
  [[2026-07-17-socatel-greenline-relance-150m-agenceecofin]].
- Successor policy frame to the earlier **"Centrafrique numérique 2018"** national digital
  strategy (largely unimplemented per the 2022 [[greater-internet-freedom]] baseline).
