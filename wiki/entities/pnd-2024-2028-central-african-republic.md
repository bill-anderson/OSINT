# Plan National de Développement (PND-RCA) 2024-2028

The Central African Republic's **National Development Plan for 2024–2028**, within which
**digital integration and the digitalisation of public services** are treated as priority,
structuring projects — the policy umbrella over CAR's e-government drive.

## Activity in this wiki

- Frames the government's push to dematerialise administrative procedures, speed processing,
  improve transparency and the business climate. Custodian ministry: **[[mepci-central-african-republic]]**
  (Minister Pr Richard Filakota). The **DUNIA** platform (launch 23 Feb 2026) is presented as
  a flagship deliverable. See [[caf--dpi-govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-11 La digitalisation des services publics et la gouvernance numerique du PND 2024-2028]].
- Successor policy frame to the earlier **"Centrafrique numérique 2018"** national digital
  strategy (largely unimplemented per the 2022 [[greater-internet-freedom]] baseline).
