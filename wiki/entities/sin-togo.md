# Société d'Infrastructures Numériques (SIN), Togo

Togolese state-owned company (Director-General Attia Byll) that wholly owns and
operates the [[lome-data-centre]] — the vehicle through which [[TGO]] pursues digital
sovereignty by keeping government and business data hosted in-country.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Partnered with ST Digital Togo to launch an in-country private cloud (~June 2026).**
  SIN signed a strategic partnership with ST Digital Togo to deploy a private cloud at
  the [[lome-data-centre]], so Togolese companies' data is hosted and processed
  domestically ([[dpi.govtech]], [[infra.store]]). See [[sin-st-digital-lome-cloud-deal]].
  ST Digital appears to be the same group as [[st-digital-gabon]], with national arms
  across West and Central Africa.
  Source: [[2026-06-02 SIN, ST Digital to deploy cloud services at Lomé data center]].
