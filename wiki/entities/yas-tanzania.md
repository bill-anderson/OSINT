# Yas (Yas Tanzania / Yas Fibre)

**Yas** is the Tanzanian mobile/fibre operator of **Axian Telecom** (the rebranded Tigo Tanzania) — the
country's largest tower operator by count. CEO (2026): **Pierre Canton (Bacara)**.

- **Network scale (2026).** **~4,800 towers** ("~1,000 more than any other operator"), **>Sh1 trillion**
  invested, 4G/5G covering **95%+ of population**; promoting **smartphone financing** and solar charging.
  Sources: [[2026-07-04-yas-invests-sh1-trillion-digital-transformation]], [[2026-04-11-yas-tanzania-hand-over-758-towers]].
- **PPP infrastructure handover (Apr 2026).** Central to a consortium that handed **758 towers + 1,100 km
  fibre** to the government (President Samia presiding); Yas delivered **261 towers (~1/3)** and upgraded
  its 2G footprint to 4G. Source: [[2026-04-11-yas-tanzania-hand-over-758-towers]].
- **Zanzibar broadband PPP (Jun 2026).** Yas Fibre + **[[zictia]]** signed a **20-year, TZS 300bn
  (~$114.5m)** deal to connect **~100,000 households** on Unguja/Pemba in three years. Source:
  [[2026-06-25-yas-zictia-expand-broadband-zanzibar]].
