# ANSI (Agence Nationale pour la Société de l'Information)

Niger's national information-society / digital agency (DG **Anne-Rachelle Marcel Inne**), a lead executor of the
junta's digital-sovereignty programme — cyber capacity, startups and rural inclusion.

- **Cyber three-pillar framing.** At the **AfricaCERT Handshake symposium** (Niamey, Jul 2025), ANSI set out
  Niger's cyber strategy as **infrastructure, human competence, regional (AES) alliance** — "transform our
  handshakes into a regional cyber-defence alliance," alongside the National Cybersecurity Strategy being
  elaborated. See [[niger--infra-cybersec]]. Source: [[2025-07-25-africacert-handshake-symposium-niamey-lesahel]].
- **Startup ecosystem.** ANSI profiles a growing Nigerien startup scene — **Qwiper, E-Mobility Niger, CodeLoccol,
  ADN, Africa Ycone, Maria Server** — under the digital-economy agenda. See [[tech.innovate]]. Source:
  [[2025-08-04-ansi-nigerien-startups-digital-economy-lesahel]].
- **Smart Villages.** ANSI runs the World-Bank-linked **Villages Intelligents** rural-inclusion programme
  (a digital centre at Tabelot, Agadez). See [[digital.rural]]. Source: [[2025-07-10-ansi-smart-villages-tabelot-lesahel]].
