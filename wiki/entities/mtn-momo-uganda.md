# MTN MoMo Uganda (MTN New FinCo)

**MTN MoMo (U) Limited** is Uganda's largest mobile-money business, being **spun out of [[mtn-uganda]]**
into a standalone fintech ("MTN New FinCo"), majority-owned by MTN Group Fintech Holdings and a
minority-shareholder trust — part of MTN Group's Ambition 2025 separation of high-growth fintech from
telecoms.

- **Spin-off approved (2025).** Announced Jun 2025, **overwhelming shareholder approval** at an EGM (Jul
  2025) after investor pushback; a future **Uganda Securities Exchange** listing eyed (dual listing in 3–5
  years, chairman Charles Mbire). MoMo handles **>$14bn (UGX 55tn) annually** across **10m+ active users**
  (MTN Uganda has 21m+ subscribers). Follows MTN Ghana/Nigeria MoMo separations. Sources: [[2025-06-11-mtn-uganda-spin-off-momo-fintech]], [[2025-07-23-mtn-uganda-shareholders-approve-momo-separation]].
