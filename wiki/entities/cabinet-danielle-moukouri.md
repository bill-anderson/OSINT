# Cabinet Danielle Moukouri

Cameroonian **digital-law firm** (principal Maître Danielle Moukouri, admitted at the Cameroon and
Nigeria bars) — the firm the government **selected on public tender to author the National AI
Strategy ([[snia-cameroon]])**: to formulate the vision, propose a financing and implementation plan,
and draft the government AI roadmap with technical/regulatory/financial recommendations after expert
consultation.

## Profile

- Specialises in OHADA corporate law, fintech and financial-services digitisation, AI applied to
  financial services, data protection, crypto/blockchain. Moukouri was the only Central-Africa
  fintech specialist in Who's Who Legal (2021) and the sole woman in Chambers Global's Cameroon
  business-lawyer ranking. That a **digital-law firm — not an infrastructure operator — holds the pen
  on the SNIA** is the analytically live fact: the wiki previously implied ST Digital led the
  strategy. Source: [[2025-03-11-leconomie-cabinet-danielle-moukouri-national-ai-strategy]].
