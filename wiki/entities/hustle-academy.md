# Google Hustle Academy

[[google]]'s practical-skills training programme for African audiences,
delivered with partner **UpSkill Universe**. Launched in 2022 as a free, weeklong
bootcamp for small-business owners in **Nigeria, Kenya and South Africa**, it was
relaunched in 2026 with a widened AI focus.

## Activity in this wiki

- **2026 relaunch and expansion (2026-04-10).** Google relaunched Hustle Academy
  to move beyond entrepreneurs and open enrolment to **employees, students and
  jobseekers** across Sub-Saharan Africa ([[XSS]]), reframing it around practical
  AI skills to close the continent's AI skills gap (per UpSkill Universe's Gori
  Yahaya). The 2026 edition combines short webinars with intensive bootcamps in
  digital commerce, marketing and growth strategy. See [[tech.ai]],
  [[capacity.training]], [[include.access]].
  Source: [[2026-04-10 Google Expands Hustle Academy to Train Africans in Practical AI Skills -]].
- **Reach since launch.** Since 2022, the programme has trained **>18,000**
  small and medium-sized enterprises across the continent (as of the 2026 relaunch);
  a Google-commissioned Public First report earlier put the figure at **10,300 SMBs**
  from Kenya, Nigeria and SA (as of 2024). Sources:
  [[2026-04-10 Google Expands Hustle Academy to Train Africans in Practical AI Skills -]],
  [[2024-01-01 Google's Impact in Sub-Saharan Africa – Google's Impact in Sub-Saharan Africa]].
- **Kenya scale-up via eTrade Alliance (2023–2024).** The Alliance for eTrade Development
  and Google (with Upskill Digital) expanded the Kenyan cohort by **1,000 MSMEs** and added
  an e-commerce course, targeting ≥50% women-led businesses, ≥50% based outside Nairobi and
  ~10% run by people with disabilities. See [[KEN]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2024-01-01 Google Hustle Academy]].
