# UNU-EGOV

The United Nations University's Operating Unit on Policy-Driven Electronic Governance (based in
Guimarães, Portugal). A standing research/advisory body on digital-government strategy and e-governance
policy; it documents and advises on national digital-governance strategies. In this wiki it recurs as
the source/author of country digital-governance strategy documentation (e.g. São Tomé and Príncipe's
national digital-governance strategy).
