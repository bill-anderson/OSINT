# Digital Cabo Verde Project (World Bank)

World Bank / IDA-financed programme (**parent project P171099**) to strengthen Cabo Verde's **digital-competitiveness foundations** and improve delivery and use of **digital public services**. A **2 May 2025 Additional Financing** added **SDR 15.1m (≈US$20.0m)**, structured around three components: (1) enabling legal/regulatory environment; (2) digital competitiveness; (3) digital public services and marketplace. Implemented via the Ministry of Finance's **UGPE** and the Prime Minister's Office **Government Digital Service Team (GDST)**, alongside **[[nosi|NOSi]]**.

Financing references (per audit ToR): IDA credits **V3050-CV (PPA), 6792-CV, 7797-CV**. Underpins the "**60% of services online by 2026 / 80% by 2030**" targets.
