# CDP — Commission de Protection des Données Personnelles (Senegal)

Senegal's **data-protection authority**, established under **[[senegal-data-protection-law|Law No. 2008-12]]**
(in force 2014). Regulates the lawful use of personal data, publishes activity reports and citizen resources.
Noted for accessibility and regular reporting. Also the body regulating the legal framework and data use for
the **[[rnu-senegal|RNU]]** social registry.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Standing regulator of Senegal's data-protection regime.** Enforces Law No. 2008-12; discussions to
  revise the 2008 law for current technology were reported underway as of 2025 (see the digital-rights
  critique of the New Deal, [[2025-03-27 Senegal's Technological New Deal – A Digital Ambition Tested by Digital Rights]]).
