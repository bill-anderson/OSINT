# Eswatini Client Management Information System (CMIS)

**Eswatini's national electronic health record management system**, run by
[[ministry-of-health-eswatini]] — the national spine into which community-level data
from [[eswatini-cbhis]] flows. See [[SWZ]], [[dpi.mis]], [[dpi.exchange]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **The integration point (2026-02-23).** [[eswatini-cbhis]] "is integrated with the
  national electronic health record management system (Client Management Information
  System, CMIS), ensuring consistency and accuracy" — making CMIS the destination for
  Rural Health Motivator data once handheld devices sync. Source:
  [[2026-02-23 From Paper to Pixels - Digital Transformation of Community Health Services in Eswatini]].
- **Thinly evidenced.** The wiki's only source is the World Bank's CBHIS feature, which
  names CMIS in passing. Nothing is held on its vintage, platform, coverage, custodian
  arrangements, or whether it interoperates beyond health — a structural gap, given the
  [[eswatini-economic-update-2025]]'s call for interoperability standards integrating
  health, education and social protection.
