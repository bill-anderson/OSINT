# Equatorial Guinea Medusa / Via Africa submarine-cable plans

Equatorial Guinea's push to **diversify beyond its problem-prone existing subsea connectivity** (the **ASD
cable** keeps degrading quality). An **Analysys Mason** study (begun 2023, presented April 2026 to VP Teodoro
Nguema Obiang Mangue) recommends connecting to the **Medusa** cable (Europe–North Africa), with a landing at
**Bome, Bata** — cost **€20–60m**, possible service **2029–2030**. A separate **Via Africa** cable MoU (12 May
2026) adds a second diversification track. See [[gnq--infra-connect]].
