# Naymote Partners for Democratic Development

A leading Liberian civil-society and democratic-accountability organisation. Executive Director
**Eddie D. Jarwolo** has become the recurring public-interest voice on the national ID crisis
([[dpi.id]], [[gov.protect]]), consistently framing a weak identity system as a **national-security
and electoral-integrity risk**. See [[LBR]], [[liberia--dpi-id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **ID-card quality critique (2025-06).** Jarwolo called the current national ID card substandard and
  lacking critical security features, warning it "could undermine national security and electoral
  integrity," and urged a full redesign (chip, fingerprints, facial recognition, holographic features),
  ECOWAS technical support, coordination with the NIR and NEC, and a subsidised rollout for poor
  Liberians. Source: [[2025-06-24-current-id-card-undermine-national-security-observer]].
- **On the stalled rollout + OSD concession (2026-03→05).** Jarwolo called low enrollment "a national
  risk" that exposes Liberia to fraud and political manipulation, criticised pushing private actors to
  carry costs upfront where repayment is uncertain, and judged "the political will is lacking." Source:
  [[2026-03-20-national-id-rollout-held-up-1-7m-debt-fpa]].
