# Malawi Communications Regulatory Authority (MACRA)

Malawi's **communications regulator**. MACRA mandated **biometric SIM registration** (effective 1 July 2025),
tying mobile subscriptions to verified identity, and runs a **National Addressing System** project to assign
standardised addresses — a registry layer feeding e-government and service delivery. MACRA is also the **designated Data Protection Authority (DPA)** under the
**[[malawi-data-protection-act-2024|Data Protection Act 2024]]**.

## As Data Protection Authority (2026)

- **Draft Data Protection Regulations + registration fees (Jul 2026).** MACRA consulted stakeholders (Mount
  Soche Hotel, Blantyre) on **Draft Data Protection Regulations, Guidelines and Registration Fees** that would
  make **registration mandatory** for organisations that collect/process personal data. Proposed fees are
  **turnover-based**: from **K50,000 (~$29)** for SMEs to **K7m (>$4,000)** for the largest controllers/
  processors — some stakeholders flagged "jumps" in the structure. Officials: Head of Data Protection **Daniel
  Chiwoni**; PS (ICT) **Harold Msusa**. Sources:
  [[2026-07-16-macra-data-protection-registration-compliance-malawi24]],
  [[2026-07-14-macra-data-protection-registration-fees-consultation-itweb]].
- **DPA + MwCERT to become independent institutions (2026-03).** With renewed **[[world-bank]]** technical
  assistance (Tim Kelly), MACRA plans to spin the **DPA** and the **Malawi Computer Emergency Response Team
  (MwCERT)** — both currently housed inside the regulator — into **independent institutions**, alongside WB
  support for critical-infrastructure security, legal/regulatory frameworks and regional cyber capacity (DG
  **Mayamiko Nkoloma**; the USF **Connect-a-School** project noted). See [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-26-world-bank-cybersecurity-data-protection-support-techafricanews]].

- **AI + digital-ID collaboration with UNDP (2026-07).** MACRA and **[[undp]]** are exploring collaboration on
  **AI, digital identity and data protection** — a priority being **integrating the National ID with SIM
  verification** (telecom integrity, anti-fraud) alongside responsible AI and youth digital skills (DG
  Mayamiko Nkoloma). See [[tech.ai]], [[malawi--tech-ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-10-macra-undp-ai-digital-id-collaboration-itweb]].
