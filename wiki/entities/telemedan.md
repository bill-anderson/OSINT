# Telemedan

A Chadian e-health startup (founder Abakar Mahamat) delivering healthcare to remote areas via
**solar-powered telemedicine kiosks**, supported by Chad's Ministry of Health and the World Bank. One of
Chad's few home-grown digital-health ventures. See [[chad--tech-innovate]], [[TCD]].
