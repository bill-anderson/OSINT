# YCELL acquisition of 80% of GAMCEL

The **privatisation of GAMCEL** — the state mobile operator (under GAMTEL). **YCELL** acquired an **80% equity
stake**. Eight companies bid; **DK Telecom** and **YCell** were the strong contenders, with YCell the most
responsive (winning proposal **D6.7bn**, disclosed Dec 2025). The **Share Investment & Network Modernization
Agreement** (GAMTEL–YCELL) was signed **10 July 2026**, with YCELL committing **GMD 6.1bn** to a network/
infrastructure modernisation programme *(the final signed commitment; distinct from the D6.7bn Dec-2025 bid
figure)*. Involves staff-restructuring. See [[gambia--infra-connect]].
