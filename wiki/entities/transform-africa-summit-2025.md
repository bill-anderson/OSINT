# Transform Africa Summit 2025 (Conakry)

The **7th Transform Africa Summit (TAS 2025)**, organised by the **[[smart-africa]]** Alliance (42 member states) with the Government of Guinea, held in **Conakry, 12-14 November 2025** under the theme **"AI for Africa: Innovate Locally, Impact Globally."** Guinea was the **first Francophone West African** country to host the continent's flagship digital forum (originally slated for 2020, postponed by COVID). Patronage of Presidents Doumbouya (Guinea) and Kagame (Rwanda, Smart Africa Board chair). A showcase both for Guinea's digital-sovereignty narrative and for Smart Africa's Single Digital Market agenda. See [[GIN]], [[XAF]], [[gov.regional]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Closing figures.** 7,000+ participants, 79 nationalities, 1,552 government representatives, 45 ministers, 584 CEOs, 590 startups; **14 MoUs** signed. Source: [[2025-11-19 Transform Africa Summit 2025 (Conakry) - Simandou 2040 Pillar 3, TELEMO, SANIA-SADX and the closing wrap]].
- **Guinea deliverables showcased.** [[dpi.govtech]] Launch of **Simandou 2040 Pillar 3** ([[simandou-2040]]) and **TELEMO** e-procurement ([[telemo]], Guinea-Rwanda); local data hosting via the Tier III data centre ([[guinea-national-data-centre]]); .GN reinstated; FUGAS ([[fugas]]); a first women's hackathon (ANSUTEN, 130+). Sources: [[2025-11-13 Transform Africa Summit 2025 opens in Conakry, epicentre of digital cooperation in Africa]], [[2025-09-05 Guinea Launches National Domain .gn and Unveils First Tier III Data Center]].
- **Smart Africa launches & MoUs.** [[finance.mou]] Smart Africa unveiled **SANIA** (innovation-ecosystem platform) and signed MoUs for the Single Digital Market — with **[[visa]]** (government services, digital payments, inclusion, e-commerce), YouthConnekt Africa, a **SADX** pilot (Benin/Ghana/Rwanda), SADA with The Gambia, AFTLD (domain sovereignty) and Women Political Leaders. Also flagged Smart Africa's new three-year strategy (flagships SANIA, SADX, Digital ID). Source: [[2025-11-19 Transform Africa Summit 2025 (Conakry) - Simandou 2040 Pillar 3, TELEMO, SANIA-SADX and the closing wrap]].
