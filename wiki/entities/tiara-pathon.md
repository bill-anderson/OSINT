# Tiara Pathon

South Africa ([[ZAF]]) National AI Skills Director at [[microsoft-elevate]]. She
frames AI literacy as a **public good** rather than a privilege, and Microsoft
Elevate as the **"connective tissue"** across government, industry and educators.

## Activity in this wiki

- **AI-literacy interview (2026-03-27).** In *The African Innovators Series*,
  Pathon set out the case that closing South Africa's AI-skills gap requires
  attacking accessibility, relevance and scalability together — meeting learners
  "where they are" (mobile-first, low-bandwidth) and localising to South African
  contexts. She named [[microsoft-elevate]] delivery partners **SABC+**, **Civic
  AI** and **Ikamva Digital** (with GIZ), and argued the critical infrastructure
  for Africa's AI future is human, not compute or connectivity. See [[tech.ai]],
  [[capacity.training]], [[capacity.literacy]], [[XSS]].
