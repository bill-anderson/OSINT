# Thales

French technology group (its Thales Digital Identity & Security arm, ex-Gemalto) supplying biometric
and identity systems internationally.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gabon biometric registry, historical and revived.** In **2012** Gabon awarded Thales a biometric
  national-registry project; the first ID cards were issued in **2013** but faced setbacks. Belgian
  firm **Semlex** was then contracted for issuance but the contract was **terminated (reported 2015)**,
  producing the ~11-year gap in card renewals. Reporting around the 2024 CNIE relaunch indicates
  **Thales is taking over the contract again**, setting up equipment and training personnel. Sources:
  [[2024-07-02-gabon-resumes-electronic-id-11-year-break]],
  [[2024-07-02-gabon-electronic-id-11-year-hiatus]].

Note a provenance tension in the Gabon ID story: the 2024-relaunch reporting credits card/software
supply to **[[in-groupe]]**, while Thales is reported to be resuming its historical registry contract —
the two vendor roles (registry vs card/credential) are distinct but under-disambiguated in the
secondary coverage. Tracked on [[gabon--dpi-id]].
