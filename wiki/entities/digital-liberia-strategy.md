# Digital Liberia Strategic Plan 2025–2029

Liberia's five-year national digital-transformation roadmap, titled **"Digital Liberia: Equity,
Sovereignty and Innovation for a Connected Future"**, owned by the Ministry of Posts and
Telecommunications and the [[lta-liberia]]. It frames digital sovereignty as reducing reliance
on external infrastructure and platforms while building local digital capacity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Strategy unveiled (~2026-02-03).** The plan sets out **eight priority focus areas** —
  universal connectivity, digital inclusion, a national digital addressing system with postal
  reform, cybersecurity and data sovereignty, e-governance, and ICT-driven innovation — grouped
  under **four strategic pillars**. It aligns to the government's **ARREST Agenda** and broader
  priorities of economic growth, job creation and improved public-service delivery. Minister of
  Posts and Telecommunications Sekou M. Kromah called it "action-oriented"; the [[lta-liberia]]'s
  acting chair Patrick R. Honnah presented it as timely direction for Liberia to position itself
