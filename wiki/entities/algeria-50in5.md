# Algeria 50-in-5 DPI campaign membership

Algeria joined the **50-in-5 campaign** (launched Nov 2023; 50 Global-South countries each building at least
one DPI component securely and interoperably) as the **15th African / 33rd overall member**, announced at the
**Global Africa Tech Summit** (Algiers, end-Mar 2026), with the **[[ministry-of-post-and-telecommunications-algeria|Ministry of Post & Telecommunications]]** representing the
government. Anchors: the 2025 digital-ID & trust-services framework; **Algérie Poste's ~29m payment accounts**;
and a government data-exchange platform under construction. See [[algeria--gov-regional]], [[algeria--dpi-id]].
