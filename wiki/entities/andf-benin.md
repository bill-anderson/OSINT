# ANDF (Agence Nationale du Domaine et du Foncier, Benin)

Benin's national land agency, operator of the **e-Foncier Bénin (e-FB)** digital cadastre — the registry
layer securing land tenure and, increasingly, a fiscal-revenue and investment-security tool. Land
digitalisation is the second pillar of Benin's registry stack alongside ANIP's population/civil registers.
See [[BEN]], [[dpi.registry]].

## Profile & activity

- **e-Foncier + the national cadastre (2025–26).** [[dpi.registry]] The e-FB platform (with the **InfoCad**
  field app and a per-parcel **Numéro Unique Parcellaire, NUP**) underpins the national cadastre.
  Registrations reached **491,420 parcels** across 12 communes (baseline, Feb 2025, nine e-services, <1%
  titled) and **445,695 parcels** in the cadastre proper (Mar 2026; full coverage in Cotonou; 24
  arrondissements in 12 target communes) — Netherlands-funded via **PMAF** (to Jun 2023) then **PPMEC**
  (Jul 2023–Jun 2026). Sources: [[2025-02-04-e-foncier-491000-parcelles]],
  [[2026-03-26-andf-e-foncier-cadastre-national-445000-parcelles]].
- **A legal lock — décret 2025-176.** [[gov.legislate]] The decree of **9 April 2025** makes the
  **Certificat d'Enregistrement au Cadastre (CEC)** mandatory for any land transaction and requires
  systematic cadastre updates — so a parcel can be sold legally even without a full land title, but only
  through e-FB, closing off the informal "foncier mafia." Women hold ~**12%** of registered parcels
  (9% in Porto-Novo). Sources: [[2026-03-26-andf-e-foncier-cadastre-national-445000-parcelles]],
  [[2026-04-22-cadastre-porto-novo-pays-bas]], [[2026-04-04-parakou-cadastre-netherlands-ambassador]].
- **Addressing + urban registries.** [[digital.localgov]] A Council-of-Ministers **national
  street/door-addressing strategy** (Apr 2026) builds a geolocated territorial database (piloting in
  Grand-Nokoué and Parakou); Abomey commissioned a **GIS-based urban land registry** explicitly as a
  fiscal-revenue tool (Jun 2026). Sources: [[2026-04-22-benin-adressage-voies-portes]],
  [[2026-06-23-abomey-registre-foncier-urbain]].
