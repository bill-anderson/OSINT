# Fon-language voice AI (Regional Innovation Lab, Benin)

The first **voice-to-voice AI model in Fon**, the inaugural project of the **Regional Innovation and
Digital Technology Lab** launched in Cotonou (June 2025) by the **Institute for Inclusive Digital Africa
(IIDiA)** and **[[asin-benin|ASIN]]**, **Gates-Foundation-funded** and co-led with the digital ministries
of **Senegal and Côte d'Ivoire**. It is explicitly framed as **digital sovereignty**: giving a "digital
voice" in a mother tongue to millions excluded by French/English-only services and written-literacy
barriers, in a region of 1,000+ languages. See [[BEN]], [[tech.ai]], [[include.divides]].

## What we know

- **Design + use cases.** A voice-to-voice model (speak and be answered in Fon), trained on **NVIDIA
  A100/H100** GPUs, with early DPI-adjacent use cases — checking a **mobile-money balance** (via Celtiis)
  and **birth-certificate** procedures — not just a chatbot but a multilingual public-service access
  layer. Sources: [[2025-06-30-techafricanews-benin-fon-voice-ai-lab]],
  [[2025-06-25-laboratoire-innovation-ia-fon]], [[2025-07-02-telecomreviewafrica-benin-fon-voice-ai-model]].
