# Model Framework for Building Internet Resilience in Africa (ATU-S Framework 001)

A national-policy template (June 2025) from the [[african-telecommunications-union]], [[afrinic]] and the
[[internet-society]] — a six-step methodology for regulators/ICT ministries covering networks/ISPs,
critical infrastructure (power, terrestrial/undersea cable, IXPs, ccTLD/DNS) and market resilience.
Modelled on the US DHS D2R2+DR approach and NIST SP800-160; prescribes resilience metrics (MTBF,
availability, MTIR) and financing routes. See [[infra.connect]]. Source:
[[2025-06-01 Model Framework for Building Internet Resilience in Africa]].
