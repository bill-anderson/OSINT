# Makerere University

Uganda's flagship public university and a node in the national innovation ecosystem via its **Makerere
Innovation and Incubation Center (MIIC)**.

- **Fintech Fridays (Jul 2026).** MIIC + Afriventia Group launched **Fintech Fridays (Uganda Edition)** — a
  recurring forum convening fintech startups, investors, regulators and policymakers (digital payments, AI,
  blockchain, cybersecurity, financial inclusion, digital lending) to position Uganda as a regional fintech
  destination. See [[uganda--dpi-pay]]. Source: [[2026-07-13-makerere-afriventia-fintech-fridays]].
