# Public Health Informatics Institute (PHII)

A programme of the **Task Force for Global Health** specialising in public-health
informatics — the design, implementation and strengthening of health-information systems.
In this wiki it appears as a **technical implementer** ([[dpi.mis]]) of continental
health-data infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Built the Africa CDC Central Data Repository prototype (2026).** Engaged by the
  **[[global-fund]]**, PHII conducted the **needs assessment, cost analysis, operational
  planning and prototype development** for **[[africa-cdc]]**'s **[[africa-cdc-cdr]]**, the
  federated continental health-data platform launched January 2026. See [[dpi.mis]],
  [[capacity.research]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-28 Africa CDC Establishes Central Data Repository to Strengthen Public Health Surveillance]].
