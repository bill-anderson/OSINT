# Ministry of Justice, Legal and Parliamentary Affairs (Zimbabwe)

**The ministry that issued [[zimbabwe-si-76-2025]]**, the statutory instrument creating
securitised deeds and digitising the [[zimbabwe-deeds-registry]]. In this wiki the
Ministry matters chiefly as the holder of a commencement power that has not yet been
exercised.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Issued SI 76 of 2025 (gazetted 2025-07-18).** The regulations were made by the
  **Minister for Justice, Legal and Parliamentary Affairs**. See [[gov.legislate]].
  Source:
  [[2025-07-28 Legal Discussions with Vengai Madzima - Digitization of the Deeds Registry in Zimbabwe]].
- **Holds the commencement trigger — unexercised as of 2025-08-01.** The 24-month deed-
  validation window runs from **the date the Minister announces commencement**, not from
  gazetting; as of the 1 August 2025 source, commencement had **not** been triggered
  ("expected to come into effect shortly"). The wiki therefore cannot date the window
  (GAP-ZWE-003). Source:
  [[2025-08-01 How to secure your property in Zimbabwe with the new digital deed system]].
- **Holds the extension power.** After the window expires only securitised deeds are
  valid — **unless the Minister, with the Registrar's approval, extends the transitional
  period by notice published in the Gazette**. Source:
  [[2025-08-01 How to secure your property in Zimbabwe with the new digital deed system]].

Nothing further about the Ministry — its officeholder, structure or wider digital
portfolio — is held here.
