# Capital Markets Authority (CMA), Uganda

Uganda's **capital-markets regulator**. In 2025 it introduced a **fintech sandbox for capital markets** and
holds a private member's bill to regulate crypto as **virtual assets**.

- **Tokenised-markets framework (2025–26).** The sandbox and pending virtual-assets bill underpin the
  **[[global-settlement-network|GSN]]/[[akiba-international|AKIBA]]** tokenised-exchange plan — regulators
  "actively building the framework for tokenised capital markets before the market forces them to." See
  [[uganda--dpi-pay]]. Sources: [[2026-04-08-gsh-acquire-akiba-tokenised-capital-markets]],
  [[2025-06-19-uganda-urged-fast-track-crypto-reforms]].
