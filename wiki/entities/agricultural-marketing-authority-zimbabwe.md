# Agricultural Marketing Authority (AMA), Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe's agricultural-marketing regulator — building a **cotton-farmer biometric ID registry**.

- **Cotton-farmer biometric ID (Oct 2025).** Cleared to begin biometric enrolment of cotton growers (field
  teams from 15 Oct 2025), to enforce **"one farmer, one merchant"** and end cross-contracting/side-marketing
  — emulating the tobacco sector's **[[tobacco-industry-and-marketing-board|TIMB]]** registry (Deputy Minister
  Vangelis Haritatos: "the bedrock of a new era of transparency"). See [[zimbabwe--dpi-id]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-10-nigeria-zimbabwe-farmer-digital-id]].
