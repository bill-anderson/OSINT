# Techno Brain

**Nairobi-headquartered technology firm** (also rendered "Technobrain") that built and managed the
technical backbone of Liberia's [[national-identification-registry-liberia|National Identification
Registry]] system ([[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]]). Techno Brain is the vendor whose unpaid balance
**locked Liberia out of its own biometric database** — the concrete mechanism behind the year-long
issuance halt, and the clearest illustration of the dependency risk in Liberia's outsourced digital
spine. See [[LBR]], [[liberia--dpi-id]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **The $1.7m debt lock (2025→2026).** Techno Brain built the Registry's system for **US$5.9m**; the
  government still owes **US$1.7m** and the firm withheld full access to the biometric registration
  database until the debt is cleared. Even the ~640,000 already registered could not be served, and a
  **$2m World Bank tranche** for expanding centres was put on hold. As of March 2026 the debt was not in
  the Registry's approved 2026 budget. Sources:
  [[2026-03-20-national-id-rollout-held-up-1-7m-debt-fpa]],
  [[2026-03-23-national-id-issuance-halt-1-7m-debt-biometricupdate]].
- **Superseded by the OSD concession (2026-05).** The original Techno Brain contract targeted only 1–2m
  people (all the government could afford); it is being replaced by a much larger PPP concession with
  Austria's **[[osd-international]]** to enroll the full ~5.3m population. Whether the outstanding
  payment to Techno Brain was ever made is unclear. Source:
  [[2026-05-13-osd-austrian-firm-rescue-national-id-security-concerns-fpa]].
