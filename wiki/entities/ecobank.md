# Ecobank (Ecobank Transnational Incorporated)

**A pan-African banking group headquartered in Lomé, operating across 35 markets (as of
2025-10).** CEO: **Jeremy Awori**. Ecobank's Lomé headquarters matter to Togo's positioning:
the country hosts the holding company of a continental banking group, which makes Togo a
natural first market for group-wide product launches and gives a small state disproportionate
weight in West African financial infrastructure. See [[TGO]], [[dpi.pay]],
[[tech.industry]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Thunes partnership — instant cross-border transfers, Togo first (2025-10-31).** Ecobank
  launched an instant cross-border transfer service with Singaporean fintech **[[thunes]]**,
  with **Togo the first market** in a rollout intended to reach the group's **35 markets**.
  Customers, individuals and businesses, can send and receive in real time from or to **more
  than 130 countries**, powered by Thunes' **Direct network** connecting **over seven billion
  mobile wallets and bank accounts**. Awori framed it as serving "borderless banking services
  and support [for] financial inclusion in Africa." Deal:
  [[ecobank-thunes-partnership]]. See [[include.access]], [[finance.mou]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-31 Ecobank launches instant international payment service in Togo]].
- **On the BCEAO instant-payment platform — dated states.** Ecobank was one of **three banks
  operational** on the [[waemu-instant-payment-platform]] in Togo **as of 2025-10-01** (with
  Coris Bank and [[orabank]]), and appears among the **seven institutions committed** or
  preparing interconnection **as of 2025-11-18**. The 2025-10-31 report separately describes
  Ecobank as **"among the three banks in Togo testing"** the BCEAO system — a third
  state-description; these are recorded as reported rather than reconciled. See [[bceao]].
  Sources: [[2025-10-01 BCEAO Launches Instant Payments Platform in Togo with Three Banks]],
  [[2025-11-18 BCEAO Launches Developer Sandbox for Instant Payments]],
  [[2025-10-31 Ecobank launches instant international payment service in Togo]].
