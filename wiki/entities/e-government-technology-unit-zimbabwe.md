# e-Government Technology Unit (Zimbabwe)

The **e-government coordination unit in the Office of the President and Cabinet** (Secretary **Tafara
Matekaire**), responsible for MDA interoperability and ICT standards. It flags **poor MDA connectivity** as
the chief obstacle to e-government. See [[zimbabwe--gov-protect]].
