# G2PX (World Bank)

**The [[world-bank]] programme on digitising government-to-person (G2P) payments, and the
publisher of the end-to-end digital G2P case-study series.** Its Togo study is the anchor
reference for **[[novissi]]**, the country's fully-digital pandemic cash-transfer
programme. See [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.id]], [[include.access]].

**Scope note.** The evidence held here establishes G2PX as the World Bank imprint under
which the Togo case study was published; the acronym is not expanded in the source, and no
wider mandate, budget or country list is asserted from it. A dated fact later drawn from a
G2PX publication becomes a normal source citing this entity.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Case Study — G2PX in Togo: End-to-End Digital G2P.** Prepared by **Brian Webster**,
  drawing on a background report by **Boko and Akogbeto (2022)**. The study documents
  Novissi as an end-to-end digital G2P scheme built where **no social registry existed** and
  national ID coverage was low, but a **voter registry covering 93% of the population** did
  exist — and records Novissi's published reach of **500,000+ recipients, 65% women**. No
  publication date is printed; references run to 2023, so the item is dated **2023 at year
  precision (proxy)**. See [[TGO]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2023-01-01 Case Study - G2PX in Togo, End-to-End Digital G2P (World Bank)]].

**Reference-study note.** Cite, do not absorb: the study's figures stay with the study and
are not promoted onto place or concept pages as current state.
