# Niger Internet Exchange Point (IXP)

An **Internet Exchange Point** inaugurated in Niamey on **19 August 2025**, allowing Nigerien networks to
exchange domestic traffic locally rather than routing it abroad — cutting latency and transit costs and
keeping more traffic (and data) in-country. It is a modest but strategic piece of connectivity
infrastructure in a market with low internet penetration (ITU: ~23% in 2025), complementing Niger's
data-sovereignty ambitions.
