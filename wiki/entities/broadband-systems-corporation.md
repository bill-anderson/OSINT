# Broadband Systems Corporation (BSC)

Rwandan digital-infrastructure company active across connectivity and data centres. In **2026** BSC
became a **Rwanda Space Agency-certified [[starlink]] reseller** (partnership since Dec 2025), offering
tailored packages for health/education/non-profits and planned SME plans up to 300 Mbps
([[rwanda--infra-connect]]); and signed an MoU with **Oman Data Park** to build an **AI-ready Tier III
data centre** in Rwanda ([[rwanda--infra-store]]). CCO: Ndoli Mitali.

See [[RWA]], [[infra.connect]], [[infra.store]].
