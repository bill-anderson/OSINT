# Unified Social Registry (Sudan)

A foundational **social-protection data registry** under development in Sudan —
an integrated, accurate database intended to improve targeting of poor and
vulnerable groups, raise the efficiency of support, and underpin fairer resource
distribution. A core DPI building-block for post-war social recovery and G2P
delivery in a country with **>14m displaced** and no census since 2008.
