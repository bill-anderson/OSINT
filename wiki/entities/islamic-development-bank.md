# Islamic Development Bank (IsDB)

A Jeddah-based multilateral development bank, a channel of Gulf ([[geopol.gulf]]) development finance in
Africa. In Mauritania the IsDB is negotiating a **$50m digital-transformation financing deal** — a **principle
agreement signed 14 April 2026** covering digital infrastructure and equipment, cybersecurity, interoperability
and system modernisation, and digital skills/connectivity/innovation, alongside the German **[[digital-y]]**
project and EU/World Bank support for the data centre and second cable.
