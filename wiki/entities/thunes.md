# Thunes

**A Singaporean fintech operating a global cross-border payments network.** CEO: **Peter De
Caluwe**. Its **Direct network** connects **over seven billion mobile wallets and bank
accounts** (as of 2025-10), and is the rails beneath Ecobank's instant international transfer
service. See [[dpi.pay]], [[tech.industry]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Ecobank partnership — entering Africa via Togo (2025-10-31).** Thunes partnered with
  Lomé-headquartered **[[ecobank]]** to power instant cross-border transfers reaching **more
  than 130 countries**, with **Togo the first market** and an eventual rollout across the
  group's **35 markets**. De Caluwe: the collaboration aims to make payments across Africa
  faster and more efficient — "Together, we provide quicker and more reliable access to
  liquidity and open new growth opportunities." Deal: [[ecobank-thunes-partnership]]. See
  [[TGO]], [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-31 Ecobank launches instant international payment service in Togo]].
