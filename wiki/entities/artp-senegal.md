# ARTP (Autorité de Régulation des Télécommunications et des Postes)

Senegal's telecoms and postal regulator (DG Dahirou Thiam). Enforces **SIM-registration/subscriber-
identification** rules at point of sale (Jan 2025 operator warning — [[senegal--infra-connect]]) and, in
2026, deployed **eight digital regulatory platforms** (incl. SUNUCARTO) to modernise its regulatory
ecosystem. See [[SEN]], [[infra.connect]].
