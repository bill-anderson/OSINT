# M-Pesa (Tanzania)

**M-Pesa** (launched 2008) is Tanzania's largest mobile-money platform, run by **[[vodacom-tanzania]]** —
**41% of ~76 million mobile-money accounts** (2026), **>TZS 16 trillion in monthly transaction value**,
and a merchant network grown to **600,000+** (from 10,000–15,000 five years earlier).

- **Fintech 2.0 platform (2 Apr 2026).** A **$28m** new-generation core: **1,000+ transactions/second
  (~86m/day)**, **99.999% availability** target, AI monitoring, auto-scaling and failover. Source:
  [[2026-04-03-vodacom-tanzania-mpesa-fintech-2-platform]].
- **PayPal alliance (May 2026).** [[paypal]] ↔ M-Pesa wallet transfers via the M-Pesa Super App —
  cross-border for freelancers/online commerce. Source:
  [[2026-05-26-vodacom-mpesa-tanzania-paypal-alliance]].
