# MTN Cameroon

Local operating company of **[[mtn-group]]** — with ~**47% mobile share** one of the two dominant CMR
mobile-money/network operators (alongside [[orange-cameroun]]), and increasingly a **private operator of
sectoral identity and monitoring verticals**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Network capex.** Worked with **Camusat/Aktivco** on network expansion, pledging **$225m over three
  years** (2025). Source: [[2025-06-17-mtn-cameroon-camusat-aktivco-network-expansion-itweb]].
- **Regulator engagement cadence.** Presented annual strategic investment plans to ART (Apr 2025, and
  again 2026) — a recurring PR ritual around the regulator ([[art-cameroon]]). Source:
  [[2025-04-10-mtn-cameroon-2025-strategic-investment-plan-trb-techreviewafrica]].
- **Privately-operated verticals.** A **student digital ID** (with the Ministry of Secondary Education,
  tied to academic/financial/disciplinary records and MoMo school-fee payments), and an **AI
  driver-behaviour / road-safety** deployment with **Camtrack** — reported with no safeguards regime
  stated. Part of a 2026 pattern of private operators running sectoral identity/surveillance rails (cf.
  the taxi ID). A **[[yango|Yango]]** dedicated-driver SIM offering also links MTN to the platform economy.
  Sources: [[2026-06-11-student-digital-id-program-mtn-biometricupdate]],
