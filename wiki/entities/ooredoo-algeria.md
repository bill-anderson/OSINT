# Ooredoo Algeria

One of Algeria's three mobile operators (Qatari Ooredoo group). Secured a provisional **5G licence** (2025-07,
ranked 2nd in the ARPCE tender), shared in the **$492m** three-operator award (Dec 2025), and rolled out **5G
across all wilayas by 2026-03 (ahead of schedule)**. Ran a **Sétif "Ganzat" smart-city** 5G+IoT pilot
(2026-02) and a series of digital-strategy / 5G-AI interviews. See [[algeria--infra-connect]], [[algeria-5g]].
