# RGPH-2 (2nd general population & housing census)

DR Congo's **second general population and housing census — the first since 1984**. Hard dates: cartography
recruitment from **1 Jun 2026**, enumeration **July 2027**; positioned at the centre of the government's
data-for-planning strategy. Source: [[2026-07-11-rgph-2-donnees-fiables-planification-actualite]].
