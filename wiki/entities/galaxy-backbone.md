# Galaxy Backbone

Nigeria's **government-owned ICT, cloud and shared-services provider** — the operator
of federal government network, hosting and data-centre infrastructure. A Tier III/Tier
IV data-centre operator (Abuja/Kano) repeatedly referenced in Nigeria's digital public
infrastructure build-out. See [[NGA]], [[infra.store]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Hosts the NGEA infrastructure (2026-04).** Galaxy Backbone provides the **secure,
  resilient hosting environment** for the [[ngea]] platform — Nigeria's whole-of-
  government enterprise-architecture and MDA interoperability infrastructure — which
  [[nitda]] formally assumed control of at an Abuja handover. This positions Galaxy
  Backbone as the underlying host for cross-government **data exchange**. See
  [[dpi.exchange]]. Source: [[2026-04-07 NITDA Assumes Control Of NGEA Infrastructure]].
