# Ministry of Post and Telecommunications (Algeria)

Algeria's telecoms/postal ministry, headed by Minister **[[sid-ali-zerrouki|Sid Ali Zerrouki]]** — the
operational driver of the "All Fiber" fibre programme, the 5G rollout and satellite-ISP liberalisation, all
framed as Presidency-directed digital-sovereignty work. Oversees **[[algerie-telecom]]** and the regulator
**[[arpce-algeria]]**. See [[algeria--infra-connect]].
