# Trident Digital Tech Holdings

Singapore-based technology firm; via its subsidiary **Trident Digital Tech DRC
Africa SAS**, the private partner building and operating DR Congo's national
digital-ID system under a 20-year exclusive concession.

## Activity in this wiki

- **RDC-PASS PPP (DRC).** Under a **20-year public-private partnership** signed
  June 2025 (value ~**$97.1m**), Trident holds **exclusive e-KYC rights for 20
  years** to operate **[[rdc-pass]]**, DRC's Web 3.0-based national digital ID
  (launch slated 13 June 2026). Trident raised **$2.6m** for initial deployment.
  See [[COD]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-11 DR Congo to Launch National Digital Identity System RDC-PASS on June 13]].
- **Anchoring the 2026–2030 digital-gov push (2026-01).** As DRC targets a functional
  digital-government platform by March 2026 (interbank payments, tax portal, customs) under
  its ~$10.4bn blueprint, reporting reaffirms the Trident-implemented, **blockchain-based
  [[rdc-pass]]** ($97.1m) as the national digital-ID underpinning the whole ecosystem. See
  [[COD]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-30 DRC envisages a functional digital government platform by March]].

## Reads

- `colonialism` / `sovereignty`: outsourcing a country's **foundational identity
  layer** — and 20 years of exclusive e-KYC rights — to a foreign private firm is
  the sharpest version of the externally-controlled-sovereignty tension seen
  across [[nigeria--dpi-id]], [[zambia--dpi-id]] and DPI generally.
