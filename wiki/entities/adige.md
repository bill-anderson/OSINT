# ADIGE (Digital Agenda for Equatorial Guinea)

Equatorial Guinea's national digital agenda (Agenda Digital para Guinea Ecuatorial), the policy frame under which the government is pursuing digitalization — nested within the **National Sustainable Development Strategy 2021-2035 (AGENDA 2035 / National Development Plan 2035)**, which added technology and innovation as a cross-cutting theme in 2021 ([[gov.policy]]). ADIGE has **four objectives**: universalize telecommunications access, digitize administrative procedures, support the ICT sector, and empower citizens to use digital tools. Implementation is early-stage and the World Bank flags a weak regulatory framework and an absent national interoperability framework. See [[GNQ]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Frame for the WB diagnostic (2024).** [[dpi.govtech]] The [[equatorial-guinea-digital-economy-diagnostic]] assesses progress against ADIGE's objectives across the five DE4A pillars. Source: [[2024-06-25 Equatorial Guinea Digital Economy Country Diagnostic - Bridging the Gaps]].
- **CEM digitalization priorities (2025-03).** The World Bank's Country Economic Memorandum ties diversification to digitalizing public services (one-stop shop, online registration, Commercial Registry). Source: [[2025-03-04 Country Economic Memorandum on Equatorial Guinea 2025 - Building Foundations for Renewed Diversified and Inclusive Growth]].
