# Moove

**Nigerian mobility fintech** — the reference case for **drive-to-own vehicle finance** in African
ride-hailing, in which drivers repay a vehicle out of platform earnings and take ownership at term.
Reported at a **$2bn valuation** (as cited 2026-07).

The model matters to this wiki because it is **credit infrastructure wearing a mobility badge**: the
platform holds the earnings data, the repayment stream and the asset, which makes ride-hailing a
consumer-lending channel for drivers outside formal banking. **[[gocab]]** applies the same structure
in Côte d'Ivoire, layering electric-vehicle fuel savings on top of it.
