# Zambia Cyber Security Agency (ZCSA)

Zambia's cybersecurity authority, **established under the Cyber Security Act No. 3 of 2025**
([[cyber-security-act-zambia]]) — mandated to protect critical information infrastructure and provide
incident-response/technical support (ransomware, compromised accounts, broadcast disruption).

- **Pre-election outreach (Jul 2026).** ZCSA (Director Nalucha Imasiku) urged journalists to learn the cyber
  laws ahead of the 13 Aug election — the government's **protective** framing of the Acts. See
  [[zambia--infra-cybersec]]. Source: [[2026-07-15-journalists-urged-learn-cybersecurity-laws]].
