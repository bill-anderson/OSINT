# Unwanted Witness

Kampala-based digital-rights CSO. Publishes the annual **Privacy Scorecard Report**
([[privacy-scorecard-report]]) and election data-governance monitoring; APC-supported.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Privacy Scorecard Report 2025 (2026-05).** Continental privacy-compliance score rose 40%→46%;
  286 organisations across 9 countries and 8 sectors assessed; Kenya leads (46%), Zimbabwe lowest
  (28%); health sector worst (22%). See [[gov.protect]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-18 Africa’s Digital Economy Is Expanding Faster than Its Privacy Protections]].
- **Uganda 2026 polls data-protection report (2026-03).** Documented an implementation deficit and
  selective enforcement around mandatory biometric voter verification and NIN integration. See
  [[UGA]], [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-31 Uneven Enforcement of Data Protection Laws Ugandas 2026 Polls]].
