# Egypt Vision 2030

Egypt's **National Agenda for Sustainable Development** (updated version published 2023) — the umbrella national strategy under which the digital agenda sits. Its digital leg, **[[digital-egypt|Digital Egypt]]** (and the Digital Transformation Strategy 2030 / ICT 2030), aligns human capital, digital services, IT infrastructure and innovation; the CBE's cashless-society and financial-inclusion goals, the health-digitisation drive (DHIS2), and social-protection platforms are all framed against it.
