# PayInc (formerly BankservAfrica)

South Africa's national low-value payments processor — operator of **[[payshap]]** and **SAMOS**-adjacent
retail rails, **rebranded from BankservAfrica** in late 2025.

- **SARB co-ownership (Oct 2025).** The **[[sarb]]** acquired a **50% equity stake**, making the central bank
  co-owner of the instant-payment infrastructure; Finance Minister **Godongwana**'s Feb-2026 Budget
  positioned PayInc as **"open, shared national payment infrastructure"** supporting interoperability across
  providers (a national payment utility for high-value and retail). See [[south-africa--dpi-pay]]. Sources:
  [[2026-04-02-payshap-r100bn-national-payment-utility]], [[2026-05-21-payshap-pivots-to-merchants]].
