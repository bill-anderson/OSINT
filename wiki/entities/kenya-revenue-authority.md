# Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA)

Kenya's national tax authority (Commissioner General Humphrey Wattanga), turning consumer digital
rails into tax infrastructure to widen the compliance net.

## Activity in this wiki

- **WhatsApp tax-filing chatbot launched (~2026-04).** KRA launched a WhatsApp-based tax-filing
  chatbot ([[meta]]'s platform) that cuts the filing process from eight steps to three. Taxpayers
  chat with KRA's verified number, verify identity via their KRA PIN, and the bot auto-pulls
  pre-filled PAYE data (gross income, taxable earnings, statutory deductions) for review before
  submission. The aim is behavioural change to close a large compliance gap: Kenya has 22m+
  registered taxpayers but only ~7m actively file. KRA's upgraded taxpayer dashboard also exposes
  invoices, imports/exports and expense claims. Tags [[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.pay]],
  [[data.statistics]] in [[KEN]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-02 WhatsApp tax filing set to pull millions into Kenya's tax net]].

- **Continental context.** Part of Africa's broader "digital rails as tax infrastructure" turn,
  alongside SARS eFiling ([[sars]]), Nigeria's FIRS and Rwanda's Irembo (cf. Malawi's
  [[malawi-revenue-authority]]).
