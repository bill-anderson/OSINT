# Rimatel

A **new (fourth) entrant** to Mauritania's mobile market, surfacing as a **provisional 5G licence winner** in
April 2026 alongside the three incumbents **[[mauritel]]**, **[[mattel]]** and **[[chinguitel]]** — the first
new operator to break the three-operator structure. Bid **MRU 265m + 5% of annual 5G revenue** (second
highest). Corporate ownership not yet established in this wiki.
