# Autorité de Protection des Données (APD)

Mauritania's **data-protection authority** — the governmental body overseeing personal-data-protection law.
Per the US ITA country commercial guide (2026), the legislation governing personal data protection exists and
the APD is **pursuing a 2023–2026 strategic plan** aimed at bringing the law into effective operation. The
APD's head attended the **national-cloud launch** (June 2026), placing data protection alongside the
digital-sovereignty push. Surfaced as a net-new reference object; its enforcement record is not yet
established in this wiki.
