# Afia (عافية)

A Sudanese **health-sector digital platform** (health MIS / e-health), launched
as part of a comprehensive health-sector digital-transformation strategy to
improve service quality, access to medical information and health governance.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Strategy meeting with WHO (2026-05).** The
  **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** met a **[[ministry-of-health-sudan]]**
  and **[[world-health-organization]]** delegation to set a national
  health-digital-transformation strategy; the WHO representative pledged support
  and international expertise. Health-sector DPI building amid post-war
  reconstruction. See [[dpi.mis]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[SDN]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-20 Comms Ministry and WHO discuss health-sector digital transformation; Afia platform (SUNA)]].
