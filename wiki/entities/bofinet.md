# BoFiNet (Botswana Fibre Networks)

Botswana's **state-owned wholesale connectivity operator**, developer and operator of the national data-centre infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Digital Delta Data Centre (DDDC), 2025.** BoFiNet launched Botswana's **first national data centre** in Gaborone — the country's only **Tier III** certified, carrier-neutral facility, fully government-owned (built by Chinese firm Zhong Gan) — positioned as a national digital hub and data-sovereignty asset.
