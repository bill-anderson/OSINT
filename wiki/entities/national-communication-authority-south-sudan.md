# National Communication Authority (NCA) South Sudan

South Sudan's communications regulator, responsible for the ICT/telecom sector,
international gateway services and the national data centre. Director General
(2026): **Rizig Dominic Samuel**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Gateway & data-centre oversight committee (2026-01-17).** The NCA formed a
  **Gateway Services and Data Centre Oversight Committee** with Swiss operator
  **[[mgi-communications]]** to digitise revenue from the South Sudan
  International Gateway (SSIGW), modernise infrastructure, and bring gateway and
  national data-centre operations under national sovereignty and security
  oversight. Source:
  [[2026-01-17 South Sudan NCA establishes gateway services and data centre oversight committee]].
- **Data-protection dialogue (2025-10).** Chol Joseph Mayen, NCA Director of
  Research and Planning, keynoted the Paradigm Initiative/Koneta Hub convening on
  the draft Data Protection Bill, framing the law as trust-building for the
  digital ecosystem. Source:
  [[2025-10-17 Paradigm Initiative partners with Koneta Hub on data protection law in South Sudan]].
