# Africa Data Protection (ADP)

A pan-African data-protection body/network whose president flagged the continent's core governance gaps: the
**Malabo Convention ratification lag**, **cross-border-transfer** gaps, and the absence of a continental
**"adequacy" standard** for data flows. See [[XAF]].
