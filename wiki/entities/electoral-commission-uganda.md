# Electoral Commission of Uganda (EC)

Uganda’s election-management body and the primary data controller for electoral data.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Mandatory biometric voter verification, 2026 (2026-03).** Deployed **109,142 verification kits** at
  **50,739 polling stations** (no ballot without biometric match) and integrated the **NIN** into the
  voter register, under SI No. 98 of 2025 — but registered with the PDPO only post-election. See
  [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-31 Uneven Enforcement of Data Protection Laws Ugandas 2026 Polls]].
