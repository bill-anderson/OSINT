# HISP WCA (Health Information Systems Programme, West and Central Africa)

**The West and Central Africa node of the HISP network — the regional implementer that built
Togo's [[dhis2]]-based education management information system.** HISP WCA is a **regional
group**, distinct from **[[hisp-centre]]** (HISP UiO, University of Oslo), the centre that
develops and stewards the DHIS2 platform itself and coordinates the network of local HISP
groups. Contact (as of 2024): **Edem Kossi**. See [[TGO]], [[dpi.mis]],
[[capacity.research]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **Togo's DHIS2-based EMIS/SIGE.** With support from **GPE KIX** (Global Partnership for
  Education Knowledge and Innovation Exchange) and **Norad** (Norwegian Agency for Development
  Cooperation), HISP WCA developed an **Education Management Information System** (EMIS /
  *système d'information de gestion de l'éducation*, SIGE) on the DHIS2 platform that **places
  schools and their local stakeholders at the heart of a decentralised information system** — the
  design response to a chain in which analysis had been centralised. Built to support
  [[mepsta-togo]]'s **2020 education sector plan**. See [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-19 HISP WCA develops DHIS2 EMIS for education data analysis and visualization in Togo]].
- **What the system delivers.** Key indicators include **enrolments by level, teacher-learner
  ratio, national exam pass rate, and pass rate by gender** (aggregated or disaggregated; the
  profile adds disability status). Schools compare themselves to peers via **school report
  cards**; sub-national actors get **real-time dynamic dashboards**; historical StatEduc data are
  extracted into DHIS2 for trend analysis; and the system supports **individual-level data to
  track each learner's progress**. Local innovations recorded: school observation tool, school
  report card, **WhatsApp bot for exam results**, MICS-EAGLE survey integration. See
  [[data.statistics]], [[stateduc]]. Sources:
  [[2024-11-19 HISP WCA develops DHIS2 EMIS for education data analysis and visualization in Togo]],
  [[2024-11-05 Togo - Integrating DHIS2 with existing EMIS systems]].
- **Research footprint.** Togo is **one of six Ministries of Education in the global DHIS2 for
  Education research project**, with **Eswatini, Mozambique, The Gambia, Sri Lanka and Uganda**.
  See [[capacity.research]]. Source:
  [[2024-11-05 Togo - Integrating DHIS2 with existing EMIS systems]].

**Contradiction — when the Togo deployment began.** The **DHIS2 country profile** records
**"Since 2019, national scale"**; the **DHIS2 article** states **"In 2021, DHIS2 was implemented
in interoperability with StatEduc"** and describes the deployment as **piloting** and **"under
review by Togo's Ministry of Education"** (as of 2024-11-19). **Resolved (2026-07-20): distinct milestones, plus a mis-dated maturity claim.** The French page
`/fr/emis-togo-2022` is a **translation**; its English original was published **2022-12-01**, so "under
review by the Ministry" is an **as-of 2022-12-01** statement, not November 2024 - the wiki was carrying a
two-year-old status as current. **2019** is when DHIS2 education-sector engagement began (year precision,
corroborated only at programme level across six countries); **2021** is the StatEduc interoperability
go-live. On maturity: **not a pilot, not fully embedded** - by 2024 national exam results sat in a national
DHIS2 instance (WhatsApp results service live 2024-06-16), but GPE's ITAP found (2024-04-08) Togo still
lacked a fully operational EMIS, and as of **2026-05-04** HISP reports DHIS2 in operational use, strongest
at regional level and weakest at inspection level, with data latency the binding constraint. **"National
scale" cannot be claimed for DHIS2 as the collection layer**: DHIS2 was added *on top of* CSPro/StatEduc,
which remains the annual school-census tool - DHIS2 is the national **analytical** layer above it.

**Provenance note.** Both accounts are the **implementer's own** (published on
education.dhis2.org); progress claims are the implementer's.
