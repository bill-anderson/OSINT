# Serpro (Brazil)

Brazil's **state-owned IT company** (Serviço Federal de Processamento de Dados) — the federal government's digital-services and data-processing arm. In the Data Landscapers corpus it appears as a **Brazilian pole** in Angola's public-finance digitalisation, advancing a **sovereign digital-state** argument.

## Activity in this wiki

- **At Angola's i.Lab launch (2026-03-30).** Serpro president **Wilton Mota** spoke at the launch of the Ministry of Finance's **[[i-lab-angola|i.Lab]]**, framing public-sector innovation as a sovereignty question — the State must "own its own digital destiny," mastering data orchestration and eliminating external dependencies that pose security risks. Signals Brazil's Serpro inside Angola's digital-governance build, alongside a March 2026 Angola–Cape Verde agreement. See [[i-lab-angola]], [[angola--dpi-govtech]], [[dpi.govtech]].
