# ASYCUDA (Automated System for Customs Data)

A **UNCTAD**-developed automated customs-management system used by customs
administrations worldwide to handle declarations, clearance, accounting and trade
statistics. A reference digital-government / trade-facilitation platform widely
deployed across Africa; tagged global ([[XGL]]), with specific places where its
national deployments are documented.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Sudan (Gezira) reactivation (2025-06).** The **[[sudan-customs-authority]]**
  reactivated ASYCUDA at Gezira state's Hantoub customs yard after war damage,
  described as "the basis for executing all modern customs transactions." See
  [[SDN]], [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-18 ASYCUDA customs system reactivated at Gezira state customs after war damage (SUNA)]].
