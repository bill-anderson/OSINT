# Ministry of Interior, Federal Affairs and Reconciliation (MOIFAR), Somalia

Somali federal ministry responsible for interior affairs, federal-member-state relations
and reconciliation. In the digital-governance domain it owns the country's **civil
registration** agenda — it announced and leads the [[somalia-crvs]] Unified Digital Civil
Registration Service (piloted across 19 districts on Africa CRVS Day, 10 Aug 2025), working
with UNICEF and Germany and integrating toward the national ID, health, immigration and
education systems.

The Interior Minister (Ali Yusuf Ali "Hosh") also leads engagement with Pakistan's NADRA on
the national ID system (e.g. the Jan 2026 Islamabad delegation).
