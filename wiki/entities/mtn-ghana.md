# MTN Ghana

The Ghana operating unit of [[mtn-group]] and the country's largest telecoms operator, with
roughly 58% market share and more than 25 million mobile subscribers. Its Mobile Money
(MoMo) platform has over 11 million active users, driving financial inclusion through
merchant payments, microloans and remittances.

## Activity in this wiki

- **$1.1bn three-year investment announced (~2026-02).** During a working visit by
  [[mtn-group]] president and CEO Ralph Mupita, MTN Ghana committed to reinvest about
  US$1.1 billion over three years to strengthen network resilience, expand 4G/5G coverage,
  build hyperscale data centres, and support Ghana's AI and cloud aspirations — plus digital
  skills (coding academies, AI training, startup incubation). Mupita framed hyperscale data
  centres and subsea-cable capacity as positioning Ghana as a West African digital hub. The
  pledge echoes a similar 2022 commitment of more than US$1 billion over five years. Tags
  [[infra.connect]], [[infra.store]], [[dpi.pay]], [[tech.ai]]. Source: [[2026-02-23 MTN Ghana secures $1.1bn boost as AI push deepens]].
