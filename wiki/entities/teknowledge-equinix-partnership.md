# TeKnowledge–Equinix partnership

A strategic partnership (joint statement, mid-2026) between technology-services firm
**[[teknowledge]]** and digital-infrastructure provider **[[equinix]]** to expand cloud adoption,
strengthen cybersecurity and build **AI-ready digital infrastructure** across **Nigeria** and the
wider **West African** region.

## What we know (as of 2026-07)

- **Shape.** Combines Equinix's interconnected data-centre ecosystem (**>280 data centres**,
  >10,000 customers worldwide, per MD West Africa Wole Abu) with TeKnowledge's AI, data, customer-
  experience and cybersecurity services (a global workforce of **>2,000 engineers**, incl. many
  Nigerian professionals; CEO Aileen Allkins).
- **Pitch.** Secure hybrid/multi-cloud deployments with **low-latency private connectivity** and
  **local data hosting** so sensitive information "remains within national borders" — data-residency
  and regulatory compliance as the selling point ([[sovereignty]]). Explicitly aligned to Nigeria's
  **Digital Transformation Agenda** and **National AI Strategy**.
- **Initial focus.** Financial services, fintech, telecoms, government, healthcare, energy/utilities
  and digital-native enterprises. See [[infra.store]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[tech.ai]].
