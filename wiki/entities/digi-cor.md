# DIGI-CoR

A **CFA 92bn (~US$163m) programme to digitise Cameroon's local governments** (councils/decentralised
authorities) — distinct from the World Bank's PATNUC, and absent from the wiki until this ingest.

## Profile

- **Hard baselines.** **68.5% of local authorities lack structured computer networks; 57.8% have no
  digitally trained staff** — the gap DIGI-CoR is meant to close (interoperability + territorial
  information systems for the ~394 decentralised authorities, a thread that follows the Jul-2025 local
  digital-government master plan). Source:
  [[2026-06-04-digi-cor-163m-digitize-local-governments-wearetech]].
- Ground-truth counterweight: ANTIC has reached **230 councils (~64% nationally)** but only **48.6% of
  North West councils have functional websites** (Jul 2026) — the ambition against the reality. See
  [[cameroon--dpi-govtech]].
