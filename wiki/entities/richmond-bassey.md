# Richmond Bassey

Co-founder and chief executive of **[[bamboo]]**, the Nigerian digital investment platform.

On the record (2026-07-18) as the operator-side voice on Nigeria's retail-investing surge. Three
positions worth holding:

- **The retail boom is recent and narrow in origin** — "the retail portion of the market or the
  retail growth of the market is very recent. It has happened in the last 18 months."
- **Retail-vs-institutional volume comparisons mislead** — "institutional capital has been building
  in the market for decades… to expect that the volume from the retail boom will suddenly outpace
  institutional investors is not practical."
- **The bottleneck is the registrar layer** — "a lot of the complaints around the market come from
  people's dividends being unpaid or not receiving dividends on time. And that is really a registrar
  domain problem." He calls for deeper broker–registrar collaboration and more user-friendly, better
  incentivised registrars.

The third is the data-governance point: a **records-quality failure in the shareholder registry**
surfacing as a consumer-trust failure in a digitising market. See [[NGA]], [[nigerian-exchange]],
[[nigeria--dpi-pay]].
