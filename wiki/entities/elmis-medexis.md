# eLMIS Medexis (Burundi health supply-chain MIS)

Burundi's **electronic logistics management information system** for medicines and health-product
inventories, run by the Ministry of Public Health with the Dutch NGO **i+Solutions** and the Netherlands
embassy (launched Dec 2022). One of Burundi's more concretely-deployed health-DPI systems, alongside
[[merankabandi|Merankabandi]]/[[openimis]] in social protection. See [[BDI]], [[dpi.mis]].

## What we know (as of 2026-06)

- **Nationwide in public + faith-based facilities.** As of 2025 the rollout covered all public and
  faith-based health facilities: **>2,000 users trained across 956 facilities**, with **839 laptops** and
  **148 routers** deployed. A **planned extension to private facilities** was under discussion (25 Jun
  2026), along with interoperability with other health platforms and **[[starlink]]** connectivity for
  underserved areas. Source: [[2026-06-26-burundi-elmis-medexis-health-rollout-wearetech]].
