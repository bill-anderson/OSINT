# MINAT (Ministry of Territorial Administration)

Cameroon's interior/territorial-administration ministry — issuer of the directive granting
**[[intouch]]** an exclusive over online-gaming payment aggregation (with unintended collateral harm to
the video-game sector; see [[kiroo-games]]), and a recurring actor in municipal-level digital ID schemes
(e.g. the Yaoundé taxi digital ID).
