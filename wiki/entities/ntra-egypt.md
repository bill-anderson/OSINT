# National Telecom Regulatory Authority (NTRA, Egypt)

Egypt's **telecommunications regulator** (under **[[mcit-egypt|MCIT]]**). Led the **nationwide 5G launch (June 2025)** across the four operators (Vodafone Egypt, Orange Egypt, e& Egypt, **[[telecom-egypt]]**) after a combined $2.7bn investment. In 2026-02 it oversaw a **$3.5bn spectrum deal** doubling mobile spectrum (**+410MHz**) under the **National Spectrum Strategy 2026–2030**. Also regulates mobile payment innovations — it issued Egypt's **first Direct Carrier Billing (DCB) licence** to **[[tpay]]** for government payments (Dec 2025). See [[egypt--infra-connect]].
