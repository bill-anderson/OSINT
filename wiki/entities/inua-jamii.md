# Inua Jamii (Kenya cash-transfer programme)

Kenya's flagship social-protection **cash-transfer programme** (for older persons, persons with
disabilities and orphans/vulnerable children), increasingly delivered on **mobile-money-enabled G2P
rails** — M-Pesa via the eCitizen platform ([[dpi.pay]], [[include.access]]). A concrete instance of
Kenya's payments DPI carrying welfare, cutting travel costs for elderly beneficiaries. See [[KEN]].

## Activity in this wiki

- **G2P shift to M-Pesa via eCitizen (2025-02).** [[include.access]] The State Department disbursed
  **KES 3.52bn to 1,760,074 beneficiaries** (Jan 2025 cycle, KES 2,000 each), transitioning from bank
  accounts to M-Pesa agents (pilot Nov 2024, Murang'a/Marsabit); withdrawal via *222# with national ID.
  **[[safaricom]]** allowed line registration for over-60 beneficiaries/caregivers. Source:
  [[2025-02-27 Mobile payments bring major relief to elderly Inua Jamii beneficiaries]].
