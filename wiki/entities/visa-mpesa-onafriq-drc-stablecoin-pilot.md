# Visa–M-Pesa–Onafriq DRC stablecoin pilot

**Parties:** [[visa]], [[safaricom]] (M-Pesa), [[onafriq]].
**What/when:** A live USD-pegged stablecoin pilot for cross-border payments in the DRC
([[COD]]), announced early July 2026.
**Terms:** Settles cross-border mobile transactions instantly on blockchain, bypassing
correspondent banking — targeting the ~8% average sub-Saharan remittance fee. Builds on Visa
Pay, launched in DRC in September 2025 via an [[onafriq]] API.
**Status:** Pilot / `needs-review`. ⚠ Provenance weak — sole source is an aggregator with no
primary citation; corroborate against Visa, Safaricom and Onafriq releases before relying on
any figure. Tags [[dpi.pay]], [[finance.new]], [[gov.protect]].
