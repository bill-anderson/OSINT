# CORIS Money (Guinea)

A **bank-issued electronic-money service** from **Coris Bank International**, launched in Guinea **16 Feb 2026**
by the **[[ministry-digital-economy-guinea|MCENI]]** (Minister Mourana Soumah) under **[[simandou-2040|Simandou
Vision 2040]]** — letting citizens access digital banking **without a traditional bank account** (150
distributors), pushing financial inclusion and interoperability in one of West Africa's least-banked markets.
See [[guinea--dpi-pay]].
