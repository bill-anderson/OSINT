# ANADEN (National Agency for Digital Development)

The Comoros' **national digital-development agency**, custodian of the country's public digital infrastructure.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Public-administration data centre (2025).** ANADEN (with **Comores Cable**) co-manages the new **Tier 3** national data centre (44.4 Tbps; inaugurated ~19 May 2025) hosting State and private-sector platforms and centralising public data — the physical anchor of the AfDB-funded **[[project-digitization-comorian-economy]]**. See [[infra.store]] and the [[COM]] hub. Source: [[2025-05-15 Comoros completes construction of public administration data centre (ANADEN)]].
