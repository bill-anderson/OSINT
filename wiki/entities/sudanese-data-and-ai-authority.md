# Sudanese Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority

National agency established by PM **Kamil Idris**'s decree of **5 November 2025**, one of three
bodies created under the **[[ministry-digital-transformation-sudan]]** (with the
**[[digital-transformation-authority-sudan]]** and **[[sudanese-cybersecurity-authority]]**).
Mandate covers national data management and AI. Context: Sudan scored **24.63/100** on the Oxford
Insights AI Readiness Index (2024, vs a sub-Saharan average of 32.70). See [[tech.ai]],
[[gov.policy]].
