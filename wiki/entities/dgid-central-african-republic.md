# DGID (Direction Générale des Impôts et des Domaines)

The Central African Republic's **tax and property administration**, operator of the country's
online tax system.

## Activity in this wiki

- **e-Tax platform (launched 24 Mar 2025).** DGID launched **e-Tax** for online tax
  declaration and payment — four modules including registration for the unique identification
  number (NIU) — following a January 2025 IMF mission that urged accelerated public-finance
  and tax digitalisation. The earliest of CAR's three named e-gov platforms (e-Tax ·
  Gouvernemental Centralisé · DUNIA). See [[caf--dpi-govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-03-25 La Centrafrique lance officiellement e-Tax, sa plateforme de paiement d'impots en ligne]].
