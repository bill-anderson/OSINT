# PayChangu

A Malawian **payment aggregator** (founded **2021** by **Morgan Tembo**, HQ Lilongwe) unifying mobile money,
cards and bank transfers through a single integration — targeting SMEs with developer-friendly tools (APIs,
plugins for Shopify/WooCommerce, payment links, invoicing, analytics) against the fragmentation of Malawi's
payments market.

- **Instant Bank Transfer with Centenary Bank (2025-06).** Powered by the national switch **[[malswitch]]**, it
  enables real-time transfers to any Malawian bank (up to **MWK 500m**) via **\*632#** USSD — reaching rural
  users and micro-entrepreneurs without smartphones — consolidating **Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba and banks**.

See **[[malawi--dpi-pay]]**.
