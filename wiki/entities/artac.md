# ARTAC (Conference of Central African Telecommunications Regulators)

The **regional body of Central African telecom regulators** — the sub-regional forum where CMR's ART/TRB
([[art-cameroon]]) coordinates policy with its neighbours. Notable for putting a **data-residency
position in writing**.

## Activity in this wiki

- **10th Ordinary Session — Kinshasa (Feb 2026).** A high-level expert panel recommended a **harmonised
  NGSO (non-geostationary satellite) framework** requiring providers — **Starlink, OneWeb and Amazon
  Kuiper named** — to (a) establish **terrestrial gateways within ARTAC's jurisdiction**, (b) meet
  financial obligations equivalent to traditional operators, and (c) **keep citizens' data stored within
  the African continent**. A Central African **data-sovereignty demand** aimed at US/UK LEO
  constellations — and the direct policy counterpoint to CAMTEL's simultaneous AWS and Avanti talks. Also:
  **free roaming mandated by 30 Jun 2026**; mobile-internet penetration **<30%** across much of Central
  Africa; revised statutes adopted, Executive Committee mandate extended to Dec 2027. Source:
  [[2026-02-24-trb-artac-10th-ordinary-session-kinshasa-ngso-techreviewafrica]].
