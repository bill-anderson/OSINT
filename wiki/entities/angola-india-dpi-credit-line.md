# India–Angola $200m DPI credit line (2025)

A **$200 million Indian line of credit** to Angola announced after the **May 2025 Lourenço–Modi** summit in New Delhi (the first Angolan presidential visit to India in 38 years) — spanning DPI, digital governance, space, defence and healthcare, with digital-skills training.

## Significance

- Angola will **adopt India's DPI model** — digital IDs, e-payments, unified social registries — and hopes to **model its digital ID on Aadhaar**. India's **MOSIP** already underpins DPI in Morocco, Ethiopia, Sierra Leone and Guinea. See [[angola--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2025-05-07 India helps Angola with DPI, digital ID as part of 200M credit line]].
- The **India-facing leg** of Angola's three-way DPI courtship — alongside the **Chinese**-devised civil-registration system (CEIEC, 2020) and the **UAE/G42** [[digital-angola-presight-mou]] (2021). A sovereignty story about whose stack Angola's foundational systems run on.
