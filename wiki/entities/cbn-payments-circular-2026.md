# CBN June 2026 Payments Circular (PSS/DIR/PUB/CIR/001/004)

A payments circular issued by the **Central Bank of Nigeria** ([[cbn]]) on
**15 June 2026**, signed by Dr. Rakiya Yusuf (Director, Payments System
Supervision). It addresses market concentration, data sovereignty, ownership
transparency and systemic oversight in a single regulatory instrument. This is
the **same June-2026 circular already summarised on the [[cbn]] hub** — this page
is the standing instrument object that dated sources cite.

## The four pillars

1. **Market structure** — an entity with >25% card-issuing share is capped at
   ≤15% merchant-acquiring share (and the reverse), assessed across a "group of
   related entities."
2. **Data localisation** — full compliance required by **1 January 2027**.
3. **Ultimate beneficial ownership (UBO) disclosure.**
4. **Systemic oversight** — monthly market-share returns.

## Assessment in this wiki

Both parts of Anjola Adebowale's *Strategy Ahead of Infrastructure* series read
the circular as **strategically sound but poorly sequenced**: the January-2027
localisation deadline is argued to outrun Nigeria's data-centre and grid
capacity (Part I), while the compliance burden also opens data-engineering and
governance opportunity for Nigerian firms if phased against infrastructure
milestones (Part II). See [[gov.legislate]], [[infra.store]], [[dpi.pay]].

> **Date note.** Part II's source frontmatter carries a suspect publication date
> (2026-06-19, i.e. before Part I and before this circular's own 15 June issue);
> flagged for human review. Does not affect the circular's own 15 June 2026 date.
