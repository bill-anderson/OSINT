# RunLevel

Regional African integrator specialising in identity and cybersecurity, led by
managing partner Miguel Guerreiro. It pairs local implementation expertise with
third-party IAM technologies for financial-sector clients.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Integrator on Mozambique national-payments biometric IAM (2026-02-24).**
  RunLevel is the delivery partner on [[bio-key-international]]'s multi-year
  agreement to deploy biometric IAM (PortalGuard, PIN:You, SSO) into
  [[simo-mozambique]], operator of the SIMOrede national payments network. The
  deal follows an earlier joint RunLevel/BIO-key deployment for a major
  Mozambican national bank in May 2025. Managing Partner Miguel Guerreiro framed
  it as combining "local expertise with advanced IAM technologies" to secure
  critical payment infrastructure ([[MOZ]], [[dpi.id]], [[infra.cybersec]]).
  Source: [[2026-02-24 BIO-key and RunLevel Deploy Biometric IAM Across Mozambique's National Payments Network]].
