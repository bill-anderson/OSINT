# Centre for International Governance Innovation (CIGI)

Canadian think tank publishing policy briefs on global digital governance.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Tech diplomacy in Africa (PB232, 2026-03).** Folashadé Soulé maps "tech diplomacy" as African agency
  amid US–China–EU rivalry (Kenya's special tech envoy; data-centre sovereignty). See [[gov.policy]].
  Source: [[2026-03-01 Tech Diplomacy and Avenues for Digital Development in Africa CIGI PB232]].
- **Governing big tech (PB226, 2026-02).** Andrew Heffernan argues Africa is positioned to *lead* on
  regulating US tech giants (Google ~90% search, Meta ~85% social) — a "data colonialism" framing. See
  [[tech.industry]], [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-01 Why Africa Should Lead on Governing Global Tech Giants CIGI PB226]].
