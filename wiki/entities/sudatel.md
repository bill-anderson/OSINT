# Sudatel (Sudatel Telecom Group)

Sudanese telecom group and international-connectivity carrier (CEO **Magdi M.
Abdalla Taha**), parent of the domestic operator **[[sudani-telecom]]** ("Sudani").
Positions itself as a regional wholesale/backbone player, not just a national
operator, and has become the vehicle for the Port-Sudan government's framing of
Sudan as a regional digital hub.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Horizon Fiber cross-border corridor (2026-02).** Sudatel signed a strategic
  agreement (4 Feb 2026, Djibouti) with **[[djibouti-telecom]]** and
  **[[ethio-telecom]]** to build the **[[horizon-fiber]]** multi-terabit
  terrestrial corridor from Djibouti's subsea landing stations through Ethiopia
  to Sudan; the underlying Dec-2024 MoU was converted to a **binding agreement**
  (reported 10 Feb 2026). Taha says Sudatel added **~2,700 km of new fibre**
  during the war years, and cited existing cross-border routes toward Chad and
  Ethiopia. See [[infra.connect]], [[gov.regional]], [[SDN]]. Sources:
  [[2026-02-04 Sudatel-Djibouti Telecom-Ethio telecom launch Horizon Fiber cross-border initiative (SUNA)]],
  [[2026-02-10 Ethio telecom, Djibouti Telecom, Sudatel Seal Binding Deal on Cross-Border Fiber Network]].
- **Post-war business-digital-transformation push (2026-01).** At a Port-Sudan
  event the digital-transformation minister praised Sudatel and Sudani for
  keeping infrastructure and services running through the war. See
  [[tech.industry]]. Source:
  [[2026-01-16 Minister praises Sudani-Sudatel role in business digital transformation (SUNA)]].
