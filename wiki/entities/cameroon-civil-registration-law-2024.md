# Cameroon Civil Registration Law (Law 2024/016)

Cameroon's **2024 civil-registration reform law** — the legal basis for the digital CRVS overhaul. Introduces electronic declaration of civil-status acts, a **national database**, **digital birth/marriage/death certificates**, and a **Unique Personal Identification Number (UPIN)** for every citizen.

## Profile

- Passed alongside a **personal-data-protection bill** (both introduced to parliament late 2024). Implemented by **[[bunec]]** (with Luxembourg's [[liber-technologies]]) toward a **fully digital CRVS by 2029**, integrated with the biometric national ID. Analysis flags barriers — weak equipment, limited internet, low digital literacy, and rights safeguards. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]].
