# OIF (Organisation internationale de la Francophonie)

The **International Organization of La Francophonie**, active on **civil registration and
vital statistics (CRVS)** — birth registration as foundational legal identity — across
French-speaking Africa.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Flagship Civil Registration project (since 2019).** Strengthens national birth-registration
  systems for rural and vulnerable populations, aligned to **SDG 16.9** (legal identity for
  all by 2030). Cumulative (since 2021): 500,000+ registered, 2.5m+ sensitised, 6,100+ agents
  trained across five countries.
- **Central Africa + Madagascar (2025).** As of the 8th Africa CRVS Day (Aug 2025): **CAR** —
  project launching, targeting 3,000 birth certificates for undocumented children/youth plus
  staff training; **Chad** 53,000+ children registered; **Cameroon** 5,400. Also supported the
  operationalisation of **Madagascar's** data-protection institution. Source:
  [[2025-08-12 La Francophonie supports birth registration efforts across Central Africa, Madagascar]].
