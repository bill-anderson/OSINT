# Stefaan Verhulst

Co-founder of **[[the-govlab]]** (NYU) and The Data Tank; originator of the
**"digital self-determination"** and **Triad of Tech Sovereignty** frameworks.

## Positions / arguments

- Technological sovereignty unfolds across three dimensions — **dependency, openness,
  agency** — and "concentration is a choice, not an inevitability."
- Sovereignty measured by *agency* (who can shape/govern/challenge systems), not
  infrastructure ownership; "tackling asymmetry by creating new asymmetries is not a
  desirable outcome." Co-authored with **Begoña Gonzalez Otero** (Max Planck). See
  [[geopol.eu]], [[gov.standards]].

## Affiliations

- Board member of **[[data-for-policy]]** (the UK CIC and its Cambridge-published *Data &
  Policy* journal), alongside Zeynep Engin and Jon Crowcroft. See [[data.open]]. Source:
  [[2023-09-04 Data for Policy - About Us]].
