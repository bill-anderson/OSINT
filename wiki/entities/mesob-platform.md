# Unified MESOB Platform (Ethiopia)

Ethiopia's AI-driven, integrated digital public-service platform, built by the
**[[ethiopian-ai-institute]]** and inaugurated by PM **[[abiy-ahmed]]** (June
2026) — a flagship **homegrown** digital-infrastructure initiative.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Launch (2026-06).** Delivered via mobile apps and web to modernise government
  service delivery and reduce administrative inefficiency; explicitly framed to
  **cut reliance on external platforms** and to be shared as a model for other
  African countries. No monetary figures or hard deadlines disclosed. See [[ETH]],
  [[dpi.govtech]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-20 Ethiopia launches unified MESOB Platform to accelerate national digital transformation]].
