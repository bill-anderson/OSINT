# RealTyme

Swiss secure-communications firm specialising in zero-trust architecture and
sovereign "communication enclave" deployments. It markets a state-controlled
alternative to commercial hyperscaler clouds, pitching data residency and
autonomy over convenience. Chief commercial officer François Rodriguez.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Partnership with the Smart Africa Digital Academy (2026-04-16).** RealTyme
  allied with [[smart-africa]]'s Digital Academy on a continental "national
  protection framework" explicitly framed as "digital decolonisation," pitched
  against both Silicon Valley and Chinese tech dominance and against foreign-reach
  laws such as the US Cloud Act. Rodriguez's framing: sovereignty must be "built
  through local resilience rather than purchased." The firm argues its sovereign
  enclave model offers state autonomy that traditional commercial clouds
  ([[microsoft]], [[amazon-web-services]]) cannot match. See
  [[smart-africa-realtyme-partnership]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-16 Smart Africa, RealTyme join forces for data sovereignty]].
