# National Computer Center (CNI, Tunisia)

**Tunisia's national computing centre — the state IT body that, with the National Digital
Certification Agency ([[ance-tunisia]]) and local telecom operators, operates the national mobile
digital identity [[e-houwiya]] under the ICT department.**

## Activity in this wiki

- **Named operator of E-Houwiya (as of 2025-03).** The CNI is one of the affiliated entities to
  which operation of [[e-houwiya]] is assigned — the system serving as centralised single sign-on
  for government portals (E-Bawaba, E-CNAM, E-People, E-CNSS, Madrassati and others), which places
  the CNI in the government-platform layer rather than the certification layer. See [[dpi.id]],
  [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-03-19 E-Houwiya - A Milestone in Tunisia's Digital Transformation Roadmap 2025 (ID Tech)]].

  *Note on provenance: this account is written by [[nizar-ben-neji]], the former minister who led
  E-Houwiya's implementation — principal testimony, not independent reporting.*
