# Zambia Information and Communications Technology Authority (ZICTA)

Zambia's ICT and telecommunications regulator, responsible for licensing operators, regulating
communications infrastructure and supporting the country's digital-government agenda.

## Activity in this wiki

- **Government–PAYZ financial-inclusion engagement (~2026-02-26).** ZICTA was present, alongside
  the Ministry of Technology and Science and the [[bank-of-zambia]], at the State House meeting
  between President Hichilema and the [[payz]] on accelerating digital financial services and
