# KomorPay / Komor Switch

A **BCC-branded national payment stack** — **KomorPay** (payment system), **Komor Switch**
(interoperability layer) and **Mali Ya Wakazi** — presidentially launched (May 2026) alongside a
bond-market component, distinct from and later than the [[paylogic|PayLogic]] interoperable-switch go-live.
Source: [[2026-05-05-komorpay-komor-switch-payment-systems-presidential-launch-alwatwan]].
