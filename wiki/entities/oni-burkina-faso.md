# ONI (Office National d'Identification)

Burkina Faso's **national identification authority**, operator of the national ID card and, from late 2025, the new AES biometric ID. DG: Arzouma Parfait Louré.

## Activity in this wiki

- **CIB-AES rollout (2025–26).** ONI unveiled the **[[cib-aes]]** (Carte d'Identité Biométrique de l'AES) on 1 Dec 2025 — the 5th generation of Burkinabè ID, replacing the CNIB — following a 6 Nov 2025 decree. Harmonised with **[[alliance-of-sahel-states]]** (Mali, Niger) standards and ICAO, while remaining a national card; deliverable from age 5, mandatory from 13, cost 3,500 FCFA. See [[burkina-faso--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2025-11-10 Burkina Faso introduces new AES biometric ID system (CIB-AES)]].
