# Algérie Télécom–Sparkle Italy–Algeria subsea cable (MoU)

MoU (2025-07) between **[[algerie-telecom]]** (CEO Adel Bentoumi) and Italy's **[[sparkle]]** (Enrico Bagnasco)
for a **new latest-generation submarine cable linking Algeria to Italy**, plus an Algérie Télécom **point of
presence in Europe**, technical support for an international-standard **data centre**, and a **cloud/networks/
cybersecurity training platform** — a route-diversification and Europe-anchoring play. See
[[algeria--infra-connect]].
