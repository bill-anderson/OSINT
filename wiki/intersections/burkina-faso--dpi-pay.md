# Burkina Faso × Digital Payments & Fintech

Burkina Faso sits **on the BCEAO regional instant rail** while a competitive domestic wallet market and a
state push into e-invoicing and treasury digitalisation build out underneath it.

## The regional rail

- **[[pi-spi]] (BCEAO).** Burkina Faso is on the **[[bceao]] [[pi-spi]]** rail — live-test **Aug 2025** →
  union launch **30 Sep 2025**; **9 institutions authorised by Feb 2026**. See [[gov.regional]],
  [[XWA]]. Source: [[2025-10-01-uemoa-pi-spi-bceao-operationnel]].

## Wallets and interconnection

- **Orange Money.** **11m clients** on the **Comviva Mobiquity** stack, **interconnected bank↔wallet with
  BSIC via \*144#**. Sources: [[2025-06-05-orange-money-bsic-interconnexion-bank-to-wallet]],
  [[2025-03-06-burkina-faso-orange-money-mobiquity-pay-x-platform]].
- **Telecel Money.** Accepted across **2,500+ pharmacies**. Source:
  [[2025-06-05-telecel-money-giephab-pharmacies-digital-payment]].
- **Wave.** **1m+ users**, in ministry talks on financial inclusion. Source:
  [[2025-07-25-ministere-wave-money-partenariat-inclusion-financiere]].
- **Home-grown aggregation.** **YENGA/KREEZUS** aggregates **3 operators / 5 wallets**. See
  [[tech.innovate]]. Source: [[2026-03-27-yenga-application-burkinabe-kreezus]].

## The state layer

- **Treasury online.** The Treasury put **LANAYA + e-BDT** online. See [[dpi.govtech]].
- **Certified electronic invoicing.** A **certified electronic invoice** became **mandatory from
  1 Jul 2026**. Source: [[2026-01-06-facture-electronique-certifiee-lancee]].
- **e-Dimaakia.** Digitised official-travel procurement. See [[dpi.govtech]].
- **PAIF-PME.** A 7-year **[[world-bank]] PAIF-PME** project closed on inclusion. See [[finance.new]],
  [[include.access]]. Source: [[2026-06-24-paif-pme-bilan-inclusion-financiere]].
