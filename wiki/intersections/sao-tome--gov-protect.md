# São Tomé and Príncipe × Data Protection & Data Diplomacy

STP's data-protection base is small but well-networked: the **[[sao-tome-data-protection-law-2016]]**
(Law 03/2016, EU-Directive-modelled) enforced by the **[[national-data-protection-agency-sao-tome|ANPDP]]**,
a Malabo signatory. Its outsized move is **data diplomacy** — the tiny DPA is a node in **two overlapping
networks**: the **Lusophone** one (anchored by Portugal/Brazil) and, newly, a **China–Lusophone** one via
Macau.

## The Lusophone network (RLPD)

- **Founding member.** The ANPDP is a **founding member** of the **[[lusophone-data-protection-network|
  Rede Lusófona de Proteção de Dados (RLPD)]]** (est. 2024) alongside Portugal's CNPD, Angola, Brazil and
  Cabo Verde — Brazil holds the presidency, Portugal the permanent secretariat. A **June-2025 CNPD–ANPDP
  cooperation visit** focused on shared concerns: **disinformation on social media, rising digital
  violence, videosurveillance**, and training data subjects — especially **children and youth**. See
  [[gov.regional]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source: [[2025-06-23-cnpd-portugal-recebeu-anpdp-stp-rede-lusofona]].

## The China–Lusophone bridge (Macau)

- **Macau MoU (1 Jun 2026).** After joining the RLPD (Apr 2026), **Macau's Personal Data Protection
  Bureau (DSPDP)** signed **cooperation MoUs with STP and Cabo Verde** (authorised by CE Sam Hou Fai) —
  covering **cross-border data flows, AI supervision, awareness, training and joint studies**. Macau
  casts itself as a **"precision interlocutor"** bridging **mainland China and Portuguese-speaking
  countries** (the China–PLP Forum) on data security and privacy. The STP delegation presented on **data
  protection and AI supervision** in Macau and toured Hong Kong's Privacy Commissioner and a Guangdong
  cyber-data-security association. See [[geopol.china]], [[tech.ai]], [[dpi.exchange]]. Sources:
  [[2026-05-27-macau-to-sign-data-protection-pacts-stp-cabo-verde]], [[2026-06-10-dspdp-macau-formaliza-cooperacao-protecao-dados-stp]].

## Enforcement at home — the electoral roll

- The ANPDP's most concrete domestic task is **overseeing the civil-registry↔electoral-roll linkage** for
  the 2026 elections — the interconnection point being exactly where voters' data must be protected (full
  case: [[sao-tome--dpi-id]]).

## Reads

- `sovereignty`: a ~241k-person state's DPA is being courted by **both** the EU-anchored Lusophone
  network and China (via Macau) as a bridgehead into Portuguese-speaking Africa. That is soft power flowing
  *through* data-protection cooperation — capability and training for STP, but also a small regulator
  becoming a venue for **China–EU data-governance competition**. The AI-supervision framing shows the
  contest is already about the next layer, not just personal data.
