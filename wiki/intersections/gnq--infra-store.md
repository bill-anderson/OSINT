# Equatorial Guinea × Data centres & digital economy

Equatorial Guinea is building sovereign hosting capacity and a digital-zone ambition — on a low maturity base.

- **Government data centres.** The **PAMFP** (public-finance modernisation project) tendered a **Primary Data
  Centre in Malabo + a Backup Data Centre in Bata** (solar-powered) for storage/processing/backup of public
  financial data (2026-06) — pairing with **World Bank / ADIGE** data-centre planning (2025-12).
- **Digital zone.** **[[zeede-djibloho|ZEEDE-GE]]** would make Djibloho a "digital epicentre" around AI, big data
  and high-performance data centres.
- **Reality check.** A **[[gitge-gnq|GITGE]]** study of the ministries (2026-07) quantified a wide digital gap —
  **interoperability ~15%, Big Data ~21%, data-analysis ~10%** — the maturity floor these builds sit on.
