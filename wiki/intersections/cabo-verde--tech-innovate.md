# Cabo Verde × AI, innovation, TechPark & skills

Cabo Verde is betting a stagnant island economy can pivot to a **"digital hub"** — anchored by TechPark
campuses, a startup fund, aggressive skills programmes, and a new data-and-AI strategy — while candidly
debating the **AI divide** in its own press.

## TechPark — the physical hub bet

**[[techpark-cv|TechPark CV]]** opened at **Praia** (May 2025; **value resolved 2026-07-21** — a
**€51.85m project** carrying **€45.59m of AfDB financing** across two phases (Phase I €35.90m project /
€31.59m Bank, approved 2013; Phase II €15.95m / €14.00m, approved Nov 2022), the balance being Cabo
Verdean counterpart funding plus a €0.10m KOAFEC grant. The **"~2% of GDP" framing is struck** — it
appears in no AfDB or government document and sets a cumulative 2013–2025 capital programme against a
single year's GDP. USD figures dropped: the Bank itself published **$50m** and **$57m** for the same
money two days apart. ZEET special economic zone; **23 firms / 7 countries, 311 jobs,
2,769 upskilled**) and a second campus at **Mindelo** (São Vicente, with a data centre) — the vehicle for a
**digital-economy-to-25%-of-GDP** target. TechCabal weighed whether CV can "rival Nigeria and Ghana"
(Pedro Lopes; $100M+ infra, 14,530 km fibre, a ZET sandbox, brain-drain reversal). Sources:
[[2025-05-05-techpark-cv-45m-tech-hub-techafricanews]], [[2025-05-06-techpark-cv-inaugurated-wearetech]],

## Startups, skills & diaspora

**[[fundo-morabeza|Fundo Morabeza]]** (€24M, AfDB-financed) funds startups and digital-nomad attraction;
the ecosystem was showcased at **Web Summit 2025 / SikaBaDu** (largest-ever CV delegation, 25 startups,
Pró-Empresa × CV Airlines) and a **Re!nventa hackathon**; the **[[skodji-digital|Skodji Digital]]**
programme targets youth for the **global gig economy** (against youth unemployment); and a national
**digital-skills strategy** (Digital Cabo Verde / WB; 90 accesses/100 inhabitants) frames the **usage
divide**. Sources: [[2025-10-06-fundo-morabeza-24-milhoes-startups-expressodasilhas]],

## AI: a strategy, and an honest divide debate

- **National Data + AI strategies (Apr 2026).** A **Data Strategy for Public Administration** and a
  **national AI Strategy** kicked off (28 Apr 2026, EU-supported) — an interoperability / de-siloing push.
  Source: [[2026-05-04-national-data-ai-strategies-techafricanews]].
- **The divide, argued in the press.** CV's own commentators debate the "AI divide": accelerate the EEDCV
  and revise the data-protection law with an AI base-law + algorithmic oversight (opinion); "without data
  we don't train algorithms" (Varela/Chantre); AI in schools + teacher training; a "digital generation"
  read on 2021 census data (483k pop, 88% literacy, 72% urban); and Uni-CV research on **AI/NLP for Cape
  Verdean Creole** (health). Sources: [[2025-04-07-revolucao-tecnologica-opiniao-expressodasilhas]],

## Tech diplomacy

A **Digital Africa Corridor with Nigeria** (Sept 2025) and a mission to Nigeria's Government-Leadership-and-
AI Summit (Pedro Lopes; She Code.ai; GITEX), plus a **Tech Ambassador (Olavo Correia)** and a Global
Institute membership protocol — small-state tech diplomacy punching above weight. Sources:
