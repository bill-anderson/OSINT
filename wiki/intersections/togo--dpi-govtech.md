# Togo × E-Government

On top of the ID/payments layers, Togo is running a **methodical, ministry-by-ministry digitisation**
programme, plus horizontal infrastructure (an authoritative infrastructure map, an SME toolbox, and an
archives/GED push) — the "consolidation" work of a state already ahead on foundations.

## Digitising the administration, ministry by ministry

- **Programme de digitalisation des administrations publiques.** After **Energy** and **Tourism**, the
  **Ministry of Foreign Affairs** began a **4-week digitisation sprint** (Mar 2026, ministers Dussey /
  Cina Lawson): connectivity, **service interconnection**, internal-process digitisation, **electronic
  mail/archiving (GED)**, a real-time sector-analysis room, and **280 agents trained** in cybersecurity
  and digital tools — the programme rolling ministry by ministry. See [[infra.cybersec]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-02-mae-digitalisation-services]].
- **Archives & GED.** A DBAN/ATBAD forum (International Archives Day, Jul 2026) framed **archives as
  central to e-government, rights and transparency**; the **Ministry of Economy & Finance** rolled out
  **electronic document management (GED)**, digitisation and the **Alfresco** platform (from Apr 2026).
  See [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2026-07-07-togo-archives-gestion-publique]].

## Horizontal infrastructure

- **Infrastructure single-window + CENTIPOS (Apr 2026).** A national **infrastructure-mapping platform**
  (web + field mobile app) centralises all network data to coordinate works and **prevent damage** to
  poorly-located underground/aerial networks (a recurring cause of power/water/telecom outages) — via a
  declare→analyse→execute→auto-update workflow. It runs on **[[centipos]]**, a **25-station national GNSS
  correction network** giving **centimetre-precision** positioning (also serving cadastre, precision
  agriculture, environmental mapping). Cina Lawson: "this precision changes the game." See [[gov.standards]],
  [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2026-04-03-guichet-unique-cartographie-infrastructures-centipos]].
- **SME digital toolbox — BoD-TPME (Jun 2026).** The **[[atd-togo|Agence Togo Digital]]** launched a
  **digital-maturity toolbox** for micro/small/medium firms (maturity diagnostic, solutions catalogue,
  certified advisors, resource library) — GIZ **ProDigiT** (Germany/Luxembourg/EU), under **Togo Digital
  2025** (target: 3,000 firms digitised). SMEs are **90%+ of the economy but <20% use digital tools**.
  See [[tech.industry]]. Source: [[2026-06-10-atd-boite-outils-digitaux-tpme]].

## The rest of the stack (see [[TGO]] hub)

The **[[services-publics-togo]]** portal (civil-status certificates online in pilot communes; energy-sector
procedures), and **gest-exo ↔ SYDONIA** (tax exemptions integrated with customs, [[otr-togo]]).

## Reads

- Togo's e-gov is **process-disciplined**: a repeatable ministry-digitisation template, an authoritative
  shared reference (the infrastructure map + CENTIPOS), and inclusion-minded tooling (SME toolbox). It is
  the least flashy but most durable kind of DPI work — building the plumbing and the standards, not just
  the front doors — consistent with Togo's status as a regional reference implementation.
