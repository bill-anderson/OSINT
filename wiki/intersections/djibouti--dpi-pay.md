# Djibouti × Digital Payments

A low bank-account base (~25% of 1.1m people) frames Djibouti's payments agenda around inclusion and cash reduction.

- **Smart Wallet (MoU 16 Sep 2025).** [[smart-wallet-djibouti]] — a national digital wallet with
  **[[visa]]** (payments + government services + identity), **[[tech5]]** biometric onboarding,
  targeting a **60% cut in cash use within three years**; inclusion via post offices and "Maisons
  du Citoyen." Rests on the [[djibouti-digital-code]]; stores the [[mobile-id-djibouti|Mobile ID]].
  Still an MoU with no rollout calendar.
- **D-Money's online payment gateway (10 December 2023).** State operator **[[djibouti-telecom]]**,
  through **D-Money**, launched **Djibouti's first online payment gateway** at a ceremony patronised by
  Prime Minister **Abdoulkader Kamil Mohamed** at the Kempinski Palace Hôtel, framed under
  **[[vision-djibouti-2035]]**. The ceremony closed with **conventions signed between Djibouti Télécom
  and multiple ministries, public companies and local banks** committing them to use the platform.
  Port-community integration was **one strand among several** — the president of the **Ports and Free
  Zones Authority**, Aboubaker Omar Hadi, attended, and Djibouti Telecom projected the port integration
  could raise D-Money transactions by **~150%**. **At December 2023**, per Djibouti Port Community
  Systems ([[dpcs-djibouti]]), port-community payment transactions ran **DJF 2–3bn/month (~US$11.2–16.9m)**,
  of which **~11%** were already paid online via DPCS integration with CacBank, BCIMR, EximBank and
  SalaamBank. D-Money itself dates to **June 2020**.

  *Dating corrected 2026-07-20 (GAP-DJI-001).* The held Developing Telecoms clip gave "11 December"
  **with no year**, and the wiki had inferred **2021** (`date_source: proxy`). The correct year is
  **2023** — two years out. The announcer-side account (La Nation, published 2023-12-11) reports the
  ceremony as having taken place **"hier" = 10 December 2023**; Developing Telecoms, published
  2023-12-12, gives 11 December, which is most likely **the primary outlet's publication date read as
  the event date** — the same defect this corpus has hit repeatedly. **Prefer 2023-12-10**; the year is
  not in doubt either way. The "port workers" framing is likewise the secondary re-report's angle, not
  the event's own billing. Source: [[2023-12-11-lanation-djibouti-lancement-premiere-plateforme-paiement-en-ligne-d-money]].

## Acquisition note (2026-07-20)

The **list of conventions signed at the 10 December 2023 payment-gateway launch is not held as of
2026-07-20.** One acquisition attempt on the operator record (`d-money.dj`, `djiboutitelecom.dj`)
found no release covering the launch event; D-Money's own site carries only the separate BCIMR
interconnection agreement of 9 October 2023. La Nation's account (held) names the *categories* of
signatory - "plusieurs departements etatiques, de societes publiques mais egalement de banques de la
place" - but **enumerates none of them**, and no other source does either. The govtech and payments
threads therefore cannot say which ministries, parastatals or banks committed to use the gateway.
