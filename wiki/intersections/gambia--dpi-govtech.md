# The Gambia × GovTech, data governance & interoperability

The Gambia is assembling the legal and architectural scaffolding for its DPI stack.

- **Data-protection law.** The **[[gambia-data-protection-act-2025|Personal Data Protection & Privacy Act 2025]]**
  (praised by Paradigm Initiative; awaited presidential assent) plus the distinct **[[gambia-communications-bill-2025|Communications
  Bill 2025]]** (e-commerce, e-signatures, cybercrime).
- **Interoperability.** A **National Enterprise Architecture** validated under the **ITU GovStack** initiative
  (GICTA) — the interoperability blueprint for citizen-centric e-services.
- **Digital addressing.** The **[[ndasc-gambia|NDASC]]** steers the National Digital Addressing rollout (Policy
  2023).
- **Blockchain DPI.** A **Kalp Foundation** MoU to build a **"Gambia One"** blockchain-powered DPI platform
  (2025-01).
- **Regional.** A **Senegal–Gambia** regulatory-cooperation agreement (**ARTP × PURA**) on telecom cooperation
  and **free roaming** (2026-06).
