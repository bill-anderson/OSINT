# Mozambique × Digital Payments

Mobile money is Mozambique's financial-inclusion engine (e-money accounts **>100% of adults**), and 2025–26
saw the state build the rails and rules around it — an **instant-payment system (METIX)**, a new **National
Payments System law**, taxation of the mobile-money economy, and operational-resilience obligations.

## What we know

- **E-money-led inclusion (2025-08).** [[include.access]] EMI (e-money) accounts grew from **28.6% (2015)
  to 109.8% of adults (Q1-2025)** — "100% of adults now have an e-money account" — even as **26 regions
  have no bank branch**; the **National Financial Inclusion Strategy 2025-2031** launched on this. Source:
  [[2025-08-06-e-money-accounts-109pct-adult-population-clubofmozambique]].
- **Taxing the mobile-money economy (2025-08).** [[finance.new]] The **IRPS** code was revised to **tax
  agent/EMI commissions** of **M-Pesa, e-Mola and m-Kesh** — modernising digital-economy taxation. Source:
  [[2025-08-25-irps-tax-mobile-money-agents-clubofmozambique]].
- **METIX / SPIM instant payments (2026-03).** [[dpi.pay]] The **[[banco-de-mocambique|BdM]]** established
  the **Instant Payment System (SPIM, Notice 1/GB/2026)** and launched it as **METIX** — banks + mobile
  wallets on one real-time platform, **free interbank P2P** for individuals, USSD access, operated by
  **[[simo-mozambique|SIMO]]**. Sources:
  [[2026-03-02-banks-unveil-instant-payment-system-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-03-16-central-bank-launches-metix-clubofmozambique]].
- **National Payments System (SNP) law (2026).** [[gov.legislate]] Cabinet approved the SNP bill (Feb),
  Parliament **unanimously approved** it (Apr — adjusting BdM powers, risk reduction, sanctions, and
  **AI/technology** security provisions), and President **Chapo promulgated it alongside the Cybersecurity
  and Cybercrime laws** (Jun). Sources:
  [[2026-02-18-national-payments-system-bill-parliament-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-04-15-national-payments-system-law-approved-parliament-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-06-13-cybercrime-cybersecurity-nps-laws-signed-clubofmozambique]].
- **Operational resilience — National Switch (2026-06).** [[infra.cybersec]] New **Notice 2/GBM/2026**
  rules require all institutions to **connect to the Single Payments Network (National Switch)** and
  **report any >10-min payment outage within 60 minutes** (in force 90 days after publication). Source:
  [[2026-06-16-financial-institutions-60-minutes-report-failures-clubofmozambique]].
