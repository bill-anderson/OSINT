# Algeria × GovTech / e-Government

Algeria runs a **Presidency-steered** digitalisation drive (the **[[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria|High Commission for Digitalisation]]**, Decree 23-314) under **[[digital-algeria-2030]]**, targeting 100% of public services online by 2030. Through 2025–26 it shipped a **wave of sectoral platforms** on the sovereign
data-centre / interoperability base ([[national-digital-services-center-algeria]]).

- **Single window.** **[[dzair-services|Dzair Services]]** (Oct 2025) aggregates public services behind one access point on a national interoperability layer (46 ministries/agencies on fibre); the older **Bawabatak** portal already offers 342 services. A 2026-04 UN/UNDP engagement (Resident Coordinator Savina Ammassari with High Commissioner Benmouloud) previewed **52 services** on the platform.
- **Registries.** **[[amlak|AMLAK]]** (Jan 2026) moves land titles fully electronic with end-to-end traceability; the **electronic commercial register** ("Sedjilkoum"/سجلكم portal) recorded **173,651 new vs 125,195 struck-off registrations in 2025** (+55k net); a **national export-data platform** (Mar 2026) and a **national import-tracking system** (Mar 2026) tighten trade controls.
- **Cross-border.** **[[algeria-apostille|Apostille]]** e-legalisation went live **9 Jul 2026** (Hague accession 25 Aug 2025) — instant recognition of Algerian documents in ~126 states, replacing the multi-step manual chain.
- **Sectoral MIS (indexed).** Tax-data integration (Djibayatic/Moussahamatic, 2025-03); a **notaries** e-services MoU (Algérie Télécom, 2025-07); a **lawyers' portal** for certified court decisions (Justice Ministry, 2026-02); **four higher-education platforms** (Baddari, 24 Feb 2026); a **national agricultural information system** (farm register, agri land register, livestock & support-programme modules, 2026-06); a **health patient-transfer platform** (priority South/Highlands, 2025-12); an **SME economic information system** (2026-05); a **unified market-supply monitoring system** (per the data-governance decree, 2026-04); a **social cartography** national-services portal (HCN tasked, 2026-06); the **National Certification & Qualifications framework (CNC)** pilot (2026-06).
- **Identity.** The **[[algeria-digital-id-trust-law|digital-ID & trust-services law]]** (2025) links a national digital identity to the biometric ID card (see [[algeria--dpi-id]]).
- **Sub-national.** Municipal digitalisation ("electronic municipalities") is an active reform strand ([[digital.localgov]]).
- **Infrastructure:** national data centres (El Mohammedia, Blida), a sovereign cloud; **67%** of procedures dematerialised by 2024 (foreign-trade clearance cut 15→3 days).
