# Senegal × Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Senegal is operationalising its **Startup Act** and building an entrepreneurship/skills ecosystem around
**DER/FJ** and **[[sonatel]]**, backed by French and DFI capital — the enabling layer beneath the New
Deal ([[senegal--gov-policy]]).

## The Startup Act, operationalised

- **Implementing decree (Jan 2025).** The government adopted the long-awaited implementing decree for
  **Law No. 2020-01** (6 Jan 2020) on startup creation and promotion, establishing a clear regulatory
  framework and incentives for young innovative firms — the labelling process later launched (2026). See
  [[gov.legislate]]. Source: [[2025-01-30-startup-law-implementation-phase]].

## The DER/FJ entrepreneurship engine

- **DER/FJ × Sonatel (Mar 2025).** The Délégation à l'Entrepreneuriat Rapide des Femmes et des Jeunes
  partnered [[sonatel]] on digital inclusion and innovation, turning the **Bambey digital campus** into
  an **innovation hub**. Source: [[2025-03-21-derfj-sonatel-entrepreneurship-pact]].
- **France–Senegal (Mar 2025).** A French ministerial visit to DER/FJ reviewed partnerships and new
  digital-entrepreneurship cooperation (North–South technology transfer and funding). See [[geopol.eu]].
  Source: [[2025-03-11-france-senegal-digital-entrepreneurship]].
- Later capitalised by the **$50m Catalyst DER/FJ** pre-seed/seed fund and a DER/FJ–Wave accelerator —
  see [[senegal--dpi-pay]].

## Digital skills in education

- **Digital Schools (Mar 2025).** The Education Ministry and the **Sonatel Foundation** signed an MoU to
  modernise schooling through digital integration and equitable access. See [[capacity.literacy]].
  Source: [[2025-03-12-digital-schools-sonatel-mou]].
