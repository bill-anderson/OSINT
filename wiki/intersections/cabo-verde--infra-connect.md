# Cabo Verde × Connectivity, 5G & telecoms

Cabo Verde has high penetration for the region and a formal **5G-to-hub strategy** — but the record adds a
**competition fight** over how the incumbent's inter-island infrastructure gets financed.

## The 5G-to-hub strategy

A **National 5G Strategy** (World-Bank-funded, DGTED) was launched (Sept 2025) then **formally approved by
Resolution 33/2026 (20 Feb 2026)** — targeting **90% internet penetration by 2026** and bundling a
**universal digital ID and a data strategy** into the "digital nation" vision. Sources:
[[2025-09-11-national-5g-strategy-hub-2030-techafricanews]],
[[2026-02-16-national-5g-strategy-digital-nation-techafricanews]].

## The €37M guarantee and the competition fight

The state extended a **€37M sovereign guarantee to the incumbent [[cv-telecom|CV Telecom]]** for telecom
infrastructure including an **inter-island submarine cable** — drawing a **formal objection from competitor
[[unitel|UNITEL T+]]**, which demanded **network neutrality, fair competition and open-access / infra-
service separation**. A rare public competition dispute over how sovereign backing distorts an island
telecom market. Sources: [[2026-04-21-estado-aval-37-milhoes-cvtelecom-telecomunicacoes-expressodasilhas]],
[[2026-05-02-unitel-t-mais-neutralidade-concorrencia-telecomunicacoes-anacao]].

## Satellite

**Starlink** entered the market, reaching **0.2% of the internet market** (ARME data, Q2 2025) — a marginal
but present LEO entrant. Source:
[[2025-09-18-starlink-conquista-02-mercado-internet-arme-q2-2025-expressodasilhas]].
