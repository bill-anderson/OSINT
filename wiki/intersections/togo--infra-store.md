# Togo × Data Centres & Sovereign Cloud

State-owned hosting capacity at Lomé, turned into a **sovereign private cloud** — with an explicit
pitch at firms too small to rent a rack.

- **Sovereign private cloud at the Lomé data centre (2026-06).** State-owned **[[sin-togo]]** —
  operator of the Tier III **[[lome-data-centre]]** — partnered **[[st-digital-gabon|ST Digital]]** to
  launch a **sovereign private cloud**, hosting local firms' data in-country to cut
  foreign-infrastructure reliance. SIN's DG **Attia Byll** pitched sub-rack access for those who
  "cannot afford to rent half a rack." See [[gov.regional]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-02 SIN, ST Digital to deploy cloud services at Lomé data center]].
- **The standing reference work.** [[xalam-analytics]]' **Togo Data Center Market Briefing** (July
  2025) is the reference study for this market — **cite it, don't absorb its figures**. Source:
  [[2025-07-01 Togo Data Center Market Briefing (Xalam Analytics)]].
