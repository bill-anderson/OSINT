# Egypt × AI, cyber, skills & digital industry

Egypt frames AI and cybersecurity as **digital-sovereignty** pillars and pairs them with a large
outsourcing/export industry and a startup push.

## AI

- **[[egypt-ai-strategy|2nd National AI Strategy (2025)]]** — six pillars; timeline NCAI (2019) → 1st Strategy
  (2021) → Responsible AI Charter (2023) → 2nd (2025). The **"Karnak" Arabic LLM** and the **ECRAI** responsible-AI
  centre (late 2025); the national council widened to **AI + quantum + emerging tech**. **1st in Africa / 51st
  globally** (AI-readiness); AI could add **up to $42.7bn** (7.7% of GDP by 2030). See [[egypt--infra-store]].

## Cybersecurity & skills

- **100% on the ITU Global Cybersecurity Index**. The **NTI Cybersecurity SuperHero Academy** (3rd edition)
  trains **6,000+ across 17 governorates** with **EG-CERT** and Fortinet/Cisco (2026-07).

## Digital industry & startups

- **Outsourcing/export:** **[[itida-egypt|ITIDA]]** anchors the $7.4bn digital-export drive; **EY MENA ITO
  delivery centre** MoU (1,000+ jobs, 2026-07).
- **Startups:** the **[[egypt-startup-charter|Egypt Startup Charter]]** (EGP 50bn) targets 5,000 startups; Egypt
  is North Africa's lead ecosystem ($524m H2-2025; GlobalFoundries acquired InfiniLink).
- **Bilateral:** **Egypt–UK** ICT/offshoring cooperation (2026-01) — positioning Egypt as a hub and gateway to
  Africa (no `geopol.uk` slug; see ISSUE-014).
