# Cameroon × Digital payments

Cameroon's payments landscape runs on a **Mobile Money duopoly** (MTN/Orange, ~80% of Francophone-African
online payments via MoMo), with the state now pushing in three directions: a **state operator entrant**,
**regional integration**, and **market-shaping directives** whose costs the wiki should track.

## The rails

- **State enters — CAMTEL Blue Money.** [[camtel|CAMTEL]] launched **Blue Money** (1 May 2025) into the
  MTN/Orange duopoly, later folded into the Ethio Telecom MSA. Sources:
  [[2025-05-02-lejour-camtel-blue-money-launch-announced]],
  [[2025-12-23-lejour-camtel-ethio-telecom-blue-money]].
- **Regional switches.** **GIMACPAY** (the CEMAC interbank rail) crossed **600bn FCFA in transactions**
  (2025), and **[[beac|BEAC]] joined [[papss|PAPSS]]** (Jul 2026) — PAPSS's entry into Francophone Africa
  via a regional central bank serving all six CEMAC states (72m people; PAPSS now 28 countries, 190+
  banks/fintechs, 16 switches; BCEAO pilot to follow; operationalisation to end-2026). Sources:
  [[2025-10-20-lejour-gimacpay-600bn-fcfa-transactions]],
  [[2026-07-09-beac-joins-papss-cross-border-payments-cemac-techafricanews]].
- **Foreign card rails vs sovereign rails.** **[[visa|Visa]] × [[afriland-first-bank]]** (Mar 2026)
  expands US card issuance/acceptance — a foreign network growing against domestic/regional rails (MoMo,
  Blue Money, GIMACPAY, PAPSS). Source:
  [[2026-03-12-visa-afriland-first-bank-digital-payments-techreviewafrica]].

## Fintech entry, and the cost of state market-shaping

- **Foreign fintech entry via a regional bank.** **[[flutterwave]]** obtained a BEAC payment licence via
  **[[ecobank]]** (Jun 2025) — the first BEAC-licensed fintech entry in CMR holdings; French fund **Digital
  Africa** seeded Cameroonian fintech **Futa** (Feb 2025). Sources:
  [[2025-06-23-flutterwave-digital-payment-license-beac-ecobank-connectingafrica]],
  [[2025-02-21-leconomie-digital-africa-invests-fintech-futa]].
- **The monopoly's collateral harm.** The state granted **[[intouch]]** an exclusive over online-gaming
  payment aggregation; because "online games" swept in video-game studios, **[[kiroo-games]]** lost 10+
  days of all payments — the rare CMR item showing a domestic policy decision **harming** a domestic firm,
  and a pointed counterpoint to the state's sovereignty rhetoric. Source:
  [[2025-02-27-kiroo-games-payment-aggregation-monopoly-intouch-wearetech]].
- **Consumer & SME layer.** CNEF's **[[bankcompar]]** bank price-transparency platform (Jun 2025); a
  **MoMo Helep** SME-treasury product (2026); a trade-finance market survey pitting banks against local
  fintechs; and Smile ID data that **AI drives 70% of biometric fraud in Africa** (Mar 2026), bearing on
  the CNI/KYC stack. Sources:
  [[2025-06-23-cnef-bankcompar-financial-literacy-platform-itweb]],
  [[2026-06-23-lejour-momo-helep-sme-treasury]],
  [[2025-10-17-leconomie-trade-finance-banks-vs-fintechs]],
  [[2026-03-12-leconomie-ai-biometric-fraud-fintech]].
