# Morocco × Cybersecurity

Morocco pairs a **fast-growing threat surface** with an ambition to be **Africa's cyber-cooperation hub** —
the **[[dgssi-morocco|DGSSI]]** anchoring both national defence (maCERT alerts) and a continental
CERT-coordination role.

## What we know

- **Market at $1.2bn, incidents +30% (2025-11).** [[tech.industry]] A Ken Research report put Morocco's
  cybersecurity market at **$1.2bn**, driven by a **30% rise in incidents** in 2025 (on 1,300+ recorded
  breaches in 2024) — among North Africa's costliest disruptions. Source:
  [[2025-11-26-itweb-morocco-cybersecurity-market-1-2bn]].
- **FortiBleed hits Moroccan institutions (2026-06).** [[infra.cybersec]] **maCERT/DGSSI** warned that
  dozens of Moroccan institutions were among global victims of the **FortiBleed** Fortinet SSL-VPN leak
  (~75,000 devices, ~200 countries), issuing MFA/password-rotation/PBKDF2 guidance. Source:
  [[2026-06-21-hespress-morocco-fortibleed-macert-cyber-alert]].
- **Rabat as regional cyber hub (2026-06).** [[gov.regional]] [[sovereignty]] DGSSI hosted the **ANCA
  Cybersecurity Executive Leadership Programme** (Rabat, 1–5 Jun) and secured a royally-approved **ANCA
  regional incident-response coordination centre** plus an **AUDA-NEPAD national office** in Morocco —
  cybersecurity framed as "a pillar of digital sovereignty" (DGSSI DG / ANCA VP Boutrig). Source:
  [[2026-06-02-assabah-rabat-cybersecurity-leadership-program]].
