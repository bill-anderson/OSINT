# Comoros × Digital payments & finance

For a country this small, the payments stack is unusually complete: a **national interoperable switch**, a
**BCC-branded public payment system**, a **private B2B platform**, and a **new regulated digital-finance
licence class**.

## The switch, dated end-to-end

The **[[paylogic|PayLogic]]** national interoperable switch's timeline is now complete: **awarded 16 Feb
2025** → **go-live 23 Aug 2025** (connecting banks, MFIs and mobile-money operators; dated penetration
figures — **internet 35.7%, mobile 77.6%** early-2025). A BCC × **Banque de France** payments-modernisation
seminar (Feb 2025) seeded the reform. Sources:
[[2025-02-20-comoros-paylogic-payment-switch-award-wearetech]],
[[2025-08-27-comoros-national-payment-switch-golive-wearetech]],
[[2025-08-27-paylogic-national-payment-switch-golive-techafricanews]],
[[2025-02-27-bcc-banque-de-france-digital-payments-seminar-lagazette]].

## A BCC-branded stack, a private platform, and a new licence class

- **[[komorpay-comoros|KomorPay]] / Komor Switch / Mali Ya Wakazi.** The **[[bcc-comoros|BCC]]** presidentially
  launched a branded payment stack + interoperability layer, with a bond-market component (May 2026) —
  distinct from (and later than) the PayLogic go-live. Source:
  [[2026-05-05-komorpay-komor-switch-payment-systems-presidential-launch-alwatwan]].
- **[[komoralink-comoros|KomoraLink]].** A private **B2B/e-commerce** platform (Kartapay, CCI), launched
  24 Apr 2026 — distinct from KomorPay. Source:
  [[2026-04-29-komoralink-digital-commerce-platform-launch-kartapay-lagazette]].
- **AXIAN Decentralised Digital Financial Institution licence (Feb 2026).** The **[[bcc-comoros|BCC]]** granted
  **[[axian-telecom|AXIAN]]** a **Decentralised Digital Financial Institution (DFI) licence** — a new
  regulated nano/micro-loan entity, a governance/licensing first for Comoros; and AXIAN × **[[mastercard|
  Mastercard]]** partnered on virtual+physical cards (Mixx/MVola) naming Comoros among five markets. Sources:
  [[2026-02-24-axian-central-bank-approval-digital-finance-institution-techafricanews]],
  [[2026-02-24-axian-digital-financial-institution-licence-comoros-itweb]],
  [[2025-11-29-axian-mastercard-digital-payments-partnership-techreviewafrica]].
