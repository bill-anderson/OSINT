# Morocco × Data centres & sovereign cloud

Morocco has made **data-centre capacity and a "third-way" digital sovereignty** a flagship — overtaking
South Africa on facility count, mandating local storage of sensitive data, and layering green-powered AI
compute on top. The through-line (MTNRA Minister **Seghrouchni**): data sovereignty as competitiveness,
not isolation.

## What we know

- **Continental #1 by facility count (2025-06).** [[sovereignty]] A Global Finance report placed Morocco
  **ahead of South Africa** with **23 data-centre facilities**, driven by investment incentives (National
  Charter of Investment) and a **2021 local-storage-of-sensitive-data law** spurring data repatriation.
  Sector led by **[[maroc-telecom]]**, Inwi, Medasys and N+One; US firm Iozera announced a **$500m**
  Tetouan build. (Complements the held "25+ in Casablanca" figure.) Source:
  [[2025-06-09-en-hespress-morocco-overtakes-sa-top-data-center-hub]].
- **Nexus AI Factory — sovereign AI compute (2026-04).** [[tech.ai]] The **[[nexus-ai-factory|Nexus AI
  Factory]]** (Nexus Core Systems + MTNRA/MICEPP/AMDIE, GITEX 2026) — an HPC data centre + Center of
  Excellence + Innovation Hub, positioned to bridge Africa/Europe. Source:
  [[2026-04-13-techafricanews-morocco-nexus-ai-factory]].
- **Green data centres (2025-11).** [[infra.energy]] **[[orange|Orange Maroc]]** inaugurated **"Orange
  Tech"** (Nouaceur) — 1.5 MW, UPTIME-certified, 1,000+ solar panels; and Morocco signed agreements for
  the **"Icudar Dakhla" green data centre** (fully renewable-powered, natural cooling) plus the
  **Al-Jazari Institute for AI & Energy Transition** (see [[jazari-institutes]]), making Dakhla an
  AI/energy-innovation hub. Sources: [[2025-11-24-techafricanews-orange-maroc-sustainable-data-center]],
  [[2025-11-18-techreviewafrica-morocco-green-data-center-ai-institute-dakhla]].
- **Cloud-First execution (2025-12).** [[gov.policy]] **Cloud Computing Week 2025** (MTNRA + **[[oracle]]**)
  trained public-institution IT/RAED admins under the **Cloud Computing Roadmap 2025–2030** and Digital
  Morocco 2030. Source: [[2025-12-02-techreviewafrica-morocco-cloud-computing-week-2025]].
- **US investment — Oracle R&D, Agadir (2026-06).** [[geopol.usa]] **[[oracle]]** inaugurated a **regional
  R&D office in Agadir** (29 Jun; PM Akhannouch, US Ambassador Buchan) — high-value tech FDI positioning
  Morocco as a regional R&D hub. Source: [[2026-06-30-techreviewafrica-oracle-rd-office-agadir]].
- **"Third-way" sovereignty framing (2026-04).** [[gov.policy]] At the Diplomatic Crossroads (Rabat),
  Seghrouchni framed digital transformation as a **sovereignty lever** — structured data management,
  secure infrastructure, service digitisation and robust data protection. Source:
  [[2026-04-21-techreviewafrica-morocco-digital-transformation-sovereignty]].
