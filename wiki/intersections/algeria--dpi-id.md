# Algeria × Digital identity, trust services & biometrics

Algeria's digital identity is built **on top of the existing biometric national ID card**
([[algeria-biometric-id-card]]), extended by a 2025 trust-services law and a growing set of biometric-gated
services.

## Legal base

- **Digital ID & trust-services law** ([[algeria-digital-id-trust-law]]) — approved by the **Council of
  Ministers (2 Nov 2025)** and before the APN; modernises the **2015 electronic-signature framework**, gives
  e-signatures/seals/timestamps/web-authentication legal parity, mandates **certified trust-service providers**,
  and links a national digital-ID system to the **biometric ID card**.
- **Electronic signature & certification** launched **Jan 2025**: **[[arpce-algeria|ARPCE]]** designated the
  **national electronic-certification authority**, alongside the **Governmental Electronic Certification
  Authority (AGCE)** and an APN framework agreement.

## Biometric-gated services

- **Remote biometric passport renewal** for the diaspora (2026-06).
- **Vehicle registration all-digital** — third phase live **5 Apr 2026**: centralised platform, manual local
  applications ended, **biometric ID card required** for transfers (traceability of the vehicle fleet).
- **CNAS electronic health card** for the **uninsured** (Decree 24-187, 2026-01) — extending e-ID into social
  security.

## Policing & forensics

The **[[dgsn-algeria|DGSN]]** (national police) signed a **2025 MoU with the UK Home Office** for digital
fingerprint-analysis training and biometric identity-verification knowledge exchange, and frames data
protection as core to national security (2026-07 study day with the ANPDP).
