# Chad × Innovation, Skills & Digital Health

Beneath the infrastructure and identity headlines, a thin but real **innovation/skills layer** is
forming — a home-grown telehealth startup, donor-funded maker and refugee digital hubs, and youth
training — much of it donor- and Francophonie-backed.

## Digital health

- **Telemedan (2025).** Chadian founder Abakar Mahamat's **Telemedan** delivers healthcare to remote
  areas via **solar-powered telemedicine kiosks**, backed by Chad's Ministry of Health and the **World
  Bank** — a rare Chadian e-health startup (also the country's main entry in continental startup coverage).
  See [[dpi.mis]], [[digital.rural]]. Source: [[2025-02-11-telemedan-solar-kiosks-rural-healthcare]].

## Youth skills and maker culture

- **D-CLIC "Makers Tchad" (Apr 2026).** The **OIF** (Francophonie) launched **Makers Tchad – Jeunesse et
  innovation pour l'emploi (D-CLIC)** at **Wenaklabs**, turning youth potential into concrete skills for
  future jobs (~150 youth, CNC/IoT). See [[capacity.training]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source:
  [[2026-04-17-d-clic-makers-tchad-jeunes-innovateurs]].
- **Digital + financial-inclusion training (Mar 2026).** The **PDMFI** microfinance-support project (with
  the young-entrepreneurs network) trained **120 youth** in digital tools and financial-inclusion
  awareness. See [[capacity.literacy]], [[dpi.pay]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-26-pdmfi-120-jeunes-outils-numeriques-inclusion-financiere]].
- **Refugee digital learning — Abéché (2025).** **UNHCR** (Italy-funded) inaugurated a **digital learning
  centre** at the Abéché one-stop-shop for refugee and host-community youth (online skills training),
  piloted for extension across eastern Chad. See [[include.access]], [[geopol.eu]]. Source:
  [[2025-02-25-centre-apprentissage-numerique-abeche]].

## The India vector

- **Chad–India talks (2025).** Chad explored a digital partnership with **India** — **AI training**,
  support for **ENASTIC** (the national ICT school) and a **postal bank** — one of the several
  geopolitical vectors Chad is courting (see [[TCD]] hub). See [[geopol.india]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2025-02-17-tchad-inde-partenariat-numerique]].

## Reads

- The innovation layer is **small, donor-scaffolded and skills-focused** — sensible for a state starting
  near the bottom of the tables. Telemedan (Chadian-founded, WB-backed, solving a real rural-health gap)
  is the encouraging signal; most of the rest is training and hubs funded by the OIF, UNHCR and Italy. The
  ecosystem is being *seeded* from outside more than *grown* from within — but seeded it is being.
