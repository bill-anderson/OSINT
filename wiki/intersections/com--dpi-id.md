# Comoros × Digital identity & registries

Comoros is rebuilding foundational identity on **three layers at once** — a **repatriated biometric
document stack**, a **digitised civil registry with a Personal Identification Number**, and a **World-Bank
social registry** — the first of which is a clean data-sovereignty story.

## The vendor handover — data repatriated from Belgium

The **Semlex → [[genkey|GenKey]]** transition is a **data-sovereignty event, not just a procurement one**:
under Semlex, **all Comorian biometric data was stored in Belgium**; **[[genkey|GenKey]] (Dutch) will store
it domestically**. The chain is dated: announced Jan 2026 (data to be domiciled in Comoros) → a **document-
production suspension window (6–26 Mar 2026)** bridging the change → **Semlex handed the data back to the
government (25 Mar 2026)** → **GenKey officially succeeded Semlex (31 Mar / 1 Apr 2026)**, with card
printing decentralised to Anjouan (Ndzuani). Sources:
[[2026-01-01-genkey-to-replace-semlex-from-april-data-domiciled-in-comoros-alwatwan]],
[[2026-03-06-biometric-document-production-suspended-semlex-genkey-transition-alwatwan]],

## The civil registry & a Personal Identification Number

Distinct from card production, **[[amecc-comoros|AMECC]]** is digitising the **civil registry**
(France/UNICEF-funded): a preliminary study launched at **Mohéli (Oct 2025)**, a validation workshop
(Mar 2026), and — foundationally — a **unique Personal Identification Number (PIN)** introduced under the
**27 Jul 2023 law**. Alongside it, island consultations advanced a decree creating a **national civil-status
agency ([[anec-comoros|ANEC]])**. Sources:
[[2025-10-14-amecc-civil-registry-digitisation-study-launch-moheli-lagazette]],
[[2026-03-26-amecc-civil-registry-computerisation-validation-workshop-alwatwan]],

## A social registry

A separate **World-Bank social registry ([[rsu-comoros|RSU]])** reached **62,000 households registered**
(Jul 2026) — a targeting/MIS layer distinct from the civil registry. Source:
[[2026-07-10-registre-social-unique-62000-households-worldbank-lagazette]].

*(Accountability lag: the GDPR-aligned [[comoros-data-protection-law-2021]]'s National Authority is still
not operational as this ID/data build accelerates.)*
