# Morocco × Digital Identity

Morocco runs one of Africa's most **advanced and layered** digital-ID stacks. The foundation is the
**[[dgsn-morocco|DGSN]]** electronic national ID (4.6m issued in 2024) and a trusted-third-party
verification platform; on top sit the **[[mon-e-id|Mon e-ID]]** national digital-identity app (2.5m users,
built by iDakto on IDEMIA foundations, privacy-preserving), the **[[idarati-x0|Idarati X.0]]** e-government
wallet, and a **[[morocco-national-population-registry|National Population Registry / Unified Social
Registry]]** (Law 72-18, MOSIP-based, ~half the population) that targets social benefits. The forthcoming
**[[digital-x0-law|Digital X.0 law]]** would bind AI governance, data governance and digital identity into
one framework — with sectoral IDs keeping data control with users.

## What we know

- **[[MAR]] DGSN electronic national ID (2024).** [[dpi.govtech]] 4.6m e-ID cards issued in 2024; a
  Trusted Third Party Identity Verification platform with 17 framework agreements and 30+ institutions;
  E-Police/E-Blagh portals. Source:
  [[2024-12-24 Morocco's DGSN issued 4.6 million electronic national IDs in 2024 (annual report)]].
- **[[MAR]] Mon e-ID + Idarati X.0 wallet (2025-26).** [[dpi.id]] The Mon e-ID app (fast-tracked after
  cyberattacks; hybrid privacy-preserving model, 2.5m users) authenticates the **Idarati X.0** national
  digital wallet (8 MoUs, Feb 2026; iDakto + ShareID on architecture). Sources:
  [[2025-09-24 Morocco fast-tracks 'Mon e-ID' digital identity after cyberattacks (iDAKTO)]],
  [[2026-02-13 Morocco launches Idarati X.0 e-government platform (8 MoUs, national digital wallet)]],
  [[2026-02-13 Morocco signs agreements with iDakto and ShareID for national digital identity wallet]].
- **[[MAR]] Population/social registry for targeting (2024).** [[include.access]] The RNP + RSU (Law
  72-18, MOSIP) enrol ~half the population and target AMO-TADAMON and the Direct Social Benefit program;
  a WB $70m second project extends them to rural/remote areas. Source:
  [[2024-12-04 World Bank approves $70M for Morocco's social protection delivery (RNP and Unified Social Registry)]].
- **[[MAR]] Digital X.0 law — AI + data + ID (2025-11).** [[gov.legislate]] Morocco's first attempt to
  legislate AI governance, alongside sectoral digital IDs and interoperability, under Law 09-08. Source:
  [[2025-11-07 Morocco introduces 'Digital X.0' law for AI governance, data governance and digital identity]].
