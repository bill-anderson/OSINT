# Nigeria × Subnational Digital Government

Digital transformation is spreading below the federal tier — states building citizen apps,
connectivity partnerships and civil-service skilling. (See also **Anambra SmartGov** in
[[nigeria--tech-ai]] and the **Lagos Identity Project** in [[nigeria--dpi-registry]].)

- **Lagos — MyLagosApp (Mar 2025).** [[mtn-nigeria]] and Lagos State launched **MyLagosApp**, a
  one-stop mobile platform for city services, navigation and digital payments — part of Lagos's
  smart-city push. Source: [[2025-03-14-mylagosapp-mtn-lagos-state]].
- **Abia — ipNX partnership (Mar 2025).** Abia State partnered ICT firm **ipNX** to design and deploy
  connectivity and managed-network services to digitise the civil service and e-government delivery.
  Source: [[2025-03-12-ipnx-abia-state-connectivity]].
- **Benue — civil-service skilling (Mar 2025).** Benue committed to training **40,000 civil servants**
  in digital tools (with [[ndpc]] input) to run new state platforms — an e-document system, a GIS
  portal, a startup-support platform and an Office of the Head of Service site; builds on a Nov-2024
  Huawei partnership. See [[capacity.training]]. Source: [[2025-03-26-benue-40000-civil-servants-training]].
