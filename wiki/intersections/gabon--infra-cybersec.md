# Gabon × Cybersecurity, AI & digital sovereignty

Gabon is building a national cyber posture around **[[aninf-gabon|ANINF]]**, framed explicitly as **digital
sovereignty** and increasingly tied to **US technical cooperation**.

- **SecBOM (2025-06).** ANINF launched a **Security Bill of Material** — a first-of-its-kind tool to **map,
  control and protect** Gabon's sensitive digital assets, blending technical rigour, legal compliance and a
  sovereigntist frame (Presidency-driven).
- **Enforcement — Interpol Serengeti 2.0 (2025-08).** Gabon took part in the Interpol operation (18 African
  countries, UK-backed): **1,209 arrests, ~$97.4m / ~55bn FCFA recovered** continent-wide.
- **Institutions & events.** A **2nd national Cybersecurity Forum** (Nov 2025; ST Digital, ANINF, CN
  Consulting, BGFI Services) on critical-infrastructure protection; an **ARCEP**-led strategy positioning Gabon
  as a **"digital soft-power hub"** (cyber / AI / sovereignty, Jan 2026).
- **US cooperation — ANINF–MITRE (2026-06).** ANINF and the **MITRE Corporation (US)** are designing Gabon's
  **operational & institutional cybersecurity architecture** for critical digital infrastructure — a
  previously-unheld US thread (`geopol.usa`).
- **Sectoral audit.** ANINF signed an **MoU with CNAMGS** (health-insurance body, 8 May 2026) to **audit its
  information systems** for security/resilience — extending cyber into the health-MIS layer ([[dpi.mis]]).
