# Libya × Digital Identity & Civil Registry

Libya's **National ID Number** — issued by the **[[civil-registry-authority-libya|CRA]]** — gates every
state service, which makes the registry's **integrity crisis** a national-security problem, not just an
administrative one. Through 2025–26 the register is dogged by fraudulent-number scandals on **both sides
of the split**, a black market in forged numbers, and an exclusion problem for holders of partial
"administrative numbers."

## What we know

- **AG crackdown — 225 Malian nationals (2026-01).** [[gov.protect]] [[sovereignty]] The Attorney
  General found **61 family records** irregularly transferred from the Ubari office to Hun/Sukna offices —
  records **not in the CRA database** — enabling **National ID Numbers and Libyan passports for 225
  foreign nationals of Malian origin**, who then drew citizen grants, public-sector jobs and benefits.
  Procedures were suspended and prosecutions opened. Extends the held 2025-12 Parliament breach warning.
  Source: [[2026-01-02-attorney-general-civil-registry-id-fraud-crackdown-libyaherald]].
- **East-side case — Al-Marj (2026-01).** A foreigner was jailed, a family record suspended, and **10
  national ID numbers cancelled** at the **Al-Marj (eastern Libya)** civil-registry office — showing the
  fraud spans both administrations. Source: [[2026-01-14-almarj-civil-registry-fake-national-numbers-alwasat]].
- **Black market persists (2026-04).** [[sovereignty]] An investigative report documented an **ongoing
  trade in forged national numbers**, framed as a standing threat to the civil-registry system and
  national identity amid the institutional split. Source: [[2026-04-19-fake-national-numbers-for-sale-alwasat]]
  *(thin excerpt — full clip recommended at promotion)*.
- **Exclusion — "administrative numbers" (2025-06).** [[include.access]] Holders of partial
  **"administrative numbers"** (rather than full National ID Numbers) protested advisory
  recommendations/measures that leave them unable to access national-number-gated services — a
  statelessness-adjacent inclusion gap inside the same system. Source:
  [[2025-06-07-administrative-numbers-holders-anger-alwasat]] *(thin excerpt)*.
