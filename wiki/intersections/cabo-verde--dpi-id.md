# Cabo Verde × Digital identity, CRVS & data protection

Cabo Verde has one of the region's stronger identity-and-rights positions — **near-universal birth
registration via a CRVS–ID interoperability platform**, and an **older, GDPR-mirroring data-protection
regime** — but the record adds live tensions (deepfakes, CCTV-access governance).

## CRVS–ID: near-universal birth registration

The standout: a **CRVS–ID linkage drove birth registration to ~99% in five years**, via the **[[autentika-cabo-verde|Autentika]]** interoperability platform, which assigns a **Unique Identification Number (UIN) at
birth** — showcased at **ID4Africa 2026**. A **universal digital ID** is also a named component of the
National 5G/data strategy. Source: [[2026-05-19-crvs-id-reforms-birth-registration-biometricupdate]].

## The CNI and its legal basis — SNIAC

Beneath the CRVS layer sits the credential itself. The **Cartão Nacional de Identificação (CNI)** is part
of the **[[sniac-cabo-verde|SNIAC]]** (*Sistema Nacional de Identificação e Autenticação Civil*), and its
statutory chain is:

| Instrument | Date | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Decreto-Lei n.º 19/2014** | **17 Mar 2014** | creates the CNI; governs issuance, replacement, use and cancellation |
| **Decreto-Lei n.º 68/2014** | **22 Dec 2014** | fee regime for issuance, replacement and delivery |
| **Portaria n.º 21/2017** | **15 Jun 2017** | regulates biometric data capture |
| **Portaria n.º 35/2017** | **2 Sep 2017** | sets the CNI's validity period |
| **Decreto-Legislativo n.º 5/2020** | **21 Jul 2020** | creates the **Chave Móvel Digital de Cabo Verde (CMDCV)** |
| **Portaria n.º 27/2022** | **24 Jun 2022** | CMDCV implementing rules |

The CNI was **officially launched on 23 January 2018**, in Paúl, Santo Antão, replacing the older *Bilhete
de Identidade*. It is a polycarbonate contact smart card whose chip carries a digital certificate and
identification, authentication and digital-signature applications — so the credential was designed from the
outset as an **authentication token, not merely a document**. Using its digital functions requires a
smart-card reader, CNI middleware and a PIN letter collected from the issuing service. The **CMDCV** is the
mobile alternative — a permanent PIN plus a one-time code per authentication — and, in the phase the source
describes, **can only be obtained by first activating the CNI's digital functions**, binding the mobile
credential to the card rather than replacing it. Both feed **[[autentika-cabo-verde|Autentika]]**, the
state's electronic-identity and authentication platform, which supports three authentication modes
(email/password, CMDCV, or CNI-with-reader).

**Provenance and date honesty.** This rests on the SNIAC and Autentika **standing government reference
pages**, which are **undated** — they expose no publication or last-updated date. The source is filed at
**`date_source: proxy`, published 2026-07-20**, the retrieval date, and that date carries **no evidential
weight**: it is when the wiki looked, not when anything happened or when the pages were last revised.
What the pages do supply are **instrument dates of their own**, and those are the table above — structural
facts that do not age. **The description of the CMDCV as available only via CNI activation is stated on the
page as "nesta primeira fase" and cannot be dated**; it may no longer hold. Source:
[[2026-07-20-cabo-verde-sniac-cartao-nacional-identificacao]].

**What this does not establish.** The reference pages carry **no enrolment count, no CMDCV activation
count and no coverage rate**, so this section describes the system's design and legal basis only. **No
dated figure for CNI or CMDCV uptake is held from any source, as of 2026-07-20** — the ~99% figure on this
page is *birth registration* via the CRVS–ID linkage, which is a different measure and must not be read as
CNI coverage.

## Regional (PALOP) & the data-protection regime

- **Lusophone cooperation.** Cabo Verde was named among **five PALOP countries** collaborating on digital
  identity and public services (Digital Governance Dialogues webinar, 26 Mar 2026). Source:
  [[2026-03-31-palop-digital-identity-collaboration-biometricupdate]].
- **A mature DPA under pressure.** The **[[cnpd-cabo-verde|CNPD]]** (President Faustino Varela) marked a
  decade: Cabo Verde acceded to **Convention 108 and the Malabo Convention**, and the CNPD has handled
  **deepfake cases since 2021**; its 10th-anniversary conference put AI harms and constitutional rights on
  the agenda (the PM speaking). Sources: [[2025-02-02-cnpd-faustino-varela-ia-big-data-direitos-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2025-03-19-cnpd-dialogo-governanca-dados-regulacao-ia-expressodasilhas]].

## Surveillance governance

A live governance question: the **Judicial Police (PJ) is seeking inter-institutional access to the
national CCTV (videovigilância) system** (backed by the Justice Minister; the system is expanding across
islands) — a surveillance-access debate under the same data-protection regime. Source:
[[2026-05-13-pj-acesso-videovigilancia-investigacao-criminal-anacao]].
