# Botswana × Data Centres

A national data centre plus two energy-anchored private builds (2025–26) — compute increasingly
tied to generation sites rather than to the grid.

- **The sovereign core.** BoFiNet's Tier-III **[[digital-delta-data-centre|Digital Delta]]** (Gaborone,
  government-owned, sovereignty-framed, Zhong Gan-built) is the hosting foundation for the **1Gov**
  migration. See [[dpi.govtech]]. Source:
  [[2025-11-26-digital-delta-data-centre-launch-connectingafrica]].
- **Gulf-linked gas-to-power build.** A **Kala DC** pairs **Tlou Energy** gas-to-power with UAE
  **Kala Data FZCO**. Source: [[2025-09-26-kala-data-centre-tlou-energy-itweb]].
- **Solar hyperscaler/AI campus, proposed.** A **Palapye** campus (US ChillMine × Dutch AAAS Energy)
  is proposed at **250 MW solar + BESS**. See [[infra.energy]], [[tech.ai]].
