# Algeria × AI

- **National AI Strategy (Dec 2024).** Adopted by the **National AI Council** under Professor **Merouane Debbah**; six pillars (research, talent, hardware/infrastructure, investment/ecosystem, data protection/regulation, sectoral AI). See [[algeria-national-ai-strategy]].
- **Economic target.** **AI = 7% of GDP by 2027**; AI market projected **$498.9m (2025) → $1.69bn (2030)**.
- **Compute base.** The state-owned **Oran AI data centre** (ground broken Mar 2025; GPU/HPC for researchers, universities, startups, public institutions) anchors sovereign AI capacity — reinforced by mandatory data localisation and a protected (hyperscaler-free) cloud market. Algérie Télécom's ~$11m fund seeds AI/cybersecurity/robotics startups.
- **Governance fit.** AI is a pillar of **[[digital-algeria-2030]]** and increasingly framed as a public-administration tool (see [[algeria--dpi-govtech]]).
