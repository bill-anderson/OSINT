# Somalia × Digital Payments

Somalia's cash-light, privately-built mobile-money economy is now getting its first
central-bank regulatory foothold via the Somalia Instant Payment System (SIPS) and the
national QR standard SOMQR.

## What we know

Somalia runs one of Africa's most cash-light economies — mobile money worth ~**$8bn (36%
of GDP)**, ~650m transactions/yr, built by Hormuud/Telesom in the state's absence. The
[[central-bank-of-somalia]] launched the **Somalia Instant Payment System (SIPS)** and a
national QR standard **SOMQR** in early 2025 to bridge isolated wallets and the emerging
banking sector — its first real regulatory foothold. A first National DPI Summit
(Mogadishu, 14–15 Feb 2026) with CBS, the NCA, [[nira-somalia]] and the Data Protection
Authority targets interoperability. See [[dpi.exchange]], [[gov.regional]],
[[include.access]].
