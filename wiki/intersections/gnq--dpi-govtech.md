# Equatorial Guinea × GovTech & e-Government

Equatorial Guinea is building an e-government layer around **[[cniapge-gnq|CNIAPGE]]** (public-administration
informatisation) with strong external partners — Morocco, China, the World Bank and UNDP.

- **Civil-service digitalisation.** The **[[sigf-gnq|SIGF]]** (M2M Group, Morocco) centralises civil-servant
  records with **biometric attendance and automated payroll** — a transparency / anti-ghost-worker instrument.
- **Tax.** The **Patente Tributaria Única** (annual tax licence) went digital (Jan 2026) — paperless tax
  administration.
- **Capacity.** A **UNDP "Digital Fitness"** programme trained multisectoral officials in digital
  transformation, citizen-centred design and cybersecurity (2025-08); a **Director-General of AI Systems**
  (Reginaldo Borico Okomo) sits in the mix.
- **China cooperation.** A tech-cooperation roadmap to become a **Central-African tech hub** — **INSTTIC**
  training (200+ students) with Chinese universities, GNQ **co-chairing FOCAC 2027**, and a **Cybersecurity & AI
  Forum in Djibloho** — a `geopol.china` vector (capture is not endorsement).
