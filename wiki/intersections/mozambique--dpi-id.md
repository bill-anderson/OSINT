# Mozambique × Digital Identity & Civil Registry

Mozambique is building foundational identity on two tracks: a **mass free civil-registration drive** to
close a large birth-registration deficit, and a **mobile digital-ID + e-signature** layer on top — both
anchored by the World Bank **[[edge-mozambique|EDGE]]** project and the new **[[atdi-mozambique|ATDI]]**
digital agency.

## What we know

- **Mass free civil-registration campaign (2026-03 →).** [[include.access]] The Justice Ministry launched
  a **free birth-registration campaign** (23 Mar → 31 Dec) targeting **~7 million people** at **25,000/day**
  via up to **500 mobile brigades** — responding to a "critical deficit" in birth registration; President
  **Chapo** personally urged undocumented citizens (Niassa) to enrol. Procurement (EDGE-funded, tender
  published 11 Dec) sought non-consulting services to strengthen civil registration and the
  **[[dnic-mozambique|DNIC]]** population register. Sources:
  [[2026-03-31-government-launches-free-birth-registration-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-05-21-chapo-free-civil-registration-campaign-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-01-05-seeks-expert-services-mass-birth-id-registration-biometricupdate]].
- **Mobile digital ID + e-signature (2026-05).** [[dpi.govtech]] [[dpi.exchange]] The Ministry of
  Communications & Digital Transformation (via **[[atdi-mozambique|ATDI]]**) tendered a **national mobile
  digital-identity and e-signature infrastructure** for identifying, authenticating and digitally signing
  for citizens/residents/companies — inclusive, scalable, device-agnostic (SIM/eSIM/Java Card), integrated
  with a secure **inter-government data-exchange (X-Road/DNIC)**. Sources:
  [[2026-05-25-tender-mobile-digital-identity-signature-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-05-26-seeks-partner-mobile-digital-id-esignature-biometricupdate]].
- **New ID-card regulations (2026-06).** [[gov.legislate]] The Council of Ministers updated the
  **Civil Identification & National ID Card regulations** under **Law 6/2022** — governing collection,
  processing, management and retention of identification data, and standardising secure BI issuance
  nationwide. Source: [[2026-06-03-cabinet-approves-new-id-card-regulations-clubofmozambique]].
- **Ethiopia FaydaVerse MoU (2026-04).** [[gov.regional]] ATDI and Ethiopia's **FaydaVerse Digital
  Solutions Enterprise** signed an MoU (Addis Ababa) on **digital identity and DPI** — open standards,
  interoperability, capacity-building (ATDI chair Adilson Gomes; see [[mozambique-ethiopia-dpi-mou]],
  [[fayda-ethiopia-national-id]]). Source:
  [[2026-04-27-mozambique-ethiopia-mou-digital-identity-clubofmozambique]].
