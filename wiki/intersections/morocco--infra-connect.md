# Morocco × Connectivity

Morocco's connectivity build pairs a **regulator-led 5G rollout** (licensing stability over public
subsidy, shared-infrastructure JVs) with its ambition to be a **Euro-Mediterranean digital corridor**
(subsea cable, enterprise partnerships). Framed as economic infrastructure for export competitiveness.

## What we know

- **5G launched (2025-11).** [[infra.connect]] All three operators switched on **5G in major cities**
  (7 Nov 2025) — cast as economic infrastructure (lower latency for factories, Tanger Med logistics,
  remote surgery, smart agriculture), moving Morocco "from digital consumption to production." Sources:
  [[2025-11-08-hespress-morocco-5g-launch-new-digital-era]], [[2025-11-07-en-hespress-what-5g-could-mean-for-morocco]].
- **Licensing via regulatory stability (2025).** [[gov.legislate]] **[[anrt-morocco|ANRT]]** ran the
  **5G tender** (Jul 2025) with **moderate one-off fees** (900m dirham Maroc Telecom; 600m each Orange,
  Inwi) and coverage targets **~25% (2026) / 70% (2030)** — driving rollout without public funds. Sources:
  [[2025-07-16-telecomreviewafrica-morocco-5g-license-tender]], [[2025-11-07-en-hespress-what-5g-could-mean-for-morocco]].
- **Shared infrastructure — FiberCo / TowerCo (2025-04).** [[infra.connect]] **[[maroc-telecom]]** and
  **[[inwi]]** formed **FiberCo** and **TowerCo** JVs to fast-track fibre + 5G on shared assets (dispute
  settled) — tying investment to shared infrastructure, though ANRT flags uneven low-density coverage
  risk. Source: [[2025-04-02-connectingafrica-maroc-telecom-inwi-fiber-5g]].
- **Vendor partnerships.** [[finance.mou]] **[[orange|Orange Maroc]]** renewed its **[[ericsson]]**
  partnership (GITEX 2026), and **[[nokia]]** was selected to **power the Medusa subsea cable**
  (Europe–North Africa, AFR-IX Telecom). **[[vodafone|Vodafone Business]]** signed a Maroc Telecom
  enterprise MoU (smart city, energy). Sources:
  [[2026-04-08-techafricanews-orange-maroc-ericsson-collaboration]],
  [[2025-07-28-itweb-nokia-subsea-cable-europe-north-africa]],
  [[2025-04-25-itweb-vodafone-maroc-telecom-digital-services-deal]].
