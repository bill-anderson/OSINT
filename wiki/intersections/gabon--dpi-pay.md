# Gabon × Digital Payments

Gabon's digital-payments push rides the **CEMAC regional GIMACPAY rail** (via **GIMAC** and the **[[beac|BEAC]]**),
with public-service payments the spearhead — and adoption frictions starting to show.

- **Interoperability vision (2025-06).** Minister Mark-Alexandre Doumba and **GIMAC** set out
  card/mobile **interoperability** to cut costs, simplify and secure electronic transactions — the policy frame
  behind the later rollout.
- **MADIGIPAIE / GIMACPAY.** The **[[madigipaie-gabon|MADIGIPAIE]]** public-payments programme (launched Dec
  2025, QR-code pay) extended to the **TaxiGab** transport network (28 Feb 2026) and won **Libreville
  municipality** backing (Jul 2026) — pushing digital payments into everyday transactions.
- **Visa partnership.** The **[[gabon-visa-partnership-deal|Gabon–Visa]]** accord (signed at the Africa CEO
  Forum, May 2025; follow-up in Libreville, 18 Jul 2025) targets **digitalising public services** and
  modernising the state's payment collection — a US-vendor thread (`geopol.usa`).
- **Adoption friction (critical read, 2026-06).** L'Union reported **GimacPay malfunctions** — cross-border
  CEMAC transfers not arriving, slow complaint handling — a rare critical counterpoint to the official
  payments narrative.
