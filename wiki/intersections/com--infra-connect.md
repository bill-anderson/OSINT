# Comoros × Connectivity, ccTLD & 5G

For its size, Comoros moved fast in 2025: **both operators launched 5G**, the **`.km` ccTLD was
repatriated to a state agency**, and a **Starlink ban** drew a connectivity-sovereignty line — while
foreign capital (IFC, ITU) financed the build.

## ccTLD sovereignty

The **`.km` ccTLD was transferred from the incumbent [[comores-telecom|Comores Télécom]] to the state
digital agency [[anaden|ANADEN]]** (arrêté n°24-02; with an ICANN re-delegation step) — moving control of
the national domain from an operator to a public registry. Sources:
[[2025-05-08-cctld-km-transfer-comores-telecom-to-anaden-alwatwan]],
[[2025-05-12-cctld-km-transfer-anaden-registry-lagazette]].

## Dual 5G launch, and the incumbent's numbers

- **Both operators launched 5G in 2025.** **[[yas|Yas Comores]]** launched 5G (Huawei) in Moroni (20 May
  2025); the incumbent **[[comores-telecom|Comores Télécom]]** followed with **5G via WTTX (1 Gbit/s)** on
  2 Jun 2025 (presidential launch, 50th independence anniversary). CT's FY2024 results put revenue at
  **17.035bn KMF** with 5G/WTTx pre-announced. Sources:
  [[2025-05-20-yas-comores-5g-launch-huawei-lagazette]],
  [[2025-06-04-comores-telecom-5g-launch-wttx-lagazette]],
  [[2025-02-01-comores-telecom-2024-results-5g-wttx-plans-alwatwan]].

## The Starlink line, and foreign capital

- **Starlink banned pending a licence.** The regulator **ANRTIC banned Starlink deployment (12 Jun 2025)**
  pending a licence — a connectivity-sovereignty flashpoint, and the opposite of neighbours licensing it.
  Source: [[2025-06-13-starlink-banned-pending-licence-anrtic-alwatwan]].
- **Foreign-financed build.** **[[axian-telecom|AXIAN]]** committed **$82m to 4G** across Madagascar,
  Comoros and Tanzania (ITU Partner2Connect); **Yas Comores secured a €25m IFC loan** (5G/FTTH/FTTO); and
  AXIAN's **NEXT 2030** vision confirmed 2025 Yas milestones (5G + FTTx live in Moroni; the Sarumaya
  women-entrepreneur programme). Sources:
  [[2025-04-01-axian-telecom-82m-4g-madagascar-comoros-tanzania-techafricanews]],
  [[2025-06-20-yas-comoros-ifc-25m-loan-techafricanews]],
  [[2026-02-02-axian-telecom-next-2030-vision-comoros-techreviewafrica]].
