# Cabo Verde × GovTech / e-Government

An island micro-state (~500,000 people) that has turned e-government into a comparative advantage — and an export.

- **NOSi, the engine.** [[nosi]] (est. ~2000) runs the open-source **IGRP** microservices
  framework, the Justice Information System and the Consular/Digital Diaspora portal, on a
  three-pillar model (digital identity & authentication, interoperability, disruptive innovation).
  It **hosts GovTech for other African states** (Equatorial Guinea, Mozambique, Burkina Faso) —
  a rare South-to-South GovTech exporter.
- **Gov.CV unified portal (early 2026).** [[gov-cv]] — a single web/app gateway to identity, tax,
  education, transport and social services — launched alongside a parallel CAR platform.
- **Targets & financing.** **60% of public services online by 2026, 80% by 2030**, backed by the
  World Bank **[[digital-cabo-verde-project]]** (P171099; +$20m additional financing, May 2025)
  and the **WebLabs** skills programme (~14,000 students).
