# Cabo Verde × GovTech / e-Government

An island micro-state (~500,000 people) that has turned e-government into a comparative advantage — and an export.

- **NOSi, the engine.** [[nosi]] (est. ~2000) runs the open-source **IGRP** microservices
  framework, the Justice Information System and the Consular/Digital Diaspora portal, on a
  three-pillar model (digital identity & authentication, interoperability, disruptive innovation).
  It **hosts GovTech for other African states** (Equatorial Guinea, Mozambique, Burkina Faso) —
  a rare South-to-South GovTech exporter.
- **Gov.CV unified portal (early 2026).** [[gov-cv]] — a single web/app gateway to identity, tax,
  education, transport and social services — launched alongside a parallel CAR platform.
- **Targets & financing.** **60% of public services online by 2026, 80% by 2030**, backed by the
  World Bank **[[digital-cabo-verde-project]]** (P171099; +$20m additional financing, May 2025)
  and the **WebLabs** skills programme (~14,000 students).

## National-press record (2025–26): the portal, the registry, the export

- **Gov.cv, dated and detailed.** A precursor "portal to modernise bureaucracy" (Oct 2025) preceded the
  **Gov.cv** launch (Feb 2026), into which **"Porton di Nos Ilha"** migrates, on an interoperability layer.
  A separate **Empresa Online** platform (Prime Solutions) digitised the **business registry**. Sources:
  [[2025-10-15-governo-prepara-portal-digital-modernizar-burocracia-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-02-24-governo-lanca-portal-govcv-modernizar-servicos-publicos-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-03-20-plataforma-empresa-online-digitalizacao-servicos-publicos-expressodasilhas]].
- **Semana do Digital — a standing policy instrument.** A Resolution instituted an **annual "Digital Week"**
  (naming electronic identity/residence/signature, interoperability and the Portal do Governo), executed in
  Mar 2026 (funded by the Morabeza Innovation Project). Sources:
  [[2026-02-13-semana-do-digital-anual-resolucao-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-03-09-comeca-semana-do-digital-2026-expressodasilhas]].
- **GovTech export deepens — [[nosi-angola-setic-mou|NOSi → Angola]].** NOSi signed a **memorandum with
  Angola's SETIC FP** to export its govtech — extending the South-South exporter role. Source:
  [[2026-03-06-nosi-setic-fp-angola-memorando-entendimento-expressodasilhas]].

## Financing & partnerships

The digital state runs on donor money and diplomacy: an **AfDB E-PFMRP phase-2** loan for the electronic
reform — reported as **$20.7M** (ITWeb) and **€17.7M** (We Are Tech / Expresso), the **same event dated
in two currencies** (incl. e-justice and digital-nomad components) — plus a **World Bank digital-
transformation support** mission (60% services online by 2026, 80% by 2030), a **South Korea** digital-
cooperation mission (data governance, e-services, cybersecurity), a **RABIDA** diaspora-engagement
initiative, and **ATU** engagement on global ICT processes. Sources:
