# Comoros × E-gov, AI, skills, EMIS & census

For its size, Comoros is running **two parallel donor tracks** (AfDB + EU) on e-government, plus a
**China-built tech-hub**, sectoral MIS in education and health, and its **first census since 2017-era
planning** — all on a very low base.

## Two donor tracks on e-government

- **AfDB — PADEC.** A governance milestone (**PTAB 2025 validation**, Mar 2025) on the AfDB digital-economy
  project (the same **[[project-digitization-comorian-economy]]** the wiki holds; the grant is stated here
  as **€9.51m**, vs the held **~$10.4m** — the same project, dated differently). Source:
  [[2025-03-20-padec-afdb-digital-economy-project-ptab-validation-lagazette]].
- **EU — e-Administration.** A **separate EU / Expertise France** programme launched (Oct 2025): a *guichet
  unique* and interoperability layer — a second donor track alongside AfDB. Plus **tax-administration
  téléprocédures** (télédéclaration/télépaiement) under the 2026 finance law. Sources:
  [[2025-10-16-e-administration-project-launch-eu-expertise-france-alwatwan]],
  [[2026-04-16-tax-administration-teleprocedures-digitisation-pagf-lagazette]].

## A China-built tech-hub, and the first AI forum

- **[[technopole-numerique-comoros|Technopole numérique]]** — a **Huawei-built** digital tech-hub (**$4.09m,
  2 000 youth, 2025–2028**, World Bank) — pilot-phase launch ceremony **29 Jan 2026** — the `geopol.china`
  vector in COM's skills build. Sources:
  [[2026-01-24-technopole-numerique-project-preview-2000-youth-worldbank-alwatwan]],
  [[2026-01-29-technopole-numerique-phase-pilote-launch-huawei-alwatwan]].
- **AI.** The **first national AI Forum** (31 Mar 2026), with a "Made in Comores" ERP (ZynBusiness); and an
  AI-training session for ~40 lycéens (Fondation Hazi Haki, Apr 2025). Sources:
  [[2026-04-01-premier-forum-intelligence-artificielle-alwatwan]],
  [[2025-04-15-ai-training-second-session-hazi-haki-foundation-lagazette]].

## Sectoral MIS & the census

- **Education — [[e-msomo-comoros|e-Msomo / e-Shiyo]].** School-management + digital-textbook platforms
  (ANADEN/UNICEF, launched 5 Jun 2025) assigning each student a **unique ID**, under "Comoros Digital 2028."
  Sources: [[2025-06-09-e-msomo-e-shiyo-education-digitisation-anaden-unicef-lagazette]],
  [[2025-06-20-comoros-eshiyo-emsomo-edtech-platforms-wearetech]].
- **Health — ODK.** First use of **ODK digital data-capture** (real-time dashboards) in a national
  measles-rubella campaign (101,000 children, Oct 2025). Source:
  [[2025-10-06-comoros-digital-measles-rubella-campaign-odk-wearetech]].
- **Census — [[rgph5-comoros|RGPH-5]].** The 5th population census officially launched (**5 Jun 2026**;
  pilot Sept 2026, count Sept 2027), preceded by **GIS cartography training** (INSEED, STATCAP-KM/ESRI +
  RCMRD, Apr 2026). Sources: [[2026-06-09-rgph5-population-census-official-launch-lagazette]],
  [[2026-04-17-inseed-rgph5-gis-cartography-training-statcap-esri-lagazette]],
  [[2026-04-14-comoros-advanced-gis-training-rcmrd-techreviewafrica]].
