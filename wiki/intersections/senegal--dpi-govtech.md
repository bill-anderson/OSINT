# Senegal × GovTech & Interoperability

The public-service digitisation pillar of the New Deal ([[senegal--gov-policy]]) runs on an
**interoperability backbone**, sector platforms and a governance layer — the e-Senegal single window
(see [[senegal--gov-policy]]) sitting on top.

## The National Interoperability Platform

- **Launched Apr 2025, phase-1 by end-2025.** Senegal launched an interministerial, interoperable
  **data-exchange platform** (JICA-supported) on **22 Apr 2025** to let ministries, agencies and private
  bodies share data securely — first use case: faster **social assistance** to vulnerable families, plus
  transparency and revenue mobilisation. A **COCOOR** coordination committee (Sep 2025) validated the
  roadmap, with **phase 1 targeted for end-December 2025**. See [[dpi.exchange]]. Sources:
  [[2025-04-22-lesoleil-interoperability-platform]], [[2025-09-19-techafricanews-interoperability-update]].

## Governance — the National Digital Council

- **CNN inaugurated (2 Oct 2025).** The **National Digital Council (CNN)** — 20 experts from public
  sector, private enterprise and civil society — was inaugurated by the PM to advise on tech trends,
  digital policy and structural projects (innovation, entrepreneurship, inclusion, cybersecurity, data
  protection). (Distinct from **GouvNum**, the Mar-2026 project-coordination committee — see
  [[senegal--gov-policy]].) Source: [[2025-10-03-techafricanews-national-digital-council]].

## Sector platforms

- **ARTP's eight platforms (Jan 2026).** The regulator **[[artp-senegal]]** deployed **eight digital
  regulatory platforms** — **SUNUCARTO** (national digital-infrastructure map), RESEAU (QoE), CATALOGUE,
  DMARKET (tariffs), NUMFLEX (number portability), CLAIMS, FSVA, VCN — a "digital sovereignty"-framed
  modernisation. See [[gov.standards]]. Source: [[2026-01-28-techafricanews-artp-digital-tools]].
- **NINEA business registry (Feb 2025).** A digital platform for the **NINEA** (National Identification
  Number for Enterprises and Associations) to streamline business/association registration. See
  [[dpi.registry]]. Source: [[2025-02-04-ninea-business-registration-platform]].

## Regional data governance

- **Smart Africa Francophone workshop (May 2026).** Senegal hosted a **[[smart-africa]]**/UNESCO
  regional data-governance workshop (SADA/WARDIP framework; Burkina Faso, Côte d'Ivoire, Togo, Mali,
  Guinea, Guinea-Bissau) aligned to the **AU Data Policy Framework** and Smart Africa's **Pan-African
  Data Governance Blueprint**. See [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.exchange]]. Source:
  [[2026-05-26-techafricanews-smart-africa-data-governance]].
- **Bilateral partnerships.** An **OPTIC–Mauritania** services-federation MoU (Jan 2025, during PM Sonko's
  visit) and a **UNDP MoU** (Apr 2026) on digital governance, inclusion and AI extend the govtech agenda
  regionally and with the UN. Sources: [[2025-01-16-senegal-mauritania-digital-mou]],
  [[2026-04-16-techafricanews-undp-senegal-mou]].
