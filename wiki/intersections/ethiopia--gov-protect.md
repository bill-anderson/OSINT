# Ethiopia × Data Protection

Ethiopia enacted a modern data-protection law **just as** it accelerated a mass identity-and-banking data-harmonisation drive — and the law is not yet operational.

- **The law.** The **[[ethiopia-pdpp|Personal Data Protection Proclamation No. 1321/2024]]** (approved 4 Apr, gazetted 24 Jul 2024) replaced piecemeal regulation with a comprehensive regime — broad definition of personal data, extraterritorial reach, controller/processor duties — designating the **[[ethiopian-communications-authority|Ethiopian Communications Authority (ECA)]]** as regulator.
- **The enforcement gap.** A year on (Nov 2025), the **secondary directives remain unissued**, public awareness is low, and the ECA has yet to enforce.
- **The tension.** Meanwhile the **[[national-bank-of-ethiopia|NBE]]** mandated harmonising **all bank accounts with the [[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]] digital ID**, and Fayda authenticates ~4m/day across 150+ agencies — large-scale processing running **ahead of** an operational data-protection regime.
