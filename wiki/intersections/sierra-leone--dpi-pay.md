# Sierra Leone × Digital Payments & Fintech

Financial inclusion has **nearly doubled — 18% (2018) → ~40% (2025)** — on the back of the
**[[sierra-leone-instant-payment-switch|Instant Payment Switch]]** (**1m+ transactions**) and the
**[[bank-of-sierra-leone]]** Regulatory Sandbox, which is now incubating a wave of card, wallet and
AI-payment launches aimed at the cash-to-digital transition.

## The enabling layer

The **[[bank-of-sierra-leone]]** has driven inclusion since 2008 via the **National Financial
Inclusion Strategy**, the **National Switch** (the interoperable IPS) and the **Regulatory Sandbox** —
the common platform beneath nearly every fintech launch below ("every mobile phone becomes a bank
branch"). See [[gov.standards]].

## Wallets, cards and the cashless push (2025-2026)

- **Vult (Apr 2025).** **Velmie × Metro Cable** launched **Vult** — a multi-currency **wallet + Visa
  card + Vult Pay** POS/merchant stack (bank/mobile-money/card top-ups, e2e encryption) — pairing
  fintech software with Metro Cable's fibre. Source: [[2025-04-22-velmie-metrocable-vult-digital-wallet]].
- **MikashBoks × Ecobank (Nov 2025).** A homegrown fintech's **"Interest Plus"** savings product (**6%
  annual interest** via app/USSD, deposits held in an Ecobank master account) — 80% UK-investor funded,
  aligned to the National Financial Inclusion Strategy; UNDP formalising female-led informal businesses.
  Source: [[2025-11-06-mikashboks-ecobank-financial-inclusion]].
- **Orange Money Mastercard (Mar 2026).** A **virtual Mastercard** linked to Orange Money wallets
  (with **Zenith Bank** and Mastercard, via the Maxit app) for online/international/cross-border
  payments, aimed at SMEs and youth. Source: [[2026-03-31-orange-money-mastercard-zenith]].
- **Afrimoney Prepaid Visa (May 2026).** **[[africell]]**'s Afrimoney wallet + **Ecobank/Visa** prepaid
  card (dial `1615*4#`, no bank account needed) — Chief Minister Sengeh's **"techquity"** framing: kekeh
  riders building credit via "Trust Fuel," retirees receiving **NACSA** support, remote students. Source:
  [[2026-05-27-afrimoney-prepaid-visa-card]].
- **Open Hub — Flot + AI chatbot (Apr 2026).** Fintech Open Hub launched **Flot Business** and an
  **AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot ("Flot")** — send money, airtime, bill pay and shopping in one
  interface, **QR** instant settlement, a no-code store builder, and multi-channel (Visa/Mastercard/
  mobile-money) acceptance. See [[tech.ai]]. Source: [[2026-04-24-open-hub-flot-platform]].

## Reads

- The pattern is consistent: **sandbox-incubated, interoperable-rail, card-bridged** products layering
  global card networks (Visa/Mastercard) onto mobile-money wallets to reach the unbanked — inclusion
  gains that still ride on foreign card rails and, increasingly, foreign-funded fintechs.
