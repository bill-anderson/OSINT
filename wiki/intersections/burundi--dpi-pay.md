# Burundi × Digital Payments

Burundi joined the continent's live instant-payment systems in 2026 — but on a base where **cash still
dominates** and most mobile wallets are dormant.

## What we know

- **[[burundipay]] (BRB, launched 23 Apr 2026).** Makes **banks, microfinance and mobile money
  interoperable** (**ISO 20022**, **USSD**, **~1,000 TPS**) — Africa's **22nd** live IPS. See
  [[gov.standards]]. Source: [[2026-04-28-burundipay-real-time-payments-platform-wearetech]].
- **Cash still dominates.** Deposits and withdrawals were **~45% of value (Q2 2025)**, and only
  **~1.6m of 7.6m mobile wallets were active**. See [[include.access]]. Source:
  [[2025-09-29-burundi-digital-payments-growth-cash-dominates]].
