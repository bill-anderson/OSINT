# Egypt × Digital Identity

Egypt is layering **multiple biometric digital-identity channels** on top of its long-standing national ID card — split across the central bank and the interior ministry.

- **Haweya (CBE, Nov 2025).** [[haweya]] — a biometric (face + fingerprint) **digital financial ID / eKYC** app for remote bank-account opening (37 banks), SIM purchase and services; the core of Egypt's national digital financial identity, driving financial inclusion. See [[central-bank-of-egypt]], [[dpi.pay]].
- **MOIEG-PASS (Interior, Feb 2026).** [[moieg-pass]] — a biometric (facial-recognition) app for accessing **government online services**, authenticating by scanning the national ID card.
- **Civil-registry kiosks (2024).** Automated mall-based machines issue birth/death/marriage/divorce certificates and national-ID forms via **fingerprint or face** verification.
- **Digital ID card (planned).** Under "Digital Egypt 2026" ([[digital-egypt]]), a **digital identity card for remote verification** is in development toward full government automation.

Governed by the **[[egypt-pdpl|PDPL]]** regime (biometric processing, cross-border-transfer licensing). See [[EGY]], [[dpi.id]], [[gov.protect]], [[egypt--gov-protect]].
