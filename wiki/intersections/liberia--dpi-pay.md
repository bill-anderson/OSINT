# Liberia × Digital Payments

Liberia stood up an inclusive instant-payment system in **73 business days** — one of the fastest IPS
deployments recorded on the continent — breaking the mobile-money silos that had split the market.

## What we know

- **Pay Na-Na (16 Dec 2025).** The **[[pay-na-na]]** Inclusive Instant Payment System, launched by the
  **[[central-bank-of-liberia|CBL]]** on **[[mojaloop]]**, makes money **interoperable across MTN and
  Orange mobile money** — deployed in **73 business days**, cost **capped <1%**. See [[gov.standards]].
  Source: [[2025-12-17-cbl-launches-iips-mobile-money-silos-fpa]].
- **Scope and roadmap.** The pilot covers **P2P + G2P**, with **P2G** and a **National Electronic
  Payment Switch** to follow. Source: [[2026-07-13 Liberia Expands Digital Finance]].
- **The base.** **52% account ownership (2024)**, **>11m wallets**. See [[include.access]].
