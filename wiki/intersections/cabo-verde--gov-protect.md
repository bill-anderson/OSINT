# Cabo Verde × Data Protection

Cabo Verde has one of the region's older data-protection regimes, now paired with new operational cyber-defence.

- **Law 133/V/2001.** [[data-protection-law-cabo-verde]] — a GDPR-mirroring framework (data-subject
  rights, special categories, international transfers, DPIAs; extraterritorial reach) — establishes
  the independent **[[cnpd-cabo-verde|CNPD]]** with fining powers (CVE 50,000–3,000,000) and
  criminal penalties up to two years' imprisonment.
- **SOC + CSIRT (Oct 2025).** A national **Security Operations Center** and **Computer Security
  Incident Response Team** within the State's private technological network (RTPE), for real-time
  monitoring and national cyber alert-and-response. Benchmarks: ITU GCI 2024 Tier 4 (17.36/20
  regulatory); UN EGDI 2024 rank 111.

## Cybercrime, an industry, and an election-night incident (2025–26)

- **The threat surface.** The PJ's Cybercrime Brigade warned of impersonation/loan-scam and **mule-account**
  fraud (SISP codes), mapped an online-fraud landscape (public-figure impersonation), and flagged rising
  **online sextortion** via fake profiles — with a digital-literacy through-line. Sources:
  [[2025-02-07-pj-alerta-novos-esquemas-burla-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2025-02-16-burlas-online-navegando-sitio-embustes-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-05-27-pj-extorsao-sexual-online-vitimas-expressodasilhas]].
- **A cyber industry inside the hub.** Portuguese firm **[[visionware]]** — first in both TechParks,
  recruiting via NOSi Akademia — casts **cybersecurity as a precondition** for the "hub digital" strategy
  ("attacking in the US or in CV is the same"; AI as an emerging threat). Sources:
  [[2025-05-31-bruno-castro-visionware-ciberseguranca-techpark-expressodasilhas]],
  [[2026-06-09-ciberseguranca-essencial-hub-digital-visionware-expressodasilhas]].
- **The election-night incident (2026).** After the **2026 legislative elections**, the **NOSi president
  apologised over an alleged cyber-attack on the results-broadcast**; NOSi then said it had **not published
  conclusions on the anomalies**, before a **preliminary report pointed to causes "of a technical order."**
  A rare test of the state's own digital infrastructure at a moment of maximum stakes. Sources:
  [[2026-05-18-nosi-alegado-ciberataque-divulgacao-resultados-legislativas-anacao]],
  [[2026-05-25-nosi-ainda-nao-divulgou-conclusoes-anomalias-noite-eleitoral-anacao]],
  [[2026-05-27-nosi-relatorio-preliminar-causas-de-ordem-tecnica-anacao]].
- **The SOC/CSIRT, more detail.** The national SOC + CSIRT was built by **NOSi with World Bank support**
  within the state RTPE network. Source: [[2025-10-30-governo-lanca-soc-csirt-rtpe-nosi-expressodasilhas]].
