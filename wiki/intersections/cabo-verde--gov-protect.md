# Cabo Verde × Data Protection

Cabo Verde has one of the region's older data-protection regimes, now paired with new operational cyber-defence.

- **Law 133/V/2001.** [[data-protection-law-cabo-verde]] — a GDPR-mirroring framework (data-subject
  rights, special categories, international transfers, DPIAs; extraterritorial reach) — establishes
  the independent **[[cnpd-cabo-verde|CNPD]]** with fining powers (CVE 50,000–3,000,000) and
  criminal penalties up to two years' imprisonment.
- **SOC + CSIRT (Oct 2025).** A national **Security Operations Center** and **Computer Security
  Incident Response Team** within the State's private technological network (RTPE), for real-time
  monitoring and national cyber alert-and-response. Benchmarks: ITU GCI 2024 Tier 4 (17.36/20
  regulatory); UN EGDI 2024 rank 111.
