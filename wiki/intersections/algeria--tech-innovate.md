# Algeria × Innovation ecosystem, startups & digital skills

Alongside the state-led infrastructure push, Algeria is building a **startup and skills layer**, still
heavily state-shaped.

## Startups & ecosystem

- **Incentives.** An **IPO-fee waiver for startups until 2028** (2026-02); the **[[african-startup-fund-algeria|Algerian/African Startup Fund]]** (whose first exit was VOLZ, 2025-12).
- **Companies.** Super-apps **[[yassir]]** (Uno-hypermarket acquisition) and fintech **[[vaulfi]]** (stablecoin);
  **Djezzy's sovereign AI cloud [[aventurecloudz]]**.
- **New sectors.** A structured **video-game / e-sports industry** plan ("Fiber Game", 2026-02); **[[cerist]]**
  incubator (100 startups/yr from 2027).
- **Research governance.** A **High Council of Scientists** and an Archaeology Agency created under the
  Presidency (2026-06) — steering the research base the AI strategy leans on.

## Digital skills

- **"7.77" National Digital Empowerment programme** ([[algeria-digital-empowerment-7-77]]) — **64,508
  enrolled** across all 58 wilayas (2026-05); **AI the top training choice (30%)**. A national **AI
  vocational-training programme** (12-week, incubator, 2026-04) and a first **virtual-education AI innovation
  centre** (2026-06) complement it.
