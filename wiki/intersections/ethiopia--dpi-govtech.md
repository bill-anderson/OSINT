# Ethiopia × GovTech / e-Government

Ethiopia is building a **homegrown, Fayda-anchored** GovTech stack, framed as digital sovereignty.

- **One-stop delivery.** **[[mesob-platform|MESOB]]** unifies **41 services across 12 federal agencies** behind the **[[fayda-ethiopia-national-id|Fayda]]** ID, cutting service time 60% — an AI-built ([[ethiopian-ai-institute]]) flagship under **[[digital-ethiopia-2030]]**.
- **Interoperability.** The **Digital Government Strategy & Enterprise Architecture 2024–2029** and the **Ethiopian eGovernment Interoperability Framework (EeGIF)** (MiNT / DT Global, GIF-inspired) aim to bring ministries and regions under one domain, digital-by-default. **[[insa-ethiopia|INSA]]** provides the cybersecurity/cloud foundations.
- **Frontier service delivery.** Africa's first **unmanned "Smart Police Station"** (AI kiosks, 24/7) illustrates the citizen-facing automation ambition.
- **Revenue GovTech.** Fayda–tax integration (since Dec 2023) and e-filing adoption studies target a broader, cleaner tax base.
