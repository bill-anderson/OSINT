# Gabon × GovTech / e-Government

Gabon runs its e-government drive through the World-Bank-financed **[[digital-gabon-project|Gabon Digital /
eGabon]]** programme, delivered technically by **[[aninf-gabon|ANINF]]** — with a fresh legal basis and a
single-window ambition.

- **Legal framework (2025-10).** An **ordinance signed by President Oligui Nguema (JO 7 Sept 2025)** gives
  e-government its **first comprehensive legal basis** — framed by Minister Mark-Alexandre Doumba as a major
  reform for service access, transparency and digital sovereignty.
- **Single window.** **[[gabon-connect|Gabon Connect]]** (IKA Technology) is the planned unified portal
  centralising public services (presented to the President, Feb 2026).
- **Programme governance.** The **5th Steering Committee (Copil)** of eGabon/Gabon Digital (Feb 2026, Min.
  Doumba) reviewed progress; a **World Bank mission** (Jun 2026) reviewed the **e-Gabon closure** and Gabon
  Digital execution — an implementation-status check.
- **Subnational & sectoral reach.** ANINF opened its **first provincial office (Franceville, Haut-Ogooué)** —
  its first regional presence since 2011; the **eGabon-SIS** health MIS received equipment to digitise health
  facilities. Gabon is framed by the state daily as a **sub-regional digital reference**.
