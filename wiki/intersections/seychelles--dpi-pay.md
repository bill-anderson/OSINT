# Seychelles × Digital Payments & Fintech

Seychelles is **retiring the cheque outright** — a state-driven mandate creating demand that new rails
are rushing to fill: India's **UPI**, a home-grown B2B fintech (**Fusepay**), and multi-currency card
products, on top of the CBS's **[[seft-seychelles|SEFT]]** transfers.

## The cheque endgame

- **CBS phase-out (2024–2026).** The **[[central-bank-of-seychelles]]** set a four-step schedule to stop
  banks issuing/accepting cheques (individuals from 2025, non-residents through mid-2026) — ~600,000
  cheques in 2023 vs 10m+ card/SEFT transactions (see [[SYC]] hub).
- **SRC completes it (2026–2028).** The **[[seychelles-revenue-commission|SRC]]** introduced **cheque
  thresholds** for taxes/duties/levies (eff. **13 Jul 2026**) as part of a plan to **discontinue cheques
  entirely by 31 Dec 2028** — pushing taxpayers to digital. Source: [[2026-07-09-nation-src-cheque-phaseout-2028]].

## New rails

- **UPI (India, 2026).** During PM Modi's **29 Jun 2026** visit, **NPCI International** and the CBS signed
  an MoU (**[[seychelles-upi-mou]]**) to roll out India's **Unified Payments Interface** by **end-2026** —
  instant QR/mobile bank-account payments, with regulatory prep (standards, cybersecurity,
  interoperability) and merchant training. Aimed at the **India-source tourism** market and financial
  inclusion; Seychelles would be among the **first small island economies in Africa** to deploy UPI. See
  [[geopol.india]], [[finance.mou]]. Source: [[2026-06-30-africabusinessinsight-seychelles-upi]]
  *(secondary — source the CBS/NPCI primary)*.
- **Fusepay — home-grown B2B fintech (2025).** **[[fusepay]]**, a **Seychelles-founded** fintech, launched
  in Victoria (first live transaction **19 Nov 2025**) with **virtual business accounts**, structured
  AP/AR, instant network transfers and **post-dated digital payments** as the modern cheque alternative —
  after a **$350k pre-seed** (Hustle Fund, Everywhere Ventures et al.), planning expansion to adjacent
  under-digitised markets. See [[tech.innovate]], [[finance.new]]. Sources:
  [[2025-11-21-techafricanews-fusepay-cheques]], [[2025-11-27-dabafinance-fusepay-preseed]].
- **Absa multi-currency virtual card (2026).** Absa Bank Seychelles launched a **5-currency in-app prepaid
  card** (SCR/USD/EUR/GBP/ZAR, up to 25 cards; live FX; prepaid design caps breach exposure), building on
  **Absa Pay** (Dec 2025) — Absa's **first island market**. Source: [[2026-06-24-seychellestimes-absa-multicurrency-card]].

## Reads

- The sequencing is unusual and effective: **mandate the death of cheques first**, then let a mix of
  state (SEFT, UPI) and private (Fusepay, Absa) rails compete to absorb the displaced volume. The open
  question the hub flags — **financial exclusion of cheque-reliant groups** — is the risk of moving faster
  than the least-digital users. That a fintech (Fusepay) was *founded in* Seychelles rather than imported
  is a notable capability signal for a micro-state.
