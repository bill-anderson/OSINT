# Mozambique × Connectivity

Mozambique's connectivity agenda pairs a **rural-divide push** (FSAU-funded sites, satellite) with a
**5G entry** and a **"Southern Africa digital hub"** ambition — against a live **internet-restriction
controversy** that tests digital rights.

## What we know

- **Rural connectivity — FSAU (2025).** [[digital.rural]] [[include.divides]] The **[[incm-mozambique|INCM]]**
  tendered a **rural-connectivity project (30 sites)** and awarded **[[vodacom]]** an expanded build of
  **60 new mobile stations across 9 provinces** — framing connectivity as "a right accessible to every
  citizen." Sources: [[2025-08-26-rural-connectivity-project-seeks-bids-techafricanews]],
  [[2025-10-20-60-new-mobile-stations-rural-connectivity-techafricanews]].
- **Starlink in flood response (2026-02).** [[data.satellite]] SpaceX donated **Starlink** terminals that
  INCM deployed at accommodation/coordination centres for flood-hit areas — emergency LEO connectivity.
  Source: [[2026-02-19-starlink-vodacom-flood-response-clubofmozambique]].
- **Internet-restriction controversy (2026).** [[gov.protect]] [[sovereignty]] The **Anamola** party
  (Venâncio Mondlane) asked the Constitutional Council to void a **decree allowing telecoms to be blocked**
  as unconstitutional (post-election tensions); Communications Minister **Muchanga denied** the new telecom
  regulations would restrict internet use. Sources:
  [[2026-02-25-telecoms-block-unconstitutional-anamola-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-03-13-minister-denies-attempt-restrict-internet-clubofmozambique]].
- **5G entry — spectrum to licences (2026).** [[infra.connect]] INCM **opened 5G spectrum bids**
  (700MHz / 2.6 / 3.5 GHz; administrative consignment) with **all three operators** interested (Vodacom
  ~11.8m, Movitel ~4m, Tmcel ~1.6m subscribers end-2025), then **awarded 5G/4G licences** to the three
  (~710k subsidised phones, schools). Sources:
  [[2026-04-20-incm-opens-5g-spectrum-bids-techafricanews]], [[2026-04-21-opens-bids-for-5g-spectrum-connectingafrica]],
  [[2026-06-26-clears-path-for-5g-services-itweb]].
- **5G roadmap + regional-hub vision (2026-06).** [[gov.policy]] [[infra.store]] President **Chapo** pledged
  **5G in provincial/district capitals, tourist zones and SEZs by 2027**; INCM set a vision for Mozambique
  as **Southern Africa's digital hub** (5th National Communications Conference). Sources:
  [[2026-06-22-chapo-pledges-5g-district-capitals-2027-clubofmozambique]],
  [[2026-06-22-5g-rollout-southern-africa-digital-hub-techafricanews]].
- **SpaceX strategic partnership talks (2026-07).** [[geopol.usa]] [[finance.mou]] The Ministry of Economy
  explored a **strategic partnership with SpaceX** to expand connectivity and digital infrastructure —
  a US-investment/geopolitics angle. Source: [[2026-07-06-explores-partnership-with-spacex-techafricanews]].
- **Vodacom CEO–President meeting (reported 2026-07-23; meeting date not stated).** [[tech.ai]]
  **[[vodacom]]** Group CEO **Shameel Joosub** met President **Chapo** in Maputo on expanding
  digital infrastructure — connectivity, **fibre expansion**, **AI adoption**, digital inclusion.
  No amount, agreement or timeline announced; a standing-investor engagement following the 5G
  licence award and the Matola data centre. Source:
  [[2026-07-23-vodacom-mozambique-president-meeting]].
