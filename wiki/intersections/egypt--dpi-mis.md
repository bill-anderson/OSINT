# Egypt × Sectoral MIS (health & social protection)

Egypt is wiring **registry + identity + payments** into its health and social-protection systems.

- **Health information system.** The MoHP launched **[[dhis2|DHIS2]]** (Jan 2026, with UNICEF and HISP Oslo) for **national ownership of health data**, under the National Digital Health Strategic Priorities 2025–2029; early use spanned refugee/evacuee care, school health and TB follow-up.
- **Social protection.** **[[takaful-and-karama|Takaful and Karama]]** runs on a **unified social registry (~12m households)**, Meeza-card payments and a national ID-based monitoring system reaching ~17m people — a DPI-for-social-protection reference case, and a candidate base for **automatic enrolment into Universal Health Insurance** (WB policy note).
- **Social insurance.** A new **NSIA digital platform** (40 → 95 e-services) with e-signatures, time-stamping and electronic payment.
