# Nigeria × Registries (population, land, address)

Behind the digital-ID system ([[nigeria--dpi-id]]) sits the wider registry layer — population,
land and address — being digitised and biometrically anchored, much of it keyed to **NIN/BVN**.

## Population — the first biometric census

- **Biometric census (2025).** Nigeria moved toward its **first biometric population census** (the
  last was **2006**), with the **National Population Commission (NPC)** briefing President Tinubu on
  readiness (digital maps, tablets, trained staff). A committee will review the **~₦980bn** budget;
  fingerprint/facial/iris capture is meant to end the historic figure-inflation disputes, and census
  data would feed the **[[nimc]]** national database. Source: [[2025-03-08-nigerias-first-biometric-census]].
- **Mandatory registration (Mar 2025).** A bill to **repeal and re-enact the NIMC Act** — making
  national digital-ID registration **compulsory for every citizen** — passed Senate second reading.
  See [[gov.legislate]], [[nigeria--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2025-03-31-mandatory-citizen-registration-regulation]].
- **NIMC platform migration (Jul 2025).** Operators briefly suspended SIM/NIN services (new
  registrations, swaps, portability) during NIMC's migration to a **self-built, self-managed identity
  platform** (BlueSalt handling operator migration). NIMC has registered **120m+** Nigerians, targeting
  100m more toward a **95% coverage** goal. Source: [[2025-07-03-sim-registration-halted-nimc-migration]].

## Civil registration and vital statistics (CRVS)

- **e-CRVS goes nationwide — VitalReg (2026-07-22; operational from 2026-07-01).** The
  **[[npc-nigeria|National Population Commission]]** commenced **nationwide** digital birth and death
  registration on its **[[e-crvs-nigeria|E-CRVS]]** system via the **VitalReg** platform (announced at a
  Lokoja briefing, federal commissioner Afolabi Yori) — the nationwide rollout of the platform Tinubu
  launched in **Nov 2025**. Coverage, dated: **birth ~57%, death <20% (2026-07)** against ~**5m births/yr**;
  **4,011 registration centres** across the **774 LGAs** → target ~**8,000**; run under a **PPP with
  [[barnks-forte-technologies]] Ltd**. Newborn registration **requires ≥1 parent to hold a valid NIN**
  ([[nimc]]) — the CRVS-to-foundational-ID link, framed to **SDG 16.9**. See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.mis]],
  [[nigeria--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2026-07-22-npc-nationwide-digital-birth-death-registration]].

## Land and address registries

- **"$300bn in dead capital."** The FG launched a **National Land Registration & Documentation
  Programme (NLRDP)** to digitise land nationwide: **>90% of land is unregistered**, locking an
  estimated **$300bn** as dead capital (Min. Housing Ahmed Dangiwa). Source:
  [[2025-03-20-fg-land-information-system-digitisation]].
- **Kaduna KADGIS (Feb 2025).** Kaduna's Geographic Information Service deployed a **Compensation &
  Valuation System** building a **NIN/BVN-keyed biometric** land database to end compensation fraud.
  Source: [[2025-02-04-kaduna-land-administration-software]].
- **Lagos Identity Project — digital addressing (Jun 2025).** Lagos launched a **digital house-
  numbering** system: **QR-coded address plates** (LGA colour codes) linking to verified property
  data, piloted in Eti-Osa, scaling to all 57 LGs/LCDAs (built with Interspatial on two years of
  aerial mapping) — to curb rental fraud, aid utilities/postal services and improve tax compliance.
  See [[digital.localgov]], [[nigeria--digital-localgov]]. Source:
  [[2025-06-24-lagos-digital-house-numbering-identity-project]].
