# Tanzania × Addressing & Registries

**[[napa-tanzania]]** — the National Addressing and Postcode System — has been registering physical
addresses **since 2010**, to a **[[upu-s42]]** certification that the **[[papu]]** Secretary General
called a benchmark for the continent (2026-02). It is the least-noticed piece of Tanzania's stack and
arguably the most structurally load-bearing: addressing is what lets postal, financial, social and
government delivery reach a household at all. The friction is the same last-mile problem that dogs
[[tanzania--dpi-id]] — **uncollected placards**, and residents unaware of their own house numbers.

## Two dated metrics that must not be charted together

**2024-07-10 — a population rate.** NaPA assistant coordinator **Arnold Mkude**, at the 48th Dar es
Salaam International Trade Fair, said NaPA had **reached 85% of Tanzanians** since the 2022 census,
targeting complete registration **"by the next fiscal year"**. Friction named: **uncollected placards**,
people unaware of their house numbers and street names. Source:
[[2024-07-10 Tanzania ramps up national physical address registration]]. [[digital.localgov]] ·
[[include.access]]

**2026-02-09 — an absolute count.** **Over 12.9 million residential addresses** registered (Deputy PS
**Nicholas Mkapa**, [[micit-tanzania]]). Source:
[[2026-02-09 Tanzania registers 12 million housing addresses to boost digital governance]].
*(Headline says 12 million; body says 12.9m — body preferred.)*

***Metric caution:*** the **85% (2024-07) is a population rate**; the **12.9m (2026-02) is an absolute
count of addresses**. **Neither is derivable from the other. Do not chart them together and do not infer
a trend between them** — both are recorded dated, and that is all the evidence supports.

## S42 certification as a continental benchmark (2026-02-09)

The programme **began in 2010** and has **29 systems interconnected**, including
**[[tanzania-posts-corporation]]**.

**[[tcra]]** DG **Dr Jabiri Bakari** framed addressing as turning **postal services into a delivery
channel** for financial, social and government services, **particularly benefiting rural communities**.
[[digital.rural]]

**[[papu]]** SG **Dr Sifundo Chief Moyo** made the regional case: without harmonised addressing
**e-commerce cannot scale and AfCFTA cannot reach its full potential** — *"Tanzania's achievements in
addressing, including its [[upu]] **S42 certification**, set a benchmark for the continent."* Source:
[[2026-02-09 Tanzania registers 12 million housing addresses to boost digital governance]].
[[gov.standards]] · [[gov.regional]]

## Civil registration

Civil registration and vital statistics sit with **[[rita-tanzania]]**, and the infant-registration
thread — NIDA's under-five pilot, the mother's-ID linkage workaround and the pending Bill — is held on
[[tanzania--dpi-id]] rather than here. Reference: **Snapshot of CRVS Systems of Tanzania Mainland**
([[idrc-crvs-centre-of-excellence]] with [[uneca]]; 2019) — cited, not absorbed.
