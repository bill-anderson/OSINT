# Sudan × Digital Identity

Sudan launched a unified national digital ID **during** an active conflict — a foundational credential
built on a population base the state cannot currently count.

## SUDAPASS

- **Launched live 2026-05-21.** **[[sudapass]]**, the unified digital ID, activates via
  **passport/national-number + face biometrics** and enables **e-services, e-transactions and digital
  signatures**. It sits under the **Baladna programme**, implemented via the **cybersecurity authority**,
  the **e-certification authority** and the **civil registry**. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[infra.cybersec]]. Source: [[2026-05-21 Sudan launches national digital identity SUDAPASS (SUNA)]].
- **The context is conflict.** **>14m displaced**; the **last census was 2008**. See
  [[include.access]].
- **Earlier framing (2026-02) called it imminent** and warned of **exclusion and surveillance risk**,
  citing Kenya. See [[gov.protect]]. Sources:
  [[2026-01-29 How to build trust alongside Sudan's digital identity system]],
  [[2026-02-02 Sudan's planned national digital ID could strengthen recovery push - Experts]].
