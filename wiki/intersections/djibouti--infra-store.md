# Djibouti × Data Storage

Djibouti is converting its **submarine-cable concentration** (among the continent's highest, with ~10 systems landing) into a carrier-neutral **data-centre and data-residency** market — the physical basis of its "digital switchboard" role.

- **Wingu TO7 (inaugurated 25 Nov 2024).** [[wingu-africa]]'s Tier-3 carrier-neutral data centre +
  cable landing station + innovation centre — the first integrated facility of its kind there,
  complementing Wingu's decade-old Djibouti data centre.
- **PAIX JIB1 (JV announced May 2024).** [[paix-data-centres]] and the sovereign
  **[[fonds-souverain-djibouti|Fonds Souverain de Djibouti]]** are building a ~50,000 sq ft, up to
  5 MW carrier-neutral facility (~$50m), first phase expected 2026 — pitched as an East-African
  interconnection hub.
- **Baseline.** The DJI hub records **three data centres and an IXP**; Wingu's regional
  cloud-exchange rollout also touches Djibouti.
