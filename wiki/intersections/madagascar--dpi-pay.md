# Madagascar × Digital Payments

Madagascar is a **cash-heavy, mobile-money-led** market — **[[mvola|MVola]]** dominant, banking thin — now
layering a **CBDC (e-Ariary)** and a **mobile-money-to-digital-banking** evolution on top, with
inclusion the recurring goal.

## What we know

- **The landscape — MVola-led, cash-heavy (2025-07).** [[include.access]] A **[[visa|Visa]]** study
  ("Understanding Madagascar's payment landscape") found **MVola at 100% awareness / 73% usage**, formal
  banking reaching only **17%** (18% with a bank account), **81% of transactions in cash**, and just
  **3% of merchants** accepting cards — recommending bank–operator partnerships, interoperability and the
  future **e-Ariary**. Source: [[2025-07-09-paysage-paiements-mobile-money-visa-newsmada]].
- **e-Ariary CBDC — vendor selected (2025-10).** [[dpi.pay]] **[[ecurrency-mint]]**'s **DSC3** platform (with
  PayLogic SA) was selected for the **[[e-ariary|eAriary]]** pilot — a post-quantum digital bearer
  instrument for **BFM** (central bank as sole issuer; private distribution). Source:
  [[2025-10-29-ecurrency-dsc3-eariary-cbdc-partner-prnewswire]].
- **Mobile money → digital bank (2025-11).** [[finance.new]] **[[axian-telecom|AXIAN]]** rebranded its
  fintech cluster to **AXIAN Digibank & Fintech** — evolving **MVola/Mixx** (Madagascar, Comoros,
  Tanzania, Togo, Senegal) from mobile money into a **full digital bank** (credit, savings, insurance,
  cross-border; AI underwriting), serving ~**500,000 merchants/month**, with a board including a former
  Madagascar central-bank governor. Source: [[2025-11-26-axian-digibank-fintech-rebrand-techcabal]].
- **Mobile-money × banking integration (2026-07).** [[finance.mou]] **[[airtel-madagascar|Airtel Money]]**
  integrated **[[baobab-bank|Baobab Bank]]** into its **Mbanking** service (bidirectional wallet↔account,
  USSD/My Baobab) — bridging mobile money and formal banking. Source:
  [[2026-07-17-airtel-money-baobab-bank-partnership-techafricanews]].
