# Somalia × Data Centres & Onshoring

Private operators hold the working hosting base; the state's own National Data Center is **declared
policy, not yet fact**.

- **Private-operator capacity (2024-12).** **[[hormuud-telecom]]** runs **11 data centres**
  (~10 MWh) — the cash-light economy's private hosting base. See [[infra.energy]].
- **National Data Center — under construction, no launch recorded.** The FGS is building a
  **National Data Center** in Mogadishu (MoCT; under construction from 2023, **"nearing completion"
  as of 2025-05-06**, no launch recorded) as the central repository for government-institution data.
  MoCT's rationale doubles as an admission of the status quo: it will "eliminate dependencies of
  national data on international data storage solutions, **that is stored outside the country**"
  (**2024-04-28**, restated 2025-05-06). Sources: [[2024-04-28-moct-national-data-center-tour]],
  [[2025-05-06-moct-national-data-center-nearing-completion]].
