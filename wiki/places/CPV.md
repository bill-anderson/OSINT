# Cabo Verde (CPV)

Atlantic island micro-state (10 islands, ~500,000 people, ~600 km off West Africa) that has made
**e-government a comparative advantage** — and, unusually, an **export**. Its digital-government
agency **[[nosi|NOSi]]** hosts GovTech for other African states, and the government is leveraging
Cabo Verde's subsea-cable landings, diaspora and stability to position the country as a **regional
digital and "tech islands" hub** (**[[techpark-cv|TechPark CV]]**), backed by the World Bank
**[[digital-cabo-verde-project|Digital Cabo Verde Project]]** and the [[african-development-bank|AfDB]].

## Recent developments

- **2026 (early)** — **Gov.CV unified government portal launched.** PM Ulisses Correia e Silva
  launched **[[gov-cv|Gov.CV]]** (web + app) as the single gateway to identity, tax, education,
  transport and social services — alongside a parallel CAR platform. See [[dpi.govtech]],
  [[cabo-verde--dpi-govtech]], [[CAF]]. Source:
  [[2026-03-03 Cabo Verde and CAR launch digital government platforms (Gov.CV)]].
- **2025-12-18** — **National Health System (SNS) digitalisation.** Health Minister Jorge
  Figueiredo announced a **unique patient identification code**, **digital medical records** and
  **electronic prescriptions**, with 24 telemedicine units handed over. See [[dpi.mis]], [[dpi.id]].
  Source: [[2025-12-18 Cabo Verde advances digitalisation of National Health System (SNS)]].
- **2025-10-29** — **National SOC and CSIRT launched.** A Security Operations Center and Computer
  Security Incident Response Team within the State's private technological network (RTPE). See
  [[infra.cybersec]], [[cabo-verde--gov-protect]]. Source:
  [[2025-10-31 Cabo Verde launches national SOC and CSIRT]].
- **2025-05-06** — **Mindelo TechPark CV campus opens ($58M).** Second **[[techpark-cv|TechPark CV]]**
  campus (São Vicente); goal to lift the digital economy from 7% to 25% of GDP. Backed by
  **[[african-development-bank|AfDB]]** (~€45.5m). See [[infra.store]], [[gov.policy]]. Sources:
  [[2025-05-16 Cabo Verde opens Mindelo TechPark CV campus ($58M)]],
  [[2025-05-14 Cabo Verde unveils TechPark CV tech-islands vision ($50m AfDB)]].
- **2025-05-02** — **World Bank Additional Financing (+$20m) for Digital Cabo Verde Project.** See
  [[digital-cabo-verde-project]]. Source:
  [[2025-05-02 WB Project Paper - Digital Cabo Verde Project Additional Financing]].

### Standing profile

- **Connectivity baseline (DataReportal, early 2025).** ~**387,000** internet users (**73.5%**
  penetration — high by regional standards); ~604,000 mobile connections (115% of population). See
  [[infra.connect]], [[include.divides]].
- **GovTech exporter.** NOSi provides data-centre hosting and e-gov systems to Equatorial Guinea,
  Mozambique, Burkina Faso and others — a rare South-to-South GovTech provider. Cabo Verde ranked
  **6th in Africa** in ICT development (ITU 2023) and **111th** on the UN 2024 EGDI (above the
  African average). See [[dpi.govtech]].
- **Data-protection maturity.** One of the region's older regimes — **[[data-protection-law-cabo-verde|Law 133/V/2001]]**,
  GDPR-mirroring, with the independent **[[cnpd-cabo-verde|CNPD]]**. See [[gov.protect]].
