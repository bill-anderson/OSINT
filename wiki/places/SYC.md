# Seychelles (SYC)

Indian Ocean island state, part of East Africa ([[XEA]]). Its digital-economy profile in this
wiki centres on its emerging role as a **continental digital-trade hub** under the
[[afcfta]] framework.

## Recent developments

- **2026-07-08** — **Seychelles to host the AfCFTA Digital Trade Corridor HQ.** The Cabinet
  approved hosting the **[[afcfta-digital-trade-corridor]]** headquarters in **Victoria**,
  making it the operational seat for cross-border digital commerce under the
  **[[afcfta-digital-trade-protocol]]** — sitting **alongside (not replacing)** the AfCFTA
  Secretariat in Accra. It builds on a precursor **[[seychelles-afcfta-mou]]** signed
  **2026-03-05** between the AfCFTA Secretariat, **[[quest-ghana]]** Ltd and the Government of
  Seychelles during a visit by Secretary-General **[[wamkele-mene]]**, and follows the AfCFTA
  Digital Trade Forum 2026 (Lagos, 2 July 2026). VP Sebastian Pillay announced the decision;
  FM Barry Faure credited. The corridor is intended to support cross-border commerce, the
  Africa Commodities Exchange, supply-chain connectivity and MSME integration. Source:
  [[2026-07-10 Seychelles to Host AfCFTA Digital Trade Corridor Headquarters]]. See
  [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.exchange]], [[finance.mou]], [[XAF]].
- **2026-01-13** *(mention)* — **Cheque tightening.** Seychelles is named (with Ghana) among
  African states **tightening cheque rules or imposing penalties** as they push digital-first
  payment agendas — context in an ITWeb Africa piece on Kenya's contrasting retention of
  cheques. Source: [[2026-01-13 The rise of cheques as Kenya's preferred payment method]]. See
  [[dpi.pay]], [[KEN]].
