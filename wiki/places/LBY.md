# Libya (LBY)

A North-African, oil-rich state whose digital transformation advances **despite a fractured polity** —
a Tripoli-based Government of National Unity and an eastern rival administration contesting control over
core institutions. The through-lines: a fast-growing **electronic-payments** system led by the
**[[central-bank-of-libya|CBL]]** (mobile banking, LYPAY ONEPAY, and a contested real-time payroll
platform); a **civil registry** ([[civil-registry-authority-libya]]) whose unique National ID Number
gates all state services but is dogged by fraud and integrity concerns; and a **data-governance /
digital-sovereignty** push by the **[[gia-libya|GIA]]** and **[[gaci-libya|GACI]]** (open data,
government reports, national root servers). Much of this reform is entangled with the east-west split.

## Recent developments

- **2026-02-24** — **Digital payments boom.** [[central-bank-of-libya|CBL]] statistics (2025 → Feb 2026):
  mobile banking **4.29m subscribers / 200.7m transactions / 313.6bn LYD**; POS 165,313 terminals; the
  **LYPAY ONEPAY** instant-transfer platform 18.3m transactions / 108.7bn LYD; 5.5m activated cards.
  Source: [[2026-02-24 Libya's digital payments exceed 313 billion dinars in record year (CBL statistics)]].
  See [[dpi.pay]], [[data.statistics]].
- **2026-02** — **GIA data-governance vision; One-Stop Shop reactivation.** The **[[gia-libya|GIA]]**
  presented "Government Data Governance Policies" at a **[[gaci-libya|GACI]]** cloud/data workshop (91
  open datasets); separately the Commercial Registry Authority moved to reactivate the **One-Stop Shop**
  for company registration. Sources:
  [[2026-02-16 Libya's GIA presents Government Data Governance vision at cloud-computing workshop]],
  [[2026-02-20 Libya to reactivate One-Stop Shop for company registration (Commercial Registry Authority)]].
  See [[dpi.exchange]], [[dpi.govtech]].
- **2025-12** — **Civil-registry breaches warning; Unified Tax System.** Parliament warned of
  "manipulation and serious violations" in the **[[civil-registry-authority-libya|Civil Registry]]**
  letting foreigners obtain Libyan national ID numbers ("a threat to national security and Libyan
  identity"); the Tax Authority launched a **Unified Tax System** and activated a Tax Police. Sources:
  [[2025-12-08 Libyan Parliament warns of breaches in Civil Registry national ID system]],
  [[2025-12-14 Libya launches Unified Tax System and activates Tax Police]]. See [[dpi.id]],
  [[gov.protect]], [[dpi.govtech]].
- **2025-10-26** — **National root servers launched (digital sovereignty).** GACI brought Libya's **root
  servers** into service (Tripoli + Benghazi), listed on root-servers.org, implementing the National
  Telecommunications Strategy 2023-2027. Source:
  [[2025-10-26 Libya launches root servers in Tripoli and Benghazi — digital sovereignty]]. See
  [[infra.connect]], [[infra.cybersec]].
- **2025-08-11** — **CBL 'Ratebk Lahzi' real-time payroll.** A real-time public-sector salary platform
  on the instant-payment network (~2m employees/pensioners), pitched as anti-fraud but resisted by the
  eastern government, which refused to share employee banking data. Source:
  [[2025-08-11 Libya's CBL launches 'Ratebk Lahzi' real-time salary system]]. See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.protect]].
- **2025-07** — **GIA: LY-GOVREP + UNDP MoU.** The GIA launched the **LY-GOVREP** government-reports
  platform (60 Cabinet entities) and signed a UNDP MoU on data systems and the Seventh National Human
  Development Report. Sources:
  [[2025-07-20 Libya's GIA launches national government-reports platform LY-GOVREP]],
  [[2025-07-02 UNDP and Libya's GIA sign MoU on data systems and human development]]. See [[data.open]],
  [[gov.policy]].
- **2025-06** — **Unified Social Registry approved; Libyan ID firm wins Niger contract.** PM Dabaiba
  approved the **[[libya-unified-social-registry|Unified Social Registry]]** draft; separately, Libyan
  biometrics firm **Al Itissan Al-Jadeed** won a Niger passport/national-ID contract (a rare identity-tech
  export, into the AES bloc). Sources:
  [[2025-06-23 Libya's PM Dabaiba approves Unified Social Registry project]],
  [[2025-06-30 Libyan firm Al Itissan Al-Jadeed wins Niger biometric passport national ID contract]]. See
  [[dpi.registry]], [[include.access]], [[NER]].
- **2025** — **Postal addressing; digital-transformation overview.** Libya Post began a national
  **digital-addressing** project (Hay Al-Andalus, Tripoli; 42,000 buildings phase 1); an industry
  overview flagged progress and gaps (rural access, skills, cybersecurity). Sources:
  [[2025-01-01 Libya launches national postal addressing project (Tripoli, Hay Al-Andalus)]],
  [[2025-05-15 The State of Digital Transformation in Libya]]. See [[dpi.registry]], [[digital.localgov]].
- **2024** *(baselines)* — **GCI 3rd tier; biometric voter verification; Intilaqa registry cleanup.**
  Libya reached the **third tier** of the ITU GCI (68.09%); HNEC piloted **biometric voter verification**
  in municipal elections; and **Project Intilaqa** matched electronic and paper Civil Registry records to
  97% completion. Sources:
  [[2024-01-01 Libya advances to third tier in ITU Global Cybersecurity Index (GCI 2024)]],
  [[2024-08-19 Libya implements biometric voter verification in municipal council elections]],
  [[2024-09-06 Libya's Project Intilaqa matches Civil Registry electronic data with paper records]]. See
  [[infra.cybersec]], [[dpi.id]].
