# Togo (TGO)

## Recent developments

- **2026-06-02** — **Private sovereign cloud at the Tier III Lomé Data Centre.**
  State-owned **Société d'Infrastructures Numériques ([[sin-togo]]**, DG Attia Byll),
  which operates the **[[lome-data-centre]]** (LDC), signed a strategic partnership with
  **ST Digital Togo** (DG Justine Adebiyi) to launch private cloud services at the
  facility. Togolese firms' data to be hosted and processed in-country to cut reliance on
  foreign infrastructure, with deliberately affordable tiers ("an individual or company
  that cannot afford half a rack") and continuing-education skills programmes; the partners
  aim to position the LDC as a leading West Africa digital hub. ST Digital is the same group
  as **[[st-digital-gabon]]** (national arms). Deal: [[sin-st-digital-lome-cloud-deal]].
  Source: [[2026-06-02 SIN, ST Digital to deploy cloud services at Lomé data center]].
  See [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]], [[gov.regional]].
- **2026-05-28** — **Togo among the 29 MOSIP MoU countries.** A Biometric Update explainer
  records Togo having issued **6 million unique numbers** under its MOSIP-based digital ID
  project — one of the platform's active African rollouts alongside Ethiopia, Uganda and
  Sierra Leone. Source: [[2026-05-28 Why decentralized digital identity looks different in every country]].
  See [[dpi.id]].
