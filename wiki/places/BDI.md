# Burundi (BDI)

One of the **world's poorest countries building digital public infrastructure almost from scratch**, largely on World Bank financing and under a dominant-party political system. A burst of 2025–26 activity — a first data-protection law, an online tax system, digitised land and social-protection registries, and a long-delayed biometric national ID — sits on a very thin base: **183rd of 193** on the UN 2024 e-government index and **127th of 133** on the 2024 innovation index. The through-line is **sequencing and trust**: institutions and rights frameworks being stood up in parallel with, and partly to enable, the ID and data drives.

## Recent developments

- **2026-01-22** — **[[obr-burundi]]** launched the **e-KORI** online tax platform (19 Jan 2026), World Bank-backed, to raise domestic revenue. Source: [[2026-01-22 Burundi launches online tax system e-KORI]].
- **2026-01-16** — National Assembly adopted Burundi's first **data-protection law** ([[burundi-data-protection-law-2026]]), creating an independent authority — explicitly to enable the biometric ID. [[burundi--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2026-01-16 Burundi adopts data protection law]].
- **2025-06-18** — **Land services digitised**: [[mediabox]]'s EDRMS for the DTFCN cadastre (BPS + PMS), World Bank-funded under [[pafen-burundi]]. Source: [[2025-06-18 Burundi's Land Services Go Digital with New Integrated Platform]].
- **2025-05-16** — **[[ceni-burundi]]** extended voter-card collection amid low turnout and documented registration coercion. [[burundi--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2025-05-16 Burundi - CENI extends voter registration card collection by one day]].
- **2025-05** — **Merankabandi** social-protection programme migrated to **[[openimis]]** (CORE-MIS), reaching **1.25m beneficiaries** across five provinces. Source: [[2026-01-01 Burundi's growing social protection system now runs on openIMIS]].
- **2025-02-18** — Burundi discussed PPP collaboration with the **[[ifc]]** for its digital agenda (after a Vietnam MoU); [[pafen-burundi]] context. Source: [[2025-02-18 Burundi sorts out collaboration terms with IFC for digital transformation project]].
- **2024-05-24** — CENI: **biometric national ID would not be ready** for the 2025 voter enrolment. [[burundi--dpi-id]]. Source: [[2024-05-24 Burundi - biometric national ID card will not be available for 2025 voter enrolment]].

## Active topics

- **Digital identity** → **[[burundi--dpi-id]]**. A repeatedly-delayed biometric national ID (pilot communes as of Jan 2026), a voter register produced amid coercion ([[ceni-burundi]]), and a DP law passed to enable the rollout.
- **Data protection** ([[gov.protect]]) — the first **[[burundi-data-protection-law-2026]]** (Jan 2026): independent authority + criminal penalties, Malabo-aligned; authority newly created and untested.
- **GovTech & tax** ([[dpi.govtech]], [[dpi.pay]]) — **e-KORI** online tax ([[obr-burundi]]); ICT coordination via **[[setic-burundi]]** under [[pafen-burundi]].
- **Registries** ([[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.mis]]) — digitised land cadastre ([[mediabox]]/DTFCN); social protection **[[merankabandi]]** on **[[openimis]]**, feeding a planned Unified Social Registry.
- **Connectivity & foundations** ([[infra.connect]], [[gov.policy]]) — the **[[pafen-burundi]]** Digital Foundations Project ($92m WB, 2022): rural broadband, digital skills, e-services; VP-launched Sept 2024.
- **Regional / payments** ([[gov.regional]], [[dpi.pay]]) — ECCAS ([[eccas]]) member (as well as EAC/[[XEA]]); remittance-policy harmonisation reference. Source: [[2025-04-01 Regional Harmonization of Remittance Policies in ECCAS - Burundi]].
