# Gabon (GAB)

## Recent developments

- **2026-07-04** — Gabon inaugurates its **first Tier III datacenter**, developed by
  **[[st-digital-gabon]]** (led by Laïka Mba), to host government and business data
  domestically and "reduce reliance on foreign digital infrastructure." President
  **Oligui Nguema** framed digital infrastructure as a strategic national asset alongside
  energy and transport. A Ministry–ST Digital **MoU** gives supported startups
  preferential hosting rates. Source:
  [[2026-07-06 Gabon Inaugurates First Tier III Datacenter to Boost Digital Sovereignty]].
  See [[infra.store]], [[tech.innovate]], [[finance.mou]].
