# Central African Republic (CAF)

A **fragile, landlocked, low-connectivity state rebuilding its digital and identity
foundations from near-collapse.** The 2013 crisis destroyed 60–80% of identity documents;
~90% of the population is offline and only ~14% has electricity. Yet since 2024 CAR has moved
on several fronts at once — a first 4G licence, three e-government platforms, biometric voter
registration, and a modern (if unenforced) data-protection law — mostly donor-driven, under
President **[[faustin-archange-touadera]]**'s PND 2024-2028 digital agenda. The through-line is
**sovereignty anxiety**: foreign vendors and foreign disinformation on one side, a state
struggling to reach its own territory on the other.

## Active topics

- **Digital identity & CRVS** → **[[caf--dpi-id]]**. Post-2013 rebuild of the CNI via
  **[[al-madina]]** (opaque contracting, Bangui-centralised, a resolved price scandal, mobile
  enrolment town-by-town); biometric voter register on **[[genkey]]** run by
  **[[ane-central-african-republic]]**; nascent **[[oif-la-francophonie]]** birth registration.
- **GovTech / e-government** → **[[caf--dpi-govtech]]**. Three platforms in a year — **e-Tax**
  ([[dgid-central-african-republic]]), **Gouvernemental Centralisé** ([[world-bank]]/EY), and
  **DUNIA** ([[mepci-central-african-republic]]) — under **[[pnd-2024-2028-central-african-republic]]**.
- **Data protection** → **[[caf--gov-protect]]**. **[[car-data-protection-law-24-001]]** (2024)
  comprehensive but the supervisory authority is unfilled; CEMAC instruments apply
  ([[data-protection-africa]]).
- **Connectivity** → **[[caf--infra-connect]]**. ~10.6% internet penetration, mobile-only,
  landlocked with no undersea cable; first 4G in 2024. Operators [[orange-centrafrique]],
  Telecel, Moov; incumbent [[socatel]]; regulator [[arcep-central-african-republic]].
- **Energy** ([[infra.energy]]) — ~14% electricity access; World Bank **PARSE** project and
  utility **[[enerca]]**; the binding constraint on connectivity/data infrastructure. Source:
  [[2022-06-03 Central African Republic - Increasing Electricity Supply and Access and Supporting the Health System]].
- **AI & data sovereignty** ([[tech.ai]], [[infra.store]]) — **no national data centre**; 2020
  France/Russia-targeting disinformation campaigns; "Centrafrique numérique 2018" largely
  unimplemented. Source:
  [[2022-10-21 La Republique centrafricaine et l'intelligence artificielle dans l'espace numerique]].
- **Regional (CEMAC / ECCAS)** ([[gov.regional]], [[dpi.pay]]) — CEMAC monetary union under
  **[[beac]]** (PAPSS accession, 2026); **[[eccas]]** as a cross-border-transfer tier. See [[XCA]].
