# Central African Republic (CAF)

Central-African CEMAC member, captured here so far only through the **regional monetary/data
bloc**. Stub — expand when a source treats CAR substantively.

## Notes

- **CEMAC / BEAC zone.** CAR is one of the six states in the Central African monetary union
  served by **[[beac]]** (with Cameroon, Chad, Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon) — the regional
  frame for cross-border payments integration and its PAPSS accession. See [[XCA]],
  [[gov.regional]], [[dpi.pay]].
