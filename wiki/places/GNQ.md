# Equatorial Guinea (GNQ)

Central-African CEMAC member, tracked here for a **cross-border subsea-cable** development and
its place in the regional monetary/data bloc.

## Recent developments

- **Nigeria–Equatorial Guinea subsea cable (2026-02).** A subsea fibre agreement between
  **[[NGA]]** and Equatorial Guinea to create a direct high-capacity link across West/Central
  Africa, with a **data centre** bundled into the deal. See
  [[nigeria-eqguinea-subsea-cable]], [[infra.connect]], [[infra.store]]. Source:
  [[2026-02-24 Nigeria, Equatorial Guinea subsea cable strengthens connectivity]].
- **CEMAC / BEAC zone.** Equatorial Guinea is one of the six states in the Central African
  monetary union served by **[[beac]]** (with Cameroon, CAR, Chad, Congo, Gabon) — the regional
  frame for cross-border payments integration. See [[XCA]], [[gov.regional]].
