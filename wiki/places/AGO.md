# Angola (AGO)

## Recent developments

- **2026-06-15** — Angola's first **sovereign national data centre** (Luanda;
  **$89m**, 6,225 m², inaugurated end-April 2026, hosted by **INFOSI**) held up as
  a model for the region — Namibia's ICT minister **[[emma-theofelus]]** cited it
  as inspiration during ANGOTIC. Source:
  [[2026-06-15 Namibia encouraged by Angola on datacentre establishment]]. See
  [[infra.store]].
- **2026-06-15** — Angolan firms signed connectivity agreements with
  **[[telecom-namibia]]**: **GGPEN** (Angosat-2 satellite trial) and **Angola
  Telecom + SATCAB** (integrating the **SARSSy** subsea system with Google's
  **Equiano** via Swakopmund; 1 Tbps dedicated capacity to Angola Telecom). Deal:
  [[telecom-namibia-angola-deals]]. See [[infra.connect]], [[gov.regional]].
  Source: [[2026-06-15 Telecom Namibia signs more satellite, subsea connectivity deals with Angola firms]].
