# São Tomé and Príncipe (STP)

One of Africa's smallest states (Gulf of Guinea; grouped under [[XWA]] in this wiki's
place authority). Enters the wiki via an early move to operationalise continental data
governance at national level. Stub — expand as sources accrue.

## Recent developments

- **2026-07-09/10** — **National adoption of the AU Data Policy Framework.** Government
  officials and data practitioners held a **Data Governance Capacity Building Workshop**
  (9–10 Jul 2026) exploring how the **[[au-data-policy-framework]]** (AUDPF) can be adopted
  nationally to enable "trusted, secure, and inclusive data governance." Run by the
  **[[d4d-hub]]** under the EU-backed **Data Governance in Africa (#D4DataGOV)** initiative
  (Team Europe / Global Gateway), with implementing partners **[[giz]]**, **[[auda-nepad]]**
  and **[[kictanet]]**. The EU Delegation's Paula Medina framed success as translating the
  workshop into "public policies, institutional reforms, improved administrative practices."
  Source: [[2026-07-13 D4D Hub on Sao Tome and Principe]]. See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.policy]],
  [[gov.regional]], [[capacity.training]], [[XWA]].
