# Cameroon (CMR)

Cameroon is running an **aggressive, state-led digital-sovereignty programme on a fragile base and
foreign finance** — and the wiki now holds the national- and trade-press record behind it (2025–26).
The rhetoric is everywhere: a **National AI Strategy** ([[snia-cameroon]]), a **sovereign government
cloud**, a **DNSSEC-signed `.cm` zone**, a Central African **data-residency** demand. The base underneath
is weaker than the rhetoric (a CENADI study found uncertified facilities, thin power/bandwidth), the
money is largely **Chinese (fibre) and the identity stack foreign-run** (German Augentic, Luxembourg
Liber, Ivorian Impact Palmarès), and a **rights-and-cost counterweight** runs through it all — a
cybercrime law used against speech, ~12,000 voters excluded by faulty biometrics, a payment monopoly that
nearly bankrupted a local studio. The unifying tension: **sovereignty as ambition vs dependence, capacity
and rights as reality.**

## Recent developments

- **2026-07** — **BEAC joins PAPSS; ANTIC pushes councils.** [[beac|BEAC]] (Yaoundé-HQ'd) joined
  [[papss|PAPSS]], taking the continental rail into all six CEMAC states; ANTIC found only **48.6% of
  North West councils** have functional websites (reached 230 councils, ~64% nationally). CAMTEL opened
  **AWS** talks — in tension with the sovereign-cloud line. See [[cameroon--dpi-pay]],
  [[cameroon--dpi-govtech]], [[cameroon--infra-store]].
- **2026-06** — **DNS sovereignty and its rights shadow.** ANTIC raised its DNSSEC target to **100% by
  end-2026** (6th DNS Forum) with an IPv6 push; days later civil society (Paradigm Initiative/Civic Watch)
  attacked the **2010 cybercrime law** ([[cameroon-cybercrime-law-2010]]) as a speech-control instrument,
  citing the **23 Oct 2025 election internet shutdown**. The Catholic Bishops' Conference voiced AI
  concern. See [[cameroon--infra-cybersec]], [[cameroon--tech-ai]].
- **2026-05** — **Sovereign-cloud pitch vs foreign approaches; smart meters.** CAMTEL presented the
  **[[zamengoe-data-centre]]** at SAGO 2026 ("largest in Central Africa / Tier III" — unverified); the
  **RGPH 2026 census** (first since 2005) ran with satellite imagery + MoMo-paid enumerators; a **20,000
  smart-meter** rollout began (data centre under construction in Douala). Orange opened its 6th Digital
  Center in **Maroua**. See [[cameroon--infra-store]], [[ins-cameroon]].
- **2026-03/04** — **China fibre + IMEI customs regime.** Decree n°2026/084 (6 Mar 2026) took a **~$195m
  China Exim Bank loan** for **Phase IV** of the fibre backbone ([[china-eximbank]]); the **[[camcis]]**
  IMEI device-duty regime and a Douala City × **Huawei** cadastre MoU landed — though IMEI enforcement
  **stalled** when operators refused. Digital-health plan **[[psnsn-cameroon]]** (~29bn FCFA) published.
  See [[cameroon--infra-connect]], [[cameroon--dpi-govtech]].
- **2026-01/02** — **DIGI-CoR, CIRT, ARTAC.** A **$163m** local-government digitisation programme
  ([[digi-cor]]); ANTIC's CIRT hardened (~FCFA 735m, PATNUC/WB, handover 20 Jan); **CAMTEL/CBC** closed
  FCFA 44.884bn (MNE phase 1); the regulator's ARTAC delegation backed a **data-residency** position; ART
  funded a digital-governance chair at **[[university-yaounde-ii]]**. See [[cameroon--infra-store]].
- **2025-12** — **South–south substitution + surveillance.** CAMTEL signed an MSA with **[[ethio-telecom]]**
  (sovereign gov-cloud, Blue Money); ANTIC signed the **`.cm` zone with DNSSEC**; a **XAF 39bn urban
  video-surveillance** loan and Impact Palmarès consular/printing deals. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]].
- **2025-07** — **SNIA unveiled; ART bites.** The **[[snia-cameroon|SNIA]]** 2040 roadmap was unveiled at
  CONIA 2 (authored on tender by [[cabinet-danielle-moukouri]], with [[st-digital]] as infra partner —
  correcting the earlier "ST Digital leads" framing); ART **fined Orange & MTN CFA 2.6bn**. The **SNK IA
  Challenge** trained 100 youths and 20 more in the West. Sources: [[2025-07-23-leconomie-snk-ia-challenge-2025]],
  [[2025-07-02-lejour-20-youth-trained-in-ai-west-region]].
- **2025-05/06** — **Innovation week + fragile-base evidence.** Digital Innovation Week put AI centre-stage
  (awards Jun 2025); CENADI's data-centre study exposed the weak base. Sources:
  [[2025-05-28-lejour-ai-at-heart-of-digital-innovation-week]], [[2025-06-30-leconomie-digital-innovation-week-awards]].
- **2025-02/03** — **Biometric CNI goes live.** Pre-enrolment via **[[idcam]]** (10,000 FCFA fee), issuance
  from 3 Mar with the 48-hour promise met; the paired data-protection & civil-registration laws
  (**Loi 2024/017** + **Loi 2024/016**, enacted 23 Dec 2024) underpin it. See [[cameroon--dpi-id]].
- **Rural/agri data (2025-10)** — agro-meteorological data pitched to North-region farmers; and civil-society
  **open-budget** oversight in the East (2026-04). Sources: [[2025-10-01-lejour-north-region-weather-data-agriculture]],
  [[2026-04-16-leconomie-citizen-budget-oversight-east]].

## Active topics

- **Digital identity & CRVS** → **[[cameroon--dpi-id]]**. Biometric CNI ([[dgsn-cameroon]]/[[augentic]] BOT,
  [[idcam]] portal), digital CRVS ([[bunec]]/[[cameroon-civil-registration-law-2024]]), the election-side
  register ([[elecam]], 2024 exclusion harm), foreign document vendor [[impact-palmares]], and sectoral
  taxi/student IDs.
- **AI** → **[[cameroon--tech-ai]]**. The full SNIA policy chain (CONIA 2024 origin, 7-pillar 2040 roadmap)
  plus societal contestation. Entities: [[snia-cameroon]], [[conia]], [[cabinet-danielle-moukouri]].
- **Connectivity** → **[[cameroon--infra-connect]]**. Chinese-financed backbone, [[camtel]] the
  wholesale-profitable gatekeeper, [[art-cameroon]] enforcing, Chad dependency.
- **Data centres & sovereign hosting** → **[[cameroon--infra-store]]**. Three poles (state [[cenadi]]/
  [[zamengoe-data-centre]], private-African [[st-digital]]/[[anthony-same]], foreign hyperscaler) and the
  [[artac]] data-residency vs CAMTEL-AWS tension.
- **Cybersecurity** → **[[cameroon--infra-cybersec]]**. [[antic-cameroon]] DNS sovereignty vs the rights
  counterweight ([[cameroon-cybercrime-law-2010]], surveillance).
- **Digital payments** → **[[cameroon--dpi-pay]]**. MoMo duopoly, Camtel Blue Money, GIMACPAY/PAPSS,
  the [[intouch]] monopoly harm to [[kiroo-games]].
- **Govtech & data exchange** → **[[cameroon--dpi-govtech]]**. [[camcis]] customs/IMEI, [[digi-cor]]
  local-gov, [[coleps]]/[[proxigab]], DGI tax, e-Visa.
- **Statistics & policy** — [[ins-cameroon]] (RGPH 2026, Open SDG), [[psnsn-cameroon]] (digital health),
  the enacted [[cameroon-data-protection-law-2024]].

> **Gazetted texts not held (as of 2026-07-20).** The official texts of **loi n° 2024/017**
> (personal data protection) and **loi n° 2024/016** (civil registration), both enacted
> 23 Dec 2024, are **not held**: prc.cm serves them only as scanned image PDFs from which no
> machine-readable text can be extracted, so the wiki still reads both laws through secondary
> reporting. **Décret n° 2025/059 of 28 Feb 2025** *is* now held, though as an `excerpt` —
> clean through art. 31, with the refugee-card, disability-card and final provisions not
> captured. Note the decree is broader than a CNI instrument: it governs all *titres
> identitaires* (national ID card, residence permit, resident card, refugee card, national
> disability card).
