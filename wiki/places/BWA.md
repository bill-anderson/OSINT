# Botswana (BWA)

Southern-African state tracked here mainly for **regional connectivity corridors**, a
**national-statistics** angle, and as a strong satellite-broadband market.

## Recent developments

- **Botswana–Zimbabwe–Zambia fibre corridor.** Botswana is an endpoint of the cross-border
  fibre corridor linking Southern-African neighbours, and a strong **Starlink** market. See
  [[infra.connect]], [[XSA]], [[ZWE]], [[ZMB]].
- **Data-for-impact / statistics.** Appears in the data-governance discussion on tying digital
  programmes to **funding national statistics to measure impact**. See [[data.statistics]],
  [[dpi.exchange]], [[MOZ]].
- **Health specimen-sharing context.** Named among countries in the continental
  specimen-/data-sharing debate (the model [[specimen-sharing-agreement]]). See [[gov.protect]].
