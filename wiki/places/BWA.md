# Botswana (BWA)

An **upper-middle-income, comparatively strong-performing** Southern-African digital economy — 4th of 32 in Sub-Saharan Africa on the 2025 innovation index — now assembling the DPI pieces: a first national data centre, a data-protection regime that finally took effect in 2025, biometric social payments, a digital Omang ID and SADC cross-border payment rails. The through-line is a **state building foundations at last** — several long-stalled elements (the 2018 data-protection act, the national DC) came good in 2024–2026.

## Recent developments

- **2026-03-02** — **Digital Omang ID + reforms.** The Ministry of Labour and Home Affairs flagged a digital
  **Omang** national ID / **Electronic Identification System**, an e-wallet and biometric e-gov services in
  the 2026/27 budget ([[omang-digital-id-botswana]]). See [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2026-03-02 Botswana - Minister envisions reforms to boost service delivery (digital Omang)]].
- **2026-03-02** — **TCIB cross-border payments activated into Botswana.** The SADC **[[tcib]]** scheme (PayInc)
  went live into Botswana via First Capital Bank. See [[dpi.pay]], [[XSA]]. Source: [[2026-03-02 PayInc and First Capital Bank Botswana Launch Instant Cross-Border Payments (TCIB)]].
- **2025-12-08** — **Digital-economy policy snapshot (US ITA).** SmartBots strategy, pending Digital Services &
  Cybersecurity bills, BOTSAT-1 satellite, satellite-broadband openness ([[bocra]] regulator). Source: [[2025-12-08 Botswana - Digital Economy (Country Commercial Guide)]].
- **2025-11-26** — **First national data centre (Digital Delta DC).** [[bofinet]] launched Botswana's only
  Tier III, carrier-neutral, government-owned national DC in Gaborone (built by Zhong Gan). See [[infra.store]].
  Source: [[2025-11-26 Botswana launches Tier III Digital Delta Data Centre (national data centre)]].
- **2025-07-16** — **Government payments digitising** under the **[[bank-of-botswana]]** "Digital BoB2024".
  See [[dpi.pay]]. Source: [[2025-07-16 Botswana advances drive to digitise government payments]].
- **2025-05-29** — **Biometric social payments.** [[smartswitch-botswana]] (Lesaka) served 75,000+
  food-voucher beneficiaries via biometric smart cards on the UEPS. See [[dpi.pay]], [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2025-05-29 Biometric payment card system pushing financial inclusion in Botswana]].
- **2025-01-14** — **Data Protection Act 2024 takes effect**, replacing the never-commenced 2018 Act.
  → [[botswana--gov-protect]]. Source: [[2025-01-08 Understanding Botswana's New Data Protection Act 2024]].
- **2024-03** — **[[bocra]] mandatory SIM registration** for all users (CRA Act 2012). See [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2024-03-11 Botswana - Mandatory SIM Card Registration public notice (BOCRA)]].
