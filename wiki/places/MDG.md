# Madagascar (MDG)

## Recent developments

- **2026-01-27** — **National biometric-ID pilot launched.** Madagascar began the pilot phase
  of a national biometric registration programme (senior government officials enrolled first)
  as the technical test ahead of a **nationwide launch in April 2026**. The **Ministry of
  Digital Development, Posts and Telecommunications** ([[mg-ministry-digital-development]])
  targets registering **2 million adults (18+) between April and June 2026**, capturing
  fingerprints, facial images and iris scans to give each citizen a **single, verifiable
  identification number** as the reference for accessing public services — aimed at cutting
  duplication and fraud and modernising civil-registry records. Source:
  [[2026-01-27 Madagascar Tests Biometric ID System, Aims to Register 2 Million People by Mid-2026]].
  See [[dpi.id]], [[dpi.registry]], [[dpi.govtech]].

## Active topics
