# Morocco (MAR)

## Recent developments

- **2026-06-01** — **Morocco's 500 MW renewables AI project as an energy-led
  sovereignty model.** Anjola Adebowale's AI-sovereignty analysis flags Morocco's
  **500 MW renewable-powered AI project** (announced Jun 2025) as a compute build
  **outside US CLOUD Act reach** — an energy-first path to AI sovereignty that
  contrasts with hyperscaler-hosted "sovereign" zones. Morocco is also a planned
  site in the **[[cassava-ai-factories]]** ~$720m five-country GPU program. See
  [[infra.energy]], [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]]. Source:
  [[2026-06-01 Africa's Place in the AI Sovereignty Debate]].
  *(NB: the batch brief mislabelled this the "pax-silica" entity; [[pax-silica]]
  is the separate US-led chip coalition — this Morocco data-centre project is a
  distinct object, flagged for a human to confirm/entity-create.)*
- **2026-06-17** — Morocco profiled as **the first country to adopt [[mosip]]** (MoU
  2018, after a 2017 delegation to India studied Aadhaar). Now registers **22m of 39m**
  people in its national population register, used to reach **5m households** with social
  programmes. Former deputy interior minister **Noureddine Boutayeb** later built
  **Maharat**, an open-source platform (on India's Sunbird) to train 26,000 preschool
  educators. Source: [[2026-06-17 They're taking DPI to the world - The Times of India]].
  See [[dpi.id]], [[geopol.india]], [[gov.standards]].
