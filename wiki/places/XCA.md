# Central Africa (XCA)

Regional hub for genuinely Central-Africa-level items. Sub-region of **[[XAF]]**; the
CEMAC/Central African bloc broadly comprises Cameroon ([[CMR]]), the Central African
Republic ([[CAF]]), Chad ([[TCD]]), the Republic of Congo ([[COG]]), DR Congo ([[COD]]),
Equatorial Guinea ([[GNQ]]) and Gabon ([[GAB]]). Tag `XCA` only when an item is genuinely
region-level; country-specific detail lives on the country hubs.

## Recent developments

- **2025-08-12** — **La Francophonie CRVS support across Central Africa.** On the 8th Africa
  CRVS Day, **[[oif-la-francophonie]]** reaffirmed birth-registration support spanning
  **[[CAF]]** (3,000 certificates targeted), **[[TCD]]** (53,000+ children registered) and
  **[[CMR]]** (5,400), plus Madagascar — legal identity for undocumented rural/vulnerable
  populations under SDG 16.9. See [[dpi.id]]. Source:
  [[2025-08-12 La Francophonie supports birth registration efforts across Central Africa, Madagascar]].
- **2026-07-11** — **BEAC joins PAPSS.** The **[[beac]]** (Banque des États de l'Afrique
  Centrale, the CEMAC common central bank) officially joined the **[[papss]]** (Pan-African
  Payment and Settlement System), extending the continental real-time cross-border
  settlement rail into the CEMAC zone — a meaningful new node in the PAPSS rollout after its
  West and East African expansion, framed as a lever for intra-African trade and financial
  integration under **[[afcfta]]**. Accession date/terms unconfirmed beyond the lede
  (paywalled source) — corroborate against a BEAC/[[afreximbank]] primary. Source:
  [[2026-07-11 BEAC joins PAPSS and accelerates cross-border payments integration in Central Africa]].
  See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]].
