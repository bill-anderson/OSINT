# Sierra Leone (SLE)

Sierra Leone is building its digital state around a single spine — the [[ncra]] national ID
and its **National Identification Number (NIN)** — onto which payments, agriculture, banking
and (prospectively) social services are being wired. Coverage headlines are strong
(93% registration, 2025) but card issuance, affordability, connectivity (20.7% internet
penetration, Jan 2025) and an un-enacted data-protection law remain the binding constraints.
The through-line across the ID work is **open standards and national ownership**
([[gov.standards]]) — from W3C verifiable credentials to X-Road interoperability.

## Recent developments

- **2026-04-21** — **Cabinet approves the first National Data Protection Policy.** Cabinet approved a
  National Data Protection Policy and authorised a new **Data Protection & Right to Access Information
  Act** — repealing the RAIC Act and merging data protection + access-to-information into one
  strengthened authority (RAIC transformed, dual mandate). Advances the validated
  [[sierra-leone-data-protection-rti-bill-2025]] thread toward Parliament. Source:
  [[2026-04-22-cabinet-data-protection-policy]]. See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]].

- **2026-07-08** — Sierra Leone (MoCTI), **[[bhutan-ndi]]** and the **[[sign-foundation]]**
  sign a tri-party MoU to build the country's **national digital identity platform** —
  open-source, **W3C verifiable credentials**, national ownership, Bhutan's NDI as template.
  Deal: [[sierra-leone-digital-id-mou]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-08 Sierra Leone partners with Bhutan, Sign on digital identity platform]].
- **2025-12-09** — World Bank analysis: **6.4m hold a NIN (~8 in 10)** toward a universal
  target of 8.8m, but **<0.5m ID cards issued as of 2024** ($6–7 fee prices most out); an
  emergency cash transfer paid **35,000 households by mobile — a national first**. Flags the
  absent data-protection framework as the key gap. Source:
  [[2025-12-09 Unlocking opportunity - How digital ID can transform lives in Sierra Leone]].
- **2025-12-08** — **[[orange-sierra-leone]]** commissions a **€23m backup data centre in
  Bo**, a disaster-recovery replica of its 2018 Freetown facility; President Bio attends.
  Source: [[2025-12-08 Orange launches backup data center in Sierra Leone (Bo)]].
- **2025-11-12** — MoCTI signs a bilateral **blockchain digital-ID MoU with Sign** (CEO Xin
  Yan): national ID, digital wallet and asset-tokenization framework via Felei TechCity —
  the lineage MoU behind the July-2026 tri-party. Source:
  [[2025-11-12 Sierra Leone partners to build blockchain-powered digital ID system]].
- **2025-11-09** — Government concludes **final national validation** of the
  **[[sierra-leone-data-protection-rti-bill-2025]]** — a single independent authority for
  both data protection and access to information; next stop Cabinet then Parliament. Source:
  [[2025-11-09 Government Finalizes Sierra Leones Data Protection and Right to Access Information Bill]].
- **2025-10-07** — Sierra Leone signs an **AI MoU with Qhala** (UNGA80): train 500 civil
  servants, build 10–15 AI workflows; a **World Bank-backed National AI Readiness Assessment**
  launched 2 Oct 2025. UN 2024 EGDI rank **172/193 (0.3042)**. Source:
  [[2025-10-07 Sierra Leone Turns to AI to Modernize Public Services]].
- **2025-06-05** — NCRA reports **93% national-ID coverage and 80% of services digitized**
  (ID4Africa 2025); NIN now required to open a bank account, enrol in public education, or
  take up employment. Source:
  [[2025-06-05 Sierra Leone reaches 93 percent national ID coverage 80 percent services digitized]].
- **2025-05-19** — NCRA and the Ministry of Agriculture agree to integrate the
  **[[sierra-leone-national-digital-farmer-registry]]** with the NIN system (Feed Salone).
  Source: [[2025-05-19 NCRA, Ministry of Agriculture partner on National Digital Farmer Registry integration]].
- **2025-02-14** — BSL launches the instant-payment phase of the
  **[[sierra-leone-instant-payment-switch]]** (13 Feb 2025): real-time interoperable
  transfers, 7 banks + 2 MNOs at launch, full integration by 1 Apr 2025. Source:
  [[2025-02-14 Sierra Leone Launches National Instant Payment System]].
- **2025 (year)** — World Bank ToR to build a **Government Service Bus prototype** (open-
  source X-Road) in the MoF data centre, interconnecting siloed PFM systems; Japan QII grant
  signed 26 Apr 2025. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 Sierra Leone Government Service Bus prototype - Terms of Reference (World Bank)]].
- **2025 (year)** — [[dial]] supports a 5-month update of the
  **[[sierra-leone-national-data-strategy]]**, aligning to the AU Data Policy Framework.
  Source: [[2025-01-01 Strengthening Sierra Leones national data strategy (DIAL)]].
- **2025-01-01** — NCRA/SLP/ONS ultimatum (dated 20 Dec 2024) makes the **NCRA ID card
  mandatory** for movement and services; voter's card no longer accepted as ID. Source:
  [[2025-01-01 Sierra Leone announces mandatory national ID cards - what you need to know]].
- **2024-03-28** — NCRA extends the ID-card deadline 31 Mar → 28 Jun 2024 (historical
  baseline). Source:
  [[2024-03-28 Government Extends Deadline for Obtaining National Identity Cards]].

## Active topics

- **[[dpi.id]]** — NIN-centred foundational ID (93% coverage 2025; mandatory since Jan
  2025), open-standards / blockchain build ([[sierra-leone-digital-id-mou]]), low card
  issuance and affordability gap; MOSIP brownfield migration; the "brokers of citizenship" last-mile
  critique. Full case: [[sierra-leone--dpi-id]]. Home: [[ncra]].
- **[[dpi.pay]]** — national instant-payment switch (2023 origin, instant phase Feb 2025);
  8.2m mobile-money accounts (2023); inclusion 18%→~40% (2025), 1m+ IPS txns; sandbox-incubated card/wallet/AI-payment launches. Full case: [[sierra-leone--dpi-pay]]. [[sierra-leone-instant-payment-switch]].
- **[[dpi.exchange]]** — Government Service Bus / X-Road interoperability; NIN-linkage across
  MDAs; MoF PFM interoperability working group (2024).
- **[[dpi.registry]]** / **[[digital.rural]]** — National Digital Farmer Registry integrated
  with the NIN (Feed Salone). [[sierra-leone-national-digital-farmer-registry]].
- **[[dpi.govtech]]** / **[[tech.ai]]** — 80% of services digitized (2025); AI-in-government
  MoU (Qhala), National AI Readiness Assessment, first **National AI Strategy** (World Bank, 2026);
  civil-service digital-skills pipeline (1,000→5,000); EGDI 172/193 (2024). Full case: [[sierra-leone--tech-ai]].
- **[[gov.protect]]** / **[[gov.legislate]]** — Data Protection & RTI Bill 2025 (validated,
  pre-enactment). [[sierra-leone-data-protection-rti-bill-2025]].
- **[[gov.policy]]** / **[[gov.standards]]** — National Data Strategy (AU DPF-aligned);
  open-standards emphasis across ID and interoperability.
- **[[infra.store]]** — NCRA Tier-3 DC; Orange Bo backup DC (€23m, 2025); NatCA HQ National Data Centre (2026); data-sovereignty/resilience framing. Full case: [[sierra-leone--infra-store]].
- **[[infra.connect]]** — 20.7% internet penetration (Jan 2025) / 20.8% (Oct 2025), 99.2% mobile
  connections; ~$400m invested but a 60% usage gap; regional interconnection (Guinea axis, ECOWAS
  roaming), Starlink, and the Sierratel MVNO rescue. Full case: [[sierra-leone--infra-connect]].
- **[[capacity.training]]** — knowledge transfer under the ID MoU; 500 civil servants (AI).
- **[[include.access]]** — ID card cost/literacy barriers; social-protection digital payments.

## Reconcile notes (dated, not contradictions)

- **ID reach — three different metrics.** NCRA "93% registration coverage" (2025-06) vs
  World Bank "6.4m NIN holders, ~8 in 10" and "<0.5m cards issued as of 2024" (2025-12).
  Registration ≠ card issuance ≠ coverage rate; each kept with its as-of date.
- **Internet penetration.** DataReportal **20.7% (Jan 2025)** vs DIAL **30.4% (2024)**
  (from 12% in 2018). Large gap, likely different definitions (individuals using internet vs
  broader access); kept dated. Sources:
  [[2025-03-03 Digital 2025 Sierra Leone (DataReportal)]],
  [[2025-01-01 Strengthening Sierra Leones national data strategy (DIAL)]].
