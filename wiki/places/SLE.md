# Sierra Leone (SLE)

## Recent developments

- **2026-07-08** — Sierra Leone (MoCTI), **[[bhutan-ndi]]** and the **[[sign-foundation]]**
  sign a tri-party MoU to build the country's **national digital identity platform** —
  open-source, **W3C verifiable credentials**, strong emphasis on local capacity building
  and long-term **national ownership**, with **Bhutan's NDI programme as the template**.
  Builds on an existing Nov 2025 SL–Sign MoU for a blockchain-powered digital ID. Deal:
  [[sierra-leone-digital-id-mou]]. Source:
  [[2026-07-08 Sierra Leone partners with Bhutan, Sign on digital identity platform]]. See
  [[dpi.id]], [[gov.standards]].
