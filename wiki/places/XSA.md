# Southern Africa (XSA)

Regional hub for Southern Africa (the SADC space). Country detail lives on the member hubs —
e.g. [[ZAF]], [[ZWE]], [[ZMB]], [[NAM]], [[AGO]], [[MOZ]], [[MWI]], [[BWA]], [[LSO]], [[SWZ]] —
this page tracks genuinely region-level developments. Political/economic blocs are entities.

## Recent developments

- **Regional connectivity build-ahead.** Cross-border fibre and satellite links across
  Namibia, Angola and Zimbabwe ("digitally connected in Southern Africa"), including the
  Angosat-2 satellite route via Angola, laid ahead of demand. See [[infra.connect]],
  [[infra.capacity]], [[NAM]], [[AGO]], [[ZWE]].
- **Payments modernisation (ISO 20022).** Southern-African payment-system reform aligning to
  the ISO 20022 messaging standard. See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.standards]], [[gov.policy]].
- **Duty relief on digital infrastructure.** Removal of import duty on digital-infrastructure
  equipment credited as a regional enabler. See [[gov.regional]], [[finance.new]].
