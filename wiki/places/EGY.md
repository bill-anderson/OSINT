# Egypt (EGY)

North-African digital state, sitting **outside the wiki's Sub-Saharan core scope** but captured
substantively here on a curator-directed basis. Egypt runs one of Africa's **most built-out DPI
stacks** — a mature GovTech platform (**[[digital-egypt|Digital Egypt]]**), layered biometric
digital-identity channels, a deep central-bank payments/inclusion system, sectoral MIS in health
and social protection, and a **submarine-cable / data-centre hub** position — all framed under
**[[egypt-vision-2030|Vision 2030]]** and, increasingly, a hardening data-protection regime.

### Standing profile

- **DPI / GovTech maturity.** World Bank **GTMI 22nd globally (2025, score 0.911, Group A "Extensive")**;
  Digital Egypt offers **210 services / 10.7m users** (25m+ transactions in 2025); AI-readiness 51st (1st in
  Africa); e-gov 77th (WIPO). Connectivity: internet penetration **81.9%** (96.3m users, early 2025); fixed
  broadband **12.6m** (Nov 2025); **"Decent Life"** rural fibre to ~5m lines / **1,250+ of 1,450 villages**;
  **digital exports $7.4bn** (outsourcing $4.8bn). See [[dpi.govtech]], [[egypt--dpi-govtech]].
- **Layered digital identity.** CBE **[[haweya]]** (financial eKYC) + Interior **[[moieg-pass]]** +
  civil-registry kiosks + a planned digital ID card, atop the biometric national ID. See
  [[egypt--dpi-id]].
- **Payments depth.** InstaPay (**16m users, EGP 4.2tn** Jul24–Jun25; fees from Apr 2025), Meeza (43.5m
  cards), 55.5m wallets, SoftPOS, carrier billing; **77.6%** inclusion; fintech leaders **[[fawry]]**,
  **[[blnk]]**, **[[onebank-egypt|onebank]]**, **[[digified]]**. See [[central-bank-of-egypt]], [[egypt--dpi-pay]].
- **Data protection tightening.** **[[egypt-pdpl|PDPL]]** + 2025 Executive Regulations + **[[pdpc-egypt|PDPC]]**;
  cross-border-transfer licensing (framed as data sovereignty); early tort-based enforcement. See [[egypt--gov-protect]].
- **Cable / data-centre hub.** One of the continent's **five main cable hubs** ([[EGY]], [[ZAF]],
  [[DJI]], [[NGA]], [[KEN]]) — **~15 active subsea systems**, Africa's largest; **[[telecom-egypt]]** RDH1/RDH2;
  **NTRA has issued 10 DC licences in two years** (**[[hassan-allam-data-centre|Hassan Allam]]** $400m, Sinai
  green hyperscale, Huawei Cloud region); **[[amazon-web-services|AWS]]** Cairo edge. See
  [[infra.connect]], [[infra.store]], [[egypt--infra-store]].
