# Egypt (EGY)

North-African digital state, sitting **outside the wiki's Sub-Saharan core scope** but captured
substantively here on a curator-directed basis. Egypt runs one of Africa's **most built-out DPI
stacks** — a mature GovTech platform (**[[digital-egypt|Digital Egypt]]**), layered biometric
digital-identity channels, a deep central-bank payments/inclusion system, sectoral MIS in health
and social protection, and a **submarine-cable / data-centre hub** position — all framed under
**[[egypt-vision-2030|Vision 2030]]** and, increasingly, a hardening data-protection regime.

### Standing profile

- **DPI / GovTech maturity.** World Bank **GTMI "Group A"** (Extensive tier, with [[KEN]], [[RWA]],
  [[UGA]]); Digital Egypt offers **175+ services**; Egypt hosted the **2024 Global DPI Summit**.
  Connectivity: internet penetration **81.9%** (96.3m users, early 2025); avg speed 75.3 Mbps;
  **"Decent Life"** rural fibre to 1.5m homes. See [[dpi.govtech]], [[egypt--dpi-govtech]].
- **Layered digital identity.** CBE **[[haweya]]** (financial eKYC) + Interior **[[moieg-pass]]** +
  civil-registry kiosks + a planned digital ID card, atop the biometric national ID. See
  [[egypt--dpi-id]].
- **Payments depth.** InstaPay, Meeza (43.5m cards), 55.5m mobile wallets, SoftPOS, carrier billing;
  **77.6%** financial inclusion. See [[central-bank-of-egypt]], [[egypt--dpi-pay]].
- **Data protection tightening.** **[[egypt-pdpl|PDPL]]** + 2025 Executive Regulations + **[[pdpc-egypt|PDPC]]**;
  cross-border-transfer licensing; early tort-based enforcement. See [[egypt--gov-protect]].
- **Cable / data-centre hub.** One of the continent's **five main cable hubs** ([[EGY]], [[ZAF]],
  [[DJI]], [[NGA]], [[KEN]]); **[[telecom-egypt]]** RDH1/RDH2 data centres (13→18 subsea systems);
  **[[amazon-web-services|AWS]]** Cairo edge. Planned **[[cassava-ai-factories]]** GPU site. See
  [[infra.connect]], [[infra.store]], [[tech.ai]].
