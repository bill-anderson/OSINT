# Eswatini (SWZ)

## Recent developments

- **2026-05-18** — Eswatini is advancing toward **[[world-bank]]** funding for a
  national **[[eswatini-digital-transformation-project]]**, via negotiations among
  the ICT, Finance and Economic Planning ministries. The project would modernise
  government systems and deliver services (passports, business registration,
  vehicle licensing, health, education, social services) through the **"Government
  in Your Hand" (GIYH)** platform, plus cybersecurity, digital skills and rural
  broadband. Funding amount not yet stated. Source:
  [[2026-05-18 Eswatini promotes digital transformation project through World Bank engagements]].
  See [[dpi.govtech]], [[infra.connect]]. *(⚠ verify against a WB project record.)*
