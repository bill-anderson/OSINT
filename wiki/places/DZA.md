# Algeria (DZA)

North-African state (population 47.1m, 2025) pursuing a **Presidency-steered, sovereignty-first
digital transformation**. Sits **outside the wiki's Sub-Saharan core scope** but is captured
substantively here on a curator-directed basis: the defining features are a **state-centric,
data-localised model** — mandatory in-country data storage, a hyperscaler-free protected cloud
market, a sovereign-cloud and state-owned data-centre/AI-compute build-out — layered under the
**[[digital-algeria-2030]]** strategy and run from the Presidency via the
**[[high-commission-digitalisation-algeria|High Commission for Digitalisation]]**.

### Standing profile

- **Connectivity baseline.** ~**36.2m** internet users (**76.9%**, DataReportal early 2025); by 2026-05
  **>7m fixed subscribers** (**>3.4m fibre**, 3m FTTH households — **1st in Africa**), **>51m mobile**; 5G
  live nationwide (2026). **ITU ICT Development Index: 74th in June 2025 (up from 89th in 2024, score 86.1)**.
  See [[infra.connect]], [[algeria--infra-connect]], [[include.divides]].
- **Sovereignty-first data model.** **Law 18-07** (in force Aug 2023) + **mandatory data
  localisation** (see [[anpdcp]]); five regional data centres; sovereign cloud in preparation; no
  global hyperscaler operates in-country (a deliberately protected market). See [[gov.protect]],
  [[infra.store]], [[algeria--gov-protect]].
- **Digital economy weight.** Three operators — state-owned **Mobilis**, **Djezzy**, **Ooredoo**;
  incumbent **[[algerie-telecom]]** (2.9m FTTH by late 2025). Low card usage; instant-payment and
  mobile-pay rails being built (see [[algeria--dpi-pay]]).
- **Continental posture.** Beyond the $1bn startup fund, Algeria hosted **IATF2025**, joined
  **PAPSS**, and appears in continental AI-cooperation framing (Africa Forward Summit, Nairobi, May
  2026). See [[gov.regional]], [[tech.ai]].
- **Surveillance context.** Algeria appears in the mapping of Chinese-supplied AI surveillance
  systems. See [[infra.cybersec]], [[tech.ai]]. Source: [[2026-03-01 Smart City Surveillance in Africa (IDS 2026)]].
- Parent region: [[XNA]] · continental: [[XAF]].
