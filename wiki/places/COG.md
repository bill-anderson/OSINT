# Congo-Brazzaville (COG)

## Recent developments

- **2026-01-22** — **National Personal Data Protection Commission established.**
  Congo-Brazzaville stood up the **[[national-personal-data-protection-commission-congo]]**,
  with ICT minister **Léon Juste Ibombo** installing members to consolidate the
  country's "digital rule of law" amid rapid digitisation of public services and
  the digital economy. The commission is an **independent authority** empowered to
  advise, warn and **sanction**, and tasked with instilling a data-protection
  culture across the public sector, business and civil society while balancing
  innovation with rights. Source:
  [[2026-01-22 Congo-Brazzaville intros data protection commission]].
  See [[gov.protect]], [[gov.legislate]].
