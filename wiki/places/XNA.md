# North Africa (XNA)

Regional hub for North Africa — member states [[DZA]], [[EGY]], [[MAR]], LBY (Libya, unhubbed), [[TUN]].
North Africa sits **outside the wiki's Sub-Saharan core scope**; it appears mainly as
Mediterranean cable-landing and GovTech/AI-comparison context, and (by the `XSS` rule) is the
one African subregion excluded from Sub-Saharan Africa. This page is a light landing point;
country detail lives on the member hubs.
