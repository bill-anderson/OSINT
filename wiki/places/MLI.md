# Mali (MLI)

## Recent developments

- **2026-02-02** — **Tier III National Data Centre inaugurated.** During Mali's
  third annual Digital Week, the Ministry of Communication, Digital Economy and
  Administration Modernisation (minister Alhamdou Ag Ilyène) launched a **Tier III
  "National Data Centre"** in the presence of digital-economy ministers from the
  **Confederation of Sahel States** ([[alliance-of-sahel-states]] / AES —
  Mali, Niger ([[NER]]), Burkina Faso ([[BFA]])). The facility is to host the
  Malian state's strategic data, e-administration platforms and public digital
  services and, in the short term, **"mutualised" AES infrastructure**. Officials
  framed it as ending **dependence on foreign infrastructure and the
  extraterritorial cloud** and as a pillar of AES digital sovereignty. Source:
  [[2026-02-02 Mali unveils modern Tier III data centre]].
  See [[infra.store]], [[infra.cybersec]], [[dpi.govtech]], [[gov.regional]].
