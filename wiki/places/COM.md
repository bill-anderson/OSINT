# Comoros (COM)

A **small Indian-Ocean archipelago building DPI from a very low base**, largely on donor financing (World Bank, AfDB) after a decade of limited progress on an earlier digital-services strategy. The building blocks are arriving quickly for its size: a national interoperable payment switch, PAPSS accession, a Tier-3 public data centre, decentralised biometric ID production, and a comparatively advanced (if unenforced) 2021 data-protection law. The recurring gap is **institutions lagging the infrastructure** — most visibly a data-protection authority that exists in law but not yet in fact.

## Recent developments

- **2026-02-16** — **Anjouan (Ndzuani) to print its own biometric ID cards.** From April 2026, Dutch firm
  **[[genkey]] replaces Semlex** producing Comoros' biometric documents; ID-card printing decentralises to
  Ndzuani so residents no longer travel to Moroni. See [[dpi.id]]. Source: [[2026-02-16 Comoros - Anjouan (Ndzuani) to produce its own biometric ID cards (Genkey replaces Semlex)]].
- **2025-08-23** — **National interoperable payment switch goes live.** **[[paylogic]]** delivered the
  switch (awarded Jan 2025) under the World Bank PADSF — connecting banks, MFIs and mobile-money operators
  ([[bcc-comoros]]). See [[dpi.pay]]. Source: [[2025-08-23 PayLogic go-live of Comoros national interoperable payment switch]].
- **2025-07-23** — **2021 Data Protection Law reviewed** ([[comoros-data-protection-law-2021]]) — GDPR-aligned,
  but its **National Authority still not operational** (mid-2024). [[gov.protect]]. Source: [[2025-07-23 Review of the Comoros Data Protection Law]].
- **2025-05-15** — **Public-administration data centre completed** (Tier 3, 44.4 Tbps; **[[anaden]]** + Comores
  Cable) — the physical anchor of the AfDB project. [[infra.store]]. Source: [[2025-05-15 Comoros completes construction of public administration data centre (ANADEN)]].
- **2024-10** — **AfDB $10.4m for the [[project-digitization-comorian-economy]]** (total ~$25m): data centre,
  digital-admin platform, digital payment system, interoperability layer, incubator, and a data-protection
  authority. See [[dpi.govtech]]. Source: [[2024-10-06 Comoros to implement digital govt program with AfDB grant (Digitization of the Comorian Economy)]].
- **2024-08** — **PAPSS accession** — the **[[bcc-comoros]]** joined PAPSS (payments to other African states
  in Comorian francs). See [[dpi.pay]], [[gov.regional]]. Source: [[2024-08-23 Central Bank of Comoros commences PAPSS implementation]].
