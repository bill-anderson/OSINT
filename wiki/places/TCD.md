# Chad (TCD)

## Recent developments

- **2026-07-01** — Following a Chadian government mission to Washington (26–29 May
  2026), the **US** reaffirmed support for Chad's digital transformation under its
  **2020–2030 National Digital Transformation Strategy**. Talks (State Dept,
  Commerce, and firms) spanned **digital infrastructure, satellite connectivity,
  cybersecurity and digital health**; goals include raising internet penetration
  **30% → 80%** and the digital economy's GDP share **1.9% → 10%**. Cybastion
  offered to help audit/certify Chad's **National Datacenter**; a minister flagged
  **US visa refusals** as a delivery risk. Source:
  [[2026-07-01 Chad and US Explore Partnerships on Satellite Connectivity and Cybersecurity]].
  See [[geopol.usa]], [[infra.connect]], [[infra.cybersec]].

- **2026-02-16** — **Sotel Tchad as a "stranded" former incumbent (Russell Southwood).**
  Chad's state fixed-line operator Sotel Tchad — sold to Lap Green (2010) then
  nationalised, staff cut from ~10,000 to <500 — features in Southwood's former-incumbents
  study; recent moves include the SudaChad 20-year concession (EU + AfDB, 20.5bn CFA), an
  **April 2025 Sotel–[[airtel-africa]] N'Djamena–Mberé corridor** deal, and a "Tchad
  Connexion" plan to invest US$1.5bn by 2030. See [[tech.industry]], [[infra.connect]].
  Source: [[2026-02-16 Waking the dead - Slow road to extinction for Africa's former telco incumbents 11]].
