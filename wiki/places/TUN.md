# Tunisia (TUN)

## Recent developments

- **2026-01-19** — **EBRD €190m loan to modernise Tunisie Telecom.** The **[[ebrd]]** signed a
  development-linked loan of up to **€190 million** (four tranches; first committed tranche €50m)
  to **[[tunisie-telecom]]**, the majority state-owned national operator, funding a **4G→5G**
  mobile upgrade, **fibre-to-the-home to 200,000 households**, backbone/core modernisation,
  energy efficiency, and connection to the **[[medusa-submarine-cable]]** — the EU-backed,
  ~8,000km Mediterranean subsea system (led by Spain's AFR-IX, ~13 countries). A complementary
  **€11m EU grant** (Neighbourhood Investment Platform) covers core-network and **cybersecurity**
  investment plus a transformation-programme technical assistance. Structured as a
  **development-linked loan** (interest tied to sustainability and SOE-reform milestones) and
  aligned with the EU **Global Gateway**; it is the EBRD's **first non-sovereign-guaranteed
  public-sector financing in Tunisia since 2012**. Source:
  [[2026-01-19 EBRD and Tunisie Telecom Partner on €190 Million Digital Infrastructure Upgrade]].
  See [[infra.connect]], [[infra.capacity]], [[finance.new]], [[geopol.eu]], [[infra.cybersec]].

- [[finance.new]] · [[geopol.eu]] — EU-EBRD financing (Global Gateway / EFSD+ first-loss

  Mediterranean.

  and the wider transformation programme.
