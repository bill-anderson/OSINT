# Contradiction: South Sudan's national instant-payment system — is NIPS (Feb 2025) the same as IBPSS (Oct 2025), a rebrand, two phases, or two separate rails?

**Opened:** 2026-07-16 (SLE·SOM·SSD·STP batch)

## The dispute

Two national instant-payment initiatives are reported for South Sudan within eight months, and it is unclear whether they are one system, two phases of one system, or two distinct rails.

- **Claim A — "NIPS", launched February 2025.** South Sudan's first National Instant Payment System, Bank of South Sudan with AfricaNenda support.
  - Asserted by: TechCabal, Fintechnews, Global Government Fintech (2025-02).
  - Source URL held: https://techcabal.com/2025/02/07/south-sudan-launches-instant-payments/
- **Claim B — "IBPSS", launched/operating October 2025.** An instant/interbank payment system referenced as the functioning national rail.
  - Asserted by: Sudans Post DPI feature (2026-01-20) and a financialafrik report (2025-10-14, URL held but not fetchable at ingest).
  - Source URL held: https://sudanspost.com/what-is-actually-functioning-as-dpi-in-south-sudan/ (financialafrik 2025-10-14 held, not fetched)

## Likely reconciliation to test

Establish, with as-of dates: whether NIPS and IBPSS are (a) the same system renamed, (b) a switch/rail (NIPS) plus its interbank settlement layer (IBPSS), or (c) two genuinely separate systems; and which is the live national instant-payment rail as of late 2025 / early 2026, and who operates it.
