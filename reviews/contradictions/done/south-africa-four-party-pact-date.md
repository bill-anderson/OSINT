# Contradiction: when was South Africa's four-party digital-ID pact signed — April 2024 or April 2025?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (ZAF·ZMB·ZWE batch)

## The dispute

- **Claim A — April 2025.**
  - Contemporaneous reporting of the signing itself: **SARS signs a multiparty collaboration agreement
    with the Department of Home Affairs, the Border Management Authority and Government Printing
    Works**, in line with the Digital Transformation of Government Roadmap "approved by cabinet late
    last month" (i.e. March 2025). The piece adds that **the SARS–DHA element specifically is a renewal
    of a Direct Master Agreement in place since 2010**.
  - Asserted by: **Biometric Update** (Ayang Macdonald), 2025-04-08.
  - Source URL held: https://www.biometricupdate.com/202504/south-african-agencies-sign-multiparty-digital-id-deal
- **Claim B — April 2024.**
  - The SARS-hosted architecture "extends an **April-2024** pact between Home Affairs, SARS, the Border
    Management Authority and Government Printing Works."
  - Asserted by: held material at `wiki/intersections/south-africa--dpi-id.md` (line ~30), sourced to
    `raw/2026-04-21 South Africa's digital ID gets a targeted launch date.md` — a retrospective mention
    a year after the fact.

## Assessment

A is contemporaneous reporting of the signing; B is a passing retrospective reference. The likely
explanation is that **B garbles two real things into one**: the **April 2025** four-party agreement and
the **2010** SARS–DHA Direct Master Agreement that it renews. If so the held "April-2024" is simply
wrong and should be corrected rather than reconciled.

A smaller related discrepancy to settle in the same pass: the held ZAF hub dates the
`sa-digital-transformation-roadmap` to **May 2025**, while the April-2025 source says **cabinet approved
it in March 2025**.

## Instruction

These sources date the same four-party agreement a year apart. Establish the actual signing date from a
primary source (a SARS or DHA release), confirm what the 2010 Direct Master Agreement covers and whether
it is being conflated, and settle the cabinet-approval date of the Digital Transformation of Government
Roadmap. Record an as-of date for each.
