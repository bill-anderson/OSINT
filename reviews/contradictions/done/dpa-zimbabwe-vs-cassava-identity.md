# Is "Distributed Power Africa" one company or two?

## The claim in dispute

Whether the **Distributed Power Africa** that signed South African solar-wheeling power purchase
agreements is the **same registered company** as the **Distributed Power Africa (Private) Limited** that
was renamed Econet InfraCo and listed in Zimbabwe — or two distinct companies sharing a brand within the
same founder's group.

## Competing accounts

**A — DPA is a Cassava Technologies (South Africa) renewable-energy business.**
Asserted by Africa Data Centres in trade-press interviews: DPA, "which is also part of Cassava
Technologies", is counterparty to a 20-year PPA signed March 2023 wheeling solar power across Eskom's grid
to the Cape Town CPT1 facility, and to a further 20-year PPA (Nov 2024) for a Free State solar farm.
Held source URLs:
- https://www.itweb.co.za/article/africa-data-centres-adding-6mw-capacity-in-cape-town/LPp6VMrPnbxMDKQz (2024-07-25)
- https://www.itweb.co.za/article/africa-data-centres-expands-by-20-megawatts-to-meet-cloud-ai-demand/xnklOqzZAOJqbXWJ (2024-11-20)

**B — Distributed Power Africa (Private) Limited is a Zimbabwean subsidiary of Econet Wireless Zimbabwe.**
Asserted by the company itself: EWZL's stakeholder notice states it consolidated its real estate and
passive telecoms infrastructure into "a dedicated real estate entity, Distributed Power Africa (Private)
Limited, trading as Econet InfraCo", "a wholly owned subsidiary of the Group". The company subsequently
converted from private to public (new Certificate of Incorporation 24 March 2026, registration number
74120A02122025) and began a name change to Econet InfraCo Limited.
Held source URLs:
- https://www.econet.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Econet-Stakeholders-Notice-24-Dec-2025.pdf (2025-12-24)
- https://econetinfracoinvestor.com/econet-infraco-supplementary-pre-listing-statement-to-the-pre-listing-statement-dated-wednesday-04-february-2026/ (2026-03-26)

## Provenance gaps

No shareholding, group-structure or registry document tying the two is held. The registration number
74120A02122025 is held only as quoted in account B; no companies-registry extract is held for either
entity. Account A never gives DPA a country of registration — only "part of Cassava Technologies" and
"DPA Southern Africa".

## Research brief (paste-ready)

Two sets of documents describe a company called Distributed Power Africa. One describes it as a renewable
energy business within Cassava Technologies signing long-term solar power purchase agreements for data
centres in South Africa. The other describes Distributed Power Africa (Private) Limited as a
Zimbabwe-registered, wholly owned subsidiary of Econet Wireless Zimbabwe Limited, which in December 2025
received that company's real estate and passive telecommunications infrastructure and now trades as Econet
InfraCo, converting to a public company in March 2026 under registration number 74120A02122025.

Investigate whether these are the same registered legal entity, two companies sharing a brand within
related groups, or a parent and a national subsidiary. Useful evidence would be companies-registry records
in Zimbabwe and South Africa, group structure charts, and the Econet InfraCo Pre-Listing Statement dated
4 February 2026. Record an as-of date for each finding, and note the date and jurisdiction of incorporation
for each entity found.
