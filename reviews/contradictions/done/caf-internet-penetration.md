# Contradiction — Central African Republic internet penetration

**Claim in dispute:** the share of CAR's population using the internet (≈2025).

**Competing values:**
- **~10.6%** — the figure currently on the wiki's CAF hub (older baseline).
- **~12% (≈670,000 users), end-2025** — DataReportal/Kepios, cited in two staged sources.
  - Source held: https://agenceecofin.com — "Le fonds du service universel prévu pour 2026" (2026-04-21)
  - Source held: https://www.connectingafrica.com — "Starlink beams into Central African Republic" (2026-03-17)
- **~14% (86% NOT using the internet in 2024)** — ITU, cited by Agence Ecofin.
  - Source held: https://agenceecofin.com — "Starlink — entre couverture nationale et défis d'accessibilité" (2026-03-17)
- **"rose from 15.5% at the beginning of 2025 to around 30% currently"** — CAR government officials, no
  methodology given (roughly triple the independent figures).
  - Source held: Africa Business Insight — "CAR seeks internet link through South Sudan" (2026-05-25)

**Instruction:** four incompatible penetration figures span the same ~18 months, with the ministry's ~30%
roughly triple the independent DataReportal/ITU values (~12–14%). Investigate the discrepancy and record an
as-of date and basis for each; the ministry claim in particular needs a methodology check.
