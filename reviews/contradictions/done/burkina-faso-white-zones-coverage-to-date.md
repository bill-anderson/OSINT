# Contradiction: how many localities in Burkina Faso already had mobile/internet coverage restored before the 750-locality phase — 138, 223, 330, 370 or 441?

**Opened:** 2026-07-20 (BFA ingest chunk, "Zéro zone blanche")

## The dispute

Burkina Faso's "Zéro zone blanche" programme is measured against a denominator of **~2,000 uncovered
localities**. But the *numerator* — how much was already done before the funded 750-locality phase — is
reported five different ways, with two different units (**sites** vs **localités**) and two different
baselines (**2019** vs **2020**). Any "progress to date" statement therefore rests on an unstable base.

- **Claim A — 223, since 2020.**
  - Asserted by: **Sidwaya**, 2025-09-30, quoting the minister — worded as **"223 *sites* depuis 2020"**.
  - Source URL held: https://www.sidwaya.info/services-de-communications-electroniques-750-zones-bientot-couvertes/
  - Asserted by: **Digital Magazine Burkina**, 2026-02-18 — worded as **"223 *zones* … entre 2020 et 2024"**,
    credited to the FASU universal-service fund.
  - Source URL held: https://digitalmagazine.bf/2026/02/18/digital-burkina-faso-focus-sur-les-12-chantiers-strategiques-du-ministere-de-la-transition-digitale-cas-du-projet-zero-zone-blanche/
  - *Note: the same number is given as "sites" by one outlet and "zones" by the other — direct evidence that
    the units are being used interchangeably in the reporting, whether or not they are the same thing.*

- **Claim B — 138, in 2024.**
  - Asserted by: **Sidwaya**, 2025-07-25, quoting the minister — **"en 2024, 138 « zones blanches » ont été
    couvertes"** (a single-year figure, not a cumulative one).
  - Source URL held: https://www.sidwaya.info/couverture-des-zones-blanches-la-ministre-aminata-zerbo-inspecte-des-sites-de-telecommunications/
  - Asserted by: **leFaso.net**, 2026-02-19 — but framed differently, as **"une première phase ayant déjà
    permis la réalisation de 138 *sites*"**, i.e. as a *phase* total rather than a calendar-year total.
  - Source URL held: https://lefaso.net/spip.php?article144560

- **Claim C — 370 localités, by early 2026.**
  - Asserted by: **Digital Magazine Burkina**, 2026-02-18 — **"370 localités avaient été connectées dès le
    début de l'année 2026"**, presented as progress *against the 750 target*, not against the ~2,000 total.
  - Source URL held: https://digitalmagazine.bf/2026/02/18/digital-burkina-faso-focus-sur-les-12-chantiers-strategiques-du-ministere-de-la-transition-digitale-cas-du-projet-zero-zone-blanche/

- **Claim D — 330, since 2019.** **NO SOURCE HELD — provenance gap.**
  - Attributed in working notes to **Burkina24, 2025-09-30**, but no such item is in the corpus and no URL
    is on file. The figure cannot currently be verified or cited.

- **Claim E — 441 sites, of which 126 rural.** **NO SOURCE HELD — provenance gap.**
  - Attributed in working notes to a **Prime Minister's statement of 2026-01-30**; no item of that date and
    no URL is on file. The figure cannot currently be verified or cited.

## The question

These figures may well be reconcilable rather than genuinely contradictory — 138 could be a calendar-2024
subset of a cumulative 223; 330-since-2019 could be 223-since-2020 plus a 2019 tranche; 441 could count
*transmission sites* where 370 counts *localities served* (one site can serve more than one locality, and
one locality can need more than one site). But no held source states the definitions, so this is currently
unresolved.

Establish, with an as-of date for each:

1. What does the programme count as a unit — a **telecom site/pylon**, or a **locality served**? Are the two
   ever equal, and what is the typical ratio?
2. Is the baseline **2019** or **2020**, and what changed at that date (fund creation, programme launch,
   reporting change)?
3. Is 138 a **calendar-2024** figure or a **phase-1** figure? Both framings appear, and they are not the
   same claim.
4. What is the **cumulative** number of localities with restored coverage, on a single consistent
   definition, at the most recent available date?
5. Do **330** (attributed to Burkina24, Sept 2025) and **441 sites / 126 rural** (attributed to the Prime
   Minister, Jan 2026) actually exist as published statements? If so, supply the URLs; if not, they should
   be struck from circulation.

## Note

`wiki/places/BFA.md` states the 223 (2020–2024) and 370 (early 2026) figures **dated and attributed**, and
carries an inline caveat that progress-to-date counts are reported inconsistently. **No single cumulative
"coverage to date" figure is asserted bare** on any synthesis page pending this reconciliation.

This is a *numerator* question only. It does **not** disturb the separately-resolved **denominator and
horizon** finding (~2,000 white zones total; 750 by end-2026, a further 750 announced for 2027, all ~2,000
by 2030) recorded on `wiki/places/BFA.md`.

## Related provenance gap

Every source in the corpus for this programme is **press reporting**. No ARCEP or MTDPCE primary document
is held, and the ministry publishes substantively via **Facebook**, which is not a sweep target — so the
definitional answer to Q1–Q3 above is unlikely to arrive through the existing acquisition route. (Already
filed as a gap; noted here because it is the direct cause of this contradiction being unresolvable from
held material.)
