# Provenance hunt: are "JO-87" and Presidential Decree 25-350 the same Algerian data-governance instrument, or two texts?

**Queued:** 2026-07-20 (migrated from gaps.md)

## The competing references

- **Claim A — the national data-governance framework rests on Presidential Decree No. 25-350, dated
  30 December 2025.**
  - Asserted by: a We Are Tech Africa report, which is held.
- **Claim B — a "JO-87" national data-governance / cyber strategy.**
  - Asserted by: an earlier LinkedIn lead. "JO-87" may be an Official Journal issue number rather than an
    instrument name.

## Sources held

The We Are Tech Africa report is held and supplies the Decree 25-350 reference. **No source is held for the

## Instruction

Establish whether "JO-87" and Presidential Decree No. 25-350 of 30 December 2025 denote the same Algerian

## Resolution (2026-07-20)

**Resolved: they are the same instrument, and neither reference was correctly stated.**

"JO-87" is not an instrument name — it is *Journal Officiel de la République Algérienne Démocratique et
Populaire* **issue n° 87 of 30 December 2025**, the gazette issue. That issue carries **Presidential Decree
n° 25-320 of 30 December 2025** establishing the national framework for the governance of public data
(pp. 4–6, 20 articles across four chapters, signed by President Tebboune), published alongside Decree
n° 25-321 approving the National Information-Systems Security Strategy 2025–2029.

**No act numbered 25-350 appears in that issue.** The We Are Tech Africa report mis-numbers the decree; the
correct citation is **Presidential Decree n° 25-320 of 30 Dec 2025, *JO* n° 87**. Verified against the
JORADP text directly, 2026-07-20, now held at
`raw/2025-12-30-joradp-decret-25-320-gouvernance-donnees.md`.

Entity page, DZA hub, `algeria--gov-protect` and both indexes carry the corrected citation with the
mis-numbering annotated. Closed alongside `done/algeria-data-governance-decree-number.md`.
