# Contradiction: what was Chad's internet penetration rate in 2024 and 2025 — 22.5% or 13.2%?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (SWZ·SYC·TCD·TGO batch)

## The dispute

Two accounts, **both tracing to the same publisher (DataReportal)**, give sharply different
internet-penetration rates for Chad one year apart. The implied direction of travel is a
**9.3-point fall in a single year**, against the same publisher's own reported **+9.2% growth
in user numbers** over the preceding year. A collapse of that size in national internet access,
unremarked, is implausible on its face.

- **Claim A — 22.5% penetration, 4.18 million internet users, as of January 2024.**
  - Also states users grew by 352,000 (+9.2%) between January 2023 and January 2024, and that
    14.38 million people (77.5% of the population) remained offline.
  - Asserted by: DataReportal / Simon Kemp, *Digital 2024: Chad* (published 2024-02-23) — the
    publisher asserting it directly, in its own report.
  - Source URL held: https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2024-chad
- **Claim B — 13.2% of the population had internet access at the start of 2025.**
  - Asserted by: We Are Tech Africa (published 2025-12-29), **relaying DataReportal second-hand**
    in a piece about Chad's talks with Huawei.
  - Source URL held: https://www.wearetech.africa/en/fils-uk/news/tech/chad-steps-up-rollout-of-tchad-connexion-2030-in-talks-with-huawei
  - **No URL is held for the underlying *Digital 2025: Chad* report itself.** The primary for the
    13.2% figure is not on file — that is itself a provenance gap.

## Likely reconciliation to test

The most probable explanation is a **methodology revision between the publisher's 2024 and 2025
editions** (the publisher cautions in both editions that its figures carry reporting lags and are
periodically rebased), rather than a real fall in access. But no source on file states this, and
it should not be assumed. Establish, with an as-of date for each: (1) what *Digital 2025: Chad*
itself states, as against the relayed figure; (2) whether the publisher rebased its Chad
penetration series between editions, and on what basis; (3) which figure, if either, matches the
ITU's own series for Chad over the same period.

Note a third figure sits nearby and must not be mixed in: the same 2025 relay reports "sharp
urban/rural disparities", and *Digital 2024* separately reports 13.06 million cellular mobile
connections (70.4% of population) — mobile **connections** are not internet **users**, and the
publisher says so explicitly.

## Instruction

These sources report sharply different values for the same metric one year apart, both attributed
to the same publisher, in a direction that contradicts that publisher's own reported growth rate.
Investigate the discrepancy and suggest a resolution, recording an as-of date for each value.
