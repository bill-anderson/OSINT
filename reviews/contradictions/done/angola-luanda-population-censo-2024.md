# Contradiction: what is Luanda's population per Censo 2024 — 7.96 million or over 8.6 million?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (AGO batch)

## The dispute

The **same newspaper** gives two different figures for Luanda province's population, **both attributed to
the definitive results of Angola's 2024 census (Censo 2024)**, four months apart.

- **Claim A — 7,961,235 people counted in Luanda.**
  - Asserted by: **Novo Jornal**, 2025-11-26.
  - Source URL held: https://novojornal.co.ao/sociedade/detalhe/quase-metade-dos-angolanos-sem-bilhete-de-identidade---so-em-luanda-ha-mais-de-24-milhoes-de-pessoas-sem-este-documento-de-identificacao-68397.html
- **Claim B — "mais de 8,6 milhões" (24.1% of the national total).**
  - Asserted by: **Novo Jornal**, 2026-03-17.
  - Source URL held: https://www.novojornal.co.ao/sociedade/detalhe/governo-fixa-bilhete-de-identidade-original-como-exigencia-para-registo-de-obitos-no-pais-70493.html

## The question

Both cite Censo 2024 for Luanda, yet differ by ~650,000. Is the difference *counted* population vs
*projected/resident* population, a preliminary-vs-definitive revision, or an error in one piece? Establish
which figure is the definitive census count for Luanda, with an as-of date. (The national total is
consistently given as 36,604,681.) The primary is the INE / Censo 2024 definitive results — hold both
Novo Jornal figures until the INE figure is confirmed.
