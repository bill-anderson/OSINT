# Contradiction: does Malawi have an enacted comprehensive data-protection law (as of mid-2025)?

**Opened:** 2026-07-16 (MUS·MWI·NAM·NER batch)

## The dispute

Two sources disagree about whether Malawi had an **enacted, comprehensive data-protection law** in force in 2024–2025.

- **Claim A — No enacted law; the Bill is stalled.** A May-2025 article on Malawi's mandatory biometric SIM registration states Malawi has **no enacted comprehensive data-protection law** and that the Data Protection Bill remained stalled, framing this as a gap for a programme that collects biometric data.
  - Asserted by: ID Tech Wire, "Malawi to Launch Mandatory Biometric SIM Registration in July 2025" (published 2025-05-21).
  - Source URL held: https://idtechwire.com/malawi-to-launch-mandatory-biometric-sim-registration-in-july-2025

- **Claim B — The Data Protection Act was enacted in 2024.** A legal review describes the **Malawi Data Protection Act, 2024** as an enacted statute (assented/passed in early 2024) and analyses its provisions.
  - Asserted by: CIPIT (Strathmore), "Review of the Malawi Data Protection Act 2024" (published 2024-06-25).
  - Source URL held: https://cipit.strathmore.edu/review-of-the-malawi-data-protection-act-2024/

## Likely reconciliation to test

The two may be reconcilable rather than truly contradictory: the **Act may be enacted but not yet operational** (implementing regulations not issued, or the data-protection authority/commissioner not yet established), which a journalist could compress into "no data-protection law in force." Confirm: (1) the exact status of the Malawi Data Protection Act 2024 (assent date; commencement/entry-into-force; whether regulations and a supervisory authority exist as of mid-2025); and (2) whether the SIM-registration article is simply imprecise or refers to a genuine operational gap.

## Instruction

These sources report different states of Malawi's data-protection law. Investigate the discrepancy and suggest a resolution, recording an as-of date for each fact (enactment vs commencement vs operational enforcement).
