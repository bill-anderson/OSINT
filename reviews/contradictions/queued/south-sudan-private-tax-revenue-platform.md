- **A privately-owned national tax and revenue collection platform, associated with Adut Salva Kiir
  Mayardit, functions as load-bearing fiscal infrastructure in South Sudan.**
  - Asserted by: a **Sudans Post synthesis** — the only account held. No primary source names the platform's
    owner, its legal mandate, its operator, or the terms on which it collects public revenue.

If accurate, this is national fiscal infrastructure in private hands, which is a first-order data-governance
question; it currently rests on a single secondary account.

## Sources held

The Sudans Post piece is held. **No primary source URL is on file** — no ministry or revenue-authority
statement, no contract, no company record. That is the provenance gap.

## Instruction

Establish what this platform is: its legal name, who owns and operates it, under what mandate or concession
it collects public revenue, when that arrangement began, and what share of national revenue passes through
it. Seek the revenue authority's or finance ministry's own record and any company registration. Record an
as-of date for each finding, and say plainly where nothing is documented.
