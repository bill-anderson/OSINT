# Provenance hunt: IDEMIA's ten-year contract to upgrade South Africa's ID system

**Queued:** 2026-07-20 (migrated from gaps.md)

## The unrecorded contract

- **IDEMIA holds a ten-year contract to upgrade South Africa's identity system, including the provision of
  80 million smart ID cards and biometric enrolment.**
  - Asserted by: a **second-hand trade report of 2025-02-04** — the only account held, despite this being
    the vendor spine of the Smart ID programme. The contract itself appears nowhere in held material: no
    award date, no value, no scope of works, no term start.

## Sources held

The 2025-02-04 trade report is held. **No award notice, tender record, departmental announcement or company
statement is on file** — that is the provenance gap.

## Instruction

Establish the terms of this contract: the awarding body, the award date, the contract value, the term, and
exactly what is being supplied — card production, enrolment hardware, the underlying identity system, or
some combination. Seek the procurement award record, the department's own announcement, and the supplier's
statement. Record an as-of date, and note any variation or extension.
