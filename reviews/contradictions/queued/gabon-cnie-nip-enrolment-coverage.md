# Provenance hunt: how many people in Gabon actually hold a NIP / CNIE?

**Queued:** 2026-07-20 (migrated from gaps.md)

## The unconfirmed figure

- **The digital-ID relaunch (CNIE / NIP) is Gabon's flagship DPI component**, and the World Bank results
  framework carries an indicator for the "% of population aged 16+ with a NIP".
- **The only value held is the baseline: zero, as of December 2024.** No source in hand gives a current
  enrolment or coverage number, so the wiki can describe the programme but cannot say how far it has got.

## Sources held

The World Bank results framework supplying the December-2024 baseline is held. **No dated coverage figure
from the responsible directorate, the national IT agency, or a later World Bank implementation status report
is on file** — that absence is the gap.

## Instruction

Establish a dated enrolment or coverage figure for Gabon's national identity number and electronic identity
card — how many people are enrolled, and as of when — from the issuing authority's own statements or a
World Bank implementation status report. Record the as-of date with the figure, and note whether the figure
counts enrolments, issued credentials, or unique numbers, since these differ.
