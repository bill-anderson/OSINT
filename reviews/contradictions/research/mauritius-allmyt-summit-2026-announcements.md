# AllMyT Summit 2026 (16–17 July 2026, SVICC Pailles): what was actually announced

All six brief claims are **confirmed as having been made**. The correction the material demands is not
about whether they were said, but about **what kind of thing each is** — and three of the six are
**announcements of intent**, not shipped facts. One is **mis-dated to the summit**.

## Claim-by-claim

**1. AWS memorandum of understanding — CONFIRMED, dated, primary-sourced.**
Signed at the opening ceremony, **16 July 2026**. Confirmed by the **Ministry of Information Technology,
Communication and Innovation / GIS News** (mitci.govmu.org, 16 July 2026): "The opening ceremony was marked
by the launch of the next evolution of My.T as well as the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding between
Mauritius Telecom and Amazon Web Services." Pre-announced by PM Ramgoolam in his own address. AWS's Jyoti
Ball (GM, Sub-Saharan Africa) welcomed it on stage. **Scope is soft**: skills, helping businesses and public
institutions "turn ideas into solutions", a scale pathway for local innovators. **It is an MoU — no
disclosed value, no binding commitment, no dated deliverable.** Write as an MoU, not a deal.

**2. NVIDIA H200 GPUs in MT's data centre — OPERATOR CLAIM, uncorroborated.**
Gungadin, 16 July 2026: *"I'm pleased to let you know that today, we already have H200 available in Mauritius
in our data centre."* No NVIDIA statement, no partner confirmation, no independent verification found.
**B300 "due by the end of the year"** is a **plan for end-2026**, not a fact. Attribute to the CEO, dated,
and do not restate as established capacity.

**3. Mauritius AI Registry (airegistry.mu) — CONFIRMED, but NOT launched at the summit.**
Live at **airegistry.mu since 9 July 2026** — a week *before* the summit (*Journal Du Dimanche*,
2026-07-12: "Accessible depuis le 9 juillet"). MT's own LinkedIn post of 2026-07-10 says "earlier this
week". The summit was a **showcase stop on a promotion tour** (also DevCon 24 July, AI Collective, La Plage
Factory, La Turbine), not the launch. **This is exactly the pattern the brief flagged.** Substance: a
discovery/trust directory of AI models, agents, skills and tools built for the Mauritian context; it
**does not host or sell** the resources — each provider retains control; each listing carries provider
identity, local relevance and, where applicable, institutional validation. Stack open-sourced.

**4. Models-as-a-Service, TM Forum — CONFIRMED, and it is a STANDARDS COLLABORATION, not a launched service.**
Gungadin billed it at the summit as MT "launching what he described as a world first from a telecom
perspective — Models as a Service". The **actual announcement came four days later**: TM Forum, **20 July
2026**, naming MT **founding contributor and co-chair** of the **MODaaS** collaboration (second round of
TM Forum initiative **GB1087**). The work is to *define* controls and conformance criteria — a certification
framework that does not yet exist ("Once complete, the framework is **expected to become** the world's
first…"). **No Models-as-a-Service product is in market.** The summit framing overstates a standards
work-stream as a shipped offering. Useful TM Forum datapoint: 72% of CSP leaders say they are confident in
their production AI's trustworthiness; **only 14% can produce evidence**.

**5. SBM Bank partnership — CONFIRMED as announced, substance is prospective.**
Announced at the summit, 16 July 2026. SBM's Officer in Charge **Gervi Gua** on stage. The substance is an
intent to **create** a shared platform (connectivity + financials + customer onboarding for entrepreneurs) —
Gua speaks conditionally: *"The idea is if we can create this platform, this will help to accelerate
everything."* **No platform live; no terms, value or date disclosed.**

**6. Mauritius–Seychelles cross-border payments with Eboo — ANNOUNCED, unverified.**
Announced at the summit, 16 July 2026, as the "launch of cross-border payment capabilities" between
Mauritius and Seychelles with **Eboo**, a Seychelles fintech (eboo.sc — an electronic payments platform for
merchants and P2P transfer; explicitly "not a bank"). Billed as the first of a series of Indian Ocean
regional payment announcements. **No Eboo statement, no Bank of Mauritius or Central Bank of Seychelles
record, no corroboration of a live service found.** Treat as announced, not verified live.

## Not in the brief — two items of real substance surfaced

**A. America-India Connect subsea cable programme — PM announcement, primary-sourced, significant.**
PM **Ramgoolam** announced at the summit opening (**16 July 2026**, MITCI/GIS record) that **Mauritius will
participate in the America-India Connect subsea cable programme**. Provenance he gave himself: he heard
Google CEO Sundar Pichai describe the project at the **AI Impact Summit, New Delhi, February 2026**, raised
Mauritius's geographic case with **PM Modi**, and Modi took it up with Pichai. This is a **government
commitment to participate**, not a signed landing agreement — no cable-party statement, consortium
confirmation, landing point, capacity or date found. Note Gungadin was pointedly non-specific on subsea in
the same session ("very soon, we will be coming up with some announcements there"), which is consistent with
nothing being contractually closed.

**B. Minister Ramtohul: National Data Governance Strategy + Data Protection Act amendments.**
In the same MITCI record: government commitment to "the implementation of a **National Data Governance
Strategy** and **forthcoming amendments to the Data Protection Act**" for cybersecurity, privacy and online
safety, "particularly for children". Directly core to this wiki; connects to the DPO Regulations 2026, the
EU-adequacy bid and the social-media ID-verification consultations already held.

## Other summit facts worth keeping

- Second edition; **1,000+ participants**; SVICC Pailles; PM Ramgoolam, Minister Ramtohul, Junior Finance
  Minister Dhaneshwar Damry, MT Chairman Sarat Lallah, CEO Gungadin.
- **MyT is 20 years old** — launched 11 July 2006 as the world's first commercial IPTV service.
- **MyTLearn** pilots completed across **ten schools**; deliberate pivot from student-facing tutor to a
  **teacher AI coach** after teacher/parent resistance ("Are we teaching kids that they should outsource
  their thinking?"); goal of a personal AI coach for **every secondary school teacher by 2027**.
- **Creole-speaking AI call-centre agent** ("Kevin") demoed live — fault diagnosis, ticket creation, human
  handoff.
- mytGPT now integrates Microsoft 365, Gmail, Shopify; agentic website generation demoed.
- Other launches: MyT SASE, CCTV managed service, FTTR, MyT Travel (eSIM+roaming), MyT Protect (Norton
  partnership; scam-protection phase later in 2026).
- Partners showcased: AWS, Huawei, Mistral, Fortinet, Lenovo. Eight AI-native scale-ups selected.
