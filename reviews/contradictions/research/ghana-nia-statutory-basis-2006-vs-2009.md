# Ghana NIA statutory basis — research findings

## Verdict

**Claim A ("National Identification Act 2009") is simply wrong. No such Act exists.** No Ghanaian
statute of that short title or that year governs the NIA. Claim B is correct and is confirmed by the
NIA's own published list of governing law.

## The correct statutory stack (as listed by the NIA itself, observed 2026-07-20)

Source: https://nia.gov.gh/legal-and-regulations/ — "Laws and regulations governing the National
Identification Authority (NIA)":

1. **National Identification Authority Act, 2006 (Act 707)** — the establishing Act.
2. **National Identity Register Act, 2008 (Act 750)** — the register legislation.
3. **National Identity Register (Amendment) Act, 2017 (Act 950)**.
4. **National Identity Register Regulations, 2012 (L.I. 2111)**.
5. **National Identity Register (Amendments) Regulations, 2018 (L.I. 2356)**.

## Corroboration from the law reporters

- **GhaLII** (https://ghalii.org/akn/gh/act/2006/707): "National Identification Authority Act, 2006",
  citation **Act 707 of 2006**; published in Ghana Gazette **21 April 2006**; assented **18 April
  2006**; commenced 21 April 2006. Amended by National Identity Register Act 2008 and by the Data
  Protection Act 2012.
- **GhaLII** (https://ghalii.org/akn/gh/act/2008/750): "National Identity Register Act, 2008",
  citation **Act 750 of 2008**; published in Ghana Gazette **15 February 2008**; assented **28

  personal information of individuals by the National Identity Authority for the issue of national
  identity cards…". Amended by the National Identity Register (Amendment) Act 2017 and the Data

- **GhaLII** (https://ghalii.org/akn/gh/act/2017/950/eng@2017-12-06/source): National Identity
  Register (Amendment) Act 2017, **Act 950**, assented **4 December 2017**, gazetted 6 December 2017;
  amends ss.4, 7, 8, 22, 75 of Act 750.
- **Refworld**: "Ghana: Act No. 707 of 2006, National Identification Authority Act", 11 April 2006.
- **Ghana BRR portal** (brr.gov.gh): Act 750, year 2008, date of gazette 15/02/2008; Act 950, year
  2017, date of gazette 06/12/2017.
- **NCA Ghana** hosts both PDFs (nca.org.gh) under the same titles/numbers.
- A **Correction Notice** to Act 707 appeared in Ghana Gazette No. 34 of 23 June 2006, correcting the
  version that had appeared in Gazette No. 28 of 21 April 2006 (GhaLII official-gazette scan). Minor;
  does not disturb the citation.

## Where the "2009" error most likely comes from

No candidate 2009 statute was found in any Ghanaian law database. The year does not correspond to
Act 707 (2006), Act 750 (2008), Act 950 (2017) or L.I. 2111 (2012). It appears to be a straightforward
citation error propagated in secondary reporting, not a real instrument.

## Note

The Acts themselves (Act 707, Act 750, Act 950, L.I. 2111, L.I. 2356) are `instrument` references the
wiki does not hold in full text. PDFs are freely available from nia.gov.gh and nca.org.gh — a fetch
list item, not a contradiction.
