# D-Money port-payment launch year — research findings

## Verdict: the year is 2023, not 2021. The wiki's proxy date was wrong by two years.

| | Date | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Event (launch ceremony)** | **2023-12-10** | La Nation, published 2023-12-11, reports it as having happened **"hier"** |
| **La Nation publication** | 2023-12-11 | the announcer-side account |
| **Developing Telecoms publication** | 2023-12-12 | the held clip (article id 15938) |

The held Developing Telecoms clip says the PM launched it **"on 11 December"** with no year. The wiki
inferred **2021** and marked it `date_source: proxy`. The correct year is **2023**.

**One-day residual discrepancy.** Developing Telecoms says 11 December; La Nation, which was in the
room, says the previous day (10 December). This looks like the classic defect — the secondary
re-report reading the primary outlet's *publication* date as the *event* date. **Prefer 2023-12-10**,
and the year is not in doubt either way.

## Framing correction — this was not a "port workers" launch

The event, at the Kempinski Palace Hôtel, was the launch of **Djibouti's first online payment gateway**
(*"la première plateforme de paiement en ligne"*), built by **Djibouti Télécom** through **D-Money**,
under **Djibouti Vision 2035**. Present: PM **Abdoulkader Kamil Mohamed** (patron), Communications
Minister **Radwan Abdillahi Bahdon**, Djibouti Télécom DG **Mohamed Assoweh Bouh**, D-Money DG **Hibo
Mahamoud**, and — relevantly — **Aboubaker Omar Hadi**, president of the **Ports and Free Zones
Authority**. The ceremony closed with **conventions signed between Djibouti Télécom and multiple
ministries, public companies and local banks** committing them to use the platform.

So the port dimension is real but is **one signatory among many**, not the event's billing. The "port
workers" frame comes from Developing Telecoms via Digital Business Africa. The wiki should describe
this as **a payment-gateway launch with port-community integration among its use cases**, not as a
port-payroll product launch.

## Figures from the held Developing Telecoms clip (unchanged, now correctly dated to Dec 2023)

- Djibouti Telecom projected the port integration could raise D-Money transactions by **~150%**.
- **Djibouti Port Community Systems (DPCS)**: port-community payment transactions ran **2–3 billion
  Djiboutian francs per month** (~US$11.2m–16.9m), of which **11%** were already paid online via DPCS
  integration with **CacBank, BCIMR, EximBank and SalaamBank**.

These are now datable to **December 2023** and should be written dated.

## D-Money timeline established in passing (useful, corrects any drift)

- **June 2020** — D-Money mobile wallet launched (La Nation, 2022-04-11: *"Mis en place en juin 2020"*).
- **2023-12-10** — first online payment gateway launched.
- **2024-09-26** — new D-Money application launched at Bawadi Mall (available from 21 Sept), adding
  **biometric identification**; presided over by Minister Radwan Abdillahi Bahdon, with Djibouti
  Télécom interim DG **Kassim Mahamoud Waïs**.
- Later, undated on the operator site: **bank interconnections** with **BCIMR** and **Bank of Africa**.
