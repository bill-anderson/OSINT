## Verdict: CONCLUSIVE — the correct number is **Décret présidentiel n° 25-320** of 30 December 2025; "25-350" is a press error.

The dispute is settled directly off the primary. The *Journal Officiel de la République Algérienne
Démocratique et Populaire* **N° 87** (64ème année), dated **10 Rajab 1447 / 30 décembre 2025**, carries at
pp. 4–6 the act titled *"Décret présidentiel n° 25-320 du 10 Rajab 1447 correspondant au 30 décembre 2025
portant mise en place d'un dispositif national de gouvernance des données"*, signed Abdelmadjid Tebboune.
The number appears twice in the gazette — once in the issue's *sommaire* and again as the heading of the
enacted text — and the decree runs 20 articles across 4 chapters, matching every substantive description
in the competing accounts. The official PDF was fetched in full from joradp.dz, the Secrétariat Général du
Gouvernement's own server, and its verbatim text is extracted to `new/`.

Two further facts make the reading airtight. First, **no act numbered 25-350 exists in JO N° 87 at all**:
the only presidential decrees in the issue are 25-320 (data governance) and **25-321** (approving the
Stratégie nationale de la sécurité des systèmes d'information 2025-2029), the two sitting consecutively.
Second, the 25-320/25-321 adjacency is itself corroborating — the two texts were signed the same day as a
paired data-governance-plus-cybersecurity package, so the number immediately after 25-320 is accounted for
and there is no room for a 25-350 on that date.

The error is more widespread than the brief assumed, and its shape is diagnostic. **25-350 is not confined
to the English trade outlet**: it also appears in Algerian French-language coverage of the *launch
ceremony* of 9 February 2026 — Le Jour d'Algérie, La Voie d'Algérie and itmag.dz all print "n° 25-350" in
their own editorial voice. Decisively, **La Voie d'Algérie prints both numbers in a single article**: its
own text says "décret présidentiel n°25-350" while the *direct quotation from Prime Minister Sifi Ghrieb*
in the same piece says "décret présidentiel n°25-320". The same PM quotation, reproduced verbatim by APS-
derived outlets (maghrebinfo.dz, press-iaa.com), reads 25-320. So the pattern is a transcription slip that
entered the Feb-2026 launch-coverage boilerplate and propagated between outlets, while the quoted official
speech and the gazette both say 320. The wiki's two 25-320 sources (El Moudjahid, Echorouk) were right.
