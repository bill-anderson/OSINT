# Research: Gabon's data-protection statute

## Verdict

**Resolved.** Gabon has had an operative data-protection statute since 2011, twice amended. The wiki
could name the regulator but not the law; the law is now established.

## The chain of instruments

1. **Loi n°001/2011 du 25 septembre 2011** relative à la protection des données à caractère personnel.
   - Promulgated by **Décret n°01098/PR du 25 septembre 2011**.
   - Published **Journal Officiel n°74, 24–31 October 2011** (pp. 491, 511).
   - Article 15 created the **CNPDCP** (Commission nationale pour la protection des données à caractère
     personnel).
   - Text: https://www.afapdp.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Gabon-Loi-relative-a-la-protection-des-donnees-personnelles-du-4-mai-2011.pdf
   - AFAPDP noted Gabon as the **39th francophone state (of 75)** and **8th francophone African state**
     to adopt such a law.

2. **Ordonnance n°00002/PR/2020 du 31 janvier 2020** modifiant et complétant certaines dispositions de
   la loi n°001/2011.
   - Amended articles 13, 14, 20, 37, 101, 102, 104.
   - Created **Article 40 bis**: the **RPPVP** levy (Redevance pour la Protection des Données
     Personnelles et de la Vie Privée), funding the authority via an accounting agent attached to the
     CNPDCP, reversed to the Trésor Public on a set distribution key.
   - Introduced the **DPD** (délégué à la protection des données), designable inside or outside public
     or private bodies, and which may be a natural OR legal person.
   - URL: https://journal-officiel.ga/4803-00002-pr-

3. **Loi n°025/2023** portant modification de la loi n°001/2011 — **the current governing text**.
   - **Article 7** creates the **APDPVP** (Autorité pour la Protection des Données Personnelles et de la
     Vie Privée), replacing the CNPDCP.
   - Promulgated by **Décret n°166/PR du 12 juillet 2023**.
   - Published **Journal Officiel n°218 Bis du 15 juillet 2023**.
   - URLs: https://journal-officiel.ga/20085-025-2023-/ ;
     https://www.apdpvp.ga/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Gabon-Loi-025-2023-du-12-juillet-2023-portant-modification-de-la-loi-001-2011-du-25-septembre-relative-a-la-protection-des-donnees-a-caractere-personnel.pdf

## Date discrepancy on Loi n°025/2023 — noted, minor, NOT a contradiction

Two dates circulate for the same instrument:

- **9 July 2023** — the form used by the **Journal Officiel's own record header** ("Loi N° 025/2023 du
  09/07/2023") and by **DLA Piper** and **Clym** (which gives "July 9, 2023: adopted").
- **12 July 2023** — the form used by the **APDPVP's own hosted PDF** and by **AFAPDP** ("Loi 025-2023
  du 12 juillet 2023"), matching **Décret n°166/PR du 12 juillet 2023** (the promulgation decree).

Most likely reading: **adopted/dated 9 July, promulgated 12 July, published 15 July 2023**. This is a
citation-form difference, not a factual conflict — both point to one instrument, JO n°218 Bis. The wiki
should cite it as *Loi n°025/2023 (promulgated 12 July 2023, JO n°218 Bis of 15 July 2023)* and note the
9-July adoption date.

## Substance of the 2023 law (relevant to wiki topics)

- Rights: access (art. 43–44), rectification (51), **erasure** (53), restriction (55), **portability**
  (58), objection (60–61), **objection to solely-automated decisions incl. profiling** (66).
- Art. 75 prohibits processing revealing racial/ethnic origin, political/philosophical/religious opinion,
  union membership, **biometric and genetic data**, health and sexual life — directly relevant to the
  CNIE/NIP biometric build.
- Art. 78: automated processing must be **declared** to the APDPVP, with exceptions (arts. 80, 81, 82, 111).
- Art. 74: lawful processing of a child's data at **18 years**.
- The 2023 text adds an extensive modern definitions article (art. 6) covering AI/ML terms — algorithm,
  supervised/unsupervised learning, adversarial-example attacks, blockchain, cookies, ad exchange,
  accountability, DPIA, certification, standard contractual clauses.
- **Sanctions** (per Clym's reading, secondary): fines CFA 1m–100m, plus up to CFA 10m for severe
  breaches; suspension of processing and criminal penalties possible.
