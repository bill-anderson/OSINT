# SOCATEL "National Digital Hub" — RESOLVED (the dataset line was real, and is now dated)

The uncited dataset line — *SOCATEL National Digital Hub, planned Tier III data centre, Bangui* — has a real, dated origin. It is the **Greenline Technologies / SOCATEL memorandum of understanding**.

- **Event:** MoU for the **privatisation, revitalisation and transformation of SOCATEL**, awarded to **Greenline Technologies Inc.** (US; HQ Cleveland, Ohio; UK office Milton Keynes; part of Octopian Global Services).
- **Date and place:** signed **18 September 2025, Casablanca**, in the presence of **President Touadéra**, at the **Ambition28 / PND 2024-2028 financing roundtable** — the same Casablanca roundtable the wiki already records for the DUNIA platform.
- **Content:** "the foundation for building a national digital hub at the heart of Africa, complete with a new **Tier 3 data center** and a modern connectivity ecosystem." **$150m** initial investment; cybersecurity frameworks; telecom infrastructure expansion.
- **Status as of 2026-07-17:** moved to an **operational phase**. Meeting **16 July 2026** between Digital Economy Minister **Roger Andjalandji** and Greenline VP of operations **Max Sicari**. Definitive takeover agreements, a joint steering committee and a deployment schedule are **still to be finalised** — Ecofin explicitly notes the deal structure, the state/Greenline responsibility split and the financing modalities are **not yet determinable**.

**So Claim A and Claim B both stand, correctly dated:** a Tier III data centre associated with SOCATEL **is** in prospect (MoU 2025-09-18; operational phase 2026-07-16), and CAR **still has no built national data centre** (nothing commissioned as of 2026-07-20). The 2022 Greater Internet Freedom baseline is not contradicted.

## Two corrections this surfaces for the wiki

1. The **Minister of the Digital Economy is Roger Andjalandji as of 2026-07-16** — the wiki records Justin Gourna-Zacko (7 sources). A change of officeholder, not a contradiction, but it dates the wiki's material.
