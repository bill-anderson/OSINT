# Where is Somalia's national-ID (SNID) data hosted — Somalia or Pakistan?

## Verdict: PARTIALLY RESOLVED — the two claims are reconcilable as a dated trajectory (offshore hosting acknowledged by the Somali government through at least 2025-05; NIRA asserts on-shore storage from 2025-11), but the CURRENT (mid-2026) physical location of the SNID production database is NOT conclusively established by any primary found.

## Prose

The dispute is best read as a **direction of travel with an unclosed endpoint**, not a straight factual conflict.

The strongest new evidence is **not** from NIRA or NADRA but from Somalia's **Ministry of Communications and Technology (MoCT)**, which twice stated on the record — **2024-04-28** and **2025-05-06** — that the National Data Center then under construction in Mogadishu would "eliminate dependencies of national data on international data storage solutions, **that is stored outside the country**" and would end "the need to host Somali government data outside the country." These are Somali-government primaries conceding, in the present tense, that **government data was hosted offshore** at those dates. That materially corroborates the *direction* of Garowe's Claim B from a far better source than Garowe's anonymous analyst — though note the MoCT statements concern **Somali government data generally**, not SNID specifically. This is corroborating context, not direct proof about the ID database.

Against that, **NADRA's own release of 2025-03-13** records that it delivered "advanced data center infrastructure" to Somalia alongside card-personalisation machines — i.e. on-shore capacity was being installed during 2025. NIRA separately launched HUBIYE, e-Aqoonsi and CDS in **2025-05**, describing them as "entirely developed by Somali engineers at NIRA," which cuts against the "NIRA staff only handle the front end" allegation for the *application* layer at least. The NIRA DG's **2025-11-17** assertion to Hiiraan that data "is stored securely within Somalia" therefore post-dates real on-shore build-out and is not obviously false — but it is an **unverified official assertion**, offered without a named facility, and no primary was found that independently confirms the SNID production database physically sits in Somalia.

Three unresolved threads keep this from being conclusive:

1. **The National Data Center had not launched.** The World Bank's SCALED-UP restructuring paper (2025-10) lists the NDC launch as *still pending* and extends the project to **2026-06-30** precisely to complete it. So the facility that was supposed to end offshore hosting was not operational as of late 2025.
2. **NIRA procured commercial colocation, not NDC space.** A contract award — "Data Center colocation service for the National Identification and Registration Authority (NIRA) of Somalia," ref 4500377, **USD 179,220**, published **2026-02-25** (a second at USD 238,960 also appears) — indicates NIRA leases third-party rack space. The award's location is behind a paywall. Somali colocation providers such as Dalkom operate facilities in **Mogadishu, Nairobi and via UAE partners**, so a colocation contract does not by itself establish on-shore hosting.
3. **No hosting or data-localisation clause is public.** The 2018 grant agreement and the SNIDS Addendum-1/-2 (2025-03) texts are not published. Nothing found states whether the NADRA arrangement contains a localisation or hosting term, or who administers production. That is itself a **provenance gap**.

Notably, the Interior Minister and NIRA DG travelled to **Islamabad on 2026-01-23** for talks on "the development and management of Somalia's national identity infrastructure" and "the exchange of expertise to ensure the integrity and security" of the system — continued deep NADRA operational involvement two months *after* the DG's on-shore assurance. This is consistent with either reading and settles nothing.

## Route (b) — has the National Data Center launched? No, as of 2026-07-20

Checked MoCT, NCA and NIRA official channels plus Somali press. **The most recent official statement on

Timeline held: construction commenced **2023-11-15** (NCA); first ministerial tour **2024-04-28**;

**The silence is evidential, not merely absent.** MoCT/NCA/NIRA publicise launches heavily and

- **2026-01-05** — Council of Ministers approves the Public Data Protection Law regulatory framework (MoCT)

- **2026-06-28** — NIRA + UNDP launch the National ID and Public Service Integration Working Group

A government that announced a CIRT launch with the Prime Minister presiding would not have

National Data Center has not been announced as operational**, past the World Bank SCALED-UP revised
closing date of **2026-06-30**. Stated as a negative finding, with its basis, rather than as proof.
