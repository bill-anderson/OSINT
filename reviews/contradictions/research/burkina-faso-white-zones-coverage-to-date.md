# Research: the Burkina Faso "zéro zone blanche" numerator

## Headline

**Resolved.** All five figures exist as published statements, and four of the five reconcile once two
distinctions are applied: **(a) sites ≠ localities**, and **(b) calendar-year increments ≠ cumulative
totals ≠ project targets**. The two "no source held" figures both turned out to be real.

## Q5 — do the unsourced figures exist? YES, both

**Claim D — 330 since 2019: EXISTS.**
Burkina24, 2025-09-30, Akim Ky, "L'inclusion par le pylône".
https://burkina24.com/2025/09/30/linclusion-par-le-pylone-comment-le-burkina-faso-veut-gommer-la-fracture-numerique-dici-2027/
Verbatim: *"Le déploiement s'accélère avec 750 sites mutualisés prévus, s'ajoutant aux 330 zones déjà
connectées depuis 2019."*

**Critical note.** This is an **unattributed closing sentence by the journalist**, not a quoted
official. It reports the **same 2025-09-30 Ouahigouya launch event** at which the minister, per
Sidwaya, said **"223 sites depuis 2020"** and per Pravda/leFaso **"223 localités entre 2020 et 2024"**.
So 330/2019 and 223/2020 are the *same event covered by different outlets*. The ministerial figure is
the better-provenanced of the two. No source anywhere states what the 2019 baseline is or what the
extra ~107 comprises.

**Claim E — 441 sites / 126 rural: EXISTS.**
Prime Minister **Rimtalba Jean Emmanuel Ouédraogo**, **Situation de la Nation** address to the
Assemblée législative de Transition, **2026-01-30**.
- Zoodomail, 2026-01-30: https://www.zoodomail.com/node/4036
- Faso Voxpress, 2026-02-01: https://fasovoxpress.com/burkina_faso-750-nouvelles-localites-rurales-connectees-a-la-telephonie-et-a-internet/
Verbatim: *"le déploiement et l'activation des services de téléphonie et d'Internet dans 441 sites dont
126 réalisés en zone rurale."*

**This figure was being read wrongly.** It is **not** a white-zone cumulative. It is a 2025 activity
total for **all** sites deployed and activated, of which **only 126 were rural**. The white-zone
programme is by definition rural. So the comparable number in this statement is **126**, not 441.
Corroborated in shape by Mousso News (2026-03-04): *"À ce jour, 434 sites ont déjà été déployés"*
alongside *"Plus de 200 localités ont été connectées pour la toute première fois"* — again two
different units in one paragraph.

## Q1 — site or locality? A dated primary answers it

**Sidwaya, 2022-05-23** (field visit 2022-05-20), quoting **ARCEP** president Tontama Charles Millogo
and project chief **Soumanan Sanou**:
https://www.sidwaya.info/couverture-du-burkina-faso-en-reseau-mobile-environ-300-zones-blanches-rayees-de-la-carte/

- **183 sites (antennes)** installed under phase 1 of the ARCEP-piloted "Service universel" project.
- Those 183 sites **covered "environ 300 zones blanches"**.
- Each antenna has a range of **~6 km**; the other two operators **can co-locate on the same pylon**.

So: **one site serves more than one locality — roughly 1.6:1 in phase 1.** The units are *not*
interchangeable, even though (per the brief's own finding) outlets swap the words freely. Phase-1 cost
was **>13bn FCFA**, Orange bearing **>60%** under a PPP. Note also a **different denominator** in 2022:
*"300 zones blanches sur 11 000 recensées sur le plan national"* — against the ~2,000 used from 2025.

## Q3 — is 138 calendar-2024 or phase-1? Calendar-2024

- Pravda/leFaso, 2025-09-30: *"138 zones blanches avaient déjà été couvertes **en 2024** grâce à une
  subvention de l'État à travers le FASU."*
- Afrique IT News, 2025-10-01: *"seules 138 localités avaient été couvertes **en 2024**, et **183 en
  2022**."* — explicitly a year series.
- Sidwaya, 2025-07-25 (held): *"**en 2024**, 138 « zones blanches » ont été couvertes."*

Three sources say calendar-2024. **leFaso.net's 2026-02-19 "une première phase ayant déjà permis la
réalisation de 138 sites" is the outlier and is wrong** — the actual first phase was the 183-site ARCEP
project of 2022.

## Q4 — the cumulative number, and a correction the wiki needs

**Primature du Burkina Faso (government primary), 2026-02-03** —
https://primature.gov.bf/contrat-dobjectifs-2025-91-pour-la-ministre-de-la-transition-digitale/

*"En matière de connectivité, **370 nouvelles localités ont été interconnectées** aux réseaux de
téléphonie"* — stated as the **2025** result in the minister's contrat-d'objectifs evaluation (scored
91%). And for 2026: *"la couverture de 750 nouvelles localités, **en complément des 370 déjà réalisées
en 2025**."*

**"En complément de" settles it: 370 is the calendar-2025 increment, and the 750 are additional to it.**
Corroborated by NetAfrique (2026-02-03) and Pravda (2026-02-03), both reporting the same evaluation.

**This corrects the wiki.** `wiki/places/BFA.md` currently reads *"370 localities were connected by
early 2026 (of 750 targeted)"* — the "of 750" framing comes from **Digital Magazine Burkina**
(2026-02-18), which wrote *"370 localités connectées sur 750 ciblées"*. That outlet misread a
calendar-year total as project progress. The government's own account says the opposite.

## Q2 — 2019 or 2020? Not resolved

Unresolved, and probably unresolvable from press. The 2022 ARCEP "Service universel" PPP phase
(183 sites / ~300 localities, Orange-financed) is **not obviously the same perimeter** as the
FASU-subsidised 223 of 2020–2024 — yet ~300 by May 2022 cannot sit inside 223 by end-2024. Either the
two counts use different units (likely: ~300 *localities* vs 223 *sites*, exactly the confusion in
Q1), or different funding perimeters. **No source states the definitions.** Burkina24's 330/2019 is
plausibly the ARCEP-phase perimeter, but that is inference, not evidence.
