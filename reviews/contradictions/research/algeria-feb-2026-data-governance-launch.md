# Algeria's February-2026 data-governance launch — provenance hunt

## Verdict: RESOLVED on the launch record; the référentiels are **established as NOT YET PUBLISHED** as of 2026-07-20.

The single most consequential finding is not the launch itself but **Art. 19**: the decree's classification
and cataloguing obligations **do not commence on the decree's publication**. They commence on the
publication of the two Art. 8 *référentiels*, which are issued not by decree but **by decision of the High
Commissioner for Digitalisation**. No such decision has been traced.

## Art. 8 and Art. 19, verbatim from the gazette

From *JO* n° 87 (10 Rajab 1447 / 30 December 2025), pp. 4–6 — the text the wiki already holds at
`raw/2025-12-30-joradp-decret-25-320-gouvernance-donnees.md`:

> **Art. 8.** — Le haut commissariat à la numérisation est chargé […] de l'élaboration, de la mise à jour du
> référentiel de classification des données et du référentiel de catalogage des sources de données et du
> suivi des modalités de leur application. **Les deux référentiels cités à l'alinéa ci-dessus, sont publiés
> par décision du haut commissaire à la numérisation.**

> **Art. 19.** — Les instituions et les administrations publiques s'engagent à procéder à la classification
> de leurs données ainsi qu'au catalogage des sources de leurs données, conformément aux dispositions du
> présent décret, **à compter de la date de publication des deux référentiels cités à l'article 8 ci-dessus.**

So the framework as gazetted is **a commencement-deferred instrument**. Until the High Commissioner
publishes both référentiels, no public institution is under a live classification or cataloguing duty. The
9 February 2026 ceremony launched the *dispositif* politically and installed its follow-up commission; it
did not, on the evidence found, trigger Art. 19.

## What was searched for the référentiels, and not found

Searched in French across Algerian national press, the JORADP corpus and general web (2026-07-20):
`"référentiel de classification des données"` + `décision du haut commissaire`; `référentiel de catalogage
des sources de données`; commencement/`entrée en vigueur` framings. **No décision publishing either
référentiel was surfaced**, in the *Journal Officiel* or in any HCN announcement.

**One decoy to be careful of.** A *"première version du Référentiel national de la gouvernance des
données"* was presented by Benmouloud at an HCN seminar in Algiers on **Thursday 20 February 2025** —
reported by APS, Radio Algérienne, El Watan and Le Jour d'Algérie on 20–22 Feb 2025, under the slogan
*"Sécurité, encadrement et organisation"*. This is **ten months before the decree** and is a different
object: a draft reference framework tied to the IRIES sovereign-network project, not the Art. 8 décision.
It must not be read as satisfying Art. 8.

A later article (Les Enjeux Eco, 2026-07-14, on the national digital-services portal launch) describes the
dispositif as resting on "un référentiel de classification, un catalogue d'indexation et un système national
d'interopérabilité" — but this restates the decree's architecture; it asserts no publication.

**Honest limit:** absence of evidence here is reasonably strong but not proof. A décision of the High
Commissioner may be published in the *Journal Officiel*'s "ARRETES, DECISIONS ET AVIS" section without
attracting press coverage. A JORADP 2026 index sweep would settle it definitively and was not performed.

## The launch event's own record — established

- **Date and place:** Monday **9 February 2026**, Centre international de conférences **Abdelatif-Rahal**,
  Algiers. Under the high patronage of President **Abdelmadjid Tebboune**.
- **Chaired by:** Prime Minister **Sifi Ghrieb**.
- **Slogan:** *"Souveraineté, organisation et transparence"* (rendered by LNR and Le Jour d'Algérie's PDF
  edition as the fuller *"Dispositif national de gouvernance des données : souveraineté, organisation et
  transparence"*).
- **Also present:** High Commissioner for Digitalisation with ministerial rank **Meriem Benmouloud**;
  presidential adviser **Farid Yaici**; members of government and accredited diplomatic corps.
- **Institutional act of the day:** Benmouloud **installed the *commission nationale* charged with
  following up implementation** of the dispositif, composed of representatives of every ministerial
  department and national body. She stated this followed instructions given by the President at the
  **Council of Ministers of 11 December 2025**. Three training workshops accompanied the launch.
- **Corroborating outlets:** APS (state agency, 2026-02-09 14:39), Maghreb Info, PressIA, El Khabar,
  Réflexion, La Nouvelle République, Le Jour d'Algérie, Algérie360, La Voie d'Algérie.

## Decree number: the 25-350 error, re-confirmed

The PM's **quoted words** say *"décret présidentiel n° 25-320 du 30 décembre 2025"* (APS, Maghreb Info,
PressIA, La Voie d'Algérie). Several outlets nonetheless print **25-350 in their own editorial voice**
(Algérie360, Réflexion, Le Jour d'Algérie, La Voie d'Algérie) — the same error the held English source
carries. This reconfirms the already-closed
`reviews/contradictions/done/algeria-data-governance-decree-number.md`. No act numbered 25-350 exists in
*JO* n° 87.

## Companion instruments

- **Decree n° 25-321** of 30 December 2025 — approves the **stratégie nationale de la sécurité des systèmes
  d'information 2025–2029**. **Confirmed verbatim in the same gazette issue**, *JO* n° 87 p. 6, on the
  report of the Minister of National Defence, signed Tebboune. Its Art. 1 notes the strategy is *"annexée à
  l'original du présent décret"* — i.e. **the strategy text itself is not printed in the JO**. That is a
  real transparency limit worth recording: the instrument is public, its content is not.
- **Decree n° 26-07** of 7 January 2026 — mandatory cybersecurity units in public institutions, reportedly
  published in the *JO* of 21 January 2026. **Not verified against the gazette in this pass.** The only
  sources surfacing it are `algeriatech.news`, which is an AI-generated content farm (see below). Treat as
  **unestablished**; it needs the JO issue.
