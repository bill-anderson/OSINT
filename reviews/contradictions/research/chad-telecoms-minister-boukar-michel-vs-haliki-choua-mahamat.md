# Chad telecoms/digital portfolio — successive holders, not competing claims

## Finding

Not a contradiction. **Successive holders of the same portfolio.** No split, no rename.

- **Boukar Michel** — Minister of Telecommunications from **1 January 2024** (Masra government),
  portfolio expanded to *Télécommunications, de l'Économie numérique et de la Digitalisation de
  l'administration* on **27 May 2024** (Halina I), retained through Halina II. Out **1 April 2026**.
- **Haliki Choua Mahamat** — appointed by **décret n°280/PR/PM/2026 du 1er avril 2026** (cabinet
  reshuffle, 37 members, PM Allah-Maye Halina). Took office at the **passation de charges on
  8 April 2026**. Same portfolio title.
- **Abel Maïna** — installed same day as **secrétaire d'État**, a newly created post in the ministry.
- Haliki is a **returning** minister ("le retour de l'ancien ministre") and former **Director-General
  of ARCEP-Tchad**; also previously ministre secrétaire général du gouvernement chargé de la
  Promotion du bilinguisme. Party: Mouvement patriotique du salut.

The wiki's 2026-07-01 US-partnership source naming Haliki is therefore **consistent** — he had held
the post for three months by then. No conflict to resolve.
