# Research: Mauritania's WARDIP digital-ID / national PKI procurement

## Verdict: RESOLVED — the tender is real, fully documented, and **has not been awarded**.
## The manifest's "possible award report" was a different procurement entirely.

### The tender (confirmed, primary held)

**AP-01_DP-15_WARDIP-2026**, credit **IDA-7445-MR**, issued **28 April 2026** by the Unité de Gestion
du Projet **WARDIP-Mauritanie** at the **Ministère de la Transformation Numérique et de la
Modernisation de l'Administration (MTNMA)**. World Bank-financed, international competitive
Request for Proposals, two envelopes.

- **Notice title:** *"Conception, fourniture, installation et mise en œuvre d'un **Système National
  d'identité numérique et d'infrastructure de certification électronique** en deux lots"*
- **Lot 01:** Infrastructure à Clé Publique (ICP) Nationale — the national PKI
- **Lot 02:** a **CMS (Certificate Management System)** and integration of electronic signature
  **with the PKI of the ANRPTS**
- Bid security 1,000,000 MRU per lot; RFP dossier 4,000 MRU
- Evaluation: originally 30% technical / 70% cost
- Beneficial-ownership disclosure required of the winner

URL: https://beta.mr/files/appls/177.pdf (clipped)

**Note a real discrepancy in the notice itself:** the **title** promises a national **digital-identity
system** plus e-certification infrastructure, but the **body (clauses 1 and 2)** describes only a
*"Système national de certification électronique"* in two lots, both of which are PKI/CMS. Both
addenda drop "identité numérique" from the title as well. On the operative text this is a **PKI
procurement that integrates with the ANRPTS's existing identity register** — not a second identity
rail beneath the app layer, as the sweep manifest supposed. The digital-ID framing survives only in
the original notice title.

### Two addenda — the deadline moved twice (not clipped, dated)

- **Additif N°1**, 15 May 2026 — reweights evaluation to **40% technical / 60% cost**; deadline
  18 June → **7 July 2026**; validity to 08/11/2026.
  URL: https://mtnima.gov.mr/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/ADDITIF-N1-AP-PKI-15-05-26.pdf
- **Additif N°2**, 10 June 2026 — deadline 7 July → **21 July 2026** at 12:00 local; validity to
  22/11/2026. (Via BidsFactory/ARMP Mauritania and BETA Conseils.)

### No award exists

As of **2026-07-20**, the **proposal deadline has not yet passed** (21 July 2026) and the financial
envelopes will not be opened until after the technical evaluation. There can be no award.
