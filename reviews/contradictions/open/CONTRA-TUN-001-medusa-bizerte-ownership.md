# CONTRA-TUN-001 — Who owns the Medusa cable's Bizerte landing (Tunisia)?

**Claim in dispute:** ownership of the MEDUSA submarine-cable landing station at Bizerte, Tunisia, and of
the Tunisian section of the cable.

**Competing values:**
- **Tunisie Telecom** presents itself as the operator that landed Medusa in Bizerte, describing a
  1,000+ km, 22 Tbps Bizerte–Marseille fibre pair that increases *its* international capacity eightfold,
  positions itself alongside its ownership of the Hannibal and SEA-ME-WE 4 cables, and (separately) is
  reported to finance "the Tunisia connection" to Medusa via an EBRD loan.
  - *Asserted by:* Tunisie Telecom (via TAP / Zawya), 2025-11-03 — https://www.zawya.com/en/economy/north-africa/tunisie-telecom-announces-landing-of-medusa-submarine-cable-in-bizerte-uuoux8vs
  - *EBRD-financing context:* https://techafricanews.com/2026/01/19/... (EBRD €190m / Tunisie Telecom — landing station's financing partner)
- **Orange Tunisia** states it **owns the Tunisian section of Medusa and the Bizerte landing station**,
  and is the landing partner in both Marseille and Bizerte, with the ViaTunisia France–Tunisia segment
  going live in June 2026 (EU CEF-Digital co-financed).
  - *Asserted by:* Orange Tunisia (via Connecting Africa), 2026-06-05 — https://www.connectingafrica.com/connectivity/medusa-s-viatunisa-subsea-cable-now-live
  - *Asserted by:* Orange Tunisia (via TechAfrica News), 2026-06-26 — https://techafricanews.com/2026/06/26/orange-tunisia-launches-medusa-submarine-cable-to-boost-international-connectivity/

**Possible reconciliation (not verified):** Medusa is a multi-pair, multi-partner open cable system; it is
possible Orange owns the physical landing station and Tunisian section while Tunisie Telecom bought/leased
a 22 Tbps fibre pair and activated it separately — but the sources do not say this, and both use
ownership/"landing" language that reads as primacy.

**Instruction:** These sources report different owners of the Medusa Bizerte landing/Tunisian section.
Investigate the discrepancy and suggest a resolution — establishing who owns the landing station, who owns
or leases which fibre pair(s), and recording an as-of date for each. The four source URLs above are the
only ones currently held.
