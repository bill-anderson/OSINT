# Contradiction: when did Togo's DHIS2-based education MIS start, and is it a pilot or at national scale?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (SWZ·SYC·TCD·TGO batch)

## The dispute

Two pages **from the same publisher (DHIS2)** give different start dates for Togo's DHIS2-based
education management information system, and characterise its maturity differently. The two
questions are entangled: the date one accepts largely determines whether the deployment reads as
a six-year national system or a recent pilot.

- **Claim A — "Since 2019, national scale."**
  - Stated as a structured field on the DHIS2 country implementation profile for Togo.
  - Same page nonetheless describes the ministry as **"piloting"** DHIS2 alongside StatEduc —
    internally at odds with "national scale".
  - Asserted by: DHIS2 (education.dhis2.org), country implementation profile. The page is a
    living profile, not a dated event report; the 2024-11-05 date on file is a page date, so it
    is held `date_source: proxy`.
  - Source URL held: https://education.dhis2.org/implementation/togo
- **Claim B — "In 2021, DHIS2 was implemented in interoperability with StatEduc."**
  - Same article adds that, as of 2024-11-19, **"the DHIS2 EMIS is currently under review by
    Togo's Ministry of Education"** — a maturity claim well short of established national scale.
  - Asserted by: DHIS2 (education.dhis2.org), article by Ellen Summey (published 2024-11-19).
  - Source URL held: https://education.dhis2.org/fr/emis-togo-2022

## Likely reconciliation to test

The dates may be reconcilable as **two different milestones** — 2019 as project inception or first
deployment, 2021 as the StatEduc interoperability go-live — in which case neither is wrong and the
wiki simply needs both, dated and labelled. The publisher does not say so, and it should not be
assumed. The maturity question is the sharper one and may not reconcile: "national scale" (profile)
against "piloting" (same profile) and "under review by the Ministry" (article, Nov 2024) are three
different states.

Establish, with an as-of date for each: (1) what happened in 2019 and what happened in 2021, as
distinct milestones; (2) whether the DHIS2 EMIS is currently a pilot, under ministerial review, or
in national production, and since when; (3) whether "national scale" refers to DHIS2 itself or to
StatEduc, the legacy collection tool it processes data from. Note the underlying URL slug for the
article reads `emis-togo-2022`, a third year — possibly the page's original publication year, worth
checking against the 2024-11-19 byline held.

## Instruction

These sources, from the same publisher, report different start dates and different maturity states
for the same system. Investigate the discrepancy and suggest a resolution, recording an as-of date
for each claim.
