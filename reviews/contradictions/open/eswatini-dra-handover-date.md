# Contradiction: on what date was Eswatini's Digital Readiness Assessment handed over?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (SWZ·SYC·TCD·TGO batch)

## The dispute

Two sources describe what appears to be **the same act** — UNDP Resident Representative Henrik
Franklin handing the Digital Readiness Assessment report to Minister of ICT Savannah Maziya — and
date it five days apart. The tension is not merely which date is right: **one of the two accounts
was published before the date the other assigns to the event**, so they cannot both describe the
same act as reported.

- **Claim A — the handover is reported in an article published 2024-12-05.**
  - ESEPARC (the report's co-author) reports the handover by Franklin. An article published on
    5 December cannot report an event dated 10 December, so either the handover preceded
    2024-12-05, or ESEPARC is describing a different act.
  - Asserted by: ESEPARC (Eswatini Economic Policy Analysis & Research Centre), published 2024-12-05.
  - Source URL held: https://www.separc.co.sz/2024/12/05/eswatini-advances-toward-digital-transformation-key-findings-from-the-digital-readiness-assessment
- **Claim B — the handover took place on 10 December 2024.**
  - UNDP states Franklin handed the report to Maziya on that date.
  - Asserted by: UNDP Eswatini, published 2024-12-10.
  - Source URL held: https://www.undp.org/eswatini/blog/unveiling-eswatinis-digital-path

A third source complicates the sequence rather than resolving it:
- **Biometric Update (published 2024-11-22)** reports that Eswatini "has unveiled the findings" of
  the assessment — three weeks before either handover date.
  - Source URL held: https://www.biometricupdate.com/202411/eswatini-unveils-readiness-report-for-digital-id-transformation-push

## Likely reconciliation to test

Most likely a **sequence of distinct events collapsed into one "release date"**: a validation
workshop (reported as 13 November 2024, Hilton Garden Hotel, Mbabane), a media unveiling of the
findings (~22 November), and a formal institutional handover (early–mid December) — possibly two
handovers, or a technical handover followed by a ceremonial one. If so, the wiki should carry the
dated sequence rather than a single contested release date.

Establish, with an as-of date for each: (1) the date of the validation workshop; (2) the date the
findings were first made public; (3) the date(s) of any formal handover, and whether there was more
than one; (4) whether ESEPARC's 5 December account describes the same act UNDP dates to 10 December.

## Instruction

These sources date what appears to be the same handover five days apart, and one was published
before the date the other assigns to the event. Investigate the discrepancy and suggest a
resolution, recording an as-of date for each claim.
