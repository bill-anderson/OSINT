# Provenance hunt: how many people has Togo's e-ID programme enrolled, and as of when?

**Queued:** 2026-07-20 (migrated from gaps.md)

## The missing dated figure

The wiki holds no cumulative enrolment count at any recent date. What it has:

- **A goal of 8 million people by June 2024**, stated as of December 2023.
  - Asserted by: the World Bank WURI implementation status report (2023-12-21). This is a target, not an
    achievement.
- **6 million unique numbers issued.**
  - Asserted by: a **May-2026 Biometric Update explainer** — but **the wiki cannot tie this figure to a
    stated as-of date from the issuing agency itself**, so it floats.
- The **November-2025 Maritime-region rollout** reports a new enrolment phase but gives **no count of people
  registered to date**.

## Sources held

The WURI implementation status report (2023-12-21), the May-2026 Biometric Update explainer, and the
November-2025 rollout reporting are held. **No agency-issued enrolment statistic with its own as-of date is
on file** — that is the provenance gap.

## Instruction

Establish a cumulative enrolment or issuance figure for Togo's national identification programme that comes
from the issuing agency itself and carries a stated as-of date. Distinguish people enrolled from unique
numbers issued from credentials delivered, since these differ. Record the figure, its basis and its as-of
date.
