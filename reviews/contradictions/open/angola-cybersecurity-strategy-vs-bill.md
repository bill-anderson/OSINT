# Contradiction: does Angola's national cybersecurity framework rest on a Presidential Decree or a law — and are they the same bodies?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (AGO batch)

## The dispute

Two Angolan sources describe what appear to be **two parallel tracks** creating the **same institutions**
— a National Cybersecurity Council and a Centro Nacional de Cibersegurança — and it is unclear whether one
supersedes, enables, or duplicates the other.

- **Claim A — an executive track: a draft Presidential Decree National Cybersecurity Strategy (Dec 2025).**
  - Asserted by: **TechAfrica News**, 2025-12-09.
  - Source URL held: https://techafricanews.com/2025/12/09/angola-unveils-national-cybersecurity-strategy-to-strengthen-digital-resilience/
  - Reports a draft Presidential Decree standing up a National Cybersecurity Council and CERTs.
- **Claim B — a legislative track: a cybersecurity bill, generally approved then stalled.**
  - Asserted by: **Novo Jornal**, 2026-01-23 (general approval, 105–1–75, UNITA abstaining) and 2026-07-08
    (committee discussion suspended unanimously for "inconformidades na redacção, na forma e no conteúdo").
  - Source URLs held:
    https://www.novojornal.co.ao/politica/detalhe/proposta-de-lei-de-ciberseguranca-aprovada-mas-com-a-abstencao-da-unita-que-receia-que-governo-recorra-ao-corte-de-internet-nos-dias-das-eleicoes-e-das-manifestacoes-69381.html
    and https://www.novojornal.co.ao/politica/detalhe/parlamento-deputados-suspendem-discussao-da-proposta-de-lei-da-ciberseguranca-para-melhorar-diploma-controverso-73097.html
  - The bill also creates a Conselho Nacional and a Centro Nacional de Cibersegurança.

## The question

These sources report different instruments (a Presidential Decree strategy vs a parliamentary law) that
appear to establish the **same national cybersecurity bodies**. Do the decree and the bill create rival
structures, or does the decree stand up the strategy/bodies administratively while the law is meant to give
them a statutory basis (and is currently stalled)? Establish the relationship and an as-of date for the
status of each. As of the sources' close (2026-07-08) the **legislative bill is suspended**, while the
strategy's decree status is not independently confirmed as enacted.
