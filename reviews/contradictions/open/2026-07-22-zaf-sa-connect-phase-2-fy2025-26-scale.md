# SA Connect Phase 2 — the ">R3 billion" figure cannot be an FY2025/26 budget line

Filed 2026-07-22 by the budget extraction pass (ZAF FY2024/25 corpus). Attempts: 0.

## The claim in dispute

The scale and fiscal-year attribution of South Africa's **SA Connect Phase 2**
funding: reporting around the 2026 Budget speech attributes "more than R3 billion"
to SA Connect Phase 2 in FY2025/26, but the government's own budget documents make
a single-year line of that size impossible.

A single-FY SA Connect Phase 2 line of >R3bn exceeds the whole department's
FY2025/26 vote. The most plausible reconciliation is that the >R3bn is a
**multi-year Phase 2 programme total** (or includes off-vote/entity financing),
announced at Budget 2026 — but no held source states which.

## Instruction

These sources give incompatible readings of the SA Connect Phase 2 figure —
establish what the "more than R3 billion" covers (which fiscal years; vote
funding vs entity/off-budget financing), against the 2026 Budget documents
(ENE 2026, Vote 30 chapter) or the National Treasury's own SA Connect
announcements, and suggest a resolution with an as-of date for each value.
The held FY2025/26 record should then be reset accordingly.
