# Contradiction — Botswana Tech Fund size (target vs committed)

**Claim in dispute:** the size of the Gaborone-based Botswana Tech Fund venture-capital vehicle.

**Competing values:**
- **£50m (~$67.5m) target.** Reported by press-release-style coverage.
  - Source held: https://www.wearetech.africa — "Botswana Tech Fund Debuts $67.5 Million Vehicle for Southern African Startups" (2026-04-24)
  - Source held: https://techafricanews.com — "Botswana Tech Fund Targets £50m to Accelerate Digital Growth Across Southern Africa" (2026-04-22)
- **£10m committed / £5m first close** (Phase One £5m; £1m pre-seed), Guernsey domicile.
  - Source held: https://techcabal.com — "Botswana Tech Fund sees opportunity where African venture capital rarely flows" (2026-04-27)

The bylined TechCabal account reports a far smaller committed amount and a Guernsey (not Botswana)
domicile than the "£50m/$67.5m" figure in the promotional accounts.

**Instruction:** these sources report different sizes (headline target vs actual committed/first-close) and
different domiciles for the same fund — investigate the discrepancy and record an as-of date and basis
(target, committed, first close) for each figure.
