# Contradiction — Burkina Faso "zéro zone blanche" completion horizon

**Claim in dispute:** the target date by which Burkina Faso's national telecom "white zone" (uncovered
locality) programme will achieve full coverage.

**Competing values:**
- **End-2027.** Asserted by the ministry / programme communications.
  - Source held: https://sidwaya.info — "Services de communications électroniques : 750 zones bientôt couvertes" (2025-09-30)
  - Source held: (Sidwaya) "Couverture des « zones blanches » : la ministre inspecte des sites" (2025-07-25)
  - Source held: (TechAfrica News) "Burkina Faso Targets Zero Telecom White Zones by 2027 as 61 Nakambe Towns Get Connected" (2025-12-18)
- **2030.** Asserted by a programme Director-General speaking at Nando.
  - Source held: https://sidwaya.info — "Le projet de couverture de 750 zones blanches présenté aux acteurs de Nando" (2025-12-09)

Same programme, same period, two completion horizons three years apart — never reconciled in the coverage.

**Instruction:** these sources give different completion dates for the same national white-zone coverage
programme — investigate the discrepancy and suggest a resolution, recording an as-of date for each figure
(e.g. whether 2027 is an optimistic political target and 2030 an operational estimate).
