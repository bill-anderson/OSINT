# TechPark CV: is the project €45.59m, €51.85m, or €45.5m of AfDB financing inside a €51.85m project?

Surfaced 2026-07-20 by lint #7, when the four sources covering the May 2025 TechPark CV
inauguration were read side by side. All four describe the **same facility and the same
inauguration week**, and they give the project's value **three different ways**. A dependent
claim — that the investment is **"nearly 2% of Cabo Verde's GDP"** — reproduces from one of
those figures and not the others, so the wiki currently has a headline framing resting on an
unsettled number.

## Paste-ready research brief

A technology park in a small island state was inaugurated across two campuses in early May 2025,
financed by a multilateral development bank. Three incompatible descriptions of its value are in
circulation, all from coverage of the same week:

1. **"€45.59 million investment from the African Development Bank"** — the whole figure
   attributed to the Bank, with the added claim that it "represents **nearly 2% of Cape Verde's
   GDP**."
2. **"€45.59 million ($51.7 million) investment from the AfDB"** — the same euro figure, with a
   dollar conversion, and the same "nearly 2% of GDP" claim.
3. **"The €51.85 million project, developed in two phases with €45.5 million in African
   Development Bank financing"** — here the AfDB number is a *component* of a larger project
   total, and it is €45.5m, not €45.59m.

A **fourth figure** appears in the same article as (3): the Bank "has provided **$57 million for
Phases 1 and 2**" of the Praia Technology Park. At the exchange rates implied by (2), €45.5m is
about $51.7m, not $57m — so either the $57m covers something wider than the €45.5m, or one of the
two is wrong.

**Questions to settle:**

1. What is the **project's total capital cost**, and what is the **development bank's financing
   contribution** to it? Are €45.59m and €45.5m the same number differently rounded, or two
   different quantities (e.g. approved envelope vs disbursed)?
2. Is the **€51.85m** a project total that includes government and private co-financing on top of
   the Bank's €45.5m? If so, sources (1) and (2) have mislabelled the Bank's contribution as the
   whole project.
3. What does the **$57m for Phases 1 and 2** measure, and at what date/rate? Money should be
   carried in the announcing party's own currency (euro here), with any USD figure written as a
   **dated conversion**.
4. Does **"nearly 2% of GDP"** attach to the ~€45m financing or to the ~€52m project total, and
   against **which year's GDP**? The claim should not be carried at all until its numerator and
   its denominator year are both established.

Prefer the development bank's own **project appraisal and approval documents** over any press
account of them.

## What the wiki holds

Four press accounts, no primary. **No AfDB project appraisal document, board approval or
completion report for TechPark CV is on file** — that absence is part of the finding, and is why
none of the four figures can currently be preferred on evidence rather than on plausibility.
