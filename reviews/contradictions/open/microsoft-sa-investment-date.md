# Contradiction: when was Microsoft's R5.4bn South Africa investment announced?

**Opened:** 2026-07-17 (ZAF·ZMB·ZWE batch)

## The dispute

The **amounts reconcile**; only the **date** conflicts — and the pattern is the one that has already
produced one confirmed error in this corpus (a secondary source's publication date mistaken for the
event date).

- **Claim A — announced on or before 2026-02-09.**
  - Reported as an accomplished joint announcement by **President Ramaphosa and Microsoft's Brad Smith**:
    **R5.4bn** in further investment, on top of **R20.4bn over the past three years**, for South Africa's
    first "enterprise-grade" data centres in Johannesburg and Cape Town.
  - Asserted by: **Daily Maverick** (Tony Carnie), 2026-02-09.
  - Source URL held:
    https://www.dailymaverick.co.za/article/2026-02-09-major-sa-data-centre-expansion-plans-emerge-set-to-more-than-double-current
- **Claim B — 2026-04-17.**
  - **$329 million (~R5.4bn)** expansion, "building on Microsoft's earlier $1.2 billion commitment".
  - Asserted by: Business Insider Africa, held at
    `raw/2026-04-17 Microsoft picks South Africa for $329 million expansion.md`; a companion held item is
    dated 2026-04-15.

## Assessment

The figures line up ($329m ≈ R5.4bn; R20.4bn ≈ $1.1–1.2bn), so this is **one investment, two dates**.
Daily Maverick, published **two months earlier**, treats the announcement as already made — which points
to the held April-2026 date being **the re-report's date rather than the event's**. Both held sources are
secondary.

This is the same failure mode as the Google Johannesburg cloud region, where an AFRIBIT blog's
publication date was ingested as the launch date and sat wrong on the ZAF hub for 14 months (corrected
2026-07-17). It is worth treating as a **class of error**, not a one-off: secondary re-reports of
corporate announcements are routinely ingested with their own publication date.

## What to establish

Microsoft's own release, or a Presidency statement, giving the announcement date and the precise
figures. Then correct the held date if warranted.

## Instruction

These sources date the same Microsoft investment announcement two months apart, with the amounts
agreeing. Establish the announcement date from a Microsoft or Presidency primary and record an as-of
date for each claim.
