# Provenance hunt: do Enugu's 267 smart green schools exist?

**Queued:** 2026-07-20 (migrated from gaps.md)

## The claim resting on a summit remark

- **267 solar-powered, satellite-connected "smart green schools" are Enugu State's flagship
  education-infrastructure programme.**
  - Asserted by: **Commissioner Ndubueze Mbah**, in **one sentence at a supplier-organised summit**, carried
    by a **sponsored TechCabal placement**. The school count is the commissioner's own figure.

This is the largest sub-national digital-education deployment on the wiki's file, and **no budget line,
procurement record, funding source, completion status, timeline, pupil numbers, connectivity vendor or
independent evaluation is held**. A programme of this size ought to leave a documentary trail — a state
budget, contract awards, a donor or development-finance facility.

## Sources held

The sponsored TechCabal placement carrying the commissioner's remark is held. **No state budget, procurement
record, or state announcement of the programme is on file** — that is the provenance gap.

## Instruction

Establish whether this programme exists at the scale claimed: find the state budget line or appropriation,
the procurement or contract awards, the funder, the number of schools actually completed and connected, the
timeline, and the connectivity supplier. Record figures with as-of dates, and distinguish schools planned,
under construction and in service.
