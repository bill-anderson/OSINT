# Contradiction: Algeria data-governance decree number — 25-350 vs 25-320

**Claim in dispute:** the official number of the Algerian presidential decree (published in the Journal
Officiel of **30 December 2025**) that establishes the national framework for the governance of public data.

**Competing values:**

- **Decree n° 25-350** — asserted by We Are Tech (English): "Algeria launches national framework to govern
  public data." URL: https://www.wearetech.africa/en/fils-uk/news/tech/algeria-launches-national-framework-to-govern-public-data
- **Decree n° 25-320** — asserted by two Algerian-press sources, same JO date (30 Dec 2025):
  - El Moudjahid (French state daily), "Décret sur la gouvernance des données : un socle technique prometteur":
    https://www.elmoudjahid.dz/fr/actualite/decret-sur-la-gouvernance-des-donnees-un-socle-technique-prometteur-245982
  - Echorouk (Arabic), unified market-supply information system (cites the same decree): article on
    echoroukonline.com (2026-04-27), held in `raw/2026-04-27-unified-market-supply-monitoring-system-echorouk`.

**Who asserts each:** one English trade outlet (25-350) vs two Algerian national newspapers, one of them the
state daily (25-320). All three agree on the JO date of 30 December 2025 and on the decree's substance.

**Provenance note:** the two 25-320 sources are Algerian-press; the El Moudjahid piece explicitly reads the
decree number off the Journal Officiel. The 25-350 source is a secondary English re-report.

**Instruction:** these sources report different numbers for the same 30-Dec-2025 data-governance decree.
Investigate the discrepancy against the Algerian Journal Officiel (JORADP, 30 Dec 2025) and determine the
correct decree number, recording an as-of date. If 25-320 is confirmed, correct
`wiki/entities/algeria-data-governance-framework.md`, `wiki/intersections/algeria--gov-protect.md`, and the
DZA hub, and rename the entity/title accordingly.
