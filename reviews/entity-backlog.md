# reviews/entity-backlog.md — entity-pass worklist (CC-owned, tier-2)

Standing worklist for the periodic **entity pass** (CLAUDE.md → *Entities: tag
always, page when material*). Every item here is an entity, resource or
instrument that is **already tagged** in `raw/` frontmatter and referenced in
prose, but has **no page** — and that the ingest batches judged material enough
to warrant one.

**These are not gaps.** A gap is a research lead for Bill to consider sourcing.
These are page-minting jobs the written rules already determine — CC actions
them in the entity pass and itemises them in the weekly digest. Nothing here
waits on a human decision. Where an item genuinely needs a *primary* before a
page can be written honestly, it also appears in `acquisitions.md`; where it
needs identity/role confirmation, in `contradictions/queued/`.

> Migrated out of `reviews/gaps.md` on 2026-07-20 (register triage — see
> `log.md`). Original wording and provenance are in git history of `gaps.md`.
